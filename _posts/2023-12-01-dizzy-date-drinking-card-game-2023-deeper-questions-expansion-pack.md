## Gameplay and House Rules: How It Shimmies on Your Table

If you’ve played the base DIZZY DATE, you know the rhythm: draw a card, read the prompt, answer honestly, and if your answer gets a nod or a chuckle, you assign a couple of sips or take a tiny consequence. The 2023 expansion keeps that tempo intact while injecting more personality into every turn. Here’s a quick mental model of how it works:

1) Set up: Shuffle the expansion deck with the base game. The more players, the better; the game thrives on a chorus of voices (and a chorus of groans when someone realizes they’ve triggered a confession they didn’t intend to share).
2) Turn order: On your turn, you draw a deeper-question card. You either answer or politely sidestep, but remember: sidestepping might come with a playful penalty from the table—think “truth or toast.”
3) Drinking rules: If you answer, you decide whether to assign sips to yourself, the person you’re aiming at, or the world at large (dramatic, but sometimes necessary). If you dodge, you must take a light drink or perform a silly dare. The expansion adds more ways to drink—sometimes an augmented consequence that makes you reconsider washing your shirt, or maybe your mouth, after that spicy prompt.

Pro tip: You can customize the drink rule to align with your group’s vibe. Want a slower pace and more conversation? Limit the number of sips. Feeling rowdy and dramatic? Go full chaotic by letting the deck decide your destiny every other round.
