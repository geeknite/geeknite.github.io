## Maintenance matrix: keeping the look fresh without turning into a full maintenance mode

Maintenance is real life, not a concept. Here is a practical matrix for keeping the rug looking good without sacrificing your time:

- Daily/weekly: light vacuum, quick spot checks for crumbs or spills.
- Monthly: rotate the rug to ensure even wear, vacuum with the pile in the same direction.
- Quarterly: consider deep cleaning or professional cleaning if you have stubborn stains or if your space is prone to spills from hobbies or experiments.
- Seasonal checks: inspect the edges for fraying and address promptly to prevent further unraveling.
