**Greetings, miniature painters and Warhammer enthusiasts!** Today, we dive deep into the world of Citadel paints, focusing on the illustrious Reikland Fleshshade Gloss. Is this the magical potion your miniatures have been waiting for, or just another bottle gathering dust on your shelf? Let's find out!

## What is Reikland Fleshshade Gloss?

Reikland Fleshshade Gloss is a shading paint from Citadel's vast range of hobby supplies. Designed to add depth and realism to your miniatures, this specific shade gives a glossy finish, making it perfect for areas that need a little extra shine.

## First Impressions

Upon opening the pot, you're greeted with a rich, reddish-brown hue. The consistency is smooth, flowing easily off the brush. It's like liquid caramel for your minis, but please, resist the temptation to taste it.

## Application and Use

### On Miniatures

Applying Reikland Fleshshade Gloss to your miniatures is like watching magic happen. It flows into the recesses, accentuating the details and making your hard work pop. Whether you're shading armor, skin, or other surfaces, this paint delivers.

### Drying Time

One of the standout features of this shade is its quick drying time. In about 20-30 minutes, your miniatures are ready for the next step. No more waiting around, twiddling your thumbs, or binge-watching half a season of your favorite show.

### The Gloss Effect

The gloss finish is a game-changer. It adds a shine that makes metals gleam and skin look more lifelike. It's like giving your miniatures a fresh coat of awesome.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Easy to Apply**: Even if you're a beginner, you'll find this paint forgiving and straightforward.
- **Quick Drying**: No more long waits. Get back to painting in no time.
- **Versatile**: Great on a variety of surfaces, adding depth and realism.
- **Glossy Finish**: Perfect for metals, armors, and anything that needs a bit of shine.

### Cons

- **Gloss Not Always Desired**: The gloss finish might not suit every painter's taste or every part of the model.
- **Price**: As with many Citadel paints, it's a bit on the pricey side.

## Tips and Tricks

- **Thin Coats**: Apply in thin coats to avoid pooling and achieve the best results.
- **Mix with Matte Medium**: If you love the color but not the gloss, mix it with a matte medium to tone down the shine.
- **Experiment**: Don't be afraid to experiment! Try it on different types of surfaces and with different base coats.

## Comparisons

### Reikland Fleshshade (Matte) vs. Reikland Fleshshade Gloss

While the matte version is great for a more understated look, the gloss version excels in making certain areas stand out. Use them together for a dynamic effect.

### Other Shades

Compared to other Citadel shades like Agrax Earthshade or Nuln Oil, Reikland Fleshshade Gloss has a more vibrant hue and, of course, the glossy finish. Each has its place in your toolkit, depending on the effect you're aiming for.

## Final Verdict

Reikland Fleshshade Gloss is a fantastic addition to any miniature painter's collection. Its ease of use, quick drying time, and glossy finish make it a versatile and valuable tool for adding depth and realism to your models. While it may not be the cheapest option, the results speak for themselves.

So, should you buy it? Absolutely. Whether you're a seasoned painter or just starting, Reikland Fleshshade Gloss will undoubtedly enhance your painting arsenal.
