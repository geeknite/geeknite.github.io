## The Basics

### What is Pocket Battle Game: Cherry Blossoms at Night?

Pocket Battle Game: Cherry Blossoms at Night is a tactical board game where players engage in strategic battles under the serene beauty of cherry blossoms. This game combines elements of strategy, luck, and beautiful artwork to create an immersive experience. Whether you're a seasoned strategist or a newcomer, this game has something to offer for everyone.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures representing different factions and units. These are so detailed that you might feel like a general commanding a real army.
- **Cards**: A wide array of cards that represent actions, abilities, and events. These add a layer of unpredictability and excitement to each game.
- **Game Board**: A stunningly illustrated board featuring cherry blossoms and various terrains. It's so pretty you might want to frame it.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track resources, health, and various effects during the game.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Pocket Battle Game: Cherry Blossoms at Night is like preparing for a historical reenactment. Lay out the game board, distribute the miniatures and cards, and choose your faction. Once everything is in place, you're ready to engage in battle.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their units and abilities. You can move, attack, use special abilities, and more. The game encourages strategic thinking and planning, so don't rush your decisions.

### Combat

Combat in Pocket Battle Game: Cherry Blossoms at Night is a blend of strategy and luck. Each unit has its own strengths and weaknesses, and players must use their abilities wisely to gain the upper hand. Did you plan an epic attack just to have your opponent counter it perfectly? Welcome to the battlefield, where anything can happen.
