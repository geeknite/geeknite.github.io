### Cleaning and care tips

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers. If your vacuum has a beater bar, switch to hard floor or low-pile mode to prevent snagging.
- For spills, blot—don’t rub. Blotting helps prevent the stain from seeping deeper into the fibers and turning your living room into a crime scene with a color palette that’s equally questionable.
- Treat stubborn stains with a mild soap solution and test in a discreet corner first. If the stain persists, a professional cleaning service is never a bad idea, especially if your schedule is busier than a microprocessor on overclock.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and minimize the sitcom-style “spotlight” effect that happens when a cat sits in one spot for too long.
- Use a rug pad to extend life, reduce movement, and improve comfort underfoot. Pads are cheap insurance against curling edges and 3 AM shuffles to the kitchen that end in a dramatic trip over the rug’s border.
