## In depth: how the kit plays

### Opening scenes and pacing

Fortune Row is designed to help you open with a strong scene that pins a mythic theme to a real city problem. The prompts are short and punchy, which means you can drop into a scene quickly without spending long minutes describing the setting. The pacing benefits from the deck approach because it gives you a ready supply of beats that you can weave into a session without losing momentum. A typical Fortune Row session might begin with a street level problem such as a storefront that suddenly hosts a film reel from a past life, or a sudden surge of a power from a character who is supposed to be just a normal citizen. The prompt then assigns a mythic layer and a motive, guiding the players toward the investigative arc that City of Mist thrives on.

### NPCs and their agendas

The NPC deck is a clever touch because these characters carry an agenda that can collide or align with the investigators. The cards present a short description, a goal, and a tie to the mythic dynamics of the city. A well used NPC can become a recurring figure across scenes, giving the investigators a sense of a living city rather than a one shot set piece. The danger with many prompts is that they feel static or generic. Fortune Row avoids that trap by coloring each NPC with a personal hook that can become a pivot point in the story.

### Thematic hooks and moral tension

If you enjoy the noir mood and the mythic overlays, Fortune Row offers hooks that maintain tension without becoming preachy or overbearing. The prompts lean into themes like power vs vulnerability, memory and identity, and the price of knowing too much in a city where myth hides in plain sight. The moral questions are not blunt or didactic, which suits City of Mist players who prefer to decide the ethical stakes rather than have the GM declare them. You will find prompts that push players toward choices that feel risky and consequential, which is where the game shines when the dice cooperate.

## How to integrate Fortune Row with the base game

The base City of Mist rules rely on a blend of investigative work, mythic symbolism, and a flexible narrative system that emphasizes character and themes. Fortune Row integrates with it by feeding your sessions with ready made prompts that align with this structure. You can drop a prompt into a scene, assign a mythic force to it, and use the NPCs to populate the social landscape of the district you are exploring. The synergy with City of Mist core material is the sense of myth revealed through everyday life, and Fortune Row gives you a dense supply of micro prompts to keep that reveal moving forward.
