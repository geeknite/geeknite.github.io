## Material Texture and Comfort

The carpet is not a plush cloud, nor is it a gravel path. It sits in the middle ground, offering reliable underfoot comfort for daily use. ### Maintenance and Cleaning

Spill management is the core of rug care. A quick blot of spilled liquids usually suffices; for tougher stains, mild detergent with water and light scrubbing helps lift the color without damaging the fibers. Regular vacuuming is recommended to keep the pile looking uniform and to reduce embedded dirt. For deeper cleanings, consider professional services once or twice a year, depending on the household traffic and whether you host a coffee-nerd convention weekly in your living room.

## Extra Geek Tips

- Care for long corridors: In rooms with multiple entry points, consider rotating the rug slightly every few months to even out wear patterns and avoid obvious foot-traffic lines.

- Pet odor control: For homes with pets, a quick baking-soda sprinkle after a vacuum can help neutralize odors between deeper cleanings.
