## What is the Gamemaster Modular Terrain Set?

The Gamemaster Modular Terrain Set is a collection of pre-made terrain pieces that allow you to create customized landscapes for your tabletop games. Whether you're running a D&D campaign, a Warhammer battle, or just want to impress your friends with your miniature architecture skills, this set has got you covered. It includes various pieces such as walls, floors, roofs, and even adorable little bushes.

### Components

The terrain set comes with:
- **Various Terrain Pieces**: Walls, floors, roofs, stairs, and more. All pieces are designed to be modular, allowing endless combinations.
- **Detailed Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures that add realism and depth to your terrain.
- **Instruction Booklet**: Filled with tips and tricks to make the most out of your modular pieces.
- **Storage Box**: Keeps all your pieces organized and ready for the next adventure.

**Pro Tip**: Don’t let your cat near the miniatures. They might think it’s a new toy and you’ll find your dungeon scattered across the living room.

## How to Use the Terrain Set

### Setup

Setting up the Gamemaster Modular Terrain Set is like assembling IKEA furniture but without the existential dread. Lay out the pieces, follow the instructions, and let your creativity flow. The modular nature means you can rearrange and rebuild as many times as you want.

### Building Your Landscape

Each turn, players can add or modify terrain pieces to create the desired landscape. Whether you're constructing a medieval castle or a futuristic space station, the modular pieces allow you to tailor the environment to your storyline.

### Interaction

The terrain set isn't just for show; it offers interactive features. Players can move miniatures through doors, hide behind walls, or climb stairs to reach higher ground.
