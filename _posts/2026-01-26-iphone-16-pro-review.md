## Design and Build Quality

The iPhone 16 Pro boasts a sleek and elegant design that Apple is famous for. With its unibody aluminum frame and ceramic shield front cover, it's both premium and durable. It's lighter and thinner than its predecessors, making it a perfect companion for those who are always on the go.

![iPhone 16 Pro Design](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/design__c2u6xn6rvs2u_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}iphone-16-pro-design)

### Ports and Connectivity

One of the most significant changes in the iPhone 16 Pro is the return of the much-loved headphone jack. Yes, you heard it right! Apple has finally listened to our cries and brought back the headphone jack. Alongside it, you'll find a USB-C port, making charging and connectivity a breeze.

## Camera and Photography

![iPhone 16 Pro Camera](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/camera__f5dhn0m5h16m_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}camera)

## Software and Features

The iPhone 16 Pro comes with iOS 20, packed with new features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and a redesigned Control Center. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the iPhone stand out from the competition.
