### What’s inside the bundle

The new sealed edition promises nothing but pure unadulterated cringe, which, in CAH terms, is basically a guarantee of entertainment.

- Core deck: A generous pile of white cards featuring prompts like the standard we all know and love, “Why can’t I get a date on a Friday night?” or “The only thing more chaotic than a group chat at 2am is…” These are your launch pads for unfiltered humor, often requiring players to lean into pop culture references you hope nobody forgets you referenced.

## The Geeknite hosting kit: tips to maximize the bundle experience

- Prep like a pro: Before the first round, declare a few ground rules and a “save the thin-skinned” card that lets players opt out of particular prompts. This small step can keep the vibes positive while still allowing for bold, hilarious interactions.
- Loot the prompt diversity: Start with prompts that reference broad pop culture; rotate to more niche references as players get warmed up. This helps everyone contribute and keeps the energy high.
- Use the card-holder trick: If you’ve got a modest collection of games, a card holder (or even a shoebox with dividers) can help you manage the red and white cards without losing track of which deck is which during a heated laugh-fest.
- Tempo matters: Don’t let the game stall between rounds. A quick buzzer round or a rotating judge role can keep jokes fresh and prevent the energy from flattening out.
- End on a high note: A closing round with a theme prompt that everyone knows—like a favorite movie line or a beloved meme—can leave the group with a perfect last moment of shared humor.
