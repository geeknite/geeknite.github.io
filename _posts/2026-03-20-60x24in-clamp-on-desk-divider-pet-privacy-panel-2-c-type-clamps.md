## Installation and setup
Setting this up is about as straightforward as a good tutorial gets. The kit includes the panel, two C-Type clamps, washers, and the little sheet of instructions that makes sense after a moment’s thought and maybe a cup of coffee.

### Step-by-step guide
1) Clean the desk edge where the clamps will sit. Dust and grime are the arch-nemeses of grip strength.
2) Position the clamps on the desk edge where you want the panel. The clamps are adjustable along the edge, so you can fine-tune the positioning.
3) Tighten the clamps slowly. There is a temptation to crank it to 11, but you should stop at a point where the desk edge feels pinned but not crushed.
4) Slide the panel into the clamps. Align the top edge with your eye line and ensure there is no interference with monitor stands or cable management.
5) Check for wobble and adjust as needed. If there is too much movement, slide the clamps a bit closer together or reposition along the edge.

Tip: If your desk edge has rounded corners or a bevel, you might need to angle the panel slightly to achieve a flush fit. The system is designed for flexibility, not for magic, so a little tweaking goes a long way.
