## Maintenance tips to keep it ping-pong-ready for years

- Clean after use: wipe down the surface with a damp cloth to remove dust, pollen, and the occasional stray leaf. The outdoors collects the little things, and little things multiply if ignored.
- Dry it properly: never store the table while moist. Drying reduces the risk of mold and corrosion on the metal parts.
- Inspect the net: over time, nets stretch. If the tension dips, adjust clamps or swap the net to maintain a tidy string of rallies.
- Check the fold mechanism: periodically test the folding action to ensure the lock pins are seating properly. If you hear a squeak or feel looseness, a light lubrication can keep the mechanism smooth.
- Weather protection: if you live in a climate with heavy rain or intense sun, consider a dedicated cover to extend the top’s life. A cover protects the color, texture, and integrity of the playing surface and aids in keeping that crisp bounce.
