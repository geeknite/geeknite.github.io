## Overview

The Local Legends Folio Pack 2 is a follow-up to the first Local Legends release, which introduced a cadre of city legends, update-friendly playbooks, and printable aids designed to streamline sessions focused on urban myths and everyday miracles. The new pack expands the roster, introduces fresh character sheets, and augments the GM toolbox with more scenarios, lore hooks, and encounter prompts. In other words: more myth for the mundane world, more ways to tell stories with your friends, and more excuses to say I have to go to the city and save a myth from itself.

## What’s inside Local Legends Folio Pack 2

This section covers the tangible goods you’ll find in the pack. While the exact page count can vary by printing batch, the essence stays the same: you’re getting a smorgasbord of local legend flavored content that slots neatly into your existing City of Mist table. Expect the following core ingredients:

### Character Folios and Templates
- A fresh slate of Local Legends character sheets designed for quick character generation and streamlined play.
- Updated stat blocks and stunt templates to fit the local myths flavor without bogging down the narrative flow.
- Design polish that feels welcoming to new players while offering enough flavor for veterans who love micro-dactions and social moves.

These folios aren’t just “blank character sheets.” They come with compact bios, suggested relationships, and a few anchor myth aspects that help players decide how their legends would respond in a given scenario. The result is a faster onboarding process for new players and a richer palette for longtime tables who want to throw a new local legend into play without reinventing the wheel.

### Local Legends Cards and Lore Aids
- A set of lore cards that outline the origin myths, neighborhood quirks, and the key NPCs that populate the legends you’ll pursue.
- Quick prompts to trigger roleplay beats, ideal for when your party needs a little nudge toward a dramatic moment or a comedic beat.
- Lore hooks that are simple to drop into sessions but hefty enough to inspire entire arcs if you want to run a localized mini-arc.

The Local Legends in Pack 2 lean into the “city as character” vibe. They’re not just procedural foes; they’re living, breathing legends that can respond to your party’s approach—whether the team leans on charm, cunning, or masked bravado. The prompts are short, punchy, and ready to be dropped into your session, which in my book is exactly what you want when you’ve only got a couple of hours before the next rule check and a looming snack deadline.

### GM Aids and Quick Reference Sheets
- A compact GM screen substitute: quick references for Stunts, Masks, and Conditions that matter in a scene.
- Encounter prompts that help you lay out a local mystery with clear, escalating stakes.
- Optional rules additions that won’t derail your table but add texture to the legend play style.

If you GM regularly, you’ll appreciate how these aids shave minutes off prep and keep the momentum through the middle act, where things can get bogged down in exposition or a surprising lack of coffee. The aesthetic of the pack is a favor to the GM’s brain: clean typography, legible charts, and a layout that tells you what you need to know without forcing you to solve a cryptogram on the page.

### Art, Layout, and Accessibility
- Polished art that continues the noir-meets-myth vibe without straining the eyes or the wallet.
- A consistent layout that helps players locate the information they need during a session, which means less time squinting at the sheet and more time pretending to be a myth on a Monday afternoon.

The visual language is a big win here. City of Mist leans into stylized art that complements the game’s tone, and Folio Pack 2 respects that lineage while amping up the polish. If you’ve invested time in running co-op scenes, city-centric capers, or mystery-tinged drama, you’ll find the art and layout amplify the mood rather than clash with it.

## How it plays on the table

The real measure of a folio pack is how readily it slots into a campaign’s momentum. Folio Pack 2 does not promise a complete rewrite of your table’s routine; it offers a toolbox you can pull from. Here are some play scenarios and how they feel with the new content:

- Local intrigue around a neighborhood myth: The new legends provide a compact cast of supporting characters with defined relationships. You can spin a mystery in under 20 minutes with a few prompts from the cards, then lean into the moral ambiguity that City of Mist does so well.
- Focus on a single myth with social emphasis: The stunts and masks support social maneuvering, so you can stage a tense negotiation where the legend’s desire clashes with the players’ goals. It’s a good reminder that in City of Mist, the heart of combat is often conversation.
- Quick one-shot side quest: The GM aids and quick reference sheets make it easy to slot a short, self-contained arc into a regular session night, ensuring you don’t derail a longer story to explain a power interaction.

The balance between narrative freedom and mechanical clarity is the pack’s strongest suit. If your tables enjoy a mix of drama, mystery, and light caper energy, Folio Pack 2 arrives with the right vibe at the right time.

## Design, aesthetics, and accessibility on the table

The design language of Folio Pack 2 is consistent with City of Mist’s core identity: moody, urban, a little smoky with neon halo lighting. That’s not just flair; it helps the players and GM stay in the right headspace. The folio’s typography supports quick scanning during combat and scene changes, which matters when you want to keep the tempo up and the table engaged.

Accessibility is a major win here. Larger headers and higher-contrast panels mean players who might struggle with small print or low-contrast visuals can still enjoy the content without a magnifying glass and a strong will to persevere. The character sheets read clearly, with essential fields obvious at a glance. This is not a fancy coffee-table book; it’s a toolbox built for gaming sessions that happen after work with pizza boxes still on the table.

In practice, I found the visual cues and layout choices particularly helpful for new players. The local legends’ backstories are not buried in blocks of text; they’re presented in digestible snippets, enabling players to onboard quickly, ask the right questions, and start weaving their legend into the city’s fabric within a session or two.

## GMing with Local Legends Folio Pack 2: tips and tricks

- Start small: Use one Local Legend as a focal point for a two-hour session; let the cards and prompts guide the narrative, and resist the urge to force a grand myth arc. Local legends work best when they feel tactile and personal to the players’ choices.
- Use the quick reference sheets to keep pace: Let the GM aids do the heavy lifting on the fly. You don’t need to memorize every power interaction; you just need to know where to glance for the essential line of play.
- Leverage the lore cards as a social engine: They’re designed to spark roleplay and moral tension. If you’re running a mystery, use a card to provoke a belief conflict within the party or between the party and a major non-player legend.
- Customize with care: The sheets are a framework, not a jail cell. If a Legend needs a tweak to fit your campaign’s tone, feel free to adjust the backstory or the relationships. The core ideas are portable across city scales, so adapt and keep the vibe you love.
- Narration beats over dice: City of Mist rewards descriptive narration. When a Legend acts, describe not just the mechanical outcome but the mythic consequence. The Folio Pack 2 nudges you toward that storytelling sweet spot by giving you vivid prompts and evocative stunts to lean into.

## How it integrates with the core rules

The Local Legends Folio Pack 2 is designed to complement the core City of Mist rules rather than replace them. It expands the mythic toolkit, allowing GMs to quickly populate a scene with ready-to-play legends while still letting players mold the story through masks, myths, and social maneuvers.

- Archetypes and masks align with the existing framework, offering alternative templates that can slot into the same mechanics without a steep learning curve.
- The lore and prompt cards sit alongside the existing GM prompts and scene hooks. They function best when used as accelerators for creativity rather than as rigid scripts.
- Expect smooth onboarding for new players, especially those who appreciate a strong sense of mood and place. Veteran players will enjoy the density of flavor, the quick-hit prompts, and the visual language that helps you narrate a living city in a single session.

If you are a completionist or a chronic worldbuilder, you may find yourself tempted to assemble a full local legends arc from the pack. That’s not a negative; it’s a testament to the materials’ strength. However, remember that the city your players inhabit can still be chaotic and alive even if you keep your focus tight on one or two legends per session.
