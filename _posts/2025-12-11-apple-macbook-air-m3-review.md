The MacBook Air M3 is as slim as your patience when your code refuses to compile. With a thickness of just 0.63 inches and weighing only 2.8 pounds, it's the perfect companion for geeks on the go. ## Performance: M3 Chip - The New Kid on the Block

### A CPU That Packs a Punch

Under the hood, the MacBook Air M3 features Apple's latest M3 chip. Boasting a 12-core CPU and 16-core GPU, this baby is designed to handle everything from video editing to conquering virtual realms in the latest games. It's like having a tiny supercomputer in your backpack.

![M3 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/m3__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: .align-right}

### Battery Life to Beat

Apple claims up to 18 hours of battery life on a single charge. In our real-world tests, the MacBook Air M3 easily lasted through a full day of intense coding, video calls, and a Netflix binge. Say goodbye to carrying around your charger like a nomad.

## Keyboard and Trackpad: Typing Bliss

## Software: macOS Monterey

![macOS Monterey](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/macos__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: .align-right}

Running on macOS Monterey, the MacBook Air M3 offers a seamless and intuitive user experience. Features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and the newly redesigned Safari browser make multitasking a breeze. Plus, the integration with other Apple devices is as smooth as ever.
