## Cleaning and maintenance

### Routine care
Light vacuuming on a regular schedule is the core maintenance ritual. This is not a rug that requires specialized cleaners or hero-level dedicated routines. A quick pass with a standard vacuum keeps it looking sharp and avoids the dreaded pie plate effect of crumbs rolling around under your desk.

### Stains and spills
For most spills, blot immediately with a clean cloth or paper towel. If you’re dealing with something more stubborn like coffee or juice, a mild cleaning solution is sufficient. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals that could degrade the fibers or alter the color balance. After cleaning, let it air dry flat to maintain the flush, even surface that gives the Glacier its clean look.

### Cleaning frequency and longevity
With proper care, you can maintain the rug’s appearance for years. Synthetic fibers generally resist wear and tear better than natural fibers when exposed to the usual daily grind of a busy home office. The Mesa Glacier is designed to be resilient, but it is not indestructible. Treat it with a bit of respect, and it will return the favor by looking neat and feeling comfortable.
