### Care, Storage, and Display Tips
Storing vintage cards requires love, not punishment. Keep them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight; UV light can fade the vibrant blue in the long run. A display case with a small, dust-free environment is ideal for collectors who want their Blue 808 gleaming for display nights.

If you’re planning to handle the deck for play, consider using a guard or a sleeve for protection, especially if you intend to pass the cards around. Nitro or alcohol-free cleansers are rarely necessary; a simple microfiber cloth will do the trick for surface dust. Do not apply oils or solvents; vintage decks hate solvent fingerprints nearly as much as villains hate bedtime stories.
