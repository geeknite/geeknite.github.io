## What is Dreams and Machines RPG: Gamemaster’s Toolkit?

Dreams and Machines RPG: Gamemaster’s Toolkit is an essential add-on for anyone looking to run a campaign in the Dreams and Machines universe. This toolkit provides gamemasters with everything they need to create an immersive and engaging experience for their players. From detailed world-building guides to quirky NPCs and intricate plot devices, this toolkit has it all.

The toolkit comes with a treasure trove of goodies, including:
- **World-Building Guides**: Comprehensive guides to help you create a rich and detailed world where dreams and machines coexist.

- **Plot Devices**: Intriguing plot twists and devices to keep your players on their toes.

## Setting Up Your Campaign

Setting up a campaign in the Dreams and Machines universe requires a bit of prep work, but fear not! The toolkit provides all the resources you need to get started. ### World-Building

Creating a rich and immersive world is key to a successful campaign. The world-building guides in the toolkit offer detailed descriptions and suggestions to help you shape your universe. Whether you're crafting a bustling metropolis or a serene village, these guides will provide the inspiration you need.

## Running the Game

### NPCs and Plot Devices

The NPC profiles and plot devices are the heart of any campaign. With the toolkit’s diverse array of characters, you'll have no trouble keeping your players engaged. From the enigmatic inventor to the mischievous dream weaver, each NPC brings a unique flavor to the game.

### Gamemaster Tips

The toolkit includes a section dedicated to gamemaster tips and tricks, ensuring that your campaign runs smoothly. From managing player expectations to handling unexpected twists, these tips are invaluable.
