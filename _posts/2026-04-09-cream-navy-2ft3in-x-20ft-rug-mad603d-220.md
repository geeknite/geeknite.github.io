## Maintenance and Longevity

Keeping a long runner like this in top shape requires routine care. Here is a simple maintenance checklist you can follow:
- Vacuum regularly with a beater bar set to a gentle setting to avoid fiber crush.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear across the hallway path.
- Use an underlay to prevent slipping and to add a layer of cushion under heavy foot traffic.
- Attend to spills quickly with blotting rather than rubbing to prevent circular stains.
- Consider a professional cleaning every 12 to 18 months depending on traffic and exposure to sunlight.

Sunlight is a color thief by nature. If your space channels a lot of afternoon sun, it is wise to use window treatments to protect the navy accents from fading over time. The goal is to retain the rug’s bold presence over the years while maintaining practical maintenance routines.
