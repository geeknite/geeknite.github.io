## Practical Tips: How to Open Without Ruining the Moment
If your plan is to eventually open this deck (it’s a perfectly valid plan), you’ll want to approach like a time capsule curator, not a demolition crew:

- Gather the right tools: a bone folder, soft gloves, and a calm demeanor. The goal is a clean unsealing that preserves the tuck and keeps the edges pristine.
- Plan your cut: Don’t tear into the packaging the moment you receive it. Inspect the seal, gauge the glue, and imagine the joy of unveiling without tearing a corner of the box that would diminish its value.
- Preserve the box: If possible, use a slipcase or acrylic display stand to minimize handling while you admire the packaging. A glass case is overkill for most living rooms, but hey, if you’ve got the shelf space, it’s a dramatic touch.
- Open with care: If you do open, consider letting the inner wax loosen with a gentle, methodical push rather than a sharp tear. This preserves the box’s integrity and makes the opening feel cinematic rather than chaotic.
