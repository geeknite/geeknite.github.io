## Preservation Tips: Keeping the Magic Alive
### Storage Tips That Won’t Break Your Heart
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and warping.
- Use acid-free storage sleeves for the cards if you’re planning to expose the box to air for any reason (this is optional for sealed sets, of course).
- Keep the box in an upright position to avoid warping or crushing the lid. Weight on top can lead to a creased spine, which hurts both display value and shelf life.
- Avoid humidity spikes. The 1995 stock isn’t exactly built to withstand multi-decade moisture attacks, so consider a dehumidifier if your environment has humidity problems.
- If you enjoy playing with the sealed set, consider opening it carefully and then keeping the opened components in sleeves or a small storage box to preserve the cards’ edges.

### Display vs. Play: Strike a Balance
If your goal is display, a clean, restored box on a shelf with a glass door is a quintessential Geeknite vibe. If your goal is play, you’ll need to decide whether you’re comfortable handling a sealed copy or if you’ll extract the cards and use a more robust modern surface to preserve the cards between rounds. Either way, the joy of discovering a 1995 Seal continues to be a whisper of the past that can be revisited with every shuffle and every joyful exhale when a perfect sequence lands on the table.
