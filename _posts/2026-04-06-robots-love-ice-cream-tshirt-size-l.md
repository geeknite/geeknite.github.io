### Care and Longevity Tips

- Wash cold, with similar colors, inside-out to protect the print.
- Avoid high-temperature drying; air-dry or tumble on low heat.
- Store folded in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fade.
- If the shirt begins to look a little stiff after the first few wears, a gentle wash and a quick heat-free stretch can help restore softness.
