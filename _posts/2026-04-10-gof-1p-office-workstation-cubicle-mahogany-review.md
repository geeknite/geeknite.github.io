## Assembly Experience

If you love a good DIY adventure and occasional algebra of hardware, you’ll enjoy the assembly process. The GOF 1P isn’t a one-person puzzle, but it’s not a full-on Tetris tournament either. A calm afternoon and a helper can get you from box to battlestation in roughly 2 to 4 hours, depending on your patience with aligning panels and your willingness to reject the first two suggested screw positions and remeasure for the third time. The printed instructions are readable, with line drawings that provide enough context to prevent grave misalignment crimes. Here is a quick assembly checklist to keep you sane:

- Inventory check: confirm you have all A and B bags, the main frame rails, desk panels, and the screws that will inevitably roll under the couch and vanish for weeks.
- Build base first: align the steel frame, secure the cross braces, and make sure you can stand the whole thing up without it flopping like a fish.
- Attach walls and desk: secure the cubicle walls to the frame, ensuring the desk surface is level and balanced across its wide horizon.
- Cable management: thread cables and set up the grommets before you forget and create the world’s most inspirational mess.
- Final check: test the height adjustments across 4', 5', 6' settings to verify the locks hold firm and the surface remains stable when you lean on it for emphasis during a video call.

In practical terms, the assembly is a weekend project for true furniture enthusiasts — or a late Friday sprint for those who enjoy the dramatic adrenaline of a project completed just before quitting time. Once assembled, the GOF 1P feels solid and substantial, like a platform ready to host your digital empire.
