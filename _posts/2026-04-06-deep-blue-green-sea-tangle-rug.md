## Durability and Maintenance
Durability is a key conversation when you’re evaluating a rug for tech-laden spaces. The rug is designed to handle the typical wear of foot traffic, spilled coffee, and the occasional pet who mistakes it for a new floor mat. The PET content makes it resilient to color fading and easy to spot clean. For spills, a quick blot with a clean cloth and water should suffice; for more stubborn stains, a mild detergent solution and a gentle rinse does the trick. Avoid harsh chemical solvents that could degrade the fiber over time.

Cleaning is straightforward: regular vacuuming with a brush attachment keeps the fibers aligned and prevents the sea tangle look from turning into a static nest. For heavy use areas, consider a rug pad to reduce wear and improve insulation. The pad also helps prevent couch legs from snagging and turning a calm coastal vibe into a moody garage band aesthetic.
