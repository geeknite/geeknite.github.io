## Maintenance: Keeping Your BT01 Playmat Fresh

### Cleaning and Care
A quick wipe with a damp microfiber cloth after a long day is usually enough to prevent residue from building up. For more stubborn marks, a mild soap solution works, but avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade the surface texture or dull the print. Let the mat air-dry completely before rolling it away. Rolling helps maintain its flat shape and prevents curling at the edges over time. If your mat has a stubborn crease after transport, lay it flat under a light book stack for a few hours and you should be back to operational glory.

### Storage and Transport
When you travel with your BT01 mat, keep it in a protective sleeve or a dedicated mat tube. The last thing you want is a crumpled art piece turning into a treasure map of creases. A rolled storage approach is favored by tournament players who want to keep their surface pristine and free of the “festival of coffee spills” look that can sometimes plague event setups.

### Long-Term Longevity
If you’re a daily gamer who uses mats year-round, BT01’s longevity is generally decent, with color fidelity holding up across multiple sessions. The print and surface do not appear to wear down quickly with repeated use and cleanings. A well-maintained BT01 mat should remain visually coherent for many tournaments and casual sessions to come.
