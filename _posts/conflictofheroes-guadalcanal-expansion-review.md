## The Basics

### What is Conflict of Heroes: Guadalcanal - Expansión Ejército y Marines?

Conflict of Heroes: Guadalcanal - Expansión Ejército y Marines is an expansion to the popular board game *Conflict of Heroes*. This expansion brings new scenarios, units, and rules that focus on the brutal battles between the U.S. Marines and the Japanese Army during World War II on the Solomon Islands.

### Components

The expansion includes:

- **New Maps**: Detailed maps representing the treacherous terrain of Guadalcanal. Remember, it's not just a walk in the park.
- **Unit Counters**: Beautifully designed counters for the U.S. Marines and Japanese Army. The artwork is so good, you'll want to frame them.
- **Scenario Book**: A booklet with various historical scenarios to test your strategic skills.
- **Rule Additions**: New rules to enhance the base game experience. Because let's face it, we all love more rules, right?

**Pro Tip**: Keep the rulebook handy. You might need it to solve disputes and avoid fistfights.
