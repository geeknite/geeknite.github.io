## Maintenance Tips for Maximum Fluffiness
- Regularly shake out the nap bed to reset the fur pile and prevent matting in high traffic spots.
- Run a quick vacuum on the fur surface to remove pet hair, dust, and the occasional crumb that dares to trespass.
- If you have a stubborn stain, spot clean with a mild detergent and air dry fully before the next nap. Do not tumble dry on high heat; fluffing is best achieved with natural air drying.
- Rotate the bed every so often to ensure even wear on the fur and padding.
- Keep a spare cover ready for deep cleans. It is never a bad idea to have a backup nap fortress in case life throws you a coffee flood or a lasagna disaster.

## Cleaning Guide: Step by Step
1) Unzip the cover and remove the inner shell. 2) Put the outer fur shell in a gentle wash cycle with cold water. 3) Use a mild detergent designed for delicate fabrics. 4) Air dry or tumble on low heat with a few dryer balls to maintain fluff. 5) Reassemble when completely dry and enjoy the fresh nap energy.

If you want a deeper clean, you can toss the entire bed into a front-loading machine for a thorough rinse, followed by a thorough drying cycle. Just be mindful of the weight distribution when the bed is wet. It can become a portable laundry item if you wring it like a towel, which you should not do, unless you want to invent a new sport called wet-napping.
