## The Basics

### What is Codex: Card-Time Strategy?

Codex is a real-time strategy card game that combines elements of deck-building with tactical combat. Think of it as if Magic: The Gathering and Starcraft had a baby, and that baby was raised by a pack of wild board gamers. The game is designed by Sirlin Games and offers a unique twist on traditional card games by incorporating real-time strategy elements into the mix.

### Components

The core set comes with cards representing different factions, heroes, and units. You'll also find various tokens, playmats, and a robust rule book that might double as a weapon in a pinch. The artwork is visually stunning, showcasing a diverse array of characters and fantasy elements.

## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Codex is like preparing for a grand battle. Each player chooses a faction, assembles their deck, and sets up their playmat. Expect to spend some time strategizing and planning your moves before the game even begins.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards, play units, and use their heroes' abilities. The game progresses in phases, where players can attack, defend, and build up their resources. The flexibility and strategic depth of Codex shine through in the variety of actions you can take.

### Combat

Combat in Codex is a mix of tactical planning and occasional luck. Units clash, abilities trigger, and heroes lead their armies to victory (or hilarious defeat). The real-time strategy elements add layers of complexity that keep every match fresh and engaging.

## The Strategy

### Factions and Heroes

Codex features multiple factions, each with unique heroes and abilities. Whether you prefer the brute strength of the Red faction or the mystical powers of the Blue faction, there's something for every playstyle. The heroes play a significant role in shaping your strategy and guiding your units to victory.

### Deck-Building and Customization

One of the highlights of Codex is its deck-building aspect. Players can customize their decks with different units, spells, and heroes, allowing for endless strategic possibilities. The customization options ensure that no two games are ever the same.
