## Assembly and maintenance tips

- Unpack everything and organize by type. Screws, cams, and brackets are your friends; random handfuls of hardware are not.
- Use a power drill with a gentle setting to avoid over-tightening, especially on veneer surfaces. A light touch is a virtue here.
- Check the level and adjust feet or shims as needed. A slightly unlevel desk can throw off the entire look and feel, like a superhero with a crooked cape.
- Cable management works best when you route cables in a neat bundle; use zip ties or Velcro straps to keep things tidy. Labeling cables is optional but extremely satisfying when you’re tracing a path at quarter past midnight during a product demo.
- Clean with a damp microfiber cloth and a mild furniture-safe cleaner. Avoid abrasive scrubbers that might scratch the surface or unleash the inevitable Yelp review rage from squeaky chair wheels.
