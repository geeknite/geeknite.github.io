## How Dixit DIX01 plays: a refresher for everyone at the table

The core idea of Dixit is deceptively simple: one storyteller weaves a clue from a card in their hand, and everyone else submits a card they think might fit that clue. Cards are revealed, players vote on which card belongs to the storyteller, and points are awarded based on how many folks guessed the storyteller’s card versus who picked non storyteller cards. The balance of guesswork and creativity is what creates the magic, and DIX01 keeps that balance intact.

The storyteller cannot reveal their card directly; the clue should guide but not shout. The other players strive to interpret the clue while projecting their own associations. The tension comes from the tension between being too obvious and being too obscure. If the clue is perfect and almost everyone picks the storyteller card, the round can feel anticlimactic. If the clue is too obscure and almost no one chooses it, the storyteller is penalized. The sweet spot is where a storyteller can nudge others toward the right card without outright giving it away.

### Round flow in practice

1) Storyteller selects a card from their hand and gives a clue. The clue can be a word, a phrase, a memory, a joke, or a fragment of a lyric, as long as it evokes the image without screaming the card name.
2) Each other player selects a card from their own hand that they think best matches the clue and places it face down in the center.
3) The center cards are revealed and all players vote on which card they believe is the storyteller's.
4) Points are awarded: the storyteller earns points if at least one but not all players choose their card; guessers who picked the storyteller’s card also earn points. If no one or everyone guesses correctly, the storyteller takes a penalty and the others gain.

DIX01 emphasizes smooth pacing and quick teaching, so rounds rarely feel long or bogged down by heavy text on the rules. The voting and guessing cycles are rapid, especially in games with 4 or more players, where the variety of interpretations creates a carnival of ideas and laughter.
