Durability is one of the most critical factors when evaluating a rug of this size. The Moonscape Night Rug is built to withstand daily wear and tear, including pet claws, spilled drinks, and the occasional chair creep. Stain resistance is advertised as a highlight, and in our test, most common household spills wiped away with a damp cloth without leaving a permanent stain. A trained eye will appreciate the fact that the surface dries relatively quickly, which reduces downtime before your next planetary party or streaming session.

Cleaning-wise, the rug responds well to regular vacuuming with a low-suction setting. High-powered vacuums can pull at the fibers if you go too fast, so a slower, steady approach is recommended. If you have children or pets who enjoy rolling around on the floor, you may want to schedule a deeper clean every so often with a gentle carpet cleaner. Always test a small, inconspicuous spot first to confirm colorfastness.
