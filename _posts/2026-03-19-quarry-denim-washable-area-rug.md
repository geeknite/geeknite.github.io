## Cleaning and maintenance
### Cleaning methods and care routines
The big selling point here: washable. The manufacturer claims machine-washable in cold water on a gentle cycle, followed by tumble drying on low. If you’re a meticulous soul who trusts only hand-washing or the patience of a saint, you’ll still find comfort in a simple spot-clean approach for most everyday spills. Use a mild detergent and avoid harsh chemicals that could fade the denim tones or degrade the backing over time.

For routine maintenance, vacuum the rug with a brush attachment to lift loose dirt. If you have pets, a weekly or biweekly vacuum should keep fur and dander in check. A quick check: if your vacuum has a “turbo” setting that can annihilate everything on the floor, resist the urge to test it on the rug as if you’re trying to extract plot twists from a mystery novel. Gentle, regular cleaning wins the race here.

### Stains and quick remedies
Spills happen. Coffee, wine, cats with dramatic personalities—these are all part of domestic life. The Quarry Denim is built to shrug off most light-to-moderate spills with minimal drama. Blot the area with a clean cloth, avoid aggressive scrubbing, and treat the stain with a mild detergent solution. For tougher stains, a quick pre-soak with a diluted stain remover designed for textiles can help, followed by a normal wash on cold. Always air dry outdoors or on a well-ventilated area to avoid heat setting depending on the fiber composition. ### Everyday care and longevity
Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear across high-traffic zones. This not only extends the life of the rug but also helps prevent a dramatic head-to-toe fade where your cat marks territory with bold swagger. A light routine like vacuuming once a week and a cold wash every few months should keep the denim vibe fresh. ## Care guide and FAQs
### Quick tips for best results
- Unroll and place on clean, dry floor; avoid sharp objects that might snag fibers.
- Vacuum weekly with a soft-bristle attachment to remove dust and pet fur.
- Treat spills promptly with cold water and mild detergent; avoid hot water that can set stains.
- When washing, use cold water and a gentle cycle; air-dry flat to prevent shape distortion.
- If the rug curls at the edges, re-position and pin the corners for a day or two while it relearns its flat stance.

### Frequently asked questions
- Is it truly machine-washable? Most models advertise machine-wash compatibility, but always double-check the care label and test any stain-removal solution on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Can it handle heavy furniture? Heavy furniture can compress pile over time. Use furniture coasters or spacing to help distribute weight more evenly.
- How long will it last in a high-traffic area? With regular care and rotation, you can expect several years of service before noticeable wear becomes distracting.
- Does it fade in sunlight? Prolonged exposure to direct sunlight can cause color fading over time. Consider rotating the rug or using window treatments to reduce sun exposure.
