## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity Tips
Caring for Bicycle decks is straightforward, but there are some smart routines worth adopting:
- Keep the decks dry. Moisture weakens stock and finish; store in a dry place. A simple card box or sleeve can provide extra protection against accidental spills.
- Avoid exposure to direct sunlight for long periods. Fading can occur on the faces, especially if you’re using colored backings. If you’re a sun-chaser, consider storing decks in a cool, shaded area and rotate decks to distribute wear evenly.
- Clean hands before shuffling. Oils and dirt can degrade the finish over time, so a quick wash or dry-down of hands before you begin is a small but meaningful habit.
- Don’t bend or crease the cards intentionally. They’re not meant to survive heroic bending tests. Treat them with respect and they’ll continue to deliver the snap you expect.

If you ever encounter a misprint or minor defect, Bicycle has a long-standing reputation for reliability, so you’ll likely be able to replace the deck with minimal fuss. The moral of the story: good stock plus good handling equals longer-lasting performance and a happier poker night.
