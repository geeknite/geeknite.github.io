## Maintenance and care: practical considerations

Cleanliness is the unstated OS that keeps a rug looking fresh. The Wild Strawberry Cream is designed with relative stain-resistance in mind, but no rug is immortal if you spill a perfectly good coffee on it and pretend it never happened. Here are practical care tips:

- Immediate action matters: blot spills with a clean cloth, don’t rub, and avoid harsh chemical cleaners that could alter the color. The sooner you act, the better your odds of preserving the cream base.
- Regular vacuuming: use a low-to-moderate suction setting, and avoid aggressively pulling at fibers. A gentle weekly pass is typically enough to maintain pile integrity.
- Spot cleaning: for common household stains, test a small, inconspicuous area before treating a larger patch. Use a mild detergent solution and a clean cloth; avoid soaking the rug.
- Professional cleaning: consider a professional cleaning every 12-18 months if the rug is in a high-traffic area or a home with kids and pets. A professional can refresh the fibers without risking color bleed.

Care instructions vary slightly by fiber mix. If your rug uses a higher proportion of natural fibers, you may want to adopt more conservative cleaning methods.
