## Collecting, Storage, and Maintenance

### Handling and storage suggestions

Foil cards demand a little extra care. Here are Geeknite-approved tips to keep BT26-134 R looking pristine:

- Use archival-quality sleeves designed for Dragon Ball Super cards. Dual-layer sleeves that provide a clear view and a snug fit help protect against corner dents and surface scuffs.
