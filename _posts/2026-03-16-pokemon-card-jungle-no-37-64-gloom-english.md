## How to display and store: care tips for a vintage Gloom
If you’re lucky enough to snag a JUNGLE Gloom, here are quick tips to keep it happy for decades:
- Keep it in a solid binder with page protectors that fit 9-pocket sheets. The mid- to late-90s card stock loves to mind its margins; protect it from bends with rigid sheets.
