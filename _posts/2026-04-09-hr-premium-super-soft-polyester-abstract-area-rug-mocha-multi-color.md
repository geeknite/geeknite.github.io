## Care and maintenance

 polyester is generally forgiving when it comes to day-to-day upkeep. The recommended care routine for this rug includes regular vacuuming at least once a week to remove surface dust and prevent dirt from embedding into the fibers. Use a vacuum with a beater brush or a gentle setting to avoid flattening the pile. For small spills, act quickly: blot with a clean cloth or paper towel to absorb, then use a mild detergent solution on the spot, followed by blotting to remove moisture. Avoid harsh scrubbing, which can push stains deeper and dull the fibers.

For tougher stains, a little patience goes a long way. Pretreat with a gentle stain remover designed for polyester fibers, test on a small inconspicuous area first, and never use bleach on colored fibers. If you ever have a gnarly spill, you can also consider a professional cleaning service that specializes in synthetic fiber rugs. And yes, you can still enjoy your coffee while the rug gets its spa day, because zero fuss is the dream here.

Rotation is also a smart move. Every few months, rotate the rug 180 degrees to ensure even wear across high-traffic zones. This helps prevent a visible “sunset” effect from shading on the color palette and keeps the pattern looking fresh for longer. Lastly, consider a rug pad. Not only does a pad increase comfort, it also reduces sliding and adds a bit of insulation against cold floors. It’s not flashy, but it’s the quiet hero of rug longevity.
