The certificate lists carat weight, color grade, clarity grade, cut grade, measurements, report number, and a description of the diamond. ## Comparisons and Alternatives

### Bezel vs prong settings

Bezel offers cleaner lines and better protection for daily wear. Prong settings can expose more of the girdle and crown, which can boost sparkle but increases snag risk. A bezel pendant is easier to care for and typically suits a modern wardrobe. If you want more visible metal profile around the diamond, consider a partial bezel or a halo variant for a different look.

### Bezel vs halo pendants

Halo settings surround the center stone with a ring of smaller stones, dramatically increasing perceived size and spark. A bezel ring is more minimalist and timeless. Your choice depends on whether you want a bold statement or a sleek, everyday luxury.
