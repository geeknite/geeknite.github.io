## Assembly and maintenance
### How hard is it to assemble?
Assemble-time can feel like assembling a spaceship if your hands are a bit clumsy and your screwdriver is determined to hide. The good news: modular cubicles are designed for on-site configuration with common tools. Expect multiple modules if you’re outfitting an entire floor, and plan for a weekend warrior session if you’re not burying the whole office in a forklift lane. Keep track of screws and brackets; a labeled hardware kit saves you from the “which screw goes where?” moment at 7 PM on a Tuesday when your brain is on its third coffee.

### Cleaning and maintenance
Fabric panels respond well to routine vacuuming and spot cleaning. If a deeply stubborn stain appears (we’ve all got the mysterious coffee-stain story), treat it with a fabric-safe cleaner and test in a hidden corner first. The surfaces aren’t dishwasher-safe, so resist the urge to toss them in the sink with your lunch leftovers. For cable management, periodic checks are wise; ensure there are no knotty bundles that could become nuisance tangles when you reposition equipment.

### Replacement parts and warranty
Most reputable vendors offer replacement panels and modest warranty options. If you encounter a rickety joint or a damaged panel, reach out to support promptly and document with photos. The more proactive you are, the quicker you’ll recover a neat workspace and your mental equilibrium.
