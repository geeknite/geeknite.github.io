## Maintenance and longevity

Like any good gadget, this luxury poker set benefits from a light maintenance routine. Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth to remove fingerprints and dust. If you spill something on the cards, avoid harsh cleaners; a damp cloth with mild soap, then air dry, should suffice. Do not put the cards or chips in a dishwasher; that would be a crime against luxury and common sense at the same time. For storage, keep the set in its original case or a padded bag to prevent nicks and edge wear. If you travel with the set, use the protective interior to keep the chips from rattling and the cards from grinding against the case walls.

Regularly check the case clasps and the hinges. A loose latch may seem minor, but it can lead to a dropped chip or a curious pet investigating the box with a whisker or a claw. A quick oiling of the hinges with a tiny dab of silicone-based lubricant will keep those doors closing with the same confidence you want at the table.
