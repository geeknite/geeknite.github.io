## Maintenance tips to maximize life and quality

To get the most out of your Fuji Xerox Cyan Toner High Capacity 11K, consider the following best practices:
- Store cartridges upright in a cool, dry place. Storing on their side is not a fashion statement you want to make with your toner; it’s just bad for flow and yields.
- Run a printer maintenance page after every new cartridge install to prime the fuser and drum for best color stability. The pages will be serviceable and provide a quick reminder that the printer exists beyond your inbox.
- Print a few light-duty color pages to distribute toner evenly before undertaking heavy color runs. This helps avoid pigment clumping and ensures consistent color from page one onward.
- If your printer is writing a calibration page, don’t panic—calibration is the printer’s way of saying, “I care about your margins.” Let it finish; you’ll be rewarded with nicer color balance.
