## How to Play: A Quick Guide (With Laugh Tracks)

Exploding Kittens NSFW Edition sticks to a simple core loop, which is why it travels so well from casual game night to those chaotic, caffeine-fueled weekends when you need a fast game that won’t overstay its welcome.

- Setup: Each player is dealt a starting hand of cards. The deck is shuffled, and the Exploding Kitten cards are placed back into the deck with a handful of Defuse cards mixed in. The goal is simple: avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten card.
- On your turn: you draw a card, and then you can play any number of actions from your hand that affect other players or classes of risk. The Defuse card lets you neutralize an Exploding Kitten when drawn; otherwise, you’re out if the kitteh goes boom. Some cards let you peek at the future, shift cards around the deck, or make others draw more than their fair share of chaos.
- Explosions and defusal: explode cards are the ticking time bombs of the table. Use Defuse cards (which can be obtained or stolen) to negate the blast and keep the party going. The last player standing wins.

What makes the NSFW Edition sing is the way the cards build tension. It’s a rhythm game of “do I risk this, or do I pass?” with a chorus of laughter that erupts when a risky move pays off—or badly backfires. The rules aren’t astonishly complex, but the entertainment value comes from misreading a card, mismanaging a risk, and watching someone with laser focus realize they’ve bluffed themselves into a bad decision.
