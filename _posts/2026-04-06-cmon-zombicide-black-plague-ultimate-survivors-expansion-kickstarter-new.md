## Gameplay: what changes on the table
The core of Zombicide Black Plague remains a cooperative, dice-chucking skirmish against a tide of undead. The Ultimate Survivors Expansion slides into this loop but adds enough complexity to feel like a new game without alienating players who adore the original. You’ll notice a few recurring themes:

- Turn order variability: You’ll occasionally see initiative effects and special actions that make planning for a few moves ahead feel more meaningful.
- Mixed equipment and synergy: New weapons and survivor abilities encourage team builds that reward planning and communication. It’s less about who rolls best and more about who orchestrates the chain of events.
- Objective-driven play: Some scenarios tilt the victory condition toward objectives rather than pure zombie-slaying; the players must balance resource management with strategic positioning to claim victory.

For a group that loves crunch, this expansion is a treat. For players who prefer quick, light sessions, the extra rules may feel a touch heavy; the good news is that the recommended difficulty levels include lighter variants to ease newcomers into the new mechanics.
