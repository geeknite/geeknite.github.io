## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity
Nothing lasts forever, including your taste for all-nighter poker marathons, but this set is built with longevity in mind. Here are a few tips to keep it performing for years:
- Wipe the case with a damp microfiber cloth to keep the finish free from fingerprints and fridge magnet stickers that somehow ended up on your carry case. It’s not a museum piece; it’s a travel partner, but it should still look cool.
- Keep the foam inserts clean. A quick vacuum or a brush can remove stray chips that have wandered astray from the stack. You want to avoid grit on the felt. Clean chips go further and feel better when you’re counting outs.
- Avoid moisture. Aluminum is less prone to rust or corrosion, but the interior foam can attract moisture if left in damp spaces. If you’ve just arrived home from a rain-soaked tournament, let the set dry before sealing it away again.
- Check the latch and keys. If you’re in a rush and you’re pretending to be a secret agent, the latch should still engage evenly. If you notice a hitch, a tiny adjustment with a tool can help. Don’t force the mechanism; a little lubrication can go a long way toward ensuring smooth operation.
