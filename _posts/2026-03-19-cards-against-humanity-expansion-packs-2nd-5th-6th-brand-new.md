## The 5th Expansion
### Flavor and tone
The 5th expansion tends to lean into even sharper social satire and pop culture caricature. It’s the kind of pack that makes you snort-laugh and then realize you just included it in a group chat with grandma and your aunt who swears she’s the cool one. The humor is unapologetic and often self-referential, which makes it a hit for players who love meta jokes about games themselves.

### Card stock, weight, and longevity
As with the other expansions, the cards in the 5th expansion are of the standard CAH stock. They shuffle well, resist warping with moderate humidity exposure, and seem designed to survive at least a dozen game nights if you don’t spill nachos on them. Card quality matters, because during a heated round the last thing you want is a corner fraying and affecting your critical decision of which white card wins.

### Playstyle considerations
If your crew enjoys witty pop culture riffs on TV shows, meme culture, and occasional political satire, the 5th expansion has a little more bite. It may be less forgiving than the 2nd in some zones, so be prepared for groans, laughter, and perhaps the occasional retreat to a quieter game while someone regroups.
