## What is Gondor at War?

### The Basics

Middle-earth Strategy Battle Game: Gondor at War is a tabletop game where players take on the roles of iconic characters and armies from the Lord of the Rings series. With stunning miniatures, detailed scenarios, and strategic depth, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted figures of your favorite Middle-earth characters and troops. Seriously, they're so detailed you may want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Game Board**: Various locations from Gondor where battles and missions take place.
- **Cards and Tokens**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Those claws are the real minions of Mordor.

## Gameplay

### Setup

Setting up Gondor at War is like preparing for a grand battle. Lay out the game board, distribute miniatures, and get your strategy brain in gear. The more effort you put into the setup, the more epic the game experience will be.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their characters' abilities. You can move, attack, defend, and use special powers. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your teammates to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Gondor at War is a blend of strategy and luck. Each character and unit has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have your opponent counter it? Welcome to Middle-earth, where plans often go awry.
