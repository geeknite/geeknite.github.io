### Weather resistance in practice

The key claim for Outsunny sets is that their wicker resists fading and their frames resist rust with powder coating. In practice, you should expect decent performance for a few seasons, depending on your climate. In moderate climates with regular rain and sun, you can reasonably expect color stability and structural integrity to hold. A cheap set that pretends to be indestructible often disappoints when the first hailstorm shows up with a vengeance; the Outsunny set tends to be a better-than-average bet in this category.

### Maintenance rituals that extend life

- Use a protective cover during extreme weather. It reduces UV damage and capillary staining from rain.
- Clean with mild soap and water; avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade wicker and fabric.
- Check the protective feet and base plates to ensure stability after heavy use.
- Rotate cushions and re-tighten hardware occasionally to prevent loosening from normal usage.
