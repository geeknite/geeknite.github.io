## House rules and variants: keeping it fresh
No board game night is complete without a couple of house rules, and the NSFW edition is no exception. Here are a few ideas that work well in practice:
- The Whisper Rule: once per round, a player may whisper a single hint about a card in their hand to one other player. This adds a layer of social deduction and misinterpretation that can cause wonderfully chaotic misunderstandings.
- The Elevator Pitch: when a player plays an especially spicy Da Bomb or Dare, they must deliver a one-sentence justification for their action. Dramatic pauses and overacting earn bonus points (or at least extra laughs).
- Content Boundary Card: designate a neutral card that marks the moment a joke crosses a line for someone. It’s a playful safety valve that reduces awkward moments and keeps everyone comfortable.
- Quick Defuse: allow players to keep a Defuse for a later benefit, such as drawing an extra card or swapping a card with another player. It reshapes late-game calculus in a satisfying way.
