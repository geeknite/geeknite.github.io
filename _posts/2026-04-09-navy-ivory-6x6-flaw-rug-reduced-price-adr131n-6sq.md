## Care and Maintenance

To keep the Navy Ivory 6x6 looking crisp after weeks of heavy use, follow basic rug care guidelines. Vacuum regularly on a soft setting to avoid pulling fibers. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and avoid heavy traffic patterns forming at one side. If you spill something, blot rather than rub. For persistent stains, a mild detergent solution works, but test in an inconspicuous corner first to ensure colorfastness. A good rug pad is essential for preventing edge curling and to provide extra underfoot comfort. If you notice a loose edge, a minor trim with scissors can help, but avoid aggressive cutting as that could worsen the issue.

Care is not glamorous, but it pays dividends in how long the rug will last and how comfortable the room will feel after you stand up from a long Zoom meeting in your socks.
