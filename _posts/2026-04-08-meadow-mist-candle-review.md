Top notes: Fresh-cut grass, dewy fern, a whisper of citrus zest that keeps things from getting too heroic.

Heart notes: Meadow flowers, clover, lilac, and a hint of rosemary that nods to the herb garden outside your apartment window.

Base notes: Soft musk, amber, and the kind of quiet woodiness that makes you want to sit down with a blanket and a fantasy novel.

In perfume terms this is a green-floral hybrid with an extra humanizing warmth; in gamer terms it's a potion you drink that makes your desk glow with less blue light, more warm hay. The result is a fragrance that reads as spring waking up instead of spring crashing into your face like a chorus of uninvited birds. There is balance.
