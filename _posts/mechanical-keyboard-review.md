## What Makes a Mechanical Keyboard Special?

If you've never used a mechanical keyboard, you're in for a treat. Unlike their membrane counterparts, mechanical keyboards use individual switches for each key, providing a more tactile and responsive typing experience. Here are some benefits of mechanical keyboards:

- **Durability**: Mechanical keyboards are built to last, with some switches rated for tens of millions of keystrokes.
- **Customizability**: Many mechanical keyboards allow you to swap out keycaps and switches, letting you tailor the keyboard to your preferences.
- **Typing Experience**: The tactile feedback and audible click of mechanical switches can make typing more enjoyable and efficient.
