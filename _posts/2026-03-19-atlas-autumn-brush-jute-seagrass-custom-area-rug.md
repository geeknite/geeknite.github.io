## Care and maintenance

Natural fiber rugs require a bit of care, but it is not rocket science. Here are practical guidelines to keep your Atlas Autumn rug looking its best without becoming a full-time cleaning project.

- Vacuum regularly: Use a vacuum without a beater bar or with the bar turned off to avoid fiber snagging. The brushed surface picks up dust more readily; a gentle pass every few days often suffices.
- Blot spills immediately: Natural fibers can absorb liquids, so act fast. Blot with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing, and treat stubborn stains with a mild detergent solution if needed. Always test a small inconspicuous area first.
- Dry thoroughly: If you clean a stain or damp area, ensure the rug dries completely to prevent mold and mildew. A dry space with air flow is your friend.
- Sun exposure: Prolonged direct sunlight can cause fading in natural fibers. Rotate the rug occasionally to even out wear and color development.
- Professional cleaning: For deep cleaning, consult professionals who specialize in natural fiber rugs. They can assess fiber health and provide safe cleaning methods that won’t compromise dye or fiber integrity.
