### Installation guide — step by step

1) Power down the Quest Pro and unplug any connected accessories. This is not the time for a midnight democracy of charging adapters.
2) Remove the Quest Pro back panel. This typically involves unscrewing a handful of small screws and carefully prying the shell away.
3) Disconnect the existing battery connector. Do this gently; you do not want to bend or shear the small pins.
4) Connect the new CS-LUS677SL battery. Ensure the connector is fully seated and aligned with the port.
5) Reattach the back plate and screws. Power on the device to verify the boot sequence and that the battery indicator shows normal operation.

If you follow these steps in a clean, static-free environment you will avoid some common mistakes such as misaligned connectors or poking the battery into the camera array. Always test before you seal the back plate permanently.

### Safety and warranty notes

- Replacement batteries may affect your device warranty. Check the policy for your region and for the exact model you own.

- Handle the battery with care. Do not puncture, crush, or bend the pack. If you notice swelling or unusual heat, stop using it immediately and seek professional service.

### Installation caveats and tips

- Ensure your connectors are clean before you plug the new pack in. A small amount of dust can prevent a reliable connection.

- If you plan to keep the Quest Pro in a carry case or your bag during transport, consider a small protective sleeve or a foam insert to minimize jostling the battery during travel.
