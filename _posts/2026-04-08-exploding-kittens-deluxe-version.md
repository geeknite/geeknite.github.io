## Introduction

If you walked into a game night wearing a wizard hat and holding a cat-shaped bomb, you might just fit right in with Exploding Kittens Deluxe. This is the kind of game that says, in the most polite possible way, that chaos is a curated experience, and you should probably bring a spare shirt for the spill of laughter that follows. The Deluxe edition is not merely a bigger box with bigger expectations; it is a love letter to meme culture, cat memes, and the old school art of bluffing your friends into not exploding your own chances of winning.
