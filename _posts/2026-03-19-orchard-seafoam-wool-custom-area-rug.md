## Construction and Durability
Wool is a traditional fiber for good reason: it wears well, resists pilling, and breathes beautifully. The Orchard Seafoam rug is presumably hand-tufted or machine-tufted with a high-density pile, which translates into durability under daily use. The pile height is the critical factor here: too low, and it won’t feel plush; too high, and it might flatten in high-traffic zones. Durability isn’t just about the fiber; it’s about the loom or machine used, the knot density, and the finishing touches such as edge binding. A well-bound edge keeps the rug from curling up at the corners, which is essential for modern open-plan layouts where cats do aerial stunts on a Saturday night. The Seafoam rug’s color holds up remarkably well over time, assuming reasonable sun exposure. If you’re a pet owner, wool rugs often handle claws more gracefully than synthetic fibers. Of course, no rug is invincible; the key is a prompt cleanup and a good rug pad underneath to reduce wear in the most-used zones.

## Care, Maintenance, and Cleaning Tips
Wool rugs look better with regular care, not heroic, once-a-year miracles. Here are practical tips that keep the Orchard Seafoam rug looking fresh:

- Vacuum regularly with a vacuum that has adjustable height settings. Use a gentle suction in high-traffic zones to avoid excessive pile compression.
-Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push the spill deeper into the fibers. For stubborn stains, a mild wool-safe cleaner applied sparingly can help; test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and exposure to sunlight, which helps prevent a sun angle romance with color fading. If your room has a lot of light, consider UV-blocking curtains to extend the rug’s life.
- Use a rug pad designed for wool rugs. A pad adds cushioning, prevents slipping, and reduces wear by creating a buffer between the rug and floor.
- Avoid high-heat cleaning methods or steam cleaning unless specified by the manufacturer. Wool loves cool to warm rather than hot environments.

Seasonal maintenance can become a ritual rather than a chore. A small lint roller can remove stray fibers, and a brush with gentle bristles can lift flattened areas, reviving the pile after weeks of couch-sitting marathons.

## Ordering and Customization Experience
Ordering a custom rug is a little like designing a spaceship: you pick the core components, specify dimensions, and let the crew (the rug makers) do the heavy lifting. The Orchard Seafoam Wool rug’s customization process is typically as follows:

1) Measure your space precisely. Note whether you want a centered rug under your coffee table or a layout that aligns with sofa legs.
