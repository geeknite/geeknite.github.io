## Maintenance, care, and longevity

Chips are durable, but they love a little care. Here are a few practical tips to extend their life:

- Wipe chips with a soft microfiber cloth after a night of heavy sweating and spilled drinks. If you are feeling fancy, a gentle wipe with a damp cloth helps remove oils that build up.
- Avoid exposing the chips to direct sunlight for long periods, which can cause colors to fade over time. If you must play outdoors, consider a covered table and shade so the colors stay vibrant.
- Use a dedicated felt mat or a playing surface that helps protect the chip edges from friction. A rough surface over many hours can nick up the edges and cause chips to look worn.
- When stacking, avoid over-tight stacking. A loose stack is easier to manage, reduces tumble risk, and makes it easier to count each denomination at the end of a hand.
