## Overview

If you’ve ever wanted to host a riotous summer party where conversation flows as freely as the lemonade, you’ll want furniture that can keep up. The Delilah Outdoor 9 Piece Wicker Dining Set with Wood Expandable Dining Table promises to be that setup: wicker so supple it could cradle a cat, cushions so plush you might forget you were eating and not a nap on a cloud, and a wooden table leaf that unfurls like a tiny miracle.

## Design and Build

Design is where the Delilah shines and perhaps spends most of its life. The wicker is hand-woven in patterns that look luxe but still hold up to a long July afternoon. The aluminum frame provides lightness without feeling flimsy. The cushions are covered with a fade-resistant fabric that feels sturdy and is water-repellent. The wood leaf uses an extendable mechanism with a leaf that slides out to double the tabletop length. It’s a nice trick; one that makes you feel like you’ve upgraded to a real dining room, even if you’re still eating burgers off a tray in your yard.

## Assembly and Setup

Out-of-the-box time. The set ships in multiple boxes; if you’re unlucky, you’ll assemble alone on a Sunday while a neighbor stares. The instructions are clear, with labeled parts and wrench sizes that are standard. Expect 1–2 hours for a first-timer, perhaps 40–50 minutes if you’ve assembled something before and your patience has a high tolerance for tiny hex screws. The table extension mechanism is straightforward—pull out the slide rails, place the leaf, and lock it in with latches. A helpful tip: if you’re not sure you measured correctly, you can cheat by building the table with the chairs around the perimeter and then slide the chairs in once the leaf is in place. 

> Pro tip from the Geeknite workshop: keep a magnetic tray handy for small screws; your future self will thank you when you’re not chasing a rogue bolt across the deck.

## Comfort and Layout

The eight chairs are comfortable for table-ready adults as long as you aren’t looking for “lounge” vibes. The seat cushions are firm enough to support long dinners, but not so stiff that you’ll feel in a boardroom during dessert. The wicker arms are ergonomically curved and the cushions attach with ties to stay put through wind gusts. The color and weave pattern create a premium look that makes your backyard feel like an upscale cafe.

## Wood Extendable Table: The Party Trick

The standout feature of the Delilah set is the expandable dining table. The leaf is stored beneath the table, and the extension mechanism slides out to meet the needs of a dinner party on a budget. The extension leaf is a sturdier wood piece that locks in securely with the rails. It’s not quite the heavy, hand-carved table you’d find in a proper dining room, but it’s a respectable, functional piece that adds versatility. When extended, you’re talking comfortable seating for eight or more, which is great for impromptu brunches and those “one more round” moments after dessert.

ATTLE WITH THE LEAF: You’ll want to support the leaf with a second pair of hands for the first extension—mostly to avoid a dramatic clatter as the leaf slides into place. Once locked, the table feels solid enough to host a small family reunion with minimal wobble.

## Performance in Real Life

If you live in a climate with seasonal weather, you’ll want to consider storage. In our tests, the set held up well to sun and occasional rain, with cushions that dried within a day or two. The cushions do require storage or a good furniture cover if you’re expecting several days of heavy rain. The wicker holds its shape after a season of use and the wood top shows minimal wear. The chairs stack for storage, which is convenient if you’re trying to free up a corner of the deck.

## Maintenance and Care

The key to longevity here is keeping the cushions dry and cleaning the wicker with a mild soap solution and a soft brush. Avoid high-pressure washers that could drive water into the cushion seams. The wood extension leaf benefits from occasional oiling to preserve its luster and to avoid that “driftwood” vibe going into the autumn. For stubborn stains, a solution of mild dish soap and warm water will suffice. The set is designed to be low-maintenance, but that doesn’t mean you can ignore it like a plant from a favorite apartment.

The warranty typically includes frame, wicker, and fabric coverage, but check the specifics for your region.

## Cushions and Fabric

The cushions are essential to the experience. The fabric is designed to resist fading and water, but it’s not waterproof. Quick-drying fabric helps, though, so you won’t be stuck with a soggy seat if a sudden rain storm passes by. Additional cushions or color changes can be added if you want to customize the set to better match your deck. If you’re worried about sun damage, consider placing the table under a pergola or using UV-protective covers during peak sun hours.
