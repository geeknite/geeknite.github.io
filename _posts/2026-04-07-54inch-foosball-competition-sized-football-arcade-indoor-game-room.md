## Maintenance and care: keeping the beast in peak condition

Maintenance is basically a series of tiny rituals that add up to big performance. Here are practical tips to keep your foosball table in prime shape:

- Clean the playing surface with a soft microfiber cloth and a light cleaning solution designed for laminate or melamine surfaces. Don’t use abrasive cleaners; you’ll regret it when your ball’s bounce becomes a commentary on your cleaning technique.
- Lubricate the rods occasionally with a recommended silicone-based lubricant. A little goes a long way, and you’ll preserve that smooth rod action for many games to come.
- Wipe down handles to remove fingerprints and oils. These little coatings can make the handles slippery over time. A quick wipe not only keeps grip solid but also keeps the look crisp.
- Inspect the screws and bolts regularly. If you hear a telltale rattle during a game, it’s probably just a screw that loosened after your last heroic save. Tighten and proceed with confidence.
- Keep the table level and stable. The leg levelers are there for a reason—don’t skip on leveling before a big match. It’s the difference between a heroic goal and a confounding bounce that looks staged by a special effects team.
