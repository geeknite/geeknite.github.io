## Practical Tips for New Owners

- Place near the entryway or inside the closet with a bit of clearance for the top shelf. If you try to cram it into a tight corner, the spin might become a glide-and-crash showcase.
- Group similar footwear by tray (sneaks on one tray, dress shoes on another) to maximize the speed of selection.
- Use a shallow shoe storage mat on each tray to reduce scuffs and make cleaning easier.
- Don’t ignore maintenance; a quick weekly wipe will keep it looking pristine and prevent dust buildup from becoming a museum curator’s nightmare.
- If you have a particularly heavy boot collection, consider a pre-assembly plan to ensure you have the necessary assistance when lifting the heavy final components.
