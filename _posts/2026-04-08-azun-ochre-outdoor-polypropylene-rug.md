## Cleaning and Maintenance
### Spill management that won’t ruin your day
Spills happen. Coffee, ketchup, the occasional kids snack, all of which are easily dealt with on polypropylene. The rug is designed to be spot-cleaned with a mild detergent and water. For larger messes, a hose-down is usually sufficient. The color tends to hide minor stains in a way that makes you feel like a responsible adult who actually cleans something without panicking.

### Routine care to extend life
A quick weekly shake or vacuum helps prevent grit from embedding into the fibers. If you want to go the extra mile, rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even sun exposure and prevent uneven fading, especially if you’ve got a sun-loving corner that looks suspiciously like a latte art project after a long afternoon in the sun.

### Dealing with mold and mildew
In damp environments, mold and mildew can crash the party. Because this rug uses polypropylene, it is comparatively less prone to mold growth than natural fiber alternatives. Still, ensure it dries thoroughly after heavy rain. If you notice any stubborn spots, a gentle cleaning solution and a thorough rinse will typically reset the material’s appearance.
