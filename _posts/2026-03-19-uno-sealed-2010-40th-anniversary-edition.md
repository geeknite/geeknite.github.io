### Care and Preservation Tips
Preservation matters, especially with a New Opened Box listing. A few simple habits will keep the deck looking vibrant for years:
- Store upright in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Colors fade, just like my teenage confidence when I pretended to know the rules by heart.
- Keep the box free from moisture; cardboard is a friend to humidity in the same way a vampire is a friend to garlic.
- Consider a card saver for long-term display if you have a shelf that catches a lot of incidental light. This is optional but nice for the display-piece crowd.
- If the box is opened but contents are pristine, consider placing the deck in a protective sleeve to prevent minor edge wear from accidental handling.

Nostalgia is a powerful agent, but so is common sense when it comes to preserving a piece of gaming history. If you treat it well, the deck can survive a few more game nights and still remind you of the era when the simplest colors could spark the loudest cheers.
