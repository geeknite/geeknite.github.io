## The Basics of Phase 10

For those who are new to the game, Phase 10 is a rummy-type card game where players must complete specific sets, runs, or colors to advance through 10 phases. The first player to complete all 10 phases wins. It's a game of strategy, luck, and occasionally, yelling at your friends for ruining your plans.

## Gameplay

The gameplay remains true to the original Phase 10, with a few deluxe tweaks. The objective is still to complete all 10 phases, but the Deluxe Edition adds some extra flair with premium components and a more refined design.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:
1. **Draw Phase**: Draw a card from the draw pile or discard pile.
2. **Phase Completion**: Try to complete your current phase (good luck).
3. **Discard Phase**: Discard a card to end your turn (and potentially ruin someone's day).

### Winning and Losing

To win, be the first player to complete all 10 phases. If you lose, be prepared for endless teasing from your friends. It's all part of the fun!

**Pro Tip:** Always keep an eye on your opponents. If you see them smiling too much, they're probably about to complete a phase.
