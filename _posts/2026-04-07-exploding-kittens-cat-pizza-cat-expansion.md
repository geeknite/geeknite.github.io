## How to Play with Cat Pizza Cat Expansion
The expansion slots into the base Exploding Kittens rules with minimal fanfare, which is exactly the kind of compliment a micro-expansion deserves. Here are the essential adjustments you’ll encounter, followed by deeper dives into the interactions:

### Setup and Integration
- Shuffle the new Cat Pizza Cat cards into the base deck. No need for a complicated reformatting of your life choices; just mingle the toppings with the base chaos and let fate decide your next slice.
- Introduce the Cat Oven Card and the Pizza Tokens into the discard chain. They’re not weapons of mass hilarity, but they can feel like it when the discard pile turns into a cheesy desert mirage.
- The rest of the rules stay the same: cats, explosions, and the endless joy of not being the person who actually explodes first.

### Card Interactions
- Cat Pizza Cat cards add flavor effects that trigger during a player’s turn like tiny, fluffy micro-shocks of delight. They reward bold plays and sometimes punish overly cautious passes with a slice of doom.
- Pizza Tokens are a neat mechanic that modifies discard-pile behavior. They can be spent to influence draws or nudge a card from the top of the deck. The tension here is real, because you are basically paying with your dignity to influence a pizza-themed fate.
- The Cat Oven Card creates a temporary zone where certain effects become more chaotic. It’s not game-breaking, but it does produce delicious moments when players realize they should have planned a bit more for the kitchen sink of chaos.

### Strategy Adjustments
- The expansion rewards players who plan a couple of turns ahead but also punishes the overly cautious. You’ll want to balance aggression with caution, like a chef who knows when to sear the toppings and when to let them rest.
- Cat Pizza Cat cards tilt the balance toward mid-game aggression, a welcome change from the long doom cycles you sometimes see in base play. Expect more heated debates about who has to draw next and who owes the next round of pepperoni slices.
