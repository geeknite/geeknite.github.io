## What is Black Rose Wars?

Black Rose Wars is a competitive fantasy board game where players take on the roles of powerful mages vying to become the Grand Master of the Lodge of the Black Rose. You’ll cast spells, summon creatures, and perform rituals to outwit and outmaneuver your opponents.

### The Basics

In Black Rose Wars, players start with a mage equipped with unique spells and abilities. The game takes place in the labyrinthine Lodge, filled with rooms that provide various magical benefits. Your goal is to gain Power Points by completing quests, defeating opponents, and exploiting the Lodge’s magic.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Intricately designed miniatures representing mages and summoned creatures. They’re so detailed, you might end up casting spells on them accidentally.
- **Cards**: Spell cards, quest cards, and event cards that drive the game’s narrative and strategy.
- **Game Board**: A modular board that changes layout every game, ensuring no two games are ever the same.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track power, damage, and other game elements.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Black Rose Wars is like preparing for a magical duel. Lay out the modular board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your mage. Make sure you have snacks handy; this setup might take a while.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions using their spell cards. You can move your mage, cast spells, summon creatures, and perform rituals. The key is to strategically use your spells and abilities to outmaneuver your opponents and gain Power Points.

### Combat

Combat in Black Rose Wars is a blend of strategy and chaos. Spells can damage opponents, summon creatures to attack, or create barriers. The unpredictability of spells adds an exciting layer to the game, making each battle unique.【4:1†source】
