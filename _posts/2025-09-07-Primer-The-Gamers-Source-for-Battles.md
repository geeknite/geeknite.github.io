## What is Primer?

Primer is a strategy board game where players assume the roles of powerful leaders vying for control over a fantastical world. With beautifully illustrated game boards, unique character abilities, and a plethora of tactical options, Primer offers an immersive experience that will keep you coming back for more.

### The Basics

In Primer, players take turns moving their units, engaging in battles, and completing objectives to gain points and ultimately achieve victory. Each leader has unique abilities and cards that can turn the tide of battle, making every game uniquely challenging and exciting.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Miniatures**: Highly detailed miniatures representing various units and leaders.
- **Cards**: Decks of cards for each leader, featuring abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: A modular board that changes with each game, ensuring varied play experiences.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track resources, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Keep your dog away from the miniatures. Trust me; they don’t mix well with chew toys.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Primer is like preparing for a grand battle. Lay out the game board, distribute the miniatures, and choose your leader. Once your battlefield is ready, it's time to strategize and conquer.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their leader's abilities. You can move units, attack enemies, build defenses, and more. Cooperation and strategic planning are key, so make sure to outthink your opponents and adapt to ever-changing battle conditions.

### Combat

Combat in Primer is a blend of dice rolls and tactical decisions. Each unit and leader has unique attack and defense values, and players must use their abilities wisely to gain the upper hand. Did you plan a brilliant flank only to have it thwarted by a surprise card? Welcome to Primer, where strategy meets chaos.
