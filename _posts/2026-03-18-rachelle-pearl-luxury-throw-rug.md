## Maintenance and care tips
### Everyday care
- Vacuum weekly with a gentle setting to maintain fiber loft.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber frizz.
- Use a damp cloth for minor stains and air dry thoroughly.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear, because your feet and your chair are not symmetrical mammals.

### Cleaning and washing
If you are brave and your machine is large enough, you can launder on a gentle cycle with cold water and a mild detergent. Do not use hot water or aggressive washing modes, as that can impact fiber integrity and the pearl sheen. After washing, air dry flat to prevent missing the rug’s shape. If you are in a high-traffic household with pets, consider a light professional cleaning every year or two to restore that like-new look without risking a shrinkage scare on a home-laundry budget.

### Longevity and care expectations
With proper care, you can expect several years of comfortable use from the Rachelle Pearl. It is not immune to everyday wear, but it is designed to rebound after common foot traffic, spills, and the occasional cat scarf attack.
