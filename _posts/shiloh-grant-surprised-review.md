## The Basics

### What is Shiloh - Grant Surprised?

*Shiloh - Grant Surprised* is a strategic wargame that captures the essence of the Battle of Shiloh during the American Civil War. Developed by Decision Games, this game is part of their Folio Series, known for its compact size and engaging gameplay. Players take on the roles of Union and Confederate commanders, strategizing to outmaneuver and outfight their opponents.

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully designed map, depicting the terrain of the Shiloh battlefield, and high-quality counters representing the units involved in the battle. The detailed artwork and sturdy components ensure an immersive gaming experience. Additionally, the game includes a rulebook that's surprisingly easy to understand – a rare gem in the wargaming world.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is straightforward. Players choose their sides – Union or Confederate – and place their units on the map according to the scenario setup instructions. The objective for each side is to control key points on the battlefield and deplete the opposing force's resources.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn is divided into phases, including movement, combat, and supply. Players move their units across the map, engage in combat, and manage their supply lines to ensure their troops remain effective. The game shines in its strategic depth, requiring players to think several moves ahead and anticipate their opponent's tactics.

### Combat and Strategy

Combat is resolved using a combination of unit strength and dice rolls, adding an element of chance to the strategy. Players must carefully position their units, use terrain to their advantage, and decide when to press the attack or hold their ground. The game rewards thoughtful planning and punishes reckless charges (unless you're feeling particularly lucky).
