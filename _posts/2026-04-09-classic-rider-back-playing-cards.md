## Care and maintenance tips

- Keep them dry and away from humidity. Cards do not enjoy sweating when you are dealing with a hot game night.
- Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight for long periods. The colors on the back may fade a bit, and no one wants a deck that looks like it has spent a decade in a sun room.
- Clean with a dry cloth if you notice a dust drift. Moisture is not your friend here; it can affect the air-cushion finish and slickness.
- Store in a cool, dry place. A simple box or tray keeps them from curling at the edges.
