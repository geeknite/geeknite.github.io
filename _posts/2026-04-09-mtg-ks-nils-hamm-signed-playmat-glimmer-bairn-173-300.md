## Practical tips for use and care

To keep the Glimmer Bairn mat looking sharp, here are some practical tips that go beyond the brochure:

- Flattening on arrival: Some mats come crumpled from shipping. If yours does, lay it flat with something heavy (but not sharp) on the corners for a day or two. Don’t try to “steam” it back to shape; that can affect the surface texture.
- Cleaning: Wipe with a damp cloth. Avoid harsh cleaners or solvents that could dull the print or affect the signature. For stubborn stains, a mild detergent wipe will work; dry with a microfiber cloth to prevent water spots.
- Handling: Treat the mat like a delicate collectible when traveling to events. Roll loosely, don’t fold, and store it in a protective tube when you’re not using it. If you’re a gamer who travels a lot, consider a dedicated carry case that can accommodate a 3mm mat without bending.
- Environment: Keep it out of direct sunlight for extended periods. The colors are vibrant, but UV exposure can cause fading over time, especially on the edges.

With reasonable care, your Glimmer Bairn mat will be a reliable companion for years, maintaining both its aesthetic charm and its practical usefulness.
