## Practicalities: Weather, Maintenance, and Real-Life Scenarios
Outdoor furniture is a long-term relationship with the weather. A good rattan set isn’t just about how it looks on a sunny day; it’s about how it behaves after a week of sudden rain or a winter frost that thinks it’s fancy. The standard care routine includes:
- Rinse off surface dirt with a light spray; avoid pressure washers that can push water into the frame joints. 
- Wipe cushions down when you’re not using them; if you don’t have quick-dry covers, you’ll want to tuck them under a canopy to prevent mold. 
- Store or cover during heavy rain or winter months to extend life. Some sets come with covers; if not, consider a breathable outdoor cover that allows moisture to escape. 
- Check the fittings and screws every few months; plastic caps can wear out, and loose joints will turn your “sitting room” into a “rattle trap” in no time.
