### Setup, installation tips, and potential pitfalls

If you are a DIY warrior, you will be thrilled by the straightforward installation path this sink promises. The most important step is to ensure your cabinet opening matches the width and depth of the sink with a little extra room for caulking. You want a snug fit without forcing the sink into place so hard that you end up with a cracked countertop or a swear jar that suddenly looks empty. A good rule of thumb is to dry fit first, then add sealant, and only then secure the mounting hardware. If you are not confident, a pro installer can do the job with clean lines and predictable results.

Maintenance is the boring but essential part. A simple routine using a mild soap and warm water is enough for most days. After meals, a quick wipe can prevent stains from appearing. For stubborn stains, a soft toothbrush dipped in mild cleaner can address small areas around the drain and lip. As with any granite finish, avoid harsh chemical products that claim to strip everything in seconds. The surface is tough, but it is not invincible; treat it with the respect you would give a beloved sci fi prop and you will be rewarded with long term reliability.
