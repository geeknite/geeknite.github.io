## Maintenance and Cleaning

One of the undeniable advantages of synthetic fibers is ease of cleaning. The Quarry Fossil is designed to shrug off many everyday mishaps with minimal fuss.

### Everyday Cleaning

- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and debris. Use a vacuum with a beater bar on low to mid power to avoid pulling fibers.
- For spills, blot—do not rub. Rubbing can push the stain deeper into the fibers and create a fossilized data point you’ll regret during the carpet-cleaning episode.
- Use mild detergents and water for spot cleaning. Avoid bleach or harsh solvents that can degrade color and fiber integrity.

### Stain and Odor Control

Most common stains can be treated with standard rug cleaners. If you’ve managed to spill an entire bowl of red wine with a side of coffee, you might need a more intense approach or a professional service. Synthetic fibers tend to resist odors better than natural fibers, but persistent moisture in the backing can cause odor issues, so ensure the rug dries thoroughly after cleaning.

### Deep Cleaning and Maintenance Schedule

A deep clean every 6–12 months is reasonable for most households, depending on foot traffic. Use a carpet cleaning machine compatible with synthetic fibers or hire a professional service. The goal is to refresh the pile without shrinking or warping the backing. Yes, you can get dramatic, but you don’t want drama you can see in your carpet edges.
