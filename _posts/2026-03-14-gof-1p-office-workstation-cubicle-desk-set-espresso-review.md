## Assembly and installation

Yes, there will be screws. No, there is no way around it—unless you hire a professional assembler who charges by the number of Allen wrenches it takes to pronounce your own name. The GOF 1P ships in a few flat-packable boxes, a little like a modern-day jigsaw puzzle where the final image is your newly organized life. The instructions are clear enough for a normal human to read; if you’re accustomed to building furniture with only a power tool and a prayer, you’ll feel right at home. A typical assembly time lands around 2–4 hours for a first-timer who takes his sweet time, which means you’ll have a perfect sitcom-length episode of DIY injuries and triumphant post-assembly posture.

One tip: sort the parts by bag and sticker, lay them out like a tiny tactical battlefield, and don’t lose the little Allen wrench. It’s your friend and your nemesis in equal measure.
