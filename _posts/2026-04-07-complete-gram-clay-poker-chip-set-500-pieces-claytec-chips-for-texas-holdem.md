## Maintenance and care: keeping the chips glorious

The long-term health of your chips is largely about routine care. Here are some practical tips:

- Wipe chips after a night of intense play. A damp microfiber cloth is usually enough to remove fingerprints and residue without damaging the print or the surface texture.
- Store chips in a dry environment. Excess humidity can affect the finish and, over time, could cause slight warping or surface wear on very cheap sets. The Claytec set is robust, but you’ll extend its life by keeping it dry and out of direct sunlight.
- Avoid chemical cleaners. Most general-purpose cleaners can degrade the surface print or the edge design over time. Stick to gentle wipe-downs and avoid abrasive pads.
- Use a dedicated dealer button and AUX bits. The less you stress the chips during action, the longer they will stay in good condition.
