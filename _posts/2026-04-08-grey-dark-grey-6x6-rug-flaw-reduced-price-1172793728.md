### Maintenance and long-term care tips
- Vacuum regularly with a standard setting to avoid pulling fibers. The goal is to keep the surface looking tidy rather than performing dental floss on each fiber.
- Blot spills promptly with a mild detergent and water. Resist the urge to rub in circles; you’ll risk fuzzing the weave and creating a new design feature.
- Rotate the rug every few months to minimize wear patterns. You’ll train your furniture to behave properly across the surface and avoid a hero’s journey to the center of the rug to show your cat who’s boss.
- Consider a rug pad for extra cushion and to reduce slipping on smooth floors. A pad can also help with noise reduction and foot comfort during long gaming marathons.
