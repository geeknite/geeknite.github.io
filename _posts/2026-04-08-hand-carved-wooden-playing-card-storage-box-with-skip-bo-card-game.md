## Maintenance and Care Tips
- Regularly dust the exterior with a soft cloth. Fine dust is a friend of finish, not its enemy, but you don’t want to turn your wooden friend into a chalkboard for every speck of debris.
- Apply a light coat of mineral oil or a wood-safe wax every few months to preserve the sheen and prevent drying. This isn’t a pass to go full oiling-obsessed; a gentle wipe is enough.
- Keep away from water. Wood and water equal a recipe for warping; Don’t test the limits of the box with a damp cloth and a questionable joke about humidity.
- Use a soft, lint-free cloth when cleaning; no abrasive pads that might scratch the gleam of a well-chosen finish.
