Let’s talk numbers and performance, because that’s why you’re here, and also because the advertising copy told you to be excited about “4 power outlets & 2 USB ports.” The four AC outlets provide enough real estate for a typical home office: PC tower or laptop dock, an external monitor or two, a printer, and still a spare for the coffee warmer if you’re into that. Still, you’ll want to keep an eye on heat during long sessions—if you’re pushing devices at max power for hours, a fan or better ventilation near the desk won’t hurt.

- Keep the surface clean with a soft microfiber cloth to preserve the finish. Avoid harsh solvents that might affect the powder coat.
- Periodically check cable channels for debris. A quick tidying now and then prevents a future tug-of-war when you unplug a device.
