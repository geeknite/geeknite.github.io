## Durability and maintenance
A mat is an investment in your table space; it should not require constant TLC. Here is what we observed:

- Cleaning: a quick wipe with a damp microfiber cloth removes most smudges and coffee ring marks. For stubborn stains from marker pens or dry-erase, a mild soap solution works well. Do not soak the mat; air-dry flat to preserve the finish and prevent any potential warping.
- Wear and tear: the print is designed for long life under typical tabletop use. If you frequently drag heavy sleeves or dice across the surface, you might see minor scuffing in the long run, but nothing alarming or performance-affecting.
- Water resistance: the top layer repels minor spills well; you can wipe up water from accidental bumps without significant staining, which is a plus for the clumsy coffee enthusiasts among us.

Tips for longevity: avoid direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent color fade, and consider rotating the mat if you notice uneven wear from one edge being more used than the other. A simple fold or roll is fine for transport, but store it flat when not in use to keep the surface pristine.
