## Strategy and tips

- Don’t hoard the new counterplay cards. You want to have options when the cat party starts, not just a cool look at your hand. There is a sweet spot where you can swing a turn from doom to glory with a single card.
- Pay attention to the tempo. The expansion is about tempo as much as it is about power. If you rush, you will explode your own chances; if you overthink, you will stretch the game into a philosophical debate about why cats are so dramatic.
- Use the new cards to set traps. The value of deception has always been high in Exploding Kittens. The new cards simply give you flashier, more creative ways to mislead your friends into a risky move that ends with a dramatic reveal and a chorus of laughs.
- With younger players, emphasize the silliness. The goal is fun first. If someone gets discouraged, pivot to a more lighthearted moment — this is a party game after all and the kitten crew is notorious for turning a tense moment into a goofy moment.
- Don’t forget the art. The visual pun is part of the experience. When you see a card with a particularly goofy expression or an over the top scenario, it helps set the tone for the rest of the game and keeps the vibe buoyant rather than brutal.
