### Teaching it to non-gamers: grandma-friendly guide

Here are a few tips to teach the game to newbies, especially those who bring snacks with the zeal of seasoned explorers:

1. Start with a warm-up round: pick a non-competitive, super simple round to get the hang of the turn structure.
2. Demonstrate a “fail-forward” moment: show that missteps aren’t fatal; they’re an opportunity for humor and learning.
3. Use the posters on the wall: If your family has a phrase they use to remember rules, write it on a sticky note and place it near the play area.
4. Celebrate little wins: Even when you fail to complete a prompt, share a fun anecdote about the moment you realized your plan wouldn’t work. Laughter builds group memory better than any victory.
