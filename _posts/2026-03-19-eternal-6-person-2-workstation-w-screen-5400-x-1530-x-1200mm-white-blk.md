## Ergonomics and Comfort: Is This The One-Desk-To-SRule Them All?
From a pure ergonomic standpoint, the Eternal 6P2W attempts to solve a classic problem: how to keep six people comfortable while sharing a single workspace. The ergonomics kit typically includes monitor arms, optional privacy panels, and adjustable keyboard trays. In practice, you’ll want to allocate space for each person’s seat with enough leg room to avoid mutual foot-in-crotch moments caused by impromptu seminars on keyboard posture.
