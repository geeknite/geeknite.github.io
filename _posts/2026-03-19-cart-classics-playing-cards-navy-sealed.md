The deck’s longevity is tied to simple care. Store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. If humidity is a concern in your environment, consider a small desiccant pack in your storage case. If the finish accumulates fingerprints or minor smudges, a quick wipe with a microfiber cloth suffices. Avoid soaking or exposing the cards to liquids; the goal is to keep the stock stable and the finish intact for as long as possible.

For those who take deck maintenance seriously, you can extend the life of your Cart Classics by using a proper card case or sleeve. Mid-to-long-term storage in a protective enclosure will help preserve the color balance and the crispness of the faces, ensuring your deck remains photogenic for a long time.
