## What is Battle of Gods?

Battle of Gods is a competitive board game where players take on the roles of various gods from different mythologies. With stunning artwork, intricate miniatures, and a plethora of divine powers to wield, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Battle of Gods, players vie for supremacy by completing missions, defeating rival gods, and gaining followers. Each player controls a unique god, each with special abilities and cards. The goal is to reign supreme, proving once and for all who is the mightiest deity.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures representing gods from various mythologies. Seriously, they're so detailed you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: Each god has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and divine powers.
- **Game Board**: Various mythical realms where battles and missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Those claws are the real divine wrath.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Battle of Gods is like staging an epic mythological battle. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your god. Now you're ready to begin your quest for divine supremacy.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their god's abilities. You can move, attack, recruit followers, and more. Strategy is key, so plan your divine moves with care.

### Combat

Combat in Battle of Gods is a blend of strategy and luck. Each god has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have the rival god escape? Welcome to the divine realms, where plans often go awry.

## The Divine Awesomeness

### Gods and Realms

One of the best parts of Battle of Gods is the diverse array of gods and realms. From Zeus to Odin, you can play as your favorite deities and face off in legendary battles.

### Replayability

With multiple gods, realms, and scenarios, Battle of Gods offers endless replayability. No two games are the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more divine battles.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new gods, realms, and missions. Your divine universe is about to get even bigger.

## Pros and Cons
