## Weather resistance and maintenance: surviving the outdoors with style

Acacia wood loves sun, but it can fade and dry out with time if left unattended. The standard maintenance plan includes:
- Regular cleaning with mild soap and water to remove dirt, pollen, and the occasional bug who forgot to RSVP.
- Application of a penetrating outdoor sealant or oil every season (or every 3-6 months in busy climates).
- Covering the set when not in use or during heavy weather to protect the finish and cushions.

Materials like powder-coated metal resist rust, but salt air and acidic rain can still take a toll. If you live in a harsher climate, plan for more frequent maintenance checks and consider storing cushions indoors during winter or heavy monsoons. Quick tip: store cushions in breathable bags to prevent mold and mildew. Also, note that wood can develop a sun-bleached look over time; if you prefer the “new furniture” aesthetic, you’ll want to re-oil the wood regularly to maintain color uniformity.
