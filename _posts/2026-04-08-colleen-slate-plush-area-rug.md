### Cleaning and Care (H3)
Weekly vacuuming keeps the pile fluffy and upright. For spills, blot gently with clean towels; avoid rubbing because that pushes liquid deeper into the fibers. If you’re dealing with a stubborn stain, a mild dish soap solution will typically do the trick; test it on a small, inconspicuous area first to confirm colorfastness. Dry cleaning or steam cleaning should be avoided unless you’re experienced with carpet care, because heat can distort the pile and backing. In short, treat it like a pet and not like a stubborn stain of your teenage years.
