## Maintenance and cleaning: keeping it fresh without a lab technician

Maintenance is the boring but essential part of any outdoor purchase. The Avenue rug is designed with cleaning in mind. Routine upkeep is as simple as sweeping away leaves and giving it a quick rinse with a hose when needed. For stains, a light soap solution and a soft brush will usually do the trick. Do not try abrasive cleaners or high heat, which can degrade the fibers and reduce the rug’s life expectancy. Think of it as similar to maintaining a sustainable gadget: you don’t torture it with harsh chemicals, you give it a little care and it lasts longer.

A minor note: if you have a small pet with occasional odor, there are odor-neutralizing sprays that can be used in moderation. Always test a small patch first to ensure colorfastness. If you store the rug during a harsh winter, make sure it is completely dry before packing away to prevent mold growth. The manufacturer’s guidance aligns with practical advice, and that alignment is comforting for those of us who want to avoid a mildew party on our balcony.
