## Maintenance, Longevity, and Practical Tips

### Cleaning and care

Carry a small tray or pouch to prevent the dice from mingling with your poker chips or snack debris. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth is enough for most scenarios. If a die ever shows a stubborn smudge, a soft damp cloth and a touch of mild soap will do—just dry thoroughly to prevent any moisture seeping into the core. The goal is to keep the color vibrant and the edges sharp. 

### Storage considerations

A padded storage case is ideal. If you’re traveling to a friend’s house or a gaming con, a hard case with a foam insert protects the dice from the inevitable rolling battles that happen in a hotel room on the fourth floor. A tidy storage solution also makes it easier to locate the mood dice when you need them, which is crucial when emotions run high and the mood shifts from Joy to Anger mid-turn.

### Longevity expectations

With normal use, a high-quality dice set should last many years. If you’re rough on your dice, consider set rotation: use one set for casual games and retire it briefly after a particularly dramatic session to preserve its wear pattern. This is especially helpful if you’re a collector who wants to maintain the pristine look across many campaigns. The payoff is not just aesthetics, but the way your future self will remember the campaign’s emotional journey through the gloss and edges of the dices’ surfaces.
