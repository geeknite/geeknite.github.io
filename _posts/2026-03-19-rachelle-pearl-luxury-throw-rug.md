## Care and Maintenance Tips
- Vacuum weekly with a gentle setting to prevent buildup of dust and fur.
- Rotate every few months to ensure even wear.
- Treat spills promptly with blotting—avoid rubbing to prevent spreading the stain.
- If needed, launder according to the tag instructions; air-dry flat to maintain shape.
- Use a rug pad underneath for extra grip and to extend its life.
- Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods to preserve color.

### Quick fix hacks
A few smart hacks keep your rug looking fresh without professional help:
- A washable rug refresher spray can help refresh odors between deep cleanings.
- A DIY lint roller for quick fur removal saves you time on pet-hair mornings.
- A simple color-safe detergent solution is all you need for most stains; always test on a corner first.
