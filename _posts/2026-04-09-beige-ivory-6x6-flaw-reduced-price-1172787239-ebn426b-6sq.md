### Care and Maintenance
This rug is not immortal; it is resilient. Regular vacuuming will keep it looking sharp, ideally with a vacuum that does not suck up the fibers in a chaotic mess. Rotate the rug every few months to maintain even wear. If a stain occurs, treat promptly with a mild cleaning solution; test a small hidden area first to avoid accidental color changes. Blot, don’t rub, to protect the weave. For deeper cleaning, consider a professional service or a reputable home care product that is safe for synthetic fibers (if the rug is synthetic). The care instructions should be straightforward, but the key is consistency: a little caregiving goes a long way.
