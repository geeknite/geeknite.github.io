If you’re planning a party, here are some house rules that work well with this pack:

- Nostalgia boosters: At the start of a round, every player shares a quick personal memory from the 90s. If your memory isn’t plausible, you must choose the imaginary one that’s the most ridiculous. This adds a storytelling element that amplifies the laughter when the cards get played.
- Torn twist: If a card is especially torn or battered from years of storage, it earns a wildcard that lets you swap it for a fresh replacement card from the core deck. Keeps things fresh and gives players a moment to complain about card care from the era.
- Tempo control: For faster rounds, each player has 15 seconds to pick their best match. Quick thinking, faster laughter, and more chaos.
- Thematic pairing: Pair three to four 90s era prompts with one modern reference card. It creates funny dissonance and highlights how the era stacks up against today’s pop culture.
