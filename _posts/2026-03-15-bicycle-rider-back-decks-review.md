## Durability and Care
Durability matters when you are performing or playing for extended sessions. Here are practical tips to extend the life of your Rider Back blue and red decks:

### Everyday Care
- Keep decks away from moisture and heat to preserve the finish. A little humidity can cause warping or the finish to feel sticky in the wrong moments.
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Prolonged exposure to sunlight can cause fading or color shift over many uses.
- Avoid wiping the faces with rough materials. Clean fingers and sleeves are enough to prevent smudges on the backs.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
- If you notice dust or small particles on the surface, use a soft brush or microfiber cloth to remove it. Do not rub aggressively; power cleaning can degrade the finish and shorten deck life.
- For stubborn smudges, a quick wipe with a lightly damp cloth followed by air drying works well. Do not saturate the deck or submerge in water; this is a card deck, not a tiny submarine.

### Long-Term Storage
- If you are collecting or storing several colorways, consider keeping them in separate sleeves to prevent color transfer or paint rubbing during long storage periods. The Rider Back pattern remains iconic, and you want to preserve that crisp edge for future performances.
