### Care and Maintenance

Let us be realistic: any rug that is this styled is going to attract spills and the occasional snack crumb. For maintenance, we recommend regular vacuuming on low suction to avoid pulling at the tufts. For stains, blot rather than rub and use a mild soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals or high heat that could melt the synthetic fibers or dull the colors. Quick action is best when you spill energy drinks or coffee on it; no one wants a dramatic Venom stain.
