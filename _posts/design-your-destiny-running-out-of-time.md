## The Basics

### What is Design Your Destiny: Running Out of Time?

Design Your Destiny: Running Out of Time is a strategy board game that challenges players to manipulate time to achieve their goals. With stunning artwork, cool miniatures, and a plethora of timelines to alter, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### Components

The game comes with an array of beautifully designed components:

- **Miniatures**: Intricately crafted miniatures that represent your characters and timelines.
- **Cards**: Each player has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations and timelines where your missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, time, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Those claws are the real time paradox.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Design Your Destiny: Running Out of Time is like preparing for a cosmic battle. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your timeline. Now you're ready to begin your quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their character's abilities. You can move, attack, manipulate time, and more. Cooperation and strategic thinking are key, so plan your moves wisely.

### Combat

Combat in Design Your Destiny: Running Out of Time is a blend of strategy and luck. Each timeline has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have the timeline shift? Welcome to the multiverse, where plans often go awry.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: The time manipulation theme adds depth and excitement, making you feel like you're part of an epic saga.
- **Replayability**: With new timelines and characters, no two games are ever the same.
- **Beautiful Components**: The miniatures and cards are a feast for the eyes.
