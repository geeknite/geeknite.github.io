## Maintenance Tips and Tricks

- Use a lint roller before washing to reduce hair buildup in the machine.
- When drying, avoid high heat to prevent the fur from clumping; low heat or air dry is best.
- If you notice flattening, you can fluff the fur with a clean comb or a hairbrush to restore the surface appearance.
- For pet drool or stains, pre-treat with a mild detergent before washing.

### Quick cleaning hack for busy geeks

If you’re in the middle of a heavy gaming session and your pet decides to make a nest in the nap bed, you can spot-clean the plush top with a damp cloth and mild soap. Then, wash the cover separately when you have an open laundry window. It’s not as dramatic as writing a piece of code in assembly language, but it gets the job done.
