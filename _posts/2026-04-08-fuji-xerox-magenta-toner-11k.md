## Durability, reliability, and maintenance tips

Toner clogging is not the worst thing to happen to a printer, but it is near the top of the list of Things That Slow Down Your Day. The 11K has a reputation for consistent performance with a design that is easy to handle, replace, and store. A few practical tips to maximize reliability:

- Store cartridges in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight; sunlight is the enemy of magenta more than you would expect and can cause color drift over long storage times.
- Handle the cartridge with care during installation. Magenta particles are visible on paper when the cartridge is distracted by a coffee spill. Keep the work area tidy and avoid sudden jolts that could dislodge the micro-scale color particles.
- Run a light calibration page after installation to ensure the colors align with your printer’s color engine. A quick calibration is usually enough to bring things back in line and prevent color drift across a batch of documents.
- If your printer has a duplex mode, enable it to maximize paper savings. This will not only reduce paper waste but also increase the total print run before you are forced to swap cartridges again.

We also recommend keeping a spare magenta cartridge on hand. Not because you want to start a scavenger hunt in the office supply closet, but because the last thing you want on a Friday afternoon is to be stuck with a half empty magenta when you have a big customer proposal due by 5 pm. A backup cartridge reduces downtime and makes IT’s day a little less dramatic.
