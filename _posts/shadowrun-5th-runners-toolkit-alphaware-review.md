## What’s in the Toolkit?

### Components

The **Runner's Toolkit: Alphaware** comes with a plethora of goodies that will make any Shadowrunner jump for joy. The box includes:

- **Rulebook**: A comprehensive guide to the 5th Edition rules, with explanations, examples, and more footnotes than you can shake a stick at.
- **Character Sheets**: Pre-generated characters that range from the street-samurai to the hacker extraordinaire. Perfect for quick-starting your campaign.
- **Maps**: Detailed maps of various locations that will make your run planning a breeze.
- **Dice**: Custom Shadowrun dice that make rolling for initiative feel like a cyberpunk dream.
- **GM Screen**: A beautifully designed screen with all the essential tables and information to keep your game running smoothly.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the dice. Those little buggers have a tendency to disappear faster than a decker escaping a failed hack.

## Gameplay Enhancements

### Rulebook and Character Sheets

The rulebook included in the toolkit is a lifesaver for both new and veteran players. It breaks down the complex mechanics of Shadowrun into digestible chunks, making it easier to understand the core rules and nuances.

The pre-generated character sheets are a boon for those who want to jump right into the action without spending hours on character creation. Each character is well-balanced and comes with a unique backstory that adds flavor to your campaign.

**Funny Moment**: During one session, a player used a pre-gen character and accidentally introduced themselves with the wrong name. The ensuing confusion and hilarity lasted for an entire run!

### Maps and Dice

The detailed maps are perfect for planning your runs and visualizing the environment. Whether you're infiltrating a corporate building or navigating the seedy underbelly of the city, these maps provide the necessary detail to make your game immersive.

The custom dice are not just stylish but functional too. The unique design adds a touch of Shadowrun flair to your dice rolls, making every roll feel like part of the cyberpunk experience.

**Criticism Alert**: While the maps are beautifully designed, they can be a bit small for larger groups. If you have a lot of players, you might need to get creative with your map placement.

## GM Screen: The Ultimate Tool

### Design and Functionality

The GM screen is a work of art. Not only does it look fantastic, but it also includes all the essential tables and information a GM needs. From initiative to damage calculation, everything is at your fingertips, making your job easier.

**Pro Tip**: Use the GM screen to hide your snacks. No one needs to know you're munching on chips while orchestrating an epic battle.

### Enhancing the Game

The screen helps to keep the game's pace smooth and prevents the GM from constantly flipping through the rulebook. It’s a must-have for any GM who wants to keep the game flowing and maintain the immersion.

**Funny Moment**: One of our GMs once used the screen to hide their cat, who decided to join the session. The players were none the wiser until a stray paw appeared from behind the screen.
