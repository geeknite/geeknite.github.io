The instruction manual is usually straightforward but never shy about telling you where you made a critical error in earlier life decisions. Expect a lot of bolt-tightening, panel aligning, and the occasional “this is the moment you realize you should have read the measurements more carefully.” The key is to organize your hardware before you start.
