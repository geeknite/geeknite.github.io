## Size options: Custom size or 5x7

### Why size matters
A rug is more than decoration; it anchors the space and defines traffic flows. Custom size gives flexibility for awkward rooms, odd nooks, or you want to cover a long hallway without wasting extra material. 5x7 is the classic living room staple that packs enough presence for seating areas without overpowering the space.

### How to order custom size
Ordering custom size typically involves selecting width and length, then providing room measurements. Common steps include measuring the space, accounting for a rug pad, and ensuring that your furniture legs sit comfortably on the rug edge. In some cases, you can specify a nonstandard shape or rounded edges, though most consumers opt for a rectangular rug in a standard alignment.

### How 5x7 compares
The 5x7 size offers a balanced footprint, ideal for small to medium rooms. It leaves room for coffee tables and sofa legs while preserving walking space. If your room is heavier on seating than open floor, 5x7 gives a grounded feel without dominating the entire area.

## Durability and Maintenance

### Stain resistance and cleaning
Life in a modern home means spills are inevitable. Mesa Glacier is designed to handle common liquids with quick blot-and-go cleanup. Use mild soap and water for spot cleaning. Avoid harsh bleaches or abrasive scrubbers that can dull the fibers. For tougher stains, a gentle commercial rug cleaner approved for synthetic fibers works best.

### Vacuuming and shedding
Regular vacuuming on a low setting helps maintain the pile and keeps the fibers looking sharp. Do not use high suction on delicate woven patterns. A portable handheld vacuum can help reach edges without destroying the border pattern.

### Rug pads and safety
A rug pad is recommended under Mesa Glacier to prevent slipping, add cushioning, and extend the rug life. Non-slip pads are especially helpful on hardwood or tile floors where movement can turn a living room into a mini skate park.

## Practicalities: care, pad, and placement

### When to use a rug pad
Rug pads add grip and cushioning, reduce wear, and prevent slipping. They also insulate a bit against cold floors. For high-traffic areas, a high-friction pad helps the rug stay in place during cardio dance breaks or intense gaming sessions.

### Placement tips
Place the rug so that at least two legs of major furniture pieces sit on it to anchor the space. In larger rooms, you may want to layer with a smaller rug to create zones. Make sure the rug edges are accessible to prevent tripping hazards and to avoid curling corners.

### Cleaning schedule
Vacuum weekly, spot clean spills immediately, and do a deeper clean as needed following synthetic-safe cleaning guidelines. Rotate slightly every few months to prevent uneven wear from sun exposure.
