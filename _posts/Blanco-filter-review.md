## What is the Blanco Filter?

The Blanco Filter is a sleek, under-sink water filtration system that's designed to give you crystal-clear and tasty water right from your tap. No more lugging around heavy water bottles or pretending that tap water tastes just fine. With Blanco, you can be sure you're getting pure, delicious H2O.

The Blanco Filter uses a combination of activated carbon, reverse osmosis, and UV light to purify your water.
