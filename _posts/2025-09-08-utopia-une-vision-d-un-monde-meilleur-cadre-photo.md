## What is Utopia?

Utopia is the imagined perfect society where everything is as it should be. It's like living in a world where your Wi-Fi never drops, your coffee is always perfect, and people actually know how to use turn signals. Ah, bliss!

### Historical Perspective

**Thomas More** coined the term 'Utopia' back in 1516, describing an ideal society on an imaginary island. Since then, the concept has evolved, influenced by philosophers, writers, and, of course, our everyday complaints about why someone hasn't invented calorie-free pizza yet.
