## Care, Tips, and Tricks from the Field
### Everyday care
- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt from embedding into the fibers.
- Address spills quickly with a clean, damp cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent pulp-fiction style stain diffusion.
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and to extend the life of the fibers under furniture.

### Deep-clean strategy
If you have a stubborn stain or a particularly messy incident, consider a gentle cleaning solution designed for synthetic fibers. Always test in a small area first. For larger cleaning tasks, you might want to hire a professional rug cleaner who can handle 2 ft 3 in by 20 ft in stride and won’t treat your living room like a crime scene.

### Sunlight and color longevity
If your space basks in sunlight, you may want to rotate the rug or place it on a schedule to minimize sun exposure in one area. This helps with color longevity and reduces the risk of uneven fading across the cream and grey sections.
