## Maintenance, durability, and long-term care

Premium products demand respect. Here’s how to keep your Altered Edition aging gracefully rather than aging like a meme:

- Cleanliness: A soft microfiber cloth and a gentle wipe are usually enough. The internal foam can attract a bit of dust, so periodic light cleaning is advisable. Avoid soaking the foam or pressing it too hard with a damp rag.
- Humidity and temperature: Like most cardboard-adjacent products, avoid damp basements and extreme heat. A stable room temperature and a dry shelf will keep the finish from warping and the magnets from losing their snap after a year or two.
- Sleeve maintenance: If you sleeve heavily, consider rotating the inserts periodically to avoid seat-of-the-pants wear on specific compartments. It’s not a mandatory ritual, but it’s the kind of ritual that keeps your collection looking fresh for festival-season photos.

If you’re thinking about long-term care, you’ll appreciate the insert design that allows you to reconfigure without destroying the internal layout. It’s not a “forever” guarantee, but it’s closer to that ideal than a lot of entry-level storage solutions.
