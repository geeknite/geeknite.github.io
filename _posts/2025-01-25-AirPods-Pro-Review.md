### Customizable Fit

One of the standout features of the AirPods Pro is their customizable fit. With three different sizes of silicone tips, you can find the perfect fit for your ears. No more worrying about them falling out during your workout or commute.

### Active Noise Cancellation

Say goodbye to background noise! The AirPods Pro feature active noise cancellation, which means you can enjoy your music, podcasts, or calls without any distractions. Whether you're on a noisy bus or in a bustling coffee shop, these earbuds will keep you in your own world.

### Transparency Mode

Need to hear what's going on around you? The AirPods Pro have a transparency mode that lets you do just that. With a simple press of a button, you can switch from noise cancellation to transparency mode, allowing you to hear your surroundings without taking out your earbuds. It's perfect for when you're crossing the street or having a quick conversation.

## Battery Life
