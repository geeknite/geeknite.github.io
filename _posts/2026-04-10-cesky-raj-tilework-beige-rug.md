Care tips: keeping the tilework look pristine
---------------------------------
- Vacuum weekly with a gentle setting to avoid fuzzing the pile or distorting the tile motif.
- Rotate the rug every few months to maintain wear evenness and prevent the central tile cluster from looking overly beaten.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Avoid rubbing; the goal is to lift moisture without pushing it deeper into the fibers.
- For stains, mix a mild detergent with water and test on a small inconspicuous area first. If you’re ever unsure, call in a professional cleaner who specializes in textured rugs.
- Use furniture coasters under heavy chairs to prevent indentations and protect the carpet’s nap.
