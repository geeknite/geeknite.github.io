## Cleaning and Maintenance

### Routine cleaning
Vacuum regularly to remove loose debris. For weekly maintenance a quick hose off or a wipe with a damp cloth is enough. The low pile means you can give it a brisk brush to raise any flattened fibers.

### Stain removal
For stubborn stains use a mild detergent mixed with water. Rinse well and air dry. Do not use bleach or strong solvents which can affect the color.

### Drying and storage
After cleaning let it dry completely before rolling or storing to prevent mildew. If you store seasonally, roll the rug rather than folding to protect the fibers.

### Color upkeep
Place under shade for longer life if your space gets full sun all day. Rotating the rug a few inches every few months can help even wear and sunlight exposure.

### Anti slip
If you do not use a rug pad, you may want to consider adding one to reduce movement on slick surfaces. A simple plastic or rubber pad can help keep the rug in place.
