### How to Play in 2 Minutes or Less

The core loop is simple, but the magic is in the cooperative timing. You set the board, pick your hero roles, reveal a threat card, and roll to perform actions like investigate, defend, or distract. The team must coordinate to unlock clues and seal gates before the monster roams the town.

- Setup: lay board, shuffle clue deck, deal a small hand to each player
- Turn order: clockwise
- Actions: investigate, defend, distract, or use gadget cards
- Goal: stop the monster and close the gate before doom arrives

The rules are compact but rich, and you can start with a gentle pace and ramp up the tension as you go. One neat design choice is the way threat cards trigger multiple effects depending on the current game state, which ensures no two turns feel the same.
