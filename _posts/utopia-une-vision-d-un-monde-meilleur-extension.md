The expansion comes with:
- **New Tiles**: Futuristic landscapes and structures that will make your city planning even more complex.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Utopia: Une Vision d'un Monde Meilleur - Extension is like preparing for a high-tech future. Lay out the new tiles, distribute character cards, and choose your scenario. Now you're ready to embark on your visionary quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their characters' abilities. You can build, innovate, negotiate, and more. Cooperation and strategic planning are key, so make sure to think ahead and adapt to the new challenges.

### Scenarios

The new scenarios introduced in this expansion are inspired by futuristic visions and dystopian challenges. From advanced technology dilemmas to environmental crises, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different strategies.
