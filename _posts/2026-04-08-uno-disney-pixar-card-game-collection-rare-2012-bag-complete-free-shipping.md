## The buying experience: is the listing’s pitch real?
Listings that feature “FREE SHIPPING” and “COMPLETE” are seductive, especially when you’ve spent serious Saturdays refreshing your feed for a moment of shopping dopamine. The reality check? Shipping terms can vary regionally, and what constitutes “complete” can be ambiguous in some listings. A careful buyer will:
- Request high-resolution photos of each deck and the bag’s interior, to verify the exact contents.
- Confirm the return policy in case some cards show misprints or surface wear larger than what you’re comfortable with.
- Check for the presence of the rule booklet, which sometimes gets separated from the decks over years of storage.

If you’re feeling adventurous, consider negotiating with the seller about bundled shipping rates or additional protective sleeves for the cards. A well-packaged set increases your odds of arriving in pristine condition and reduces the brief moment of heart-attack when you see a corner ding under LED lighting.
