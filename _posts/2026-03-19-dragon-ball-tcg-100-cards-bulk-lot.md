Practical Tips for Sorting and Deck Building
Sorting 100 cards can be a meditative activity or a chaotic sprint depending on your mood. Here are some practical tips to maximize the fun and value:
- Create a gentle sorting workflow: Common, Uncommon, Rare, SR, Foil, Alt Art if present. It helps you see patterns and potential duplicates quickly.
- Set aside SRs and Foils immediately to prevent accidental resale guilt. You can weigh whether they belong in your binder, a trade pile, or a deck.
- Use a reliable binder system. Foils make great front-page material for your display pages.
- Consider building a theme deck around the SRs you pull. It might be a fun, casual project that you can show off to friends or at local events.
- Photograph and document the pulls. A simple catalog helps you track your growing collection and makes selling cards in trades easier later on.
