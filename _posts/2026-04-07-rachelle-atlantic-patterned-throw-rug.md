## Care, Cleaning, & Longevity
Long-term durability matters as much as looks. The Rachelle Atlantic Patterned Throw Rug is designed to withstand everyday life: spills, pet traffic, and the occasional dance-off in the living room. Here are practical tips to maximize longevity:
- Vacuum regularly to keep fibers plump and patterns sharp.
- Blot stains immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber damage.
- For stubborn marks, use a mild detergent solution and test on a hidden area first.
- Avoid machine washing if possible, to prevent potential print fading or edge curl.
- Check the underside occasionally to ensure the backing remains secure and free of debris.
