## What is Uno Attack and how does a You Pick SINGLE Replacement Card fit in?

The You Pick SINGLE Replacement Cards are a tiny subset of replacement cards designed to be swapped into the deck in place of certain standard cards. The idea is simple: you pick one replacement card, you insert it into the flow, and you watch the group re-calibrate their risk tolerance accordingly.

This is not a full deck overhaul. It is a targeted adjustment. The appeal is that for very specific table dynamics, a single replacement can tilt the balance in a satisfying way without forcing a full renegotiation of Uno rules.
