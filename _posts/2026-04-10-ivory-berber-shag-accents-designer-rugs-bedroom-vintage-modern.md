## Maintenance and care: keeping ivory bright
Maintenance matters when you choose ivory. The goal is to preserve the look while making life easier. Here are practical tips to care for Ivory Berber Shag rugs:

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle, low-suction setting. High power can flatten the pile and cause uneven texture. Work in sections so you cover the entire rug without missing edges. Use a brush attachment rather than beater bars that can pull fibers.
- If you spill something, blot gently with a clean cloth to absorb liquid. Avoid rubbing; friction can push the spill deeper into the fibers and create a stubborn stain. For stubborn stains, treat with a mild rug cleaner recommended by the manufacturer. Always test in an inconspicuous corner first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear, especially in high-traffic areas. Rotation helps prevent the rug from developing a distinct traffic pattern that can ruin the visual texture you paid for.
- Protect from sun exposure when possible. Ivory tones can yellow slightly if exposed to intense sunlight for long periods. If you have large windows, consider UV-blocking curtains or a rug that is placed where it gets less direct sun during peak hours.

### Stain prevention tricks
- Use a rug pad to minimize bunching and extend life. A pad also prevents slipping on hard floors, which reduces the risk of accidents.
- Treat light stains quickly. A dab of mild dish soap or a gentle rug cleaner often does the trick. For more stubborn marks, consult the rug care guide provided by Ivory Home Decor and follow their recommended steps.
- Avoid heavy foot traffic during cleaning sessions; you want to keep the pile erect and the fibers fluffy for as long as possible.
