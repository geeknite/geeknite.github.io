## Charging and safety: What to watch for
Charging behavior is another important piece of the puzzle. If you have a dedicated high-output charger, you can approach the upper end of the pack’s charging curve.

Safety is central to any third-party battery upgrade. Look for the usual protections: overcharge protection, short-circuit protection, and thermal safeguards. Battery health is another consideration. A 5540mAh pack may experience lower real-world capacity after hundreds of cycles, as Li-ion chemistry gradually loses some of its oomph. The exact cycle life is something manufacturers provide in datasheets; in real life, expect a few hundred cycles before notice of substantial drop-off. That means your battery might not feel as zippy after a year if you’re a heavy user, but for most folks, it’s still a major upgrade over a worn original.
