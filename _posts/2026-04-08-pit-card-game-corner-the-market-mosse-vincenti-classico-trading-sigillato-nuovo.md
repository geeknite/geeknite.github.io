## Solo play and variants

The Market shines most in a group setting, but there are room friendly variants worth trying. For solo players or a paired down session, you can experiment with a house rule that reduces the number of active trades per round while increasing the value of each successful trade. The aim is to preserve the energy of the original while making it adaptable to a tighter play environment. In our sessions, solo runs tended to focus on card counting and risk evaluation, which adds a puzzle element to an otherwise social experience.

### House rules and speed rounds

- Speed round: set a timer for 1 minute per round, force faster negotiation, and go for a quick endgame trigger.
- Team play: pair up players into trading duos and run simultaneous negotiations between teams.
- Silent round: remove shouting and rely on whispered deals to keep pace and humor intact.

These variants are optional and designed to keep the session fresh. The Market remains the same at its core, but these twists alter the tempo and give you a reason to bring the game back to the table next week.
