## Gameplay and mechanics
### How UNO Canvas differs from classic UNO
The core mechanics of UNO remain intact: numbers, colors, reverse, skip, draw two, wilds, and wild draw fours. The Canvas variant introduces an art-inspired twist that affects scoring or draw mechanics in some editions. The Billie Eilish edition emphasizes mood and atmosphere rather than a radical change in rules, which makes it approachable for purists and newcomers alike. The JFP02 edition, being sealed, keeps the experience pristine and ready to demonstrate to friends as you exhale softly and reveal the intricate card backs for the first time.

During play, players still need to match color or number, and strategic timing remains crucial for when to drop a wild card.
