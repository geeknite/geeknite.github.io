## Overview

**Good Cop Bad Cop: Promoted** is an expansion to the original **Good Cop Bad Cop** game. It maintains the same principles of bluffing, social deduction, and backstabbing, but adds new roles and abilities that make the game even more dynamic and chaotic. For those unfamiliar with the original game, **Good Cop Bad Cop** is a social deduction game where players are divided into two teams: Good Cops and Bad Cops. The objective is simple - find and eliminate the opposing team's leader.

### What's New?

The **Promoted** expansion introduces new roles, equipment cards, and abilities that add layers of strategy and complexity to the game. From the crafty **Sniper** to the unpredictable **Psychopath**, these new roles will have you second-guessing every move. The equipment cards also provide unique powers that can change the tide of the game in one dramatic move.

## Gameplay

### The Basics

At the start of the game, players are dealt three Integrity cards which are either **Good Cop** or **Bad Cop**. One of these cards will be revealed to the player to help them determine their loyalty. Players also receive one equipment card that gives them a special ability to use during the game. The goal is to find and eliminate the opposing team's leader using deduction, bluffing, and strategic use of equipment cards.

### New Roles

The **Promoted** expansion introduces new roles such as:

- **Sniper**: Can take out any player from a distance, but must be careful not to reveal their position.
- **Psychopath**: Has unpredictable abilities that can turn the game on its head.
- **Double Agent**: Works undercover and can switch sides at a crucial moment.

These roles add unpredictability and require players to adapt their strategies on the fly.

### Equipment Cards

The new equipment cards provide powerful abilities like instant reveals, protective gear, and extra actions. They can be game-changers when used effectively. However, they also make players targets, as everyone will want to neutralize the most powerful equipment.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Increased Complexity**: The new roles and equipment add depth and replayability.
- **More Chaos**: The game becomes even more unpredictable and hilarious.
- **Strategic Depth**: Requires players to think on their feet and adapt.
- **Great for Social Gatherings**: Perfect for parties and game nights.

### Cons

- **Learning Curve**: The new roles and abilities may be overwhelming for new players.
- **Potential for Frustration**: The unpredictability can lead to chaotic and unfair outcomes.
- **Reliance on Player Interaction**: Requires active participation and bluffing skills.

## Comparison to Original Game

While the original **Good Cop Bad Cop** is already a fantastic game, the **Promoted** expansion takes it to new heights. The added complexity and chaos make the game more engaging but also more challenging. It’s perfect for seasoned players looking for a fresh experience but may be daunting for novices.

## Final Recommendation

**Good Cop Bad Cop: Promoted** is a fantastic expansion that breathes new life into the original game. It’s chaotic, hilarious, and strategically deep, making it a must-have for fans of social deduction games. Just be prepared for the learning curve and potential frustration that comes with the added complexity.
