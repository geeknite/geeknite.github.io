## Stain Resistance and Maintenance
The creeping reality of homes with kids and pets is spills, stains, and the occasional lava-lamp moment when a candle is knocked over and the wax tries to audition for the rug. The Back Stain Rug promise implies resilience, though no rug is truly stain-proof. In practice, this rug handles common household mishaps with grace. The color tone and texture help camouflage the kind of accidents that happen in living rooms after dinner and a movie.

### Cleaning routine
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to prevent flattening and to keep fibers looking fresh.
- For spills, blot—not rub—using a clean cloth. Rubbing invites the spill deeper and increases the likelihood of a stubborn stain that requires more dramatic measures.
- For tougher stains, use a mild detergent solution and test on an inconspicuous corner first. Patience and a little elbow grease can go a long way.
- If direct sunlight is a problem, rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even aging and color wear.

### Longevity considerations
Rugs are long-term investments in both comfort and aesthetics. This Maize Linen rug shows promise for households that adopt a prudent cleaning schedule and avoid harsh chemical cleaners that could alter dye and texture. It’s not invincible, but it’s reliably durable enough to feel like a steady companion rather than a one-season fling.
