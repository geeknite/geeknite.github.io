All-weather claims aren’t magic; they’re a practical expectation. PE wicker holds its color better than natural wicker under sun, and the aluminum frame won’t rust. ### Assembly and maintenance
Assembly is the moment of truth for many sets. The good news: most 7-piece wicker configurations are designed for two-person assembly and ship with clearly labeled pieces and Allen wrenches. Expect roughly 60 minutes of assembly if you’re comfortable with basic tools and a solid playlist. Pro tip: sort pieces by type before you start, lay out cushions, and imagine you’re building a Lego set with a mortgage payment attached to it.

Maintenance is mostly about cleanliness and protection. Wicker likes dust and pollen, so a quick wipe with a damp cloth usually suffices. Removable cushions can be spot-cleaned or washed according to the fabric care instructions. If you’re in a climate with heavy rain or snow, store cushions indoors and use breathable covers to prevent moisture buildup. For stains, a mild detergent solution and gentle scrubbing do the trick without dulling color.
