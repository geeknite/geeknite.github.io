## Rules and How to Play in a Nutshell

If you are new to Phase 10, here is the gist you can skim while your coffee cools:

- The goal is to be the first to complete all ten phases across the rounds. Each phase has a defined combination, from two sets of three to a run of seven or more, or a mix depending on the phase number.
- On your turn you draw one card, then discard one card to your discard pile.
- You can also use wild cards to fill in gaps or substitute for any required card, depending on the phase you are attempting.
- The round ends when a player completes the current phase and discards their last card. Then scoring comes into play, and the next round begins with a new phase order.

The two-pack variant keeps these bones intact but adds two decks. This can affect how you plan the late-game strategies when the table is flush with options and everyone suddenly wants to go out with a bang.
