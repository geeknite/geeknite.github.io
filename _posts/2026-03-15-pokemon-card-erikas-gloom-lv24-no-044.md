## Collectors’ Guide: Grading, Protecting, and Displaying Erika’s Gloom LV.24

Let’s get practical. If you own Erika's Gloom LV.24 No. 044, here are some actionable steps to protect your investment and maximize display appeal:

- Storage: Use acid-free sleeves and a rigid top-loader or binder pages. Keep away from humidity and direct sunlight to prevent color bleed and edge wear.
- Handling: Wash hands or use cotton gloves if you’re a passionate purist. Avoid excessive handling; oils from fingers can cause gloss changes over time.
- Environment: Maintain a stable temperature and avoid basement or attic storage—extreme heat can warp the card stock and degrade the print quality.
- Display: A small acrylic stand or a display binder with a glass cover can showcase the Gloom without sacrificing protection.
- Documentation: Keep a simple note with provenance—where you acquired it, approximate date, and any grading that has been performed. A small record adds to its story and helps future collectors understand its journey.
