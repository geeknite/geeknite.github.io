## Durability and Longevity: How Long Will This Deck Last?

### How It Holds Up to Everyday Abuse

Like all good playing cards, this deck is designed for a life of micro-abuse: shuffles, drops, accidental spills of coffee that you pretend didn’t happen, and the occasional “I’ll just skim the deck to see the art” moment that ends with a corner crease. The Webbed deck will survive typical home use, with edge wear mainly showing up after a few weeks of heavy performance practice. It isn’t a professional-grade exercise in long-term archival quality, but it’s not a fragile glassine artifact either. If you treat your cards like a diligent pet, you’ll be fine.

### Cleaning and Maintenance Tips

Dust the deck lightly with a soft cloth; don’t soak it, and avoid direct heat. If you’re a sticky-fingered hero who loves to snack while you play (we’ve all been there), consider keeping a small sleeve for accidental smudges. For staining, a mild approach—water on a lightly damp cloth—will do the job. Do not run it through a washing machine. The deck is not a garment.
