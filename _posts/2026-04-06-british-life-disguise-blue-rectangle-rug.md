## Maintenance Rituals Worth Keeping

Maintenance is a rhythm more than a task. The rug rewards regular care with a longer life and a more consistent appearance. Here are some practical rituals to keep your blue rectangle looking fresh without turning your weekend into a cleaning crusade:

- Vacuum weekly to keep dust from embedding in fibers. A quick once-over is usually enough to restore the surface sheen.
- Treat stains promptly with a mild cleaner that is safe for synthetic fibers. Blot—do not rub—so you don’t create wear patterns that resemble old map routes.
- Rotate the rug seasonally if your living space gets more sunlight in certain corners. This helps prevent uneven fading and keeps the color tone even throughout the year.
- Use a non-slip pad if you have hardwood or tile floors and deal with high-traffic zones. It adds a safety buffer and helps the rug maintain a flat, polished look.

These small routines pay off in the long run, turning what could be a chore into a simple habit that protects your investment and your eyes from rug wrinkles that refuse to cooperate with your design scheme.
