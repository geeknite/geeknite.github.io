## Maintenance and Durability

A subconscious test of any desk is how well it survives daily wear. The GOF 1P’s mahogany veneer handles scuffs and minor spills with grace, provided you treat the surface with care. A little furniture polish every few months keeps the veneer looking rich. Immediate cleaning of spills with a microfiber cloth can prevent staining, and a damp cloth with a mild cleaner is generally sufficient for day-to-day maintenance. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the finish; think more “gentle spa treatment” than “industrial sandblasting.”

Durability-wise, the desk shoulders typical office loads well. If you’ve got a habit of heavy objects landing on the desk while you juggle cables, you’ll want to be mindful to distribute weight evenly and avoid extremely heavy equipment directly on the desk surface. The legs stay steady, the joints don’t loosen easily, and the overall build inspires a sense of confidence that’s rare in budget-friendly office furniture.
