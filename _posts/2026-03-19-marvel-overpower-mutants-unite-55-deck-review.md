## Card mechanics refresher

### Hero cards
Each hero card has a name, a power value, and a set of special abilities. The text on these cards guides how you engage enemies, boost allies, or chain combos. In Mutants Unite 55 you will see a focus on mutants with flashy power sets that reward you for building a cohesive team.

### Mutant allies and gear
Mutant ally cards provide additional bodies on the field and sometimes grant passive bonuses. Gear cards boost power or grant utility effects that help you navigate the chaos of a skirmish.

### Plot cards
Plot cards flip the script. They can alter initiative, grant extra actions, or twist the rules in a way that pays off when you plan two moves ahead.

### Power mechanics
Power economy matters. You manage energy, activate abilities, and time your big plays. The deck is designed to reward players who plan around two or three turns rather than brute-force solo power.

### Rarity and collectibility
In sealed form, Mutants Unite 55 promises a premium vibe and a small chance at a standout card that becomes a signature piece in a collection. The thrill of pulling a coveted mutant from the deck is part of the retro charm.
