## Grading and NM considerations
Near Mint is a standard grade for glossy holo cards that have minimal surface scratches and no significant whitening. The Gloom SV4a has a natural tendency toward micro-scratches from careless handling, so if you plan to invest, you want to request a deep inspection image set and a consistent light source. If you are buying raw, inspect the holo pattern under four different angles to confirm that you are getting that extra sparkle and not a washed-out image. If you are lucky, you will see the Gloom silhouette crisp against the shimmering background with edges neatly intact. Remember that a pristine NM card is often a conversation piece that becomes a focal point of any display, so handle with care and keep in mind that a small fingerprint smear can ruin the magic of the holo in a single moment.
