## Care and maintenance
### Cleaning basics
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers and curling edges.
- Wipe spills immediately with a soft, absorbent cloth; avoid rubbing vigorously on delicate fibers.
- If a stain sets, use a mild detergent mixed with water; test on a small inconspicuous patch first to avoid color loss.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to distribute wear and minimize obvious traffic lines.

### Rug pads and longevity
A quality rug pad under a 20' runner helps prevent curling and increases comfort. It also reduces the risk of slipping on hard floors. The pad creates a little air pocket that makes vacuuming easier and reduces noise when you move across the hallway. If you have hardwood or tile floors, this can be a small but mighty upgrade.
