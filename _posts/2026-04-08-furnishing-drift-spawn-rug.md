## Cleaning and Maintenance
### Routine Care
The standard care routine for the Drift Spawn Rug is delightfully straightforward. Vacuum regularly to remove dust and loose fibers; a low to medium suction is recommended to preserve the pile. If you notice dirt in the pattern, a quick damp cloth wipe can help without soaking the fibers. For spills, the sooner you address them, the better the outcome. Blot, don’t rub, and follow up with a light pass of the vacuum to re-fluff the fibers. A rug pad underneath can also extend the rug’s life by reducing movement and improving underfoot feel, especially if your floor is slick.

### Spill Management and Odor Control
Spills happen. That is the cosmic truth of house living. For coffee, juice, or cat milk, a blot-and-clean approach works wonders. For stronger odors from aromatic meals or late-night experiments with garlic bread, consider a brief wipe with a mild detergent and warm water, followed by air drying. The pattern will still be there to remind you that you once conquered a culinary experiment with the help of a rug rather than a towel on the floor.
