## What is Republic Struggle?

Republic Struggle is a board game where players take on the roles of various political factions vying for control of the republic. Through cunning strategies, alliances, and a bit of luck, players must navigate the tumultuous political landscape to come out on top.

### The Basics

In Republic Struggle, players must build coalitions, pass legislation, and engage in political intrigue. Each player starts with a unique faction, each with its own strengths and weaknesses. The goal is to gain influence and control of the republic by the end of the game.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Faction Cards**: Each faction has a deck of cards representing their abilities and strategies.
- **Board**: The game board depicts the republic’s political landscape, including key areas of influence.
- **Tokens**: Used to represent influence, resources, and various effects during the game.
- **Dice**: For those unpredictable moments where luck decides your fate.

**Pro Tip**: Don’t underestimate the dice! Sometimes, a well-timed roll can turn the tide of the game.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Republic Struggle involves laying out the board, distributing faction cards, and placing tokens. It’s like preparing for a political debate, but with more fun and less controversy.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their faction’s abilities. Actions can include forming alliances, passing legislation, or sabotaging opponents. The key is to balance your moves and keep an eye on your rivals.

### Combat

Combat in Republic Struggle isn’t about physical fights; it’s about political battles. Players must strategize to outmaneuver their opponents and gain control of critical areas. Expect a lot of “he did WHAT?!” moments.
