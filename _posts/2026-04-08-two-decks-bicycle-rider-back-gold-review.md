## Practical tips for use and care
- Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight to preserve that gold finish and prevent any edge fading on the foil
- Store the decks in a cool, dry place; humidity can affect the stock’s snap and the back’s gloss over time
- When performing, avoid heavy-handed shuffles early on; allow the deck to settle in with a few light shuffles and fans before attempting advanced moves
- Clean the surface with a dry cloth if you notice fingerprints; avoid liquids near the foil finish to prevent staining
- If you perform frequent color-change routines, subtle differences in red and blue can aid in misdirection, but ensure your lighting makes both colors legible for your audience
