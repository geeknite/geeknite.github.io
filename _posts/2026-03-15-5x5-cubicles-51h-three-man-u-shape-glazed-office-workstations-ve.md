## Maintenance and Care Tips
- Clean regularly: wipe glass with a microfiber cloth and a gentle cleaner to avoid streaks; laminate surfaces can handle water-based cleaners but avoid abrasive pads.
- Check connectors and joints every few months: tight screws are the difference between a stable workstation and a wobbly throne you only notice during a sprint review.
- Manage cables proactively: plan routes that minimize bends and keep cords out of the walking path.
- Use proper lighting: ambient lighting with task lamps reduces glare on the glass and eases eye strain over long days.

N.B.: The VE edition’s finishes may require slightly different care depending on material specifics. Always follow the manufacturer’s guidance for recommended cleaners and maintenance intervals.
