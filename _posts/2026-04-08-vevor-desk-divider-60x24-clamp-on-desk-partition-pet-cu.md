## Cleaning, maintenance, and durability
PET Cu surfaces are relatively easy to clean. A damp microfiber cloth with a mild cleaner is usually enough to remove fingerprints, coffee smudges, and the occasional stray marker scribble from a whiteboard brainstorming session. Avoid abrasive pads; you don’t want to convert your shiny copper tone into a scratched, dull patch that resembles a weathered shield after a dragon-slaying session.

For stubborn marks, a gentle cleaner or a dilute isopropyl alcohol solution works without harming the finish. Dry thoroughly to prevent water spots. With regular care, the panel maintains its aesthetic appeal and privacy function for a long time, which is exactly the kind of reliability most of us crave when the workweek starts to resemble a software update: slow, iterative, and hopefully painless.
