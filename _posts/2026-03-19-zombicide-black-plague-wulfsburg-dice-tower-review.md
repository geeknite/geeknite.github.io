The dice funnel is designed to minimize bounce and bounce-induced misreads, which means fewer do-overs and fewer moments where you swear you rolled a critical hit when the die clearly rolled off the table and into your beer. The tower is less a gimmick and more a tool for maintaining rhythm in the game’s tempo, which suits Zombicide fans who crave a sense of order amid the horde of undead.

In terms of compatibility, the tower plays nicely with the Wulfsburg board and the Black Plague components. The design feels like an extension of the campaign aesthetic rather than a separate add-on you must hide under the table when guests arrive.
