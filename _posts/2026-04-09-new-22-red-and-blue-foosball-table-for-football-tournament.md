## Maintenance: Cleaning, Longevity, and a Simple Care Routine
Like any fine-tuned instrument, this foosball table rewards a regular care routine. The surface should be wiped down with a damp cloth after use to remove fingerprints and any spilled beverages—yes, this is a real problem in households where snacks and competitive fervor collide. Avoid harsh chemicals that could strip the veneer; a mild soap and water solution works best, followed by a quick dry with a microfiber cloth.

The rods benefit from a light lubrication every few months to maintain smooth action. Use a silicone-based lubricant and avoid petroleum-based products, which can degrade plastic components over time. A quick wipe of the ball return track will keep it from getting jammed with dust and stray game pieces. If you keep the playing area free of clutter and the balls swapped out when they start to feel heavy or dented, you’ll extend the life of this table well beyond your first season of nightly showdowns.

Storage is simple: fold away any accessories, cover the table when not in use, and keep the area dry. You’ll be amazed at how this helps prevent warping and keeps the vibrant red-blue finish looking fresh for longer.
