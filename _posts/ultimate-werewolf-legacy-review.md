## What is Ultimate Werewolf Legacy?

Ultimate Werewolf Legacy is a legacy-style board game designed by Ted Alspach and published by Bezier Games. Unlike regular Werewolf, this version introduces an ongoing storyline that evolves over multiple sessions, with each decision impacting future games. Think of it as "Werewolf" meets "Game of Thrones" but with fewer dragons and more fur.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Legacy Decks**: These dictate the storyline and events that unfold throughout your campaign.
- **Character Cards**: Villagers, werewolves, and other roles you'll play during the game.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track various game elements, including village status and character conditions.
- **Legacy Journal**: A special booklet to record the outcomes of each session and track the evolving story.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the character cards. They might have a vendetta against werewolves!

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Ultimate Werewolf Legacy is like preparing for a medieval festival, but with more paranoia. Distribute roles, read the scenario from the legacy deck, and prepare for a journey full of deceit, trust, and occasional betrayals.

### Gameplay

Each session follows a similar structure to traditional Werewolf. Players take on secret roles and try to uncover who the werewolves are before they can eliminate the villagers. But in Ultimate Werewolf Legacy, each game impacts the next. Decisions carry over, characters evolve, and new roles are introduced.

### The Legacy Twist

Unlike regular Werewolf, the legacy aspect means you'll be playing a series of connected games. Your village changes, characters can gain abilities or be cursed, and new roles might appear. It's like playing an epic TV series where you're the star.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, a player who had previously been a werewolf made a heartfelt plea to be trusted as a villager. Spoiler: They were still the werewolf. The resulting betrayal was legendary.

## The Story

### Campaign Mode

Ultimate Werewolf Legacy is divided into chapters, each containing multiple scenarios. The story evolves based on the outcomes of each game, with new twists and turns that keep players engaged and excited for what's next.

### Character Evolution

Characters can gain abilities, become cursed, or change roles entirely as the campaign progresses. This adds a layer of complexity and strategy, as players must adapt to the evolving storyline.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Engaging Storyline**: The legacy aspect adds depth and continuity, making each game feel like part of a larger narrative.
- **High Replayability**: With multiple scenarios and evolving roles, every campaign is unique.
- **Strategic Depth**: Decisions have lasting consequences, requiring careful planning and strategy.
- **Fun and Suspenseful**: The social deduction elements create memorable moments and hilarious betrayals.

### Cons

- **Complex Setup**: Setting up for each session can be time-consuming, especially with the legacy components.
- **Requires Commitment**: The campaign mode means you'll need a dedicated group willing to play multiple sessions.
- **Punishing Mechanics**: Some players might find the legacy penalties harsh, especially in early games.
- **Limited Player Count**: Best played with larger groups, which might not appeal to smaller gaming circles.
