### Strategy tips: making the most of the Raccoon chaos
A few practical pointers to help you maximize fun and minimize chaos-induced heartbreak:

- Start with a short practice round to let everyone acclimate to new interactions before committing to a full game. This reduces early misplays and keeps the mood light.
- Appoint a chaos captain. This player explains new interactions to newer players and keeps the table moving, preserving fairness and fun.
- Don’t hoard too hard. The expansion rewards dynamic decision-making. Clinging to one card for the perfect moment usually means you miss a game-changing swap.
- Embrace misdirection. The new mechanics invite bluffing and social engineering. If your group enjoys playful deception, lean into it and collect the stories that follow.
- Pair with a movie or a short-form media binge. For longer game nights, a light, humorous backdrop can keep energy buoyant and reduce fatigue during tense moments.
