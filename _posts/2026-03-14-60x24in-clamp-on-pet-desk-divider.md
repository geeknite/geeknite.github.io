## Maintenance and cleaning
PET is relatively easy to clean. A wipe-down with a damp cloth is usually enough to remove smudges and coffee splashes. For stubborn grime, a mild soap and water solution works well. Avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the surface or dissolve the fragility of your self-esteem when you realize you spilled coffee again. ## Comparisons: how does it stack against the other options?
- PET vs acrylic: PET provides good clarity and a lighter heft, with less risk of cracking under accidental drops. Acrylic can offer slightly crisper optics and higher scratch resistance, but it’s more prone to shattering if you drop a stapler on it or someone attempts a dramatic desk dive. In a spicy workplace, PET’s forgiving nature can be a blessing.
- Fabric panels: Fabric layouts give a soft aesthetic and some noise dampening but generally lack the snap-on convenience and clarity of PET. If you want a more “soft room” feel, fabric may be preferable; if you want a clean, modern, low-profile shield, PET does well.
- Glass: Real glass is heavy, fragile, and terrifying to purchase secondhand. PET is the friendly alternative, offering a similar visual vibe without the glass-like fragility.
