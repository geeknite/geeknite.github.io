## Maintenance and Longevity
Caring for the pod is straightforward. Wipe the exterior with a microfiber cloth and a mild cleaner as needed. Avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the matte finish. The interior fabric and acoustic panels can collect dust over time; use a soft brush or a handheld vacuum to keep the panels resonating with optimism rather than dust particles. Inspect the seals around the door every few months and re-seal if you notice any gaps; better to address a tiny air leak now than fight it during a crucial meeting.

Over the years, the screws and brackets will see the most wear. Check them periodically and tighten as needed. If you use the pod heavily, you might want to stock a small set of replacement screws and brackets so you can do quick fixes rather than waiting for a repair call.
