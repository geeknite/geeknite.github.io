### Maintenance and care: keeping blocks book-ready

Wooden blocks respond well to light cleaning with a dry microfiber cloth. Avoid soaking blocks or letting them linger in damp environments, which could cause warping or finish wear. If you notice rough edges after extended use, a very gentle sanding with fine-grit sandpaper can refresh the feel, followed by a light coat of wood-safe oil or wax. The goal is to preserve the tactile quality and the aesthetic of the book-spine finish without turning the whole thing into a DIY project that consumes your entire weekend.
