## Practical Considerations
### Size and Placement
You’ve probably seen the standard rug sizes: the gym mat for a treadmill, the airplane runway for the coffee table, or the “I have no idea what I’m doing” large room staple. Cambridge Contemporary Grey Rug comes in multiple sizes, which makes it easier to tailor to your space. Here is a quick sizing cheat sheet in Geeknite style: measure your sofa width, subtract two feet, and you’re probably in the right ballpark. If you’re unsure, opt for something a bit larger; bigger is almost always better than smaller in the world of rugs.
