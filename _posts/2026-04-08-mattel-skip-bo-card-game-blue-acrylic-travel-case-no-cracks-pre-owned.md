## Extra considerations: care, maintenance, and longevity

Acrylic is generally robust but not invincible. To keep your blue beauty looking fresh, follow a few simple steps:

- Cleaning: Wipe with a soft microfiber cloth and a mild soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that can dull the surface or remove the blue tint. If you spot a stubborn smudge, a touch of isopropyl alcohol on a cotton swab will do the trick, presumably without compromising the protective seal—though we always recommend testing on a small corner first.
- Avoid heavy impact: As with any glasslike material, avoid dropping the case on hard floors. A friendly reminder for you to slow down during those airport sprint finals because the only thing you want cracking is the sound of your neighbor losing to your strategic Skip-Bo feats.
- Storage and humidity: Keep the case in a dry environment. While acrylic is moisture resistant, you do not want to bake the surface in direct sunlight for hours on end, lest you risk warping the edges or making the blue look more electric than a fluorescent lightning bolt.

We also want to emphasize the pre owned factor again: you are purchasing a piece with a bit of history. That history gives it character and a narrative that you can brag about at your next game night. The case can serve as a reminder that gaming gear evolves with your adventures, and sometimes the best finds are those that carry a story you can actually tell without technical jargon and a ring-bound manual in your pocket.
