## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity
Refurbished units deserve some extra love. Here’s a quick care checklist to extend the life of your Skyler Gen 1:

- Clean the lenses with a microfiber cloth and a gentle, alcohol-free cleaner. Avoid abrasive materials that could scratch the optical surface.
- Store in the included case when not in use. The case isn’t just for show; it helps protect the lenses from smudges and accidental drops.
- Regularly check the hinges and frame for looseness. If you notice any wobble, a quick visit to a tech shop or your local optician for preventive tightening can save you headaches later.
- Keep firmware up to date if the device supports OTA updates. Updates often include performance improvements and security patches that make your experience smoother.

Careful handling ensures your Skyler continues to look sleek and perform reliably—like a gadget that’s had a second chance and decided to go full superhero.
