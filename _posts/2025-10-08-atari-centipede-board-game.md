## What is Atari Centipede Board Game?

Atari Centipede Board Game is a tactical game where players take on the roles of either the heroic gardener or the malicious centipede. It's a fight for survival in the garden, with beautiful artwork and miniatures bringing the classic arcade game to life on your table.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of centipedes, spiders, and the brave gardener.
- **Cards**: Action cards that represent your moves, attacks, and special abilities.
- **Game Board**: A colorful garden battlefield where the epic showdown takes place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the miniatures. Those fangs are the real garden pests.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Atari Centipede Board Game feels like preparing for a gardening battle. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your side. Now you're ready to begin your quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their character's abilities. You can move, attack, and use special powers. Cooperation is crucial for the gardener, while the centipede player tries to wreak havoc.

### Combat

Combat in Atari Centipede Board Game is a blend of strategy and luck. Each character has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic move just to have a spider ruin it? Welcome to the garden, where plans often go awry.
