### Thematic Cards and Special Moves

What would a Tampa Bay edition be without a few thematic twists? In the Red Yaya edition, you’ll encounter a handful of new special-action cards designed to evoke football strategy rather than typical UNO chaos. Expect modes like:

- Huddle Up: Forces all players to skip one turn and draw two cards to simulate a quick team huddle.
- Blitz Draw: The player who plays this card makes every other player draw four cards, mirroring a high-pressure blitz in real football.
- Red Zone Reversal: Reverses play direction when you land in the red zone (i.e., a special sub-zone in the deck that sits between Score card and Wilds).
- End-Zone Wild: Wild card with a quarterback-style animation on the foil. You can declare any color, but you must shout a football-y line to justify it.

All of these are designed to feel like a game-day playbook rather than a mere card deck. They are fun, loud, and a little cheeky, which is exactly what you want when your friends insist on turning UNO into a three-hour strategy seminar.
