## Assembly and maintenance
Assembling a 7-piece set can be an exercise in patience, but it often becomes a family bonding activity—mid-assembly, you realize your veggies and chips have become the real entertainment. The instructions are usually straightforward, with numbered parts and labeled screws. A basic toolkit is often included, but having your own Allen wrench set on standby is a good idea.

### Step-by-step assembly overview
- Unpack and sort parts by type. - Attach legs to the table, then secure the chairs to the frame. - Attach cushions and test for wiggle or squeaks. - Tighten all bolts after the first use and re-check after a few days of weather exposure.

### Maintenance routine
- Clean regularly with a mild soap solution and rinse; avoid heavy pressure washers on the wood. - Treat the wood with a suitable outdoor wood oil or sealer to preserve color and resistance to humidity. - Store cushions indoors during extended rains or winter months to prolong cushion life. - Covering the set when not in use can help preserve color and finish.
