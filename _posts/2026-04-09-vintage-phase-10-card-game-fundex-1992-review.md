## H2: What is Phase 10? A Quick Refresher
Phase 10 is a game about progression through ten distinct phases. The goal is simple: complete Phase 1, then Phase 2, up to Phase 10, before your opponents. Each phase has specific requirements, such as collecting sets of matching cards or creating runs of consecutive numbers. As you advance, the difficulty scales, and the deck provides the tools to improvise, adapt, and occasionally bluff with the confidence of a card-counting wizard who forgot to count cards.

For those unfamiliar with the core concept, Phase 10 is often described as a blend of rummy-like sets, a dash of canasta, and a pinch of Gin, all wrapped in a kid-friendly package with a splash of 90s optimism. The Fundex edition faithfully preserves the core mechanic: complete your current phase, then Press the Next Phase button on the fate of the deck and hope your opponents don’t ridicule your choices too harshly.
