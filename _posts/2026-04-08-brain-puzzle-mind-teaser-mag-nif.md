- Each challenge presents a board configuration and a target pattern. You must rearrange the magnets on the board so that the arrangement satisfies the rules of the current puzzle.
- Most puzzles require you to create a specific path or shape with magnets that will lock into place by magnetic attraction, turning a potential chaos into a choreographed dance of metal and math.
- Some challenges introduce constraints such as time limits, minimum number of moves, or a need to assemble multiple sub-structures that must coexist without interfering with one another.
