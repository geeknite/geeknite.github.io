## The Basics

### What is The Great War: Whippet Tank Pack?

The Whippet Tank Pack is an expansion for the acclaimed *The Great War* board game, designed by Richard Borg. This expansion adds the iconic British Whippet tanks to your armies, enhancing the tactical depth and historical authenticity of the game. If you're a fan of tank warfare and historical strategy, this expansion is a must-have.

### Components

The expansion pack includes beautifully detailed miniatures of the Whippet tanks, new scenarios that incorporate tank warfare, and additional rules for incorporating these behemoth machines into your battles. The quality of the miniatures is top-notch, with intricate details that will delight model enthusiasts and gamers alike.

### Setting Up

Adding the Whippet Tank Pack to your game is straightforward. ### Strategic Depth

The introduction of the Whippet tanks introduces new layers of strategy. These tanks are not just powerful in combat but also require careful planning and coordination with your infantry units. The balance between mobility, firepower, and armor makes them a valuable asset on the battlefield.

### Tactics and Strategy

The Whippet tanks excel in breaking through enemy lines and providing support to advancing infantry.
