### Care and maintenance
Chips tend to be robust, but they aren’t immune to fingerprints and grime, especially in homes with pets or kids who sometimes treat chips like small toys. A quick wipe with a soft, dry cloth after a game night is usually enough to keep them looking fresh. If you do need to clean them more thoroughly, avoid soaking them in water; a light damp cloth followed by air drying tends to work well.

For the case, keep the exterior clean with a mild cleaner and a soft cloth. The interior foam should be kept dry and free of moisture to prevent peeling or molding in humid climates. If you store the set for long periods, remove the chips from the case every so often to prevent the foam from forming an imprint on the chips.
