## Care and Maintenance Guidelines
### Routine care
- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt from embedding in the short pile.
- Rotate the rug every couple of months to ensure even wear.
- Use a rug pad under the rug for extra grip and longevity.

### Cleaning spills and stains
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Do not rub, or you’ll drive the stain deeper.
- For most stains, a mild detergent solution works well. Test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rinse lightly with clean water to remove soap residue, then blot dry.
- For tougher stains, repeat the process or consider a professional cleaning if the stain persists.

### Washing instructions
- Check care labels; if machine washable, use a gentle cycle with cold water.
- Use a mild detergent and avoid bleach unless specified by the manufacturer.
- Dry flat or hang to air-dry; avoid high heat to prevent shrinking.
- Do not tumble-dry on high heat unless the label explicitly states it’s safe.

### Long-term care tips
- Place a rug pad under the rug to reduce wear and prevent slipping.
- Keep direct sunlight to a minimum to preserve color vibrancy.
- Address pet hair with a microfiber cloth or a vacuum attachment designed for hair removal.
