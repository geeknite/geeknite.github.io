### Size and Fit: Finding Your Floor’s Destiny
Rug sizing is a life choice, not a one-liner. The Stockholm Jan Rectangle Rug is offered in several standard rectangular sizes that can accommodate everything from a compact apartment foyer to a living room with the scale of a science fiction epic. The right size depends on your space and your furniture footprint planning. A rug that is too small will visually disappear under a coffee table, making your room feel chopped up. A rug that is too large can overwhelm a smaller living area and turn foot traffic into a floor-based obstacle course. The trick is to outline the rug with a generous breathing room: a few inches around the coffee table and chairs to create a cohesive anchor for the seating area.

In practice, most geeks prefer a rug that sits in the sweet spot between under-table coverage and visible floor frame. It is the design equivalent of installing a GPU cooler with just enough clearance: not a brick, not a whisper, just right. If you are unsure, a simple rule of thumb is to let the front legs of the main sofa rest on the rug, with at least the front legs of the coffee table lounging on top as well. It creates a unified base that screams style without shouting, a vibe that is very much in line with the Geeknite aesthetic: functional, comfortable, and a little bit witty.

### Maintenance and Cleaning: The Practical Nerd Handbook
The best rugs in nerd culture knowledge are the ones you do not have to think about every day. The Stockholm Jan Rectangle Rug is designed with maintenance in mind. Routine vacuuming to remove dust and pet hair should be enough for daily life. If there are spills, blot immediately with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing which can push stains deeper, and if you must, you can treat with a mild detergent solution recommended for synthetic fibers. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first. For stubborn stains, a gentle cleaning with a carpet cleaner or a soft brush followed by air drying can bring back the texture without leaving a stiff outline around the spill site.

Next-level nerds will appreciate that the rug dries relatively quickly, thanks to the fiber blend and backing composition. This minimizes the downtime between epic gaming sessions and the dreaded post-laundry mountain. It also helps prevent that dreaded smell of damp fabric that can accumulate when a rug sits in a humid corner after a spill. If you live in a climate with damp winters, you might want to consider rotating the rug seasonally or investing in a rug pad to add extra cushion and reduce moisture-related concerns.
