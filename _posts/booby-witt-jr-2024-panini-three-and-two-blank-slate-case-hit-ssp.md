## The Basics: What is a Case Hit-SSP?

In the world of card collecting, terms like “case hit” and “SSP” (Super Short Print) are thrown around like confetti at a parade. But what do they mean? Simply put, a case hit is a rare card that is guaranteed to be found in one out of every case of card packs. An SSP is even rarer, with a much more limited print run compared to your average card. Combine these two, and you've got a card that's rarer than a unicorn riding a rainbow.
