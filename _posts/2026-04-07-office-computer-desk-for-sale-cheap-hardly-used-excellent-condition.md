## How to Buy with Confidence

- Inspect the surface for scratches, dents, or water damage. A light wear is normal; deep gouges should be negotiated into the price or flagged for replacement.
- Check the frame and joints for wobble. If you can shake the desk and it rattles, you’ll want to re-check the screws and perhaps request a discount. A desk that wobbles is rarely a joy to type on.
- Confirm the dimensions against your space. Measure your room, doorways, and the chair’s clearance to avoid a post-delivery “oh no” moment.
- Confirm delivery or pickup options. If you’re not sure about hauling a desk up a flight of stairs, the seller might offer curbside delivery or professional assembly for an additional fee.
- Ask about warranty or return options. Some listings come with limited warranty against structural issues for a short period; others are strictly final sale. It’s worth asking, even if you plan to treat this as a solid purchase rather than a risk-laden gamble.
