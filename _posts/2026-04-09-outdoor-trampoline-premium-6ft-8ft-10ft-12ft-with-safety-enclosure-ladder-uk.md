## Maintenance and safety tips for the practical Brit
- Inspect monthly for rust spots on the frame and worn padding around the springs; replace before the patchwork appears.
- Check that the enclosure netting has not started to sag—if it has, it's probably time to tighten or replace.
- Clean your jumping mat to avoid slick algae patches after a wet spell; a simple soapy rinse works wonders.
- Anchor the unit if you live in a windy region or if your trampoline sits on soil that refuses to stay put.
- Keep a basic first aid kit nearby; you might not use it often, but you will thank yourself when you need a Band-Aid for a small misstep.

### Safety considerations specific to UK weather
The United Kingdom has a reputation for mood-swing weather: one minute you have sunshine, the next you're negotiating a downpour while your kids pretend to be pirates.
