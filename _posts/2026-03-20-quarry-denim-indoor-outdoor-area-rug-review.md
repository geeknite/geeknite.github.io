## Cleaning and Maintenance

Cleaning is a reality check for most rug buyers. The Quarry Denim Indoor-Outdoor rug is designed to be low maintenance. Spills should be blotted as soon as possible to avoid staining, but the color and fiber composition are forgiving enough to handle the occasional contact with food or beverage mishaps. For surface dirt, a quick shake or sweep usually suffices. When deeper cleaning is needed, a mild detergent and a light scrub followed by a thorough rinse can restore the look without harming the fibers. For severe stains, a targeted stain remover rated for synthetics is a sensible option, but always test on a discrete area first to avoid surprises.

If you live in a high humidity environment, occasional air drying after a cleaning session will prevent any residual moisture from lingering in the fibers. And yes, you can hose this rug down if you want to conquer the biggest outdoor cleanup challenge of your life. Just be mindful of the location and avoid power washing directly on delicate corners to reduce edge fraying risk.

### Care Tips

- Rotate every few months to even out wear and sunlight exposure
- Use a rug pad to prevent edges from curling and to improve comfort
- Clean spills promptly and avoid harsh solvents on synthetic fibers
- Protect from direct aim of water from heavy irrigation to minimize pooling around the edges
- Check for sun fading after extended exposure and adjust placement if needed
