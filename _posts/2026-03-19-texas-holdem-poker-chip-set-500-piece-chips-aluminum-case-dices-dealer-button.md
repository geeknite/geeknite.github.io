## Care, maintenance, and longevity tips

To keep your chips looking fresh and your dealer button legible, a couple of practical tips:
- Keep the set away from direct sunlight to avoid color fading on the chips.
- Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth after a spicy night when you’ve spilled drinks or snack crumbs on the table.
- When transporting the kit, use a protective sleeve or bag to avoid scratches on the case and to keep chips from rattling against each other.
