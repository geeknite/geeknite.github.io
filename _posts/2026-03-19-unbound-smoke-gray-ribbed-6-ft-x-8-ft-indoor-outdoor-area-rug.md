## Maintenance and care: how to keep it looking legendary

### Day-to-day care
- Shake out or vacuum regularly to remove dirt and hair clumps. A lightweight vacuum head or a broom can do wonders here.
- Wipe spills immediately with a damp cloth. Don’t rub; blot to avoid fiber distortion.
- Use a mild detergent if needed. Avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the fibers or the UV stability that keeps color from ghosting over the years.

### Deep cleaning and stain removal
For deeper grime, a gentle rinse with water and air-drying is usually enough. If you’re dealing with stubborn stains from kids or pets, a mixture of water and mild dish soap can be applied with a soft brush. Rinse thoroughly and lay flat to dry; avoid direct heat that could warp the backing. Remember: patience is a virtue, and a well-dried rug is a happy rug. If you’ve got a machine-wash option, verify the manufacturer’s guidelines first. Some indoor-outdoor rugs tolerate machine washing, others don’t; in the spirit of Geeknite, we respect the sacred ritual of reading the manual before attempting a space-roll of doom.

### Cleaning schedule as a minimalist protocol
- Weekly quick clean: vacuum or shake.
- Monthly deep clean: floor-safe cleaner and spot treatment as needed.
- Seasonal refresh: rotate the rug if you’ve got inconsistent foot traffic patterns or if you’re staging a new decor moment.
