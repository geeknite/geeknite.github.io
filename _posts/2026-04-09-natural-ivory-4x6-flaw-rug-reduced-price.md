## Cleaning, Care, and Durability: The Practical Geek’s Guide

### Routine Care
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling threads.
- Treat spills as soon as possible with blot-and-dab techniques to minimize staining, especially if the dye variation is anywhere near a dye-lot quirk.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to test whether the flaw keeps playing peekaboo in the same spot or moves its dramatic stage presence.

### Deep Cleaning and Maintenance
- For heavier spills, consider a mild specialty carpet cleaner test on a small area first. Always follow the manufacturer’s instructions and test a corner that isn’t in sight from the main seating area.
- Professional cleaning every 12–18 months can restore softness, lift dirt, and keep color tones balanced, especially for ivory tones that are more susceptible to visible wear.
