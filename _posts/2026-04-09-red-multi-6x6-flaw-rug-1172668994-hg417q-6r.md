## Maintenance and Care Tips
To preserve the look and life of any rug, you need a simple care routine. Here are practical guidelines for the Red Multi 6x6 Rug with Flaw that align with Geeknite values of doable, not-OCD, and a sense of humor:

- Vacuum regularly. Do not vacuum the stain or the drama. The goal is to remove dirt before it embeds itself in the fibers.
- Blot spills promptly. Do not rub; that is how you create a bigger patch of drama. Use a clean cloth and a mild detergent mixed with water if needed. Work from the outside of the spill toward the center to prevent spreading the stain in a circular fashion.
- Use a rug pad. A good pad reduces slipping and improves underfoot comfort. It also helps in preserving the backing and extending the life of the rug.
- Rotate occasionally. If sunlight hits one side more than the other, rotation helps ensure even fading and reduces the appearance of wear in a single zone.
- Spot-clean with color- safe products. Test any cleaner in an inconspicuous corner first. The goal is to maintain color integrity while removing grime.

If you ever doubt the value of rug care, consider the alternative: a rug that looks like a crime scene after a party with bold judges and a low lighting scheme. The right care routine keeps the drama contained and your floor looking fabulous.
