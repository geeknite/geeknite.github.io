## The Basics

### What is Zombicide: Prison Outbreak?

Zombicide: Prison Outbreak is a cooperative board game that pits players against relentless waves of zombies in a prison setting. Developed by Guillotine Games, this expansion brings new challenges, characters, and scenarios to the popular Zombicide series.

### Components

The game is packed with high-quality miniatures, detailed tiles, and a plethora of cards and tokens. From the grotesque zombie figures to the gritty prison tiles, every component is designed to immerse players in the post-apocalyptic chaos.

## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Zombicide: Prison Outbreak is like preparing for a horror movie marathon. Lay out the tiles, shuffle the cards, and place the miniatures. Don't forget to keep the snacks within reach because once the zombies start coming, there's no time for breaks.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions such as moving, searching for weapons, and attacking zombies. Cooperation is key, so coordinate with your fellow players to make the most of your abilities and stay alive. And remember, running away is a perfectly valid strategy.

### Combat

Combat in Zombicide: Prison Outbreak is a blend of strategy and luck. Players roll dice to determine the outcome of their attacks. Did you plan an epic attack just to roll a one? Welcome to Zombicide, where the odds are never in your favor.
