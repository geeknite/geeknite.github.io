## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Verdant Echo Verdant
### Routine Care: Vacuuming and Light Refresh
To keep the color consistent and avoid a sleepy pile, vacuum regularly with a standard beater-bar setting if your vacuum has one. For delicate fibers, a gentle setting or a no-beater option is fine. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to prevent any obvious flattening in high-traffic zones. If you notice that your vacuum plays tug-of-war with the fibers, step back and switch to a gentler approach: you’ll thank yourself later when you’re not trying to pick up lint that refuses to leave your living room.

### Stains and Spills: The Quick-React Playbook
Act fast. Blot spills with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing as that can push the stain deeper. For water-based spills, a quick blot is usually enough. For more stubborn spots, apply a small amount of mild detergent and water to the area, blot again, and allow to air-dry. If a color transfer occurs (for example, from a stained beverage or from a new item of clothing), treat as soon as you notice it. Always test any cleaner in an inconspicuous area first to avoid any color changes that would be dramatic enough to warrant its own post.

### Dealing with Odors: Freshness Over Fragrance
If your rug develops a smell after a week of living with you, air it out and consider light vacuuming with a deodorizing, fabric-safe powder. Avoid heavy scents or chemical sprays that might react with the fibers or the backing. The goal is fresh, not overwhelmed by perfume.

### Longevity and Warranty Considerations
Fontaine’s product warranties tend to cover manufacturing defects and certain wear thresholds. Read the fine print, of course, but think of it as a safety net that lets you sleep a little easier after you’ve spilled two coffees on it in one morning. If you’re a “buy once, cry once” type, this rug respects that ethos without demanding you to replace it every other year. It’s a reliable mid-range purchase that won’t force you to mortgage the living room for a single color mood piece.
