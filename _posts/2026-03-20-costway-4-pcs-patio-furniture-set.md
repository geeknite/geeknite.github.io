## Assembly experience
Assembling the Costway 4-Pcs Patio Set is not a personal test of strength, but it is a small endurance challenge. The included tools are functional, and the instructions are clear enough to prevent you from assembling a coffee table that tilts at a jaunty angle. If you’ve built a similar kit before, you’ll finish in the 45–90 minute range; if you’re new to flat-packs, you’ll likely spend a little longer but enjoy the process as a puzzle-solving mini-quest.

### Step-by-step rough timeline
- Step 1: Unpack and inventory. Check you have all the frame pieces, cushions, and hardware. If you find a few spare washers, you’ve done something right.
- Step 2: Assemble the frames. Use the included Allen wrench and bolts to connect the sofa frame first, then the two chairs. It’s a two-person job for best stability.
- Step 3: Attach cushions. Slide cushions onto the frames and align the seams. If the cushions are a bit stiff, that’s normal—the foam will loosen with use.
- Step 4: Place the table and top. Ensure it’s level and snug against the frame. If your table top is glass, wipe it down and set it gently so you don’t scratch the surface.

### Common assembly gotchas
- Don’t over-tighten bolts. It’s tempting to crank them for rigidity, but over-torque can strip threads or warp the frame.
- Double-check alignment. Misaligned frames can lead to wobble or a slightly skewed seating surface—nothing that a quick bolt-tighten can’t fix.
- Cushion placement isn’t cosmetic. It affects comfort, back support, and how your guests’ legs align with the sofa and chairs.

## Care and cleaning in detail
Caring for outdoor furniture is part science, part ritual. Here are practical, repeatable steps you can follow to keep the Costway set looking good for years:
- Fabric care: Removable cushion covers mean you can machine-wash when needed. Use cold water and a mild detergent; air dry to prevent heat damage.
- Foam care: If cushions feel a bit saggy, rotate or flip them periodically to distribute wear evenly.
- Frame care: Wipe down with a damp cloth to remove dirt and dust. If you notice tiny rust specks on the frame, address promptly with a light rust-preventive spray and a wipe-down.
- Storage strategy: In off-seasons, store cushions indoors or in a breathable container; keep the frame dry and covered to reduce weathering.

Rotation and replacement tips:
- Rotate cushions across seats every few weeks to balance wear.
- Store small cushions in a dry place during wet seasons to preserve their shape and color.
- Consider purchasing extra covers if you entertain often; this makes cleanup quick and keeps a consistent look.
