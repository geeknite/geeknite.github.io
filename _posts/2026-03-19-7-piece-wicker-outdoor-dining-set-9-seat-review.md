## Cleaning and care routines
Cushions can be spot cleaned with a mild soap solution and cold water. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the fabric over time. For deeper stains, a gentle pre-treatment can help remove the mark without compromising the fiber. It is best to air dry cushions after cleaning since a damp cushion can take longer to dry and may develop a musty odor if stored while still damp. 

For the wicker frame, a light brush or a microfiber cloth will remove dust and pollen that accumulate during peak spring and summer. If you notice any loose strands, address them early to prevent unraveling. A small amount of warm water combined with a drop of mild soap is enough to wipe down the surface. Rinse and allow to dry completely before sitting again. 

If you are the meticulous type who loves a schedule, you might set a monthly routine to wipe down the set and do a quarterly cushion wash. Treat it as a quarterly maintenance ritual so you can brag about your patio care discipline at your next gathering.
