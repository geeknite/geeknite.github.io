## Durability and daily carry
### Everyday care
Acrylic is resilient but not invincible. The case should be wiped with a soft microfiber cloth to maintain its shine and to avoid micro-scratches that accumulate with rough handling. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the acrylic surface or cloud the clarity of the interior. The included foam insert will show signs of compression over time if exposed to heavy abuse, so treat it with some respect. If you are a slam-dunk packer, the case should still survive a reasonable carry, but you might want to consider a soft pouch for extra protection if your bags tend to be tossed around like a dragon in a cave full of treasure.

### Longevity considerations
Mint new is a great baseline, but real-world use demands a longer horizon. The blue acrylic case should be expected to hold up to a couple of seasons of travel if you are careful. Scratches on the exterior may occur, especially in high-traffic airports, but they do not compromise the structural integrity. The interior foam should retain its shape if you keep the box dry and avoid heavy moisture exposure. If you plan to cross environments with humidity swings, you might encounter minor fogging on the interior, which is not harmful but can slightly affect visibility of the cards inside. A little silicone treatment around the gaskets can help with longevity, though that step might be optional for casual users.

## Maintenance and care
### Cleaning tips
Keep the exterior clean by wiping with a soft microfiber cloth. If you notice smudges from your chalky coffee cup or from your fingerprints after a particularly intense victory dance, a mild soap solution should do the trick. Do not use ammonia-based cleaners that can scratch the acrylic. For the interior foam inserts, a quick vacuum and spot clean will keep the internal environment pleasant and free of crumbs or tiny playing tokens that somehow migrate into every crevice of your luggage.

### Storage and long-term care
If you are storing the case away, keep it in a cool, dry place away from prolonged sunlight. Prolonged sun exposure can cause a slight yellowing effect on acrylics over time, which is not dangerous but can diminish aesthetics. Rotate the way you store inserts if you own multiple configurations, and avoid bending or applying force that might deform the interior layout. The more you respect the interior, the longer the interior will maintain its shape and protective function.
