## Practical tips: how to get the most out of Cheaters Edition

- Start with a short cheat card set: This is not a race to see who can cheat the most. The goal is to create moments of hilarity and exciting negotiation that keep everyone engaged.
- Set expectations: At the start, remind players that the cheat deck is there to enhance fun, not to escalate conflict. If someone feels uncomfortable, pause and switch to a no-cheats mode.
- Use a timer: When a cheat is triggered, a five-minute window for negotiation is often plenty; more time can lead to dragging the game indefinitely.
- Balance the board: If you find a particular cheat card too overpowered, ban it or limit its use in the next game. The rules exist to facilitate fun, not to turn your living room into a courtroom.
- Rotate players: If you have a bigger group, ensure everyone gets a turn to draw a cheat card and to lead a few rounds to keep energy positive.
