## Strategies, Variants, and House Rules
As with any CAH expansion, the best play often comes from the people around the table and the house rules you adopt. The 2012 Holiday Expansion introduces material that rewards improvisation, quick thinking, and a willingness to lean into absurd connections between prompts and responses.

### House Rules to Try
- Quick Pick Rule: End the round with the quickest and most concise pairing. It keeps the pace snappy and prevents a single player from hogging the mic.
- Silent Round: For a twist, the Card Czar picks a prompt and the rest of the players reveal their top 3 responses face-down. The Czar selects the funniest one without seeing who played it, adding an element of mystery to the decision.
- Thematic Bump: Exclude a few prompts that obviously rely on more mature humor if your crowd has younger members present. This keeps the mood inclusive and still very funny.
