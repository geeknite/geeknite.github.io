Cable management is the silent killer of data center aesthetics. A well-managed rack is the difference between a room that breathes and a room that groans every time a GPU fan hits full throttle. The Avit 45 RU is built with a thoughtful approach to cable channels, rails, and door openings that minimize cable fatigue and airflow obstruction. Thermal performance in a rack is often about airflow. The Avit design does not promise miracles, but it does promise predictability. If you deploy modern blade servers with hot-swap capabilities, the front door options and optional perforated panels help ensure that hot air doesn’t linger in the middle of the chassis.
