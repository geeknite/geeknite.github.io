### Maintenance: Cleaning, Drying, and Longevity
Maintenance is fairly simple: wipe down after use, let dry, and store away from sharp objects or pets that think padding is a chew toy. A quick wipe-down prevents the foam from absorbing sweat and dirt, which helps with odor control if you’re tempted to turn your driveway into a full-on training session. The fabric can handle light machine washing if you remove the foam carefully, but hand-washing is perfectly fine and kinder to the foam’s long-term integrity.

One important caution: avoid direct, intense sunlight for long periods. The black fabric does a good job hiding stains, but extended sun exposure can cause the foam to become a little stiff and the fabric to fade. If you’re in Arizona in July or Melbourne during a heatwave, bring the practice indoors or set up shade to preserve the padding’s softness. It’s not dramatic, but it’s a little nerdy thing worth knowing.
