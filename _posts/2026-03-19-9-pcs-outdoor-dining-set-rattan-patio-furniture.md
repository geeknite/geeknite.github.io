## Maintenance Tips
Outdoor furniture loves a good maintenance plan that’s simple to follow. Here are a few Geeknite-endorsed tips to keep your set looking sharp:
- Regularly wipe down the wicker with a soft cloth and mild soap solution. Rinse and dry to prevent soap buildup.
- Use cushion covers or store cushions indoors during heavy rain or winter to extend life.
- Check and tighten bolts every few months to maintain stability.
- Keep cushions out of direct sun when not in use to minimize color fading.
- If you notice any loose weaving, address it early with a professional refinishing kit to prevent unraveling.

A small investment in routine maintenance pays off in the long run and helps keep your space looking like a curated sci-fi lounge rather than a garage-sale display.
