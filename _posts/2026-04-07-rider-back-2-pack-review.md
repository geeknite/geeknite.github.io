## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity
### Cleaning and storage tips
Keep your Rider Back decks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and humidity. A little care goes a long way toward preserving color accuracy and card integrity. Wipe spills promptly and avoid leaving cards in your car’s trunk on hot days. If a deck gets sticky, a gentle wipe with a dry microfiber cloth can restore a clean surface; avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the stock. Storage in a soft card sleeve reduces wear from friction and accidental drops. If you’re a gamer who travels, consider a small travel case that fits both decks—also a nice place to keep spare pennies for a quick “that card is missing” joke during the endgame.

Don’t force the break-in; patience is a virtue in the card world. If you’re a cardist or a magician, you’ll want to start with simple flourishes to avoid warping the edges. You’ll notice the difference after a handful of sessions when the deck begins to respond to your cues rather than the other way around.
