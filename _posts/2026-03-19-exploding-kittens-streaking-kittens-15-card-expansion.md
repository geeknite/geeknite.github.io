## Rules and Gameplay Tweaks
The expansion does not rewrite Exploding Kittens rules in their entirety. There are no mandatory new turn phases or heavy mechanical overhauls. Instead, it introduces a light sprinkle of new meta mechanics that players can adopt with ease. Here are the core ideas you will encounter in most games that include this expansion:
- A streak meter that can grant a small but meaningful advantage if left unbroken through several turns.
- A band of counter cards that let players interrupt or reset streaks, forcing a tactical choice between safety and risk.
- A handful of cat cards that redraw the focus toward cooperative mischief while maintaining hot potato style chaos.

If you want to tailor the experience, you can adopt a few house rules.
