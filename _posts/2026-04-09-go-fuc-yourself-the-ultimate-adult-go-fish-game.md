## How to Play With the Adult Twist
Go Fish core mechanic is simple you ask someone for cards of a rank and if they have them they hand them over. If not you draw from the pile and your turn ends. The goal is to collect four of a kind. The adult edition adds truth and dare prompts plus optional drinking rules you can toggle on or off.

A sample round shows the flow:
- Player A asks for Kings. If player B has kings they hand them over if not B draws a card.
- If B could not supply the rank a truth or dare may appear asking someone to share a story or perform a silly action.

This is a game built to generate laughter not a spreadsheet of probabilities. If you want to keep things light you can opt for safe prompts and keep the dares firmly in the silly lane. If your group is up for it you can push the envelope a little but always with consent at the table.
