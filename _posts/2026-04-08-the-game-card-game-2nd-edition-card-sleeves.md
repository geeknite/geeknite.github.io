## Tips, Tricks, and Care for The Game Card Game - 2nd Edition Card Sleeves
- Pre-sleeve the cards in a clean, dust-free environment. A stale apartment is not a place for pristine plastic.
- Store sleeved decks upright or in a dedicated box that minimizes bending; avoid crushing sleeves under heavy objects.
- If you notice stiffness after extended use, give the deck a few minutes on a flat surface to acclimate; the sleeves will loosen slightly with time.
- Use the color-coding options mindfully: designate expansions with distinct color sleeves or patterns to speed up setup.
- Keep spare sleeves around for replacements during a long campaign or seasonal events. You’ll thank yourself later when a critical corner turns into a victory instead of a minor fiasco.

### Maintenance Tips in a Nutshell
- Wipe with a dry microfiber cloth occasionally to remove dust and fingerprints.
- Avoid cleaning agents that could degrade the plastic. No acetone, no magic erasers, no paranormal rituals—stick to gentle care.
- If a sleeve becomes warped, don’t force the card back in; replace that sleeve and keep the deck’s integrity intact.
- Rotate your sleeves if you’re that kind of meticulous organizer, so wear is evenly distributed over the pack.
