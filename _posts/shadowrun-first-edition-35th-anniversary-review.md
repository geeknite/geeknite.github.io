## A Brief History

Shadowrun, first released in 1989, is a unique blend of cyberpunk and fantasy. It introduced players to a world where elves, dwarves, and trolls coexist with hackers, megacorporations, and cybernetics. The game quickly gained a cult following, thanks to its innovative setting and mechanics. Fast forward 35 years, and we're celebrating its anniversary with a special edition that promises to bring back all those fond memories.
