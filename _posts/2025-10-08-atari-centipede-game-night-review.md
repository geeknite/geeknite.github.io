## The Basics

### What is Atari Centipede Game Night Edition?

Atari Centipede Game Night Edition is a nostalgic blast from the past. The game includes not only the original Centipede arcade game but also several new modes and features designed for multiplayer fun. Think of it as a remastered classic with a modern twist, including enhanced graphics, new challenges, and an expanded multiplayer mode.

### Components

This edition comes with a plug-and-play console pre-loaded with Centipede and other classic Atari games. It also includes wireless controllers, a revamped game board for table-top play, and a deck of cards with challenges and bonuses to spice up your gaming sessions. The nostalgic artwork and durable components are built to last, ensuring many game nights to come.

## Gameplay

### Original Mode

In the original mode, you can relive the classic Centipede experience. Players use their joystick to move the shooter horizontally at the bottom of the screen while firing at a descending centipede, spiders, and other critters. The goal is to score as many points as possible before getting wiped out by the bugs.

### Game Night Mode

Game Night Mode introduces a competitive and cooperative element. Up to four players can join in, each controlling their own shooter. The game board and deck of cards come into play here, adding new rules and challenges.

1. **Action Cards**: Draw cards that either give you bonuses (like extra lives) or impose hindrances on your opponents (like freezing their shooter for a few seconds). 
2. **Team Play**: Pair up and strategize with a partner to defeat waves of centipedes while sabotaging the other team.
3. **Battle Royale**: Every player for themselves in a chaotic free-for-all.

**Funny Moment**: During a heated game night, one player drew a card that forced their opponent to play the next round using only their non-dominant hand. The result? Hilarious crashes and a lot of good-natured ribbing.
