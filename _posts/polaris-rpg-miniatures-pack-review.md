## What is Polaris RPG: Miniatures Pack?

The **Polaris RPG: Miniatures Pack** is an expansion for the Polaris RPG, which takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity has found refuge underwater. The miniatures pack includes finely detailed figures that represent various characters and creatures from the game. These miniatures are here to make sure your underwater adventures are as visually stunning as they are narratively gripping.

### Components

The pack includes:
- **Miniatures**: A variety of beautifully crafted miniatures depicting characters and sea creatures. These figures are so detailed, you might want to put them in a display cabinet instead of using them in the game.
- **Cards**: Each miniature comes with a corresponding card detailing its abilities and stats. Perfect for those moments when you need to quickly reference your character’s super cool underwater powers.
- **Base Stands**: Custom stands for each miniature to ensure they don’t topple over during an intense underwater battle. Because nothing ruins immersion faster than a fallen hero.

**Pro Tip**: Keep these miniatures away from your goldfish. They might get jealous of the new aquatic heroes and start staging their own underwater rebellion.

## How to Use the Miniatures

### Setup

Setting up the miniatures pack is straightforward. Simply place the miniatures on their custom stands and distribute the corresponding cards. Now you’re ready to bring your underwater adventures to life!

### Gameplay Integration

The miniatures are seamlessly integrated into the gameplay. They can be used to represent characters in combat, exploration, and other game scenarios. The detailed designs add a layer of visual immersion that enhances the overall gaming experience.
