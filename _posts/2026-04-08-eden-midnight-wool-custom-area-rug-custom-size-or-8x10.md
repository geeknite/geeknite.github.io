## Underfoot Comfort, Durability, and Care

### Comfort Underfoot
Wool’s natural elasticity provides an inviting underfoot feel. The Eden Midnight piles just the right thickness to cushion footsteps without bottling up your energy—perfect for TV-watching marathons, game nights, or the occasional impromptu dance-off with a vacuum cleaner. If you’re one of those people who stand and work at a standing desk for eight hours, expect the rug to feel cozier than the floorboard does after you forget the heating is on.

### Durability and Longevity
Wool rugs hold their shape well due to natural crimping in the fibers. The Eden Midnight is designed for resilience, so it should resist flattening in high-traffic zones. A potential caveat is fiber shedding during initial weeks: expect a small amount of fuzz as the fibers settle in. Regular vacuuming with a soft nozzle, and avoiding extremely aggressive brush settings, will keep the pile intact without turning your floor into a felt republic.

### Maintenance and Cleaning Tips
- Vacuum regularly (at least once a week). Use a low-power setting and avoid beater bars that could pull fibers.
- For spills: blot—don’t rub. Wool tends to absorb liquid quickly; act fast, blot, and if needed, use a mild wool-safe cleaner following product directions.
- Rotation: Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and color distribution.
- Sun exposure: Prolonged direct sunlight can cause fading. If possible, use window treatments to keep the hue intact.
