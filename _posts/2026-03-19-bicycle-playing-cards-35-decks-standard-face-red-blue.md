## Care, maintenance, and longevity tips
We’re dealing with 35 decks here. That’s a lot of paint and glue, all bundled into a compact rectangle of playing-card glory. Here are some practical tips to extend the life of your collection:

- Keep them dry: moisture is the silent enemy of stock and finish. Store in a cool, dry place and use a dehumidifier if your room tends to be damp.
- Avoid direct sunlight: long-term UV exposure can fade back designs and cause discoloration over many decks. A simple, nice, dark shelf is better than a sunny window ledge.
- Gentle handling: teach players to riffle with a light touch; no need for “brute force card one-upmanship” in a magic routine—your wrists will thank you.
- Storage strategy: invest in a sturdy, stackable deck rack or a box with dividers so each color is separated. This reduces wear and helps you quickly identify the deck you need during a show or a game night.
- Cleaning: a soft, slightly damp microfiber cloth can wipe away fingerprints between performances. Avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade the finish.
