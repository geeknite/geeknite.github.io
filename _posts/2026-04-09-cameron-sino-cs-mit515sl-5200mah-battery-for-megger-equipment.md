## Installation tips and common pitfalls
Installing a replacement battery is rarely dramatic, but a few simple steps can save you headaches:

- Safety first: Wear eye protection if you’re prying open a sealed battery bay. Avoid bending tabs or prying at the pack’s edges with metal tools.
- Clean workspace: Make sure the device’s contact points are clean and dry. A little dust is fine, but grime can disrupt connections.
- Disconnect power: Ensure the Megger unit is powered down before removing the old pack and inserting the new one.
- Check orientation: Observe the polarity and alignment to ensure you don’t accidentally reverse connections.
- Test briefly: After seating the new pack, power on the device and run a quick self-test to ensure the unit recognizes the battery and reports a healthy voltage.
- Calibration considerations: Some devices require a brief calibration cycle after battery replacement. Check your Megger manual for exact steps.
