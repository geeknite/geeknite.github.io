## What is Middle-earth Strategy Battle Game: Armies of The Hobbit?

Middle-earth Strategy Battle Game: Armies of The Hobbit is a tabletop miniatures game that allows players to recreate the intense battles and epic moments from J.R.R. Tolkien's The Hobbit. You command armies of heroes, villains, and creatures, striving for victory in scenarios inspired by the iconic films and books.

### The Basics

In this game, players form their armies from a diverse range of characters including Thorin Oakenshield, the goblin king, and Smaug the dragon. Each character and unit has unique abilities and stats, making every army customizable to fit different strategies.

### Components

The game includes beautifully crafted miniatures of your favorite characters and units from The Hobbit. The attention to detail is impressive, from the intricate armor of dwarves to the menacing features of orcs. Additionally, you'll find a variety of game tokens, dice, and terrain pieces to bring your battles to life.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Middle-earth Strategy Battle Game is akin to preparing for the Battle of Five Armies. Lay out your terrain, position your miniatures, and brace yourself for an epic clash. Choose your scenario, deploy your forces, and you're ready to start the battle.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players alternate activating their units, performing actions such as moving, attacking, and casting spells. The strategic depth comes from managing your units' abilities and positioning them to outwit your opponent.

### Combat

Combat in Middle-earth Strategy Battle Game is a blend of strategy and luck. Each attack is resolved by rolling dice, adding modifiers based on your units' stats and abilities. Did you plan an epic charge only to fumble the dice roll? Welcome to Middle-earth, where even the best-laid plans can go awry.
