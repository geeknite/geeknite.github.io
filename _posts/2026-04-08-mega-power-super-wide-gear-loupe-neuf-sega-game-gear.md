## Lighting and Environment: Practical Tips
Your best friend with this setup is ambient lighting. A soft, diffuse light helps the garish Game Gear color palette shine without turning the screen into a mirror. If you’re indoors and under a single overhead bulb, consider angling the device so that your own shadow doesn’t play referee with the lens. If you play outdoors, a small portable shade or even a compact hat with a brim might save your day. The goal is to avoid direct glare while keeping the magnified image crisp and readable.

If you want to go full retro-science, combine the loupe with a tiny LED clip-on light. The LED can provide a consistent light source that doesn’t wash out the screen’s colors, allowing you to keep playing while your friends glare at the battery drain from afar.
