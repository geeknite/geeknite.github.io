## Installation: It’s Literally “Clamp and Go”
Installing the VEVOR privacy panel is designed to be quick and painless. Here’s the typical process we followed:

1. Identify a desk edge that’s solid, not brittle, and within reach of your chair’s clearance. If you have a glass desk, you might want to proceed with caution or consider an alternative clamp method.
2. Open the clamps to the maximum width, place the panel against the edge, and align the top and bottom for a balanced shield. The goal is not to topple the entire workstation picnics but to secure the panel without overpowering the desk.
3. Tighten the clamps evenly. If your desk edge is uneven, you might notice a slight tilt, which is fixable with micro-adjustments but not ideal for those who crave perfect symmetry.
4. Test with mild pressure on the panel to ensure it doesn’t slip during normal use. If it moves, tighten a bit more or reposition.
5. Clean up. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth will remove fingerprints and dust that inevitably collect after the “I’m organizing my desk” montage.

In our experience, the clamp-on installation is refreshingly simple. Within 10–15 minutes, the panel was attached, aligned, and ready to block the world, or at least the neighbor’s chair-lifter commentary on your video call.
