## Weather Resistance and Maintenance

Outdoor furniture inevitably faces sun, rain, wind, and the occasional stray bird dropping a dramatic opera on your coffee table. The wicker construction tolerates sun and rain, especially when you choose a model with UV-resistant wicker and rust-resistant frames. The cushions, pampered with water-repellent fabric, dry out reasonably quickly after a shower; however, if you leave cushions outside in the rain without covers, you should expect some color loss and possible mildew. A simple maintenance ritual includes wiping down the wicker with a mild soap solution and hosing off the cushions when necessary. If you are in a climate with heavy rain, consider storing cushions indoors during the heaviest months to extend their life. For sunny climates, a mid-season cleaning and inspection of the weave will keep the set looking fresh and feeling sturdy.

Pro tip: invest in diagonal covers or a patio furniture cover to guard against the harshest autumn winds. A cover helps preserve color and keeps your cushions fluffier longer. If you are the type who forgets to close the cover, set a reminder on your phone and imagine it as your own tiny butler named Jeeves who doubles as a weather assistant.
