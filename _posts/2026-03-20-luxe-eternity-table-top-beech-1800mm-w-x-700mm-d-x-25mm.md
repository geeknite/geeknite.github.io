## Material Handling and Durability

### Surface Hardness and Wear

Beech is known for holding its edge when compared to softer species; it’s not as brittle as some maple finishes, yet not as prone to denting as some oak varieties after a long, dramatic office chair session. Expect the Luxe Eternity to shrug off minor scuffs and recover well after contact with moderate weight. For households with kids or a teething Labrador, consider a protective me­dium coat or a felt‑backed placemat to cushion every accidental culinary experiment.

### Stability and Humidity

Beech has decent dimensional stability but reacts to humidity like a true drama queen. If you live in a tropical climate or you’re the kind who leaves a window open during a rainstorm, you may notice slight seasonal expansion and contraction. The Luxe Eternity’s recommended humidity range should be observed; a small dehumidifier in summer and a humidifier in dry months can keep your top from warping into a modern art sculpture of despair. Treat it well, and it will repay you with years of reliable service and a surface that still feels premium after twenty coffee spills.

### Maintenance and Cleaning

Maintenance is simple: wipe with a damp microfiber cloth, then dry. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that can erode the finish or cloud the grain. For stubborn stains, a mild wood cleaner will do, and a light buff with a beeswax polish can rejuvenate the luster without turning the table into a glossy mirror that makes your reflection judge your life choices. If you enjoy DIY, you can even apply a fresh top coat every couple of years to keep the Luxe Eternity looking like a showroom piece while preserving its natural warmth.
