## Maintenance and care

### Cleaning basics

A simple rinse with a hose, a soft brush for stubborn dirt, and a mild detergent is enough for most stains. Avoid harsh solvents which can wear down fibers over time. The rug dries rapidly, especially when laid flat in the sun. If you store it for winter, roll rather than fold to avoid creases that refuse to go away come spring.

### Drying and storage tips

Sunlight is your friend here. Allow both sides to dry fully to prevent mildew. Store in a dry place when not in use, preferably rolled and not folded to keep the ribbing intact.
