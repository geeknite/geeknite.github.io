## Paint supply chain: assembling and painting tips
If you intend to display these miniatures or run them heavily on the table, here are a few practical tips to get the best results without becoming a full-time hobbyist:

- Prime with a neutral gray or ivory to bring out Kopinski’s line work and to ensure contrast in the darker palette.
- Use contrast paints for quick base shading, then layer with mid-tones to preserve the sculpt’s edge lines.
- For metallics, a simple wash on the blades and armor will bring out the sculpt details without making the figures look overly glossy.
- Don’t fear a little weathering on the armor and leather. Kopinski’s pieces often benefit from a touch of wear that tells a story of field battles and long marches through plague-ridden terrain.
- Display them on a light-colored base to maximize the sculpt’s silhouette against the backdrop of your display shelf.
