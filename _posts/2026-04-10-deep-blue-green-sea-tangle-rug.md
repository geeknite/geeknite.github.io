## Maintenance and Longevity
### Cleaning rituals fit for a sea captain and a dungeon master

The trick with any textured rug is patience; you want to avoid back-and-forth aggressive scrubbing that can disturb the pile and leave you with a patchy appearance. Instead, treat it like a gentle dialogue with your furniture: calm, persistent, and a little bit witty.

## Care and maintenance: a compact guide
- Vacuum regularly to maintain surface lift and keep twigs and pet hair at bay.
- For spills, blot gently with a dry cloth; then spot-clean with a mild detergent and cold water as needed. Avoid bleach and harsh solvents.
- Let stains air-dry fully before placing back into heavy traffic areas to avoid setting residues.
- Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent color fading, but do not fear a little sun, as it can add a luminous sheen to the sea-like fibers.
- If you want to refresh the texture after heavy use, a low-heat carpet rake can lift the pile and restore its bounce without deforming the fibers.
