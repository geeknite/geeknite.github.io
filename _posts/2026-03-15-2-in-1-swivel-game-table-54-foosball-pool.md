## Maintenance, Longevity, and Everyday Care

Proper care extends the life of your table and keeps the playing surfaces in decent shape for years to come. The pool felt should be kept clean and dry to prevent mold and wear; a light brush can help maintain the pile; avoid spills on the pool surface and wipe any splashes promptly. For the foosball rods, a light silicone spray on moving parts can reduce squeaks, but be careful to avoid overspray on the ball returns and the felt surfaces.

Check the joint screws and swivel mounting quarterly if you’re a heavy user. A table that is played every weekend will naturally loosen some hardware; the reality is that you’ll want to re-tighten a few screws to keep the structure stable.
