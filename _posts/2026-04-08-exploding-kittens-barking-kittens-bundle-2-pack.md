## How to Play Quick Start Guide

1. Separate the Exploding Kittens deck and Barking Kittens deck Shuffle each then place them within easy reach
2. Deal a starting hand to each player the exact number depends on the rulebook typically around 5 cards
3. Decide who goes first Pro tip the best players are those who can laugh at themselves when they misjudge a draw
4. On your turn you draw a card then you may perform one or more actions according to the card you ve drawn or the options in the rulebook This is where the chaos begins
5. If you draw an Exploding Kitten card and don t have a Defuse you re out for that round If you do have a Defuse you may play it to survive then secretly place the Exploding Kitten back into the deck somewhere the next player might hate
6. The Barking Kittens portion adds its own mechanics for barking counter barking and dog themed twists that can alter the flow of a round Use them to your advantage or watch your strategy hilariously crumble when a paw s itively chaotic card flips the table in your favor
7. The round ends when the remaining players reach a winning threshold or when a predetermined number of rounds are completed
