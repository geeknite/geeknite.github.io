## A few practical tips for getting the most out of NS7 R

- Place a non-slip rug pad underneath to reduce movement and extend life. It also adds a tiny cushion boost that the feet will thank you for.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear, especially in high traffic zones like entryways and living rooms where sunlight shines through the window in dramatic fashion.
- Vacuum with a gentle setting and a cleaner with a wide nozzle to avoid concentrating pressure on a single spot. This helps prevent pulled fibers and keeps the pattern crisp.
- When spills happen, blot rather than rub. Give the fibers time to recover and treat stains early for best results.
- Pair with furniture coasters under heavy items to prevent indentation marks that can ruin the smooth surface you’re aiming for.
