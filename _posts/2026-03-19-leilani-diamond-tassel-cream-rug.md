## Care Tips and Pro Tips from the Guild of Cozy-Home Nerds

- Vacuum weekly with a beater bar on a low setting to avoid shedding the fibers into a festival of static electricity. If you have a mind to use a rotating brush, go gentle—this is a cream rug, not a snowstorm.
- For spills, blot—not rub. Rubbing invites those stubborn stains to perform a full-arc ballet on your fibers. Patience pays off.
- Treat, not trick: use a mild detergent, test on a hidden corner first, then proceed. Your future self will thank you when you’re not auditioning for a carpet-cleaning reality show.
- Rotate the rug every few months to prevent uneven wear from furniture footprints. It’s a small maintenance ritual with big payoffs—like tuning a game console before a long mission.
