## Maintenance and Longevity How it Holds Up to Everyday Life

### Cleaning Routine and Spill Response
We recommend a weekly vacuum with a hard floor setting to prevent dust from matting into the fibers. For spills blot rather than rub the goal is to prevent the liquid from wicking deeper into the pile. For stubborn stains a gentle carpet cleaner is acceptable but test on a small inconspicuous area first. Use cold water and mild detergent not bleach. The rug is not a miracle cure for all stains but with prompt action most accidents can be contained and resolved without a dramatic rescue mission.

### Longevity and Pilling
Synthetic fibers can pill when exposed to rough wear or friction from heavy furniture but the Leilani s construction minimizes that risk with a tight weave. If pilling occurs a fabric shaver can help restore the surface. Regular rotation switching the rug s position every few months helps distribute wear and maintain an even appearance. Over time you might notice the pattern slightly flattening in high traffic zones which is normal. If you re chasing a consistently immaculate look you ll want to commit to occasional fluffing sessions with a brush or gentle comb.
