### Triple Back To Back Charcoal Ash System

The core novelty here is the triple back to back charcoal ash system. Three parallel charcoal trays sit side by side, each capable of independent fueling and airflow control. This design is intended to give you a few different temperature zones, more consistent heat, and the possibility to run a direct grill on one tray, an indirect roast on another, and a dedicated smoke path on the third. The logic is simple in principle: more controlled heat means more predictable cooks. The execution is where the gears start to sing. With three trays, you can fine-tune airflow by adjusting the air vents and damper to coax a gentle, even burn rather than the old school practice of letting a single bed of coals run you ragged with hot spots.

The downside to triple trays is maintenance. Three trays mean three times the chance of ash buildup and the potential for charcoal residues to venture into places you did not know existed on a grill. The Core Lounge D Triple handles this with an elongated ash pan and a clever tray alignment that minimizes ash migration. It is not perfect, but it is far smarter than your average three-tray system that treats ash like an afterthought. If you like to optimize your heat, this system is a dream; if you hate cleaning, you may want to practice a little meditation and breathing exercises before you start the cleanup ritual.
