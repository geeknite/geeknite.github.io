H2: Cleaning, maintenance, and long-term care

Maintenance is where doormats either win or lose hearts. PVC mats like SM0005 are typically easy to clean. A quick rinse with water and light soap usually suffices. If you have a heavy foot traffic area, consider occasional light scrubbing with a soft brush to lift ground-in grime. Do not use abrasive cleaners that could dull the print. Drying is best done flat in a well-ventilated area to avoid any curled edges. Long-term sun exposure might gradually weather the color slightly, but this is a common fate for most outdoor textiles. Keeping your mat out of direct, relentless sun when possible can extend its life, though in a real world sense the porch is often where the sun wants to hang out and say hi.

Care tips: avoid bleach on the printed surface, avoid strong solvents, and store the mat flat when not in use for extended periods. If you need to store it for a season, roll it rather than folding to minimize creases. The design can handle a little bending, but repeated folding will reduce the lifespan.
