### Fog as a visual device

Fog in photography is more than a weather condition; it’s a storytelling device. It invites viewers to create narratives: Are the towers waking up? Are the people down below already late for work? The fog muffles sound in your imagination too, which is excellent for a space you want to feel “quietly alive.” The Dubai print uses fog to simultaneously reveal and conceal, giving the composition an almost literary rhythm. The viewer’s eyes travel from foreground haze to mid-level silhouettes and finally to the crisp pinnacle of the skyline. It’s a route your brain travels while sipping coffee and pretending to work in a fancy workspace.

## Print Quality and Materials

Material options include archival matte, semi-gloss, and canvas wrap. ### Color reproduction and archival quality

Color accuracy matters a lot when you’re dealing with dawn hues and fog.
