## Flavorful notes and practical takeaways

- Rarity matters in long-term collectibility and price stability. Mythic Rare cards often carry the allure of a single, game-changing moment, while Rare cards tend to reward consistent value and enduring utility over time.
- Mixed set play can introduce unusual synergies, as a card designed for a specific block can unexpectedly shine in a stranger context. Don’t be afraid to experiment with cross-set interactions; sometimes the best plays come from unlikely pairings.
- Mint NM condition matters for display value and future resale. If you plan to collect, consider protective sleeves and display boxes. The difference in condition can be the difference between a card that looks vintage and one that looks newly minted on a coffee table.
