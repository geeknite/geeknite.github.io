## The Bad

### Lens Quality

While the BBB Impress offers adequate UV protection, the lens quality isn't top-tier. In bright sunlight, you might experience some glare and distortion. It's like looking through a funhouse mirror, but without the fun part.

### Limited Availability

Finding the BBB Impress sunglasses can be a bit of a scavenger hunt. They're not as widely available as some other brands, so you might need to engage in some detective work to track them down.

### Non-Polarized

If you're a fan of polarized lenses, the BBB Impress might disappoint you. These sunglasses aren't polarized, which means they might not reduce glare as effectively as you'd like. Sorry, no magical glare-reduction powers here.

### Scratch Resistance

Although the frame is durable, the lenses can be prone to scratches. Handle them with care, or you might end up with a pair of sunglasses that look like they've been through a catfight.
