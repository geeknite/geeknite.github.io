## Weather Resistance and Durability
### Cushions and Covers Longevity
Weather durability is the big selling point here. The cushions are designed to resist moisture absorption, and the fabric is typically quick-drying and able to shed light rain without becoming a soggy disaster. Still, we recommend bringing cushions indoors during extended rain or winter months to extend their life—though many users report minimal color fade after a couple of summer seasons when kept under direct sun only during occasional outdoor coffee breaks.

### Frame and Weave Longevity
Synthetic rattan weaves are built to beat the elements more gracefully than natural wicker. The woven strands are designed to resist cracking and color fading, and the underlying frame resists rust or corrosion better than you might expect from an outdoor piece in a budget category. The real test, of course, is how well it ages when you accidentally leave it outside during a heavy windstorm and discover that “outdoor furniture” is not a guaranteed safe harbor against the universe’s bending reality.

### Maintenance Tips for Longevity
- Wipe down regularly with a damp cloth to remove dust and sunscreen residue.
- Use a mild soap solution for spills; avoid harsh solvents that can degrade the fabric.
- When not in use, store cushions in a dry place or use breathable storage bags to prevent mildew.
- If wind becomes a factor, consider securing the unit with simple outdoor-rated ties or moving pieces to a sheltered corner.
