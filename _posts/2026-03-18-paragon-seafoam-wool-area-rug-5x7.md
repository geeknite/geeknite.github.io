### Cleaning and maintenance: how forgiving is this rug?
Wool rugs are generally easier to clean than you’d think, especially when you treat them with a modicum of care. The Paragon Seafoam wool rug benefits from typical wool-cleaning principles: quick blotting of spills, a gentle vacuum with the beater bar off, and avoiding saturating moisture into the fibers. When accidents happen, a clean towel can remove the majority of the liquid, followed by a mild wool-safe cleaner if needed. The key with wool is to avoid aggressive scrubbing, which can felt the fibers and create a dull surface that defeats the point of that seafoam glow.

For stubborn stains, a spot-clean approach using a wool-safe stain remover is usually sufficient. It’s worth noting that persistent stains may require professional cleaning, especially for set-in liquids or colors that have bled onto neighboring fibers. The 5x7 size makes spot cleaning feasible without hauling the entire rug to a cleaner, which is a nice balance between handy and heroic maintenance tasks.

Dust and soil accumulation can build up in wool fibers just like any natural material. A weekly vacuum with a brush attachment is a good practice, and rotating the rug a few degrees every couple of months helps with even wear and prevents the most-traveled areas from looking worn before the rest of the piece catches up.

## Practical care cheats you can actually live with
- Vacuum weekly with the suction setting tuned for wool and without the beater bar on. This keeps dirt at bay without crushing the pile.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can drive the liquid deeper into the fibers.
- Use a wool-safe cleaner for spot stains; test on a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure color stability.
- Rotate the rug seasonally to promote uniform wear and prevent high-traffic edges from looking tired.
- Consider a rug pad suitable for wooden floors to improve underfoot comfort and minimize slipping.

If you follow these steps, the Seafoam 5x7 should stay visually appealing for years, while your imagination runs wild with color palettes and room layouts.
