## Materials and Construction
### Floor Friendly
The Colleen Slate Rectangular Rug is described as made from a synthetic blend designed to balance softness and durability. The exact fiber mix is not disclosed on the packaging, but we can identify a few characteristics: stain resistance, looped or cut-pile texture, and a polyester or polypropylene base. The underlayment is usually a woven fabric that helps reduce curling along the edges.

### Build Quality
The edges are sewn with a tight overcast stitch that resists fraying after a few passes of a vacuum cleaner. The corners have a slight rounding to prevent corner peeling, which is nice for those of us who accidentally dribble coffee while navigating around the coffee table. The backing is designed to stay flat on most hard floors, with some anti-slip features but not a heavy grip that would damage delicate wood finishes.

### Health and Safety
For households with pets, kids, or existential dread about allergens, the rug is marketed as low VOC and easy to clean. While this is not a full atmophilic claim, you will still want to avoid direct exposure to solvents or household cleaners that could degrade the fibers or remove the dye.

### Room Planning Tips
- In small living rooms, place the rug under the front legs of the sofa to anchor the seating area while leaving some floor visible beyond the sofa.
- In bedrooms, place the rug under the bed with two-thirds of the rug under the bed for a cohesive look when you step out of bed in the morning.
- For dining rooms, measure the table and chairs so that the chairs fully fit on the rug when pulled out.

### Underlay Considerations
A rug pad is highly recommended for all sizes. It helps reduce slippage, adds a touch of cushioning, and improves acoustic dampening. A good pad ensures that the edges stay flat, preventing those fun little trips when you forget which room you are in.

## Practical Use and Comfort
### Underfoot Feel
We tested a 6x9 sample of Colleen Slate on a hardwood floor. The fibers feel plush but not overly fluffy. You get enough cushion to feel comfortable while standing for long gaming sessions or long board game marathons, but the rug still allows for easy rolling of chair wheels. If you want more plush, you can pair it with a thick pad, but you might lose some of the edge that defines the pattern.

### Durability
The rug had some initial shedding, which is common for new synthetics. We observed minimal fuzzing after the first few vacuum sessions. In high-traffic areas, the rug held up to daily use with only minor flattening in the center. If you have pets, you might see a few pulled fibers from claws; in that case, a gentle comb or brush can help restore the pile.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
- Regular vacuuming on a low to medium suction setting.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth to prevent staining.
- For tougher spots, use a mild detergent solution; test a small inconspicuous area first.
- Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach that could strip fibers.
