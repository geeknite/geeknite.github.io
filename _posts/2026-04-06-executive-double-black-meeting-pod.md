The Executive Double Black Meeting Pod isn’t shy about its mission: it wants to be the sanctuary where executives brainstorm, negotiate, and occasionally plot the next quarterly retreat to a resort that comes with a resort-like Wi-Fi password. The acoustic performance lives up to the hype.
