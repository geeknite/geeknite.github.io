## Maintenance and care: keeping the shine alive
Poker chips take a beating from sticky snacks, soda spills, and the occasional hot-blooded victory dance. Here’s how to keep your set looking sharp:
- Wipe chips with a soft cloth after sessions to minimize fingerprint buildup.
- Avoid harsh solvents or abrasive scrubbers that could dull edge milling or chip artwork.
- Store in a cool, dry location; direct sunlight can fade prints over time, especially on cheaper ink processes.
- If chips start to feel gritty, a light hand with a chip-smoothing tool can restore smooth edges, but test on a few chips first to ensure you don’t inadvertently remove color or laminate.
