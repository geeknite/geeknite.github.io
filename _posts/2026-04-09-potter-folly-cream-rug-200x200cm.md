## Durability, Cleaning & Longevity

Durability is the beating heart of any rug purchase, especially for a high-traffic area or a space that doubles as a gaming battlestation. The Potter Folly Cream Rug feels built to handle daily life without turning into a ball of frayed threads after a few weeks. The fiber density and weave pattern help resist casual wear, and the color is forgiving enough that minor scuffs won’t instantly betray your late-night snack runs.

Cleaning-wise, the routine is straightforward:
- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt buildup.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent pilling.
- For stains, test a small area with a mild detergent solution before full application.
- For deep cleaning, you can rent or borrow a rug-cleaning machine or have it professionally cleaned. Always follow care label guidance.

One potential caveat: cream shades can show dried spills more easily than darker tones. It’s not a deal-breaker, just something to be mindful of if you have kids, pets, or an impulsive tea-brewing habit at 3 am. Quick action keeps the rug looking fresh longer.

### Longevity Tips from a Fellow Nerd
- Implement a “wipe-down zone” near doorways to capture grit before it lands on the rug.
- Use a high-quality underlay if you have a very smooth floor; it reduces slippage and adds a touch of cushion when you’re sprinting from one monitor to another.
- Rotate usage area every few months to even out wear patterns.
