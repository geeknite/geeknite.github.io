## Care and maintenance
The care routine for this rug is delightfully uncomplicated. Vacuum regularly to prevent grit buildup. Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth, avoiding rubbing that can push liquid deeper into the fibers. For deeper cleaning, use a mild detergent solution and test in a small, inconspicuous area first. Avoid harsh chemicals that could alter the color balance of red taupe. The goal is to preserve softness and color richness while keeping the minor flaw from becoming more visible than you barged into the room with a snack during a late-night work session.

### Cleaning tips from the pros
- Regular vacuum with a gentle setting; avoid high suction on the warp where the minor flaw exists and you will be fine.
- Spills: blot, blot again, then blot some more with a clean cloth; if it returns, consult a professional cleaner rather than trying to DIY a miracle.
- Rotate periodically to maintain even wear and to give the blemish a chance to stay a quiet whisper rather than a loud shout.
- Use a rug pad to extend life and reduce shifting, especially on hardwood floors.
