## What is Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7?

For those uninitiated in the great art of Passe-Trappe, allow me to enlighten you. Passe-Trappe, or Tabletop Puck Slinging, is a frenetic, fast-paced game where players use elastic bands to flick wooden pucks through a small gate at the center of a board into their opponent's side. The goal? Clear your side of pucks before your opponent clears theirs. It's simple, it's chaotic, and it's a whole lot of fun.

## How to Play?

The beauty of Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7 lies in its simplicity. Set up the board, split the pucks between the two sides, and get ready to flick. The game starts with a count to three, and then it's a mad dash to flick all your pucks through the gate into your opponent's side. The first player to clear their side wins. It's that easy!

### Strategy and Skill

While the game may seem like pure chaos at first glance, there's actually a surprising amount of strategy involved. Sure, you can try to brute force flick your pucks as fast as possible, but precision and timing can often outmatch sheer speed. A well-placed flick can block your opponent's gate, buying you precious time to clear your side.
