### Collecting Tips: How to Protect Your Gloom AR SV3

- Storage: Use rigid card sleeves and a top-loader for NM conditions. Consider double-sleeving if transport or travel is involved.
- Handling: Clean hands, avoid metal rings, and keep the card away from heat and humidity. The embers don’t like moisture; they like drama, which is different.
- Grading: If you plan to submit for grading, choose a reputable service and ensure you have the card in mint condition before sending. A well-graded AR alt art can fetch a premium, especially if its centering and edges are pristine.
- Display: A small acrylic display with a light accent can highlight the fiery throne and add to your shelf’s ambience.
