### How to play the Gift Game UK edition
The rules remain delightfully simple: one player is the Card Czar each round, who reads a black card out loud. Everyone else selects a white card from their hand that they think best, or worst, depending on your mood, fits the prompt. The Card Czar then picks their favorite or most distasteful answer (the rules are intentionally ambiguous to encourage debate and shouting). The winner takes the round card and a point, and the next person becomes Card Czar. In Gift Game mode, there are usually extra twists designed to keep rounds fresh, such as limited-use wild cards or special prompts that encourage team play or quick-fire rounds.

In the UK edition, you’ll notice some prompts that lean into British culture, pop culture references, and culinary quirks. That means more jokes about tea, football, weather, and the eternal struggle to find a decent vegan sausage roll in hostile grocery aisles. The pacing tends to be brisker when your tea is cooling, and the room’s temperature is a perfect 23 degrees because everyone else refused to turn on the central heating.
