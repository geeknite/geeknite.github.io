# Setting the Stage

### What is Imperial Tide?

Imperial Tide: The Great War 1914-1918 is a grand strategy board game that lets players take control of the Central Powers or the Entente forces during World War I. The game focuses on the strategic and operational aspects of the war, providing a deep and immersive experience for players.

### Components

Imperial Tide comes with beautifully designed components, including a detailed map of Europe, army counters, cards, and a rulebook. The artwork and quality of the pieces are top-notch, making it a visual treat.

# Gameplay Mechanics

### Turns and Actions

Each turn in Imperial Tide represents a period of the war, with players taking actions such as moving armies, engaging in combat, and managing resources. The game uses a card-driven system that adds an element of unpredictability and requires strategic planning.

### Combat

Combat in Imperial Tide is resolved through a combination of dice rolls and modifiers based on terrain, unit types, and other factors. This system adds a layer of complexity and realism to the game, making each battle feel significant.

### Strategy and Planning

One of the standout features of Imperial Tide is the emphasis on strategic planning. Players must carefully manage their forces, anticipate enemy moves, and make decisions that will impact the course of the war. This makes the game both challenging and rewarding.
