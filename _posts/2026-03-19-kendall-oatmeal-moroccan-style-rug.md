## Care, Maintenance, and Durability

### Cleaning Basics

Cleaning is the boring yet essential act that determines the long term happiness with any rug. Kendall Oatmeal Moroccan Style Rug responds well to routine vacuuming on a low to medium setting. The goal is to remove loose dirt before it embeds itself in the fibers. If you have a pet with a love for dramatic entrances, you might want to vacuum more often in high traffic zones to prevent stubborn pet hair from clinging to the fibers.

For spills, act quickly. Blot from the outside of the spill toward the center with a clean cloth rather than rubbing, which can push the stain deeper. Mild cleaning solutions are usually sufficient; always test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure color fastness. If you happen to spill something intensely colored, a gentle color safe cleaner can be used in small amounts and blotted away completely. For persistent stains, consider professional cleaning service to maintain the integrity of the fibers and the geometric print.

### Stain Resistance and Color Stability

The oatmeal base helps hide minor discolorations and coffee rings for a reasonable amount of time. The geometric pattern does a surprisingly good job at masking small spills that would otherwise become obvious on a solid color rug. In terms of color stability, you may notice minor shade variation across production batches since natural fibers and dye lots can drift over time. This is the natural world reminding you that your rug has a life and a story to tell, not a perfect lab specimen.

### Longevity and Value

## Practical Tips for Placement and Styling

Here are a few practical tips to maximize the look of the Kendall Oatmeal rug in real homes:

- Place the rug so that at least the front legs of your main seating furniture rest on it. This defines the space and prevents the rug from looking like a photo prop.
- Use furniture pads to minimize shifting and wear in high traffic spots. It protects both your floor and the beautiful geometry of the rug.
- Layer with a smaller textured throw or a leather chair nearby to create an interesting texture contrast without competing patterns.
- If you have underfloor heating or radiant heat, ensure the rug is compatible with such systems and follow the manufacturers guidelines for heat exposure.
- Balance the room color by picking wall art or cushions that echo the tones in the rug. Do not overpower the palette; the goal is harmony, not a color party that nobody invited.

## Maintenance Roadmap: Quick Start Guide

- Vacuum weekly on a low setting to prevent heavy soil buildup.
- Blot spills promptly with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing which can push stains deeper into the fibers.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear. Yes, even rugs appreciate a good life rhythm.
- Schedule professional cleaning every 12 to 24 months depending on usage and household activity. A professional touch can refresh the fibers and restore the original vibrance.
