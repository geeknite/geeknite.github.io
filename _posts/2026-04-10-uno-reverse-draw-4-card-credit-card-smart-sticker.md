## Installation and Everyday Use: Mission Possible

### Step-by-Step: How to Apply It Without Creating a Sticky Mess

1) Clean the card. Use a microfiber cloth and a mild isopropyl wipe to remove oils. If you’re fancy, you can use a contact cleaner. The goal is to present a dry, oil-free surface. 2) Dry thoroughly. Humidity is the enemy of adhesives. 3) Peel slowly. Don’t yank; you don’t want to burn through the adhesive’s bite. 4) Align with the card’s edges. It helps to hold the sticker at a slight angle to reduce air traps. 5) Press and smooth from center outward. A microfiber cloth is your friend here; you’re basically playing a tiny game of origami with a numeric card.

In practice, most people can install the sticker on the first try, with a minimal number of repositionings. If you find yourself fighting air bubbles, you can prick them with a pin and gently push the air out toward the edge. Do not overdo it; you’re not deflating a tire, you’re positioning a cosmetic skin on a card.
