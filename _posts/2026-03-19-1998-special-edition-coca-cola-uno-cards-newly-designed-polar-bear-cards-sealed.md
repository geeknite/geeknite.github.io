## How to Verify Authenticity (Quick Checklist)
- Look for consistent card stock and print quality across the deck. Uneven color or rough edges can indicate a reprint or a knockoff.
- Check the back design and the signature Coca-Cola branding for accuracy. The polar bear motif should be integrated in a way that matches other Coca-Cola promo materials from the era.
- Inspect the seal integrity if it is sealed. A tampered seal can be a red flag for authenticity or a previously opened deck.
- Compare the card faces to known examples from the era. Minor print quirks can exist, but major misprints or missing features can indicate a reproduction.
- Confirm the packaging dimensions and outer box typography. A faithful edition should align with the market standard of that year and region.
