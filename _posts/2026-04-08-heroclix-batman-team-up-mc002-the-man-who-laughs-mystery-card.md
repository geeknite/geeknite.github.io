## Dial and Powers

The Man Who Laughs in MC002 is designed to disrupt the flow of a game rather than just smash faces. It thrives when used as an anchor piece in a Batman Team-Up squad, forcing your opponent to respect its range, control, and the occasional snicker of malice.

### Basic stats

- Points: ~60
- Speed: 6
- Range: 4
- Attack: 9
- Defense: 17
- Damage: 3

### Special powers

- Laughing Gas Cloud (range 4, lasts 2 turns): Any enemy within the gas cloud gets a -1 modifier to attack rolls and can find it harder to move out of it once trapped. The gas lingers, turning the battlefield into a literal punchline.
- Fearsome Presence (Trait): On reveal, place a marker that makes your opponent think twice before approaching the gas cloud. Not included here: actual fear; just the fear of The Laughs.
- Stealth and Gag Reflex (Special): The Man Who Laughs resists mind-control attempts better than your average squishy minion and can exploit chaos to reposition.

## Strategy and Gameplay

### Opening gambit
The goal here is not to end the encounter in two turns. You want to tilt the map in your favor by placing the Laughing Gas cloud to block critical lanes, forcing your opponent to route around your support pieces.

### Mid-game
If you’re running Batman Team-Up characters, MC002 shines as a control engine. The synergy between The Laughs and Batman’s takedown pressure means you can press your opponent into a corner and keep them there.

### Late-game
With a solid control footprint, you can enclose a ring of influence that makes it difficult for your opponent to focus on a single target. If they try to retreat, you rotate through the gas cloud and keep the pressure up.

#### Tips and tricks
- Use terrain to your advantage: gas clouds in corridors? Delicious. Elevated lines? Even better.
- Protect MC002: it’s not the tank of the team, but it isn’t a complete liability either. If a fast attacker puts a hit on it, you’ll be sad in the morning.
- Pairings: Batman Team-Up pieces that excel at lane control amplify MC002’s strengths in a big way.
