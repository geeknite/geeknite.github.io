### Characters

- **Mr. Ideal**: The protagonist who believes in the vision of utopia and works tirelessly to make it a reality. Picture a cross between Elon Musk and Gandhi.
- **Ms. Skeptic**: The voice of reason who questions the feasibility of a perfect society. Think of her as your inner critic, but with better dialogue.
- **The Bureaucrats**: The antagonists who throw red tape and paperwork at every revolutionary idea. Imagine your last encounter with DMV employees, but with more sinister motives.

### Themes

The film tackles major themes such as:

- **Equality**: Can true equality ever be achieved, or are we doomed to always have someone richer, smarter, or just plain luckier?
- **Freedom**: How much freedom are we willing to sacrifice for security and harmony?
- **Human Nature**: Are humans inherently good, or do we need constant supervision to stay out of trouble?
