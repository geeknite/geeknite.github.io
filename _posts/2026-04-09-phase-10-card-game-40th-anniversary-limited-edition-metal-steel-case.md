## Care, storage, and practical tips

### Keeping the gleam without the glare
Maintain the case with regular dusting and a gentle wipe with a microfiber cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the etched details. For the cards, a simple dry deck brush can reset any fingerprint smudges after a long night of intense bidding and card juggling. If you plan to store the edition in a high-humidity area, consider a silica gel sachet inside the case to protect both the metal exterior and the card stock from moisture.

### Display vs storage considerations
If you decide to display the metal case, place it away from direct sunlight to prevent any potential fading of interior hues. The interior lining should remain plush and protective; keep the box closed when not actively playing, to maintain the creature of the moment that keeps guests curious about what exactly is inside this enigmatic metal box.
