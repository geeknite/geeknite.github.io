## Care and Longevity

### Longevity Considerations

No rug lasts forever, but you can maximize lifespan with a combination of good placement, routine cleaning, and a gentle touch when moving heavy furniture. The CY7938’s loom quality and fiber choice position it well for several years of moderate use. If your household includes small children or pets, you’ll want to inspect the edges periodically for fraying and address it early to prevent further unraveling. A well-maintained rug ages gracefully and continues to look stylish rather than tired.

### Rotation and Flipping

Rotating the rug 90 degrees every few months can help even out wear patterns, especially in high-traffic zones. Flipping isn’t generally required for most rugs of this type, but if you want to check the backing or secure edges, a careful lift-and-shift can reveal any lurking snags or loose seams. The goal is to keep this rug looking sharp rather than letting it become a casualty of the furniture shuffle gala.

### Storage Tips

If you ever need to store the rug for a season, roll it (don’t fold) and wrap it in breathable fabric. Avoid plastic wrap that traps moisture. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color shift and fiber damage. Yes, even rugs deserve a spa retreat every so often.
