### Maintenance and Cleaning Tips

- Vacuum weekly with a brushless attachment to avoid fiber damage. Use a low-to-medium suction setting to maintain the pile’s height.
- Treat spills immediately by blotting with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can embed liquids deeper into the fibers.
- For stains, use a mild detergent solution and test on a small area first. Rinse with a damp cloth to remove soap residue.
- If the rug needs a deeper refresh, surface-clean with a damp cloth and let air-dry completely before placing it back on the floor. Don’t machine-wash unless the care label explicitly says so.
- Rotate occasionally to ensure even wear and color consistency across the surface.
