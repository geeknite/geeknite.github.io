## Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping the Sunshine 24/7 Without a Crew of Cleaning Goblins
No rug review would be complete without a practical section on maintenance. The goal is to keep the Shankill Sunflower Yellow Round Rug fresh, clean, and worthy of a never-ending sunlight metaphor even after months and months of everyday life.

Care basics:
- Vacuum regularly to prevent embedded grit from grinding the fibers into mini-sandpaper. Use a gentle setting for plush piles, and avoid high-power beater brushes that could fray the fabric.
- Spot-clean spills promptly with a mild detergent and water. Do not aggressively rub; instead, blot with a clean cloth to lift the spill without pushing it deeper into the pile.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to distribute wear evenly. This helps prevent uneven flattening in high-traffic zones and keeps the circular shape from looking like a slightly deflated pizza.
- Avoid direct sun for extended periods to minimize color fading. If your room basks in sunlight, consider using window treatments or rotating the rug’s position every few months for color consistency.

Durability-wise, polypropylene-based piles are typically moisture-resistant and easy to clean, which is a big win for households with kids, pets, or the occasional accidental espresso shot homage to the coffee gods. Longevity also ties into backing materials; a well-made backing helps prevent buckling and keeps the rug from wrinkling under furniture pressure. If you stay on top of maintenance, this rug should hold its shape and color for years rather than months, which keeps your wallet happier and your floor happier.


### A Quick Troubleshooting Guide
- Edges curling up: Place furniture legs or a rug gripper along the edge to restore a neat circle.
- Color dullness after a few months: Rotate placement and consider a light refresh with color-safe cleaners; consult the manufacturer’s care instructions for color retention tips.
- Fast flattening in the center: A dense rug pad helps preserve pile height and reduces fatigue from seating weight.
