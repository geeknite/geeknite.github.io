### Care and Maintenance: Tips from a Friendly Geek

- Vacuum regularly to maintain pile health and avoid compaction. A robot vacuum can handle most of the missionary work here, freeing you to conquer other home-improvement quests.
- Spot-clean spills promptly using a mild detergent solution and blotting technique. Do not rub; rubbing is for poor life decisions and dramatic movie montages.
- Use a rug pad to prevent sliding and add comfort underfoot. The pad is the unsung hero of the flooring world—like a nimble, silent sidekick that keeps your furniture from creeping away from you at night.
- Rotate the rug every couple of months to even out wear and exposure. This is the textile equivalent of defragmenting your hard drive: boring, but incredibly satisfying when you notice the improvement in appearance.
