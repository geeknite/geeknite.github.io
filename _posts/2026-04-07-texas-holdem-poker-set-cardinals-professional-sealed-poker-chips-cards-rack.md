### Maintenance tips and care

To keep the set looking fresh when you host weekly games, a little maintenance goes a long way. Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth to remove fingerprints and oils; avoid harsh solvents, which can degrade surface finishes. Do not store cards in extreme humidity; you want to avoid warping over time. If you ever see chips losing their color or the edge spots chipping away, it may be time to retire a few for a new batch or to rotate denominations to reduce wear on specific colors. Keep the rack clean and free of dust; a dusty rack can create a mild shuffle friction that slows things down and makes you wonder if you just discovered a new form of physics in your living room.
