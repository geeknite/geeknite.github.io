Putting Vegeta on the table is one thing; piloting him to consistent wins is another. Here are practical tips to help you turn theory into actual table-dominance, without making your friends think you’ve adopted a villainous gaming cult.

- Early game pressure: your goal is to apply pressure without doom-scrolling your life total. Establish a couple of solid threats that force your opponent to answer without giving you a balanced board position.
- Mid game decisions: this is where you decide between continuing aggression and holding back a moment to deploy a bigger, more efficient answer. The temptation to commit everything is real, but the wiser path often looks like a deliberate hold until you can follow through with a stronger sequence.
- Late game finish: you should be hunting for the exact window to deliver your finisher. If the window isn’t obvious, you should still have a plan to push through a few extra points of damage while keeping your life total intact enough to survive one more round of pressure.
