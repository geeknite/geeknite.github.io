## Setup, Maintenance, and Everyday Use
### Unrolling and settling in
New rugs can arrive with a memory of their packaging. For best results, unroll and let it rest for 24–48 hours to allow natural flattening. Gently press out wrinkles with your hands, and if needed, place a few heavy books along the edges for a day to accelerate the flatness. A well-settled rug instantly makes a room feel more finished and gives the furniture a sense of grounding—almost like your couch finally found its true calling.

### Cleaning and stain removal
Vacuum regularly, ideally without a beater bar on the highest setting to avoid fiber crush. For spills, blot—do not rub—with a clean cloth and use a mild detergent solution. Rinse lightly and blot again until dry. If you’re dealing with stubborn stains, a fabric-safe stain remover designed for synthetic fibers should do the trick. Always test a small area first. External rug care guides recommend avoiding harsh chemicals and ensuring complete drying to prevent mold growth, which you definitely do not want in your living room shroud. ## Care Tips and Maintenance Routines
- Vacuum weekly with a gentle setting; avoid aggressive suction that could pull fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing which can push stains deeper.
