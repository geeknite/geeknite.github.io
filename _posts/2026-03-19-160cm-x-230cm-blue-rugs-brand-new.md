## Care and Cleaning: Keeping the Blue Fresh

Maintenance is the truth serum for area rugs. The good news: this rug is forgiving. Vacuum weekly on a low-suction setting, keeping the vacuum head away from the edges to avoid pulling threads. If you spill coffee, juice, or a sports drink, blot immediately with a clean cloth. Don’t rub; you’ll push the spill deeper into the fibers. For stubborn stains, use a mild detergent with water and test in an inconspicuous corner first. For more aggressive staining, you may consider professional cleaning, especially if the rug is an investment to you.

Edge care is important. If you notice slight curling on the long sides, you can place a heavy book along each edge overnight. This helps edges settle into the floor. If you are worried about shedding, you can vacuum with the lowest suction slowly to pull out any loose fibers.
