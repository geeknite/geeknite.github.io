## Curation, authenticity, and storage tips

### Preserving the sealed condition

If your plan is to keep the box sealed for years, you need a simple care routine:
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading and cardboard brittleness.
- Avoid high humidity, which can cause warping or moisture damage to the inner components.
- Use acid-free tissue for extra internal protection if the box is showing signs of age.
- Consider a display solution that keeps the box horizontal to prevent pressure on the seals.
