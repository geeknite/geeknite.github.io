### Care and maintenance: keeping the rainbow fresh
Maintenance is where this rug earns its stripes. Routine vacuuming once or twice a week helps maintain that crisp multitone look. If pet hair becomes a problem, a quick pass with a rubber brush can help lift fibers without flattening the color blocks. For accidental spills, act fast: blot, don’t rub, because rubbing is a tiny sci-fi disaster in disguise. If a stubborn stain appears, use a mild detergent solution and dab gently. A tiny secret: rotate the rug 90 degrees every few months to ensure even wear and to keep you from claiming that the rug is “definitely biased toward the left side.”

Pro tip for gamers and remote workers: a rug protector or a small area rug under your desk can reduce chair noise and keep the main rug pristine for longer. If you own a robot vacuum, you’ll be happy to know the Torino rug plays nicely with suction, so long as you don’t mind the occasional sniff of color-coded confetti as the machine maps your floor plan.
