## What is Lankhmar: The Roleplaying Game - Dice Set?

The Lankhmar Dice Set is a collection of polyhedral dice designed to enhance your roleplaying experience in the gritty and fantastical world of Nehwon. With stunning designs and intricate details, these dice are not just tools for rolling - they're artifacts that bring your adventures to life.

### Components

The set includes:

- **Polyhedral Dice**: A complete set of seven dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and percentile d10) with unique designs inspired by the lore of Lankhmar.
- **Dice Bag**: A velvet bag to store your dice, adorned with the emblem of Lankhmar. Perfect for keeping your precious rolls safe from prying eyes.
- **Collector's Box**: A beautifully crafted box to display and protect your dice. It’s so fancy you might want to keep it on your mantle.
