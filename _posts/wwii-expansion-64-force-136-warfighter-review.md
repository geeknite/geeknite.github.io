## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is straightforward. The special forces player secretly plans their moves on a hidden board, while the enemy soldiers are positioned on the main board. The objective for the special forces is to complete their mission objectives without being detected, while the enemy soldiers must work together to locate and capture the infiltrators.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, the special forces move stealthily across the board, using various actions to avoid detection, such as moving quietly, creating distractions, or taking out guards with silent kills. The enemy soldiers, on the other hand, move and search based on limited information, trying to predict the special forces' movements and set traps.
