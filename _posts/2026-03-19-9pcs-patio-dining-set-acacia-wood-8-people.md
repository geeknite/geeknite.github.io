## Assembly and maintenance tips
- Lay out all parts and verify you have everything before diving in. Missing bolts will cause the group chat to devolve into a scavenger hunt. 
- Follow the sequence in the instruction manual, as skipping steps can lead to wobble city or misaligned chair legs. 
- When tightening, avoid overtorquing metal hardware to prevent stripping or warping. A snug fit is enough for everyday use. 
- For maintenance, wipe the wood with a mild soap solution and dry promptly. Apply a protective sealant or oil every 6 to 12 months depending on climate. In areas with heavy sun exposure, consider more frequent treatment to maintain color and moisture balance. 
- Cushion care: if cushions are included, store them indoors during heavy rain or winter months to extend their life. A simple cushion storage solution can save you from swapping them out every season.
