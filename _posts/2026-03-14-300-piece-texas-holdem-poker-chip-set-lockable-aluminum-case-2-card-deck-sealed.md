## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity Tips

To keep your 300-piece set looking sharp and playing smoothly for years, a few care tips go a long way:

- Cleaning: Wipe the chips with a soft, slightly damp cloth to remove fingerprints and residue. Avoid harsh solvents that can wear down the color finish.
- Deck care: Keep the decks sealed and store them in a cool, dry place. This helps prevent warping and curling and keeps the cards in a predictable shape.
- Case care: Periodically check the latches and hinges. A little lubrication (careful with hardware, use a tiny dab of light oil if you’re comfortable) can prevent stiffness and ensure longevity.
- Chip storage: When not in use, keep the chips in the case or a dedicated tray to prevent chips from rolling away under couch cushions or behind the sofa. Trust me, I’ve found a few in the strangest places after a marathon session.

With a little routine maintenance, your set will survive more game nights than your Spam folder has jokes. And yes, that long-term durability is what makes this kit an investment, not just a one-and-done novelty.
