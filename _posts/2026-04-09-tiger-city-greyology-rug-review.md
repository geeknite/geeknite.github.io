### Cleaning and Maintenance

Stain resistance is a big selling point for the Greyology line, and for good reason. The rug smiled on coffee spills, tomato sauce, and the occasional muddy paw print from the resident canine. For the inevitable spill, blot (not rub) with a clean cloth, then use a mild cleaning solution according to the manufacturers safe guidelines. We linked up a reliable care guide in the sources of this review; you can also check [a general rug care guide](https://www.realhomes.com/advice/how-to-clean-a-rug) for extended techniques. Quick note: do not expose to direct heat for long periods; we do not want a melted marbled effect in the center. A quick vacuum weekly keeps the dust to a minimum and helps preserve the color integrity over time.

The rug is also compatible with common vacuum cleaners; you can use a rotating beater bar on low to medium settings. If you own a robot vacuum, you will be pleased to know that the Greyology rugs handle the pass quite nicely; the robot bounces once or twice as if to say hello to the fiber but quickly recovers. It is a good match for modern city living where efficiency is the currency and time is your most precious asset.

## Practical Facts and Geeky Specs

### Dimensions and Weight

The 5x8 variant weighs around 18 pounds, a sensible heft that ensures it stays put during light gusts from an open window and the occasional cat sprint. The 8x10 version steps into the range of 40 to 50 pounds, depending on moisture content and manufacturing batch. If you have a narrow staircase or tight corners to navigate during setup, plan the move with a friend and a dolly, because nothing kills your vibe faster than wrestling a giant rug into a cramped hallway.

### Cleaning Methods and Care

As noted earlier, blotting spills and cleaning with mild detergent is recommended. For deep cleaning, a mixture of water and white vinegar can do wonders on organic stains; always do a small patch test first to ensure nothing fades or bleaches. Vacuuming is recommended weekly to prevent dirt from embedding, particularly in city environments where dust floats in from the street like tiny, unwilling tourists. If you want to avoid color fade, try to prevent prolonged exposure to direct sunlight; you can use curtains or blinds to filter the light because the Greyology fibers do respond to UV exposure over long periods.

### Warranty and Return Policy

The Tiger City brand offers a standard one-year warranty on manufacturing defects and a reasonable return window if the rug does not meet your expectations. The policy is friendly to customers who attempt to return rugs after two years of heavy foot traffic; it is best to check the latest terms on the official site to confirm, as these policies sometimes change with the tide of online shopping and seasonal sales.
