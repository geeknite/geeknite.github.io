## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Frostpunk: The Board Game is like preparing for an Arctic expedition. Lay out the game board, distribute resources, and choose your scenario. Each scenario presents unique challenges and objectives, ensuring that no two games are ever the same.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players must make decisions on how to manage their city's resources, build structures, and address various crises. Actions include gathering resources, constructing buildings, and dealing with random events that can either help or hinder your progress.

### Survival and Strategy

The game shines in its strategic depth. As the leader of a frozen city, you must carefully plan your moves, balance the needs of your citizens, and make tough moral decisions. Cooperation and communication are key, as you'll need to work together to overcome the game's brutal challenges.

**Pro Tip**: Don't neglect the Generator! Keeping it operational is crucial to your survival. Trust me, you don't want to deal with a city full of frozen citizens!

### Replayability
