## Display, Care, and Long-Term Preservation

If you own Gloom Shiny Rare SV4a, you’ll probably want to display it rather than file it away in a stack of sleeves. The holo finish demands a careful eye for lighting: you’ll want a shelf or display case that lets the light kiss the surface without scratching the foil. A display stand with a soft support helps avoid edge wear, and a UV-protective acrylic cover can prevent the color from fading over years of occasional sunlight exposure in a well-lit room. For casual storage, keep it in a high-quality sleeve, a top-loader, and a rigid card binder with anti-glare pages. The longevity of a shiny card depends on your environment as much as your care routine, so remember to keep humidity stable, avoid stacking heavy objects on the binder, and resist the urge to rub the surface like you’re conjuring a confetti cannon.
