## Cleaning and care: keeping the set pristine
Cleaning is a small ritual that pays big dividends. Wicker or rattan style surfaces benefit from a soft brush or microfiber cloth to remove dust and debris. For the storage cover, a light rinse and complete drying before storing is a good habit, especially if you are using the cover to shield from spring pollen or the occasional flying leaf.
