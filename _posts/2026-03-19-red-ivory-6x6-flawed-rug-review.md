## Care and Maintenance: Keeping the Drama Lean and Clean
Care for this rug doesn’t require a science degree or a small army of cleaning robots named after Star Trek captains. Here are sane, practical guidelines:

- Vacuum regularly with a non-beater bar setting to minimize fiber pull and maximize longevity. If you have a hand-me-down vacuum that makes you look like you’re performing surgery on your floor, you’ve probably found its perfect symbiotic relationship with this rug.
- Blot stains, don’t rub. Rubbing can push the spill deeper or create friction that distorts the weave. Be calm. Channel your inner zen monk and dab away.
- Address pet hair with a lint roller or a gentle vacuum pass. The rug’s pile won’t magically levitate hair away, but you’ll minimize the tumbleweed effect around the couch legs.
- For deeper cleaning, a mild detergent solution with water on a soft cloth can lift dirt without saturating the backing. Always test in a small area first to ensure color stability.

If you’re the sort who loves a care regimen with color-coded charts, you’ll enjoy a small joy in maintaining this rug. If you’d rather forget maintenance exists, you’ll want a different rug that makes spillage less dramatic. The truth is somewhere in between: this rug rewards routine maintenance, but isn’t a black belt in upkeep.
