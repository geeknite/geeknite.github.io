## What is Unmatched: Battle of Legends Volume 1?

Unmatched: Battle of Legends Volume 1 is a tactical miniatures game where players take on the roles of iconic legends from different genres and mythologies. The game's unique combat system, stunning artwork, and hilarious character matchups make it an unforgettable experience.

### The Basics

In Unmatched, players choose a hero and battle it out on beautifully illustrated maps. Each hero comes with a unique deck of cards that dictate their abilities, attacks, and special powers. The goal? To defeat your opponent and assert your legend as the greatest of all time. Whether it's King Arthur wielding Excalibur or Medusa petrifying foes with a glance, every battle is epic and unpredictable.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed and colorful miniatures of legendary characters. Seriously, if you don't want to play the game, you might just want these for display.
- **Cards**: Each hero has a deck of cards representing their actions, abilities, and attacks.
- **Game Board**: Various maps where battles take place, each with its own strategic elements.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track health, actions, and special effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Keep snacks away from the game components. Sticky fingers and legendary battles do not mix well.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Unmatched: Battle of Legends Volume 1 is like preparing for an epic showdown. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your hero. Now you're ready to dive into the chaos!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their hero's abilities. You can move, attack, defend, and use special powers. Strategy is key, so plan your moves carefully and anticipate your opponent's actions.

### Combat

Combat in Unmatched is a blend of strategy and luck. Each attack can be modified by drawing cards from your deck, leading to unexpected twists and turns. Did you plan a perfect attack only to have your opponent counter with a surprise move? Welcome to Unmatched, where legends clash and chaos reigns.
