## Tips for Maximizing Fun

- Establish a quick ground rule about friendly competition and safety. Foam burritos can surprise people who think they are out of reach, so remind players to avoid aiming at faces and to keep throws within a reasonable arc.
- Rotate the burrito color codes between rounds. This prevents one color from accumulating too much advantage and keeps the game fair-ish in a wacky sense.
- Use a timer to wrap up rounds and prevent the chaos from running away with your evening. A 60 to 90 second quick round rhythm is ideal.
- Create a snack break between rounds to keep everyone energized and to avoid a meltdown when someone reveals they forgot to bring a charger for their phone.
- Consider streaming or recording a round for your friends to relive and laugh at during future game nights.
