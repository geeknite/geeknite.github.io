## The Wild Foil Flux SP Mechanic
Let’s talk about the elephant in the room—the Flux SP mechanic. In practice, Flux SP is a foil-coated set of rules that can be introduced at the host’s discretion. It’s marketed as adding dynamic strategic options to UNO, but the reality is a mix of typical UNO with a splash of collectible-card-game vibes.

Key ideas behind Flux SP:
- Flux: A color-agnostic, optional effect that triggers when a player plays a Flux card. It may allow you to copy a number or color from another card, or to swap a card from your hand with one from the draw pile—depending on the spin from the current Flux deck. It’s basically a tiny, legal chaos engine.
- SP: Special Power—certain specially marked cards grant a one-time power that can be used to modify the current state. Think of it as a mini-ability that can rearrange turn order, reassign color restrictions, or steal a few moments of advantage. It’s not game-breaking, but it can swing momentum if used at the right moment.
