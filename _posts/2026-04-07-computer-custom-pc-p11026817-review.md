## Troubleshooting and tips
- Boot issues: reseat RAM and GPU, then clear the CMOS. It’s the modern equivalent of turning it off and on again with more ceremony.
- Fan ramp on startup: check the BIOS cooling profiles; if needed, revert to a balanced default. The default is rarely wrong and often quietly competent.
- NVMe not detected: check that the drive is seated correctly and that the BIOS version supports your drive; if not, a firmware upgrade for the motherboard might be your best friend.
- Cable spaghetti: label cables as you route them; you’ll thank yourself when you upgrade next year and remember which cable goes where.
