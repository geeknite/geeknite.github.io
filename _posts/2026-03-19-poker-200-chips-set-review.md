## Care, maintenance, and longevity tips

- Keep chips away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading; a simple cabinet or shelf can extend their life.
- Brush the felt surface after use to remove dust and stray chips; this helps maintain a smooth dealing surface.
- Clean cards occasionally with a soft cloth to avoid gunk buildup; sticky cards are not fun to deal with on a pressure-filled river bet.
- Store the set in a cool, dry place to prevent moisture damage to the felt and card stock.
- If you notice any warping or curling, don’t panic — a light press under a heavy book overnight can help restore flatness.
