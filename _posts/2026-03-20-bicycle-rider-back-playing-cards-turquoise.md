Care Tips: Keeping the Color True

To maintain that turquoise aura, follow simple care steps. Avoid exposing the deck to direct sunlight for extended periods, which can slightly fade color over long durations. Clean your cards with a dry microfiber cloth if you see fingerprints or smudges; avoid water or chemical cleaners that could affect the finish. When not in use, store the deck in its tuck box or a dedicated card case to prevent bowed edges. If you’re transporting multiple decks on a gig, consider a soft organizer that protects against sudden drops—nobody wants a handful of turquoise cards turning into a cyan-colored confetti moment. A little care goes a long way toward preserving that crisp, stage-ready look.
