### How to care for your sleeves
A quick care guide: wipe the outer sleeve with a microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints and dust. Avoid harsh solvents that can degrade the print or the internal coating. If you need to re-shuffle mid-match, do a gentle riffle to minimize edge wear, and consider replacing worn sleeves before the next round. If you keep them in a protective deck box, you will extend their life and keep the artwork looking vibrant. When storing, store away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading.
