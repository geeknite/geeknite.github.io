The book covers the Battle of Shiloh, a key event in the American Civil War where Union forces, led by Ulysses S. Grant, were taken by surprise by Confederate troops. It's a detailed account of the two-day battle that unfolded in April 1862, highlighting the strategies, errors, and sheer chaos that characterized the confrontation.
