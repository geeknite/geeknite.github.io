## What's in the Box?

### D-Day Dice: Base Game

First up, the base game of D-Day Dice. This is where it all begins. You're a brave soldier trying to storm the beaches of Normandy. Sounds easy, right? Think again! You'll need to gather resources, recruit specialists, and roll your way to victory. Just remember: if at first you don't succeed, blame the dice.

### Camino Al Infierno (Road to Hell)
![Camino Al Infierno](https://i.imgur.com/wlDQUJGm.jpg)

This expansion takes the game to the next level. More enemies, more obstacles, and of course, more ways to die. It's called "Road to Hell" for a reason! But fear not, with great risk comes great reward. Just be sure to keep an eye out for those sneaky landmines.

### Dentro del Búnker (Inside the Bunker)
![Dentro del Búnker](https://i.imgur.com/kZ2D7bHm.jpg)

Ever wondered what it's like to be trapped inside a bunker with enemy forces closing in? No? Well, now you can find out anyway! This expansion adds intense bunker scenarios where you'll need to strategize and make quick decisions. Because nothing says "fun" like a claustrophobic death trap.

### Dios con Nosotros (God with Us)
![Dios con Nosotros](https://i.imgur.com/5T2Y5QHm.jpg)

The final expansion, and possibly the most divine. "Dios con Nosotros" introduces new divine intervention cards that can either save your skin or doom you further. It's like having an angel on one shoulder and a devil on the other. Which one will you listen to? Only the dice know.

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Resource Management

In D-Day Dice, managing your resources is key. You'll need to collect soldiers, tools, and courage points. Yes, courage points. Because storming a heavily fortified beach requires an abundance of intestinal fortitude. Plan wisely, or you'll find yourself courage-less and cowering behind a sand dune.

### Rolling the Dice

Ah, the heart and soul of D-Day Dice. Rolling the dice will determine your fate. Will you gather enough soldiers to push forward, or will you roll snake eyes and end up as cannon fodder? Luck and strategy go hand in hand here. Just don't blame the dice too much when things go south.

### Specialist Recruitment

Recruiting specialists can turn the tide of battle in your favor. Medics, engineers, and even snipers can be added to your team if you have the right resources. Choose your specialists wisely, as they can make or break your mission. Or, you know, just look really cool on your game board.

### Cooperative Play

One of the best aspects of D-Day Dice is its cooperative gameplay. You and your friends will need to work together to achieve victory. Or, more likely, to collectively groan at the cruel fate the dice have bestowed upon you. Either way, it's a bonding experience like no other.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- **Immersive Theme**: If you're a history buff, you'll love the detailed WWII theme.
- **Strategic Depth**: Requires both luck and strategic thinking.
- **Cooperative Play**: Great for team bonding (or blaming).
- **Expansions Add Variety**: Each expansion brings new challenges and excitement.

### Cons
- **Difficulty Level**: Can be punishingly difficult. Be prepared to lose. A lot.
- **Setup Time**: Setting up can be time-consuming. Not for the impatient.
- **Luck Factor**: Heavy reliance on dice rolls can be frustrating.
