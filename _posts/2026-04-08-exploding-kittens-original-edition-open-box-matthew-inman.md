## How to Play: A Practical Walkthrough
### Turn Structure
On your turn you draw a card and may then play any number of action cards from your hand. The objective is simple in theory — avoid drawing the exploding kitten — but the path to victory is full of misdirection and counterplay. The action cards let you alter the course of the deck, manipulate other players, or simply add a layer of chaos that keeps everyone on their toes. A well-timed See the Future card can reveal a few upcoming cards, giving you the edge you need to decide whether to push your luck or shield yourself for a round or two.

### Core Cards and Deck Dynamics
The heart of Exploding Kittens rests in the interplay between Defuse cards, Exploding Kitten cards, and various action cards. The See the Future cards, Shuffle, Attack, and Favor cards create a lively ecosystem where you are always deciding whether to risk a draw or to retaliate against a rival. Defuse cards feel precious, and every time someone plays one to avert disaster, the room breathes a collective sigh of relief. The deck dynamics encourage a balance of risk and bluff, so even a modest hand can become your strongest weapon when played with the right timing.

### Social Play and Strategy
Strategy in Exploding Kittens is less about number crunching and more about social timing. Players who excel at this game are adept at reading cues from others, predicting when an opponent plans to use a particular card, and exploiting the moment when a defuse is spent. The humor helps lubricate social friction, turning near misses into shared laughter rather than private grudges. It is the kind of game where you want to lean into the chaos and allow the table to guide the moment rather than trying to control it entirely.
