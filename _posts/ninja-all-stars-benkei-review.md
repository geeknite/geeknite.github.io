## Who is Benkei?

Benkei is not your average ninja. He's a hulking powerhouse with a penchant for smashing enemies and creating chaos. With his impressive strength and resilience, Benkei is a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield. Think of him as the Hulk, but with a lot more finesse and ninja skills.

### Abilities

Benkei boasts a range of abilities that make him a versatile and formidable character:

- **Strength**: Benkei can deal massive damage with his powerful attacks. His enemies often find themselves cowering in fear.
- **Resilience**: With a high defense stat, Benkei can take a beating and still keep fighting. He's the perfect tank for any ninja squad.
- **Special Moves**: Benkei has some unique moves that can turn the tide of battle. From smashing through enemy lines to protecting allies, he's got it all.

## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Ninja All-Stars involves arranging the game board, miniatures, and cards. Don't worry, it's not rocket science – unless you're trying to fold an origami ninja star while balancing on one foot.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players will take actions like moving, attacking, and using special abilities. Benkei's movements might be slower due to his size, but his attacks more than make up for it. Coordinate with your team and watch the enemy ninjas tremble!

### Combat

Combat in Ninja All-Stars is fast-paced and thrilling. Players roll dice to determine the success of their attacks and defense. Benkei's strength means he can knock out enemies with fewer hits, making him a valuable asset in any skirmish.

### Campaign Mode

Ninja All-Stars features a campaign mode where players can progress through a series of missions. Benkei's story arc is filled with epic battles, surprising twists, and a lot of smashed enemies. It's like reading a manga, but you're in control!
