## What is Bloodborne: The Board Game - Hunter's Dream?

Bloodborne: The Board Game - Hunter's Dream is an expansion to the original Bloodborne board game. It introduces new mechanics, enemies, and quests that add depth and replayability to an already intense game. If you've ever wanted to relive the terror and excitement of Yharnam in tabletop form, this expansion is a must-have.

### The Basics

In Bloodborne: The Board Game, players take on the roles of Hunters, battling through a series of challenging encounters, uncovering lore, and ultimately facing off against fearsome bosses. The Hunter's Dream expansion takes it a step further, adding new layers of strategy and horror.

### Components

The expansion includes:
- **Miniatures**: New beautifully crafted miniatures, including Hunters, enemies, and bosses.
- **Cards**: Additional cards for new abilities, equipment, and quests.
- **Board Tiles**: Extra tiles that expand the game board and introduce new locations to explore.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Bloodborne: The Board Game - Hunter's Dream is like preparing for a nightmare. Lay out the new board tiles, distribute the Hunter miniatures, and shuffle the new quest and enemy cards. Take a deep breath and brace yourself for the horrors to come.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their Hunter's abilities. You can move, attack, heal, and use special abilities. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your fellow Hunters to survive the onslaught of enemies and complete your quests.

### Combat

Combat in Bloodborne: The Board Game is a mixture of strategy and luck. Each enemy has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic attack just to miss completely? Welcome to the nightmare, where nothing goes as planned.
