## The Basics

### What is That Rug RPG?

**That Rug RPG** is a humorous role-playing game set in a world where rugs hold secrets and midnight investigations are the norm. Designed by the brilliant minds at Geeknite Games, this RPG combines mystery-solving with comedy, allowing players to embark on hilarious adventures filled with absurdity and intrigue.

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully illustrated rulebook, character sheets, and various tokens representing clues, suspects, and, of course, rugs. The artwork is top-notch, bringing the hilarious world of That Rug RPG to life. Additionally, the game includes a set of dice that will become your trusty companions in solving mysteries.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up **That Rug RPG** is as easy as laying down a rug. Distribute character sheets, gather your tokens and dice, and choose your starting scenario. The objective is to solve mysteries while dealing with the comedic challenges that come your way.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions based on their character's abilities and the clues they uncover. You can investigate suspects, inspect rugs, and use special abilities to outwit your opponents. Cooperation and humor are key, so strategize with your teammates to crack the case and have a good laugh.

### Humor and Strategy

The game shines in its blend of humor and strategy. You'll need to think on your feet, utilize your character's unique skills, and laugh off the ridiculous situations that arise. For example, who knew that inspecting a rug could lead to uncovering a hidden treasure or a secret passage?
