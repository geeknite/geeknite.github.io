## Maintenance and Cleaning
### Everyday Cleaning
Synthetic fibers respond well to routine vacuuming, and the Quarry Oyster is no exception. Regular passes with a vacuumer of moderate suction will collect dust, pet hair, and the occasional crumb that pretends to be a tiny sculpture left by a bored traveler of your kitchen. For spills, blot gently with a clean cloth and avoid rubbing, which could push the stain deeper into the pile. A mild cleaning solution or water with a hint of dish soap can help lift most common messes without leaving a soapy residue. The key with any synthetic rug is to address accidents promptly, then avoid the temptation to go full chemistry lab on it.

### Stain Resistance and Special Care
The major selling point of a synthetic 9x12 rug is its resilience to stains. Coffee, red wine, and the occasional juice spill will not vanish with wishful thinking alone, but they will respond well to a little water, a dab of mild detergent, and a pat on the back for your bravery. For stubborn stains, test a small hidden area first and then repeat the treatment on the visible area. Do not machine wash the rug unless the care instructions explicitly authorize it; otherwise you risk unraveling the fiber and turning your living room into a performance piece about rug distress. If you have pets that love to mark their territory with random puddles, a pet-specific cleaner is a safe option, and a quick rinse after cleaning helps prevent any lingering odors that could trigger a dog’s scent memory against your area rug.
