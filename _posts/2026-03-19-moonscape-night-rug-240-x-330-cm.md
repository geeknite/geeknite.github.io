## Underfoot Experience and Practicality
### Comfort level
If comfort were a species, this rug would be a well-nurtured, softly glowing nebula. The pile height is forgiving for bare feet and socks, but not so long that it threatens to swallow a telepresence robot. It’s the sort of rug you can sit on for hours during a weekend gaming binge, with enough cushion to feel like you’re stepping onto a small platform in a sci‑fi video game.

### Durability and maintenance
Synthetic blends in rugs of this scale are typically chosen for stain resistance and color retention. That said, the Moonscape Night Rug isn’t a miracle worker; it’s better at hiding the occasional coffee spill or pet fur tumble than at resisting a violently spilled smoothie. Routine vacuuming works well, and for deeper cleaning, a spot-clean approach is recommended—no aggressive washing machines that could warp the lunar surface. For those who insist on all things practical, a professional cleaning every 12–18 months can help maintain the surface’s crisp crater textures.

### Cleaning tips for stargazers
- Vacuum with a brush attachment set to low suction to avoid pulling fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth to prevent staining; do not rub—this is space, not a playgroundSAAS—sorry, still talking about the rug.
- For stubborn smudges, use a mild detergent diluted in water and test a small area first.
- Dry thoroughly; moisture can shift backing or encourage mold growth in humid climates.

### Durability notes
The edge stitching on most high‑quality large rugs is critical to longevity. Expect reinforced edges and a tightly woven border to resist fraying during daily traffic. If you have energetic pets, consider a heavier underlay to reduce wear at high-traffic seams.
