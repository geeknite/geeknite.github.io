## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

### Cleaning tips

A quick wipe with a damp cloth after a night of gaming is usually enough to knock off dust and stray snack crumbs. For tougher smudges, a gentle soap solution works, followed by a thorough air dry. Do not soak the interior in water; the last thing you want is warped dividers or a soggy cardboard disaster. If you store the case in a humid environment, consider silica gel packs (which sometimes come in the box) to prevent any moisture from creeping into the card stacks. Regularly check the zipper and strap attachments; a tiny squeak now is a silent warning later that the case could lose its seal under stress. 

### Long-term durability

As with any gadget with moving parts, the real test is time. The case should stand up to repeated takeouts and restacks, which is the true test of a travel case. If you are a heavy traveler who plays several nights a week, you may want to inspect the dividers every few months and replace any elastic bands if they show signs of wear. In our testing, the case held up with minimal signs of fatigue after several weeks of frequent trips, which is a strong indicator that the design is robust enough for serious hobbyists.
