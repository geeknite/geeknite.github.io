## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity: Keeping the Shine Without Looking for a Repair Wizard

### Cleaning and Handling
Clean your Skyler with a soft microfiber cloth and mild cleaning solution designed for lenses and screens. Avoid aggressive solvents that might damage the coating on lenses or the frame finish. The more you treat the lenses as you would treat a camera lens, the better your visual overlays will feel, especially in bright outdoor light. A quick wipe after a coffee spill keeps the AR from becoming a visual reminder of your latte artistry.

### Storage and Travel Tips
When traveling, use the protective case that comes with most refurbished sets. Keep the unit away from extreme heat and direct sunlight for extended periods, as UV rays can degrade plastic components and reduce battery efficiency over time. If you carry the Skyler in a bag with other gadgets, use a soft sleeve to prevent minor scratches on the frame.

### Maintenance Schedule
Do a light firmware update check every few months and re-calibrate overlays if you notice drift in the alignment. This is a good habit for all wearables. In refurbished units, a gentle recalibration can make a surprising difference in how crisp the overlay lines appear and how well the camera focuses on your real-world surroundings.
