## What is Ubongo Brain Game?

Ubongo is a fast-paced puzzle game that challenges players to fit geometric shapes into a puzzle grid as quickly as possible. The game is designed to test your spatial reasoning and quick thinking skills. With vibrant artwork and engaging gameplay, Ubongo offers a thrilling experience that will keep you on your toes.

### The Basics

In Ubongo, players race against each other to solve puzzles using a set of geometric shapes. Each player has a puzzle board and a collection of pieces. The goal is to complete the puzzle before time runs out and collect gems as rewards. The player with the most gems at the end of the game wins.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Puzzle Boards**: Each player gets a board with different puzzles to solve.
- **Geometric Pieces**: Colorful shapes that players use to complete the puzzles.
- **Timer**: Adds a sense of urgency to the gameplay.
- **Gems**: Rewards for completing puzzles.

**Pro Tip**: If you find yourself struggling, try rotating the pieces. Sometimes the solution is just a twist away.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Ubongo is quick and easy. Distribute the puzzle boards and pieces to each player, set the timer, and you're ready to go. Now it's time to unleash your inner puzzle master.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players race to complete their puzzles using the geometric pieces. The timer adds a layer of pressure, making each round a thrilling challenge. Once the timer runs out, players check who completed their puzzles and collect gems accordingly.

### Strategy

Ubongo is all about quick thinking and spatial reasoning. The key to success is to stay calm and think logically. Don't be afraid to try different combinations and rotations of the pieces. Sometimes the most unconventional solution is the right one.
