## Gameplay

### How to Play

Players are dealt a hand of cards and take turns playing one card each, trying to avoid taking the last trick. The player who takes the last trick receives a point, and the game continues until someone reaches a predetermined number of points, resulting in their ultimate defeat. The player with the fewest points at the end is the winner.

### Special Cards

Some cards have special abilities that can change the flow of the game. These abilities add a layer of strategy and unpredictability, making each round a unique experience.
