## Setting Up Your Course

Setting up the course is as easy as pie, which is great because you might want to snack while playing. Simply arrange the modular tiles to create a unique layout, place the flags, and you're ready to tee off. The course can be as simple or as complex as you like, allowing for endless replayability.

## Gameplay Mechanics

The game mechanics are a blend of strategy, dexterity, and sheer luck (much like real golf!). Players take turns flicking their golf balls towards the hole, navigating hazards, and attempting trick shots.

### Flicking and Trick Shots

Flicking the ball is the heart of the game. This requires a gentle touch and a bit of finesse. Overzealous flickers beware—you might send your ball flying off the table!

### Hazards and Obstacles

Watch out for sand traps, water hazards, and the occasional squirrel. These obstacles add a layer of strategy, forcing players to think carefully about their moves.

### Special Abilities

Each player token has a special ability, from extra flick power to the squirrel's uncanny ability to find nuts (and maybe steal your ball).
