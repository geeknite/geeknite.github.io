## Setup and care: quick tips to stay in the good graces of your table
A little preparation goes a long way. The setup is straightforward, but a few best practices will keep your table performing at its best:

- Lay the table on a flat, clean surface to ensure the legs sit evenly. A small carpet ripple can throw off the alignment and make card reveals awkward.
- Check the leg locks before each game. A quick wiggle and a confirm-click will prevent mid-hand wobble that could derail a bluff.
- Keep a soft cloth handy for quick surface cleans. Felt holds dust and spill residue more than you’d think, so a light wipe between sessions is worth it.
- Use a table cover when not in use, especially if you’ve got pets or kids around. It protects the felt and keeps your table ready for the next round.

For those who want to go deep into maintenance, consider a quarterly check-up: verify leg alignment, clean the inner rails, and look for any signs of fabric wear around the chip racks. Small maintenance actions can extend the life of your table and prevent a big repair bill later on.
