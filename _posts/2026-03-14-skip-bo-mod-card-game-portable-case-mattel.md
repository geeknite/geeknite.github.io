## Maintenance and care

A plastic shell is easy to wipe, which is nice when you spill coffee or a stray chip on the surface during a late-night game. A damp cloth is usually enough. Do not soak the case; this is not a submarine. If you’re a heavy-handed player, you might end up with a scratch or two, but most cosmetic flaws can be buffed away with a bit of elbow grease and a microfiber cloth. Keep the cards in sleeve protectors if you play in areas with a lot of dust or pet hair; it will extend the life of the sleeves and reduce the friction that can occur when you shuffle.

Pro tip for collectors: store the case in a dry place away from direct sunlight. The colors on the dividers and the lid can fade over long periods if you leave the box in a bright window. A simple shelf away from the window is enough to preserve the aesthetics and the soul of your modular card fortress.
