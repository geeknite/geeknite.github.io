## Care tips and keeping the vibe alive

- Clean the cushions' covers according to the care label. Most outdoor fabrics love a gentle wash with mild detergent and a rinse that would embarrass a water jet.
- Wipe the wood with a dry or lightly damp cloth; avoid soaking the wood frame to reduce moisture absorption.
- Apply a protective wood sealant at the start of each season to maintain color and reduce surface cracking.
- When storing, keep cushions dry and off the ground. A simple mesh bag or breathable storage box works wonders.
- Consider rotating cushions and modular pieces for even wear and a balanced look across the season. Your set will thank you for equal attention, and so will your back.
