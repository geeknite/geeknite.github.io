## How to Play: A Quick Refresher
Dixit is a storytelling game with a simple core loop that somehow remains endlessly surprising. The 2021 Creative Edition does not modify the basic rules beyond clarifying how to weave the new art into clues. Here is the essence, in Geeknite friendly terms:

### Setup
- Each player receives a set of voting tokens in a color that will hopefully not be mistaken for a piece of fruit.
- A storyteller is chosen for the first round. The storyteller looks at their hand of cards and picks one card for which they can think of a one sentence clue that is both accurate and delightfully vague.
- The selected card is placed face down and then revealed to the table alongside the other players’ chosen cards.

### Round Structure
- All players, including the storyteller, choose a card from their hand that best matches the storyteller’s clue. The twist here is that players want to be clever enough to mislead others, but not so obvious that everyone guesses their card instead of the storyteller’s card.
- The chosen cards are shuffled and laid out face up. The storyteller earns a small bonus if at least one player guesses their card, but not all players. The other players earn one point for each correct guess of their own card and possibly a small trickle of points if their clue successfully misleads others.
- A tally on the storyteller board tracks each player’s points. The first player to reach a threshold wins, but let us be honest, the fun is in the journey and the art spoilers on the cards.

### Scoring Details
- Storyteller scoring: 3 points if at least one player guesses incorrectly and at least one player guesses correctly; otherwise 0 points. This creates delightful tension between revealing the clue and keeping it mysterious.
- Card players scoring: 1 point for each vote that matches a player card that is your own, and 3 points for guessing the storyteller’s card correctly.
- End-of-round dynamics are what keep the table talking long after the last card is picked. It is the subtle art of the wrong clue that often steals the show.

If you want to see a quick visual walkthrough of the flow, you can imagine the table turning into a tiny art gallery where everyone pretends to be a curator and then bursts into laughter when the clues become too on the nose. The important thing is to remember that Dixit is about storytelling and vibe first, scoring second. The 2021 Creative Edition reinforces this by presenting art that begs to be described in outrageous ways, and then judged by how well the group identifies the creator of the world you just described.

## Playtips: Getting the Most Out of Dixit 2021 Creative Edition
- Embrace the absurd: The beauty of Dixit is in the surprising. A clue that sounds offbeat but still plausible often yields the most interesting rounds.
- Read the room: The storyteller should pay attention to who is listening and who is not. If the table is silent after a clue, it might be time to pivot the tone in the next round.
- Use the cards as a shared language: Some players will connect certain images to personal memories. Let that happen. The more players find personal resonance in the cards, the more the game feels like a creative collaboration rather than a competition.
- Mix it up with light rules tweaks: If your group is new to Dixit, start with the standard rules for a few rounds, then add a twist such as awarding extra points for a clue that references a group in-joke. Small modifications can keep the night fresh without breaking the core experience.
- Create a storytelling corner: If you have a larger group, consider rotating storytellers and giving each storyteller a theme for a block of rounds. The variety in prompts can keep energy high without forcing participants to recreate the same narrative loop.
