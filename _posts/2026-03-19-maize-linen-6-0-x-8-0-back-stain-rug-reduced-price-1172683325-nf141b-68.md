## Cleaning and Maintenance
### Everyday Care
The general maintenance plan for this rug is simple: vacuum regularly, blot spills promptly, and avoid standing on the rug with greasy or inky hands. The maize/linen combination benefits from minimal routine care and a schedule that won’t make you dread cleaning day, which is the ultimate win for anyone who grew up in a cluttered gaming basement.

### Stain Management
Spills should be blotted—don’t rub, or you risk spreading the stain and fluffing the fibers. For common culprits like coffee, tea, and soda, a mild detergent solution with cold water is usually sufficient. The “back stain” feature implies a dye or treatment intended to help resist staining to some degree, but treat it as a helping hand, not a miracle. Stains with time and proper treatment stand a much better chance of fading rather than becoming a permanent badge of honor on your living space.

### Cleaning Schedule and Pro-Tips
A weekly light vacuum plus a quarterly deep clean should keep appearance consistent. If you spill something particularly robust (think red wine or a superhero smoothie), act fast and use an appropriate stain remover. Always test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure there’s no color bleed or shrinkage—especially important for the delicate maize tone. A fan and patience will help you avoid heat damage during any light steam clean attempts.
