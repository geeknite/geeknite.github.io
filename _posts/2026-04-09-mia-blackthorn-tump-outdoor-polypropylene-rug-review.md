## Cleaning and Maintenance
Maintenance is the thing with outdoor rugs, and the Tump rug makes it painless. Routine care is minimal: vacuum weekly, shake out dust, and spot clean as needed. Rinsing with a hose is fine, but make sure you allow it to dry fully to avoid any musty smell that could tempt a ghost to set up a residence.

This rug does not require special cleaners or heavy equipment. It is not a duty at all; it is a practical friend. If you enjoy the ritual of deep cleaning, you can set up a weekend project with a gentle detergent, a soft brush, and a little music from the era of cassette tapes for motivation.
