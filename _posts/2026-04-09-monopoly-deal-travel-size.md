## Tactics for Travel: How to Play Smart on the Go

Travel-friendly gaming is all about balancing speed, clarity, and subtle depth. Here are a few tactics that shine in the Travel Size edition:

- Early rent planning: The trick is not to hold too many money cards late in the round; instead accumulate enough to anchor a rent that could disrupt another player’s plans. The travel deck forces you to be decisive. It’s better to strike early than to wait for the perfect setup that may never come.
- Don’t hoard too long: Since the deck is smaller, you’ll run through it faster. Don’t be afraid to take calculated risk and bluff a little. A well-timed debt collection can tilt the balance even if you’re not sitting on a full property set yet.
