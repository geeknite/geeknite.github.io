The Lair Pro is happy with most standard sleeves, including something like the usual 100–120 micron thickness. It’s a trade-off worth noting, not a deal-breaker unless you’re optimizing a tournament loadout.

## How to use and maintenance tips

- Keep the interior dividers aligned to preserve the neat stacks of sleeves. Misaligned dividers can cause a surprising number of sleeved cards to shift when you tilt the box, which can feel like a small, unscheduled drama.
- Clean the exterior with a soft, dry cloth. The finish is designed to be low maintenance, but you’ll thank yourself for catching dust and minor smudges before they set in.
- Check the magnetic closure periodically. If you notice a quieter “pop” rather than a satisfying “click,” it might be time to realign or test the latch. Best practice is to treat the magnetic latch like a friend who respects your space: firm but gentle.
- Consider labeling dividers. If you’ve got a multi-deck strategy, labeling each compartment helps speed up your pre-game organization. It’s a small effort that pays off in a big, card-sorting way.
