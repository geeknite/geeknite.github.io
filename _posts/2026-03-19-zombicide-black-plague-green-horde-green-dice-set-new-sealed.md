## Maintenance, Storage, and Longevity

New dice surprise: you don’t actually need to baby them, but you should treat them with a little respect. Here are a few practical tips:

- Storage: Use a soft dice bag or a small compartment in your game box to prevent chipping or rattling against sharper objects. The green dice look amazing when displayed in a clear pouch that shows off the translucence; it’s basically cosplay for your dice.
- Cleaning: If you spill something on them, a quick wipe with a damp cloth should do the trick. Avoid harsh cleaners or solvents that could dull the gloss or affect the translucence. And please don’t lick them to test the flavor of your victory — that’s not a recommended tabletop hygiene protocol.
- Travel: If you’re a con attendee or a road warrior of table tops, check that your dice don’t rattle loose in luggage. A small bag inside a larger tote is enough to prevent the dice from becoming little green marbles on the road.
