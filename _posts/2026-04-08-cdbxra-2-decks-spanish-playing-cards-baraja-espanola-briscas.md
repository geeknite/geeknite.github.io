## How Brisca Works With Two Decks
Brisca is a trick-taking game from the Spanish and Italian families, often played with a 40-card deck. You deal four cards to each player (for a standard four-player game) and then use a trump mechanic to win tricks. With two decks, you might be tempted to try a double-rotation variant, where players draw from the top of each deck alternately. You can also choose to play with the two Decks simply as a backup in case a miscut card tries to moonlight as a spy and slips into the pack. The key advantage is: you rarely run out of cards mid-game, and you can continue playing if someone loses a card to a steam-powered chair leg incident.
