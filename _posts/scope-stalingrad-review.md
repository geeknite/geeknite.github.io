## Gameplay Basics

### What is SCOPE Stalingrad?
SCOPE Stalingrad is a tactical board game that recreates the infamous Battle of Stalingrad. Players take on the roles of commanding officers, leading their troops through perilous scenarios to achieve objectives and claim victory. It's a game of intense strategy, cunning maneuvers, and an occasional bit of luck. 

### Components
The game includes a detailed board representing the war-torn city of Stalingrad, miniature soldiers, tanks, and various cards that add depth to your strategies. The artwork is exceptionally realistic, bringing the dire atmosphere of the battle to life. 

### Turn Phases
Each turn consists of several phases:
1. **Planning Phase**: Players secretly plan their moves and allocate resources.
2. **Movement Phase**: Troops move according to their planned actions. Beware of ambushes!
3. **Combat Phase**: Engage in fierce battles, using the unique abilities of your units and tactical cards.
4. **Resolution Phase**: Assess the outcomes, manage resources, and prepare for the next turn.

## Strategy and Depth

### Tactical Choices
SCOPE Stalingrad is all about making tough decisions. Should you advance aggressively or hold a defensive position? Will you risk sending your elite units into a risky operation for a significant payoff? Every choice matters, and the fate of your troops hangs in the balance. 

### Resource Management
Managing resources effectively is crucial. You'll need to balance ammunition, fuel, and reinforcements while ensuring your units can maintain their fighting strength. Exhaust your supplies, and you'll be in deep trouble.
