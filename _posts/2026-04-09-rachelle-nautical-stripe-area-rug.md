## Maintenance and Cleaning Tips
Nobody enjoys cleaning, but the reality is rugs are a living fabric in a living space. Here are practical tips to keep the Rachelle Nautical Stripe Area Rug looking shipshape.

- Vacuum regularly to remove surface dirt. Use a gentle setting to avoid pushing dirt into the fibers.
- Wipe up spills immediately with a clean cloth. For stubborn spills, blot rather than rub to minimize fiber disruption.
- For stains, test a small hidden area with your chosen cleaner before applying widely. Avoid harsh solvents that can dull the stripe pattern.
- Consider a rug pad to reduce movement and improve longevity. A pad also buffers foot traffic noise and adds a touch of comfort.
- If your rug is suitable for machine washing or spot cleaning according to the care label, follow those guidelines. When in doubt, consult a professional cleaner to preserve the colors and stripe integrity.

## Styling Tips and Tricks
A rug is a stage for your furniture. Here are practical ideas to help you stage your space like a pro.

- Anchor seating with the rug. The rug should be large enough to sit under at least the front legs of the sofa and chairs, if possible. This creates a cohesive seating area that feels intentional rather than floating.
- Use cushions in complementary color families. A few navy cushions with white piping or a pop of sandy beige can echo the stripes without competing with them.
- Consider lighting that highlights the stripes without washing out the color. Warm lighting can enhance the cozy coastal vibe, while cooler lighting emphasizes the crisp, nautical contrast.
- Layer with a solid color throw rug or a textured textile if your space calls for more depth. Layering can add warmth and dimension without breaking the color scheme.
- If you are working with a small room, choose lighter wall colors to keep the space feeling open. The navy stripes will provide necessary contrast without making the room feel closed in.
