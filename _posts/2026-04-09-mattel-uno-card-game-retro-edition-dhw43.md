### Quick Start Guide: How to Jump In Fast
1) Shuffle the deck thoroughly. The Retro Edition lives and breathes on a good shuffle. 2) Each player is dealt seven cards. 3) The top card of the discard pile starts the game; the action starts there, not in the next turn. 4) On your turn, you must play a card that matches color or number, or you can play a Wild. 5) Use action cards to disrupt opponents, force draws, or flip the direction of play. 6) When you have one card left, shout Uno.
