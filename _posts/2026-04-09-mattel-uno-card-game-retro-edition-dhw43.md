### Quick Start Guide: How to Jump In Fast
1) Shuffle thoroughly. The Retro Edition thrives on a solid shuffle. 2) Deal seven cards to each player. 3) The top card of the discard pile starts the game; the action begins there. 4) On your turn, you must play a card that matches color or number, or you can play a Wild. 5) Use action cards to disrupt opponents, force draws, or flip direction. 6) When you have one card left, shout Uno. If you forget, a penalty may ensue depending on your house rules. 7) First player to reach a preset score wins. The Retro Edition keeps this flow while adding a nostalgic boost every time you flip a card.
