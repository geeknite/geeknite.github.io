## Care, storage, and longevity

Card stock longevity depends heavily on handling. Here are some pointers to help your 2011 blue replacement deck last longer than a couple of fizzy pop days:

- Store in a cool, dry place; avoid direct sunlight to reduce color fade and keep the numbers crisp.
- Use a small deck box or a zip pouch to protect from bending and to prevent the cards from drifting into the hallway of random toy parts.
- If your cards get sticky from juice spills or snacks, a gentle wipe with a dry cloth is usually enough. Avoid soaking them; cards do not enjoy baths nearly as much as you do.
- Consider a soft card sleeve for extra protection if you intend to use the deck for teaching games or for competitive play with slightly older kids who take their strategy seriously.
