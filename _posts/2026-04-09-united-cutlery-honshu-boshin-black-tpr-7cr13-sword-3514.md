## Maintenance and longevity
Budget blades deserve a bit of care to maximize their lifespan and keep the finish looking good for as long as possible.

### Cleaning and corrosion resistance
7Cr13 stainless steel offers moderate corrosion resistance for a budget blade. After display or cosplay use, wipe the blade with a dry cloth to remove moisture and oils. If you’re in a humid environment or you store the blade for a long period, a light coating of protective oil will help prevent spotting. Do not soak the blade in water, and avoid aggressive cleaning methods that could degrade the finish or the grip over time.

### Grip care
The TPR grip resists moisture but can still attract oils and fingerprints. Wipe with a damp cloth, then dry thoroughly. If the grip starts to feel sticky, a mild cleaning solution and a quick wipe will restore the tack without harming the material. Avoid solvents that could degrade the TPR material.

### Storage and display tips
Store the sword away from direct sunlight to prevent the coating from fading. If you have a display case, use anti-UV glass or curtain the case to preserve the finish. If you’re rotating display items, consider placing the Honshu Boshin in a safe corner where it won’t be knocked over during marathon streaming sessions or intense gaming marathons.
