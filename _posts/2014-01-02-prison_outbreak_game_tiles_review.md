## What is Prison Outbreak Game Tiles?

Prison Outbreak Game Tiles is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of survivors trying to escape a zombie-infested prison. With stunning artwork, cool miniatures, and a plethora of zombies to battle, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Prison Outbreak Game Tiles, players work together to complete missions and escape the prison while battling hordes of zombies. Each player controls a unique survivor, each with special abilities and cards. The goal is to complete missions, defeat zombies, and survive the outbreak.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of your favorite survivors and zombies. Seriously, they're so detailed you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: Each survivor has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations within the prison where battles and missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Those claws are the real supervillains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Prison Outbreak Game Tiles is like staging an apocalypse movie. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your mission. Now you're ready to begin your quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their survivor's abilities. You can move, attack, search for items, and more. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your teammates to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Prison Outbreak Game Tiles is a blend of strategy and luck. Each zombie has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have a zombie horde overrun you? Welcome to Prison Outbreak, where plans often go awry.

One of the best parts of Prison Outbreak Game Tiles is the diverse array of survivors and zombies. From the tough-as-nails ex-cop to the sneaky hacker, you can play as your favorite characters and face off against different types of zombies.

### Replayability

With multiple survivors, zombies, and scenarios, Prison Outbreak Game Tiles offers endless replayability. No two games are ever the same, ensuring that players will return to the game time and time again for new challenges and experiences.

### Pros and Cons

#### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: Captures the essence of a zombie apocalypse, providing an engaging and intense experience.
- **High-Quality Components**: Excellent artwork and sturdy miniatures enhance the overall gaming experience.
- **Strategic Depth**: Offers a balance of survival and strategy, catering to both casual and hardcore gamers.
- **Replayability**: Various missions and setups ensure that each game is unique and exciting.
- **Humorous Moments**: The game offers plenty of opportunities for laugh-out-loud moments.
