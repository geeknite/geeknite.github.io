## Care and maintenance

The kit is designed for regular use, not museum display. Proper care helps the chips maintain their feel and color for longer. Here are a few do's and don'ts:

- Do wipe chips with a dry cloth after a particularly sweaty session. Sweat and oils can dull the finish over time.
- Do keep the case closed when not in use to prevent dust from creeping into grooves.
- Don’t soak chips in water; they are not bath toys. A damp cloth is enough if you need a deeper clean.
- Don’t expose the set to direct sunlight for extended periods. Fading happens, and then your green chips look suspiciously like yellow ones after a summer on the porch.

If you want a weekend warrior upgrade later, there are ceramic-coated chip options that offer superb longevity, but they tend to be pricier. For most casual players, this 500 piece set hits the sweet spot between cost, feel, and durability.
