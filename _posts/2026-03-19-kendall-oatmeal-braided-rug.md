## Practical geek notes: care, care, care
### Cleaning hacks and routine maintenance
- Vacuum regularly with a low power setting to preserve fiber integrity.
- Blot spills immediately; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber fraying.
- For stubborn stains, test a mild detergent on a hidden patch, then treat with a gentle brush.
- Consider a rug pad to reduce movement and to add a touch of prime-nerd stability to your floor plan.

### Troubleshooting common scenarios
- Edge curling: a couple of strategic rug grips or a heat-free weight on the corners for a day can help reset the edges.
- Fading under sunlight: rotate the rug periodically to even out sun exposure if your room has large south-facing windows.
- Odor from new rug: air out for a day or two before heavy use; if needed, a light washing can help but avoid saturating the fibers.

### Pro tips for alignment and layering
Layer Kendall with a smaller geometric rug to create an underfoot color block that plays well with your wall art. When you layer, ensure the top rug does not overwhelm the braided texture; let Kendall do the heavy lifting in the background while the top rug adds a pop of color or pattern. This approach is a sweet spot for geeks who want their decor to look intentionally curated rather than chaotic.
