## The Setup: A Quick Start Guide
Like many party games, setup is designed to be fast. You’ll separate Exploding Kitten cards, Defuse cards, and the rest, then shuffle. The difference here is how you prepare the NSFW flavor tokens—call them dares, jokes, and roast prompts—that you’ll use in place of certain standard actions.

- Remove a subset of cards if your group wants a shorter game (common adjustment).
- Shuffle the NSFW-specific cards into the main deck.
- Deal a set of Defuse cards to each player (the standard distribution remains familiar for folks who’ve played the base game).
- The Exploding Kitten cards are shuffled into the deck, and the game begins once the first player draws.

If you’re playing with a mixed-age group, consider creating a policy card that clarifies what counts as “off-limits” jokes. A simple no-kink-shaming policy can help, but the ultimate power to veto remains with the host. The key is to keep it fun, not uncomfortable.

## Gameplay Mechanics: What Actually Happens on the Table
The NSFW edition preserves the backbone of Exploding Kittens: draw cards until you explode—or you don’t. Defuse cards can save you. The rest of the deck contains cards that let you peek, skip, attack, shuffle, or downright boop other players into chaos.

### Turn Flow
- On your turn, you can play as many cards as you want from your hand, or you can draw one card from the deck.
- If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you’re not necessarily out—unless you lack a Defuse card. Defuse cards act as a one-time life saver that lets you survive the draw by placing the kitten back into the deck (in a location of your choosing, after you reveal the draw). In the NSFW edition, there are sometimes additional interaction cards that modify or threaten the Defuse mechanic—intentionally adding tension.
- The “boop” mechanic (you can boop another player to force them to draw) remains a core engine of chaos; NSFW adds spicy flavor options here, including dares that must be completed or replaced by a special card.

The balance of risk and reward remains the heartbeat of the game. You want to push your luck just enough to force others into dangerous choices, but you don’t want to be the one stuck with the final, dramatic loss because your hand collapsed under a chorus of ridiculous dares.

### The NSFW Cards: A Taste of Impertinence
The NSFW content is not gratuitous; it’s designed as a joke machine tuned to keep energy high. The cards typically offer one of a few kinds of actions:
- Dare prompts that require players to perform a short, silly action (e.g., a quick improv line, a goofy accent, or a goofy wiggle). These are optional or required depending on how you want the night to go.
- Roasts and zingers that players deliver to the person on their left. This is where the confident crowd shines and the shy crowd may cringe with delight.
- Witty punishments: if you’re targeted by a card, you get a playful penalty that’s usually reversible—that’s the sugar in this game’s tea.

If you’re not into public speaking or cringe humor, you can switch to a “mature-yet-subtle” setting where dares stay inside the realm of silly and less personal. The beauty of this edition is in its flexibility; you can dial the flavor up or down, depending on how.boisterous your party wants to be.
