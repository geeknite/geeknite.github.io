## Features

One of the standout features of the Forerunner 945 is its training load focus. This feature sorts your recent training history into different categories based on the structure and intensity of each workout. It's a great tool for athletes looking to balance their training and avoid over or underdoing it.

The Forerunner 945 also excels in the area of health monitoring. It includes a Pulse Ox sensor, which measures your body's oxygen absorption, a Body Battery energy monitor that helps optimize your workout schedules, and a menstrual cycle tracker. It's like having a personal coach, doctor, and cheerleader all on your wrist!
