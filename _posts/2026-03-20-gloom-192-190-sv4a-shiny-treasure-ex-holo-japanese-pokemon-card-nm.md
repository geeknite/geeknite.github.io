## How to Care for Your NM Gloom
### Handling Tips
Always handle with clean hands or gloves. The holo surface can smudge, and the last thing you want is a fingerprint smeared across the sunburst. Hold by the edges, avoiding pressure on the center art area. If you must handle the card fully, cradle it gently and avoid flexing the surface where the holo is most reflective.

### Storage Solutions
Invest in acid-free sleeves, a rigid top loader, and a protective binder with UV filtering. Direct sunlight is the enemy of any holo card, as is long-term exposure to harsh indoor lighting. If you display this card, rotate it periodically so you can enjoy the shimmer from different angles without keeping it in a constant state of brilliance that could lead to uneven wear.

### Display Staging and Presentation
Consider a small display with glass that’s anti-glare and a light source that doesn’t overpower the card’s colors. A subtle stand or a page in a ring binder can show off the holo’s micro-edges without requiring you to tilt the card at an unnatural angle to notice the shimmer.
