## Maintenance and care
Maintenance for a blade like UC3394 centers on preserving blade integrity and visual appeal. Here are some practical tips:

- Wipe down after use to remove fingerprints and moisture; a light coat of oil helps prevent corrosion.
- Keep the leather sheath dry and conditioned to retain its shape and flexibility.
- If you notice micro-scratches on the blade surface, a light polishing with a non-abrasive cloth can keep the finish looking sharp for display photos.
- Store in a dry, well-ventilated area; avoid crowded display cases that press the blade against metal shelf edges or other hardware.

### Cleaning tips
- Use a microfiber cloth and synthetic oil to keep the finish bright
- Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that could mar the surface
- Dry thoroughly before placing back in the sheath to prevent moisture buildup
