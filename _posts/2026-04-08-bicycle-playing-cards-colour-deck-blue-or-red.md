## Maintenance, care, and durability tips

- Keep away from extreme humidity. Card stock swells and softens; your color on the back will remain vibrant, but the overall feel may be compromised.
- Avoid moisture, heat, and prolonged sunlight, which can cause fading over months. If you’re a sun-chaser, keep the Colour Decks in a protective case when not in use.
- Clean hands before dealing to preserve the finish. Grit and oils can micro-scratch the surface, and you don’t want a rough back to interfere with your smooth reveals.
- Store decks in a dry, cool place. If you’re a serious collector, consider a small cabinet that keeps each deck’s back pattern pristine and avoids rubbing between two different colors.

In short, treat the Colour Decks like valuable props in a narrative you tell with your hands. The more you care for them, the more they’ll care for your tricks and games in return.
