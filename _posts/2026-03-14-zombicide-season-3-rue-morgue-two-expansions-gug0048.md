## H2: Gameplay Deep Dive

### H3: Core mechanics in Rue Morgue

### H3: How the campaigns weave through the game
The campaign structure remains the star in Rue Morgue. Each mission pushes you toward an objective, whether it is to secure an artifact, escort a survivor, or hold a chokepoint long enough to unlock the next chapter. The expansions give you additional chapters that slot into the overarching arc, lengthening your play sessions and increasing the stakes each time. You might think, more content means more monotony; counterpoint: it does not. The expansions add novelty without letting the base cadence slip into repetitive fatigue.

### H3: Difficulty curve and pacing
Zombicide games are built on a delicate balance: be strong enough to survive but not so strong that the challenge evaporates. Rue Morgue leans into the tension in a fairly satisfying way. The base game offers a meaningful challenge, and the expansions push you toward riskier moves, occasionally forcing you to make moral choices about which survivors to preserve or abandon in a given mission. The tempo can swing wildly turn to turn depending on how you manage your resources and how you handle the spawns. It is not a game that rewards headlong rushing; it rewards strategic zoning and smart weapon deployment.
