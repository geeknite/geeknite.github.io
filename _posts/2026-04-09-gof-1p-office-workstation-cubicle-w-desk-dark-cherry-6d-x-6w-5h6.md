No product is perfect in every configuration. Here are a few caveats you might want to consider before you commit:

- If you’re planning a very tall standing desk setup, ensure you measure clearance. The GOF 1P’s height options are flexible, but you may need to adjust monitor arms or CPU placement to avoid collisions with overhead shelves.
- Cable management is helpful, but if you have an army of peripherals, you may want to invest in additional cable sleeves or zip ties to prevent the spaghetti effect from turning into a gelatinous mass behind your desk.
