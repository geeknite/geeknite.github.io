The heart of the matter is playability. A good foosball table should deliver fast, precise ball control with the rods offering a satisfying resistance and return. The VEVOR 121 cm aims for that balance: the rods should move smoothly, with little friction, and the ball should travel predictably. ## Maintenance and care
### Keeping it dialed in
Maintenance is the unsung hero of long-term foosball joy. Wipe the playing surface after use to remove fingerprints and dust. Check screws and legs periodically to prevent any wobble from turning into a weekend drama. Lubricate the rods with a light silicone-based lubricant if you notice any stiffness. Avoid petroleum-based products that can degrade the plastic components over time. Consider a cover when not in use to protect the laminated surface from sunlight, pet hair, and the occasional stray pizza grease fingerprint.

### Common issues and quick fixes
- Stiff rods: loosen the tension slightly and re-lubricate.
- Misaligned players: re-align the players on the rods and ensure the end caps seat properly.
- Uneven bounce on the ball: verify the playing surface is clean and free of warps; if the table is on an uneven floor, re-leveling is your friend.
- Scoring not responding: check the mechanism for obstructions and reset if needed.
