## How to play with Bakugo energy

UNO Flip remains a two sided deck with red and blue sides. The Bakugo edition adds a handful of action cards that can cause a ribbon of chaos on the table. Here are a few practical tips:

- Start slow if you are new to UNO Flip, especially with special cards that trigger flips. 
- When you flip to the blue side, plan your last few moves carefully since a single flip can shift the entire game.
- Use the Bakugo themed cards to add a moment of drama, but keep your voice down so you do not scare the pets.
