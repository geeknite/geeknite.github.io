### Ergonomics: height and monitor alignment
Typical desktop height is 28–30 inches from the floor. If you’re tall, you’ll want to adjust your chair to achieve a 90-degree elbow angle and keep your wrists neutral while typing. A monitor stand or VESA arms are a good companion to this desk, ensuring that your eyes are level with the top third of your monitors to minimize neck strain. If you’re sharing this desk, you’ll want to ensure monitor stands don’t crash into each other during a heated coding session or a heated “who left the mug of coffee here” moment. A well-positioned keyboard tray can be a nice addition if you want the main surface free for documents and doodles.
