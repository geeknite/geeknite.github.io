## Packaging, care, and display tips

A sealed deck is a small investment in time and display. If you plan to store Mutants Unite 55 long term, handle the packaging with care. Here are some practical tips that collectors often use:

- Store the deck away from direct sunlight. UV exposure can affect card stock and the wrapper print over time.
- Keep the deck at a moderate temperature and away from moisture. This helps prevent warping or any potential glue deterioration around the wrapper.
- Consider a proper display area that keeps the deck upright and free from dust. A dedicated display shelf or a glass case helps preserve the sealed condition and shows off the art without the risk of fingerprints.
- If you ever decide to open the deck, do so with patience and a clean desktop. A quiet, well-lit space is perfect for unsealing a retro treasure without damaging the packaging or the cards inside.
