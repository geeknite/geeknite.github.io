## Design, Materials, and Build Quality

## Assembly and Maintenance
### Quick Start Guide
1. Layout all pieces and bolts.
2. Attach the table legs to the table top (or central base, depending on the model).
3. Secure chair legs and attach cushions.
4. Tighten all bolts, but not so tight you strip something precious.
5. Place cushions, wipe down, and UV-protect those cushions with a sunblock of your choosing—metaphorically, not literally.

The assembly is straightforward but not instantaneous. If you’re a DIY skeptic, plan for a weekend afternoon and a playlist of your choice. The included tools are adequate, and the manuals typically offer diagrams that make you feel like you’re learning a new puzzle game rather than solving a furniture riddle.

### Maintenance Hacks
- Regularly wipe the frame with a damp cloth and mild soap to prevent buildup of dirt and pollen.
- Remove cushion covers for washing; air-dry fully before reinstalling.
- Store cushions indoors during heavy rain or winter months if you want to preserve color and softness.
- Use coasters and placemats to protect the glass top from scratches during enthusiastic culinary experiments.

External maintenance tips: keep a little bottle of protective spray for the wicker that says “Protects the weave and keeps the color rich.” This is not a miracle spray, but it helps reduce dryness and cracking over time.
