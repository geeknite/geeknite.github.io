### Durability and care basics
Wool’s natural resilience is your friend here. Vacuum regularly with a low-suction setting or a brush attachment designed for delicate fibers. If you have pets or a long-haired companion, a quick weekly grooming is a good idea to minimize shedding on the surface. Spills should be blotted—don’t rub—to protect the nap. For stubborn stains, test a gentle wool-safe cleaner in an inconspicuous area first. Always follow the care guidelines provided by the retailer to maintain the rug’s integrity over the years.

External care tips: wool rugs benefit from professional cleaning roughly every 12-18 months in heavy-traffic rooms, depending on use. If you want a nerdy anchor, Woolmark and other textile associations offer care guidelines and standards you can skim like a science-fiction manual for optimal fiber longevity.

## Customization in any size: a deeper dive
### Why size matters
Rugs are not just floor mats; they’re room-proportional statements. If a rug is too small, the furniture looks floating and disjointed; if too large, the room can feel crowded. Custom sizing makes it possible to define zones, create breathing space, and protect high-traffic areas with a soft landing. The Ivory Pebble’s customization option lets you tailor the scale for your sofa’s length, coffee table footprint, or to define a reading nook. It’s the difference between a design accident and a curated scene.

### How the process works
- Start with room measurements: length, width, and clearance around furniture.
- Choose the base rug size that most closely matches your needs.
- Confirm edge finish and color consistency if your order involves multiple pieces.
- Preview the layout in your space plan and adjust as necessary.
- Confirm the delivery and installation timeline.

This process is smooth enough that you can almost imagine a tiny, polite robot courier delivering your rug in a perfect-feet-first landing.

### Pros and potential caveats
Pros:
- Perfect fit for awkward spaces.
- Seamless coordination with modern, warm, or eclectic interiors.
- Durable wool that handles daily life with a touch of luxury.

Caveats:
- Custom sizes may take longer to manufacture and ship than standard offerings.

## Care and maintenance essentials
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting.
- Blot spills immediately; avoid rubbing.
- Rotate the rug every 3-6 months to ensure even wear in high-traffic zones.
- Consider professional cleaning every 12-18 months for heavy-use rooms.
- Protect from direct sunlight to minimize ivory-yellowing over long periods (still beautiful, just a tad slower to age gracefully).

For ongoing care knowledge, you can consult general wool care resources like [Wool Care Basics](https://www.woolmark.com/) and tailor tips from your rug retailer. The key is consistency: a little upkeep goes a long way toward preserving the rug’s luxury appeal.
