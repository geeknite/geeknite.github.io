## What is Tearable Quest?

**Tearable Quest** is a unique and innovative board game where players must complete quests by tearing pieces of paper. Yes, you read that right—tearing paper. It's like arts and crafts met a strategic board game and had a hilariously chaotic baby.

### The Basics

In **Tearable Quest**, players embark on various quests that require them to tear pieces of paper in specific ways. Each quest has its own set of rules and objectives, making every game session different and entertaining. The game combines elements of strategy, dexterity, and sheer luck, resulting in an unpredictable and laughter-filled experience.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Quest Cards**: Each card outlines a unique quest and the tearing rules associated with it.
- **Paper Sheets**: Specially designed sheets of paper that players must tear to complete quests.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress and score points.

**Pro Tip**: Hide your cat. Trust me, you don't want a furry intruder tearing your precious quest papers.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up **Tearable Quest** is as straightforward as it gets. Lay out the quest cards, distribute the paper sheets, and grab your tokens. Now you're ready to embark on your tearable adventure.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw quest cards and attempt to complete the tearing tasks outlined on them. You can move, tear, and laugh hysterically as your carefully planned tear goes hilariously wrong. The game encourages creativity and thinking outside the box (or outside the paper).

### Scoring

Scoring in **Tearable Quest** is based on the accuracy and creativity of your tears. Completing a quest perfectly will earn you maximum points, whereas a tear gone awry might still earn you sympathy points for effort (and laughs).
