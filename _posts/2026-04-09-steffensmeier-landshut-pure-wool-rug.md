## Care and Maintenance
Caring for wool requires tenderness, not a power tool.

### Cleaning Scenarios
- Daily care: vacuum with a brush attachment on low suction; rotate every 3–4 months
- Spill handling: blot, do not rub; mild blotting with water works for most coffee or wine mishaps
- Deep cleaning: professional wool cleaning every 1–2 years depending on traffic and pets

### Longevity Tips
- Use rug pads to prevent movement and wear
- Rotate periodically to avoid wear patterns in heavy traffic zones
- Remove any pet hair with a gentle brush or a vacuum designed for pet hair
