## Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping It Fresh
### Cleaning Tips
- Wipe down the wicker with a soft cloth and mild soapy water; avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the wicker’s finish.
- Remove cushion covers and machine wash on gentle cycle; air-dry thoroughly to prevent mildew.
- Check the screws and joints periodically; a loose bolt can quickly turn your outdoor lounge into a “malsa-drawn” work of aerodynamic art (aka an accidental sculpture).

### Seasonal Storage
If you live in a climate with sharp winters, consider covering the set with a breathable outdoor cover or storing cushions in a dry area to extend life. A cover with vents helps prevent moisture buildup, which is basically a love letter to mold that you don’t want on your furniture. A little routine goes a long way: clean, dry, cover, rotate cushions if you store them for extended periods, and you’ll be rewarded with many more sunny afternoons of lounging without rearranging broken furniture in the spring.
