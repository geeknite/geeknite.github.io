## The Basics

### What is Imperial Tide?

Imperial Tide is a strategic board game that simulates the entirety of World War I. Players take on the roles of the Central Powers or the Allies, maneuvering their forces across the globe to achieve military and political objectives. The game covers the years 1914-1918 and aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the Great War.

### Components

The game comes with:

- A beautifully detailed game board depicting the various theaters of the war
- High-quality counters representing army units, fleets, and resources
- Event cards that add historical flavor and strategic options
- A rulebook that's thicker than most history textbooks (but don't worry, it's surprisingly readable!)

**Pro Tip**: Keep your pet hamster away from the counters. They're small and tempting for tiny paws.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is relatively straightforward. Place the board on a flat surface, distribute the counters, and shuffle the event cards. Each player starts with a set number of forces and resources, depending on their chosen faction.

### Turn Structure

Each turn in Imperial Tide represents a season of the war and consists of several phases:

1. **Reinforcement Phase**: Players receive new units and resources based on their current holdings.
2. **Movement Phase**: Maneuver your forces across the board to engage the enemy or secure strategic locations.
3. **Combat Phase**: Resolve battles using dice rolls and modifiers.
4. **Event Phase**: Draw and play event cards that can provide bonuses or hinder your opponent.

### Winning and Losing

Victory in Imperial Tide is achieved by meeting specific military and political objectives. These can vary from capturing key cities to maintaining control of certain regions. The game ends in 1918, and the player with the most victory points wins.

**Pro Tip**: Always keep an eye on your supply lines. Running out of resources in the middle of a major offensive is a surefire way to lose the game (and your sanity).
