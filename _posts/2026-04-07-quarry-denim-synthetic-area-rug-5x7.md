## Cleaning and Maintenance
Maintenance is where rugs either become a friend for years or a forever project. Here’s a practical run-through of how Quarry Denim behaves in a typical home environment:

- Vacuum regularly with a standard vacuum that has a beater bar. A few passes will lift surface dirt without pulling loose fibers.
- For spills, blot with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can embed the stain deeper. Mild soap and water usually do the trick for most common spills.
- For tougher stains, a gentle rug-safe cleaner works well. Always perform a spot test in a small area first.
- Deep cleaning: most synthetic rugs can be steam-cleaned ormachine-washed if the care label allows. If machine washing is recommended, follow the manufacturer’s guidelines; do not overstuff the machine, and ensure you use a cold water cycle with a mild detergent.
- Drying: air-dry flat. Avoid exposing to direct heat like a radiator or sun-drenched balcony for extended periods, which can fade colors.

In practical tests, the rug handles coffee with grace, as long as you catch it promptly. Red wine requires a bit more care, often needing a gentle cleaner and a light blot. The rug’s design helps camouflage minor spills thanks to denims’ natural variance, but you’ll want to maintain a quick cleanup habit to keep it looking its best.
