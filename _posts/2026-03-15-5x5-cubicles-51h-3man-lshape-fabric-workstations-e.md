## Setup, Assembly, and First Impressions

The unboxing experience for a three-man L Shape cubicle is part puzzle, part gym workout, and part ceremonial unwrapping of a product that promises to help you feel like you have your own productivity culture. ## Maintenance and Cleaning

Fabric panels are straightforward to vacuum and spot clean. Use a mild fabric cleaner and avoid aggressive solvents that could degrade the texture or color. A routine wipe-down of panels with a damp microfiber cloth keeps the space looking crisp, while the metal frames appreciate a quick wipe with a non-abrasive cleaner to avoid any risk of corrosion. If you share your space with coffee-fueled humans, you will want to schedule periodical deep cleans to maintain that showroom vibe, or at least a vibe that says we care about staying professional and coffee-stain free.
