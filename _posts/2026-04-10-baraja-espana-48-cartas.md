- Sota (Jack), Caballo (Knight), Rey (King): The classic trio. In most traditional games, these face cards are powerful movers, sometimes affecting the score or dictating who has the last laugh. They are the drama queens of the deck, always ready to cameo in the final trick and demand an ovation from the audience (your fellow players).
