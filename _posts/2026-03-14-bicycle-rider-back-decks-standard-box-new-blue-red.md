## Care and maintenance

A little care goes a long way with playing cards. Here are a few quick tips to extend the life of your two decks:

- Store in a cool, dry place. Excess humidity can cause warping and unwanted stickiness.
- Avoid exposure to direct sunlight for extended periods, which can fade color and degrade the finish over time.
- Use a card clip or a simple case when transporting to gigs or events—this minimizes edge wear and helps prevent accidental bends.
- Clean hands before handling to reduce oils and residue that can degrade grip and feel.

If you’re older school and want to preserve the longevity of your decks, a basic card stand or display sleeve can help keep them pristine between sessions.
