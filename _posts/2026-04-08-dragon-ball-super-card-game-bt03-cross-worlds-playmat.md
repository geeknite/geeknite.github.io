## Maintenance and Longevity

Long-term maintenance is one of those things you don’t think about until you realize you’ve spent six weeks blaming coffee spills for your life total miscounts. The Cross Worlds Playmat can handle the usual wear and tear better than some cheaper fabrics, but it’s not invincible. Here are a few tips to maximize longevity:

- Clean regularly: A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth to remove dust and fingerprints can keep the surface looking sharp and the zones crisp.
- Use sleeves and a deck box: While the mat is sturdy, consistent friction from bare card edges over thousands of moves can dull the surface slightly. Sleeves and a deck box reduce the direct contact between card edges and the mat.
- Keep away from direct sunlight: UV exposure can dim the art over time. If you have a sunny game store or a bright gaming corner, rotate mats or store them in a shade-protected area when not in use.
- Store rolled or flat: If you don’t want to crease the mat, lying it flat is ideal. If you need to roll it, keep it loose and avoid extreme curling on the ends. Basic care yields best results for long-term playability.

As for the artwork, the print is durable enough for years of play, and the colors stay vibrant with basic care. If you’re worried about colorfastness, avoid using strong solvents or bleach on or near the mat. The last thing you want is to discover your Cross Worlds mat now looks like a vintage poster from a sci-fi museum.
