## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity
### Cleaning Routine
- Vacuum regularly with a beater bar on low or off to prevent tangling in the pile.
- For spills, blot rather than rub with a clean, dry cloth. If a stain persists, follow the manufacturers suggested detergent solution.
- Occasional professional cleaning helps maintain color and pile height, especially in high traffic zones.

### Stain Resistance and Practical Tips
Stain resistance is a feature touted in many synthetic blends. It is not invincibility, but it is good enough to prevent a red wine incident from becoming a crime scene worth a documentary.
