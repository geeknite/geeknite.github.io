## Maintenance and care guide

To maximize longevity, consider the following guidelines:

- Keep away from direct heat and sunlight; high temperatures can accelerate wear on plastic components.
- Wipe components with a dry microfiber cloth after sessions to maintain the finish and prevent fingerprints from becoming permanent artwork on the chips.
- Rotate the chips occasionally to ensure even wear across denominations; it will keep the color distinctions crisp and easier to spot in the dark.
- Store light, dry, and flat; this helps prevent pressure-induced warping and avoids accidental bending of the dealer button.

These steps aren’t glamorous, but they’re the difference between a set that lasts you years and a set that looks tired after a single season.
