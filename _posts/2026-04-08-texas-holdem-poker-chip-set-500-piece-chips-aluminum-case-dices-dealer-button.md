## Care and Maintenance
A kit this nice deserves a little love. Here are practical tips to keep your chips bright and your case unbroken:

- Wipe chips with a soft cloth after a night of play to remove oils from hands and snacks from the snack table.
- Avoid exposing the chips to direct heat or sunlight for extended periods, which can fade inlays or warp edges.
- Clean the aluminum case with a mild detergent and a dry microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that could mar the finish.
- Store the foam insert with the chips in a dry environment to prevent moisture damage and musty dice.
- If the dice is a keepsake part of your game-night routine, treat it like a prop: clean and sanitize before and after use to prevent any “gamer sneeze” incidents.
