## Introduction

Hello, fellow dungeon crawlers! Have you ever wondered what it would be like to delve into the depths of a mysterious dungeon, facing off against fearsome monsters and uncovering hidden treasures? ## The Basics of Karak

Nuevos Heroes: Karak is a tile-based dungeon crawler that combines strategy, exploration, and a dash of luck. Designed for 2-5 players, this game promises endless fun as you navigate through the labyrinthine corridors of Karak.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Tiles**: Modular dungeon tiles that create a new layout with each game, ensuring replayability.
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures of heroes and monsters. They might be small, but they pack a punch!
- **Cards**: Treasure, monster, and hero cards that dictate the flow of the game.
- **Dice**: Used for combat and determining outcomes. Get ready to roll!
- **Tokens**: Track health, magic, and other essential stats.

**Pro Tip**: Keep snacks handy. Dungeon crawling can be hungry work.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Karak is like assembling a jigsaw puzzle with a twist. Lay out the dungeon tiles, place your hero miniatures, shuffle the cards, and get ready to embark on an epic adventure.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players explore the dungeon by revealing new tiles, battling monsters, and collecting treasures. Actions include moving, attacking, and using special abilities. Cooperation is key, but don't forget to watch your back!

### Combat

Combat in Karak is simple yet strategic. Roll the dice to determine your attack and defense, and use hero abilities to gain an edge. Will you defeat the dragon or end up as its lunch? Only the dice know!

## The Humorous Side of Karak

One of the best parts of Karak is the humor woven into the gameplay. From the quirky hero abilities to the absurd monster encounters, there's never a dull moment.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our wizard hero tried to cast a spell to defeat a goblin but ended up accidentally turning himself into a frog. Ribbit!
