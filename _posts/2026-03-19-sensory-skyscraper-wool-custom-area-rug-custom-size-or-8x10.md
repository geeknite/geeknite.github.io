## Maintenance and care
Maintenance matters as much as the initial wow factor. The rug’s wool composition and pile height mean a regular care routine is worth investing in to preserve texture and color. Here are practical care notes that balance realism with long-term beauty:

- Vacuum regularly with a non-beater head to avoid pulling fibers. A slow, methodical pass across the surface keeps the pile looking fresh rather than frizzing out of shape.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear. This is especially important in rooms where furniture sits on only a portion of the rug; rotation helps keep the plush appearance uniform.
- Address spills promptly. Blot with a clean cloth rather than rubbing. For stubborn stains, refer to a wool-friendly cleaning guide and test any method on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Consider a rug pad. A high-quality pad not only stops slipping but also adds another layer of cushion and helps protect the underside from wear. In a city apartment, a pad can significantly improve the living experience by dampening footsteps and improving acoustics.

With proper care, the Sensory Skyscraper Wool Rug can sustain its plush feel for years. It’s not a disposable product; it’s a long-term design investment that should become a staple in the room—and yes, you will end up telling guests that your rug is basically the furniture equivalent of a high-rise condo.

## Maintenance myths debunked
Myth: Wool rugs require delicate cleaning rituals that involve a branded cleaning service and a small loan.
Reality: Regular vacuuming, spot cleaning, and a good rug pad go a long way. Wool fibers enjoy movement in moderation, so a gentle pace with your vacuum is better than a brute-force assault. If you live in a high-humidity zone or have pets, consult a local rug-care specialist for a seasonally appropriate plan. The key is consistent care rather than extraordinary measures.

Myth: The bigger the rug, the more you must pay in maintenance.
Reality: The cost of maintenance is more tied to fiber choice and the cleaning method than to the rug’s size. A well-made wool rug with periodic professional cleaning can outperform cheaper alternatives in the long run because it retains its shape and color beautifully.
