## Safety and handling
Treat Li-ion cells with care. Do not puncture or expose to heat beyond safe levels. Use a proper charger designed for Li-ion packs in the 4.2 V range. Store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight. The pack should be charged at a rate that does not exceed the recommended current.

## Real world usage
### Installation steps
1. Power down your Velux system and disconnect the control module from the battery harness.
2. Remove the old battery pack from its housing.
3. Align the new CS-VRA100SL so that the connector lines up with the harness.
4. Reconnect and secure the battery using the existing housing screws or mounting bracket.
5. Use the original charger for a gentle initial charge until the system recognizes the new pack.
6. Run a test cycle to ensure proper movement and response.

### Charging and maintenance
With Li-ion packs, try to avoid leaving the battery in a deeply discharged state for long. If you travel or will be away for weeks, consider a smart charger with a safe float voltage. Expect the first few cycles to optimize capacity as the chemistry settles in with your device.

### Performance in different climates
In normal indoor climates you will usually see reliable run times, often months between charges depending on shutter use. In hot climates Li-ion capacity can degrade faster, so expect a bit more frequent top ups in summer. In cold environments charging performance can dip a bit but shutter operation remains robust.
