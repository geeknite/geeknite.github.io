### Maintenance and Care Tips
A finish like Amour Black rewards gentle care. Regular dusting with a microfiber cloth will keep the oak legs looking as if they just emerged from a showroom. For fabric, check the care label; if the fabric is stain-resistant, a quick spot clean with water and a mild detergent should do. Avoid harsh chemicals that could dull the finish or compromise the upholstery. If you spill red wine on the fabric, treat it promptly with a fabric-safe stain remover and blot rather than rub. For the wood part, a light wax application every six months will help preserve the finish and keep the legs from looking sleepy after a weekend of heavy use. Think of it as giving the chair a spa day, minus cucumber slices on its eyes.
