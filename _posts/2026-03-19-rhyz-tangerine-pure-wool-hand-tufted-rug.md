## Maintenance and Cleaning Tips

### Routine care

Regular vacuuming with a gentle setting keeps the wool nap vibrant. Rotate the rug occasionally to prevent uneven wear in high traffic areas. Use a soft brush to lift any surface dirt from the nap and avoid aggressive scrubbing. Wool is tough but persistent abrasion can dull the fibers.

### Stains and quick fixes

Spills should be blotted not rubbed. For spills like coffee or juice use a damp cloth with a wool safe cleaner working from the outside toward the center to prevent spreading. For oil based stains you may need a stain remover designed for wool test in a hidden area first to ensure colorfastness.

### Freshening up between cleanings

A quick vacuum and a gentle shake if you can pick up a portion can refresh the nap. If the room smells a bit stale a light air out and a few hours of sun without direct heat on the wool helps maintain freshness. Do not leave the rug in direct intense sun all day color can fade over time even for high quality wool.
