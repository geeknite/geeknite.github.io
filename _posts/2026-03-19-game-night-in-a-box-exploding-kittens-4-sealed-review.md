### Setup Tips and Quick Strategies
- For Exploding Kittens, always count your Defuse cards. They’re rare life rafts, and you’ll need them when the tension spikes. Keep Defuses in a safe pocket, and never show your Defuses early unless you’re theatrically confident you’ll survive the next two turns.
- For each sealed game, skim the quick-start rules. If a game demands a central sheet for remembering power interactions, read it aloud with dramatic pauses to maximize the audience. Remember: your group is here for the social experience as much as the strategic edge.
- Use a timer. Five-minute rounds with a countdown are perfect for ensuring the session doesn’t drift into the twilight zone where nobody knows whose turn it is anymore.
