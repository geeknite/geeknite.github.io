## Care, cleaning, and carelessness management
Maintenance matters, especially with a rug that has a reduced price due to a flaw. Here’s a plan to keep the AQUA rug looking pleasant without turning your weekend into a deep dive into fabric science:

- Vacuum regularly: Use a vacuum with adjustable height to avoid snagging the fibers. If you have a pet with a dramatic shedding habit, consider a handheld attachment for quick cleanup.
- Blot spills immediately: Don’t rub; blot. Rubbing can push liquid deeper into the pile and make the stain a resident guest. If you can, dab with a mild detergent mixed with water or a carpet-safe cleaner according to the manufacturer’s guidelines.
- Treat specific stains early: For beverages or ink, use a stain-removing solution designed for synthetic fibers. Test in a hidden corner first to verify colorfastness.
- Rotate the rug: Every few months, rotate the rug to even out wear patterns, especially in high-traffic zones. This also helps ensure that the flaw remains something you notice rather than a dull baseline.
- Protect from direct sun: Prolonged exposure can fade color. If you have strong sun in your space, pair the rug with curtains or blinds and enjoy the long-term color stability.

If you’re anal about maintenance, this rug will reward your diligence with a longer life and a consistently stylish presence in your space. If you’re more of a “live in the moment” type, you’ll still appreciate its resilience and low-maintenance vibe.
