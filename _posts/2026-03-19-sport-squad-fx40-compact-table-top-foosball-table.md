### Tips for a smooth build

## Maintenance, care, and longevity
Tabletop foosball demands a little maintenance to keep things feeling fresh. Here are practical tips to keep your FX40 running smoothly for years:

- Clean the rods occasionally with a microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints, oils, and the occasional snack crumb. A slightly damp cloth works fine; wipe dry afterward to prevent moisture from affecting the wood veneer.
- Check the rod screws and handle caps every few weeks. A loose screw can degrade shot accuracy and cause wobble that ruins your rhythm.
- Keep balls in a dedicated container when not in use to avoid misplacement and to extend their life. A dull, squished ball will wreck your game’s tempo.
- Use a light silicone spray on non-contact areas sparingly if you notice a squeak or drag; avoid over-lubrication, which will attract dust and ruin the feel.
- Store in a dry environment away from direct sunlight to preserve color and prevent veneer warping.

Cleaning, tightening, and a little routine check-up will keep the FX40 feeling like new long after the initial wow factor wears off. It’s not a maintenance nightmare, and that’s a big win for a family-friendly gadget that everyone will actually use.
