## Practical tips for players and collectors

### Sleeving and protection strategies

If you plan to own this card, you should sleeve it. Foil cards are attractive but slightly more prone to micro-scratches than standard cards. A simple standard top loader with a semi rigid sleeve should do. If you want to go the extra mile, consider a foil-specific storage binder that has non glare pockets. Keep the card away from direct sunlight for extended periods; the foil can be sensitive to consistent UV light exposure, which may cause the color to fade in a way that only a mythical wizard would appreciate.

### Display options

There is a certain joy in displaying the card. A well lit shelf with a micro-LED strip can make the foil pop in a way that would make a disco ball jealous.
