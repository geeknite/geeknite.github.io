## Maintenance tips: keep it looking fresh without a PhD in cleaning
Maintaining outdoor wicker is less about mastering a ritual than about consistency. Here are practical steps:
- Cleaning: Use a soft brush or cloth with a mild soap solution. Rinse with a faucet sprayer; avoid power washers that can push water into the weave and cause mildew. A light rinse weekly can prevent buildup.
- Cushion care: If cushions get splashed, remove and air dry. Check care labels for machine washing instructions. A waterproof spray can help extend the life of fabric coverings.
- Storage: If you have a shed or covered patio, store cushions during heavy rain or winter months. If not, use breathable storage bags to prevent moisture buildup.
- Staining and spills: Wipe up spills quickly and treat stains per the fabric care instructions. Quick action prevents set-in marks that will start a small, angry rebellion in your living space.
