## How to Use: Setup, Operation, and Tips

Setup is genuinely straightforward:

1) Place the shuffler in a convenient location on the table. 2) Connect power or install batteries according to your model. 3) Open the deck loader and place your cards inside. 4) Close the loader and press Start. 5) Watch the usual suspects gather around with bated breath as the shuffle completes.

A few practical tips:
- Always ensure the cards are well within the input tray so the machine has room to grab the edges. If you cram too many thick sleeves into the loader, the shuffle may not be as even. 
- If you notice clumping of heavily worn cards, consider a quick hand check to ensure nothing is snagging on the edges. 
- For intense game nights, designate a dedicated “shuffler time” so players can coordinate the end of a round and the start of a new shuffle for dramatic effect.
