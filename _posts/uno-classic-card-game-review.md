## Gameplay

Alright, let's dive into the gameplay. If you're a first-time player, buckle up! It's going to be a wild ride.

### How to Play

1. **Setup**: Shuffle the deck and deal seven cards to each player. Place the remaining cards face down to form the draw pile.
2. **Starting**: Flip the top card from the draw pile to start the discard pile. If it's a special card, follow its instructions.
3. **Turns**: On your turn, play a card that matches the color, number, or symbol of the top card on the discard pile. If you can't, draw a card from the draw pile.
4. **Special Cards**: Uno's special cards include Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, Draw Four, and Wild cards. These cards add excitement and unpredictability to the game.
5. **Winning**: The first player to get rid of all their cards wins the round. Points are calculated based on the remaining cards in opponents' hands.
