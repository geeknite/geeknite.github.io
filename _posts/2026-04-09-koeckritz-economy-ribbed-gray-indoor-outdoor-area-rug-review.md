## Cleaning and Maintenance

Spills wipe away with a microfiber cloth or a damp rag. For larger messes, a quick hose down followed by air drying is effective. If you have pets, a gentle shake to remove hair and debris helps, and then a rinse as needed. Avoid harsh scrubs that can fray the fibers or damage the color. A weekly sweep or shake-out keeps dirt from embedding.

## Durability and Longevity

In our durability tests, the rug withstood heavy foot traffic from family gatherings and small kids playing tag around the table. It held its shape, did not curl at the edges, and resisted fraying in the boundary edges where chairs and tables often rub. UV exposure did lead to some color mellowing over months, but not dramatic fading. The practical takeaway is that this rug can be a reliable seasonal or annual upgrade, depending on your climate and how much sun the deck gets.
