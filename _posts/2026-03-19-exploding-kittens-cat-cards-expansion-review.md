## What is the Cat Cards Expansion

The Cat Cards Expansion brings a pocketful of feline chaos to the Exploding Kittens universe. Think of it as a homage to every cat that ever walked across a keyboard and still decided your board game night was worth it. The expansion introduces a set of new Cat Cards with varied effects that lean into cat stereotypes: mischievous, aloof, dramatic, and occasionally helpful only to themselves. The flavor is pure Geeknite — we are here to celebrate the ridiculous, not to pretend we are saving the world with our strategic prowess.

The core premise is simple: you draw a Cat Card, you reveal it, and then you trigger the card's effect in addition to your normal actions. Some Cat Cards require you to collect sets or play them in combination with other Cat Cards. Others offer the possibility of sabotaging an opponent by rewarding them with a cute but inconvenient consequence. The humor lands because cat cards often play on real cat behavior: dramatic purrs, sudden zooms across the room, the sudden need to sit on the most inconvenient part of your friend’s hand. If you have ever owned a cat, many of these moments will feel painfully familiar — except now they interrupt your turn with the satisfaction of a well-timed meow.

The expansion also includes a handful of specialized Cat Cards designed to create memorable table moments. There are cards that force two players to trade hands in ridiculous ways, cards that give a temporary advantage but at a cost, and cards that simply say you must meow loudly for dramatic effect before you take your action. The result is a deck that feels like a cat decided to join your game night and brought a suitcase full of tiny paw-shaped chaos.

Artwork and design are full of color and personality. The illustrations lean into the cutesy meme aesthetic without crossing into cringe. This is a relief for players who enjoy lighthearted visuals in board games rather than grim, hyper-detailed grimdark art that makes you anxious about your next draw. The Cat Cards have their own distinctive look while still feeling cohesive with the base game art. It is entirely plausible to mix Cat Cards with base cards and other expansions without a visual mismatch that makes you want to cry into your meeple collection.

In terms of content, the Cat Cards Expansion aims to strike a balance between wacky and playable. Some cat effects are straightforward, while others offer room for clever misdirection. This means you can pilot the expansion with a light, breezy approach or lean into the chaos with rigorous social deduction vibes. The game night becomes less about optimizing draws and more about reading the room — or in this case, reading the cat. The cat does not miss; the cat observes, glances, and then pounces on your least prepared moment.

There are also practical considerations for setup and gameplay. The expansion is designed to slot into the existing Exploding Kittens experience without requiring a full reprint of your rules sheet. Setup remains quick; you will still shuffle, distribute, and set up the suspenseful possibility of an explosive kitten moment. Because the Cat Cards are cards in their own right, you do not need to discard the original rules entirely; instead you weave Cat Card effects into your ongoing play.
