## The Social Dance: House Rules and Tips

CAH is as much about the people at the table as it is about the cards. The Nerd Bundle invites you to experiment with a few light, optional rules that can elevate or derail your evening depending on the crowd. Here are a few to try, if you’re feeling spicy:

- Theme Nights: Pick one pack per round to keep a coherent vibe. If you’ve got a large group, designate a “theme captain” who curates prompts that fit the night’s mood.
- Card Economy: Only allow players to draw a certain number of white cards per round. It forces smarter pairing and reduces chaos-induced despair.
- Story Mode: After a round or two, challenge players to weave a tiny narrative that ties the selected prompts together. This is where the real nerdy gold shines.
