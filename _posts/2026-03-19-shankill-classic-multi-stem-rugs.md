### What is a “Multi Stem” rug, anyway?

### Maintenance tips to keep it divine

- Vacuum regularly but with a gentle setting to avoid fraying the stems.
- Wipe up spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the liquid deeper into the pile.
- Use a rug pad to reduce slippage and to extend the life of the weave.
- For stubborn stains, consult the care label or test a small inconspicuous area before attempting any cleaning solution.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and prevent one side from looking older than the other.
