## Care, Storage, and Longevity

Careful storage is the unglamorous hero of long-term deck life. Here are a few practical tips to maximize your Blue & Red two-pack:

- Keep them away from moisture. Cards don’t like humidity; it can cause warping, ink bleed, or war-torn pips. A dry, cool shelf is perfect.
- Avoid direct sun for long periods. Fading is slow but real, and you don’t want a purple-tinted Red deck after a weekend-long marathon of sunlight exposure.
- Don’t stack too aggressively. A heavy stack or frequent bending can cause warping over time. Use a proper card storage box if you’re moving between locations.
- Clean gently. If you notice a few fingerprints or slight residue, a soft microfiber cloth with a whisper of water can help, but avoid soaking or washing the cards—no one wants permanent aftershocks of a wacky party trick embedded in the stock.

If you’re a road warrior or a performance artist, consider an additional card-care kit: a small silica gel pack in your box, a soft organizer sleeve, and a dust-free environment for storage. It’s not glamorous, but it’s the kind of practical luxury card players appreciate when a game night goes past midnight.
