### What this expansion is and isn’t
Dixit Harmonies isn’t a standalone game; it’s a deck of 84 new creative prompts designed to slot neatly into any existing Dixit game. The expansion doesn’t reinvent the wheel; it refines the wheel by giving players more colors, more textures, and more opportunities to tell a story that only makes sense to your particular group. Harmonies leans into the “dreamlike, surreal, and sometimes illogical” vibe that makes Dixit so charming. You’ll find images that pair with the original cards in tone, but with a fresh twist—sometimes a little more whimsical, sometimes a touch more cryptic, and occasionally downright punny.

From a designer’s perspective, Harmonies does something clever: it broadens the range of associations without forcing a new set of strict game rules. It might feel like a natural extension rather than a separate product, which is exactly what you want from a Dixit expansion.

## How Harmonies interacts with Dixit mechanics
Dixit operates on storytelling as a shared mechanic: the storyteller gives a clue, players pick a card that best matches that clue, and everyone guesses which card was chosen. The challenge is to pick a card that’s specific enough to be distinguishing but not so obscure that no one recognizes it. Harmonies injects more possible prompts into that decision space—more potential associations, more red herrings, and more opportunities for a storyteller to mislead with a card that’s perfectly suited to the vibe they’re chasing.

### A few practical tips for integration
- Start with a hybrid approach: use Harmonies cards in while still using a portion of the base deck to reduce prediction volatility.
- The more people you have, the more likely players will play it safe. Encourage bold prompts and wild associations to unlock the expansion’s strengths.
- Use Harmonies to run themed rounds (dream-night, ocean-night, forest-night) to boost thematic cohesion during a single session.
