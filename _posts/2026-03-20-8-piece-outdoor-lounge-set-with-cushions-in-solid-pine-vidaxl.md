## Weather resistance and care
Pine is a wood that enjoys sunny days but can sulk in wet weather. The VidaXL set, being pine, benefits from sealing and periodic maintenance. The typical procedure is: seal the wood with outdoor-grade oil or varnish to keep moisture out; use cushions with weather-resistant covers if possible; store cushions indoors or in a dry place during heavy rain or winter. If you neglect the care, you’ll get more wood checks, minor cracks, and a character that says, “I survived a thousand summers.”

A practical tip: treat it with a cedar-tone sealant or natural oil every season to keep color uniform. A cover when not in use is your best friend. Also, keep cushions dry between uses; damp cushions can take longer to rebound and may develop mildew if left under a damp cover. The brand often includes care guides; follow them, and your set will outlive your patience for trendy patio updates.

## Maintenance tips and care
- Clean regularly with a mild soap solution and rinse with water. Do not use high-pressure washers that can force water into joints.
- Re-oil or reseal the wood annually to keep moisture out and color even.
- Use cushions covers that are removable and washable; dry them thoroughly before putting back on the furniture.
- When not in use for extended periods, store cushions indoors or in a dry place; rotate seat and back cushions to avoid uneven wear.
- Consider adding furniture covers to protect from harsh sun and heavy rain.
