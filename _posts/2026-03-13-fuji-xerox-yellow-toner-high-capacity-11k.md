### Maintenance tips and storage: keeping yellow fresh
A few practical tips to maximize the Yellow 11K’s life and performance:
- Store cartridges in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Extreme temperatures can affect toner flow and density.
- Avoid exposing the cartridge to moisture. The toner is not a fan of humidity spikes that could cause clumping during transfer.
- When loading, handle with clean, dry hands to minimize the introduction of oils that could affect transfer quality.
- Run a nozzle check or alignment print after installing a new cartridge to confirm the color channel is aligned with the other cartridges.
- If you run long print jobs, consider a brief pause halfway through to prevent overheating the printer’s internal drum assembly. It sounds nerdy, but it’s practical.
