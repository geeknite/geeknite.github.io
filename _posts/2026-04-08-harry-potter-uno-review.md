### Quick-start guide for new players
- Shuffle and deal seven cards to each player.
- The top card of the draw pile becomes the starting play card.
- On your turn, you must either match the color or the number with the card on the pile, or play a special HP action card if you have one of the corresponding effects.
- If you cannot play a card, you draw one from the draw pile. If that card can be played, you may continue on the same turn; otherwise, your turn ends.
- When you have one card left, shout UNO to warn others that you are about to become the most dangerous person in the room. If you forget and someone calls you out, you draw two cards or face the penalty as defined by the house rules in the pamphlet.
- The first player to shed all their cards wins the round. The rest of the players tally their points based on the cards left in the opponents' hands, just like regular UNO.

The HP twists are designed to be intuitive for kids while still offering a little extra fun for adults who want to reminisce about their favorite scenes from the films and books. It tends to be a crowd-pleasing option for family game nights or casual get-togethers where you want something quick and thematic without requiring an advanced degree in fantasy lore.
