## The Set

### Components

The Fellowship of the Ring Chess Set comes with beautifully crafted pieces representing characters from the beloved fantasy series. The detail on each piece is stunning, making it feel like you have a piece of Middle-earth right on your chessboard.

- **White Pieces**: Led by Aragorn, the white pieces include Gandalf, Legolas, Gimli, Frodo, Sam, Merry, and Pippin. If you’ve ever wanted to command the Fellowship in battle, here’s your chance.
- **Black Pieces**: Commanded by the ominous Sauron, the black pieces feature Saruman, the Witch-king, the Nazgûl, and various Orcs. So much evil packed into one side; it's like Mordor had a clearance sale!

## Gameplay

### The Pieces

Each piece in the set is designed to be both functional and visually appealing. Playing chess with characters from The Lord of the Rings adds an exciting layer of thematic enjoyment to the game.

1. **Channel Your Inner Gandalf**: Use your white pieces wisely and remember, much like Gandalf, to never let the enemy know your next move.
