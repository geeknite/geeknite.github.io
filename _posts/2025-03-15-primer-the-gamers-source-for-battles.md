## What is Primer?

Primer is a strategy board game where players take on the roles of various heroes, each equipped with unique abilities and cards. Your mission? To complete objectives and defeat your opponents in epic battles across fantastical realms.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures representing your chosen heroes. Watch out for the "Banana Barbarian"—he's slippery!
- **Cards**: Each hero has a deck of cards depicting their skills, actions, and special moves. Don't underestimate the "Cowardly Wizard"—he packs a punch!
- **Game Board**: Various locations filled with traps, treasures, and enemies. Beware of the "Pit of Eternal Flatulence"—it’s as deadly as it sounds.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Track your progress, health, and effects during the game. Just try to keep them away from your cat.

**Pro Tip**: Always keep the "Bug Spray" card handy. It may not save you, but it will give you a fighting chance【4†source】.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Primer is like preparing for a grand adventure. Lay out the game board, distribute miniatures and cards, and choose your heroes. You might need a snack break—or two—before diving into the fun.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and perform actions based on their hero's abilities. You can move, attack, defend, and more. Strategy and cooperation are key, so make sure to coordinate with your allies.

### Combat

Combat in Primer is a mix of strategic planning and luck. Each hero has unique mechanics that add twists and challenges to battles. Did you plan an epic combo only to have the "Cowardly Wizard" trip over his own robe? Welcome to Primer, where chaos reigns supreme.
