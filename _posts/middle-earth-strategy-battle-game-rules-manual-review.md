## The Basics

### What is the Middle-Earth Strategy Battle Game?

The Middle-Earth Strategy Battle Game (MESBG) is a tabletop wargame that brings the iconic battles of Middle-Earth to life. Players take on the roles of generals, commanding forces from various factions, including the noble Elves of Rivendell, the sturdy Dwarves of Erebor, and the menacing Orcs of Mordor. The game is based on the works of J.R.R. Tolkien and captures the essence of his epic tales.

### Components

The game comes with a comprehensive Rules Manual that covers everything you need to know to play MESBG. It includes rules for movement, combat, magic, and more. Additionally, the game features beautifully designed miniatures, representing the heroes and villains of Middle-Earth, as well as terrain pieces that bring the battlefield to life.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up MESBG is like preparing for an epic quest. Lay out your battlefield, arrange your miniatures, and get ready to dive into the strategic depths of Middle-Earth. You'll need a good amount of space, so make sure your table is as vast as the plains of Rohan.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players alternate in taking actions with their miniatures. These can include moving, attacking, casting spells, and more. Strategy is key, so plan your moves carefully and anticipate your opponent's actions. Remember, a well-timed spell or a surprise ambush can turn the tide of battle.

### Combat

Combat in MESBG is a blend of strategy and luck. Dice rolls determine the outcome of attacks, and modifiers from weapons and abilities add depth to the gameplay. Did Legolas just miss his shot at a crucial moment? Welcome to Middle-Earth, where even the best archers can have an off day.
