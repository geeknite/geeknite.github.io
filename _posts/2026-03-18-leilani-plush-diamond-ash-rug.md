## Maintenance and Cleaning
### Everyday Care
Regular vacuuming is the hero move here. Use a vacuum with a gentle beater bar; aggressive suction can cause a little wear on the plush over time. We recommend vacuuming in a couple of directions to keep the pile evenly plush and to avoid any suspicious “footprint” patterns that look suspiciously like that one meme you saved three years ago.

Spills should be blotted, not rubbed. Rubbing can embed the stain deeper and nudge the fibers into a mini desert of dreadlock-like tangles. For stubborn spots, a mild detergent solution or a dedicated rug cleaner works wonders. After cleaning, allow the rug to dry completely before heavy traffic resumes. Pro tip: avoid direct heat sources that could warp the fibers, especially near heat vents or sunny windowsills where your rug might decide to audition for a sun-drenched drama.

### Longevity and Wear
