## Zombicide 1st Edition: a quick refresher
If you jumped straight into the Special Guest talk, here is a quick refresher on what makes Zombicide 1st Edition tick. The core loop is classic co op: a handful of players work together to finish objectives while a horde of zombies converges on the board.
