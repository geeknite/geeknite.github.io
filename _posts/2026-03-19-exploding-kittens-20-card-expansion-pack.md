### Card design and art style

The 20 new cards embrace the same design philosophy: bold icons, legible text, and quick-read mechanics. This expansion is a social accelerant rather than a tactical overhaul. It’s designed to amplify what you already love about Exploding Kittens: chaotic fun, social reading, and the moment when someone misreads a hand and takes the entire table on a detour through ridiculous consequences.

- For casual players: you’ll notice a lighter learning curve and more opportunities to lean into humor rather than crunch numbers. The new cards make it easier to pivot from “this is how we survive” to “this is how we win the laugh race.”

## Variants, House Rules and Solo Play

Not every group plays the same, and that’s part of the charm here. The 20 Card Expansion Pack supports several variants and light house rules that help keep chaos manageable while preserving the spirit of spontaneous hilarity.

- Action declaration: require players to verbally declare their planned action before playing. It’s a ritual that heightens anticipation and gives the table something to mock after-the-fact.
- Solo or cooperative twists: use the expansion to create puzzle-like modes where you attempt to manage the deck with friends acting as saboteurs. The expansion can function as a catalyst for solo challenges or cooperative kill-the-boredom endeavors, though its primary design remains as a party-and-social-experiment kind of product.
