Mechanics Overview

For a card titled Loch Ness Monster of the Mind, you expect a performance rooted in control and card selection rather than brute force. The mechanics deliver that promise with a quiet confidence. Here is a compact snapshot of what this card does and why you might want to run it in a tuned deck:

- Card type: Creature — Cryptid
- Mana cost: 3U
- Power/Toughness: 3/4
- Key abilities:
  - When Loch Ness Monster of the Mind enters the battlefield, target opponent reveals their hand. You choose a nonland card from it and exile it until end of turn. If you exile a spell that your opponent casts this turn, you may cast that spell once before the end of your next turn and you must pay its mana cost again.
  - If you control a lake homeland, Loch Ness gains hexproof until end of turn.
- Flavor text: Some legends drift beneath the surface, and some minds dive after them, hoping to pull a thread that unravels the entire loom.
