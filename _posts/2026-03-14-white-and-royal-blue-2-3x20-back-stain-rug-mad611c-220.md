### Cleaning and maintenance tips

- Vacuum regularly with a low to medium suction. Avoid high power that could fray fibers or scatter the blue into your white zone like a tiny paint party. 
- Blot stains with a clean cloth, using a mild detergent if necessary. Do a test patch first on a hidden area because dye migration in cheaper rugs can be unpredictable. 
- For deeper stains, consider a gentle rug shampoo and avoid harsh bleach which can fade the blue. 
- Let the rug dry completely before stepping on it again to avoid slipperiness that would ruin your morning stroll to the coffee maker.
