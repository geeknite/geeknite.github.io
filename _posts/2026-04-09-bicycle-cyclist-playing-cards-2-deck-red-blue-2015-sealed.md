The face cards aren’t radically altered from the standard Bicycle line—again, a deliberate choice for collectors who want familiarity with a dash of novelty. The court cards feel like a nod to deck-building — not just face value, but storytelling. On a casual toss, you might notice the “at speed” posture of the riders on the Queen and Jack; on a serious flourish stream, you’ll pick up the way the cards lay in hand, with a little extra room for the fingers to breathe during a fan or a slice flourish.
