## Installation: Setup and First Impressions
The 2 TB SN850X comes in a package that includes the heatsink variant designed to fit inside the PS5's internal slot. For those who want a simpler route, there are bare drives (no heatsink) that require their own cooling solution. Either way, the process is straightforward:

1. Power down and unplug the PS5.
2. Remove the side panel and locate the M.2 slot with the cooling shroud (the exact location may differ slightly between models).
3. Insert the SN850X into the slot at an angle, then secure it with the mounting screw.
4. Replace the heatsink shroud if needed and reinstall the side panel.
5. Boot up the PS5 and format the new drive.

We tested with the heatsink variant to emphasize what the PS5 is capable of when thermal throttling remains in the shadows. The drive formatted quickly, and the PS5 recognized it as a high-performance storage tier, enabling you to move games between internal and expanded storage with little friction.

The installation is so simple that even a non-technical gamer can do it without becoming a device-obsessed meme.
