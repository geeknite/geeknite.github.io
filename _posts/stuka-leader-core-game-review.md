## What is Stuka Leader Core Game?

Stuka Leader Core Game is a solitaire strategy game that places you in command of a squadron of German Stuka dive bombers during World War II. Your mission? To complete various objectives while dealing with the chaos of war. It's like chess, but with more explosions and fewer knights.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures of Stuka dive bombers and enemy units.
- **Cards**: A plethora of cards representing missions, events, and strategic options.
- **Game Board**: A beautifully designed board depicting various wartime locations.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, damage, and other game elements.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the miniatures. They may think they're chew toys, and nobody wants a slobbery Stuka.
