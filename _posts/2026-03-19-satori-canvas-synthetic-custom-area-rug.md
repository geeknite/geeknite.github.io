## Build and performance in daily life

### Stain resistance and cleaning 101
Synthetic rugs like this typically handle spills with grace. Blot spills as soon as possible with a clean cloth to prevent staining, then clean with a mild detergent and water. Avoid aggressive scrubbing that can fray the surface fibers or dull the color. For tougher stains, you can reach for a gentle rug cleaner recommended by the manufacturer. Vacuum regularly with a standard setting to avoid pushing dirt deeper into the fiber. Quick tip: work in sections and avoid a single aggressive pass that could misalign the backing or cause curling along the edges.

### Pet ownership on a canvas replica
If you share your space with a pet who thinks the rug is a launchpad for acrobatics, you’ll appreciate the synthetic fiber’s resilience. ## Care tips for long-lasting appeal

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid fiber fray.
- Blot liquids immediately with a dry cloth; don’t rub, or you’ll push the stain deeper.
- Use a rug pad under the rug for stability and to extend the carpet’s life by reducing friction with the floor.
- Clean spills with a mild detergent and warm water; test any cleaner on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Keep away from direct heat sources to prevent any fiber damage or fading over time.
