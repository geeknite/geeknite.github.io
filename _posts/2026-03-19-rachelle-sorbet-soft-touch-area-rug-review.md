## Maintenance and Longevity: Real-World Expectations

No rug is magic; all rugs require some care. The Rachelle Sorbet Soft Touch Area Rug is designed to resist everyday wear and tear while still feeling luxurious. Expect normal flattening in high-traffic areas over time; this is natural for plush fibers. Regular rotation — moving the rug 90 degrees every few months — helps to distribute wear evenly, which means you won t get a patchy patch where your chair wheels have carved their own topography.

Stain resistance is decent but not magical. If you spill juice or coffee, blot it promptly with a dry cloth and then clean with a mild solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that can break down fibers and alter color. For tougher stains, consult a professional cleaner who understands synthetic fiber blends. The goal is to maintain the rug without turning it into a science project in a beaker full of cleaning agents.

One handy tip is to keep pet nails trimmed and to use a rug pad that offers extra grip. This reduces the risk of curling edges and helps to maintain the clean lines you want in your interior design. A well-kept rug also looks better in photos, which means your next streaming session might include a quick interior shot for the fans who care about decors and life choices.
