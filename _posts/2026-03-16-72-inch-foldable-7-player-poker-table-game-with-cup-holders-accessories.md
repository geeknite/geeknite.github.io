## Maintenance, cleaning, and care
### Cleaning the felt
Light vacuuming is sufficient for most nights. For spills, wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid soaking or saturating the felt; moisture can cause warping over long time periods.

### Cleaning the rails and legs
Wipe with a damp microfiber cloth; avoid abrasive cleaners. If you notice metal rail parts getting squeaky, you can apply a light silicone spray to keep movement smooth.

### Longevity and warranty
A table built with good hardware and sturdy wood will last years with routine care. Check the warranty details from the manufacturer to understand coverage on fold mechanisms and rails.
