## Installation and setup tips
### How to install the Yellow 11K cartridge
1. Power down your device and wait a few moments for any residual charges to settle.
2. Open the printer’s access area and locate the yellow cartridge slot that matches the color code.
3. Remove the packaging fully and peel off any protective seals.
4. Slide the cartridge into place with a firm but gentle push until you hear a click.
5. Close the access area and run a calibration, if your printer offers one, to ensure full contact with the drum and proper toner distribution.

### Common issues and quick fixes
- Cartridge won’t click into place: re-check the locking tab and reseat with a straight, even push. Misalignment is the most common culprit.
- Print shows spots or ghosting: run a cleaning cycle and re-calibrate. A small amount of residue can cause print artifacts.
- Colors look off: perform a printer profile check or re-run the color calibration. A store-bought color profile can harmonize yellow with the other hues.
