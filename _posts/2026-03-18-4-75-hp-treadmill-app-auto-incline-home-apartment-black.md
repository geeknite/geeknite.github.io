## Noise, stability, and ride feel
Noisy workouts are a religion you do not want to practice in an apartment. This treadmill stays within modest noise levels for a motor of its class. Lubrication is part of the maintenance ritual. A simple belt take up and a periodic wipe down keep the surface smooth, so friction does not become a drama during a 5K tempo segment. The manual suggests a simple maintenance schedule that even busy geeks can follow: inspect belt alignment once a month, clean the deck every couple of weeks, and recheck mounting bolts after a few dozen workouts. You do not need a PhD in mechanical engineering to follow this, but a moment of attention pays off in smoother runs and less squeak drama.
