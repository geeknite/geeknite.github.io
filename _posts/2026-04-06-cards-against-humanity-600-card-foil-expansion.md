## Storage, Maintenance, and Longevity
Foil cards demand a touch more care than standard cards. Storing them in a solid box with a divider helps keep the glassy sheen intact and reduces the chance of edge wear. Here are practical tips:
- Use a dedicated CAH organizer or a sturdy box with a gentle insert. This minimizes surface scratching and keeps the foil shimmering for longer.
- Keep the deck out of direct sunlight. The glare is fantastic for a moment, but prolonged exposure can fade the foil’s brilliance and make the cards look dull.
- Don’t over-shuffle. Treat the foil as you would treat precious vinyl; a few confident riffles will do, but a manic shuffle could help turn your shiny cards into a scratched dance floor.
