## Storage, care, and display tips
Storage is the silent guardian of card value. If you want to maximize the NM condition and enjoy the long game, here are a few practical tips:

- Use quality penny sleeves or soft sleeves and place the cards in a two piece binder or a dedicated Dragon Ball Super storage box. The combo is not just about protection but the ritual of preserving a narrative you built with your own hands.
- Keep away from humidity and temperature extremes. Foils and color can shift with heat and moisture. If you live in a swampy climate or a kitchen that doubles as a science experiment, consider a dedicated climate controlled room, or at least a well sealed plastic box with desiccant.
- Invest in a solid storage binder with acid free pages. The last thing you want is yellowing or edge wear creeping in after months of proud display.
- For display pieces, consider a glass case or a lighted display shelf. This is optional but recommended for those who want their NM cards to feel like a museum piece rather than a forgotten souvenir.

If you want your All NM pre release lot to stay pristine, you will want a plan for long term care. Here are a few strategies we recommend:
