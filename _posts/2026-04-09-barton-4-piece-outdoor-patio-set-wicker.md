## Comfort and cushions
Cushions are the soul of any outdoor seating. Barton cushions provide a good mix of firmness and sink, enough to cradle your post lunch nap but not so soft you slide off. The back cushions hold shape after an afternoon of sun and a few dramatic apartment-dweller speeches about climate change. Seat depth is generous enough for a cozy stretch with a drink and a book, yet compact enough for a chatty group of two or three to fit.

### Cushion care
Removable covers are a blessing, and machine washing here is recommended according to the label. For stubborn stains, a mild detergent and a soft brush do wonders. Dry thoroughly before you seal them up again to avoid that map-like wrinkle pattern that appears when cushions are damp. We also suggest rotating cushions every couple of weeks to ensure even wear and to keep your cushions from developing diva personalities.

## Maintenance and cleaning tips
Maintenance is simple. Wipe the frame with a damp cloth; if you get a bit of grime on it, a mild soap solution is sufficient. The cushion covers can be machine washed according to the label. If you have stubborn marks, a gentle soft brush does wonders without scrubbing away the fabric’s integrity. For storage, keep cushions dry and use breathable covers to maintain airflow and avoid trapped moisture. Regular quick wipe downs prevent dust and pollen from embedding into the weave over time.

### Cleaning recipes and routines
We keep a small spray bottle with a mild detergent and water for quick cleanups after outdoor gatherings. A quick pass after an afternoon party keeps your Barton set looking fresh through the season. If you love tech, you can apply a small amount of furniture wax to the metal parts to retain shine and help with water beading on the surface.
