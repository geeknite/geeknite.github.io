## Mechanics and Play Experience
### Tag system
City of Mist uses a tag based approach to actions. A tag is a short descriptor that adds a twist to your roll. During a scene, you will declare tags that apply to your action, roll a pair of 6-sided dice, and consult the result against the target difficulty. A high roll can yield a full success, a mid roll a partial success, and a failure means you will have to declare a new plan. The PDF outlines the tag taxonomy, the way to create new tags for custom legends, and how to escalate stakes as the scene unfolds. This system favors improvisation and scene rhythm, making every roll feel like you are peeling back another layer of the citys mythic skin.
### Characters and play style
Character creation in City of Mist emphasizes the secret identity and the mythic persona. You are a human with a name that hides a thousand legends, a tag cloud that reveals your abilities, and a desire that drives your investigative arc. The PDF contains sample templates and suggested mythic identities to help players avoid the trap of coming up with a hero that is too on the nose. It also suggests pairing a city investigator with a mythic power that complements other players rather than duplicates their shtick. If you have ever played a detective who discovers they are also a dragon, you know the joy of this system.
### Scenes and adventures
The PDF offers a curated list of scenes and adventures that can function as a veneer for ongoing campaigns or as one shots. Each scenario is structured as a three beat arc with a set of leads, a few challenges, and a climactic reveal that ties back to the myths installed in the city. The scenarios emphasize mood, theme, and narrative pace rather than grid-based combat. If you like games where the tension comes from what the city remembers rather than the number of hit points you have, you will appreciate this approach.
