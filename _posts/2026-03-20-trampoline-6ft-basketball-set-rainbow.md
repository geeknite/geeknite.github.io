## Safety Considerations: Use Cases and Boundaries
Backyard play is all fun and games until someone’s elbow meets a neighbor’s mailbox. Here are safety pointers that will keep your yard from becoming a cautionary tale:
- Supervise younger children at all times. The trampoline is stable, but gravity is sneaky.
- Ensure the frame is on a flat surface. A wobbly base is not your friend and may lead to a dramatic, accidental cha-cha.
- Check the safety net’s zipper before every session. You don’t want to witness the Great Escape during a bounce.
- Don’t attempt stunts beyond a basic jump and aim. The basketball hoop is there to add a playful challenge, not to turn this into a stunt show.
- Regularly inspect springs for corrosion or wear. If you see winded springs that look suspicious, replace them. It’s cheaper than a trip to the ER and far less dramatic.
