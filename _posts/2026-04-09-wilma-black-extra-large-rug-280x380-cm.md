Maintenance, Durability, and Warranty
In the world of rugs, durability is a quiet hero. Wilma’s construction should stand up to daily vacuuming and occasional spills with minimal fiber shedding. The color is excellent at resisting light fading in typical living room exposure, though intense direct sunlight can eventually alter the surface hue over time. Care Tips and Cleaning Protocols
- Vacuum regularly with the beater bar off to avoid fiber damage.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing.
- For stains, treat with a mild solution and test in a small area.
- For deep cleaning, hire a professional cleaner or follow the rug’s care label instructions if provided by the manufacturer.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear and avoid visible foot-traffic patterns in the same areas.
