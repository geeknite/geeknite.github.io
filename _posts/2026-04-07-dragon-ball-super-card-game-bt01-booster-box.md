## Deck Building with BT01: A Geeknite Guide
- Start with a core engine: identify cards that enable your preferred play style—aggressive surge, mid-range tempo, or control via disruption. BT01 gives you options to either bolster a classic engine or try something new.
- Balance your energy curve: Dragon Ball games reward efficient energy use. Include cards that let you accelerate early but avoid flooding your hand with dead cards on turn six.

- Consider your budget: rare and foil pulls can swing your wallet as much as your deck. If you’re a collector, you might chase those display-worthy foils; if you’re a player, build around affordable staples first.
