## Comfort, durability, and cleaning: day-to-day life with a wool hero

### Pile height and feel

The Kendall Oatmeal Wool Rug sits at a comfortable mid-height—soft enough for bare feet, resilient enough to handle bare feet plus a couple of shoes if you’re in a hurry. The sensation underfoot is plush but not so fluffy that it’s like walking on a cloud you would never reach again. This is the kind of pile that makes standing desk sessions less punishing and couch-diving movie nights more civilized.

### Stain resistance and maintenance

Wool has natural resistance to wrinkles and some stains, but it’s not invincible. We tested it with coffee, red wine, and a questionable smoothie mishap that may or may not have contained spirulina. The key takeaway: act quickly. Blot, don’t rub, and use a mild wool-safe cleaner if needed. For routine upkeep, a vacuum with a gentle setting is your best friend. The rug held its own against daily life and showed only minor evidence of wear after several weeks of heavy living and occasional drama (the cat, the cat’s friends, and a stray Lego brick). The result: it looks better than it should for how much life it endures.

### Cleaning and care tips

- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting to preserve the pile’s loft.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing as it can push stains deeper.
- For stubborn stains, use a wool-safe cleaner and test on a hidden corner first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to maintain even wear and color distribution.
