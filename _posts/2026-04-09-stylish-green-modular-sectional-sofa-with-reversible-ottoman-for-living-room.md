## Space and Room Considerations

### Room size and traffic flow
Modular seating works well in spaces where the furniture can be moved around. For small rooms, you can create a compact call center with a chaise at one end. For larger rooms, you can create more open floor space by using fewer modules and letting the ottoman anchor a central conversation zone.

### Placement and lighting
Place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading over time. Use warm lighting to keep the green vibrant and avoid fluorescent glare that makes the color look unnatural.

## Maintenance and Cleaning

### General care
Regular vacuuming with a soft brush and occasional lint removal should keep the fabric looking crisp. Wipe spills promptly with a clean damp cloth, and avoid harsh solvents.

### Stain treatment and fabric care
For stains, test a fabric safe cleaner on a small area first. Avoid bleach or strong solvents. A protective fabric spray can be reapplied after cleaning.

### Odors and humidity
A quick air out after long gaming sessions helps keep the fabric fresh. If the room is damp, maintain humidity control to prevent musty odors.
