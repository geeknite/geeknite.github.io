## Display, Preservation, and Care

### Protecting the No 261 Treasure

The easiest way to preserve a card like No 261 is to sleeve it in a standard trading card sleeve and place it into a rigid top loader. This setup protects from fingerprints, moisture, and the little accidents that happen when you reach into a bag full of other geeky loot. For display, consider a light diffusing acrylic frame that allows the art to shine without reflections that wash out the color. If your display area gets sunlight, consider a UV protective display case to keep the colors vibrant and avoid yellowing over years.

### Handling and Cleaning Tips

Always handle cards with clean, dry hands. If you must handle the card with bare fingers, keep the contact light and avoid touching the image area excessively. A soft microfiber cloth can help you gently wipe away dust and fingerprints. Avoid dampness and harsh cleaners that could affect the surface or the back text. Remember that patience is a virtue in collecting; a calm approach reduces the risk of accidental damage during a sale or transfer of ownership.
