## The Basics

### What is Shiloh - Grant Surprised?

The Shiloh - Grant Surprised board game is part of the Folio Series, an educational and entertaining range of historical board games. This game recreates the Battle of Shiloh, where Union General Ulysses S. Grant was caught off guard by the Confederate army. Players can take on the roles of either side, strategizing to change the course of history or maintain an accurate outcome.

### Components

The game includes a detailed map of the battle area, counters representing units, and a rulebook that feels more like a historical tome. Don't worry, it's less daunting than it sounds—unless you're allergic to history.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Shiloh - Grant Surprised feels like preparing for an epic Civil War reenactment. It’s time to lay out the map, position your troops, and try not to get too distracted by the historical tidbits scattered throughout.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions such as moving troops, attacking, and executing special orders. The decisions you make can lead to dramatic shifts in the battlefield dynamics, proving that history is never set in stone.

### Combat

Combat involves rolling dice and comparing unit statistics. It’s straightforward but strategic, requiring you to balance aggression with caution. Remember, Grant's surprise was partly due to underestimating his opponent—don't make the same mistake!

## The Historical Experience

The game does an excellent job of capturing the historical essence of the Battle of Shiloh. From the unit formations to the terrain, every aspect feels meticulously researched. You’ll find yourself immersed in the period, whether you’re a seasoned historian or a curious newcomer.
