## Care and maintenance
- Storage: Keep the decks in their tuck boxes when not in use to preserve edges and prevent bending in transit. A cool, dry place away from direct sunlight is ideal to keep the colors vibrant.
- Cleaning: If fingerprints or smudges accumulate, a gentle wipe with a dry microfiber cloth is enough. Avoid damp cloths and avoid any sleeve cleaning that could leave residue.
- Handling between sessions: Let the cards breathe out of the box for a few minutes before you use them again after a long break. That quick rest helps restore the natural bounce and keeps dealing smooth.
- Sleeves: If you’re planning to sleeve the entire two-pack for travel or performance, ensure the sleeves don’t add too much bulk or friction that could affect shuffles. For most casual uses, sleeves aren’t necessary.
