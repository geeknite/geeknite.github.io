## How to use and dosing tips for large dogs
Always follow the label directions, and use the product as part of a balanced approach that includes proper diet and exercise. For large dogs, the typical regimen is one tablet per day per dog, with adjustments based on body weight as advised by your veterinarian. Some owners opt to split the dose, giving half with breakfast and half with dinner to ensure the dog eats and the supplement dissolves well into the system. If you notice any gastric upset or changes in appetite after starting Multicare, stop the product and consult your vet—odd behavior can sometimes be a red flag that something else is going on.

Here are a few practical dosing tips that might make life a little easier:
- Use a pill dispenser or split the tablet with a clean knife if you need to tailor the dose for weight management within the range specified on the label.
- Mix with a small amount of wet food or a tasty treat to improve palatability for dogs who are picky about pill time.
- Keep a consistent routine. Dogs like predictability, and taking supplements at the same time each day can help them form a positive association with the ritual rather than seeing it as a weird, new obstacle.
