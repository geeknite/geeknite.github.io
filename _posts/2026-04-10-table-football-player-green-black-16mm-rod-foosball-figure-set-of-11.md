## Maintenance and Care
Foosball figures are not fragile statues; they are workhorses for fast-paced tabletop soccer. A simple cleaning routine keeps the finish intact and the rotation smooth. Use a mild soap solution and a soft microfiber cloth to wipe down the figures after epic sessions. Avoid solvent-based cleaners that can degrade the paint or plastic over time. Periodically inspect the rod ends for burrs or nicks that could snag the figures during rotation, and if you find any rough edges, a quick pass with a mild ceramic polish will help restore a clean glide.

### Quick maintenance checklist
- Wipe any residue after use to prevent buildup on joints
- Check the rod holes for burrs; sand lightly if needed
- Lubricate the rod ends sparingly with a neutral oil to improve glide
- Inspect paint edges after heavy sessions and re-touch if necessary
