## Hosting tips for a smooth night
Running Werewolves well is less about who you have at the table and more about how you run the table. A few simple practices can improve the experience dramatically:
- Use a timer or a free online timer to keep each phase on track. A well-timed night and a crisp day keep the energy high and prevent the round from spiraling into a drag.
- Establish a few ground rules at the start. For example, no reveals during the day unless a vote is about to occur, and no shouting above a certain decibel to keep the table from becoming a chaotic chorus.
- Create a comfortable space. The mood matters; candles, soft lighting, and comfortable seating encourage folks to lean in and participate rather than watch from the perimeter.
- Prepare a quick one-page cheat sheet for players. Even a tiny reference card that outlines the order of play, the roles, and the top tips for bluffing can dramatically reduce table friction.
