Box Contents and What You Can Expect
-----
Box content in a Set Booster Box varies a bit, but here’s the broad stroke: a box with 12 booster packs, each containing 15 magic cards plus a token or add-on. That’s 180 cards, give or take a few miscounted sleeves and a stray foil you’ll swear is the “one.” The Baldur’s Gate edition adds a splashy level of reprint glory and new cards with the Baldur’s Gate flavor poured into every corner. The highlights: a higher density of legendary creatures, more powerful partner commanders and combos, and a handful of cards that feel like they could rewrite a table’s entire draft dynamic.

What’s inside per pack? The usual: five commons, three uncommons, one rare or mythic, plus a guaranteed showcase or border variant slot that gives you that “this is cooler than a basic foil” moment. In practice, you’ll see a lot of fun tribal synergies, some artifact convergence, and a surprising number of cards that reward multi-player spike drafting. If you’re building a cafeteria table commander atmosphere in which the party uses swords, sorceries, and the occasional suspicious chair, this set is basically a buffet of spicy options.
