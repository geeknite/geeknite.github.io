## Display and care tips

for the display, consider a simple shelf or a non-glare acrylic case that allows the patch and autograph to shine without risk of scuffs from handling. avoid placing the card directly under harsh sunlight; the ink may appreciate a little pigment protection, and the patch fabric could fade with time if exposed to direct UV light. a thin layer of protective sleeve and a top loader is standard, but if you want to go full showpiece mode, a 360-degree display frame with adjustable lighting can make the patch look like it just walked off the ice after a dramatic overtime goal. the key is to keep it clean: avoid fingerprints, dust, and the occasional nibble from curious labrador retrievers that somehow think these cards are fancy chew toys. a quick once-over with a microfiber cloth every few months should keep the autograph crisp and the patch vibrant.
