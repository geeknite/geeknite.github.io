## Care and storage tips
Caring for vintage sealed decks extends their life and preserves the joy of unboxing later. Here are practical guidelines:
- Store in a cool, dry place; avoid basements with humidity and attics with heat spikes. A climate-controlled closet is ideal.
- Shield from direct sunlight. UV can fade ink and degrade tuck edges over time.
- Avoid drastic temperature changes. Rapid shifts can warp stock or loosen glue.
- If you plan to open, approach with care. A clean cutting tool and a steady hand go a long way in preserving packaging integrity.
- For display, consider a small acrylic stand or a shadow box that won’t press the tuck into awkward angles.
