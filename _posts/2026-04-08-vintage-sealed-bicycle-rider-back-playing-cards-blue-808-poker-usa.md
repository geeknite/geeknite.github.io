## Care and storage tips
Caring for vintage sealed decks extends their life; failing to do so can lead to brittle stock, which is the enemy of both display and play. Here are a few practical tips:
- Store in a cool, dry place. Avoid basements with humidity and attics with heat spikes. A closet in a climate controlled room is ideal.
- Keep away from direct sunlight. UV exposure can fade ink and degrade the tuck edge. Think of it as sunscreen for your cards.
- Avoid drastic temperature changes. Rapid shifts can cause the stock to warp and the glue to relax, which affects how the deck feels when you eventually open it.
- If you plan to open, do so with care. A clean cutting tool and a steady hand go a long way in preserving the deck's pristine packaging.
