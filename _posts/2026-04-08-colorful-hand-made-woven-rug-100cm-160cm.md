## Care and maintenance extended
### Cleaning routine
A routine cleaning helps preserve color and texture. Vacuum weekly, using a gentle setting to avoid pulling. If you notice shedding, a light vacuum pass with a soft brush head can help, and the occasional shake out can reduce compacted dust in the fibers. For spills, blot from the outside toward the center to avoid spreading.

### Dye stability and sun exposure
Natural dyes will typically hold up well indoors, but extended direct sun exposure can cause color to fade. Consider rotating the rug or using window treatments to minimize sun exposure on bright days. If you want to refresh the colors after years of use, professional dye refresh or careful dye retouching by a pro might be an option.

### Seasonal care
In humid climates, ensure the rug dries evenly after cleaning to avoid mold or mildew. In dry climates, you may need to lighten heavy foot traffic with occasional shifting of the rug to prevent permanent creasing.
