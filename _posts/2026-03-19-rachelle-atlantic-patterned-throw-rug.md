### Cleaning and maintenance tips
- Vacuum regularly to remove surface dust and pet hair. Use a gentle setting to avoid bending the fibers.
- For spills, blot (don’t rub) with a clean cloth. Mild detergent diluted with water can help lift typical kitchen spills; avoid harsh solvents.
- If a spot proves stubborn, test clean a small area first, then treat the stain in the center of the rug rather than the edges to prevent color migration.
- Rotate every few months to even out wear and ensure the pattern ages gracefully.
