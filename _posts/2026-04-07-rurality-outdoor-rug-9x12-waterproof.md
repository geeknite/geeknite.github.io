## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity: Keeping the Rug Alive and Stylish

- Routine cleaning: A quick hose-down or a wipe with a damp cloth will handle most dirt and pollen. For deeper cleaning, use a mild soap and soft brush, then rinse and air-dry.
- Stains: Treat promptly. Most stains lift with a combination of gentle soap and water; avoid abrasive scrubbers that could wear the surface.
- Storage: When not in use for extended periods or during inclement weather, roll it up or store it flat in a dry area to minimize creasing and potential mildew.
- Odors: If you notice a musty odor after a long rain, a light spray with a fabric-safe freshener and a sunny day do wonders. If you’re really hardcore about freshness, a quick wash and air-dry does the trick.
