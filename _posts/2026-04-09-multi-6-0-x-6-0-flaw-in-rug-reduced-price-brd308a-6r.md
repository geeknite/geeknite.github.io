## Maintenance and care tips
Care is simple, which is exactly how most geeks like their life: simple and repeatable. Here are a few practical tips:
- Vacuum regularly on a low setting to avoid pulling fibers. If you own a robotic vacuum, set it to a gentle mode for the first runs to acquaint it with the rug texture.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Do not rub, or you risk matting the pile in the area of the spill.
- For stubborn stains, treat with a mild detergent diluted in water and blot again. Avoid harsh chemicals that could compromise color or backing integrity.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear in high-traffic zones.
- If you have pets, trim nails regularly to reduce snagging. Your furniture will thank you, and so will your carpet pattern alignment, which will stay more consistent.

If you store the rug during seasonal shifts, roll it, don not fold, and keep it away from direct sun to prevent color fading. It is not rocket science, but following these steps will maximize the rug life and keep your space looking fresh.
