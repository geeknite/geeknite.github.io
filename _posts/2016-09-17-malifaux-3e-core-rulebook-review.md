## What is Malifaux 3E?

Malifaux is a skirmish-level tabletop miniatures game set in a fantastical world where magic, monsters, and mayhem reign supreme. The third edition of Malifaux brings refined rules, new characters, and an even deeper storyline that will have you hooked faster than you can say "Soulstone".

Setting up Malifaux 3E is like preparing for a grand theatrical performance. You need your miniatures, terrain, and a rulebook that could double as a weapon in a medieval battle. Lay out your pieces, gather your friends, and get ready to dive into a world where strategy meets sorcery.

## The Core Rulebook: A Treasure Trove of Information

The **Malifaux 3E Core Rulebook** is a comprehensive guide that covers everything from character stats to intricate rules about magical combat. It's beautifully illustrated, with artwork that brings the game's dark yet whimsical aesthetic to life. The rulebook is divided into sections that cover:

- **Game Basics**: Learn the fundamental rules and mechanics that govern movement, combat, and magic.
- **Factions**: Discover the diverse factions, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. Whether you're a Guild enforcer or a Neverborn trickster, there's a faction for every player.
- **Scenarios and Missions**: Engage in various scenarios that challenge your strategic thinking and offer endless replayability.
- **Lore and Background**: Immerse yourself in the rich lore of Malifaux, with stories that are as intriguing as they are unpredictable.

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Turns and Actions

Each turn in Malifaux is packed with strategic decisions. Players alternate activating models, performing actions such as moving, attacking, and casting spells. The use of a deck of cards instead of dice adds a unique twist to the game, ensuring that luck and strategy go hand-in-hand.
