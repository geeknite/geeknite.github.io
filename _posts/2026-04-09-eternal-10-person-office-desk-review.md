## Build and Design
### Materials and Construction
The Eternal 10 Person Office Desk uses a heavy-duty steel frame and a laminated wood top with a matte finish. The ten modular segments slide together with hidden fasteners, leaving a seamless surface that is a trap for small bits of torn paper and the occasional stray USB stick. The steel legs are powder-coated to resist dings. The connectors between segments are designed to minimize flex, a feature that becomes obvious when someone tries to plug in a charger while standing up.

### Configuration and Size
- Overall length: about 3.6 meters (roughly 12 feet). Yes, you can host a full sprint planning session and still have room for a celebratory pizza.
- Depth: 75–90 cm, depending on year and whether you demand extra space for mouse mats.
- Segments: ten modules, each about 360 mm wide. The modular design means you can reconfigure into a straight row, a U-shape for meetings, or a creative constellation that resembles a star map of the backlog.
- Height: adjustable feet with leveling options. If the floor is a lava field of carpet, you can stabilize with risers.

### Aesthetics
The desk carries a minimalist, professional vibe. The matte finish hides fingerprints and coffee stains with equal grace. The edges are rounded to avoid the accidental elbow of doom. It looks like something that belongs in a modern startup, a cooperative hacker space, or a corporate buzzword convention.

## Setup and Assembly
Assembly is a two-person job, ideally with a person who knows which end of a screwdriver is up and a person who can read instructions without turning it into interpretive dance. The ten modules ship in a single pallet with a few heavy boxes that proclaim this will be a project for the long weekend. The instruction manual is concise and tolerable, a small miracle given the complexity of aligning ten segments into a single, smooth surface.

Helpful tips:
- Do not over-tighten — the bolts; they only need enough torque to stay in place without becoming a power drill.
- Use rubber mallet taps to guide connectors; avoid walls of frustration that end with a screwdriver stuck in the carpet.
- Cable management: central trough, optional channel covers for a clean look. Plan the path of cables before you mount the desk; otherwise you will find yourself chasing a cable dragon for hours.

### Cable management and comfort
Power, data, and the occasional mount for a 3D printer can accumulate in a single mass of wires. The central trough helps, but you will still want to add under-desk trays for adapters and a couple of zip ties to corral the chaos. If your team loves stand-up huddles and laptop juggling, you will appreciate the way the trough keeps cords contained and accessible.

## Ergonomics and Productivity
Ten people on a single surface is a human experiment that might result in higher collaboration or a chaotic chorus of keyboard taps. Each module offers a small range of height adjustment, allowing you to tailor the vibe: some members can be at standing height while others are at soft chair height. The result is a dynamic blend of shared space and personal space. The desk supports a mix of hot desks, team gaming nights, and meeting rituals that involve more whiteboards than actual meetings.

We tested ergonomics by having ten people type away while a pretend product owner read a generated backlog aloud. The result: a mix of laughter, groans, and occasional deep thinking about whether coffee counts as a cognitive booster. If your team has ten lefties and one right-handed hero, the modular design makes it simpler to assign the odd one to a segment compatible with their setup. But we still recommend adding personal keyboard trays for comfort.

Note: This desk is not a substitute for a proper ergonomic assessment. If you have chronic back pain or a spine that refuses to cooperate with a group desk, consider a dedicated ergonomic chair and independent workstation configuration alongside the Eternal desk.

## Engineering and Design Notes
The design team used a tongue-and-groove connector system to minimize gap lines and maintain visual continuity. The joint tolerances are tight, but not ridiculous; this means assembly is predictable and the desk does not present unexpected misalignment after heavy use. The top's laminate is selected to reduce scratches and wear in the exact spots where elbows will inevitably rest during brainstorming marathons.
