## Care, Handling, and Display Tips

Display is the dream and care is the art. Here are a few practical tips to keep your 12-card foil lot pristine:

- Handle with clean hands and avoid nail tips that could scratch the foil layer. If you have long nails, consider using gloves during handling.
- Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading and foil bloom. If you must display, use a glass case with UV-protective coating.
- Use acid-free card sleeves and keep the cards in a temperature-stable environment. Sudden changes in temperature can cause curling or warping.
- If you want to frame them, use a matte frame with non-reflective glass to keep the shine in the right place under display lighting.
