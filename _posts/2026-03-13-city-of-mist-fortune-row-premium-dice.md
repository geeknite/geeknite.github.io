## Feel and Balance: How Do They Roll in Actual Play?

The real test for any premium dice set is playability. City of Mist’s vibe leans into the mythic bajo—the everyday person who has a secret ancient power, not a full-on sorcerer. The dice should facilitate tense moments rather than distract you with glitz. Fortune Row meets this test most of the time. The edges are chamfered with care, which matters when you’re rolling repeatedly in a single scene. The balance is generally good, with V-rolls and mid-range outcomes that feel satisfying rather than “just okay.” The resin composition tends to produce a satisfying tilt to the right or left, depending on the batch, which can influence the feel of a roll when you’re deciding between bluff, pry, or escalate in a confrontation scene.

Durability is not just about surviving a drop test; it’s about surviving the tempo of a live game. Fortune Row dice typically demonstrate solid longevity, resisting micro-scratches and maintaining their color saturation after repeated rolls on a variety of surfaces. If your gaming nights involve a cat, a spilled mug, or a particularly dramatic tumble off a table, these dice are built to withstand the drama without turning into a collection of dull, lifeless orbs.

## Comfort, Longevity, and Practical Considerations

Premium dice are an investment, and like any investment, you want to know what you’re getting beyond the glow of the photos. Here are some practical considerations based on our testing:

- Cleaning and maintenance: Resin dice can attract fingerprints and dust more readily than metal or gem dice. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth after you finish a session keeps them looking like new for photos and for the next session.
- Compatibility with play surfaces: If your table isn’t perfectly flat, a heavier dice set may produce more dramatic rolls on the edge of a hardcover book or a coffee table. Fortune Row’s heft helps you keep a controlled roll, but you’ll still want a soft surface if you’re worried about table chatter or accidental chucks into the air during a climactic moment.
- Storage: The premium presentation is part of the charm, so a matching dice tray or velvet pouch helps preserve the look and feel. If you’re traveling to a con or a gaming night, consider a protective case that keeps the finish pristine and your table-mates impressed before you even say a word.
