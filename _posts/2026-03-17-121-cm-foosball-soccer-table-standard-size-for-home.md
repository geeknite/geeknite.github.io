## Maintenance and Care: Easy Mode vs Expert Mode
Maintenance is not glamorous, but it matters more than most people admit until they find themselves mid-game with a sticky ball and a wobbly table. Here are some practical tips:
- Clean regularly: Wipe the surface after play sessions to keep friction predictable and extend the life of the field printing.
- Lubrication: If you’re using wooden rods, a touch of silicone spray on the joints occasionally can keep things moving without squeaks. Avoid oil-based lubricants on MDF surfaces; they can seep and degrade the finish.
- Inspect and replace: Check the rods for bending, the players for cracks, and the leg levelers for reliability. A small maintenance habit now saves a big rebuild later.
- Cover when not in use: A table cover protects from dust and sunlight, preserving the finish and maintainable look.
