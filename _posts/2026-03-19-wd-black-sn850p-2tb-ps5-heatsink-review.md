## Design and Installation Notes

WD kept the design clean: a single M.2 2280 form factor SSD with an integrated heatsink. The heatsink adds some bulk, but not enough to interfere with the PS5's internal bay.

### Installation steps in the PS5

1. Power down and unplug your PS5.
2. Remove the side cover.
3. Locate the Expansion Slot cover and remove the screw.
4. Insert the SN850P into the M.2 slot with the heatsink oriented so it doesn’t collide with the side panel.
5. Secure with the screw that ships with the PS5 expansion kit or the included screw if WD provides one.
6. Reinstall the side cover and boot up. The PS5 will recognize the drive, and you’ll be asked to format the SSD.
