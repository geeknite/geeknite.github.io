## Ingredients and Preparation

Sugar Cake is made with the usual suspects: flour, eggs, butter, and sugar—lots and lots of sugar. But the magic lies in its preparation. Here’s how you can make your very own Sugar Cake at home:

### Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 cup butter (because why not?)
- 4 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking powder
- A pinch of salt

### Preparation

1. **Preheat the Oven**: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). If your oven is anything like mine, it takes forever, so get this step done first.

2. **Mix Dry Ingredients**: In a bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, powdered sugar, baking powder, and salt. Try not to eat the mixture—yet.

3. **Cream Butter and Sugar**: In another bowl, cream the butter until it's as smooth as a baby's bottom. Gradually add the sugar and keep mixing until it's fluffy.

4. **Add Eggs and Vanilla**: Crack those eggs and add them to the butter-sugar mixture one at a time. Add the vanilla extract and mix until combined. Resist the temptation to drink the batter.

5. **Combine Everything**: Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients and mix until you have a smooth batter. This is where the magic happens.

6. **Bake**: Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes. Use the toothpick test to check if it’s done—poke the cake, and if the toothpick comes out clean, it's ready.

7. **Cool and Serve**: Let the cake cool for a bit before slicing. Serve with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of ice cream for extra awesomeness.
