## What is City of Mist and The Secrets of the City?
City of Mist is a tabletop RPG that sits at the intersection of detective noir and mythic grandeur. Players become operatives in a city where mythos and reality shimmer at the edges of perception. The Secrets of the City is a standalone supplement that expands the core experience: new mythoi (the waking legends behind each mask), new secrets that drive plot hooks, and GM tools designed to pull players deeper into the tangled alleys of their city. The PDF claims to deliver a more modular approach to storytelling, with modular encounter seeds, new factions, and ready-made scenarios that can slot into a campaign with minimal prep, which is the dream if you are running a con-artist masquerade or a vigilante romance night at the local coffee shop turned crime-lounge.

### What's inside the PDF?
From the cover to the back matter, The Secrets of the City aims to give you:
- A handful of new mythoi with revised mechanics and flavor variants.
- Expanded Secret Cards and their narrative implications, providing both players and GMs with more texture for their masks.
- GM tips for running city-wide arcs that feel cohesive rather than a disconnected sequence of scenes.
- A toolbox of narrative prompts and conflict engines designed to punch up both investigative threads and mythic reveals.
- Optional rules for urban dungeon design, if you want a pseudo-heroic heist that still feels like a meandering stroll through a labyrinth of myths.

The PDF also uses the City of Mist’s beloved visual language—slick neon, noir silhouettes, and an obsession with the city as a living character. If you loved the core book’s vibe, you’ll feel like you are slipping into a familiar coat that somehow fits a size larger when you reach for this expansion. The world-building remains handsomely consistent, but the Secrets of the City adds a sheen of depth via carefully curated prompts and a few smartly designed mechanics.

## Mechanics and Content: What Has Been Tweaked or Expanded?
### Mythoi Expanded
One of the core pleasures of City of Mist is tapping a mythic identity to guide your choices. The Secrets of the City adds several expanded mythoi with fresh flavor variants and tie-ins to existing city factions. The mechanics around how a mythoi interacts with your mask’s public persona receive some polish, making it easier to weave mythic themes into the investigative beats rather than shoehorning a divine reveal at the end of a chase scene.
- Each new mythoi comes with a suggested set of Secrets (narrative hooks), a few recommended relationships with factions, and a handful of signature moves that can be used to sculpt scenes on the fly.
- The expanded mythoi are designed to be adaptable; you can easily piggyback them onto existing PCs or NPCs who have developed an undesired mystique around them.

### Secrets and Clues Toolkit
The Secrets of the City provides an expanded toolkit for players and GMs alike. There are new secret cards with prompts that encourage more dynamic reveals and twist possibilities. The payoff is a session that feels less like a mechanical puzzle and more like a living nightmare masked by a smile. A well-placed clue can ripple through a campaign, changing alliances, shifting the mood of a villain, or tipping the scales of a case in progress.

### GM Tools: Bridges and Barriers
The GM section includes a few additional hooks for running longer arcs. There are ready-to-run “City Arcs” that can be dropped into a single night or spooled out into a longer campaign. These arcs are designed to feel cohesive, offering a sense of place, motive, and consequence that makes the city feel alive rather than a backdrop to your players’ antics.
- Encounter seeds are detailed enough to save prep time but flexible enough to adjust to player choices.
- There are guidance notes on tone management: how to balance noir grit with mythic awe so you don’t tip into melodrama or beat-level incongruity.

### Balance and Power: What’s Drastically Different?
The Secrets of the City doesn’t flip the core power calculus on its head. If you are chasing a big power spike, you may be disappointed; if you are chasing a structured, character-driven narrative with a noir pulse, you’ll likely be satisfied. The supplement leans more into narrative leverage and story momentum rather than adding a slew of overpowering abilities. In practice, this keeps the game accessible to new players while giving veterans room to experiment with more complex mythic-layered storytelling.

## Play Experience: How It Feels at the Table
### Thematic Atmosphere
The city in City of Mist is a character in itself. The Secrets of the City leans into this with scene-setting prompts, atmospheric cues, and a set of “city secrets” that create a sense of place. When you present a location that has an urban legend tied to a mythoi, the session tends to ratchet up in tension. Players lean into their masks more readily because there are tangible consequences to every reveal, and every clue has a shadow side.

### Storytelling Flow
In practice, the PDFs’ suggested arcs encourage a three-act rhythm: establish the mystery through investigation, escalate the mythic conflict with a reveal that reframes the stakes, and resolve with a consequence that alters the city’s balance. The pacing tips are useful, especially for GMs who struggle with keeping the “where is this going?” momentum alive while juggling multiple players’ goals. The Secrets of the City offers cues to shepherd scenes toward these milestones without forcing a pre-scripted path.

### Group Dynamics and Roleplay
For players who relish character-driven sessions, this supplement shines. It provides prompts to deepen relationships with factions, spur rivalries between mythoi, and create personal stakes tied to the city’s past. For groups who chase fast-paced action above character consequences, it still offers hooks and combat-ready moments; you just may want to lean more heavily on the “narrative consequences” side of the table to keep tension high without grinding into combat repetition.

### How to Run It in a Session
- Start with a strong hook that ties to a local mythoi or a city secret. A well-crafted hook anchors players to the setting and motivates them to engage with both the mask and the myth.
- Use the Secrets toolkit to seed at least two narrative threads: one personal (character-focused) and one city-wide (location or faction oriented). This dual-track approach helps balance private character arcs with public stakes.
- Roll with the GM tools. The arcs are designed to be flexible; don’t be afraid to adjust on the fly if a scene requires a different emphasis—scale the tension, shift the mood, and let the city respond to player choices.
- End with a consequence that hints at future secrets. City of Mist loves a good tease; conclude your session with a reveal that redefines what the players think is true about their city and their masks.
