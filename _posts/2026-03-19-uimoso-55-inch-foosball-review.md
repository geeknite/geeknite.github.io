## Maintenance and Durability: Keeping the Party Running Year After Year

Durability is not sexy in product descriptions, but it is the quiet backbone of a successful foosball experience. The Uimoso is designed to withstand the occasional accidental spill, a rogue beer bump, and the systematic abuse of enthusiastic players who forget their own strength. The surface is easy to clean; a quick pass with a damp microfiber cloth keeps the green felt looking fresh and prevents staining from snacks near the play zone. To keep the rods functioning smoothly over time, routine lubrication is recommended. A light lubricating oil applied to the rod bearings every few months will preserve the glide and preserve the feel that players love. If you notice friction or squeaking, address it sooner rather than later; a small maintenance habit today prevents a big repair bill tomorrow.

### Longevity tips

- Cover the table when not in use to protect from dust and sunlight, which can fade the felt over time.
- Check leg levelness occasionally and adjust as needed; a slightly uneven surface can skew shots and ruin a hot streak.
- Inspect the ball return and replace any worn balls promptly; a scuffed ball is a stubborn foe to flawless play.
- Store spare parts or alternatives in a dedicated box to avoid hunting down a missing bolt during a late night rematch.
