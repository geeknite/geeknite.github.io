- Placement matters: position the feeder where birds feel safe from big raptors but close enough to let you enjoy the show. A tree that’s not too exposed to the wind will minimize seed loss and maximize bird drama.
- Seed choice: mix locally grown seeds with a touch of nyjer to attract a wider range of guests. The feeder’s ports accommodate both without clogging.

- Cleaning regimen: avoid harsh chemicals. A simple rinse with warm water and a soft brush will do, preserving the seeds and the wood’s natural character.
