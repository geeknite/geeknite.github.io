## What is Skip-Bo?
[![Skip-Bo](https://i.imgur.com/XYZabc.jpg)]({{ site.constants.wsib }}skip-bo)

Skip-Bo is a card game for 2 to 6 players where the objective is to be the first to get rid of all your cards. The game involves strategy, a bit of luck, and, of course, the ability to mess up your opponent's plans.

### Components
The game box includes a deck of 162 cards, instructions, and a whole lot of potential for chaos. The cards are divided into numbered cards (1-12) and special Skip-Bo cards, which act as wild cards.

## The Gameplay
### Setting Up
Setting up Skip-Bo is as simple as shuffling the deck and dealing cards. Each player gets a stockpile of 30 cards (or fewer if you're playing with more people). The top card of each stockpile is flipped up to start the game.

### Turns and Actions
Each turn, players draw cards from the draw pile and play them to build up sequential stacks from 1 to 12. The goal is to use the cards in your hand and discard pile strategically to get rid of your stockpile cards.

### Strategy
The key to winning Skip-Bo lies in managing your stockpile and using the Skip-Bo cards wisely. You can block your opponents or prepare the perfect sequence to clear your stockpile in one go.

**Pro Tip**: Don't be afraid to hold onto those Skip-Bo cards. They can save you in a pinch or completely ruin your opponent's turn.
