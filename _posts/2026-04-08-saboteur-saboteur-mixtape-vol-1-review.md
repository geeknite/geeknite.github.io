## Authenticity and verification
Here is a quick checklist to help you decide if a sealed Saboteur Mixtape Vol.1 is the real thing:
- Inspect the shrink wrap: tight on all edges, no signs of reseal or re-taping.
- Check the back cover for the correct barcode and the printed text alignment.
- Look for the hype sticker location and content; authentic copies keep the sticker in its original place.
- Examine the inner booklet for printing quality and accurate credits, if included.
- Look on the disc for a runout or matrix number that indicates a legitimate pressing plant.
- Compare to reference photos from trusted sellers or established collector communities.

If in doubt, ask questions about the edition number and the printing date. A credible seller will have no problem sharing this information. The goal is to establish that the item you are buying is indeed the sealed Vol. 1 edition produced in France in 2022 and not a later reissue or a counterfeit attempt that rides on the name.
