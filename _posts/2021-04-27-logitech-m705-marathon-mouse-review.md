## Part 3: The Legendary Battery Life

### Three Years on Two AA Batteries?

Logitech's claim of **up to 3 years** battery life sounds like marketing nonsense, but it's surprisingly accurate for many users.

**âœ… VERIFIED TESTING:**
After 18 months of daily use (8+ hours/day):
- Original AA batteries still functioning
- Battery indicator shows ~40% remaining
- No noticeable performance degradation
- Auto-sleep mode working as designed

**How They Achieve This:**

**Power-Saving Technology:**
1. **Auto-Sleep Mode**: Mouse enters sleep after 5 minutes of inactivity
2. **Instant Wake**: Movement or click wakes instantly
3. **Manual Power Switch**: Turn completely off when not in use for days
4. **Efficient Sensor**: Laser sensor uses less power than high-DPI optical sensors
5. **Smart Transmission**: Reduces wireless transmission frequency intelligently

**Real-World Battery Life Estimates:**

| Usage Pattern | Expected Battery Life |
|--------------|---------------------|
| **Light Use** (2-3 hours/day) | 3+ years |
| **Moderate Use** (4-6 hours/day) | 2-3 years |
| **Heavy Use** (8+ hours/day) | 18-24 months |
| **Power User** (12+ hours/day) | 12-18 months |
| **Power Switch Off Nightly** | Maximize lifespan |

**Battery Monitoring:**
- LED indicator light flashes when batteries low
- Logitech Options software shows percentage
- Plenty of warning before complete depletion
- Standard AA batteries easy to replace anywhere
