## Assembly and setup
- Step-by-step instructions included, plus diagrams that look like they were drawn by someone who loves Tetris
- Tools: usually included; if not, a basic screwdriver will do the job
- Time: 20-45 minutes for first-timers, depending on the level of baby distraction and the crispness of your coffee
- Tips: measure doorways before buying; consider room layout and where you actually want to gate off space rather than where it looks coolest on the diagram

### Common setup pitfalls
- Over-tightening screws can warp panels — back off one notch and re-check for smooth operation
- Misaligning hinges can create a gate that sticks; take a breath, loosen, realign, and re-tighten in a star pattern
- Forgetting to test gate movement with a test toy; toddlers love to test what adults consider safe, so simulate a wiggle with a plush toy on the other side

## How to choose the right playpen
- Size: measure your space, including the path you want to walk through with a full cup of coffee
- Material: metal vs. plastic; metal is typically sturdier and looks a touch more grown-up
- Type: is it a playpen plus gate or a separate gate; dual-function devices can be happier, or less flexible depending on your layout
- Portability: if you move often, consider a model with light-weight panels and a simple assembly that doesn’t require an engineering degree
- Price vs. value: consider how often you’ll actually use it and whether the extra pieces are worth it for your space
