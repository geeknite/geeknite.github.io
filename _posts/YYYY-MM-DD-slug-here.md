## Durability, weather resistance, and daily weather drama
Outdoor furniture faces a daily gauntlet: sun, rain, wind, and the occasional accidental tumble of a garden gnome. The pinewood used in this set is selected for outdoor use, but the care routine matters as much as the wood selection. A good sealer or protective finish helps prevent moisture intrusion, warping, and those tiny cracks that show up after a few seasons and give your furniture a character arc that says I have stories to tell if only the windy evenings could talk.

- Weather resistance: most pinewood sets withstand seasonal weather well enough when maintained with the right sealants. If you live in a region with heavy humidity or salt air, you’ll want a maintenance schedule that includes resealing every 1–2 years and bringing cushions indoors during prolonged rain.
- Wear and tear: cushions may show signs of use in high-traffic zones, but fabrics designed for outdoor use track weather resistance and UV stability. Expect a bit of fading in sun-exposed areas, which can add a vintage vibe if you lean into it with tasteful decor choices.
