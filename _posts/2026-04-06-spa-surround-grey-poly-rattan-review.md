### Maintenance Tips: Keep It Looking Fresh

- Regularly wipe down the weave with a damp cloth to remove dust and pollen. A soft-bristle brush can help remove stubborn buildup without scratching the surface.
- Use a mild soap solution for tougher stains, then pat dry with a clean towel to expedite drying.
- Remove cushion covers for washing according to care labels. Air-dry completely before reassembly to avoid mildew.
- For winter storage or prolonged rainy seasons, consider storing cushions indoors or under a weatherproof cover to extend their life.
- Inspect the frame joints every few months. Tighten any loose screws and check for wobble. A little maintenance goes a long way in keeping your throne rock-steady.
