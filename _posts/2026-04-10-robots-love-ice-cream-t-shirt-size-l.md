## Care and Longevity
### Cleaning Guidance
Care instructions, broadly: wash inside out in cold water, use a mild detergent, and avoid bleach. Drying on low heat or air-dry is recommended; heat can cause minor shrinkage and potential cracking in the print. The goal is to keep the color vibrant without turning the robot into a sticker that peels mid-conversation.

### Longevity and Maintenance
Expect the print to remain crisp after about 20-25 washes with normal use. If you’re a heavy-handed washer or you’ve got a child who insists on playing “robotic laundry mode” every Sunday, you may see some slight fading after a dozen extra cycles. A gentle wash cycle and turning the garment inside-out before washing should extend the life of both fabric and print. The fabric itself holds up nicely to everyday wear; you won’t feel like you’re wearing an old mattress pad when you’re out on the town.
