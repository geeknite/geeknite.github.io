## Maintenance and care tips
- Clean periodically with mild soap and water; avoid harsh chemical cleaners that might degrade wicker or cushion fabric.
- Use a soft-bristle brush to remove dirt from the weaving without snagging threads.
- Protect cushions from heavy moisture; store them in a dry space or use breathable covers when not in use.
- For stain management, blot spills immediately; don’t rub, as it might push the stain deeper into the fabric.
- Check the hardware occasionally and tighten screws as necessary. A loose chair leg is a party foul that ruins dinner and dance-worthy footwork alike.
- Consider a seasonal check: reseal or re-treat the wicker if your climate demands it, and rotate cushions to even out sun exposure to prevent uneven fading.
