## Installation Guide (A Lightweight Tutorial)
1. Clear a stable surface for the rack. A desk or tabletop with a generous, level area works best.
2. Attach the mounting rails to the side posts according to the included instructions. Ensure the holes align with your devices’ mounting pattern.
3. Place heavier devices toward the bottom to lower the center of gravity and reduce tipping risk.
4. Thread data and power cables along the rails using zip ties or Velcro straps. Leave enough slack for future changes.
5. Secure all devices with the appropriate screws and washers. If your devices use cage nuts, install those before mounting the equipment.
6. Plug in your power and data lines, then power up to verify POST (Power-On Self-Test) if applicable. If you see a blinking light on a router or switch, you’re probably doing it right.
7. Tidy up cables with additional ties or channels to keep airflow clear and the workspace tidy.
