## What is Cerebria The Inside World Anyway

Cerebria is a game about internal politics played on a large modular board representing the inner world of the person who is playing. Two sides, the Conscious and the Subconscious, maneuver Emotion minions across provinces to influence territories that determine victory points. The flavor text is lush, the art is dramatic, and the components were designed with a level of heft that makes you believe every decision matters—even your fly fishing for Victories in the most dramatic of moments.

The base game comes with a surprising amount of depth right out of the box, including a thoughtful (if intimidating) rulebook, a stack of decks that feel like they were curated by a therapist and a game designer who obsesses over tempo, and board sections that click together with satisfying clicks when you push your strategies forward. The core rules emphasize simultaneous planning and reactive play, with an emphasis on controlling emotional currents rather than merely grinding your way to victory through numbers on a sheet.
