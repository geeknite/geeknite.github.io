## How It Plays and What Makes the Anniversary Edition Different
UNO remains one of the simplest and most approachable party games around. The core mechanics are unchanged: players try to get rid of cards by matching color or number, while special action cards add spice. The 50th Anniversary Edition does not attempt to rework the wheel; instead it offers a refined wheel that rolls smoother and looks nicer while still turning the same way.

### Rules overview for the uninitiated
- Each player starts with a hand of seven cards.
- The play proceeds in a clockwise direction and players must match the previous card by color or number.
- Special cards include Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, Wild, and Wild Draw Four, each with strategic potential and occasional chaos value.
- The first player to discard all cards wins the round and earns points equal to the total of the cards left in opponents hands. The 50th Anniversary Edition keeps this skeleton intact while providing a few optional house rules for big party nights.
