## Durability, care, and long-term maintenance

### Stain resistance and longevity

Yellow patterns often worry about staining; this rug, however, seems to balance style with practicality. The pattern’s complexity helps hide minor scuffs and everyday spills, especially if you’re careful with tea, coffee, and that suspicious stray glitter from a weekend craft project. For more intense messes, treat the stain promptly with a gentle cleaner, blot gently, and avoid harsh scrubbing that could damage the pile. With proper care, you’ll enjoy the look for years, or at least until your cat discovers a new scratching post that doubles as a fashion critique.

### Cleaning routine and tips

Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting to avoid pulling fibers. For spills, blot, don’t rub; use a mild cleaner in a small inconspicuous area first to check colorfastness. If you’re feeling fancy, a professional cleaning every couple of years can refresh the fibers and restore the vibrancy without risking the pattern’s integrity. Also consider rotating the rug 180 degrees every few months to even out wear and keep the pattern looking fresh from every corner of the room.
