## What is Legacy of Thracks?

Legacy of Thracks is a strategic board game where players assume the roles of powerful leaders, each with unique abilities, striving to control territories, gather resources, and achieve ultimate domination. The All In Edition brings together all expansions and extra content, delivering a colossal gaming experience.

### Components

The game boasts high-quality components that are as aesthetically pleasing as they are functional. From beautifully designed maps and cards to detailed miniatures representing heroes and monsters, Legacy of Thracks is a treat for the eyes and hands.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Legacy of Thracks feels like preparing for a grand quest. Lay out the map, distribute resources and cards, and position your miniatures. You might need a map to navigate the setup itself, but once ready, the adventure begins!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn consists of distinct phases:
1. **Planning Phase**: Players plan their moves and actions.
2. **Action Phase**: Players execute their strategies, which can include moving, attacking, gathering resources, and using special abilities.
3. **Resolution Phase**: Effects and outcomes of the actions are resolved, and victory points are awarded.

### Combat

Combat is a blend of strategy and luck. Players must balance their forces and use their leader’s abilities to outwit opponents. Did you plan a devastating attack only to roll a 1? Welcome to Thracks, where even the best-laid plans can be thwarted by fate.

## The Adventure

### Scenarios

The All In Edition introduces diverse scenarios that keep the game fresh and exciting. Each scenario offers unique challenges and objectives, ranging from rescuing captured allies to defeating monstrous foes.

### Characters

Play as various leaders, each with distinct abilities and strategies. From the cunning Thravian Spy to the mighty Thrackian Warlord, each character adds a unique flavor to the game.
