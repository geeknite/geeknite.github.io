## What is Tower of the Lich Empress?

Tower of the Lich Empress is a cooperative tabletop game where players take on the roles of daring adventurers aiming to dethrone the sinister Lich Empress. With intricate mechanics, beautifully designed components, and a narrative that would make even J.R.R. Tolkien raise an eyebrow, this game promises an adventure like no other.

### Components

The game comes with a treasure trove of components:

- **Miniatures**: Detailed figures representing the heroes, monsters, and of course, the dreaded Lich Empress herself.
- **Cards**: Spells, items, quests, and more are all represented with beautifully illustrated cards.
- **Game Board**: A multi-layered board depicting the treacherous tower and its various levels.
- **Tokens and Markers**: To track health, magic, and other vital stats.

## Setting Up

Setting up Tower of the Lich Empress is like preparing for a grand heist. First, lay out the board, place the miniatures, and distribute the cards. It might take a bit longer than your average game, but trust me, the anticipation is half the fun. Pro tip: Keep snacks handy to avoid a mutiny from your party.

## Gameplay

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and perform actions based on their character's abilities. Actions include moving, attacking, casting spells, and completing objectives. Coordination and strategy are key, as the tower is filled with traps and the Lich Empress's minions.

### Combat

Combat in this game is a mix of strategy and luck. Players roll dice to determine the success of their attacks and spells, with various modifiers adding depth to the system. It's like D&D on steroids, but without the risk of your dungeon master flipping the table.

## The Story

### Quests and Plot Twists

The narrative of Tower of the Lich Empress is rich and engaging. The game comes with multiple quests and plot twists that keep players on their toes. One moment you might be battling skeletons in the dungeon, and the next, solving a riddle to unlock a hidden treasure.

### Characters

Players can choose from a variety of characters, each with unique abilities and backstories. Whether you're a brooding sorcerer or a boisterous warrior, there's a role for everyone. And yes, there's even a cheeky bard to make your life more... musical.
