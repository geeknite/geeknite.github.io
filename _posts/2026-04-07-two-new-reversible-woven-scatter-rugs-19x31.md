## Cleaning and Care
Care is not a drama. Vacuum regularly on a gentle setting, using a nozzle attachment to avoid snagging the fibers. For spills, blot with a clean cloth and apply a mild detergent if needed. Do not rub aggressively, as this can push stains deeper into the fibers. If you encounter a stubborn spot, test a small inconspicuous area first, then treat accordingly. The reversible design gives you a fallback plan for the inevitable messes that life throws at your floor. In most cases a simple cleaning routine will bring the rug back to life without requiring a laboratory.
