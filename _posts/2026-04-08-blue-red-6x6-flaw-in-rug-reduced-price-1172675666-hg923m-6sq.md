## Durability and Maintenance: Real World, Real World-Ready

Nobody wants to baby a rug like it’s a fragile relic from a museum you’ll never visit because you’d rather be speed-running a new game. This rug is designed for everyday life. Dirt emerges easily from traffic and spills—think coffee, soda, and snacking mishaps—are manageable with a simple blot and light cleaning. Here are practical tips for keeping the rug fresh:

- Vacuum weekly with a standard vacuum head. Avoid aggressive beater bars that could pull loose fibers.
- Wipe up spills promptly with a dry cloth. For tough stains, a mild cleaning solution tested on a hidden corner is your best friend.
- Rotate the rug every few months to minimize wear patterns from chair wheels and the path from your desk to the coffee machine.
- Use a rug pad to improve underfoot comfort, reduce bunching, and help with noise dampening. A good pad is the unsung hero in any rug setup.
