## The Basics

Lumberjacks with Rocket Launchers is a competitive board game designed for 2-6 players. The game revolves around lumberjacks who, for reasons unknown to anyone, have decided to take up rocket launchers. The objective is simple: chop down trees, avoid getting blown up, and cause as much chaos as possible.

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully illustrated board featuring various forest locations, such as Timberland, Explosive Grove, and Boomwood. The components include miniatures of lumberjacks, trees, and rocket launchers, along with a deck of cards containing events, items, and objectives. The artwork is top-notch, capturing the ridiculousness of the game's premise perfectly.

**Fun Fact**: The game includes a special "Rocket-Boosted Chainsaw" card that will make you question your life choices every time it appears.

## Gameplay

Lumberjacks with Rocket Launchers is all about strategy, chaos, and laughter. Players must chop down trees to gather resources while simultaneously trying to blow up their opponents with rocket launchers. Each player takes on the role of a lumberjack with unique abilities, ranging from the "Explosive Expert" to the "Tree Hugger".

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions like chopping down trees, launching rockets, and using special abilities.
2. **Event Phase**: Draw event cards that introduce new challenges or opportunities for mayhem.
3. **Chaos Phase**: Rockets fly, trees fall, and lumberjacks run for cover. Prepare for absolute chaos!

### Winning and Losing

To win, players must gather the most resources by the end of the game while avoiding getting blown up too many times. If a lumberjack is blown up thrice, they're out of the game. The last lumberjack standing or the one with the most resources wins. Simple, yet explosively fun.

**Pro Tip**: Always keep the "Tree Shield" card handy. It won't save you from a direct hit, but it will give you a fighting chance.
