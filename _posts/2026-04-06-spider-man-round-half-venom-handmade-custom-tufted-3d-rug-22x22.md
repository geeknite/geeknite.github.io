## Maintenance and care: keeping the look sharp
Cleaning a tufted rug is easier than teaching your cat to fetch. Here are a few practical tips:
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to prevent fiber pull. A handheld vacuum works well if you don’t own a full-size model.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper into the pile.
- For stubborn stains, use a mild detergent diluted in water and a soft cloth. Test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and to avoid constant traffic patterns.
- If the fibers start to look a bit fluffy, a quick brush with a soft-bristle brush will even things out.

Safety-wise, keep the rug away from direct heat sources and open flames. The last thing you want is a heroic blaze in the lair. It’s not a dramatic ending; it’s just bad for business.
