### Cleaning and maintenance when life happens
Spills happen. A dash of coffee, a splash of juice, a rogue salsa spot from a patio dinner gone too festive—these are the moments that test outdoor furniture resilience. Fortunately, the set responds well to routine care. The recommended routine is simple: wipe, dry, and store cushions in dry weather. If you’re a meticulous warden of the patio environment, consider swapping in quick-dry cushions that accelerate drying after a surprise shower.

## Setup, maintenance, and accessories
### Quick-start guide to get you lounging
1. Unbox and lay out the modules in your preferred arrangement.
2. Attach the corner piece, if applicable, ensuring the connectors click into place snugly.
3. Place the coffee table in the center or off to the side for easier foot traffic.
4. Add cushions and throws. If you want extra pop, mix in a few accent pillows of complementary color.

### Maintenance tips from the Geeknite desk of common sense
- Rotate cushions regularly to ensure even wear and UV exposure across all surfaces.
- Use a breathable cover during rainy seasons to minimize moisture buildup.
- Clean with a mild soap solution and rinse thoroughly; never use harsh solvents that could degrade fabric or weave.
- Check the joints occasionally for looseness and re-tighten as needed to preserve the integrity of the modular system.
