## Design and Build Quality

### Sleek and Stylish

Let's talk about the design. Apple has always been known for its sleek and minimalist design philosophy, and the AirPods Pro 2nd Gen are no exception. These earbuds are tiny, lightweight, and come with that signature Apple polish. The in-ear design ensures a comfortable fit, and the silicone tips come in three sizes to cater to all ear shapes and sizes. Plus, they come with a MagSafe charging case, which means you can snap them onto a MagSafe charger and watch the magic happen.

### Durability

Don't let their small size fool you! The AirPods Pro 2nd Gen are built to last. They boast an IPX4 water resistance rating, meaning they can handle a bit of sweat and rain. However, we don't recommend taking them for a swim. Save the synchronized swimming routine for your waterproof speakers.

## Battery Life

### All-Day Listening

Battery life is always a concern with wireless earbuds, but the AirPods Pro 2nd Gen won't let you down. You get up to 4.5 hours of listening time on a single charge with ANC enabled, and up to 6 hours without ANC. The MagSafe charging case provides multiple additional charges, giving you a total of 24 hours of listening time. So, whether you're on a long-haul flight or just binging your favorite series, these earbuds have got you covered.

Whether you're taking a call on your iPhone, watching a movie on your iPad, or joining a virtual meeting on your Mac, the AirPods Pro 2nd Gen will automatically switch to the active device.

### Spatial Audio

Whether it's the roar of a dragon in your favorite fantasy series or the subtle notes of a classical piece, Spatial Audio adds an extra layer of immersion that will leave you in awe.
