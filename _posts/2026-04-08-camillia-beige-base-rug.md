## Installation, Care, and Longevity

### Placement Tips
Installations with any rug benefit from a little pre-planning. For this rug, consider a non-slip rug pad to keep the base anchored, especially if you have wooden or tile floors that love to steal your rug’s attention. A pad means less shifting, less curling at the edges, and fewer opportunities for your cat to invent a new scratch-escape route in the living room.

### Cleaning Routine
Vacuum weekly to keep the base clean and free of dust, crumbs, and the occasional seed from your rebellious snack stash. If you have pets, you’ll want to do spot cleaning as needed. A mild detergent applied in a small test area is usually sufficient for most everyday messes. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that could degrade the weave or cause chemical reactions with the color tones. If you ever think you’ve found a stain that is “mystery-level,” remember the debugger’s motto: isolate, test, re-run.
