### Creating a Homebrew Stat Block
To run this model on a DnD table, a simple stat block that aligns with 5e is a good starting point. Here is a suggested homebrew CR 1/2 Orc Zombie (Undead) for quick use and easy adaptation. Use as a baseline and adjust to your party’s level and tone.

Orc Zombie (Undead)
- Armor Class 10 (natural armor)
- Hit Points 22 (4d8)
- Speed 20 ft
- STR 14, DEX 8, CON 12, INT 3, WIS 6, CHA 5
- Damage Immunities Poison
- Condition Immunities Exhaustion, Poisoned
- Senses Darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 9
- Languages Understands Orc and Common but cannot speak
- Challenge 1/2

### Actions
- Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6+2 bludgeoning.

Undead Fortitude
- If damage reduces the Orc Zombie to 0 HP, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 5) or remain at 1 HP.
