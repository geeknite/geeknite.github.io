## Maintenance Guide: Pro Tips to Extend Life

4) Cushion care: store cushions in a dry area when not using them for extended periods or during inclement weather.
5) Inspect hardware for rust or loosening screws and tighten as needed. A little DIY maintenance goes a long way.
