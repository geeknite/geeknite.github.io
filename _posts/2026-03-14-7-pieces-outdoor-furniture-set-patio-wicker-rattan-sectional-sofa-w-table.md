## Assembly, maintenance, and daily care

Maintenance is where you’ll see the practical side of the design shine through. The cushions have removable covers; wash them according to the label instructions, and you’ll be returning to a crisp look after a quick cycle. The wicker and frame require minimal upkeep: wipe down with mild soapy water and a soft cloth, and you’re done. A light coating of UV protection spray every season can help preserve color and reduce the risk of fading. If you live in an area with heavy rain or snow, consider storing cushions indoors or using waterproof covers during the off-season; this will extend the life of both cushions and the wicker frame.

Stains are not the end of the world here. If something spills, blot it quickly with a clean cloth and a mild cleaning solution. Do not scrub aggressively; the goal is to preserve the weave rather than to create a distressed “antique” look that only exists in marketing photos.

A note on cushions: if you’re prone to long backyard marathons (the kind where you binge-watch a show and forget there’s sunlight), you’ll love how easily the cushions compress and rebound. They hold shape well, and you’ll notice the color remains consistent after months of sun exposure, which is a small but mighty win.
