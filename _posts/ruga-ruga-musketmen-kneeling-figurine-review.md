## Historical Significance

The Ruga-Ruga were irregular troops in East Africa during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. These mercenaries played pivotal roles in various conflicts, often hired by powerful leaders such as Tippu Tip and Nyamwezi. Known for their distinctive attire and fierce combat skills, they remain a fascinating subject for historians.

### Why Kneeling?

You might wonder why this figurine captures a Ruga-Ruga musketman in a kneeling position. Historically, this stance was commonly used for stability and precision during battle. It’s not just a dramatic pose; it’s a tactical one. Think of it as the historical equivalent of a sniper taking a prone position.
