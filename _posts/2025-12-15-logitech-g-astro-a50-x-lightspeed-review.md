Comfort is key when it comes to gaming headsets, and the Astro A50 X doesn't disappoint. The ear cups are plush and breathable, so your ears won't feel like they're trapped in a sauna during those marathon gaming sessions.

If you're looking for crystal clear audio, the Astro A50 X has got you covered. The sound quality is top-notch, with deep bass and crisp highs that make you feel like you're right in the middle of the action. Whether you're sneaking around in _Fortnite_ or getting scared silly in _Resident Evil_, this headset delivers.

Immerse yourself in the game with Dolby Atmos and surround sound. The Astro A50 X creates a 3D audio environment that makes every game feel like a blockbuster movie.

Want to tweak your sound settings? The Astro Command Center software lets you customize audio profiles to suit your preferences. You can adjust the EQ, mic levels, and more to get the perfect sound for every game.
