## Maintenance and Care
A chair this stylish deserves some care to keep it looking like a star in the living room. Cleaning the Amour Black is straightforward: wipe with a soft, dry cloth to remove dust and use a slightly damp microfiber cloth for minor smudges. Avoid soaking the wood as water can cause the finish to swell and potentially alter the color a bit over time. For stubborn marks, a gentle wood cleaner designed for dark finishes can help without removing the stain or lightening the grain. Regularly check the joints for any looseness and tighten the screws as needed. A little maintenance goes a long way toward preserving both the aesthetics and the performance of the chair.

If you have children or pets, you might appreciate a clear-coat touch-up policy. The Amour Black finish holds up well to routine use, but chips can happen if you are a heavy-handed climber or have a small dog that thinks chairs are a launching pad for upper body acrobatics. In those cases, we recommend a simple touch-up kit and a clear sealant to prevent further chipping before it becomes a full-on furniture saga.
