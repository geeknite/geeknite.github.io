## Size and customization: custom-sized dreams or confident 8x10 must-haves

The big question is size. Waking up to a rug that’s too small is like installing a 1080p TV in a 4K cinema: you notice the limitations, and it hurts. Moonstone Mink offers:

- Custom sizes: order in a wide range of dimensions to fit odd rooms, alcoves, or that exact length necessary to wrap your couch in cosiness. Width and length can be specified to the centimeter.

- Standard size option (8x10): a versatile, reliable footprint for most living rooms, great under a coffee table or as a lounging area.

### Practical tips for choosing your size
- Sketch the space. Measure wall-to-wall to place the rug; ensure at least 6–12 inches of floor visible for balance; adjust as needed based on layout.
- Consider furniture placement: if you want the rug under a coffee table, let it extend at least 6–12 inches beyond the table edges.
- Traffic patterns: high-traffic rooms benefit from 8x10; for oddly shaped spaces, custom sizes are your friend.

### Ordering process and lead times

Custom options usually involve a measurement stage, color check, and a production window that varies by size. Expect lead times of a couple weeks for standard sizes, longer for truly bespoke dims. If you’re on a schedule, the 8x10 option is your best bet.

## Materials, feel, and the care kit

### Fiber chemistry and texture

The Moonstone Mink rug uses a PET-based synthetic designed to mimic mink’s sheen and plushness while staying vegan and affordable. Pile height sits at the plush end of the spectrum, providing a cushiony feel. Fibers are engineered for colorfastness and resistance to common rug foes like spills, pet hair, and coffee disasters.

### Backing and stability

Most Moonstone Mink rugs have a non-slip backing or are compatible with rug grippers. Stability matters in busy living rooms. If using on hard floors, pair with a rug pad to avoid creeping and maximize comfort.

### Cleaning and maintenance

Spills happen. Blot quickly, avoid aggressive scrubbing, use mild detergent with cold water for spot cleaning. For stubborn stains, a gentle rug shampoo, then thorough drying to prevent mold. Vacuum regularly on a gentle setting.

### Durability and wear in real life

Synthetic fur-like rugs hold up well in family life. They’re less prone to shedding than some wool blends and easier to clean than natural fibers. For kids or pets, regular vacuuming plus prompt spot treatment helps.

Edge curling: thicker piles can show slight curling along edges on smooth floors without pads. Use a grip pad to minimize.
