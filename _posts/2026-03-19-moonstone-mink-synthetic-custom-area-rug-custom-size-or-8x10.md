## Size and customization: custom-sized dreams or confident 8x10 must-haves

The big question when you’re planning a new rug is size. Waking up to a rug that’s too small is like installing a 1080p TV in a 4K cinema: visually okay, emotionally tragic. Moonstone Mink addresses sizing in two major ways:

- Custom sizes: The rug can be ordered in a wide range of dimensions to fit odd-shaped rooms, alcoves, or the exact length needed to wrap a couch in cosiness. Custom sizing lets you specify width and length to the centimeter (or at least to a decent decimal place) so your rug aligns with architectural quirks rather than fighting them.
- Standard size option (8x10): 8x10 remains the most common and versatile size for living rooms and family spaces. It provides a generous footprint under a coffee table, a comfortable lounging area, and enough rug to keep your toes warm during long gaming sessions.

### Practical tips for choosing your size

- Sketch the space: measure the wall-to-wall area where you want the rug to live. A rug that’s too large can crowd a small room, while a rug that’s too small can make furniture look like it was misplaced by a furniture elf. A good starting rule is to have at least 6–12 inches of floor visible around the rug for a balanced look, but this is adjustable based on your room’s layout.
- Consider furniture placement: if you want the rug under a coffee table, ensure the rug extends at least 6–12 inches beyond the table’s edges on all sides. That ensures your chair legs don’t escape into the void when you push back to stand up.
- Relative to traffic patterns: high-traffic rooms benefit from the 8x10 size, as it minimizes edge curling and keeps walking paths neat. Custom sizes excel in halls, entryways, or oddly shaped spaces where a standard rectangle just won’t cut it.

### Ordering process and lead times

Moonstone Mink’s custom options usually involve a brief measurement stage, a color check (to ensure the moonstone vibe is captured in the dye), and a production window that varies by size. Expect typical lead times to range from a couple of weeks for standard sizes to several weeks for truly custom dims. If you’re on a tight schedule, the 8x10 option is your best friend—often available faster and with a lower complexity factor for fulfillment.

## Materials, feel, and the care kit

### Fiber chemistry and texture

The Moonstone Mink rug uses a synthetic fiber (often PET-based) designed to mimic the sheen and plushness of mink without the ethical baggage or cost. The pile height is typically around the plush end of the spectrum—think a soft, cushiony layer that’s gratifying to step on with bare feet. The fibers are engineered for colorfastness and resistance to common rug foes: spills, pet hair, and coffee disasters.

### Backing and stability

Underneath, Moonstone Mink rugs usually feature a non-slip backing or are compatible with rug grippers. Stability matters in busy rooms where kids zoom around on scooters or where a goblin in socks performs interpretive dance on the living room rug. If you’re using it on a hard-surface floor, pair it with a proper rug pad to avoid creeping and to maximize comfort.

### Cleaning and maintenance

Life happens. Spills, muddy paws, and the occasional pizza crumb tumble can test any rug’s mettle. Moonstone Mink is designed for straightforward cleaning: blot spills quickly, avoid aggressive scrubbing, and use a mild detergent with cold water for spot cleaning. For more stubborn stains, a gentle rug shampoo can be used, followed by thorough drying to prevent mold growth. Regular vacuuming with a gentle setting keeps the fibers looking pristine without flattening the plush vibe.

Tip: never rely on hot water for stain removal; heat can set stains and shrink fibers in synthetic rugs. Keep a small stain-removal kit handy and label it “Rug Rescue Ops.”

### Durability and wear in real life

Synthetic fur-like rugs hold up well under typical family life. They’re less prone to shedding than some wool blends and easier to clean than high-pile natural fibers. If you’ve got kids who practice cartwheels in the living room, Moonstone Mink’s durable fibers are your ally. Pets? The fibers resist odors better than you might expect, especially if you commit to regular vacuuming and prompt spot treatment.
