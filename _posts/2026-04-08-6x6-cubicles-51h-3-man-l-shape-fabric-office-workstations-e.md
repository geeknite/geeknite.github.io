## Maintenance and care: keeping it fresh

Fabric panels like this deserve a simple care routine:

- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and stray coffee beans (they do decide to fall in odd places).
- Wipe surfaces with a mild cleaner; avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the fabric or the paint on the frames.
- Address spills promptly. A quick blot with a clean cloth is better than waiting until the stain becomes a character in your quarterly report.
- Inspect the frame screws periodically and re-tighten if you notice any wobble. The last thing you want is a wobbly cube that makes you feel like a cat on a keyboard during a sprint task.

With a little routine maintenance, these cubicles stay looking sharp and performing well, even when your crew treats them like a living lab for new ideas and questionable battle plans.
