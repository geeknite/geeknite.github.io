## Care and Maintenance Guidance
A rug that looks great for years requires a simple care routine. Here are practical steps tailored for a Maize Linen rug with a busy life:
- Vacuum regularly using a low-suction setting to avoid pulling fibers. High suction can snag edges on some fibers, which is a mood-killer for design fans.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper and increase the risk of pilling.
- Address pet accidents promptly with a gentle cleaner suitable for synthetic blends. Always test in an inconspicuous area first to ensure color stability.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and prevent a single high-traffic path from becoming an unwelcome feature.
- For deeper cleaning, consult a professional rug cleaner with experience in synthetic-blend rugs. A thorough once-a-year refresh can make the difference between “new rug vibe” and “still-new vibe.”
