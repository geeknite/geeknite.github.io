## Maintenance: Cleaning, Storage, and Longevity Tricks
Maintenance is mercifully boring—precisely what we like. The mat can be wiped with a damp cloth and a mild cleaner. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could degrade the surface. For storage, fold and tuck away in a dry place to prevent any moisture-related corrosion on the frame. If you live in a climate that loves humidity and drama, consider storing the trampoline with a breathable cover to discourage rust, mold, and the occasional dramatic crying session from your toddler when the toy is put away mid-play.

Inspect the assembly points regularly. If any bolt or connector shows signs of loosening, tighten it. It’s not a model rocket; you don’t want the thing to launch all by itself mid-jump. A quick quarterly check is enough to keep the bounce happy and safe for your little humans.
