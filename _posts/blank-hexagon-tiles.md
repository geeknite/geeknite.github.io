## What Are Blank Hexagon Tiles?

Blank Hexagon Tiles are versatile components used in various board games. They can serve as terrain, resources, or even as part of game mechanics. Measuring 79x93mm, these tiles are big enough to write on or decorate, but small enough to fit neatly on your game board.
