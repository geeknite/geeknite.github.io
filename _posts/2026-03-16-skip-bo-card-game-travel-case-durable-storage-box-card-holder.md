## Maintenance and longevity: keeping it in fighting shape

To keep the travel case performing like a well-oiled gaming machine, consider a few simple maintenance steps:

- Clean out crumbs and snacks: It’s not just about cleanliness; it’s about maintaining the pleasant experience of opening a case without discovering last week’s questionable granola bar. A quick wipe with a damp cloth is usually enough.
- Inspect the zippers and straps: Look for wear and tear on zippers, seams, and the strap attachments. If you notice fraying, replace or repair before the case fails when you need it most.
- Dry storage rules: If you’re playing in humid environments, ensure the interior remains dry. Moisture can warp cardstock over time, and nobody wants a soggy deck that squeaks when you draw.
- Card sleeve compatibility: If you use protective sleeves, verify that the interior slots accommodate the thickness. A snug fit can keep sleeves from bending or compressing, which is a small but satisfying detail for card purity enthusiasts.
