## Care and Durability
Care is straightforward which is exactly what we want in a busy life full of coffee and deadlines Vacuum regularly to remove dust and pet hair for spills blot gently with a clean cloth and dab from the outside in to prevent the stain from spreading Most denim rugs benefit from quick local spot cleaning with a mild detergent Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach the denim tones can fade if exposed to direct sunlight for extended periods If you can swing it use a rug pad underneath to prevent slippage and protect the flooring beneath.

Rotate the rug every 3 6 months to even out wear especially in high traffic areas or near the foot of the bed where feet repeatedly press down Professional cleaning every 12 18 months can help preserve color and texture particularly in homes with heavy sunlight exposure or large families.

Durability is one of this rugs selling points Denim fibers are sturdy they resist pilling more than cotton does and the backing helps keep the surface flat If you have a pet with a proclivity for chewing corners you will want a corner rounding solution or to keep that fur baby off while you are away.
