## What is The Mind anyway
The Mind is a cooperative card game in which players try to play cards in ascending order from 1 to 100 without talking or signaling. There are no turns in the traditional sense; on your turn you simply slide a card from your hand onto the central pile if you think it is safe to do so. If someone plays out of order, you made a social misstep and the game slings a few points to the penalty pile. The core mechanic is simple, quiet, and dangerously addictive.

- Setup: shuffle the deck and deal a number of cards to each player depending on the number of players
- The Mind objective: play cards 1 to 100 in ascending order without speaking
- Game flow: players decide when to play a card, relying on collective sense of timing rather than direct communication
- Scoring: errors add to a penalty pile; too many penalties and the game ends in defeat
