## Care tips and maintenance

Like any premium deck, the Reverse benefits from a little habit maintenance. Here are a few tips to keep your greens vibrant and your edges pristine:

- Store the deck in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fade. The green hue can be susceptible to color drift under harsh UV exposure, so keep it in a pouch when you’re traveling to avoid the sunlit catastrophe that is a green deck left in a car.
- Use a protective card box or a tuck-in sleeve during long sessions to reduce edge wear. A little care goes a long way for an aesthetically pleasing deck.
- If you notice stickiness after a spell of humid weather, give the deck a few passes of a soft cloth and allow it to air-dry; this can restore a smoother feel without compromising the stock integrity.
- For magic routines, consider sleeve pulls that are clean and consistent to minimize friction when producing or ditching cards. A little practice and a good technique will keep the deck whisper-silent under stage lights.
