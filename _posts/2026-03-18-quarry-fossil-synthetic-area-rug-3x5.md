## Maintenance and Cleaning (H2)

### Daily Care (H3)
- Vacuum regularly with a basic vacuum attachment to remove surface dust and pet hair.
- Rotate the rug every month to ensure even wear and avoid noticeable patterns where foot traffic is heaviest.
- Use a rug pad if your floor is slippery or if you have a high-traffic entryway.

### Stain Handling (H3)
Accidents happen, especially if you’re entertaining a crowd of guests or a family with snack-dispensing toddlers. Most synthetic fibers respond well to a mild cleaning solution. Blot rather than rub to prevent the stain from grinding into the fibers. Test any cleaner on a small, inconspicuous area first to avoid color bleeds or texture changes.

### Deep Cleaning (H3)
Occasionally, plan a deeper cleanse. A gentle carpet shampoo or a DIY soap-and-water solution can refresh the pile. Avoid hot water unless the cleaner explicitly says it’s safe for synthetic rugs. After washing, dry flat or with a fan to prevent mildew in moisture-prone environments.
