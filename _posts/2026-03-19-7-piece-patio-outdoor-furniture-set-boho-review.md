## Maintenance Tips and Seasonal Use

Seasonal flips are the secret to outdoor furniture longevity. Here are a few practical tips:
- Use a lightweight outdoor cover during heavy rain seasons to reduce color fade and pollen buildup.
- Store cushions indoors or in a dry area during extended wet periods to prevent mildew.
- Clean cushions with a mild fabric cleaner and a soft brush to remove dirt without wearing down the fabric.
- Wipe metal frames with a damp cloth; avoid harsh chemicals that can strip the powder coating over time.
- Check bolts and connectors annually; a quick tighten keeps wobble at bay and proclaims the furniture to be a responsible adult.
