## Maintenance and Safety
Treat the wood with care; wipe with a dry cloth to prevent dust buildup that could affect ball trajectory. Avoid soaking the surface; water and wood finish do not mix well, and you do not want to watch a tiny ruin unfold before your eyes. Keep small parts away from young children who tend to treat everything as a chew toy, and check the rods periodically to ensure they stay securely attached. A quick wipe-down after a use keeps the game looking pristine and reduces the chance of sticky residues from beverages turning the surface into a non-ideal playing field.
