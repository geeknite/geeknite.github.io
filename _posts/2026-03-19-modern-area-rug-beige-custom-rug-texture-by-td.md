## Care tips and longevity hacks
- Rotate quarterly: This helps even out wear and keeps the texture looking fresh.
- Use a rug pad: It extends life, improves acoustics, and makes vacuuming less chaotic.
- Blot, don’t rub: When spills happen, act fast to prevent stains from setting. Rubbing can embed the liquid deeper into the fibers.
- Gentle cleaning: For routine cleaning, a standard vacuum with a brushless attachment does the job. For deeper refreshes, consider a light steam cleaning, followed by thorough drying.
- Sun exposure: Prolonged direct sunlight can tint beige over time. If your room is a sun trap, consider rotating the rug or using window treatments to protect color fidelity.
