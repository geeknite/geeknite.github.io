## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity
Long-term happiness with any foosball table depends on a few simple maintenance habits:

- Clean regularly: Wipe the play surface and the rods with a soft cloth. A gentle wipe-down after play sessions keeps oils and sweat from affecting performance.
- Check screws and joints: Every couple of months, give the screws a quick check and tighten any that feel loose. This prevents wobble and odd table behavior during a clutch moment.
- Protect from spills: While the laminate is spill-friendly, it’s wise to wipe up liquids quickly to prevent staining or damage to the finish.
- Ball care: Use standard foosball balls; if one gets dented, replace them to preserve consistent play. It’s not a wedding ring, but ball uniformity matters in close games.

In short: treat it well, and you’ll enjoy smooth play and fewer headaches for years. Treat it poorly and you’ll be the star of a cautionary tale told by the maintenance manual at every party.
