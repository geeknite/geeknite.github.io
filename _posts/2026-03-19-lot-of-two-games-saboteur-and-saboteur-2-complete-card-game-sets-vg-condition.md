## How Saboteur Works and Why It Remains Fun
Saboteur games hinge on hidden roles and evolving board state. In Saboteur you are secretly assigned roles that can be miner or saboteur. The miners try to build a route from the mine entrance to a path leading to gold. The saboteurs try to derail the mine route by playing cards that block paths or add detours. You do not know who is who until the end of the round, which keeps the table guessing and the trash talk flowing. The core mechanic is simple: draw a card each turn, play cards to extend or block the path, and try not to betray your own team in plain sight by overacting in the first round. The game moves quickly and has a nice balance of pressure and hilarity when the fake confessions begin.

### Saboteur 2 adds a new layer
