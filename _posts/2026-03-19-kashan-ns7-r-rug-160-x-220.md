## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity
### Everyday care: a routine that doesn’t require a magic wand
Dust, grit, and pet fur—these are your rug’s daily nemeses. The NS7R responds well to regular vacuuming with a gentle setting and without a beater bar to avoid fiber fraying on the nap. Use a vacuum with adjustable height so you don’t “eat” the top layer in a heroic attempt to clean it in a single pass.

Rotation is your friend. Every 3–6 months, rotate the rug 180 degrees to ensure even wear and shade exposure. This is especially important if your space has a strong directional light or heavy furniture footfall on a particular corner. Think of it as a polite turnover for the rug’s social life.

### Spill response and stain management
We’ve all spilled red wine or curry on a rug and watched the blot migrate across the weave like a bad sci-fi invasion. The NS7R’s wooly composition handles stains better than you might expect, especially if you address them quickly. Blot—not rub—with a clean cloth to absorb as much liquid as possible. For persistent stains, use a mild wool-safe cleaner or a diluted solution of warm water and a tiny amount of gentle detergent. Always test in an inconspicuous corner first to ensure colorfastness.

Avoid harsh chemicals and bleach; you don’t want to watch your rug emit a chemical yawn after a week. If you’re dealing with a stubborn set-in stain, consider professional cleaning every 12–24 months, depending on footfall and pet activity. The investment will pay back in the rug’s longevity and the room’s long-term appearance.

### Longevity and warranty vibes
Kashan NS7R rugs with higher knot density and premium wool can last decades with proper care. A well-maintained piece can become a family heirloom—passed down like a vintage console or a beloved lunchbox. The warranty varies by vendor and production batch, so keep your receipts and document care instructions.
