### Cleaning and Care
Careful maintenance is the secret sauce. A quick vacuum with a standard beater bar on low suction will keep the tufts fluffy without accidentally shredding the plush. We recommend a weekly light pass to prevent matting and to keep the nap looking like a fresh firmware update. For spills, act fast like a sysadmin on a dashboard full of errors. Blot, do not rub, with a clean white cloth. If you spill something stubborn like red wine or dragon fruit juice, a mild detergent with water works well. Avoid hot water as it can shrink or warp the pile.

The rug is resistant to mild static shock, but if you live with a wizardry cat who loves to throw elbows when you open a snack drawer, anticipate some fur cluster formation on high traffic spots. Regular rotation helps even out wear and keeps the nap looking balanced. For deeper cleans, a professional cleaning is recommended every 6 to 12 months, depending on traffic.

## FAQ
### Is it kid friendly
It is soft and cushy, making it a fun spot for kids to lounge, build blanket forts, and tell stories about friendly dragons. The pile is thick enough to be comfortable but not so tall that it becomes a hazard for little feet clumping around. Regular cleaning and stair-step vacuuming will keep it safe for toddlers and curious pets alike.

### Does it shed
Yes, initial shedding is common with new shag rugs. We saw a light nap-off in the first few weeks, mostly on the edges and in the seat of the couch. Regular vacuuming reduces shedding, and after the first month or two, shedding typically becomes negligible.

### Will it stain
Colorfastness is decent, but all rugs stain; the Slate color helps hide some stains and hides more than a clearly white rug. Quick blotting is essential for any spill. Avoid harsh chemicals and test detergents in a small inconspicuous area first.

### How should I clean spills
Blot, do not rub, with a clean white cloth. If you must use a cleaning solution, use a mild detergent and water. Avoid hot water. For larger stains or persistent odors, consider a professional cleaning service every 6 to 12 months depending on traffic.

### Is it comfortable barefoot
Yes, it feels plush under bare feet. The 2.5 inch pile offers a satisfying cushion without bouncing you into a couch pit like some low bounce foam material.

## Buyer's Guide: How to Measure and Place
Before you buy, measure your space and think about the traffic patterns around the rug. A 5 by 8 rug should leave at least 18 inches between the rug edges and walls, and ample space around the coffee table. If you have a small room, consider a smaller size to avoid overwhelming the space. If you have a large open floor plan, you can pair this rug with other textures to create depth and balance.

### Floor compatibility and safety
This rug adheres well to most hard floors thanks to its backing; however, if your space has a high edge-rise carpet that tends to shift, you might want to add a non-slip rug pad under the Colleen Slate to keep it in place. This pad also adds to comfort and reduces curling edges.
