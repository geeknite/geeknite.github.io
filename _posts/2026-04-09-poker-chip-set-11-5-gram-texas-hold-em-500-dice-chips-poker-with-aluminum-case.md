## Care, maintenance, and longevity
Chips take a bit of a beating from spills, dust, and heavy-handed table talk. A few simple care tips keep them looking fresh longer:
- Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth after games to remove dust and oils. Avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade the surface over time.
- Store the kit in a dry, cool place; moisture can affect color integrity and the foam support inside the case.
- If you notice chips rubbing color onto a table, rotate or switch out the chips for a while. It’s common with sets that see heavy use.
- Inspect the case latch and foam insert occasionally. A loose foam edge can lead to chips rattling during transport, which ruins the moment when you’re counting the pot mid-hand.

If you’re planning long-term use or travel frequently, you might consider a protective sleeve or a soft bag to cushion the aluminum case further. A little extra care goes a long way toward keeping your chips sharp and your bets sharper.
