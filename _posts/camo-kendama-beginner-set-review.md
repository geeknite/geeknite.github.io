## What is a Kendama?

For those of you who might be scratching your heads and wondering what on Earth a Kendama is, let me enlighten you. A Kendama is a traditional Japanese skill toy that consists of a wooden handle (ken) and a ball (tama) connected by a string. The goal is to catch the ball in various cups on the handle and perform tricks. It's like juggling but with more zen vibes.
