## Maintenance tips to extend life and keep it chic
- Vacuum weekly with a gentle setting and a soft-bristled attachment to preserve fiber integrity.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, absorbent cloth. Do not rub; you’re not polishing a turret in a video game—don’t create a furrow in your carpet.
- For stains, use a mild solution recommended by rug care instructions. Always test in an inconspicuous corner first (we’re not trying to redecorate the entire rug with a DIY experiment).
- Rotate the rug every few months if placement causes uneven wear or if you’re heavy on one foot while playing late-night strategy games. Think of it as a “patch day” for your floor.
- Avoid direct sunlight for long periods, which can fade colors over time. If your room is a solar furnace, consider curtains or blinds to protect the rug’s gentle palette.
