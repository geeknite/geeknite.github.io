Most hand tufted rugs feature a sturdy backing material like cotton or felt. ## Care and Maintenance Guide

Maintaining the look of a hand tufted rug requires a few simple habits. Vacuum regularly with a low suction setting to avoid pulling tufted threads. Rotate the rug every 3 to 6 months to ensure even wear. Blot spills with a clean cloth rather than rubbing, which can push the liquid deeper into the pile. For stubborn stains, test a gentle cleaner on a hidden area first and work slowly. Avoid direct heat or steam, which can distort fibers. If you live in a humid climate, consider occasional air circulation to prevent mold or musty odors. For serious spills, call a professional rug cleaner that specializes in wool and tufted works.
