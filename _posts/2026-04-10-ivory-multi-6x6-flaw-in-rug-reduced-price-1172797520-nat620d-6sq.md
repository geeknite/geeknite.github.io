These rugs are generally designed for everyday living, not for the bazaar of a rug shop with a heavy, foot-stomping crowd. ### Care and maintenance
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling or shedding more than necessary.
- Treat spills promptly with blotting and a mild detergent, testing in an inconspicuous corner first.
- Rotate every few months to preserve even wear and avoid color pooling in one area.
- Consider a quality rug pad to prevent slipping and to add cushion—this is where the nerds cheer for frictionless floor experiences.
