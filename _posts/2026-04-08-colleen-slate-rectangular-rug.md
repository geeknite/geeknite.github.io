## Maintenance and Cleaning
If you’re reading this with a mug of tea and a cat on your lap, you know that maintenance is where the rubber hits the fiber. The Colleen Slate is designed with everyday life in mind, not a showroom that never breathes. Cleaning strategies matter because your rug will inevitably collect things—dust, crumbs, stray socks, and the occasional mysterious spill that only appears after you’ve told your partner it’s just a coffee stain.

### Everyday Cleaning
Regular vacuuming is your friend here. A vacuum with a brush roll set to low can suck up surface dust without pulling fibers loose. Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear, especially in high-traffic zones. For pet hair, a quick pass with a lint roller or a pet-friendly brush can help. The goal is to keep the surface looking fresh without turning it into a Herculean chore. In other words, it should be easier than cleaning up after a Photoshop project where every layer is a sneaky chair leg in disguise.

### Stains and Spot-Clean Methods
Stains happen. The best approach is to treat them quickly with a mild cleaning solution and blot rather than rub. Harsh chemicals are unnecessary and often counterproductive, potentially dulling the color or damaging the fibers. Always test cleaning products on a small, inconspicuous corner first. If you’re dealing with a stubborn spill, a mix of warm water and a gentle detergent can break down the mess without turning the rug into a science experiment. If you don’t have a stain-removal plan, the internet is full of memes and tip-y collections; just don’t rely solely on memes for actual cleaning results. 

### Longevity and Care
A rug’s longevity depends on care, not magic. We recommend periodic professional cleaning if you want to stretch the life of the Colleen Slate beyond a couple of seasons, especially in homes with heavy traffic or little humans who treat the rug like a science project. The general rule: keep it clean, keep it flat, and keep the foam pets away from the edges (we all know what a curious cat can do with a loose corner). The rug can handle daily life with a bit of respectful maintenance, which makes it a reliable centerpiece rather than a high-maintenance diva.
