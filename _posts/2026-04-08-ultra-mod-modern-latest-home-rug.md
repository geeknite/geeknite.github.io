## Practicality: Care, Cleaning, and Longevity

A well-made rug isn't just about looks; it's about being able to live with it. We ran the Ultra Mod Modern through a few tests that any modern homeowner would appreciate.

### Cleaning and Maintenance

- Vacuum regularly with a high-suction vacuum, using a gentle setting for the fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a microfiber cloth to avoid setting stains.
- For tougher stains, mix a small amount of mild detergent with water and dab lightly; avoid aggressive scrubbing to prevent fiber damage.
- For pets, a quick spot-cleaning protocol and a rotating rug position helps distribute wear more evenly over time.

### Non-slip Backing and Safety

The backing is designed to reduce slipping on slick floors, but we still recommend a rug pad for extra grip. In a geeky way, think of the rug pad as the anti-boss-level-cheat code for slippery floors.

### Durability: How It Holds Up Over Time

After six months of continuous use, the rug shows minimal signs of wear. The fibers maintain their color, the edges stay intact, and the rug continues to lay flat after vacuuming. If you live in a climate with heavy humidity or direct sunlight, rotating the rug every few weeks can help maintain its edges and prevent curling.
