The big claim with this rug is machine washable for real life messes, not a marketing dream you wake up from after a single wash cycle. ## Cleaning tips and tricks
- Immediate blotting is your friend. Do not rub; dab to avoid pushing the stain deeper.
- For oil or grease, a small amount of dish soap with cold water can break the bond, followed by a blot with a clean towel.
- For pet accidents, a mild enzymatic cleaner helps to dissolve organic stains while preserving color. Always test on a hidden corner first.
- If you have hard water or mineral buildup, a gentle wash once in a while can keep the look fresh.
- Vacuum frequency depends on traffic; two to three times a week is a reasonable baseline for living areas.

We also include a quick note: do not use bleach or chlorine based cleaners on denim color rugs, as this can alter color and fiber integrity. If you accidentally used something harsh, do a small patch test on the backside before you commit to a full wash.
