## How to play in under 5 minutes
### Getting started
UNO Junior Paw Patrol The Mighty Movie Card Game sticks to the familiar UNO rules with light thematic tweaks. The objective is simple: be the first to discard all your cards. On your turn you match the top card by either color or number, or you play a Paw Patrol action card that triggers a friendly rescue or a silly effect. The Lite Rules of UNO Junior are designed for small kids, so no heavy arithmetic and no mind-melting strategy; just enough thinking to feel clever while still letting your little one feel like a hero.

### Card types you’ll encounter
- Number cards in the Paw Patrol color scheme
- Paw Patrol action cards that skip, reverse, or force a draw, all with cute pup art
- Mighty Movie tie-in cards that add a little movie lore flavor to the chaos

### Basic flow
1) Shuffle and deal a small hand. 2) Flip the top card to start the discard pile. 3) On your turn, match color or number, or play a Paw Patrol action card. 4) If you can’t play, draw one card. 5) When you’re down to one card, yell UNO in a way that doesn’t panic your pets. 6) The first to empty their hand wins, but not before the final doggy hug from the Mighty Movie universe.
