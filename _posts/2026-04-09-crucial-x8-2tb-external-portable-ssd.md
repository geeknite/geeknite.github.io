## Specs and What They Mean
The Crucial X8 2TB External Portable SSD uses USB 3.2 Gen 2 (10 Gbps) through USB-C, delivering theoretical speeds up to around 1050 MB/s read and about the same ballpark write speeds. In practice, your numbers will swing depending on the host controller, cable quality, and file type. For large sequential transfers, you’ll be in the 900-1050 MB/s range on modern machines with proper cables. For random 4K workloads and small-file lots, you’ll see a more modest, but still respectable, set of performance numbers. The important bit: you are not waiting at coffee shop level speeds; you’re moving with enough swagger to keep up with 4K video edits and game library dumps.

Compatibility: Windows, Mac, Linux, and even Android devices that support USB-C storage. No special drivers required; plug-and-play with formatting options offered by your OS.

If you’re curious about durability data from other fans, many online reviews show the X8 surviving typical everyday abuse tests—drops, bumps, and the occasional luggage misadventure—pretty well.
