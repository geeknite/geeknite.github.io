## Maintenance, care, and longevity hacks
### How to store your two-pack for maximum life
- Store away from direct sunlight to prevent color fade and edge wear.
- Keep in a cool, dry place; humidity can affect both stock rigidity and coating longevity.
- Use a card guard or tuck box to reduce edge dings during travel.
- Avoid exposing the cards to sharp objects or rough surfaces that could nick the edges.

### Quick-care tips you can actually use
- If your cards feel a touch stiff after a few sessions, gently fan them and let them rest for a few minutes; friction from basic shuffles will naturally loosen them up over time.
- For stubborn marks or smudges, a quick wipe with a clean microfiber cloth (no liquids) can extend readability and overall presentation.
- Rotating which packs you use for different activities (games vs. practice vs. magic) helps balance wear across the four decks, prolonging the life of the set.
