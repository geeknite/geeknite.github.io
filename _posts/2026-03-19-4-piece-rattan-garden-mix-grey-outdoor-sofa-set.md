### Weather Resistance and Maintenance
Outdoor furniture is a long-term relationship with sun, rain, wind, and occasional hail of questionable quality. The PE rattan is designed to resist fading, and the cushions are water-repellent to a degree. Real-life test: after a big rain, you should check for damp cushions and let them dry naturally before you store them. If your climate is particularly harsh, you may want to bring cushions indoors during the wet season to preserve their life expectancy. A light wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap is usually enough to remove dirt; avoid harsh solvents that can degrade the fabric or cause the cushions to develop a personality crisis.

## Assembly and Maintenance Tips

- Assemble with a friend or a sturdy sense of humor. Put the legs on squarely and torque evenly. Do not overtighten; you don’t want to strip the threads and hear the furniture whisper, I am not your friend.
- Use a furniture cover during off-season to keep the cushions crisp and the weave free of dust and mysterious seeds. A simple breathable cover can save you from the tangled web of leaves and the creeping belief that the set has a life of its own.
- For daily care, wipe with a damp microfiber cloth. For stubborn stains, mild soap and water will usually do. Do not soak the cushions; soggy cushions are the enemy of your bliss and your neighbor’s curious eye.
- Keep cushions dry. If they do get wet, let them air dry completely before storing to prevent mold and mildew. You do not want your outdoor lounge to smell like wet gym socks.
