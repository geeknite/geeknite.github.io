## Why You Need a Lens Protector

- **Resale Value**: If you ever decide to upgrade, a well-maintained headset fetches a better price.
- **Peace of Mind**: Knowing your lenses are protected allows you to enjoy your VR sessions without worry.

## How to Install Your Lens Protector

Installing a lens protector can be a nerve-wracking experience, but it doesn't have to be. Follow these steps for a smooth application:

1. **Clean the Lenses**: Use a microfiber cloth to remove any dust or fingerprints.
2. **Align the Protector**: Carefully align the protector with the lens. Ensure it’s centered.
3. **Apply**: Gently place the protector on the lens. Use a credit card to smooth out any bubbles.
4. **Check**: Ensure there are no dust particles trapped under the protector. If needed, lift and reapply.

**Pro Tip**: Do the installation in a dust-free environment to avoid particles getting trapped.
