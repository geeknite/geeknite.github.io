## Maintenance and Care

Caring for premium dice is not rocket science, but it is worth doing right. Wipe the dice with a soft microfiber cloth after a night of gaming to remove fingerprints and oils. Avoid exposing the dice to harsh cleaners or long periods in direct sunlight, which can dull the finish over time. A light dusting and occasional polish with a recommended resin-safe cleaner will keep Fortune Row looking as good as new for sessions that span years. If you have a dedicated display case, the Glow of Destiny effect will look even more dramatic when the case lights are turned on at night. In a pinch, a gentle wipe and a light beam of lamp light are enough to reacquaint your group with the mood of the moment.

### A Note on Longevity
The etched pips hold up well with regular play, but if you are a table where players drop dice like miscast spells, you might consider a small tray liner to cushion the edges. The goal is to preserve their crisp feel and keep the gold flecking free from chips. With standard usage, Fortune Row should remain a beloved feature of your City of Mist sessions rather than a fragile ornament.
