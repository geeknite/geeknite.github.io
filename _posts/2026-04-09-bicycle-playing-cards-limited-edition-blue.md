How to Care for Your Limited Edition Blue Deck

- Keep it sealed when not in use if you’re a collector and you want to preserve the value of the plastic seal. This is a reasonable approach if you’re in it for the long haul.
- If you plan to use it, consider a card holder or safe storage to prevent moisture damage. The blue back might show slight whitening if exposed to humidity and heat, so a cool, dry place is your friend.
- Clean your hands before shuffling to minimize oils on the tuck or card faces. It sounds silly, but you’ll thank yourself later when the finish remains unblemished after a few years of use.
- Water and heat are not your friends here. Avoid leaving the deck in a hot car or on a radiator. The blue could shift in hue ever so slightly, and you don’t want to be the guy who explains “the shade changed” at every game night.
