## Variants and Platinum Mode
UNO Platinum Edition dangles a couple of variants that you can sprinkle into your rounds for extra spice. The goal is to keep the core experience intact while offering a few clear choices for players who crave a twist.

### Platinum Rush (optional)
Platinum Rush turns the normal pace into a countdown-style sprint. When a player has one card left, a timer set to 30 seconds starts (you can adjust). If they don’t call UNO or play their last card within the timer, they draw extra cards as a gentle penalty. The tension is real, the giggles are frequent, and you’ll find that even the most chill gamer becomes a dramatic announcer of “PLATINUM RUSH!”

### Platinum Shuffle
This variant gives every player the option to shuffle the discard pile back into the deck after a set number of rounds. It’s a harmless way to reset momentum and prevent someone from feeling locked into a losing streak. It also keeps players from having a solitaire-style block of draws that ruins the vibe.

### Family-friendly mode
If the group includes kids or new players, you can disable or ignore the Platinum-specific cards and just play the classic UNO with premium components. It’s a gentle reminder that upgrades should serve play, not override it.
