## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity: Keeping the Shine Without the Scratches

- Storage: Keep the deck in the provided sleeve or a protective card box to minimize edge wear. Avoid stacking heavy objects on top of the deck; foil + strong pressure = micro-scratches that catch the light in weird ways.
- Cleaning: Gently wipe with a soft microfiber cloth. Avoid solvent-based cleaners; they can degrade the foil finish.
- Handling: Wash hands before gaming if possible; oils from fingers can accumulate on foil over time. A little care goes a long way toward preserving the deck’s luster.
- Travel: If you’re taking this edition on the road, consider using a protective pouch. The foil’s shine draws attention, and you don’t want a stray key or coin scratching your precious cards mid-flight.
