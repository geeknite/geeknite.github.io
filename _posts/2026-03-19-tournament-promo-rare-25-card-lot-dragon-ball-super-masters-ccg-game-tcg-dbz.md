### Promo Rares vs Tournament Rares: A Quick Tutorial
If you have not stepped foot inside a tournament environment, you might be wondering what distinguishes a promo from a tournament rarity. Promo rares are distributed as rewards or promotional items; they could be given out at events, included in special promotions, or used to entice players to demos. The foil patterns, border designs, and sometimes even the card art will be unique to the promo run. The tournament rares, conversely, are printed for use in competition, typically with stronger stats, new abilities, or synergy within a specific archetype. They are not necessarily printed in huge volumes, but they are intended to be accessible to players who attend events.
In a 25 card lot, you may see a blend of these categories. The impact on your deck could be tangible: you might pull a card that slots into an existing deck without requiring you to add a bunch of other components. Or you might pull a card that is primarily collectible, with limited play viability but immense aesthetic appeal. Either way, the bundle is an interesting microcosm of the game’s ecosystem.
