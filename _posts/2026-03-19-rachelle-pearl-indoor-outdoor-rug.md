## Care and Maintenance Quick Reference

- Vacuum weekly to remove dirt and pet hair.
- Blot spills promptly with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber damage.
- Clean with mild soap and water; avoid bleach or harsh detergents.
- Rinse and air-dry completely before repositioning.
- For heavy dirt, consider a gentle rinse with a hose and a light scrub, followed by thorough drying.
