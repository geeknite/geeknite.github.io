## Materials, Build, and Practicality

This rug uses a polywoven construction that balances softness and durability. The combination makes it less likely that you’ll find your rug sliding away during a dramatic pivot in a dance game or during a sudden exit from a fierce narrative boss. ### Cleaning and Maintenance
In the kitchen or at the desk, spills happen. The Subaquatic rug is built with stain resistance and ease of cleaning in mind. A quick vacuum on a low setting is usually enough to keep surface debris at bay, and most stains respond to a damp cloth and mild detergent. For stubborn marks, a gentle spot-clean is recommended; avoid aggressive scrubbing that could rub color into the fibers or distort the pattern. If you have pets, the rug’s texture helps minimize the visibility of hair and paw prints, particularly with a weekly maintenance routine. The backing’s grip remains effective after regular cleaning, ensuring that you won’t be wrestling a moving underwater landscape during cleaning time.

### Durability and Longevity
Designed for high-traffic areas, the rug should last for years with reasonable care. The print holds up well against sun exposure if placed away from direct, intense sunlight, which can cause fading over very long periods. The edges are well-finished to resist fraying, a small but very satisfying detail that prevents the rug from looking worn at the entrance after frequent foot traffic. In short, this rug is built to withstand the chaotic life of a modern home: kids’ games, pet antics, and the occasional frantic late-night streaming session, all without becoming a faded, lifeless sea floor.
