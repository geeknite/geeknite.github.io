## Maintenance and practical care
This rug’s cotton blend suggests that daily care is simple, but a few guidelines help it stay vibrant longer.

- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting to maintain the texture and prevent dirt from embedding in the fibers.
- Clean spills promptly using a mild detergent mixed with water; dab rather than rub to avoid pushing the liquid deeper into the fibers.
- For deeper cleaning, spot clean or consult a professional if the manufacturer’s care label recommends it. Avoid machine washing unless explicitly stated as safe by the brand.
- Dry flat, away from direct sunlight, to minimize color fading and fiber stress.
- Use a non-slip rug pad if you are placing it on a smooth surface to prevent movement and curling at the edges.

### Common issues and DIY fixes
- Fringes curling up: Lightly trim and apply a tiny bit of fabric glue along the base to prevent unraveling.
- Flattened pile after cleaning: Gently brush with a soft-bristle brush to restore the texture.
- Color migration: If you’re prepping a new rug, test the colorfastness on a tiny unnoticeable patch first; if you see dye transfer, air-dry in the shade and avoid direct sun for the first few days.
