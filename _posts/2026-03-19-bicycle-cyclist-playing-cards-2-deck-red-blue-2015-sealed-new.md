## Care and Maintenance
Caring for decks is not glamorous, but it is essential for prolonging life and preserving presentation. Keep them away from moisture and heat. Excess humidity can lead to warping or edge curl, particularly in heavier stock. Avoid exposing decks to direct sunlight for extended periods, as UV exposure can fade color richness and dull the gloss over time. When not in use, store the decks in a dry place, ideally in a case or sleeve designed for two deck sets. Break in the decks gently before heavy daily use to allow the stock to relax without cracking the edges. If you plan to display the set, consider a shallow clamshell display or a dedicated shelf with LED lighting that preserves color fidelity while keeping the iconic cyclist motif in view for visitors and fellow geeks.
