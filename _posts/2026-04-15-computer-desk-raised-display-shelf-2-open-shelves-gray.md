## Design Details and Practicality

### The Raised Display Shelf: Pros and Trade-offs
Pros:
- Eye-level monitors reduce neck strain and create an instant sense of “pro workspace.”
- Elevated monitor stand frees desk surface space for paperwork, a coffee mug fortress, or a tiny plant army.
- Aesthetically pleasing, especially if you’re chasing that clean, modern vibes in your home office.

Trade-offs:
