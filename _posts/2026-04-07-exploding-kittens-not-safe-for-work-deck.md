## How to play NSFW Deck: quick refresh

### Setup and basic turn order
- Shuffle the NSFW Deck with the base game’s cards (if you’re playing with an existing kit).
- Deal a starting hand to each player, the typical number being three or four four depending on house rules.
- Place the Exploding Kitten cards and Defuse cards in their respective piles, as per standard rules.
- The first player draws a card and the round begins, with players taking turns playing one card from their hand to influence the draw or affect other players.

### Core mechanics: defuse, explode, and misdirection
- Defuse cards are your lifeline. Use them to neutralize an exploding kitten when you’re unlucky enough to draw one.
- Some NSFW cards sabotage other players’ plans, forcing them to swap cards, skip turns, or reveal contents of their hands. The social dimension shines here, as timing and reading your table matter just as much as luck.
- Like the classic version, the deck contains a mix of offensive and defensive options. You’ll stack a few defensive cards in your hand, anticipating a late-round fireworks show, and you’ll keep a handful of provocative cards to nudge others into risky draws.

### Endgame dynamics: tension and payoff
As the deck thins, the tension heightens. The presence of adult-themed humor can make players more cautious about what they reveal, how they read the table, and when it’s best to push for a risky move. The payoffs are sweet when a perfect card chain lands on a target who thought they were safe, but the risk is equally sweet when another player misreads the room and ends up in a hilarious conundrum. The endgame in NSFW Deck often becomes a dance of misdirection, misplaying, and inevitable hilarity. It’s not about winning at all costs; it’s about who can stay cool while everyone else shoots themselves in the foot with a well-timed “totally not jury-rigged” combo.
