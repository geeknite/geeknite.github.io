### Care and Maintenance: The Minimalist's Guide to Longevity

- Vacuum regularly on a low setting to prevent dirt from embedding deeper into the fibers. Use a suction level that does not threaten the rug’s plush soul.
- Wipe up spills promptly with a blotting motion. Do not rub; this helps protect the pile’s integrity and prevents color bleeding.
- For stains, a mild detergent solution works for most routine marks; test in a hidden corner first.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear, especially in busy rooms where sunlight can create odd patterns over time.
- If you have pets, keep claws trimmed and consider a feng-shui shield of space around the edges so paws do not snag delicate fibers.

With this routine, Monica will not just sit pretty; it will age gracefully, like a hero who has seen a few campaigns but still leads the party with confidence.
