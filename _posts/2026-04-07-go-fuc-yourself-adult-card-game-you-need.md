## How to Play: Quick Start Guide for Maximum Mayhem

Playing Go Fuc Yourself is about as simple as a rocket launch, but with fewer preflight assurances and more audience participation. Here’s a quick setup path to get from “Is this legal?” to “Where do we sign up for the chaos?”

1) Shuffle, deal, and decide the dealer. The dealer rotates clockwise, because space-time is tired of fairness and loves a good twist.
2) The prompt card is revealed. It’s the seed, the starting line, the thesis statement of the round.
3) Players select one response card from their hand and lay it down facedown. The tension builds as you hear the collective intake of breath in the room.
4) The dealer reads all the responses aloud, pairing them with the prompt. Laughter is optional but strongly recommended.
5) The dealer awards the round to the best pairing. The winner draws a new round card, and the chaos continues.

If you want to make it a longer game, add a “mulligan” rule where a player can trade in two or three cards to the deck for a fresh take. If you want to go faster, shorten the response deck and watch the room react in real-time chaos.
