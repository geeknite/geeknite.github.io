In other words, you can load it with your favorite seed blend. If your primary goal is attracting songbirds or finches, you’ll likely want a classic Nyjer (thistle) or sunflower seed mix. ## Practical setup guide: turning your yard into a bird-friendly theater

- Step 1: Choose a quiet corner of your yard with natural cover. Birds feel safer when they can quickly retreat to shrubs or trees.
- Step 2: Mount the feeder at an appropriate height to avoid ground predators and to give birds a platform for takeoff and landing.
