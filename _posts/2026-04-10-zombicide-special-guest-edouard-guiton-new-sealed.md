## Strategy tips for the new edition
- Know your survivor roles: In this edition, as with the base game, your best bet is to assign a heavy-hitting survivor who can take a hit and a nimble survivor who can scout and grab objective tokens. Balancing offense and safety is the key to survival.
- Protect the corner cases: Zombies love to spill into tight corridors; plan escape routes and keep an opportunistic ally with a ranged weapon near chokepoints.
- Don’t ignore the equipment: Loot is life, even more so when you are playing with a new edition where some items might have a fresh flavor. Prioritize items that increase mobility and shield you from the undead onslaught.
- The scenario matters: Some scenarios reward aggressive play, others reward cautious, methodical approach. The edition with Edouard’s ghostly signature might tilt scenarios toward immersive storytelling, so read the objective deck and plan a narrative as you play.
- Learn from mistakes: Zombicide is famously punishing; the best lesson you can take away after a setback is to adapt your plan and try a different character combination next round. The better you understand the board, the more you can bend luck in your favor.
