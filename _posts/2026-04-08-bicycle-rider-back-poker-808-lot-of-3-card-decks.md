## Care, maintenance, and carelessness-proof tips
- Always return the decks to their individual tuck boxes after play; this protects the seals and keeps the decks away from dust and environmental contaminants.
- If a deck gets sticky due to humidity, fan gently to air them out; avoid forcing a heavy shuffle until they regain their natural springiness.
- Clean edges with a dry microfiber cloth if you notice minor dust or residue; avoid liquid cleaners that could seep into the box or the card surface.
- Keep away from extreme temperatures; heat can warp card stock and dampen the finish’s tactile feel, while cold can stiffen the texture and slow down shuffles.
