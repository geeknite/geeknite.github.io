## How to play with the two packs together
### Setup and tone management
To get the most out of the two packs, consider a quick pre-game chat to set ground rules. Decide on whether to use all black cards from both expansions or to rotate by night. Some groups prefer to shuffle only a subset to maintain a brisk tempo. The key is balance: you want enough variety to keep it interesting but not so much that rounds become a scavenger hunt for the right card text.

### Round flow snapshot
1) A judge draws a black card from the combined deck. 2) Each player selects a white card (or multiple, depending on your house rules). 3) The judge reads the combinations aloud, selects a favorite, and awards a point. 4) Rotate who is the judge and repeat. 5) At the end, tally points and declare a winner who must suffer a playful, ceremonial badge of victory.
