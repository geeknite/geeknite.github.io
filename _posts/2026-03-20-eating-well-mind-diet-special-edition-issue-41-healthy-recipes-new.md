### What is the Mind Diet?

The Mind Diet blends elements of the Mediterranean diet with specific brain-healthy foods. The science underpinning the diet centers on reducing inflammatory markers and supporting neural function through a deliberate focus on leafy greens, berries, whole grains, fish, nuts, and olive oil while keeping red meat, butter, and sugary snacks in check. This edition does a great job translating those abstract concepts into actionable steps you can actually cook, not just memorize. The tone remains friendly and witty, a rare combination of microbiology jokes and grocery lists that won’t make your eyes glaze over.
