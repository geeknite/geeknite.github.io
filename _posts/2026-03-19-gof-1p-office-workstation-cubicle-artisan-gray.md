## Design and Build Quality

### Aesthetics and finish
The Artisan Gray finish leans into a modern industrial vibe with a soft, non-flashy texture that photographs well but won’t dominate your aesthetic if you prefer brighter accents. It’s the kind of gray that looks at a sunbeam and politely says, “I’m not impressed, but I won’t judge you.” This makes it a versatile choice for video calls, product photo shoots, or those late-night coding marathons where you hide under a blanket and pretend you’re in a vertical cave.

### Materials and hardware
GOF emphasizes a steel-and-composite build that keeps the structure sturdy without turning your budget into a black hole. Durability-wise, you’re looking at a product built to tolerate the usual office rituals—keyboard drops, cable yanks, the occasional rolling chair mishap—without turning into a rattling javeline by week two. The load-bearing capacity is ample for a standard office setup: monitor, keyboard, a small printer if you’re feeling retro, and the occasional stack of notepads that seem to multiply when you’re not looking.

### Ergonomics at a glance
Ergonomics is where this design could actually shine. The built-in desk gives you a stable seating plane, with room for a height-adjustable chair and a monitor at roughly eye level.
