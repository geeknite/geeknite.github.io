A good sleeve should feel comfortable in your hand when you’re flipping through a stack, but it should never feel slippery. The PRO Altered Edition sleeves provide a satisfyingly grippy surface, especially around the outer edge, which helps when you are riffling through a deck that has seen several rounds of mulligans. Shuffling is where the rubber meets the road. These sleeves shuffle with decent ease. In practice, we found these sleeves performed well with standard riffle, faro, and Hindu shuffle techniques. We put them through a few sessions where a card got dropped and dragged across the table, and the edges remained intact with no visible cracking or whitening. ### Maintenance Tips
- Clean sleeves regularly to remove oils and dust. A microfiber cloth can extend their life and keep your art looking fresh.
- Avoid bending sleeves excessively. A vampire-silent drop screen or accidental warp can degrade readability over time.
- Store sleeves flat when not in use to prevent edge warping. A simple binder or deck box with a padded sleeve can preserve their shape between sessions.
