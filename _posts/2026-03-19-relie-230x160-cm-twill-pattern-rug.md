## Cleaning, Care, and Longevity

The Twill weave isn’t a magic carpet, but it’s designed to be forgiving for everyday life. Routine vacuuming is your friend, ideally a gentle setting that won’t pull at the fibers. For spills, act fast: blot, don’t rub, and consider a mild detergent if you’re dealing with colored liquids. For more stubborn stains, test any cleaning solution on a small, inconspicuous corner first. The goal is to avoid discoloration or fiber damage, which is a party foul in any decorating circle.

To maintain the rug’s shape, rotate it every few months. This helps reduce wear in high-traffic zones, especially if your gaming chair has a love affair with one particular spot. If you have pets, a regular lint-roll after vacuuming keeps shedding from turning into a floor décor theme. The rug’s backing should be resistant to slipping, but consider adding a non-slip underlay for extra grip on smooth floor types.
