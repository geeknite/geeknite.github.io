## How to Use Fortune Row in Your Campaign

There are a few plausible paths to integrating Fortune Row into your ongoing city narrative. Here are some play-tested approaches you can adapt to your group:

- Path of the Investigator: Launch a case that gradually uncovers a larger mythic pattern tied to Fortune Row. The new playbooks give players fresh angles to approach the mystery, and the location cards provide ready-made scenes for pace and mood.
- Path of the Myth: Treat Fortune Row as a recurring motif—an anchor for a city-wide arc or the backdrop for a campaign-long rivalry between factions. The block acts as a living stage where the myths of the city react to what players do there.
- Path of the Heist: Use Fortune Row’s assets to stage a tense caper with social maneuvering, leverage, and a few risky gambits. You’ll maximize dramatic tension and keep the action kinetic without requiring a full rules overhaul.

Fortune Row is not a complete system overhaul. It’s a toolbox extension that respects the core premise of City of Mist: mythic identity in a modern city, built through relationships, choices, and the stories your players tell. The goal is to help you run faster, narrate more vividly, and give players more meaningful levers to pull without drowning in option paralysis.
