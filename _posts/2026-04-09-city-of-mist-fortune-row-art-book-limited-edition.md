## What is Fortune Row, and why should you care?

It is a love letter to the visual language of City of Mist—an RPG that treats superheroes as personas chosen by myths rather than capes slapped on by a fashion trend.

This Limited Edition is often described with a grin and a wink: it’s number X of Y copies, a combination of refined production and a sense that you’ve joined an exclusive club whose dress code is “neon lights and rain.” If you’re chasing the thrill of owning something that feels more like an investment in mood than an impulse buy, Fortune Row might be your happy place. Fortune Row brings together a curated collection of artists who understand the City of Mist vibe: the interplay of myth and noir, the sense that every face you meet on the street might be wearing a hidden mask, the world where legends are as common as streetlamps and as dangerous as a rumor you heard at the corner bodega.
