## Maintenance, care, and durability tips

- Keep chips clean by rotating them gently with your fingers after a long session; no complicated cleansing required, just a quick wipe with a dry microfiber. Avoid soaking them or exposing them to harsh chemicals; chips are tough but not immune to fashion crimes.
- Cards benefit from occasional light shuffles and avoiding unnecessary bending of the cardstock. If you’re using a card protector, ensure it sits securely to prevent accidental bending or misplacement during a heated hand.
- The storage case should be kept dry and away from high humidity environments. A tiny silica gel packet in the box (not in the open box with the cards) can help keep everything in good shape, especially in damp climates or if you stash the kit under a humid couch cushion.
