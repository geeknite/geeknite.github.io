## Maintenance and Cleaning Tips (Expanded Protocol)
Maintenance makes or breaks a rug’s long-term love affair. The Ornamentation Green Rug is relatively low-maintenance and designed for regular vacuuming rather than professional cleaning. Here are practical tips I’ve tested:

- Vacuum once or twice a week, more in high-traffic zones. Use a vacuum with adjustable height to avoid the dreaded wiry, comb-like sound that scares neighbors when you forget to mute the mic.
- For spills, blot—not scrub—with a clean cloth to lift liquid. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners unless you want discoloration; if you must treat a stain, use a mild cleaner and test on a small corner first.
- For deeper cleaning, do a light hand wash with a mild detergent and cold water, then air dry flat. Never twist or wring the rug; it’s designed to hold its shape.
- Rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear and keep the ornamentation from developing a vacuum-cleaner stripe down the middle.
