## Maintenance and Cleaning Tips
Maintenance is where many rugs either shine or fade into the background like a forgotten console port. The Ornamentation Green Rug is comparatively low-maintenance and designed to be vacuumed regularly without needing a specialist service. Here are a few practical tips I’ve found handy:

- Vacuum once or twice a week, more often in high-traffic zones. Use a vacuum with adjustable height to avoid a wiry, comb-like sound that can wake the neighbors only when you forget to mute the mic.
- For spills, blot—not scrub—with a clean cloth to lift the liquid. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners unless you want to risk discoloration; if you must treat a stain, use a mild cleaner and test on a small corner first.
- For deeper cleaning, you can do a light hand wash with a mild detergent and cold water, then air dry flat. Never twist or wring the rug; it’s designed to keep its shape and avoid warping.
- Rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly and keep the ornamentation from developing a vacuum-cleaner stripe down the middle.
