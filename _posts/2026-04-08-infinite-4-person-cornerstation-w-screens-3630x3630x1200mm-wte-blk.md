## Dimensions and space planning
At 3630 mm by 3630 mm by 1200 mm, we’re not talking about a compact napping corner. This is a real piece of furniture that redefines how you’ll use a room. Here are some planning notes:

## Maintenance, care, and longevity
Durability matters when you’re investing in a four-person station. The panel finishes are designed for everyday use, but you’ll still want to:
- Wipe down surfaces with a non-abrasive cleaner. Avoid harsh solvents that can degrade finishes.
- Check the cable channels for dust and re-seat cables during quarterly maintenance. A tidy cable system reduces the risk of accidental unplugging during a crucial moment.
- Inspect the screens for glare and alignment; if you notice screen drift or misalignment, a quick recalibration or re-seat is usually enough.

Warranty terms vary by vendor, but typical coverage for a product like this includes structural frames and integrated screens. Always review the specifics with your dealer, because you know… paperwork. Still, in proper maintenance, this cornerstation should remain a centerpiece of your workspace for years to come.
