## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Care Tips

Maintenance is where most folks either sigh in relief or sigh in resignation. The Fontaine Silver Grey rug is relatively low maintenance for its class, which means you can live your life without a cleaning schedule that looks like a military operation. Here are practical care tips that keep your rug looking crisp:

- Regular vacuuming with a soft brush attachment to avoid pulling fibers. Do not use a high suction setting that could flatten the pile over time.
- Address spills promptly. Blot with a clean white cloth or paper towel; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper. For stubborn spills, a mild detergent solution can work, but test on a small area first to ensure no color migration.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear, especially in high traffic areas. It also helps keep the overall look balanced if your room is exposed to seasonal lighting that might emphasize one corner more than another.
- If you need a deeper clean, consider professional rug cleaning services that specialize in synthetic pile and neutral tones.
