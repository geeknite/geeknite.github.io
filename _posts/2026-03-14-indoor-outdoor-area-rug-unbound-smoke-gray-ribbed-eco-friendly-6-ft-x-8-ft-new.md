### Cleaning and Care in Detail

For best results, perform a light vacuum regularly to remove surface dust. When needed, rinse with a hose or wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap. Make sure to allow the rug to dry completely before placing furniture back on top to prevent moisture buildup. Avoid high heat or dryer use, as heat can cause fiber distortion over time. If you notice any color fade in sun-exposed areas, rotating the rug every few months can help maintain a uniform appearance.
