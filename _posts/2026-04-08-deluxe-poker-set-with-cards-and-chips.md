Like any good gadget, a little care goes a long way. Wipe the cards with a soft, slightly damp cloth if you notice sticky residue from snacks. Let them air dry before stacking them back into the case. Chips respond well to a quick wipe with a dry microfiber cloth; avoid soaking them or letting them sit in a humid corner because damp chips are a magnet for rust-like micro-disasters in your own living room.
