### How It Plays: Quick Rules, Big Laughter

This isn’t a museum-quality, ultra-competitive trivia gauntlet. It’s a family-friendly product designed for quick setup and dynamic play. Here’s the gist for newcomers and veterans alike:

The exact scoring varies by house rules, but the energy remains constant: you’re here to laugh and share a moment together, not to publish a scholarly paper on Disney canon.

The flow is intentionally approachable. Rules are explained succinctly, so you won’t be stuck reading the rulebook while your kids practice their victory dances.
