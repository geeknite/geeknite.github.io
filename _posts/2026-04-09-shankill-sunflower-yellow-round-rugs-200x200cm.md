## Care and maintenance: keep the sun blooming (H2)
### Routine care (H3)
- Vacuum weekly with a regular setting; avoid high suction on delicate fibers.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear patterns and avoid visible traffic lanes.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean towel; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber pull.
### Deep cleaning and stain removal (H3)
- For stubborn stains, treat with a gentle detergent solution applied to a clean cloth, then blot and rinse. Avoid soaking the rug completely, as moisture can lead to color bleed or base material swelling.
- For odors, sprinkle a light amount of baking soda, let it sit briefly, then vacuum. This helps refresh the scent without overpowering the bright color.
- If you ever need professional cleaning, check with your local rug care specialists for a low-moisture cleaning option suitable for synthetic blends.
### Durability in the wild (H3)
In real-world households, the rug stands up to foot traffic and pet paws without too much drama. It’s not a miracle magnet for stains, but it performs well with routine care. The color is resistant to initial fading if you keep it out of direct harsh sun for extended periods; a sunny room will actually help it look more vibrant, while a window seat might require the occasional UV-aware rotation.
