## Care and Maintenance: Keeping It Clean Without Losing Your Mind

Cleaning a PVC mat is a walk-in-the-park compared to cleaning a wool rug after a barbecue. Here are our go-to tips:

- Wipe with a damp cloth or rinse with a hose. If you’re dealing with a stubborn patch of dog food or coffee spill, a mild dish soap solution does the trick. Rinse, air-dry, and you’re back in business.
- For deeper cleaning, you can scrub lightly with a soft-bristled brush. Avoid abrasive pads that could scratch the surface or dull the lettering.
- Avoid harsh chemicals and bleach that can degrade the PVC over time. It’s not a superhero cape—no need to turn it into a chemical experiment.
- Let it dry completely before placing it back into service; otherwise, you risk mold if you leave it damp in a moist corner of the patio.
