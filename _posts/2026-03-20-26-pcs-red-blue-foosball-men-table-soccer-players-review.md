## How to Install and Use Quickly
### Quick Swap Guide
1) Remove the old foosball men from the rods. 2) Align the new pieces so the toes point toward the center of the board; 3) Slide them onto the rod with a gentle twist; 4) Repeat for all 26 players. 5) Test by spinning the rods and performing a few practice passes. If a piece binds, check for excess flash on the mold that needs trimming.

### Tips for Best Results
- Ensure your rods are well-lubricated but not overly slippery. A tiny amount of oil can do wonders for spin accuracy.
- Level your table before intense play; a tiny tilt drastically changes ball travel and can make the game frustrating.
- Use a consistent ball type to reduce variability in bounce and speed.
