### How to Play: Quick Start Guide for Bakugo Edition
If you’ve never played UNO Flip!, here’s a concise primer tailored to this edition:

1) Setup: Shuffle the deck and deal seven cards to each player. Place the remaining deck in the center, one flip-palace away from chaos.
2) Turn and Flip: The top card of the draw pile starts the game. If a card matches color or number, you can play it. When a Flip Card appears, flip the entire deck to the opposite color side. The dark side introduces stronger penalties and the posibilidad of higher-stakes plays.
3) Special Cards: Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, and Wild Draw Four still function as expected, but the dark side adds new nuance with some extra penalties. The Bakugo toy sits on the table as a reminder of the heroic chaos that can erupt if you’re not paying attention.
4) Victory: First player to get rid of all cards wins. The Bakugo figure can be used as a group cheerleader, emitting a momentary loud “KABOOM” reaction (via pre-recorded audio in some sets), or, more realistically, you’ll likely just grin at your own dramatic victory pose.
