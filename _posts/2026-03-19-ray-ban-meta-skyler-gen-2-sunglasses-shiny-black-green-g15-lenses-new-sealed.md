## The Meta Angle: AR Shoes, Not Unicorns

The Skyler Gen 2 includes AR-ready hardware: small cameras, audio sensors, and an array of micro-lenses designed to support augmented reality experiences. If you’ve owned earlier Skyler Gen 1 units, Gen 2 isn’t just a cosmetic refresh. The improvements run across several facets:
