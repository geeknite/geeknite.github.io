## Durability, Weather Resistance, and Maintenance
### Weather tolerance
This set is built to take on sun, rain, and the occasional wind‑in‑the‑hair moment. The wicker finish resists fading, while the cushions dry quickly so you’re not rolling up damp pillows after a sudden squall. If you live in a climate that flirtates with heavy humidity, you’ll appreciate the quick‑drying cushions and the fabric’s moisture resistance.

### Cleaning and care tips
- Wipe frames with a damp cloth to remove daily grime.
- Use a mild soap solution for stubborn dirt; avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the finish.
- Bring cushions inside during heavy storms or winter to maximize longevity. Rotate cushions periodically to prevent uneven fading.
- If you ever spill a dramatic beverage, blot first, then wash later; avoid heavy scrubs that could loosen weave integrity over time.
