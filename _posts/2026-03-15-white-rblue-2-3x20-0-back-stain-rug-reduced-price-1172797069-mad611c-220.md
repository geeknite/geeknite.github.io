## Maintenance and Cleaning: keeping the royal blue from becoming royal mess
Maintenance is where the true test lies. The rug is designed to be easy to clean, but it’s not immune to human shenanigans. Here are some practical tips to keep MAD611C-220 looking sharp:
- Vacuum regularly with a floor nozzle to avoid snagging fibers; avoid rough agitation tools that can fray the edges.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; dab, don’t rub, to minimize fiber lift. For your favorite red wine moments, treat promptly and use a gentle stain remover recommended for synthetic fibers.
- For deeper cleans, consider a light shampoo with a mild detergent designed for synthetic carpets. Rinse thoroughly and dry flat to avoid corner curling.
- Rotate the rug every few months if possible to even out wear and exposure from sunlight. This prolongs the bold color contrast and prevents a foot-shaped glow from forming in soft light.
- In high-traffic areas, placing a doormat on both sides can reduce the amount of dirt tracked onto the rug, which translates to fewer obsessive vacuuming sessions.
