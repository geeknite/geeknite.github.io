## What is Smash Up: Awesome Level 9000?

Smash Up: Awesome Level 9000 is an expansion for the popular card game Smash Up. This expansion introduces new factions, each with unique abilities and cards, adding more variety and strategies to the game.

### The Basics

In Smash Up, players draft two factions, shuffle their decks together, and use their combined forces to battle for control of bases. Awesome Level 9000 adds new factions such as Steampunks, Bear Cavalry, Ghosts, and Killer Plants, each with their own quirky mechanics.

### Components

The expansion includes:
- **Faction Decks**: Four new faction decks, each with unique abilities and strategies.
- **Base Cards**: Additional base cards that bring new challenges and opportunities.
- **Rule Book**: Clear and concise rules to help you integrate the expansion into the base game.
