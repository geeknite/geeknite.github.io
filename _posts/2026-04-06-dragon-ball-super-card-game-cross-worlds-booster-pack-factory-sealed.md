## Authenticity, Tamper-Evidence, and Quality
Factory sealed means different things in the wild. The best-practice approach is to buy from reputable retailers and examine packaging for authenticity markers. When you’re investing in sealed product, you’re also investing in trust, and the ability to confirm that trust is worth more than a single card pull.
