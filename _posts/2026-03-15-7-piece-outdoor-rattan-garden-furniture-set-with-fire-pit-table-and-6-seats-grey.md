Maintenance and Weather Resistance: How to Make It Last

Rattan furniture shines when you remember a few simple maintenance steps. The PE rattan weave is designed to be weather-resistant and less prone to cracking than natural rattan, but it’s not invincible. A quick rinse with mild soap and water will remove most dirt and pollen, and a soft-bristle brush can lift stubborn grime from the weave. Cushions with removable covers should be washed according to the care tag; if your climate is humid or you store cushions outside year-round, consider using a waterproof cover and bringing cushions indoors during the harshest weather.

Protection strategies:
- Use a breathable outdoor cover when the set is not in use for extended periods.
- Bring cushions indoors during heavy rain or snow to prevent moisture retention and mildew.
- Clean the fire pit components and confirm there are no gas leaks or unusual wear before use. Safety matters as much as aesthetics.
- Check for fading in strong sun and rotate cushions to avoid uneven exposure.

This isn’t a “forever in the sun” miracle; think of it as a prudent, stylish investment in outdoor living that will look good for several seasons with proper care. It’s a modern, versatile set that balances a sleek aesthetic with practical comfort.
