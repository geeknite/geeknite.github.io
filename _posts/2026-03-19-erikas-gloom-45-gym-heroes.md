## A quick primer on Gym Heroes and where Erika fits in
Gym Heroes is the kind of set that makes veterans grin and new players tilt their heads like a curious Cubone. It’s centered around Gym Leaders from the Pokémon anime plus their signature squads, which meant the card pool leaned heavily into Trainer-focused strategies and “character mystique” rather than the dry procedural vibe of early Base Set. Erika is one of those gym leaders who feels like a warm hug and a sunbeam colliding in a forest of lavender. The Erika archetype in Gym Heroes gave players a gentle nudge toward grass-based strategies, card draw, and a lot of flavor text about flowers and calm when the battlefield got chaotic.
