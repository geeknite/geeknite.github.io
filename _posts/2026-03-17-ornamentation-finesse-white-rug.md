## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

White rugs are bold statements but high-maintenance celebrities. The Ornamentation Finesse White Rug promises easier upkeep than you might fear, thanks to a stain-resistant backing and a fiber blend designed to resist everyday grime. Still, pretreating spills is the wise play here—waiting until coffee has time to set is not a good idea in any universe.

### Cleaning Tips for Everyday Spills

- Act fast: blot gently with a clean cloth. Do not rub, or you risk pushing the stain deeper into the fibers.
- Use a mild detergent solution for most liquid spills. Avoid bleach or harsh solvents that can degrade the ornamentation.
- For stubborn stains, a specialized rug cleaner designed for synthetic blends is your friend. Always test a small hidden area first.
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to maintain the pile height and prevent grit from wearing down the fibers.

## Size, Fit, and Room Planning

Rug sizing is the invisible art of balance. The Ornamentation Finesse White Rug comes in a few standard sizes that work well for most living rooms, bedrooms, and home offices. Here are practical guidelines:

- In a compact living room, a 5x8 or 6x9 rug defines the seating area without overpowering the space.
- For a larger living area, an 8x10 or 9x12 can ground the focal furniture and still let you walk around with ease.
- In a bedroom, a slightly larger rug under the bed can create a cozy underfoot moment when you rise in the morning.

If you’re unsure about size, consult a simple rule: the rug should extend at least 18 inches beyond the furniture on two sides in the main seating area. This creates a natural “island” feel that keeps walkers in the loop and doesn’t trap legs under coffee tables.
