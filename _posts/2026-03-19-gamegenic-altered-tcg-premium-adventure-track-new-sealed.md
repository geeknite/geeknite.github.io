## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity Tips

- Wipe down after sessions with a microfiber cloth to prevent residue from aging the surface. A quick pass keeps the glare under control and the look pristine.
- Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight. The colors may fade over time when left in the sun for days, which is a tragedy for the aesthetic at your kitchen table.
- When storing, keep the corner pieces aligned and avoid forcing pieces in places they do not belong. It’s a modular thing, not a puzzle you want to wrestle with after a long night of card-slinging.
- If you sleeve your cards, test the fit. Some combinations can result in a slightly bulkier deck that still slides smoothly, while others may need a notch of space adjustment within the trays.
