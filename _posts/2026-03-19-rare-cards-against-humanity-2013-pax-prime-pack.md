## Collectibility and care: keeping the magic alive

Owning a rare pack isn’t just about possessing a set of cards; it’s about maintaining a relationship with the artifact. Collectors emphasize a few care steps:

- Store away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and keep colors as vibrant as possible. CAH cards don’t like sun tans any more than you do.
- Keep the deck intact. If the edition includes special seals or signatures, avoid removing them. The more you preserve these small theatrical momentos, the more it tells a story when you eventually display them.
- Document provenance. A simple note about where and when you acquired the pack can significantly increase its value to future you or fellow collectors. It also helps you remember the story behind the moment when your friends first learned that yes, someone did indeed design a card to roast their cosplay choices.
