## Gameplay dynamics
The core mechanic is simple and effective. On your turn you play a card from your hand to a central pile. The catch is that cards must be played in ascending order across all players. There is no metagaming with talking you cannot signal your plans aloud you simply hope that your neighbors have a higher card ready. When a mistake is made the round ends and you tally how many lives you have left. In the German edition the rule sheet uses simple diagrams to illustrate the timing of plays and you can switch to the English text if any nuance feels off.

The base Mind experience is repeatable because each round you add more cards and more pressure. For players who crave variety a few optional house rules can be introduced. ## Strategy hints
While there is not a traditional strategy in the Mind the following tips help:
