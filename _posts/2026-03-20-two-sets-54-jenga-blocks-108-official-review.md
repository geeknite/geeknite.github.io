## Rules and healthy variants
The classic Jenga rules apply here as well, with one major note: you have more blocks to choose from, so you can craft longer and more dramatic moves. Some fun variants to try:
- Team challenge: two teams take turns pulling a block, with the team that makes the toppling lose
- Blind move variant: a helper calls out the block category but you cannot see the block until you pull it
- Time trial variant: a small sand timer sets a limit for each move
- Color cue variant: if your blocks are not perfectly uniform in color, you can use color cues to decide which layer to build next
- Mixed supply variant: combine blocks from both sets into a single tower, but require that the top layer uses blocks from each set in alternating order
The official rulebook covers the basics, but you can customize the game to fit the energy of your crew. The goal is to keep the toppling dramatic but not catastrophic, and to keep the smiles constant even as gravity asserts its authority.
