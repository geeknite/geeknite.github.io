## Practicality: Maintenance, Durability, and Cleaning Tips

Maintenance matters, especially for a rug that is bright, high-energy, and lying under the foot traffic of everyday life. Here are practical tips to keep your Red Multi 6x6 looking sharp:

- Vacuum regularly with a rug attachment. A light pass to lift surface debris will keep the pile looking fresh and help reduce wear patterns.
- Address spills quickly. Most synthetic blends tolerate quick blotting with a clean cloth and a mild soap solution. Avoid heavy scrubbing that can push the stain deeper or fuzz the fibers.
- Rotate occasionally. This helps even out wear and keeps the color from appearing uneven due to sunlight or room geometry.
- If you have pets, consider a quick spot-clean routine for any accidents and a light grooming for hair buildup. The goal is to preserve the pile and the structure rather than create a second drama in your home.
- Consider a rug pad. A good pad under a 6x6 helps reduce slipping, extends the life of the rug, and adds a touch of cush to that midnight treadmill run during your favorite sci-fi show.

Regarding the flaw, assess its visibility from your typical seating distance. If you notice a slight variation in texture or color that only becomes evident up close, you may decide that the novelty and price offset the blemish. On the other hand, if the flaw is glaring and disrupts the overall readability of the pattern, you might want to tuck the rug into a more forgiving corner or pass entirely.
