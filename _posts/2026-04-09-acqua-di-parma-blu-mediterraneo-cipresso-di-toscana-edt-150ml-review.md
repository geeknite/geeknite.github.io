## Longevity and Projection: How Long Does the Magic Last?

Let’s get practical. In the world of fragrance, longevity and projection are after-sales conversations you have with your skin. - Early hours: Projection sits at a comfortable medium-to-tag-on-one’s-shirt level. It’s enough for you to catch a whiff when you move, but not so loud that you’re triggering allergic reactions in the break room.
- Midday: The heart of the fragrance remains recognizable without overpowering the people around you. If you’re in a mid-size office or a coffee shop, Cipresso di Toscana tends to become a friendly, “Oh, you smell good” rather than a wall of scent that leaves others whispering behind your back.
- Evening: It sits closer to the skin, making it a good choice for dates or dinners where you want your presence to be felt but not a public performance. Note that longevity is highly personal. If you’re curious about your own skin’s chemistry, a test spray on different days can reveal variations you won’t notice in a single wear. A second spray on pulse points can help, but beware of over-saturation; this is not a cologne that benefits from a heavy-handed approach.
