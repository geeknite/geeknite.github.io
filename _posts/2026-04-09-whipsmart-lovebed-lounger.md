## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity

### Materials Care

The nylon sling is straightforward to clean: mild soap, warm water, air dry. Do not bleach; unless your dream is a squeaky-clean swing with a comic-book gleam. The metal frame benefits from the occasional wipe-down to remove dust and any potential moisture that could cause corrosion over the years. A silicone-based lubricant on moving joints can help maintain smooth operation, but avoid oil-based lubricants that can degrade the sling material.

### Cleaning Protocols

For the cushion and padding, spot-clean with a gentle fabric cleaner and avoid soaking. If you’re dealing with spills, blot quickly and rely on the fabric’s built-in resistance rather than aggressive scrubbing.
