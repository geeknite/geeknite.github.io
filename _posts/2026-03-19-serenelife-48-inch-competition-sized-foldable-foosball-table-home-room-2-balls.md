## Maintenance, Durability, and Longevity
### Cleaning and care
Maintenance is relatively low effort. A quick wipe with a damp cloth after intense sessions keeps the play surface in good condition. Avoid harsh cleaners that could strip the surface or degrade the paint on the players. A light wax or silicone-based spray can help with stubborn squeaks or sticky spots, but don’t overdo it—excess wax is a magnet for dirt and can cause the ball to slide unpredictably. Regular inspections of fasteners and leg bolts will preserve stability and prevent sudden wobble mid-tournament.
