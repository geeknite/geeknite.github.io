## Maintenance and Care Tips

- Clean after use and dry completely before storage
- Lightly oil the blade to prevent rust and corrosion
- Periodically condition the leather sheath to prevent cracking and maintain fit
- Sharpen with the bevel you prefer; a guided system helps maintain consistency
- Inspect strap (belt) retention and knife fit to ensure safe carry in daily use
- Avoid exposure to extreme heat or sweat exposure that could degrade materials over time
