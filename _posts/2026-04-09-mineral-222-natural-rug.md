## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity

Maintenance is the adult version of “don’t spill ink on your sweater.” The rug’s natural fibers respond well to routine care: regular vacuuming, quick blotting of spills, and an occasional rotation to even out wear. For deeper cleaning, you’ll want to follow the manufacturer’s guidelines to avoid compromising the material’s integrity. A gentle approach typically yields the best results, preserving the rug’s softness while maintaining its color integrity.

Sunlight is a factor—like everything else in interior design, UV exposure can lead to fading over time. If your prime sunlit area is near a window, consider rotating the rug every few months to balance sun exposure and wear. The natural dyes used in Mineral 222 are intended for color stability, but even the best-colored pebble can fade under a relentless sun cameo.
