## How to Get the Best 30-Second Video
Here are practical, no-nonsense tips to maximize your 30-second clips:
- Plan a micro-story: think of a short, visible sequence with a clear beginning, middle, and end you can convey in 30 seconds.
- Master framing with natural light: position yourself so light is at your back or at a comfortable side angle to minimize harsh shadows.
- Stabilize your shot with a natural stance: avoid jarring camera moves; tiny adjustments are enough to improve clarity.
- Narration not required: the clips can speak for themselves via ambient sound and visual storytelling.
- Edit lightly: trim, add a caption, and publish quickly to maximize social engagement without over-processing.

30 seconds is a sandbox. You can be creative, playful, or downright cinematic in tiny doses. That’s the beauty of wearable cameras: they invite micro-stories rather than epic sagas.
