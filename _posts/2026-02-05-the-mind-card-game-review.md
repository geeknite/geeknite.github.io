## The Basics of The Mind

**The Mind** is a cooperative card game designed for 2-4 players. The goal is simple: play cards in ascending order without communicating with your teammates. Sounds easy, right? Wrong! Prepare for a hilarious journey filled with intense staring contests and frantic hand gestures.

### Components

The game consists of:

- **Numbered Cards**: Cards numbered from 1 to 100.
- **Life Cards**: Represent the number of lives you have.
- **Shuriken Cards**: Help you discard the lowest card in your hand.

**Fun Fact**: If you ever wondered what it feels like to telepathically communicate with your friends, this game is your gateway to becoming Professor X.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up **The Mind** is a breeze. Shuffle the numbered cards, distribute the life and shuriken cards, and you're ready to dive into the mind-blowing madness.

### Turn Phases

Each turn involves the following phases:

1. **Card Distribution**: Players receive cards based on the level they're on (e.g., 1 card for Level 1, 2 cards for Level 2).
2. **Silent Communication**: Players must play their cards in ascending order without speaking or signaling.
3. **Life and Shuriken Management**: Use life cards and shuriken cards wisely to stay in the game.

**Pro Tip**: Develop a sixth sense for card timing. It’s like synchronized swimming, but for card games.
