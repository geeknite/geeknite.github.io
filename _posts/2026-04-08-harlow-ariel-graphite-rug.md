## Cleaning, Care, and Chemistry: Keeping Graphite Graceful
### Everyday maintenance
Regular vacuuming—ideally with a brush setting that’s not too aggressive—should keep this rug looking sharp. Spills? Treat promptly with a mild cleaner suitable for synthetic blends, blot—not rub—to avoid pigment migration. If you have pets, you’ll appreciate how the fibers hide hair and how lint rollers become optional after a moment or two of real-life heroics.

### Deep-clean options
Occasionally, you’ll want a deeper clean without sending your rug to the spa. A light rinse with minimal soap and careful drying can refresh the surface, but avoid soaking. The goal is to keep the pile’s integrity, not to reinvent the rug into something that looks like a swamp monster after a flood.
