## Cleaning, Care, and Chemistry: Keeping Graphite Graceful
### Everyday maintenance
Regular vacuuming with a brush setting that isn’t too aggressive should keep the rug looking sharp. Spills? Treat promptly with a mild cleaner suitable for synthetic blends, blot rather than rub to avoid pigment migration. If you have pets, you’ll appreciate how the fibers hide hair and how lint rollers become optional after a moment or two of real-life heroics.

### Deep-clean options
Occasionally you’ll want a deeper clean without sending your rug to the spa. A light rinse with minimal soap and careful drying can refresh the surface, but avoid soaking.
