## What is Bloodborne: The Board Game - Hunter's Dream Expansion?

Bloodborne: The Board Game - Hunter's Dream Expansion is an add-on to the main Bloodborne board game, bringing new content, mechanics, and challenges. Designed by Michael Shinall and Eric M. Lang, this game captures the essence of the video game's dark and immersive universe.

### The Basics

In this expansion, players continue their quest to cleanse Yharnam of its nightmarish creatures. Each player takes on the role of a Hunter, working together to defeat powerful bosses and uncover secrets. The expansion adds new enemies, bosses, and scenarios, ensuring that even seasoned players will find fresh and thrilling challenges.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Exceptionally detailed miniatures of new enemies and bosses. Trust me, you'll want to put these on display (right after you stop screaming at them).
- **Cards**: New weapon, item, and enemy cards that add depth and complexity to your strategies.
- **Game Board**: Additional tiles to expand the hauntingly beautiful map of Yharnam.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Hide these components from your pets. The miniatures look like chew toys, and your cat might take your Hunter figurine on an unexpected quest.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Bloodborne: Hunter's Dream Expansion is as detailed as planning a heist in Yharnam. Lay out the game board, distribute cards, and assemble miniatures. Now you're ready to face the horrors lurking in every corner.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their Hunter's abilities. You can move, attack, use items, and more. Cooperation is essential, so communicate with your team to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Bloodborne is a blend of strategy and tension. Each enemy has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic attack just to have the enemy dodge and counter? Welcome to Bloodborne, where plans often lead to unforeseen madness.
