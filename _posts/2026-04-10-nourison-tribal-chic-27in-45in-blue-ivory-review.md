### Care and maintenance
Cotton woven rugs like this one typically respond well to light vacuuming and spot cleaning. For a quick refresh, rotate the rug every few months to even out wear. If you have pets, a lint-roller can become your best friend, because shedding is a lifestyle. Spot-clean stains with a mild detergent and water; avoid aggressive scrubbers that could fray the fibers. The key is to be gentle but persistent — much like a friendly neighbor knocking on your door with a plate of cookies, except these cookies are stain removers in disguise.

## Maintenance tips from a geeky decorator
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid fraying the fibers.
- Rotate every 3-4 months to even out wear, especially if you have a favorite seat with a habit of dragging its feet in a particular direction.
- Treat spills quickly by blotting rather than rubbing; this helps preserve the weave integrity.
- Consider a rug pad to prevent slipping and to reduce wear on the backside.
- If you want a deeper clean, use a mild detergent and a soft brush, then air dry; avoid heat that can shrink cotton fibers over time.
