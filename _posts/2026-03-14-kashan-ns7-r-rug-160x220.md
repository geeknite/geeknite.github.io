## Cleaning, Care, and Longevity: Keeping the Cosmos Clean

### Regular Care Routine

Vacuum regularly, at a gentle rate, focusing on square sections to avoid missing areas. A weekly circuit of the room ensures you don’t develop a static rhythm of dust bunnies. If you have pets, keep an eye on shedding and occasional hair accumulation; a quick pass with a vacuum designed for pet hair will do wonders.

### Stain Management: Quick Blot, Not Quick Fix

Accidents happen. The key is to blot, not rub, to prevent fiber fraying. For tougher stains, a mild detergent solution and a careful blot can work wonders. Always test in an inconspicuous area first to ensure the dye won’t migrate. Steam cleaning is generally not recommended for delicate wool blends; check your rug's care guide before committing to steam.

### Longevity Tips from a Ruggeek

Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear. Use furniture coasters under heavy items to prevent denting. Keep sunlight exposure in check to minimize color fading. If you’re a fixture in the geek community who loves to optimize every square inch of space, you’ll appreciate that a well-maintained rug remains a prime feature in your interior plotline for years to come.
