## Printing, Grading, and Storage: How to Treat Your NM Copy
Here are practical tips for evaluating and maintaining NM Alpine Meadows in your collection:
- Inspect the surface gloss under bright light. A gentle swirl or fingerprint won't disqualify NM status, but deep scratches or dull patches do.
- Check for edge wear. NM copies should have crisp edges with minimal whitening.
- Store in protective sleeves and top-loaders designed for standard MTG cards. For long-term storage, consider a binder with acid-free sleeves.
- Keep away from humidity and direct sunlight. The sun’s glare can give a false sense of wear when you tilt the card for photos.
- If you’re rating a lot of Kaldheim lands for a display, group NM copies by set and color to simplify inventory management.
