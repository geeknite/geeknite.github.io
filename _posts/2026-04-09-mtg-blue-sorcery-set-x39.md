## Display, Storage, and Care
A well-loved stack of blue sorceries deserves to be treated with a little ceremony. Here are a few tips to get the most out of this set, even if you don’t plan to use every card in actual play:

- Sleeving strategy: Use protective sleeves (and ideally top-loaders) if you intend to display them or run them in a casual deck. Sleeves keep the wear down and preserve the readability of those subtle foils or printer blemishes that sometimes charm more than they annoy.
- Binder organization: Group by set, by edition, or by the type of effect. A thoughtful binder arrangement makes it easier to pick a theme for your next game night and to show off your blue sorcery vibe to curious friends.
- Storage environment: Keep cards away from moisture, sunlight, and snacks. They don’t like a sticky fingers situation and they definitely don’t appreciate direct sunlight turning a pristine back into a curious gradient. A dry, dark shelf is a dream for card storage and a nightmare for rogue sandcastle projects.
- Display ideas: Create a small display panel of favorite sorceries, complemented by a color-moded binder page or an acrylic stand that showcases the stack’s mood: cool, calculated, and a little bit cheeky.
