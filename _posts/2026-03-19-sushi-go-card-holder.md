## Maintenance and Care
### Cleaning Tips
Fingerprints and occasional food smudges are inevitable. A quick wipe with a damp cloth will typically do the trick. For stubborn residues, a gentle soap-and-water solution works well; avoid harsh solvents that could haze the plastic or degrade the clarity you rely on for card visibility. Dry thoroughly before your next session to prevent water spots and weird bead formations on the surface.
### Storage
Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight when not in use. If you’ve got a gaming closet, a small padded bag or a dedicated organizer helps keep it scratch-free and ready for the next raid of pudding diplomacy. The goal is to preserve that pristine look so your cards continue to gleam like a glossy menu at a seaside restaurant.
