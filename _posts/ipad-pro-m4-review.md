Hold onto your seats, fellow geeks, because Apple's iPad Pro M4 is here to blow your tech-loving minds. ## Design and Build Quality

The iPad Pro M4 boasts a sleek and elegant design that Apple is renowned for. If you thought your laptop was fancy, wait until you get your hands on this beauty.

### Ports and Connectivity

Apple has finally decided to bless us with a plethora of ports. The iPad Pro M4 comes with a USB-C port, Thunderbolt 4, and even a headphone jack. Yes, you read that right. A headphone jack! It's like Christmas came early for audiophiles.

## Display and Audio

![iPad Pro M4 Display](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipad-pro-m4-display)

The iPad Pro M4 features a stunning 12.9-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology. The colors are so vibrant that even your cat videos will look like they deserve an Oscar. The 120Hz refresh rate ensures buttery smooth scrolling and gaming experiences. As for audio, the device comes equipped with a six-speaker sound system that makes you feel like you're in a mini home theater. Whether you're binge-watching the latest series or jamming to your favorite tunes, the sound quality will blow you away.

## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by M4 Chip

![M4 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/m4__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}m4-chip)

At the heart of the iPad Pro M4 is Apple's revolutionary M4 chip. With a 12-core CPU, 16-core GPU, and a 32-core Neural Engine, this beast is designed to handle everything from video editing and 3D rendering to running multiple virtual machines and playing the latest games. It’s so powerful it might just be smarter than you.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 20 hours of battery life on a single charge, and in our tests, it didn't disappoint. Whether you're coding, designing, or binge-watching your favorite series, the iPad Pro M4 will keep you going all day long and then some. No more lugging around heavy chargers like a nomad.

## Keyboard and Trackpad

![Magic Keyboard](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/keyboard__f5dhn0m5h16m_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}magic-keyboard)

The Magic Keyboard is back and better than ever. With a refined scissor mechanism and perfect travel distance, typing on the iPad Pro M4 feels like a dream. The large Force Touch trackpad is highly responsive and supports a plethora of gestures, making navigation a breeze.

## Software and Features

![iOS 16](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/ios__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ios-16)

### iOS 16

The iPad Pro M4 comes with iOS 16, packed with new features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and redesigned widgets. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the iPad Pro M4 stand out from the competition.

### FaceTime and Webcam

The 1080p FaceTime HD camera ensures you look your best on video calls. Coupled with the studio-quality three-mic array, it's perfect for remote meetings or catching up with friends and family.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Exceptional Performance**: The M4 chip delivers unparalleled speed and efficiency.
- **Stunning Display**: The Liquid Retina XDR display is a visual treat.
- **All-Day Battery Life**: No more carrying around chargers like a nomad.
