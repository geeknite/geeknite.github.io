## Maintenance and Care Tips

- Keep liquids away from the surface to prevent staining. A coaster habit is worth adopting, even if you think you’re careful. A hot mug can do subtle, long-term damage that’s hard to identify until it’s too late.
- Dust regularly and wipe with a microfiber cloth. If you’re using any cleaners, choose wood-safe formulas and avoid ammonia-based products that can wear down the veneer’s protective layer over time.
- Check the hardware periodically: tighten screws on the pedestal rails and ensure drawer slides operate smoothly. A little TLC now saves you a lot of drilling later.
