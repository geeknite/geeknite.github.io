City of Mist has always been the kind of game that looks you in the eye and says, well hello detective noir with a dash of mythic heroism, now please decide if you want to be a mask or the mask you wear. The Crime Wave PDF is a bite sized expansion that promises to lift a city of crime from its usual moody mugging to a cinematic spree through alleys that smell vaguely of coffee and burnt sugar. In this new PDF, the designers lean into the classic crime wave trope, where every corner holds a clue, a con, or a catastrophe, and every lead threatens to unravel the mythic underpinnings of your city. Below is a detailed tour through what you get when you crack open this document, how it plays, and whether it belongs on your virtual table or under your physical stack of rulebooks that you swear you will someday read cover to cover.

## Overview of the Crime Wave PDF

The Crime Wave PDF is not a giant rulebook, nor is it a cosmetic add-on. It sits in a comfortable middle ground. It gives you a curated set of scenarios, new masks and motifs tailored to urban chaos, and a toolkit for GMs to stage a crime wave that feels both mythic and plausible. The design choices lean into City of Mist core elements: noir atmosphere, a city as a living character, and characters who balance personal legend with the gravity of real world crime. What you will notice right away is the PDF's emphasis on modular play. It offers anchor scenarios that you can drop into your ongoing campaign or run as a weekend one shot with minimal prep. If your group is into long arcs, you can thread the wave into the broader city narrative; if you want a fast session that still looks cinematic, you can run it as a standalone sprint with the same vibe minus the baggage of a sprawling campaign.

### What you get inside

- A compact set of crime wave scenarios, each with a clear villain, a set of escalating acts, and a few moral forks that will force players to confront what their masks stand for amid increasingly chaotically timed events.
- Fresh masks and motifs tuned to city level threats. Some of these are clever riffs on classical archetypes and others push into new mythic idioms that City of Mist players will recognize and adore.
- GM tips that acknowledge the unique pacing of a city beset by waves of crime. The advice feels practical rather than preachy and is peppered with crisp options to pivot if the table mood shifts mid-session.
- Quick reference charts and five to six ready-to-run scenes. The intention is to reduce setup time so you can actually play a game rather than spend an hour scrawling notes.
- Optional encounters for street level investigators that can scale up to epic confrontations without breaking the suspension of disbelief.

## The PDF’s layout and design worth noting

The Crime Wave PDF carries the City of Mist aesthetic with pride. The page layout is practical, not precious; the designers opted for readability and speed over lavish complexity. The typography is clean, and the color palette nods to rain slick streets and neon signs without assaulting the eyes. The art style respects the noir vibe while lending a modern, watercolor-like quality that makes the pages feel alive rather than sterile. The integration of art with the text helps to anchor scenes quickly, especially when you are juggling a handful of players and a board full of suspects.

From a production standpoint, the PDF is a solid example of budget-conscious design that still looks and feels like a premium product. The file is well structured, with bookmarks for the major sections, and printable one-pagers that you can lay out on a table for quick reference. If your table runs six or seven players, the ability to pull up a single scene on a tablet or print a clean copy for the GM screen is a genuine plus.

## Mechanics and how the Crime Wave affects play

City of Mist leans into a light engine that emphasizes outcomes and narrative advancement over mechanical friction. The Crime Wave PDF does not reinvent the wheel; it refines how you use the wheel in a city noir setting. Here are a few mechanics and design choices that stand out:

- The wave structure itself creates a rising tension curve that feels cinematic without requiring a huge amount of prep. Each act escalates the stakes in a way that naturally suits a mystery or pursuit arc. It encourages players to decide whether to chase the clue at street level or dive deeper into the mythic underbelly of the city.
- Masks and motifs are central. The Crime Wave expands the roster with new options that fit the wave theme. This gives players fresh angles for their characters, enabling creative reboots of their identities in response to the crisis on the street.
- Investigation flow remains accessible. There are tools to track leads, consequences, and the emotional weight of the wave. GMs can quickly measure how a given action pushes a city closer to meltdown or clarity, depending on who the heroes are and what they carry in their pockets—figuratively and literally.
- Combat and risk mechanics stay lean. If you are playing with “soft” combat rules or a cinematic approach, the PDF respects that choice and provides scenes where fights can feel as stylish as a montage. If your table prefers gritty realism, the wave still lands as a narrative beat that can be resolved with time skipped or a dramatic reveal.

The bottom line is that Crime Wave respects City of Mist players while offering enough new content to justify a purchase for groups that want to chase a tightly themed arc with a strong cinematic vibe. It is not a replacement for the core book; it is a focused extension that slots neatly into a campaign with minimal friction.

## Getting into the scenes

The set of scenes in this PDF is the heart of the product. You will find a handful of investigations and confrontations designed to be run over a single evening or broken into several sessions. The strength lies in how these scenes weave the mythic and the mundane. A crime wave in City of Mist is not merely about who is stealing what; it is about what this wave reveals about the city’s myths and about the characters masking their true selves behind the crime and chaos.

For example, one scene might revolve around a string of apartment break-ins tied to a mythic figure who manipulates fear. The GM can layer in social consequences, where neighbors react to the wave with suspicion and solidarity in equal measure. The players may choose to pursue the mythic antagonist directly or to chase the human network behind the wave. Either path can be satisfying if you lean into the narrative textures and the emotional stakes. The scenarios encourage players to question what their masks say about them when the city is under pressure. Are they chasing glory or protecting the people who live in the cracks between the neon and the rain?

## Character options and play style

City of Mist shines when players lean into the dual nature of their characters: the ordinary person with an extraordinary legend. Crime Wave broadens the palette by offering new masks that align with the wave theme. You will see options that push players toward moral conflict and identity exploration, not merely gadgetry and combat prowess. This aligns with the essential tone of City of Mist: myth meets the real world in a city that never truly sleeps.

If your table likes grounded investigators with a dash of mythic power, you will feel right at home. If you prefer heroic myth in a modern setting, you will find that the wave is a perfect pressure chamber for your characters to reveal what they truly stand for when faced with crisis. The balance between personal story and city wide stakes remains intact, which is a major win for a product that could easily tilt too far toward blockbuster spectacle or too far toward procedural noir.

## GM tips and session flow

A good GM guide is worth its weight in coffee rings and sticky notes. The Crime Wave PDF includes practical tips that help you keep the table moving. Here are a few of the useful patterns you will find:

- Start with a horizon moment. Anchor the session in a scene that gives players a quick sense of the wave and its impact on real lives. A strong opening makes players lean in and want to solve the next clue rather than drift into side conversations.
- Use the wave momentum as a pacing tool. The escalation built into the scenes provides natural cliffhangers. If a player misses a lead, the wave continues to the next beat. You can still bring the party back with an improvisational pivot that makes sense in the city’s mythic logic.
- Keep a two track narrative. One line follows the supernatural lifeblood behind the crime wave; the other tracks the mortal consequences for everyday people. Switching between the two keeps tension high and prevents the game from dissolving into a single chase sequence.
- Encourage mask reflection. The best moments often come from characters evaluating their legend under stress. When a hero faces a moral fork, give them space to decide not just what they want to achieve, but what they want to be known for in the city once the wave breaks.

## Artwork and accessibility

The artwork in the Crime Wave PDF maintains the noir vibe without becoming needlessly grim. It balances mood with legibility, which matters in a game where you are juggling stats, notes, and social consequence tokens. The files are accessible to late night gamers who might be on a single monitor or a small tablet, and the contrast choices make reading easier in a dim room with a coffee mug pretending to be a lamp. If you like to print, there are clean one page play aids that reproduce well on standard printers. The PDF format itself is friendly to newcomers and veterans alike, because the emphasis remains on narrative flow rather than a thousand micro rules that you must memorize before the first roll.

## Value for money and timing

At a price point that sits in the mid range for City of Mist supplements, Crime Wave offers several hours of play with a focus on a strong thematic hook and practical GM tools. The return on investment is largely measured by how quickly you can start a session and how smoothly you can transition between scenes. If you have a regular group who enjoys weekly hunts through neon nights and mythic complications, Crime Wave will feel like a polished add on that respects your time. If you only play sporadically, the modular nature of the content ensures you can run it in bite sized chunks without missing connective threads to your ongoing campaigns.

## How it compares to the physical book and other PDFs

If you own the core City of Mist books, Crime Wave increases the table stakes with a tighter thematic focus. The PDF stands up well next to other expansions in the line and doesn’t force you to relearn the system for a dozen new pages. For players who prefer physical products, Crime Wave is worth getting as a supplement to the core book. It is compact enough to slip into a shelf with minimal space yet generous enough to feel like a complete mini campaign package.

Compared to other PDFs in the same genre, Crime Wave brings a strong sense of mood and a cohesive arc. It also avoids overlaying new mechanics that would slow the table down. For players who value story over crunchy new rules, this is a win. For groups chasing the latest mechanical gimmick, the PDF may feel a touch conservative. Either way, it nails the vibe City of Mist users expect and delivers a serviceable structure for the wave narrative.

- Groups that enjoy urban noir and mythic storytelling with a modern edge
- Anyone looking for a compact, modular adventure that can be slotted into an existing city narrative without a lot of prep

If you want something fitting for a one shot weekend or a short campaign arc that can be braided into your ongoing city narrative, Crime Wave hits a sweet spot between convenience and thematic richness.
