## Care tips from the lab
- Regular sweeping minimizes grit that can abrade fibers; a weekly sweep is a good baseline.
- For tough stains, act fast; dab with mild soap and water rather than scrubbing aggressively.
- If you use a rug pad, select one that is breathable to avoid moisture entrapment.
