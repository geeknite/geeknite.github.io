Cleaning, Care, and Longevity

Here’s where the practical side of rug ownership shines. Routine maintenance matters more than you’d think, and Sorbet responds well to a simple care routine. Vacuum on a low to medium setting, rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear, and keep liquids away from the edges to prevent migration that can cause the pile to look uneven. It’s not a miracle solution, but it’s a rug that makes basic care feel almost like a personal grooming routine for your floor.

For stains, your best bet is a quick blot rather than a heavy scrub. Use a mild detergent solution and pat—not rub—your way back to a clean surface. If you’re worried about colorfastness, a small test patch in a hidden corner is never a bad idea. The rug’s fiber blend is designed to resist frequent staining, but no fabric is immune to the occasional gaming ravenous snack spill you’ll regret at 2 a.m.
