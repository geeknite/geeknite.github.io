## Maintenance and care tips
### Cleaning routines that keep the cosmos fresh
- Regular upkeep: a quick broom sweep or dusting clears leaves and debris that would otherwise dull the design.
- Spot cleaning: use mild detergent mixed with water for stains; avoid harsh chemicals that could erode fibers or fade colors.
- Deep cleaning: a gentle wash with a garden hose or a low-pressure rinse works well. Avoid high-pressure washers that could force water into the backing.
- Drying: let it air dry completely before returning to outdoor use to avoid moisture pockets that may harbor mildew.

### Seasonal storage and longevity
If you have a harsh winter or a rainy season, consider storing the rug indoors or covering it with a breathable protective cover. Rolling rather than folding helps preserve the weave over time and minimizes creases. When you unwrap it again, you’ll likely find those celestial lines still crisp and ready to guide your next gathering.
