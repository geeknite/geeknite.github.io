## The Basics

### What is Victorian Nightmares?

Victorian Nightmares is a cooperative horror-themed board game set in the spooky streets of Victorian England. Players take on the roles of intrepid heroes, each with unique abilities and backstories, as they face off against monstrous entities and unravel dark mysteries. The game features beautifully illustrated cards, detailed miniatures, and an atmosphere so thick you could cut it with a knife.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Intricately designed miniatures of both heroes and monsters that will either make you gasp in admiration or scream in terror.
- **Cards**: Each hero and monster is represented by a deck of cards detailing their abilities, actions, and stats.
- **Game Board**: A richly illustrated map of Victorian England, complete with haunted mansions, foggy alleyways, and ominous graveyards.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects throughout the game.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Victorian Nightmares is like preparing for a seance. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your heroes. Remember to dim the lights for extra spookiness. You're now ready to face the horrors that lurk in the shadows.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their hero's abilities. You can move, attack, investigate suspicious locations, and more. Cooperation is crucial, so strategize with your fellow adventurers to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Victorian Nightmares is a blend of strategy and luck. Each monster has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic attack only to have the monster vanish into thin air? Welcome to Victorian England, where the supernatural reigns supreme.
