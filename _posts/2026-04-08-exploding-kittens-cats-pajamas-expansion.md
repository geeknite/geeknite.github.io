## What is Cat's Pajamas Expansion?
The Cat's Pajamas expansion is not a tiny add-on; it is a full-on meow-mentum booster for Exploding Kittens. It introduces a set of Cat Cards that come with humorous art, cat-themed gags, and some truly savage combos that will make you question if your friends are secretly feline overlords. The expansion also includes new card types that interact with base game rules in surprising ways, so you end up with more chaos at the table than a litter box at the edge of a thunderstorm.

### The Cat Cards
There are fifteen new Cat Cards, each one a tiny feline frenzy of chaos. Some trigger chain reactions where collecting multiple Cat Cards unlocks a big effect; others act as counters or fakes to force players to adjust their bluffing game. In short, these cats like to play hardball, and by hardball I mean purring into your ear while the board erupts in tiny giggles (and occasional real explosions figuratively in your expectations). The Cat Cards in this expansion can be used individually or to complete a Cat Card set for greater effects, creating a new layer of deck-building mischief on top of Exploding Kittens. The art is darling, the jokes are sharp, and yes, some cards require you to perform a silly cat pose to activate them. If you are playing with kids, beware of excessive laughter that might break the sofa.

### New Mechanics
Beyond the Cat Cards, Cat's Pajamas introduces some lightweight new mechanics designed to speed up the chaos without overcomplicating it. Expect a slight shift in tempo: more moments to curse softly under your breath as you realize you should have defended against the furry menace. The expansion also adds new tokens that track special cat combos and a couple of paws actions that let you steal a card or peek at someone else’s hand with a dramatic meow. The designers clearly had fun with the theme, because nothing says strategy like a card that requires you to meow before you play it.

## In Practice: The Chaos Playground
If you are familiar with Exploding Kittens, you know the game can swing from lighthearted cat jokes to edge-of-seat tension in about five minutes. The Cat's Pajamas expansion amplifies that experience. In a typical round, you might find yourself negotiating with a handful of Cat Cards, eyeing the paw-some combos, and hoping your opponents underestimate you enough to miss your next big play. The theme remains consistent with the base game: zany, a little dark, and absolutely ridiculous in the best way possible. The pacing is still quick; this is not a heavy euro. The new Cat Cards add variety without dragging the game into a longer, more complicated version of itself. The biggest win here is that the expansion preserves the core DNA while injecting a fresh novelty that feels both new and familiar.

### House Rules and Variants
Want to tailor the chaos to your crew? Here are a few lightweight ideas that keep things breezy while dialing up or down the silliness:
- Cat Pose Mod: Allow players to perform a silly pose instead of a regular action, but give a tiny bonus for successful execution (to encourage participation).
- Minion Mode: Introduce a “feline minion” card that acts as a wildcard for a turn but must be played with a cat yowl.
- Tempo Tuner: Add a rule to accelerate or slow the deck based on how many Cat Cards have been played, affecting round length without changing core interactions.

Cat's Pajamas keeps the learnability curve in check. New players pick up the core flow quickly, aided by the visual cues on Cat Cards and the intuitive nature of the paw tokens. If you’re bringing this to a classroom or family game night, a brief “cat orientation” can help first-timers understand when to meow and when to bluff with paws up.

## Strategy Tips and Teachable Moments
- Look for cat-set opportunities: The Cat Cards reward collection of specific sets. Pay attention to whether your opponents are close to completing a set and consider shifting your play to disrupt their progress.
- Bluff with paw tokens: Paw tokens aren’t just cute; they encode possible future swings. Use them to threaten a big payoff at the right moment and keep your tablemates guessing.
- Timing is everything: The most devastating plays often arrive when your opponents are feeling overconfident. Hold a few Cat Cards in reserve for late-round chaos.
- Don’t underestimate the power of silliness: Sometimes the best move is a dramatic meow followed by a well-timed card flip. The mind games as much as the cards.
