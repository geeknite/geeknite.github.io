### H3: Storage, protection, and display tips
Protecting your BT01 collection is as important as protecting your ever-so-delicate keyboard during a late-night build session. Here are a few practical tips:
- Invest in proper sleeves: Soft outer sleeves with a snug inner pocket for the actual card helps prevent edge wear and keeps foils looking spectacular between play sessions.
- Use solid binders: A binder with sturdy separators makes it easy to showcase your collection without grinding your thumbs on sticky labels.
- Temperature and humidity: Card integrity appreciates a stable environment. Avoid leaving sets in direct sunlight or under extreme temperature swings—your art deserves a comfortable climate, too.
- Display with flair: If you’re the type who wants to show off, consider a small display rack in your gaming room. Framing BT01s is optional, but it’s a vibe that hardcore fans can appreciate.
