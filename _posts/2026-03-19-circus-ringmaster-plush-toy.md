## Care, maintenance, and longevity

Care is straightforward. A light hand wash or spot cleaning keeps the coat looking crisp, while avoiding heat is recommended to preserve the plush texture. Air drying is preferred to keep the hat perched just so. If you want to keep the ringmaster pristine for years, consider placing it away from direct sunlight that can fade the red tailcoat over time. When not in use, store the plush in a cool, dry location to prevent mold or mildew from creeping into the fabric. The life of a plush is long if you treat it with patience and a sense of humor; you do not need to turn every day into a cleaning operation, but a gentle refresh now and then helps keep the character alive.
