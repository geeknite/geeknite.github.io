## Cleaning, maintenance, and daily care

Maintenance is where a lot of outdoor furniture either earns you a gold star or drops it into the “easy-to-forget” pile. The Gardeon set makes life simple:

- Wipe frames with a damp cloth and mild soap to remove dirt. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the finish.
- Cushions: remove and spot-clean as needed. If you’re dealing with a spill, dab gently and let air-dry. The cushion fabric is designed to resist moisture, but it’s not magic; large spills deserve a quick cleanup to prevent staining.
- Storage cover: give the cover a quick wipe and ensure it’s dry before putting it away to avoid mold. The cover is a small shield that pays off when you’re not in the mood to redecorate the patio after a rainstorm.

With routine care, the set should maintain a respectable appearance for several seasons. The key is to keep cushions clean and dry, store them under the cover when you’re not using the space, and resist the urge to leave the furniture out in heavy storms without protection.
