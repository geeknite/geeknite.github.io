## Introduction: Enter the Mystery of Silent Falls

Welcome, fellow detectives and mystery enthusiasts! Today we're diving deep into **Alice ha desaparecido: Silent Falls** (Alice has disappeared: Silent Falls), an innovative cooperative mystery board game that revolutionizes the detective genre. If you've ever wanted to experience the suspense of solving a missing person case without leaving your gaming table, this unique text-based thriller is your perfect case file.

## Game Overview & Fact-Checking

### Publisher and Design

**Alice ha desaparecido: Silent Falls** is published by **Ludonova**, a Spanish board game publisher known for innovative narrative-driven games. The game was designed specifically for the modern era, incorporating smartphone technology as a core gameplay mechanic.

#### Key Information

- **Players:** 3-6 players (optimal: 4-5)
- **Age Range:** 16+ (mature themes)
- **Play Time:** 60-90 minutes
- **Game Type:** Cooperative text-based mystery
- **Language:** Originally Spanish, with English adaptations
- **Technology Required:** Smartphones for all players

### The Premise: Where is Alice?

In the seemingly quiet town of **Silent Falls**, local resident Alice has vanished without a trace. You and your fellow players take on the roles of townspeople connected to Alice in various ways - friends, family members, acquaintances, and locals who knew her. Through text messages, notes, and investigative cooperation, you must piece together the truth about Alice's disappearance before time runs out.

**What sets this apart:** Unlike traditional board games with physical pieces and boards, Alice ha desaparecido primarily uses **text message communication** between players, creating an intimate, modern mystery experience.

## Innovative Gameplay Mechanics

### Text-Based Communication System

**Core Innovation:** Players communicate primarily through **written messages** (physical notes or text messages), creating an authentic investigation atmosphere.

#### How It Works

1. **Character Assignment:** Each player receives a character card with their relationship to Alice
2. **Information Distribution:** Players start with different clues and secrets
3. **Timed Investigation:** Players have limited time to share information and solve the mystery
4. **Message Exchange:** Communication happens through written notes or smartphone messages
5. **Revelation Phase:** Players collectively piece together Alice's fate

### Character Roles and Relationships

**Available Characters:**
- **Close Friends:** Alice's inner circle with intimate knowledge
- **Family Members:** Relatives with family secrets and history
- **Acquaintances:** Casual connections with peripheral information
- **Local Residents:** Townspeople who witnessed unusual events
- **Authority Figures:** Officials with access to formal information

Each character possesses **unique information** crucial to solving the mystery, making every player essential to the investigation.

> **Pro Tip:** Take detailed notes during play - small details often become crucial clues later in the investigation!

## Gameplay Experience Deep Dive

### Setup and Initialization

**Quick Setup Process:**
1. **Character Selection:** Players choose or are assigned character roles
2. **Information Distribution:** Secret information given to each player
3. **Communication Setup:** Establish messaging groups and protocols
4. **Timer Preparation:** Set investigation timeframe (usually 45-60 minutes)
5. **Ground Rules:** Establish communication guidelines and restrictions

### Investigation Phases

#### Phase 1: Information Gathering (15-20 minutes)
- Players read their character information privately
- Initial communication begins between players
- Basic timeline and facts established
- First theories and suspicions emerge

#### Phase 2: Deep Investigation (20-30 minutes)
- Players share more detailed information
- Cross-referencing clues and testimonies
- Event cards introduce new developments
- Pressure builds as time runs short

#### Phase 3: Resolution (10-15 minutes)
- Final theories presented and discussed
- Players vote on most likely scenario
- Solution revealed and compared to player theories
- Success or failure determined

### Communication Dynamics

**Successful Communication Strategies:**
- **Information Sharing:** Balance between revealing and concealing
- **Active Listening:** Pay attention to subtle details in messages
- **Question Asking:** Probe for inconsistencies and additional details
- **Timeline Building:** Collaborate to establish chronological events
- **Theory Testing:** Propose scenarios and test against available evidence
