## Practicality: Cleanliness, Care, and Everyday Use
Caring for the Cloud Sofa is surprisingly manageable for a fleece-covered monster of plush joy. The fleece itself doesn’t demand a lot of special treatment. Routine vacuuming to remove dust and hair is sufficient. For stains and spills, most fleece fabrics respond well to a quick spot-clean with mild detergent and water. Always test a small area first; you don’t want to discover the couch’s darker side during a big spill. The slipcovers, if included and washable, can simplify maintenance significantly. If slipcovers are not removable, you still have the option of using a damp cloth and gentle cleaners; just avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the fabric or fade the color.

A few tips from our testing: keep a small stash of microfiber cloths handy for quick touch-ups after gaming snack attacks. Use a lint roller occasionally to manage pet fur. If you’re a party host, place a couple of blankets over seating areas to keep upholstery pristine while guests hog the cushions in a relaxed fashion. It’s not a fashion statement; it’s a life hack for couch longevity.
