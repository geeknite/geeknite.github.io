Maintenance: Keeping Your Poker Set in Shiny Shape
-------------------------------------------------

- Wipe cards with a dry, soft cloth if you notice fingerprints or oil smudges. Avoid moisture; water and poker do not mix well unless you’re intentionally turning your game night into a science demonstration about surface tension.
- Wipe wood with a microfiber cloth; consider a light wood-safe oil if you want to keep the finish looking fresh for years. Do not saturate; you’re not refinishing a ship’s deck here.
- Chips can be cleaned with a quick wipe; if you’re worried about color wear, a gentle polishing with a lint-free cloth will do. Avoid harsh cleaners; you’re not polishing a car; you’re polishing a vibe.
- Dice can be wiped dry; if you notice seizing in a rolling night, check for nicks or rough edges and swap them if necessary.
