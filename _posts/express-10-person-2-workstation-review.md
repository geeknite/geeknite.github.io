## Introduction

If you’ve ever wondered what it feels like to cram an entire mini-ecosystem of office workers into a single workstation, boy, do we have the review for you! Today, we’re diving deep into the Express 10 Person 2 Workstation with Screens. Is it a haven of productivity or a haven of chaos? Let’s find out!

## Specs and Features

Let’s start with the basics. What are you getting with this monstrous piece of office furniture?

- **Dimensions**: 9400mm x 1530mm x 1200mm (yes, it's basically the length of a small yacht)
- **Capacity**: Comfortably fits 10 adults (or uncomfortably fits 15 if you’re into sardine-style seating)
- **Screens**: Each workstation comes with its own screen (privacy, who’s she?)
- **Colors**: White and Black (because why complicate things?)

## Installation: The Ultimate Team-Building Exercise

Installing this workstation is not just a task; it’s an event. Gather your coworkers, bring snacks, and maybe even some team-building exercises because you’re going to need it. 

### Pros:

- **Team-Building**: Nothing says camaraderie like collectively figuring out which screw goes where.
- **Exercise**: You’ll hit your step goal just by walking around the thing.

### Cons:

- **Complexity**: Have you ever tried to solve a Rubik’s cube blindfolded? This is harder.
- **Time-Consuming**: Plan to dedicate at least a weekend (or two) for assembly.

## Functionality: Productivity or Pandemonium?

### The Good

- **Spacious**: Each person gets their little slice of office heaven.
- **Screens**: Provides a nice sense of semi-privacy, which is about as much privacy as an office dog gets.
- **Aesthetically Pleasing**: It’s modern and sleek, perfect for the Instagrammable office.

### The Not-So-Good

- **Noise Levels**: Did you know that 10 people can make a lot of noise? Now you do.
- **Personal Space**: If you’re a fan of personal bubbles, you might want to keep looking.
- **Cable Management**: More cables than your old PlayStation setup.

## Comparison: Express 10 vs. The World

How does this workstation hold up against its competitors?

### Express 10 vs. Individual Desks

- **Price**: Surprisingly cost-effective for 10 people.
- **Space**: Takes up less room than 10 individual desks.
- **Collaboration**: Easier to collaborate when you’re all within arm’s reach (literally).

### Express 10 vs. Open Office Plans

- **Privacy**: More privacy than a completely open plan.
- **Noise**: Similar noise levels (aka, high).
- **Aesthetics**: Much more organized and less chaotic.

## Final Thoughts: Yay or Nay?

The Express 10 Person 2 Workstation is a bold choice for any office. In the right environment, it can foster collaboration and save space. In the wrong one, it can devolve into chaos. Consider your office needs, your team’s work style, and maybe invest in some noise-canceling headphones.
