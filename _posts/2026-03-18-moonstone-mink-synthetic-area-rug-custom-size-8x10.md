## Performance and Maintenance
Synthetic rugs shine in the cleaning department and Moonstone Mink is no exception. The fibers are designed to resist staining from common culprits like coffee, wine and pet accidents. The cleaning routine is straightforward: blot spills immediately, avoid hot water unless you want a chalky residue, and spot clean with a mild detergent diluted in water. For deeper cleaning you may use a gentle shampoo and rinse with clean water, then let air dry. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that can degrade the fiber or fade the color.

Vacuuming is a breeze on low to medium suction since aggressive suction can push the pile into an uneven surface. Use a vacuum without a beater bar or with a gentle setting to reduce fiber fraying. The rug is designed to resist shedding more than typical synthetic rugs, which makes upkeep easier for households with kids and pets.

### Pet and Kid Friendly
If you share your space with a shedding pet or little explorers, Moonstone Mink handles the chaos with ease. The color is forgiving for pet hair, and the fiber does not trap odors as stubbornly as some natural fibers. A light sweep or a quick spray of fabric freshener can revive the surface between deeper cleanings. It is not advisable to use bleach or harsh solvents that may degrade the dye and fiber of the rug over time.

## Installation and Care Tips
- Place a non slip pad under the rug to keep it flat and avoid curling at the edges
- Vacuum weekly with a gentle setting
- Clean spills immediately with a clean absorbent cloth
- Use a mild detergent for stubborn stains, test on a small area first
- Avoid direct heat sources when drying after a cleaning
