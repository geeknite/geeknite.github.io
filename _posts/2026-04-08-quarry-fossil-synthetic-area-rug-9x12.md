## Care Schedule and Maintenance Cheatsheet
- Vacuum at least once a week; more often in high-traffic zones.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean towel; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper.
- For sticky messes, use a mild dish soap solution and gently wipe; rinse with a damp cloth and blot dry.
- Periodic professional cleaning is optional but can extend the rug’s life if you’re dealing with stubborn pet odors.
- Rotate the rug every few months to avoid uneven wear and to preserve the fossil pattern’s balance.
