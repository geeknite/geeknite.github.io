## The Care and Feeding of a Rug That Has Opinions

Care is king, especially with rugs that sport character. Here are practical tips to keep the Cream Navy rug looking sharp while preserving the “quirky charm” vibe of its imperfection:

- Vacuum weekly to prevent dirt from embedding in the weave. Use a vacuum with adjustable height so you don’t suck the entire rug into your canister of regret. 
- Attend to spills immediately. Blot with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can spread the stain and make the flaw look like a feature in a sci‑fi light show. 
- Use rug pads to enhance stability. A pad reduces slipping, helps with foot comfort, and can also slightly mask minor edges where the weave may shift.
- Consider occasional professional cleaning if you’re dealing with tough stains or odors from life’s culinary experiments. It’s cheaper than sofa replacement and less dramatic than repainting the walls to match your mood.
- Rotate the rug occasionally if the flaw catches the light differently in different parts of the room. This is the decor equivalent of a director calling “cut” and then changing the lighting to reveal a new side of the scene.
