## How to verify authenticity and avoid scams
The used market can be a minefield. Here are pragmatic steps to verify authenticity and ensure you’re not buying a counterfeit or stolen device:

- Check the device’s IMEI/serial against the original purchase information and country of origin
- Confirm the activation lock status and ensure you can sign in with a valid Apple ID (to avoid bricks post‑purchase)
- Inspect battery health and screen condition as part of your due diligence
- Ask for a short video walk‑through showing the iPhone booting up and operating the camera and fingerprint/face ID
- If possible, perform a live test with your own SIM to confirm network compatibility and data performance

With a bit of vigilance, you’ll end up with a very capable phone that’s still a joy to use, and you’ll avoid the regret of a nonfunctional brick at the worst possible moment.
