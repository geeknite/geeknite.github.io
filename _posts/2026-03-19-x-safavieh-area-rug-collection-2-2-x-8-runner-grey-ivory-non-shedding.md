## Cleaning, Care, and Real-World Durability
Let’s talk reality: rugs aren’t just decorative; they’re workhorses that bear the brunt of real-life messes. The Grey & Ivory combo helps hide minor stains, dirt, and the occasional misbehaving crumb from a late-night snack run. The cleaning approach is straightforward:

- Vacuum regularly, using a clean-beater bar setting if your vacuum supports it. This keeps the pile looking plush and minimizes embedded dirt.
- For spills, act quickly. Blot with a clean cloth and avoid rubbing, which can work the stain deeper into fibers.
- For tougher stains, a mild detergent mixed with water can be applied with a cloth. Test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure colorfastness.
- If the rug gets damp, ensure it dries thoroughly to prevent mold and mildew. A dry environment, seasonal airings, and occasional sun exposure can help maintain color integrity and reduce odor buildup.

One common caveat with many machine-made rugs is the potential for color migration if you pair with heavy-wetted cleaning or strong chemical solutions. The Grey & Ivory palette isn’t as prone to dramatic color bleeding as more vibrant patterns, but you should still moderate cleaning solutions and always blot rather than saturate. In our experience, a light cleaning schedule keeps the rug looking new for longer and helps it resist staining if you have kids or pets with snack habits that would make a food critic faint.
