## How to play
This is where the hybrid vibe gets wild. The UNO part of the game remains familiar: match color or number to the card on the discard pile, draw cards when you cant, and play action cards to disrupt your rivals. The slot machine flavor adds a few twists:

- On certain turns, you may spin the mini wheel for a chance at a jackpot effect
- Wild Jackpot cards may let you choose a color with an extra twist
- Credits or chips act as a scoreboard for bragging rights rather than real currency

The exact rules can vary by edition, but the spirit is consistent: maximize chaos, minimize house edge which is zero in a home game unless you count your friends stubbornness as a cost.

### Step by step playthrough
1) Shuffle and deal according to UNO rules. 7 cards for each player is the standard, though house rules may adjust for 2 players. 
2) On your turn, you can either play a matching card, draw a card, or trigger a slot wheel moment if the reference card instructs you to. 
3) If you draw, you are still in the game, but the slot wheel might have new consequences when your turn returns. 
4) When a Wild jackpot or Wild Draw Four is played, the color changes as usual, but the slot flavor may alter the next few turns in fun, unexpected ways, depending on the individual box variant you purchased.

The endgame remains the same: be the first to shed all your cards. The fun lies in the detours the tiny, colorful detours that keep everyone guessing what card will come next.
