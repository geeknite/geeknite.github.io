## The Basics

### What is Gondor at War?

Middle-earth Strategy Battle Game: Gondor at War is an expansion for the Strategy Battle Game, bringing you new scenarios, heroes, and armies from the iconic battles of Gondor. Whether you're defending Minas Tirith or laying siege to Osgiliath, this game offers a rich and immersive experience that will transport you straight to the heart of Middle-earth.

### Components

The game comes with beautifully crafted miniatures of your favorite characters, from Aragorn to Sauron. The detailed artwork and high-quality components will make you feel like you've stepped into the pages of Tolkien's epic saga. Additionally, the expansion includes new scenario books, army lists, and special rules to enhance your gameplay.

**Pro Tip**: Keep your cat away from the miniatures. Not only are they precious, but your cat might also think they're tasty.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Gondor at War is akin to preparing for an epic battle. Lay out the game board, assemble your miniatures, and choose your scenario. Whether you're defending the White City or leading an orc invasion, the setup is straightforward but requires a bit of patience.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players activate their characters and perform actions based on their abilities. From moving to attacking, casting spells, or rallying troops, the game offers a wide array of strategic options to keep players engaged.

### Combat

Combat in Gondor at War is a blend of tactics and chance. Each character has unique abilities and stats that influence the outcome of battles. Did you plan an epic charge only to roll a critical failure? Welcome to Middle-earth, where even the best-laid plans can falter.

## The Epic Experience

### Heroes and Villains

One of the best aspects of Gondor at War is the diverse array of heroes and villains. Play as Aragorn, Gandalf, or the Witch-king of Angmar, each with distinct abilities and stats. Relive iconic moments from the books and movies or create your own legendary battles.

### Replayability

With multiple scenarios and characters, Gondor at War offers endless replayability. No two games are ever the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more strategic warfare.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for additional expansions that introduce new heroes, villains, and scenarios. Your Middle-earth universe is about to get even bigger.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: Feel like a hero (or villain) as you battle for Middle-earth.
