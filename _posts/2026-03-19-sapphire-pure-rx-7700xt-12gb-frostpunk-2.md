Overclocking and headroom
--------------------------
If you enjoy chasing a few extra frames, the RX 7700 XT 12GB does offer modest overclocking headroom via the Sapphire Pure firmware and the AMD driver stack. In practice, we saw a few extra frames per second in some scenes, and the clock boost remained stable during long Frostpunk 2 sessions. However, the returns aren’t dramatic. You’ll likely gain a handful of FPS with a mild power bump and a well-ventilated case, but you’ll also push temperatures higher and risk slightly more audible fans. If your goal is maximum OC for a festival of micro-bursts in the city’s early game, we suggest a cautious approach: small increments, stress testing, and mindful fan curve changes.
