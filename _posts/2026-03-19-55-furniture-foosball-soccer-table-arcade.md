## Maintenance, Warranty, and Longevity

Maintenance on a 55 inch foosball table is not rocket science. A quick wipe-down after a session keeps grime from building up on the rods and rails. A periodic lubrication of the rod bearings helps maintain smooth action over time, particularly if you host regular play sessions where the rods are in near-constant use. The warranty, typical for this category, covers manufacturing defects and hardware issues for a reasonable period. As with any product in this category, following the setup instructions is essential; improper assembly can void parts of the warranty and lead to misalignment issues that are more annoying than a plot twist in a bad sci-fi movie.

### Cleaning Tips

- Wipe the surface with a damp microfiber cloth; avoid harsh cleaners that could strip the finish.
- Check for loose screws and tighten as needed; the last thing you want is a loose rail nuzzling your shin during a dramatic comeback.
- Inspect the ball return and ensure it is free of debris; a blocked return path is the fastest way to kill the momentum of a game night.
- If your room has humidity swings, keep the table away from direct heat sources to preserve the wood and the alignment of the rods.
