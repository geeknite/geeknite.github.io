## Troubleshooting and Common Pitfalls
No product is a perfect rainbow unicorn, and the XL expansion pack is no exception. Here are a few common pitfalls and how to avoid them:

- Pitfall: Incompatible revision. Solution: Double-check the exact model revision and the supported expansion packs before purchasing. If in doubt, contact support or consult the official compatibility matrix.
- Pitfall: insufficient space for mounting. Solution: Measure the UPS footprint and the expansion pack, and consider a different mounting approach or rack configuration.
- Pitfall: Firmware drift. Solution: Ensure firmware on the UPS is current and run a self-test after installation.
- Pitfall: Overloading the system. Solution: Remember that while the expansion pack increases capacity, you should still design your load within 70-90% of the UPS rated capacity for efficient operation.

If you encounter an alarm, the best approach is to re-check connections, reseat cables, and perform a clean test cycle. If the alarm persists, consult the manual or contact support for guidance.
