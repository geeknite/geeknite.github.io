### Abilities and their flavor
The Man Who Laughs is designed to feel like a narrative pivot on the battlefield. His array of abilities focuses on disruption, positioning, and a helpful nudge to Batman’s own attack efficiency. The Laughing Gas aura creates a zone where enemies suffer a slight debuff if they stay within range, which encourages a chess-like approach to map control. Contagious Grin provides a straight-up synergy bonus for Batman, provided you keep your distance window intact. Unpredictable Tactics offers a dynamic edge, giving you meaningful tactical choices after you land an attack.
