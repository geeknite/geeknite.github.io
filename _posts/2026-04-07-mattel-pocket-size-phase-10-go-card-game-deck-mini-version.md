### How to Play in a Nutshell
We are not here to replace the rules with a Reddit tutorial. We are here to summarize what you need to know without turning your brain to mush.

1) Each player draws a hand from the pocket deck. The GO! variant adds a few speed-up moments, but keep your cool because timing matters.
2) The goal is to complete ten phases, each one requiring specific sets of cards. Think of it as a scavenger hunt with a deadline.
3) On your turn, you draw and discard as you would in most card games, but there are GO! cards that can accelerate play or penalize opponents in amusing ways. You will feel the urge to sprint across the table when you hit a streak, even if the table is not the actual track.
4) The first player to finish all ten phases wins the round. The rest of us settle for the glory of being the person who did not drop a card in the wrong place, or who successfully whispered two sentences without crying about a bad draw.
