## What is the A Year of Sex Card Game

At its core this is a card driven party game that uses a yearly arc as the central motif. Each turn a player draws a card from a deck that contains prompts and dares. The players respond with short explanations or perform tasks depending on the card and the role they assume. The objective is simple be funny be bold and try not to spill your drink while everyone else is laughing. The game balances bold content with light mechanics to keep things moving and prevent long debates in the middle of a round.

## How to Play

### Setup

The players sit in a circle. The deck is shuffled. Each player is assigned a role for the round perhaps the narrator the judge the dare master or the confidant. The calendar board is placed in the center and a starting year marker is set to January. The rules are intentionally breezy but not so breezy that you can forget which month you are in. The goal is to accumulate points by delivering the most entertaining responses or by successfully completing dares.

### Turn structure

On a turn the active player draws a card and reads the prompt aloud. The other players may contribute suggestions or act as the audience depending on the card type. In some variants the active player earns points for the funniest answer while others award points to showmanship or boldness. The entering clock may be used if a card requires a time bound response. The turn ends when the prompt is fulfilled or when the timer runs dry.

### Card types

- Prompt cards: offer a witty question or situation that must be answered. The aim is to craft a short answer that lands with the most humor while staying within the agreed bounds of the table.
- Dare cards: require a gentle challenge such as performing a silly action a mock date or a stage whisper of a line from a cheesy romance trope. The dares are designed to be lighthearted and are optional when the table chooses to skip.
- Rule cards: introduce a temporary twist for the round such as everyone must answer in a fake accent or the narrator must speak only in rhyme.

### Scoring and win condition

Points are awarded by the table based on laugh count creativity and willingness to commit to the bit. There are no heavy dice rolling math moments here just a little friendly competition. The game typically ends after a certain number of rounds or when the calendar reaches December. The player with the most points at the end of the year wins a small bragging right and perhaps a ridiculous virtual trophy that you can show on social media without leaving the room.

### Content and tone notes

The content can lean spicy yet it remains within a comfortable boundary for most adult gamers. It is not a raunchy game for the sole purpose of shocking others; rather it uses humor to navigate occasional awkward territory with a friendly wink. It is up to the group to set limits and the rule set encourages consent and respect alongside laughter.

## Variants and House Rules

No two geek circles are identical. A Year of Sex invites house rules and optional twists to keep it fresh across multiple sessions. Here are a few ideas that have worked well in our theater of ridiculousness:

- Consent check rule: before prompting a dare the table votes to confirm that all participants are comfortable with the general theme of the prompt. This keeps the mood inclusive and prevents anyone from feeling cornered.
- Time crunch twist: every prompt has a 30 second to 60 second timer to push for rapid improvisation. This amplifies energy and reduces stall time.
- Role rotation: rotate the narrator role every round; the narrative voice can dramatically alter the tone making even familiar prompts feel new.
- Crafting a running story: maintain a loose ongoing story across December of the year to give players a sense of narrative continuity beyond each card.
