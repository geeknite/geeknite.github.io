## Setting the Scene

Fortune Row is an expansion for the popular City of Mist RPG, a game where players take on the role of ordinary people with extraordinary powers. Set in a noir-inspired city, Fortune Row introduces new locations, characters, and mysteries that will leave you questioning everything you thought you knew about the game.

### The Streets of Fortune Row

Fortune Row adds a new district to the City of Mist: an area teeming with hidden wealth, secret deals, and dangerous liaisons. The streets are alive with the buzz of fortune-seekers and shadowy figures. Think of it as Wall Street meets the underworld, where your next fortune or misfortune is just around the corner.

## New Mechanics: Fortune and Misfortune

Fortune Row introduces new mechanics that revolve around the concepts of fortune and misfortune. Players can gain fortune points that offer unique advantages or misfortune points that bring unexpected challenges.

### Fortune Points

Fortune points can be earned through successful actions and decisions, granting players special bonuses. Whether it's a sudden insight, a lucky break, or an unexpected ally, these points can turn the tide in your favor.

### Misfortune Points

On the flip side, misfortune points are awarded for failures and poor choices. These points introduce new obstacles, such as hidden traps or sudden betrayals. They add a thrilling unpredictability to the game, ensuring that no two sessions are ever the same.
