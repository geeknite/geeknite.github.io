Cushions are the soft landing in any outdoor arrangement. The high density foam in these cushions offers support that holds up after long sessions and when the gang sprawls into a starfish pose. ### Weather resistance and maintenance
Pinewood outdoors is all about a routine. An annual sealant touch up helps keep water and sun from gnawing away at the surface. In rainy climates, you may want to store cushions indoors during heavy storms or winter months. The frames respond well to light cleaning with mild soap and water; avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the finish. Note that wood tone can vary from batch to batch, which is normal. If you want a perfectly uniform shade across your space, you may need to rotate cushions or mix in a second batch, or simply embrace the variegation as a sign of natural character.
