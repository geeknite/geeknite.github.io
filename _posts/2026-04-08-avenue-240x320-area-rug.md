## Durability and maintenance: built for the long haul

### Spills and stains

Stained floors are a tragedy wearing the mask of a dramatic scene. The Avenue rug handles routine spills with minimal drama. Wipe, blot, and move on — if you can read the label quickly, you are already doing better than most of us during a Friday night pizza expedition.

### Cleaning routine

Vacuum regularly on a gentle setting. Avoid the beater bar on high power to reduce fiber agitation. If a stubborn stain appears, a mild detergent with a bit of water works wonders. For tougher messes, professional cleaning every so often helps preserve color and texture over the long run.

### Durability test summary

We tested the rug with daily foot traffic, a couple of chair wheels, and the occasional pet wander. The performance was steady, with no obvious pilling or unraveling after extended use. The fabric fiber structure holds up well under normal wear and tear, which is exactly what most people want from a mid range area rug.
