## Care and Maintenance
### Cleaning basics
Maintenance is not a nightmare; this rug is designed with a practical approach. Regular vacuuming keeps dirt from embedding in the fibers, and light brushing can help keep the pile looking even. For spills, act fast: blot with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing, and consider a mild soap solution if needed. Test any cleaner in an inconspicuous area first to prevent color changes. The pattern is forgiving, but that doesn’t mean you should invite stains as a challenge.

### Deep cleaning and longevity tips
Every so often, a gentle full-surface clean helps maintain the vibrancy of the color stripes. A machine wash is generally not recommended for most rugs of this weight, but a professional cleaning every 1–2 years can refresh the fibers without compromising the pattern. If you must do a DIY deep clean, use a mild detergent and ensure the rug dries completely to prevent mold and odor development. The key is to avoid oversaturation and to give the backing time to air out fully—which is a good practice for any rug with a busy color story.

### Pattern care and color longevity
Sunset hues, especially magenta and pink tones, can be susceptible to fading with heavy sun exposure. If your rug sits in a sunlit area for long periods, consider rotating the rug or using window treatments to preserve the intensity of the stripes. A simple UV-blocking curtain can extend the life of the color and keep the gradient looking as fresh as the first install day.
