If a player plays a card that breaks the order, the team loses a life and reattempts the round with the same cards. 5) The game ends after your final round or when you exhaust lives.\n\nThis quiet tempo is where The Mind shines. The rules are approachable enough for a curious eight-year-old, yet the optics of alignment—where you’re secretly synchronizing with teammates—feel like a verb you haven’t learned yet.
