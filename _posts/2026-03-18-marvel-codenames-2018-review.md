## What is Marvel Codenames (2018) and how is it different?

Marvel Codenames is essentially a standard Codenames setup, but the grid doesn’t show generic words; it shows Marvel character portraits or icons. The 2018 release by USAopoly converts those word cards into character cards, each with a Marvel personality that fans will recognize. It’s not a spoiler to say you’ll see a lot of superheroes and a few cinematic twists that reflect the MCU’s pop-culture era. The core rules stay the same: the spymaster gives a one-word clue plus a number, pointing to multiple cards on the table. The rest of the team tries to guess which cards match the clue without stepping into the assassin card, which ends your team’s turn in a very dramatic, board-game thriller fashion.

## How the game actually plays with Marvel characters

The core flow of the game remains faithful to Codenames: spymasters craft clues that link several of their team’s words, while the field team sifts through the grid to uncover those words. What changes with Marvel Codenames are a few thematic cues: character imagery, occasional references to movie scenes, and the occasional pun that lands a little easier if you’re a fan of the MCU. It’s not trying to be a cinematic encyclopedia; it’s trying to be a shared joke between players who happen to love superheroes.

### Rules recap tailored to Marvel Codenames
- Two teams, each with a spymaster and several field operatives.
- A 5x5 grid is laid out with Marvel character cards.
- The spymaster gives a single word clue plus a number, indicating how many characters relate to the clue.
- Team members discuss which Marvel cards fit the clue and guess until they hit an incorrect card (opponent’s card, neutral card, or the assassin card).
- First team to uncover all its agents wins. If you unmask the assassin, you lose instantly.

The experience is familiar if you’ve played Codenames, but the Marvel theming gives your tables a different energy. It’s a light, accessible lift for players new to word games and a comforting nostalgia blast for fans who grew up with Spider-Man action figures and the four X-Men who somehow never aged.

## The design, the theme, and the vibe at the table

Marvel Codenames leverages strong visual storytelling. The character portraits are recognizable, the colors pop, and the overall design keeps the focus on the table rather than on the box. The theme helps lower the barrier to entry for newcomers who might be overwhelmed by heavy rulebooks.
