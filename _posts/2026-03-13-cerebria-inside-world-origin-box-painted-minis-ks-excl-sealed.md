## Assembly, Display, and Painting Tips

If you have the itch to customize or expand beyond the KS exclusive paint jobs, here are practical tips to preserve the beauty of the Origin Box minis while also encouraging your own painting growth:

- Inspect parts before washing. Some variants have fine details that can be damaged by aggressive washing. A gentle rinse with warm water is often enough to remove residual film.
- Use a hobby knife to remove mold lines with care. Mindclash sculpts are crisp, but you will still find the occasional seam you want to reduce for that flawless finish.
- Primer matters. A uniform primer helps the paints grip evenly and reduces the risk of chipping during play or transport.
- Thin your paints. The gods of miniature painting love thin layers; they reveal nuances in shading and keep edges crisp without obscuring detail.
- Consider a quick contrast wash for depth on cloth and leather. Don the highlight of the highest points to catch the light when displaying.
- Protect your investment. A matte varnish can preserve color depth and prevent the patina of handling from dulling the finish.
