## Cleaning and Care

### Maintenance

Vacuum with a standard vacuum head, not a turbo brush, to avoid fraying the fibers. The rug's color helps hide dust, but you should still schedule a monthly refresh to avoid fur tumbleweeds rolling across the living room.

### Stains

Most common spills clean up with a quick blot and a mild detergent solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade fibers or fade color. For stubborn stains, a warm water and mild soap mix typically does the job; test in an inconspicuous corner first.

### Rotation

Occasional rotation helps even wear across the rug. Because it's large, a quick rotation while cleaning is essentially a forearm workout, but it's worth it for even aging.

### Care Tips and Maintenance Schedule

- Vacuum weekly on a low setting; for high-traffic areas, you can increase to twice weekly.
- Rotate every 6–12 months to even wear.
- Use a rug pad to reduce slippage and prolong lifespan.
