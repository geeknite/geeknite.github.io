## What this expansion brings to the table

### Campaign mechanics deep dive
Campaign style cards introduce a narrative framework. The story progresses as players complete episodic goals that influence later chapters. Consequences of decisions ripple forward, altering card draws, character abilities, or the available missions in subsequent sessions. This creates a sense of agency that’s rarer in quick-play card games and more common in sprawling tabletop RPG campaigns. The campaign screen is protected by a layer of fog, but the fog is more charming than scary and much easier to walk through with family members who have different risk tolerances.

The expansion uses a modular approach: you can play a few standalone adventures in a single night, or you can chain them into a full campaign spanning several weeks. It’s flexible enough to accommodate a busy family calendar, which is essential because nothing ruins a night like three kids disagreeing about which chapter to start next. The modular nature also means you can tailor the tension and difficulty to the crowd—gentle for newcomers, a tad more challenging for seasoned players who enjoy raking chaos over a teacup.

## Gameplay flow: rhythm, pace, and a dash of narrative spice

### Turn structure with a twist
Each round, players perform a sequence of actions that feels pleasantly familiar to anyone who’s played a modern card game: draw, play, and discard or resolve. But the campaign context injects a narrative layer. Actions aren’t just about numerics; they reveal lore, unlock new scenes, and create expectations for the next round. The narrator (which can be a rotating role among players) sprinkles flavor text that makes the room feel like a Gothic living room rather than a sterile gaming arena.

### How the campaign shapes choices
Because outcomes feed into future episodes, you’ll often weigh short-term benefits against long-term consequences. Do you heal a character now, risking a future penalty, or do you press your advantage and risk a future cliffhanger? In the family setting, this becomes a social exercise as much as a mechanical one: negotiating with siblings, trading favors, and deciding who gets the last bite of snack while a fate card waits in the wings. The sense of consequence is thematic rather than punishing, which keeps tension high without turning the table into a battlefield.

### Difficulty and pacing controls
The expansion includes scalable difficulty. If your table includes younger players or newcomers, you can drop the intensity by reducing the number of episodes per session or toggling a gentle mode. If your crew thrives on the drama and has a taste for near-disaster moments, there are options to intensify the challenge and increase the stakes between sessions. The design respects the reality that families aren’t always in the mood for doom and gloom; it offers a spectrum that lets everyone meet the mood where it sits.
