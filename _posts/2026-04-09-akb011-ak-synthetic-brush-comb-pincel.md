## Cleaning and Maintenance: Keeping the Gear Clean Is Part of the Mission

No tool is truly useful if it becomes a biofilm of powder and mascara after a week. Here’s a practical maintenance routine that keeps your AKB011 in top shape without requiring a lab-grade cleaning station:

- Clean the bristles after each major makeup session. A quick rinse with lukewarm water and a mild cleanser is usually enough. Squeeze out the water gently; don’t wring the bristles like you’re trying to remove a stubborn jam from a bottle.
- Do a weekly deeper clean. Soak the bristles (not the handle) in a gentle brush cleanser for a few minutes, then rinse until the water runs clear. Squeeze and reshape before air drying with bristles facing downward. This helps prevent bristle shedding and keeps the brush from becoming a magnet for dust and old product residue.
- The comb end benefits from a wipe-down with a small amount of gentle cleaner. If you’re dealing with waterproof mascara or heavy brow products, a brief soak in mild detergent followed by a rinse helps prevent buildup on the teeth.
- Let the tool dry completely before reinsertion into the makeup kit. A damp tool is a magnet for mold and bacteria—two things we definitely want to avoid in any self-respecting Geek-Nite cosmetics lab.
