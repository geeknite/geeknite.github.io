### Design and Build Quality: The Walnut Whisper
Walnut finishes are tricky. They’re not as forgiving as some painted laminates; scratches show up more easily and coffee rings have opinions. The GOF 3P uses a walnut veneer over a sturdy core (the exact materials vary by SKU, but you can expect a solid plywood or medium-density fiber core with a veneer face). The result is a desk that looks premium from a few feet away, and only a few more inches when you gather the courage to inspect the edges under a bright LED lamp. The edges are rounded and neat, which reduces that “your coworker’s arm has entered a combat zone” feeling during mid-day collaborations.

What matters in practice is how this holds up to real office life: the inevitable pairs of headphones snaking across the desk, the occasional spilled water bottle, and the day someone decides to place their monitor arm slightly askew because “the view is better there.” In all these scenarios, the GOF 3P shows resilience: the veneer resists chipping with careful use, the desk surface handles everyday use without showing scuffs as quickly as you might fear, and the frames stay square even after you’ve moved around the cubicles to chase the last-minute tasks of the day.

- Use a microfiber cloth for the veneer to avoid scratches and preserve the finish.
