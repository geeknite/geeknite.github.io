Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7 is the latest iteration of the beloved Passe-Trappe series, a fast-paced dexterity game that challenges players to flick pucks through a small opening in the board faster than their opponents. If you're a fan of quick reflexes, competitive gameplay, and a bit of friendly chaos, then this game is for you!

## The Basics

### What's in the Box?

Unboxing Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7 is like opening a treasure chest. Inside, you'll find:

- A compact wooden game board
- 10 pucks (5 for each player)
- Elastic bands (for flicking)
- A set of rules (which you'll probably ignore after the first game)

The components are well-crafted and sturdy, ensuring that the game can withstand the intense flicking battles that are bound to ensue.

### Setting Up

Setting up the game is a breeze. Simply place the game board on a flat surface, divide the pucks equally between the players, and ensure the elastic bands are taut. And just like that, you're ready to flick!

## Gameplay

### The Objective

The goal of Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7 is simple: be the first to get all your pucks through the small opening into your opponent's side. Sounds easy, right? Well, think again! The small opening and the fast-paced nature of the game make it a thrilling challenge.

### Game Mechanics

The game starts with both players simultaneously flicking their pucks through the central opening. The key to victory is maintaining a steady hand and a quick flicking motion. The game can get quite chaotic, with pucks flying everywhere, so be prepared for some hilarious moments!

The first player to clear their side of all pucks wins the round. It's a best-of series, so get ready for multiple rounds of flicking fun.

### Strategy

While Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7 might seem like a game of pure dexterity, there's a surprising amount of strategy involved. Players must decide whether to focus on defense, blocking their opponent's pucks, or go on the offensive, flicking pucks as quickly as possible. Finding the right balance is key to victory.
