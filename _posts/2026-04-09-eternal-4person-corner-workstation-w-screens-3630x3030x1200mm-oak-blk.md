## Ergonomics and comfort: keeping the team healthy while pounding the keyboard

### Adjustable features and seating ergonomics
Ergonomics is the word we keep hearing whenever someone mentions productivity, correct posture, and the occasional backache that sneaks in after a long design sprint. The Eternal corner desk typically offers adjustable height options for the main desks, enabling sitting or standing configurations. For four users, you want enough leg clearance to allow minor leg twitches without triggering a chain reaction of foot taps across the table. The keyboard trays, if included, should be adjustable to maintain a 90-degree elbow angle and reduce wrist extension. If your team uses trackballs or vertical mice, make sure there’s enough space to accommodate their hardware while avoiding yet another round of “whose mouse is this?” in the shared space.

### Monitor height and posture strategies
The monitor height should align with the user’s eye level when seated, typically around 90 to 110 degrees of neck extension. With four screens in play, the seating arrangement becomes part choreography. A common strategy is to assign each seat a dedicated screen or pair a couple of screens into a single shared view for collaborative tasks. The goal is to prevent readers from craning their necks like owls during the weekly knowledge transfer session. For extended tasks, consider adding anti-glare screen protection and an anti-fatigue mat in the standing zones to reduce foot fatigue after long sprints.

### Cable management and workspace hygiene
If there is a single thing that ruins a great desk faster than a spilled coffee, it is a spaghetti tangle behind the workstation. The Eternal system’s cable channels are meant to route power, data, and peripheral cables in a clean, modular way. You want enough slack to adjust devices but not so much that the floor becomes a trap for cable dragons. A well-kept cable management strategy reduces trip hazards and makes it easier to clean the desk surface between brainstorming sessions. Remember, cables are the plumbers of the tech world and deserve a tidy, labeled path so you can find the root cause of a buzzing monitor in under five minutes.
