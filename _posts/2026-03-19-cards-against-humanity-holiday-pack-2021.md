## How to Run It: Quick Setup and Round Play Tips
### Setup in 2 Minutes or Less
1) Shuffle the black prompt pile and place it within easy reach. 
2) Shuffle the white cards and deal 7–10 cards to each player (adjust for desired round length). 
3) Choose a Card Czar to read the black card first round; rotate the role each round to keep energy dynamic. 
4) The rest is simple: each player submits the best white card that fits the prompt, the Card Czar picks a winner, and the next round begins.

### Round-Tuning Tips for Maximum Laughter
- Encourage players to draw on personal holiday memories when selecting a response card; personal experiences often yield the sharpest punchlines.
- If the room grows tense, swap in fewer explicit prompts or temporarily pause to switch to lighter prompts.
- Use a timer to keep rounds snappy; tight pacing tends to maximize laughs and prevent derailment.
- Consider a wildcard round where players submit a two-word or short multi-card answer for added creativity.
