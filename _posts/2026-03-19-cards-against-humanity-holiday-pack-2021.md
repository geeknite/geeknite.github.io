The standard CAH formula thrives on sharp phrasing and timely interventions from the white cards. ## Experience: How It Plays in Real Life

Cards Against Humanity is at its best in a lively multi-player setting with 6 to 10 players. CAH has relatively low entry barriers: you don’t need to memorize complicated rules, and the game thrives on quick decisions.
