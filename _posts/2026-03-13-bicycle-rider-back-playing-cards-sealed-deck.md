Care and Maintenance

Careful handling is the secret sauce to prolonging a deck’s life. Wash your hands if you’ve been handling more than a handful of decks in a day; oils and residues can alter the feel of the cards for the worse. Store the deck in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. A slight change in humidity can sometimes alter how the cards respond to your touch; the goal is to maintain the “feel” you first encountered when you opened the box. If you’re moving from a performance space to a classroom or a coffee table session, consider stowing the deck in a soft pouch or a small case to minimize edge chipping and corner wear.
