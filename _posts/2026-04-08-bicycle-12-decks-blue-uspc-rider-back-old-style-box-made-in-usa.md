## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity Tips
To get the most out of your Bicycle Blue Rider Back decks, a few simple maintenance steps can extend their lifespan and preserve their feel:

- Storage: Keep decks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Sunlight can fade color slightly and humidity can affect the finish.
- Handling: Clean hands help. Oils and sweat can wear down the finish faster. If you’re performing a lot of shuffles, consider rotating decks every so often to distribute wear evenly.
- Cleaning not mandatory: For routine play, you don’t need to wash the cards. A light wipe with a dry cloth is usually sufficient if you’ve spilled something accidentally—just don’t soak them in water.
- Deck rotation: If you use these for performances, designate some decks as “stage decks” and others as “practice decks” to maximize life across your collection.

These care tips are pretty standard for quality playing cards, but they make a noticeable difference when you’re dealing with twelve decks that you want to feel and perform consistently.
