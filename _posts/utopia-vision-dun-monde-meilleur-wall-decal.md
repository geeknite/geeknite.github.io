The decal is made of high-quality vinyl, which means it's durable and easy to apply. No need to call in a professional decorator – you can slap this bad boy on your wall in minutes. ## Installation

### Step-by-Step

1. **Prepare your wall:** Make sure it's clean, dry, and free of dust. If your wall has more grime than a teenager's room, you might want to give it a quick wipe-down.
2. **Position the decal:** Hold the decal up to the wall and figure out where you want it to go. Use a pencil to mark the corners – or just wing it if you're feeling adventurous.
3. **Peel and stick:** Peel the backing off the decal and stick it to the wall. Start from one corner and smooth it out as you go to avoid bubbles. If you end up with bubbles anyway, pretend it's part of the design – you're just adding character.
4. **Admire your handiwork:** Step back and bask in the glory of your new utopian landscape. Feel free to take selfies and show off to your friends.

### Tips for a Perfect Application

- **Use a credit card:** Smooth out the decal with a credit card or a similar tool to get rid of any bubbles.
- **Work slowly:** Take your time to ensure the decal adheres properly. Rushing will only lead to tears – both yours and the decal's.
- **Ask for help:** If the decal is large, enlist a friend to help you apply it. Offer payment in the form of coffee or witty banter.
