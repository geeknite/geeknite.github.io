## Maintenance Tips: Keeping the Geeky Glow Alive
- Regularly vacuum and sweep to remove dust and hair. A clean surface reveals the rug's true colors and helps you avoid that stubborn pancake of dirt that forms when you neglect the basics.
- For spills, blot rather than rub. Rubbing can push stains deeper into fibers and create a more dramatic scene than a soap opera cliffhanger.
- A mild soap solution works great for most stains. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade color or texture. If you test a chemical, test in a small inconspicuous area first—nerds appreciate controlled experiments.
- Allow the rug to dry completely before storage. Dampness can lead to mildew, which is the universe's way of telling you that your lawn memes deserve a small tragedy.
- If you want to refresh the look, rotate or reposition the rug seasonally. A simple shift in orientation can make your outdoor space feel new without new furniture or a new design budget.
