Caring for the fossil-floral print means a sane approach to spills and regular maintenance. ### Cleaning and maintenance tips

- vacuum on low suction to avoid fiber sacking and keep the pattern crisp.
- rotate every few months to maintain even wear—and to prevent the fossil motif from developing a “where did that edge go?” look.
- blot spills immediately; avoid soaking the fibers.
- use a rug pad to reduce movement and extend life.
- consider professional cleaning periodically for a refresh that preserves color depth.

## Size, placement, and room mapping

### Picking the right size

The rug’s footprint matters as much as its print. For a standard living room with a sofa set, a 8x10 or 9x12 footprint typically works well, allowing all front legs of the seating to sit on the rug.
