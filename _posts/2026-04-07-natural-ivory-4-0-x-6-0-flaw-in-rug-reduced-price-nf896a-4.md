Maintenance and practicality: keeping the aura intact

Cleaning this rug is straightforward. Regular vacuuming with a low setting is recommended to avoid pulling threads or disturbing the weave more than necessary. If you own pets, you will be pleased to know that many fiber blends are designed to resist pet hair clinging to every thread. For spot cleansing, a mild detergent solution or rug cleaner applied sparingly typically does the job. Let the spot air dry — no heavy scrubbing to avoid flattening the weave’s gentle character. The rug should be kept out of direct intense sunlight for extended periods to prevent color fade while still allowing the ivory tone to glow with natural lighting during the day.
