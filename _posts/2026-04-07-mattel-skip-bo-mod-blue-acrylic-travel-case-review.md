## Maintenance and Care

### Cleaning Tips
Wipe with a soft microfiber cloth. For stubborn smudges, use a mild cleaner and a soft cloth, and avoid harsh solvents that could dull the acrylic’s clarity. Don’t flood the interior with liquids; the padding is designed to stay dry and clean, not soaked. If you notice dust collecting in the dividers, a gentle blast from a can of compressed air will do the trick without scattering card sleeves into the wild.

### Handling and Storage
Store in a dry, cool place away from prolonged sun exposure. Acrylic can yellow over many years in UV-heavy environments, but with normal indoor exposure, you should be fine. Keep the case closed when not in use to protect the interiors and preserve the magnet mechanism. If you’re a traveler who loves to see their cards glow under soft cabin lighting, you’ll want to treat this case with care but also with a bit of pride—the kind you feel when you know you’ve got your game on lockdown.
