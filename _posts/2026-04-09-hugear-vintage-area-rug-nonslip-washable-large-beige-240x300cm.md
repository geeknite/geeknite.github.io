## Care and Maintenance
### Routine Care
Vacuum regularly to keep dust levels low and to maintain the plush feel underfoot. Use a vacuum with a gentle setting to avoid pulling threads. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and avoid one area becoming more worn than another.

### Stain Removal
For stains, blot rather than rub. Use a mild cleaning solution and test on a small area first. If a stain persists, consider a professional cleaning option to avoid damage. The washable aspect says you can handle small messes at home but big ones should be treated with care.

### Deep Cleaning
For a deeper clean you can cycle a gentle wash, but ensure you read the care label and follow the guidelines for water temperature and cycle type. Drying should be done flat and away from direct heat sources to maintain shape and avoid shrinkage or distortion.
