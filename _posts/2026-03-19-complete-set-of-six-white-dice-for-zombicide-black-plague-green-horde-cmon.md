### Handling and care
White dice can show wear more quickly than dark dice, particularly if you use them on rough surfaces or occasionally drop them into the carpet of doom (also known as your couch cushions). A quick wipe with a damp cloth after a session is usually enough to remove dust and finger oils. If you want to go the extra mile, a gentle soap wash will do, but avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive scrubbing that could degrade the pips.

Stow them in a dice tin or in your Zombicide box if you’re traveling to a convention or your friend’s living room. They’re compact enough to fit into a standard storage sleeve, which makes them a decent travel companion for road trips to gaming nights.
