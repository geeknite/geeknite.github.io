## **Specifications**

Let's kick things off with the specs:

- **Form Factor**: ATX
- **Chipset**: Intel Z170
- **Memory Support**: DDR4-2133
- **Expansion Slots**: 2 x PCIe 3.0 x16, 2 x PCIe 3.0 x1, 1 x PCIe 2.0 x16
- **Storage**: 6 x SATA 6Gb/s, 1 x M.2 Socket 3
- **USB Ports**: 6 x USB 3.0, 6 x USB 2.0
- **Audio**: Realtek ALC892
- **Networking**: Intel I219-V, 1 x Gigabit LAN

Impressive, right? But numbers only tell part of the story. Let's see how it performs in real-world scenarios.
