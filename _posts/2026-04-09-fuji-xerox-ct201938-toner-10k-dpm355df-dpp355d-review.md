## Practical Tips to Maximize Yield

- Run small print batches rather than all-at-once dumps. This helps with printer warm-up and toner settlement, which can affect density consistency.
- Do a calibration page after installing a new cartridge. Your printer will thank you with more uniform output.
- Use the correct media type and density settings. Thick or glossy stock can affect toner deposition and paper curl, altering perceived density.
- Keep humidity in mind. Toner performance can wobble a bit with humidity shifts, especially in non-climate-controlled spaces.
- Avoid letting the printer sit idle for long periods with a full cartridge. Some laser printers benefit from periodic use to distribute toner evenly.
