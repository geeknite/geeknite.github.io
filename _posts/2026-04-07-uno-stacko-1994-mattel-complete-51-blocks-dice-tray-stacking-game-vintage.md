The block you choose must comply with the color or number rule dictated by the current variant. If you are using the dice tray, roll the die to add a constraint — color, target, or action. Some variants include a penalty for topple days, such as extra blocks or missing a turn, to keep the pace brisk and the mood playful.\n\n### Endgame\nThe last player to successfully place a block without toppling wins.
