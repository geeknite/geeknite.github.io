## Storage, Care, and Longevity: Keeping the Box and Its Treasures Safe

Like any good collector item, you’ll want to keep this box in decent shape to preserve its resale value and aesthetics. Here are a few practical tips:

- Store in a dry environment to prevent cardboard warp and mini paint bleed. Yes, I have become emotionally attached to cardboard boxes.
- Keep the plastic inserts intact if possible; they’ll help prevent damage during transport if you ever decide to move, which you probably will at some point because life happens.
- Paints and brushes should be organized away from the cards; you don’t want a stray brush brush-streaking across a flavor card and ruining a gag.
- For photos and display, use the included art cards to frame your shelf display and rotate content to keep the visuals fresh.

If you’re planning to run a game night around this box, consider setting up a mini “art corner” on the table where the Kovalic artwork can be appreciated during waiting periods between turns. It’s a fun way to keep the energy high even when the last player forgot to roll a die and you’ve spent three minutes arguing about initiative order.
