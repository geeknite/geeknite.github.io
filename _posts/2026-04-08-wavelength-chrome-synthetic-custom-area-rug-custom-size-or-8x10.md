## Care and Cleaning Hacks
- For everyday grime, a quick vacuum will keep the reflective surface pristine.
- For spills, blot, don’t rub; mild detergent and water are enough for most household mishaps.
- For pet-related stains, treat as soon as you remember; synthetic fibers resist odors and stains better than natural fibers, but nothing is perfect.
- Deep cleaning can be done with gentle carpet cleaning solvers; test on a small area first to ensure no discoloration.

## Color and Lighting Tips
Lighting changes perception. A bright warm light can emphasize the chrome’s warmth, while a cool LED setup might skew the rug toward a high-tech blue-gray look.
