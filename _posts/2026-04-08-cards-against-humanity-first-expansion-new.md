H2: Strategy Tips: Getting the Most Laughs Without Getting Grounded

- Start with the group: Before jumping into the new cards, scan your table’s vibe. Are you all here for a quick, silly session, or are you chasing epic, show-stopping lines? The answer will guide how you leverage the expansion’s density.
- Use “green” room for comfort: When in doubt, keep a few white cards in your hand that you know will land with your particular group. The expansion won’t replace the need for timing; it merely adds more options to misinterpret the prompt.
- Rotate the judging seat thoughtfully: The judge’s sense of humor matters more than their knowledge of pop culture. A quick rotation helps prevent one player from monopolizing the vibe, especially as more cards flood the deck.
- Pair wisely: Since there are more cards, you’ll want to keep a mental catalog of which white cards tend to pair well with which styles of black prompts. It’s not a perfect science, but a rough heuristic: wittier, more absurd white cards tend to pair well with offbeat prompts; sharper, more sarcastic lines pair well with blunt prompts.
- Manage the energy: If a round goes too dark or too chaotic, switch modes temporarily. The expansion’s strength lies in its ability to swing the tone, so don’t be afraid to reset and re-aim the vibe.
