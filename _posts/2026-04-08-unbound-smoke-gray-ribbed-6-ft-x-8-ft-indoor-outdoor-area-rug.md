## Cleaning and maintenance: keep it looking fresh without turning your life into a spa day
Caring for this rug is as painless as possible in a world full of pretentious cleaning manuals. The practical playbook:
- Regular maintenance: vacuum weekly to remove dirt and hair. Use a brush attachment to maintain the rib texture and prevent hair from hiding in grooves.
- Spot cleaning: blot spills with a clean cloth, then clean with a mild detergent solution. Rinse with water and blot dry.
- Outdoor care: in dusty areas, hose it down occasionally and allow it to air dry completely to prevent mildew.
- Stains: tougher stains may require a stronger detergent and a soft-bristle scrub. Test a small area first.
- Storage: roll, don’t fold, and keep out of direct sunlight to minimize color fading.

The goal is simple: keep this rug looking fresh without turning your life into a cleaning crusade. The materials are forgiving, and the color helps you forgive yourself for culinary experiments that wander onto the deck.
