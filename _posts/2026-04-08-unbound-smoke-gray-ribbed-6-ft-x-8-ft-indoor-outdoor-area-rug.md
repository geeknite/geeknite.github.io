## Cleaning and maintenance: keep it looking fresh without turning your life into a spa day
Caring for this rug is as painless as possible in a world full of pretentious cleaning manuals. Here’s the practical playbook:
- Regular maintenance: Vacuum weekly to remove dirt and hair. Use a brush attachment for the ribbed texture to prevent hair from hiding in the grooves.
- Spot cleaning: For spills, blot (don’t rub) with a clean cloth, then clean with a mild detergent solution. Rinse with water and blot dry.
- Outdoor care: If you live in a high-dust area, hose it down occasionally and let it air dry completely to prevent mildew.
- Stains: Tough stains may require a slightly stronger detergent and a quick scrub with a soft-bristle brush. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first.
- Storage: If you need to store it, roll (don’t fold) to prevent creases and keep it away from direct sunlight to minimize color fading.

The goal is simple: keep this rug looking fresh without turning your life into a cleaning crusade. The rug’s materials are designed to be forgiving, and the color helps you forgive yourself for any culinary experiments that wander onto the deck.
