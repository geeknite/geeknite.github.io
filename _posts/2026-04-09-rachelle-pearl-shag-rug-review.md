### Performance in spills and life
We tested spill management with coffee, tea, and a dramatic splash of blueberry juice (just to test dye stability). Blotting works reliably; blot from the outside in to prevent ring formation. For larger mishaps, a mild detergent solution is friendly to the fibers; avoid aggressive scrubbing that can loosen loft. The color holds well after gentle cleanup, and the pearl tone remains bright enough to light up the room even on a cloudy afternoon.

### Maintenance and cleaning
The rug is easy to clean. Vacuum regularly with a brushless attachment to avoid fiber pull. If you must use a vacuum with a beater bar, set to the gentlest setting and keep the height at the top fiber level. For spills: blot, then clean with a mild detergent. Do not rub vigorously, which can distort loft. Sheds are normal during the first several vacuum cycles; expect a few stray fibers before the rug settles into its plush state. For pet odors and stains, a light sprinkle of baking soda, wait a few minutes, then vacuum.

### Durability and long-term care
The rug’s dense pile resists flattening, and the backing handles wear with grace. Rotating the rug every few months spreads wear evenly and reduces the risk of permanent indentations from heavy furniture. If you have kids or a busy household, place a sturdy coffee table or ottoman on a larger rug to distribute weight more evenly.
