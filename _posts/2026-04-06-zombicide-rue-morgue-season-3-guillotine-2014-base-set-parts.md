In solo mode the Season 3 content plays itself with a hint of chaotic energy, as the bot turns sometimes appear to have read your diary and decided to torment you with a cunning plan. In cooperative play, the base set parts mingle well with Season 3. ## Component quality and art

The minis are crisp, clean, and sturdy. The new survivors have distinctive sculpts that each tell a tiny story of what it would be like to fight the undead in a sunlit cafe. The zombie sculpts are familiar enough to recognize, but there are enough new poses to avoid boredom during the second blood-splatter of the evening. Card stock is solid, boards lie flat, and the artwork is a moody mix of noir and neon. The base set parts are reliable and durable; the Season 3 pack blends well but adds its own color palette for variety. The Guillotine 2014 tokens are fun, not essential, but they definitely earn a few laughs when your friends realize you can stage a dramatic fade-out moment with a cardboard guillotine token.
