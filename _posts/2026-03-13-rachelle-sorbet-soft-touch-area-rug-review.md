### Maintenance and Cleaning Tips

Maintenance is a key metric for any area rug. The Sorbet rug cleans easily thanks to a fiber composition that does not trap dirt with the fervor of a black hole. Regular vacuuming on a low to medium setting lifts surface dirt without pulling at the fibers excessively. For spills, blot gently with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push liquids deeper into the pile. Most common beverages will show up as mild stains rather than manifesting as dramatic blotches, provided you act quickly. For more stubborn stains, you can use a mild detergent solution and a soft brush, followed by a thorough rinse with water and air drying. Do not place the rug in direct sunlight for extended periods if you want to preserve its color integrity; the sorbet-esque tones can fade under harsh sun exposure. If you have pets that like to chew or nibble on the edges, you may want to keep an eye out for fraying and trim any loose fibers promptly to extend the rug's lifespan. In a household with kids and pets, rotation every few months helps prevent visible wear in the most walked-on zones. A common-sense approach to care is sufficient here: keep the rug clean, keep it dry, and let the soft touch work its quiet magic.

### Cleaning and Care Special Notes

A quick reference cheat sheet for regular care is as follows: vacuum weekly on low to medium, spot clean spills immediately, rotate the rug seasonally to avoid uneven wear, and protect from direct sun to preserve color. If you ever consider professional cleaning, check with local services to ensure they can handle delicate fibers without over-agitating the surface. For those of you who like to DIY small improvements, a fabric-safe stain remover and a gentle scrubbing with a soft bristle brush should solve most common issues without compromising the pile. Remember, the goal is to keep the rug looking fresh without sacrificing its plush feel. If you want to preserve the sorbet aesthetic, store any strong chemical cleaners away from the rug and maintain a mild care routine.
