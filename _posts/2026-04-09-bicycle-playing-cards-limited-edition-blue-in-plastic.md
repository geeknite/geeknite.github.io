### Practical Tips: Storing and Caring for a Sealed Deck
Storing a sealed deck is a different art form than actually using it. For collectors who plan to keep the deck sealed, consider:
- Temperature control: avoid damp basements or sun-drenched shelves. Heat and humidity can degrade the tuck and reduce the crispness of the seal.
- Humidity considerations: a little moisture can cause warping or tackiness on the plastic wrap. Keep it in a dry place where the air is calm and merciful.
- Handling: if you must handle the deck to admire the foil stamping, do it with clean hands and consider a soft cloth to minimize fingerprints.

If you decide to unwrap it for use, you’ll uncover a classic Bicycle experience: a comfortable shuffle, a soft feel, and a deck that looks premium enough to deserve your best card tricks. The moment you break the seal, you reveal the true character—the tactile joy of card edges meeting finger pads.
