## Care and maintenance
### Cleaning and storage tips
- Wipe chips with a soft cloth to remove oils and fingerprints after heavy sessions. A quick wipe keeps colors vibrant and reduces glare on the felt.
- Store the kit in a dry place away from extreme temperatures. Wood can warp if left in a damp or overheated environment.
- Periodically check the case’s latch and hinges; lubricate if necessary with a light oil designed for wooden hardware to keep everything smooth.

### Card and accessory care
- Keep the cards in their deck sleeve or box when not in use to prevent bending and edge wear.
- Store dice and dealer buttons in their compartments to prevent loss during moves and to keep the table tidy during late-night shuffles.
