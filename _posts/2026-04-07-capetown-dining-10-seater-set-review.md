## Maintenance and Durability
### Everyday care
Durability is the practical side of aesthetics. A set with a strong frame and good finishing can survive the daily chaos of a busy household. Cleaners should be chosen according to the finish, but in most cases a soft cloth with mild soap and water is sufficient for routine cleaning. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that can degrade the finish over time. Spills should be tended to quickly to avoid staining, especially on lighter finishes. A quick wipe, a gentle scrub, and a pat dry is usually enough to keep the table surface looking respectable for a long time.

### Durability tests in the wild
In real life, parts can loosen, screws can settle, and the occasional chair may become your favorite seat in the whole house. The Capetown set typically holds up well to daily use, with the strongest signals coming from the table legs and the chair frames. If you plan to move the set around during cleaning or rearrangement, consider doing so with a helper to avoid scratching floors or bending legs. Shipping damage is always a remote possibility, but most retailers provide solid packaging and protective components to minimize damage during transit.

### Longevity and warranty considerations
Like any major furniture purchase, warranty matters. Look for coverage on structural integrity as well as upholstery. If a long term plan matters to you, check whether the warranty covers things like loose joints, finish wear, and accidental damage caused by an enthusiastic game night attempt at a dramatic victory dance post win. A good warranty gives you peace of mind that this is not a one season wonder but a piece that remains part of your home for years.
