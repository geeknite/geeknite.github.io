## Maintenance and Cleaning: Keeping the Cream Clean in a Real World

Cream rugs have a reputation for showing dirt more readily than darker tones. The NNEDSZ Madras Parker Cream does not escape this fate. However, with routine cleaning routines, you can maintain its crisp look without turning your home into a cleaning lab. Vacuuming at least once a week helps remove surface dust and pet hair before they become a permanent feature on your floor like a memory of the last bad haircut. A vacuum with a rotating brush can help lift fibers without dragging the fluff out of the pile, which is a small tragedy you want to avoid. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear. This is not just the moral support you owe to your rug; it also helps avoid the dreaded pattern imprint that happens when one part of the rug sees heavy traffic and the rest behaves like a ghost.

### Stain Management

For spills, act quickly. Blot, don’t rub, with a clean cloth. A water-based cleaner is often sufficient for most household spills, though if you have a stubborn stain and you are the kind of person who believes in the power of patience and a little elbow grease, you can use a mild detergent solution. Remember: test in a small inconspicuous corner first. If the stain persists, you might consider professional cleaning after a few months of use. The key here is moderation and patience; we are not eradicating a plague, just cleaning a rug, which is guidance you could apply to your laundry detergent routine as well.

### How to Protect the Crest of Cream

Use furniture coasters or felt pads under heavy chairs to avoid indentations from chair legs. This not only protects the rug but also keeps your floor from turning into a chessboard of circles. If you have pets, consider a quick grooming routine to reduce shedding on the rug. While the color can hide some pet fur, steady maintenance helps keep the entire aesthetic coherent.
