## Durability, maintenance, and longevity
Durability is the quiet killer of many sets. You want something that survives a few accidental drops, a rambunctious celebration after a big pot, and a dozen cleaning sessions without losing its luster. The acrylic case, with proper care, should stay pristine for years. The chips, if not subjected to harsh solvents or extreme environments, should hold their color and shape for quite a while. A few tips to prolong life:

- Keep chips away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading.
- Use a soft cloth to wipe surfaces—no abrasive cleaners that could dull the edge lettering.
- Store in a dry environment to avoid moisture warping the acrylic case.
- Avoid stacking too high; although stable, a tall stack increases the risk of tipping if someone leans in for a dramatic all-in moment.

If you plan to host frequent sessions, consider buying a couple of extra decks or sleeves to keep the edges crisp and the organization consistent.
