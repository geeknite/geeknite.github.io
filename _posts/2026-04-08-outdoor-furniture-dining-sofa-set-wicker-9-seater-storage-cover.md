## Storage cover: protection that earns its keep
The storage cover included with the set is not just a fancy accessory. It’s a weather shield meant to keep cushions dry, reduce mildew risk, and slow color fading on the wicker weave. A good cover should be UV-stabilized, breathable, and fit snugly enough to resist flapping in windy days without turning your patio into a sailboat.

Pros of the cover include: reducing cushion maintenance, extending the life of the weave, and making your backyard look neat even when you’re not cooking a feast for a family reunion. Cons might include the occasional need to secure the cover in-very-wind days and the fact that covers can become moisture traps if not vented properly. Most covers come with adjustable straps or elastic hems; take a minute to fasten those so you don’t wake up to a pool of rainwater pooled on top.

If you want to maximize cover effectiveness, consider a sheltered corner or a light windbreak to minimize direct exposure. You can also pair the cover with a basic dehumidifying routine inside—once a season, unwrap, dry, and air out the cushions to keep that “new furniture” scent intact.
