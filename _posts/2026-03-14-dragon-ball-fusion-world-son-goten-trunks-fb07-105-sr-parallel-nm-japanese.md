## Display, care, and a few handy tips for keeping your NM JP gem pristine
Care matters when you own a beautiful JP SR parallel. Here are a few practical tips to ensure your Son Goten Trunks stays as pristine as the day you peeled it from its protective wrapper:

- Sleeve aggressively: choose a high-clarity sleeve that doesn’t hide the holo but protects it from scratches. If you have the budget, consider anti-glare sleeves to reduce reflections in photos.
- Top-loader is your friend: a rigid top-loader adds a layer of protection during transport and storage. Place the card in the top-loader before sleeve insertion for added safety.
- Bindery and display: store in a binder with page protectors designed for the card width. Keep the binder away from direct sunlight to guard against color fading and holo dulling.
- Handling etiquette: always wash hands before handling and avoid finger oils on the surface. If you have sweaty palms, a quick wipe with a microfiber cloth is a smart move before you line up a player photo for social media.
- Environment matters: humidity and temperature swings can affect cardboard integrity. A stable room with moderate humidity is ideal for long-term storage.
