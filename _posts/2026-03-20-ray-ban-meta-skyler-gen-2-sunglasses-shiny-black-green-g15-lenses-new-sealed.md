Care and Maintenance: Quick Tips

- Always store in a proper case when not in use. This minimizes the risk of scratches and accidental bending. 
- Clean with a microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that can degrade coatings.

- Protect from extreme temperatures. A hot car can warp the frame and a long exposure to sun can degrade the lens coatings over time.
