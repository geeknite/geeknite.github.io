## Care and maintenance: keeping the finish bright
### Regular cleaning routine
- Wipe with a soft cloth and warm water after use to minimize water spots and fingerprints.
- Use a mild pH-balanced dish soap to avoid surface wear.
- Dry with a microfiber cloth to prevent water marks, especially on the rose-gold finish.
- For mineral deposits, apply a diluted white vinegar solution for a few minutes, rinse, and dry.

### What to avoid
- Abrasive cleaners, steel wool, or anything that literally sounds like a crime against kitchenware.
- Harsh acidic cleaners left on the finish for extended periods; they can dull the rose-gold sheen.
- Leaving standing water with minerals for long periods in areas close to the finish. Water spots left to dry can be stubborn.

### Long-term care tips
- Consider applying a finish-safe sealant recommended by the manufacturer if you want extra protection. Reapply as directed by the product instructions.
- Use a sink rack to prevent scratches when loading heavy pots.
- If you have a garbage disposal, run cold water for a minute after use to help clear the drain and reduce the chance of staining.
