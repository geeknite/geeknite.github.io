- "Alchemists" expansion: Adds potion challenges, new ingredients, and exciting new gameplay mechanics.

## Gameplay

In "Les Charlatans de Belcastel," players take on the roles of quack doctors brewing potions in a medieval town. Each player has their own pot board where they draw ingredients blindly from their bag, hoping to create the best potion without it exploding. Sounds easy? Think again! The game is a delicate balance of risk and reward.

### Setup

Setting up the game is as easy as shouting "Abracadabra!" Each player receives a pot board, a bag of starting ingredients, and a trusty spoon (because who doesn't need a spoon?). The game board is placed in the center, and the ingredient books are laid out for easy reference.

### Turns and Rounds

The game is played over nine rounds, each consisting of several phases:
1. **Fortune Teller Phase**: Draw a fortune teller card that affects all players.
2. **Ingredient Phase**: Players draw ingredients from their bags and place them in their pots. Be careful not to draw too many cherry bombs, or your pot will explode!
3. **Buy Phase**: Use the coins you've earned to buy new ingredients and improve your bag.
4. **Cleanup Phase**: Prepare for the next round.

### The Expansions

The "Herb Witches" expansion introduces three powerful witches who offer one-time-use abilities. Whether it's re-drawing ingredients or gaining extra points, these witches add a new layer of strategy to the game.

The "Alchemists" expansion brings potion challenges and new ingredients that can drastically change your strategy. It's like adding a pinch of dragon's breath to your already chaotic concoction!
