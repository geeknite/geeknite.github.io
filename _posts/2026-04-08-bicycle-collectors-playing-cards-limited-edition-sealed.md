Storage matters. Keep sealed decks away from damp corners and direct sunlight. If you’re displaying the deck, consider a glass or acrylic case that protects the cards from dust and curious pets. A soft cloth ridge helps prevent the foil from scratching, though the foil is robust enough to survive light handling if you care for it. When you decide to open the deck, do so with ceremonious slower-than-milkshake care; a moment of reverence before you break the seal may increase the thrill of your first riffle.

Don’t bend it; don’t spill soda; don’t let your dog attempt a magic trick with the Joker card at 2 AM. A little common sense goes a long way toward preserving your Limited Edition’s beauty and value for a longer time.
