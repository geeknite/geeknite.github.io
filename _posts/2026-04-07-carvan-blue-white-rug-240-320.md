### Cleaning and maintenance
Spill response is easier than you might expect. A wipe with a clean cloth and a mild detergent is usually enough for most stains. For tougher stains, a gentle cold water rinse and repeated blotting helps. The synthetic fibers resist absorbing many liquids, which means fewer times you need to call in the miracle cleaner. Regular vacuuming with a floor setting that avoids aggressive agitation keeps the pile from fraying. If you have pets, consider a quick brush to lift set fibers after muddy play sessions. The mat dries quickly in a sunny window, making weekend cleanup a breeze rather than a chore.
