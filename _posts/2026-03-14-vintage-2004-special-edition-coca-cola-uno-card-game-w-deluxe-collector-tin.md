## Care Tips: How to Preserve Your Coca-Cola UNO Tin

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent branding fade and yellowing on white card faces
- Keep the tin closed when not in use to maintain deck rigidity and fend off dust
- Use acid-free sleeves for individual cards if you’re aiming for long-term preservation
- If you display the tin, consider a small display case that keeps humidity in check and shields from curious pets
- Periodically handle the cards with clean hands to preserve print integrity and avoid grease-darkening the card edges
