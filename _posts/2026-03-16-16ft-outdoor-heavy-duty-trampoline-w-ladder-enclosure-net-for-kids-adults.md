## Durability and Maintenance
Outdoor gear faces sun, rain, and the occasional unwelcome wildlife audition. The trampoline reviewed here uses corrosion-resistant framing, weatherproof mats, and protective coatings that help extend the life of the unit. Regular maintenance tips include:
- Clean the frame and net with mild soap and water to remove dirt and avoid mold buildup.
- Inspect springs for signs of rust or deflection. Replace any springs that show stretch marks or loss of tension.
- Ensure the enclosure net remains taut. A sagging net can lead to zipper jams or safety gaps.
- Store the ladder when not in use to reduce weather exposure and minimize the chance of accidental tripping.
- Consider a seasonal cover to protect the mat from harsh sun and pine needles that can accumulate over time.

Durability is never as exciting as the first bounce, but it matters when you\'re spending real money and time on backyard adventures.
