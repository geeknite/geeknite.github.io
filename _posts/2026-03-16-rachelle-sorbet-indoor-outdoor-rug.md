## Durability and Maintenance in Depth
### Routine Care and Spills
A disciplined care routine pays off here. Weekly vacuuming prevents dirt from embedding itself into the fibers and keeps the appearance crisp. For spills, quick blotting with a clean cloth is the first line of defense. If the mess is more stubborn, a gentle detergent mixed with water can help release the stain. Rinse thoroughly and let air dry completely before placing the rug back into service
