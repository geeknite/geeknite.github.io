- Type: Legendary Planeswalker — Teferi
- Starting Loyalty: 4
- Collector number: 221
- Condition: Lightly Played (LP)

The six-mana cost is not nothing, and Teferi Time Raveler rewards you for controlling the pace of the game. The LP copy is perfect for a commander-table presence as well as a casual Modern or Legacy deck if your local scene allows for that kind of spin on blue control.

### Mechanics and Thematic Spice

Time Raveler is, at its core, a timing-focused planeswalker. The essence of its power lies in how it reshapes a typical turn cycle: you upend your opponent’s expectations by offering them fewer reliable opportunities to answer your threats, all while you leverage your own draw and spells to pressure through a win condition. It’s a card that rewards careful sequencing and reading of the table state. A well-timed Time Raveler can turn a potential tempo loss into a lasting advantage, which is exactly the kind of dynamic that makes blue control players grin (gently, like someone who’s just found the perfect card for a puzzle they’ve been solving all week).

#### Tempo, Card Advantage, and The Stack Ballet
When Teferi Time Raveler enters the battlefield, you’re committing to a plan: grind your opponent down using card advantage and resilient answers, while ensuring your own threats resolve in a way that your opponent cannot easily disrupt. The card’s presence demands respect, because it changes the calculus of every multicolor deck that tries to interact on the stack. If you’re building around Time Raveler, you’ll want a robust suite of draw effects to maintain your hand size and options.
