## Maintenance and care: keeping Moonstone Mink looking moonlit
### Everyday care
- Vacuum regularly with a low-suction setting to prevent fuzzing and frizzing of fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Avoid rubbing, as that can push the spill deeper and rough up the fibers.
- For minor stains, use a mild detergent diluted with water; test in an inconspicuous corner first to avoid color transfer.
- If you have pets, a quick brush can help keep the surface fluffy and reduce shedding on the top layer.

### Cleaning tips and tricks
- For tougher stains, a carpet-safe stain remover (as recommended by the manufacturer or your rug-care pro) works best. Always follow product guidelines and test first.
- A gentle steam clean can refresh the fibers without soaking the backing, though this is usually optional unless the rug has seen heavy foot traffic or spills.
- Rotate occasionally to ensure even wear, especially if you have a directional lighting setup or a particularly favorite spot on the rug that gets walked over the most.

### When to call in the pros
If you’ve discovered a stubborn stain that refuses to budge or if you have a big spill near the edge, it’s perfectly reasonable to reach out to a professional rug cleaning service. Moonstone Mink’s materials are designed to withstand professional cleaning methods, but you’ll want to ensure they know you’re asking them to work with synthetic fibers and a non-slip backing.
