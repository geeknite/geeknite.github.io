## Care and Longevity: Practical Tips
- Wipe down after use: A quick microfiber wipe removes fingerprints and minor smudges.
- Avoid sharp impacts: While the acrylic can handle travel, brutal drops onto hard surfaces can cause micro-chips along the edges.
- Keep away from high heat: Prolonged exposure to high temperatures can warp or discolor acrylic over time.
- Inspect latches: Periodically check that the latch remains aligned; a misaligned latch can lead to accidental openings mid-flight—trust us, airport security is not the place to learn this lesson the hard way.
