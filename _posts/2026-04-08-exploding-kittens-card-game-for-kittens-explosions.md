## How to Play: The Quickstart Guide
This is the part where the cat video becomes a strategy session. The gist: avoid drawing the Exploding Kitten. Everyone starts with a hand of cards; the deck contains a handful of Exploding Kitten cards and Defuse cards plus other action cards. The game ends when only one player remains.

1) Setup
- Remove one less Exploding Kitten than players from the deck.
- Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards to each player.
- Each player gets at least one Defuse card; otherwise you risk a quick knockout.

2) Turn structure
- On your turn, you draw a card from the draw pile
- You may play action cards to influence the next player
- If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you either Defuse or you are out

3) Exploding Kitten resolution
- If you Defuse, discard the Exploding Kitten back into the draw pile anywhere you like
- If you cannot Defuse, you are out of the game

4) Ending the game
- The last player standing wins

Note the presence of counter actions: Nope can cancel many actions, adding a layer of social banter.

## Strategy and Tactics
Beginners often overplay. The key is to manage your hand, balance Defuse cards, and use action cards to push risk toward others. See the Future reveals the next three cards, giving you a window into probabilities. Attack accelerates the doom for the next player, Shuffle randomizes the deck, and Skip ends your turn to avoid an awkward mid-draw moment.

House rules can shift this balance: some groups ban the Nope, some insist on a minimum 6 cards in hand before a player can draw again, etc. The point is to tailor to your crew while preserving the core chaos.
