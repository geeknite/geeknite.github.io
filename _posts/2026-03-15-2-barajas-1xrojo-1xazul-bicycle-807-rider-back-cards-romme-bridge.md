## Care, maintenance, and survival tips

- Avoid exposing to heat. Heat is the silent deck killer. If you run a hot game night in a stuffy apartment, consider a small fan and keep the deck away from direct sunlight.
- Keep humidity moderate. Cards like a bit of humidity but hate being soaked. A dry, controlled environment prolongs life and prevents warping.
- Don’t peel the corners while clinging to a misdeal. We’ve all been there, and the urge to strip a corner off a card like some weird peel-off sticker is strong. Resist it; you’ll regret it when you need that precisely shaped corner for a critical play.
- Clean gently. A soft microfiber cloth is enough to dust the surface; avoid abrasive cleaners that could degrade the finish or the printed pips.
