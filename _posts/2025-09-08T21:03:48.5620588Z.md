## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Waste Knights: Expansion Pack feels like preparing for a nuclear fallout. Lay out the cards, assemble the miniatures, and try not to get distracted by the sheer awesomeness of the components.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and use them to perform actions. These could range from salvaging and attacking to supporting other knights or thwarting enemy plans. The synergy between characters and their unique abilities is where Waste Knights shines.

### Combat

Combat is straightforward but strategic. Players must coordinate their attacks and special abilities to defeat enemies and complete objectives. Remember, teamwork makes the dream work — or in this case, saves the wasteland.

## The Story

### Scenarios

The Waste Knights: Expansion Pack introduces new scenarios that are inspired by iconic post-apocalyptic themes. From battling renegade warlords to stopping mutant invasions, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different strategies.

### Characters

This expansion brings a host of new characters to the mix. Play as a fearless ranger, a cunning rogue, or even a mutated monster. Each character has distinct abilities and combos that keep the game fresh and exciting.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: The post-apocalyptic theme adds depth and excitement, making you feel like you're part of a Mad Max movie.
- **Replayability**: With new scenarios and characters, no two games are ever the same.
- **Beautiful Components**: The miniatures and cards are a feast for the eyes.
- **Strategic Depth**: Coordinating attacks and abilities adds a layer of strategy that keeps players engaged.

### Cons

- **Complex Setup**: Be prepared to spend a good amount of time setting up each scenario.
- **Enemy Overload**: Some scenarios can have overwhelming numbers of enemy minions, which might frustrate newer players.
- **Expensive**: Collecting all expansions could be a hit to your wallet.
