### How you customize
The process is simple enough for kids and adults who still pretend to be adults. You decide the meta you want for the night, then you assign a few cards as wild variants. You can set up a house rule such as all wild cards must be color chosen by the person who plays them, or you can create team style play where some players get to pick color only when the wild card is played. The key is to have a quick agreement before the first blind draw and then let chaos take over. It is essential to keep the rules light, otherwise you risk turning your living room into a courtroom where a judge is required to decide whether a color change is valid or not.
