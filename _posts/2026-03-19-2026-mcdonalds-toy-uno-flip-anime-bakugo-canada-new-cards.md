## What is UNO Flip, anyway?
UNO Flip is essentially UNO with a villain arc. The deck has two sides: a bright, happy side (Light Mode) and a darker, more punitive side (Dark Mode). The Flip Card toggles you from a forgiving light color palette to a menacing dark side where color-matching and number-deduction feel like a stealth mission through a neon-noir alley. This Canada edition keeps that core idea intact but sprinkles in Bakugo’s explosive personality and a few My Hero Academia references that won’t require a degree in anime linguistics to decode.

In the McDonald’s toy universe, the UNO Flip cards are not just tiny cards; they’re compact props that fit into a lunch bag, a hoodie pocket, or a backpack that pretends to be a holographic tech pouch.
