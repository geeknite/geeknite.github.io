## Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping It Fresh
Maintenance is minimalistic but wise. Wipe the surface with a damp cloth after long sessions to remove sweat, oils, and the occasional stray piece of lunch. Dry thoroughly to prevent moisture from creeping into the joints. A quick inspection after every few uses to ensure screws are snug, the net posts are still taut, and the wheels rotate without friction will save you from future headaches.

Lubrication is rarely necessary, but if you do encounter squeaks or stiffness, a tiny dab of silicone-based lubricant on the moving joints can work wonders. Do not overdo it; too much lubricant can attract dirt and cause more problems in the long run. If you keep the table folded for extended periods, consider loosening a couple of screws slightly so the frame does not seize up from rust-inducing tension. It sounds like a betrayal to your future self, but it is proper maintenance 101.

## Tips, Tricks, and Expert Advice
- Level is your friend. A slightly uneven table turns a quick rally into a rollercoaster of misfires. Take a moment to calibrate the legs, then test with a few light practice rallies before a real match.
- Net tension matters. A sagging net makes rallies frustrating and reduces your ability to judge height. If the tension feels off after a dozen uses, adjust the clamps and re-tighten. You will thank yourself later.
- Consider a small padding kit. If you plan to install this table on a balcony or near fragile items, add corner guards or soft mats to protect walls and furniture from errant balls.
- Outdoor maintenance. If you leave the table outside, use a protective cover and check for condensation on the surface in the mornings. A damp surface can affect bounce and shorten surface life.
- Storage strategy. When folded, store vertically or horizontally depending on your space. Ensure the surface is clean and dry to prevent mold and mildew in humid environments.
- Upgrade path. The paddles and balls that come with the set are serviceable, but if you love ping pong, you will soon crave higher-performance paddles. The foldable design makes swapping components easy without needing specialized tools or a home improvement degree.
