## Comfort & Fit
### Adjustable Retention System
The Bluegrass Rogue helmet boasts an adjustable retention system that lets you dial in the perfect fit for your head. Adjustable between three positions in height, it also has a dial at the back that tightens or loosens the fit around your head. It’s like having your own personal head masseuse, without the awkward conversation.

### Lightweight Shell
At only 750 grams (size M), this helmet is light enough to make you forget you're wearing it. And trust me, after a full day of riding, that's a blessing. The shell is soft padded, cushions your head, and absorbs sweat. The padding is removable and washable, so you can keep it fresh—no more smelling like a locker room!

## Ventilation
### Vents, Vents, and More Vents
The Bluegrass Rogue helmet has 16 vents distributed across the shell, allowing air to enter and exit, creating a cooling effect on your head. Imagine your head is the VIP at an exclusive air-conditioning party. The vents on the front channel air into the visor cutouts, preventing fogging, while vents on the top and back create a negative pressure zone that draws hot air out of the helmet. The internal channels ensure continuous airflow, so your brain doesn’t turn into a baked potato.

## Safety
### Durability & Certification
Safety first, right? This helmet has a durable shell made of polycarbonate and ABS, designed to withstand impacts and absorb shocks, reducing the risk of head trauma. The multi-density EPS liner further enhances shock absorption and energy dissipation. Plus, it’s certified by CE EN 1078, ASTM F1952-15, ASTM F2032-15, and AS/NZS 2063:2008. In layman’s terms, it’s like having a fortress on your head.

### Versatility & Design
The helmet performs well in different terrains and weather conditions. Whether you're riding on smooth trails or rough tracks, sunny days or rainy days, the Bluegrass Rogue helmet adapts to your needs.
