### Step-by-step build notes
- Lay out the components and identify the sofa frame, chairs, and table
- Attach the legs to the sofa and chairs if not preassembled
- Attach the arms to the sofas and place the cushions on top
- Secure the coffee table legs and ensure the glass top is properly seated
- Place the cushions and zip up the covers
- Do a quick wobble test and tighten anything that feels loose
- Dress the set with cushions, pillows, and maybe a small outdoor rug to anchor the space

If you have ever built a basic piece of furniture, this setup should feel familiar. The most important tip is to not over-tighten screws during the early steps. You want to ensure that the frame remains square and that the weave aligns evenly. A little patience goes a long way, and a smartphone flashlight can be a helpful assistant when you are trying to thread a bolt through a burly corner. After a few adjustments, you should be ready to showcase your newly minted outdoor living zone to your neighbors, your social media followers, and the local birds who enjoy a front-row seat to your garden theater.

### Maintenance during and after assembly
After you finish assembling, consider wiping down the frame with a damp cloth to remove any dust or fingerprints. For the cushions, inspect the zippers and ensure the covers are fully closed. If a spill happens, remove the cover and rinse with mild soap and water, then air-dry completely before reassembling to prevent moisture buildup that could affect the cushion foam over time. A simple routine of cleaning once every few weeks or after heavy use will keep the set looking fresh without requiring weekly deep cleans. Some readers will appreciate the removable cushion covers for easier laundering; others may prefer spot cleaning for quicker results. The beauty of this design is that it accommodates both mindsets without sacrificing core comfort or aesthetics.

## Maintenance Tips: Keeping the Off White Crisp and Charming

- Regularly wipe the frame with a damp cloth to remove dust and pollen.
- Use a mild soap solution for stubborn dirt on both the weave and the table surface.
- Remove cushion covers and launder according to the care label; air dry completely before reassembling.
- Store cushions in a dry place during prolonged rain or winter seasons; consider protective covers for extra longevity.
- Protect from direct, harsh sun for extended periods to slow color fading.

If you are proactive about maintenance, you will find the off white cushions stay nicer longer, preserving the crisp, modern vibe that this set aims to deliver.
