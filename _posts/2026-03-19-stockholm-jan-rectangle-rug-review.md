### Comfort and practicality
Underfoot comfort depends on the pile height and density; the Stockholm Jan offers a modest pile that is cushy enough to stand on while not feeling like a marshmallow. This matters for those who work from home and need to stand up every couple of hours for a stretch. The rug provides enough softness for bare feet but not so much that it becomes a little cloud of distraction. Practicality comes from the non-slip backing, which helps when you host a coffee spill or a pet parade across the living room floor. The texture is also forgiving of minor spills; a quick blot with a clean cloth will often suffice.

### Cleaning and maintenance
Maintenance considerations matter in any home; rugs collect dust, pet hair, and the occasional mystery crumb from a midnight snack. The Stockholm Jan Rectangle Rug is designed for easy care. Regular vacuuming with a standard beater brush is recommended; avoid aggressive agitation that might damage the fibers. In case of spills, attend quickly with blotting and cleanup; if needed, a mild detergent solution can be used on stubborn stains. For persistent marks, consider a professional cleaning every 12-18 months depending on foot traffic. The key is consistency and a little patience; we know this is a lot like software updates, but rug updates are less dramatic and have fewer reboot requirements.
