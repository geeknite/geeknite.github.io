## Maintenance and care
Like any good gadget, the Uimoso benefits from occasional maintenance. Here are some practical tips to keep it in top shape:

- Wipe the playing surface regularly with a soft, damp cloth to remove dust and minor fingerprints. Build-up on the surface can affect ball bounce over time.
- Lubricate the rods sparingly if you notice sticky movement; avoid over-lubrication as the balls can pick up residue and slow down play.
- Check the screws periodically. A loose screw can introduce wobble that distracts from the game and may cause unwanted noises during a heated round.
- Clean the balls occasionally to maintain consistent roll behavior. A dirty ball can ruin the feel of the game, and you do not want to be the team known for the rogue ball that bounces out of control.

Storage tip: if you fold the unit and store behind a couch, make a habit of checking there every few weeks for small toys or stray cables that might get pinched in the mechanism. Far too many players have learned this the hard way, usually while chasing a lost ball under the sofa in a dimly lit room while the dog watches in judgment.
