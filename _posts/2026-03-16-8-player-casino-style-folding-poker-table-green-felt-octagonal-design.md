## Maintenance: Keeping It Sharp and Scratch-Free
### Cleaning Do’s and Don’ts
- Do wipe spills promptly with a soft cloth. Mild soap and water are usually enough.

- Do cover the table when not in use to prevent dust buildup and pet hair from becoming a feature of the next game.

### Storage: The Right Home for the Right Table
A dedicated space is best, but if you’re tight on space, a sturdy closet or garage shelf can work. Keep the table away from moisture and direct sun to avoid warping or fading of the felt. If you live in a humid climate, consider a dehumidifier or a climate-controlled room for long-term storage.
