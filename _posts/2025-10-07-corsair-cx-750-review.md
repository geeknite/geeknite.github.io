## Performance

### Power Delivery

The Corsair CX 750 is rated at 750 watts, which is more than enough to power most gaming setups. Whether you're rocking an RTX 4080 or a Ryzen 9, this PSU has got you covered. The 80 PLUS Bronze efficiency rating ensures that you're not wasting precious electricity (and money) heating your room.

### Stability

With great power comes great responsibility, and the CX 750 is up to the task. It delivers consistent power with minimal fluctuations, which is crucial for maintaining the health and performance of your components. No one wants a sudden power spike to fry their brand-new GPU, right?

### Noise Levels

Noise is always a consideration, especially if your rig is situated next to your bed or in a shared space. The Corsair CX 750 features a 120mm fan that operates quietly, even under load. You won't be serenaded by the sound of a jet engine while you're trying to focus on your gaming.

### Durability

Corsair is known for its quality, and the CX 750 is no exception. It's built to last, with high-quality components and a robust design. Plus, it comes with a 5-year warranty, so you can rest easy knowing that you're covered in case something goes wrong.
