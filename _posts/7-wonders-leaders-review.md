## Introduction

Welcome, fellow board game enthusiasts! Today, we're diving into the world of **7 Wonders Leaders Expansion**. If you're a fan of the original 7 Wonders game, this expansion promises to add new layers of strategy, depth, and of course, leaders who will lead your civilization to glory (or ruin, no judgment here).

## The Basics

### What is 7 Wonders Leaders Expansion?

7 Wonders Leaders is an expansion to the popular 7 Wonders board game. Designed by Antoine Bauza, this expansion introduces leaders to your civilization, providing new strategies and abilities that can significantly impact the game.

### Components

The game comes with 49 new leader cards, 4 new guilds, a new wonder, and plenty of coins to finance your new leaders. The artwork is beautiful, with detailed illustrations on each card that bring these historical figures to life.

[![7 Wonders Leaders Components](https://i.imgur.com/3zHcfgxm.jpg){: style='float: right;'}]({{ site.constants.wsib }}7-wonders-leaders)

## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up 7 Wonders Leaders is straightforward. Each player receives a set of leader cards, which they can draft and play at various stages of the game. The leaders provide unique abilities and benefits, making each game feel fresh and different.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players can recruit leaders, build structures, or participate in military conflicts. The leaders add an extra layer of strategy, as you must plan not only your buildings but also how your leaders will complement your overall strategy.

### The Leaders

The leaders in this expansion are historical figures such as Julius Caesar, Cleopatra, and Alexander the Great. Each leader has unique abilities that can provide significant advantages if used correctly. For example, Caesar might boost your military strength, while Cleopatra could enhance your trade capabilities.

## The New Elements

### Guilds and Wonders

The new guilds and wonder introduced in this expansion provide additional strategies and ways to score points. The new wonder, Rome, offers unique abilities that can turn the tide of the game in your favor.

### Coins

Coins play a more significant role in Leaders, as they are required to recruit your leaders. Managing your finances becomes crucial, adding another layer of depth to the game.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Strategic Depth**: The leaders add new strategies and ways to win.
- **Replayability**: With so many leaders to choose from, no two games are the same.
- **Beautiful Artwork**: The illustrations on the leader cards are stunning.
- **Easy Integration**: The expansion integrates seamlessly with the base game.

### Cons

- **Complexity**: The additional rules and strategies can be overwhelming for new players.
- **Balancing Issues**: Some leaders may feel more powerful than others.
- **Expensive**: Collecting all expansions can be costly.

## Final Thoughts

7 Wonders Leaders Expansion is a fantastic addition to the base game. It adds depth, strategy, and replayability, making it a must-have for any 7 Wonders fan. Just remember, with great power (leaders) comes great responsibility (strategy).
