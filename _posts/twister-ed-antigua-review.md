## Gameplay

Twister is a game that tests your flexibility, balance, and willingness to get uncomfortably close to others. The game mat consists of large, colorful dots, and the spinner dictates which hand or foot goes on which color. Sounds simple, right? Think again.

### How to Play

1. **Set up:** Lay the twister mat flat on the floor. Make sure you have enough space around to avoid knocking over grandma’s antique vase.
2. **Spinner:** One player spins the spinner and calls out the moves. If you’re the spinner, use this power wisely and don’t spin yourself dizzy.
3. **Get Twisting:** Players follow the spinner’s instructions, placing hands and feet on the appropriate colored circles. The result is a tangled mess of limbs and laughter.
4. **Stay Upright:** The goal is to remain standing while others collapse into a heap. The last player standing wins, but let’s be honest – in Twister, everyone’s a winner, except maybe your dignity.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Simple Rules:** Easy to learn and play, no Ph.D. in board games required.
- **Hilarious:** Endless laughs as players contort themselves into ridiculous positions.

### Cons

- **Potentially Awkward:** Be prepared for some uncomfortably close encounters.
- **Injury Risk:** Playing Twister on a slippery surface or with too much enthusiasm can lead to minor injuries. Stretch those ligaments before you start!
