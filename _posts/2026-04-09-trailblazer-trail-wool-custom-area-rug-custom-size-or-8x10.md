Care and maintenance
---------------------
Wool rugs require a bit more patience than their synthetics counterparts, but the long-term payoff is worth it. Gentle cleaning, quick spot treatment, and periodic rotation can keep the Trailblazer looking fresh for years.

Cleaning basics:
- Vacuum regularly with the beater bar on a low setting or use a suction-only vacuum to avoid disturbing the pile
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth; avoid rubbing which can push the liquid deeper
- For stubborn stains, test a mild wool-safe cleaner in an inconspicuous area first
- Rotate the rug every 6-12 months to ensure even wear

Special notes for wool:
- Sun exposure can cause fading; if you have a sunlit living room, consider seasonal rotating or a window with UV-protective film
- Professionally cleaning is recommended for significant stains or to refresh the fibers every few years
