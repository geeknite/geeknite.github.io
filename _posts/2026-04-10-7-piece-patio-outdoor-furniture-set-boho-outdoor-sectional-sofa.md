## Durability, weather performance, and climate considerations

The boho look loves the sun, but the sun loves to fade fabrics. The good news: this set uses fade-resistant fabrics and UV-treated fibers, which should keep color fastness in a typical seasonal cycle. The cushions are designed to resist moisture, but it’s still smart to store them under cover or bring them indoors during heavy rain or winter.

- Rain and humidity: A light drizzle is not a catastrophe for most synthetic-weave sets. Dry cushions promptly to prevent water staining or mildew. If you live in a humidity-heavy area, consider elevating cushions off the ground with small feet under the sofa modules to improve airflow.
- Wind and movement: The modular design means you can anchor the pieces with weighted ottomans or add outdoor rug and planters to create a stable seating cluster. If you’re in a windy region, a tighter arrangement reduces the risk of pieces being pushed around by gusts.
- Temperature swings: Aluminum frames tolerate heat and cold well, but be mindful of cushions freezing in extreme temps if stored outside. The general rule is to store cushions in a dry place during the winter and cover the set with a breathable outdoor cover when possible.

## Care, maintenance, and longevity tips

A good-looking outdoor setup is a commitment to occasional care. Here’s a practical maintenance checklist:

- Regular cleaning: Wipe cushions with a mild soap solution and let air-dry fully. This keeps the fabric looking fresh without the risk of mildew. For the frame, wipe with a damp cloth and dry to prevent water spots.
- Stain management: Treat common stains as soon as possible. Blot, don’t rub, to avoid embedding stains into the fabric. A fabric-safe stain remover can be handy for stubborn marks.
- Storage strategy: When possible, store cushions indoors during heavy rain or winter. If storage isn’t feasible, use breathable covers and elevate cushions a bit to prevent moisture buildup at the base.
- Sunscreen and sweat: We all know how sunscreen can find its way into your cushions. A quick wipe or a light wash keeps the fabric looking vibrant. Regularly rotating cushions can also help even out wear.
