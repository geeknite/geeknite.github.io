## Cleaning and care: how to keep the tide from receding
Cleaning a rug with ocean vibes should be more than a once-a-year ritual; it should be a small daily ritual you can tolerate while sipping your tea or energy drink of choice. For routine care, a standard vacuum with a gentle setting should suffice. If there are spill stains, treat them promptly with a mild detergent and water, then blot—don’t rub—to preserve the pattern. In case you’re curious about deeper cleaning, check the manufacturer’s care guide and your retailer’s recommendations; the surface pattern can handle a light cleaning, but avoid harsh chemical cleaners that could dull the blues and teals.

Pro tip: rotate the rug every few months to prevent uneven wear from sunlit windows or the gamer chair that always turns in circles. This not only helps preserve color uniformity but also gives you a fun opportunity to rearrange your room’s layout—think of it as a tiny interior design reset with fewer widgets and more floor real estate.
