## Display, storage, and care tips

The original sealed box has a certain heft and presence on a display shelf. If you want to keep the vibe alive while protecting the set, consider the following:
- Keep the box away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and warping.
- Use a display case with UV blocking if you plan to show it prominently.
- Store the sealed deck in a cool, dry place with consistent humidity to avoid warping the shrink wrap or the inner deck.
- If you decide to open, do so carefully and consider preserving the unsealed contents in a protective sleeve for display purposes.

This is not only about playing a game; it is about maintaining a slice of a time when superheroes could be heroically printed on cardboard and still feel relevant in a world that was about to get internet chatrooms and dial-up tones.
