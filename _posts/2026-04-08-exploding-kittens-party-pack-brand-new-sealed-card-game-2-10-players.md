## Strategy and tips: how to maximize chaos without losing your mind

- Learn to read the room: some players love chaos; others fear it. If you identify the chaos enthusiasts early, you can tailor your plays to fuel their fire while giving the rest a few easier wins to keep morale high.
- Save defuse cards for the right moment: you will need them, but not every peek requires a defuse. The art is in knowing when to save and when to burn those defuses on a card that might still be annoying but not game-ending for your own team.
- The See the Future cards are powerful but give away information. If you want to maximize misdirection, use them at pivotal moments when you suspect someone’s about to pull a risky move. It’s a mini-game within the game: who can exploit knowledge before it becomes obvious?
- The new action cards in the Party Pack often introduce micro-games within the macro-game. Use them for tempo shifts: a round that would normally drag on can be livened by a well-timed distraction or a card that forces a rewrite of the rules for a short stretch.
- Roleplay a little. The aliens who invented the party pack love a good narrative as much as a good draw. A little theatrics goes a long way toward building memorable moments that you’ll retell at the next grouping of friends or family.
