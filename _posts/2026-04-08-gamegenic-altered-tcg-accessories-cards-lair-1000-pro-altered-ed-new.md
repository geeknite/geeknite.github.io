## Care and maintenance tips
- Regularly dust the exterior and interior with a soft microfiber to keep static and dust levels low.
- When reconfiguring, do it in stages rather than unloading the entire bin in one go to avoid misplacing dividers.
- Inspect hinges and dividers every few months; tighten any loose screws or re-seat dividers if they shift.
- Use the space deliberately; overpacking can strain the hinges and reduce the life of the mechanism.
- Store away from extreme heat or moisture to preserve the clarity of the polycarbonate and prevent warping.
