## Durability, cleaning, and maintenance essentials
Durability is the superhero cape of any rug, and the Avenue Rug wears it proudly. The synthetic fibers offer stain resistance that is not a fairy tale; it is a real-world feature that helps you survive a house full of snacks and spilled beverages. The backing is designed to stay put on both hardwood and laminate floors, which prevents the drama of the rug sliding away during a dramatic throw pillow toss. Maintenance is straightforward:
- Vacuum weekly to keep the surface free of grit that can dull the texture over time.
- Rotate every two to three months to ensure even wear and color consistency across the surface.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing that can push stains deeper into the fibers.
- For more serious blemishes, use a mild detergent with water and blot gently; test on a hidden corner first to ensure colorfastness.

If you share your space with pets, the Avenue rug remains a faithful ally. It resists claws and scratches well enough to keep the look tidy for a longer period. For odorous incidents, a quick spot-clean followed by a gentle spray of a pet-safe deodorizer should do the trick. This rug is not a miracle worker, but it does help ease the typical messes of everyday life without requiring a full-scale renovation each time a coffee cup takes a dive off the coffee table.
