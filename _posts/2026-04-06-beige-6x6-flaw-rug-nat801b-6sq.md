## Care and Maintenance

### Cleaning basics
- Regular vacuuming with a suction-only head, not a brush roller, to avoid fuzzing the pile
- Spot cleaning with a mild detergent solution for spills; avoid harsh chemicals
- Blot rather than rub to prevent color shifting and pile flattening

### Stain handling and odor control
Beige carpets show stains more readily than darker colors; the key is quick action. For minor spills, blot with a clean cloth and use a lightweight carpet cleaner following the product instructions. For pet odors, consider a baking soda treatment followed by vacuuming after a few hours. If the spot holds stubborn stains, consult a professional cleaner who specializes in carpets and has a sense of humor about your life choices.

### Maintenance schedule
- Vacuum weekly for normal usage; more frequent vacuuming if you have children or pets who enjoy secret snack distribution on the rug
- Rotate every 3 months to avoid uneven wear and color fading in one corner due to sun exposure
- Professional cleaning recommended every 12 to 18 months depending on traffic
