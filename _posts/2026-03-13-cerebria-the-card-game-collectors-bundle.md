How to Play: A Quick Refresher

- Objective: In Cerebria: The Card Game, players embody emotional factions that influence the board and attempt to guide the “heart” of the game world toward victory. The Collector’s Bundle doesn’t alter the core objective; it just makes the path to victory feel a little more ceremonious.
- Setup: Shuffle the main deck, deal a hand, lay out the mood tiles, and assemble yourTokens of influence. If you’re a stickler for order, arrange them by color and size—that’s power you can see.
- Turn structure: On your turn, you’ll perform a series of actions—play cards, adjust moods, snatch victory points from the ether—before passing to the next player. The tempo is steady, the tension is delicious, and the occasional “oh no, I misread that rule again” moment is part of the charm.

You’ll tally points, and the emotional ledger will reveal who’s the most melodramatic about their own motivations. It’s high-fantasy politics with a dash of therapy-speak and a lot of cute tokens.
