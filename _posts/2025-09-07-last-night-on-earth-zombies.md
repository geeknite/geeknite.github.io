## The Basics

### What is Last Night on Earth?

*Last Night on Earth* is a survival horror board game that pits humans against zombies in a desperate fight for survival. The **Zombies with Grave Weapons Miniature Set** adds a new layer of fun and horror, introducing zombie miniatures equipped with grave weapons. These zombies are not just here for brains; they mean business!

### Components

The miniature set includes:

- **Zombie Miniatures**: Various zombies armed with weapons like shovels, axes, and other graveyard tools. Because why wouldn't zombies recycle weapons?
- **Grave Weapon Cards**: Special cards that detail the unique abilities and attacks of each weapon.
- **Rulebook**: Easy to understand, even for zombies. (No, seriously, it's straightforward.)

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up the game is a breeze. Humans and zombies choose their characters and weapons and then set up the board. The **Zombies with Grave Weapons** expansion integrates seamlessly with the base game, adding depth and new strategies.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Move, attack, or use special abilities. Zombies can use their grave weapons for extra mayhem.
2. **Event Phase**: Draw cards that introduce new challenges or bonuses. Keep an eye out for those surprise attacks!
3. **Zombie Phase**: Zombies move and attack. Humans, brace yourselves.

### Winning and Losing

Humans win by completing objectives before they become zombie chow. Zombies win by turning all humans into one of their own. Sounds easy? Think again!

**Pro Tip**: Equip yourself with the "Grave Weapon" card for some serious zombie-fighting power.
