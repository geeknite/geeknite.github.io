## Storage, display, and care tips
- Sleeving: use standard card sleeves for protection. If you re planning a public display, consider a binder with clear page protectors to minimize handling damage.
- Sorting: group by category first (Heroes, Missions, Allies, Villains) and then by color or rarity if your copies show visible differences.
- Environment: keep away from high humidity and direct sunlight to prevent color fade and edge wear from accelerating.
- Insurance: if you re treating this as a real asset, keep a simple inventory with photos and a rough value estimate to help with insurance or resale conversations later on.
