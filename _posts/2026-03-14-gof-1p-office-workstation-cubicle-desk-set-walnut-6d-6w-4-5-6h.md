## Dimensions and footprint (the practical part)
The GOF 1P footprint is described as roughly 6 feet deep by 6 feet wide, with height options that can be adjusted to around 5 or 6 feet. In real terms, that means you’re getting a respectable corner‑office vibe in a compact space. Before you buy, measure your space, ceiling height, and the route your furniture will take through doors and hallways. Cable routing matters, too. The GOF 1P is not a miracle worker for bad cable management, but it does provide a solid framework to build a neat setup around.

### Finish and maintenance
Walnut veneer can present a learning curve: it forgives minor spills but dislikes neglect. The finish accepts occasional polishing with a microfiber cloth but resists foodie fingerprints with a light spray of furniture polish. Pro tip: avoid placing hot mugs directly on the desk surface. Coasters are not just a courtesy; they’re a life choice if you want your finish to stay gorgeous for a longer arc of time.

If you’re worried about scratches, you’ll be glad to know that the panels are solid, and the surface feels more commuter‑friendly than cliff‑face rugged. It’s not immune to scuffs, but the veneer can be touched up with a matching veneer marker or a soft cloth and proper furniture polish to maintain the aesthetic you paid for.
