## Cleaning and Maintenance
Beige rugs require routine care. Vacuum regularly with a clean belt and an adjustable height so you dont snag fibers. For stains, blot and treat with a mild cleaner; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper. If you re using a steam cleaner, test a hidden spot first. Let the rug dry completely before resuming heavy foot traffic. If you re eco-conscious, look for cleaning solutions that are non-toxic and fragrance-free to minimize allergic triggers. The rug s synthetic blend can handle occasional deep cleanings, but keep the heat mild and avoid rough scrubbing that might flatten pile or alter color.
