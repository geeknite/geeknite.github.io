## Weather Performance and Maintenance
### Sun and UV resistance
Synthetic rattan holds color well but may fade if left in direct sun for months on end. A simple routine of occasional shade during the harsh hours can preserve the look. The cushions may fade a touch but remain comfortable and presentable.

### Rain and moisture
Most cushions are water resistant and the weave is designed to shed moisture. If heavy rains are expected, cover the set with a breathable outdoor cover or move cushions indoors to avoid mold.

### Cleaning routine
- Wipe the frame with a damp cloth
- Vacuum the weave to remove debris
- Spot clean cushions with mild soap and water
- For stubborn stains, use a gentle fabric cleaner

### Maintenance tips
Tighten screws and bolts periodically; a little maintenance goes a long way toward preserving the aesthetic. If you hear a tiny squeak every now and then, it just means your set is happy and talking to itself about your grill technique.
