## Performance and Practicality
### Durability for Real Life
We know you’re not shopping for a museum piece—you’re shopping for a rug that will survive coffee spills, paw prints, and the occasional treadmill misadventure. The Parker Dove holds up well to mid-traffic areas. The tight weave reduces shedding, and the colorway hides minor stains better than a chameleon hiding from a tabloid photographer. If you’ve got kids who sprint from room to room with unidentifiable snacks on their socks, this rug won’t betray you at every step.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
Cleaning is the boring part, yet it’s also the part that decides whether you’ll keep the rug for years or replace it next season. Vacuum regularly with a standard vacuum, and address spills as soon as you can. Blot with a clean cloth, avoid aggressive rubbing, and tempt fate with professional dry cleaning only if necessary. For most households, spot cleaning suffices. If you’re into science experiments, you can test a gentle cleaning solution on a hidden corner first—you owe it to your future self to avoid accidental dye migration.

### Rug Pad and Installation Tips
A good rug pad changes a rug from “nice to step on” to “awesome to live on.” For the Madras Parker Dove, pair it with a medium-density rug pad to keep the surface flat, minimize slipping, and slow down any curling edges. If you’ve ever tripped on a lift in your own living room, you know how important a stable pad is. Place the rug so its pattern aligns with your furniture anchors—sofa legs, coffee table, or the exact spot where your cat likes to stare judgmentally at you while you binge-watch unknown sci-fi shows.

## Sizing, Fit, and Room Planning

Common sizes for the Parker Dove range from 5x8 feet to 8x10 feet, with oversized variants available for larger living rooms. ## Maintenance Schedule and Longevity
### Routine Care
- Vacuum weekly to prevent the buildup of dirt and pet hair.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear.
- Address spills immediately with blotting and a mild cleaner appropriate for synthetic fibers.
- Consider a professional cleaning every 12-24 months depending on foot traffic and pets.

### Longevity Expectations
With proper care, a mid-range woven rug like the Madras Parker Dove can maintain its style and functional life for 5-10 years in a typical home. If you’re counts-of-threads nerdy, you’ll appreciate that consistent maintenance helps preserve the texture and pattern integrity longer, which means fewer visual surprises when you finally decide to redecorate.
