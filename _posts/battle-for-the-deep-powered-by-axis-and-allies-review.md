## The Basics

### What is Battle for the Deep?

*Battle for the Deep: Powered by Axis & Allies* is a naval strategy board game that combines the classic mechanics of Axis & Allies with the added excitement of underwater combat. Players take command of their fleets, submarines, and underwater bases, aiming to dominate the deep seas and outsmart their opponents.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed ships, submarines, and underwater bases.
- **Game Board**: A beautifully illustrated map of the deep seas.
- **Cards and Tokens**: Representing various resources, actions, and special abilities.
- **Rulebook**: Comprehensive and easy to understand, with enough naval jargon to make you feel like Captain Nemo.

**Pro Tip**: Keep your snacks and drinks away from the game board. The sea might be deep, but cleaning soda off your components is even deeper.

## How to Play

### Setting Up

Setting up *Battle for the Deep* is like preparing for a naval expedition. Lay out the game board, distribute the miniatures and cards, and choose your faction. Now you're ready to embark on your underwater adventure.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and perform actions based on their faction's abilities. You can move your fleet, attack opponents, gather resources, and deploy special abilities. Strategy is key, so plan your moves carefully and anticipate your enemies' tactics.

### Combat

Combat in *Battle for the Deep* is a blend of strategic planning and luck. When ships clash, players roll dice and use cards to modify their attacks. Did you plan an epic ambush just to roll a series of ones? Welcome to the deep, where even the best-laid plans can go awry.
