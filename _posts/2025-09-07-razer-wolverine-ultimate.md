## The Bad

### Price

Let's address the elephant in the room: the price. The Razer Wolverine Ultimate isn't cheap. At around $160, it costs more than some people's grocery bills. But hey, quality comes at a price, right? Just make sure to hide the receipt from your significant other.

### Wired Connection

For a controller that screams premium, it's a bit of a bummer that it's wired. While this does mean lower latency, some gamers might miss the freedom of a wireless setup. If your room looks like a snake pit from all the cables, you might need some serious cable management skills.

## The Ugly

### The Learning Curve

With great customizability comes great responsibility... and a steep learning curve. Getting this controller set up just right takes time and patience. If you're the type who likes to dive right into a game without reading manuals, you might find yourself a bit overwhelmed.

### The Weight

The Wolverine Ultimate is hefty. This isn't necessarily a bad thing, but if you're used to feather-light controllers, you might feel like you're pumping iron during those long gaming sessions.
