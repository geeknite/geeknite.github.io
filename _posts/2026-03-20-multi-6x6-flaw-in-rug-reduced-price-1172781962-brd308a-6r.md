Maintenance is not a dramatic affair here. ## Care, Longevity, and Aftercare
Maintenance is not glamorous, but it is essential. A routine that works for most households includes:
- Regular vacuuming to remove dust and micro-scraps that accumulate in the weave.
- Immediate blotting of spills to prevent staining from setting in.
- Occasional professional cleaning if you suspect ground-in grime or you have persistent odors.

Avoid steam cleaning repeatedly, as it can cause fibers to relax excessively and slightly compress the pile, altering the texture you originally fell in love with. Always test cleaning solutions on a hidden corner first, then proceed to larger areas if the test proves safe.
