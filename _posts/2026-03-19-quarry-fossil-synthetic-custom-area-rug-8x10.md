## Maintenance and cleaning: upkeep without fossil-tailing pain

### Routine care
Regular vacuuming keeps the fossil layers crisp. Use a vacuum with adjustable height and a soft brush attachment to avoid tugging at the edges. If your household includes shedding pets, a monthly deeper clean with a carpet cleaner is prudent. Synthetic fibers dry quickly, so you can resume lounging in under a couple of hours after cleaning.

### Stain removal tips
For spills, blot, don't rub. Mild detergent mixed with water works well on most stains; test a small area first. Oil-based stains respond best to blotting and a gentle solvent if needed. Avoid bleach, which can alter the color tones of the fossil stripes. For stubborn marks, a carpet-safe stain remover label works; always follow instructions and ventilate the room—micro fossils appreciate fresh air too.
