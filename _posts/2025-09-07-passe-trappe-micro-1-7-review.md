## What's in the Box?

Imagine opening a box and finding a portal to a world of chaos and hilarity. Inside the Passe-Trappe Micro 1.7 box, you'll discover:

- A wooden game board
- Elastic bands (for slinging pucks and potentially launching them into orbit)
- A bunch of wooden pucks (the doom disks)

It's essentially a tiny battlefield designed to test your friendships and your finger dexterity.

## Objective

The goal is simple: use the elastic bands to shoot all your pucks through the center hole into your opponent's side. Sounds easy, right? Well, it's not. Your opponent is doing the same thing, and the result is a frantic, hilarious showdown that usually ends with one player questioning their life choices.

## Gameplay

### Step 1: Set Up

Find a stable surface. Trust me, you don't want this game flipping over mid-match. Place the board between you and your soon-to-be enemy—uh, I mean friend.

### Step 2: Prepare for Battle

Place the pucks on your side. Take a deep breath. Maybe say a prayer. You're gonna need it.

### Step 3: Unleash the Fury

Using the elastic band, sling the pucks through the center hole as fast as your fingers can manage. Remember, your opponent is doing the same thing, and chaos ensues. The game ends when one side is completely clear of pucks.

## Strategy Tips

1. **Finger Fitness**: Build up your finger strength. This isn't a game for the weak-fingered.
2. **Puck Deflection**: Sometimes it's strategic to defend rather than attack. Use your pucks to block the center hole and foil your opponent's plans.
3. **Mind Games**: Trash talk is not only allowed, it's encouraged. Get into your opponent's head and watch them crumble.
