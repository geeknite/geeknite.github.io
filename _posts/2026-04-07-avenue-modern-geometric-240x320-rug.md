## Care and Longevity: Keeping the Geometry Sharp
### Routine Maintenance Checklist
- Vacuum weekly with a beater brush on low setting to avoid fiber damage.
- Wipe up spills immediately with a clean cloth; blot rather than rub to prevent color migration.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear in high-traffic areas and preserve the visual balance of the pattern.
- Use a rug pad to reduce slippage and extend the life of the fibers.

### Deep-Cleaning Guidance
If you ever need a deeper clean, consider professional cleaning for delicate spots or a home clean using mild detergent and cold water. Always air-dry completely before placing back on the floor to prevent any damp odors developing in the pile.
