## Display and Performance

The Meta Quest 3 features a stunning OLED display with a resolution of 3664 x 1920 pixels per eye, delivering vibrant colors and sharp visuals. The 120Hz refresh rate ensures smooth motion, making every VR experience more immersive.

### Key Specs
- **Processor:** Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2
- **RAM:** 8GB
- **Storage:** 128GB
- **Battery Life:** Up to 3 hours
- **Weight:** 503g

Whether you're battling space pirates or exploring enchanted forests, the Meta Quest 3's performance is top-notch. The Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2 processor provides the power needed for seamless gameplay, while the 8GB of RAM ensures smooth multitasking.
