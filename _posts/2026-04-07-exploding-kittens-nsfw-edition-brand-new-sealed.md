## How to play a round (the quick refresher)
### Core loop
Exploding Kittens is a take-that card game: on your turn you can play a card with flavor text that either helps you survive or sabotages someone else. The NSFW edition adds a deck full of cards that push adult humor into the forefront, but the core loop stays intact: risk versus reward, mind games with your friends, and the occasional betrayal you’ll pretend you saw coming from a mile away.

The NSFW edition includes the usual suspects: See the Future, Skip, Shuffle, and more.
