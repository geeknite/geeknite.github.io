## Setup and Assembly: A Quick Mission Impossible, But Shorter
If you’re joining this review expecting a mount-and-play experience reminiscent of a self-assembly IKEA saga, you’ll be pleased and somewhat relieved. The HOMCOM 2ft Foosball Table is designed for a quick setup, with clear instruction steps and a handful of screws, some tiny washers, and a rubber mallet that you’ll only need if your table refuses to sit flat on the first try. If you’ve ever assembled a normal-size foosball table, you’ll feel like you’ve unlocked a cheat code. For those who treat furniture assembly as a mild cardio workout, this will feel like a spa day. The most time-consuming step tends to be aligning the rods in the correct order so you don’t have a mismatch that makes the players spin out when you try to initiate a pass. Once assembled, you’ll be ready to unleash your inner tournament host without needing a pair of gloves or a team of interns to set the stadium lights.

### Pro Assembly Tips
- Lay out all screws and washers before you start; you’ll thank your past self for not rummaging through a chaos of loose hardware.
- Use a small standard screwdriver rather than a battery-powered drill; you don’t want to overtighten and risk cracking the cabinet.
- Check rod alignment before the final tightening; misaligned rods will cause the players to awkwardly bind and encourage jittery gameplay.
- Level the table; uneven surfaces turn a “friendly game” into a physics lesson on torque.
