## The Basics

### What is SCOPE Stalingrad?

SCOPE Stalingrad is a hidden movement and strategy board game that puts players in the shoes of a sniper in the Battle of Stalingrad. Designed to recreate the tension and strategic depth of sniper warfare, one player takes on the role of the sniper, while others control the soldiers trying to hunt them down.

### Components

The game includes a beautifully designed board depicting war-torn Stalingrad, high-quality miniatures, and various tokens and cards that enhance the immersive experience. The artwork is top-notch, and the components are built to last, ensuring that your strategic battles can continue for years to come.

The game shines in its strategic depth. As the sniper, you must carefully plan your moves, utilize cover, and think several steps ahead to outwit the soldiers. ## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: Captures the essence of sniper warfare, providing an engaging and intense experience.
