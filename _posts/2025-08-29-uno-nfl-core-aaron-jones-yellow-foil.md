- Packaging: Cards with their original blister packaging or protective sleeves and a receipt or a proof of purchase often command a higher price to collectors who want display-worthy pieces.
- Demand: The Packers fan base, the foil variant fans, and UNO collectors all influence the demand curve for this variant.

If you are buying for investment, consider the longer run of premium variants in this line. The Aaron Jones Yellow Foil is not a guaranteed home run, but it tends to perform respectably in a diversified collection. As with any collectible, deliberate patience and a careful storage strategy will do more for your long term value than a last minute impulse buy during a black Friday sale.
