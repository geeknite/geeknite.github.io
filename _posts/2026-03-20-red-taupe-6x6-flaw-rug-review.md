## Care and Maintenance for a Rug With Character

The key to maintaining a rug with tiny quirks is consistent, gentle care. Regular vacuuming helps to remove dust and hair that accumulates in the weave. For spills, blotting is better than rubbing. The goal is to preserve color and minimize additional wear. If the rug shows a pattern misalignment, do not panic. Cosmetic adjustments in furniture placement can hide the irregularities while also creating a more dynamic seating arrangement.

A tip: rotate the rug every few months to help distribute wear and avoid pattern orientation bias. If you have a cat that loves to sharpen claws on everything in sight, you might consider a scratch post near the rug’s corner to encourage focus away from the fibers.
