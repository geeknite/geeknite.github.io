## How to Display and Store a Vintage Deck
### Display ideas that honor the era
- A wall-mounted shadow box featuring a sealed deck on one side and a vintage magicians’ prop on the other creates a retro vignette. 
- A glass-fronted display case for a single deck helps you appreciate the back design’s intricacy, while keeping the seal intact.
- A dedicated shelf of gaming ephemera with a small placard noting the year, stock type, and a brief designer note for fun historical context.

### Storage tips to preserve mint condition
- Keep the deck in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight; UV can fade that iconic blue and the white pips. 
- Avoid damp basements and humid attics; humidity is the silent enemy that turns a crisp edge into a wavy souvenir.
- If you own more than one sealed deck, consider interleaving silica gel packs in your display. Just don’t forget to check them periodically so they don’t become a new hazard.
