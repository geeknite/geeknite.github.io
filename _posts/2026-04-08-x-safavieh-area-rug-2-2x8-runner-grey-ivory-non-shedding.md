### Care and Maintenance: Keeping the Good Vibes Long-Term
Cleaning a rug is not a sexy topic, but it’s a necessary one. The practical care routine for this Safavieh runner should be straightforward:
- Vacuum regularly with a non-beater bar setting to avoid fiber damage and to minimize shedding voice-overs.
- Spot clean stains promptly with a mild detergent and cold water. Avoid harsh solvents that could affect color integrity.
- Rotate periodically to even out wear, especially if the space has heavy foot traffic or a sun-exposed wall.
- For deeper cleans, follow the manufacturer’s guidelines or consider professional rug cleaning every 12-18 months depending on traffic.

A quick caution: if you have pets that tend to “mark” the hallway, you’ll want to address odors promptly. The Ivory in the pattern can show minor discoloration more readily than a darker shade, but with a routine cleaning schedule, it remains a durable, stylish choice rather than a maintenance headache.
