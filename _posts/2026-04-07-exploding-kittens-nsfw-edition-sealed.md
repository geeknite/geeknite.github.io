### Turn Structure and Card Types

On your turn, you perform actions by playing cards and drawing from the deck until you either blow up or avoid blowing up. Typical actions include:

- Attack: force the next player to take multiple turns in a row
- See the Future: peek at the top few cards of the deck
- Shuffle: shuffle the deck to mess with opponents’ predictions
- See the Future/Naughty Twist: a NSFW variant of the seeing-the-future action with adult humor
- Defuse: neutralize an Exploding Kitten
- Exploding Kitten: beware the ticking kitty of doom

The NSFW edition adds a handful of thematic cards that rely on adult references and cheeky humor. Some players will adore these, others might find them crass.
