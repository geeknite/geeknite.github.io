Pack 10 refers to a special edition distributed around PAX Prime 2013. ## Contents and Artwork
### Black Cards
The Black Cards in Pack 10 continue the CAH tradition of asking players to complete a sentence with a punchline that can be deliciously inappropriate, sometimes politically charged, and always funny in a we should laugh but we will anyway sort of way. In Pack 10, you’ll find prompts that nod to the internet era, reality TV, video games, and the kind of humor that benefits from a room full of strangers who know too much about their own pop culture encyclopedias. The prompts are designed to be daring enough to elicit a chorus of groans, nods, and maybe a few you had to be there moments. In short: it’s not a gentle take-home card collection; it’s a dare.

### White Cards
Complementing the prompts are White Cards that function as the perfect punchlines or mismatched responses. Pack 10’s White Cards lean into topical humor, references to memes, and the sort of witty replies that age like a fine meme: they might be borderline offensive, but they’re also sharp, clever, and surprisingly versatile. You’ll see lines that could work at a late-night game table or in a hallway of a convention where everyone’s trying not to cry from laughter. It’s a mix of outrageous, absurd, and surprisingly specific options that let players tailor the humor to their own party’s vibe.
