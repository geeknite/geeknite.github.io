## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity
Life happens. Spills happen. Dust bunnies write tragic love poems about your soil. The Quarry Denim is designed with cleaning in mind. The polypropylene fibers resist staining and are relatively quick to clean compared with natural fiber rugs. Here’s a quick maintenance playbook:

- Daily/weekly: Vacuum with the beater bar on a low setting, or use a standard vacuum to avoid fraying the fibers. This reduces the appearance of wear along the walking paths.
- Spills: Blot, don’t rub. Use a clean cloth to soak up the liquid, then treat with a mild soap solution if needed. For a more stubborn stain, a gentle carpet cleaner used according to the product’s guidelines can do wonders.
- Odors: Good ventilation helps. If you notice a lingering odor after a spill, a quick turn, flip, and light vacuum can help. Some users like to sprinkle a light amount of baking soda and vacuum after an hour.
- Odors vs. pets: If your household has pets that are friendly with couches but enthusiastic about carpets, you’ll appreciate how the Rug’s color hides minor pawprint messes. Still, a periodic trailing-off cleaning that addresses pet hair is advisable.
