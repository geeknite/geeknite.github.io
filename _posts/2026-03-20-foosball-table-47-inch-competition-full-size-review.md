## Maintenance and care: longevity is a vibe
To keep the table performing well, you should treat it as a living thing you feed with elbow grease and mild soap every so often. Wipe down the rods with a soft cloth after a gaming session to remove fingerprints and sweat, and check the screws periodically to keep things from loosening over time. If a player figure loosens, a quick tighten with a hex key usually does the trick. Keep the balls clean and away from dust, because a peppering of dust can slightly dull the ball's bounce on a long shot. The goal is to maintain the playing surface as a smooth, responsive stage for micro-moments and dramatic tie-breakers.
