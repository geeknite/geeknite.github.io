- Setup: The tin acts as a storage case, but it is also a stage: you can perform a dramatic reveal of the deck to the applause of your group. A dramatic opening and a quick shuffle can set the tone for a fun session.
- Turn flow: The core UNO flow remains the same: match numbers or colors, play action cards, and try to rid your hand first. The Polar Bear variants are typically limited to optional wild cards or theme prompts, not a total rework of UNO.
