## Maintenance and care
A high-quality poker set should be straightforward to care for. The aluminum case is metal, so it resists rust and survives the occasional spill if you wipe it down after a sweaty session. The chips benefit from a quick wipe with a soft cloth if you notice any residue or ghost marks after a night of heavy play. It is best to avoid soaking the chips, since moisture can alter their balance and feel. If a chip somehow develops a surface sheen or smear, a light cleaning with mild soap and water can restore the finish; just be sure to dry thoroughly before stacking again. For the cards, store them away from heat and humidity to keep them from warping. A dry, cool room is ideal for preserving the life of the entire kit.

### Cleaning tips and tricks
- Wipe the exterior case with a soft microfiber cloth to preserve the finish.
- Use a damp cloth for the interior foam if needed, but avoid soaking the foam as it can deform over time.
- Gently dust the chip grooves with a soft brush to prevent dust from settling in the color lines.
- Inspect the latches and hinges periodically and apply a tiny amount of dry lubricant if you notice stiffness.
- Keep the set in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading of the chip inlays.
