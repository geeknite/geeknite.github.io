Compatibility with your exact City of Mist edition is another potential hiccup. The core system is stable, but minor field names can vary across printings or fan-made variants. The Fortune Row pack stays compatible in spirit, but if you’re running a campaign with house rules, you might need to tweak a field here and there to keep everything aligned.

Finally, if you’re coming from a world of strictly minimal rules, Fortune Row’s density can feel like a data-rich upgrade you didn’t ask for. It’s part of the charm, but it’s worth stepping into with a plan for how you’ll use the prompts and districts without overloading sessions.
