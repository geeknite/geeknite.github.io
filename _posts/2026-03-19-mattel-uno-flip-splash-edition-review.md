## Strategy tips for conquering the SPLASH waves

- Watch the flipping rhythm: as soon as you see a card that suggests a flip is coming, begin mentally mapping your future moves on both sides. If you get better at predicting the moment the dark side will surface, you gain more control over your own endgame.
- Balance risk and reward: certain splash cards push you toward taking aggressive action; others reward patience. Weave these decisions with your reading of the table—the person who holds a lot of cards in their hand is your biggest threat and your best distraction.
- Keep track of color distribution: with two sides, the distribution of colors can swing unpredictably. If you’re on the dark side, you might want to lean into color control to push opponents toward color scarcity on their turns.
- Plan for the endgame: a smart player doesn’t just dump cards; they manipulate the hand to maximize the number of turns they control. Use your last few cards to set up a clean win path even as your opponents scramble to respond to sudden flips.
- Don’t neglect the social: UNO is a social game, and SPLASH Edition thrives on light banter, misdirection, and cheerleading when someone lands a perfect flip. Embrace the chaos and maintain a good-natured vibe; it makes a world of difference in how much fun everyone has.
