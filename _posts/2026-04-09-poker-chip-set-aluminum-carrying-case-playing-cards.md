## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity: Keeping Your Set Alive

Like any mechanical doohickey in the realm of tabletop gaming, this kit benefits from a little love and routine care:

- Chips: Wipe chips with a dry microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints or dust. If you’re dealing with sticky residues (think nacho cheese and a bad decision), a damp cloth with a touch of gentle cleaning solution will do, followed by a dry wipe. Avoid harsh chemicals that could dull the edge spots or fade the colors.
- Cards: Store decks in a cool, dry place. If cards get warped or bent, let them breathe out at room temp and consider replacing them sooner rather than later to avoid shuffling chaos.
- Case: Clean the exterior with a mild damp cloth. The interior padding helps protect the chips, but avoid soaking the foam — you don’t want a soggy cheese animal on your foam life.
- Storage: Keep the set in a dry area away from direct sunlight to preserve color integrity and avoid warping. If you live in a humid climate, consider a small desiccant pack inside the case to manage moisture.

The key with maintenance is consistency. A quick wipe-down after games and a periodic check of the latch tension will extend the life of your investment and keep it looking fresh for photos and streams alike.
