A good rule of thumb is to have at least 24 inches of rug extending beyond the edges of your coffee table if you want that floating island vibe; if you have an open plan, you can push toward 30 inches or more to create a distinct seating zone. ## Design and Surface Feel
### Texture and Comfort
The canvas surface aims for a balanced feel between softness and structure. It should be comfortable under bare feet and sturdy enough to resist flattening in the center from a heavy chair. The synthetic fibers are designed to bounce back after traffic patterns are created, which means your gaming chair can loop around without leaving a permanent furrow behind. ### Color Performance and Light Interaction
Canvas rugs typically show color well under a variety of lighting. However, synthetic fibers can have a slightly different color depth compared to natural fibers. In daylight the rug can take on a bright, clean appearance, while under warm indoor lighting it can read as cozy and grounded. If you are chasing a specific hue, request a color swatch or tested sample before finalizing the order to avoid any midnight regret purchases during a late night browsing session.

## Durability and Maintenance
### Cleaning Routine
Synthetic canvas rugs are built for life in busy homes. Spills often wipe away with a damp cloth and mild detergent, and most everyday stains respond well to standard cleaning methods. Avoid aggressive scrubbing as this can fray fibers and shorten the rug’s life. For more stubborn spots, you can treat with a fabric safe cleaner and rinse with a clean damp cloth. For pet owners, a quick vacuum pull is typically enough to remove loose hair and dust before it has time to settle into the fibers.

### Longevity Expectations
With proper care, a rug like the Satori Canvas can last many years in a typical living space. The synthetic composition resists moisture and sun fade reasonably well, which means you can place it near a window or in a sunlit office without worrying about rapid color loss or fiber degradation. The key is consistency in cleaning and avoiding high heat exposure that could weaken adhesives or backing over time.

## FAQ
### Can this rug be used under a dining table with chairs that slide in and out often?
Yes, a canvas rug with a sturdy backing can handle chair movement. It is best to choose a size that allows the chairs to fully sit on the rug when pushed in, to prevent the chairs from catching on the rug edge.

### Is it safe for children and pets?
The synthetic fibers are durable and easy to clean, which makes it a good choice for households with kids and pets. Just be mindful of any colorfastness if your pet has heavy shed seasons or if you plan to place food spills on the rug.

### How does custom sizing affect shipping and returns?
Custom sizes usually require more time to produce and ship. Ensure that the return policy covers custom orders in case the sizing or color is not what you expected.
