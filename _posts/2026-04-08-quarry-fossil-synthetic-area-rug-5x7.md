## Durability and maintenance

Synthetic rugs like this one tend to be easy to care for. The fibers resist pilling and are generally colorfast. The fossil color palette is forgiving when it comes to minor stains and everyday dirt. Cleaning is simple: vacuum regularly, blot spills promptly, and for deeper cleaning you can spot clean with mild detergent.

For stubborn marks, a gentle cleaning solution and a soft cloth will usually do the trick. Avoid soaking the rug; water can seep through the backing and cause long drying times. If your space has frequent sunlight, consider rotating the rug every few months to even out fading.
