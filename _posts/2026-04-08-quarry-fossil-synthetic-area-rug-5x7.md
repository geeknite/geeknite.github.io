#### Why polypropylene and backing choices matter

Synthetic rugs like this one tend to be easy to care for. The fibers resist pilling and are generally colorfast. The fossil color palette is forgiving when it comes to minor stains and everyday dirt. ## Care and cleaning instructions

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth
- Use a mild detergent for spot cleaning
- Do not machine wash or tumble dry
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping

A regular care routine keeps the fossil texture crisp and reduces the chance of permanent staining from everyday life. If you need to refresh the fibers, a quick vacuum with a soft brush attachment is usually enough to restore the nap.

### Deep-clean guidance (if absolutely necessary)
If you ever need a deeper cleanse for a stubborn stain, use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth. Test a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure colors don’t bleed. Avoid soaking or saturating the rug, and always air-dry flat to prevent backing damage.
