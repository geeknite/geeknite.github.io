## How to Use and Care for Your Cross Worlds Sleeves
A sleeve is only as good as its care routine. Here are a few tips to get the most out of your Cross Worlds experience:
- Keep your sleeves away from moisture and heat sources. While most sleeves tolerate normal room conditions, excessive moisture can soften the plastics and cause warping.
- Clean your sleeves with a microfiber cloth. Do not use abrasive cleaners or solvents. A light wipe-down can remove fingerprints and minor smudges without damaging the surface.
- Store loosely stacked decks in a cool, dry place. Avoid stacking heavy objects on top of the sleeve-wrapped deck, which could cause edge deformation over time.
- If you notice corner wear after a tournament, rotate in fresh sleeves. It may be worth having a spare bag of sleeves ready for day-two events so you can swap out if needed.

Maintenance can be part of the ritual of the hobby: a quick ritual to keep your tabletop space tidy and your cards pristine.
