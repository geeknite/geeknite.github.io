## Key features in detail
### LCD interface: your power heartbeat
The LCD is the hero of this machine. It shows real-time information like input voltage, output voltage, load percentage, battery capacity, estimated runtime, and any fault codes. ### Auto-Voltage Regulation (AVR)
AVR is the magic that keeps your gear safe during minor voltage fluctuations. Instead of pulling energy from the battery every time the line voltage wobbles, AVR dynamically boosts or trims voltage to normalize the output. It saves battery life and gives you a better chance of prolonged runtime during a small spike or dip in the grid. If you live in an area with occasional brownouts or gusty power lines, AVR is your best friend.
