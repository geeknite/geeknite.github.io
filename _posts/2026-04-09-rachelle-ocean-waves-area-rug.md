## Care and Maintenance
### Cleaning Tips
- Vacuum regularly with a low-to-medium suction setting to avoid pulling fibers. A gentle approach helps preserve the wave pattern’s integrity.
- For spills, blot—don’t rub—with a clean cloth. A quick, gentle blot helps prevent staining and keeps the pattern crisp.
- For durable spot treatment, use a mild detergent solution or a dedicated rug cleaner suitable for synthetic fibers. Always test in an inconspicuous area first.
- Consider a professional cleaning every 12-18 months if your household has heavy traffic or pet friends who love to test the acoustics of your living space.

### Stain Resistance
No rug is hulk-proof, but this one handles everyday mishaps with grace. Oil-based stains, red wine, and pet-related trouble spots can be treated with standard stain removers formatted for synthetic fibers. The key is to act quickly and avoid harsh solvents that could affect colors. If you want the long game, pair your care routine with a high-quality rug pad to extend its life and protect the underlying floor.
