Care, Cleaning, and Maintenance

Stain resistance: The Kendall Oatmeal Farmhouse Rug is designed with stain resistance in mind. Spills happen; you’re not being summoned to a court-martial for a spilled beverage. For most common spills (coffee, juice, sauce), blotting immediately with a clean cloth and a gentle approach will usually suffice. Avoid harsh chemicals; warm water and a mild detergent are typically enough to set you back on track. If you’ve got pets, a dedicated pet-safe cleaner may be used when needed, but ensure you test a small area first to ensure colorfastness.

Cleaning schedule: Regular vacuuming is recommended to prevent debris from embedding in the fibers. A low-suction or brush setting works best to avoid pulling fibers. For pet hair or stubborn debris, use a gentle vacuum that excels at picking up lint without fuzzing the pile. Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear, especially if you have high-traffic zones.

Maintenance tips: Because the rug sits in a farmhouse-inspired vibe, it’s a good idea to protect it from direct sun exposure in the long term, which can cause color fading. If you have a sunny living area, consider rotating or using curtains to cut down on UV exposure. If you’re assembling a look that combines modern and rustic, accessorize with textiles and decor that echo the rug’s warm base rather than clashing with it.
