## Practical session workflow and sample arc ideas
If you want a quick schematic for running this in a real table, here is a compact five-session arc idea built around the folio prompts:
- Session 1: Identity reveal — players define their mythic archetype and establish 2–3 key tags. Prompt seeds are dropped as questions the table answers over coffee and a few rounds of introductions.
- Session 2: The hook — a local rumor about a symbol that appears on the city’s walls at night becomes a live clue. The GM uses faction cards to seed competing loyalties.
- Session 3: Infiltration — players pass the symbol to a contact who is untrustworthy or morally ambiguous, forcing a choice about loyalty to myth or to people.
- Session 4: Consequences — a side quest erupts into a larger conspiracy; the prompts push players to redefine their relationships with their mythic identities.
- Session 5: Reckoning — the city’s mythic sponsor makes a dramatic appearance and demands a price. The ending should feel earned, not telegraphed.
