## Durability and maintenance: how it holds up to chaos

Maintenance tips from the trenches:
- Vacuum regularly to remove surface debris; do not use a beater bar on high setting if your vacuum has one.

## Care and maintenance: keep it looking fresh
A rug is not a set-and-forget item; it needs occasional attention. Here are some practical steps:
- Vacuum 2-3 times per week in high-traffic rooms; otherwise, once a week keeps dust at bay.
- Address spills immediately by blotting and using a mild detergent if needed. Avoid bleach or harsh solvents.
- Use furniture coasters to minimize indentations and keep legs from snagging the weave.
- Consider rotating the rug if one section receives more sunlight to minimize color fade.

If you want a deeper clean, you can rent or purchase a rug cleaning machine or consider professional cleaning every 12-18 months depending on usage. For pet owners, a quick spot clean after messes prevents stains from becoming permanent features in the carpet.
