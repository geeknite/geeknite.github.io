## What is Unwelcome Guests Expansion?
The Unwelcome Guests expansion for Gloom! 2nd Edition introduces a new layer of misfortune by inviting interlopers into the haunted manor. These aren’t your typical polite relatives who bring over bread and leave with your best china. No, these guests arrive with agenda, schemes, and a surprising amount of menace hidden behind their tiny card sleeves.

In practical terms, you can expect:
- New guest cards that change the way gloom counters are placed and tracked
- A handful of event cards that spice up the table talk and the occasional raised eyebrow
- A couple of unique items and tokens that alter how you assign a death doll or a family person’s fate
- A handful of house rules that emphasize negotiation, betrayal, or chaotic cooperation depending on how you want to torture your friends

Thematically, the expansion leans into the concept that in a spooky mansion, the more guests you invite, the higher the chance you will regret it around midnight when your dice refuse to roll under any sensible probability curve.

## How to set up and play with Unwelcome Guests
If you are familiar with the base game, you know the general flow: you acquire gloom, you try to minimize your own family’s tragedy while maximizing others by dropping gloom on their side of the table, and you eventually reveal a card that makes someone cry while you grin like a kid in a candy store. The expansion adds a few twists that keep the energy high and the table snarkier.

### Set-up steps
1. Lay out the base game board as usual.
2. Shuffle the new guest deck and the new event deck separately.
3. Decide whether you will include the new items and tokens, depending on your group’s appetite for chaos.
4. Draw the appropriate number of guest cards to begin. These guests function like wild guests: they will find your gloom and multiply it, all while asking awkward questions about your life choices.
5. Pro tip: keep a calm voice and a steady supply of snacks. The gloom is not hungry for snacks, but the players are.

### Core rules adjustments introduced by this expansion
- Guest cards can trigger at different phases of the round, adding a layer of timing and strategy. A guest may nudge gloom toward a target, or inject a twist that affects the entire table, including you.
- Events can create dramatic swing moments. You might pass a turn with a sigh of relief only to realize an event card has flipped the entire table vibe in a way no one asked for.
- The items and tokens emphasize the moral of the mansion: sometimes you should just pretend you didn’t see that ghost at the door and move forward with a napkin in your hand.

### Thematic notes on guests
The new guests are designed to be both humorous and a touch sinister. They can be cunning hosts, forgetful specters, or guests who insist on performing an impromptu séance with your character’s last name. The flavor text is where the expansion earns its keep—the lines are witty, often dark, and sure to generate some giggles even as doom is being doled out in tidy little sleeves.

## Core mechanics vs expansion specifics
A good expansion should not feel required to enjoy the base game, but should add enough to justify a revisit. Unwelcome Guests hits this balance with a few elegant touches:
- The guest triggers feel integrated rather than tacked on. They often interact with the gloom track in ways that force you to re-evaluate your risk management strategy in real time.
- The events scale in unpredictability. You can know roughly where the doom is headed, but not exactly how you get there.
- The items and tokens emphasize the moral of the mansion: sometimes you should just pretend you didn’t see that ghost at the door and move forward with a napkin in your hand.

The result is a sort of chaotic diplomacy: you want to keep your neighbors friendly, but the expansion is designed to provoke a bit of friendly hostility as you duke it out over who gets to reveal the next gloom milestone.

## Strategy and tips for new players
If you are a new player, the key is to avoid getting overwhelmed by the new cards. Start simple: pick a small subset of guest cards to begin with, and gradually introduce more as you become comfortable. Here are a few practical strategies:
- Focus on controlling the timing of when gloom accelerates. The expansion often rewards players who can anticipate the shift, rather than those who panic when an anchor card shows up.
- Use events to create opportunities for others to stumble forward. The best gossip at the table is the one that drags the loudest player into your plan while you remain behind the velvet curtain.
- Watch for synergy between guests and your own gloom track. Some guests will amplify gloom that is already creeping in on a particular side of the board; others will swing the pendulum in a way you can exploit with careful planning.
- Don’t forget to keep a sense of humor. Gloom is a shared doom party, and a good joke can soften a bad roll and turn a misfortune into a memorable moment.

## Table talk moments and memorable anecdotes
One of the joys of Gloom is the ridiculous stories that emerge from doom. The Unwelcome Guests expansion amplifies that. A well-timed guest can provoke a cascade of jokes about who gets to reveal which gloom milestone, who gets stuck with the worst fate, and who ends up becoming the hero of the haunted house story not because of their decisions but because of their ability to roll with ridiculous misfortune.
