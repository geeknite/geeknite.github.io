## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by the M3 Chip

![M3 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-13/a/images/overview/m3__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}m3-chip)

The heart of the iPad Pro 13 is its revolutionary M3 chip. With an 8-core CPU, 10-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine, this tablet is designed to handle everything from 8K video editing to playing the latest games. It's so powerful, you might forget it's a tablet and start treating it like a desktop replacement.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 12 hours of battery life on a single charge. In our tests, it held up well during a day of mixed use, including browsing, streaming, and some light gaming. You'll finally be able to leave your charger at home without anxiety.
