## Weather Resistance, Longevity, and Care
Outdoor furniture is a test of time and tolerance for sun, rain, and enthusiastic weekend barbecues. This set is designed with weather resistance in mind: rust-resistant hardware, cushions with water-resistance, and a finish that can withstand some sun exposure. Still, you’ll want to cover the set or store cushions indoors during heavy rain or winter months. A good outdoor cover can be your best investment if you want to preserve color and maintain that “new-furniture” feel for longer.

Cleaning is straightforward: a mild soap solution and water, then air dry. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could scratch the finish, and wipe away leaves or debris before they harden into stubborn grime. If you notice color fading over time (which is possible in extreme sun), you can apply a UV-protective spray designed for outdoor furniture to extend the life of the fabric.
