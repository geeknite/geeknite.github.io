## Installation: step by step, with occasional humor
### Step by step setup
1. Clear the area where the panel will mount and ensure your desk edge is clean. A cluttered desk is a poor audience for the dramatic entrance of a new panel.
2. Unpack the PET panel and the two C clamps. Check for burrs or rough edges that could scratch the desk or your pride.
3. Position the panel behind the workspace and align it so that it covers the essential view area. The goal is privacy without turning your workstation into a black box. A quick dry fit helps you visualize the final placement.
4. Attach the clamps to the desk edge and begin tightening. Alternate tightening on each clamp to ensure even pressure and avoid warping. Do not over tighten; this is not a car engine. A firm but gentle approach keeps the desk and the panel happy.
5. Check for wobble and adjust as needed. If the panel wobbles with your dramatic typing, you may need to reposition or tighten further. If it still wobbles, consider adding a small spacer between the desk edge and the clamp to boost stability.
6. For extra rigidity, install the lower edge piece to hold the panel in place without sagging under heavy monitor arms or tall stacks of reference books that you never read.

### Quick tips
- Avoid placing the panel behind monitor arms that swing into the open space. The panel is sturdy but not a universal joint for your entire desk ecosystem.
- If you share the desk with a multi monitor setup, ensure the panel does not obstruct the top corners of any monitor. You want privacy, not a blocking shield that creates new glare.
- Keep a small cleaning cloth handy to wipe away fingerprints. PET surfaces are friendly but they do show smudges if you treat them like a human touch screen.
