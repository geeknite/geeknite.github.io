## Care and maintenance

- vacuum with a soft attachment to prevent fiber pulling
- rotate every few months to promote even wear
- spot clean with mild detergent and water
- avoid direct sun to minimize color fade

A high pile can feel luxurious underfoot, but it also collects pet hair and crumbs more easily than low pile options. If you have a fur brigade in your home, you might want to budget for a high quality vacuum with a good beater bar.
