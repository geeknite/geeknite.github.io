## Fortune Row in a Nutshell
Fortune Row builds on the idea that players are more than their masks. In City of Mist, everyone is a walking myth, wearing a skin of the mundane. Fortune Row expands the toolset game masters use to capture that mythic mood by providing a curated set of custom character sheets. These aren’t just pretty templates; they’re designed to guide players toward the fusion of identity and power that City of Mist games thrive on.

Think of Fortune Row as a character sheet cookbook: you’ll find formats that emphasize relationships, power sources, and the quirky, often hilarious masks that players wear when they pretend to be ordinary folks with extraordinary agency. You’ll also find mechanical conveniences, like slots for perks, weaknesses, and faction ties, all color-coded and ready to print.

### A quick caveat on scope
Fortune Row doesn’t rewrite the core rules. It respects the City of Mist framework—mythology, masks, skills, and Tag mechanics—while offering fresh layouts and prompts to help you visualize a character’s myths as a character sheet, not just a paragraph on a page. If you want to see how your current party can slot their identities into Fortune Row’s templates, you’ll be happy to know that compatibility is a feature, not a bolt-on.

## What’s Inside Fortune Row
Fortune Row isn’t a single, monolithic PDF. It’s a curated collection of sheets designed to cover a wide range of play styles, from one-shot merriment to long-running city-wide intrigues. Here’s the lay of the land:

### The core sheets
- Identity-first sheets that foreground the Mask concept

### Thematic variations

## How to Use Fortune Row at the Table
There’s a surprising amount of practical utility here. The sheets are built for speed, clarity, and a touch of whimsy. Here are some ways to integrate Fortune Row into your sessions:

### Quick-start for new players
Provide a trimmed-down sheet that highlights identity, anchor relationships, and a small set of power tags. Encourage new players to describe their masks through short vignettes—this is where the sheet’s prompts become player-driven story hooks rather than dry data entry.

### Mid-game reconfiguration
Fortune Row’s layout makes it easy to swap a mask or add a new power as the story evolves. You don’t need to rewrite pages of text; you can print a replacement page or make a quick digital copy for the evolving identity.

### Campaign scaffolding
For longer arcs, Fortune Row can be used as a campaign toolkit: place a character’s growth in a dedicated section of the sheet and prompt players to reflect on how their myths shift as the city shifts. It’s not just a character sheet; it’s a narrative instrument.

### Integrating with the core rules
To keep things clean, maintain the relationship between masks and tags as your primary mechanic and use Fortune Row as a way to visualize those relationships. If you’re the type who uses a villain tier chart, Fortune Row can contain a dedicated page for rival masks and their plots.
