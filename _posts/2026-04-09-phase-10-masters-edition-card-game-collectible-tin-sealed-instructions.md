## The Core Gameplay and Masters Variants
Phase 10 is simple in concept: complete a sequence of ten phases by collecting and discarding cards to form sets and runs. The Masters Edition takes that simple concept and introduces new cards and rules that can tilt balance in surprising ways. These rules can make the game feel fresh even for players who routinely bully a standard Phase 10 cycle into a familiar rhythm.
