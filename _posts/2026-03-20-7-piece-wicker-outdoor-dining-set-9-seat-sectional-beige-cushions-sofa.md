Weather resistance is the core of outdoor durability. ### Weather resilience
Outdoor furniture faces sun, rain, wind, and the occasional insect audience. The wicker weave typically shrugs off sun glare and light rain, while cushions dry quickly thanks to water-repellent fabrics. The key is to let moisture escape rather than trap it; if cushions stay damp for days, you may want to swap them out or temporarily store them indoors if you can. In a windy climate, consider using furniture breather tools such as chair covers to prevent wind-borne debris from becoming permanent souvenirs. The bottom line: this set can endure a lot, but a little care goes a long way.

### Cleaning and care
A mild soap and water solution is usually enough for daily cleaning. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the weave or fade cushion color. For stains on cushion covers, pre-treat with a gentle stain remover and air dry. Regularly inspect the joints and screws to maintain wobble-free seating. A small amount of maintenance can dramatically extend the life of outdoor furniture, turning what could have become a seasonal fling into a long-term love affair with your backyard space.
