## Care and Maintenance

### Cleaning

Regular vacuuming is essential to keep the rug looking its best. For stains, spot cleaning with a mild detergent and water will do the trick. Avoid using harsh chemicals, as they can damage the fibers.

### Rotation

Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and tear. This will help maintain its appearance and extend its lifespan.

### Professional Cleaning

Consider professional cleaning once a year to give your rug a deep clean. This will remove embedded dirt and keep the colors vibrant.
