Maintenance and Preservation
Preserving a 1998 Coca-Cola edition isn’t rocket science, but it does require care if you want it to survive another couple of decades with its charm intact. Here are practical tips:
- Store in a stable climate; excessive humidity can encourage moisture damage, and temperature swings can cause the tin to warp slightly.
- Keep the tin closed when not in use to prevent dust accumulation and to maintain the design’s aesthetic integrity.
- If the cards show minor sticking or slight residue, a gentle wipe with a microfiber cloth is usually enough; avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the card stock or graphic print.
- For the serious collector, maintain the original insert and the inner deck in protective sleeves to reduce wear to the card faces.
