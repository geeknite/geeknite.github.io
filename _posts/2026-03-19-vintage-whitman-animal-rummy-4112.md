## Preservation and Handling

This is a vintage item, not a modern novelty. If you’re going to display it, handle gently. Here are some tips:

- Keep it out of direct sun. The colors on the box and the card faces are best preserved when they aren’t baked by the sun.

- Store in a dry environment. Paper and card stock hate moisture; you don’t want to discover your Animal Rummy set is more swamp than safari.

- Consider a sleeve or archival box for protection. If you’re going to play, use clean hands or even cotton gloves to preserve the card faces.

- If you’re lucky enough to have the complete set, consider a display option that’s both aesthetic and protective. A glass-topped display in a home library is always a good idea.
