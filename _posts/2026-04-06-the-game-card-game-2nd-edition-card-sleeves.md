Care and maintenance tips

To keep your sleeves in tip top shape, you will want to avoid exposing them to direct sunlight for long periods, as UV light can lead to discoloration over time. If you notice slight warping after many games, you can place the deck in a flat, cool area to let the sleeves reset before your next big night. For those who have a proclivity for food and drink near the gaming table, a quick wipe with a damp cloth will take care of accidental residues without compromising the sleeve material. The long and short of it: treat your sleeves with respect, and they will repay you with improved table presence and fewer stray crinkles.
