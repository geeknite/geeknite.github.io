## Maintenance and Care
### Cleaning and Lubrication
Like most stainless-ish or steel blades used for display purposes, you’ll want to wipe the blade after handling to remove fingerprints and oils. A soft cloth with a gentle metal-safe cleaner does wonders. Avoid harsh solvents that could dull the finish or degrade any leather wrappings. A light coat of blade oil can help protect against moisture when you’re not displaying the sword in a glass case.

### Storage Tips
Keep the sword in a dry environment. Humidity is your enemy when you’re aiming for a pristine showcase piece. A dedicated sword stand or wall mount can both display the UC3245 and help prevent misplacement in the chaos of daily life. If you travel with it for cosplay shoots, ensure the scabbard is secure, so you don’t end up with a halfway-open case and a sword performing a dramatic escape routine down the hotel hallway.

### Longevity and Display Life
Display swords like the UC3245 excel when treated with a light dosage of respect and regular care. They aren’t meant to endure brutal handling or heavy-duty use, but with proper storage and occasional wipe-downs, they can remain striking for years. The true test is how well the finish holds up under ambient light and the occasional dusting session you perform with the dedication of a curator.
