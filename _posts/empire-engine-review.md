## **Game Overview**

**Empire Engine** is a unique, steampunk-inspired board game designed by esteemed creators Chris Marling and Matthew Dunstan. The game thrusts you into the role of a cunning engineer, vying to build an industrial empire that outsmarts your rivals. With its intricate mechanics, engaging gameplay, and a dash of humor, Empire Engine promises a rollercoaster of fun.

### Components

The game includes:
- 4 Player Boards
- 16 Gears
- 24 Resource Tokens

## **Gameplay Mechanics: A Steamy Affair**

Empire Engine is all about managing your resources and making strategic decisions. Each player has a set of gears that determine the actions they can take. The game is played over multiple rounds, with players simultaneously choosing and revealing their actions.

### The Basics

At the start of each round, players secretly choose two actions using their gears. Actions range from gathering resources to attacking opponents. Once everyone has chosen, actions are revealed and resolved in a specific order. The objective? Amass the most valuable empire by the end of the game.

### Strategic Depth

What sets Empire Engine apart is its deep strategy. Do you focus on building your resources early on, or do you go on the offensive and disrupt your opponents' plans? Every decision matters, and the game rewards those who can think ahead and adapt to changing circumstances.

**Pro Tip**: Always keep an eye on your rivals' actions. Predicting their moves can give you a significant edge (and a lot of smug satisfaction).
