## Care and Maintenance: Longevity Without Living in a Vacuum
### Cleaning Routine
- Vacuum weekly with a brush attachment to keep the fibers upright and the surface looking fresh.
- Treat spills promptly with a blotting technique: dab, don’t rub, to prevent the stain from wicking deeper into the pile.
- For tougher stains, use a mild soap solution or a carpet-cleaning product recommended by the rug’s manufacturer. Always test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate every few months to promote even wear and avoid high-traffic patterns from becoming permanent nap marks.
- A periodic professional cleaning is a good idea for high-traffic areas or families with kids and pets.

### Durability and Fade Resistance
The Mountain Taupe rug is designed to hold up to daily living, which means it’s resistant to common wear-and-tear culprits like foot traffic, spilled beverages, and the occasional dropped snack. If you’re worried about sunlight, consider window treatments that reduce UV exposure during peak sun hours to keep the rug’s color looking consistent over the years.

## How to Choose the Right Rug Padding and Accessories
If you’re serious about making the Mountain Taupe rug perform at peak efficiency, invest in a quality anti-slip underlay and consider a thin, dense pad that offers cushioning without adding too much height.
