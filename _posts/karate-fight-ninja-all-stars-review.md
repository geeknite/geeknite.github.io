## Gameplay

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Movement Phase**: Ninjas move around the board, navigating obstacles and heading towards power-up areas.
2. **Attack Phase**: Players use their abilities and weapons to attack opponents. The hilarious outcomes are as unpredictable as a cat in a cucumber patch.
3. **Power-Up Phase**: Ninjas collect power-ups that grant temporary boosts or special items.
