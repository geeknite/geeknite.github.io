## How to Store and Preserve Your Foils

Foil cards require a little more care than their standard counterparts. Flat storage is your friend. Here are a few practical tips to keep your foil magnets in top condition:

- Use acid-free sleeves to protect the foil face and keep the card edges pristine.
- Store upright in a sturdy binder or case to prevent bending. Foils are more susceptible to corner dings.
- Avoid direct sunlight. The holo tint can fade or become uneven if exposed to long periods of bright light.
- Consider a display case with UV-resistant glass if you plan to show off your collection in a public space.
- Maintain humidity around 40-60 percent to prevent paper warping and foil separation in extreme cases.

With proper care, your 30 rare foils and the foil promo can remain visually striking for years to come, which is exactly what you want when you are showing off to friends who think your hobby is a passing phase.
