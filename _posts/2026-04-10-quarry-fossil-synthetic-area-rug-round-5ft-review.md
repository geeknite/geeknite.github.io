## Cleaning, Care, and Maintenance
Care is boring but essential. Fortunately, the Quarry Fossil Round 5ft isn’t a mission-critical maintenance drama. For everyday care, vacuum regularly using a low-suction setting. This helps remove surface dust without pulling fibers or causing wear on the pile. For spills, you want to address them quickly. Blot, don’t rub, with a clean, absorbent cloth. Mild soap and water can handle most light stains, and you can resume normal use once the area is dry. Avoid harsh chemicals that might discolor or degrade the synthetic fibers, and never place the rug near open flames or heat sources that could soften or warp the pile. If you do encounter a stubborn stain, test any cleaner on a small, inconspicuous corner first.

Rotating the rug every few months helps distribute wear and prevent flat spots from forming where heavy furniture sits. A rug pad is advised if you want to minimize slippage and improve overall comfort underfoot. The combination of a rug pad and a round 5-foot rug helps create a stable foundation, reducing shifting and curling edges that irritate you whenever you stand up after a long design binge.

### Care patterns that work well
- Vacuum weekly on a gentle setting to keep the fossil pattern crisp.
- Spot-clean spills promptly with mild detergent and water.
- Use a rug pad to reduce movement and add cushion.
- Rotate the rug every 2–3 months to balance wear.
- Keep away from direct sunlight to minimize color fading over months.
