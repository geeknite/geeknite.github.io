## What is Charcuterie: The Board Game?

Charcuterie: The Board Game is a strategic and competitive experience where players create the ultimate charcuterie board. With beautifully crafted components and mouth-watering artwork, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged and might make you hungry.

### The Basics

In Charcuterie: The Board Game, players work to create the most impressive charcuterie board by collecting meats, cheeses, fruits, and other gourmet items. Each player has a unique set of objectives, and the goal is to earn points by fulfilling these objectives and creating aesthetically pleasing arrangements.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures of various food items, perfect for assembling your charcuterie board.
- **Cards**: Objective cards, action cards, and special ability cards that add depth and strategy to the gameplay.
- **Game Board**: A beautifully illustrated board depicting a rustic kitchen setting.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, bonuses, and special actions during the game.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Charcuterie: The Board Game is like preparing a gourmet meal. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your starter items. Now you're ready to embark on your culinary quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their objectives. These could range from collecting specific items to making trades or using special abilities. Cooperation and sabotage are both part of the fun, so strategize wisely!

### Scoring

Scoring in Charcuterie: The Board Game is a mix of strategy and creativity. Players earn points based on the variety and quality of items on their board, as well as how well they fulfill their objectives. Did you plan an epic combo just to have your friend snatch the last piece of gourmet cheese? Welcome to the delicious chaos.
