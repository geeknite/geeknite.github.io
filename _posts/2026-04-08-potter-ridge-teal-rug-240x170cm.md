## Durability and Maintenance
Durability is the quiet hero in any rug purchase. The Potter Ridge Teal Rug is marketed as a product designed to resist flattening, with fibers that bounce back after foot traffic and gaming sessions. In practical terms, this means your rug should retain its shape even after months of use under a busy coffee table and a steady stream of foot traffic during epic movie nights.

Maintenance is easier than you might fear. Regular vacuuming is recommended, ideally with a vacuum that has a gentle setting to avoid pulling up loose fibers. Immediate blotting of spills with a clean cloth is advised, followed by a light cleaning with a mild detergent if a stain persists. For more persistent marks, a carpet cleaner on a cool setting can be used, but always test a small hidden area first to ensure the color does not bleed or fade. If you re a tidy person, you can set a weekly rotation: Monday for vacuuming, Friday for quick spot checks, and Sunday for a quick sniff test to make sure the room still smells like a cozy den rather than a gym locker. Yes, rugs have smell memory, and teal is a color that can help keep things calm.
