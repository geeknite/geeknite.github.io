## Performance on the table
The true measure of any playmat is not how cool it looks in product photos but how it feels when you’re really gaming. We tested the Artist Gallery Black Playmat across a few genres: first person shooters, real-time strategy, and tabletop simulator sessions where precise mouse control is everything.

### Surface feel and mouse tracking
The surface provides a balanced combination of glide and control. The fabric texture offers a micro-grip that gives you the sensation of precision without the jitter of an overly grippy mat. In FPS titles, you don’t want to feel like your cursor is fighting gravity; this mat keeps things smooth, allowing for both slow, surgical movements and rapid, sweeping drags. For RTS titles, the smoothness means you can execute multi unit commands with a consistent cursor path. For a creative touch, we ran a few drawing tests with a stylus and found it to be a perfectly playable surface for digital sketching during breaks between rounds.

### Durability and wear
When you’re gaming heavily, you expect a mat to take a beating. The Artist Gallery Black Playmat survived weeks of daily use with only minimal signs of wear. The edges remained intact, the surface did not peel or develop crunchy fibers, and the rubber backing showed no signs of cracking or delaminating. A few mild scuffs appeared after repeated dragging of heavier mouse weights, but nothing dramatic that would suggest it’s going to fall apart after a month of use. Cleaning is straightforward, too: a quick wipe with a damp cloth is usually enough to remove dust and minor spills; for more stubborn marks, a gentle wash with mild soap and water does the trick. Do not put it in the washing machine; the thought might be tempting, but we’re not trying to invent a new chaos run in the laundry cycle.

### Cleanliness and maintenance tips
- Blot spills immediately with a dry cloth.
- For sticky residues, a bit of diluted dish soap and warm water works well.
- Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the surface over time.
- Let it air dry fully before rolling or storing to maintain its shape.
