## Care and Longevity
### Cleaning spills and maintenance
The polypropylene fiber is stain resistant and durable in the face of the typical living room chaos. A quality rug pad underneath will keep it from sliding and protect your floor from scuffs. Regular vacuuming is advisable, with a gentle approach to avoid pulling threads at the edges. For deeper cleaning, a mix of warm water and a mild detergent, with careful blotting rather than scrubbing, will preserve color and texture. Avoid bleach and harsh solvents, which can fade the blue and turn the red into a pinkish regret you’ll want to forget.

### Color stability and sun exposure
In direct sunlight, colors can shift slightly. It is not dramatic, but if your space has a wall of windows, you might notice the blue losing some of its deep glow after months of afternoon sun. If this bothers you, rotate the rug or place it in a less sunny corner.
