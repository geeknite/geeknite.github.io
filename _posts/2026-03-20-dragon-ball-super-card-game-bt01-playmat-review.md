### Practical Tips and Care
A quick care guide to keep BT01 looking sharp:
- Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight, which can fade the print over months.
- Gently wipe with a damp cloth to remove residue; avoid harsh chemical cleaners that could degrade the surface.
- Let it air-dry flat to prevent warping if you’ve rolled it for travel.
- When not in use, store it in a clean bag or sleeve to avoid dust-induced micro-scratches on the surface.

If you want to keep it pristine, consider a light protective sleeve for your entire mat when transporting. It may seem a little overkill, but it’s the kind of small investment that pays off by preventing a $2 sleeve-like disaster on a mat that cost more than your first car ride allowance.
