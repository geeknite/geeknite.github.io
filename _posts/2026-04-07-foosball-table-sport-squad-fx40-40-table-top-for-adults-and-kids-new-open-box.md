Setup and Maintenance
The unboxing experience sets the tone; however, the real magic happens during setup. Assembly is straightforward, even for people who have not assembled a single piece of furniture since college, and the instructions are clear, with diagrams that actually make sense. You’ll need a few basic tools which are included or easily found around the house. The process typically takes under an hour for a first-time assembler. After assembly, you can align the rods and adjust the leg levelers. The FX40 benefits from a quick wipe-down before first use to remove any packaging dust and the residual fear of misplacing a rod.

Maintenance is minimal: keep the surface clean, avoid slamming the board with a heavy object, and occasionally check the screws for tightness. The rods can be oiled with a small amount of silicone-based lubricant if they feel stiff, but do not overdo it. A little lubricant goes a long way, and the last thing you want is a squeaky rod during a dramatic two-goal comeback in the final minute.

Tips for getting the most out of your FX40
- Practice your passing; the pass is crucial for setting up a shot on goal.
- Keep the ball in the middle of the field to avoid giving the ball away to the other players.
- Use the rods to control the ball with a controlled motion; avoid over-swinging or slamming the ball across the field.
- Encourage fair play and fun; foosball is a game, not a war.
