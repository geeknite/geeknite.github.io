## Box design, packaging, and preservation tips
A sealed 1995 Overpower deck is a little time capsule that deserves care. To preserve its aesthetic and structural integrity:
- Store in a cool, dry, dark place away from direct sunlight. UV light is the sworn enemy of any printed art from the 90s. 
- Maintain a stable temperature to avoid warping or inline deck warping. A climate‑controlled closet is ideal.
- Handle the wrapper and box with clean hands; residue or oils can slowly degrade the wrapper and the interior card stocks.
- If you open the deck for playing, consider preserving the outer box in a protective sleeve to maintain display value.

The box art itself is a piece of design history. In the Mutants Unite variant, expect bold color blocks, dramatic typography, and the kind of character roster that would sell a toy line in a single image. The packaging is not just a vessel; it’s a marketing artifact that conveys the energy of the era — a time when comic book universes were expanding into every corner of pop culture and every kid knew the name of at least three mutants by heart.
