## Setup Tips and Maintenance Hacks
- Plan the layout: Clear a path and mark a spot for drinks and snacks; foosball is not forgiving of spilled beverages.
- Unpack strategically: The boxes will look big, but you can often carry pieces one by one and avoid a backache from lifting a heavy single carton.
- Align the legs: Use the levelers to ensure a flat playing surface; otherwise, the ball will pretend it’s on a wobbly roller-coaster and you will pretend it’s a strategy.
- Break-in the rods: Before the first big match, run the rods back and forth to loosen them; this reduces stiffness and makes you look like you know what you are doing.
- Clean after play: A quick wipe with a damp cloth is usually enough; avoid soaking the surface with harsh chemicals that could degrade the finish.
- Ongoing care: Light oil on the rods every few months helps maintain the smooth action and prevents the dreaded squeak evolution.
