## The Basics

### What is Forbidden Psalm: They Came from the Necropolis?

Forbidden Psalm: They Came from the Necropolis is a miniatures game expansion for the Mörk Borg RPG. This expansion brings new scenarios, characters, and challenges that will have you battling undead horrors and trying to survive in a world that feels like it was designed by your worst nightmares.

### Components

The game comes with an array of new miniatures, cards, and tokens that are beautifully designed — if you find decayed zombies and grotesque monsters beautiful, that is. From the intricate details on the Necromancer's robes to the unsettling emptiness in the undead's eyes, these components are a collector's dream (or nightmare).

## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Forbidden Psalm feels like preparing for a séance. Lay out the cards, assemble the miniatures, and try not to get too disturbed by the ominous atmosphere. You might want to keep a flashlight handy — not that it'll help against the horrors you're about to face.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and use them to perform actions. These could range from moving and attacking to summoning unholy creatures or trying to cleanse cursed artifacts. The synergy between characters and their unique abilities is where Forbidden Psalm shines.

### Combat

Combat is straightforward but strategic. Players must coordinate their attacks and special abilities to defeat enemies and complete objectives. Remember, teamwork is key — unless your teammate gets possessed. Then it's every person for themselves.

## The Story

### Scenarios

This expansion introduces new scenarios inspired by tales that would give Edgar Allan Poe nightmares. From battling hordes of undead in the Necropolis to trying to escape the cursed catacombs, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different strategies.

### Characters

This expansion brings a host of new characters to the mix. Play as the fearless Gravekeeper, the tormented Soul Collector, or even the infamous Necromancer. Each character has distinct abilities and combos that keep the game fresh and exciting.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: The necropolis theme adds depth and excitement, making you feel like you're part of a gothic horror novel.
- **Replayability**: With new scenarios and characters, no two games are ever the same.
- **Beautiful Components**: The miniatures and cards are a feast for the eyes — if you can stomach them.
- **Strategic Depth**: Coordinating attacks and abilities adds a layer of strategy that keeps players engaged.

### Cons

- **Complex Setup**: Be prepared to spend a good amount of time setting up each scenario.
- **Horror Overload**: Some scenarios can be overwhelmingly creepy, which might not be everyone's cup of tea.
- **Expensive**: Collecting all expansions could be a hit to your wallet.

## Final Thoughts

Forbidden Psalm: They Came from the Necropolis is a fantastic addition to any tabletop collection, especially for fans of horror and dark fantasy. **Criticism Reminder**: While the game is incredibly fun, it’s not without its flaws. The setup can be time-consuming, and the horror elements might be too much for some players. But for those willing to embrace the chaos, Forbidden Psalm: They Came from the Necropolis is an absolute blast.
