## Maintenance Hacks You’ll Want to Bookmark
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and fading.
- Treat spills promptly using blotting rather than rubbing; your future self will thank you.
- Use a rug pad to reduce slipping and to add a layer of cushion and insulation against cold floors.
- Clean stains with a mild solution appropriate for the fiber type, then blot and air-dry.
