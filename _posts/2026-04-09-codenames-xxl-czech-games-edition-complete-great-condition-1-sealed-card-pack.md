## Practical Tips for Your First 10 Rounds

- Readability first: place the cards where the team can comfortably see them without leaning or squinting.

- Rotate spymasters: swapping who gives the clue every few rounds keeps the energy fresh and prevents the game from devolving into “one person’s inside jokes about synonyms compiled over three years.”
