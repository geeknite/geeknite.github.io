Assembly and Maintenance Tips
------------------------------
- Do a quick inventory of parts before you start; pulling out the wrong panel at the wrong stage is a fast path to the neighbors hearing your loud complaints.
- Group similar screws together to avoid the classic moment of mixing too many small pieces and turning the project into a puzzle with missing pieces.
- Keep the area clean during assembly; you will thank yourself when you don’t have to fish a dropped screw out of a crevasse between panels.
- After assembly, test the alignment with a level, then tighten the brackets gradually to avoid later ridge lines that would make a carpenter laugh and a user cry.
- For ongoing maintenance, wipe down the laminate with a soft cloth and a mild cleaner. Avoid harsh chemical sprays that could dull the finish; you want a desk that looks good for years, not a catastrophe that looks good for a week.
