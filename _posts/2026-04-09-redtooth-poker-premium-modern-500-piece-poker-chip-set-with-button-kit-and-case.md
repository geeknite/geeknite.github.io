## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity
Your poker chips deserve occasional love and maintenance. A simple routine will extend their life and preserve readability:
- Wipe them down periodically to remove oils from hands, which over time can degrade the edge spots.
- Avoid exposing them to sunlight for long periods; color fading can occur with prolonged sun exposure.
- Keep them in the case when not in use to minimize dust and accidental chips-above-the-lap accidents.
- If a chip shows a small crack or edge chip, remove it temporarily from active stacks to avoid further damage from shuffles.

For the home gamer who wants things to stay pristine, a little routine maintenance goes a long way. It won’t turn a lifetime into a myth, but it will protect your investment and keep your chips looking sharp for more sessions than your last pizza.
