## H2: Assembly and Maintenance

Assembling a 7-piece dining set plus a sectional is not exactly a weekend at a DIY camp; it’s more like a plant-by-plant gardening session where you’re building a small indoor forest outdoors. Expect to dedicate 2-4 hours for the dining set with a second person; the sectional may take longer depending on how modular you want it.

Maintenance is straightforward:

- Wipe frames with a soft cloth and mild soap solution; avoid abrasive cleaners.
- For cushions, spot-clean stains as soon as they appear; air-dry completely before using again.
- Use covers during heavy rain or winter months to prolong life.
- Store cushions indoors if possible; if not, use breathable storage bags to reduce mildew risk.
