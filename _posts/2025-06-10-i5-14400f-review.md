## **Performance**

### **Cooling Performance**

In cooling benchmarks, the Hyper 212 EVO holds its own against more expensive coolers. Whether you're pushing your CPU with intense gaming sessions or demanding productivity tasks, this cooler keeps temperatures in check. During our tests, we saw idle temperatures around 30°C and load temperatures peaking at 70°C. Not bad for a cooler that costs less than dinner at a fancy restaurant!

### **Noise Levels**

Nobody likes a noisy cooler, and the Hyper 212 EVO does a great job of keeping things quiet. At idle, it's whisper-quiet, and even under load, it doesn't sound like a jet engine taking off. The noise levels stay within a comfortable range, ensuring you can concentrate on your tasks or enjoy your games without distraction.

## **Installation**

Installing the Hyper 212 EVO is a breeze, even if you're not a seasoned PC builder. Cooler Master provides clear instructions, and the mounting system is straightforward. Just make sure you have enough clearance in your case, as the cooler isn't exactly compact.
