## Materials, care, and longevity tips

To preserve the print and keep colors vibrant, consider the following:

- Frame under UV protective glass if your room gets direct sun during peak hours. The glass helps preserve color without sacrificing the overall mood. 
- Use a consistent humidity level in the room to prevent warping if you chose a wood frame. A simple hygrometer can be a nice touch for the geeky set.
- Clean the glass gently with a microfiber cloth. Do not spray liquids directly on the print surface.
- If you plan to store unframed prints, keep them flat and avoid rolling with heavy objects that could create creases.
