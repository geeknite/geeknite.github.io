## Strategy and tips for players
Streaking Kittens is not a brain melting puzzle that requires intense calculus to win; it is a social card game that rewards flexible thinking, nerve, and listening skills. Here are some practical tips to help your crew maximize their chances of surviving the stampede of fur and chaos:

- Read the room before you draw. The best time to gamble with a risky draw is when your table is laughing, not when everyone stares blankly with a silent agreement that they are done with your antics.
- Save your defuse cards for the moments that matter. In this expansion defuses remain the best defense against a sudden cascade of exploding laughter and exploding kittens, so use them when you can deny a major payoff to the table rather than when you are just trying to protect a fragile ego.
- Use mischief cards to target a player who is clearly on a hot streak or just beginning to stack a lead. For newer players this can be a good way to disrupt momentum and spark a dramatic comeback.
- Pay attention to card counting. Not the math heavy version, just a mental note of cards that have already shown up, so you can predict how the next few draws might unfold. In chaotic games this often translates to not overextending your own plan and letting the table level the playing field in humorous fashion.
- Embrace the chaos but keep a level head. It is easy to let the enthusiasm of the moment propel you to reckless decisions. The trick is to channel that energy into making others laugh while you keep your own status alive, at least for another round.
