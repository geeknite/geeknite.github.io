## Storage Tips and Table Hygiene
A well-sorted token set is a joy to use, but you’ll still want a bit of table etiquette to keep things pleasant. A few quick tips:
- Pre-sort tokens into a small tray or labeled zip pouch before you start a game night. It reduces downtime and the “is this mine or is this a mis-placed token from last game?” moments.
- Keep a dedicated damp cloth or gentle wipe to clean tokens occasionally. In a world where a snack crumb is a hazard to a clean token count, cleanliness matters.
- Consider a soft mat or felt under your game area to prevent tokens from rolling away during spikes in action. A little cushioning reduces the chance of misreads when you’ve got two dragons in your line of sight.

Pro-tip: if you’re playing with a tall group, designate a token captain at the start of the round who will be responsible for calling out which tokens are in use. It sounds silly, but you’ll be amazed how much speed you gain when one person becomes the on-table token wrangler instead of three people arguing about which tile a token should live on.
