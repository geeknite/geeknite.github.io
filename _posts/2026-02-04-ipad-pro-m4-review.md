## Introduction

Welcome back, Geeknite readers! The iPad Pro M4 has landed, and it's causing quite a stir in the tech world. Is it worth your hard-earned cash, or is it just another shiny toy? Let's dive into the nitty-gritty and see what makes the iPad Pro M4 the ultimate geek's dream tablet.

## Design and Build Quality

The iPad Pro M4 sports a sleek and modern design that Apple is famous for. The unibody aluminum chassis feels both premium and durable. It's lighter and thinner than its predecessors, making it perfect for those on the go.

![iPad Pro M4 Design](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/design__c2u6xn6rvs2u_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipad-pro-m4-design)

### Ports and Connectivity

Apple has surprisingly listened to user feedback and included a USB-C port, alongside Thunderbolt 4, and even a headphone jack! Yes, you heard it right. Apple remembered audiophiles exist.

## Display and Audio

![iPad Pro M4 Display](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipad-pro-m4-display)

The iPad Pro M4 features a stunning 12.9-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology. Colors are vibrant, blacks are deep, and the brightness could practically light up a small village.

The audio quality is impressive. With a six-speaker sound system and force-canceling woofers, it feels like having a mini home theater in your tablet. Whether you're watching movies or jamming to tunes, the sound will blow you away.

## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by M4 Chip

![M4 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/m4__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}m4-chip)

The heart of the iPad Pro M4 is its revolutionary M4 chip. With a 12-core CPU, 16-core GPU, and 32-core Neural Engine, this powerhouse is designed to handle everything from video editing to running multiple virtual machines.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 20 hours of battery life on a single charge. In our tests, it didn't disappoint. Whether you're working, gaming, or binge-watching, the iPad Pro M4 will keep you going all day and then some.

## Keyboard and Pencil

![Magic Keyboard](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/keyboard__f5dhn0m5h16m_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}magic-keyboard)

The Magic Keyboard for iPad Pro M4 is back and better than ever. Typing on it feels like a dream, and the large trackpad is highly responsive. The Apple Pencil 3 supports a plethora of new gestures, making navigation and drawing a breeze.

## Software and Features

![iPadOS](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/ipados__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipados)

### iPadOS 16

The iPad Pro M4 comes with iPadOS 16, packed with new features like Universal Control, redesigned widgets, and enhanced multitasking. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the iPad Pro stand out from the competition.

### FaceTime and Webcam

The 4K FaceTime HD camera is a significant upgrade, ensuring you look your best on video calls. Coupled with the studio-quality three-mic array, it's perfect for remote meetings or catching up with friends and family.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Exceptional Performance**: The M4 chip delivers unparalleled speed and efficiency.
- **Stunning Display**: The Liquid Retina XDR display is a visual treat.
- **All-Day Battery Life**: No more carrying around chargers.
- **Improved Connectivity**: USB-C and headphone jack included.

### Cons

- **Pricey**: The iPad Pro M4 doesn't come cheap.
- **No SD Card Slot**: Still no love for expandable storage enthusiasts.
- **Limited Pro Apps**: Some pro apps still run better on a laptop.

## Conclusion

The iPad Pro M4 is a remarkable piece of technology that sets a new standard for tablets. Its blend of performance, design, and features makes it the ultimate dream machine for geeks and tech enthusiasts. If you're in the market for a new tablet and can afford the hefty price tag, the iPad Pro M4 is worth every penny.
