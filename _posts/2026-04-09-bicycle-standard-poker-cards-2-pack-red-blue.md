## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity
To maximize lifespan, treat these decks with care. A few simple practices can extend their life more than you might expect:

- Store them in a cool, dry place out of direct sunlight. Prolonged UV exposure can fade backs or cause warping over a long period.
- Keep them away from moisture. Spilled drinks happen; the faster you dry them, the less risk of warping or staining.
- Use a gentle shuffle. You don’t need to perform a breakneck riffle every time. A mindful, even shuffle keeps the corners intact and reduces edge wear.
- Consider a deck wallet or case if you travel with your cards. A simple sleeve can dramatically reduce edge damage when thrown into a bag or backpack.

If you treat your cards like a small instrument (guitar pickers, we see you), you’ll find the Bicycle deck ages gracefully with continued use. They don’t become brittle after five minutes of sloppy handling; instead, they become a well-worn companion that shouts, “We’ve played a thousand hands and we’re ready for a thousand more.”
