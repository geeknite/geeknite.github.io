## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is straightforward. Players choose their characters and equip them with starting items. The game board is laid out with tiles representing different areas of Raccoon City, and the scenario cards are prepared. The objective for the players is to complete their mission objectives without being overwhelmed by the undead hordes.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions like moving, attacking, and using items.
2. **Reaction Phase**: Zombies and other creatures react to the players' actions and move accordingly.
3. **End Phase**: Resolve any end-of-turn effects and prepare for the next round.

### Survival and Strategy

The game shines in its strategic depth. Players must carefully plan their moves, manage their resources, and think several steps ahead to outwit the zombies. Cooperation is key, as players will need to support each other to survive the increasingly difficult scenarios.
