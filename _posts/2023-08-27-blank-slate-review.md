## What is Blank Slate?

Blank Slate is a party game designed for 3-8 players. The premise is simple: fill in the blank in a word prompt and try to match your answer with others. Sounds easy, right? Well, it can be surprisingly tricky when everyone has their own unique way of thinking. The objective is to earn points by matching answers with other players, but beware, the most obvious answer might not always be the best choice!

## How to Play

1. **Setup**: Each player receives a dry-erase board and a marker.
2. **Draw a Card**: A player draws a card with a word prompt (e.g., "___ Car" or "Hot ___").
3. **Write Your Answer**: Players secretly write a word to complete the phrase.
4. **Reveal Answers**: All players reveal their answers simultaneously.
5. **Score Points**: Points are awarded for matching answers. If you match with one other player, you both score 3 points. Matching with multiple players nets 1 point each.
6. **Continue**: Draw a new card and repeat until the end of the game.
