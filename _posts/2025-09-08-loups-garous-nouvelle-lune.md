## The Core Game

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of the Nouvelle Lune expansion, let’s take a quick refresher on what makes Loups-Garous de Thiercelieux such a beloved game. Loups-Garous is a fast-paced social deduction game where players take on secret roles as villagers and werewolves. The objective? Figure out who the werewolves are before they eat everyone—or if you're a werewolf, devour the villagers without getting caught.

Each player is assigned a role, and the game progresses through a series of days and nights. During the night, werewolves secretly choose a victim, while special roles like the Seer can peek at other players' identities. During the day, everyone discusses and votes to eliminate a suspect, trying to figure out who the werewolves are.

**Pro Tip:** If you're a villager, trust your gut—but don't be too quick to accuse, or you might get yourself voted off! And if you're a werewolf, play it cool and blend in. Being too aggressive or too passive can be a dead giveaway.

## Expansions and Compatibility

Loups-Garous: Nouvelle Lune is fully compatible with the previous expansions, **Village** and **Characters**. This means you can mix and match elements from all expansions to create your ultimate werewolf experience.

- **Village**: Introduces new villagers and tactics, allowing up to twelve players to join the game. It also adds more strategic depth with new villager abilities.
- **Characters**: Adds character-specific abilities that can dramatically change the outcome of the game. Want to add a healer to your village? Go for it!

**Criticism Alert:** While the compatibility is a great feature, combining all expansions can make the game feel a bit overwhelming for new players. It’s like trying to learn how to play chess while juggling flaming torches.
