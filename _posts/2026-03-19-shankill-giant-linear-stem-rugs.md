### Care and Maintenance
Maintenance is where many rugs lose their charm, because life happens and life sometimes spills. The Shankill rug thrives on routine vacuuming with a gentle setting to prevent fiber pull. For spills, act fast but calmly—blot with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing, and consider a gentle waterless cleaning product if needed. For more serious cleaning, schedule a professional service; the rug’s construction tolerates deeper cleansing when performed by trained hands. If you have pets, you will appreciate the wool’s natural resilience, while the fiber blend helps prevent rapid fraying at the stem lines.

### Practical Sizing Tips
Measure your room and use a rug size calculator in your head if you must. The rule of thumb is to leave a border of exposed floor around the rug, typically about 6–12 inches in smaller rooms and 18–24 inches in larger spaces. If you have a seating group, the rug should extend beyond the front legs of the sofa and chairs. The linear stems will look dramatic whether the rug fits perfectly or slightly overlaps the furniture, but the more cohesive your layout, the more confident your interior will feel.
