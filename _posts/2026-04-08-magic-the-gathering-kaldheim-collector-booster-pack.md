### Evaluating Value

- Art value: Some foil or borderless cards become long-term display pieces that outlast casual play staples. For people who care about aesthetics, there is value here beyond the card’s gameplay utility.
- Financial value: The market for foil variants can swing. A card might be expensive in foil today, cheaper tomorrow as print runs stabilize, and expensive again in a few years as nostalgia cycles. Predicting the exact value is a parlor game you should play carefully and with a high tolerance for risk.
- Play value: In most formats, the foil versions of cards in Collector Boosters do not replace the need for standard functional copies. They are often decorative or used in premium decks, but they do not magically win you a tournament by themselves.
