## What is the Crucial T705?

The T705 is Crucial’s Gen5 NVMe drive, designed to slot into standard M.2 slots on modern motherboards. It’s built for speed but also designed to be reliable enough to survive your lunchtime coffee spill and the occasional system reboot at 2 a.m. Yes, those are the real life stresses. The T705 sits squarely in the category of premium consumer storage that doesn’t scream for fanboys, but you’ll still hear a chorus of fans in your chassis when you hit the 4K video editing render.

## Specs and Features

- Interface: PCIe 5.0 x4
- Form factor: M.2 2280
- Capacity: 4 TB
- Sequential read: up to 12,000 MB/s
- Sequential write: up to 11,000 MB/s
- Random read/write IOPS: up to 1,500,000 / 1,400,000
- Endurance: robust for consumer-grade use; warranty: 5 years
- Encryption: AES-256 hardware-based (some models)
- Thermal management: optional heat spreader; heat sink available
