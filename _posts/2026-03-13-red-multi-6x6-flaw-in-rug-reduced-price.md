### Impact on cleaning and maintenance
Flaws often invite extra maintenance questions. A seam misalignment could catch on a vacuum edge, depending on your vacuum model. If the corner is slightly uneven, you might see a small buoyant effect under a heavy piece of furniture where the rug meets the floor. Our recommendation is to use a high quality rug pad to stabilize the base and reduce movement. Another step is to rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly and prevent a single high-traffic area from becoming the telltale mark of where the kids practice their cartwheels and where the dog claims the prime sunbeam.

## Practicality: Size, Placement, and Maintenance
### Room fit and layout ideas
A 6 by 6 rug is versatile. It works well under a coffee table in a small to mid-size living room, under a dining table with six chairs if space allows, or in a cozy listening nook with a couple of lounge chairs. The size is large enough to anchor furniture groupings yet not so large that it dominates the room. When placed correctly, it creates a sense of cohesion. When placed poorly, it can make the room feel disjointed and the furniture look like it wandered in from elsewhere.
### Placement do and donts
Do place the rug so that the front legs of sofas and chairs rest on the rug to unify seating areas. Dont center the rug in a way that leaves all four legs off the rug unless your goal is a floating furniture arrangement. Avoid placing the rug under a table that sits on a rug with a seam that meets a chair leg; this can create a tripping hazard or a visual mismatch at foot level. The goal is to create a harmony that is easy on the eyes and not a puzzle that requires a map to navigate.
### Cleaning and care routine
We recommend a weekly vacuum with a beater bar setting on low or a standard suction setting for delicate fibers. Address spills quickly with blotting techniques rather than rubbing, and use a mild cleaning solution recommended by rug care guides. For tougher stains, test a spot cleaner in a hidden corner first. If color bleed occurs, act fast and rinse with water and blot. Regular rotation helps even out wear and maintain a balanced look across the surface.
