## Durability and care tips

Bicycle stock decks aren’t built like tank armor, but they are durable enough for a lot of play with normal handling. The key to longevity is keeping the cards dry and clean. Wipe away fingerprints with a dry microfiber cloth if necessary, and store the decks in a cool, dry place where humidity won’t turn the linen finish into a wrinkled mess. If you notice curling or edge wear after heavy use, it’s a sign you’re playing a lot and that your deck got its fair share of action. Two decks, as this pack provides, also allows you to rotate sets when one deck goes to the hospital for a rest or when you want to run a multi-table event where you need backups.

Avoid moisture and direct heat; these are the rare factors that can warp the stock or fade the back design over time. And if a card does take a spill, lay it flat to dry and then reintroduce it to the rest of the deck gradually to prevent warping from abrupt air exposure. Pro-tip: for long-term storage, consider a small card case or a zip pouch to keep dust and humidity at bay between game nights.
