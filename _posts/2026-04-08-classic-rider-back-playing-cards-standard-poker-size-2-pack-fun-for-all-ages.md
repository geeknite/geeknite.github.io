## Durability and care tips
Bicycle stock decks aren’t built like armored tanks, but they’re durable enough for a lot of play with normal handling. The key to longevity is keeping the cards dry and clean. Wipe fingerprints with a dry microfiber cloth if necessary, and store the decks in a cool, dry place where humidity won’t turn the linen finish into a wrinkled mess. If you notice curling or edge wear after heavy use, it’s a sign you’re playing a lot and your deck is getting its fair share of action. Two decks, as this pack provides, allow you to rotate sets when one deck goes to “card haven” for a rest or when you plan a multi-table event.

Avoid moisture and direct heat; these are the rare factors that can warp the stock or fade the back design over time. If a card takes a spill, lay it flat to dry and then reintroduce it to the rest of the deck gradually to prevent warping from abrupt air exposure. Pro-tip: for long-term storage, consider a small card case or a zip pouch to keep dust and humidity at bay between game nights.
