If you do, you get a comedic but non-lethal penalty that turns into the kind of dramatic moment you can brag about at work the next day.

The rules use a familiar shorthand that will feel welcoming to players who know the original game and not terrifying to first timers. Exploding Kittens is a game where you win by reading the room and misdirecting your friends. The new edition adds a few extra card types that spice up the late game and give players a few more opportunities to twist the knife with a clever play.

In practice, the best players are the ones who can keep track of what has been played, what defuses are left, and what their friends would consider a reasonable sacrifice to avoid catastrophe. It is not about min-maxing the deck to perfection; it is about turning a simple draw into a moment that makes the table roar with laughter or groan in delightful disbelief.
