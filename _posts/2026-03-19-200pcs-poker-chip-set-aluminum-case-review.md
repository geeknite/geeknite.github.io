## Care and maintenance tips

To keep your chips looking good and the case functioning as a reliable travel companion, follow a few simple maintenance guidelines. A little care goes a long way in preserving the tactile feel and the visual appeal of your kit.

### Cleaning the chips
Wipe chips with a soft microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints and smudges. If chips accumulate a sticky residue from snacks or drinks, use a mild soap and warm water on a soft cloth. Do not soak the chips and avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the edge designs or color coatings. After cleaning, dry them thoroughly to prevent moisture from seeping into the core material.

### Caring for the case
Wipe the aluminum case with a microfiber cloth. If there are stubborn fingerprints or smudges, use a gentle metal cleaner or a mixture of warm water and mild detergent. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could scratch the finish. If you have a snap-latch mechanism, check it occasionally to ensure it closes snugly. If you travel frequently, consider wrapping the case in a soft cloth or storing it in a protective bag to guard against dings during transport.

### Storage and climate considerations
A dry, cool environment prolongs the life of chips and card stock. Keep away from direct sunlight, which can fade color layers, and avoid leaving the kit in a hot car where the chips could warp slightly or the foam padding could degrade. If possible, store the kit in a dedicated poker cabinet or shelf to reduce the risk of misplacing pieces in the couch cushions during a marathon game night.
