## Design and Build Quality

The iPhone 16 Pro continues Apple's tradition of combining sleek design with premium materials. This beauty sports a titanium frame that not only looks stunning but also feels indestructible. The ceramic shield on both the front and back ensures your phone remains scratch-free, even if you decide to have a wrestling match with a porcupine.

![iPhone 16 Pro Design](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/design__c2u6xn6rvs2u_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}iphone-16-pro-design)

### Ports and Connectivity

Apple has finally heard our pleas and reintroduced the much-missed headphone jack! Just kidding. But the iPhone 16 Pro does come with a new and improved USB-C port, making it easier to connect and charge universally.

## Display and Audio

![iPhone 16 Pro Display](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}iphone-16-pro-display)

The iPhone 16 Pro boasts a breathtaking 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology. Colors are vibrant, blacks are deeper than my existential crises, and the 120Hz refresh rate makes scrolling smoother than a baby's bottom.

The audio system is equally impressive, with spatial audio and Dolby Atmos support, turning your phone into a mini home theater. Whether you're binging the latest sci-fi series or jamming to your favorite tunes, the sound quality will blow you away.

## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by A18 Bionic Chip

![A18 Bionic Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/a18__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}a18-bionic-chip)

At the heart of the iPhone 16 Pro is the revolutionary A18 Bionic chip. With a 10-core CPU, 12-core GPU, and 20-core Neural Engine, this powerhouse can handle everything from gaming to augmented reality with ease. It's so fast, it might just finish your sentences for you.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 24 hours of battery life, and in our tests, it didn't disappoint. Whether you're snapping photos, streaming videos, or playing the latest games, the iPhone 16 Pro will keep you powered all day and night.

## Camera System

![iPhone 16 Pro Camera](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/camera__f5dhn0m5h16m_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}iphone-16-pro-camera)

The camera system on the iPhone 16 Pro is nothing short of magical. The triple-lens setup includes a 108MP main sensor, a 48MP ultra-wide lens, and a 12MP telephoto lens with 10x optical zoom. Night mode, Deep Fusion, and Smart HDR 4 ensure that your photos are Instagram-ready even in challenging lighting conditions.

### ProRAW and ProRes

For the photography and videography enthusiasts, the iPhone 16 Pro offers ProRAW and ProRes capabilities, allowing for greater control and higher quality in your shots and videos. Move over, Spielberg!

## Software and Features

![iOS 20](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/ios__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ios-20)

### iOS 20

The iPhone 16 Pro comes with iOS 20, packed with new features like Focus mode, redesigned widgets, and enhanced privacy controls. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the iPhone experience so magical.

### Face ID and Security

Face ID has been upgraded with faster recognition and broader angles. And for those who miss Touch ID, Apple has introduced an under-display fingerprint sensor. Double the security, double the fun!

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **All-Day Battery Life**: No more carrying around chargers like a nomad.
