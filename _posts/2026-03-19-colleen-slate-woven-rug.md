## Durability, Care, and Longevity

### Maintenance Routine
- Vacuum weekly with a brushless attachment to prevent fiber pull.
- Blot spills immediately; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber distortion.
- For stubborn stains, use a mild detergent solution and a soft brush.
- Use a rug pad to enhance durability and reduce wear in high-traffic areas.
- Rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly.

### Spot Treatments and Cleaning Tips
If you’re dealing with everyday stains (coffee, wine, or mysterious toddler ink), the Colleen Slate responds well to standard spot-treatment methods. For tricky stains, test your cleaner on an inconspicuous area first to ensure there’s no color transfer or texture change. The goal is to preserve the rug’s look without resorting to heavy solvents. For odor management, sprinkle baking soda in small amounts and vacuum after a few hours. The rug is built for resilience, but it’s not invincible; treat it with consistent care, and it will reward you with years of use.

### Longevity Considerations
With proper care, the Colleen Slate Woven Rug can appear affected by years of everyday life.
