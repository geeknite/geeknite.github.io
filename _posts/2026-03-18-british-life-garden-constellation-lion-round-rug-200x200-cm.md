## FAQs
### Is this rug suitable for direct sunlight outdoors daily?
Yes, the rug is designed for outdoor life and handles sun fairly well, but expect some fading over many seasons if it sits in direct sun all day. Rotating it occasionally helps keep color even.
### Can it be used indoors as a centerpiece rug?
Absolutely. The 200 cm diameter makes it a striking centerpiece for a large living room or sunroom. It may dominate a small space, but for open floor plans it can pull the room together with a bit of space around it.
### How do I clean pet accidents on outdoor rugs?
Blot the area first to remove as much liquid as possible. Clean with a mild detergent and warm water, then rinse and blot dry. Avoid harsh solvents that can damage fibers. A quick clean after each incident keeps odors at bay and preserves color.
