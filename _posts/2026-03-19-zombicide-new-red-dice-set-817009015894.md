## Care, storage, and travel
The dice are robust but not invincible. When you travel, a padded dice tray or a small, soft case keeps them from being tromped by car keys and stray rulebooks. If you need to clean them, a damp cloth with a touch of mild soap works fine; avoid harsh solvents that may alter the color. For long-term storage, keep them away from direct sunlight to preserve the vivid red hue—nobody wants retro-orange dice.

## Compatibility and care (expanded)
The New Red Dice Set is fully compatible with standard base-game dice and maps. They don’t come with special storage so if you’re traveling to your friend’s place, consider a dedicated dice tray to prevent scuffs and chipping. A simple, soft case will do. And yes, you should probably wash them with mild soap and water if you’ve accidently used them as “mini swords” during a dramatic moment.

Care tips:

- Store away from children if you’re worried about a sudden need for extra adrenaline.
