### Safety tips for UK backyard trampolines
- Check the netting and padding before every session.
- Use a safe landing area free of obstacles.
- Install on level ground or use packers to adjust the frame height.
- Avoid bouncing during windy weather or after heavy rainfall when the ground is slick.
- Stop bouncing if you feel dizzy or anything unusual happens.

## Maintenance and care
Keeping your premium trampoline in top condition is a seasonal ritual. Clean the frame and springs with mild soap and water to remove dirt, clinging leaves, and the occasional bird droppings. Dry thoroughly to prevent rust. Inspect springs for wear and replace them when necessary. Check the enclosure seams for tears and repair promptly to keep the jumping party intact.
