## Cleaning and Maintenance
This rug is designed for easy care, which appeals to busy folks who want to maximize leisure time rather than labor hours.

### Routine Care
- Regularly shake out debris to prevent buildup of leaves and grit.
- Vacuum with a brush attachment on low suction to avoid fuzzing the fibers.
- Hose it down or wipe with a damp cloth for spot cleaning. Let air dry completely.

### Stain Removal
- For most stains, a solution of mild detergent and water works well.
- For stubborn spots, use a soft brush and circular motion. Rinse thoroughly and avoid saturating the rug with water.

### Weather Considerations
In regions with heavy rain or snow, it helps to pull the rug up during extreme weather to avoid long-term moisture exposure. If you must leave it outside, ensure the rug dries quickly after wet spells and rotate it periodically to prevent uneven fading.

## Installation and Placement Tips
- Underlay matters: a non-slip pad can improve safety on slick decks. If your surface isn’t perfectly flat, consider a thin, flexible underlay to level out high spots.
- Align with existing furniture: center the rug under the main seating arrangement to visually anchor the space. A touch of overhang around edges often looks more polished.
- Temperature and storage: roll the rug for storage and avoid folding to prevent creases that are hard to remove later.
- Seasonal rotation: if you own more than one outdoor rug, try rotating them to distribute sun exposure evenly and prevent one rug from aging faster than the other.
