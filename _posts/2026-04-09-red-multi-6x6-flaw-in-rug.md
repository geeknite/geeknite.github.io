## Maintenance tips to extend the life of your bold carpet
- Vacuum regularly to keep the pile fluffy and to minimize dirt embedding in the fibers. A vacuum with adjustable height helps prevent snagging.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear, especially in high-traffic zones like entryways or gaming corners.
- Address spills promptly with blotting rather than rubbing. The less you rub, the less likely you are to push color around or fray edges.
- Use a rug pad or underlay to improve grip and provide extra cushioning, which is particularly nice for late-night chair spins and accidental dance moves.
- Consider a quick professional cleaning every couple of years if you want to preserve the texture and the punchy color for longer.
