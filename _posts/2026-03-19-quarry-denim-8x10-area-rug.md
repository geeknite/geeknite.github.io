## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity

### Routine Cleaning

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers.
- Spot-clean spills promptly with a mild detergent and water; blot, don’t rub.
- Rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly.
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and extend life.

### Deep Cleaning Considerations

For a deeper clean, many households opt for professional cleaning on a semi-annual basis, especially in high-traffic rooms. If you prefer DIY, a light steam-clean can be effective, but ensure the rug dries completely to prevent mildew. The key is to avoid saturating the backing and to follow the manufacturer’s guidelines for your specific fiber blend. ### Material Tradeoffs: Nylon vs Polypropylene vs Wool

- Polypropylene (PP) and its blends are stain-resistant, fade-resistant, and easier to clean; great for high-traffic areas.
- Nylon offers higher resilience and softness but can be pricier and may show wear patterns slightly more easily in some light conditions.
- Wool brings luxury and warmth but requires more maintenance and a higher price point.

Quarry Denim leans toward a PP-based construction, balancing durability and cost, with wool-like color depth achieved through dyeing processes and texture.
