## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity
### Routine Cleaning
- Vacuum regularly on a gentle setting to avoid fiber pull.
- Blot spills promptly with a mild detergent and water; avoid rubbing.
- Rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly.
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and to extend life.

### Deep Cleaning Considerations
For a deeper clean, many households opt for professional cleaning semi-annually, especially high-traffic rooms. If you DIY, a light steam-clean can work, but ensure thorough drying to prevent mildew. Avoid saturating the backing and follow manufacturer guidelines for your fiber blend.
