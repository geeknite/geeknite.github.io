## Care and Maintenance: Keeping the Drama in Check

Your rug’s care regimen is less about ritual and more about consistent habits:
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers that already look like they’ve had a rough day.
- Address spills quickly (blot, don’t rub). This helps the colors stay vibrant and the “flaw” from becoming a feature of coffee-stain geometry.
- Rotate every few months to distribute wear evenly. This helps keep the 6x6 square looking as symmetrical as possible, which is the noble goal of any room that wants to look intentional rather than accidental.
- Consider a rug pad to reduce slippage and to give your knees fewer reasons to protest when you stand up after a long Netflix binge.

For deeper care tips, you can explore standard rug-care articles from major retailers, but remember: the true secret to rug longevity is consistent care, a little patience, and the occasional nap you sneak while the rug does your eyes a favor by softening the lighting of the room.
