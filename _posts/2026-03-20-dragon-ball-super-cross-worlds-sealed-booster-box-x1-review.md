## Box Opening Techniques and Practical Tips
### How to maximize value per pack
- Set a packing rhythm: count to 6 after every third pack to structure your expectations.
- Separate pulls into piles by rarity or color coding to see progress at a glance.
- Track foil vs non-foil distribution and note which paths yield the most value for your collection goals.
- If you pull a particularly desirable card, consider immediate sleeving and top-loading to protect it during further play sessions or sale attempts.

### Handling and storage tips
Store cards in a cool, dry place to preserve foils and avoid warping. Use soft sleeves for individual cards and a rigid top loader for high-value pulls. A small, climate-controlled storage solution can extend card life and preserve the vivid colors that designers worked hard to achieve.
