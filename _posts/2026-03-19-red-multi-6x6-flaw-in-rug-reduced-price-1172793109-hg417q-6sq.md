## Care, Cleaning, and Longevity: Keeping the Bold Quiet Occasionally

### Everyday Maintenance
Regular vacuuming is essential for any rug, but for a rug with character, you’ll want to avoid aggressive suction that might exaggerate the flaw’s “quirk.” Use a gentle setting and a vacuum without a beater bar if possible. Blot stains gently with a clean cloth; avoid harsh chemicals that could turn the rug into a modern art piece titled “What Not to Do to Red Fleece.”

### Deep Cleaning: When the Coffee Monster Attacks
Oil-based stains, ink, or experimental sauces may require a targeted approach. For most home scenarios, a mild detergent solution and a swift pat-down will do. If you’re fancy, you can spin up a rug cleaning night with friends as a social event—remember to bring snacks and a playlist that doesn’t make the stain feel judged.

### Longevity and Fabric Considerations
Pile height matters for foot comfort. A 6x6 rug typically offers a plush feel without becoming a literal marshland for your slippers. This one’s a balanced choice: not too high to swallow your furniture, not too flat to feel like a bathroom mat. Pattern integrity, however, will depend on where you place it and how often you walk across it in socks that may or may not be clean.
