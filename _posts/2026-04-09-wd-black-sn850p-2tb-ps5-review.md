## Installation in PS5: step-by-step and tips

Installing the SN850P 2TB in PS5 is a straightforward process, but a few tips can save time and avoid a few oops moments:

1. Power down the PS5 completely and unplug it from the wall. Do not attempt to install while it is still in rest mode.
2. Remove the sector of the side panel to access the expansion slot. Depending on your model, you may need to gently pry open the panel with a card or a non-mcratch tool.
3. Remove the existing expansion bay bracket if you have one attached, or prepare the drive in the slot with the built-in heatsink oriented toward the PS5 interior as designed by WD.
4. Align the drive with the notch and slide it in at a slight angle, then secure it with the retention screw provided by Sony in the slot or the screw on the WD drive if applicable. The aim is to have the heatsink seated flush against the inner wall of the PS5.
5. Reattach the panel and power on the PS5. The console should recognize the new drive automatically and prompt you to format the drive. Follow the on-screen steps to format and name the drive.
6. Once formatted, you can move games onto the new SSD or keep your PS5 games on the internal 825 GB base if you prefer. The system will show the storage distribution clearly in the settings menu.

A small note on compatibility: if you have a non heatsink version or a different generation, some setups will require a separate heatsink. The SN850P 2TB with heatsink is designed to be a plug-and-play PS5 upgrade, reducing the risk of incompatibility with the cooling requirements.
