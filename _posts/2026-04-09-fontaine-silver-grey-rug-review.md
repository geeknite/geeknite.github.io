## How to Spot a Quality Rug Like Fontaine in a Sea of Options
If you are shopping in a real store or online, here are a few quick cues to gauge quality without becoming a rug scientist:
- Look for a tight, dense pile with consistent height across the surface.
- Inspect the edges: stitched seams should be even and not curling.
- Check the backing for durability; it should be firmly attached and not easily separate from the pile.
- Run a quick test with a sample by gently tugging a corner; good rugs resist unraveling or heavy shedding.
