## Weather and Maintenance: How to Keep It Looking Fresh
Outdoor furniture is a long-term relationship with the elements. The rattan set we tested handles rain and sun the way a veteran superhero handles a routine mission: with steady, predictable moves and a few protective accessories.

- Cleaning: Use a mild soap, warm water, and a soft brush. Do not submerge the cushions into a washing machine. They’ll thank you later.
- Storage: If you’ve got harsh winters, consider storing cushions indoors to prevent mildew. If you don’t, remove cushions during heavy rain and wipe down the frames afterward.
- Protection: If you want to maximize the lifespan, use outdoor furniture covers or place the set under a pergola. The goal is to keep the weave looking like-new rather than characterful.

We tested a light shower, and the set survived without scuffing or warping. The round dining table, with its tempered glass top, held up well to water droplets and the occasional clumsy wine spill. Pro tip: use a coasters ritual, not the untouch glass with mysterious coating method.

## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Your Set Looking Sharp
The beauty of rattan furniture lies in its timeless look, but maintaining that look requires a routine.

- Clean regularly: Wipe with a damp cloth to remove dust and debris. For stains, a gentle soap solution works wonders.
- Cushion care: Remove and wash cushion covers as recommended by the label. Dry thoroughly before reattaching to avoid mold. A quick tip: rotate cushions to keep wear even.
- Table care: Clean the glass with a glass cleaner for that crystal-clear shine. For the weave, use a soft brush or cloth to remove dirt trapped in the weave.
- Weather protection: If you live in a region with heavy rainfall or snow, invest in a weather-proof cover or store cushions indoors when not in use.

By following these steps, your 4-seater set can remain a stylish centerpiece for years to come.
