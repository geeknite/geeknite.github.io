## Gameplay Experience

### Setup and rules quick
If you know the standard Exploding Kittens rules, you basically know this edition. The setup remains simple: shuffle, deal a hand, toss the rest of the deck on the center, and mention the rule about not triggering the titular catastrophe unless someone has the right defuse card. The NSFW Edition adds a handful of new cards that tilt the odds and a few that alter how a player can react to a looming explosion. The main mechanic — draw, or defuse or counter — remains the spine of the game. The difference is a higher tempo and more moments where the table erupts in shared laughter or embarrassed groans.

Flow of rounds

Each round unfolds with players taking turns to draw cards and play action cards. Defuse cards, the classic safety nets, remain essential. In this edition a few cards allow players to cheat fate in more humorous ways, and some cards have effects that escalate the drama quickly. The pacing can be brisk, especially with a loud group that loves to heckle the deck when the action stacks up. The balance between risk and reward feels just right for a party setting; not too long, not too short, and always ready for a dramatic reveal at the perfect moment.

Round pacing tips

- Keep the pace snappy by keeping the deck face down until it is time to draw. 
- Use the loudest voice you can summon when the Exploding Kitten is on the verge of popping. 
- If the room grows tense, switch to light commentary and keep the jokes quick.
