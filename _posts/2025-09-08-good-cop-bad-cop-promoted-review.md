## The Basics: What’s the Game About?

Good Cop Bad Cop: Promoted is a social deduction game where players take on the roles of cops with hidden loyalties. Some are honest officers, while others are corrupt. And then there’s the Kingpin and the Agent, adding more complexity. Your goal? Discover who’s who and lead your team to victory—or hilariously fail in the attempt.

### Gameplay Mechanics

**Setup**: Each player receives three “Integrity” cards which determine their loyalty. Reveal just one, and the guessing game begins.

**Rounds**: Players take turns investigating others, equipping weapons, and making accusations. The tension builds as suspicions rise and alliances form—and crumble.

**Victory Conditions**: The game ends when the Kingpin or Agent is eliminated, or when all players on one side are taken out.

## Strategy and Tactics

The key to success in Good Cop Bad Cop: Promoted is balancing investigation and deception. Are you a good cop trying to root out corruption, or a bad cop trying to sow chaos? Here are some tips to keep you ahead of the game:

- **Investigate Wisely**: Use your actions to gain crucial information, but don’t be too obvious about your intentions.
- **Bluff**: Sometimes, pretending to be something you’re not can yield hilarious and effective results.
- **Equip Strategically**: The equipment cards can turn the tide. Choose wisely and keep your cards close to your chest.

## Fun Factor and Replayability

One of the best aspects of Good Cop Bad Cop: Promoted is its high replay value. With different roles and strategies to explore, each game feels fresh and exciting. The social dynamics and the ever-present element of surprise keep players coming back for more.

**Cons**:
