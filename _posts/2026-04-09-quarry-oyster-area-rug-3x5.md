### Fiber and weave
Most Quarry Oyster area rugs in this class lean on synthetic fibers like polypropylene or polymer blends. ## Maintenance and Cleaning

### Daily care
Vacuum regularly with a standard vacuum; avoid high suction that could pull fibers. Rotate every few months to avoid uneven wear and lines on the rug from heavy traffic.

### Spills and stains
Treat spills quickly with blotting and a mild detergent solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that could discolor fibers. For stubborn stains, a spot cleaning with a carpet safe cleaner usually does the trick.
