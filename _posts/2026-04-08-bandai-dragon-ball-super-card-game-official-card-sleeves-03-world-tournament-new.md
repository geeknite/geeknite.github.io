## H2 Care, Cleaning, and Longevity Tips

Even the best sleeves benefit from occasional maintenance. Here are a few practical tips to extend their life:

- Keep away from direct sunlight: The colors are vibrant, but prolonged sun exposure can fade the artwork. Do not leave your deck sleeve display on a windowsill while you nap after a long match.
- Remove dust carefully: A soft microfiber cloth or a gentle brush can remove dust. Avoid abrasive materials that can scratch or dull the finish.
- Avoid water contact: Sleeves are not waterproof; if your water bottle explodes during a tense moment, you will want to replace affected sleeves rather than attempt a DIY fix.
- Store properly: When not in use, keep your sleeves in a clean container, away from heat sources. A simple cardboard box or a plastic storage bin works fine as long as it’s dry and cool.

Caring for your sleeves is not glamorous, but it pays off: your World Tournament 03 sleeves will stay crisp and protective for many matches to come, even after you have finally unlocked that character you have been grinding for in the daily login rewards.
