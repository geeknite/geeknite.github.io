## Assembly Tips and Care Cheats: Quick Win Tips

- Sort and group hardware before starting. It’s less dramatic than a scavenger hunt and more efficient in the long run.
- Place the table and sections on a soft surface (like a rug or cardboard) while you assemble to avoid scratching the frames.
- Use the included Allen wrench or a proper driver to avoid stripping screws. Don’t force anything—if it doesn’t fit, double-check the alignment.
- After assembly, wipe down the surfaces to remove any packaging residue. This helps you identify any cosmetic issues and ensures everything sits properly from the first sunny day.
- For cushions, store them in a dry, ventilated area when not in use for extended periods. This reduces mold risk and extends the life of the fabric.
