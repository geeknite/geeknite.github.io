## What is Utopia: Vision d'un Monde Meilleur?

Utopia: Vision d'un Monde Meilleur is a game set in a utopian world where players strive to create their perfect society. The set includes intricately designed miniatures that represent various characters and elements within this idealistic universe. With stunning details and high-quality craftsmanship, these miniatures are not just game pieces; they're miniature works of art.

## Components

The Utopia miniatures set comes with:

- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted figures representing characters and elements from the game. Seriously, these miniatures are so detailed that you might want to display them on your mantle and pretend they're part of an avant-garde art installation.
- **Cards**: Each miniature comes with a card detailing its abilities and role within the game.

- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, resources, and various effects during the game.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Utopia: Vision d'un Monde Meilleur is like preparing for an elaborate art exhibition. Lay out the game board, distribute the cards and miniatures, and make sure everything is perfectly arranged. It requires precision, patience, and perhaps a cup of calming tea.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their miniature's abilities. You can move, build, negotiate, and occasionally engage in utopian shenanigans. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your fellow players to create the perfect society.

### Combat

Combat in Utopia is a blend of strategy and luck. Each character has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic negotiation just to have the other player flip the table? Welcome to Utopia, where even peaceful societies have their drama.
