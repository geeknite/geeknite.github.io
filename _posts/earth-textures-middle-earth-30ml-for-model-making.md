## Application Tips

### Prepping Your Surface

Ensure the surface is clean and free of debris. You wouldn't want orcs ruining your masterpiece, would you?

### Using the Right Tools

For best results, use a medium-sized brush or a spatula. Avoid using your fingers unless you want to look like you've been digging through Mordor.

### Layering

Apply in thin layers for a more natural look. Thick layers might make your model look like it was hit by the Ents.

### Sealing

Use a clear sealant to protect your textures from wear and tear. You wouldn't want Saruman messing with your work.
