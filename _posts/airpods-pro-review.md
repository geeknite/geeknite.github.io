One of the standout features of the AirPods Pro is the **Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)**. Imagine you're on a plane, and the person next to you is snoring louder than a lawnmower. Voice commands? Check. Just say, "Hey Siri," and your virtual assistant is ready to take orders. Want to change the song, adjust the volume, or check the weather? Siri's got your back. The force sensor on the stem allows you to control music playback, answer calls, and switch between ANC and Transparency Mode with a simple squeeze. It's like magic, but real.

## Battery Life

### All-Day Listening

Apple claims up to **4.5 hours of listening time** with ANC on, and in our tests, they didn't disappoint. The wireless charging case extends the battery life to 24 hours, so you can keep the music going all day and night. Charging is a breeze with the included Lightning to USB-C cable, or just plop them on a wireless charger if you're too lazy to plug in.
