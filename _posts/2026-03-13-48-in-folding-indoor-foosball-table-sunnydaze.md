- Clean the surface after every use with a soft damp cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners that may dull the finish. A light wipe-down is enough to keep fingerprints at bay and maintain the aesthetic.
- Lubricate the rod joints sparingly. A tiny amount of silicone-based spray or light machine oil on the rod bearings can improve glide, but don’t go overboard. Too much lubricant can attract dust and debris that reduce the quality of play.

## Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping the Party Alive
A foosball table is a commitment to chaos: you want it to be fun, not a maintenance nightmare. The Sunnydaze 48 in folding model is designed with that reality in mind.
