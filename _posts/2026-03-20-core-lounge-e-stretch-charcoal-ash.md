## Setup and Maintenance: From Box to Cozy Without Losing Your Mind

Assembly is straightforward, though not exactly “pop the pieces together with your coffee and swagger.” You’ll need basic tools (included) and about 30–60 minutes to assemble depending on how ambitious your layout is. The most important part of setup isn’t the manual—it’s aligning modules so that gaps are minimal and you don’t end up with a “slightly misaligned” seam that gnaws at your inner sense of order. We recommend laying everything out and dry-fitting before you start tightening screws. Cushions are designed for easy removal and cleaning. The fabric resists staining reasonably well, but it’s not a miracle. The key is to treat it with ordinary care rather than treating it like a vintage car that you’ll never drive in the rain. For pet owners, there’s a minor caveat: pet hair loves this fabric about as much as your cat loves pouncing on the remote control. Regular maintenance is simple: vacuum the seams every couple of weeks, rotate cushions to keep wear even, and check screws for tightness every month.
