Care and Maintenance: Keeping the Unbound in Prime Form

Maintenance is where the practical side of rug ownership shines through. Here are the best practices we recommend for keeping the Unbound Smoke Gray ribbed rug looking shipshape:

- Regular vacuuming: A weekly pass with a standard vacuum using the beater bar turned off or on a low setting helps prevent dirt from embedding itself in the fibers.
- Spot cleaning: For common spills, blot—do not rub—with a clean cloth, then use a mild detergent solution to lift stains. Rinse the area with clean water and blot dry.
- Deep cleaning: Every few months, you can wash the rug with a mild soap solution and a soft brush; ensure the rug is fully dry before placing it back to avoid mold. If you’re on a balcony or patio, you may want to do this outdoors to avoid mess indoors.
- Storage: If you’re storing the rug for a season, roll it rather than folding to prevent creases. Store in a dry location to prevent mold and mildew when not in use.
