### Cleaning and care routine in practice
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to remove loose fibers and avoid abrasion to the weave.
- If a spill occurs, blot it immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing as that can push the stain deeper or distort the pile.
- For stubborn stains, a mild detergent solution can help, followed by blotting to remove moisture.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and prevent hot spots from forming under heavy furniture.
- Consider professional cleaning every 12–24 months depending on traffic and household pets.
