## Practical Advice for Collectors
- Storage and handling: The old-school blister-pack era cards often benefit from acid-free sleeves and a proper binder. Keep them out of direct sunlight to preserve the color balance and avoid fading.

- Documentation: Retain any packaging or provenance if you have it. Even a simple, hand-written note about where you found the cards can add to the narrative value when reselling later.
