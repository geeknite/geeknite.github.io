## H2: Rules and setup: how hard can it be to become the master of chaos?
### H3: Setup basics
Setting up NSFW Edition is roughly identical to the standard game: shuffle the Defuse deck, deal a certain number of cards to each player, place the bomb token somewhere on the table, and ensure everyone is aware that the kittens are plotting mayhem. The key difference is the card text and the additional add-ons that change when you can pull a card and what you’re allowed to say in a family-friendly environment after the first three drinks have been poured.

### H3: Core rules in plain geek-speak
- On your turn, you draw cards and may play action cards to alter the play state, manipulate other players, or defend against potential detonations. 
- The Exploding Kitten cards are your primary hazard; draw one and you’re one step closer to the spicy, itchy truth of perpetual cat chaos—but you can Defuse it if you’ve got the right card. 
- Defuse cards are the safety rails of this ride; they prevent the explosion and grant you another chance to narrate your life choices with grand flair.
- There are wild cards that add extra turns, force players to do ridiculous tasks, or swap hands—thus ensuring that your strategy isn’t your only weapon; your ability to talk your way through chaos is equally relevant.

### H3: Quick tips for success
- Don’t overthink the art. The NSFW edition rewards bold moves and ready-made punchlines more than intricate long-term strategy. If you’re the kind of player who plans 12 moves ahead, you’ll still want to plan for 12 jokes ahead.

- Read the room. If your group is adventurous, lean into the adult humor. If you’re at a mixed-age or more conservative event, be mindful of comments and keep it inclusive.
