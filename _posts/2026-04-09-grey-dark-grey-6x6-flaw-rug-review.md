## Care guidelines: longevity tips
Caring for this rug is straightforward. Regular vacuuming keeps dust under control and helps maintain the color variation as a feature rather than a byproduct of neglect. A rug pad is recommended to extend life and to minimize slipping. For spills, act quickly: blot with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing, and treat the surface gently to prevent the dye from migrating. If a deeper clean is needed, use a mild detergent and water, test a small inconspicuous area first, and allow to air dry completely before walking on it again.

Rotation every few months can help even out wear and keep the color variation looking intentional rather than isolated to a single patch. If you have sunlight streaming through windows, consider rotating the rug occasionally to avoid uneven fading across the patterned areas. This is the kind of small maintenance that pays off in a space that looks thoughtfully curated rather than randomly arranged.
