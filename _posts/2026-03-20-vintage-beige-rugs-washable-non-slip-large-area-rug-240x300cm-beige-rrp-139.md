## Care tips to extend life and keep beige from becoming a memory
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to remove surface dirt. A weekly pass keeps the pile from compacting into the fibers.
- Rotate the rug a few degrees every couple of months to ensure even wear and fade patterns.
- Address spills promptly. Blot with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing (which can push stain deeper).
- For machine washing, follow label instructions and avoid overloading. If you have a small machine, consider washing in halves for better results.
- Use a rug pad for added non-slip safety and extra comfort. A pad also reduces wear by acting as a cushion between the rug backing and the floor.
