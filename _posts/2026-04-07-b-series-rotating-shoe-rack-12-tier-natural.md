## Maintenance and care tips
- Wipe down the surfaces with a damp microfiber cloth to remove dust and minor scuffs.
- Avoid harsh chemicals that might dull the natural finish.
- Tighten screws occasionally if you notice any wobble; the rotation mechanism should stay smooth with light maintenance.
- Keep the rack away from direct water exposure to prevent wood warping over the long term.
- If you’re dealing with sticky residue from glue or rubber from worn-out soles, a gentle soap solution works well—just avoid soaking the wood.
- Periodically rotate the entire rack to ensure every tier ages evenly and to enjoy that small dopamine spike when the shelves align again.
