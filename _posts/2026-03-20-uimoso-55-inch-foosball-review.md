Maintenance Tips and Longevity Tricks
- Keep it clean: Wipe down the surface after use to prevent fingerprint buildup and chalk dust from muting the ball’s roll.
- Lubrication: Apply light oil to the rods as recommended by the manufacturer. Do not over-lubricate; a little goes a long way.
- Tighten regularly: Check leg screws, rod connectors, and cross-braces during the first month after setup to avoid wobble creeping in later.
- Ball care: Store an extra set of balls in a dry place to avoid scuffing and to keep playing consistent during tournaments or long sessions.
- Floor protection: If you have hardwood floors, consider felt pads under the legs to prevent scratches and reduce vibration when the ball crashes into the rails after a glorious goal.
