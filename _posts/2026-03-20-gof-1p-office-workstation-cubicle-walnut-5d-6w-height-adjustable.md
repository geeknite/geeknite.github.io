## Maintenance and Care

Walnut veneer is durable but not invincible. The best approach is simple: avoid standing coffee mugs directly on the wood surface, wipe spills promptly with a microfiber cloth, and avoid harsh cleaners that could strip the finish. A mild wood cleaner used sparingly is fine, as is a light dusting to preserve that warm, natural look. Pro tip: for stubborn smudges, go with a tiny amount of furniture polish and a firm, circular wipe. Don’t overdo it, or you’ll end up with a shiny surface that resembles a somewhat guilty mirror of your own productivity. If you’re curious about upkeep and wood maintenance generally, check the walnut care cautions from reputable wood-database sources; they’re good to know if you’re renovating a room around this piece or planning to pass it down to your future time-traveling descendant who wants a desk with character.
