## How to play (the basics and the edition twists)
UNO’s core concept remains the same: match color or number and be the first to discard all your cards. The BTS Special Edition keeps that, but with a few twists that aim to quicken the pace or give fans something to cheer for mid-game.

Edition tweaks you’ll notice:
- A handful of BTS-themed icons on select action cards that add flavor rather than re-route core rules
- A lightweight concert encore mechanic that rewards group energy with a minor edge
- Subtle house-rule nudges to maintain rhythm and avoid grinding out endless rounds

If you’re new to UNO, this edition is still approachable. For the seasoned players who want to discuss color psychology or the tempo of a round, the edition offers enough flavor to keep things lively.

Quick-start guide:
1) Shuffle the BTS deck and deal seven cards to each player
2) Flip the top card to start the discard pile
3) On your turn, match color or number or play a BTS-tinted action card
4) Call UNO when you’re down to one card
5) Win a round by shedding all cards first

House-rule ideas:
- Optional karaoke break after a special card is played
- Small “encore” bonuses for winners to celebrate a round, but keep it light
