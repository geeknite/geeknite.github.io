## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Color and Texture Alive

- Regular vacuuming: Helps prevent dust buildup on the pile and extends lifespan. Use a vacuum with a beater bar or switch to a low-suction setting if you notice any fiber disturbance.
- Immediate spill response: Blot, don’t rub. Rubbing can push liquid deeper into fibers and cause faster staining or pile distortion.
- Cleaning agents: Use mild detergents or rug-specific cleaners. Avoid bleach and solvents that can degrade color or fiber quality.
- Rug pads: A pad under the rug reduces slipping, cushions foot traffic, and preserves rug edges. It also slows edge wear from chair legs and heavy furniture.
