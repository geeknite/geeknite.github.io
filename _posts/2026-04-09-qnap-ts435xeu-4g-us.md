Performance and real-world use cases

Connectivity and smart use of the network

The dual 2.5 GbE ports are a win for anyone who has faced the tyranny of gigabit bottlenecks during simultaneous file transfers and streaming. In practical terms, you can have a backup job running to the cloud while streaming a high-bitrate movie to a client and still feel comfortable with latency. If you outgrow the built-in networking, the PCIe slot is your escape hatch to 10 GbE or other high-speed NICs. Drive configuration and data protection

RAID is not a substitute for backups, but it is a safety net that helps you minimize downtime and data loss. TS-435XeU supports several RAID configurations; mirrored arrays (RAID 1/5/6) or JBOD and single-disk modes provide the flexibility you need. The bigger story here is how easy it is to set up snapshots and schedule backups. Snapshots capture the state of your data at a moment in time, letting you recover from accidental deletions or ransomware encryption without sweating the small stuff. The setup wizard guides you through creating volumes, enabling data protection features, and mapping shared folders across your network.
