## Durability and Care
### How Tough Is It?
Durability is where a rug earns its keep in a busy household. The Potter Folly Cream Rug is built for life with a finish that’s designed to resist everyday wear and tear. It’s not a “hide-in-the-closet” rug; it’s the kind you want front-and-center for guests and team movie nights. The dense weave helps reduce the risk of loop-pulling and fibre snagging from curious pets or curious little humans. Still, like any light-colour textile, you’ll want to be mindful of spills and avoid direct, frequent contact with dyes or pungent sauces. Quick action on spills—blotting rather than rubbing—will help preserve the cream aesthetic for the long haul.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
Maintenance is the boring-but-important chapter you can’t skip if you want your rug to last. The pack includes standard cleaning guidelines that align with most mid-range synthetic-foam blends. For everyday care, vacuum regularly with a suction-only setting and steer clear of aggressive beater bars that could threaten the pile height. Spot-cleaning with a mild detergent is recommended for accidental spills; always test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure no colour transfer or fabric distortion. If you spill something particularly dramatic—think red wine, turmeric, or that mysterious curry that somehow finds its way onto every textile in the house—address promptly with blotting, a gentle cleaner, and a patience that would make a monk jealous. In terms of deep cleaning, you could consider professional cleaning every 12-18 months if your space sees heavy traffic. The goal is to keep the rug looking uniform rather than “well-loved” in the most affectionate sense possible.

### Durability in Daily Life
This rug handles chairs being dragged across it (carefully), kids tumbling on it (gently, please), and the occasional canine conga line with a surprising amount of dignity. It’s not indestructible, but it’s reassuringly sturdy for a modern home where life happens in sprinting bursts and nap breaks, not in formal waltzes. If you’re someone who treats their furniture like a museum piece, you’ll appreciate the way this rug ages gracefully—no random bald spots, no obvious wear patches, just a mellow patina that tells a story of everyday life without shouting for attention.
