## Assembly and Maintenance: How Hard Is It, Really?

The big question: does this thing require a DIY PhD in engineering? The quick answer: not necessarily, but you’ll want a little patience and the right tools. Most sets ship with all required hardware and a simple instruction sheet. Expect a couple of hours to assemble the table, and maybe a little more for the chairs if you’re meticulous about aligning the wicker weaving at every seam. A power screwdriver can save you time, but a good manual screwdriver will get you there with a little elbow grease and a few choice jokes about “fastening with gusto.”

Maintenance is surprisingly friendly. Wicker, when properly treated, enjoys sunlight and rain without throwing a tantrum. Wipe-downs with mild soap and water are typically sufficient for daily grime and barbecue splatter. If you’re in a region with heavy pollen or seasonal debris, you’ll appreciate a quick wipe-down between summer shows and winter shows. Cushions, if included, often benefit from removing them when rain is forecast. If cushions aren’t included, consider adding removable cushion covers for easy washing and color swapping with the seasons.

A practical pro tip: keep a spare screwdriver and a small spray bottle near the door so you can quickly address a loose screw or a stubborn stain while you dream about dessert.
