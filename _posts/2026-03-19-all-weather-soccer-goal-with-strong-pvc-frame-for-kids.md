## Maintenance: Keeping it Fresh for the Season
Maintenance is painless. Rinse the frame and net occasionally to remove dirt and grass stains. Dry after use to prevent any moisture from building up around the joints. If you live in an area with heavy pollen or tree sap, a quick wipe-down with a mild detergent does wonders. The PVC should remain flexible, not brittle, as long as you avoid leaving the unit outside in subzero conditions for extended periods.

Storage matters too. If you have a shed or a garage, a dry, cool place is best. Coil the net loosely rather than folding it into a tight bundle for long-term storage. The goal will thank you for giving it a little space to breathe between seasons.
