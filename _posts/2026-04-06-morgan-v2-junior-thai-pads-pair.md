## Maintenance and Cleaning
Keep the pads happy with a quick post-workout routine:

- Wipe with a soft cloth to remove sweat and chalk.
- Air-dry in a well-ventilated area away from direct heat sources.
- Inspect the straps and Velcro for wear; replace as needed to avoid mid-session glove slippage.
- Store in a dry place; avoid leaving them in a damp gym bag where moisture can linger.

With regular maintenance, you can extend the life of the pads and keep them smelling fresh for the next training session. A little care goes a long way when your kids are hard on gear and their enthusiasm is boundless.

## Safety Considerations
Always pair pad work with proper supervision and coaching. Even the friendliest gear can contribute to injury if kids are not guided on technique and safe spacing:

- Teach proper guard and stance to prevent wrist injuries when punching.
- Demonstrate correct pad alignment to avoid forcing an awkward angle that causes poor form.
- Encourage breaks and hydration to prevent fatigue and sloppy technique.

Morgan V2 Junior Thai Pads give a reliable baseline for safe pad training as long as you combine them with proper coaching and a sane training plan.
