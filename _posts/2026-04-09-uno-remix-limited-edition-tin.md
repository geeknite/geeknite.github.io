## Gameplay: Remix Rules and Flavor
What makes UNO Remix distinct from the classic UNO experience? It’s the remix layer. The deck introduces a series of new action cards and altered rule interactions that emphasize rapid, social play. The pace is faster than standard UNO, and the opportunities for dramatic shifts in advantage are greater.

### Remix Cards
Remix emphasizes action-based cards that can switch up the current color, force hand swaps, or trigger additional draws in new ways. These cards create a dynamic, chatty atmosphere where players must stay alert and ready to pivot. If someone says I’ve got a color change you might quickly find your strategy shift from grinning to panic in a blink.

Remix cards are clearly marked and described to minimize confusion during game night. We found that new players pick up the flow surprisingly quickly, provided someone is patient enough to summarize the unusual action in the moment.
