### Washability and cleaning: the true test

Here’s where the Quarry Denim rug earns its stripes. It’s advertised as machine-washable, and while I don’t recommend trying to do your entire apartment’s rug rotation every Sunday since you have a social life (or a cat who believes in chaos), the rug can survive the classic messes: kid fingerprints, spilled drinks, pet hair, and that post-latte-cleanup swirl that happens when you attempt a DIY cleaning routine and end up with more soap suds than a science fair volcano.

Recommended care: cold water wash on delicate, tumble dry low or air dry. Avoid high heat, as heat and denim-like dyes can be mischievous bedfellows. For spot-cleaning, you can use a mild detergent and a soft brush; the texture handles light scrubbing without fuzzing into a needlepoint disaster. For stubborn stains (think red wine, blueberry smoothie, or that strange sauce you invented by accident), treat promptly and avoid rubbing in circles like you’re performing a sacred rug exorcism. Blot, don’t rub; proceed with gentle patience, and you’ll survive to tell the tale.
