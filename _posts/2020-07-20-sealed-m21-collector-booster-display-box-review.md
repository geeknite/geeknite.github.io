## Storage, display, and care
If you decide to go all-in on the M21 Collector Booster Display Box, you should plan for proper storage and display. Foil cards are more prone to slight edge wear in handling and scratching if not stored carefully. Here are a few practical tips:
- Use card sleeves designed for foils for the occasional foiled card you pull
- Place foils in a binder with anti-glare pages to reduce scuffing
- Keep the display box in a cool, dry place away from sunlight to avoid color fade
- Consider a small desiccant pack in your sealed product storage area to prevent moisture damage
