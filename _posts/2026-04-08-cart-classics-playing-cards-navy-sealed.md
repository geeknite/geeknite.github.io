## Care and Maintenance
Good decks are not immortals; they deserve a little care. Here are a few quick tips to keep your Cart Classics Navy Sealed deck looking sharp and feeling fresh for longer:

- Keep them away from moisture and extreme heat. Navy photos may look dramatic, but water damage is a mood killer for any card stock.
- Store in a cool, dry place. If you’re a field agent of table games, a small protective box can shield them from the chaos of your coffee mug and your anxiety about a losing streak.
- Clean gently if needed. If the corners get sticky or the finish looks a touch dull, a light wipe with a dry microfiber cloth is usually enough. Avoid soaking them or using aggressive cleaners, unless you want to twist your own deck into a pretzel of regret.
- Avoid exposing them to fans that are too cold or too warm. Temperature extremes can cause warping or stress on the stock, which is a heartbreak even a spoiler can’t fix.

With proper care, your Cart Classics Navy Sealed deck will age gracefully, becoming a better companion for your long evenings of strategy and bad puns.
