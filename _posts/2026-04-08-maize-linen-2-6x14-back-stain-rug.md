Edge curling is minimized when a non slip pad is used. Without padding, the ends may start to curl, especially in rooms with high air flow. ## Installation and care routine

### Quick install guide

- Measure the space and plan the run direction to maximize the visual length. The long axis is flattering in most hallways.
- Place a non slip pad or rug grip to reduce movement and minimize curling at the ends.
- Unroll the rug and smooth it from the center outward to avoid air pockets and buckling.
- Allow it to settle for a day before heavy foot traffic or furniture placement to reduce initial stretching or flattening.

### Maintenance schedule

- Vacuum weekly to keep grit from embedding in fibers.
- Blot spills immediately, then clean with a mild solution and warm water when needed.
- Avoid heat sources directly on the rug and keep away from intense sun for long periods to minimize color change.
- Consider rotating the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly in high traffic zones.
