## Maintenance and Care
### Cleaning as a lifestyle, not a chore
Life happens. Spills happen. The Lucas rug is designed with stain resistance in mind, thanks to its synthetic fiber core and protective top layer. Routine care is straightforward: vacuum regularly, use a gentle setting, and spot-clean as needed. For most everyday mishaps, a mixture of mild detergent and warm water should do the trick. Blot rather than rub to avoid pushing the stain deeper into the pile.

If you spill something particularly stubborn, act quickly. The sooner you address the spill, the better your chances of removing it completely. Be mindful of colored liquids that might bleed; test a small, inconspicuous patch first. For deep stains, you may consider professional cleaning every few years, depending on foot traffic and whether your space doubles as a runway for muddy boots after weekend hikes.

### Vacuuming and care frequency
Because this is a mid-pile rug, you don’t need to vacuum daily—keep it to every other day or two, especially in high-traffic areas. Use a vacuum with adjustable height to avoid snagging the fibers. If possible, rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and sun exposure, because yes, even rugs get tan lines when they bask in the sun like a starlet on holiday.
