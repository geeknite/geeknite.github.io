## Maintenance and Care
### Cleaning as a lifestyle, not a chore
Life happens. The Lucas rug is designed with stain resistance in mind, courtesy of its synthetic core and top layer. Routine care is simple: vacuum regularly on a gentle setting, spot-clean when needed, and blot spills quickly. For most day-to-day mishaps, a mild detergent mixed with warm water will do the trick. The key is blotting, not rubbing, to avoid pushing stains deeper into the pile.

If a spill sticks around longer than your patience, act fast. Colored liquids can bleed, so test a small, inconspicuous patch first. For stubborn stains, a professional cleaning every few years might be worth considering, especially in high-traffic zones or if your space doubles as a muddy boot runway after weekend adventures.

### Vacuuming cadence and care frequency
This is a mid-pile rug, so you don’t need to vacuum daily. A few passes every couple of days in high-traffic areas should suffice. Use a vacuum with adjustable height to prevent snagging. Consider rotating the rug every few months to ensure even wear and sun exposure. Yes, even rugs get tan lines when they bask in the sun like a celebrity on vacation.

### Dealing with moisture and longevity notes
The Lucas uses a synthetic backing designed for stability on hard floors. Occasional humidity is usually fine, but avoid prolonged moisture exposure. If you live in an overly humid climate or have pets that track in rain, you may want to pair the rug with a proper rug pad to reduce slipping and minimize moisture contact with the backing.
