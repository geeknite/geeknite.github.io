## Preservation, storage, and care tips
A sealed NOS deck is a dream when it comes to preservation, but it still needs a little love. Here are practical tips to keep your set pristine:

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent paper yellowing and ink fading.
- Keep the original tin or packaging intact to preserve the display value.
- If you decide to unseal for a spin, do so gently and mindfully; let the deck acclimate to room temperature before handling thoroughly to avoid humidity-related warping.
- For magicians and performers, consider maintaining a separate, lightly aged deck for practice to avoid weakening the nostalgic aura of your NOS set.
- Avoid stacking excessive weight on top of the tin. The corners can suffer under pressure, and you don’t want your vintage beauty to suffer a dent in its time capsule swagger.
