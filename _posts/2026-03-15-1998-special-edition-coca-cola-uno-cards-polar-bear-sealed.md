## Preservation and Storage Tips
Preservation is an often overlooked facet of collecting, especially with paper stock and glossy finishes from the 1990s. Here are practical tips to maintain the integrity of your Coca-Cola UNO Polar Bear edition:
- Store in a climate-controlled environment with stable temperature and humidity. A humidity level around 40-50% is generally safe for paper-based collectibles.
- Avoid direct sunlight and harsh fluorescent lighting, which can cause yellowing and color fading over time.
- If displaying, consider acrylic display cases with UV-filtered glass to protect against light exposure while still letting you enjoy the artwork.
- Keep the deck away from heat sources and moisture. Card stock is surprisingly susceptible to warping if storage conditions fluctuate.
- For sealed decks, consider a frame or archival storage that prevents pressure on the blister while preserving its visibility.
- Handle with clean hands (or cotton gloves) to avoid fingerprinting and oil transfer that can degrade the surface over long periods.

From a digital perspective, photographing the deck under diffuse light can yield wonderful reference images for your collection catalog. A consistent light setup helps document color accuracy and edge wear across the years, a habit that can be invaluable when you eventually decide to part with or trade the deck.
