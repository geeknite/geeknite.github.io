## How Animal Rummy Works
This is a family friendly variant of the classic rummy concept. The goal is to form sets and runs with the cards in your hand and to improve your hand by drawing and discarding. In this version the animal portraits form the suits, with rules that encourage a mix of strategy and quick glances at what the other players are collecting. The exact rules are printed on the interior of the card box, but here is the gist to get you started:

- Draw a card from the stock pile
- Form a set (three or four cards of the same rank) or a run (three or more consecutive cards in the same suit)
- Discard one card to end your turn
- The first player to empty their hand wins the round

Because this is a vintage set, many households played with flexible interpretations. The deck invites creativity, and the box invites memory sharing as you and your friends try to recall whether a certain animal belonged to the cat family or the dog family. The point is not to memorize a million rules, but to enjoy the process of turning over cards and trading smiles.

## Practical Care and Restoration Tips
Vintage sets can be delicate. Here are practical steps to keep your Whitman Animal Rummy deck in decent shape for future generations of geeks and game night heroes:

- Inspect the box for moisture damage and try to store in a dry, cool place. If there is any mold risk, handle with care and consider a professional restoration option.
- Check the cards for creases and edge wear. If there is minor curling, place the deck under a light weight for a few days to flatten it. Do not try to iron cards; that is a bad idea in any century.
- For surface cleaning, use a soft brush or a gently damp microfiber cloth to remove dust. Avoid soaking cards; water and cardboard are not friends.
- If the card faces have mild discoloration, do not attempt aggressive whitening methods. A gentle breath and a careful wipe may be enough; if not, keep the deck as is and let it tell its story.
- Store the deck in its original box if possible. If the inner tray is missing, consider a custom tray or a simple pouch to keep the pack from sliding around.
