## Introduction

Welcome, fellow sleuths and mystery aficionados! Today, we're diving into the smoky, noir-filled world of **City of Mist: Fortune Row - Starter Set**. If you've ever dreamed of being a private detective with a penchant for the supernatural, grab your fedora and trench coat because this game is calling your name.

## What is City of Mist: Fortune Row?

City of Mist is a role-playing game that blends noir detective stories with supernatural powers. Think Sherlock Holmes meets the X-Men, but with more trench coats and dark alleys. The Fortune Row Starter Set is designed to introduce players to the world of City of Mist, providing everything you need to start your investigation.

### Components

The starter set includes:
- **Rulebook**: A comprehensive guide to the game mechanics and world.
- **Character Sheets**: Pre-generated characters to jump right into the action.
- **Mystery Booklets**: Scenarios to challenge your detective skills.
- **Dice and Tokens**: Essential tools for any good investigation.

**Pro Tip**: Don’t let the cat near the tokens. They might think they’re toys and your investigation will turn into a game of “Catch the Token.”

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up City of Mist: Fortune Row is like prepping for a detective movie marathon. Lay out the rulebook, distribute character sheets, and choose your mystery. Now you're ready to delve into the foggy streets of the City of Mist.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players describe their actions, draw cards, and roll dice to determine outcomes. You can investigate clues, confront suspects, and use your supernatural powers to uncover the truth.

### Combat

Combat in City of Mist is more about strategy and narrative than rolling high numbers. Use your powers wisely and be prepared for unexpected twists.

## The Mysteries

### Scenarios

The Fortune Row Starter Set introduces several scenarios that will test your detective skills. From solving murders to uncovering hidden secrets, each mystery offers unique challenges.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our team was so focused on chasing a suspect that we didn’t notice the ghost lurking in the alley. It was like a Scooby-Doo episode, but with more grit.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: The noir theme and supernatural elements create a captivating experience.
- **High-Quality Components**: The artwork and materials are top-notch.
- **Replayability**: Multiple scenarios ensure you’ll return to the City of Mist.
- **Narrative Depth**: The game encourages storytelling and character development.

### Cons

- **Complex Setup**: The initial setup can be time-consuming.
- **Learning Curve**: New players may find the mechanics challenging at first.
- **Expansions Needed**: To keep the game fresh, expansions are almost a must.

## Final Thoughts

City of Mist: Fortune Row - Starter Set is a fantastic introduction to a world where noir meets the supernatural. Its immersive gameplay, high-quality components, and narrative depth make it a must-have for fans of mystery and role-playing games. Just remember, the fog is thick and the streets are dangerous – but the truth is out there.
