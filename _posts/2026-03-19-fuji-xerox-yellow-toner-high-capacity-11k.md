## Durability, storage, and maintenance tips
A cartridge is more than pages; it is a tool that has to live in the office as well. Here are practical tips to maximize shelf life and performance:

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Heat and humidity can ruin toner stability.
- Keep cartridges sealed until use. The desiccant helps prevent moisture that can affect print quality when you finally install it.
- Do not shake too aggressively during installation. Gentle shakes are enough to settle pigment. Overzealous shaking can cause clumping and headaches you dont need.
- If you move printer housing around, keep the cartridge seated securely. This reduces powder leakage and the inevitable cleanup ritual that follows.

Maintenance wise, the 11K Yellow doesnt require any special rituals beyond standard printer upkeep. A routine internal cleaning cycle and occasional alignment checks are usually enough. If your printer supports automated calibration, run it after the swap to keep color alignment on point.

## How to spot genuine vs counterfeit and why it matters
Counterfeit toner exists and can ruin your day faster than a coffee spill on a slide deck. Here are quick checks:

- Buy from reputable retailers or official distributors. If the price looks crazy, walk away.
- Inspect packaging and labeling. Genuine cartridges have consistent fonts and logos; counterfeit ones often show misprints.
- Check the serial number and model code on the cartridge. If it does not match the supported models, skip it.
- Run a few test pages and look for consistent color and density. Inconsistent output is a red flag.
