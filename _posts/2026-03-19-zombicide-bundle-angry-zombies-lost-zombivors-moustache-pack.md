### What is Zombicide in a nutshell
For those who are new to the concept, Zombicide is a cooperative board game where players coordinate to survive zombie onslaughts across a modular city or scenario board. It is essentially a dungeon-crawler vibe without the dungeon, a tactical skirmish with a big brain-eating grin. The game is famous for its modular components, mission variety, and the fact that every playthrough can feel wildly different depending on who shows up to the party.
