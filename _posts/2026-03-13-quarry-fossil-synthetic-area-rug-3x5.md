### Keeping It Fresh: Cleaning and Care Tips

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to remove dust and crumbs that accumulate in the fossil grooves. 
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth to prevent staining. Avoid harsh chemicals that could fade the color or weaken the fibers.
- For spot cleaning, use a mild detergent solution and test on a small inconspicuous area first. If color migration occurs, stop and reassess your plan.
- Consider rotating the rug every few months to even out wear and extend the life of the pattern. Yes, this means you might have to lift the furniture a few inches; your back will thank you later.
- If you need deeper cleansing, check the manufacturer care guide for approved cleaning methods. If in doubt, a professional cleaning service is a solid option that does not require you to become a rug maintenance wizard.
