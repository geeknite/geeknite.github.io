## Assembly, Maintenance, and Daily Use

### Assembly Time and Tips
Expect 30-60 minutes for one person, a little longer if you’re meticulous or you have tiny bits of hardware that seem to vanish into the couch dimension. Pro tips:
- Sort hardware by size before you start.
- Pre-thread bolts loosely to align the frame, then go back and tighten in a cross pattern for even tension.
- Dry-fit the cushions before fully tightening; this helps you confirm alignment and reduces frustration later.

### Maintenance Routine
Maintenance isn’t glamorous, but it is essential for longevity. Here’s a practical routine:
- Wipe down the frame with a mild soap solution and a soft cloth every couple of weeks to remove dust and dirt.
- Spot-clean cushion covers as needed; if machine-washable, follow care labels and air-dry to prevent shrinkage.
- Use a light protective cover during heavy weather; this can double the lifespan of both the cushions and the weave.
- Rotate or reposition cushions occasionally to prevent uneven wear and to keep every seat feeling equally inviting.
