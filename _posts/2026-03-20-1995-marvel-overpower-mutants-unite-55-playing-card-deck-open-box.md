## Overview

### What is Marvel OverPower?
OverPower was a collectible card game that hit shelves during the early-to-mid 1990s, pitched as a lighter, more approachable cousin to the heavy hitters of the era. It combines action role-playing flavor with card-based combat, where characters wield powers and events shape the battlefield. The system is approachable enough to teach your aunt and deep enough to reward the die-hard collector who loves to pore over ability lines and card art. The Mutants Unite 55 deck sits squarely in the meet-cute moment of the game’s life, where Marvel’s mutant heroes collide with event cards, power bursts, and the occasional ego battle among X-Men-like factions.
