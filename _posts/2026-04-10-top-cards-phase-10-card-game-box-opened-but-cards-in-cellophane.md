## Practical Care and Sleeving Tips
- If you want to maximize the life of your Phase 10 deck, sleeve the cards early. Sleeves protect edges and preserve color integrity for years of use.
- Store the deck in a cool, dry place and avoid humidity that can cause warping or sticking of cards to one another.
- When not playing, keep the box and cards in a dedicated container to prevent accidental mixing with other games.
- If you have kids who want to play, consider a second, lower-stakes pack for practice rounds. It reduces wear and gives everyone a fair shot to learn the phases without it feeling like a big competition.
