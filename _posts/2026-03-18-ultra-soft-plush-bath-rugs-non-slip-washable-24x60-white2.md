## Care Guide and Longevity: Keeping It Fresh

- Wash every 1–2 weeks or when it looks a bit bedraggled. If you have kids who run through the bathroom like a relay race, more frequent cleaning might be needed.
- Use a gentle cycle with cold or warm water and avoid chlorine-heavy detergents that can degrade fibers over time.
- Tumble dry on low; avoid high heat which can shrink fibers or warp the backing. Air drying is fine, but avoid long sun exposure that can cause color fading in some White2 variants.
- Spot clean as needed. For the occasional spill, dab and blot rather than rubbing; your carpet will thank you for not turning the spill into a snow globe of fibers.
