## Maintenance, Storage, and Care
To preserve the tin and the cards, store the set in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Light can fade the colors over time, and humidity can cause warping or mildew on the score pad. Keep the tin lid closed to protect the cards from dust or little hands with questionable impulse control. If the card edges show wear, you can gently clean them with a dry microfiber cloth, avoiding any liquid cleaners that could bleed color onto the surface. For the vintage collector, the pencil is part of the set’s charm, but you should treat it as a fragile piece. It is not a writing instrument meant to withstand a lifetime of use; more like a relic that deserves respectful storage and occasional admiration.
