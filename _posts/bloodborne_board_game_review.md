## The Basics

### What is Bloodborne: The Board Game?

Bloodborne: The Board Game is a cooperative, dungeon-crawling adventure based on the popular video game Bloodborne. Designed by Michael Shinall and Eric Lang, this game captures the essence of its digital counterpart, bringing the horrific yet captivating world of Yharnam to your tabletop. Players take on the role of Hunters, working together to defeat monstrous foes and uncover the secrets of the city.

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully crafted board, depicting various locations in Yharnam, and high-quality miniatures representing Hunters and the myriad of grotesque enemies they'll face. The detailed artwork and sturdy components ensure an immersive gaming experience. Additionally, the game includes numerous cards, tokens, and a rulebook that might as well be your new bedtime story (if you enjoy nightmares).

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is straightforward, though you might want to schedule a tea break halfway through. Players choose their Hunter, set up the board with locations and enemies, and prepare their decks. The objective is simple: survive. Oh, and maybe save Yharnam while you're at it.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and perform actions like moving, attacking, and using their Hunter's special abilities. Combat is a blend of strategy and luck, with players rolling dice to determine the outcome. Just remember, cooperation is key. And so is not dying.

### Dark Humor and Strategy
