## Cleaning, Care, and Longevity

### Everyday Cleaning Routine
Spills and pet accidents are part of life; rugs aren’t immune. The recommended approach is to act quickly: blot the spill with a dry cloth, then dab with a mild soap solution or a dedicated carpet cleaner suitable for synthetic blends. Avoid harsh solvents that could discolor the material. Regular vacuuming with a standard setting—avoiding high suction on the fringes (if any)—will keep the pile looking crisp. For deeper cleaning, consider a professional service, especially if your space sees heavy foot traffic or you’re hosting messy board-game nights that end with a river of soda on the rug.
