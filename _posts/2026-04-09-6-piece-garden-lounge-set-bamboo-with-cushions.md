## Style, Maintenance, and Keeping It Fresh
### Aesthetics That Play Nice With Real Backyards
The bamboo finish brings warmth that pairs well with greenery, string lights, and potted palms. It blends with wicker and metal, and it looks surprisingly good with cushions in bold colors or neutrals. The natural tone is versatile and forgiving for seasonal swaps.

### Cleaning and Routine Care
A simple wipe with a damp cloth keeps dust away. A mild soap solution tackles stubborn spots, followed by a rinse. The removable cushion covers simplify maintenance for families with kids and pets. If mildew or mold appears after long wet spells, sun-dry and run a fabric safe cleaner through the fabric, then air out before reassembly. Dry thoroughly so moisture does not linger and invite a damp seat vibe that no one wants.

### Storing and Longevity Tips
In severe winters or windy zones, store cushions indoors when not in use. The rest of the set can stay outside year round with a cover to shield from patina and UV wear. Avoid pool chemicals that can affect frame finishes, and remember that bamboo needs a little breathing room to stay happy.
