## Cleaning and Maintenance
### Routine Upkeep
- Vacuum weekly to lift loose dirt and hair.
- Shake it out on a calm day to dislodge stubborn debris.
- Rinse with a garden hose when needed; avoid high-pressure washers that may fray the fibers.
- Let it dry fully before placing back on the floor to prevent mold growth.

### Stain Management
Most common stains from beverages or pet accidents can be spot-cleaned using a mild soap solution. Don’t scrub aggressively; a gentle approach preserves the rib texture and prevents fuzzing. For tougher stains, consider repeating the cleaning cycle and applying a light deodorizer if needed. If you’re worried about odors in enclosed porches, a quick air-out session after rain can keep things fresh without chemical assistance.

### Durability Tips
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear patterns if you have a high-traffic entry or a sun-drenched porch corner.
- Use a rug pad under smooth surfaces to prevent curling and to extend life by reducing friction.
- Keep sharp objects away; a corner of a chair leg can snag the fibers if you aren’t careful.

### Maintenance Schedule
