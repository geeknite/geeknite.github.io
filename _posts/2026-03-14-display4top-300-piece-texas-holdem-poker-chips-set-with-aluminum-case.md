## Care, Cleaning, and Longevity Tips

- Wipe chips with a soft, dry microfiber cloth after use to remove oils and residue from handling.
- Keep the case closed during transport to prevent chips from rattling around; this also protects the metal finish.
- If you spill a drink, wipe immediately and dry thoroughly before reseating chips.
- Store chips away from direct sunlight to minimize color fading on edge inks.

Pro tip: rotate denominations every few months if you’re using chips as a teaching tool or for tournaments. It helps players learn to recognize values by feel, not just color. This can be a fun way to turn a casual game night into a mini-lesson on probability and math, which you can pretend is part of your “strategy” plan.
