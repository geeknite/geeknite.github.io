## What is Kitty Cat Clash?

Kitty Cat Clash is a competitive card game where players take on the roles of adorable kittens with unique abilities. With charming artwork, hilarious card names, and a plethora of strategies, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Kitty Cat Clash, players compete to be the top cat by using their kitten's special abilities and playing action cards strategically. Each player controls a unique kitten, each with special powers. The goal is to outwit your opponents and be the last cat standing.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Kitten Cards**: Beautifully illustrated cards of your favorite kittens. Seriously, they're so cute you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Action Cards**: Each kitten has a deck of action cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations from the kitty kingdom where battles and missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the cards. Those claws are the real supervillains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Kitty Cat Clash is like preparing for a cat show. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and tokens, and choose your kitten. Now you're ready to begin your quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their kitten's abilities. You can move, attack, steal catnip, and more. Strategy is key, so plan your moves carefully to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Kitty Cat Clash is a blend of strategy and luck. Each kitten has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have your opponent use a "Hairball" card? Welcome to the kitty kingdom, where plans often go awry.
