## Maintenance Tips and Care Plan

- Vacuum regularly to prevent dust and lint from settling into the fibers. A weekly pass is often enough for low-traffic areas and nothing too fancy.
- Clean spills promptly with a blotting technique rather than rubbing. Rubbing sends the spill deeper into the pile and makes stains harder to remove.
- For tougher stains, consider a mild cleaning solution appropriate for synthetic fibers. Always test on an inconspicuous corner first to avoid color bleeding or material damage.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to even out wear and prevent visible patterning from foot traffic and sunlight.
- Use furniture coasters to minimize edge curling and to preserve the rug’s flat lay.

Following these steps will help you maximize the lifespan of a rug with an intentional flaw and a reduced price, ensuring that the trade-off stays favorable rather than turning into a maintenance nightmare you dread after week two.
