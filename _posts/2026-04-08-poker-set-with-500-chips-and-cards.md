## Maintenance and Cleaning
- Chips: Wipe down with a dry or lightly damp microfiber cloth. Avoid soaking; chips are not built to double as a hockey puck. If you notice color transfer, a quick wipe and a gentle air dry should restore brightness after a few plays.
- Cards: Keep them in a dry place away from direct sunlight. Plastic-coated cards can tolerate humidity better than plain cardboard, but excess moisture can still lead to warping and war stories during a tense river. If you need to clean them, a soft cloth and minimal moisture are enough. Let them dry completely before placing back into the box.
- Case: Wipe the interior to remove dust and stray chips that have snuck inside during dramatic all-in finales. Ensure the latches close properly and that the case remains sturdy enough to protect the entire kit during travel or storage.
