## Maintenance and care

A good maintenance routine extends the life of your portable poker set. Here are a few practical tips:

- Clean chips with a light damp cloth periodically to prevent sticky residue from coffee or soda from dulling the color edges.
- Keep the case dry and away from direct sunlight to prevent warping of the plastic shell.
- Inspect the rims for chips or cracks; damaged chips can nick cards or teeth if you’re not careful.
- Replace worn decks when they show excessive wear or you’re starting to notice mis-deals due to bent cards.
- Store in a cool, dry place. This helps prevent warping of the tray and keeps the case latch functioning smoothly.

If you’ve got a messy gaming habit (and who doesn’t), consider a spill-proof setup or a travel-friendly microfiber cloth to handle the occasional spill scenario. It’s not glamorous, but it’s practical, and practicality is the unsung hero of weekend gaming.
