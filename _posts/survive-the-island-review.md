## The Basics

### What is Survive The Island?

"Survive The Island" is a strategy and survival board game where players are stranded on a deserted island and must use their wits and resources to survive. The goal is simple: be the last one standing or successfully escape the island. But beware, your fellow islanders might not have your best interests at heart!

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully designed board featuring various island terrains such as forests, beaches, and mountains. High-quality miniatures represent the stranded survivors, and a deck of cards introduces challenges, resources, and events. The artwork is detailed and immersive, enhancing the overall gameplay experience.

**Fun Fact**: The game includes a special "Coconut Cannon" miniature for some extra island-themed chaos.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is straightforward and quick. Each player chooses a survivor character and places their miniature on the starting location. Shuffle the event cards, resource cards, and distribute initial resources. Get ready for an adventure filled with twists and turns.

### Game Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions such as moving, gathering resources, and building shelter.
2. **Event Phase**: Draw event cards that introduce new challenges, opportunities, or disasters.
3. **Conflict Phase**: Players may engage in conflicts to steal resources or sabotage opponents. (Remember, it's a matter of survival!)

### Survival and Strategy

The game shines in its strategic depth. You must carefully plan your moves, manage resources, and anticipate others' actions. Cooperation can be valuable, but betrayal is always lurking around the corner. The key is to adapt and outsmart your opponents while dealing with unpredictable island events.
