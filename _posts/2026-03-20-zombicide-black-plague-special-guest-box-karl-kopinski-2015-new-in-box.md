## Unboxing and contents

The unboxing ritual is a rite of passage for a collector. In 2015 this Special Guest Box typically included a few key elements that set it apart from the core game:

- Kopinski designed miniatures with dramatic posing and unique styling
- Alternate card backs and a handful of tokens with Kopinski inspired visuals
- A small scenario or campaign guide that uses the new minis to weave a short narrative
- An outer and inner packaging designed to feel premium and display worthy

A typical New In Box item should present a sealed inner bag or protective casing around the minis, crisp card stock for the tokens, and minimal signs of wear. If you are buying used, inspect the minis for flash, any missing bases, or bent parts. A sealed box is the best bet if your goal is pure display or investment value.

Kopinski s contribution shines most when you place the minis on a lit board. ## Packaging and presentation

Packaging quality matters for long term value. Kopinski s design language appears on the outer box and the minis, with strong contrast and bold visuals that make for a dramatic display on a shelf.
