Why the Double Deck matters
----------------------------
Double deck technology is not a gimmick dressed in fancy marketing. The idea is to separate the top surface where your feet land from a secondary surface that participates in dampening and distributing load. The result is less impact transmitted to your joints and a ride that feels more stable, particularly on higher incline settings. If you’ve ever felt your ankles protest after a few weeks of aggressive interval training in a small space, the double deck approach is a quiet way of saying, we’ve got your back—and your ankles—and probably your neighbor’s eardrums too.
