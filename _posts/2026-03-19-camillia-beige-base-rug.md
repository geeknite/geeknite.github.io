## Materials and Build Quality

The materials used in the Camillia Beige Base Rug are aimed at balancing softness with durability. It is described as a blend that emphasizes resilience with a plush feel.

### Texture and Pile Height
The top pile is plush enough to feel comfortable under bare feet, yet not so long that you feel like you stepped into a walking pillow fort. The pile height is thoughtfully chosen to stride into the realm of practical luxury. The fibers are tightly woven, reducing the likelihood of excessive shedding and pilling, which is a major win for busy households.

### Backing and Stability
The backing is designed to keep the rug anchored on hard surfaces. If you have a rug pad, you will notice a significant improvement in stability and underfoot cushioning. Without a pad, the rug remains functional but a pad will increase comfort and reduce movement as you shuffle around at night while searching for that last snack.

### Colorfastness and Maintenance
Colorfastness is a big deal with light neutrals. The Camillia Beige Base Rug holds color well, but you should still avoid exposing it to direct sun for extended periods if you want to preserve the color. Rotating the rug every few months helps mitigate uneven fading and keeps the beige tone consistent across the room.

## Size, Fit, and Placement

Rug sizes and placement matter more than you might think. The Camillia Beige Base Rug is usually available in 5x8, 6x9, and 8x10. For larger rooms, consider 9x12 or larger if you want the rug to anchor an expansive seating area.

Placement tips:
- In a small living room, choose a 5x8 to support a sofa and chair arrangement while staying out of the traffic flow.
- In a medium space, 6x9 does the job, leaving a few inches of visible floor around the rug to create a frame for the furniture.
- In a large space with a couch and coffee table, an 8x10 gives you room to define the seating area without feeling cramped.

Pro tip: You can experiment with layered rugs as a design choice, layering a smaller textured rug on top of the Camillia base to create depth. This is a bold move that may require precise measurement and a careful eye for color and pattern.

## Cleaning and Care Tips

Keeping a rug clean is not glamorous, but it is essential. Here is a practical care plan:
- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting.
- Rotate every quarter to avoid uneven wear.
- Blot spills immediately; avoid rubbing to minimize fiber distortion.
- For stubborn stains, test mild cleaning solutions on a hidden area before treating the whole rug.
- If needed, professional cleaning every couple of years can help preserve its appearance.
