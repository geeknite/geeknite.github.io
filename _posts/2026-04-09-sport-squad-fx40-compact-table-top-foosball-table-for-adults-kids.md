## Assembly tips and gotchas
- Do not over tighten bolts; this is a common rookie mistake that can warp the frame.
- Use a level to ensure the table sits evenly before you start playing; a slightly off level game is not fun when the ball keeps deflecting in odd directions.
- Make sure you align the players on rods properly before installing them; misalignment can yield unpredictable results that ruin the first round.
- Have a friend to help with the longer rods; two hands make the process quicker and safer for your fingers.

## Maintenance tips for long-term joy
Warranties and maintenance are not the first things people discuss during a game night, but they sure make life easier when the inevitable bolt squeak or surface nicks show up. The FX40 is relatively forgiving, but a few routines will keep it looking and playing great for years:

- Regularly wipe the playing surface with a mild cleaner and a soft cloth to avoid sticky residues that make the ball cling to the field.
- Check the rod screws every few weeks and tighten as needed to maintain smooth operation.
- Keep the balls clean and free of debris that could hinder their roll. A quick wipe or a rinse every few months is enough for most households.
- Store the table away from direct sunlight to preserve the finish and avoid warping on the panels.
