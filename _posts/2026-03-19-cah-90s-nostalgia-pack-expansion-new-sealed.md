## Gameplay Dynamics and Balance

The expansion doesn’t reinvent the wheel; it refines it with a bit of retro flair. The core CAH formula remains intact: someone reads a black card, everyone else submits a white card, the reader chooses the funniest or most fitting response. The 90s pack adds more options that tilt the humor toward retro references, which tends to favor players who lived through the era and can pull the exact phrase from memory without peeking at a calendar.

Balance-wise, the pack is well-tuned for a mixed-age group. Younger players may rely on broad pop culture humor, while older players will relish the inside jokes and the sharper references.
