### Cleaning Tips

- Pretreat stains with a mild detergent and a gentle rub. Harsh scrubs are for video game controllers, not rugs.
- Use a gentle or normal cycle with cold water. Hot water can cause fiber shrinkage and questionable shrink tension in the realm of fabric physics.
- Air dry or tumble dry on a low setting. High heat can warp the pile; low heat keeps your geometry intact. Do not over-dry; you want the fibers to rebound like a well trained cat after a zoomies session.

## Practical Setup and Care Tips

### Getting It Into Place

Due to the anti slip backing, you should be able to place the rug in a room without too much wrestling. If you have a heavy sofa or coffee table, consider sliding it into position first and then laying the rug around it. This helps maintain the lines and prevents the rug from bunching in the corners.

### Routine Maintenance

- Vacuum weekly, more often in high traffic zones.
- Spot clean spills immediately with a mild detergent and a blotting technique rather than scrubbing. Scrubbing creates texture chaos and can ruin the uniform look of the cut pile.
- For deeper cleaning, follow the machine wash instructions and ensure the rug is fully dried before placing heavy furniture back on top to prevent moisture seepage. A damp rug under a chair can lead to big squeaks and a small meltdown in anyone who is allergic to damp felt vibes.

### Storage and Longevity

If you ever need to store the rug, roll it rather than folding it. Folding can create creases that are difficult to remove. A rolled rug wrapped in breathable fabric will store more gracefully and come out looking crisp when you unroll it again. Your future self will thank you for this small-yet-smart habit.
