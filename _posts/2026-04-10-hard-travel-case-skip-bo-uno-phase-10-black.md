## Maintenance and care

### Cleaning and care tips

A quick wipe with a damp microfiber cloth is usually enough to remove fingerprints and dust from the exterior. For the interior foam, a gentle puff of compressed air works wonders to dislodge tiny specks left behind by your sleeves. If you spill a beverage, do a gentle wipe to avoid saturating the foam; this isn’t a waterproof submarine. Let it dry fully before placing decks back into their slots. The foam is designed to recover its shape after light compression, so you won’t need to replace inserts every few months unless your packing habits are spectacularly chaotic.

### Longevity and storage

If you’re a collector, consider rotating decks and occasionally re-allocating foam slots to keep everything snug. Avoid stacking heavy objects on top of the case when stored in a closet; gravity is a cruel physics experiment when it’s applied to a fragile card ecosystem. Treat the case like a well-loved tool: it should be used, not worshiped. A little routine maintenance goes a long way toward preserving the value and usability of your gaming arsenal.
