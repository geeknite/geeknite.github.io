## Box contents and unboxing: what you really get

At the heart of the Huntsmen Box is William—the titular huntsman who believes the best defense is a well-placed arrow. William carries a longbow, a hunting knife, and a few trick arrows designed to tip the balance in skirmishes where a standard archer would blink at the wrong moment. He’s the archetype you want on a map: disciplined, precise, and capable of turning a tight corridor into a sniper’s perch. His sculpt reads like a movie poster in miniature form: cloak flaring, bowstring taut, eyes narrowed as if he’s about to reveal the proper alignment of the constellations that guide your aim.

Accompanying William are three other survivors who feel tailored to his playstyle: a skirmisher with a crossbow, a healer who herbs their way through wounds, and a trapper who manipulates terrain to corral the undead.
