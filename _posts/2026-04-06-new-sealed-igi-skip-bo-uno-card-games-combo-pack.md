## How to Play Across The Combo Night
Here is where the magic happens and also where players with different preferences will find their sweet spot.

### UNO Rules in Brief
- Match color or number to discard
- Use special cards such as draw two, skip, reverse, wild to shift momentum
- The goal is to shed all your cards before everyone else

### Skip-Bo Rules in Brief
- Build built up piles in ascending order using stock cards and visible discard piles
- Use Skip-Bo wilds to fill gaps or accelerate a difficult stage
- The player who empties their stock pile first wins

In this combo pack you do not get a literal merge rules option, but you do get two distinct play modes in a single box. It is like having two separate game nights that fit into the same weekend and share the same snack supply. This means less searching for extra components and more time laughing at your own mistakes.

A few practical tips:
- Establish a loose house rule: if UNO starts devolving into a chaotic guild for color re-organization, switch to Skip-Bo for a round to give everyone a breath
- Set a timer for twenty to thirty minutes of UNO, then switch to Skip-Bo for a longer stretch. It keeps energy levels balanced
- Consider using a shared snack model for all games: chips, pretzels, and the occasional chocolate to keep players moody but entertained
