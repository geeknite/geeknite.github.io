## Weather, Maintenance, and Longevity
Solid pine wood is relatively weather friendly but it is not indestructible. A light sealant or outdoor varnish once a year helps preserve color and prevent moisture uptake. Prolonged exposure to heavy rain is not a friendship, so use a cover if your climate is wild.
