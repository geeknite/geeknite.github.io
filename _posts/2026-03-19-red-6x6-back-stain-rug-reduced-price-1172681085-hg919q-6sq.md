## Cleaning and maintenance

### Daily upkeep
Most rugs in the price range of this model benefit from routine vacuuming once or twice a week. This helps prevent dirt from set-in and keeps the red color vibrant. A canister or upright vacuum with a beater bar that you can turn down for delicate fibers will do the trick. If you spilled something obvious like juice or coffee, blot rather than rub to avoid color transfer. For drying spills, a clean cloth or paper towel pressed gently on the area is your friend, not a foe.

### Stain management
Stains happen. We are humans, and humans spill things with ridiculous regularity. The back stain aspect implies a level of protection, but do not assume this rug is a magic stain shield. For older spills, you can treat with a mild carpet cleaner or a solution of dish soap and water. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure color retention. For more stubborn marks, consider a professional cleaning service; a little professional help can extend the life of the rug without the drama of trying to scrub away every memory of spilled red wine from your life choices.

### Grooming and fluffing
Rotate the rug every few months to prevent uneven wear. Brush lightly with a soft-bristle tool if you notice any matting in high-traffic zones. If you have kids or pets, you might see some flattening in spots where the rug faces the doorway or under the coffee table; this is normal. A quick fluff with your hands or a low-power vacuum can restore the pile’s look, and your space can go from lived-in to magazine-spread worthy in minutes.
