## Care, maintenance, and longevity

Taking care of playing cards extends their life and their charm. The Rider Back standard index finish tolerates a fair amount of handling, but it is not immortal. The following tips help you extend life:
- Keep cards away from moisture and heat; a hot car is a known villain to card stock.
- Store decks in a cool, dark place; avoid direct sunlight that can fade inks over time.
- Clean your hands if you have greasy fingers; oils can make shuffles feel sticky and encourage edge wear.
- Replace worn cards in pairs when signs of edge fraying appear; using one deck beyond its prime can impair your performance and your group’s confidence.

The two decks in this package should last you months of regular play and practice if you take reasonable care, with minimal edge fray in normal usage. If you push the finish too hard with aggressive cutting and extremes of humidity, you may see some flattening of the stock along the edges, but that is not a catastrophe; it is more a badge of battle scars that tell stories of dramatic wins and epic misdeals.
