## What is Robots Love Ice Cream: The Card Game?

Robots Love Ice Cream: The Card Game is a cooperative card game where players take on the roles of ice cream truck drivers defending the world from robot invaders. With colorful artwork, delightful ice cream-themed cards, and a plethora of robotic enemies to battle, this game offers a whimsical experience that will keep you entertained for hours.

### The Basics

In Robots Love Ice Cream: The Card Game, players work together to thwart the plans of mechanized ice cream thieves. Each player controls a unique ice cream truck, each with special abilities and cards. The goal is to complete missions, defeat the robots, and save the world's ice cream supply from impending doom.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Cards**: Beautifully illustrated cards representing ice cream truck abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations where battles and missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the tokens. Those teeth are the real supervillains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Robots Love Ice Cream: The Card Game is like preparing for an ice cream party. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and tokens, and choose your robot enemies. Now you're ready to begin your quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their ice cream truck's abilities. You can move, attack, rescue frozen treats, and more. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your teammates to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Robots Love Ice Cream: The Card Game is a blend of strategy and luck. Each robot has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have the robot escape? Welcome to the ice cream wars, where plans often melt away.

With multiple trucks, robots, and scenarios, Robots Love Ice Cream: The Card Game offers endless replayability. No two games are the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more frozen battles.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new trucks, robots, and missions. Your ice cream journey is about to get even bigger.
