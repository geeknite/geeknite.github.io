## The Core Game: Cyclades

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of the expansions, let’s take a quick refresher on what makes Cyclades such a beloved game. Cyclades is a fast-paced strategy game set in the Greek islands where players compete to build cities, armies, and fleets while gaining the favor of the gods. The objective? Conquer two islands and build two metropolises.

The game’s mechanics include bidding for the favor of gods like Zeus, Athena, and Poseidon, each granting special abilities that can drastically alter the game's dynamics. The bidding aspect adds a layer of strategy and unpredictability that keeps players on their toes.

**Pro Tip:** Always keep an eye on your opponents' bids—Zeus might just grant them the ability to crush your fleet when you least expect it.

## Cyclades Expansions: More Gods, More Chaos

The **Cyclades expansions** take an already fantastic game and crank it up to eleven. If you thought the base game was intense, wait until you see what these expansions bring to the table. Here’s a breakdown of each expansion and what it adds to the game.

### Cyclades: Hades

![Cyclades Hades](https://i.imgur.com/CycladesHades.jpg){: .align-right }

**Hades** introduces the god of the underworld and his minions into the fray. This expansion adds a whole new dimension to the game with undead armies that can be summoned to do your bidding. The favor of Hades can be a game-changer, especially if you find yourself on the losing side of a major battle.

**Funny Moment:** During one game, a player summoned an army of undead right before losing their last fleet. The laughter was only matched by the groans of everyone else at the table as they realized the undead were about to wreak havoc.

### Cyclades: Titans

![Cyclades Titans](https://i.imgur.com/CycladesTitans.jpg){: .align-left }

**Titans** adds the powerful monsters and titans to the game, allowing players to summon these colossal beings to fight for them. The expansion also includes new island tiles and additional game mechanics that make the strategy even deeper.

**Pro Tip:** Always expect the unexpected—those titans can change the dynamics of the game in ways you never imagined.

### Cyclades: Monuments

![Cyclades Monuments](https://i.imgur.com/CycladesMonuments.jpg){: .align-right }

**Monuments** brings new buildings that can be erected, providing players with unique advantages and abilities. These monuments can turn the tide of battle and introduce new strategic options for players.

**Gaming Insight:** Building monuments isn't just about gaining new abilities; it’s about blocking your opponents from getting them first.

### Cyclades: Heroes

![Cyclades Heroes](https://i.imgur.com/CycladesHeroes.jpg){: .align-left }

**Heroes** introduces legendary figures from Greek mythology who can join your armies and fleets, providing powerful abilities and bonuses. These heroes can lead your forces to victory or save your cities from destruction.

**Funny Moment:** Watching your opponent get overconfident because they have Achilles in their army, only to see him taken down by a surprise naval assault.
