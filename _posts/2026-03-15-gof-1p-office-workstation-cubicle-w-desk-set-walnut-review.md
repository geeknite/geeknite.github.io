## Maintenance and Cleaning

### Cleaning routines for the walnut surface

A soft microfiber cloth with a mild wood-safe cleaner will keep the Walnut finish looking rich without too much elbow grease. Avoid harsh solvents that could dull the veneer. A weekly wipe-down helps preserve the finish and prevents the built-up grime from becoming a battle between your desk and your keyboard. A small tip: if you spray a little cleaner onto the cloth rather than directly onto the surface, you reduce patchy finishes and keep your wood looking like it belongs in a showroom rather than a campus library.

### Cable management upkeep

Occasionally, you might need to tidy the cable channels to prevent a small cable avalanche. A quick reroute with the included clips will have your wires behaving, which is essential if you want to avoid the suspicious humming sounds when you start up a heavy program. Regularly check the connectors and test your power strip to ensure nothing starts fizzing in the night. Your future self will thank you for that small responsibility.
