## What is Heroes of Normandie: US Army Box?

### The Basics

Heroes of Normandie: US Army Box is a standalone expansion for the popular strategy board game Heroes of Normandie. This box focuses on the brave soldiers of the US Army and brings new units, scenarios, and challenges to the game. With beautifully designed components and a rich historical theme, this expansion is perfect for fans of tactical combat and World War II history.

### Components

The US Army Box includes:

- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures representing the US Army units, including infantry, tanks, and heroes.
- **Tiles**: Various terrain tiles to create diverse and challenging battlefields.
- **Cards**: Action cards that add strategic depth and surprise elements to the game.
- **Tokens**: Tokens for tracking health, objectives, and special abilities.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Heroes of Normandie: US Army Box is like preparing for a military operation. Lay out the terrain tiles, assemble your units, and choose your scenario. Make sure to keep your dog tags handy and don't forget to bring snacks – war is hungry work.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions based on the units and cards they have. You can move, attack, use special abilities, and support your comrades. The key to victory is strategic planning and effective teamwork. Remember, there's no "I" in "Army" – but there is in "Victory"!

### Combat

Combat in Heroes of Normandie: US Army Box is intense and exhilarating. Players must coordinate their attacks, use cover effectively, and manage their resources wisely. Whether you're leading a tank assault or sneaking through enemy lines as a lone hero, every decision counts. Just watch out for friendly fire – it's not as friendly as it sounds.

## The Story

### Scenarios

The US Army Box introduces new scenarios inspired by real historical battles. From storming Omaha Beach to securing strategic locations in the Normandy countryside, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different strategies. History buffs will appreciate the attention to detail and historical accuracy.

### Characters

This expansion brings a host of new characters to the battlefield. Play as iconic heroes like Captain Miller or Sergeant Rock, each with special abilities and unique combos. The diversity of units and characters keeps the game fresh and exciting.
