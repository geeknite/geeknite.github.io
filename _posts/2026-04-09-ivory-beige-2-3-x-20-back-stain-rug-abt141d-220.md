## Maintenance and care tips

- Vacuum weekly to prevent dirt from embedding in the fibers. Use gentle suction to protect the pile height.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid scrubbing to prevent pushing the liquid deeper.
- Test stain removers on a small area first, then treat the stain and blot again.
- Use a rug pad underneath to prevent slipping, reduce wear, and insulate against floor noise.
- Rotate the rug every few months for even wear and fade distribution.
