## Size and Fit: Custom Size vs 8x10
### Dimensions that actually matter
- Custom Size: Define exact length and width. Ideal for rooms with unconventional layouts, or for perfectly framing a desk, sofa, or entertainment center. The custom option minimizes awkward gaps and ensures you’re not fighting for space around chair legs.
- 8x10: The workhorse standard. At about 96 inches by 120 inches, this size is a staple for medium-to-large rooms. It binds seating areas, provides a soft runway for foot traffic, and helps keep cables and cords visually contained.

Low-profile options offer simplicity in movement and maintenance but trade some cushion for practicality. ## Performance, Durability, and Care
### How it wears
Cotton-wool blends are generally durable enough for everyday life while staying comfortable underfoot. Expect good resilience against moderate foot traffic and gaming chair inertia. The rug will show some wear in the most-traveled zones, which can add character if you’re into a lived-in look.

### Cleaning and maintenance
- Vacuum on a gentle setting with a brush attachment to avoid fiber pull.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent color transfer.
- For stains, use a mild detergent solution and test a hidden area first to ensure colorfastness.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and color uniformity.

### Allergens and breathability
Cotton-wool blends can provide a breathability edge that helps with overall air quality in a busy room. If you’re worried about dust, a reliable vacuum with adjustable height can help you keep the rug fresh without shredding any fibers. Regular cleaning and a mindful approach to pet and kid traffic will maximize the rug’s lifespan.

## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Tempo pristine
### Routine care
Vacuum regularly, rotate occasionally, and spot-clean as needed. A well-maintained rug stays vibrant longer and helps your room feel intentional rather than an accidental collection of pieces.

### Spot treatment tips
Blot spills, don’t rub. For stubborn marks, use a mild detergent solution with a cautious test area. If you encounter persistent stains or odors, consider a professional cleaning service after several months of heavy use.

### Color retention and aging
A well-kept cotton-wool blend can retain color well with proper care. If your space experiences strong sun exposure, think about UV-protective window treatments to minimize color fade over time. The rug’s longevity will improve with routine care and responsible placement in high-traffic zones.
