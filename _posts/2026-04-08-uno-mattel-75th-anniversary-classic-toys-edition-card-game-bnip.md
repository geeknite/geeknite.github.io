### Rules Snapshot for Quick Reference

- Objective: Get rid of all your cards.
- On your turn: you may play a card that matches the color or the number of the top card on the discard pile. If you can’t play, you draw one card.
- Special cards: Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, Wild, and Wild Draw Four create the classic strategic pressure that makes UNO both chaotic and enormously entertaining.
- Call UNO when you have one card left. Do not forget, or you’ll be hit with a penalty—usually drawing two extra cards.
- In the 75th Anniversary edition, optional house rules offer longer matches and more inventive penalties, but you can still play straightforward UNO if you’re in a hurry to settle a score.
