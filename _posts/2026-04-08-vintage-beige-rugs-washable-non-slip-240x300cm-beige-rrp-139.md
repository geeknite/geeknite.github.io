## Maintenance Tips for Your Beige Bastion
- Regular vacuuming is your friend. A weekly pass will keep surface dirt from embedding and maintain that plush feel.
- Treat spills quickly. Blot—don’t rub—with a clean cloth, then use a mild detergent solution if needed.
- Wash as recommended by the manufacturer. For oversized rugs, you may need a professional wash or a large-capacity machine. Always check your care label.
- Air-dry thoroughly. A damp rug is a magnet for mildew and curious pets. If possible, dry flat and out of direct sunlight to prevent color fading.

A light rotation every few months can help prevent uneven wear and keep the beige tone evenly distributed across the pile. If your space has heavy foot traffic in certain zones, consider pairing the rug with a stylish rug pad to reduce wear and maintain shape integrity over time.
