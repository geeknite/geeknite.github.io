## Maintenance and Longevity Tips

To keep your table performing well, adopt a few simple practices:

- Clean the surface after use to remove sweat, oil, and stray snacks. A microfiber cloth is gentle and effective.
- Inspect the net and posts for wear. Replace if sagging starts to degrade the playing experience.
- Use protective covers when storing outdoors for extended periods.
- Rotate usage between indoor and outdoor settings to distribute wear and tear evenly.
- Tighten any screws or bolts periodically to avoid looseness that leads to wobble.

With proper care, your mid-size foldable table can stay in good shape for many seasons and hosting countless memes-worthy rallies.
