## Safety and side effects
No medication is 100% risk-free, and dewormers are no exception. Here’s the real-talk version:

- Common side effects: Some dogs experience mild GI upset, transient vomiting, or a temporary decrease in appetite. If these symptoms persist beyond a day or two, contact your vet.
- Rare side effects: Allergic reactions (itchiness, swelling, hives) or more serious GI disturbances could occur, though these are not common. If you see any signs of a severe reaction, seek veterinary care immediately.
- Precautions: Do not use if your dog has a known hypersensitivity to praziquantel, pyrantel embonate, or any other ingredients. If your dog is pregnant, nursing, or has pre-existing health issues, involve your vet in the decision to deworm and choose the appropriate product.

Important general rule: dewormers are part of a broader parasite control strategy. They don’t replace routine fecal checks, good hygiene, and preventive measures like regular heartworm prevention where indicated.
