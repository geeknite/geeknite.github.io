## Cleaning and Maintenance: Keeping It Fresh Without a Full Spa Day
Cleaning a runner is one of those tasks people often underestimate until the first coffee stain hits. Here’s how to keep this Safavieh runner looking sharp:
- Vacuum regularly on a low setting. Avoid high suction if the vacuum has sharp beater bars that could snag the fibers.
- Treat spills as they happen. Blot with a clean cloth, then use a mild detergent solution on the spot. Rinse with a clean, damp cloth to remove any soap residue and blot dry.
- For tougher stains, test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure colorfastness before proceeding.
- Periodic deep cleaning is a good idea, especially in high-traffic zones. A professional cleaning every 12–18 months can extend the rug’s life in busy homes.
- Do not use harsh chemicals or bleach on the fibers; this can compromise color and fiber integrity.

Colorfastness and maintenance go hand in hand. In spaces that receive a lot of natural light, colors can experience slight fading over years. The Grey & Ivory combo tends to age gracefully, but if you have sunlit corridors, rotation of decor and light control can help retain the color story you love today.
