## Strategy and play tips

Exploding Kittens is as much about risk management as it is about card play. Here are a few tips for handling the NSFW edition without losing your mind or your dignity:

- Remember that defuse cards are precious. Use them to survive the right moment, not the first moment you think you might want to save them for later. The NSFW deck adds an extra layer as some defuses tilt the game toward a more chaotic finish.
- Keep an eye on the table chatter. In party games, the social deduction moment is often as valuable as the cards. The NSFW flavor adds to that dynamic but also can become a distraction. If your group tends to derail with side banter, set a light pace and decide that the first person to explode buys the next round of snacks.
- The new cards affect timing. Some new draw cards force you to take more draws or to skip a turn. Use those mind games to your advantage and force the person who is currently winning to pivot their strategy.
- If you are unsure about pace in a mixed crowd, try a few rounds with house rules to establish tempo. A common approach is to play a best of five rounds and declare a winner only when someone hits a small, non dramatic explosion threshold.
