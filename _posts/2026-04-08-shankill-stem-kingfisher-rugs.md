## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity Tips
Here are practical tips to keep your Shankill Stem Kingfisher Rug looking fresh for years:
- Vacuum weekly with a brush attachment on low-to-medium suction. This helps lift the pile and prevent flattening in high-traffic zones.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing; it can push stains deeper into the fibers.
- Treat pets’ paws—if you have a curious feline or canine companion, consider a washable spot cleaner for occasional mishaps.
- Use a rug pad underneath. The pad reduces shifting, adds comfort, and prolongs the rug’s life by distributing weight more evenly.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear across the surface. This is especially important in rooms with direct sunlight or heavy foot traffic in specific areas.
- Protect from direct sun when possible. Prolonged UV exposure can fade color over time. If your room has a sunny corner, consider window treatments that balance light without blocking the rug’s character.

Maintenance isn’t glamorous, but it’s the difference between a rug that looks worn after a year and one that remains a reliable anchor in your space. The Shankill Stem Kingfisher Rug is friendly to the basic care routine, which makes it a dependable choice for busy households.
