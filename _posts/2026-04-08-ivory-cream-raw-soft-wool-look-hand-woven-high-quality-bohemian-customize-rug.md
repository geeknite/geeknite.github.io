### Practicality and Care
A rug this size and texture deserves practical care that matches its high quality. Here are tips to keep it looking classy and comfortable for years:
- Vacuum regularly with a low suction setting to avoid yanking fibers; use a gentle attachment for edges
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber lift
- Use a wool-safe cleaner only after testing in a hidden corner; apply with a light hand and blot, not scrub
- Rotate the rug periodically to promote even wear, especially in high-traffic zones such as near entryways or under a dining table
- Place a non-slip rug pad underneath to minimize movement and edge curling; this also helps protect floors from scratches

### Sizes and Fit for Your Space
Size planning is essential when you are choosing a rug with boho charm. You want a piece that anchors the seating area without overpowering the room. A common approach is to let the rug extend beneath the furniture so it creates a unified footprint, but in small rooms you may opt for a smaller patch that still feels cohesive with surrounding pieces.

Here is a practical sizing framework you can adapt:
- Living room: aim for a rug that fits under the front legs of the main seating group, or a rug large enough to sit under all major furniture for a grounded feel
- Dining room: the rug should extend beyond the chairs when pulled out to allow comfortable seating and easy movement
- Bedroom: consider a rug that peeks out from under the bed by at least 18 inches on each side for a soft landing zone
