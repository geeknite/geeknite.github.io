## Installation and compatibility with PS5
### Compatibility notes
The PS5 M.2 expansion slots support PCIe 4.0 x4 NVMe drives with a 2230 to 2280 length. The WD_BLACK SN850 2TB with heatsink is a snug fit in most PS5 units, and it’s designed to meet the PS5’s clearance and thermal guidelines. Do keep in mind that some early PS5 models and certain OEM console revisions may have tight tolerances for heat sinks. If you’re uncertain, check your PS5’s interior dimensions and verify the heatsink height before purchase.

### Installation steps (quick guide)
1) Power off the PS5 completely and unplug it. 2) Remove the side panel and locate the M.2 expansion slot cover. 3) Remove the screw securing the expansion slot cover and gently lift the cover. 4) Carefully insert the WD_BLACK SN850 with heatsink into the M.2 slot, aligning notches. 5) Fasten the drive with the screw you removed earlier. 6) Reattach the slot cover and the side panel. 7) Power up PS5 and format the new drive as instructed by the console. 8) Install games and data, start gaming, revel in the speeds, and pretend you’ve always had enough space for clip after clip of your latest speedruns.
