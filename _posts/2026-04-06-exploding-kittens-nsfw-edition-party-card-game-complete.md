## How to Play: Core Mechanics and the Chaotic Dance
Understanding Exploding Kittens NSFW Edition is all about embracing the chaos while keeping a few core principles in mind. Here’s a concise walkthrough to get you from zero to exploding-free hero (for at least one round, hopefully).

### The Setup
- Shuffle the deck and deal a hand to each player (the exact number varies by edition and house rules).
- Place the Exploding Kitten cards into the deck, interspersed with Defuse cards so that doom isn’t guaranteed in the first few draws.
- Decide on the number of rounds or play until a winner emerges (often best-of-three in social settings).

### The Turn Structure
- Draw a card from the deck. You can play cards from your hand as you draw, using Defuse cards or strategic actions to alter fate.
- Resolve special cards in order, according to the text on the card. Some cards force you to skip turns; others steal or skip opponents.
- If you draw an Exploding Kitten and don’t Defuse it in time, you’re out for that round. Rinse and repeat until one player remains.

### Defuse and Survival Strategies
Defuse cards are your lifeline. They can be the difference between “you’re out” and “you’re still in, but now you owe the table a story about that one time.” A few strategic notes:
- Use Defuse cards when you’re near the end of a round and a rogue kitten lurks near the top of the deck.
- If you have multiple Defuses, save at least one for the later rounds where probability shifts and the table gets nastier with every card drawn.
- Don’t rely on Defuse-only strategies; combine them with a subset of cunning card plays that force other players to draw more than they want.
