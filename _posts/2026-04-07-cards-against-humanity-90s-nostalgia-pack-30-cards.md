## Play Experience and House Rules
A big part of CAH style is the ability to customize, and the 90s pack plays well with simple house rules. Here are a few ideas to maximize your experience:

- Timed rounds: set a 30 second timer for responses to keep the pace brisk and to add an extra layer of pressure that often yields the best punchlines.
- Theme nights: pick a subtopic within the 90s for a round, such as tech, fashion, or kid culture, and filter prompts accordingly. It is a fun way to build a cohesive narrative around the night.
- Team play: pair up players to brainstorm a few lines for each black card; teams can joke around for extra laughs and then reveal their selected answer. This works well for larger groups or households with a mix of introverts and extroverts.
- Safe humor filter: if some prompts veer into territory that makes players uncomfortable, set a quick store rule to replace or skip those black prompts with a preselected substitute. The goal is to keep the energy inclusive and fun for everyone present.
