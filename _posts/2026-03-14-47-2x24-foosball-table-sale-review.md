### Maintenance and Durability: What It Takes to Keep the Magic Alive

A foosball table is a like a pet dragon: it breathes life, requires care, and occasionally eats your will to live when you forget to oil the rods. In practice, maintenance is straightforward:

- Regularly check rod tension and tighten as needed. Loose rods cause wobble and questionable morale on the field.
- Wipe the playing surface to remove dust, fingerprints, and the occasional heroic streak of spilled beverage. A clean surface translates to truer ball movement.
- Inspect ball returns and ensure no jams occur. A jammed ball can derail a match quicker than you can say penalty kick.
- Lubricate the rods sparingly. Over-lube is a slippery slope into maintenance chaos and misthrown passes.
- Inspect legs for levelness. An uneven table turns even expert shots into comedic tragedies.

Durability questions often revolve around the table’s ability to survive rowdy tournaments. In most cases, the build holds up to enthusiastic play. The lacquer finish stands up to minor scuffs, and the edges are chipped less frequently than your patience during a long game night. A robust warranty helps if your crew discovers a manufacturing issue. Always check the warranty length and coverage for components such as the rods and the playing surface.
