Cable management is all about visibility and ease of access. Use the desk’s channels and grommets to route power cables and data lines in neat, separated planes. If you’re addicted to cable clips, you can always add more—just be mindful of weight distribution and the risk of pulling everything down if someone trips over your headphone cable in the middle of a critical gaming moment.
