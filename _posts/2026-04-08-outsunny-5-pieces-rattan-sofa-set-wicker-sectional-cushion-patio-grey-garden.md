## Weather, maintenance, and longevity

### Sun, rain, and fading
The PE wicker resists UV rays better than many natural fibers, but it will fade a bit over time. Keep expectations reasonable: you will not get a lifetime of showroom shine, but you will get a solid several seasons and a decent resale value if you decide to upgrade. A UV-protective cover during peak sun hours can extend the color life and keep your set looking fresh for longer.

### Cleaning and upkeep
A damp cloth and mild soap are enough for routine cleaning. Cushions unzip for washing; treat stains as soon as possible. For heavy staining or mildew, a gentle cleaning solution or a specialized outdoor upholstery cleaner is recommended. After a season of use, a quick wipe-down and a re-zip of the cushion covers keeps the sofa looking neat and inviting.

### Storage and covers
If you live in a climate with winter weather, consider using outdoor covers or storing cushions indoors to extend their life. A breathable cover helps prevent moisture buildup. Do not stack heavy objects on top of the wicker as it may deform under pressure. A well-fitted cover is your best friend and your wallet will thank you when spring arrives with cushions in good shape rather than flattened relics of a summer gone by.
