## Connectivity, Software, and Features: What the SubMonitor Actually Does
### Ports and power delivery
The 17MT70 relies mainly on USB-C for both power and data transmission. That means fewer cables on your desk and fewer tiny adapters to lose in the couch cushions. Depending on the exact model, you may see PD passthrough that can deliver power to the Gram while it fuels the USB-C display. If you’re plugging into a USB-C hub or a docking station, you’ll likely appreciate additional ports for peripherals. Note that this monitor is designed as a secondary display; it doesn’t replace the need for a primary GPU or a main display and might not support all HDMI devices directly without a USB-C adapter or hub.

### On-device controls and color modes
There are typically on-device controls to adjust brightness, contrast, and sometimes color presets.
