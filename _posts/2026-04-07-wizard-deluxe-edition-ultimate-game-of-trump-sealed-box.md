## Blind Spots, Common Pitfalls, and How to Avoid Them
No game is perfect, and The Original WIZARD Deluxe Edition is no exception. Here are a few things to watch for when you bring it to the table:

- Overplanning your strategy: It’s tempting to craft a flawless arc in your head before the game begins. Reality check: your plan will crumble the moment someone drops a line so savage it wins the round by a simple, well-delivered punchline. Embrace it. The chaos is where the fun hides.
- Rule misunderstandings: If you start arguing about the official rulebook paragraphs instead of playing, you’ve already lost. A moment of shared laughter at the confusion beats a long, dry argument every time.
- Token management: Don’t lose track of trump tokens. They’re small and easily mistaken for other game bits. If you find yourself searching under a couch for a token, you’ve probably had a great night already.

To minimize these, designate a moderator for the first few rounds. Let them be the referee of the room, not the villain in your story. After a couple of games, you’ll find your groove and the table will settle into a rhythm that feels like a well-rehearsed improv troupe.
