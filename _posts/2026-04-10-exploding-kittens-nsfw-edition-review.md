### A quick refresher on Exploding Kittens mechanics
If you already know the original Exploding Kittens, the NSFW Edition keeps the bones but adds more spice. The core loop is simple and perfect for a quick game night: players draw cards from a single deck, with the aim to avoid drawing an exploding kitten unless you have a defuse card. Defuse cards typically require you to disarm the cat with a laser pointer or a bomb that would bemuse any civil engineer, depending on the card art and the joke you’re currently laughing at.

Your turn usually looks like this:
- Play one or more cards from your hand to affect the game state (attack, skip, shuffle, etc.)
- Draw a card from the deck
- End turn, passing to the next player
