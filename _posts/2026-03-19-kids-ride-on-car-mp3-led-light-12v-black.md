## Charging and Battery Maintenance

Battery care matters for the long game. We suggest:

- Charge fully before first use
- Avoid letting the battery completely discharge on a regular basis
- Store in a cool, dry place when not in use
- Use protective covers to prevent dust ingress in outdoor storage

With consistent care, the car should provide months of fun and reliability. If you notice reduced top speed after extended play, you may be dealing with a fatigued battery that is due for replacement. Replacements are widely available from third party suppliers, but be sure to verify compatibility with the vehicle’s control system.
