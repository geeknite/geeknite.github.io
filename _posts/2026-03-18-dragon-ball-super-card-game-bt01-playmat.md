## Care, maintenance, and longevity
Caring for your BT01 Playmat is straightforward. Wipe down any spills promptly with a damp cloth. Avoid using harsh cleaners, which can degrade the print and the surface texture over time. If you’re a neat freak who wants to avoid powdery residue and dust, a quick weekly wipe-down keeps things looking fresh. For stubborn marks, a mild soap solution and a soft cloth will do the trick. Let it air dry completely before rolling or folding for travel.

Stitching longevity is another factor to consider. While the BT01 mat is built to last, you should avoid excessive folding or crunching into tight corners. If you’re carrying it in a bag with other sharp-edged items, consider a protective sleeve. Even champions need armor, right?

If you’re a traveler, a few tips: roll the mat rather than folding it to minimize creases, and store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Prolonged exposure to heat or sun can fade the print and weaken the edges over time.
