## Care and Maintenance Tips
- Vacuum regularly with a beater bar set to a gentle height so you don’t pull up loose fibers.
- Blot spills quickly with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper.
- For spots, test a mild detergent mixed with water on a small corner first.
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and to extend the life of the rug by reducing fiber movement.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color fading from sunlight or lamp exposure.
- A gentle deep-clean every 12–18 months can refresh the fibers without compromising the weave structure.
