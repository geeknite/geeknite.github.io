### Setup in 60 Seconds or Less

The setup is intentionally minimal. You shuffle the 100 card stack, distribute to players according to round number, and remove the rest. The first round sees each player getting one card. In round two you get two cards, and so on. It is worth noting that as you scale up the round number, the tension scales along with it. ### How to Play in the Mind, Not in the Mouth

The actual rules are short, which is why so many Geeknite readers adore it. There is no talk in the Mind. No shouting across the table about strategy, no whispered hints, no telepathic wink that translates to a command like play your lowest card. Players secretly keep their cards, then when the moment is right, they place a card face up into the middle, attempting to maintain a global ascending sequence from 1 to 100. If you fail the sequence, the round ends in a fail and you have to start over.

### Practical Tips for New Players

- Embrace silence: the more comfortable you are with not saying a word, the faster you will move through rounds.
