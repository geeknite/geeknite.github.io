### Maintenance and Care

Maintenance is straightforward and aligns with what you would expect for a budget carbon steel sword. A quick wipe after handling removes fingerprints and moisture. Lightly oil the blade with a non reactive oil to prevent rust; avoid over oiling, which can attract dust and create a sticky film during long display sessions. Do not soak the core or scabbard in water; moisture can seep into the wood and may cause warping or lacquer degradation over time. If you store the sword in its scabbard, check for humidity levels and ensure that you recheck the fit periodically to keep things snug yet not overly tight.
