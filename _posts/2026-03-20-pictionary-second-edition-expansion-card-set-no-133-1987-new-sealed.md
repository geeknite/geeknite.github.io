### Condition and preservation considerations
 sealed packaging is a rare kind of time machine. The No 133 set, being from 1987, invites a preservationist s sensibilities: heat, humidity, and the occasional invading moth can ruin card stock longer than a viral meme, so seales upgrading is a careful business.
