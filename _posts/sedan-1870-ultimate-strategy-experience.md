### Overview

**Sedan 1870** is a historical strategy game that places you in command during the Franco-Prussian War. Specifically, it focuses on the Battle of Sedan, where the fate of nations was decided. The game challenges you to outmaneuver your opponent, manage limited resources, and ultimately, emerge victorious in a historically accurate setting.

This means that not only will you be tested on your strategic prowess, but you'll also get a history lesson along the way.

#### Tactical Depth

The game's tactical depth is where it truly shines. Whether you're commanding the French or the Prussians, you'll need to consider a myriad of factors, such as terrain, troop morale, and supply lines. Every decision you make can have far-reaching consequences, so plan your moves carefully.
