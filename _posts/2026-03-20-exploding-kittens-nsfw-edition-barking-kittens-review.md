### The Comedy of Card Interactions: A Quick Flavor Tour
Let’s break down a few card archetypes you’ll encounter in this duo:

- Barking Kittens: The expansion gives you dog-themed alternatives that tilt risk and reward in clever new ways. It’s a palate cleanser for the cats, a cunning trap for the overconfident, and a reminder that dogs can be surprisingly tactical when they’re not chasing the laser pointer of doom.
