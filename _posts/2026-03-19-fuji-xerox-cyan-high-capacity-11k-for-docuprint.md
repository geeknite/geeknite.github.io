## Troubleshooting: Common Issues and Fixes

### Common issues
- Print quality degrade: check cartridge seating, run calibration again, and ensure the printer firmware is up to date.
- Color gaps: reinsert the cartridge or reseat to fix alignment issues, then run a test page.
- Smudges on color pages: check paper quality and humidity; toner may smear if the paper moisture is high.
- Mismatched cyan tint: adjust the color balance in the printer settings. If you see a persistent bias toward green or blue, recalibrate.

### Quick fixes checklist
- Reseat the cyan cartridge and run auto calibration.
- Use a clean, lint-free cloth to wipe the cartridge area if you suspect dust.
- Ensure the printer is not jammed or obstructed in the color path.
- If issues persist, consult the vendor support page or contact a service technician.
