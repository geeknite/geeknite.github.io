## Design and Materials

### Cleaning and Upkeep
Maintenance is easier if you build a routine. Wipe the wicker with a damp cloth every few weeks, wash cushions as needed, and shield covers when not in use. Avoid high-pressure washers on the wicker weave and cushions, as that can push water into seams and cause premature wear. A yearly treat with a mild soap solution and a soft brush will keep the surface free of grime that builds up from outdoor life. If you store cushions seasonally, rotate colors to prevent uneven fading in direct sun spots.

## How to Care for Your Wicker Outdoors
- Keep cushions protected and sun exposed only when necessary. Use the storage cover during weather swings. 
- Clean the wicker with a soft brush and mild soap solution. Rinse with gentle water to avoid soap buildup in seams. 
- Inspect the weave periodically for loose strands and repair promptly to avoid unraveling. 
- Use UV protective sprays sparingly, and always test on a small area first. 
- Bring cushions indoors during heavy snow or prolonged rain to preserve fabric.
