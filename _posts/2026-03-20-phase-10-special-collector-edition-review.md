## Gameplay and Rules, with Collector''s Edition Flavor
Phase 10 in its classic form is a light, looping card game that cycles through ten predetermined phases. Each phase challenges players to assemble a specific combination of cards: sets, runs, and mixes that fit the phase criteria. The Special Collector''s Edition keeps the core mechanic intact but introduces some quality-of-life improvements and optional twists to keep long sessions engaging.

### How Phase 10 Works (a quick refresher)
- Each player gets a hand of cards and follows a turn order.
- On your turn, you draw and discard; your goal is to complete the phase you are currently working on.
- When you finish a phase, you advance to the next one and can begin completing it on subsequent turns.
- The first player to complete all ten phases wins.

These mechanics translate cleanly into a Special Collector''s Edition that feels more tactile and presentational. The upscale components don''t change the math of Phase 10; they change the experience. It''s the difference between watching a sci-fi saga in a standard TV and in a home-theater setup with a popcorn budget that matches the film budget. The rules themselves remain approachable for newcomers, while veteran players enjoy a nicer surface to justify their obsession with phase-tracking and card ordering.
