## Maintenance and care: keeping the glow without the drama
A proper care routine is essential for any diamond ring, especially one with a vintage design and a 10k base. Here are a few practical tips that align with Geeknite’s practical humor:

- Cleaning: Soapy lukewarm water and a soft brush are your friends. Gently scrub the ring to remove oils and dirt. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could scratch engraving or the metal surface.
- Ultrasonic cleaners: They can loosen settings if the ring has prongs or delicate components. If you are a heavy user of ultrasonic devices, you might want to skip them for this particular ring or consult your jeweler first.
- Jewellery storage: Store the ring in a soft pouch or a lined jewelry box to prevent scratches from other pieces. The engraving wants to stay crisp, not marred by other metals.
- Regular checkups: Have your setting inspected periodically. It’s a good habit to confirm the stone sits secure, especially if the band is subject to daily wear. A quick safety check now can save you from a dramatic, middle-of-the-night panic later.
