## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

To keep the beast looking as good as it performs, a simple care routine goes a long way. Regular dusting with a soft cloth preserves the veneer; avoid harsh cleaners that can strip the finish. For the metal parts, a gentle wipe with a damp cloth followed by a dry microfiber helps maintain the matte finish and prevents corrosion over years of use. If you spill coffee, act fast—damp microfiber first, then a dry cloth. Because in the world of coffee and code reviews, timing is everything. For cables, set up a routine once every quarter to re-optimize routing as teams shift, screens migrate, and new gadgets enter the space. The main enemy here is chaos; the secret weapon is a well thought out cable plan and a dedicated technician who appreciates a clean desk just as much as a clean commit history.
