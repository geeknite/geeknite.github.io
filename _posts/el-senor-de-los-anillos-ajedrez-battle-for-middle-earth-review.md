## What is El Señor de los Anillos Ajedrez: Battle for Middle Earth?

El Señor de los Anillos Ajedrez: Battle for Middle Earth is a themed chess set that brings the characters from J.R.R. Tolkien's beloved series to life on the chessboard. Imagine Gandalf as your king and Frodo as your knight. This isn't just chess; it's a battle for Middle Earth.

### The Basics

In this game, players use intricately designed pieces representing characters from The Lord of the Rings. The rules are the same as traditional chess, but the added fantasy elements make it a unique experience.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Chess Pieces**: Each piece is a detailed character from the series. From the noble Gandalf to the menacing Sauron, every piece adds to the immersion.
- **Chessboard**: The board itself is designed to resemble Middle Earth, complete with landmarks such as Mount Doom and Rivendell.
