## Maintenance, Cleaning & Everyday Care

No rug review would be complete without a chapter on dos and don’ts. The Parker Cream isn’t a white-glove celebrity; it’s more like a reliable friend who enjoys a coffee stain in a non-dramatic way. Here are the essentials:

- Vacuum weekly to prevent grit from turning into sandpaper on the pile. Use a gentle setting and a nozzle that doesn’t aggressively pull at fibers.
- Blot, don’t rub, if you spill coffee, juice, or that mysterious sauce your partner swears was “just a drizzle.” Acting fast reduces staining risk.
- Treat with a spot-clean solution designed for synthetic blends if you need to address spills. Test in an inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate every few months to promote even wear and prevent color fading from sun exposure in the same patch of sunlight.

If you’re the sort who loves pro tips, consider a rug pad beneath it for extra padding and to minimize bunching. The result is a room that feels curated and comfortable—without turning the floor into a trampoline for your guests.
