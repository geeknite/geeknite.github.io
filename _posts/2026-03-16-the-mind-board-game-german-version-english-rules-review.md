Instead, you and your fellow players must channel your inner telepaths and play numbered cards in ascending order without communicating directly. It’s basically a group therapy session for people who can count and who enjoy catching their own mistakes before the universe does. The German Complete Version adds a polished physical package and, crucially, included English rules that make the experience accessible to non-German speakers who still want to enjoy the quiet drama of a perfectly timed card drop.
