## Gameplay

### The Basics

*Battle-Automata: Mechanicum Legions* is a tactical warfare board game where players command legions of mechanized units in battle. The game is set in a dystopian future where the Mechanicum, a faction of highly advanced robots, fight for supremacy. With stunning artwork, detailed miniatures, and a plethora of strategic options, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of various Mechanicum units. Each unit has unique abilities and stats, making for a diverse and dynamic battlefield.
- **Game Board**: A modular game board that can be configured in multiple ways, ensuring no two battles are ever the same.
- **Cards**: Each unit has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Those claws are the real supervillains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up *Battle-Automata: Mechanicum Legions* is like staging a futuristic warzone. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your units. Now you're ready to begin your quest for domination.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their units' abilities. You can move, attack, deploy special tactics, and more. Cooperation and strategy are key, so plan your moves wisely and coordinate with your teammates.

### Combat

Combat in *Battle-Automata: Mechanicum Legions* is a blend of strategy and luck. Each unit's attack can be modified by drawing a card from your personal attack modifier deck. Did you plan an epic attack just to draw a card that reduced your damage to zero? Welcome to the mechanized battlefield, where plans often go awry.

### Units and Abilities

One of the best parts of *Battle-Automata: Mechanicum Legions* is the diverse array of units and their abilities. From towering Titan-class robots to swift Scout units, you can command a variety of mechanized forces, each with their own strengths and weaknesses.

With multiple units, abilities, and game board configurations, *Battle-Automata: Mechanicum Legions* offers endless replayability. No two games are the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more mechanized mayhem.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new units, abilities, and missions. Your mechanized army is about to get even bigger.
