## The Basics

### What is Abstract Donjon - Initiative?

Abstract Donjon - Initiative is an innovative and mind-bending board game where players take on the roles of powerful wizards navigating through a magical donjon (which is a fancy word for dungeon, but with more flair). The game is known for its abstract strategy and unique mechanics that will keep you scratching your head and plotting your next move.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Wizard Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of various wizards, each with their own unique abilities.
- **Hexagonal Tiles**: These tiles make up the donjon and can be rearranged for endless replayability.
- **Spells and Artifacts Cards**: Each card brings new spells and artifacts into play, adding depth to your strategy.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and magical energy. Just make sure your cat doesn’t think they’re snacks.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Abstract Donjon - Initiative feels like preparing for a magical ritual. Lay out the hexagonal tiles to form the donjon, choose your wizard, and distribute spells and artifacts. Now, you’re ready to dive into the arcane fun.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and perform actions based on their wizard’s abilities. You can cast spells, collect artifacts, and navigate through the donjon. The key is to be as strategic as possible while keeping an eye on your opponents’ moves.

### Combat

Combat in Abstract Donjon - Initiative is a blend of strategy and luck. Each wizard has unique abilities that add layers of complexity to battles. Did you plan an epic spell combo only to have your opponent counter it? Welcome to the magical mayhem!

## The Arcane Awesomeness

### Replayability

With multiple wizards, spells, and donjon layouts, Abstract Donjon - Initiative offers endless replayability. No two games are the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more arcane battles.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new wizards, spells, and artifacts. Your magical universe is about to get even more enchanted.

## Pros and Cons
