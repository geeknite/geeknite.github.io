## Strategy and deck-building tips

If you are venturing into Set 11, the overarching tip is to embrace synergy and avoid overcommitting to a single engine. The universal angle invites cross-franchise ideas, so think about building around cards that support multiple universes rather than those that demand a single, fragile combo. Here are a few practical tips:

- Start with a flexible leader that gives you options on turn 1 through 3. Avoid leaders that require a long setup unless you truly enjoy the long con.
- Build a balanced energy curve. You want enough energy generation to cast your key plays, but you also need to avoid being flooded when you need a specific resource later in the game.
- Include a couple of disruption options. A counterplay card or two can swing momentum in a control-heavy matchup, preventing you from losing to a single large burst.
- Plan for common matchups. If your playgroup gravitates toward a particular archetype, consider sideboarding or having a parallel plan that can adapt mid-game.
