Keep the desk looking pristine with a quick wipe using a soft cloth and a mild cleaner. Avoid abrasive materials that might dull the bright white surface. When rearranging for a reversible layout, take photos of the cable routing patterns to help you replicate the setup later. If you want to refresh the surface, a gentle wood-safe cleaner or a dedicated whiteboard cleaner can help restore the surface shine without leaving fuzzy residue.
