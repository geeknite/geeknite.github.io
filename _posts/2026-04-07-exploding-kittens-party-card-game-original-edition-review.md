### How to play in a few sentences

- The goal is simple: avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten. If you draw one, you must either defuse or face elimination, depending on your luck and the circumstances. 
- On your turn you can play action cards to alter the draw dynamics, peek at future cards, or lean on allies to turn the tide. 
- Cat cards create partnerships or mischievous combos that can swing rounds in surprising ways. 
- The Defuse card saves you from a wrong turn, but there are limits and the group will remind you of them in the loudest possible way.

The elegance of the system is that it scales. With more players, the potential for chaos grows, and the table dynamics become more important than the exact card in anyone's hand. This is not a game to huff about if you like strict rules; it is a game to laugh about as you negotiate with friends, call out a bluff, and hope your next draw goes in your favor while your opponent tries to enjoy your misfortune as much as their own.
