## How to Play UNO All Wild (7+ Edition)
A quick primer for the curious and the cautious.

### Setup
- Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards per player.
- Place the remaining cards as a draw pile and flip the top card to start the discard pile.
- If the first card is Wild, follow its effect per the All Wild rules; if not sure, re-shuffle and try again.

### Basic Rules
- Play a card that matches either color or number on the discard pile.
- Wilds can change the color; All Wild cards add new layers of interaction.
- If you can’t play, draw a card from the draw pile and pass.
- When you’re down to one card, shout UNO. If someone calls you out for not saying UNO, you’ll draw extra cards or face a light-hearted penalty.

### Special Cards Guide
- Wild: Change color (and possibly trigger a mood-based effect)
- Skip: Pass the turn to the next player
- Reverse: Reverses the direction of play
- Draw Two / Draw Four: Penalties that can be complemented by All Wild twists
- All Wild: The headliner card with variable effects that depend on the current game vibe

### House Rules and Variants
- Mood-based All Wild: The player who plays All Wild can declare a theme (e.g., superheroes, movie quotes) and others must respond in kind.
- Quick-dare rounds: A short 5-second dare to keep the energy high.
- Team play option: Split players into two teams for a longer game experience
