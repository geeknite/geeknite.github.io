## Weather performance and seasonal use

In terms of weather, bamboo is generally robust but not invincible. Long exposure to heavy rain and humidity can lead to swelling or warping over time if not properly treated. The key to long-term performance is proactive maintenance and storage practices:

- Use protective sealants or oils recommended by the manufacturer to keep the bamboo’s natural fibers hydrated and resistant to moisture.
- When possible, store cushions indoors or use breathable, weather-resistant covers during prolonged rain or winter months.
- Check joints annually for looseness and re-tighten screws as needed to prevent wobble. A wobbly chair not only ruins photos but also your back.

If you live in a climate with strong seasonal shifts, you’ll likely want to bring the cushions inside and cover the furniture during the harsh winter months. In milder climates, the set can stay outdoors year-round with proper maintenance and a little common sense.
