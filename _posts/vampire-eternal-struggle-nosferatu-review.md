## The Basics

### What is Vampire Eternal Struggle?

Vampire Eternal Struggle (or VTES for those in the know) is a multiplayer collectible card game that plunges players into the sinister world of vampire politics and bloodsucking mayhem. In its 5th edition, the game introduces new mechanics, characters, and scenarios, and Nosferatu is here to make sure your nightmares come true.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Cards**: Lots of them! Each card represents vampire abilities, influence, and other spooky stuff.
- **Tokens**: You'll need these to track the amount of blood left in your system before you turn into a vampire popsicle.
- **Board**: The battleground where all the vampire action happens.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up VTES is akin to preparing for a dark ritual—sans the goat sacrifices. Lay out the board, distribute cards, and choose your clan. If you're playing as Nosferatu, be prepared for some serious skulking in dark alleys.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their vampire's abilities. From attacking rival clans to influencing mortal politics, every action counts. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your fellow vampires to suck the competition dry.

### Combat

Combat in VTES is as thrilling as finding yourself in a dark alley with Nosferatu. Each vampire has unique combat mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo only to have Nosferatu laugh in your face? Welcome to the dark side, where plans (and often players) get sucked dry.
