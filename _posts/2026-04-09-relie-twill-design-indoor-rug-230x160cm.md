## Care and Maintenance: Practical, Not Pretentious
- Vacuum weekly to keep fibers standing tall and proud. - Blot spills promptly with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper. - For spot cleaning, use a mild detergent diluted in water; test on a small corner first. - Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to prevent visible patterns from forming where your couch sits. - For more intensive cleaning, professional services are your friends; do not attempt to perform a DIY miracle.
