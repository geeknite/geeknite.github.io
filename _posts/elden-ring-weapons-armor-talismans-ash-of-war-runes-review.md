## Weapons: The Art of Combat

Elden Ring offers a plethora of weapons, each with its distinct playstyle and stats. Whether you prefer slicing through enemies with a katana or smashing them with a colossal hammer, there's a weapon for every Tarnished.

### Best Weapons

- **Rivers of Blood**: A katana that excels in both physical and fire damage. It's perfect for those who enjoy a mix of speed and power.
- **Moonveil**: A magical katana that scales with Intelligence. Ideal for magic users who want to strike with both spells and steel.
- **Great Club**: A massive hammer that deals devastating damage. Perfect for those who prefer brute strength.
- **Erdtree Bow**: A bow that scales with Faith, allowing for ranged holy attacks. Great for keeping enemies at a distance.

## Armor: Shielding the Tarnished

In Elden Ring, the right armor can be the difference between life and death. Armor not only provides protection but also affects your mobility and stamina.

### Best Armor Sets

- **Lionel's Set**: High defense and poise, perfect for tanky builds.
- **Night's Cavalry Armor**: Offers balanced protection and agility, ideal for mounted combat.
- **Spellblade Armor**: Boosts spell damage and provides decent protection for magic users.
- **Godskin Apostle Set**: Provides balanced protection and enhances specific Ashes of War.

![Armor](https://i.imgur.com/armor.jpg)

## Talismans: Enhancing Your Abilities

Talismans in Elden Ring provide various buffs and can significantly enhance your build. Whether you're looking to boost your health, stamina, or damage, there's a talisman for every need.

### Best Talismans

- **Radagon's Scarseal**: Increases Vigor, Endurance, Strength, and Dexterity but also increases damage taken.
- **Erdtree's Favor**: Boosts maximum HP, stamina, and equip load.
- **Green Turtle Talisman**: Increases stamina recovery speed, essential for aggressive playstyles.
- **Flock's Canvas Talisman**: Greatly increases the potency of incantations.

![Talismans](https://i.imgur.com/talismans.jpg)

## Ashes of War: Unleashing Special Powers

Ashes of War are special abilities that can be applied to weapons, providing unique and powerful attacks. These can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

### Best Ashes of War

- **Bloodhound's Step**: Allows for quick and evasive movements, perfect for dodging enemy attacks.
- **Sacred Blade**: Imbues your weapon with holy energy, dealing extra damage to undead and other dark creatures.
- **Flaming Strike**: Engulfs your weapon in flames, adding fire damage to your attacks.
- **Thunderbolt**: Summons a bolt of lightning to strike your foes, ideal for Faith builds.

![Ashes of War](https://i.imgur.com/ashesofwar.jpg)

## Runes: The Currency of the Lands Between

Runes are essential in Elden Ring, serving as both currency and the means to level up. Knowing how to efficiently farm runes can make your journey much smoother.

### Best Rune Farming Spots

- **Mohgwyn Palace**: The area near the Palace Approach Ledge-Road Site of Grace is filled with enemies that drop a significant amount of runes.
- **Stormveil Castle**: The courtyard is teeming with soldiers and birds that can be farmed for runes.
- **Liurnia of the Lakes**: The large crabs and enemies near the Academy Gate Town are excellent for rune farming.
