Playing with a factory-sealed deck is not only about the cards—it’s about how you handle them. Here are a few practical tips to maximize your enjoyment and longevity of the Legendary Modern Decks 2026.

- Use proper sleeves: The deck benefits from sleeves that offer both protection and a clean look on camera. Clear or light-spined sleeves work well for showing off the foil accents while protecting the stock from thumb dents.
- Shuffle with care: While the cards feel sturdy, you don’t want to nick the foil or the text. A solid shuffle is a blend of overhand and riffle with a gentle touch—especially after you’ve witnessed that one “lucky break” that propelled a win.
