## Maintenance, Longevity, and Care Tips

Your Phase 10 Card Holder will outlive your questionable phase strategies if you treat it right. Here are a few practical tips:

- Clean it with a damp cloth after spicy game nights, especially if snacks of questionable origin were involved.
- Avoid harsh chemicals; some acrylic surfaces can get foggy with the wrong cleaners.
- If you notice wobble, check the feet. A quick leveling tweak can restore stability without breaking your back and budget.
- Store it in a dry place; moisture is the enemy of plastic or acrylic over time, and we don’t want to see your card holder warp into a pretzel of regret.

### Cleaning Best Practices

- Wipe with a microfiber cloth to avoid micro-scratches.
- For sticky residue from spilled drinks, use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth, then dry thoroughly.
- Remove the cards before storage to prevent stress on the slots, which can lead to misalignment after repeated use.
