### Cleaning, Care, and Maintenance
The care routine for a rug like this isn’t rocket science, but it’s not as simple as “spot-clean and forget.” Regular vacuuming with a gentle setting helps maintain pile integrity and keeps your foot traffic happy. Rotate the rug occasionally to encourage even wear, especially if one side has more furniture weight or if your cat has discovered a favorite sunlit corner. For spills, quick blotting with a clean cloth is your best friend. Use a mild detergent if needed, but always test on a small inconspicuous area first to avoid any dye migration or color shifts that might turn your living room into a living science experiment.

If you have pets, you’ll appreciate that the fiber blend tends to resist staining better than a pure acrylic or nylon rug.
