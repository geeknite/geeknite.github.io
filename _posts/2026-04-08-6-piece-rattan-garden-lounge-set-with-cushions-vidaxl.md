## Durability, weather, and care

Outdoor furniture lives outdoors. It is not glamorous, but it is honest work. The VidaXL set is designed to hold up to sun and rain better than your typical interior couch, but it is not invincible. Here are practical care notes to extend the life of your set:
- Clean regularly. A damp cloth or a gentle spray with water will remove dust and pollen. A mild soap solution is fine for tougher grime.
- Protect cushions in heavy rain. If you do not have a cover, store the cushions or bring them indoors during prolonged downpours to prevent moisture buildup.
- Check the weave periodically. If you see loose fibers, address them early to prevent fraying.
- Consider a protective cover for winter or off-season storage. It can dramatically extend life when you are not using the set for months at a time.

A key point for any outdoor furniture: you will get the most longevity if you use common sense and a little maintenance. VidaXL’s set is designed to be resilient, but it still appreciates some TLC and a yearly glow-up with a soft-bristle brush and a careful eye for the weave health.
