## Durability, weather, and care
Outdoor furniture lives outdoors. It’s not a glamorous life, but it’s honest work. The VidaXL set is designed to hold up to sun and rain better than your typical interior couch, but it’s not invincible. Here are practical care notes to extend the life of your set:
- Clean regularly. A damp cloth or a gentle spray with water will remove dust and pollen. A mild soap solution is fine for tougher grime.
- Protect cushions in heavy rain. If you don’t have a cover, store the cushions or bring them indoors during prolonged downpours to prevent moisture buildup.
- Check the weave periodically. If you see loose fibers, address them early to prevent fraying.
- Consider a protective cover for winter or off-season storage. It can dramatically extend life when you’re not using the set for months at a time.

A key point for any outdoor furniture: you’ll get the most longevity if you use common sense and a little maintenance. VidaXL’s set is designed to be resilient, but it’s still a set that appreciates respect and occasional TLC.
