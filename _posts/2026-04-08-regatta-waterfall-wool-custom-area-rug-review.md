Durability and stain resistance
Wool fibers have natural resilience and will bounce back after compression. The Waterfall weave is designed to avoid visible crushing in high-traffic zones, which means your rug should hold its shape longer than a cheaper synthetic rival. When it comes to spills, act fast. Blot with a dry cloth and use a wool-safe cleaner for stains. Avoid rubbing, as this can drive stains deeper into the fiber. We tested a few common kitchen spills and found that moderate cleaning preserves the look and feel of the rug.

Care and maintenance
Vacuum regularly on a gentle setting; avoid aggressive beater bars that can fray the surface. Rotate the rug every 6–12 months to promote even wear. Spot clean promptly for liquid spills and consider professional cleaning every year or two, depending on foot traffic. Sun exposure should be minimized to maintain color saturation, as UV light can gradually fade the gradient.
