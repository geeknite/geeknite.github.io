## How to integrate Fortune Row into an existing campaign
If you already run City of Mist, Fortune Row slots in cleanly in several ways. Here are practical ideas to maximize the utility of the pack without disrupting your campaign rhythm.

### Quick start tips
- Introduce fortune prompts at the end of a session to seed future threads. A prompt read aloud in that moment can keep players thinking about upcoming reveal possibilities and keep the next session feeling connected to what happened last.
- Use prop slips to anchor a single location. If your group visits a club or a back alley a lot, a set of slips tied to that location can help create a sense of place without another long narration by the GM.
- Assign tokens to characters whose paths are becoming central to the current mystery. The tokens become visual reminders for everyone at the table and can speed up decision making during tense moments.

### Campaign pacing and arc structure
Fortune Row is especially useful during the mid to late act when the true nature of the conspiracy starts to emerge.
