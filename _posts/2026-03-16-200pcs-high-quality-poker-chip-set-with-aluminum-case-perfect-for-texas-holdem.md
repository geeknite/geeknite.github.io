## Maintenance and Care
A long-term investment in a poker set also includes how you treat the chips and the case after the game ends. Here are practical tips to keep your set in tip-top shape:

- Cleaning: Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth if they accumulate fingerprints or dust. Avoid harsh cleaners or solvents that could affect color or edge finish. A mild soapy mist and a gentle wipe can work for heavy grime, but dry immediately.
- Handling: Don’t drop chips onto hard surfaces repeatedly. While they’re built to withstand abuse, repeated hard impacts can chip the edges or cause surface scratches.
- Case upkeep: Keep the aluminum case dry and free of moisture. If you travel in a humid environment, consider a silica gel pack inside the case to reduce moisture buildup. Check the latches periodically and lubricate if necessary with a light, non-resin lubricant.
- Storage: Store the set in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Prolonged sun exposure can fade edge designs and affect color readability over time.

A little maintenance goes a long way toward maintaining the classy feel of your set and preserving its resale value if you ever decide to upgrade or retire it to a guest room where it can be enjoyed by future poker prodigies.
