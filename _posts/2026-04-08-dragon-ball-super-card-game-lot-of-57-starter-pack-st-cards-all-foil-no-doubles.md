### Handling and Storage Tips
Foil cards deserve gentle handling. Use soft sleeves, avoid shoveling them into a tight binder, and store them in a climate-controlled environment. Foils hate humidity and sharp temperature changes about as much as we hate spoilers in our favorite cliffhangers. If you plan to keep these long-term, consider putting them in labeled cases, with a separate stack for common staples vs standout foils. Your future self will thank you when you don’t spend an afternoon trying to separate the adhesive from your fingertips after a sleeve mishap.

## Visuals, Condition, and Display Value

Foil cards can draw positive attention at table side and in photos, but they also require a bit more care in storage and transport. If you’re into showpiece displays, consider a small display binder or a rigid card case that emphasizes the foil finish rather than hiding it behind a crowded page.
