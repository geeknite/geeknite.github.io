### Care and maintenance: keeping the constellation bright

Maintenance is where a product either earns its stripes or reveals its kryptonite. The Virgin rug is designed for straightforward care: regular vacuuming to pick up dust and crumbs, a quick blot of spills with a mild soap solution, and an occasional rotation to ensure even wear across the surface. It’s worth noting that the rug’s colorfastness holds up well under typical lighting and exposure patterns. If you have a sun-facing room, you might want to rotate every few months to prevent uneven fading—a simple trick for preserving the print’s crispness over years.

In terms of maintenance specifics, here are a few pro-tips:
- Blot, don’t rub, when cleaning spills to reduce fiber damage and color migration.
- Use a pointed nozzle on your vacuum to lift the pile and prevent matting in high-traffic zones.
- For set-in stains, test a small hidden area before applying any cleaning solution to ensure it won’t alter the color or pattern.
- Consider a rug pad underneath for extra grip and extended life; the pad helps reduce shifting and protects floors from scratches.
