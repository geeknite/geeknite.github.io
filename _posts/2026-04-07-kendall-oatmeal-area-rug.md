## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity
### Daily Care
Regular vacuuming prevents dirt build-up that could dull the color and texture. The Kendall Oatmeal area rug handles daily vacuuming with ease, thanks to a dense weave that resists the “roller-coaster ride” look when your vacuum is set to high power. Blot spills quickly with a clean cloth; avoid scrubbing as that can push stains deeper into the pile. If you have pets, use a pet-safe cleaner for any accidents and consider spot-cleaning to prevent the stain from setting.

### Stain Management
For stubborn stains, a mild cleaning solution with a gentle blot works best. The goal is to lift the stain rather than blend it into the fibers. Quick action yields better results than dramatic heroics with a bottle of harsh chemicals. If you ever spill coffee on a coding binge or a sauce accident during a pan-packed ramen night, remember this rug is built for such disorders and will forgive you after a proper rinse.

### Maintenance Myths Debunked
- Myth: You must rotate a rug weekly to prevent wear patterns. Reality: rotating every few months is often sufficient; weekly may be excessive unless your living room doubles as a dance floor.
- Myth: High-end rugs demand professional cleaning every six months. Reality: routine vacuuming, spot cleaning, and occasional professional cleaning can extend life without draining your wallet.
- Myth: Shaggy textures hide dirt better. Reality: shaggy rugs show wear in high-traffic areas; Kendall Oatmeal’s clean mid-pile handles traffic while still looking sophisticated.

### Longevity and Warranty
Durability is the name of the game here. With proper care, the Kendall Oatmeal rug should remain a faithful floor companion for years. Check the specific warranty terms from your retailer; many offers include protection against manufacturing defects and some coverage for accidental damage under normal usage. If you live in an area with heavy dust or seasonal changes, a quick note: consistent vacuuming and a mild cleaning routine can help maintain its vibe and longevity.
