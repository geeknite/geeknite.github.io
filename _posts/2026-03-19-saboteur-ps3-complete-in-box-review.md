## Condition, Authentication, and Value in 2026

### How to Verify CIB Status

When you pick up a complete in box Saboteur PS3, you want to ensure that the core components are all present and in serviceable condition. Quick checks include:

- Box integrity: no major dents or tears; spine legible; seal if applicable.
- Disc surface: light scratches are common; check for deep gouges or missing protective coatings.
- Manual: pages intact, no major creases, binding solid.
- Any inserts: warranty cards, registration cards, or any accessories that were expected to be present at the time of release.

A quick boot test on a PS3 console is ideal if you can arrange it, but if you are buying from photos and a seller with a solid feedback history, you can still gauge value reasonably well through physical condition alone.
