## Maintenance and durability

### Care instructions

Meadow Floral is machine washable, which is the modern knight’s decree. Use a gentle cycle with cold water, tumble dry on low or air-dry for best longevity, and avoid high heat that could compromise the plushness. A quick tumble every few washes helps reset the pile and keep it fluffy. If you’re dealing with coffee sips and accidental latte splashes—welcome to the club—pre-treat stains with a mild, color-safe detergent. The floral print tends to hold up well with gentle care, and you won’t see dramatic color bleed unless you deep-clean it in a too-warm bath.

### Durability over time

In our controlled geek-lab life, the Meadow Floral has shown solid resilience. The edges stay intact with standard wear; there’s no rapid unraveling along the hems, and unless you perform a dramatic static-cling experiment with your cat, you shouldn’t expect significant pill formation after a few months. Of course, like any textile, it will show wear after an excessive number of gaming marathons or if you’ve got a pet with an affinity for cords and yarn. For the typical home user, it remains a reliable friend who doesn’t require constant companionship with a lint roller.
