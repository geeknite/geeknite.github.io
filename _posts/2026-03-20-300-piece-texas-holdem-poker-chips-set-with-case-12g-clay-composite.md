## Care and maintenance: keeping chips pristine
Clay-composite chips hold up well, but they’re not invincible to neglect. Here are a few simple tips to keep them looking sharp game after game:
- Wipe with a soft microfiber cloth after use to remove oils from hands. This preserves color fidelity and prevents smudges.
- Avoid harsh solvents or chemical cleaners. Water and a mild soap wipe is usually enough for dust and residue.
- Store in the included case or a padded chip tray to avoid scratches and edge wear.
- If stacking becomes problematic after long sessions, rotate chips when shuffling to reduce edge wear from repeated rubbing against felt.
