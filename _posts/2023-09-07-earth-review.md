## The Basics

### Size and Structure

Earth is a champ when it comes to being the fifth largest planet in the Solar System. With a diameter of about 12,742 km (7,918 miles), it proudly flaunts its size. It consists of several layers, starting from the crust, moving to the mantle, outer core, and finally the inner core. Think of it as a cosmic layer cake, but less delicious and more molten.

### Atmosphere

Our planet's atmosphere is like a protective bubble, composed of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and a sprinkle of argon, carbon dioxide, and other gases. This mix not only makes it possible for us to breathe but also shields us from harmful solar radiation and space debris. So, next time you take a breath, thank the atmosphere! üçÉ

## Unique Features

### Oceans and Water Bodies

Earth is the only planet known to have liquid water on its surface. Oceans cover about 71% of the Earth's surface, making it the ultimate beach destination. With stunning marine life and mysterious depths, our oceans are a treasure trove waiting to be explored.

### Biodiversity

From the lush rainforests to the arid deserts, Earth is teeming with life. With millions of species, our planet hosts an incredible array of flora and fauna. Whether it's the majestic lions of Africa or the tiny hummingbirds of the Americas, biodiversity is Earth's crown jewel.
