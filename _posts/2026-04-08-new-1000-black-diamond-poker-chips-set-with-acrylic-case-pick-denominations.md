## Maintenance: Keeping Your Chips Looking Fresh

Chips in a home environment accumulate fingerprints, dust, and the occasional stray snack crumb from a late-night afterparty. Cleaning is simple: wipe with a soft microfiber cloth, avoid abrasive cleaners, and store in the acrylic case when not in use. If chips pick up a static cling, a quick wipe with a slightly damp cloth is enough, then dry with a clean microfiber. Do not soak chips or submerge the acrylic case; water and electronics never played well together in our experiments.
