## Why Card Games?

Card games are a timeless form of entertainment. They come in various shapes and sizes, fitting any occasion, from family gatherings to competitive tournaments. They're portable, easy to set up, and offer a vast array of themes and mechanics that keep things exciting.

## Top Card Games for 2026

Here are the top card games you should consider adding to your collection:

### [The Crew: Mission Deep Sea]({{ site.constants.wsib }}The Crew Mission Deep Sea)

[The Crew: Mission Deep Sea]({{ site.constants.wsib }}The Crew Mission Deep Sea) is a cooperative trick-taking game that takes you on underwater adventures. The game is challenging and requires strong teamwork, making it perfect for players who enjoy strategic thinking and collaboration.

### [Exploding Kittens]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Exploding Kittens)

[Exploding Kittens]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Exploding Kittens) is a hilarious and fast-paced game where players draw cards and try to avoid the exploding kitten. With quirky illustrations and simple rules, it's a great choice for parties and family game nights. Check out our full [Exploding Kittens review]({%- post_url 2015-07-15-exploding-kittens-card-game-review -%}) for more details.

### [Gloomhaven: Jaws of the Lion]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Gloomhaven Jaws of the Lion)

[Gloomhaven: Jaws of the Lion]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Gloomhaven Jaws of the Lion) is a cooperative card-driven dungeon crawler set in the popular Gloomhaven universe. It's perfect for players who love deep strategy and immersive storytelling. Don't miss our in-depth [Gloomhaven review]({%- post_url 2022-02-04-gloomhaven-review -%}).

### [Dominion]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Dominion card game)

[Dominion]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Dominion card game) is the quintessential deck-building game that started it all. Players compete to build the most efficient deck and score the most points. With numerous expansions available, the game's replayability is virtually endless.

### [7 Wonders]({{ site.constants.wsib }}7 Wonders card game)

[7 Wonders]({{ site.constants.wsib }}7 Wonders card game) is a civilization-building card game where players draft cards to develop their societies. It's quick to play but offers deep strategic choices, making it a staple in any card game collection.

For family gatherings, consider games that are easy to learn and accommodate various ages. **[Exploding Kittens]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Exploding Kittens)** and **[Love Letter]({{ site.constants.wsib }}Love Letter card game)** are perfect choices.
