Assembly Tips & Tricks
-----------------------
- Unpack all pieces and identify hardware before you start; keep a tiny tray handy so you don’t lose screws that look suspiciously like extra buttons from a control panel. 
- Don’t overtighten the bolts on the first go; snug fits give you room to adjust alignment. 
- Keep a small level nearby to ensure the coffee table sits flat; you don’t want a wobble turning your beverage into a DIY drip experiment. 
- Have a helper or two; modular sets are easier when you’re not trying to balance a corner while inserting a bolt with one hand. 
- For cushions, use the zippered cover to wash occasionally; air-dry away from direct sun to avoid color fading. 

Care & Cleaning
----------------
- Wicker: Wipe with a mild soap solution and rinse. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the weave integrity over time. 
- Cushions: Remove covers and wash per label instructions. If the fabric is machine-washable, use a gentle cycle and air-dry. If not, spot-clean with a mild detergent. 
- Frames: Keep metal parts dry to prevent rust; inspect screws for tightness every few months and re-tighten as needed. 
- Storage: Use breathable covers to protect from rain and sun when not in use. Storing cushions indoors during heavy rain seasons can dramatically extend their life.
