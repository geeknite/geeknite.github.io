### Cleaning and Longevity
Espresso finishes are a double-edged sword: they look amazing, but they reveal fingerprints and smudges in the same way a cat reveals your midnight snack habits. Regular dusting with a microfiber cloth and occasional wipe-down with a slightly damp microfiber cloth keeps the finish looking sharp. Avoid harsh chemicals that could dull the finish or erase your sense of humor. The frame should be metal with powder-coating that resists chipping; this is crucial for a piece that will sustain the inevitable adulting that comes with professional life.

Maintenance isn’t glamorous, but it’s essential. The modular nature makes it easier to upgrade or replace components than you’d expect. If a power strip or shelf starts to feel tired after a year or two, you can swap it with minimal tools and maximum swagger. It’s a furniture system that believes in longevity and in your ability to resist the urge to buy a new desk every 18 months just because your monitor grew a new feature.
