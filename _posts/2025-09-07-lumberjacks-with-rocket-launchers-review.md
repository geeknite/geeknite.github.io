## The Basics

### What is Lumberjacks With Rocket Launchers?

In this game, you play as a lumberjack equipped not just with an axe but also a rocket launcher. Your mission? Clear the forest and defend it against rival lumberjacks and other threats. The game is designed for both solo and multiplayer modes, ensuring you can enjoy the chaos whether you're a lone wolf or part of a rowdy pack.

The game includes an array of beautifully designed maps featuring various terrains, from dense forests to snowy mountains. ## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up the game is straightforward. Choose your lumberjack, customize your loadout, and select your map. The objective is to chop down trees, gather resources, and fend off enemies using your rocket launcher. The game offers various modes, including campaign, survival, and multiplayer battles.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Move, chop trees, and fire rockets at enemies.
2. **Resource Phase**: Gather resources from fallen trees to upgrade your gear and ammo.
3. **Enemy Phase**: Prepare for incoming attacks from rival lumberjacks and forest creatures.

### Strategy and Chaos

While the premise might seem chaotic, there's a surprising amount of strategy involved. Balancing resource gathering with combat is crucial. Do you spend time chopping trees for upgrades, or do you focus on eliminating threats? The choice is yours, and each decision impacts your survival.
