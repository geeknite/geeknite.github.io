## Maintenance and seasonal care
In spring and summer, you can leave the cushions out for several days of sun, but be mindful of UV exposure; rotate cushions to prevent uneven fading. In fall and winter, store the cushions inside and cover the furniture with a breathable outdoor cover. If you live in rainy climates, you’ll appreciate that the wicker dries quickly after a shower, but you should still dry it off to prevent water marks.

## Comparing to alternatives
If you’re deciding between a 6-seater and an 8-seater, think about your entertaining style. If you frequently host big gatherings, the 8-seater offers more table seating and more lounge corners; if you like intimate dinners with a couple of friends, a 6-seater may be a more manageable footprint.
