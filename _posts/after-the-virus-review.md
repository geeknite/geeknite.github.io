## The Basics of After The Virus

After The Virus is a cooperative deck-building game designed for 1-3 players. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies, and your goal is to complete various missions while fending off the relentless undead. The game mechanics are straightforward, but the strategy and teamwork required make it a thrilling experience.

### Components

The game includes a variety of components, such as:

- **Character Cards**: Each player chooses a character with unique abilities and attributes.
- **Mission Cards**: These cards outline the objectives you need to complete to win the game.
- **Zombie Cards**: These cards represent the undead horde that you'll need to deal with.
- **Item Cards**: Tools, weapons, and resources that will aid in your survival.

The quality of the components is top-notch. The artwork is detailed and immersive, and the cards are durable enough to withstand countless zombie attacks.

## Gameplay: Survive the Undead

### Turn Phases

Each turn in After The Virus consists of several phases:

1. **Draw Phase**: Draw cards from your deck.
2. **Action Phase**: Play cards, use items, and fight zombies.
3. **Zombie Phase**: Zombies attack and new zombies spawn.

### Strategy and Teamwork

The key to success in After The Virus is strategic planning and teamwork. Players must work together to complete their missions while managing their resources and dealing with the ever-growing zombie threat. Communication and coordination are crucial, especially when things start to get chaotic.

**Pro Tip**: Don't hoard resources. Share items and support your teammates to ensure everyone's survival.

## The Humor Factor

Despite the grim theme, After The Virus is filled with humorous moments. From the quirky character abilities to the ridiculous situations you'll find yourself in, there's never a dull moment. The game's lighthearted approach to the zombie apocalypse adds a layer of fun that keeps players engaged and entertained.

**Funny Moment**: In one game, our group ended up using a frying pan to fend off zombies while another player tried to fix a broken radio. The absurdity of the situation had us laughing for hours.
