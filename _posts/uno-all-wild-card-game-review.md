## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Uno All Wild is as simple as it gets. Shuffle the deck, deal seven cards to each player, and place the remaining deck in the center. Draw a card to start the discard pile. Now, brace yourself for the wild ride ahead.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players play a card from their hand. Since every card is wild, you can choose any card to play. The fun part? Each card has unique effects, such as swapping hands, skipping turns, or drawing extra cards. The key is to adapt quickly and keep your opponents on their toes.

### Winning

To win, be the first to get rid of all your cards. But beware—the wild nature of the game means victory can slip through your fingers at any moment.
