## Maintenance and Storage: Keeping the Magic Fresh

Like any good gadget or collectible, proper storage matters. Here are some practical tips to keep your 808 Blue deck pristine:

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. The blue back color can fade slightly with long-term UV exposure, and your court cards will thank you for preserving color depth.
- Keep the deck sealed until you’re ready to use it. The novelty of newness is part of the appeal; once you break the seal, you’ve transitioned from “new deck” to “my deck.”
- Use a card clip or a small box to avoid bending in transit if you’re carrying it in a bag with pencils or other paraphernalia. The goal is to avoid creases that would ruin the crisp lines of the Rider Back design.
- If you’re into cleaning, gently wipe any fingerprints with a dry microfiber cloth. No chemicals—card surfaces aren’t fans of solvents plus you don’t want to degrade that finish.
