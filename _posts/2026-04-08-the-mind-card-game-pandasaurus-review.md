How to play in a nutshell
- The Mind is a silent, cooperative challenge: everyone holds a hand of cards and the group must play their cards in ascending order without talking.
- The first card played in a round should be the smallest card across all players’ hands. If someone plays a card that belongs later in the sequence, the team loses a life and the round resets with a new wave of cards to manage.
- There are no individual turns; the group moves as a single organism, each member reading the room and feeling the energy of the others. The pacing is guided by a communal rhythm rather than a deliberate plan.

This is where the mind truly shines: reading room energy and trusting teammates without verbal cues.
