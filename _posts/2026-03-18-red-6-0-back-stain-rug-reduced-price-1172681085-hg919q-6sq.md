## Care Guide: Keeping Red Looking Regal
A practical care plan keeps the red from fading into a pinkish rumor mill after a few seasons. Here’s a simple routine:
- Regular vacuuming to remove surface dirt and to prevent grit from grinding the fibers.
- Immediate blotting of spills with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber damage.
- For tougher stains, test a gentle cleaner on a hidden corner first; some cleaners can affect color slightly, others won’t. Always follow the cleaner’s instructions and the rug’s care guidelines if available.
- In high-traffic rooms, rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color exposure. It’s like rotating your tires, but for living room vibes.
