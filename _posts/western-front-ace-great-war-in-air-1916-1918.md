## The Basics

### What is Western Front Ace?

Western Front Ace is a strategy board game that simulates the aerial combat experiences of World War I. Players take on the roles of fighter pilots, navigating the treacherous skies, engaging in dogfights, and undertaking various missions. The game is designed to recreate the historical context and challenges faced by pilots during the Great War.

### Components

The game comes with a slew of components that make the experience immersive and engaging:

- **Game Board**: A beautifully designed board depicting the Western Front.
- **Miniatures**: Detailed airplane miniatures representing different aircraft from the era.
- **Cards**: Mission cards, pilot cards, and event cards that add depth and variety to the gameplay.
- **Tokens**: Various tokens representing fuel, damage, and other game mechanics.
- **Rulebook**: An easy-to-follow rulebook that guides you through the game's mechanics and objectives.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is relatively straightforward. Each player selects a pilot and aircraft, with corresponding cards detailing their abilities and stats. The game board is laid out, and mission cards are drawn to determine the objectives for the session. Players place their miniatures on the board, and the aerial showdown begins.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn consists of several phases, including movement, combat, and resolution. Players move their aircraft across the board, engaging in dogfights and attempting to complete their missions. The game uses a combination of dice rolls and card draws to determine the outcomes of engagements and events.

### Strategy and Tactics

Success in Western Front Ace requires a careful balance of strategy and tactics. Players must manage their resources, plan their movements, and outmaneuver their opponents. The game encourages players to think several steps ahead, anticipating enemy moves and adapting to changing circumstances.
