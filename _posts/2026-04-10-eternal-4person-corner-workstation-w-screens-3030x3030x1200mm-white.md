## Cable Management and Maintenance
### Keeping It Tidy
A desk this size invites cables like a magnet attracts hummingbirds. The Eternal’s built-in channels help, but you’ll still want to up your cable management game with zip ties, Velcro wraps, and perhaps a desktop cable tray if you’re the type who insists on a perfectly clean surface. Keep power strips accessible but not visually conspicuous. You don’t want the heartbeat of your office—your power strip—to be the center of attention in a perfectly white environment. Minimalism is great until you realize you can’t find your keyboard because you placed the USB hub two inches below the desk and the cable runs show like an urban sculpture.

### Cleaning and Durability
White surfaces are gorgeous but they demand a routine. A microfiber cloth, warm water, and a mild cleaner will keep the planks and panels looking like new. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the laminate edges. If you spill coffee or tea, wipe promptly and avoid letting the liquid pool around the corners where the panels meet the frame. The build quality helps resist typical wear and tear, but the real life test is how long it can survive the occasional game-night hackathon where snacks get involved and random elbow bumps occur.

### Cleaning Schedule and Maintenance Tips
- Weekly: wipe down surfaces; check screws for tightness.
- Monthly: inspect cable channels for pinch points; rearrange any misrouted cables.
- Quarterly: consider a fresh coat of light glare-protection or a screen refresh if you’re using heavy-duty whiteboard markers.
