## Setup and maintenance: keep the table crisp and the cards happy
### First-use setup tips
- Unfold in a spacious area to avoid banging walls or furniture into your precious game night groove.
- Tighten all locking mechanisms to ensure the table remains rigid throughout the session.
- Install felt protectors if you’re worried about floor scuffs or if you’re playing on delicate surfaces.
- Calibrate the level by adjusting the leg feet individually if your floor isn’t perfectly flat.

### Cleaning and upkeep
- Regularly brush off debris and trim loose threads along the rails to prevent snags on chip edges.
- Clean with a mild cloth and a non-abrasive cleaner; avoid harsh chemicals that could fade or degrade the felt.
- When folding, ensure all players step back to avoid pinching fingers—this is a gentlemanly reminder, not a threat.
- Store in a dry, cool place; moisture can affect both the felt and the wooden frame over time.
