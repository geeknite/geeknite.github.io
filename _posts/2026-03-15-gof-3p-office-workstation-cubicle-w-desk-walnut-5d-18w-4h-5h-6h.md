## Maintenance and Care Tips
- Clean the walnut surface with a soft microfiber cloth and a mild wood-safe cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals that can dull the finish.
- Use coasters for drinks and avoid placing hot items directly on the desk to prevent heat damage to the veneer.
- Check cables annually and re-route to maintain the neat look. A small investment in cable sleeves or clips pays off in aesthetics.
- If you disassemble and reconfigure, store screws and small hardware in labeled bags to avoid the dreaded missing part moment.
