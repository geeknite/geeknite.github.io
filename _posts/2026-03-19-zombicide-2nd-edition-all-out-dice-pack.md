### The Dice, by Color and Purpose

## Setup and Home Rules: Keeping It Smooth
Like any good upgrade, the All-Out Dice Pack benefits from some light house rules to keep things smooth. Here are a few practical tips to minimize chaos and maximize fun:
- Designated dice tray: Create a small tray or dice tower for the pack’s dice to keep the table from becoming a rainbow avalanche of plastic.
- Clear the turn window: Decide early how many additional dice you’ll roll per turn. A common starting point is adding 2-3 dice per relevant action, with the option to escalate on a per-mission basis.
- Quick-re-roll policy: If a roll is pivotal (think “mission critical” moments), you can opt for a once-per-turn re-roll on one die—the table will thank you for not inventing a new die-rolling ritual every time.
- Icon glossary: Keep a quick reference sheet on hand so players aren’t stuck flipping the core rulebook to decipher every new symbol the pack introduces.

These tweaks help the All-Out Dice Pack feel integrated rather than tacked on. The goal is to keep the flow cinematic rather than let the dice bureaucracy slow you down.
