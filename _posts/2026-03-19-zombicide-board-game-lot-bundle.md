Zombicide thrives on cooperative chaos turned into tactical decision making. The lot bundle amplifies this dynamic in a few predictable but enjoyable ways. If you are a veteran, the bundle becomes your sandbox to craft brutal, memorable campaigns that your group will still be talking about in the afterglow of victory or the throes of defeat a week later.
