### 3. Sold by Amazon? Yes, Please!

Try to buy products sold directly by Amazon. This allows you to combine shipping costs for different items. Third-party products are fine as long as Amazon handles the shipping【4†source】.

### 4. Compare Prices Across European Amazons

Before making any purchase, compare prices across all European Amazons. A single Amazon account works for all countries, so add the product to your cart and proceed as if you're going to pay to see the final price with shipping. This way, you can compare the real cost【4†source】.
