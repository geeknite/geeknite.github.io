## Maintenance and care guidance

- Wipe with a damp cloth and mild cleaner; avoid harsh abrasives that could dull the finish.
- Tighten bolts after a few weeks of heavy use. The frame may settle a bit, which is normal with new assembly.
- Check cable trough seals and grommets occasionally to ensure nothing is snagging or pulling on cables.
- For spills, blot rather than wipe to minimize staining; a quick wipe after you finish folding chairs into your workspace is a good habit.

This isn’t a desk that needs a shrine; it’s a desk that earns its keep day after day. And if you ever decide to upgrade to a standing-friendly setup, the steel frame can be a reliable anchor for your future upgrade path.
