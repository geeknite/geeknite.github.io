### What is Senjutsu? 

Senjutsu: Battle for Japan is a competitive strategy board game where players take on the roles of samurai generals vying for control of Japan. With beautiful miniatures, diverse tactical options, and a rich historical theme, this game offers a deep and immersive experience for strategy enthusiasts.

## Gameplay 

### Setting Up 

Setting up Senjutsu is a breeze, as long as you remember to keep your katana away from the game pieces. Lay out the game board, distribute the miniatures, and shuffle the decks of cards. Each player selects a samurai general to command, and the battle for Japan begins.

### Turns and Actions 

Each turn, players draw cards and use them to perform actions. These actions can range from moving your samurai forces, executing powerful attacks, and even engaging in diplomatic maneuvers. The key to victory lies in balancing aggression with strategic planning.

### Combat 

Combat in Senjutsu is both exciting and nerve-wracking. Samurai warriors engage in tactical duels where every move counts. Players must anticipate their opponent's actions and strike at the right moment to gain the upper hand. Did you plan an epic attack only to miss your target by a fraction? Welcome to the world of Senjutsu, where even the best-laid plans can go sideways.
