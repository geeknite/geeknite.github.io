### The Gameplay: What The Game Even Is

Let us get to the heart of the roost: the game itself. The Game: Limited Edition is billed as a hybrid experience. Expect plot twists that feel earned, not merely engineered to drum up fear that your friends will escape the table without paying for the next expansion.
