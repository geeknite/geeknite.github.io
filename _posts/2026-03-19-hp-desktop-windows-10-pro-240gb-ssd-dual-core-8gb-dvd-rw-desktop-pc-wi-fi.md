### Storage and Upgradability
The 240GB SSD is a strong starting point for your OS install, but it’s not the end of the road. Modern software and media libraries tend to outgrow a 240GB footprint quickly. The good news is that many HP desktops offer room for expansion—whether that means adding another SSD for speed, or a larger HDD for bulk storage. If you keep your media off the primary drive, you will be fine for quite a while; if you accumulate large video projects or high-res photo libraries, you will probably want to add more space at some point. Upgrading RAM is another accessible path, often requiring simple steps and a spare slot or two. It may not be a Sunday afternoon case-lab project, but it is manageable for the average DIY-er.
