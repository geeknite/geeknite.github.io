## The Cards: A Closer Look at White vs. Black Dynamics
### White Cards: The Quick-Draw of Absurdity
White cards in any CAH expansion function as the set of potential punchlines. The 2012 Holiday Edition adds several new cards that emphasize holiday mishaps, awkward traditions, and the universal truth that family is chaotic, on a schedule, and somehow always there when you least expect it. The strength of these White cards lies in their versatility. Some are punchy one-liners; others are more situational and require the Black Card prompt to steer the laugh in the right direction.
