Assembly and maintenance

Setup tips for a smooth experience
- Unpack everything and lay out the legs in the order specified by the manual. A tidy bench area helps during a late-night build.
- Attach the foldable legs and ensure the locking mechanism clicks firmly. A loose leg is a nightmare scenario that can ruin a bluff and a carpet.
- Place the tabletop on a stable surface and press along the edges to ensure the cushion is evenly seated. A quick check with a level is optional but recommended for the perfectionist in you.
- Insert a couple of practice chips and run a couple of sample hands to verify card-surface friction and rail contact. This is the gaming equivalent of test driving a rocket scooter before you commit to the launch sequence.

Cleaning and upkeep

- Wipe with a damp microfiber cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners that might degrade the faux leather texture.
- For spills, blot quickly and wipe from the outside in to minimize staining along the seams.
- If chips leave scuffs on the rail, a mild cleaner and a soft cloth will remove most marks without harming the cushion.
- Periodic checks on the folding joints will keep the table stable for years of use. A stiff screw is better than a wobbly gamble when the river is in view.
