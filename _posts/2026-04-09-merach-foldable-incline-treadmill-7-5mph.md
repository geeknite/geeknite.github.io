## Durability, Warranty, and Maintenance

In this price range, you’ll typically find a two- to three-year warranty on the frame and a shorter warranty on moving parts such as the belt and motor. MERACH’s warranty details for this foldable incline model should be checked in the user manual or official site for the exact terms in your region. A couple of practical maintenance tips to keep your treadmill running smoothly:

- Regular belt cleaning and dust removal; dust can accumulate on the motor and affect performance.
- Periodic belt tension checks; a loose belt can cause slippage and noise.
- Squeak management: if you hear creaks, it’s usually due to loose bolts or improper folding; re-tighten where necessary and use a dab of silicone-based lubricant per the manual.
- Safety key care: don’t lose the safety key; it’s your on-off switch in emergencies and a reminder that you’re in control of your own cardio destiny.

Maintenance is straightforward and relatively low-cost, which makes this treadmill a practical long-term companion if you’re on a budget but want something that can stay with you for a while.
