## Black Screen Feature
The strongest part of the Eternal is the Black Screen concept. It is a privacy panel that slides into position with a satisfying click. When deployed, it creates a visual boundary without isolating individuals from the team vibe. Think of it as wearing noise-canceling headphones for your eyes; you can still hear the chat, but the glare on your monitor disappears.

In practice, the black screen helps teams manage meetings more gracefully, because it reduces the sense that you are constantly being watched. This is not just about privacy; it is about focus. When you want to chase a sprint without your neighbors asking how the performance reviews are going, you can activate the screen and pretend you are in a bat cave with your own personal data.

Note that the Black Screen is not opaque to your boss; they can still reach in and tap your shoulder with a friendly reminder about deadlines. However, the screen makes the experience more comfortable, especially when presentations involve bright projectors that wash out the entire room.
