## Maintenance and care
Gadget maintenance is not glamorous, but it matters. Here’s how to keep the set looking fresh and performing reliably:
- Keep decks dry and clean. A quick wipe with a dry cloth is enough. Cards dislike moisture and sticky fingers, especially if you’re playing near a kitchen or a rainy window.
- Avoid prolonged sunlight on the chips. Fading is not dramatic, but it’s avoidable. If you’re hosting outdoors, consider a quick shade cover or a cute table umbrella to keep the colors vibrant.
- Store the set in its case when not in use. It’s tempting to keep it out on the coffee table where it’s convenient, but a tidy storage habit extends the life of the chips and cards and saves you from the mystery of the lost chips.
- If a chip ever feels loose in the mold, contact the manufacturer for a quick replacement or, if you’re feeling adventurous, glue a tiny felt pad on the underside to quiet any rattling. This is not official advice, but it’s a fun little hack if you want to feel like a tinkerer.

Overall, care is straightforward: keep it dry, keep it in the case, and you’ll be stacking chips for seasons to come.
