## A Quick Tuning and Maintenance Checklist

- Level the table with adjustable feet and recheck after the first few games. A level surface prevents wackiness and helps you avoid accidental cross-table dribbling between the players.
- Clean the playing surface regularly with a soft, lint-free cloth. Avoid harsh cleaners that can damage the finish.
- Lubricate the rods periodically to maintain smooth movement. A light petroleum-based lubricant or silicone-based product works well; avoid heavy grease that can attract dust and cause stickiness.
- Inspect the leg brackets for wear. If you notice looseness, tighten the screws to preserve stability during dramatic saves and celebratory high-fives.
- Replace worn-out balls promptly. A battered ball will ruin your shot accuracy and your sense of pride after you miss a simple pass to your own goalie.
