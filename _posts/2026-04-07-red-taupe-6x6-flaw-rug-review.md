## Cleaning, care, and long-term maintenance: keeping the drama manageable

Caring for a rug with a flaw follows the same general rules as caring for a high maintenance pet. Vacuum regularly to prevent matting and shedding, rotate occasionally to even out traffic wear, and address spills promptly to avoid staining. A mild detergent and warm water are usually sufficient for most synthetic blends, but always check the care label if available. Avoid harsh cleaners that might intensify color variance or damage fibers. If the rug develops a stubborn stain, a professional cleaning may be wise rather than a DIY experiment that turns a minor issue into a festival of regret.
