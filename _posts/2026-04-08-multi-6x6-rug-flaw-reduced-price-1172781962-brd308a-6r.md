### Cleaning, care, and maintenance
Like any good rug, the BRD308A-6R benefits from regular vacuuming and quick spill management. The pile is moderately dense, which means it can trap tiny debris if you neglect routine care. Here are some practical steps:
- Vacuum the rug at least once a week with a vacuum that has a brushless suction head to prevent fiber damage.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push liquid deeper into the fibers.
- For basic stains, use a mild detergent solution and test on a small area first.
- Rotate the rug every three to six months to even out wear and color exposure.
- Keep away from direct sunlight for long periods to minimize color fading.
- If there is heavy staining or stubborn dye transfer, consider professional cleaning or a rug care service.
