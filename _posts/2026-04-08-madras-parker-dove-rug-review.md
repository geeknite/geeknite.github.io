## Cleanliness, Maintenance, and Longevity
Maintenance is where most rugs test your patience. The Madras Parker Dove is relatively straightforward:
- Vacuum regularly with a hand-held attachment or limited-depth vacuum to avoid fiber pull.
- For spills, blot with a clean cloth and avoid harsh scrubbing; if the spill is stubborn, you may need a mild-clean solution and a gentle blot, always testing on a small hidden area first.
- Rotate occasionally to maintain even wear and prevent obvious pattern fading due to sun exposure.
- Deep cleaning every 12-18 months can extend the rug’s life, but be mindful of the backing and fiber type. Check the manufacturer’s care guidelines for the exact cleaner recommendations.

The lifespan is decent for its price point, and with a little care it can remain a reliable centerpiece for years. The color does a decent job hiding light footprints and everyday dust, which is always a nice upgrade in a busy home.
