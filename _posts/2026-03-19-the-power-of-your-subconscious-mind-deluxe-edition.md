A practical, nerdy exercise set you can start tonight

Here’s a quick, geek-friendly starter pack to test-drive the deluxe edition principles:

1) Set a micro-goal you want to achieve in 7 days. Visualize the moment you’ve achieved it every night before bed, using all five senses: what do you see, hear, feel, smell, and taste in that moment?
2) Write a 3-sentence autosuggestion that aligns with your goal. Read it aloud with conviction each morning and night.
3) Create a 2-minute post-hoc reflection ritual: every evening, jot down a single thing you did today toward your goal and a 1% improvement for tomorrow.
4) Pair this with a small daily action that compounds. It could be a 5-minute learning sprint, a 10-minute walk, or a 2-page writing goal. The key is consistency, not intensity.
