## Maintenance: Cleaning, Storage, and Longevity Tips
Maintenance is the boring part that keeps your furniture looking sharp. Here are a few practical tips that will help you get the most from this 4-piece set:

- Regular cleaning: A quick wipe with a damp cloth will remove dust and minor grime from the rattan weave. For the cushions, follow the care instructions on the cover label. Most covers are machine-washable on a gentle cycle, but check the tags to confirm.
- Stain management: Accidents happen. Treat spills promptly by blotting (not rubbing) with a clean cloth. For stubborn stains, a mild soap solution and a soft brush work wonders. Rinse thoroughly and air-dry; avoid direct heat sources that could shrink fabrics.
- Weather protection: In rainy seasons or long downtime, consider storing cushions indoors or using breathable cushion covers to prevent mold and mildew. The cushion covers being removable makes this a lot easier.
- Storage: If you have limited outdoor space during winter, consider a storage solution that keeps the set dry but accessible. A simple outdoor storage box or a dedicated furniture cover can extend the look and life of your investment.
- Cleaning schedule: A monthly wipe-down and seasonal cushion removal can help you keep the set looking showroom-fresh. If you have pets or kids who love to leap onto furniture, you’ll want to do a more frequent check for wear and tear.
