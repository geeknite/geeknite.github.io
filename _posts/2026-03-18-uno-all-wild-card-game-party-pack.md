## Strategy Tips for Maximizing Fun and Victory

A winner is not always the person with the most cards left in hand; sometimes the most entertaining victory is the one that leaves everyone still smiling. Here are a few practical tips to get more value from your All Wild Card sessions:

- Read the room as you would a crowd at a stand-up show. If energy is high, lean into rapid-fire rounds. If your crew is more methodical, slow it down and savor the card reveals.
- Save your wilds for moments that affect multiple players rather than just your own hand. A well-timed wild can reset momentum for an entire table in a single move.
- Pay attention to color stacking opportunities. If you have a color with your last two cards and a wild on deck, you can force a pivotal turn with minimal risk.
- Use the house rules variants to shape the pace. Short rounds are great for newcomers; longer rounds reward the players who pay attention to patterns and memory.
