## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Daily Care: Keeping the shine without turning it into a chore
Maintenance is easier than you might fear. Glass panels wipe down clean with standard glass cleaner and a microfiber cloth. You can maintain the panels with minimal effort, keeping them free of fingerprints and smudges that creep in after a long week of back-to-back video calls. Desktops can be cleaned with mild cleaners suitable for melamine finishes and should resist common office stains like coffee, marker, and that one sauce you spilled during a lunch break catering event.
