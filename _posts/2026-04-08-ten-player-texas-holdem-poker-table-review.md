### Seating and Reachability
A well-designed 10-person table will balance seating so that each player has comfortable reach to their own chips and to the center pot. This is critical: if the dealer’s button is too far away from a player, it can disrupt the rhythm of betting and create unnecessary confusion, which is not the sort of drama you want in a friendly game either. The best tables provide enough distance between players to prevent accidental chip-knockovers while allowing easy access to the pot for everyone. The seating arrangement around an oval table also helps minimize the “back-of-seat envy” that can occur when someone near the middle gets a better angle on the action.
