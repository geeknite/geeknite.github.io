## Tips and tricks

- Set up a dedicated area for mood tokens to avoid confusion. A shallow tray or a small cloth can help you quickly grab the tokens you need and keep the rest within easy reach.
- Create a token management ritual. For example, at the start of each round, scan for the mood shifts and arrange the tokens in a predictable pattern. This reduces search time and increases the pace.
- Use the large round markers as a quick read of the round structure. They serve well as visual reminders of when certain effects reset and when the round moves to a new phase.
- Consider color coding for new players. If you have players with color sensitivity, ensure you adjust lighting or use a color coding sheet so there is no confusion during critical moments.
- Practice with the tokens in a quick sandbox scenario before a competitive round. This will help you internalize token meanings and reduce hesitation during real games.
