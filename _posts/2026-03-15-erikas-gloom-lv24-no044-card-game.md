## Card Handling, Storage, and Display Tips

If you’ve decided to bring Erika’s Gloom LV.24 No.044 into your personal collection, here are a few practical tips that keep the card safe while preserving the vibe:

- Storage: Use soft, acid-free card sleeves and a sturdy binder with archival pages. For the real nostalgia factor, place it in a binder with other gym-themed or early era cards to create a mini-exhibit.
- Handling: Always handle cards with clean hands. Oils and sweat can degrade the card’s surface over time. Avoid pinching edges; hold from the sides rather than the corners to minimize stress.
- Display: Consider a shallow frame or a display binder page that allows you to show the art without removing the card from its protective sleeve. Framing behind UV-protective glass also helps preserve the colors.
- Cleaning: If dust gathers on the sleeve, gently use a soft brush. Do not use liquids on the card itself; moisture can cause warping or staining.
- Environment: Keep it away from direct sunlight, high humidity, or extreme temperature shifts. The 90s aesthetic is best enjoyed intact, not warped by climate swings.
