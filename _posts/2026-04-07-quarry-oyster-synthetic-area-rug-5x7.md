## Cleaning and Maintenance Tips

- Vacuum regularly with a standard floor nozzle to avoid excessive piling and to keep the micro texture intact.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper into the fiber.
- For tough stains, use a mild detergent diluted in water and dab gently with a cloth; avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the fiber.
- If you have kids or pets, consider a quick weekly shake outside to reduce embedded debris.
- Use a non slip rug pad underneath to prevent shifting and to extend the life of the edges.
- Rotate the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly if your space has varying sunlight or traffic.
- When storing, roll rather than fold to prevent creases; keep in a dry space to avoid moisture issues.

### Quick Care Checklist
If you want a one page checklist, here is a quick reference in plain language:
1. Blot spills immediately; 2. Vacuum weekly; 3. Avoid heat and harsh chemicals; 4. Use a pad for stability; 5. Rotate regionally to keep wear even.
