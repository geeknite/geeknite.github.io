## What Are MCC Codes?

MCC stands for Merchant Category Code. It's not a secret society or the latest superhero team, but a four-digit number that identifies the type of business you're dealing with. Think of it as a barcode for merchants, helping banks and credit card companies identify where you're spending your hard-earned cash.

### Fun Fact

MCC codes are like the Hogwarts Sorting Hat for businesses—they categorize merchants into different groups, from supermarkets to travel agencies. So next time your credit card statement reads like a Harry Potter spell, blame the MCC codes.
