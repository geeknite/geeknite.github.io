## How to Play the NSFW Edition
If you already know the base Exploding Kittens, the NSFW version is a friendly addition. The NSFW edition adds a handful of new cards that tilt the balance toward unpredictability and social risk. The learning curve remains gentle, which makes this edition accessible to new players while offering something extra for veterans.

### Quick Start Guide
- Set up as you would for the base game: shuffle the deck, deal two cards, and place the defuse and the exploding kitten cards into the draw pile as indicated by player count.
- On your turn you draw one card, then you may play one or more cards from your hand that affect the play: skip the draw, steal a card from another player, copy a strategy from an ally, etc. The exact actions depend on the NSFW cards in play.
- If a player draws the exploding kitten, they are out unless they hold a defuse card. If they use a defuse, they place the exploding kitten back into the deck, usually somewhere around the bottom depending on the house rules. The round continues until one player remains.
- The NSFW cards add risk by forcing players to reveal their hand or to perform a silly action in order to avoid a punishment; they keep the game fast, goofy, and a little chaotic.
