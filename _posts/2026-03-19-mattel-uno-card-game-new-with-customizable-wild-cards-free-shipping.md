## House Rules, Quick Wins, and Quick Fails
To help you get off the ground without rewriting the entire rulebook on night one, here are a few starter house rules that work well with this kit:
- Swap Hands: when a wild is played, the active player swaps hands with the person across the table. If that person has UNO, you’ve just invented culinary-level drama for the deck.
- Color Reboot: a wild that changes color forces all players to switch to the new color on the next turn, even if they haven’t finished their current draw.
- Uno Rhymes: a wild that rewards players who shout UNO with a rhyme instead of a straight shout. It’s optional but adds a touch of theatrical flair.
- Chalkboard Rule: write the new rule on a whiteboard before the round; keep it visible to minimize misreads and maximize laughter.

Feel free to adapt, summarize, or discard as needed. The aim is social clarity and shared humor rather than brain-melting math.
