## House Rules, Quick Wins, and Quick Fails
To help you get started without rewriting the entire rulebook on night one, here are a few starter house rules that work well with this kit:
- The Swap Hands rule: when a wild is played, the active player swaps hands with the person across the table. If that person has UNO, the moment becomes cinematic.
- The Color Reboot: a wild that changes color forces all players to swap to the new color at the next turn, even if they haven’t finished their current draw.
- The Uno Rhymes rule: a wild that rewards those who shout UNO with a rhyme instead of a shout. It’s optional, but it adds a theater factor.
- The Chalkboard Rule: you write the new rule on a whiteboard before the round, then keep the board visible. It reduces misreads and increases laughter.

Feel free to adapt, summarize, or discard as needed. The point is social clarity and shared humor rather than mind boggling math.
