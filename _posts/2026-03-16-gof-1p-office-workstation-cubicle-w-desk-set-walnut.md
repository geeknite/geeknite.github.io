## Durability and Maintenance

### Cleaning and Care
Walnut finishes look fabulous but can show fingerprints and dust more than a glossy black panel. Regular wiping with a microfiber cloth and a mild cleaner will keep the surface looking respectable. Avoid abrasive pads that could dull the grain or leave micro-scratches that would make a woodpecker cry. Hook a simple routine: quick wipe-down after the end of the workday, a weekly deeper wipe-down, and a quarterly inspection of screws to ensure nothing has decided to loosen its grip on reality. If you’re one of those people who spills coffee like it’s an annual ritual, invest in a coaster under your mug and a small cable trap under the desk to prevent random mugs from turning into landmines for your keyboard.

### Long-Term Durability
The modular design is meant to be resilient, but remember: furniture ages. The beauty of a modular unit is that you can replace pieces rather than buying an entirely new workstation when a panel warps or a drawer starts squeaking like a haunted closet.
