## The Basics

### What is Ninja All-Stars: Uzumaki?

Ninja All-Stars: Uzumaki is a competitive board game where players take on the roles of rival ninja clans vying for dominance. With a mix of strategy, luck, and a bit of backstabbing, this game brings the essence of ninja warfare to life. Think of it as "Naruto" meets "Risk", but without the need for a thousand episodes.

### Components

The game comes with a plethora of beautifully designed miniatures, cards, and tokens. The attention to detail on the miniatures is so good, you might just mistake them for tiny real ninjas. The game board, cards, and tokens are equally impressive, making the setup a feast for the eyes.
