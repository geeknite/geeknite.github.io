## Strategy tips: what to do, what to avoid

If you want to walk away from the table with your sanity intact and your mini figures still pretty, here are a few practical pointers:

- Coordinate multi-target focus when possible. ABOMINALPHA’s presence often means you’ll be facing multiple threats. Planned, token-by-token focus fires beat chaotic, everyone-for-themselves shooting.
- Don’t ignore the terrain. The board’s layout matters more than usual with a boss that leverages zones and line of sight. If you can block a crucial corridor or force the boss to maneuver into a disadvantageous position, you’ll thank yourself later.
- Keep an eye on the timing of special abilities. Some attacks punish you for delaying, others reward you for rapid, decisive actions. Track the boss’s phase and align your survivor abilities to exploit openings.
- Don’t underestimate escalation. The longer the encounter lasts, the more leverage ABOMINALPHA gains to flip the board in dramatic ways. Short, precise bursts of coordinated action usually beat a prolonged, bumbling exchange.
- Communication is king. The boss changes the pace of a game; your communication strategy should adapt as well. Quick calls like “we focus this turn on ABOMINALPHA’s backline” or “we split to deny the next board-wide event” can save more lives than fancy dice rolls.
