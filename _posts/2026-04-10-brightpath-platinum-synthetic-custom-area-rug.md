## Practical Performance

### Cleaning and Maintenance

Spill management matters. Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth, then use a mild detergent mixed with water for spot cleaning. Avoid harsh abrasive cleaners or bleach as they can dull the color and wear down the fibers. Routine vacuuming is recommended with a gentle setting to prevent fiber fraying. A rug pad is recommended to reduce slipping and protect both rug and floor.

### Stain Resistance and Colorfastness

Synthetic polypropylene fibers with a protective finish typically resist common household stains. The Platinum tone can show mineral spills and some pet-related mishaps, but speed is your friend here. For persistent stains, a mild solution and some patience should do the trick. Colorfastness is usually good; the shade holds up under daylight exposure without dramatic fading over months in typical living spaces.

### Durability and Wear

In a real world test, the rug handles daily foot traffic, pet traffic, and the occasional dropped remote without showing obvious pilling or thinning. The backing stays anchored to the floor with minimal creeping, assuming proper rug pad use. The edge binding remains intact after repeated vacuum sessions.

## Size Options: Custom vs 8x10

The option for custom sizing is one of the main selling points for this product. If you have an irregular room or a unique layout, you can request a size that fits perfectly. The 8x10 remains a popular standard for many living rooms, but custom options can tailor to smaller lounges, larger family rooms, or oddly shaped spaces.

### When to Choose Custom Size

If your room has an architectural niche or a difficult-to-fit configuration, a custom size rug can minimize wasted floor space and create a cohesive look. It also helps with furniture placement in a way that standard sizes cannot.

### When to Choose 8x10

For most medium to large living spaces, 8x10 offers a balanced ratio of floor space to furniture. It accommodates a sofa, chairs, and a coffee table with enough margin to create a comfortable walking area.
