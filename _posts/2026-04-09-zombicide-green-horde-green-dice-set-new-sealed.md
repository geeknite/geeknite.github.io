## Maintenance, Storage, and Care
### Keeping the Emerald Dice in Good Shape
Like any good artifact in a nerdy collection, your dice deserve a bit of care. Here are practical tips to keep your Greens from turning into a dull olive if you’re not careful:
- Store in a soft pouch or a dedicated dice tin to prevent chipping from table edges and from rolling under the couch of doom (the one where you lost your last session’s keys).
- Clean with a dry microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals; you’re dealing with plastic and pigment, not a chemistry set.
- Keep away from direct sunlight for extended periods. The greens might shift slightly with long UV exposure, though you’ll probably notice it only after a few campaigns. Still, better to preserve the color integrity.
- If you want to maintain sharp edges, don’t throw them around like a tiny hero’s weapon in a chaos ritual; treat them with respect, and they’ll continue to perform in critical moments.

In short: they’re durable for normal gaming life. If you’re using a dice cup, you’ll have less risk of losing a die to the abyss of the couch cushions, which is a win for everyone involved.
