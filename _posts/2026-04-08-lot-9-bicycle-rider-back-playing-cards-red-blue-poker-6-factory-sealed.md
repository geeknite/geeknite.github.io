## Maintenance and care: keeping plastic fantastic
The lifeblood of any card collection is maintenance. Here are a few simple steps to keep these decks in top condition:
- Store away from direct sunlight to preserve color and edge integrity.
- Use a dedicated card box or a segmented organizer to keep opened decks segregated from sealed ones.
- Avoid moisture exposure; humidity is a stealthy enemy of card stock that can cause curling and slow friction changes.
- If a deck gets a little sticky from fingerprints or handling, a gentle wipe with a dry cloth is usually enough; avoid liquid cleaners that could warp the stock.
- For long-term collectors, consider occasional reshuffling of the sealed decks to ensure the air inside the wrap remains evenly distributed and the stock remains consistent when eventually opened.
