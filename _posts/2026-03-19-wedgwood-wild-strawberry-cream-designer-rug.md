### Maintenance and Practicality: Care That Doesn’t Kill the Mood

Every good designer rug should be designed with a life outside the showroom. The Wedgwood Wild Strawberry Cream is built with this in mind. Here are the practical notes you’ll want to know:

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting. Avoid high-powered suction that can snag the fibers; treat it like your favorite sweater instead of a siphon to space dust.
- Blot, don’t rub, for spills. The aim is to lift the liquid without driving it deeper. A clean cloth, a little patience, and maybe a heroic sigh will do the trick.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to even out wear. This isn’t a highway; you don’t want a single well-worn highway mark right in the middle.
- Consider a rug pad. If your floor isn’t perfectly flat, a pad provides grip and life-extension while minimizing slippage during intense Netflix marathons.
