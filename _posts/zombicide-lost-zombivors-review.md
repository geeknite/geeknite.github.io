## What is Zombicide: Lost Zombivors?

Zombicide: Lost Zombivors is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of survivors in a zombie apocalypse. With stunning artwork, cool miniatures, and a plethora of zombies to battle, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Zombicide: Lost Zombivors, players work together to complete missions while fending off waves of zombies. Each player controls a unique survivor, each with special abilities and cards. The goal is to complete objectives, defeat zombies, and avoid becoming a zombivor (a zombie survivor).

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of your favorite zombie apocalypse characters. Seriously, they're so detailed you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: Each survivor has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations from the post-apocalyptic world where battles and missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Those claws are the real supervillains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Zombicide: Lost Zombivors is like staging an apocalypse movie. It requires careful planning and maybe a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your mission. Now you're ready to begin your quest for survival.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their survivor's abilities. You can move, attack, search for items, and more. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your teammates to make the most of your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Zombicide: Lost Zombivors is a blend of strategy and luck. Each zombie has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic attack just to have the zombie horde overwhelm you? Welcome to Zombicide, where plans often go awry.

One of the best parts of Zombicide: Lost Zombivors is the diverse array of survivors and zombies. From the stealthy assassin to the brute force tank, you can play as your favorite characters and face off against legendary foes like the Abomination and the Necromancer.

### Replayability

With multiple survivors, zombies, and scenarios, Zombicide: Lost Zombivors offers endless replayability. No two games are ever the same, ensuring that players will return to the zombie apocalypse time and time again for new challenges and experiences.
