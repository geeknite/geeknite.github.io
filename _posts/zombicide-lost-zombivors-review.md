## What is Zombicide: Lost Zombivors?

Zombicide: Lost Zombivors is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of survivors in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies. The game is part of the Zombicide franchise, known for its intense gameplay, detailed miniatures, and hilarious scenarios. In this expansion, things get even more chaotic as you encounter zombified versions of your favorite characters.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Miniatures**: High-quality miniatures of survivors and zombies, including the new zombivors (zombie survivors).
- **Game Boards**: Modular boards depicting various locations such as the city streets, abandoned buildings, and creepy alleys.
- **Cards**: A plethora of cards for equipment, events, and zombie abilities.
- **Tokens**: Various tokens to track objectives, wounds, and other game elements.

**Fun Fact**: The zombivors come with their own unique abilities, making them both a threat and an invaluable asset in your fight against the undead.

## Gameplay

### Setup

Setting up the game is like preparing for a zombie apocalypse. Lay out the modular board, distribute equipment cards, and place your miniatures. Now you're ready to face the horde.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:
1. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions such as moving, attacking, and searching for equipment.
2. **Zombie Phase**: Zombies move and attack, following specific rules that mimic their relentless pursuit of fresh brains.
3. **End Phase**: Resolve any end-of-turn effects and draw new event cards.

### Combat

Combat in Zombicide: Lost Zombivors is fast-paced and thrilling. Use your weapons and abilities to take down zombies and survive their attacks. The dice rolls add a layer of unpredictability, ensuring that no two battles are the same.
