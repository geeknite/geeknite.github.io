## Performance and Durability

### Stomp Test: Passed

We had everyone in the office take turns stomping across the rug. Not because we’re mean like that, but because we wanted to see how it holds up to foot traffic. The result? It barely showed any signs of wear. This rug can handle your wild parties, rambunctious kids, and even your Great Dane’s zoomies.

### Spill Test: Challenge Accepted

Spills happen. Especially when you're hosting a wine and cheese night. We tested the rug with red wine, coffee, and even ketchup (we like to cover all bases). The rug held up surprisingly well. Most spills were easily blotted out with a damp cloth, and there was no staining. Just don’t spill an entire bottle of wine. That’s a waste.
