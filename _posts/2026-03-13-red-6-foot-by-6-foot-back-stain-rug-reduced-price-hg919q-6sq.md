### Care and maintenance
Maintenance is a key part of any rug review. This rug responds well to routine vacuuming and a quick spot clean. For tougher stains, we recommend blotting rather than scrubbing, to avoid pushing the stain deeper into the fibers. A mild detergent or a dedicated rug cleaner works fine, and you should avoid bleach or harsh solvents that could degrade the color. For professional cleaning, schedule a lightweight session rather than a full immersion wash. Regular rotation across the room helps even wear and keeps the color from looking patchy over the years.
