### Performance and ease of cleaning
Synthetic fibers are treated to resist stains and moisture, making it easy to wipe up spills. A damp cloth and mild detergent will usually do the trick. For more stubborn stains, you can turn to mild bleach solution but test on a hidden area first. The rug dries quickly, minimizing the risk of mold in humid climates.

### Durability and wear
Compared to natural fiber rugs, synthetic rugs like Mesa Sterling hold up well under daily traffic. They resist color fade and maintain a consistent look even after months of use. If you have kids or pets, you will appreciate the dirt repellency and easy cleaning.

## Care guide
Regular vacuuming with a beater bar on a low setting helps maintain the pile’s integrity. Rotate occasionally to ensure even wear. For spills, blot rather than rub; avoid harsh chemical cleaners. For deep cleaning, consider professional cleaning or a gentle home machine wash if the product page permits.

## How to choose the right size and color
Measure your space carefully and consider the surrounding furniture. A 5x7 rug is usually ideal for a small living area or under a coffee table, but larger rooms can benefit from a custom size that fits snugly under all seating. Color selection should align with wall tones and accent pieces. If you love a dramatic look, a darker color adds contrast; if you want something airy, a light neutral opens the space.

## Rug care and maintenance
Vacuum weekly, rotate monthly, and deal with spills promptly. For more thorough cleaning, check the manufacturer guidelines. A rug pad helps prevent slipping and reduces wear. Protect from direct sunlight to minimize color fade.
