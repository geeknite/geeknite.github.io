## Practical usage tips: maximizing your Lair with other Geeknite favorites

- Sleeve compatibility: If you’re using premium sleeves, ensure the tray depth accommodates your sleeve thickness. Double-sleeving can alter the fit, so plan for it in your layout. In many cases, standard 60-card deck sleeves will leave room for top dividers and still maintain clean stacks.
- Divider strategy: Use color-coded or labeled dividers to separate formats, editions, or even local tournament groups. Don’t be afraid to rearrange as your collection grows or changes with new sets.
- Display vs. storage balance: If you like to showcase your collection, place your favorite or most recently played decks in accessible, upper trays. Reserve the deeper, lower trays for long-term storage and bulk card piles.
- Maintenance cadence: Do a monthly wipe-down and quick reorganization. A small maintenance ritual keeps things from sliding into “battlefield overflow” territory where you can’t find your favorite deck without performing a treasure hunt.
