Cleaning and maintenance: keeping the set shipshape

- Cushions: Remove covers if possible and launder according to the tag. If you’re strapped for time or washing facilities, a quick wipe with a mild soap solution and water should keep the cushions presentable between deeper cleans.
- Weave: The PE wicker resists stains and reduces moisture absorption. A damp cloth is typical for a light wipe-down; avoid harsh abrasives that could scratch the finish.
- Frames: A light rinse with water and a quick wipe-down to remove dirt and pollen should be enough. If you’re in a coastal area, you might want to rinse after storms to avoid salt buildup.

Weather resistance: rain, shine, and the occasional neighborhood rainstorm

A set in wicker is often pitched as rain-friendly but not rain-delirious. The synthetic weave sheds water well, and the cushions’ covers are designed to dry quickly. If your climate is heavy on rain, consider storing the cushions indoors during long downpours or using furniture covers. The aluminum frame resists rust, which means you won’t visit the hardware store for a replacement frame after a season or two of umbrella storms. Pro tip: rotate the seating arrangement to prevent sun-bleaching on one side if you’re in a very sunny locale—balance your aesthetic with longevity.

Price, value, and warranty: what you’re really buying

Outdoor furniture sits in a tricky price zone.
