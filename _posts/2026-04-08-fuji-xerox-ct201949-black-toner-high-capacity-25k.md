### Installation Quirks and Troubleshooting

No cartridge review would be complete without the usual adventures. Some users report minor alignment prompts after installation, requiring a short calibration cycle, a printer restart, or a soft reset. Others may see a temporary white line or a slight density shift on the first pages after swapping. These are not catastrophes; they are the printer’s way of catching up with the new drum. Most issues are resolved with a quick calibration print, followed by a couple of quiet office runs to settle the toner into the developer unit.

If your printer stubbornly refuses the new cartridge with a cryptic error, double‑check the cartridge part number, ensure the seal is fully removed (you would be surprised how many times we see a stubborn seal once the printer starts complaining), and verify that the cartridge is properly seated. In rare cases a firmware update may adjust cartridge recognition behavior. As always, keep your firmware up to date for the smoothest experience.
