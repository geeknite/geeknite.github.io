## The Basics

### What is Struggle of Empires?

Struggle of Empires is a strategic board game where players take on the roles of colonial powers vying for control of the world. Designed by Martin Wallace, the game involves diplomacy, warfare, and resource management. The deluxe edition enhances the experience with metal coins and high-quality components.
