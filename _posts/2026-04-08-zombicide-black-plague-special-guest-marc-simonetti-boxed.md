## Practical tips for owners
### Setup tips
- Start by checking the art book s protective sleeve and remove it carefully to avoid reflections on the glass table. 
- Inspect new miniatures for flash and mold lines and use an appropriate hobby knife if you find the sterling lines will hinder the first few turns. 
- Create a play area that has enough space for the new tiles and ensure the lighting accentuates the Simonetti art without washing out the color palette.

### Maintenance tips
- After long sessions consider using a matte protective spray on the cards to preserve the printing quality and prevent color transfer during chaotic shuffle moments. 
- Store the box in a dry environment with the artwork facing outward so your collector self can be pleased even when the table is cleared.
