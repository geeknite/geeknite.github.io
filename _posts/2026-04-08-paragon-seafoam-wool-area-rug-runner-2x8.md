### Stain Resistance and Cleaning

Stain resistance in wool is a team sport. The natural lanolin in wool helps repel moisture to a degree, but you still want to address spills quickly. Blot, don’t rub, with clean cloth or paper towels. For tougher stains, a gentle wool-safe cleaner works best. Test a small inconspicuous patch first—because even wool superheroes have weak spots around the cape’s embroidery. Vacuum regularly with a beater-bar setting or a suction-only attachment to keep the pile looking sharp without flattening the luxury. If you have pets, the wool’s natural fibers tend to trap hair and dander less stubbornly than some synthetic rugs, making cleanup easier on your nerves and your vacuum cleaner.

### Pet-Friendliness and Child-Friendliness

Wool fibers are generally good at hiding minor imperfections and do not attract static as aggressively as some synthetic fibers. This is a nice feature for homes with kids and pets, as it reduces the likelihood of visible pilling after a few weeks of life’s chaos. Of course, you should still supervise novelty plastic toys, rogue crayons, and runaway LEGO pieces; wool isn’t immune to the occasional melted crayon meltdown. Overall, the Paragon Seafoam Runner is a practical, stylish choice for families and fur families alike.

### Heat, Sunlight, and Colorfastness

If your hallway basks in sunlight, the seafoam hue will gently fade over time—though not as dramatically as cotton or natural fibers that aren’t colorfast. Wool tends to hold its color better than many synthetics, but prolonged sun exposure can still shift tone subtly. If you have windows that glare with a full-on solar laser, consider a window treatment or rotating this rug’s position every few months to preserve its vibrancy. ## Care Guidelines: Simple, No-Nonsense Routine

### Quick Weekly Ritual

- Vacuum once or twice a week with a gentle setting. Avoid the rotor brush if you have a delicate pile, to prevent fuzzing.
- Rotate the rug every few months to maintain even wear and avoid hot spots under furniture.
- Flip or flip side? Some wool runners are reversible. If the Paragon Seafoam Runner is reversible, you’ll get a bonus of two looks in one, effectively doubling your mood-rotation options.

### Stain-Busting Strategy

1. Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Do not rub, as rubbing drives stains deeper into the fiber.
2. For liquid spills, use a tiny amount of water and blot again. If color starts to bleed, stop and consult a professional. Wool is resilient but not invincible.
3. For stubborn stains, consider a wool-safe stain remover. Always test on a hidden corner first.

### Cleaning Frequency and Deep-Cleaning

Deep-cleaning wool rugs is a cautionary tale you tell your future self: you can steam-clean, but ensure your machine’s temperature and agitation are not harsh on wool fibers. If you aren’t sure, hire a professional cleaner who specializes in wool and natural fiber rugs. Your rug will thank you, and your cleaning bill will appear slightly less monstrous when you realize you avoided DIY missteps that could ruin the weave.
