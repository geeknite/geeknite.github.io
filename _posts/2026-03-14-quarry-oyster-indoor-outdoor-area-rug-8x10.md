## Cleaning and Maintenance: Keeping Your Rug Fresh Without Wearing Out Your Will to Live

### Regular cleaning routines
- Vacuum weekly: A simple pass or two to lift dust, pet hair, and epic dust bunnies.
- Shake or beat outdoors: If you’ve got a porch or balcony, a brisk shake helps dislodge stubborn debris.
- Quick damp-wipe for spills: A mild soap solution and a soft cloth can handle most accidents. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade color or fibers.

### Stain removal tips
- Act fast on spills: Blot rather than rub to prevent the stain from diving deeper into the fibers.
- Mild cleaners only: Use gentle, pH-balanced cleaners. No need to turn your rug into a chemistry experiment.
- Test first: Always test a small, inconspicuous area before applying cleaners widely.

### Storage and winter care
If you’re in a climate where you bring outdoor furniture indoors for the winter, consider rolling the rug or storing it in a dry space. The low-profile weave packs nicely and won’t mind a little roll-and-stow. If you prefer to keep it in place year-round, ensure there’s adequate drainage to deter mold or mildew during wet seasons.
