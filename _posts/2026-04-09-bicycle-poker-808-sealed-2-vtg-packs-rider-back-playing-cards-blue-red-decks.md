## Storage, Care, and Longevity
### How to Store Your Decks Properly
Store them away from direct sunlight and extreme humidity. Heat can warp the stock, humidity can encourage warping, and sunlight can fade the color export of the Rider Back’s ink. A dedicated card box or a small drawer in a wood shelf with a decent climate tends to be an ideal home. If you’re into populating a card wall, the two boxes can be displayed side-by-side to emphasize the “Blue vs Red” dialogue you and your friends keep having.

### Handling Habits That Prolong Life
- Avoid bending the cards beyond reasonable degrees.
- Keep cards clean and dry; moisture isn’t your friend here.
- Use a proper card shuffler to minimize edge wear during heavy-use nights.
- If you play with a dusty room, consider a quick wipe or a gentle fanning practice before you go full tilt.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
If you ever need to refresh the feel, a light wipe with a dry microfiber cloth can help. Avoid chemical cleaners that can degrade the finish or strip color.
