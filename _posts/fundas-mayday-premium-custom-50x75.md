## **Why Sleeve Your Cards?**

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of these particular sleeves, let's address the age-old question: *To sleeve or not to sleeve?* This is a common dilemma for many board gamers. Here's a quick rundown of the pros and cons:

### **Pros**

1. **Protection**: Sleeves protect your cards from dirt, spills, and general wear and tear.
2. **Durability**: Sleeved cards last longer and maintain their condition, which is especially important for collectors or expensive games.
3. **Aesthetic Appeal**: Sleeved cards often look nicer and have a consistent feel during gameplay.
4. **Resale Value**: If you ever decide to sell your game, sleeved cards can fetch a higher price.

### **Cons**

1. **Cost**: Premium sleeves can add up, especially for games with a lot of cards.
2. **Storage**: Sleeved cards take up more space and might not fit back into the original game box.
3. **Shuffling**: Some people find sleeved cards harder to shuffle.

Now that we've covered the basics, let's get into the specifics of the Fundas Mayday Premium Custom 50x75 mm sleeves.
