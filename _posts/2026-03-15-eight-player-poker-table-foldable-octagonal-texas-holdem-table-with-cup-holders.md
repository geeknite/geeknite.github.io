## A Quick Compare: Octagon vs. Square vs. Round (In Geeknite Humor)
- Octagon: Socially inclusive seating, less angular aggression, looks unique, folds down to a portable beast, and somehow still looks like a pro piece of gaming gear. It’s the “cool uncle” of poker tables.

## Maintenance: How to Keep It Alive for Years
- Wipe down after use, especially after beverages, to prevent long-term staining. Quick, simple cleans beat deep cleans that involve your family’s heirloom furniture polish.
- Check the hinge screws every few months—vibration and enthusiastic cards can loosen fast if you’re not paying attention.
- Keep the table away from direct sunlight to prevent fading of the felt and the wood/laminate edges from becoming brittle over time.
- If chips or cups gather moisture in the cup holders, dry them out with a cloth to avoid corrosion around any metal components.
