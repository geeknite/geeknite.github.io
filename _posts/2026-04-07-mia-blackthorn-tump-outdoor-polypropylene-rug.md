## Maintenance and Care Tips
- Regular cleaning: A weekly or biweekly shake-out or vacuum (with a hard floor setting) can keep dirt from embedding itself into the fibers. If you’re using a vacuum, ensure you don’t snag the edges with a high-suction setting.
- Stain management: Act quickly on spills. Blot with a dry cloth to remove excess liquid, then clean with a mild soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the fibers or affect color.
- Storage: Roll or fold loosely and store in a dry area when not in use for extended periods. If you’re stacking multiple rugs, place a protective layer between them to minimize creases.
- Seasonal rotation: If you have multiple outdoor rugs, rotate them to ensure a more even sun exposure and color retention across your patio design.
