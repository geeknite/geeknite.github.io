### Cleaning and care: keeping the beige ivory pristine without turning you into a cleaning monk
- Vacuum regularly with a suction setting that is gentle enough to avoid rough treatment of the pile.
- Clean spills promptly with a blot method; avoid rubbing as that can push stains deeper.
- For stubborn stains, consider a mild carpet cleaner that is safe for synthetic fibers. Always test in a small corner first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to maintain even wear and to avoid one corner fading differently if sunlight is uneven.
- Use a rug pad to extend life and prevent slipping, especially if you have a busy household where pets roam and remote controls disappear.
