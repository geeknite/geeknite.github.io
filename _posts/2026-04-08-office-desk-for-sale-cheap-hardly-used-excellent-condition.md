## Cable Management and Maintenance
The built in cable management is basic but workable. A single grommet hole allows entry for power and data cables, while a shallow channel runs along the underside to keep things from sliding off during a dramatic keyboard slam or an accidental elbow jab during a late-night caffeine sprint.

### Cable Organization Tips
- Use Velcro wraps to bundle related cables together and reduce clutter.
- Label each cable with a small tag so you can identify it without tracing every cord in the bundle.
- Consider a small under-desk tray for power strips and hubs to lift them off the floor and reduce heat buildup.
- If you add a monitor arm or a taller PC case, ensure the cords have slack to avoid mere inches of bending that could lead to wear over time.

### Maintenance Routine
Schedule a quarterly check on screws around the legs and cross braces to maintain stability. A quick tightening can prevent wobble that might occur after repeated rearrangement of devices. Clean the surface with a microfiber cloth and mild cleaner to maintain the veneer finish and keep coffee rings from becoming permanent artifacts of your work life.
