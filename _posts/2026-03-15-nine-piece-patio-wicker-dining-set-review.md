Outdoor furniture is a commitment, like choosing a familiar character in a long-running sci-fi series. Resin wicker is resistant to sun and humidity, but prolonged exposure to harsh weather still demands a cover or occasional cleaning. The cushions should be stored indoors or in a patio box during heavy rain or winter months to prolong their life. ### Cleaning hacks you’ll want to bookmark
- Wicker: brush or vacuum to remove dust, then wipe with mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals.
- Cushions: remove covers and wash per label. Dry completely before returning to the chair to prevent mildew.
- Glass: a microfiber cloth with a window cleaner keeps the surface streak-free.
