## Weather Resistance and Maintenance
### How Does It Handle the Great Outdoors?
Rattan and cushions are tested in the brutal arena of sun, rain, wind, and the occasional bird that thinks your chair is a restroom. This set is designed to handle typical drizzle and the occasional summer squall. The synthetic rattan resists UV damage, which means color won’t disappear into a pale whisper after a season of sunbathing and coffee sips outdoors. The cushions are water-resistant but not waterproof; expect to dry out cushions if you leave them out in a downpour. A simple solution is to store cushions during heavy rain or bring them inside when storms threaten to bring their own weather drama.

### Cleaning and Care Tips
- Wipe the rattan with a damp cloth; avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the weave over time.
- Use a mild soap solution for stubborn stains on the cushions; let them air dry completely before placing back in the set.
- For deeper protection, consider applying a light sealant to the rattan frame every season, but test on a small area first to ensure color consistency.
- Cover the set during extended periods of non-use to minimize the impact of dirt, bird droppings, and curious neighborhood squirrels.

For reference on long-term care, you can skim external guides, but remember: your mileage may vary, and your garden might have more sun exposure than a suburban solar panel installer on a sunny Tuesday.

### Weather-Related Scenarios: Rain, Shine, and the Cat-Alarm Clock
- Light rain: You’re fine keeping cushions outside with a quick towel dry after the rain passes.
- Heavy rain: Move cushions indoors, collapse the modular pieces if you need to, and give the frame a wipe-down after the sky stops weeping.
- Snow or frost: The set isn’t designed for subzero adventures; store cushions indoors and use a breathable cover to preserve the weave until spring returns with a proper snowfall-free horizon.
