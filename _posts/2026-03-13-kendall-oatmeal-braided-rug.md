### Durability and Maintenance

Durability is the real test for any rug that gets daily use. Kendall Oatmeal Braided Rug passes the drop test with minimal drama. The cotton blend resists pilling and shows resilience against vacuuming. The braided construction reduces edge fray, which is nice because frayed edges are the uninvited guests of interior decor. When it comes to stains, the rug handles most common kitchen and coffee accidents: a splash of red wine will show but can be spot cleaned with a mild solution. For tougher spots, a gentle scrub with a soft brush works, though you should avoid aggressive chemical solvents that could degrade the fibers.

To keep the color honest, it helps to rotate the rug every few months to even out any sun exposure patterns. This is not a NASA level operation; it is more like sweeping the legs of your furniture to ensure even wear. We also recommend using a rug pad to prevent slippage and to add a little extra cushioning underfoot. A pad also reduces noise and helps protect wood floors from scratches.

### Care and Cleaning Guidance

- Vacuum regularly with a brush attachment; avoid beater bars that can snag the braids.
- Spot clean spills immediately with a mild detergent and water; avoid excessive moisture that can cause the fibers to swell.
- Rotate the rug 2-4 times per year to balance wear and sunlight exposure.
- If you need a deep clean, consider professional cleaning or a gentle machine wash if the manufacturer permits it; check the care label before dunking in a washing machine.
