## Maintenance and Care
Outdoor pine requires a simple care routine to keep its charm intact. Regular cleaning with a mild soap solution followed by a rinse helps prevent grime buildup. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade finishes. A seasonally or biennially applied water-repellent sealant will extend the life of the wood by creating a protective barrier against moisture. Remember, outdoor furniture is a long-term investment in your outdoor lifestyle, not just a one-season fling.

Cushions deserve love too. If removable, wash according to the label, and when in doubt, spot clean with mild soap and a damp cloth. For non-removable covers, a gentle approach with mild soap is still the way to go. Replace covers when fading or fabric wear becomes unsightly; it’s a way to refresh the look without replacing the entire set. This approach keeps your investment costs down while keeping your patio fresh and inviting.
