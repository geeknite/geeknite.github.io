## Maintenance and Care
Caring for pine wood outdoors involves routine checks and light maintenance. Clean the furniture with a mild soap solution and rinse with water. Avoid harsh chemicals that can strip the protective finish. A maintenance schedule that includes reapplication of a water-repellent sealant every season or two can extend the life of the wood significantly. Remember that outdoor furniture is a long-term commitment. You are not just buying a set to fill space; you are investing in a lifestyle decision that says you will host more barbecues than you thought possible and you will do it with a smile.

For cushion care, remove removable covers if possible and wash per label instructions. If your cushions are not removable, you can spot-clean with mild soap and a damp cloth. If you notice fading or fabric wear, you can replace the covers to freshen up the look without buying a whole new set. This approach keeps your investment cost down and your patio looking fresh.
