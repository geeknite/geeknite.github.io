## Maintenance myths debunked and practical care tips

- Myth: Braided rugs are high-maintenance and require professional cleaning. Reality: Regular vacuuming, quick spot cleanings, and occasional shaking out plus a light rotation go a long way. Most spills respond well to blotting and a mild detergent.
- Myth: Color fades quickly. Reality: The 782 color blends are designed to hold up to daily life. Don’t scrub aggressively; gentle care preserves the vibrancy for longer.
- Myth: All rugs smell strange after cleaning. Reality: If you use a mild detergent and air-dry properly, you’ll avoid the musty aftertaste. A quick vacuum between cleanings helps keep them fresh without overpowering scents.
