Care and handling tips

To maximize the longevity of your pulled cards, handle them with clean hands and avoid bending or flexing the cards when sorting. Use protective sleeves and, if you plan to display, consider top loading for the most valuable pieces. For foils and rarities, keep a separate binder page or a dividers system to prevent the finish from getting scratched against other cards. If you trade, do so in a controlled environment with protective sleeves on both sides and a clear understanding of the card values you are trading away or seeking.
