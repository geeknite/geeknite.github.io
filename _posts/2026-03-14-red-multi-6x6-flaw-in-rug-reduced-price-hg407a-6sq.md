## Care tips and tricks: keeping the color bloom alive

- Vacuum regularly with a light hand to avoid fiber distortion. This helps maintain the pile’s evenness and reduces the risk of visible wear in high-traffic zones.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can drive stains deeper into the fibers.
- Test any cleaning solution on a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure color stability. If you are unsure, use water or a mild detergent.
- Consider rotating the rug every few months to minimize wear patterns that could develop from furniture placement or sun exposure.
- For stubborn stains, consult a professional rug cleaner with synthetic fiber experience, especially if you want to preserve the dye integrity.
