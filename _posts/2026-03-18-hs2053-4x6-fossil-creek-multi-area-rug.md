## Cleaning and Maintenance: Keeping It Fresh Without Losing Your Mind

Maintenance is the boring part of rug ownership, but it is the part that actually matters when your carpet becomes the stage for daily chaos. Fossil Creek Multi is designed with everyday life in mind. The fiber density helps resist heavy foot traffic, and the colorway hides minor dirt until you can no longer pretend the dog walked in mud when the sun is shining at noon.

Vacuuming is straightforward—no special attachments required, though using a standard beater-bar vacuum is recommended for deeper cleaning. For spills, act quickly. Blot (do not rub) with a clean cloth. Blotting helps prevent the stain from seeping deeper into the fibers. If something stubborn happens, a mild cleaner approved for synthetic fibers should take care of the mess. Always test any cleaner on a small hidden area first.

The rug dries fairly quickly after a spill, which is a small mercy that prevents the tyranny of damp floors for the rest of the day. If you have pets that like to claim a corner for themselves, you will appreciate how well the pattern camouflages fur unless you are currently staring at a cat with a white belly fur stain. In that case, you might consider a lint roller as a best friend rather than an accessory.
