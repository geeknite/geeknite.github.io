## What is The Mind: Special Edition?

The Mind: Special Edition is a cooperative card game where players must work together to play cards in ascending order without communicating. Yes, you read that right—no talking, no signaling, just pure intuition and mind-melding. This special edition adds new twists and challenges to the base game, enhancing the experience with new cards, rules, and unpredictable situations.

### The Basics

In The Mind: Special Edition, players receive a hand of cards numbered from 1 to 100. The goal is for the team to play these cards in ascending order without discussing their hands. Sounds simple, right? Well, get ready for chaos and hilarity as you try to read each other's minds!

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Cards**: Numbered cards and special action cards that add layers of challenge and fun.
- **Level Cards**: Tracks the team's progress through increasingly difficult levels.
- **Life and Star Tokens**: Used to track the team's lives and special abilities.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the cards. Unless you want a chewed-up game where the cards themselves become a mystery.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up The Mind: Special Edition is quick and easy. Shuffle the deck, deal the appropriate number of cards to each player based on the level, and distribute life and star tokens. Now you're ready to begin your mind-melding adventure.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players silently decide the order in which to play their cards. When someone thinks their card is the next in the sequence, they place it on the table. If the team successfully plays the cards in ascending order, they advance to the next level. If not, the team loses a life.

### Special Challenges

The Special Edition introduces action cards that can be used to shake things up. These cards might allow players to skip a turn, swap cards, or even peek at each other's hands. Use them wisely, or face the hilarious consequences.
