## Care and maintenance
Card care matters. Avoid high humidity environments or moisture that can cause warping of the cards edges. After playing, a quick wipe with a dry cloth can help reduce surface residue and keep the cards feeling fresh. If you are a streamer, you may want to keep a gentle humidifier in the room to ensure the cards do not dry out and crack in extreme climates.

### Cleaning tips
Keep them away from direct sunlight to prevent color shifting; store in a cool, dry place for long-term storage, and keep the backs crisp and readable for your next routine.
