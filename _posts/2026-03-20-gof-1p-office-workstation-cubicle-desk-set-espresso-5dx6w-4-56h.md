## Durability and Maintenance

For daily desk duties: typing, writing, occasional printer-harassment, this setup holds up. Espresso surfaces wipe clean with a damp microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh solvents; water and mild soap are your friends. If you spill coffee, wipe promptly; you’ll want to preserve those nourishing coffee notes as a reminder that you earned your caffeine. For stubborn stains, a gentle wood cleaner will help. If you really want it to look pristine for meetings, a light buff with a dry cloth once a week keeps things looking showroom-ready.
