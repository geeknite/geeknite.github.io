Maintenance and care: keeping the set youthful and photo-ready
- Cushions: wash covers on a gentle cycle when necessary, air dry away from direct heat. If you’re stubborn about stains, post-wash sunlight can help but avoid overheating.
- Wicker: wipe with mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive brushes. If you encounter scuffs, a little petroleum jelly on the edges can help restore some shine (test on a small area first).
- Frames: wipe with a damp cloth and check the screws for tightness a couple of times per season. A little DIY elbow grease goes a long way toward maintaining the set’s structural integrity.
- Storage cover: clean with a mild soapy solution and let dry fully before returning to storage. A dry cover is less likely to trap moisture and encourage mildew growth, which is not the look you’re going for in a backyard photoshoot.
