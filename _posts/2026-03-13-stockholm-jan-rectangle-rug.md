## Maintenance and Durability

Maintenance with Stockholm Jan is straightforward. Regular vacuuming to remove dust and pet hair is enough to keep it looking clean for longer periods. For spills, blotting with a clean cloth is recommended rather than rubbing, which can push stains deeper into the pile. A mild cleaning solution is acceptable if a more thorough clean is required, but avoid harsh chemicals that could compromise the fiber integrity. For those worried about color longevity, the colorfastness of the synthetic fibers has been tested to resist fading under normal indoor lighting conditions. If your space experiences heavy sunlight midday, consider rotating the rug occasionally or pairing it with window treatments that temper intense rays.

Rot and wear over time are normal for any rug that sees daily use. The Stockholm Jan is designed to age gracefully, developing a comfortable patina rather than a dramatic wear pattern. If you notice edges curling or compressive wear in high traffic zones, a non slip pad or rug grippers can help flatten the edges and extend the life of the rug. The wear pattern will be most noticeable in spots where a chair is frequently moved or where cozy lounge areas invite foot traffic; otherwise, the rug will age with character rather than showing a fast track to retirement.
