## Care and maintenance: keeping the rainbow vibrant

Colorful rugs demand gentle care to retain their vibrant look over time. Here’s a practical care checklist you can print and pin to your fridge, right next to the grocery list of doom:

- Vacuum regularly with a low-suction setting and a standard bare-floor attachment. Avoid aggressive beater bars that could disturb the pile’s uniformity.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Do not rub, as rubbing can push stains deeper and cause color bleed into neighboring sections.
- Use a mild detergent solution for spots and test on a hidden area first. If you’re allergic to drama, avoid using harsh chemicals that could alter the color balance.
- Keep out of direct sunlight for extended periods to minimize dye fading. If you’re in a sun-drenched apartment, rotate the rug occasionally or use UV-blocking window treatments.
- Consider a rug pad under the Torino to reduce slipping, improve comfort, and help preserve the pile over time.

If you’re curious about more advanced care, there are plenty of reputable sources that discuss synthetic fiber maintenance and dye-fastness.
