## Storage and care tips

Proper storage protects your investment in both playability and aesthetics. A few sensible steps can extend the life of your holo cards and the rest of the lot:

- Use acid-free sleeves for each card, especially for the holo variants which can be more brittle over time.
- Store in a binder with protective sheets designed for trading cards. A binder with a rigid cover can also serve as a display case when you want to brag about your pull lines.
- Keep your cards away from direct sunlight and humidity. The holo surface can become dull if exposed to intense light for long periods.
- When shuffling, use a gentle overhand method to reduce edge wear on non foil cards, and consider a top-loader approach for your most valuable holo pulls.
