## Assembly and Setup

### How Hard Is It to Put Together?
If you’ve assembled a bookshelf from a flat-pack and lived to tell the tale, you’ll be fine here. Metropolitan ships mostly pre-assembled in large sections, but you will still need to do a proper setup, which includes attaching rails, mounting the legs, and dialing in the levelers. Expect about 60–120 minutes depending on your confidence with hex wrenches and the occasional swear word that may escape during the wiring of those levelers. Pro tip: clear the space first, lay out all the parts, and read the manual like it’s a treasure map to avoid turning this into a three-hour puzzle that ends with you ordering more parts.

### Maintenance and Routine Care
A quick wipe-down after intense sessions helps preserve the finish. Use a damp cloth for the playing surface and avoid soaking it with liquids because foosball can get dramatic; one spill and you’ll have to call it a “field accident.” Keep the balls clean and replace them if they start to feel like blender blades rather than friendly projectiles. Regularly check the leg levelers and tighten any loose hardware. A small bag of spare washers and a couple of wrenches are helpful, and don’t forget to give the rods a quick spin every so often to prevent rusting and to remind your friends that you mean business.

## Maintenance and Care

To keep the Metropolitan in top shape, treat it like an investment in your own future bragging rights. Wipe the surface with a microfiber cloth; avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the finish. If you spill soda, wipe it up quickly—carbonation is not a friend of foosball ethics. Periodically check the screws on the legs and rails; a loose bolt can turn a smooth game into a wobbly chaos event. If you’re serious about longevity, consider silicone mats under the table to catch drips and prevent leg slip on slick floors. And yes, please rotate your players if possible; a balanced rotation keeps wear even across the field and preserves the “new table” look longer than a typical gaming weekend would deserve.
