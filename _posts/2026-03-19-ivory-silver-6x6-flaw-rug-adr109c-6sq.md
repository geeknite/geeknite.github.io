## Build quality and maintenance: What you should know before buying

### Cleaning and care tips

- Vacuum regularly with a setting that respects pile height to avoid flattening the texture. A gentle setting is your friend here.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper or distort the pile alignment.
- For stubborn spots, consider a mild detergent diluted in water and test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Use a rug pad to minimize friction against the floor and to keep the rug in place, preventing edges from curling and creating a trip hazard.

### Durability expectations

Rugs of this size and construction are built for daily life, not for stunts. If you’re a guest who does cartwheels in the living room, you might want to consider a sturdier option or simply not admit to cartwheels as a daily hobby. For households with kids and pets, this rug offers a practical balance: it’s resilient enough to handle everyday wear, and the reduced price offset makes up for minor cosmetic quirks that may show up over time. The bottom line is that you’re buying a functional piece that also adds visual interest, not a display-only artifact.
