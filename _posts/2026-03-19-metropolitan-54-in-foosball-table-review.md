### Gameplay Experience

In actual play, the Metropolitan performs with a fairness you’d expect from a product aimed at both enthusiasts and casual players. ### Maintenance and Longevity
To keep things playing well for years, a little care goes a long way. Wipe down the rods to prevent corrosion, lubricate the bearings as needed, and check the leg bolts periodically—especially after a wild, celebratory spin of the ball after a big goal. The MDF or resin playing surface is durable enough for daily use, but like all surfaces, it benefits from occasional protection against heavy scratches and excessive weight from overzealous players who might think they’re auditioning for a demolition derby of couch cushions. If you treat it well, this table can be the star of your home-game nights for a long time.
