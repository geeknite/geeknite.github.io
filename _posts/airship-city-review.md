## Gameplay Mechanics: How Do You Fly This Thing?

### The Basics

Airship City is a strategic board game where players take on the role of city builders in the sky. Your mission? To construct a thriving metropolis by gathering resources, completing contracts, and outmaneuvering your rivals. Sounds simple? Think again!

### Resource Management

The game revolves around collecting various resources, such as wood, iron, and fuel. These resources are vital for building structures, upgrading your airship, and fulfilling contracts. The catch? Resources are limited, and competition can be fierce. You’ll need to plan your moves wisely and anticipate your opponents' actions to stay ahead.

### Contract Fulfillment

Fulfilling contracts is a major part of Airship City. Contracts give you victory points and special bonuses that can give you an edge over your competitors. However, contracts come with specific requirements, and you’ll need to balance your resource management with contract completion to maximize your points.

### Building and Upgrading

Building and upgrading structures in your floating city is crucial for gaining advantages and unlocking new possibilities. From workshops to factories, each building offers unique benefits. Strategically placing and upgrading your buildings will be key to your success.
