## Care tips and maintenance: keeping the look alive

- Vacuum regularly using a suction-only setting; avoid beater bars for extended periods to prevent frizzing of the pile.
- Treat spills immediately by blotting with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push liquid deeper into the fibers. A mild soap solution can help, but test in an inconspicuous spot first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to prevent predictable sun fading in one corner if your rug sits near a window.
- For stubborn stains, consult a professional rug care service to avoid irreparable dye bleed or fiber damage.


### Maintenance myths debunked

- Myth: Velvet touch means fragile fibers. Reality: hand tufted wool can handle regular use with proper care.
- Myth: Dark colors hide dirt. Reality: light creams show wear more quickly; this palette balances that risk with forgiving strawberry accents.
- Myth: All modern rugs are machine-made. Reality: this piece proves you can have a handmade vibe with consistent quality.
