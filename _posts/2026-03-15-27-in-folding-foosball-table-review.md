## Build Quality and Specs

This table isn’t carved from a single block of hardwood, but it isn’t a flimsy plastic toy either. Expect a laminated or veneered surface with steel rods and plastic players. The folding design shines when you’re ready to go from storage to stadium. Unfold the legs, extend the frame, and lock each section with the built-in latches.
