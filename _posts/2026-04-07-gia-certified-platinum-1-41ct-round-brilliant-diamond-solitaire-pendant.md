## Care and maintenance
### Cleaning routine
A gentle routine is best: warm water, mild dish soap, and a soft brush. Avoid abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals, especially chlorine, which can dull the finish of platinum and affect the diamond’s appearance over time. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a soft cloth. If you wear the pendant daily, a monthly polish from a jeweler can keep the metal bright and the setting tight.

### Storage and travel tips
Store separately from other jewelry in a soft pouch or a jewelry box with compartments. When you travel, keep the pendant in its own case to prevent tangling or scratching. If you travel by air with the jewelry, keep it in your hand luggage and avoid exposing it to X‑ray machines too many times—no, they will not mutate the diamond, but it never hurts to be cautious.
