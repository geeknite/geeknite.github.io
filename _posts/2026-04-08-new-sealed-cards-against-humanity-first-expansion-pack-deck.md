How to play with the expansion
If you know the base game, you already know the rhythm: players submit white cards in response to a black prompt. The Card Czar (the rotating judge) picks the funniest, the weirdest, or the most brutally honest response, and points are awarded accordingly. The expansion keeps that backbone intact while giving you more prompts and more opportunities to get creative. The new black prompts are designed to work with a broad mix of white cards, which means you can mix and match with your existing card pool and keep the rounds feeling fresh.

Tips for getting the most laughs
- Shuffle the new whites into your existing pile and let the chemistry between old favorites and new additions do the heavy lifting.
- Start with a short hand in the first few rounds to gauge your group’s humor temperature. It’s better to build to bigger punchlines than to overwhelm players with a flood of options.
- If someone is sensitive to certain topics, have a curated subset of cleaner cards ready for a quick pivot. The expansion still plays nicely with lighthearted humor.
- Consider a calendar night: pick a theme for a few rounds (nerd culture, pop culture, office humor) and see how the new prompts interact with those categories.
