### Gameplay and strategies

To play the deck well, keep a few guiding principles in mind:

- Early tempo matters more than you think. Use your first few turns to establish pressure and demand answers from your opponent.
- Don’t over commit on the board if your opponent has a clean answer or a path to game stabilization. It is better to preserve your threats for a stronger moment than to flood the board prematurely.
- Plan for the late game. Even if the deck aims to win early, you want options to finish on the long game when your opponent finally stabilizes.
