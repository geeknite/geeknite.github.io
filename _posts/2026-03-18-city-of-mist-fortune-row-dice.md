## Maintenance, Durability, and Care

Premium dice should last you many campaigns if looked after well. Fortune Row dice benefit from the same care you give to your other resin dice: clean with mild soap and warm water, dry thoroughly, and avoid extreme temperature swings that could stress the resin and slowly impact clarity or color depth. A padded storage case or a dedicated dice tray is a good investment; it protects the finish from dings and keeps them from rolling into remote corners of the table where you can pretend that you did not notice their disappearance for a week.

If you travel with the set, wrap gently in soft cloth or a dedicated dice sleeve. Avoid crowding them with metal coins or keys—metal on resin is a recipe for micro-scratches and a less-than-sparkling aesthetic after a few sessions. The fortune of fortune row is that these dice look better with a bit of history, so don’t panic if you notice a minor scuff or two; it may even add character to your set and remind you of the many sessions those dice have graced.
