If you think UNO is simply draw a card and yell UNO, you are in for a surprisingly comprehensive upgrade. The result is a familiar framework with enough fresh twists to keep even seasoned players engaged.

### The Wild Card Variants
- Wild Swap Hands: This is the strategic power move that turns the table in a single moment. When played, you choose another player to swap hands with. The execution can lead to dramatic turns when someone is holding a handful of color chaos while you hold a strategic card that could end the game on your next turn.
- Wild Color Lock: Forces a color lock for a couple of turns, making players plan more than one move ahead. It can create tense moments when everyone realizes they have to chase a single color that keeps eluding their reach.
