## Maintenance and Cleaning: Practical Magic
### Daily Care, The Simple Math
- Vacuum regularly to remove grit that grinds fibers down.
- Flip or rotate the rug to promote even wear. Yes, even long runners deserve a rebrand from time to time.
- Address spills promptly with blotting (don’t rub; you’ll push the stain deeper). Mild cleaning solutions are fine—test on a small area first.

### Stains: Quick Response Guide
1. Blot, don’t rub. 2. Use a mild detergent solution (a drop of dish soap in warm water). 3. Blot again and rinse with clean water. 4. If color transfer seems stubborn, dab with a white cloth and a bit of white vinegar solution, then blot dry.

Note: Persistent stains may require a professional cleaning. If you’re dealing with a pet accident, you may want to consider a stain-specific enzymatic cleaner—just follow the product directions.

External tip: For high-traffic entrances, consider rotating the rug every few months to distribute wear evenly. It’s like scheduling a tiny spa day for your floor. Your feet will appreciate it, even if your cats do not.
