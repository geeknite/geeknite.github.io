## Care along the Way: Cleaning and Maintenance

Treat the finish with care. Dry microfiber cloths are sufficient for routine dust. Avoid moisture on the foil and the card faces; humidity and moisture can cause micro-staining or warping of the foil paint. If you perform, rotate decks to minimize wear, and keep a backup in case a dramatic flourish goes wrong.
