## Gameplay overview: what you actually do when the box is open
Zombicide Black Plague is a co op experience where everyone plays a survivor with a unique ability set. The core loop is simple in the grand scheme: you explore, you fight, you level up, you bring a friend to the brink of a failed rescue, and you do it all before the clock runs out or the undead overwhelm you.

### The core mechanics in layman terms
- The map is a modular grid of tiles that you assemble to form your doom-laden city block. Each tile has doors, objectives, or hazards. The board grows as players decide to reveal more of the map, which is how you get that delicious sense of escalation without burning precious brain cells on a complex setup.
- Each turn you perform a number of actions per your survivor card. Actions include moving, searching, unlocking doors, picking up loot, and attacking with your weaponry. The order matters, because the layout of corridors can trap you like a sitcom roommate who forgot to pay rent.
- Combat uses dice, and Zombicide is famous for its dice mechanic. You roll a handful of special combat dice and hope the undead fail their moral compass checks. Critical hits and misses land with satisfying moments of cheers or groans that reverberate through your living room like a stadium of gamers.
- Zombies come in different flavors: walkers, runners, hulking brutes, and special zombies with unique abilities. They spawn in a way that makes you question whether you should have stayed in bed, or at least kept that extra shield in your inventory for the final boss moment.
- The game scales: more players mean more time pressure but also more firepower and a chance to coordinate slick combo moves. It is a dance of chaos where your planning often gets overtaken by a lucky dice roll and a heroic sprint through a narrow hallway that somehow makes perfect sense in the moment.

The essence is teamwork: communication, shared risk assessment, and the occasional dramatic sacrifice that would make even a soap opera blush. If you and your friends enjoy solving puzzles while under pressure, Zombicide Black Plague is basically a gym for your brain and a zoo for your nerves.
