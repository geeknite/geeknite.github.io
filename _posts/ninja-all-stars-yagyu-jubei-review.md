### Abilities and Skills

Jubei's abilities are where he truly shines. He combines offensive prowess with defensive tactics, making him a versatile character on the battlefield. His key abilities include:

- **Bushido**: Allows him to gain extra movement points after a successful attack, turning him into a swift and deadly force.
- **Parry**: This skill gives Jubei a chance to block incoming attacks, reflecting the blows back at his opponents. It's like having a ninja-themed boomerang!
- **Shadow Strike**: A powerful attack that can bypass enemy defenses, perfect for those pesky opponents who think they can hide behind their shields.
