## Sound Quality

### Advanced Audio Technology

The **AirPods Pro** feature **Adaptive EQ**, which automatically tunes the music to the shape of your ear for a personalized listening experience. Sounds fancy, right? It is! Whether you're jamming to your favorite tunes or listening to a podcast, the audio quality is crisp, clear, and immersive.

### Active Noise Cancellation

The **Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)** on the **AirPods Pro** is nothing short of magical. With a simple press of the stem, you can block out the world and dive into your favorite song, audiobook, or conspiracy theory podcast. Need to be aware of your surroundings? Switch to **Transparency Mode** with another press, and you'll hear everything around you without removing the earbuds.

## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by H1 Chip

![H1 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/airpods-pro/a/images/overview/h1__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}h1-chip)

The **H1 chip** is the heart of the **AirPods Pro**, delivering faster wireless connectivity, improved sound quality, and lower latency. Whether you're gaming, streaming, or just chatting with Siri, the H1 chip ensures everything runs smoothly.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 4.5 hours of listening time with **Active Noise Cancellation** enabled, and in our tests, it didn't disappoint. The **Wireless Charging Case** provides more than 24 hours of additional battery life, making the **AirPods Pro** perfect for long trips, commutes, or just avoiding human interaction.
