### Routine Maintenance
- Wipe down the cabinet with a mild cleaner after every few uses to keep the faux wood laminate looking sharp.
- Check leg levelers monthly and adjust as needed if you notice wobble on floor transitions.
- Clean the rods periodically to preserve smooth movement and minimize metal-on-metal noise.
