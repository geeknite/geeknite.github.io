### Micro Strategy Tips
- When in doubt, aim to short-circuit a sequence rather than waiting to complete a long run. Short-term gains on a tiny board can feel just as satisfying as a perfect long-term play in a larger game.
- Use the edge of the mini-table as a spatial cue. If you can drop a card near the edge to force an awkward but useful pile alignment, do it. Small nudge, big effect.
