## Battery Maintenance and Longevity
Most UPS batteries have a limited lifespan and should be tested and replaced every few years depending on usage and environmental conditions. Here are best practices:
- Periodically test the unit by simulating a power outage to verify the runtime and the shutdown process. Do this during non-crucial times, for example, when you’re not in the middle of a 2-hour render, so you don’t accidentally take a few extra minutes to recover.
- Keep the UPS in a cool, dry place. Heat degrades battery life, and humidity can create condensation on electronics—yes, even in a home office.
- Replace batteries per manufacturer’s schedule, or sooner if runtime degrades noticeably. It’s the small maintenance that saves you big headaches later.
