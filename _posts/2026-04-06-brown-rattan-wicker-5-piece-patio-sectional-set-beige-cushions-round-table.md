## Assembly and Maintenance

### Assembly experience

Most 5-piece patio sets arrive in modular components with minimal tools (an Allen wrench or standard screwdriver). The process is typically straightforward, with assembly times ranging from 45 minutes to two hours depending on your propensity for reading instruction diagrams aloud while muttering “I’ve got this.” The trick is to lay out each piece, match connectors, and let gravity do most of the heavy lifting. Pro-tip: a magnetic screwdriver or a helper can dramatically speed things up.

### Maintenance routines

- Wipe down wicker with a damp cloth to remove dust and pollen. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could degrade the resin weave.
- Clean cushions with mild soap and water; air-dry completely before reassembly to prevent mildew.
- Inspect for loose connectors or wobbly legs and tighten as needed. Regular check-ups keep the set feeling new for longer and prevent ghost-like creaks when you shift positions during a long chat with your neighbor about the weather.

### Weather considerations

PE wicker is designed to endure sun and moisture better than natural rattan. However, prolonged exposure to harsh weather can still cause color fading and fiber wear. A protective storage strategy—whether seasonal covers or a sheltered patio—extends the life of the weave and cushions. If you live in a region with heavy snowfall or hail, consider bringing cushions indoors during off-seasons or storing them in a dry, ventilated space.
