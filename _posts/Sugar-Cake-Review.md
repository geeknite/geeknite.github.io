## Ingredients: The Magic Behind the Sweetness

Let's start with the basics. A sugar cake is not just any cake; it's a symphony of sweetness, a ballet of buttery goodness, and a marvel of moist texture. Here are the key ingredients:

- **Sugar**: No surprise here. The more, the merrier.
- **Butter**: Because everything is better with butter.
- **Eggs**: They give the cake its fluffy structure.
- **Flour**: The foundation upon which our sugary dreams are built.
- **Milk**: Adds moisture and richness.
- **Vanilla Extract**: The secret weapon for that heavenly aroma.

## Preparation: The Road to Culinary Bliss

### Step 1: Preheat and Prep

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease your cake pans like your life depends on it—because sticking cake is a baker's nightmare.

### Step 2: Mixing Magic

Cream the butter and sugar together until it's as smooth as a geek's pick-up line at Comic-Con. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract, which is basically the cake's version of a superhero's cape.

### Step 3: Flour Power

In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and baking powder. Gradually add this flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. You've now unlocked the secret to a well-balanced sugar cake batter.

### Step 4: Bake to Perfection

Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 25-30 minutes. When a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, you're ready to ascend to sugar cake nirvana.
