## How to Verify Authenticity
To separate the gullible from the nostalgic, you need to check a few boxes. First, confirm the year 1998 is stamped somewhere on the tin or the inner ring. Look for Coca-Cola branding that matches the period, not a recent reissue. The UNO deck should be standard in size and color, with the same card backs as common UNO runs of the late 90s. Look for a serial or batch code on the bottom; a lot of copies have those tiny numbers that appear useless until you are hunting for a matching set. Finally, verify that all cards are present, and that the polar bear art is intact on the lid, not replaced by a modern reprint.
