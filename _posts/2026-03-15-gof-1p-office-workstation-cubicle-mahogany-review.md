The mahogany finish is relatively forgiving with normal care. Regular dusting and a quick wipe with a slightly damp cloth are enough to keep the finish looking rich and warm. Avoid harsh cleaners that could strip or dull the finish, and promptly deal with spills to prevent staining. The desk surface should resist minor scratches, but you might still want to use a cutting mat or a desk mat in front of the keyboard if you regularly use the area as a crafting or note-taking zone. For longevity, consider occasional reconditioning with a small amount of furniture wax or finish restorer that is designed for wood surfaces. This will revive the depth of color and keep the texture feeling smooth under your fingertips.

As with any modular system, periodic checks on the hardware connections and panel alignments are a good habit. A small amount of maintenance now can prevent bigger headaches later when you decide to reconfigure or expand your workspace.
