## Care and Maintenance

To keep your Fortune Row dice looking as good as the night you first opened the box, a few simple care steps go a long way:

- Wipe gently with a soft cloth after use to remove fingerprints and oils.
- Store in the provided pouch or display tray to prevent scuffs and chips.
- Avoid harsh solvents or abrasive cleaners that could degrade the enamel or resin surface.
- Keep away from direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent color fading.

If you enjoy long campaigns, consider rotating dice during different arc chapters to reduce wear on any single color. A little rotation goes a long way toward keeping the set vibrant and legible for many sessions to come.
