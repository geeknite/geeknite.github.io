# Cultistorm Kickstarter Edition Review: A Lovecraftian Board Game Adventure

![Cultistorm](https://i.imgur.com/Jm8ynpT.jpg)

## Introduction

Hello, fellow cultists! Are you ready to plunge into the depths of madness and summon some eldritch horrors? Today, we embark on a journey through **Cultistorm** (Kickstarter Edition), the cooperative board game that will have you questioning your sanity. Grab your Necronomicons and let’s get started!

## The Basics of Cultistorm

Cultistorm is a cooperative board game for 1-5 players set in the dark and mysterious world inspired by the works of H.P. Lovecraft. Players take on the roles of investigators trying to thwart the plans of cultists attempting to summon their dark gods. Think of it as "Clue" meets "The Call of Cthulhu," but with more tentacles and less Miss Scarlet.

### Components

The game boasts a plethora of high-quality components, which include beautifully illustrated cards, detailed miniatures, and a modular game board that changes with every playthrough. The artwork is stunning, capturing the eerie and unsettling vibe of Lovecraft’s universe. There’s also a rulebook that’s longer than the list of Lovecraft’s phobias, but fear not, for it is well-written and easy to follow.

**Fun Fact**: The game includes a miniature of a Shoggoth that is so detailed you can practically see the madness oozing from its many orifices.

## Gameplay

Cultistorm revolves around strategy, teamwork, and a bit of luck. Players must work together to gather clues, fight cultists, and perform rituals to close portals before the dark gods break through and doom the world.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Investigation Phase**: Players gather clues and items to aid them in their quest. This is also where they get to move around the board and interact with various locations.
2. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions such as fighting cultists, sealing portals, and using special abilities. This is where the game gets intense and where most of the strategic planning happens.
3. **Mythos Phase**: Draw Mythos cards to introduce new challenges, spawn more cultists, or trigger otherworldly events. This phase ramps up the difficulty and keeps players on their toes.
4. **Resolution Phase**: Resolve any ongoing effects and prepare for the next turn. This is a brief moment to catch your breath before plunging back into the madness.

### Winning and Losing

To win, players must seal all the portals and defeat the final boss before the cultists complete their summoning ritual. If the players fail to do so, or if too many investigators are driven insane or killed, it’s game over. Humanity is doomed, and you’ll probably need a hug (or a stiff drink).

**Pro Tip**: Always keep an "Elder Sign" token handy. It might just save you from an untimely demise at the hands (tentacles?) of an Ancient One.

## Cultistorm: The Humor Factor

Despite its dark theme, Cultistorm manages to inject a good dose of humor into the gameplay. The various investigator characters come with quirky backstories and abilities that will have you chuckling. The game doesn’t take itself too seriously, which is a refreshing twist in the horror genre.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our "Librarian" character tried to fend off a cultist with a "Strongly Worded Letter" card. Needless to say, the cultist was not impressed, but we laughed for a solid five minutes.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Engaging Gameplay**: The mix of strategy, cooperation, and humor keeps players invested.
- **High-Quality Components**: The artwork and miniatures are top-notch, adding to the immersive experience.
- **Replayability**: With modular boards and various scenarios, no two games are ever the same.
- **Humor**: The light-hearted elements provide a nice balance to the dark theme.

### Cons

- **Complexity**: The game has a steep learning curve, which might be daunting for new players.
- **Length**: Games can be quite long, so be prepared for a marathon session.
- **Storage**: The sheer number of components can make storage and setup a bit of a hassle.

## Final Thoughts

Cultistorm (Kickstarter Edition) is a fantastic addition to any board game collection, especially for fans of Lovecraft and cooperative games. Its engaging gameplay, high-quality components, and humorous twist on the horror genre make it a must-play. Just remember to keep your wits about you and your sanity in check.
