## Maintenance and Longevity: Caring for the Beast

To maximize the life of your Altered Edition Playmat, treat it like a small investment in your competitive identity. Here are a few simple rules we endorse:

- Clean it after every heavy session. A quick wipe keeps oils and residues from building up on the surface.
- Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods. Fading is not dramatic, but over time you’ll notice a tonal shift that might undermine the matte finish you love.
- Keep your sleeves in good condition. While the mat itself resists wear, damaged sleeves can scratch surfaces and cause micro-abrasions that affect card placement.
- Don’t fold it into a suitcase-sized burrito. Rolling or lying flat is fine; folding reduces those pristine edge lines and can lead to creases that are stubborn to remove.

In short: a little care goes a long way. The mat will take a beating through normal play but should hold up to routine maintenance with grace and a dash of gamer pride.
