## Introduction

Hold onto your seats, tech enthusiasts! The iPad Pro M4 is here, and it's about to blow your mind. Apple has outdone itself with this one. But is it worth selling your kidney for? Let's dive deep into the magical and somewhat mysterious world of the iPad Pro M4.

## Design and Build Quality

The iPad Pro M4 is a sleek, elegant beast. Encased in an aluminum unibody, it feels like holding a piece of futuristic technology sent back in time to remind us just how mediocre our gadgets are. It's incredibly light, making it perfect for on-the-go use, yet solid enough to withstand the occasional drop (or the wrath of a toddler).

![iPad Pro M4 Design](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/design__c2u6xn6rvs2u_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipad-pro-m4-design)

### Ports and Connectivity

Apple decided to be generous with the iPad Pro M4 by including a USB-C port, Thunderbolt 4 support, and a magnetic connector for the new Magic Keyboard. Sadly, the headphone jack decided to remain a distant memory. But hey, who needs wired headphones?

## Display and Audio

![iPad Pro M4 Display](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipad-pro-m4-display)

The iPad Pro M4 features a stunning 12.9-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology. The colors are so vibrant, they might just make your old television weep in jealousy. Blacks are deep, whites are blinding, and the brightness can probably guide ships lost at sea.

The audio system is nothing short of spectacular. With four-speaker audio and spatial audio support, it's like having a surround sound system in your hands. Whether you're watching Marvel movies or jamming to your favorite tunes, the sound quality won't disappoint.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 15 hours of battery life on a single charge, and in our tests, it didn't disappoint. Whether you're sketching, designing, or binge-watching your favorite series (again), the iPad Pro M4 will keep you going all day long and then some.

## Keyboard and Pencil

![Magic Keyboard](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/keyboard__f5dhn0m5h16m_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}magic-keyboard)

The new Magic Keyboard is back and better than ever. With a refined scissor mechanism and perfect travel distance, typing on the iPad Pro M4 feels like a dream. The trackpad is highly responsive and supports a plethora of gestures, making navigation a breeze.

The Apple Pencil 3rd Gen is a match made in tech heaven. It magnetically attaches to the side of the iPad, charges wirelessly, and offers a seamless and responsive drawing experience. Whether you're an artist or just like doodling during meetings, it's an invaluable tool.

## Software and Features

![iPadOS 16](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/ipados__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipados-16)

### iPadOS 16

The iPad Pro M4 runs on iPadOS 16, packed with new features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and redesigned widgets. The integration between hardware and software is so seamless, you might start believing in tech magic.

### FaceTime and Webcam

The 12MP Ultra Wide front camera with Center Stage ensures you always look your best on video calls. Coupled with studio-quality microphones, it's perfect for remote meetings, classes, or catching up with friends and family.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Exceptional Performance**: The M4 chip is a beast.
- **Stunning Display**: The Liquid Retina XDR display is a visual treat.
- **All-Day Battery Life**: No more mid-day charging panics.
- **Improved Connectivity**: Thunderbolt 4 and USB-C are game changers.

### Cons

- **Expensive**: Be ready to part with a significant amount of cash.
- **No Headphone Jack**: Wireless headphones are a must.
- **Limited Port Selection**: Some might still miss the variety of ports.
