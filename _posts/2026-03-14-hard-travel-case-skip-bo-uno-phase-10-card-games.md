## Maintenance and care
Cleaning is simple: a damp cloth for the exterior and a gentle vacuum for the interior seams work well. Avoid soaking the foam though, as water and foam do not always play nice together. If you spill something sticky on the interior, a mild dish soap solution with a soft cloth will do the job. Let it air dry fully before you repack the decks to avoid any moisture affecting card surfaces. A quick weekly check during travel is not overkill; it simply ensures nothing migrates to the wrong pocket and creates a miniature card conspiracy theory under the zippers.

Pro tip for card athletes: if you plan to bring along Phase 10 with heavy13 card holdings, consider instituting a small, lightweight organizer for your score sheets. It can be a simple pad of paper and a pencil, tucked into the outer pocket. You will thank yourself on the flight when you realize you no longer have to conjure quick math on the back of a hotel napkin at the end of a long game session.
