## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Azul is as straightforward as my grandma's cookie recipe (minus the flour explosion). Each player gets a board, and the tiles are placed in the center. The goal is to place tiles on your board to complete patterns and score points.

### Turns and Actions

Players take turns drafting tiles from the center and placing them on their boards. The challenge comes from choosing the right tiles to complete your patterns while blocking your opponents from doing the same. It's a delicate balance of strategy and colorful chaos.

### Scoring

Scoring in Azul is like a math test where you actually want to solve the problems. Points are awarded for completed rows, columns, and specific patterns. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.
