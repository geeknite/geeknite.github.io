1) Unpack with a careful but optimistic attitude. 2) Attach the legs to the main desk surface using the included hardware. 3) Install the shorter leg to form the L-shape. 4) Mount the drawers and the file cabinet in their assigned positions. 5) Route cables through the channels, pop the grommets into place, and thread the power and data lines to the back. 6) Attach the LED strip and connect the controller. 7) Stand back and admire how much space you can turn into a data center without feeling like you’re living inside a computer.
