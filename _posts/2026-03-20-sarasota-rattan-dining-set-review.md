## Maintenance: Simple Habits for Longevity

Maintenance doesn’t have to be a full-time job. Sarasota’s design aims for low-maintenance living with a few simple steps:

- Clean the wicker with a soft brush or cloth and a mild soap solution. Rinse with clean water and let dry. No power washers unless you want a DIY abstract sculpture.
- Cushion care: Remove and wash fabric cushions following the manufacturer’s instructions. Air-dry completely before returning to the frame.
- Check hardware periodically. Over time, screws can loosen with the rhythm of the seasons and your enthusiastic reactions to a good dinner conversation.
- Store or cover cushions during heavy rain or winter months to extend their life and keep colors vibrant.

If you’re the type who likes a faster schedule, you can schedule a quarterly maintenance check that combines a quick wipe-down with a quick tighten-up session of all fasteners. It’s a small ritual that pays off in the long run by keeping everything looking sharp and feeling sturdy.
