## Storage, Care, and Handling

We’ve touched on care briefly, but here are a few more practical, actionable tips to keep your 112-card deck in good shape:

- Always use a card box or pouch to prevent edge bending.
- Keep cards away from moisture; even a little dampness can cause the card backs to rub off.
- When not in use, store the deck flat in a dry area to prevent curling.

If you’re playing with kids or animals around, consider rules that restrict certain cards to avoid rough handling or accidental spills. CAH is tough enough to survive a night of chaos; give it a little extra care to ensure the cards continue to read clearly for many future nights.
