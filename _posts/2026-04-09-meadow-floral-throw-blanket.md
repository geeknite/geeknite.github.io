## Care and Maintenance
### Washing and Drying Practicalities
Care is where many fans of lightweight, bright patterns fear the worst. The Meadow Floral Throw Blanket is machine washable (cold cycle recommended) and tumble dry low. This is the kind of care protocol that encourages you to actually wash the blanket rather than letting it fester in the laundry basket like a forgotten side quest. In practice, we found that the colors stayed vibrant after several cycles, and there was no noticeable pilling after a handful of wash sessions. The microfiber composition plays nicely with standard detergents, and the blanket comes out soft rather than stiff, which is crucial for ongoing comfort.

### Maintenance Tips for Longevity
- Avoid hot water: It may affect colorfastness over many cycles.
- Use a gentle detergent: A regular detergent works fine; avoid harsh bleaches that can dull the floral palette.
- Fold rather than cram: When storing, fold rather than crumple to reduce crease wear along the pattern lines.
- Quick air-out: If it starts to pick up a musty scent after extended storage, give it a quick air-out outside or near a sunny window before the next use.
