## Application

Athonian Camoshade glides onto your miniatures like butter on a hot toast. It's particularly effective on textured surfaces, bringing out those nooks and crannies with a delightful dark green tint. Perfect for eerie cloaks, sinister armor, or that questionable green slime monster you’ve been working on.

### Tips for Use

- **Be Generous**: Don't be stingy. Slather your model like you're auditioning for a high-budget horror movie.
- **Layering is Key**: For deeper shade effects, apply multiple layers. Just like how you handle your emotions—layers upon layers.
- **Dry-Brushing**: After it dries, dry-brush with a lighter green to make it pop. You'll thank me later.
