In real-world tests, this combination of materials tends to perform well in typical suburban climates. ## Maintenance Tips: Keeping It Looking Sharp
Keeping poly rattan looking sharp is largely about routine care. Here are practical, no-nonsense steps:

- Regular wipe-downs: After meals, wipe the table and chairs to remove crumbs and sticky residues. A microfiber cloth is your best friend; it catches drips and dust without leaving lint strands behind.
- Cushion care: Remove covers to wash when needed. Air-dry completely before reassembling. Consider storage in a dry place during heavy rain or winter to prevent mold and mildew.
- Sun protection: If your patio receives intense sun for long hours, consider a shade solution to preserve color consistency and reduce the heat impact on cushions.
- Stain management: For stubborn stains on cushions, tackle them with a mild detergent solution. Test in an inconspicuous area first to ensure colorfastness.

Overall maintenance is straightforward, which means more time enjoying the space and less time fiddling with furniture maintenance compared to a high-maintenance indoor set. The key is consistency; quick wipe-downs after each use go a long way toward preserving aesthetics and resilience.
