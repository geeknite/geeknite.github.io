## Setup, Maintenance, and Longevity

### Assembly Time and Tips

Expect a methodical 45–60 minutes for one dedicated assembler. Use the step-by-step diagrams, align modules with correct edges, and keep the hardware bag separate to avoid the chaos that often accompanies a late-night furniture assembly. A power drill can speed things up but be careful not to over-tighten. 

### Cleaning and Care

A quick rinse with water and a mild soap solution is usually enough to keep the wicker looking fresh. Cushions should be spot-cleaned or machine washed per label instructions. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the fabric; if you want to maintain color, limit sun exposure on the cushions during peak hours and consider a seasonal cover. 

### Durability and Warranty

This set is built for outdoor life, but it is not instantaneous rocket science. The build quality is solid for the price range, and the modular design reduces the risk of large-scale failure when one module experiences a problem. Warranty terms vary by retailer and region, typically offering one year or more for frame and upholstery. If you need extended protection, look for extended warranty offers or protective covers to extend the life of cushions and fabric.
