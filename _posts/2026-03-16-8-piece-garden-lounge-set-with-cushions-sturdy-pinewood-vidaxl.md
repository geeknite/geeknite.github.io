## Weather Resistance and Maintenance
VidaXL’s pinewood components are usually treated to resist moisture, essential in climates with rain, snow, or dramatic spring showers. Regular checks for loose screws and signs of wood splitting after a season of use are wise. A protective sealant or outdoor furniture oil can extend the wood’s life, especially if you host backyard celebrations that would make a resort jealous. Cushions can be spot-cleaned; for deeper cleaning, remove covers if possible and wash per manufacturer instructions. If you live in a region with heavy rainfall, consider storing cushions during the off-season or investing in breathable cushion covers.
