## What is Kendama?

For those of you living under a rock (or perhaps just not obsessed with Japanese toys like we are), Kendama is a traditional Japanese skill toy consisting of a handle, called the ken, and a ball, called the tama, connected by a string. The goal is simple: perform tricks by catching the ball in the various cups on the ken or by landing it on the spike. Simple, yet maddeningly addictive!
