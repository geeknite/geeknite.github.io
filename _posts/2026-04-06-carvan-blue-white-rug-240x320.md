Maintenance and Cleaning

A rug in a tech den faces a particular hazard profile: coffee spills, energy drink splashes, and the occasional dust bunny that looks suspiciously like a tiny droid. The Carvan Blue White Rug handles these with a practical, no-drama approach. Spot cleaning is straightforward: blot, rinse, blot again, and you’re done. The color contrast helps hide minor smudges, and the stain resistance means you won’t be scrubbing away forever after that dramatic mid-game snack attack.

For routine care, vacuuming with a standard rug mode is recommended. Don’t be tempted to run a high-heat wash or toss it into a conventional laundry cycle; this rug is designed for surface care and occasional professional cleaning if you’re worried about long-term grime buildup. If you have pets, a quick weekly vacuum to capture hair and dander will keep the surface looking sharp.

In the realm of spills, the advice is simple: act fast, blot—not rub—and use a mild detergent if needed. A small sprinkle of baking soda after a spill can help neutralize odors and extract moisture, making the next vacuum pass that much more efficient.
