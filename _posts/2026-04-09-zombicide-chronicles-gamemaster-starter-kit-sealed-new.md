The Gamemaster Starter Kit is designed to arm a single, brave person with enough toolkit to run a campaign while the party debates whether to split the loot or turn tail and run. Here is a typical contents snapshot, framed as a checklist so you can pretend you are preparing for a top-tier heist rather than a roleplay session with undead allies.

- Gamemaster Screen: a sturdy, foldable set of panels that makes you feel like a controlling deity rather than a mere referee. This is where the chaos cards and the event deck get their own private little giggle session.
- Rulebook and Chronicles Campaign Book: the spine of your campaign world. The Chronicles book adds a narrative thread that makes you feel as if the zombie threat has a personal vendetta against your group, which is a surprisingly thematic touch for a game about zombie mayhem.
- Scenario Book: a collection of ready-to-play missions that stitch together a mini-campaign. Each scenario is a little story with objectives, time pressure, and opportunities to mispronounce a few monster names for comic effect.

- Event Deck and Card Decks: piles of destiny that determine how the night goes. They often feel a little sadistic, but that is exactly the vibe we paid for.
