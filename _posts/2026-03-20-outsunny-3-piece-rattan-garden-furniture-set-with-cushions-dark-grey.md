Weather resistance and durability
Synthetic rattan is made to survive the outdoors better than real wicker. UV stabilization helps with color fade; the weave resists cracking after long exposure to sun. The frame is powder-coated to resist rust and corrosion. That means if you forget to bring the set inside during a surprise rainstorm, you’re less likely to come out to a warped chair that sighs whenever you sit down. Of course, like any outdoor furniture, the life expectancy improves if you store cushions indoors when not in use or cover the set during heavy weather.

Maintenance and care
- Clean the rattan with a damp cloth and a mild soap solution. Avoid abrasive cleaners that can dull the surface.
- Remove cushion covers and machine wash on gentle cycle if possible; air-dry away from direct heat.
- Check screws every few months to maintain tightness; outdoor furniture can loosen with heavy use or strong winds.
- If mold appears, treat promptly with a mild solution and ensure cushions are fully dry before reassembling.
