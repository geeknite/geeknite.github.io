## Care, Preservation, and Display
Preservation is part science, part art. Here are Geeknite-approved tips for keeping your 1985 UNO set in top shape:
- Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight to minimize cardboard yellowing.
- Leave the shrink wrap alone if it’s in good shape; it’s part of the artifact’s character.
- If the box has a lid, keep it propped on a shelf to prevent warping; a gentle dusting with a microfiber cloth is sufficient.
- Consider a display case with UV-filtering glass for showpieces that deserve daily admiration.
- If you want to play with the cards, it’s fine—but treat them with care. The older stock can be grittier, and you’ll want to avoid any sharp bending that could compromise the print.
