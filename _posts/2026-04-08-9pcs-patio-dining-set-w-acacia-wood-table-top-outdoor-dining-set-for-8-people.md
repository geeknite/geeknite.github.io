## Care and Maintenance: A Geek’s Manual for Keeping Your Table Top Turring Like a New Gadget
Care is where a lot of folks underestimate the long-term value of a well-made outdoor set. Here are practical tips to keep your Acacia top looking sharp and your cushions dry:
- Clean the surface with a mild soap solution and soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that strip the finish. Think gentle, like your favorite sci-fi character’s best non-violent approach to conflict resolution.
- Reapply protective oil or sealant according to the manufacturer’s schedule. It’s not glamorous, but it’s how you keep the wood from morphing into a dry, parched landscape after the first snow or summer heatwave.
- Cushion care varies by fabric. Quick-dry foams are great, but if you opt for fabric covers, remove cushions during heavy rain. Store them in a dry place when possible to prevent mildew; a storage bench on the patio can be a lifesaver for multi-season use.
- Protect the surface from heat sources and sharp objects. Trivets and coasters are the reason your table top lasts longer; your future self will thank you when the wood doesn’t get scorched by a hot grill pan.

Incorporating these habits will extend the life of your set and preserve its aesthetic. It’s not glamorous, but it is practical—a key trait in any gear that wants to act like an ally rather than a one-season fling.
