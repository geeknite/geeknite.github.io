### Interaction with Dixit 2 Core Rules

Dixit 2’s core loop is fairly simple: storyteller picks a card, gives a clue, other players choose a card they think matches, all cards are revealed, players bid on which card belongs to the storyteller, points are awarded based on correct guesses and deception. The Gift Bag adds a layer of interpretive flexibility. It does not replace core mechanics; it enhances the storytelling engine by introducing a broader palette of metaphors, enabling more inventive, less obvious associations.

In practice, this can produce two distinct outcomes:

- Positive: The storyteller can guide players toward a more cohesive narrative arc, weaving multiple art styles into a single story and rewarding players who listen closely.
- Negative: If overused or misunderstood, it can muddy clues and create analysis paralysis as players debate the meaning of the metaphor instead of focusing on the story itself.

If your group values high-interpretation rounds and loves discussing “why this clue works,” The Gift Bag can be a hit. If you prefer quick, punchy rounds with obvious connections, you might want to limit the card’s usage or pair it with a timebox to keep the pace brisk.
