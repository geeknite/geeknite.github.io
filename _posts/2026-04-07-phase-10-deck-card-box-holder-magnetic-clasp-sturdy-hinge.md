## Maintenance and care: keeping the box running for the long haul
- Cleaning: Wipe with a damp microfiber cloth. Avoid soaking. A quick wipe down after a messy snack run is all you need.
- Avoid overloading: Do not cram extra long cards or oversized inserts. If the fit is tight, you risk warping the lid or stressing the hinge. Think of this as a light gym regimen for your box—gentle, consistent, with no dramatic failure.
- Check the hinge periodically: If you hear a squeak, a small dab of lubricant on the hinge pin (a tiny amount) can extend the life of the mechanism. Wipe away any excess to avoid attracting dust. If the hinge starts to loosen, a tiny drop of glue can re-secure it. This is not glamorous, but it is the secret sauce of long-term reliability.
