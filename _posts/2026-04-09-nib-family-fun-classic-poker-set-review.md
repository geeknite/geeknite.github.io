### Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping It Sharp
To keep things in tip-top shape, a light routine does wonders. Wipe the case and chips with a dry or lightly damp microfiber cloth after a game to remove fingerprints and minor dust. Avoid harsh cleaners on the aluminum surface; you don’t want to dull that nice finish. If chips develop chalky residue, lightly wipe them down and rotate chips regularly to maintain a consistent feel across the stack.

Store the set in a dry place away from direct sunlight. Prolonged exposure to humidity or salt air can affect the finish and the felt’s longevity over the long haul. A simple, dedicated shelf or closet bin will do wonders for preserving both form and function.
