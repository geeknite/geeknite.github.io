## Texture, Comfort, and Underfoot Experience
Wool fibers deliver a nice mix of softness and resilience. The pile height on the Illusion Trustee is designed to feel plush underfoot without becoming a shaggy hazard around the coffee table. The density is sufficient to support feet and the occasional spill of cereal, remote controls, or the existential dread that accompanies another season of your favorite show.

Underfoot comfort is subjective, but you can expect a cozy step-in with a noticeable pillowiness when you stand. For households with kids, pets, or enthusiastic foot-traffic, a wool rug often outperforms synthetics in bounce-back and long-term appearance.

Maintenance-wise, wool fibers are relatively forgiving: they hide micro-sheens of dust better than nylon, and they respond well to occasional professional cleaning. Vacuum with a beater brush on a gentle setting, and do not over-wet the rug when cleaning. For spills, blot from the outside to the center and use a mild detergent only if necessary.

For a tactile experience, the Illusion Trustee delivers what wool does best: warmth, softness, and a sense of luxury. If you’re the type who touches every rug before buying, you’ll appreciate the texture enough to justify a longer deliberation period.

## Durability and Care
Durability for wool rugs comes from fiber strength, loomed density, and the quality of the backing. The Illusion Trustee’s construction is designed to resist fringing, matting, and fiber shedding that plagues bargain wool products. The pile recovers well after foot traffic, and the surface retains its color better than many lower-cost alternatives.

Care is straightforward but not minimalist. Routine maintenance includes:
- Vacuum weekly using a suction-only setting or a low-power beater brush
- Use a rug pad to reduce movement and extend the life of the backing
- Clean spills promptly with blot blot blot technique
- For stains, test a small area with a gentle cleaner before full application

If you want to dive deeper into wool care, see the Woolmark care guide above. For more discussions on rug fibers and performance, you can also peruse our Rug Materials 101 post: [Rug Materials 101]({% post_url 2023-01-15-rug-materials-101 %}).

Durability is closely tied to how you place the rug. A high-traffic entry, hallway, or open-plan living area benefits from a robust backing and a non-slip pad. In contrast, a wool rug in a formal dining room will still look pristine after weekly meals if you avoid direct sunlight and use rug protectors under heavy chairs.
