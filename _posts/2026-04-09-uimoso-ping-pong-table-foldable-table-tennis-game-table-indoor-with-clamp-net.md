## Assembly and Setup

The assembly steps are straightforward and well within the skill set of a casual DIYer:

- Attach the crossbars for horizontal rigidity
- Place the top on the frame and align corners
- Attach the clamp nets at each end and tighten the net tension

First timers may take a little longer, but you’ll be playing within a half hour. The heavy lift moments come during the initial lift and seating the top on the frame; after that, the process becomes mental model of one step per minute rather than one hour of confusion.
