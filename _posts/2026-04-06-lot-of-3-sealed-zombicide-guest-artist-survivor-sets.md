### Storage and maintenance tips

- Keep the three sets in their original packaging until you’re ready to play or display. The boxes are designed to handle stacking, but you’ll thank yourself later for the extra padding if you ever move houses.
- Use silica gel packs to prevent humidity damage if you live in a damp climate. The last thing you want is a miniature warped by moisture or a card that curls at the corners.
- Store the cards and inserts flat to avoid warping. If you’re worried about UV exposure fading the artwork, display it behind glass or in a shaded area of your room.
- For painting, use primer and apply thin layers. The bold lines and textures in these sculpts respond well to light airbrushing and wash layers. If you’re new to painting, consider starting with the simpler one and work your way up to the more intricate pose—your future self will thank you.
