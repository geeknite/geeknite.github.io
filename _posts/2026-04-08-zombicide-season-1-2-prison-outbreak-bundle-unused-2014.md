Expect a decent setup time if you’re playing with four or more players. The more you have, the more time you’ll invest in organizing tokens, setting up missions, and clarifying the effects of each card. Table space matters. You’ll want a stable 4x6 or larger layout (or the equivalent in modular boards) with a comfortable margin of play around the edges. The more expansions you add, the more sprawling your board becomes, which can be thrilling and chaotic in equal measure.
