### Playing styles and tactical tips
- Use controlled shots rather than wild swings. The smaller surface rewards precision more than raw power.
- Practice your bank shots off the side rails; it’s a classic trick that still works in tabletop foosball and earns you those priceless smug grins.
- Take advantage of the counterbalanced players when defending. The ability to hold a position and respond to a rapid shot can swing a game in your favor.
- Consider a quick baseline strategy for beginners: keep the ball in play, pass to your better shooter, and avoid the temptation to chase every flashy, Hollywood-style maneuver.
