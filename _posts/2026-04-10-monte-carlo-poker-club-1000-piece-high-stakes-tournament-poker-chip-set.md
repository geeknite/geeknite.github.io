## Care and maintenance tips
A quick care ritual can extend the life of your chips and keep their look consistent. Wipe chips with a soft dry cloth after a game to remove any sweaty residue or dust. If you notice some edge wear, you can lightly rub the surface with a clean cloth to reduce roughness. Don’t soak chips in water or any cleaning solution; they are not designed to be immersed and prolonged moisture can affect printing and coloring. If you are moving a large number of chips between rooms, use a chip rack or tray to minimize the chance of scuffs and to keep your stacks tidy. Finally, store your set in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading over many playing nights.
