### What is Exploding Kittens, anyway?
If you’ve somehow not encountered Exploding Kittens in the wild, picture a cartoonish, fast-paced card game where players try not to draw the dreaded Exploding Kitten card. The deck is a mix of preventives, defuses, and ridiculous cards that let you peek, shove, skip turns, or gently nudge someone into a spicy front-row seat to your chaos. It’s the kind of party game that works best when the room is loud, the snacks are plentiful, and the players are just shy of licensing-approved, adult-level chaos. It’s silly, it’s chaotic, and it’s perfect for game nights when someone yells “uno” and everyone immediately goes 10 steps too far.

For the uninitiated, the core mechanics revolve around not exploding, using Defuse cards, and playing action cards to mess with opponents. The art style is goofy enough to feel like a Saturday-m morning cartoon, which means you’re more likely to snort your drink through your nose than to feign sophistication. And yes, there are expansions, card jokes, and a surprising number of memes you forgot you needed in your life. ### Setup and Rules: A Quick Refresher

In the nine-pack setup, you’ll pile nine decks into a shared pool or hand them out to players, depending on how you want to run the session.
