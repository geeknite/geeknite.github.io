## Context and Practical Use
### Historical context of the wakizashi
The wakizashi is a compact companion blade in traditional Japanese sword culture, historically paired with a longer katana as part of the daisho. It served as a tool, a status symbol, and a quiet reminder that sometimes the shortest blade carries the weight of the strongest stories. This Honshu model nods to that heritage while letting modern collectors enjoy the silhouette without needing to learn koto rhythms or the rules of the samurai code. The fusion here is clear: a nod to history with a modern collectible badge, wrapped in a market-friendly price and usability profile.
