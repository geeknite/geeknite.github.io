## What is Sushi Go! Vegan Edition?
Sushi Go! Vegan Edition takes the beloved quick-fire drafting game and swaps out every fishy card for plant powered standins. Think avocado nigiri, cucumber maki, tofu nigiri, mushroom sashimi, seaweed rolls, and a rainbow of veggie sides that encourage color variety just as much as point variety. The core mechanic remains intact: you draft a hand of cards, reveal one, pass the rest, and chain that cycle until the deck is exhausted. The scoring system is updated to reward vegan combinations without punishing the original meat loving players who still enjoy the occasional eel sashimi in a different game night.

What the edition does best is flip anxiety into delight. You are not chasing a fish scale you cannot pronounce; you are chasing a crunch of veggies that somehow feels like a victory lap for dinner.

## How to Play: quick setup and scoring vibes
The rules don’t rewrite themselves; they re-skin them with a green glow and a cheerfully crunchy texture. The quick setup takes about a minute: shuffle, deal a hand to each player, and prepare to chase veggie glory. On your turn you pick one card from your current hand and pass the rest to the person on your left. The play continues until the deck is exhausted, and then you sum up the points.

The core curve remains familiar to anyone who has played Sushi Go! before: you want to collect sets, form pairings, and avoid leaving too many high risk items on your side that another player can crash with a last second veggie snatch. The vegan edition adds a handful of new scoring opportunities that reward diversity and color combos. You might find yourself chasing a cluster of green items to trigger a color-powered bonus, or aiming for a curated trio of veggie mains that unlocks a surprise multiplier near the end of the round.
