## Maintenance Guide: Cleaning, Care, and Longevity

### Routine Cleaning
- Vacuum regularly to prevent grit from grinding the fibers. A weekly pass should suffice for typical families and desk-joggers alike.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and fading, if you’re living where sunlight hits the floor like an orbital sunbeam.

### Spot and Stain Care
- Blot spills immediately with a clean microfiber cloth. Don’t rub; you’ll only push the stain deeper and rage your aura.
- For stubborn spots, use a mild detergent with water. Test on a small corner to ensure color stability. Avoid bleach or harsh chemicals.
- If you’re dealing with red wine, club soda, or something equally dramatic, dab, don’t drown. A little patience and a dab of patience later, you’ll be back in business.

### Deep Cleaning and Pro Maintenance
- A light steam clean every 6–12 months can refresh the fibers. Avoid soaking and follow manufacturer guidelines for your machine.
- If you have pets, consider a protective spray designed for pet stains to reduce odor and fiber contamination.
- Store tips: When not in use (rare but possible if you rotate space use), roll rather than fold the rug to minimize creases.
