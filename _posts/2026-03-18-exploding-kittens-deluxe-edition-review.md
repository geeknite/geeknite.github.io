## How to play in 2 minutes
If you need to teach this to your group without a cheat sheet, here is the gist. The deck contains kitten cards, which are wild in the sense that they do not explode you unless you draw the wrong one. Each turn, you draw a card, and you can play cards that force other players to draw or dodge the explosion. The aim is to avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten card. If you draw it, you are out of the round unless you have a defuse card that can save you from the blast. Defuse cards are precious, and you will learn to cherish them like that one friend who always brings snacks. If you have a defuse, you can use it to avoid elimination and continue playing until someone else flips the wrong card. The Deluxe Edition keeps all the same rules but adds a little extra pomp to each move with new flavor cards and better art.

- Players take turns drawing cards and playing one or more action cards
- If the Exploding Kitten card is drawn and you cannot defuse, you are out
- The last player standing wins the round and the glory of bragging rights
- Special cards like See the Future or Shuffle may reveal or rearrange upcoming draws
- The new promo cards add mild chaos that keeps even veterans guessing

## Deep Dive: Strategy, Pacing, and Social Dynamics
Here are a few pro-tips that do not require a min-max build but help you avoid becoming the hero of the memes for all the wrong reasons.

- Timing matters more than power. Cards that alter the draw order or force a player to draw can create space to survive when your shield is thinning. Save your defuse until you truly need it and not just to show off your dramatic entrance.
- Reading the table is half the game. Some players are clearly trying to maintain a poker-face while others go full reality show. Recognize your cohort’s thresholds for chaos and tailor your moves to maximize the laughter while reducing your own risk.
- The deluxe extras are not just window dressing. The new cards alter the expected duration of rounds and can create clever combo plays that catch your friends off guard. Use them to create moments of triumph and embarrassment in equal measure.
- The defuse cards are the lifebuoy. If you are chasing a long game, stacking defuses with the right timing can give you the edge you need to survive the late rounds with style.
- Keep the table light. The beauty of Exploding Kittens is not just the luck element; it is the camaraderie and the ridiculous moments you can later retell with time-honed embellishments.

## Variants and House Rules
If you like to spice things up, you can introduce house rules to keep your group engaged. Some favorites include:

- Quick Draw variant: Everyone draws until someone explodes, then you switch to normal play for the next round.
- Team Chaos: Teams of two share a defuse card in turn-based fashion, which creates opportunities for shady trust and questionable alliances.
- See the Future spice: Use the See the Future cards to peek at the next three draws and decide who to bully with your next move.
- Solo mode: A friendly challenge where one player tries to survive as long as possible against the deck, which can be surprisingly tense for a one-person challenge.
