## Maintenance and Care
Here are a few practical tips to keep your sealed deck pristine and your future self impressed:
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading of the blue/red inks.
- Keep the deck sealed if you’re not using it for display or light play. Exposure to humidity can degrade the tuck’s integrity and the deck’s finish over time.
- If you eventually decide to open and use the deck, handle with clean hands to minimize oils that can affect shuffles. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth after use helps keep the finish smooth.
- Consider a display case or a small shadow box to highlight the blue/red color palette while protecting against dust.
