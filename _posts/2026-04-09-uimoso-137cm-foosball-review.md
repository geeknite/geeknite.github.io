## Maintenance and Care

A key factor in any long-term home investment is maintenance. The Uimoso model is comparatively forgiving. Wipe the table surface with a mild cleaning solution and a soft cloth to remove fingerprints and stray crumbs—yes, that happens. Avoid abrasive cleaners that can dull the laminate finish. Periodically check the rods for any looseness or wobble; a quick tighten of the end caps usually resolves minor issues. If you plan to use the table in a bright room, consider a cover to keep dust away from the rods and the tabletop.

If a ball starts to feel sluggish, you may need to replace it with a fresh one from the standard foosball ball lineup. Keep a couple of spare balls around; they’re cheap, quick to swap, and your guests will be impressed by your organization—and your ability to keep the ball in play for longer stretches.
