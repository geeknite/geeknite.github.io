## Sizing Options: Custom Size or 8x10
Here’s where your brain can finally rest. The Satori Royal offers two mainstream routes for fitment: a truly custom size or the dependable 8x10. If you have a uniquely shaped living space, an overlapping L-shaped room, or a dining-area nook that would benefit from a rug hugging every edge to the point of obsession, the custom size option is your friend. For most living rooms that follow a traditional rectangle or near-rectangle layout, the 8x10 is the tried-and-true king of versatility.

### Custom Sizing Considerations
- Measure twice, cry once: use a laser or a careful tape with multiple passes to account for wall recesses, door swings, and furniture footprints.
- Edge considerations: decide whether you want a fully floored edge or a border that defines zones within a larger space.
- Underlay planning: a good rug pad helps maintain shape, reduces slipping, and adds an extra layer of comfort underfoot.
- Transportation and installation: for truly bespoke sizes, make sure your doorway clearance and stair winder permits easy passage during delivery.

### The Classic 8x10: Why It Works
- Proportion: the 8x10 works well under most mid-sized sofas and coffee tables, creating a cohesive seating zone without overwhelming the space.
- Availability: easier to source padding, underlays, and cleaning supplies for the standard shield of the consumer ecosystem.
- Cost-to-area ratio: generally favorable for those who want a large, present rug without entering the realm of boutique pricing.

## Durability and Maintenance
Synthetic fibers shine in the maintenance department, and the Satori Royal takes full advantage of that power. Spills wipe away with a damp cloth and a mild cleaner safe for synthetics. For routine care, a standard vacuum on a low to medium setting keeps the pile tidy without sacrificing its density.

### Cleaning Protocols
- Blot, don’t rub: immediate blotting minimizes set-in stains.
- Gentle cleaners: opt for mild detergents designed for synthetic fibers; avoid solvents that could degrade the fiber.
- Vacuum regularly: this is not a one-and-done cleaning crown; regular maintenance preserves the rug’s appearance.
- Edge care: lift the edges occasionally to ensure the backing remains flat and doesn’t peel up where the dog naps.
- Rotation: rotate the rug every few months to minimize wear patterns and keep it looking evenly lived-in.

### Stain Resistance and Colorfastness
The Satori Royal is engineered to resist common household stains and color fading under typical indoor lighting.
