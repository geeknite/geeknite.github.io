## The Basics

### What is SCOPE Stalingrad?

SCOPE Stalingrad is a strategic board game that simulates the Battle of Stalingrad during World War II. Players take on the roles of either the German or Soviet forces, each with unique strengths and weaknesses. With detailed mechanics, immersive gameplay, and an occasional laugh when things go hilariously wrong, SCOPE Stalingrad offers a gaming experience like no other.

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully designed board depicting Stalingrad, high-quality miniatures representing both armies, various tokens, cards, and easy-to-understand rulebook. The detailed artwork and sturdy components ensure an immersive and long-lasting gaming experience.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up SCOPE Stalingrad is straightforward. Players choose their sides and arrange their units according to the scenario. The objective for both sides is to achieve their mission objectives, whether it's capturing key locations or defending strategic points.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Command Phase**: Players issue orders to their units.
2. **Movement Phase**: Units move across the board to position themselves strategically.
3. **Combat Phase**: Engaging the enemy, rolling dice for attacks, and resolving battles.
4. **Supply Phase**: Managing resources and logistics to keep the army running.

### Strategy and Tactics

SCOPE Stalingrad shines in its strategic depth. Players must carefully plan their moves, utilize cover, and think several steps ahead to outmaneuver their opponents. Cooperation and communication are key, especially for the Soviet players tasked with defending their homeland.
