## Common myths about garden tool batteries
### Myth 1: Bigger Ah always means better
Truth: Ah matters, but runtime is a function of both Ah and power draw. A 6Ah pack powering a high-amp motor may drain faster than a 4Ah pack on a modest saw. The key is to match the battery to the tool’s consumption profile and use cases.

### Myth 2: Heavier equals more power
Truth: Weight contributes to perceived durability and feasibility, but the actual power comes from the chemistry and motor efficiency. A heavier pack doesn’t guarantee longer working time if it’s not optimized for your specific tool.

### Myth 3: All Li-ion packs are the same
Truth: Battery management systems, cell quality, and pack design matter a lot. A well-designed 6Ah pack with robust protection can outperform a cheaper 8Ah pack that lacks effective voltage balancing and thermal protection.
