## How to Wear It: Grooming Tips and Application Dos and Don’ts
- Application: A couple of fine mists on pulse points (wrists, neck) is enough. One extra puff if you’re planning a long day, but avoid drowning yourself in the scent. Remember, fragrance should be a whisper, not a shout.
- Layering: If you wear a matching lotion or body product (if available), layering can extend longevity. If not, a neutral lotion under the fragrance helps keep the notes in balance.
- Scent etiquette: If you work in tight spaces, consider lighter application in the morning and a refresher before a long meeting rather than a full-on reapplication in a glass-walled conference room.
- Seasonal strategy: Spring and early summer are perfect, with autumn cool-down days also workable. Winter can still be pleasant if you want a refined scent with a touch of brightness to counter the gloom.
