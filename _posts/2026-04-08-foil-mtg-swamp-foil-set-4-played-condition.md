The surface gloss on four swamp basics in played condition tends to be uneven in a few places due to prior handling. Foil swamps do not change color drastically because they are basic lands; however the foil can darken slightly under lamp glare, creating a moody, dungeon-atmosphere vibe that might be perfect for your goth goblin deck aesthetic.
