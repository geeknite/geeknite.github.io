## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity: Surviving the Real-Life Lab

No product review would be complete without a section on cleaning. Rugs in a gaming setup are magnets for dust, crumbs, and the occasional coffee splash. How the Castor responds to this life test is crucial for long-term satisfaction.

### Routine Care

- Vacuum regularly to prevent crumb build-up; a quick pass weekly is usually enough for low-traffic spaces.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Avoid aggressive scrubbing; you don’t want to push coffee deeper into the fibers and create a fossilized stain that becomes a legend among dust bunnies.
- For stubborn stains, test a mild detergent on a hidden corner first. If the dye remains stable, proceed with a gentle cleaning routine. If the dye shivers in fear, consider professional cleaning or a rug swap that won’t traumatize your carpet underworld.

### Stain Resistance and Colorfastness

Light beige shades can show new stains more readily than darker hues. This isn’t a condemnation; it’s a call to action to be mindful of spills and to enjoy the ritual of keeping your workspace pristine. The rug’s ability to maintain a clean look under routine maintenance is a practical measure, not a fashion miracle. If you’re worried about colorfastness, look for a wash test or a care label in the packaging, as this is where brands often hide their biggest surprises (either good or bad).
