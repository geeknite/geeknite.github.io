## The Basics

### What is Age of Conan: The Strategy Board Game?

**Age of Conan** is a strategy board game set in the savage world of Conan the Barbarian. Players take on the roles of powerful kingdoms vying for control over Hyboria. With beautifully designed components, detailed miniatures, and a plethora of strategic opportunities, this game offers an immersive experience for all strategy enthusiasts.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures representing armies, leaders, and the mighty Conan himself.
- **Cards**: Various cards for quests, events, and actions.
- **Game Board**: A map of Hyboria where the kingdoms clash in epic battles.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track resources, influence, and the ever-important Conan marker.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your cat near the miniatures. Their claws are the real barbarian threat.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Age of Conan is like preparing for a grand battle. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your kingdom. Now you're ready to conquer Hyboria!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their kingdom's abilities. You can move armies, attack enemies, and use diplomacy to gain influence. Strategy is key, so plan your moves carefully to outwit your opponents.

### Combat

Combat in Age of Conan is a blend of strategy and luck. Each battle involves rolling dice and using cards to boost your chances. Did you plan an epic attack just to roll a critical fail? Welcome to Hyboria, where even the boldest plans can go hilariously wrong.

## The Hyborian Epic

### Quests and Events

The game includes various quests and events inspired by the Conan stories. From battling ancient sorcerers to conquering distant lands, each quest offers unique challenges and rewards.

### Kingdoms and Leaders

Players can choose from different kingdoms, each with unique abilities and leaders. Play as Aquilonia, Turan, or Stygia, and command legendary leaders to guide your armies to victory.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: The Hyborian theme adds depth and excitement, making you feel like you're part of an epic saga.
