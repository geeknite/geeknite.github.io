## Setup and Assembly

Let us be honest here: a ten person bench is not a one person weekend project. The initial assembly will take a solid afternoon plus a helper. The package typically includes all the major components, a hardware kit, and a set of clear instructions. ### The Realities of a Large-Scale Install

When you install a bench for ten, you are essentially coordinating a tiny build project. You must consider floor leveling, wall clearance, and the path for transporting the large panels to their final positions. The good news is that the bench is modular enough to adapt to uneven spaces, but you will want to test your workflow before you commit to the configuration. A little planning goes a long way, especially if your office has a strict policy about moving furniture after a remodel. The worst case scenario is discovering mid install that one corner is slightly off and the entire line has to be re-leveled. The best case scenario is you finish early, celebrate with a victory lap around the workspace, and avoid the group chat drama about delayed velocity.

### Practical Tips for a Smooth Setup

- Assign a lead installer who can read the instruction diagrams without turning them into a garage wall art piece
- Group small parts into labeled bins for quick access during assembly
- Use a level and a tape measure to confirm straight lines after you place the first few stations
- Plan for cable management from day one rather than pretending you can jerry-rig the power later
- Allow time for the first week of usage and adjust as needed after real workflows emerge
