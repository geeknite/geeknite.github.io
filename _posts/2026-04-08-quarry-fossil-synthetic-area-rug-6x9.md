## Cleaning, care, and long-term upkeep

A big part of choosing a rug is knowing how easy it is to keep clean, especially in homes with kids, pets, or an overenthusiastic houseplant clan. The Quarry Fossil rug cleans up with a simple routine:

- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt from embedding in the fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth or paper towels; avoid rubbing, which can push staining deeper.
- For tougher stains, use a mild detergent mixed with water and gently dab, then rinse with a damp cloth.
- Allow to air dry completely to prevent mold or mildew in humid environments.
