## Display, storage, and care: protect your flame-walker

Storage and care are the unsung heroes of any rare card collection. Here are practical tips tailored for a NM-grade Gloom AR from SV3 Japanese:
- Use high-quality penny sleeves inside a rigid top-loader. Don’t skip the inner sleeve; micro-wear can accumulate from rubbing against the binder spine.
- Store upright in a cool, dry environment away from direct sunlight. UV can fade those dramatic blacks and purples in the blink of an eye.
- Consider a mint-condition binder with acid-free sheets. If you’re into value retention, you’ll want a storage solution that minimizes handling while maximizing visibility.
- When photographing for display, light from the side can exaggerate the variegated hues in the alternate art, making the flame glow pop in photos. If you’re serious about social media, you’ll tame the lighting to show both the deep blacks and the ember glow.
