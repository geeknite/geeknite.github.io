## Gameplay and Strategy
### Core Mechanics with Theo
Theo’s core mechanics revolve around: shoot first, talk later. He’s not the stealthy silent type; he’s the loud friend who yells \"I got you!\" while sprinting into the zombie horde with a smile and a roll. The synergy with other survivors is where the real fun begins. He can support teammates by pulling focus, drawing attack dice away from squishier survivors, and punching above his weight when the dice gods decide to cooperate.

### Movement and Positioning
The Wulfsburg map design gives you opportunities to place Theo on the edge or in the middle, depending on your risk tolerance. The key is to leverage doorways, choke points, and escalated floors to maximize action economy. Theo’s kit often rewards re-positioning, which means you’ll be playing a game of tetris with zombies as your obstacles.

### Combat Scenarios
In combat, Theo tends to excel in mid-range skirmishes. He benefits from supporting fire and can hold a critical line long enough for the rest of the group to rally. If you’re playing co-op, coordinate with your tank, your sniper, and your mage-adjacent survivor to create combos that feel like a perfect heist montage.
