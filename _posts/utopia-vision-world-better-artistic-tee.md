## Material and Comfort

### Soft as a Cloud

The Utopia tee is made from 100% organic cotton, which means it's soft, breathable, and kind to your skin. **Pro Tip**: Pair it with skinny jeans or a stylish belt to add some shape and make it look like you intentionally chose the relaxed fit.
