These Five Ten are a more casual approach to biking shoes, more inspired by skate shoes but with the grippy soles Five Tens are known, not as much as grippy and more durable than the Five Ten Freerider ones. The upper part is made of polyester canvas which look more resistant and elements protective than skate shoes canvas, also some faux leather interior details give them a soft touch.

For the price could be an ideal beginner to intermediate biking shoe, not the best for bad weather but perfect for the casual commuting or the aggressive dirt jumper.
