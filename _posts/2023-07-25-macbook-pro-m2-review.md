## Why Mechanical Keyboards?

Mechanical keyboards aren't just for show. They offer superior typing experience thanks to their individual key switches. This means better tactile feedback, durability, and customization options. Plus, they make you feel like a hacker from a 90s movie. Who doesn't want that?

## Customization Galore

One of the best things about mechanical keyboards is the endless customization options. From keycaps to switches, you can tweak your keyboard to your heart's content. Want a rainbow of colors or a stealthy monochrome look? It's all possible. Just remember, the only limit is your imagination (and maybe your budget).

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Durability**: Mechanical keyboards are built to last. Each key switch can withstand millions of keystrokes, making them ideal for heavy typists.
- **Typing Experience**: The tactile feedback and audible clicks make typing a joy. You'll feel more connected to your work or game.
- **Customization**: From RGB lighting to custom keycaps, you can make your keyboard uniquely yours.

### Cons

- **Price**: Quality mechanical keyboards can be expensive. But think of it as an investment in your typing happiness.
- **Noise**: The clickety-clack sound can be annoying to those around you. Consider getting quieter switches or a sound dampening mat if you share your space.
