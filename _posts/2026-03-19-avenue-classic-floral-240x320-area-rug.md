## Maintenance and Cleaning: Keeping It Fresh Without Turning It Into a Chore
Maintenance is where many modern rugs earn their keep or their suspicion. The Avenue Classic Floral is designed to be user-friendly. Regular vacuuming on a low-suction setting helps maintain pile height and prevent dust buildup in the fibers. For sporadic stains, a mild detergent mixed with water and performed blotting (not rubbing) usually suffices. Test any cleaning solution on a small inconspicuous area first to confirm colorfastness.

Immediate spill response matters. Blot gently with a clean cloth to absorb liquid, then treat with a mild cleaner if necessary. Avoid bleach or harsh solvents, which can degrade the fibers or alter the color balance of the floral print. If a spill is stubborn, you can spot-clean using a mixture of water and white vinegar in equal parts, then blot empty again and let air-dry.

Periodic maintenance is simple: rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and to keep the pattern looking balanced from every angle. The rug’s low profile means it doesn’t trap a lot of dust or pet hair, which is a nice bonus for households with fur-generating friends.
