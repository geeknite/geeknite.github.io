## The Basics

### What is Enemy At The Gates: Lock´n´Load Heroes?

Enemy At The Gates: Lock´n´Load Heroes is a tactical combat board game set during the Battle of Stalingrad in World War II. Players take on the roles of German and Soviet forces, battling for control of the war-torn city. The game features detailed miniatures, stunning artwork, and a plethora of scenarios that bring the historical conflict to life.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Highly detailed miniatures representing German and Soviet soldiers and vehicles. You'll want to keep these away from your cat, unless you want a reenactment of Godzilla.
- **Game Board**: A modular board depicting various locations in Stalingrad, including factories, streets, and bunkers.
- **Cards**: A deck of cards used for actions, events, and objectives. Pro tip: Don't use them as coasters.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track movement, damage, and various effects during the game.

### Setup

Setting up Enemy At The Gates: Lock´n´Load Heroes is like preparing for a military operation. It requires careful planning and sometimes a snack break (or two). Lay out the game board, distribute miniatures, and prepare your strategy. Now you're ready to enter the fray.

## Gameplay

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their units' abilities. You can move, attack, defend, and use special abilities to outmaneuver your opponent. Cooperation and strategy are key, so plan your moves carefully to gain the upper hand.

### Combat

Combat in Enemy At The Gates: Lock´n´Load Heroes is a blend of strategy and luck. Each unit has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic ambush just to have your opponent escape? Welcome to the battlefield, where plans often go awry.
