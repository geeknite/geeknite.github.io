## Practicality and Weather Readiness

Outdoor furniture must contend with sun, wind, rain, and the occasional wayward sprinkler. The Outsunny sofa rises to the challenge with practical design choices.

### Weather Resistance

- Rattan weave: UV-resistant, designed to resist fading from sunlight exposure. While nothing lasts forever, expect this weave to maintain its appearance through multiple summers with proper care.
- Cushions: The cushion covers are removable and can be washed. When rain happens (and it will), a quick wipe-down and return to a dry spot will help avoid waterlogging and mildew. If you’re in a climate with heavy rain, storing cushions indoors during storms is a good habit to build.
- Frame: The steel frame with powder coating helps resist rust and corrosion. It’s not a fortress against rust in a tropical monsoon, but it’s a sturdy choice for most temperate climates.

### Maintenance Tips
- Regularly brush or wipe the rattan to remove dirt. A damp cloth is enough for most casual cleans. Avoid harsh abrasives that could scratch the finish.
- For cushions, let them dry fully before returning them to the sofa. A sunny patio day is ideal, but a warm indoor space works too.
- If you’re storing the cushions during winter, use a breathable storage bag to extend their life and avoid musty smell pitfalls.
