## Care and Maintenance: Practical Tips for the Busy Geek

### Cleaning 101: Quick Responses to Everyday Drumrolls

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting. Do not pick the setting that sounds like a tiny jet engine unless you want a bald patch in the carpet. 
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Don’t rub; you’ll push the stain deeper and invite a small existential crisis.
- For stubborn stains, use a mild carpet-cleaning solution suited to synthetic fibers. Always test a hidden area first to ensure the color won’t migrate like a rogue spreadsheet.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and fading.
- If you have pets, trim nails and keep their roaming to a reasonable schedule. A calm animal equals fewer claw marks and better long-term appearance.

### Storage and Longevity: Slower Fade, Stronger Brand Loyalty

If you’re moving or redecorating, roll the Fontaine rather than folding it. Folding can cause creases that stubbornly persist. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to minimize color fade. If you ever want to switch things up, the rug can be rolled and stored in a clean way without losing its bounce and color fidelity.
