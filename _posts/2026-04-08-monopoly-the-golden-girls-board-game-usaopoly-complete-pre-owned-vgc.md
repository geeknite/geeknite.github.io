  The tokens imbue the game with recognizable show motifs; the tactile experience is the same as classic Monopoly, but the moment-to-moment decisions are colored by the knowledge that you’re stepping around a world where the Golden Girls resolve petty disputes with wit rather than with a bare-knuckled street negotiation.

## Maintenance and care for pre-owned editions

- Cleaning and upkeep: Wipe surfaces with a dry or slightly damp microfiber cloth to avoid staining. Don’t immerse the board in water; this isn’t a hero’s quest for moisture resistance, it’s a board game. If there are stubborn marks on the money, a gentle wipe with a mild cleaner can help, but don’t overdo it.
- Storage: Keep the game in a place where humidity is controlled and away from direct sunlight; prolonged exposure can dull the colors and degrade the cards. A dedicated game closet or shelf is ideal, with the pieces neatly kept in their original insert or an appropriate organizer insert.
- Longevity and future purchases: If you’re planning to use this edition as a centerpiece for a recurring game night, consider investing in protective sleeves for the cards. They’ll protect the artwork and prolong readability for many more games.
