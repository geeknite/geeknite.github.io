### Maintenance routine: a realistic care plan
- Daily: quick vacuum to capture surface dust and pet hairs before they redeploy in battle formations.
- Weekly: thorough vacuuming with a setting appropriate for nap length and fiber resilience.
- As-needed: spot cleaning for spills—blot, don’t rub; use a mild detergent intended for synthetic fibers, and test on a hidden area first.
- Seasonal: rotate rugs if you have multiple or swap rooms to balance wear and sun exposure.
- Long-term: consider a rug pad for extra support and to mitigate wear on the underside.

Cleaning tips: avoid aggressive cleaners that can degrade the fiber or alter color. For stubborn stains, a gentle, fabric-safe cleaner applied with a blotting motion tends to work best. After a particularly dramatic spill, allow the rug to dry completely before placing furniture back on top, to prevent a temporary underwater-rug situation where you feel like you’re living in a ship’s hull.
