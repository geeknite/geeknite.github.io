## Setup and Sizing
Sizes vary; the typical options include 5x7, 6x9, and 8x10. Consider your furniture footprint and traffic patterns when choosing size. A larger rug can unify a sitting area, while a smaller rug can anchor a reading nook. If you’re using the rug under a coffee table, ensure there’s adequate clearance between edges and furniture to maintain flow.

## Maintenance Routine: A Realistic Plan
- Daily: light vacuuming to remove surface dust and pet hair.
- Weekly: thorough vacuuming with a brush setting appropriate for the nap length.
- As-needed: spot cleaning for spills; blot, don’t rub; use a mild detergent specifically for synthetic fibers.
- Seasonal: rotate rugs if you have more than one or swap rooms to balance wear and light exposure.
- Long-term: consider a specialized rug pad for extra support and to reduce wear on the underside.
