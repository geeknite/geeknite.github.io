## Authenticity and Counterfeit Awareness
As with any collectible, there are fakes and misprints out there. No. 148 is no exception, so be mindful when you check photos from sellers. A few practical checks include:
- Compare the back design to known authentic examples; look for inconsistent fonts or misaligned logos.
- Check the foil quality and gloss; counterfeit cards sometimes look too “flat” or overly reflective.
- If buying internationally, verify that the seller offers a return policy and clear proof of condition.

The best defense against fakes is buying from reputed sellers who provide close-up photos, a known card’s grade, and a straightforward description of any wear. If you’re uncertain, ask for more pics or request a video of the card under proper lighting.

### Protecting the Artwork
