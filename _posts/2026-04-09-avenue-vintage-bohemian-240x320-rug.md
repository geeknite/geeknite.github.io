Cleaning, maintenance, and longevity
Daily maintenance is simple. Vacuum with a standard hard surface attachment or a low-pile brush setting. Avoid rotating your rug like a rotating sushi belt? No, rotate it. Rotating every few months helps to ensure even wear, and it also prevents one corner from absorbing too much sunlight and fading into a mysterious relic of the 70s. For spills, blot (don’t rub) with a clean cloth, then treat the area with a mild detergent solution if needed. Avoid harsh bleaches; we’re trying to keep the Bohemian dream alive, not ransom it to a fluorescent nightmare.

- Consider layering: Bohemian spaces benefit from layering various textures. Pair this rug with a textured throw, a velvet sofa, or a leather accent chair to add depth.
