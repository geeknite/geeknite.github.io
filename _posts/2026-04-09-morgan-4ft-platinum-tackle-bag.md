## Maintenance and Care Tips
- Wipe down after exposure to saltwater or muddy banks; salt and mud are the arch-nemeses of tackle bags in the wild.
- Keep zippers clean; a quick spray of silicone-based lubricant every few months keeps them sailing smoothly like a starship AI in “systems functional” mode.
- Dry storage is ideal; if you get a chance to let it air out instead of letting it sit in a damp car, do it. Your future self will thank you when you race out for dawn patrol.
- Inspect the seams periodically; catch a loose stitch early, and you’ll avoid the “one lure pouch away from catastrophe” scenario.
