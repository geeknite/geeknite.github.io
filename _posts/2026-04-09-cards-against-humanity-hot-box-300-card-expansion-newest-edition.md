## Hosting tips and house rules
- Layout your space for a comfortable vibe: enough table space for cards, a drink station, and a friend who’s willing to call out the “that went too far” moment.
- Create a rotating Czar rotation list to avoid fatigue. The Czar gets to pick the best line for the round, but they also set the tone for the rest of the night.
- Consider a no-repeat policy for the first hour to maximize novelty. Then, if the group is still hungry for more, loosen the rules and let the chaos reclaim center stage.
- If you’re worried about content, curate a personal blacklist or a “soft rules” approach where players can opt out of playing certain prompts.
- For larger groups, consider a multi-round tournament style with knockout rounds to keep momentum high and pressure manageable.
