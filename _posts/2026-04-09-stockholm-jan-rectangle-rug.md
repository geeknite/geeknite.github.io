Care and Maintenance: Quick Tips
--------------------------------
- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting to prevent fiber lift and maintain the crisp texture. High suction can pull at some of the looser fibers, so you want to avoid the “zip-zap” effect on this rug.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Do not rub; you’ll push the stain deeper and your rug will pretend not to be your friend anymore.
- For stubborn stains, use a mild detergent solution and test in an inconspicuous area first. If you’re unsure, call in a professional cleaner who specializes in carpets—your back will thank you when you’re older.
- Rotate every few months to ensure even wear. This is less about fashion and more about physics; even slight foot traffic can create subtle wear patterns that affect your room’s vibe.
