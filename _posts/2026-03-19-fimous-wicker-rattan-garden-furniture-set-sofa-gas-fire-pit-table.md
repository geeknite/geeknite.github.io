## Safety, Regulations, and Responsible Use

### Fire Pit Manners
Gas fire pits are fantastic, but they demand a responsible mindset. Always operate flames within local fire codes, maintain clearance from cushions and drapes, and ensure you have proper ventilation and propane storage. If your space has kids or pets, set up a clear perimeter and educate your household about fire safety. A small reminder sign or a decorative rule board can be a cute addition, as long as it doesn’t take away from the fun atmosphere around the fire pit.

### Maintenance for Fire Safety
Regularly inspect gas connections, keep the area clear of flammable objects, and follow the manufacturer’s maintenance instructions for the igniter and control valve. It’s not exciting, but it’s essential. You don’t want a dramatic sunset that ends with a drama you can’t explain to your insurance provider. Safety first, style second, and s’mores always at the ready.
