## Maintenance and Longevity

### Everyday care
Wipe the surface with a microfiber cloth after a session to minimize stickiness, especially if you’ve been snacking during the tournament. If you’re a coffee fanatic, keep the table away from steam and spills; the veneer will thank you. For foosball rods, a light wipe-down with a silicone-based spray can keep them moving smoothly and deter rust. Paddles and balls should be stored in their dedicated compartments to prevent misplacement and to preserve the life of your gear.

### Long-term durability considerations
This isn’t a lifetime investment, but with reasonable care it should last several years of regular use. The swivel mechanism, when treated gently, remains reliable and gives you many hours of mixed-order chaos. The point where two modes meet—the transition from foosball to ping pong—will be the area to monitor for any looseness. If you hear tings and feel a wobble, tighten the bolts. A little maintenance goes a long way toward preserving functionality and the bragging rights you’ll accumulate with your crew.
