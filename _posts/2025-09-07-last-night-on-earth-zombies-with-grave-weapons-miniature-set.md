## Overview

### What is Last Night on Earth?

*Last Night on Earth* is a thematic zombie survival board game where players can either take control of the heroes trying to survive the night or the zombies aiming to feast on brains. The *Zombies with Grave Weapons Miniature Set* adds a gory twist to the game, introducing zombies wielding grave weapons such as shovels, sickles, and pickaxes. Because eating brains isn't enough, they also want to show off their DIY skills.

### Components

The miniature set includes:
- **Grave Weapon Zombies**: A variety of zombie miniatures equipped with grave weapons.
- **Cards**: New cards to integrate these zombies into the base game.
- **Rule Book**: Instructions on how to use the new miniatures and cards.

**Pro Tip**: Try not to trip over the zombies in real life while setting up the game. They are just as dangerous on the floor as they are on the board.

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Setting Up

Setting up the *Zombies with Grave Weapons Miniature Set* is straightforward. Integrate the new zombies into your existing *Last Night on Earth* game. Make sure the heroes are equipped with enough sarcasm and horror clichés to survive the new wave of zombies.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of:
1. **Hero Phase**: Heroes move, search, and attack. Cue dramatic monologues and terrible one-liners.
2. **Zombie Phase**: Zombies shuffle, attack, and turn heroes into brain snacks. Now with added grave weapon flair!
3. **Event Phase**: Draw event cards that can drastically change the game. Hope your heroes don’t end up in a cliché horror movie scenario.

### Winning and Losing

Heroes win by fulfilling objectives or surviving until dawn. Zombies win by eating all the heroes. Spoiler: The zombies are really good at eating.
