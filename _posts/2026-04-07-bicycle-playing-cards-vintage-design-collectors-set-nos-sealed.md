## Care tips and long-term preservation

Even sealed sets benefit from a little care. Here is a quick checklist to keep your NOS sealed set in top shape for as long as you want to admire it:

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. UV exposure can fade packaging colors and weaken the seal over time.
- Keep away from humidity swings. Extreme moisture can affect the tuck box glue and the inner finish, even if the deck remains sealed.
- Handle with clean hands. Oils from skin can affect the edges of the box and the surface of any exposed area if you ever decide to peek inside.
- If you ever decide to open, do so carefully. A precise, slow lift will keep the seam intact and allow you to resell or store the box without scarring.
- Consider a display case with humidity control if you are serious about presenting multiple NOS sets as a small gallery piece.

If you want to go the extra mile, consider keeping a tiny humidity indicator card tucked into your display setup. It’s not glamorous, but it is dramatically practical when your wall of vintage decks starts whispering about 60% humidity like it’s the only anti-aging cream that actually works.
