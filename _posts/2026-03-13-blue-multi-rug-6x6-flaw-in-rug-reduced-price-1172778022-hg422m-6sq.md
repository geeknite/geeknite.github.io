### Cleaning and Maintenance Tips

- Vacuum regularly with a standard vacuum set to low pile. High suction can cause little frays along the edges if you are not careful.
- Blot stains immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to minimize fiber damage. For stubborn stains, a mild solution of water and gentle detergent works well; test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear. It helps to treat the rug as a rotating cast member in your living space rather than a static prop.
- If you have heavy foot traffic, consider a rug pad to minimize curling and to extend life.
