From Pack to Deck: Practical Deck-Building Tips
------------------------------------------------
If you’re brand-new to the DBS Card Game, BT01 can feel overwhelming at first. Here are a few practical tips to get you from “open packs” to “winning games” without needing a PhD in Dragon Balls:
- Start with a 50/50 split between offense and defense. BT01’s card pool rewards a balanced approach where you can respond to aggression while setting up your own offense.
- Build around a core leader card you enjoy. The leader often dictates the color and energy management path you’ll be taking in your deck.
- Prioritize 2-3 synergy cards. Cards that enable your main combo or enable your defensive responses are worth hunting even if they’re not the flashiest pieces in the box.
- Foils matter. In this game, foils can swing your opponent’s perception of your deck’s power, which may influence game tempo and decision making.
- Don’t neglect the sideboard. Depending on your local meta, you’ll want a handful of anti-meta counters to address the most common decks you meet.
