## From Pack to Deck: Practical Deck-Building Tips

If you’re brand-new to the DBS Card Game, BT01 can feel overwhelming at first. Here are a few practical tips to go from open packs to winning games without needing a PhD in Dragon Balls:
- Start with a 50/50 split between offense and defense. BT01’s card pool rewards a balanced approach where you can respond to aggression while setting up your own offense.
- Build around a core leader card you enjoy. The leader often dictates the color and energy management path you’ll be taking in your deck.
- Prioritize 2-3 synergy cards. Cards that enable your main combo or enable your defensive responses are worth hunting even if they’re not the flashiest pieces in the box.
- Foils matter. In this game, foils can swing your opponent’s perception of your deck’s power, which may influence game tempo and decision making.
- Don’t neglect the sideboard. Depending on your local meta, you’ll want a handful of anti-meta counters to address the most common decks you meet.

### A Simple, Realistic Build Path
Consider a starter approach that blends reliable removal with tempo options. Start by picking a leader that aligns with your playstyle, then slot in a few energy accelerants to hit your early plays, followed by mid-game threats that force your opponent to answer or lose in two turns. BT01’s new cards tend to support this rhythm rather than breaking it entirely, so you can adapt your existing DBS knowledge rather than relearn the game from scratch.

## Production Quality: Handling and Storage

Your BT01 collection deserves to be treated with care. Card sleeves, a sturdy binder, and zone dividers help you keep track of your pulls and ensure your investments stay pristine. A good binder, acid-free sleeves, and proper card boxes will preserve your BT01 era for years to come, much like a well-aged bottle of spicy sauce—spicy and memorable, not a disaster when you crack the cap.

### Storage Tips

- Keep a backup plan for moisture: silica gel packs in your storage containers help keep things crisp.
