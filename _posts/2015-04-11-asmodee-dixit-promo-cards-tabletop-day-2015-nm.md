## Gameplay implications

### How do promo cards change Dixit rounds?

Dixit thrives on ambiguity, poetic cues, and the tension between the storyteller and the guessers. Promo variants naturally shift those dynamics in two ways: the card prompts provide fresh reference points, and the art style can influence players to describe things differently. The result is often a few extra rounds of lively negotiation as players decide whether to chase the wild, dreamlike interpretation or to anchor the round with a safer but less imaginative description.

In practical terms, the promo set can lead to:
- More varied storytelling prompts as players reach for the new art cards instead of reciting familiar phrases.
- A slight shift in vote distribution because the images are perceived as more unusual than standard Dixit cards, prompting riskier or more imaginative guesses.
- A potential increase in group cohesion when everyone agrees on a shared interpretation of a card that appears abstract enough to be argued about for longer than a standard round.

If your table tends to lean toward strongly narrative play, these cards can extend rounds by nudging players to justify more outlandish connections. If your group prefers quick rounds with a high ratio of immediate guesses, you may experience a few more moments where players deliberate over what the card is trying to evoke before submitting a guess.

### Balancing and house rules

Promo cards do not radically overhaul Dixit rules, but they do encourage players to think about memory and association in new ways. As with any new card set, consider a few gentle guidelines to keep things fair:
- Start a round with a standard prompting phrase to anchor the storyteller, then switch to a more open-ended description as players get comfortable with the new imagery.
- If players struggle with a particularly abstract card, allow a brief discussion period to brainstorm possible interpretations before guessing.
- When using promos in a rotation, establish a consistent deck mix so that promos complement rather than dominate the storytelling flow.

In short, the promo cards are a spice, not a fundamental seasoning change. They taste better when you allow the table to experiment with the prompts but keep enough structure so you can still tell a story that everyone can follow, even through the fog of imaginative imagery.

## How to use these promo cards in your Dixit nights

In practice, the best way to incorporate a promo pack is to treat it as a separate encounter that you cycle into your regular Dixit rotation. Here are a few practical ideas to ensure the experience remains fun and not feng-shui confusing:

- Thematic nights: Pair the promo cards with a theme night such as dreamlike travels, secret identities, or surreal urban scenes. Thematically aligned prompts help players lean into the mood and can yield surprisingly consistent storytelling threads.
