### House Rules to Elevate the Chaos

To maximize the glow of the evening, consider these optional house rules. First, implement a safety hand signal or a pinch rule so that players can opt out of a prompt that crosses their line. Second, rotate the card czar with a rule that the czar cannot be the same person for more than two rounds in a row, which forces social dynamics to adapt and prevents a single player from always owning the moment. Third, add a time cap on rounds to keep things moving. Fourth, consider a mild penalty for a player who runs away with a string of weak cards; maybe a forced draw of two extra white cards that you must play in the next round as a kind of comedic penance. These small changes can transform a chaotic night into a memorable performance rather than a long slog.
