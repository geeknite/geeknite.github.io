## Practicality: Placement, Slippage, and Care
Every rug is a little physics problem waiting to be solved. The Madras Marlo Natural Rug excels when paired with a proper rug pad and placed on a dry, clean floor. ### Placement and Sizing
Room geometry matters. A rug that’s too small can make a space feel unfinished, while a rug that’s too large can overwhelm a room faster than a pop-up ad for a couch in 4K. The recommended sizing for a typical living room seating area is a rug that fits under the front legs of all major furniture pieces or at least the front legs of the sofa and two chairs. In narrower rooms, a runner version in a hall or entryway can be a dramatic, space-saving hero. The Madras Marlo line often offers several standard sizes that align with common sofa dimensions, which helps remove the guesswork from interior planning.

### Slippage and Rug Pads
A rug pad is not optional; it’s a safety feature and a performance enhancer. A good pad prevents the rug from creeping under the coffee table, reduces noise when shoes meet the floor, and adds a touch of plushness that makes your step feel rewardingly cushioned. If your floor is slippery or if you share your apartment with enthusiastic pets that treat the rug like a stage, a non-slip pad is your best friend. It also helps preserve the rug’s shape over time, which is a gift you’ll thank yourself for during summer mortgage checks and seasonal vacuum storms.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
Cleaning a natural-fiber rug is not as dramatic as a soap opera but requires consistent, sensible care. Vacuum regularly with the beater bar turned off or set to a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers. Treat spills promptly by blotting with a clean, dry cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the spill deeper into the fibers. For stubborn stains, test a small, inconspicuous area with a mild detergent solution, and always follow the manufacturer’s care recommendations if provided. Avoid soaking the rug; moisture is a natural enemy of many natural fibers and can lead to swelling, odor, or mold over time.
