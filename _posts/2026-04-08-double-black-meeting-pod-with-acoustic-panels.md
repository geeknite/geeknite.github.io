Overview
What is this beast, and do you actually need it?

What is the Double Black Meeting Pod with Acoustic Panels
The Double Black Meeting Pod is a compact, stand-alone enclosure designed to deliver a controlled acoustic environment for calls, recordings, and focused work in spaces that are not labs or studios. The hallmark feature is a set of acoustic panels lining the interior, built to dampen reflections and control noise intrusion from the outside world. The pod includes a built-in LED lighting strip, a small ventilation system, a USB-C charging port, and power outlets. It is not a sound stage; it is a portable silence machine.

Design and Build Quality
Exterior and materials
The exterior shell is matte black, with a sturdy frame and flush-fitting panels. The look is modern minimalism, with clean lines that look good in most office decors and avoid shouting at the rest of the room. The surface finish resists fingerprints and the occasional spill, which is a huge win in a world where coffee is the only constant.

Interior and acoustic panels
The interior is lined with acoustic panels that use a rock based mineral wool core wrapped in a durable fabric. The result is a space that feels composed rather than claustrophobic. The panels are installed with a modular, replaceable design; if one panel wears out after years of use, you can swap it without disassembling the entire pod. This is a thoughtful touch for long term use.

Assembly and setup
Setting up the pod is a two person job, with a few hours from start to finish if you take your time and align things carefully. The included hardware is plentiful and labeled; the diagrams are clear; and there is little guesswork. A trick we found helpful is to sort the screws by length before starting, to avoid a moment of which is which in the middle of the project.

Ventilation and comfort
Ventilation is essential in a sealed space. The pod provides a quiet, low power fan system that maintains air exchange without producing the sonic signature of a jet engine. The seating is comfortable for most people for up to 60 minutes in a row; longer sessions may require breaks or a more ergonomic seat. The door seals well, reducing heat loss and noise leakage.

Connectivity and features
Power and data
The pod includes a power strip with surge protection and a handful of outlets for charging devices. A USB C port is available for fast charging, and there is a data jack for a wired network if needed. The LED strip around the interior provides adjustable lighting that can shift from a cool daylight to a warm ambiance, helping you set the mood for a video call or a long writing sprint.

Optional extras
Some retailers offer add ons such as a microphone stand, a clip on camera mount, or extra acoustic panels for higher sound isolation. In some configurations, you can also upgrade the lighting to RGB or incorporate a small screen mount. If you are a streamer or podcaster, these extras can be compelling.

Acoustic performance
How quiet is quiet
We tested the 3D echo within the pod, a typical office cacophony outside the pod, and a voice recording sample inside. The panels do a solid job of reducing early reflections and absorbing mid frequencies. In our tests, the outside noise drop ranged from 15 to 25 dB depending on the variety of noise and the presence of direct line of sight to the hallway. The net effect is a space that reads as much quieter in a typical video call as it does in a calm studio.

Room impression
The interior does not feel claustrophobic; the space design prioritizes a sense of openness while still maintaining acoustic treatment. The result is a comfortable environment for voice work and small team discussions. Some users may find the interior shape slightly boxed, but it is functional, not claustrophobic.

Energy efficiency
The pod is designed with energy efficiency in mind. The LED lighting is low power and can be configured to a range of brightness levels. The ventilation system is modest in its energy consumption, and the overall draw is manageable for most office setups, which means you should not fear a shocking electricity bill if you use the space daily.

Buying guide
