### Battery: Because Power Matters

Equipped with a 4500 mAh battery, the iPhone 16 Pro promises to keep up with your all-day activities. Even with the most intense usage, you can expect it to last a full day. And if you're a responsible adult who charges their phone overnight, you'll be ready to conquer the world by morning.
