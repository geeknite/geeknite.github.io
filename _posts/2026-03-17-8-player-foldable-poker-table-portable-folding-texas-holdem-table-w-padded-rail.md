## Setup, Breakdown, and Real-World Play

Setting up the table is a two minute affair once you get the rhythm. Here is the quick run-down:

1) Unfold the legs and lock them into the base. The design typically features a cross brace that snaps into place with a hardware click.
2) Open the playing surface and align with the rail. Some models require a quick readjustment for slight lateral movement; this is normal and quickly solved by tightening a couple of knobs.
3) Check the alignment of the felt surface. If it looks a bit skewed, simply shift the table and re-secure the rails.
4) Place chips, cards, and beverages in a safe, non-spill zone away from the magnets. Yes, the world is full of magnets; your chips probably contain some.
