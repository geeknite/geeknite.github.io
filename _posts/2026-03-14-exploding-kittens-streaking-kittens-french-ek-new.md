## Overview

### What is Streaking Kittens
Streaking Kittens is an expansion pack for Exploding Kittens that introduces flashy new mechanics intended to speed up the tempo and inject more chaos into a game that’s already basically a kitchen timer for friendship. In this French edition, the cards are bilingual-friendly in flavor, but the puns and cat-based shenanigans land in a way that will make bilingual players snort with laughter or groan in solidarity.

### The Quick Pitch
If you enjoyed Exploding Kittens and you enjoy a little risqué humor in a card game that rewards chaos management, you’ll probably like Streaking Kittens. It adds new card types, some rule variants, and enough extra action to turn a 15-minute game into a slightly longer, much louder battle of welinear luck and cat-themed mischief. It’s not a total overhaul; think of it as a booster shot of chaos for your base game, with French flavor to boot.

## How to Play: A Quick Refresh

### Turn Flow and Tempo
If you already know Exploding Kittens, you largely know the drill: draw cards, play actions, avoid the exploding kitten, and pray that your luck holds while your tablemates sabotage themselves with overconfident plays. Streaking Kittens accelerates the tempo by introducing cards that reward aggressive play and punish passive shenanigans. The typical playtime threads a little longer than the base game, but the pacing feels more alive, like your cat finally decided to chase the laser dot with a speed that could outrun a toddler on a scooter.

### New Card Types and Interactions
Streaking Kittens adds several types of cards that interact with the core mechanics in fresh ways. Some cards force players to pass their hand around more quickly, some trigger mini-brawls where players must respond with cat-pun counters, and others create temporary alliances of convenience that crumble the moment someone mentions snacks. The interactions are designed to be chaotic but ultimately understandable, which is important when you’re playing in a language that isn’t your first tongue.

### A Short Play Sequence
1) Shuffle, deal, and glance at your feline fate. 2) Play a card that shifts the turn order or forces a discard. 3) Draw a card and prepare for the inevitable cat-based chaos. 4) Watch as someone accidentally reveals a prank card that flips the table morale. The sequence is simple enough for new players to pick up quickly, while still offering enough variety to keep veteran players amused for several rounds.

### Streak Cards
These are the marquee additions. Streak Cards introduce a rapid-fire pattern of actions that push players to act quickly. They tend to escalate the speed of decisions, so you’ll experience the thrill of “blink and you’ll miss it” moments where a single misread or misplay can cost you a devastatingly cartoonish loss. The essence is pushing the game toward a more kinetic style of play that rewards sharp reflexes and a willingness to embrace chaos.

### Cat Cards and Interaction Loops
Cat Cards exist in multiple flavors, each with a unique interaction that triggers when played or drawn.
