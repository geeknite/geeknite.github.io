## Setup and Assembly Tips

- Group similar components: screws with screws, dowels with dowels. It sounds obvious, but it helps to keep the assembly process streamlined. 
- Don’t overtighten screws: you want stability, not cracks. If you feel resistance, back off half a turn and proceed with caution. 
- Use a small rubber mallet or a soft hammer for snug joints to avoid denting the veneer. 
- Check level frequently: place a straightedge or a smartphone level app on the top surface as you tighten things up.
