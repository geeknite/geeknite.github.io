## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity
To keep these chips looking sharp and the aluminum case functioning properly, here are a few practical tips:

- Wipe chips with a soft microfiber cloth after use to remove fingerprints and oils. This helps prevent the coloration from showing wear in high-traffic areas.
- Avoid harsh cleaners. A damp cloth with mild soap is enough for routine cleaning. Abrasive cleaners can dull the surface and edge details, which defeats the purpose of a premium feel.
- Store the set in its case when not in use. The foam inserts are designed to cradle chips and provide padding against jostling during storage or transport.
- Keep the case away from extreme temperatures and moisture. While aluminum is tough, the interior lining and any foam components can degrade over time if exposed to humidity and heat.
- If edge wear appears, light polishing with a gentle compound can help restore the edge look. Practice on a single chip first to ensure you don’t alter the engraving or color consistency.

With proper care, this set should serve you for countless gatherings, from casual hangouts to “hey, let’s finally settle who’s the best after three beers” tournaments. It’s not immortal, but it’s built to endure the sort of mischief that table games invite.
