## Care and maintenance

Chips and acrylic cases are relatively low-maintenance, but there are a few best practices to keep them looking knights-legendary for years. Here’s a quick care checklist:

- Keep chips away from moisture. Water and chips aren’t friends in the long run; moisture can cause discoloration in some dye batches.
- Wipe dust regularly. A microfiber cloth keeps dust from settling into edges and numbers.
- Avoid harsh chemicals on the acrylic. Mild soap and water work well for most cases; if you’re worried, test on a small corner first.
- Regularly check the hinges and latches, especially after a few “late-night” games where the case has to survive a bit of rough handling.
