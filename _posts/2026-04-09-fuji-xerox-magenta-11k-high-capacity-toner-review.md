## Reliability, Maintenance, and Longevity
### Common Pitfalls and How to Avoid Them
- Early leakage or spills: This is rare but manageable with proper storage. Keep the cartridge upright and avoid temperature extremes prior to installation.
- Banding or uneven coverage: Recalibrate the color profile, run a calibration page, and ensure you’re using fresh paper that’s not overly damp or dry.
- Ghosting: A sign of toner residue on the imaging drum; run a cleaning cycle if your printer supports it and check for any maintenance advisories from Fuji Xerox.

### Longevity and Standby Time
If you print sporadically, the toner can settle a bit. A quick shake before insertion helps, but don’t overshake; you don’t want to create clumps that clog the nozzle or cause rough distribution.

## Troubleshooting Quick-Fire Guide
- Page comes out faded? Check for calibration, verify you’re using the correct printer profile, and ensure the page coverage isn’t exceeding recommended limits for magenta.
- Color mismatch between pages? Run a color alignment and compare against a known-good color chart. You may need to adjust the printer’s color temperature or saturation settings.
- Printer won’t recognize cartridge? Confirm that the cartridge is seated properly and the firmware is up to date. If needed, reset the printer and re-insert the cartridge.
