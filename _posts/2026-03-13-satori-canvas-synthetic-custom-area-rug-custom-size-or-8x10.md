## Custom Size vs 8x10: Practical implications

The option to choose a fully custom size or an 8x10 is a big win for flexibility. Here’s how these choices play out:

### Custom Size
- Ideal for irregular rooms, alcoves, or layouts with unique furniture footprints.
- Lets you align the rug edges with wall lines or furniture legs for a cohesive look.
- Lead times and pricing: standard practice for fully custom rugs includes longer production times and potentially higher cost. If you’re in a rush, the 8x10 can still be a strong fit while you wait for a bespoke piece.

### 8x10 Standard
- The most versatile default size for medium-to-large spaces.
- More price-conscious and readily available from a wider set of retailers.
- Great for defining a seating area in a sunlit room where you want to keep things simple and stylish.

## Durability and Maintenance

Durability is where synthetic fibers shine. The goal is a rug that resists spills, cleans up easily, and maintains its shape under normal use. The Satori Canvas line checks those boxes with a backing that helps keep everything in place and a face that cleans up with standard household care.

- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt from embedding in the fibers.
- Blot spills with a clean cloth and a mild detergent solution; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper.
- For larger cleanings, consult the product care guide and consider a gentle wash according to the manufacturer’s recommendations.
- Drying: ensure the rug is completely dry before placing it back on the floor to prevent mildew and to keep the backing from losing grip.

We also recommend a rug pad for added stability, particularly on hard floors. It helps with edge curling and can improve underfoot comfort.
