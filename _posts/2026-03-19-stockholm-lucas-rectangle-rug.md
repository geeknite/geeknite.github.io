## Care, Cleaning, and Maintenance: Keeping It Fresh

Maintenance is the boring-but-important part of rug ownership. Here’s a concise checklist:
- Vacuum weekly with a standard attachment; avoid high-suction modes that might pull fibers out of alignment.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear patterns and prevent noticeable color drift in direct sunlight.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth or paper towel. Avoid rubbing—this can drive stains deeper into the fibers.
- For stubborn stains, test a mild detergent on a small inconspicuous area first, then gently dab the stain until it lifts. Rinse with a damp cloth to remove detergent residue.
- For deep cleaning, consider professional cleaning or specialized rug-cleaning solutions that are safe for synthetic fibers.
