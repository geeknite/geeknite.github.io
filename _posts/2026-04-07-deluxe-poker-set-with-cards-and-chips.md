## Maintenance and care
A deck that looks and feels premium also rewards careful maintenance. Here are practical tips to maximize the lifespan of your Deluxe Poker Set:
- Clean cards occasionally with a soft, dry cloth to remove fingerprints and smudges that can affect shuffling. If you go deep into the card soil and you need a real clean, use a damp cloth very lightly and let cards air dry before reusing.
- Avoid exposing chips to direct sunlight for extended periods, which can cause color fading. A quick wipe with a dry cloth is usually enough to remove surface residue after a game.
- Store the set in its case in a cool, dry place. Humidity can warp both cards and the foam inserts, so keep the environment comfortable for your prized kit.
- Do not stack too many heavy objects on top of the case during transport. This reduces the risk of warping and helps preserve the case’s lid alignment.
- If a chip edge or card edge shows wear, consider rotating the deck or chip set as part of your rotation plan. This helps prevent corner wear that becomes a distraction mid-game.

These are small steps, but they compound over time into a kit that remains enjoyable to use and looks good on night one and night 1,000 alike.
