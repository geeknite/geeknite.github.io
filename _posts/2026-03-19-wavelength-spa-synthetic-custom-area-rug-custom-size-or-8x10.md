### Durability and maintenance

Synthetic fibers shine in the durability department. Expect excellent stain resistance, colorfastness, and resistance to wear from high-traffic zones. Cleaning is straightforward: blot spills promptly, use a mild detergent for stains, and avoid harsh bleach or solvents that can degrade the backing. Routine vacuuming keeps dirt from grinding into the fibers, and periodic professional cleaning can refresh the backing and resettle the fibers for that just-installed vibe.

- Spill management: quick blot, mild cleaner, non abrasive sponge
- Vacuuming: regular but not aggressive; rotate your passes to avoid a track mark that looks suspiciously like a runway for your dog's paw prints
- Cleaning frequency: every season or after that chili spill that refuses to be silenced

### How to measure and order

To order a rug of your dreams, start with precise room measurements. Measure width and length, then add a small buffer so the edges do not butt up against doorways or furniture legs. If you choose custom sizing, confirm the exact width and length, consider door clearance, and ensure your rug pad is compatible with the backing. The ordering process typically includes a confirmation step where you can approve the exact dimensions before production begins.

- What is the maximum size available for custom orders

  Production times vary; expect a few weeks for custom sizes depending on scheduling and material availability.
