## Durability and maintenance
Durability in decorative floor coverings is a balancing act between aesthetics and practical life. The Mineral 555 Rock Rug leans into durability by using a hard-wearing top layer and a backing that resists creeping. We tested the rug under varying conditions: foot traffic, cat-scratching attempts, and the occasional furniture drag test. The results were decidedly positive: the surface remained stable, resistant to scuffs, and the backing stayed in place better than a sliding chair on a polished floor.

Maintenance is straightforward. Regular vacuuming is recommended to remove loose particles. The top surface resists staining after quick spot-cleaning with mild soap and water. For deeper stains, the recommended approach is to treat promptly, blot, and repeat. Avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the resin binder or cause discoloration. We also tested the rug against hair of different lengths. Short-haired dogs and long-haired cats left behind some hair, but the low nap meant hair did not penetrate deeply, which is a blessing after a long workday.

Caring tips:
- Vacuum in the direction of the pattern to avoid marring the surface.
- Blot spills with a soft cloth instead of rubbing, which can push liquids into the fibers.
- If you have spills with colorfast dyes, address quickly to minimize staining.

## Maintenance and care tips
To maximize life and appearance of the rug, keep these in mind:

- Use a rug pad: For larger spaces, a rug pad improves stability and reduces wear on the backing.
- Rotate occasionally: Yes, rotating the rug can help even out wear patterns and keep the surface looking balanced across foot traffic hot spots.
