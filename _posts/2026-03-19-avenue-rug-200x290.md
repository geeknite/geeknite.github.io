## Care and Maintenance Tips

- Vacuum regularly on a low setting to avoid matting the fibers.
- Blot spills with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper.
- For tougher stains, use a mild detergent solution and rinse with clean water; blot dry.
- Consider a professional cleaning every 12–18 months, depending on foot traffic.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color retention.

### Special scenarios

If you have pets that shed, a quick weekly pass with a vacuum and a grooming brush can help keep hair from sticking to fibers. If you have young kids who leave crayons on the rug, a gentle cleaner should lift color without leaving residues that attract more crayons next week. In all cases, test any cleaner on a small inconspicuous area first to confirm colorfastness. And yes, you can pretend you are a stealth cleaner, because nothing screams adulting like a stain-removal montage narrated by a coffee-fueled YouTuber voice.
