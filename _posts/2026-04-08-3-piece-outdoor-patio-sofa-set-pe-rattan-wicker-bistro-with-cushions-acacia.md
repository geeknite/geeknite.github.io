## What’s in the Box and What It Means for Your Patio
PE rattan wicker is not your grandmother’s wicker. It’s a weather-resistant, flexible material designed to mimic natural rattan without the fragility. That means you can show off a sun-kissed look without babysitting a real palm tree in a hurricane season.

The Acacia wood frame is where the real backbone lies. Acacia is known for its dense grain, natural oil content, and respectable weather tolerance. ## Weather, Care, and Longevity
Outdoor furniture deserves a care routine, just like your faithful gaming chair. Here are practical tips to maximize life:
- Cover when not in use: A breathable furniture cover protects against UV and moisture without trapping humidity.
- Clean gently: Use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the wicker or fade the cushions.
- Protect the wood: Apply a sealant or light oil to the Acacia frame periodically to prevent cracking and to extend life.
- Cushions: Store or cover cushions during heavy rain or long periods of non-use to prevent mildew. If you see spots, a gentle wipe and air-dry can work wonders.

If you live in a coastal or humid climate, you’ll want a moisture management plan. Salt air can corrode metal hardware faster than you think, so check screws occasionally and replace any that show signs of corrosion. A little preventive maintenance goes a long way toward keeping your set looking fresh for seasons to come.
