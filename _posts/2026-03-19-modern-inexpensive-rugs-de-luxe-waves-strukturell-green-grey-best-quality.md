## Care and Cleaning Tips
We all want our rugs to stay gorgeous without requiring a PhD in textile engineering. Here are some simple, nerd-friendly care tips:

- Vacuum regularly: Gentle, steady passes prevent dirt from embedding into the fibers. Use a vacuum with a beater bar on low power if possible.
- Immediate spot treatment: Blot spills with a clean cloth—do not rub, or you risk frizzing the wave pattern. For tougher stains, a mild detergent solution can help. Test in a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rotation: Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear, especially in high-traffic zones like entrances or under a gaming chair.
- Rinse and dry: If using water, ensure the rug dries thoroughly to prevent mold. A warm room and good ventilation speed things up.
- Professional cleaning: Every 12-24 months, consider a professional cleaning if you notice deep-seated grime or if you’re dealing with pets that have a “personal brand” of odor.

## How to Buy and What to Watch For
If you’re ready to pull the trigger, here are practical buying tips:
- Check the size math: Measure your room for rug placement. The general rule is to allow 6-12 inches of floor showing around the rug, depending on furniture layout.
- Inspect the pile and construction: Look for even pile height and confirm there are no loose threads. A quick visual inspection helps avoid post-purchase regrets.
- Consider the backing: A non-slip backing reduces movement on smooth floors. If the rug lacks this feature, buy a quality rug pad to prevent sliding and protect floors.
- Return policy: Budget rugs are best bought with a forgiving return policy in case the color or feel doesn’t meet expectations in your space.
- Shipping and handling: Check for hidden fees and delivery times. Some retailers bundle shipping with discounted prices, which can affect the overall value.
