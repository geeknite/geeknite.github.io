## Durability and Material

This rug is marketed as an outdoor rug, which immediately brings durability into question. The Azun Ochre rug is crafted from a sturdy polypropylene material, which is essentially the superhero of outdoor furnishings. It's resistant to fading, stains, and whatever nature throws at it – rain, sunshine, or the occasional squirrel with a vendetta.

The polypropylene construction also means it's easy to clean. If you spill your wine (or your toddler decides the rug is their new canvas), a simple hose down or a quick scrub should do the trick. The rug dries pretty fast too, saving you from the dreaded mold invasion.

## Comfort Level

Despite its tough persona, the Azun Ochre rug is surprisingly soft. It's not going to give you the plush feel of a shag rug, but it's comfortable enough for barefoot lounging. Think of it as the firm handshake that turns into a warm hug – reliable and cozy.

Being a 5x7 rug, it offers substantial coverage without overwhelming your space. It’s perfect for defining areas in your patio or balcony. Need a dedicated area for your yoga practice? Or maybe a cozy spot for your al fresco dining setup? The Azun Ochre rug has got you covered – literally.
