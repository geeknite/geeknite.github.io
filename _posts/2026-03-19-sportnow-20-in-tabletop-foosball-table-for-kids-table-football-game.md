## Maintenance, Tweaks, and Longevity
Keep your SPORTNOW 20-In looking and performing its best with a few simple maintenance steps:
- Wipe the surface with a damp cloth after messy snack sessions. A sticky surface is the enemy of smooth ball movement.
- Periodically check that screws remain tight and that rods rotate without obstruction. A phone flashlight can help you spot any wobble you might otherwise miss.
- If the rods start feeling “gritty,” you can use a tiny amount of dry lubricant designed for metal-on-plastic interfaces. Don’t overdo it—excess lubrication invites grimy buildup.
- Inspect the ball for nicks; a dinged ball can alter the way it moves, reducing the predictability of shots and increasing the chaos factor (which, for kids, is mostly a feature, not a bug).

With responsible maintenance, your SPORTNOW 20-In remains a reliable, family-friendly entertainment hub for many play sessions to come. It’s not a lifetime investment, but it’s a highly replayable one that earns its keep during those “what do we do now?” moments.
