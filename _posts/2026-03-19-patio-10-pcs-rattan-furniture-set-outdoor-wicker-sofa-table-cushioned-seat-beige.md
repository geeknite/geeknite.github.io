The frames feel solid enough to handle real human weight without showing signs of woe, like creaking or collapsing if someone tries to do the physics of a two-handed push-up on the armrest.

- Cushions: The cushions are zipped and removable, which is excellent for cleaning or swapping covers if leaves decide to move in for a season. The cushion density is not rock-hard, nor is it too springy; it lands somewhere in the sweet spot that says comfort without being a full-blown cloud. 

A practical note: the beige tone is stylish but not mug-shot beige. It hides dirt well enough for a family that occasionally drifts into a yard that has seen worse weather and worse fashion. If you have a dog, a cat, or a plant that likes to shed your furniture, the color is forgiving but not magical. Regular dusting and occasional wipe-down with a mild soap solution will keep the vibe fresh. ## Weather resistance and maintenance routines

Rattan sets of this type are designed with weather in mind. Resin wicker resists fading under sun exposure better than natural wicker, and the aluminum or steel frame may be powder-coated to better handle moisture and temperature changes. You can expect the set to tolerate light rain, afternoon sprinkles, and the odd summer thunderstorm that sneaks up on you while you are not looking. Condensation on the table glass is a minor giggle when you realize your cold beverage has become an accidental sculpture.

Maintenance is straightforward. Clean with a mild soap solution, rinse with water, and air dry. For cushion care, remove covers and wash following the manufacturer instructions if possible. A yearly check for loose screws or wobbly legs helps extend the life of the set and keeps your party guests calm and safe. If you live in a region with heavy snowfall, you might want to store the cushions indoors during winter or use a dedicated patio storage bag.
