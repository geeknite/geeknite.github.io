## Photography Tips for Tiny Furniture Fun
- Light up the scene from multiple angles to reveal texture. Wicker catches highlights in a way that can be dramatic with the right backlight.
- Use a shallow depth of field to isolate the tea set as a focal point. This helps the tiny cups look intentional rather than accidental props.
- Experiment with color temperature. A warmer light can emphasize the vintage vibe of the wicker, while a cooler light can push toward a modern crisp look.
- Add context with a prop or two, such as a miniature book or a tiny umbrella hat, to tell a story. The more narrative your photo, the more fans will engage with your diorama.
