## What is inside a booster pack and this eight-pack bundle

A typical modern MTG booster pack is a curated little ecosystem: 15 cards consisting of one Rare or Mythic, three Uncommons, and eleven Commons, plus a basic land and sometimes a foil or variant. Rare cards are the heart of the chase in booster packs. Mythic rares are the elusive stars that can turn a casual hobbyist into the center of attention at the kitchen table for about 37 seconds. ## Practical tips to maximize your eight-pack experience
### Before you open
- Inspect the wrapper: look for any obvious tampering or flaws. If a pack looks suspicious, set it aside and proceed with the rest. Trust your instincts and your local game store if you must.
- Decide your approach: do you want to draft eight packs with friends or open them one by one to savor the moment? Maybe you want to do a blind-value challenge where you assign value to each card as you reveal them. The choice is yours, and that is the beauty of eight little mysteries.

### During the opening
- Pace yourself: one pack at a time helps you savor the drama. Resist the urge to scream after the first rare and then forget the rest. You owe it to your future self to appreciate the journey.
- Sorting matters: after you reveal each card, place them into piles (rare/mythic, uncommons, commons, lands) to keep your brain from melting. This makes it easier to see potential trades and deck-building opportunities.
- Foil fortune or drought: if you get a foil, celebrate with a tiny happy dance. If not, you still have the joy of knowing you control eight different storylines—at least for the next few hours.

### After the unboxing
- Trade and sell wisely: if you get duplicates, look for trades with friends or local stores. Card value can be volatile; the time to trade is when you are feeling generous and your friend is also wearing a suspiciously calm expression.
- Build and brew: use the pulled cards to draft or build a deck. Even if you do not end up with a bomb rare, you can always craft a fun deck and pretend you are a legendary card designer in your own tabletop RPG.
