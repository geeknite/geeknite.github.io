## Maintenance and care

To keep the Platinum Tackle Bag looking fresh, a quick wipe-down after use is advisable. The nylon shell resists moisture but is not immunized against the occasional dunk in saltwater or the inevitable spill of your questionable lunch. After a trip, empty the bag and give it a light wash with mild soap, then air-dry. Avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade the fabric or the zippers. If you have a habit of leaving gear out in the rain, consider a rain cover to keep the bag in pristine condition. In our tests, the zippers never stuck, and the stitching remained taut after several uses, which is a small miracle worth noting.
