### Maintenance and care: keeping a floor-model edge sharp

-The edge: even if you plan to use it sparingly, a light touch-up with a high-grit stone or strop is wise after purchase. Display edges can dull a bit due to handling and storage vibrations.
-Handle and wood care: wipe down after use, keep the handle dry, and consider a light oiling if you’re storing for long periods. This prevents micro-cracks and keeps the grip feeling tacky instead of slick when your hands are sweaty.
-Sheath maintenance: occasionally inspect the retention screws and belt-loop stitching. Leather sheaths show wear differently than synthetic ones; if your floor-model came with leather, you might want to condition the leather with a proper product to avoid cracks.
