## Care Tips: Keeping the Rug Looking Fresh
- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and crumbs. Use a standard vacuum with a beater bar on low or a suction-only setting for delicate rooms.
- Blot spills rather than rub. Rubbing invites fiber distortion and makes stains set deeper.
- For tougher stains, use a mild cleaning solution. Test in an inconspicuous corner first to ensure colorfastness.
- Place a rug pad underneath to reduce sliding and improve comfort. A pad also protects the floor beneath from daily wear and tear.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color exposure. If you’re tempted to rotate with every season, you might be the kind of homeowner who has a calendar for everything—embrace it.
