## Introduction

Hello, fellow tech enthusiasts! If you thought the iPad Pro couldn't get any better, think again. The new **iPad Pro M4** has landed, and it's packed with enough features to make even a robot envious. But is it really worth the price tag, or is it just another shiny gadget? Let's dive deep and see what makes the iPad Pro M4 the future of tablets.

## Design and Build Quality

The **iPad Pro M4** is a sleek, futuristic slab of aluminum and glass that feels like it was designed by a collaboration of NASA engineers and fashion designers. It's thinner and lighter than its predecessors, but don't let that fool you—this thing is built like a tank.

### Ports and Buttons

Apple has finally decided to give us more ports! The iPad Pro M4 includes not just one but two USB-C ports, a dedicated magnetic charging port for the Apple Pencil, and even a headphone jack! Just kidding, there's no headphone jack—this is Apple, after all.

## Display: A Feast for the Eyes

![Display](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipad-pro-m4-display)

The iPad Pro M4 features a stunning **14.6-inch Liquid Retina XDR display**. With a resolution so high, you might start questioning if reality is really in 4K. The ProMotion technology ensures everything is butter-smooth, whether you're scrolling through memes or editing 4K videos.

## Performance: A Powerhouse

### Powered by M4 Chip

![M4 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/m4__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}m4-chip)

The iPad Pro M4 is powered by the new **M4 chip**, which is so fast it might finish your tasks before you even start them. With an **8-core CPU**, **12-core GPU**, and a **16-core Neural Engine**, this tablet can handle anything you throw at it—from gaming and video editing to running multiple virtual machines and AI tasks.

### Battery Life

Apple claims the iPad Pro M4 can last up to **18 hours** on a single charge. In our tests, it easily handled a full day of mixed usage, including video streaming, heavy browsing, and some light gaming. No need to carry around a charger like a nomad.

## Software and Features

![iPadOS](https://www.apple.com/v/ipad-pro-m4/a/images/overview/ipados__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}ipados)

### iPadOS 16

The iPad Pro M4 comes with **iPadOS 16**, packed with features like Universal Control, which allows you to use a single mouse and keyboard across your Mac and iPad. The new **Focus mode** helps you stay concentrated by filtering notifications based on what you're doing.

### Face ID and Cameras

The iPad Pro M4 features **Face ID** for secure authentication and a **12MP Ultra-Wide front camera** with Center Stage, which automatically keeps you centered in the frame during video calls. The **12MP Wide and 10MP Ultra-Wide rear cameras** ensure you capture every moment in stunning detail.

## Accessories: The Perfect Companions

### Apple Pencil 3

The new **Apple Pencil 3** attaches magnetically and charges wirelessly. It now features haptic feedback, making it feel like you're writing on real paper. Perfect for artists and note-takers alike.

### Magic Keyboard

The **Magic Keyboard** has been updated with backlit keys and a larger trackpad. It now includes a function row, making it even more versatile. Typing on the iPad Pro M4 feels almost as good as on a MacBook Pro.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Exceptional Performance**: The M4 chip is a beast.
- **Stunning Display**: The Liquid Retina XDR display is a visual treat.
- **All-Day Battery Life**: Up to 18 hours of usage.
- **Improved Connectivity**: More ports, including dual USB-C.

### Cons

- **Pricey**: Not for the faint of wallet.
- **No Headphone Jack**: Still no love for wired headphone users.
- **iPadOS Limitations**: Still not a full desktop OS.

## Conclusion

The **iPad Pro M4** is a remarkable piece of technology that sets a new standard for tablets. Its blend of performance, design, and features makes it the ultimate dream machine for geeks and tech enthusiasts. If you're in the market for a new tablet and can afford the hefty price tag, the iPad Pro M4 is worth every penny.
