## The Concept

TRIO Country isn't just a band; it's an experience. Imagine if The Three Stooges decided to swap slapstick comedy for guitars and banjos. That's pretty much what you get with TRIO Country. The band consists of three zany characters: Hank, Bubba, and Cletus, each bringing their own brand of humor to the stage. Together, they create a musical rollercoaster that's equal parts funny and fantastic.

### Meet the Members

- **Hank**: The ringleader, known for his witty lyrics and infectious energy. He can strum a guitar like nobody's business.
- **Bubba**: The lovable goofball, always ready with a joke or a prank. His harmonica skills are second to none.
- **Cletus**: The silent genius, who lets his banjo do the talking. When he does speak, it's usually something hilariously profound.

## The Music

TRIO Country's music is a delightful blend of country classics and comedic parodies. They take well-known country hits and give them a humorous twist, making you laugh while you tap your feet. Their original songs are just as entertaining, filled with clever wordplay and hilarious anecdotes.

### Hit Songs

1. **"My Dog Ran Away (And Took My Wife Too)"**: A side-splitting tale of love, loss, and canine betrayal.
2. **"Truck Broke Down (But My Spirits Are Up)"**: An upbeat anthem about finding joy in the little things, even when your truck is in the shop.
3. **"Y'all Got Problems (But I Got Beer)"**: A hilarious ode to drowning your sorrows in a cold one.

## Live Performances

If you think their recordings are fun, just wait until you see TRIO Country live. Their concerts are a whirlwind of laughter, music, and unexpected antics. From Bubba's surprise pranks on Hank to Cletus's impromptu banjo solos, there's never a dull moment. Audience participation is encouraged, and you might even find yourself on stage, part of the hilarity.

### Concert Highlights

- **Prank Wars**: Bubba often initiates prank wars in the middle of their sets, leading to hilarious results.
- **Audience Banjo Battle**: Cletus challenges audience members to a banjo duel, with the winner receiving a shiny golden kazoo.
- **The Hank Dance**: Hank's signature dance move that gets everyone on their feet, trying to mimic his crazy steps.
