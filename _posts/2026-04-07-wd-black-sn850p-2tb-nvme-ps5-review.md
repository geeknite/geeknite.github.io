## Installation and compatibility

### PS5 expansion bay fitment

The PS5 M.2 expansion bay has a strict set of physical dimensions, and not all drives fit with all heatsinks. The SN850P 2TB is a typical 80 mm M.2 drive and is a good match for PS5 expansions, particularly when paired with a heatsink designed for the PS5. If you already own a PS5 heatsink included in a specific bundle, double check clearance in the bay and the screw alignment. The last thing you want is a creaky drive that whispers complaints every time you boot a game.

### Heatsink considerations

Sony recommends using a drive with a heatsink for safe operation. Some PS5 expansions come with integrated heatsinks and others require a separate heatsink module. The SN850P can survive in PS5 in less than ideal cooling scenarios, but you will likely see sustained performance by ensuring adequate cooling. If you opt for a separate heatsink, consider one that is compliant with PS5 guidelines and does not block adjacent bays or cables. Heat = stability, and stability keeps you from having to reload a save after a crash in a hot corner of your favorite game.

### How to install in a few steps

1) Power down and unplug the PS5. 2) Open the side panel and locate the expansion bay cover 3) Remove the bay cover screw 4) Insert the SN850P in the correct orientation 5) Attach the heatsink (if not preinstalled) 6) Reassemble and boot up. 7) Format the drive from the PS5 UI.
