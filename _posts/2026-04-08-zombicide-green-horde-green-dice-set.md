## Maintenance, Storage and Care

### Cleaning and Handling
Dice accumulate fingerprints, dust, and snack crumbs during long sessions. The Green Horde dice are easy to clean with a soft cloth and mild soap if needed. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the surface. If residue shows on faces a quick wipe with microfiber cloth after playing does wonders. The color should not rub off easily.

### Storage Recommendations
Keep them in the resealable bag or transfer to a small dice tin. If you travel with Zombicide gear you will appreciate the snug fit of the bag; the last thing you want is a bag of zombies rolling around your luggage and escaping into your socks. A foam insert can prevent rattling and keep faces pristine. In a game with many expansions you may accumulate a lot of dice; the green set will integrate nicely into your storage system while maintaining convenient accessibility.
