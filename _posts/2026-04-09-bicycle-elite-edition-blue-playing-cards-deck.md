To maximize life, treat this deck as you would a fine Swiss watch: avoid leaving it in extreme heat, don’t soak it in coffee (we’ve all tried that move, no judgment), and avoid bending the cards into impossible shapes to prove a point at the table. The Elite Edition is reasonably durable; with proper storage in the tuck box and a dry environment, it will outlive several standard decks in terms of both looks and performance. - Store in the tuck box when not in use to prevent edge curling
