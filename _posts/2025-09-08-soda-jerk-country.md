## What is a Soda Jerk?

Before we go any further, let's clarify what a soda jerk is. No, it's not someone who rudely slings sodas at you in a diner (although that could be entertaining). A soda jerk is a skilled worker who prepares and serves soda fountain drinks, typically in a retro diner setting. They are the unsung heroes of the beverage world, combining flavors with flair and creating frosty masterpieces.
