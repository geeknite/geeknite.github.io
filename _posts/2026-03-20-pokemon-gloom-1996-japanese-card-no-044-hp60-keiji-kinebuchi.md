## The Practicalities: Caring for a Vintage Card
If you plan to own a Gloom 1996 JP 044 as part of a display or a personal binder, a few tips to help it survive the ages:
- Keep it in a dry, cool environment away from direct sunlight that can fade the colors and brittle the corners.
- Use sleeves that fit standard size cards plus a top loader for extra protection on the keepers in your collection.
- Avoid needless handling. The oils from fingers can cause gloss loss and edge whitening over time, which is a drama you want to avoid unless you enjoy letting your cards be as unique as you are.
- Consider grading if you want to unlock potential value, but remember that the value of a card comes from the connection you have with it. A well loved piece that carries memories can be more valuable than a pristine but unloved specimen.
