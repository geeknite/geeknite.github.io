### Durability, Maintenance, and Care
Wool rugs require a light touch. Here’s a practical care plan:
- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting; avoid aggressive brushes that bend fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. For stains, a mild wool-safe detergent solution works well. Avoid rubbing; blotting is your best friend.
- For tough stains, consider professional cleaning; a wool-safe specialist will preserve the fiber integrity better than a DIY attempt.
- Rotate the rug every 6-12 months to ensure even wear.

Non-slip and rug pads: a rug pad is strongly recommended to keep the rug in place and protect flooring. It also adds a layer of plushness underfoot.
