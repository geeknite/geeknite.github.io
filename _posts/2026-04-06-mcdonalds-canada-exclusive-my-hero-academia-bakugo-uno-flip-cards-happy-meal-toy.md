## How It Works: UNO Flip Mechanics, Bakugo Style

UNO Flip is famous for its reversible deck: light colored cards on front side, darker cards on back. Bakugo variant preserves core mechanic while injecting anime flair. In practice, you’ll see Bakugo imagery on select action cards, with actual gameplay mirroring classic UNO Flip rules:

- Flip the deck to traverse between Light and Dark sides.
- Light side uses standard UNO color mechanics; dark side introduces new actions that change how turns flow.
- The Bakugo art does not alter the rules; it enhances theme for fans and collectors.

From a design standpoint, this is a clever integration. It’s simple enough for kids learning to count, while still delivering a satisfying aha moment when you flip to the darker side and unleash a new string of actions.
