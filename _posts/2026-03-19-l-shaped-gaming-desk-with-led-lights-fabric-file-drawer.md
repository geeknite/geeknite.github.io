## Layout and Ergonomics
### The L Shape Advantage
Corner desks are the unspoken kings of space management. The L configuration lets you place your primary monitor on the longer arm while the shorter arm hosts the keyboard, mouse, and perhaps a coffee mug shaped like a small black hole. This separation helps reduce neck twisting and gives you a natural resting place for the non-dominant arm while your dominant arm handles the controlling. In practice, the L shape turns awkward corner space into a dynamic workstation. It is the grown-up version of a bunk bed for your desk warrior knights—two levels of seating that you only partially share with the cat.
