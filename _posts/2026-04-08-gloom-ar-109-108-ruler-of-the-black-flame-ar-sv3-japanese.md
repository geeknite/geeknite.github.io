## Display, Storage, and Handling Tips
If you’re investing in a card like Gloom AR, display quality matters as much as the price tag. Here are practical tips to keep your AR looking sharp:
- Use a UV-protective display case to minimize color fading and foil degradation.
- Keep the card away from humidity and high-heat environments; sunlight can dull foil and cause minor edge wear.
- Consider a magnet or acrylic stand for gallery displays to minimize handling damage.
- Store in sleeves designed for holo or foil cards, and use a card binder designed for high-grade Pokemon ARs.

If you post pictures of your collection online, you’ll want to ensure the lighting does justice to the foil and to the black flame details. A neutral white balance with a small fill light can help the embers glow without washing out the Gloom’s outline.
