## What is the Middle Earth Chess Collection?

The Middle Earth Chess Collection is a beautifully crafted, thematic chess set featuring characters from J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle Earth. ### The Basics

In the Middle Earth Chess Collection, each piece is designed to represent a character from the Lord of the Rings series. The white pieces are led by Aragorn, while the black pieces are under the command of Sauron. The game follows traditional chess rules but with an added layer of fantasy flair.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up the Middle Earth Chess Collection is like preparing for the Battle of Helm's Deep. Lay out the board, arrange the pieces, and choose your side. Now you're ready to embark on an epic quest of strategy and skill.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players move their pieces according to traditional chess rules. The goal is to checkmate the opponent's king, which in this case is represented by either Aragorn or Sauron. Use your knights (Legolas and Gimli) wisely, and don't forget the powerful wizards (Gandalf and Saruman).

### Combat

While the game follows standard chess mechanics, the thematic elements add an extra layer of excitement. Picture an epic showdown where Gandalf takes down a black knight with a spell, or Frodo sneaks past enemy lines to deliver a surprise check.
