## Campaign Mechanics and Design Philosophy
### Campaign structure and persistence
What sets this expansion apart is how it treats progress. Each chapter feels like a bridge to the next, with decisions creating a sense of consequence that persists beyond a single night. You are rewarded for planning and cooperation, but not punished for a misstep so long as you adapt in the following chapter. The persistent track of influence and fear creates meaningful tension: the more influence you gain, the more likely you are to secure a favorable outcome, but fear can tilt the scale toward riskier plays that pay off in dramatic ways. It is a balancing act between security and story-driven risk, and that tension is where the drama lives.

### Card interactions and synergy
The new characters and event cards are designed to interact with the base game’s suite in smart ways. Some cards grant temporary bonuses that only matter in a given arc; others unlock new actions that alter tempo, enabling chain reactions when used in the right moment. The designers seem to have embraced the idea that campaign-era gloom should feel like a living system, not a series of one-off puzzles. You will discover combos that you wish you had found earlier in the campaign, and you will lament the ones you missed. The fun comes from exploring these interactions together and seeing how your table’s dynamic shifts as the arc progresses.

### Pacing and table dynamics
Campaign arcs work best when the group respects a rhythm: discuss, decide, execute, reflect. The boxes of doom (aka gloom tokens) are intentionally punishing, which makes every success feel earned. The campaign structure encourages quiet negotiation in the corners of the table as players hedge bets about which NPCs to recruit, which rumors to chase, and how to allocate resources before a critical encounter. This pacing is not about speed; it’s about narrative tension and shared suspense. If you enjoy the feeling of a serialized fantasy through a card game, you will find yourself leaning into the arcs and savoring the slow-burn drama rather than sprinting to victory.
