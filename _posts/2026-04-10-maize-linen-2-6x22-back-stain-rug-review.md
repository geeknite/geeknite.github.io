## Materials, Build, and Back Stain Technology

### Material Breakdown

### Back Stain rug meaning

Back Stain signals an approach to minimize staining from the rug’s underside when spills creep in from the bottom. In practical terms you’ll find it easier to spot-clean and wipe down, which is a major win in high-traffic areas.

## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

### Cleaning Guidelines

- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Do not rub; this pushes the stain deeper into the fibers.
- For general cleaning, vacuum regularly with a vacuum that has adjustable height and mindful of pile height to avoid pulling threads.
- For tougher stains, use a mild detergent solution and test in a small inconspicuous area before cleaning the whole rug.

### Does Back Stain mean more resistance?

In practice the surface will resist minor spills fairly well, but this is not a miracle rug. It is a practical one. It will not replace a spill-proof lifestyle, but it can reduce the time and effort needed to clean up.

### Maintenance Hacks

- Rotate every few months to even out patina and wear.
- Use a rug guard or low-friction pad on high-traffic runs.
- For pet owners, consider a pet stain remover and test on a hidden area before applying.
