## Assembly and maintenance
### Step-by-step assembly feel
- Unpack and organize: Put each piece in a designated spot, pair up bolts and screws, and keep the Allen key nearby like a tiny key to your outdoor paradise.
- Dry-fit before final assembly: Lightly connect pieces to ensure alignment; you don’t want to snap a bolt thread after the first turn.
- Tighten incrementally: Tighten gradually in a cross pattern to ensure even pressure and avoid warping the frame.
- Final checks: Inspect all joints for tightness and wobble; a little shake test goes a long way.
The process is straightforward but will require a bit of patience. The payoff is a sturdy, modular seating arrangement you’ll enjoy countless evenings with.

### Maintenance routine for longevity
- Wood care: Lightly sand any rough patches and apply a protective sealant every season or two, depending on exposure. This preserves the pine’s color and prevents moisture from creeping into the grain.
- Cushion care: Remove covers as needed and follow care labels for washing. For heavy rain, store cushions indoors or use breathable rain covers. Dry thoroughly before reassembling.
- Hardware care: Check screws and bolts for looseness, tighten as needed, and prevent rust by wiping down any damp metal components after use.
- Weather management: If you live somewhere with harsh winters or look at this set like a long-term investment, consider storing the cushions during winter and using a breathable outdoor cover for the frame.
