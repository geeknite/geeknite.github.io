## Cleaning, Care, and Everyday Maintenance
Cleanliness is a virtue, especially when your living space doubles as a tiny studio for the soul. The Shore Truffle is relatively forgiving. Here are some practical care tips, delivered in a tone that won’t judge you for leaving the last slice of pizza on the coffee table:

- Vacuum regularly with a standard residential vacuum. Use a beater bar on a low setting only if you’ve got a static issue or tiny crumbs; otherwise, a standard suction works fine.
- Clean spills ASAP. Blot—don’t rub—blot again. A mild detergent solution can help with stubborn spots; test in an inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear. This is the adult version of rotating the cast in your favorite long-running show.
- Use a rug pad for grip and extra cushioning. It helps reduce curling edges and increases longevity by absorbing the occasional heroic high-five from the resident dog.
- For deep-clean needs, consult professional cleaning. This is not a DIY “rip out the nap and call it a day” situation; keep the nap intact and your warranty happy by following manufacturer guidelines.
