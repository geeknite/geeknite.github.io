A quick wipe-down after use, a little oil on the blade, and you’re good. Avoid prolonged exposure to moisture, and store in the included sheath to prevent tarnish on the leather. For deeper maintenance, a light pass with mineral oil and a soft cloth will keep the blade and sheath in serviceable shape for many seasons.
