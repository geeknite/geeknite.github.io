## Creating Your Masterpiece

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Start with a Sketch**: Use a pencil to outline your idea. Don't worry, you can erase it later if it looks like a potato.
2. **Mix Your Colors**: Get creative! Mix colors on your palette until you find the perfect shade. Or just mix them all together and call it abstract.
3. **Layer Your Paints**: Start with the background and work your way to the details. Remember, art is all about layers ‚Äì like an onion, but less smelly.
4. **Add Details**: Use finer brushes for intricate details. Don't be afraid to make mistakes; happy accidents are part of the process.
5. **Let It Dry**: Resist the urge to touch your painting. Trust me, fingerprints are not a good look.
