## Durability, Care, and Longevity Tips

A couple of simple habits can extend the life of your two decks:
- Keep them away from moisture and extreme heat. A humid environment can cause warping and warpage of the card stock.
- Store them in their tuck boxes when not in use. A dedicated card case is a plus if you’re commuting or traveling with them.
- Wash hands before handling if you’re planning a performance. Oils and lotions can reduce friction and affect handling.
- Avoid using lotion or greasy fingers during performances; prefer dry hands for the smoothest shuffles.

If you treat these decks well, you’ll likely get a lot of use out of them before you consider replacements. The brand’s classic approach means you won’t be chasing after a marketing gimmick to keep your tricks going; you’ll rely on dependable stock and finish that age gracefully rather than flaking apart after a few good shuffles.
