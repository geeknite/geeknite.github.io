### How to play: a quick tour of the turn structure
The core loop is deceptively simple, which is a hallmark of good co-op design. Each round, players plan, reveal, and execute a set of actions that advance the shared objective. You’ll be juggling two or three key ideas at once: how to maximize your role’s unique power, how to support teammates who are low on resources, and how to anticipate the next card that could turn the tide (usually in the most inconvenient moment possible).

Turn structure typically follows a rhythm like this:
- Plan: players discuss a strategy, often assigning temporary duties or focus targets. You’ll use your role powers and any team-wide actions to prepare for a crucial moment.
