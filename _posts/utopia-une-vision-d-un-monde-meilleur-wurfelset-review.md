### The Basics

In Utopia, players work together to build a perfect society. Each player has unique abilities and uses dice to gather resources, resolve conflicts, and construct various utopian structures. The goal is to create the most harmonious and prosperous utopia.

### Components

The game comes with:

- **Cards**: Each player has a deck of cards representing their abilities and utopian structures.
