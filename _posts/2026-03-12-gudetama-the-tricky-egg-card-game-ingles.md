- The bluff factor: some cards enable you to pretend you have a stronger position than you actually do. The moment of truth arrives when your friends call your bluff or fall for it entirely, and the table erupts in laughter.
- Risk management: a central mechanic is the choice between a safe draw and a riskier one that could pay off with a perfect combination or leave you with a messy hand. The correct choice is frequently the one that makes the entire table groan with recognition of a missed opportunity.

The game rewards you for paying attention to what is visible in the hands of other players and what is likely in the discard pile.
