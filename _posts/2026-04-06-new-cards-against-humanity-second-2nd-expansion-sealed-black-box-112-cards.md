## Mechanics and How It Plays with the Base Game
Cards Against Humanity is famously simple: one player plays a black card with a prompt, the others submit white cards that best answer the prompt, and a judge selects the funniest response. The expansion does not reinvent the wheel; rather, it enriches the wheel with more spokes, more wheels within wheels, and more opportunities to watch your friends improvise under pressure.

### Compatibility and Setup
To use this expansion, you need the base Cards Against Humanity game. The expansion cards integrate seamlessly, using the same card sizes, print quality, and color palette.
