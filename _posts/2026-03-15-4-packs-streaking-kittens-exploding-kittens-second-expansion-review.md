### Card types and their vibes
The Streaking Kittens expansion introduces a handful of novelty cards that nudge the rhythm of the game without turning it into a chess match. Expect things like:
- Cat-advantage cards: These let you piggyback off someone else’s turn or steal a card in a way that’s both cheeky and strategically risky.
- “Streak” mechanics: A few cards trigger chains of actions, not unlike a party game version of a domino effect, but with more fur and fewer safety rails.
- Mischief items: Cards that force players to swap hands, discard key Defuse potentials, or swap deck order with a wink and a nudge.

The net effect is a slightly faster pace than pure base-game play, with an extra layer of table banter and a few, “Did that just happen?” moments. If you enjoy the last-minute save of a Defuse card by pure luck, you’ll appreciate how Streaking Kittens nudges you toward riskier plays and bigger swings.
