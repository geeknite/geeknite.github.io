## What is Tide of Iron Designers Series, Vol. 1?

**Tide of Iron Designers Series, Vol. 1** is a strategic board game that lets players recreate historical battles from World War II. It's designed to offer an immersive experience with detailed miniatures, maps, and scenarios. The game is known for its complexity and requires careful planning and execution to achieve victory.

### Components

The game includes:

- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures representing infantry, tanks, and other units.
- **Maps**: Large, beautifully illustrated maps that depict various battlefields.
- **Cards**: A deck of cards representing special actions, unit abilities, and events.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track resources, objectives, and damage during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the miniatures. Unless you want a historically inaccurate version of the Battle of the Bulge.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up **Tide of Iron Designers Series, Vol. 1** is like preparing for a military operation. Lay out the map, place the miniatures, and distribute cards and tokens. Choose your scenario, and you're ready to begin your tactical journey.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their units and strategies. You can move, attack, fortify positions, and use special abilities. Coordination and planning are key, so communicate with your teammates (or plot against them) to make the most of your resources.

### Combat

Combat in **Tide of Iron Designers Series, Vol. 1** is a mix of strategy and luck. Each unit has unique stats and abilities that influence the outcome of battles. Did you plan an ambush only to roll poorly? Welcome to the fog of war, where nothing goes as planned.
