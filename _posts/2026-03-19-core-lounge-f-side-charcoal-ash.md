## Practical Tips for Maintenance and Cleaning
- Regular vacuuming helps keep the textured Charcoal Ash fabric looking fresh and reduces the buildup of crumbs and pet hair.

- Rotate cushions every few months to ensure even wear and prevent sagging in specific spots.

- Check the modular connectors periodically. A quick tighten of the joints can keep everything aligned and feeling solid.
- For deep cleans, test a mild fabric cleaner on a hidden area first. You’re not trying to restore a vintage vinyl, but you’re not living in a lab either.
