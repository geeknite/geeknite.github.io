## Durability and Maintenance: The Tiny Doomsday Scenarios

One of the most common questions is how the mat handles wear and tear. The BT01 mat is built to last, but it is not invincible. Here are the typical life-cycle expectations and maintenance routines:

- Expect moderate wear after 6 months of weekly play, including minor corner curling if kept rolled up constantly. The key is to store it flat when not in use.
- Use a microfiber cloth to wipe away dust and oils from hands. A damp cloth is fine for more stubborn smudges. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the surface texture.
- If the mat gets creased from storage, lay it flat under a heavy book for 24-48 hours. It will gradually restore its flatness.
- For international travel tournaments, consider rolling the mat with a protective sleeve to prevent creases and keep it crisp in transit.

Maintenance, like any hobby, is a ritual. It’s not glamorous, but it’s the difference between a mat that ages gracefully and a mat that looks like it survived a dragon’s fiery breath.
