## How to Play and Core Mechanics

### Setup and Turn Structure

To set up, shuffle both decks and place the tokens within easy reach. In each round, a judge reveals a meme card, and the other players choose a caption card from their hand. The judge selects the best pairing, awards a token to the winner, and then a new round begins with the role of judge rotating. A timer adds a gentle sense of urgency that keeps things moving while still letting the table savor the humor. The game ends after a set number of rounds, or when you decide it is clearly time to stop while you are still ahead.

### The Core Mechanic in Simple Terms

The heart of the game is the collision of a caption with a Disney scenario. The humor lands when players manage to tie a witty caption to a familiar image in a way that feels both fresh and accessible. The joke should be quick enough to land before the moment passes, but clever enough to make the table groan and cheer in equal measure. This mechanic rewards sharp timing, cultural literacy, and a willingness to be a little ridiculous on cue.
