## H2: What are sleeves and why do they matter for Cerebria?
### H3: The basics of card sleeves

## H2: Size, fit, and the practicalities of Cerebria sleeves
### H3: Size matters (even if you don’t want to admit it)
Most Cerebria cards skew toward mid-size sleeves. The trick is to pick sleeves that fit snugly without bending corners. Overly loose sleeves encourage over-shuffling and warping, while sleeves that are too tight wear edges down faster and make flipping feel like surgery with a butter knife. If you run inner sleeves or top loaders, keep overall thickness in mind to preserve smooth shuffling.

### H3: Finishes: gloss, matte, and the unforgettable sheen
Gloss sleeves make art pop but can stick under bright lights. Matte finishes cut glare and feel nice in hand, though art may read a tad dull in certain rooms. For Cerebria, matte is a fantastic middle ground: legible, protective, and less glare when you’re squinting at victory conditions across a sunlit table.

### H3: Color and readability
Color-coded sleeves aren’t just for aesthetics; they’re utility. Assign a color per faction or per board phase to make navigation instant during complex turns. In larger tables or streams, color-coded sets help teammates identify which cards belong to whom, reducing mis-sends and accidental reveals.

## H2: Early game tips and long-term maintenance
### H3: First-time sleeve setup tips
- Pre-sleeve the core deck in a standard color you’ll always use; this becomes your home base and helps you recognize key cards during play.
- Consider labeling the back of the sleeve with a small marker for quick identification in a pinch (avoid marking the actual card).
- Keep spare sleeves for expansions or different playgroups; nothing derails a session like running out mid-game.

### H3: Maintenance and care
- Wipe sleeves with a dry microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints; avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade the sleeve material.
- Store sleeved decks flat or upright in a protective box to reduce curling; if you travel, use a padded case that fits sleeves and tokens.
- Rotate colors for ongoing campaigns; it offers a refreshing look and reduces color fatigue for regular players.
