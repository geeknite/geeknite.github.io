## How to integrate with the base game and other expansions
The beauty of CAH is its modular design. The 1st and 2nd expansions slot neatly into the base game without requiring a complete rewrite of how you play. You can mix and match these packs with any other expansion pack in your library. If you have a friend who swears by a specific expansion, you can still maintain a cohesive game flow by following standard CAH protocols: one black card prompt is drawn, players submit their best white card response, and the round ends with a judge selecting the winner. The expansions simply give you a broader palette to draw from when constructing responses.

As a general guideline, you can rotate unused cards back into the box and keep the active deck lean for faster rounds. If you are playing with a crowd that loves to test boundaries, you can opt for a shorter timer on the judging segment to keep things moving and to maximize the number of rounds you can squeeze into a single evening. The most important rule is simple: have fun, and adapt the testy prompts to the comfort level of your group.
