## Care and maintenance tips

- Avoid exposing chips and cards to moisture. Water and chalky chips is an ugly combination.
- Keep chips in a designated tray or bag to prevent them from walking away.
- Clean cards lightly with a dry microfiber cloth; avoid harsh cleaners.
- If you have dice, treat them gently; not all dice are equal, and you don't want a dice that misreads your numbers when you're bluffing.
