## Care and Maintenance
Care instructions (in plain, non-creative language):
- Vacuum regularly on a low setting to maintain pile integrity and avoid crushing the fibers.
- Spot-clean spills immediately with a mild detergent; blot, don’t rub.
- For deeper cleaning, machine wash on a gentle cycle with cold water, then air-dry flat. Do not tumble dry, as that can distort the pile and ruin the wave motif.
- Avoid strong solvents or bleach that could fade the colorway over time.

If you love the idea of a “set-and-forget” textile, you’ll appreciate that this rug plays nicely with the rest of your decor and is not a ceiling-level commitment in terms of maintenance.
