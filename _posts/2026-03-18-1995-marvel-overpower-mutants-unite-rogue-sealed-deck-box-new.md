## Storage, Care, and Longevity: Keeping the Magic Fresh

The best part about owning a sealed 1995 deck box is the confidence that you’re keeping the memory intact. Here are a few practical tips for longevity:
- Store in a climate-controlled space; avoid basements with humidity that could encourage mold or cardboard distress.
- Keep away from direct sunlight to preserve the box art and prevent color fading.
- If you ever open it, be mindful of card corners; mid-90s card stock can be sturdy but prone to tiny wear in the edges if not handled carefully.
- For display purposes, consider placing the box in a lockable display case or a high on a shelf where it can be admired without being touched constantly—let the memory breathe.
