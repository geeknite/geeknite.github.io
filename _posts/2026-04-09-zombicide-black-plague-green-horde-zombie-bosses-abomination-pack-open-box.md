## Open Box Thoughts, Tips, and House Rules
Open boxes are a chance to develop your own play style. Here are a few guidance notes:

- Keep a dedicated tray for boss cards and terrain tokens to avoid a scavenger hunt mid-game
- Use a simple “boss focus” rule for introductory nights: assign a single survivor to tag the boss and a second to support; this helps new players understand the flow
- Consider a two-session arc for the campaign to savor the dramatic moments of the Abomination encounter

For players who want to push through quicker, consider a house rule that trims down one or two of the boss’s unique abilities in the first game, enabling a smoother ramp for new players while maintaining the thematic feel.
