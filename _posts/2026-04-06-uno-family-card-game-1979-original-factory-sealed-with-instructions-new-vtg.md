Display, Care, and Preservation: Keeping a 1979 NOS Treasure in Prime Shape
---
If you’re fortunate enough to own one of these NOS gems, here are some practical tips to keep it alive for future generations of gamers:

- Climate control: Keep the box in a dry, cool space away from direct sunlight. Long-term exposure to heat and humidity can warp the cardboard and fade the inks.
- Handling: Handle the cards with clean hands to prevent oils from transferring onto the stock. A soft cloth for quick dusting helps.
- Display: A display case with UV-filtering glass can help preserve the colors without turning the box into a fragile relic.
- Storage: If you’re not playing with it, consider a breathable sleeve for the instruction sheet and a non-acidic storage box that matches its vintage vibe.
