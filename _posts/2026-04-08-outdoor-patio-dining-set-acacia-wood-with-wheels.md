### Weather resistance and maintenance

Acacia is relatively tolerant of moisture and sun, but like any good warrior, it benefits from a little maintenance. We recommend applying a light coat of mineral oil or outdoor-friendly sealant every season or after a heavy rain. The goal is to keep the wood’s oils replenished so it ages gracefully rather than drying out and cracking in a dramatic anime moment. Wipe spills promptly to prevent staining; use a damp cloth and mild soap for routine cleaning. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that will strip the natural oils faster than a rogue patch update on a beta app. If you live near salt air, consider additional protection or movement of the set indoors during the peak salt spray days. The chairs with wheels don’t require separate care, but you should check wheel locks for dirt buildup to ensure the wheels don’t slip when you stand up to fetch another beer.
