## Maintenance and care
Like any piece of gym gear that lives in your house, a foosball table needs occasional TLC. Here’s the Geeknite-approved maintenance routine:
- Clean surfaces weekly with a damp microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals on the playing surface as they can degrade the laminate.
- Lubricate rods every few months with a light silicone-based spray. This keeps the action smooth and avoids the dreaded squeak-squeak from the bearings.
- Check the screws and bolts monthly. A loose bolt can turn a casual match into a wobble-fest and then into a DIY stress test, which is not what you want when you’re trying to win a championship in your living room.
- Protect the table from humidity. MDF loves to absorb moisture, which can cause warping over time. If you’ve got a humid climate, consider a dehumidifier or at least keeping the area ventilated.
- Clean the scoring units occasionally. A buildup of tiny chalk marks and coffee dust can make the scoreboard less legible during a tense match. A mild cloth wipe does wonders.
