Deducing your fortune requires a bit of care. The dice are resin-based, which means you should avoid harsh solvents and long sunlit marathons on a windowsill. If you notice micro-scratches after heavy use, a gentle polish with a dedicated resin cleaner can help restore the gleam. The goal is to keep the dice looking as sharp as the fog between streetlamps, not as battered as a stakeout notebook after a long night in the field.
