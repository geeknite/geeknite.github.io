In the air, the overhead bin is a wild arena where snacks and headphone cables roam free. The hard shell held its ground, and the hinge action survived a thousand impatient flaps from fellow travelers. The zip is sturdy enough to cycle through a few thumbs and a couple of TSA inspections without giving up.
