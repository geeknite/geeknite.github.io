## Care and Maintenance: Keeping It Fresh Without Turning It Into a Full‑Time Job

- Vacuum regularly: A weekly pass keeps dust at bay. Use a vacuum with adjustable height so you don’t flatten the pile in one corner and leave a quasi‑Mario‑level trench in another.
- Spills: Wipe immediately with a clean, damp cloth. Blotting is your friend; rubbing invites fiber wear and color fatigue. For tougher stains, a mild detergent solution works wonders; always test on a hidden spot first.
- Odors: If you’re worried about lingering scents from cooking or pets, air it out for a day or two and consider light baking soda deodorizers before vacuuming again.
- Pets: A quick routine grooming session helps. If you have a pet with an excess of shedding, a handheld vacuum can be a great companion to keep things tidy between deep cleans.
