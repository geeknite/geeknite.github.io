## Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping Your Table in Top Form
Like any hobby gear, a little maintenance goes a long way. Here are practical tips to keep the Sunnydaze table performing at peak level for years:

- Clean the playing surface regularly with a soft, dry cloth to remove dust and finger smudges. Avoid harsh chemicals that might degrade the finish.
- Wipe down rods after heavy sessions to minimize moisture and oxidation. A light lubrication with a silicone-based spray on the rod joints helps ensure smooth movement, especially during long tournaments.
- Inspect the rod ends for burrs or rough spots. If you feel any snagging, gently sand the rough edges or replace the worn bushings if your model offers them.
- Level the table occasionally. Even a 2–3 degree tilt can affect ball stability and lead to frustrating matches. A simple bubble level placed on the playing field can save your sanity.
- Store spare balls in a pouch away from direct sunlight. UV light can degrade the surface texture, increasing unpredictable bounces over time.

For households with kids or heavy gaming sessions, you’ll want to revisit the screws and brackets every few weeks. A loose bracket is the silent assassin of foosball fun; you’ll notice wobble and misalignment long before you realize what happened to your perfect shot lineup.
