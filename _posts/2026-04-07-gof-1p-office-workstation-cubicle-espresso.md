## Maintenance and Care

Espresso finishes are relatively forgiving, but a quick wipe-down once a week keeps the surface looking fresh. Use a microfiber cloth with a mild cleaner to prevent streaks and keep the wood looking even. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the finish over time. For the metal components, a light dusting supplemented with a tack-free wipe will prevent the accumulation of grime from cups and snacks. If you spill something sticky, clean it promptly to prevent residue from building up in the grooves of the cable channels. Your desk will thank you with fewer squeaks and more productivity—probably.
