### Care, Maintenance, and Longevity: Keeping Your Tiny Arena Ready
- Keep it dry and away from direct sunlight to preserve the wood finish. Prolonged exposure can cause warping and color fading.
- Wipe with a soft, dry or slightly damp cloth; avoid harsh cleaners that can strip the finish or leave residues that affect grip.
- Lubricate the rods sparingly if they start to feel stiff; a tiny dab on the joints keeps them moving smoothly without gunking up the surface.
- Store in a dry place when not in use; if you can, keep it in its original box or a dedicated shelf to prevent scuffs and accidental tipping.
- Periodically inspect the ball and replace if it shows signs of cracking or excessive wear. A fresh ball improves accuracy and speed consistency on the field.
