## Cleaning and Maintenance

Stains happen. It is part of the human operating system. The Cambridge Contemporary Grey Rug offers a forgiving surface that handles typical spills with the right approach. The care routine is straightforward:

- Vacuum weekly or more often in high traffic zones to prevent grit from breaking down the fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing which can push stains deeper.
- For tougher spots, a mild detergent solution used sparingly can help; test in an inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to prevent one side from looking more lived in than the other.
- Use a quality rug pad under hardwood or tile floors to maintain grip and protect flooring from scratches.

Pro tip from the Geeknite lab: a quick wipe with a damp microfiber cloth after a coffee spill will save you a late night scrub session and preserve the color story for longer. If you have pets, the fibers are easy to clean and resistant to typical pet mishaps, which translates to less drama and more game time.
