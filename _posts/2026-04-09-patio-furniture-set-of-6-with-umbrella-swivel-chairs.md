## Maintenance and care: keeping the glow without becoming a full-time custodian
Maintenance is the boring but essential part of the romance of outdoor furniture. Here are practical tips:
- Cushion care: remove cushion covers when possible and machine wash on a gentle cycle. If covers aren’t removable, treat stains promptly with a mild cleaner. Let everything dry completely before putting cushions back on to avoid moldy vibes.
- Frame care: wipe aluminum frames with a soft cloth and mild soap. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the finish. If you notice a scratch in the resin weave, don’t panic; it’s cosmetic and can be hidden with a decorative throw pillow or two.
- Umbrella care: close the umbrella during storms or when you’re not actively using the shade. A strong wind can invert the canopy or bend the frame. Lubricate the crank occasionally and check for any loosened bolts.

Seasonal maintenance may include storing cushions indoors or covering the set during rain or winter. A good cover adds years to your investment and saves you from the romance of a moldy cushion discovering an alternate universe on your porch.
