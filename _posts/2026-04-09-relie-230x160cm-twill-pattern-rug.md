### Cleaning and maintenance
Maintenance matters in a world where coffee, tea, or the occasional GPU coolant spill occur. The Relie rug is designed to be relatively low‑maintenance compared with shag or high‑pile options. A quick vacuum on a regular basis helps maintain the look, while occasional spot cleaning with a mild solution can handle most common spills. It is worth noting that twill patterns can show lint more readily than solid flatwovens, so you might want to run a microfiber duster across the surface from time to time to keep it looking crisp. For tougher spots, a gentle approach using a damp cloth and a touch of mild detergent typically handles most incidents without requiring a full rug surgery.
