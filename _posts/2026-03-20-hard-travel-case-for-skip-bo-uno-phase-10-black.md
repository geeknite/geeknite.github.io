## Maintenance and Longevity
A tool of travel longevity is how you treat it between adventures. Here are some practical tips to extend the life of your hard travel case:

- Clean the exterior with a damp cloth; avoid abrasive cleaners that might dull the matte finish. A quick wipe-down after a smoky convention hall session goes a long way.

- When not in use, store the case upright on a shelf to prevent the interior from warping. It’s not a heavy marinara noodle dish, but it appreciates a proper resting place.
