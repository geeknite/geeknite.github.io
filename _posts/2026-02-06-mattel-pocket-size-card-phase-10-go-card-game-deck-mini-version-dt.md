Players must complete phases by forming specific sets and runs of cards. The first player to finish all 10 phases wins the game. Simple, right? Well, not quite.

### Phase Breakdown

Each phase presents a unique challenge, from collecting sets of numbers to creating runs of consecutive cards. The game keeps you on your toes, constantly adapting your strategy to outwit your opponents. Watch out for those sneaky phase-skippers!
