### Cleaning and maintenance
Let’s keep it practical: vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to maintain the weave’s neat appearance. For spills, blot (don’t rub) with a clean cloth, then treat with a mild detergent and water. Stubborn stains? A light steam clean every few months can restore a crisp look without harming the fibers. The rug dries fairly quickly, which is a blessing if your life involves coffee splashes, snack dips, and the occasional dramatic late-night snack-run in slippers.

## Size, space, and placement tips
The 3' x 5' footprint is surprisingly versatile. It’s perfect for entryways, under a coffee table, or as a visual anchor in a small to mid-sized living room. In larger spaces, it can function as a stepping-stone between zones, a subtle signal that “this is a seating area” without visually overpowering the room.

### Placement guidelines
- Entryways: creates a memorable first impression that’s both stylish and practical for daily use.
- Under a coffee table: leave at least 6–8 inches of rug beyond the table legs to establish a defined area and to avoid a “table on rug” look that feels cramped.
- Sofa-based layouts: ensure the front legs rest on the rug to unify the space and prevent the room from feeling disjointed.

### Layering with other textures
Layering can add depth and warmth. If you’re bold, place this flat weave over a larger, warmer-toned rug to create a chic, multi-textured ground while preserving a clean base. Layering is the mas­tered trick for geeks who appreciate structure but still want a room that’s comfortable and inviting.
