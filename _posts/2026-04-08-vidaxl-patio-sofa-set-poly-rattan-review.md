What is Poly Rattan and Why It Matters
Poly rattan is synthetic wicker material engineered to mimic the look of natural rattan while resisting UV damage, moisture, and temperature swings. In geek terms, it is the armor plating of outdoor furniture. Natural rattan can crack, fade, or warp when exposed to sun and rain; poly rattan uses resin coated fibers woven over a lightweight frame, usually aluminum, so the set remains flexible, colorful, and shrugging off the elements.
Poly rattan advantages
- Weather resistance: UV stabilized fibers resist fading
- Low maintenance: a quick wipe down beats a full furniture restoration
- Light but sturdy: aluminum frames keep things robust without making you drag a forklift around
- Modern aesthetics: sleek lines with a weatherproof finish
