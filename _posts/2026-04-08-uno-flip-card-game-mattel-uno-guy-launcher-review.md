## Variants, house rules, and clever tweaks
House rules are the lifeblood of any UNO night. Here are a few you might try to get additional mileage from UNO Flip with the launcher:
- Flip Frenzy: whenever a flip occurs, all players must swap hands with the person on their left. It wrecks plans, amuses spectators, and creates chaos in a good way.
- Launcher Jackpot: set a round limit and assign a special token that, when launched, earns a temporary immunity from Draw cards for one turn. No more excuses about unlucky draws—now there’s a tangible buffer, which is exactly what players secretly crave.
