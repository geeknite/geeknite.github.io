## Cleaning, Care, and Longevity

### Routine Cleaning

The best daily care for Parker Cream is straightforward: a regular vacuum to remove dust and grit, with a low-to-mid suction setting to avoid pulling fibers. If you have a vacuum with a beater bar, consider turning it off for high-pile sections to prevent fuzzing. For occasional refresh, a quick shake-out or beat-out (if you have a covered outdoor space) can help.

### Stain Handling

Stains happen. The key is speed and the right technique. Blot (don’t rub) spills with a clean, white cloth. If a stain lingers, a pH-balanced carpet cleaner applied to a small, inconspicuous area can help—test first in a hidden corner to ensure colorfastness. Avoid harsh solvents and make sure you always blot toward the center to prevent ring formation.

### Longevity Expectations

With regular maintenance, the Parker Cream holds up well for a mid-to-high-traffic living room. The cream color will show wear more than a deeply saturated rug, but the subtle texture helps camouflage minor wear. If your room experiences heavy footfall in the evening or has a nightly movie marathon, you’ll notice slight flattening in the highest-traffic lines. The upside is that, in most lighting conditions, the wear reads as character rather than a stain on your taste.
