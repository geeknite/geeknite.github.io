## What is a UPS and why should you care?
- UPS stands for Uninterruptible Power Supply. It isn’t just a fancy battery; it’s a little architect of calm that bridges the gap between the blackout and the moment you remember to save your work and shut down gracefully.
- Use cases: keep your desktop PC from turning into a blue-screen sculpture, protect your router so you can finish that game round or a remote work task, and give your NAS a mercy break when the lights go sideways.
