## What is Struggle of Empires?

Struggle of Empires is a strategic board game designed by Martin Wallace and published by Eagle-Gryphon Games. Set in the 18th century, players take on the roles of competing European powers, seeking to expand their empires through war, diplomacy, and economic prowess.

## Components

The game comes with a beautifully illustrated board featuring various regions, including Europe, Africa, and the Americas. The components include miniatures representing armies and fleets, tokens for resources, and a deck of event cards that introduce new challenges and opportunities. The artwork is top-notch, and you'll find yourself marveling at the historical details.

**Fun Fact**: The game includes a special "Plague" token that will have you screaming "NOT THE PLAGUE!" every time it appears.

## Gameplay

Struggle of Empires is all about strategy, alliances, and the occasional betrayal. Players must work together—and sometimes against each other—to expand their empires. Each player takes on the role of a European power, with unique abilities and strengths.

### Phases of Play

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Alliance Phase**: Players form alliances with other powers, negotiating terms and planning strategies.
2. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions like moving armies, attacking rivals, and building infrastructure.
3. **Event Phase**: Draw event cards that introduce new challenges or opportunities.

### Winning and Losing

To win, players must accumulate victory points by conquering territories, building trade routes, and forging alliances. If your empire collapses due to war or economic failure, it's game over. Sorry, Napoleon.

**Pro Tip**: Always keep an eye on your allies—they might be planning to stab you in the back!
