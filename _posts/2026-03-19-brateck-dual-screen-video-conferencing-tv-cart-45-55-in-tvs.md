## Setup and installation experience: A practical guide
If you’re a proficient IKEA pirate who thrives on the box opening adrenaline, you’ll enjoy the packaging and instructions: straightforward diagrams, a handful of screws, and a few tools you’ll recognize from other furniture assemblies. The assembly is typically doable in under an hour with all parts present. Here are a few practical tips to smooth the process:

- Unbox everything and lay out the hardware by size. It reduces the risk of mixing screws that look similar but do very different things.
- Pre-insert bolts into the frame where possible. It speeds up the main assembly and minimizes the “where the heck does this go?” moment.
- Check weight distribution before you mount the screens. A quick dry run with no power helps you confirm the cart’s balance without the screens weighing you down emotionally and physically.
- Use a helper if you’re mounting two large TVs. Two hands are better than one when you’re lifting a 55-inch panel that could pass as a small TV itself.
