The core idea behind the Multi-language Pack is to provide translations and regional phrasing so players who aren’t fluent in the base language can still grok the card text without needing a translator sitting at the table the whole night.

What you get (in general terms) often includes:
- New hero cards that can be used with the existing core box and expansions.
- A set of item and zombie encounter cards that incorporate language variants.
- A few language-specific reference cards to help non-English readers parse effects at the crucial moment.

In practice, this means your table will have a few new verbs to shout when you need to describe what your heroes are actually doing.
