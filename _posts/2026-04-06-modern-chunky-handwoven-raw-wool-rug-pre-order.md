## Care and maintenance
Wool is naturally self-cleaning to a degree, but not invincible. Routine vacuuming with a low-suction setting helps lift surface fibers. Spills should be blotted rather than rubbed, and a gentle wool-safe cleaner can be used for stubborn spots. Pet accidents are easier to manage on a chunky rug because the thick loops absorb liquid rather than letting it pool. If you treat it kindly, you can expect decades of use with only occasional maintenance. We recommend rotating the rug to avoid wear patterns in high traffic zones.
