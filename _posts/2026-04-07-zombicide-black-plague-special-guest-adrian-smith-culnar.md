## Gameplay and Balance with Special Guest
This is where the editorial heart of the review starts beating faster. The Cul'Nar hero's abilities bring a mix of close-quarters prowess and necromantic support. You’ll be balancing a straightforward melee approach with the occasional dark surge that lets you convert a zombie into a hazard that you can exploit or chain-react into your advantage. The dynamic is reminiscent of a well-timed guitar solo: you want to push the tempo, but you don’t want to overdo it and crash the stage.

From a balance standpoint, the addition of Cul'Nar doesn’t tilt the table in a way that makes other survivors obsolete. If anything, Cul'Nar demands that you rethink positioning and synergy. The aura mechanic creates opportunities for teammates to coordinate their actions with surgical precision: you can trigger an extra action for a neighboring survivor after Cul'Nar slays a zombie, enabling a domino effect of tactical plays. The potential downside—the possibility of spawning more zombies when you go for the big play—feels fair and thematically consistent. It’s not a win button so much as a creative problem you must solve in real time.

### Tactical tips with Cul'Nar
- Begin with Cul'Nar near a corridor, where you can maximize the aura’s reach and deny the zombies a straight shot to your mage-lab of a hideout.
