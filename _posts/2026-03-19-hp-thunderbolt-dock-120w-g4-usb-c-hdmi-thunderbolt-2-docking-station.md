## Real-world use cases and tips
Real-world usage rarely matches the neat schematics in your head. Here are some tips and scenarios from actual use:

- The two-monitor powerhouse: When running two 4K displays, ensure your laptop’s GPU can comfortably drive both. The HP dock handles it, but you might need to tweak the scaling and refresh settings to keep things snappy.
- The content creator corner: If you are editing video or running heavy apps, assign performance-critical devices to dedicated USB ports rather than relying on shared buses. A fast NVMe SSD on a USB-C port can accelerate file transfers dramatically.
- The meeting maestro: If you have a high-quality USB-C webcam and a separate mic array, connect them to the dock and use the laptop’s built-in mics only for backup. The goal is a clean audio feed with minimal latency.
- Cable discipline: Use the upstream TB-C cable as the “one cable to rule them all” and keep the rest tidy with cable sleeves. A tidy desk is a productivity accelerator disguised as organization.
