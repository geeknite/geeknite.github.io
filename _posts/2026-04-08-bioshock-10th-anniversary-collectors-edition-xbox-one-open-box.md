## Display and care guidance for open-box collectors
Display considerations are crucial for the longevity of the physical components:
- Keep the art book and lithographs protected from humidity and direct sunlight to prevent color fading.
- Store posters in protective sleeves or frames with UV-resistant glass to guard against yellowing.
- Handle CDs and discs with clean hands to avoid fingerprints that could affect readability.
- Use acid-free mats or corner protectors on shelves to mitigate edge wear on the box art.
- Confirm the exact item list before purchase, especially if you care about any limited numbers or variant inserts that may have different print runs.

This kind of care ensures your open-box edition remains a hyphen between a memory and a display piece rather than a cautionary tale about carelessness and packaging.
