## Maintenance and care
A maintenance routine can extend life and ensure safe operation. Regularly inspect the frame, springs, and protective padding for signs of wear. Clean the mat and enclosure with mild soap and water, avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade UV treatments. Lubricate moving parts where appropriate and replace any worn components promptly. If you hear unusual creaks or notice wobble during use, pause and inspect the joints. It is not fun to have a failed frame mid bounce test. For weather protection, use a trampoline cover during off-seasons to reduce UV exposure and keep leaves from getting tangled in the netting.
