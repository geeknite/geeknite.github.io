## Care and Maintenance

Wool rugs require a gentle approach. Vacuum regularly with a suction head that is not too aggressive to avoid pulling fibers. Use a low setting for maximum life; you do not want to treat the rug like a lawn mower. For stains, blot the area gently with a mild detergent solution or a wool specific cleaner. Do not rub, as this will push stains deeper into the fiber. If a spill occurs, act quickly to minimize absorption.

### Cleaning tips

- Vacuum frequently, but rotate your vacuum head to avoid wearing one area faster than another.
- Treat spills promptly with a blotting motion; avoid aggressive scrubbing.
- For deep cleaning, consider professional rug cleaning services if you want to preserve color and fiber integrity.

### Care for color and fibers

Wool has natural stain resistance but is not stain proof. It is advisable to address spills within a short time frame to minimize staining. If sunlight over time bleaches color, consider rotating the rug or using drapes to protect it from direct sun.
