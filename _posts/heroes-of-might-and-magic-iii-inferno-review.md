## The Basics

### What is Heroes of Might and Magic III: Inferno?

Heroes of Might and Magic III: Inferno is an expansion pack for the beloved Heroes of Might and Magic III. It introduces the Inferno faction, a group of fiery, demonic creatures who are ready to wreak havoc on the battlefield. With new units, spells, and maps, this expansion adds a fresh layer of strategy and chaos to the game.

### Components

The Inferno expansion includes:
- **New Units**: From imps to devils, the Inferno faction brings a host of fiery creatures to the game.
- **New Heroes**: Lead your infernal army with new heroes who have unique abilities and skills.
- **New Spells**: Unleash powerful spells that can turn the tide of battle in your favor.
- **New Maps**: Explore new terrains and conquer new territories.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Heroes of Might and Magic III: Inferno is like preparing for a barbecue in Hell. You'll need to arrange your units, choose your heroes, and prepare for a fiery battle. The expansion integrates seamlessly with the base game, so you'll be up and running in no time.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players move their heroes, build their towns, and engage in combat. The Inferno faction brings new strategies to the table, with units that have unique abilities and spells that can devastate your enemies.
