## Maintenance Habits: Keeping it Fresh

Maintenance is not glamorous, but it is essential for longevity. Here are some practical routines you can adopt:

- Periodically wipe the wicker with a damp cloth to remove dust and pollen. Use a mild soap solution if necessary, then rinse and air dry.
- Inspect cushions for wear and tear; if you notice loose stitching or fading, address it early to prevent more significant problems.
- Rotate cushions seasonally to ensure even wear and color distribution.
- Use a protective cover during heavy rain spells or winter months to extend cushion life and reduce the risk of mildew.
- Store bistro items and cushions separately if you have a storage space to reduce humidity exposure and keep the fabric fresh.
