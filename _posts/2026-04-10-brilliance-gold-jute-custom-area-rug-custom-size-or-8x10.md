## Performance and durability
Natural fiber rugs carry an aura of eco-conscious chic. They age gracefully if treated with a light touch. Brilliance Gold Jute is no exception, but as with all jute-based rugs, there are caveats.

### Durability notes
Jute fibers are strong but not indestructible. They handle foot traffic in living rooms and offices but may not be ideal for households with heavy pet claws or high moisture exposure. The Brilliance Gold finish is designed to resist fading from indirect sunlight and mild cleaning solutions, but it’s not a miracle worker. If your space has a constant risk of spills from beverages like red wine or coffee, you’ll want to act fast with blotting rather than rubbing.

### Stain resistance and cleaning
- Vacuum regularly with a vacuum that has a brushless suction to avoid debonding the fibers
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth
- For tougher stains, a gentle solution of water and mild detergent can be used; test on a small area first
- Avoid soaking the rug in water and never use hot water on natural fiber rugs

### Comfort and feel
Underfoot, jute provides a robust but comfortable texture. It’s not fluffy; it’s more like a smart, grown-up textile that says you take your decor seriously but still like to stand up from your chair with a bit of swagger. Paired with a quality rug pad, the feel improves and the rug stays in place.

## Maintenance and cleaning
Maintenance is a big deal with jute. If you’re looking for something that only needs a weekly pass with a vacuum and never a careful washing, this rug might not be your soulmate. On the other hand, if you’re happy with a straightforward routine, Brilliance Gold Jute is manageable and crisp.

- Vacuum weekly; use a low suction setting
- Rotate every few months to promote even wear
- Clean spills immediately with blotting techniques
- Use a rug pad to reduce movement and prevent fraying at the edges
- For deeper cleaning, consider professional cleaning, especially if your space has a lot of sun exposure or humidity

## Edge finishing and fraying
One of the genuine concerns with natural fiber rugs is edge fraying over time. Brilliance Gold Jute features reinforced edging designed to minimize unraveling and keep edges crisp even after years of furniture shifts. If you notice slight fraying, you can trim away loose fibers with clean scissors to maintain a sharp look. Avoid rough detergents near the edges, which can weaken fibers.

## Colorfastness and dye testing
Gold tones on natural fibers can react to strong sunlight or certain cleaning solutions. The Brilliance Gold finish is designed to be colorfast in typical home lighting conditions, but you should still avoid direct, intense sun for extended periods and test cleaning solutions in an inconspicuous area. Rotating the rug helps even out color exposure and reduces uneven fading that sometimes happens with long-term sunlight.
