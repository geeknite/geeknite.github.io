## What is Skip-Bo again? A quick refresher
Skip-Bo is essentially a card-driven puzzle with a social layer. Each deck contains numbered cards from 1 to 12 and Skip-Bo wild cards that can stand in for any number. The core goal is to be the first player to play all cards from your personal stock pile by playing them in ascending order onto central piles. The mechanic rewards strategic hand management, memory, and a bit of luck—because sometimes the wilds behave like benevolent deities and other times like chaotic gremlins. The game supports a handful of players, depending on the rules you choose, and can be whipped out in the time it takes to heat water for tea or assemble a shelf with questionable carpentry skills.
