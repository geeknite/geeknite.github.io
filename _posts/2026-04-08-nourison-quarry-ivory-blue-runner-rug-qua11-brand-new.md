## Cleaning and Care Guide: Keeping the Look Without the Drama
### Routine Care: A Simple Routine for Real Life
Vacuum weekly on a gentle setting to keep the pile intact and the pattern discernible. Regular maintenance prevents dirt buildup from creeping into fibers and ensures a longer-lived aesthetic. Avoid aggressive scrubbing—it can distort the pile and flatten the texture you paid for.

### Stain Removal Steps: A Gentle Yet Effective Playbook
Blot first (don’t rub), then apply a mild cleaning solution if needed. Always test the cleaner on a small, inconspicuous area to verify color fidelity before treating a larger visible area. For more stubborn spills, a dedicated rug cleaner compatible with synthetic fibers can help, but avoid bleach, which can compromise color integrity.

### Special Care Notes: What Not to Do
Avoid steam cleaning unless explicitly recommended by the manufacturer, as high heat can distort fibers. Stick to water-based cleaning solutions and keep moisture away from the backing to prevent curling or mildew growth. If you live in a sunny space, place UV-blocking curtains on the windows to reduce color shift over long periods.
