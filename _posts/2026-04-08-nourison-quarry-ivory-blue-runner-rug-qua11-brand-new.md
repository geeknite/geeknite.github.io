## Cleaning and Care Guide

### Routine care
Vacuum the rug weekly with a gentle setting. Avoid aggressive scrubbing; this helps preserve the pile height and pattern.

### Stain removal steps
Blotting first, then mild cleaner if necessary. Always test the cleaner on a small inconspicuous area first to ensure there is no color transfer.

### Special care notes
Avoid steam cleaning unless recommended by the manufacturer, as high heat can cause fiber distortion. Use water-based cleaning solutions only, and avoid bleach at all costs.
