### Maintenance and cleaning tips

Cleaning is where synthetic rugs often shine. You’ll find that vacuuming is straightforward, with little risk of threadbare wear in high-traffic areas. Most spills can be blotted up with a clean cloth and warm water, followed by a gentle detergent if needed. For tougher stains, consider an enzyme-based cleaner or a color-safe stain remover. Avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the fibers or the printed design. A quick note for pet owners: the rug should be resistant to pet hair clinging, though nothing is perfect; a quick vacuum or lint roller should keep things tidy between deep cleans.
