## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity Tips

Your sleeves are not immortal. Here are a few practical tips to maximize your Cross Worlds sleeves’ lifespan:

- Keep them dry: moisture is the enemy of any paper-based game gear; store and play in dry environments. If you spill a drink, wipe gently with a dry microfiber cloth and let air-dry.
- Avoid heat: direct sunlight for long periods can cause color fading; if you Instagram your battles, rotate sleeves occasionally to keep the colorways vibrant.
- Separate foil and non-foil: if you routinely separate foils from non-foils, consider naming or color-coding your inner and outer sleeve layers.
- Clean gently: surface dust can accumulate on sleeves; a light brushing or a quick wipe with a dry cloth should do. Avoid solvents or abrasive cleaners; they’ll ruin that matte sheen.
