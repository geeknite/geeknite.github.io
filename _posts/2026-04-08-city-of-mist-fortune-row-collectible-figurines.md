## Care, Maintenance, and Customization: keeping the glow intact

To keep Fortune Row champions looking as sharp as a neon sign on a rainy night, follow a few simple tips:
- Dust regularly with a soft brush; avoid microfiber cloths that shed into crevices.
- Use a coat of spray varnish (matte or satin) to protect against tabletop chaos.
- If you’re customizing, apply thin layers of paint and allow each layer to dry fully before the next; this reduces chipping when you handle figures during play.
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to preserve color fidelity and prevent yellowing.
