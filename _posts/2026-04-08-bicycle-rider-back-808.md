## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity
A sealed deck, by definition, asks for minimal maintenance before you crack it open. Once opened, your care routine becomes a little more involved if you want to maximize longevity:

- Avoid exposing the deck to extreme heat or moisture. Even a small amount of humidity can affect the finish and feel over months and years.
- Store the deck away from direct sunlight, which can cause color fading or yellowing of the tuck over time.
- If you’re a frequent player, consider a breathable storage box that keeps dust and humidity at bay while you enjoy casual games and showmanship.
- For collectors, keep the deck in its protective presentation, and consider desiccant packs in your display cabinet to minimize moisture risk.
