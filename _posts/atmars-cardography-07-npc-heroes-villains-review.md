## What is Atmar's Cardography?

For those new to the world of Atmar, the Cardography series is a collection of dungeon crawl modules that double as a deck of cards. Each card represents a different section of the dungeon, creating a unique and modular experience every time you play. Atmar's Cardography 07 focuses specifically on **NPC Heroes & Villains**—a treasure trove of characters to enrich your campaigns.

## The Contents

### Heroes

The heroes in this deck are not your run-of-the-mill adventurers. These NPCs come with rich backstories, unique abilities, and enough personality quirks to keep your players guessing. Here are some notable mentions:

- **Thalor the Brave**: A knight with a penchant for dramatic entrances and an unfortunate allergy to dragon scales. Don't ask.
- **Lyra the Swift**: A rogue with nimble fingers and an insatiable curiosity. She once stole the heart of a prince—literally.

### Villains

No campaign is complete without a solid lineup of villains. Atmar's Cardography 07 does not disappoint:

- **Grimfang the Merciless**: An orc warlord with an axe to grind (literally and figuratively). Known for his love of bad puns and worse hygiene.
- **Elara the Enchantress**: A sorceress with a flair for the dramatic and a love for complicated schemes. Think soap opera, but with more fireballs.

### Additional Features

Apart from the heroes and villains, the deck includes various items, spells, and plot hooks. Imagine stumbling upon a cursed goblet that makes you speak in rhymes or a scroll that summons a swarm of angry platypuses. The possibilities are endless and endlessly entertaining.

## How to Use the Deck

### For Dungeon Masters

Dungeon Masters can use the deck to quickly populate their world with memorable characters. Whether you need a last-minute villain or a hero to save the day, simply draw a card and let the adventure unfold. The cards are also perfect for improvisation—ideal for those moments when your players decide to go off-script and head straight for the tavern.

### For Players

Players can use the deck to create rich backstories for their characters. Draw a card to determine a mentor, a nemesis, or even a long-lost sibling. The deck adds depth and complexity to character creation, making your role-playing experience even more immersive.
