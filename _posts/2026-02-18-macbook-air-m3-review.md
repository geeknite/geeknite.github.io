## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by M3 Chip

![M3 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/m3__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}m3-chip)

The MacBook Air M3 is powered by the revolutionary M3 chip. With its 8-core CPU, 10-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine, this laptop can handle everything from heavy-duty video editing to running multiple virtual machines without breaking a sweat.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 18 hours of battery life, and in our tests, it didn't disappoint. Whether you're binge-watching Netflix or writing your next novel, the MacBook Air M3 will keep you going longer than your last relationship.

## Keyboard and Trackpad

The Magic Keyboard is back and as magical as ever. Typing on it feels like a dream, and the large Force Touch trackpad is super responsive. It's like having a magic wand that makes your cursor dance across the screen.

## Software and Features

![macOS Big Sur](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/macos__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}macos-big-sur)

### macOS Big Sur

The MacBook Air M3 comes with macOS Big Sur, packed with features like Universal Control and a redesigned Safari. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes this laptop stand out from the competition.

### FaceTime and Webcam

The 1080p FaceTime HD camera ensures you look your best on video calls, whether you're chatting with friends or attending virtual meetings. Coupled with the studio-quality three-mic array, you'll sound as good as you look.
