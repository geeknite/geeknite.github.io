## How It Plays: Quick Start Guide

Turn order is simple: draw, play, end your turn. Cards do everything from skipping turns to swapping hands, to forcing someone else to draw extra kittens. The goal is to avoid drawing an exploding kitten card, which ends the round with spectacular chaos and some good natured shouts from the group.

### Turn structure
- Draw one card, then you may play as many cards as your hand allows that do not cause your own catastrophic demise
- You can chain effects with combo cards in ways that feel like a chaotic dance that occasionally ends with one confused survivor and a room full of giggles
- When the exploding kitten card hits the table, the round ends and someone is eliminated from the current cycle; the last player standing reaps the glory until the next round starts

### Card types
- Attack, See the Future, and Exploding Kitten type cards that drive the pacing of the game
- Expansion cards that add new effects, from stealing extra cards to forcing group draws, to special one time effects that can swing a turn into chaos
