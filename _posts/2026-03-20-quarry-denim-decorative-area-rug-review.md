## Cleaning and Maintenance

### Spills and Stains
Quarry Denim is not a miracle stain shield, but it is forgiving. Blot spills promptly with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push the spill deeper and cause minor fibrillation at the surface. For tougher stains, a mild detergent with cold water works best. Avoid bleach and harsh solvents that could disrupt the denim-like finish.

### Vacuuming and Care
Use a vacuum with a low-suction setting or a standard vacuum without a rotating beater bar to maintain the pile height and texture. Regular vacuuming removes grit that can dull the texture over time. For households with pets, a quick daily sweep of hair and dander helps keep the denim look pristine rather than a magnet for fur humors. A rug pad underneath improves longevity and reduces wear from chair casters and high-traffic zones.

### Drying and Laundry Considerations
Most denim-look rugs are not machine washable. The Quarry Denim rug falls into the same category: spot clean when necessary and allow ample air to dry after any damp cleaning. Depending on your climate, expect 24 to 48 hours to fully regain its shape after a deep clean. Pro tip: if you need to accelerate drying, a gentle fan placed nearby can speed up the process without warping the fibers.
