## How to Use Drakenhof Nightshade

### Application

Using Drakenhof Nightshade is as easy as pie (but less messy, unless you're doing it wrong). Simply load your brush with the shade and apply it generously to the area you want to darken. The shade will flow into the recesses and create natural shadows, giving your model a more dynamic look.

### Effects

The beauty of Drakenhof Nightshade lies in its versatility. Here are a few ways to use it:

- **Shading Power Armor**: Perfect for adding depth to blue and black power armor. Your space marines will thank you.
- **Creating Shadows**: Use it over metallics and grays to create realistic shadows and enhance details.
- **Glazing**: Thin it down and apply multiple layers to create a smooth transition between colors.
