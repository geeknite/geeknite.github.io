Most Luna 8x10 rugs are made from polypropylene, a man-made fiber known for its stain resistance, color retention, and budget-friendly price tag. Polypropylene is the hero of “easy to clean after spilled red wine or muddy paw prints” without forcing you into a mortgage for premium wool. It dries quick, resists moisture, and holds color better than your average synthetic.

The rug’s construction is designed for indoor and outdoor use, which means it’s woven to withstand sunlight and foot traffic. It’s not a luxury-texture experience, but it’s perfectly functional. We’ll note: polypropylene can feel a bit slick on very smooth floors; if you’ve got hardwood or tile, consider a rug pad for traction. A pad also helps extend the life of the rug by reducing wear in high-traffic zones.

## Cleaning and Maintenance
One of the strongest selling points for polypropylene rugs is their ease of maintenance. The Luna rug is listed as spot-clean. That’s good news for households with kids, pets, or connoisseurs of coffee disasters. A mild detergent and water should handle most stains; for tougher spots, a gentle scrubbing with a soft brush is usually enough to lift the mark without mushing the fibers.

For routine care, a quick shake-out or a light vacuum (no beater bar on high) can help remove dust and pet hair. If you’re using it outdoors, you can hose it down from time to time and let it dry completely before bringing it back inside. The quick-drying property of polypropylene means you won’t be dealing with a moldy rug after a light rain shower.

### Spot Cleaning Techniques
- Blot, don’t rub: Use a clean cloth to absorb the spill.
- Mild detergent solution: A teaspoon of dish soap in a cup of warm water works for most stains.
- Rinse and dry: Wipe with clean water to remove soap residue and let air dry.

In addition to spot cleaning, you might want to consider a light monthly lift and shake to reduce compacted fibers, especially in high-traffic zones. If you’ve got a stubborn stain, test a small hidden area first to ensure the color remains consistent.
