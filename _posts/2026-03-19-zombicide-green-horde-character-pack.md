## How It Changes Gameplay
The core hook of any character pack is how much it nudges the game’s dance steps. The Green Horde Character Pack introduces four survivors with unique abilities that interact with the Horde faction in thematic and sometimes practical ways. The aim is not to reinvent the wheel but to offer smarter routes through the same corners you’ve learned to dread and love. Expect increased emphasis on mobility, positioning, and selective risk-taking rather than brute force alone.

### New survivors and their abilities
- Survivor A emphasizes controlled aggression and short-range prowess. Think of them as a scout who thrives on punishing exposed zombies without over-committing. Their kit rewards careful timing and a nerve-wracking sense of precision.
- Survivor B slots into the loot economy, offering more robust options for scavenging and resource management. This character often unlocks better drop rates or cheaper item exchanges when timing is right, turning every loot pursuit into a mini-game of timing and risk assessment.
- Survivor C is a mobile harrier type, providing repositioning tricks that can swing a clump of zombies in your favor. Speed and space control are your best friends here, and you’ll find yourself drafting escape routes like a field marshal with a painter’s toolkit.
- Survivor D is a crowd-control specialist, capable of softening zones through timed effects and calculated positioning. They’re the kind of hero you want when the Horde piles into a choke point and you need to convert chaos into an opening.

In practice, you’ll find these survivors reward careful planning: you might bounce a zombie group into a trap, or set up a turn where one survivor creates an advantageous pivot for the rest of the team. The new mechanics aren’t world-shattering, but they weave neatly into the core action economy and add a texture that can feel like a new puzzle on the board.
