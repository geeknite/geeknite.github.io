## How to Play: Quick Start Guide for a Geeknite-Approved Night

1) Spread the cards, shuffle, and decide who is the Card Czar for the round. The Czar picks a black card and reads it aloud with maximum dramatic flair.
2) Everyone else selects a white card that they think best answers the prompt. Players submit their cards face down.
3) The Czar reads the chosen responses aloud and awards points to the funniest (or most fitting) pick. The process repeats with a new Czar each round.
4) House rules optional: soften or intensify by adding a “Censored Mode” where players can opt to replace certain taboo words with playful alternatives. Or, if you’re feeling spicy, run a “Speed Round” with a timer to keep the energy high.

Pro-tip for first-time flippers: keep a list of your favorite combos. You’ll thank yourself later when you don’t have to conjure the exact same pun again. For veteran players who want to expand the play set: try pairing prompts with entirely new categories (workplace inside jokes, college memories, sci‑fi tropes) to see what new humor emerges.

A note on pacing: CAH thrives on social dynamics. If your table is full of quick-witted folks, rounds will feel fast and furious. If your crew tends to deliberate for a minute per card, you’ll get thoughtful, slower humor that builds over time. The Blue Box is flexible enough to accommodate both styles, which is one of its underrated strengths.
