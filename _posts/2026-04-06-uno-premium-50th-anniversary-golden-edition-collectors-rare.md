The core UNO mechanics are unchanged in the Golden Edition: color matching, number matching, action cards that skip or reverse play, and the dreaded Wild Draw Four. The novelty here is mostly in the extras and in the premium feel, not in the rules. The draw two and skip cards look slightly more premium with embossed icons that catch the light; the Wild cards still demand you to pick colors the way a tailor chooses fabric. The anticipation of the last few cards remains the same, and that is the beauty of UNO; you can change nothing and still create chaos.
