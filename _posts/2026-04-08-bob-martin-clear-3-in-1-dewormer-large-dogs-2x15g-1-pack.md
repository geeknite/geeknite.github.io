## Safety First: Side Effects and When to Call the Vet
No dewormer is 100 percent risk-free, and dogs vary as wildly as people do in a gym class. Common concerns with any dewormer paste may include:
- Mild vomiting or drooling shortly after administration
- Temporary loose stool or minor GI upset
- Rare allergic reactions such as facial swelling, hives, or breathing trouble

If you observe anything alarming after dosing, contact your veterinarian immediately. If you notice severe symptoms like persistent vomiting, blood in stool, or extreme lethargy, seek emergency care. Remember, the goal is to improve gut health, not to volunteer your dog’s abdomen for a microbiome sitcom.
