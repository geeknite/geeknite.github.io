## Maintenance and Longevity
A kit like this thrives with minimal upkeep, but a little routine care goes a long way. Practical tips to maximize your set’s life:

- Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth to remove oils and fingerprints
- Avoid harsh cleaners that can strip color or damage edge spots

With normal care, the chips and case will stand up to repeated game nights and casual tournaments for years.
