## Maintenance and Longevity
A kit like this thrives with minimal upkeep. Here are practical tips to maximize your set’s life:
- Keep chips away from excess moisture; moisture can affect pigment and finish over time
- Wipe chips with a soft, dry cloth to remove oils and fingerprints
- Avoid harsh cleaners that can strip color or damage the edge spots
- Regularly inspect the case hinges and latches for looseness and tighten as needed

With normal care, the chips and case will stand up to repeated game nights and casual tournaments for years. If you host monthly sessions, you’ll appreciate the stability and consistent feel across sessions. If you host weekly tournaments with an audience, you might consider rotation or backup chips, but this kit should still handle a good amount of use.
