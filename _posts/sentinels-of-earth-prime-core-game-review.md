## What is Sentinels of Earth Prime Core Game?

Sentinels of Earth Prime is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of various superheroes. With stunning artwork, cool miniatures, and an array of villains to thwart, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Sentinels of Earth Prime, players work together to defeat a nefarious villain and prevent global catastrophe. Each player controls a unique superhero, each with special abilities and cards. The goal is to complete missions, defeat the villain, and save the Earth from certain doom.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of your favorite superheroes. They're so detailed you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: Each hero has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations around the Earth where battles and missions take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Keep your miniatures away from pets. Trust me, those claws are the true nemesis.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Sentinels of Earth Prime is like planning a superhero team-up. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your villain. Now you're ready to begin your heroic quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their hero's abilities. You can move, attack, rescue civilians, and more. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your teammates to maximize your abilities.

### Combat

Combat in Sentinels of Earth Prime is a mix of strategy and luck. Each villain has unique mechanics that introduce challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have the villain counter your attack? Welcome to the world of superheroes, where plans often go sideways.

## The Earth Prime Awesomeness

### Heroes and Villains

One of the best parts of Sentinels of Earth Prime is the diverse array of heroes and villains. From mighty champions to cunning tricksters, you can play as your favorite characters and face legendary foes.

### Replayability

With multiple heroes, villains, and scenarios, Sentinels of Earth Prime offers endless replayability. No two games are the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more epic battles.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new heroes, villains, and missions. Your superhero universe is about to get even bigger.
