## What is Sentinels of Earth Prime?

Sentinels of Earth Prime is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of superheroes to fight against powerful villains and save the world. With immersive gameplay, detailed hero cards, and a variety of scenarios, this game offers endless hours of fun and strategic thinking.

### The Basics

In Sentinels of Earth Prime, players work together to defeat a villain and thwart their evil plans. Each player controls a unique superhero with special abilities and a custom deck of cards. The goal is to complete missions, protect the city, and ultimately save the Earth from destruction.

### Components

The game comes with:

- **Hero Cards**: Each hero has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Villain Cards**: Villains come with their own decks and mechanics, adding variety and challenge to each game.
- **Environment Cards**: Different locations with unique effects and challenges.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track health, effects, and progress during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the tokens. Trust me, those paws are the real supervillains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Sentinels of Earth Prime is like preparing for an epic showdown. Lay out the hero, villain, and environment cards, distribute tokens, and choose your characters. Once everything is set, you're ready to embark on your heroic quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and perform actions based on their hero's abilities. You can attack the villain, protect teammates, or use special powers to alter the game. Teamwork and strategy are crucial, so plan your moves carefully.

### Combat

Combat in Sentinels of Earth Prime is a blend of strategy and luck. Each villain has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic combo just to have the villain counter your move? Welcome to the world of superheroes, where plans often go awry.

## The Heroic Awesomeness

### Heroes and Villains

One of the best parts of Sentinels of Earth Prime is the diverse array of heroes and villains. From the mighty Captain Earth to the sneaky Shadow Fox, you can play as your favorite characters and face off against legendary foes like Dr. Destruction and The Obliterator.

With multiple heroes, villains, and scenarios, Sentinels of Earth Prime offers endless replayability. No two games are the same, ensuring you'll want to come back for more epic battles.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new heroes, villains, and missions. Your superhero universe is about to get even bigger.
