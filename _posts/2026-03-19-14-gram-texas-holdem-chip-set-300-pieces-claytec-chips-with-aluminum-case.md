## Maintenance and Longevity
What you put into a chip set is what you’ll get out of it. Claytec 14 g chips are robust, but they’re not invincible. Here are a few practical tips to maximize longevity:

- Keep them clean: Wipe down chips and case with a dry microfiber cloth. If you spill drinks, dry promptly. Leaks in the foam can cause chips to shift and rub against each other, wearing edges.
- Avoid dropping: You’ll want to avoid dropping chips onto a hard surface from a height. The case is sturdy, but the chips inside are not unbreakable.
- Storage: Store the case in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading of edge spots. This is not a marketing line; it’s common sense for long-term color retention.
