## Space Planning and Installation
### Placement strategies
Place the pod in a relatively quiet corner away from printers that think they are the office morning radio. Leave enough space around the pod for doors to open without colliding with chairs. If you plan to host video calls frequently, ensure there is adequate space for cameras and screens to be placed properly.

### Accessibility considerations
The pod should be accessible to all, including team members with mobility needs. The door width and handle height should allow for easy access. Check floor mount anchoring to avoid movement during heavy foot traffic.
