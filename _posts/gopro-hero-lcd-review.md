# GoPro HERO LCD Review

![GoPro HERO LCD](https://example.com/gopro-hero-lcd.jpg){: .align-right}

## Introduction

Welcome, fellow adventurers and tech enthusiasts! Today, we're diving into the world of action cameras with the GoPro HERO LCD. If you thought capturing your epic moments was all about pressing a single button, think again. This review will take you through the ups and downs, the quirks, and the sheer genius of the GoPro HERO LCD. Brace yourselves for a wild ride!

## Design and Build Quality

### Sleek and Durable

The GoPro HERO LCD sports a sleek and durable design that's built to withstand the rigors of outdoor adventures. Whether you're diving into the ocean, skiing down a mountain, or accidentally dropping it while trying to impress your friends, this camera can handle it all.

**Pros:**
- Rugged and waterproof
- Compact and lightweight
- Easy to mount on various surfaces

**Cons:**
- Fingerprint magnet
- Buttons can be a bit too small for those with sausage fingers

## Display and Interface

### LCD Magic

One of the standout features of the GoPro HERO LCD is its built-in touch screen. No more guessing if you've framed your shot correctly or accidentally recording your feet instead of the breathtaking landscape.

**Pros:**
- Intuitive touch screen interface
- Easy navigation through settings and modes
- Real-time playback of your epic fails and triumphs

**Cons:**
- Touch screen may be less responsive with wet fingers
- The screen's brightness could be improved for sunny days

## Performance and Features

### Crystal Clear Action

The GoPro HERO LCD captures stunning 1080p video and 8MP photos. Combined with advanced stabilization technology, your footage will be smooth even if your moves are not.

**Pros:**
- High-quality video and photo resolution
- Impressive stabilization
- Wide range of shooting modes

**Cons:**
- Battery life could be better for extended adventures
- Audio quality is decent but not spectacular

### Funny Quirks

Now, let's talk about some of the quirks that make the GoPro HERO LCD unique (and sometimes a bit amusing):

- **Voice Control Mishaps:** Ever tried to command your GoPro to start recording, but it misunderstood and started playing music instead? Good times.
- **Overenthusiastic Timelapse:** The timelapse mode might capture a bit too much, making a mundane sunset look like a psychedelic experience.
- **Touch Screen Sensitivity:** The touch screen is responsive, but it can be a bit *too* sensitive. Accidentally brushing it with your glove might change your screen mid-action.

The GoPro Hero 10 Black offers 5.3K video and 23MP photos, making it a premium choice for serious adventurers. However, it comes at a significantly higher price point. If you're serious about capturing top-quality footage, it's worth considering.

The DJI Osmo Action offers 4K video and 12MP photos, along with a front-facing screen. It's a solid alternative if you're looking for a more budget-friendly option with high performance.

### [Eken H9]({%- post_url 2017-01-02-h9-action-cam-review-primeras-impresiones -%})
The Eken H9 is a great budget option for casual adventurers. While it lacks some of the advanced features of the GoPro HERO LCD, it's a fantastic entry-level camera.

## Final Thoughts

The GoPro HERO LCD is a fantastic action camera that offers a perfect blend of performance, durability, and user-friendly features. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or just looking to capture your weekend escapades, this camera is worth considering. Its quirks only add to the charm and will keep you entertained both on and off the field.
