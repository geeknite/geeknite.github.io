## Care, Cleaning, and Longevity: Keeping the Story Alive

Care is the boring but incredibly important part of rug ownership. The good news is that the NAT331E-6R is designed for everyday use with standard care routines. Here are practical tips that keep this rug looking respectable without turning your weekly cleaning routine into a chore list of epic proportions:
- Vacuum regularly to prevent dust and hair from embedding into the pile. Use a vacuum with adjustable height to avoid snagging the fibers. 
- Tackle spills promptly. Blot gently with a clean cloth or paper towel. Avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper into the fibers and potentially highlight the flaw that you are trying to hide.
- For stubborn stains, consider a mild solution of dish soap and water or a commercially available rug cleaner. Always test on a small hidden area first to avoid color bleed or texture changes. 
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear. This is particularly important if your space has a sunny window that paints the rug with daylight like a dramatic silhouette. 
- Keep pets and heavy furniture from wearing into the pile by using furniture protectors or area rug grippers. This keeps the rug in place and minimizes the risk of further distortion near the flaw area.
