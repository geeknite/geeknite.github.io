## The Basics

### What is Ninja All-Stars: Clan Tora?

Ninja All-Stars: Clan Tora is an expansion for the popular Ninja All-Stars board game. This expansion introduces Clan Tora, a new faction of tiger-themed ninjas with unique abilities and miniatures. The game combines elements of strategy, combat, and character development, providing a rich and immersive gaming experience.

### Components

The Clan Tora expansion comes with beautifully crafted miniatures, character cards, and various tokens. The attention to detail in the miniatures is impressive, making them not only fun to play with but also great for display. The component list includes:

- **Miniatures**: Tiger-themed ninja figures that are both fierce and adorable.
- **Character Cards**: Detailed cards that outline each character's abilities and stats.
- **Tokens**: Various tokens used for tracking health, abilities, and other in-game effects.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Ninja All-Stars: Clan Tora is straightforward. Lay out the game board, place your miniatures, and shuffle your cards. Each player selects their ninjas and prepares for battle. The goal? Outsmart and outfight your opponents to achieve victory.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions such as moving, attacking, and using special abilities. The key to success lies in strategic planning and anticipating your opponent's moves. Players must balance offense and defense while making the most of their ninja's unique skills.

### Combat

Combat in Ninja All-Stars: Clan Tora is a mix of strategy and luck. Players roll dice to determine the outcome of attacks, with various modifiers based on their character's abilities and position. Did you just roll a critical hit and take out an opponent's ninja in one move? Congratulations, you're a certified ninja master!
