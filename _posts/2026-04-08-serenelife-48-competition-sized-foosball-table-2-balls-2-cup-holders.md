### Cleaning and Upkeep
Foosball tables crave a clean playing surface. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth after a night of sweaty play will do wonders. The rods should be checked for smooth operation, and you might want to lubricate the joints every so often if you notice any sticking. A dab of silicone-based lubricant is a safe choice for metal rods; avoid petroleum-based products in order to prevent any odd chemical interactions with the wood veneer. In short: wipe, test, and gently lubricate as needed. It’s not rocket science, but your future self will thank you for not letting crud and dust take over the rails.

### Durability and Longevity
For a piece of gear designed for the home, you’ll be pleasantly surprised by the durability.
