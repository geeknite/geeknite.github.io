## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Your Chips and Case in Top Form
Chips and acrylic aren’t immortal, but they’re surprisingly forgiving when treated well. Here are practical maintenance tips to extend your set’s life without requiring a PhD in Chipology:

- Cleaning: Wipe chips with a soft microfiber cloth. If you notice oils or residue, a tiny dab of mild soap on the cloth works. Never soak chips—edge spots may wear and the inlays can show odd glows you won’t enjoy at the table.
- Storage: Protect the acrylic from direct sunlight. Colors can fade over time, and prolonged sun exposure can cause micro-scratching or hazing. Keep the case latched when not in use to keep dust and curious pets away from your precious cargo.
- Handling: Avoid dropping the case on hard floors. Acrylic is tough but not invincible; a hard drop can crack the surface. If your table has sharp edges, consider a soft cover during transport.
- Chip wear: Edges will wear with heavy use. Rotate chips between denominations occasionally to keep wear even and aesthetics balanced.
