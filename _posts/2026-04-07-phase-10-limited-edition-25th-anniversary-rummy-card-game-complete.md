## Gameplay and Rules Refresh
Phase 10 is a race to complete ten phases across rounds. Each phase is a specific combination you must achieve with your hand before everyone else. The 25th Anniversary edition keeps the spirit intact while easing some friction points that show up in a group of late night players.
### How a typical round plays out
- Each player is dealt a hand of cards
- On your turn you draw and discard to improve your hand
- You yell when you think you meet the current phase requirement
- If you complete a phase, you move to the next one and draw a new hand from the deck
- The first player to complete all ten phases wins

### Variants included
The edition includes a couple of optional variants that can shuffle the meta:
- A quicker mode that reduces the number of cards you draw on each turn
- A cooperative twist where players can share a phase under strict conditions
- A point heavy variant for a longer evening of tabletop chaos
