## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity: Plan for the Long Game
Retaining the rug’s look over years requires a light touch and a consistent routine:
- Vacuum weekly to prevent the buildup of dust and grit that can dull color or exacerbate the look of any slight weaving imperfection.
- Treat spills promptly with blotting rather than rubbing, to avoid setting stains and pulling fibers in the wrong direction.
- Rotate the rug seasonally if you have strong light exposure, which can cause uneven fading over long periods.
- Consider a rug pad to reduce bunching, protect underlying floors, and provide a little extra cushion for comfort.

If you’re a top-tier gear nerd, you’ll appreciate that these steps are the same as maintaining any micro-tech gadget: regular care keeps things humming.
