## Size and fit: 380x280 cm rectangle in real spaces

### Placement tips
- Center the rug under the main seating arrangement so that at least the front legs of the sofa touch the rug. This clarifies the room’s logical flow and makes vacuuming easier.
- Ensure a comfortable border along the walls: aim for roughly 15–25 cm of rug showing beyond the furniture on all sides. If you have a sectional, align the rug’s edges with the outermost points of the seating cluster.
- For dining areas, ensure the rug extends far enough beyond the chairs when pulled out; you’ll want the chair to remain on rug while someone sits down.
- Consider rug underlay to minimize movement and increase underfoot stability—especially in high-traffic zones.

## Practicality: Cleaning, maintenance, and care

### Everyday care
Regular vacuuming is key. Use a suction-only approach or a beater-bar setting on low to mid power to prevent snagging the fibers. Rotating the rug every six months helps even out wear patterns, especially in high-traffic living rooms. Immediate blotting of spills with a clean cloth can prevent staining from setting in; polypropylene fibers are relatively stain-resistant, but they’re not invincible.

### Stain resistance and spills
One of the practical advantages of this material mix is its resistance to most common household spills. Juice, wine, and light sauces are typically manageable with quick action. For tougher stains, a gentle, diluted detergent solution and a light blotting can work wonders. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first.

### Cleaning schedule and professional care
- Vacuum weekly to maintain fiber integrity.
- Rotate the rug every 6 months to balance wear.
- For deep cleaning, consider a professional service or follow manufacturer guidelines for gentle cleaning. Avoid hot water extraction on some synthetic blends, as heat can affect fiber performance.

### Longevity and value proposition
A good polypropylene rug can last several years with proper care, and the NNEDSZ Madras Parker Cream sits in that reliable category.
