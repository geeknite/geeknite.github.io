## Performance and Features

This UPS is packing some serious wattage. With 2200W and a 15A output, it can power a small army of devices. It's like the Schwarzenegger of UPS units, providing enough juice to keep your critical systems running smoothly during outages.

### Pure Sine Wave Output

Unlike those cheap, imitation UPS units that give you a modified sine wave, the Cyberpower Pro Rack Tower delivers a pure sine wave output. This means cleaner power and better performance for sensitive electronics. Your servers, workstations, and gaming PCs will thank you.

### Line-Interactive Topology

The line-interactive topology is like having a bodyguard for your electrical supply. It automatically regulates voltage without resorting to battery power, reducing wear and tear on the batteries and extending their lifespan. It's like having a bouncer at a nightclub, making sure only clean power gets through.

### Battery Backup and Runtime

When the power goes out, the Cyberpower Pro Rack Tower steps up. The built-in batteries provide ample runtime for you to save your work, shut down gracefully, or continue binging that Netflix series you can't get enough of. Depending on your load, you can expect anywhere from a few minutes to over an hour of backup power.

### Pros:
- High wattage and amperage output
- Pure sine wave for cleaner power
- Line-interactive topology for better voltage regulation
- Decent battery backup time

### Cons:
- Battery replacement can be a bit pricey
- The runtime decreases significantly under heavy load
