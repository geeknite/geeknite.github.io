## Size, room fit, and nuance

### How to measure before you buy

If you are unsure whether a 2-3x7-7 rug will fit your space, there are a few quick heuristics:

- In a living room, ensure at least 18–24 inches of rug extending beyond the seating area on all sides to create a sense of containment.
- In a bedroom, place the rug so that it sits under the bed and extends on both sides, allowing at least a foot of rug beyond the bed frame for visibility when you rise.
- In a dining area, remember that chairs should be able to slide out without catching on the edge; leave space beyond the chairs so you can pull out without tripping over fabric.

## Durability and everyday life

### Pet and kid friendly?

No rug is 100 percent foolproof, but this one aims to be forgiving. Pets tend to leave behind marks that blend into the pattern rather than stand out like a sore paw. For kids, the rug provides a soft playground for crawling or practicing cartwheels (we do not endorse actual cartwheels on hardwood, but we know life happens). ### Stain resistance and cleaning realities

Stains happen. The key is prompt action and a basic stain treatment plan. Blot with a clean cloth to soak up liquids; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper. For tougher spills, a mild detergent solution or a stain remover appropriate for synthetic fibers can be employed. Always test in an inconspicuous area first. When in doubt, you can consult a local rug cleaning professional for a more thorough refresh every 12–24 months depending on foot traffic.
