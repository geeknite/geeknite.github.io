## How to integrate promo cards into a session
If you’re new to Dixit, start with the base deck to get the flow. Once players are comfortable, try one of these strategies:

- Themed nights: assign each promo card a theme (space, folklore, culinary oddities, etc.) and require a clue that ties into that theme.

- Rating rounds: after a round, players rate how well the clue matched the chosen card on a 1-5 scale. The promos provide more varied contrasts to gauge how abstract or direct your storytelling was.
