## Maintenance and longevity

Two things matter most for long-term satisfaction with any toner cartridge: keeping your printer clean and replacing the cartridge before you’re forced to endure the dreaded “low toner” warning. The 11K Yellow is designed to be robust enough for heavy use in a busy office, but it’s not invincible. Here are a few maintenance tips to keep your prints crisp:

- Run occasional printhead cleaning cycles if your printer has them; this helps avoid color banding and ensures the toner is used evenly.
- Keep the printer in a dust-free area; a dusty environment can cause minor defects in color deposition.
- Use good quality paper to avoid static and curl that can affect alignment.
