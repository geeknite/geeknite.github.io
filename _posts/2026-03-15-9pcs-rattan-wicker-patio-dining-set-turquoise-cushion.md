## Comfort and Cushions: Sit, Stay, Smile

## Weather Resistance and Durability: Rain, Sun, and the occasional hail of garden gnomes

The PE rattan’s main advantage is weather resistance. It resists fading under sunlight and handles moisture better than natural wicker. Still, there are caveats:
- UV exposure over long periods can degrade plastic fibers; a protective cover helps.
- Prolonged rain exposure without drying can promote mold growth on cushions; ensure cushion storage during heavy wet seasons.
- Aluminum frames resist rust, but they can still dent if mishandled during moves or hurricane dips in your yard’s wind tunnel.

If you live in a humid or coastal climate with salty air, you may want to rinse the set occasionally and inspect joints for looseness. A simple wipe-down with a damp cloth helps maintain the finish, and a light silicone spray on the frame joints can keep things moving smoothly for longer.

### A note on stability
The set’s stability is generally decent for normal everyday use. If you routinely host large, boisterous gatherings with kids running around and a few inevitable football tosses, you might want to add a few non-slip pads under the legs or a ground-friendly weight to prevent wobble on soft ground.

## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity: Keeping the Vibe Alive

Maintenance is not glamorous, but it’s essential for longevity. Here’s a simple routine:
- Wipe frames with a damp cloth every couple of weeks to remove dust and pollen.
- Clean cushions with a mild detergent and water; let them dry completely before storing or reassembling.
- Use protective covers during off-season or extreme weather to reduce fading and material stress.
- Rotate chair cushions periodically to promote even wear and avoid one side permanently facing the sun.
