## What is Zombicide: Black Plague?

**Zombicide: Black Plague** is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of survivors in a medieval fantasy world overrun by zombies. With beautiful miniatures, engaging gameplay, and endless scenarios, this game promises hours of fun and excitement.

### The Basics

In **Zombicide: Black Plague**, players work together to complete objectives and survive the zombie apocalypse. Each player controls a unique survivor with special abilities, and together, they must fend off waves of zombies and complete missions.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures of survivors and zombies, perfect for painting enthusiasts.
- **Cards**: Various cards representing equipment, spells, and zombie types.
- **Game Tiles**: Modular tiles that create different scenarios and maps.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Keep the miniatures away from your pets. Trust me, they love chewing on these tiny warriors.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up **Zombicide: Black Plague** is an adventure in itself. Lay out the game tiles, distribute character cards and equipment, and prepare the zombie deck. Take a deep breath and get ready for chaos.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw zombie cards and take actions based on their survivor's abilities. You can move, search for equipment, attack zombies, and cast spells. Cooperation is key, so plan your moves strategically to survive.

### Combat

Combat in **Zombicide: Black Plague** is straightforward but intense. Roll dice to attack zombies, and hope for the best (or the worst, if you're into that). Did you plan an epic attack just to roll a bunch of misses? Welcome to the world of Zombicide, where luck can be as deadly as the zombies.

## The Zombicide Experience

### Survivors and Zombies

One of the best parts of **Zombicide: Black Plague** is the diverse array of survivors and zombies. From the brave warrior to the cunning rogue, you can play as your favorite medieval character and face off against various zombie types, including runners, fatties, and abominations.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our team was so focused on defeating an abomination that we didn't notice a horde of runners sneaking up behind us. It was like watching a comedy of errors unfold.

### Replayability

With multiple survivors, zombies, and scenarios, **Zombicide: Black Plague** offers endless replayability. Each game is unique, with different challenges and strategies to explore.
