### Care and Maintenance

A blade like this loves to be dry, oiled, and stored in a breathable sheath after use. Wipe the blade down after any moisture encounter, wipe the handle if you get a bit of kitchen oil on it, and keep the sheath free of grit. A light coat of oil on the blade will prevent rust and keep the edge in shape for your next weekend dungeon crawl or camping trip. Do not soak the sheath in water for extended periods; leather enjoys a little romance, but not when it turns into a sponge.
