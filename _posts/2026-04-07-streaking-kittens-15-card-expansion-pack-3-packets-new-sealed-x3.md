## Gameplay Impact and Card Interactions

This is where expansion packs can make or break your experience. The 15-card set aims to offer a light boost to downtime, a few bold options, and some interactions that encourage teamwork or competitive chaos depending on your playgroup. The cards do not drastically alter the core mechanics; instead, they nudge the meta in small, humorous directions.

A few notable features you’ll encounter include:

- Speed boosters: Cards that reduce the time between turns or shorten round lengths. These are great for groups that love rapid-fire play but can backfire if players are not ready to commit to snap decisions.
- Risk and reward: Cards with high payoff that come with the risk of public embarrassment or a misstep. They are perfect for groups who want to amplify tension without venturing into heavy strategy.
- Synergy cues: Cards that reward players for forming temporary alliances or for coordinating a sequence of moves. These encourage a little social engineering without becoming too nerdy about the math.

In practice, you’ll find that some rounds feel like a sprint and others feel like a playful dance through chaos. The key is balance: enough new options to keep things fresh, but not so many that the table devolves into a chaotic shouting match with no sense of structure. The expansion succeeds when it nudges the game into new social dynamics without overshadowing the base rules or the group’s existing vibe.
