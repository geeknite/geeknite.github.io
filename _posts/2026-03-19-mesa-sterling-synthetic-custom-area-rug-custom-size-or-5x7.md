## Size Options: Custom Size vs 5x7 in Real Life

The traditional 5x7 works well in many apartments and cozy living rooms. It anchors a seating arrangement without hiding all the floor space beneath. ### Custom Sizing: The Freedom to Fit Your Space
Custom sizing is where Mesa Sterling earns extra geek cred: the rug becomes a scalable partner to your floor plan rather than a square peg forcing your furniture into a circle. Custom sizes can be built to cover odd hallways, under desks with unusual footprints, or in rooms with multiple seating zones. The result is a more cohesive aesthetic, with fewer awkward gaps and more room for the eye to rest.

### Practical Guidance for Choosing Size

- Think about seating zones: a larger rug can visually ground a sofa-and-chair arrangement, while a smaller rug can anchor a reading nook or coffee station.

## Maintenance: Cleaning, Care, and Longevity
### Cleaning Routine
The maintenance approach for synthetic rugs is typically straightforward. A quick vacuuming routine, weekly or biweekly, helps keep surface debris in check and prevents the fibers from flattening in high-traffic zones. For spills, blotting (not rubbing) with a clean cloth is usually sufficient. When dealing with common household liquids, many users find a mild detergent solution or a manufacturer-recommended cleaning agent helpful. Do not over-wet the rug, as moisture can seep into the backing and lead to longer drying times or potential odor concerns.

### Stain Resistance in Real-Life Scenarios
Stain resistance is one of the practical selling points of synthetic fibers. Common household stains—coffee, juice, red wine—are much more manageable with a quick response window. The Mesa Sterling line emphasizes stain resistance that holds up under daily use, though some stubborn stains may require a repeat treatment. If you have kids or a frequent party crowd, you’ll appreciate the rug’s resilience.

### Long-Term Care and Lifespan
With proper care, synthetic rugs typically maintain their appearance for several years. The right-size rug that fits your space can help avoid unnecessary wear on the edges and the high-traffic zones near seating areas. Padding can dramatically extend the life by reducing friction between the rug and the floor, which helps avoid edge curling and premature wear.

### Cleaning Tools and Accessories
- Vacuum with adjustable height settings to avoid fiber pulling.
- Gentle carpet cleaner or mild detergent for spot treatment.
- A clean, damp cloth for quick spills.
- A soft brush for restoring pile after vacuuming.
