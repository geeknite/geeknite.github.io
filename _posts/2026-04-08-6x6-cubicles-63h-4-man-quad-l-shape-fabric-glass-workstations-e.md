## Maintenance and cleaning: what a geek must know
Fabric panels love light cleaning, vacuuming, and gentle brushing to remove dust. Theyre not meant to be scrubbed with abrasive cleaners or aggressive scrubbing sponges; you want to preserve the nap of the fabric so it continues to dampen sound rather than become a magnet for coffee stains and questionable afternoon snack residues. Glass panels, on the other hand, love a wipe-down with a standard glass cleaner, dry microfiber cloth optional. Be careful with the edges; they can be sharp if youre in a hurry and you slip. The frame and connectors can accumulate dust in the corners, so a soft brush or vacuum nozzle helps. For cable channels, a quick wipe of the inside with a damp cloth is sufficient; if youre dealing with sticky remnants, a mild detergent solution works, but avoid strong solvents that could degrade the surfaces.

Tip: schedule a quarterly maintenance check. Its the Geeknite way: a little cleaning, a little tightening, and a little celebratory snack to remind your team that you survived another quarters planning meeting.
