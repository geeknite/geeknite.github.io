## Care and Maintenance
### Cleaning Tips
Wipe each piece with a soft cloth after wear. Avoid harsh chemicals that dull the finish; for enamel, a gentle damp cloth works wonders. If you spill a beverage, blot gently rather than rubbing, which can wear enamel edges.

### Storage
Store in a dry place with the velvet lining or in a dedicated jewelry box to prevent tangling and scratches. If you travel with it, use a compact organizer that cushions pieces separately; the baton motif looks best when undisturbed by other metal items.

### Warranty and Returns
Warranty and return policies vary by retailer. Confirm whether the retailer offers wear-and-tear replacement for enamel chips and whether the clasps are guaranteed for a reasonable period. Geeknite recommends checking the policy before committing.
