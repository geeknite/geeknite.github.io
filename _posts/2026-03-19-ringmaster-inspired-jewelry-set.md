## Care and Maintenance
### Cleaning Tips
Wipe each piece with a soft microfiber cloth after wear. Avoid harsh chemicals that dull the finish; enamel can tolerate gentle damp cloths, but avoid soaking or using abrasive cleaners. If you spill a beverage, blot gently rather than rubbing, which can wear enamel edges and dull the shine. For the ring, a quick wipe with a jewelry-safe cleaner is fine, but avoid ultrasonic cleaners that might loosen enamel or patina in ways you do not want.

### Storage
Store in a dry place with the velvet lining or in a dedicated jewelry box to prevent tangling and scratches. If you travel with it, use a compact organizer that cushions pieces separately; the baton motif looks best when undisturbed by other metal items. A small pouch or a segmented travel case can be your best friend here, keeping the pieces whisper-quiet when your luggage goes through the chaos of airports.

### Warranty and Returns
Warranty and return policies vary by retailer. Confirm whether the retailer offers wear-and-tear replacement for enamel chips and whether the clasps are guaranteed for a reasonable period. Geeknite recommends checking the policy before committing. If you treat the pieces like a beloved prop rather than a disposable prop, you’ll maximize the enjoyment and minimize returns.
