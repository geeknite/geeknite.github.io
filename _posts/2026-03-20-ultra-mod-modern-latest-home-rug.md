## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity

Care is where the geeks in us get practical. Here is how to keep your Ultra Mod Modern rug looking sharp for years to come:

- Vacuum regularly using a low suction setting to prevent fiber damage. If you own a robotic vac, set it to gentle mode to avoid pouncing on the rug with the precision of a tiny vacuum saboteur.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Do not rub, as that pushes the stain deeper into the pile and turns your coffee into a darker, more dramatic version of itself.
- For tougher stains, a mild detergent diluted with water works well. Test on a small, inconspicuous area first to make sure the color remains stable.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color distribution. It also helps to avoid the odd impression of a rug that looks tired in one corner while the rest looks bright and energized.
- If pet hair is a concern, a sticky lint roller or a damp microfiber cloth can save you from becoming the tournament champion at the end of every pet play session.

For cleaning and care specifics, the official care guide from the brand is a useful reference, though you can rely on common sense and a strong disdain for stains to get by in a pinch.
