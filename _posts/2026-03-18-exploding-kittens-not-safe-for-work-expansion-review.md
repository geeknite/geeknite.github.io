## How to play with the expansion

Setting up is straightforward if you know Exploding Kittens already: shuffle the expansion’s new cards into the base deck or run them as a separate mini-deck depending on how spicy you want the night to be. The core loop remains the same: players draw, play, and attempt not to explode. The new cards introduce interactions that reward boldness, punish hesitation, and sometimes force you to reveal a bit more about your own boundaries than you planned.

### Setup and integration

A practical approach is to start with a 60-40 split: 60 of base deck, 40 of NSFW cards. If your group is already comfortable with adult humor, you can even swing to 70-30 or 80-20 to push the tone. If you’re testing the waters, start light and gradually bring in the more explicit cards once everyone is on board. The rules insert offers optional guidelines for content toggles. If you’re hosting a mixed group, this is a good feature to lean on. A quick pre-game check-in about comfort levels can prevent a single card from derailing a night you want to remember for the right reasons.

### New mechanics and flow

The NSFW expansion does not reinvent Exploding Kittens’ mechanics; it refines them with new triggers and rewards. Expect new interaction types that can:
- Create tension by forcing players to reveal preferences or preferences about the humor at the table.
- Reward risk-taking with momentum swings that shift who is in the lead and who is on the edge of elimination.
- Enable banter that can quickly become the highlight of a session.

The pacing remains strongly dependent on group dynamics. In some tables, the new cards generate rapid, hilarious back-and-forth and a cascade of “one more round” declarations. In others, the tone can become a little heavier and slower if players linger on a card’s thematic moment.
