## How to Set Up and Use
Getting started is straightforward. Open the case, lay out the chips by denomination, slide the cards into the card box, and place the dealer button where everyone can see it. A recommended setup is to arrange chips in concentric rings by value to make counting at a glance easier. The case interior often has compartments to separate chips from cards, allowing you to pull out a single stack for chip counts without disassembling your entire arrangement. If you’re hosting a longer game night, consider placing a small tray or bowl for chip tips and a water bottle to avoid the ring of sweat on the table.

If you’re new to organizing, here are a few quick tips:
- Use the higher value chips at the center of the table and lower values on the periphery for easier counting.
- Keep the decks in a dry, accessible spot away from food and drinks.
- Invest in a good felt or table cloth to reduce slipping and elicit those dramatic pot raises.
