## What is in the Origin Box

The core draw of the Origin Box is not merely the quantity of minis but the quality and the vibe they bring to your table. If you are one of those players who fears the long painting journey or lacks a dedicated hours-for-mini-assembly space, the painted minis in this box are nothing short of a joy. ## Painting tips and setup advice for the ambitious hobbyist
If you are a painter in the mood for a side quest, here are quick tips to maximize the joy of the painted minis in the Origin Box without turning your hobby into a second job:

- Start with a solid undercoat and plan your color palette around the emotions your faction embodies. This keeps your painting cohesive and thematically consistent.
- Use a washing technique to bring out the crevices in the robes and armor. A little shading goes a long way toward giving depth without turning your minis into a muddy mess.
- Prime and seal to protect the paint job during play. A matte sealant reduces glare on the table and keeps your minis looking fresh after heroic dice rolls and catastrophic strategy shifts.
- Display after play: a simple display stand is a great way to show off your best pieces while you brainstorm new tactics between sessions.
