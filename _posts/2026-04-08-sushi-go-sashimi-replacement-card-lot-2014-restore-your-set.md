If you are chasing a precise aesthetic for display or a perfect match to your existing set, you want to verify that the replacement lot explicitly states compatibility with the 2014 Sashimi edition and not with more recent print runs.

## How to buy wisely

- Check seller credibility: a well-described listing with multiple photos and a clear statement that the cards match the 2014 Sashimi edition is gold.
- Ask for a sizing check: confirm that the cards match standard Sushi Go card dimensions. If they are off, you may be looking at a mismatch that makes it impossible to sleeve or shuffle smoothly.
- Request a sample or subset: if possible, ask for 1 or 2 sample cards to inspect color accuracy and print quality before committing to a full lot.
- Review the photos for wear: corner wear, edge nicks, and creasing can tell you how the lot has lived prior to your ownership.
- Confirm shipping and handling: you want tracking and a reasonable window. Replacement cards are not fragile, but they are not immune to misdelivery.
