## Battery life and maintenance

The core asset of any UPS is the battery. In refurbished units you want a clean bill of health. The CP1500AVRLCD-R uses a sealed lead acid battery that should serve you well for several years given normal use. Battery life is highly dependent on usage patterns and environmental conditions. Heat is your enemy here, so keep the unit in a cool, ventilated spot away from direct sun and hot electronics. If you notice swelling, excessive warmth, or a failure to hold a charge after a full discharge, that is a sign to replace the battery. It is worth keeping a spare battery on hand or planning a battery replacement as part of your upgrade cycle.

Routine maintenance is straightforward. Run the self test via the LCD, monitor the runtime estimates, and check the battery health via the PowerPanel software whenever you want a quick health snapshot. Cleaning the unit is simple; unplug it, wipe with a dry microfiber cloth, and ensure the ventilation slots are clear. Do not use chemical cleaners on the plastic housing as it can degrade the finish over time.
