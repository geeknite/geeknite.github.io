### Box Design and Presentation
Hasbro has a distinctive graphic language for its packaging. ### Storage Tips for Long-Term Enjoyment
- Keep it sealed until you are ready to play: a sealed deck is a time capsule that resists humidity and general wear. If you own a sealed deck that appears to be missing the shrink wrap, contact the seller for a replacement or a discount.
- Store away from direct sunlight: UV light can fade art and back designs that might be graded by collectors.
- Use a display-friendly storage box: a shallow acrylic tray or a dark velvet-lined box can protect edges while preserving the vibe.
- Consider desiccants: a small silica gel packet can help manage humidity, especially if the box has traveled through naive shipping routes.

## Caring for Your Card Deck
A good older deck deserves a little attention. Here are practical tips to keep your Monopoly Millionaire Deal deck alive and well:

- Handle with clean hands: oils can affect card surfaces over time.
- Use a card sleeve if you plan to play in a group frequently: sleeves protect against scratches and allow you to maintain a crisp edge on each card.
- Avoid stacking heavy objects on the box: gravity is not your friend when it comes to box corners.
- Keep away from moisture: humidity can cause warping or sticking of cards, which is no one’s idea of a good time.
- Periodically flip the deck in storage: this discourages permanent bending along one axis.
