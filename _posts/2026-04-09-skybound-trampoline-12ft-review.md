## Safety tips

- Supervise all bounce sessions, especially with children. The device is safe by design, but no design can replace adult supervision.
- Maintain a clear zone around the trampoline. Avoid proximity to hard surfaces, fences, or outdoor furniture.
- Regularly inspect the springs, frame, and net for wear and tear. Replace any damaged components before use.
- Ensure the enclosure net is fully closed during use to prevent accidental ejections from the bounce zone.
- Observe weight limits and avoid overcrowding. A bounce session with many people can become chaotic.
- Keep jewelry, hats, and loose clothing away from the jumping surface.
