## Comparisons: plastic tokens vs metal tokens vs cardboard tokens
- Plastic tokens like these offer excellent durability, are generally cheaper than metal options, and do not risk scratching your cards or board as much as heavier metal tokens might.

If you want a middle ground, plastic tokens represent a practical compromise between price, durability, and tactile satisfaction. They won’t rattle on your table when you roll dice, but they’ll still satisfy the tactile itch that a well-made token is supposed to deliver.
