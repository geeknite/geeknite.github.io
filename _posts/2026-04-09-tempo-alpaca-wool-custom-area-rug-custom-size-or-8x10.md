### Care, Maintenance, and Longevity

Alpaca wool has natural stain resistance and is relatively easy to care for with regular vacuuming and spot cleaning. The general care approach for a Tempo rug includes:

- Vacuum weekly with a gentle suction, using a handheld wand if necessary. Do not use a beater bar on high mode; you are not detailing a spaceship, you are caring for fibers.
- Treat spills promptly with blotting; avoid rubbing, which can push liquids deeper into the pile.
- For stubborn spots, use a mild wool-safe cleaner and test on a hidden corner first.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear, especially in high-traffic zones.

Because alpaca fibers resist pilling better than many wool blends, you get a cleaner surface longer. But do not mistake this for invincibility. A sharp object like a pet claw or a dropped knife in a kitchen redesign scenario can still leave an unwelcome mark.
