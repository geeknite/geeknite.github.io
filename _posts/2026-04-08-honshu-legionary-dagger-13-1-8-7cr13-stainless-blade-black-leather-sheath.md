Sharpening and Maintenance Tips
---------------------------------

- Regular stropping and light oiling will help maintain corrosion resistance and edge life.
- Use a wheel or guided sharpener appropriate for a flat grind; avoid heavy grinding if you’re new to blades, since the aim is to preserve the bevel and avoid overheating the edge.
- Keep the blade dry after use to prevent surface rust on the exposed metal. 7Cr13 isn’t immune to moisture, but it handles normal kitchen-meets-workshop environments just fine with a little care.
- Periodic leather conditioner on the sheath will keep the sheath supple and guard against moisture-related wear.
