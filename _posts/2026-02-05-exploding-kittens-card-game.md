## What is Exploding Kittens Card Game?

Exploding Kittens is a highly strategic card game that was originally funded on Kickstarter and quickly became a sensation. Designed by Elan Lee and Matthew Inman (The Oatmeal), it combines elements of Russian Roulette with quirky card mechanics. The goal? Avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten card and be the last player standing.

### The Basics

In Exploding Kittens, players take turns drawing cards from a deck. Most cards have special abilities that help you avoid exploding, such as defuse cards, skip cards, and attack cards. However, if you draw an Exploding Kitten and don't have a defuse card, you explode and are out of the game.

### Components

The game comes with:

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Exploding Kittens is incredibly simple. Shuffle the deck, deal cards to each player, and place the Exploding Kittens and defuse cards back into the deck. Now you're ready to begin the chaos.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw a card and perform actions based on the cards they have. You can use cards to skip your turn, attack other players, or look at the top card of the deck. Bluffing and strategizing are key to staying alive.

### Exploding and Defusing

If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you must play a defuse card to stay in the game. If you don't have a defuse card, well, it's game over for you. Insert dramatic music here.

## The Feline Frenzy

One of the best parts of Exploding Kittens is the diverse array of cards and abilities. From the "Nope" card that cancels any action to the "See the Future" card that lets you peek at the top few cards of the deck, each card adds a unique twist to the gameplay.
