## Expansions and Compatibility

The Game Board Expansion is fully compatible with the previous expansions, including **Invaders from Afar** and **The Wind Gambit**. This means you can mix and match elements from all expansions to create your ultimate Scythe experience.

- **Invaders from Afar**: Introduces new factions and abilities, adding more strategic depth.
