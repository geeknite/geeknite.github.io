### Verification and provenance
The path to verification runs through the box, the foil, the seal, and the ever-so-slight curvature of the corners. Here are practical steps to confirm authenticity:
- Inspect the seal for consistency with known batches. A misaligned or damaged seal can signal a counterfeit or mispackaged item.
- Look for authentic USPCC marks on the tuck flap and the card back. Authentic decks carry specific typography and logo placements that collectors memorize.
- Check the color bleed and print alignment on the tuck. If the red looks off or the edges appear smudged, set the deck aside for a closer look or a second opinion.
- Compare the card stock with standard Rider Backs. A truly premium finish should feel balanced, with a slight tackiness that hints at the air-cushion finish without being sticky.
