## Maintenance and longevity

### Cleaning routine

A simple routine keeps your set looking fresh: wipe down with a damp cloth to remove pollen and dust, use a mild soap solution for tougher grime, and air dry thoroughly before using again. The cushions can be spot-cleaned with a mild detergent, but you should avoid bleach and aggressive solvents that can degrade fabric or discolor seams. Regular vacuuming around the weave helps you keep small particles from becoming embedded in the crevices. 

### Fabric care and storage tips

If you live in a climate with frequent rainfall, consider rotating cushions seasonally so that all cushions experience sun and shade evenly. You can also switch to storage during off-season, or at least place cushions in a breathable storage bag to minimize mildew. The goal is to keep the cushions comfortable and the fabric looking fresh enough for your next social media post without needing to replace them every summer. 

### Durability expectations

With proper care, poly rattan furniture can outlast many budget options and maintain its structural integrity through several seasons. The key is to avoid extreme misuse, heavy loads beyond the specified capacity, and rough handling during relocation. If you treat it like a piece of furniture rather than a toy, you will be rewarded with more lounging minutes and more compliments from guests after they realize they are not sitting on a dilapidated plastic chair.
