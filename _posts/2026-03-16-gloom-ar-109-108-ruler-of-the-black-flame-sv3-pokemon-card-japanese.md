## Care, Storage, and Preservation Tips
Preservation is important for any rare AR card, especially in Japanese NM condition. Here are practical tips to keep your Gloom AR looking as pristine as the day it left the printer:
- Use protective sleeves specifically designed for Pokemon cards, ideally with a matte internal finish to reduce glare and prevent foil abrasion.
- Store in a binder with two-page sleeves that accommodate AR cards without forcing edges. Avoid overcrowding; give the card space to breathe.
- Keep away from direct sunlight and extreme humidity. The foil can bloom under intense UV exposure, and we don’t want that to dull your flame.
- Consider a top-loader for transport if you’re traveling to a card show or trading event.
- If you plan to grade, ensure you handle the card with clean hands or gloves and store it flat to prevent warping of the foil layer.
