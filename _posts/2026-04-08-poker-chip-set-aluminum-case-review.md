## Maintenance and longevity

### Cleaning and storage tips
Caring for the kit is straightforward. Wipe the aluminum exterior with a soft microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints and dust. Avoid harsh cleaners or scourers that might scratch the finish. The foam interior should stay dry; if it begins to sag or smell, you may need to refresh the foam or the entire insert to maintain slot integrity.

Chips can pick up oils from fingers and traces of snacks. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth is enough for daily use, but you can go deeper with a light dish-soap solution if you notice stubborn grime. Let chips dry completely before returning them to the case. If you’re a spill-savant, consider labeling a napkin stash to keep the foam from absorbing every mysterious liquid in your kitchen.
