Assembly and installation
Two people are recommended for assembly, though a lone engineer with a contact lens crisis can do it with careful planning and a YouTube playlist. Typical steps:
- Unbox, inventory parts, and verify you have the hardware kit.
- Assemble the frame on the floor; you’ll want to check that the legs are aligned and the crossbars lock firmly.
- Attach the tabletop to the frame; you may prefer to have a helper hold one end while you fasten the screws on the other.
- Mount the cable tray underneath; thread the cables as you go so you don’t end up with a nest behind the desk.
- Level the desk, adjust the feet to ensure a stable surface, and do a quick wobble test with a laptop to ensure nothing jiggles during a panel discussion.
