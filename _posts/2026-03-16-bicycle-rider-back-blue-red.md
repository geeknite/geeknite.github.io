## Durability, care, and keeping your two-pack in peak condition

Durability matters with any deck you plan to use frequently. The two decks in this pack are built for routine use, not marathon magic shows with a 10-hour grind. Here are a few maintenance tips to keep your Blue + Red duo performing like new:

- Keep them away from extreme humidity and direct moisture. A little humidity is okay; a flood of moisture is not.
- Store in a cool, dry place. The air-cushion finish doesn’t love heat and humidity as much as a cactus loves sun, but it tolerates normal room conditions just fine.
- Avoid bending or crumpling the tuck boxes. Cards will thank you in shuffles and in the way the color pops when you spread them.
- Clean hands make for happier shuffles. If your hands are slick with sauce from dinner, maybe set the cards down and enjoy the moment—your fans will forgive you if you own the sequence with a well-timed pause.

As with any playing card product, longevity comes from mindful use. The two decks are not fragile, and with normal care you’ll be flipping and fanning them for months (if you’re a intense practice enthusiast, years) before wear becomes a noticeable issue.
