### Real-world spill protocol (because you deserve a plan, not a sermon)
Spill happens. Here’s a friendly and practical approach:

1) Blot, don’t rub. You’re not buffing a car; you’re coaxing liquid out of textile fibers.
2) Treat with a gentle detergent solution if needed. Test on an inconspicuous corner first.
3) Rinse with clean water to remove any soapy residue that could attract more dirt later.
4) Let it air dry completely before walking on it again.
5) For stubborn stains, a light application of a fabric-safe stain remover works, but avoid bleach or harsh solvents.
