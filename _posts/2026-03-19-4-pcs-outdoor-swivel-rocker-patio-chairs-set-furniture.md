### Maintenance Routine
Cushion care is the name of the game. Remove covers for washing according to the care label. If you can’t recall the last time you washed them, you’re in good company. Wash on a gentle cycle or hand wash with mild detergent and air dry. The wicker frames love a gentle wipe-down with a damp cloth; avoid high-pressure washers that can push water into the frame seams. Cushions dry out relatively quickly if placed in direct sun or near a gentle fan. If you see color fading or wear in the cushions after a season, it might be time to consider replacement covers rather than a whole new set.

## Buying Guide and What to Watch For
If you are shopping for a 4 piece outdoor swivel rocker set, here are a few things to consider before you click buy:

- Frame material and weight capacity: Make sure the frame can handle more than just your weekly sun salutations. Check the weight rating and ensure you have a couple of inches of clearance above the ground.
- Cushion fabric and density: Look for fabric that resists fading and moisture. Dense cushions tend to hold their shape longer and last through more seasons.
- Comfort balance: Swivel and rocker mechanics should be smooth without requiring excessive effort. If the chair drags or sticks, that’s a sign to look elsewhere.
- Maintenance needs: Some wicker is a breeze, some requires a bit more care. Decide how much time you’re willing to invest in upkeep.
- Assembly effort: If you dread any assembly, look for sets with simpler assembly steps or a video tutorial from the manufacturer.
