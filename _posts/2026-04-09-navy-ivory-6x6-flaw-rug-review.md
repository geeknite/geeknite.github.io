## Care and Maintenance: Easy Does It
One of the biggest perks of a mid-range rug is that maintenance tends to be straightforward. Here’s a sane care plan that keeps things simple:
- Vacuum regularly: Use a vacuum without a beater bar on high-traffic areas to prevent fiber damage. If you have pets, you’ll likely want to vacuum more often than your life insurance agent makes house calls.
- Address spills promptly: Blot with a clean cloth, then treat with a mild detergent solution if needed. Avoid aggressive scrubbing; you don’t want to create a second “flaw”—this one in your carpet’s fiber structure.
- Rotate every few months: This helps even out wear and prevents obvious color variation in areas with more foot traffic.
- Clean spills with care: For stubborn stains, consult a rug-specific cleaner that’s compatible with synthetic fibers. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first to prevent color bleeding.
