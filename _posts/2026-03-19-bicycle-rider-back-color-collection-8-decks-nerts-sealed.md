### Storage and maintenance tips
- Keep the sealed decks away from direct sunlight; colors can fade over time if exposed to long sun exposure, and we don’t want your emerald-green deck turning into a vibe where the color used to be green and is now “almost green.”
- If you open the decks, store them in a cool, dry place to prevent warping. Humidity can lead to slight warping of the outer wrap and a less-than-ideal feel when you shuffle.
- For long-term collectors, consider a display cabinet with UV-protective glass to preserve the color integrity while you marvel at the rainbow lineup on your shelf.
