## What is Blanco (Blank)?

Blanco (Blank) is a unique board game where players take turns drawing cards and filling in blanks on a board. The goal is to create the most hilarious or strategic sentence, depending on your group's sense of humor. It's like Cards Against Humanity meets Scrabble, but with a twist!

### How to Play

The game starts with each player drawing a card with a sentence that has one or more blanks. Players then use their tiles to fill in the blanks with the funniest or most strategic word they can think of. The judge (a designated player for each round) decides the winner based on creativity or humor.

**Pro Tip:** Use obscure and hilarious words to stump the judge and win the round!
