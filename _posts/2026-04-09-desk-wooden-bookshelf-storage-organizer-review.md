## Assembly Experience

If you’re a fan of adult LEGO or adult-ish puzzles, assembly will feel oddly satisfying. The instructions are straightforward, and the predrilled holes align with the dowels in a way that makes you feel like you’re solving a tiny architectural riddle. Expect a 30–60 minute build depending on your level of attention and whether you read the step-by-step or the “trust the force” method (highly not recommended for the first timer).

Tips for a smoother build:
- Organize parts before you start; a clean workspace reduces existential dread.
- Don’t tighten all screws at once; tighten in a cross pattern to keep alignment true.
- Have a friend nearby for moral support; furniture assembly is basically a trust exercise with a hex key.

### Step-by-step quick guide
1. Lay out all components and verify you have everything. If something’s missing, blame the mysterious “box gremlins” and continue.
2. Attach side panels to the base with the provided screws. A little patience goes a long way.
3. Slide in the internal shelves, adjusting the spacing if needed. This is where you decide which books will be visible and which ones stay shy in the back row.
4. Attach the top panel and secure with the final screws. Stand back and admire your adult achievement.
5. If you included anti-tip brackets, install them and pat yourself on the back for safety.
