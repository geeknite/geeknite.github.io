## Care and Maintenance: Keeping Peak Sap Green in Prime Condition

### Cleaning basics
For routine maintenance, vacuum on a low to medium setting to avoid pulling fibers. If your space hosts furry friends, consider more frequent vacuuming to prevent pet hair buildup. When dealing with spills, act fast. Blot with a clean cloth to absorb as much liquid as possible, then treat with a mild detergent solution. Avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper. Rinse with clean water and blot dry. If you notice any color transfer from fabrics onto the rug, address it promptly before it sets. For larger stains, you may want to consult professional rug cleaning services, particularly if the setting is a hospitality space, showroom, or a room you’ll potentially photograph for a feature post.

### Vacuuming and grooming schedule
Aim for a weekly vacuuming routine, adjusting to your household’s traffic. If you have kids and pets, two to three sessions per week are not overkill. This keeps dirt from embedding and maintains the plush feel. A simple brush or comb attachment on your vacuum can help refresh the pile, especially if you have longer synthetic fibers that tend to lay flat after heavy foot traffic. Rotating the rug a few degrees every few months helps distribute wear evenly, particularly in rooms with strong light sources that cause color shift on one side.

### Rug pads: undetectable help that changes the game
A good rug pad is the unsung hero of many interiors. It reduces sliding, protects floors, and extends the rug’s life by reducing friction. If your floor is prone to scratches (hardwood or tile), a pad becomes essential. For high-chair zones or heavy furniture, a denser pad offers stability and comfort. In other words, a pad is not a luxury; it is a floor investment you will thank yourself for later.

### Seasonal care and color refreshers
Light exposure and seasonal lighting can influence color perception. Consider rotating seasonal decor and performing a light refresh of cushions and wall art to keep the Peak Sap Green looking like a deliberate design choice rather than a lucky accident. If you want an almost ritualistic maintenance routine, schedule a quarterly glow-up for your room and let the rug be the anchor that stays constant while the rest of the space flexes with trends.
