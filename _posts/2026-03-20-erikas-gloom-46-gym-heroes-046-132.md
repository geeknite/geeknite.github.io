## Care, Storage, and Preservation
Preservation is the unsung hero of card collecting. Here are practical tips to keep Erika's Gloom looking good for decades:
- Sleeves: Use semi-rigid sleeves to reduce surface friction when flipping through the binder.
- Top loaders: Place the sleeved card into a top-loader for additional protection.
- Binders: Use page protectors that are acid-free and designed for standard card sizes.
- Environment: Keep cards away from direct sunlight, moisture, and extreme heat. The 90s beloved color palette deserves to stay intact, not to fade into a pale memory of its former self.
- Handling: Clean hands, wipe-down cloths, and minimal handling while maintaining the “feel” of the card is a mark of a responsible collector.
