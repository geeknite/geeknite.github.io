### Maintenance: keeping it fresh without losing your sanity
- Clean as needed: Wipe with a damp cloth for day-to-day grime. For stubborn spots on cushions, use mild detergent and warm water; avoid aggressive solvents.
- Store cushions properly: Remove covers if washable and dry completely before reusing; this prevents mildew and keeps the cushions fresh for next use.
- Cover when not in use: A breathable cover protects against rain and dust while still allowing moisture to escape.
- Check hardware periodically: Tighten screws if you notice any wobble; the last thing you want is an accidental “lean-in” demonstration turning into a sudden selfie with the floor.

If you’re the type who writes “maintenance plan” in your calendar, you’ll appreciate the straightforward care routine.
