### Inner Layer & Card Interaction

The inner lining is a smooth, low-friction surface designed to minimize edge wear on your cards. Protection is where older sleeves sometimes let you down: you want protection from wear and tear, but you don’t want to add so much bulk that you hamper your deck’s ability to fit into boxes and boxes.
