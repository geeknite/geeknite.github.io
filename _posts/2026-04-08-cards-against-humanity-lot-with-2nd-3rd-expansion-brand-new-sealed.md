## How a typical round plays with the expansions
CAH rounds progress quickly, but the fun compounds with each round as players reveal their chosen white cards. The host reads a black card aloud; players submit white cards. The chosen one (the Card Czar) selects the funniest or most fitting combination. Laughter erupts, a few groans happen simultaneously, and the group moves on to the next prompt with a renewed sense of mischief. With two expansions, you’ll see a broader spectrum of responses that can outclass the base game in creative yes and no moments.

### Quick play guide for newcomers
- Gather 4 to 8 players who are okay with the potential for awkward but hilarious moments. The more creative your crew, the better the outcomes.
- Shuffle the white cards and deal each player a hand of 10 cards. Shuffle the black prompts separately.
- The first round host reveals a black card and the rest of the players submit their white cards face down. The host picks the winner, and that player keeps the black card as a point marker (optional in many groups).
- Refill hands as needed and repeat. The game ends when you have a winner by points or when the laughter fades into tears of joy or existential dread.
