## Care, Storage, and Preservation Tips for Vintage Cards

### Handling and sleeves

Handle with clean hands, preferably after washing away the old pizza crust of your youth. Use breathable sleeves to avoid warping and humidity damage. A cool, dry corner of your gaming closet is ideal. Some collectors insist on one touch per card per year, which sounds ominously like a gym membership plan but is actually just a superstition that helps preserve the gloss.

### Storage solutions

Consider quality card boxes with silica gel packs to discharge moisture. The goal is to preserve the card in a way that would let future archaeologists decipher your hobby as a legitimate cultural artifact rather than a meme you left in a dusty binder. Labeling your boxes with year ranges helps you find the Dark Gloom without a small existential crisis when you realize you have three dozen dark corners in your life you have yet to label properly.
