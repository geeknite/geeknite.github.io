### Opening and Storage: Respecting the Seal

Opening a sealed deck is both a thrill and a ritual. The plastic wrap, while stubborn, has aged like fine scotch—imperfect but deeply satisfying. If you’re a purist, you might choose to store the deck sealed forever, letting the legends of 808 live in a glass case.
