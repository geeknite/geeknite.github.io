### Maintenance and cleaning: keeping chips sharp
Chips don’t need to be pampered like a precious gemstone, but they do benefit from a little routine care. Here are some practical tips to maximize longevity:
- Wipe chips with a dry microfiber cloth after use to remove fingerprints and dust.
- Avoid solvents or cleaners that could degrade the surface or fades the edge spots.
- If chips get sticky from spills, let them air dry fully in a clean space before stacking again.
- Store chips in a dry area with stable temperature to minimize warping or moisture-related issues.
- Rotate chips if you’re replacing a few to keep the distribution balanced, especially if you’re playing with a mixed-denomination approach.

A well-maintained set stays legible and visually appealing longer—great for keeping your table looking pro when friends bring their “I learned from the internet” strategies to the table.
