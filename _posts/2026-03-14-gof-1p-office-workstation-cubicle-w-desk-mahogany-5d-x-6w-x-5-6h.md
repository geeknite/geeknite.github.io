## Durability, Maintenance, and Everyday Use

Durability is a strong suit for the GOF 1P. The steel frame provides an anchor for long-term use, while the veneer is durable when cared for properly. Routine cleaning with a soft damp cloth and occasional wood-safe polish will keep the surface looking sharp and vibrant. Avoid harsh solvents that could strip the veneer and always mind the humidity level in your workspace; wood can react to expansions and contractions, which is the furniture world’s friendly reminder to keep things consistent.

Maintenance is straightforward: wipe gently, avoid excess moisture, and give the product some breathing space from heat sources that could prematurely dry out the veneer.
