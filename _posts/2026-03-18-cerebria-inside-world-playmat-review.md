## Maintenance tips for happy tables
A happy table is a well-maintained one. Here are some practical tips to keep your Cerebria playmat looking as cool as your player aura:

## Maintenance of the brain and the mat: care and cleaning
We already touched on cleaning, but let’s codify it a bit. A mindful approach to maintenance ensures the mat lasts long enough to outlive your most dramatic Play Mood moments.
