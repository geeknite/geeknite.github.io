## Cleaning and Maintenance
Maintenance is the boring-but-crucial part of rug ownership. The washable nature makes life easier, but it doesn’t grant you license to neglect. Here is a practical maintenance routine you can actually stick to:

### Routine maintenance routine
- Vacuum weekly to keep surface dust and pet hair at bay
- Treat spills immediately with a blot-and-dab approach (no rubbing, you’re not ironing a sweater)
- Schedule a wash every few months if you have kids or pets, or sooner if traffic is high
- Inspect the backing every so often; clean backing edges to prevent curling

### Common issues and fixes
- Edges curling: a quick tumble dry on low heat followed by flattening with a book or heavy object can help
- Backing slickness: if the rug slips on slick floors, place a rug pad underneath and retest
- Colors fading after many washes: switch to cold water and gentle detergent, and avoid direct sun exposure for extended periods
