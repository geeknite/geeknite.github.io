## Care and Maintenance

Like all things precious, these sleeves benefit from a little care. Here are practical tips that help you squeeze maximum life out of your Lair sleeves:

- Keep them away from moisture and heat that could soften the outer layer. A dry, cool table is your friend.
- Avoid sharp objects near the edges. You do not want your brush of doom to catch the sleeve as you clean up after a game night.
- If you accidentally spill something, wipe gently with a dry cloth to absorb moisture before it seeps into the inner sleeve.
- Store decks with sleeves in their own properly sized box to protect from pressure and warping during transport.
