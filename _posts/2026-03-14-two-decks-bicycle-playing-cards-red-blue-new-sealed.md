## Care and Maintenance

Like any well-loved gaming tool, you should treat these decks with a little care if you want them to stick around. Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight for extended periods—colors can fade in some lighting, and you don’t want the red to bleed into the blue deck during a hot summer session. Clean them gently with a dry microfiber cloth if your hands have a habit of getting sweaty during a marathon game night. And while you won’t need a formal cleaning ritual, a quick wipe before and after intense practice can extend their life significantly. If you use them for magic, be mindful of dirty or sweaty hands; oils can affect how smoothly the stock fans, so a quick hand rub or a ceramic brief wipe can help keep the edges crisp.
