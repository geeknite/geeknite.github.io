## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity
Cleaning a rug should feel like a tiny quest with a satisfying payoff. You don’t want a rug that requires a dungeon-level cleaning protocol every weekend. Quarry Fossil is designed to be user-friendly, but it still benefits from a bit of routine care.

### Routine Cleaning
- Vacuum regularly with a standard vacuum. If your vacuum has a beater bar, consider turning it off or using a gentler setting to avoid roughing up the pile.
- For small spills, blot with a clean cloth or paper towel, then treat with a mild cleaner or soapy water. Avoid harsh chemicals that could discolor the pattern.
- Allow the rug to dry thoroughly after any cleaning to prevent mold or odor from creeping into your space. Quick drying is aided by good air circulation and, if necessary, a fan to speed up the process.

### Stain Management
Synthetic fibers resist many common stains, but it is not invincible. The sooner you blot and clean, the better your odds of keeping the fossil pattern crisp. For more stubborn stains, test a small inconspicuous area first and use an appropriate cleaner recommended by rug manufacturers.
