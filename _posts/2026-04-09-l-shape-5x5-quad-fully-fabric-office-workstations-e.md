## Maintenance Tips and Quick Care

- Wipe down fabric surfaces with a mild cleaning solution and a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade fabric finishes.
- Check panel screws and joints every few months and tighten as needed. It’s amazing how many squeaks and creaks can be quelled by a simple twist of a screwdriver.
- Use cable management to prevent tangles. A tidy cable route isn’t just pleasing to the eye; it reduces wear on cords and makes maintenance a breeze.
- Rotate monitors and peripherals to prevent uneven wear on your desk surface. Variety is the spice of life and the best way to avoid the dreaded desk dent.
