## Maintenance and Longevity (Deep Dive)

A well-maintained table remains a joy, and a poorly maintained one turns into a dust collector with a loudly clacking heart. Here are deeper maintenance tips to help you squeeze even more life out of this table:

- Regularly inspect for loose hardware. Tighten screws and bolts every few months, or sooner if you notice wobble during heated matches.
- Clean the playing surface weekly to remove dust and hair. A damp microfiber cloth followed by a dry wipe keeps the surface smooth.
- Lubricate rods sparingly. A light silicone spray on the rods can improve glide without attracting dirt. Avoid heavy lubricants that can attract debris and degrade play over time.
- Protect the table when not in use. A breathable cover is ideal to prevent moisture buildup and tarnish on metal components.
- Rotate players if you notice wear patterns forming on certain spots of the rod. Even distribution helps prolong life and keep the game fair.

With these practices, your Sunnydaze 48-inch can become a staple of your game room for years rather than a one-season wonder.
