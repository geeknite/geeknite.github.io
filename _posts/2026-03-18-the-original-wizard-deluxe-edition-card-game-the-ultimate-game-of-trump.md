## How to Play: the basics
If you want to know what you are in for, here is the high level. The game is a cross between deck building, area control, and social bluffing. The rules can be learned in a single sitting, and then the real challenge begins: keeping your cool while a wizard who is clearly pressuring you for a policy vote performs a dramatic spell to seal the deal.

Turn structure is straightforward: draw a hand, play cards to enact effects, resolve outcomes, and pass the turn. The objective is to gain influence by completing tasks and manipulating the landscape with your spells and misdirections. Some cards change the meaning of other cards, others allow you to steal influence, and some simply require you to perform the theatrics and hope the crowd buys it.

The Deluxe Edition adds a few new card types that encourage more talking, more storytelling, and more dramatic misdirection. Expect lots of banter, a few strategic alliances that dissolve as soon as someone whispers the secret phrase, and moments when a single card swing shifts the entire table’s mood.
