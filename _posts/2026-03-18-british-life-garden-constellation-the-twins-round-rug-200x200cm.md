## Maintenance: Keeping the Cosmos Clean
Maintenance is where the practical meets the aspirational. Routine maintenance involves vacuuming to remove loose dirt and debris, with spot-cleaning for spills. For stains, a mild soap solution and a soft brush are your best friends. Avoid abrasive cleaners or high heat when drying; letting the rug air-dry preserves its fibers and keeps colors from bleeding into the white lines.

After a party or a light garden event, you may find some leaves or pet fur on the rug. A quick pass with a lint roller or a soft-bristled brush generally takes care of this, and then a light rinse can help wash away pollen and sap. The goal is to keep the map crisp and legible; after all, you want your guests to wonder if those stars were etched by a stargazer or printed with a designer’s precision.
