## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity
### Routine Care
Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to prevent fiber friction. Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear. Treat any spills promptly—blot rather than rub, and use a mild cleaning solution appropriate for the fiber type. If you are uncertain about the cleaning product compatibility, test in a small inconspicuous corner first.

### Stain Management
Spot-cleaning is essential for maintaining overall appearance. Color-safe stain removers are usually a safe bet for synthetic blends. Avoid harsh solvents that could affect dye stability. When in doubt, consult the rug’s care guide on the retailer’s site or a reputable home care resource.

### Longevity and Warranty Considerations
A flaw in a rug does not necessarily void warranties, but it’s wise to review the terms for cosmetic defects. The 6x6 scale is sturdy enough for many environments, and with proper care, you can expect several years of appearance retention. ## A QuickNote on Assembly and Getting It to Your Floor
### Flattening and Settling In
New rugs can look a little wrinkly out of the box. The post-unboxing experience often includes a short period of flattening as the fibers bounce back from packing. This is normal. To help speed up the process:
- Lay the rug flat in the intended space for 24 to 72 hours, occasionally rotating it.
- Use a light weight or heavy books on the corners for a few days if needed, avoiding any pressure on the stressed weave area.
- Ensure your rug pad is sized correctly to prevent bunching and to improve long-term flattening and cushioning.

### Placement Tips for Maximum Impact
Position the rug so that the longest side aligns with your primary wall or main seating arrangement. If possible, have at least a few inches of visible floor around the rug to create a frame that makes the color pop. The visual trick here is to treat the rug as a painting for your floor, not just a floor covering for your painting. It’s a tiny mindset shift that changes how you interact with the space.
