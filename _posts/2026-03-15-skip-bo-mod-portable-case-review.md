## Maintenance and Troubleshooting
A practical note: keep the case away from extreme heat, which can cause the foam to deform and cards to buckle. If you notice you have one or two loose slots after heavy travel, a quick re-foam or a drop of adhesive can fix it, but proceed with caution to avoid permanently locking in misalignment. Regular cleaning and occasional vacuuming of the interior will keep the inside fresh, because your card stock deserves a spa day every few months.
