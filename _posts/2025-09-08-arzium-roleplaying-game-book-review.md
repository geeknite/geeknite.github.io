Creating a character in Arzium is both a joy and a challenge. With numerous classes, races, and backgrounds to choose from, you can craft a hero or anti-hero that's truly unique. The book provides detailed guidelines and examples to help you along the way.

Arzium uses a custom d20 system that's easy to learn but offers depth for those who crave complexity. Combat, spellcasting, and skill checks are all streamlined for fast-paced gameplay, but there's plenty of room for strategic planning and epic moments.

The world of Arzium is divided into various regions, each with its own distinct culture, history, and challenges. From the bustling cities of Vesh to the haunted forests of Loth, there's always something new to explore. The book includes detailed maps and descriptions to bring each region to life.
