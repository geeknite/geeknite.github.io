### Care and maintenance

Care of ivory-toned fibers is a delicate dance. Here are our practical tips for keeping this rug looking good between weekend cleaning marathons:

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle suction setting to avoid mushing the pile or drawing fibers from the flaw area.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain into the fibers and highlight the flaw even more.
- Use a mild detergent solution for stains; test on an inconspicuous corner first.
- Rotate the rug seasonally to even out wear and prevent a single spot from standing out due to sunlight.
- Consider a rug pad to minimize movement and reduce the risk of the flaw catching on vacuum wheels.

Pro-tip: ivory rugs show every crumb, dog nose print, and remote-control fingerprint. If you have kids or a particularly enthusiastic dog, prepare for a daily detective briefing to identify the latest “spot” and decide whether to treat it as character or catastrophe.
