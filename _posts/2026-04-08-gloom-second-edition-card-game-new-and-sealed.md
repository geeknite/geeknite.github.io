### What is Gloom Second Edition
Gloom Second Edition is a revised take on the original mood-driven family tragedy party game. In short, you are trying to make your family members as miserable as possible while ensuring your opponents' families are equally miserable, because gloom loves company and the afterlife loves drama. The box includes cards describing family members, events, and artifacts that swing fortunes one way or another. The theme stays darkly humorous, embracing macabre puns, tragic misfortunes, and the occasional spectral cat to grumble at your nap.

In the sealed copy you will find updated art, more accessible rules, and a slightly streamlined deck layout to reduce the time spent shuffling the deck like a caffeinated poker dealer. The new edition remains faithful to the core values of gloom: a tableau-building game where each card you play alters the fate of a family member and everyone else's happiness score. It is possible to win by losing the least, which is the exact opposite of how most games work, and that is part of the charm.
