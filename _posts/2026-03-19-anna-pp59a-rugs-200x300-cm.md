Cleaning and care:
- Vacuum regularly with a beater brush on the lowest setting to avoid pulling fibers.

Maintenance suggestion:
- Rotate the rug 180 degrees every few months to ensure even wear. It also gives you an excuse to rearrange the furniture the day you forget your own rotation schedule.

## Maintenance and Pro Tips
- Rotate and rearrange furniture every few months to ensure even wear and a sense of continuous renewal.
- Vacuum using the appropriate height setting to prevent fiber crushing; avoid high heat sun exposure to protect color integrity.
- Use rug pads to improve grip and extend longevity.
- For spills, blot, then dab with a mild soap solution; never rub in circles.
- For deep cleaning, professional rug cleaning services can be used if you want to keep the color vibrant and the fibers robust.
