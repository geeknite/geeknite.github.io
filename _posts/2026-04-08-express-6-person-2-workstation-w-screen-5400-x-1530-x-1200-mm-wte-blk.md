## Ergonomics and comfort during long sessions

Ergonomics aren’t glamorous, but they’re essential. The Express system helps with a couple of crucial things:

- Height-adjustable options: While this particular model leans toward a static height, many configurations support adjustability to accommodate sit-stand workflows. ### External considerations and real-world integration

- Office layout: The sheer length demands a wide floor plan. Ensure doorways, corners, and access paths won’t collide with the assembly process. Measure twice, assemble once, and have a friend bring coffee—because you’ll need a lot of caffeine for this project.
- Power planning: Confirm that your electrical circuit supports the load, not just in aggregate but per section of the desk. Overloading a single power strip can turn a productive day into a dramatic scene from a sci-fi movie where the power goes out mid-code review.
- Data management: If you’re wiring a team to a central server, plan for network switches, UPS backups, and proper cooling. You don’t want a sweaty server room vibe invading your open-plan sanctuary.
