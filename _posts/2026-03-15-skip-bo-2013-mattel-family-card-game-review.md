What is Skip-Bo? A Quick Refresher
Skip-Bo is a race-style card game where the objective is to be the first to empty your stock pile by playing cards in ascending order. Cards are numbered 1 through 12, with Skip-Bo wild cards that can stand in for any number. The core mechanic is deceptively simple: you have stock piles you draw from, and you play onto build piles in ascending order. The thrill comes from the tension of whether your hand will cooperate with your stock pile, and whether your opponents will steal the glory with a last-second play. The 2013 edition sticks to the familiar rules that fans love, with a few production tweaks that make the cards feel slightly heavier and the print a touch crisper than some earlier printings.
