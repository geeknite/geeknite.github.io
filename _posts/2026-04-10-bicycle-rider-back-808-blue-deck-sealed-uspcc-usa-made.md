## Care and Longevity
### Storage and handling
Keep this deck away from moisture and heat. A humidity-controlled environment helps prevent warping and edge chipping, especially if you rotate the deck through cardistry sessions and magic practice. When you’re not using it, store the deck in a protective sleeve to prevent edge wear or surface scratches. A standard practice is to rotate through decks so you don’t overplay any single one and create awkward variations in feel.

### Cleaning and rotation
If you find fingerprints building up on the back design, a gentle wipe with a microfiber cloth is perfectly acceptable. Some players use a quick dry brush to dust the deck before a show; this is optional but helpful if you’re performing in dusty venues.
