## Care tips to keep the drama alive (without turning cleaning into a quest)
- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and hair, but avoid aggressive agitation that could exaggerate the flaw or disturb the pattern.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and to prevent any one area from turning into a runway for foot traffic.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stain into the fibers and possibly turn a small mishap into a dramatic coffee stain saga.
- Use rug pads to improve stability and to reduce movement when people walk around with swagger and snacks.
- For deeper cleaning, consult the manufacturer guidelines and consider professional cleaning if you have stubborn stains or if you want to preserve the color integrity of the blue and red contrast.
