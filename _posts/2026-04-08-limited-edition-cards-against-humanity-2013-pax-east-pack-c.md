## Care, Storage, and Display
### Keeping Your Pack C Fresh
Display-worthy collectibles deserve respectable storage. Keep Pack C away from direct sunlight to preserve card colors, minimize humidity exposure, and consider a UV-protective sleeve if you plan to display it openly. If you play with the pack, store the cards in a clean, dry environment to prevent edge wear. Display cases with small footprints can turn a shelf into a talking point without turning your living room into a dungeon of dust.

### Handling and Display Tips
- Use a soft rag or microfiber cloth to wipe the box surface if needed.
- Store with the seal intact if you’re a purist; otherwise, a card-safe binder can keep white cards in good condition.
- Consider a light, humidification-friendly environment that keeps cardboard from becoming brittle, but avoid moisture that could warp the stock.
