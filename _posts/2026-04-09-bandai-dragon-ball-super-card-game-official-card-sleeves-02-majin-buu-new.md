## Care, storage, and upkeep

### Cleaning and maintenance tips

- Keep sleeves away from direct sunlight and moisture to prevent color fade and warping.
- Wipe any spills with a soft, dry cloth promptly; avoid harsh cleaners that can degrade the matte finish.
- When storing, lay sleeves flat or stack gently to avoid creasing the backs or bending corners.
- If sleeves become stiff after long storage, a light dusting with a microfiber cloth helps maintain the smooth feel.

### Long-term storage strategies

Storing a DBS deck with sleeves in a protective box or organizer helps preserve their condition. Avoid overstuffing your deck box, as pressure can bend sleeve corners and degrade protection. If you rotate between casual and tournament play, consider keeping a dedicated tournament set with fresh sleeves for best results.
