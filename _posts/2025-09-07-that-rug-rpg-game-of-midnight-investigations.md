## Introduction

Greetings, fellow gamers! Today, we're unraveling the mysteries and madness of **That Rug RPG: A Game of Midnight Investigations**. If you're a fan of investigative RPGs with a dash of humor and a sprinkle of chaos, then buckle up, because this game promises a wild ride!

## What is That Rug RPG?

That Rug RPG is an investigative role-playing game where players become a team of quirky detectives solving bizarre mysteries. Imagine film noir meets Monty Python, with a touch of Scooby-Doo. The game combines detailed storytelling, unique characters, and plenty of opportunities for hilarity.

### The Basics

In That Rug RPG, players work together to crack cases while navigating through a series of humorous and often absurd scenarios. Each player takes on a role with unique abilities and traits, contributing to the team's overall success (or failure). The goal is to solve the mystery before the clock runs out, using wit, strategy, and sometimes sheer luck.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Character Sheets**: Each player gets a character sheet outlining their role, abilities, and backstory.
- **Mystery Cards**: These cards provide clues, red herrings, and plot twists to keep the game interesting.
- **Game Board**: A beautifully illustrated board depicting various locations where the investigations take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and other important aspects of the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don’t spill your drink on the game board. It might add to the mystery, but it won't help your investigation.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up That Rug RPG is as simple as cheering for a cat meme. Lay out the game board, distribute character sheets, and draw the first mystery card. Gather your friends, put on your detective hats, and get ready for some midnight investigations!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players can move, investigate, interrogate suspects, and use their unique abilities. Cooperation is vital, so communicate with your team to piece together clues and solve the mystery.

### Solving the Mystery

Solving mysteries in That Rug RPG involves collecting clues and making deductions. Each mystery card presents challenges and plot twists that can either lead you closer to the truth or send you on a wild goose chase. Beware of red herrings!
