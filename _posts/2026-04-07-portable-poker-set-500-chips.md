## Maintenance and durability tips

A portable set is built for life on the move, not for a lab in a vacuum. Here are a few tips to keep your 500-chips kit in fighting shape:

- Clean chips occasionally with a soft cloth to keep color clarity. A little gentle dusting removes the grit that steals chips from your precise stake. Avoid harsh cleaners that may degrade the paint on the denomination digits.
- Inspect the case latches and zipper regularly. A well-maintained case protects the chips during travel and reduces the risk of catastrophic spill-damage to your last-minute tournament setup.
- Store chips in their designated foam wells to prevent shifting during transit. The more the chips stay in their lanes, the more consistent the table dynamics stay.
- When not in use, keep the decks and dealer button with the chips. It feels ceremonial to pull the set out of the bag and lay everything on the table with a little flourish, and that ritual helps maintain the vibe of a genuine game night.
