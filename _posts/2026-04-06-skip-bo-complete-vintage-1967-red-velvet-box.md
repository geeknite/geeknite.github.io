### How Skip-Bo Works in a Nutshell (without giving away the mystery)

Skip-Bo, in its elegant simplicity, asks you to build sequences from 1 to 12 using your stock pile and the shared draw piles. The goal is to be the first player to shed all your stock cards. Sounds simple enough, but here’s the secret sauce: you can strategically play from your hand and from your stock piles, but you must follow the order in which numbers appear on the central stock piles. Throw a wild card into the mix, call your luck, and watch as a game of arithmetic becomes a social sport. The vintage edition makes this more of a ceremonial event; every decision feels like a duel with time, or at least a duel with your sister who is convinced that you are hiding a secret “skip-bo” strategy that can only be revealed after a dramatic sigh.
