## How to store and display this card
- Sleeves: use dark, reflective sleeves to enhance the flame effect and keep the art from washing out under desk lamps.
- Binders: choose a binder page with a black or deep purple background to frame the art. A double sleeve setup may protect the AR while allowing a nice display without scratching.
- Handling: AR cards can be more delicate than common cards, so avoid excessive bending and ensure you store them away from high humidity.
- Lighting: soft ambient light works best; avoid direct bright light that could fade the art over long periods.
