## How this expansion changes the game flow
### The storytelling dynamic gets wider wings
Dixit thrives on the storyteller’s ability to craft a clue that is neither too obvious nor too obscure. Expansion 84 expands the palette from which you draw clues. A single session can now tilt toward quirkier or more poetic prompts without feeling like you are pulling the same material from a smaller stack. The larger pool reduces the likelihood of an overused descriptor becoming a de facto game mechanic. If your table tends to rely on a few go-to phrases, the new imagery pushes you toward fresh phrasing and novel angles, which is a boon for everyone’s creativity.

### Scoring remains the same, but the psychology shifts
The core Dixit scoring system—players vote for the card they think matches the storyteller’s clue, while the storyteller earns points if some (but not all) players pick their card—remains intact. Expansion 84 doesn’t alter the math; it alters the psychology. With more varied imagery, the risk of players guessing the storyteller’s card exactly on the nose can go up or down depending on your group’s interpretation speed and the degree of cryptic storytelling you prefer. The best approach is to lean into the deliberate ambiguity: give a hint that resonates with the art’s mood rather than describing a concrete scene. This expansion rewards players who can describe emotion, tone, or metaphor without turning into a spoiler-magician who gives away the entire plot of a dream.

### Interaction with other Dixit expansions
If you already own multiple Dixit expansions, Daydreams 84 is a welcome addition that broadens the symbolic vocabulary your table can draw from. The synergy is strongest when you mix across two, three, or even four sets. You will likely notice a few new combinations of cards that pair surprisingly well with your existing favorites. Some players will discover that a card from Expansion 84 takes on new life when paired with older imagery, creating a layered storytelling experience that is both surprising and oddly satisfying.

## Gameplay tips to maximize the Daydreams expansion
### Start with a loose theme, then pivot mid-story
Because there are more images to choose from, you can begin with a loose theme (for example, “dreams about travel”) and gradually narrow in on a more specific vibe. If a player’s clue seems to be narrowing down too quickly, the storyteller can pivot to a different card from the 84-card pool that shares a similar mood but is less obvious in its content. This kind of dynamic keeps the game moving and ensures participants stay engaged rather than slipping into a tautology of same-old clues.

### Use the new cards as a shared vocabulary
Treat Daydreams 84 as a vocabulary upgrade for your table. Create a mini-glossary during the first few sessions where players jot down what certain images evoke for them. Not only is this a playful activity, but it also helps shy players feel more confident contributing a clue. You may be surprised how a card with a seemingly random motif becomes your group’s “in” for a storytelling concept that everyone understands and loves.

### Crafting clues that balance risk and reward
With more cards, the range of possible clues broadens. The trick is to balance difficulty with accessibility. If your clue points toward a card that most players can identify, you risk everyone voting for the storyteller’s card, which yields zero points for the storyteller. Conversely, a clue that is too abstract may cause players to miss the intended card entirely. Practice makes perfect, and with Expansion 84 you’ll learn how to calibrate your cues more precisely by observing your group’s favorite types of imagery over multiple sessions.

## Play sessions: sample game flow with Expansion 84
- Setup: Shuffle Expansion 84 with the base Dixit set (or any other expansion you already own) and deal out a hand to each player.
- Storyteller step: The storyteller selects a card from their hand and gives a clue (which can be a single word, a phrase, or a short sentence) that hints at the card’s image without giving it away.
- Guessing step: Each other player selects a card from their hand that best matches the clue and places it face down.
- Reveal: All chosen cards are revealed, and players vote on which card matches the clue. The storyteller earns points if some (not all) players choose their card; the voters earn points if they correctly identify the storyteller’s card as well as for others who chose the same card.
- Scorekeeping and next round: Repeat with a new storyteller. Expansion 84’s 84 cards provide fresher prompts for each round, which can help keep the game dynamic even after multiple sessions.

In a typical 60–90 minute session, you’ll likely run through 4–6 rounds depending on the group size and how long players dwell on each clue. The expansion’s richness often leads to longer discussions about the imagery and the stories players imagined, which is exactly the kind of social fun Dixit excels at fostering.
