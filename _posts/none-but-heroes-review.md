## The Basics

### What is None But Heroes?

None But Heroes is a strategic board game where players take on the roles of heroic commanders leading their troops to victory. With stunning artwork, detailed miniatures, and a plethora of tactical options, this game promises an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of various heroes and troops. These are so detailed, you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: Each hero and troop has a deck of cards representing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: Various locations where epic battles and strategic maneuvers take place.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the miniatures. Those claws are the real enemies.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up None But Heroes is like preparing for a grand chess tournament. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your heroes. Now you're ready to embark on your quest for glory.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their hero's abilities. You can move, attack, support your troops, and more. Cooperation and strategy are key, so plan your moves carefully.

### Combat

Combat in None But Heroes is a blend of strategy and luck. Each hero and troop has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic attack just to have your hero trip over a rock? Welcome to the battlefield, where plans often go awry.
