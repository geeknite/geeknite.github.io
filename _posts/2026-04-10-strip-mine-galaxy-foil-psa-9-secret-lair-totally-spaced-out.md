## Grading realities: PSA 9 implications for value and display

PSA 9 is a strong grade, especially for a foil that tends to demand a lot from surfaces and edges. So while you are getting a card with a bold aesthetic and a near mint presentation, remember that the bar for perfection in foil cards remains high because of the way light interacts with the foil layers.

## Practical display and care tips

- Store in a climate controlled environment to ensure centering and corners stay in prime condition.
