## Care and Maintenance
Real talk: fabric justice can be harsh on the curious dust-bunny. The round half Venom rug is designed for everyday life, not for a biohazard lab. Regular vacuuming on a gentle setting keeps surface fibers clean and the color crisp. If you spill something—a heroic deed gone wrong, a beverage betrayed by gravity—blot immediately with a clean cloth and mild detergent. Avoid harsh solvents or boil-near-the-rug experiments; you want the design to stay bold, not to fade into a dull puddle of misinterpreted heroism.

Spots and stains respond well to prompt attention, and the fibers resist pilling with normal use. If you have pets, a weekly gentle vacuum is typically enough to keep fur from turning the design into a fuzzy suddenly-heroic mess. For deep cleaning, spot clean with a mild detergent and water solution, then let the area air dry completely before stepping back onto the surface. Do not machine wash or tumble dry, as the tufted construction and backing aren’t made for that level of spin cycle drama.
