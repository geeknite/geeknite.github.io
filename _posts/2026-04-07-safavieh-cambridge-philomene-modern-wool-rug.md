## Maintenance and Cleaning
### Routine Care
- Vacuum regularly with low suction or without the beater bar to protect fibers.
- Rotate the rug every 3–6 months to promote even wear across high traffic zones.
- Use a rug pad to improve stability and reduce bunching under furniture.
- Address spills promptly with blotting rather than rubbing; blotting prevents fiber crush and color migration.

### Stain and Spill Management
Wool handles spills better when treated quickly. For common culprits like coffee, wine, or curry, start with blotting up liquid, then apply a mild detergent solution to a white cloth and dab—do not rub—until the stain lifts. Always test a small hidden area first to confirm dye stability. If a stain is stubborn, a professional carpet cleaning can be wise. Wool is resilient but not magical.

### Cleaning Compatibility and Service Life
Avoid steam cleaning or high moisture cleaning on wool rugs, as color bleed or backing damage can occur. If you have a pet with occasional accidents, act quickly to minimize odor and saturation. With proper care, a handmade wool rug like Cambridge Philomene can last many years, maintaining its structure and feel as a small triumph of craftsmanship in your living space.
