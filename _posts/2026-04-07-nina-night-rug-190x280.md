## Cleaning and Maintenance

Care is the boring part of rug ownership, but the Nina Night Rug makes it pleasantly doable. Routine vacuuming two to three times a week will do wonders for keeping the fibers looking crisp. A vacuum with adjustable height and a setting for low pile works best; avoid aggressive beater bars that could disrupt the fiber structure and reduce the life of the rug. When spills occur, act quickly with blotting rather than scrubbing; a clean cloth and a mild detergent solution applied gently can remove most common stains without leaving behind a ghost of the crime scene. For tougher stains, the polypropylene fibers respond well to standard rug cleaners and enzymatic stain removers in small, tested amounts.

It is worth noting that polypropylene rugs are not inherently flame-retardant, so keep fire hazards away and consider using a carpet pad or underlay to reduce friction and to improve stability. Underlay also helps with sound insulation, which is a nice side effect if you have hard floors and a neighbor who plays the drums at 2 AM.

## Underlay and Placement Ideas

A rug pad is a good idea for most 190 x 280 cm rugs. It reduces friction on wood or laminate floors, increases comfort, and prevents the rug from sliding around. For open-plan living spaces with high foot traffic, a pad ensures the rug remains anchored under the sofa and chairs rather than being relocated every time someone stretches for the television remote. If your floor is slightly uneven, the pad can help achieve a near-perfect flat plane, which is essential for furniture alignment and for those who insist their coffee table lines up with the rug’s edges as if calibrated by a robot.

Placement is about rhythm. If you place the rug under a coffee table with a generous seating area, you create a comfortable and inviting space. If you place the rug in a dining area, ensure the rug is large enough to accommodate the chairs being pulled out without catching the edges. The Nina Night Rug’s 190 x 280 cm size often works well in rooms that measure roughly 4 by 6 meters, but don’t quote me—your space may defy conventional geometry and surprise you with Tetris-like fits.
