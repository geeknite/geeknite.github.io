## Maintenance and Longevity: Keeping It Fresh
Here’s the honest truth: no rug can be a miracle worker. But the Avenue 240 x 320 is designed to minimize high-maintenance headaches. Vacuuming is straightforward, spot-cleaning works on most common spills, and the pile texture is forgiving of small missteps (like that kids’ juice box that decided to audition for a spill role). Over time, expect slight texture flattening in high-traffic paths—normal for most machine-made rugs—yet the color and pattern should remain relatively stable with routine care.

Pro-tip for longevity: rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear. It also helps to place heavier furniture on the rug’s edges, although this isn’t mandatory. If you’re worried about curling at the edges, you can use non-slip pads or carpet grippers. Nothing fancy required—just practical steps that stop the rug from doing the limbo every time someone forgets their sock game.
