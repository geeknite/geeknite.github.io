### Care tips that actually work
Cleaning is not glamorous, but it is essential. Vacuum the rug regularly to keep the pile fresh and avoid a dusty memory lane from forming under your desk. For spills, blot rather than rub; a dab of mild detergent and water works for most common stains. Avoid harsh chemicals that could fade the batik texture over time. If you own a pet that treats the rug like a personal playground, a quick surface wipe after a night of chaos should suffice, followed by a thorough vacuum when you have the energy for a proper ritual. In short, you can treat this rug with everyday care, and it will repay you with color and comfort greet you at the door after a long day of coding and coffee‑drinking rituals.

### Maintenance myths debunked
Some people fear that batik styles and synthetic blends will crumble after a few heavy wear cycles. The truth is that many rugs in this category are designed for durability, with fibers selected to resist pilling and maintain the integrity of the pattern. The Tiger City Batik Rug falls into this category for the most part, though you should still rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear across the surface. A smart practice is to flip the rug occasionally to expose both sides to foot traffic—this also helps you to check the underside for wear, which is a grown‑up way of saying you care about long‑term performance rather than short‑term aesthetics.
