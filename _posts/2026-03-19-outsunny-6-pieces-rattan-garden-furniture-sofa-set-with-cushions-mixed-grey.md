## Maintenance and Cleaning Tips
Outdoor life means dirt happens. A quick wipe-down after a windy day will keep your set looking sharp. Here are practical care tips:
- Wipe the rattan weave with a soft cloth dampened with mild soap and water; avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the weave.
- Remove cushion covers and wash as per the care label. Most covers tolerate gentle cycles; air dry rather than tumble dry to preserve the fabric.
- Inspect and tighten screws periodically. A loose part can turn a nice afternoon into a wobble-fest.
- Store cushions in a dry place when not in use for extended periods, or use breathable covers to reduce mildew risk.
