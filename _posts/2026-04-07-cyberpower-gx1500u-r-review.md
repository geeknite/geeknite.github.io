## Quick Guide: Setup Tips and Best Practices

- Place the UPS in a cool, ventilated area. Heat is the mortal enemy of batteries, and the last thing you want is a warm brick in your entertainment center.
- Keep critical devices plugged into battery-backed outlets and non-critical devices into surge-only outlets. This ensures you get runtime where you actually need it during a blackout.
- Calibrate the LCD with the load you’ll actually use. Don’t chase unrealistic runtimes; calibrate for your real-world usage to avoid misaligned expectations.
- Use the PowerPanel software to set an automated shutdown and to monitor health. It’s not necessary to run all the time, but it’s incredibly handy when storms roll in.
- Schedule regular battery health checks. A battery that’s six months old behaves differently from a fresh one. If you’ve had the unit for a while, consider a battery replacement plan.
