## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity

### Cleaning Tips

The nylon-based cushions make cleaning easy. Use a mild detergent and a soft brush to lift dirt from the fabric. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the fabric or fading color. For the wicker, wipe with a damp cloth, then dry thoroughly to prevent water from seeping into the weave joints. If you get a stain on the cushion, treat it promptly with a fabric cleaner recommended by the manufacturer.

### Storing and Weather Considerations

During heavy rain or winter, consider placing cushions in a ventilated storage bin. If you don’t have an indoor space, use breathable outdoor storage bags. A quick note: the cushions’ color will last longer if you don’t leave them in direct sun for prolonged periods while wet.

### Longevity and Warranties

Most sets in this category come with warranties ranging from one year to five years for the frame and cushions. The exact warranty depends on the retailer and the specific model. If you’re shopping, verify the warranty period for both cushion fabric and frame hardware. It’s a good idea to register your product with the manufacturer to ensure you get timely notices on spare parts or updates to the upholstery.
