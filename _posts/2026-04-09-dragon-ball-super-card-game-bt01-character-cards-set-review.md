## Storage, care, and long-term value
When you invest in any card game set, you’re also investing in storage and care. BT01 cards are no exception. Here are practical tips to maximize durability and readability:
- Use high-clarity sleeves with a reliable thickness. A 100-120 micron sleeve is a good general choice for most players who want both protection and shuffling ease.
- Store BT01 cards in a rigid binder or deck box that fits your typical deck size. If you rotate frequently, consider a dedicated BT01 binder with labeled sections for easy retrieval.
- Keep a note of print runs and foil variants. If you’re a collector, you’ll want to track which cards have foil versions or alternate art in your set, as those can influence resale or trade value.
- Protect your play area with a good gaming mat. It’s not just about aesthetics; a mat helps you quickly locate the card icons and energy costs during a heated match.
