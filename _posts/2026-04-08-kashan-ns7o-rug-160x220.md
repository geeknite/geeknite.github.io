## Cleaning and Care
Maintenance is where many rugs either shine or start collecting dust (literally and figuratively). The Kashan NS7O is designed to be low-maintenance relative to high-pile wool options. Here are practical care tips:
- Vacuum regularly with a suction-only setting; avoid a beater bar on delicate patterns to preserve the fibers.
- Blot stains immediately with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper. For common spills like coffee or juice, a mild soap solution and water typically do the trick.
- For tougher stains, follow the manufacturer recommendations for synthetic fibers: test a small, inconspicuous area first and consider a professional cleaning if the stain is stubborn.
- Rotation: rotate the rug 180 degrees every few months to even out wear and exposure to light, especially if your space has dominant sun exposure on one side.
- Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods to preserve color longevity; use blinds or UV-blocking curtains when possible.
