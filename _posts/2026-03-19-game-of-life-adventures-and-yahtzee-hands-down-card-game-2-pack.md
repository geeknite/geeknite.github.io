## How to Play: A Dual-Track Experience
### The Game Of Life Adventures
You roll dice to move along a life track and encounter events that mimic adult life in whimsical fashion. Career paths, education choices, and unexpected curveballs all show up as the board nudges you toward a finish line that secretly wants you to reflect on your own choices. The tone stays light, with moments that feel personal without tipping into grim realism. The dice introduce tension, but the real heart comes from how players tell the story of their character within the constraints of the events.

### Yahtzee Hands Down Card Game
This portion uses a deck of action cards in concert with a Yahtzee-like dice rolling mechanic. The aim is to build combos, trigger special effects, and outscore your opponents by balancing risk and timing. The decisions are bite-sized, but they stack up as the round progresses, creating a rich tapestry of little tactical moves that pay off with big, satisfying moments when someone hits a perfect run of combos.

### Interplay between tracks
The cradle of the two-game setup lies in the cross-pollination between Life events and Yahtzee scoring. A well-timed dice roll can unlock a life milestone, while a clever card play can alter the odds in a life event. The result is a loop of interaction that rewards both storytelling and quick strategic adjustments. It is not a heavy strategy experience, but it does reward clever play with a sprinkling of luck.
