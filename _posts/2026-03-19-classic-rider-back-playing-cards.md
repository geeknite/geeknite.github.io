## Care and Maintenance: Longevity Through Simple Habits
Keeping any deck in top condition means a few simple steps. Store the Rider Back decks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Extreme heat or humidity can degrade the finish over time and cause warping if you’re not paying attention. When not in use, keep the packs snug and avoid bending or folding. If a card gets bent, don’t panic; a gentle press between heavy books overnight can often restore its shape, but don’t push it too far — you don’t want to introduce new wrinkles to your table’s morale.

If you’re serious about keeping your decks pristine, consider a dedicated card box or case. A simple box with a soft interior can extend the life of the cards considerably, especially if you’re a heavy user who shuffles at the speed of plot twists. A small investment here pays for itself in fewer scratched edges and less drift in the back pattern when you’re doing a dramatic reveal.
