## How to play with the expansion pack
### Setup
- Gather your crew, a comfortable surface, and drinks that you will not regret tomorrow (or you will, but in a dramatic fashion).
- Shuffle the expansion cards and mix them with the base deck if you want a richer, denser experience.
- Decide on a current challenge mode (for example, silent round, single bottle round, or no repeating questions for a whole hour). This is optional but highly entertaining.

### Rules (the quick version)
- The active player draws a card and follows the directive. If it is a question, answer honestly or with the most entertaining lie you can sustain for 20 seconds.
- If it is a dare, complete it or trade it for a chug of the drink designated on the card. You should also be prepared to justify your choice with a short, glowing personal endorsement of your own decision making.
- Other players vote on the best answer or the most entertaining attempt. The winner gets a small bragging-right token and control of the next draw.

## Strategy tips for getting the most from the expansion
- Mix and match: The expansion is designed for integration with the base deck. Don’t feel compelled to keep all prompts in the same mood. A two or three card rotation can turn a tense moment into a heartfelt laugh with the right players.
- Role rotation: Consider designating a roaming moderator either for a few rounds or for an entire game night. A moderator can keep the energy even, call out if a prompt crosses a line, and remind everyone to hydrate between rounds.
- Audience awareness: Know your crowd. If you have newer players or folks who are more private, start with lighter prompts and gradually drift toward the deeper questions as everyone warms up.
- Safety first: It is all fun and games until someone regrets the pineapple pizza life choices. Set your own boundaries and encourage everyone to opt out of any prompt that feels uncomfortable.
- Replay value: Since the expansion packs more prompts, you can shuffle and reintroduce prompts across different sessions. A good mix can keep the game fresh across multiple nights without losing the spark.
