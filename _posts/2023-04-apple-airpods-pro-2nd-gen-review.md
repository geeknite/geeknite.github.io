Battery life on the AirPods Pro 2nd Generation is solid, but not groundbreaking. You'll get around 4.5 to 5 hours of listening time on a single charge, with the case providing multiple additional charges. That's more than enough for a day's worth of listening.

### Pros:

- **Quick Charging**: A quick 5-minute charge gives you about an hour of listening time.
- **Wireless Charging**: Convenient if you have a Qi-compatible charger.

### Cons:

- **Average Battery Life**: Some competitors offer longer battery life.
