## Maintenance, care, and prolonging the life of a used deck

To keep a 2001 UNO Deluxe deck in decent shape:

- Keep the deck in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight to minimize color fade on the card backs.
- If the box insert is still present, store the deck inside the tray to prevent the cards from getting shuffled into a tangled mess between rounds.
- Use a soft cloth to wipe down any surface stains on the box if needed. Don’t soak the cards; UNO cards don’t like spa days any more than you do after a long game night.
- Avoid leaving the deck in hot cars or damp basements. Extreme humidity can cause warping in older card stock.
- If the rulebook is worn, consider scanning a digital copy to keep the RAM of rules in your head while preserving the physical booklet for display.

With proper care, a 2001 UNO Deluxe can remain a playable artifact for many game nights to come. The key is to treat it with a little respect—just enough to appreciate the tactile joy of a well-placed draw card while still accepting that a used copy is inherently part of its story.
