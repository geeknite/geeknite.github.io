## Practical Considerations

### Where to Display and How to Care

Fridge magnets require a trusty mount and a little care to stay vibrant. Here are a few tips to keep the Queen magnet looking as bold as the day you got it:

- Place it on a clean, dry metal surface to reduce fingerprint smudges and ensure a stronger grip.
- Avoid placing near strong magnets that can cause interference with other electronics or decorative pins. Let the magnet be the star, not the supporting actors.
- Wipe gently with a dry microfiber cloth if dust begins to accumulate. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the gloss or erode the color fidelity.
- If you decide to relocate it, do so slowly to prevent the magnetic bond from snapping off and giving you a tiny magnetic heart attack when it lands on your foot.
