## Maintenance and Durability
Maintenance for a budget foosball table is surprisingly simple. Regular cleaning of the surface with a damp microfiber cloth will keep the playing field free of dust and fingerprints. The rods should be wiped occasionally to prevent stickiness; a light silicone spray or a specialized foosball rod lubricant can extend rod life and keep the motion silky. Do not spray excessive lubricant directly onto the playing field, or you will create a slippery chaos zone that would make even the best goalie cry. If you notice the rods becoming misaligned or wobbly after many matches, re-tightening the end caps and checking the rod bearings is a good idea.

Durability-wise, this table should stand up to normal play and the occasional accidental elbow bump. It is not built to survive a basement flood or a rogue puppy, but it does handle day-to-day use with minimal fuss. If you have kids who love fast-paced games that involve dramatic table flipping, you might want to consider an edge guard or corner protection to prevent accidental chipping. In practice, the VEVOR 121 cm is a sturdy, serviceable piece that will serve as a reliable hub for weekend tournaments and weekday goofs alike.
