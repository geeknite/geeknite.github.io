## Where to Buy and How to Decide

Tempted by the reduced price? Here are practical steps to decide whether this rug deserves a spot in your den or if you should keep scouting for a flawless icon:

- Inspect the flaw closely: If you can see it clearly from your normal viewing distance, decide whether it will bother you or become a quirky signature. A tiny flaw can be cleverly hidden by furniture; a large one becomes a magnet for attention. When buying sight unseen, request close-up photos and describe the flaw with exactness so you know what you’re committing to.
- Consider room lighting: Bright rooms reveal more color variation; dim spaces can soften the variation. Lighting dramatically affects how the flaw reads in your environment.
- Test the scale: A 6x6 rug is versatile but not universal. In a small room, it can feel oversized; in a large space, it can appear undersized if paired with wrong furniture layout. Visualize the footprint with painter’s tape on the floor to see how it will sit in real life.
- Check return policy: If buying online, confirm return window and shipping costs. A discount is great, but a painful return process is not a bargain.
- Collectors’ note: If you’re into matching a lot of other pieces to your rug, maintain a color guide that tracks neutrals, accents, and lighting options so you don’t end up with a color carnival in your living room.
