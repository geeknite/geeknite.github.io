## Maintenance and care tips

- Keep decks dry and away from humidity. A little moisture can cause warping and  misfires during a crucial moment.

- Use a dedicated card case or small box to reduce edge wear and keep corners sharp.
- When you first start using the deck, consider a few light shuffles to break in the air cushion finish for smoother handling.
- Avoid exposing to direct sunlight for long periods as this can degrade the back design and the finish after repeated exposure.
