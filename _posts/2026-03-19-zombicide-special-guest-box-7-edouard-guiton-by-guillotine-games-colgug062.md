## Painting and display tips

- Start with a quick primer, then apply a mid-tone base. The models respond well to mid-level contrast, so don’t overdo the wash unless you want a moody, film-noir vibe.
- Use a cool highlight color on armor and clothing to help figures pop against darker basing. This is especially effective for Edouard’s survivors, whose silhouettes can support bold color choices.
- For bases, a dusty, urban look works nicely with the zombie variants. A touch of gravel and a subtle dry brush can elevate the display without overwhelming the miniature’s sculpt lines.
- Store miniatures securely if you’re transporting to a gaming club. The special guest minis are worth showing off, but they are also a little sensitive to rough handling during travel.
