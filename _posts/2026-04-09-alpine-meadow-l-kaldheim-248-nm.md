## Care, storage, and display tips for the mindful MTG aficionado

- Keep your Alpine Meadow in its protective sleeve when not in play. A soft, non-scratch sleeve helps preserve the surface and keeps the edges sharp for years. 
- Store upright in a padded box or binder with magnetic or zip compartments to prevent bowing and moisture exposure.
- Avoid stacking heavy items on top of the binder; gravity is a cruel, silent card thief.
- Check humidity levels and rotate storage positions occasionally to prevent warping or corner damage.
- Use a soft cloth to wipe dust off the surface. Do not spray cleaners directly on the card; you want the gloss to stay intact, not become a questionable fingerprint exhibition.
