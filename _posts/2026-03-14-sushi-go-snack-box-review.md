### The Snacks, in Detail
Here we go into the crunchy-obsessed heart of the matter. Each item is designed to be snackable in a gaming session while still delivering on flavor. The seaweed bites deliver a clean sea-salt kiss with a toasty crunch that stands up to a late-night chai latte without wilting. The wasabi-lime crackers provide that necessary zing—heat that doesn’t derail the entire party with a hiccup of spicy chaos. Then there are the almond clusters; a mix of sweet and salty that behaves like a well-tuned pair of pilot shoes for a clumsy goblin rider.

For the more adventurous palate, the dashi pretzels offer a savoury, slightly smoky depth that can spark a debate on whether umami counts as magic in a snack. The gummy nigiri is adorable and oddly satisfying—a reminder that not all gummies have to be sour or overly fruity to have charm.

If you’re comparing with a standard snack box, this lineup leans more toward “savory umami-forward” rather than pure sweet, which is a welcome deviation for many gamers who worry about their hands turning into sugar-powered maracas between turns.
