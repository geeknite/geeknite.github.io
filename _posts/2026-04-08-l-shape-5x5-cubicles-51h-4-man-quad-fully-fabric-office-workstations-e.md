## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Long-Term Care
The fabric panels, while comfortable, will demand some upkeep. Here are practical tips to keep the setup looking fresh and functioning smoothly for years:

- Regular vacuuming: A quick weekly pass with a small upholstery attachment reduces dust and crumb buildup. It also keeps your monitors from turning into accidental dust collectors.
- Stain management: For spills, blot rather than rub. Use a fabric-safe cleaner if needed, and test on a hidden area first. If the fabric accepts stain-treated patches, you can extend the life of your modules without a full replacement.
- Monitor placement: Avoid placing heavy items directly on fabric panels. It’s not a wide-scale weight test, but it helps prevent sagging and keeps the aesthetics intact.
- Cleaning schedule: Align cleaning with your building's maintenance cycle. A little routine goes a long way toward keeping the look professional and the panels in good shape.

In short, treat the fabric like your favorite hoodie: it’s comfortable, it shows wear, and with a little care, it lasts longer than you expect.
