## Introduction

Ladies and gentlemen, geeks and tech enthusiasts, gather around! The MacBook Air M3 has landed, and it's here to claim the throne as the featherweight champion of laptops. ## Design and Build Quality

### Sleek and Lightweight

The MacBook Air M3 is so light, you might accidentally toss it out with your junk mail. Weighing in at just 2.5 pounds (1.13 kg for our metric friends), this laptop is the epitome of portability. The unibody aluminum chassis feels both premium and durable, making it a perfect companion for those who are always on the move.

![MacBook Air M3 Design](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/design__c2u6xn6rvs2u_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}macbook-air-m3-design)

### Ports and Connectivity

The MacBook Air M3 comes equipped with two Thunderbolt 4 ports and a headphone jack. Yes, a headphone jack! In a world where dongles reign supreme, Apple has graciously kept this little port for those of us still clinging to our wired headphones. While the port selection isn't vast, it's adequate for a laptop this thin and light.

## Display and Audio

![MacBook Air M3 Display](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}macbook-air-m3-display)

### Retina Display

The MacBook Air M3 features a stunning 13.6-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with True Tone technology. The colors are vibrant, the blacks are deep, and the brightness can rival the sun on a good day. Whether you're binge-watching your favorite series or editing photos, this display delivers a visual feast.

### Audio Quality

The audio quality is nothing short of impressive. With a four-speaker sound system and wide stereo sound, it's like having a personal concert hall in your laptop. Whether you're jamming to your favorite tunes or having a video call, the sound will blow you away.

## Keyboard and Trackpad

![Magic Keyboard](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/keyboard__f5dhn0m5h16m_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}magic-keyboard)

## Software and Features

![macOS Monterey](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/macos__c1hwqr0tmcq6_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}macos-monterey)

### macOS Monterey

The MacBook Air M3 comes with macOS Monterey, packed with new features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and redesigned Safari. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the MacBook Air stand out from the competition.

### FaceTime and Webcam

The 1080p FaceTime HD camera is a significant upgrade, ensuring you look your best on video calls. Coupled with the studio-quality three-mic array, it's perfect for remote meetings or catching up with friends and family.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
