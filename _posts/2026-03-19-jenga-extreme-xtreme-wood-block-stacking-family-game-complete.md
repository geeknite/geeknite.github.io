## How to play: setup and rules basics
On your turn you pull one block from anywhere below the topmost level, then place that block on top in the alternating pattern. The aim is to keep the tower upright long enough for someone else to find a block that will dramatically disturb your carefully planned ego. The Extreme Xtreme edition adds optional twists: a timer for a little speedrun chaos, and a few house rules that your group can agree on before the first block is tugged.

- Start with all blocks arranged in the classic alternating pattern on the base
- Remove a block from anywhere except the topmost layer
- Place the removed block on the top, continuing the alternating order
- If the tower wobbles or collapses, everyone reveals their inner drama queen and you count the laughs as you reset

If you want extra spice, use a sand timer or a countdown app to inject a bit of panic into each move. The rules are simple enough for a kid to grasp yet agile enough to challenge adults with a flair for performance.
