## House Rules and Variants: Tweaks to suit your crew

Part of CAH’s charm is that it invites house rules. Here are a few ideas to maximize fun with the 2013 Holiday Expansion:
- The Czar's Burden: After a card is chosen, the Czar can earn bonus points if they can defend their selection with a short, improvised monologue. This adds performance flair and reduces the risk of silence around the table.
- The Quickfire Round: Limit rounds to 30 seconds per white card, forcing players to trust their first instincts. This can speed up the game and boost the energy in a rowdy room.
- PG-13 Mixer: Create a separate pool of white cards that are a touch milder for mixed-age groups. You can run a side-by-side comparison: one normal deck, one PG-13 deck, then combine results strategically.
- The Nostalgia Rule: Include a nostalgia boost card that makes players lean into jokes about mid-2010s pop culture. It can be a one-game addition to the deck, to celebrate the era the expansion hails from.

Tip: The most important rule is to keep things light and ensure everyone is having a good time. If a particular card ruffles someone’s feathers, move on and don’t pick it again in future rounds. The humor is best when everyone leaves with a story they’ll retell at future gatherings.
