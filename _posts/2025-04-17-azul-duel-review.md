## Components and Quality

Azul Duel comes with beautifully crafted tiles, a dual-sided board, player mats, and a stylish bag to hold the tiles. The quality is impeccable, and the aesthetics are a feast for the eyes. Honestly, it’s like playing chess with Fabergé eggs.

**Criticism Alert**: While the tiles are gorgeous, they’re also slippery little devils. Be prepared for some unexpected tile acrobatics during intense sessions!

## Gameplay

Azul Duel is all about strategically placing tiles to optimize points. Players take turns drafting tiles and placing them on their boards, aiming to complete specific patterns and objectives. The game requires careful planning, a bit of luck, and a dash of foresight.

**Pro Tip**: Don’t underestimate the power of blocking your opponent. The most beautiful patterns can sometimes be the ones that leave your rival in tears.

### The Duel Mechanic

The duel mechanic is where Azul Duel truly shines. Players draft tiles from a central pool, but there’s a twist – you can also pick tiles to deny your opponent. The game becomes a delightful dance of strategy and sabotage.

**Criticism Alert**: The duel mechanic adds a layer of complexity that can be punishing for newcomers. Expect a few “Why did my masterpiece turn into chaos?” moments.
