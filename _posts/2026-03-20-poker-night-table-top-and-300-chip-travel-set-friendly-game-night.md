## Quick tips for maximizing fun

- Create a rotation: Each round, the dealer changes to keep the game dynamic and to reduce fatigue and pressure on any one person.  
- Use side bets sparingly: It’s easy to escalate bets when a night is going well; keep it friendly with predefined side-bet rules.  
- Keep snacks close but not too close: A dicey candy-based hazard is a thing; keep chips and snacks separated to avoid sticky hands and gnawed chips.  
- Use the cut card to maintain deal integrity: It’s not just a prop—it signals a clear break in the action and keeps everyone honest.
