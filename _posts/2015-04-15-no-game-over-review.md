## What Is No Game Over?

**No Game Over** is a board game designed for 2-6 players. It's all about collecting retro gaming consoles and games while avoiding the dreaded 'Game Over' cards. The game is packed with references to classic gaming culture, from Nientiendo to Play and everything in between.

### Components and Quality

The game includes:

- **Game Board**: Featuring various retro gaming locations.
- **Cards**: Collect consoles, games, and items. Beware of the 'Game Over' cards!
- **Tokens**: Representing lives, coins, and power-ups.
- **Miniatures**: Cute little retro gaming icons.

**Pro Tip**: Keep the 'Game Over' cards away from your siblings. They might use them against you in real life.

### Gameplay

Setting up **No Game Over** is like preparing for a retro gaming marathon. Lay out the board, shuffle the cards, distribute tokens, and you're ready to start your nostalgic adventure.

#### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards, move their tokens, and try to collect gaming consoles and games. You can use power-ups to boost your score or sabotage your opponents. The goal is to avoid drawing 'Game Over' cards while amassing the most impressive retro gaming collection.

#### Collecting Consoles and Games

Collecting consoles and games is the heart of **No Game Over**. Each console and game card adds points to your score. Special cards can multiply your score, give you extra lives, or even let you steal items from other players.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, a player managed to collect all the Nientiendo consoles but kept drawing 'Game Over' cards. The resulting frustration was only matched by the laughter from everyone else at the table.

### The Retro Vibe
