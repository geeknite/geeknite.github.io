## Maintenance, care, and longevity
To preserve the look and feel of your Fortune Row dice, treat them well. A microfiber cloth, a protective case or box, and a desk mat can go a long way toward extending their life. Avoid dropping dice on hard surfaces; if you are prone to pizza night hammering, consider a soft dice tray to absorb the impact. In time, slight wear on edges is normal and can even add character, but you do not want the dice to look like they’ve lived in the bottom of a car ashtray. Clean them after sessions to prevent resin or acrylic residues from accumulating.

If you want to ensure your dice age well, avoid exposing them to direct sunlight for extended periods. Prolonged UV exposure can cause color fade, particularly on translucent dice. If you are a table full of dramatic lighting and candles, you should be fine, but if your gaming space is a sun trap, you might want to store the dice when they are not in use.
