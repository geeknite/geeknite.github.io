### Construction and Materials

Because this is a woven rug, not a plump shag, the structural expectations are different. The construction is designed to deliver durability in a compact footprint. The 24x36 dimension means you can place it in front of a sofa, under a desk chair, or in an entryway that sees more traffic than a launch day at a gaming store. The weaving technique yields a low to mid-pile height, which helps with ease of cleaning—vacuuming is straightforward and the surface resists crushing over time. The reversible design means you get two color stories for roughly the same price. In practice, most reversibles use a double-sided construction, so the edges stay crisp even after monthly turnover in a busy living room. While the exact fiber blend can vary by batch, you will likely encounter a synthetic-blend rug that balances softness underfoot with stain resistance—ideal for coffee spills, that splash of red wine you didn’t see coming, or tears of joy when your team finally wins a fictional epic.

Care instructions: vacuum regularly with a beater bar on low setting or a straight suction mode. Avoid aggressive agitation on delicate edges. For spills, blot—don’t rub—and clean with a mild detergent. For deeper stains, test in an inconspicuous corner first. Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear, and consider using a rug pad to reduce slipping.
