We recommend using coasters for each workstation and maintaining a regimented cleaning schedule to preserve the appearance of the panels.

The underlying frame is tough, designed to support heavy loads and the wear-and-tear of daily use. Expect the metal substructure to hold up well under occasional chair bumps and repeated assembly of monitor arms. The manufacturer typically offers a standard warranty (details vary by region), covering manufacturing defects and certain wear issues. As with any big-ticket purchase, register the product and keep a copy of your receipt; it makes future service visits smoother and reduces the odds of the warranty doing the moonwalk when you need it most.

- Periodically tighten screws and check panel fasteners; vibration from chair movement can loosen things over time.
