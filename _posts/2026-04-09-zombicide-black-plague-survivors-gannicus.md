## The card design and what it promises

Gannicus is built to be a frontline bruiser with a flair for turning teeth into tomato sauce. The card emphasizes melee power, resilience, and a knack for turning momentum into more action. In practical terms this means Gannicus can push through the zombie horde with confidence, carve out space for the rest of the team, and dish out damage that actually matters when the bite counters start racking up.

### Abilities at a glance
- Heavy hitter with elite melee dice; expect that shield and sword to ignore a few low level zombies with clean efficiency.
- Special rule named Spartan Resolve that gives you a chance to shake off a bad turn and press forward, turning a setback into a clean next move.
- Team oriented edge; when paired with other survivors who specialize in disables or crowd control, Gannicus can create space for searches and rescues without needing to burn extra actions.
- A thematic ability that rewards flamboyance; yes, we mean landing stylishly into a cluster of z-Very-Aggressive Zombies and feeling like the crowd in a coliseum would cheer if they were alive to watch it.

If you want to see exactly how a gladiator in a plague infested world behaves, you will want to test him in a few different missions. He is not a one trick pony; he scales up when you have the right gear and a little dice luck, and he punishes hesitation with the same ferocity you expect from a Spartan duel.

## How the Gannicus card plays in actual scenarios

### Starting stance and early rounds
Gannicus excels when you set him up to press the zombie front line. In the early rounds he can march forward with confident steps, roll those melee dice, and create space for objective tiles or clue searches. His presence allows other survivors to take cover behind him or to pivot to a strategic role where they can search or lure zombies while Gannicus holds the line. The synergy here is simple: keep him in the front line while teammates handle utensils such as blades, bows, and ranged weapons that complement his close quarters power.

### Midgame pivot and tactical shine
As the game progresses, your team will face more intense beasts and larger clusters of undead. That is where Gannicus shines as a momentum engine. A well timed Spartan Resolve can turn a near wipe into a lasting stand, and that moment of resilience often translates into a search or a light objective completion that changes the narrative of the game. When you pair him with other survivors who can dispatch strays or juggle multiple alerts, Gannicus becomes a living breach that the team can rely on to break a choke point and keep the mission on track.

### Potential drawbacks and how to mitigate them
No survivor is perfect, and Gannicus has his share of tradeoffs. His melee strength can become underutilized if you end up facing screens of zombies with high armor values. In that case you want to bring along a heavier weapon or an ally who can help you break through that armor. Also remember that the glory of a Spartan is not limitless; he does best with a second line of defense backing him up and some reliable backup in terms of morale and resources. The good news is that in a typical Black Plague round you will likely get multiple turns, allowing you to capitalize on Gannicus luck and his position on the board.
