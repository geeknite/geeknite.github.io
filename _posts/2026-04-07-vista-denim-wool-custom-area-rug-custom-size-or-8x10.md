## Durability and maintenance
### Everyday care
Cleaning denim-wool blends isn’t as scary as some folks fear. Regular vacuuming helps keep dirt from embedding in the fibers. For spills, blot—don’t rub—with a clean cloth to prevent staining. If you do end up with a stubborn stain, test a small inconspicuous area first and then consider a gentle wool-safe cleaner. Avoid bleach and harsh solvents which can alter color or fiber integrity. The key is to maintain the rug’s texture and color balance through simple, routine care rather than heroic, carroty stain rescues.

### Stain resistance and repellents
Many denim-wool rugs respond well to routine maintenance but aren’t marketed as stain-proof or water-repellent. You’ll want to address spills quickly, particularly on light-to-mid blue tones that reveal mark lines sooner than darker shades. A mild cleaning solution suitable for wool blends can help; always follow manufacturer recommendations for the specific fiber mix and backing. If you’re in a heavily trafficked area or have kids and pets, consider a protective treatment that doesn’t compromise the fabric’s breathability or color integrity.

### Protective measures for longevity
- Use a rug pad to prevent shifting and reduce wear along the edges
- Rotate every few months to distribute foot traffic evenly and avoid pattern indentation
- Keep sunlight exposure in check to prevent color fading; UV-blocking window treatments can help preserve the denim-like hue
