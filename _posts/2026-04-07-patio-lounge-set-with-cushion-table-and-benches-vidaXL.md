## Weather Resistance and Maintenance

This is where the real test begins: can the furniture withstand a summer rainstorm and a winter wind without turning into a flappy sculpture? Poly rattan is designed for weather friendliness, but nothing is truly maintenance-free. Here are practical steps to maximize longevity:

- Use a breathable furniture cover during heavy rain and snow. This reduces moisture buildup and protects color fidelity.
- Tighten screws periodically to prevent wobble. A simple screwdriver will do most of the work.
- Clean with a mild detergent and water solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that may degrade the weave or cushion fabrics. Rinse and dry thoroughly before storing cushions. 
- Rotate cushions to ensure even wear. A bit of rotation helps maintain a uniform look across the set.

While it’s not a miracle worker against wind-blown debris, this vidaXL set performs admirably with proper care.
