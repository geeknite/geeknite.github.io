## Maintenance and care
To keep the set looking good, perform basic maintenance: wipe down wooden surfaces with a damp cloth, reapply sealant if you notice dryness on the surface, and fluff cushions regularly. Remove and wash cushion covers when possible, then air-dry to avoid mold. Clean cushions with mild soap and water; avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that can degrade fabric. For storage, place cushions indoors or in a waterproof box during heavy rain or winter.
