## Size Guide and Placement Strategy

### Understanding Room Proportions
Choosing the right size is often the difference between a room that feels cohesive and a space that feels off balance. In most living rooms, a 5x8 or 6x9 rug under a standard sofa arrangement can anchor the seating area without overwhelming the space. For larger living rooms with spacious seating areas, an 8x10 rug can define the center and allow for generous padding under all seating components. ## Care, Cleaning, and Longevity Tips

### Regular Care Routine
Vacuum regularly on a low setting to avoid pulling fibers. Use a brush attachment or a soft head to remove dust and loose debris. Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear patterns, especially in high-traffic areas. If you notice any discoloration, address it promptly with a damp cloth and a mild cleaning solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade fiber integrity or alter the color palette.

### Spill Management and Stain Removal
Spills happen. The key is to blot, not rub. Gently press a clean cloth onto the spill to absorb the liquid. For stubborn stains, test a small, inconspicuous area first and then treat the affected area with a mild detergent solution. Work from the outside of the stain toward the center to avoid spreading.
