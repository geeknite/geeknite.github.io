## Maintenance and Care
Keeping a vintage style set in good shape is mostly common sense, but a few tips help extend its life. Store the set in its leather slipcase when not in use to protect from dust and accidental spills. Wipe the wood and brass with a dry or slightly damp cloth, then dry thoroughly. Avoid excessive moisture or direct sunlight which can warp wood or fade the antique finish.

Occasional light polishing of the brass hardware can help maintain shine without risking removal of the patina that gives the set character. If chips start showing edges, handle them with a gentle touch rather than a rough shove. The goal is a long life of elegant table presence rather than a quick facelift that leaves you with misaligned hinges.
