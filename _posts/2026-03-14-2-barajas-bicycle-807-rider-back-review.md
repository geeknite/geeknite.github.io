## Care and maintenance: keeping your Bicycle 807 Rider Back decks fresh
Long-term use means better longevity. Here are practical care tips to extend the life of your two-deck set:

- Storage: Keep decks in a cool, dry place. Heat and humidity are the sworn enemies of playing cards; they can make the stock warp and the finish lose its snap.
- Handling: Avoid bending or folding the cards; even though they’re not as fragile as fancy casino stock, repeated bending can create small creases that affect shuffling and dealing.
- Sleeves: If you want to keep them looking pristine, consider sleeves that fit typical Bridger/Romme formats. Sleeves protect backs from scuffs if you’re the type to play on busy cafe tables where a stray mug could nudge a deck.
- Cleaning: Wipe with a dry cloth if needed. Avoid liquids and harsh cleaners that could affect the finish.
