## What's inside a Draft Booster (M21)
Draft boosters are designed to fuel your drafting sessions. Each booster contains 15 cards, with a blend of commons and uncommons, a rare or mythic rare, and at least one card with a foil treatment or a showcase version in some printings. Core Set 2021 is known for a streamlined drafting experience and a bright color-tone with a few surprises. You will typically see reprints and a handful of new cards that push your decisions in the middle of the pack rather than forcing you down a single strict plan.

In three words: color, tempo, and chance. The six-box case ensures you can test a dozen strategies without reusing stale openings. It is a lab of mana curves and misplays, which is to say: perfect for content creators.

## Drafting in M21: Colors, Balance, and Strategy
Drafting Core Set 2021 is about balance and tempo more than about crushing your opponent with a single combo. The color pairs are generally well-supported, and the removal suite is robust enough to control the mid-game traffic without destroying board development.

Here are a few drafting takeaways:

- Early picks: identify the most efficient two-drop or removal spell; these often anchor your deck.
- Color-splash decisions: sometimes you pass on a strong card to land a reliable two-color pair with a good mana base.
- Removal vs. threats: you will want enough removal to survive the early assault and enough threats to catch up in the mid to late game.
