The key is to avoid vigorous scrubbing that can cause color transfer or fiber damage. For tougher stains, you’ll often find guidance recommending a gentle fabric-safe cleaner. The beauty of Pivot Rain is that it forgives a lot of human error: no immediate need to call a professional cleaning service after a single glass of red wine. Measuring, Ordering, and Installing: A Practical Guide
-----------------------------------------------------
1) Measure twice, order once. Draw your room on grid paper, mark existing furniture, and measure the clearances to doors and walkways. If you’re in a climate with humidity fluctuations, consider a rug with a slightly higher pile to account for expansion and contraction.
2) Decide on the backing and pad. A non-slip pad can extend the rug’s life and keep it from migrating across the floor. It also protects wood floors and reduces noise in small apartments.
3) Understand the return policy. Custom sizes might have different return constraints than standard 5x7 rugs. Read the fine print before committing to a long-term arrangement.
4) Prepare for installation. Rolling a rug into place with one person can be a challenge. A second pair of hands or a helper makes aligning corners with walls a breeze.
