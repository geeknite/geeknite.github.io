Cleaning a card holder is generally straightforward. Remove cards, wipe with a damp cloth, and let it dry. Avoid letting liquids spill onto the card trays, which could warp or degrade the card edges. If you’re worried about wear and tear, choose a model with a washable, removable liner for easy cleaning.
