## Durability and Cleaning: A Real-World Test

Cleaning is where the magic of a good rug meets real life. The Aubrey Taupe Rug is designed to handle spills and pet accidents with grace. For routine maintenance, a standard vacuum with a low-pile setting will do the trick. If a spill happens, blot—don’t rub—using a clean cloth. For tougher stains, a mild detergent solution or a gentle enzymatic cleaner tends to do the job. Avoid bleach, hot water, or harsh chemicals that could degrade the fibers or alter the color.

Sun exposure is a factor. Prolonged direct sunlight can lighten taupe over time, so you may want to rotate your room layout or use blinds to keep the color stable. If you’re a sun worshiper who wants to keep a rug pristine, consider using a UV-blocking window treatment or simply rotate your furniture so the sun does not beat on the rug’s face all day.

If you have pets, you’ll be delighted to learn the rug resists staining better than many other textiles, but no rug is miracle-proof. Treating stains promptly is a sure-fire way to keep it looking fresh for years. For routine pet shedding, regular vacuuming and a quick shake-out in a dry, sunny spot will keep hair to a minimum on days you’re hosting game night.

In terms of wear, the Aubrey Taupe Rug doesn’t pretend to be a luxury item with a fragile soul. It’s designed to weather entryway chaos, kids drawing with markers that your dog insists are tasty, and the occasional coffee ring that only appears after a late-night debugging session.

Maintenance Hacks and Routine Care

To keep your rug looking fresh, incorporate the following into your weekly routine:
