## Maintenance and Durability
### Cleaning
Spills clean up with minimal fuss. Blot, don’t rub, and use a mild detergent if necessary. For stubborn stains, a light steam clean works wonders without removing the pattern from the fibers. Vacuum on a low setting, and rotate the rug every few months to maintain even wear. The backing stays flat and does not curl at the edges, which is a big win for those of us who hate wrestling with a curling rug while trying to watch a movie.

### Stain Resistance and Longevity
The fabric is designed to be stain resistant; it doesn’t guarantee magic, but it behaves well under the most suspicious of coffee spillages and occasional red sauce experiments. With proper care, the Mineral 555 should maintain its color and texture for several years, which is not the worst retirement plan for a piece of floor-art. The edges stay sealed, so you don’t have to worry about unraveling threads when you step on it with your winter boots.
