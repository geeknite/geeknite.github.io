### Maintenance: Caring for Your Chips and Cloth
Like any fine-tuned gadget (or a beloved collectible), maintenance matters. The Cardinal Games chips take well to a quick wipe-down with a dry cloth to remove fingerprints or crumbs from a late-night snack. If you’re dealing with spills, a damp cloth with a touch of mild soap is usually enough to remove sticky residues. Avoid using harsh cleaners that could degrade the surface finish; you’re not restoring a vintage aquarium in there, just trying to keep the chips looking fresh.

The felt mat, if included, should be cleaned with the same gentle approach you’d use on a tablecloth. Some kits include a small brush or microfiber cloth; use it to keep the surface free of lint, as lint can interfere with chip counts and cause your shuffles to appear more dramatic than they actually are. In short: treat your set as you would a well-loved video game controller—clean, cool, and a little bit stylish.
