## How to Play at a Glance
Exploding Kittens is a take that turns the table into a drama board. The rules are simple enough to be taught in under five minutes, but the strategic depth is where the real magic happens. The core idea is to avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten card while using Defuse cards to neutralize a threat. In the bundle you will often see the following flow

1. The deck is shuffled and each player receives a few Defuse cards plus a handful of regular cards. The setup is quick, which means the first round is practically started before anyone has finished their snack.
2. Players take turns performing actions from their hand. These actions can affect the order of the deck, force opponents to draw cards, or save themselves from a near catastrophic draw. The tension rises as the deck grows thinner and the probability of pulling an Exploding Kitten increases.
3. If a kitten is drawn, you must use a Defuse card or suffer the consequences. If you do use Defuse, you get to place the Exploding Kitten back into the deck strategically, often turning the game into a battle of wates and wiles as you try to predict where it will appear next.
4. The last surviving player wins. There is drama in the victory lap as the winner walks with the confidence of someone who just survived a cat themed apocalypse, while losers are left to contemplate their life choices.

In the bundle, the extra cards may alter the odds, create new opportunities for bold plays, and introduce quirky twists that keep everyone guessing. It is a party game in the truest sense, where the story is as important as the score, and where the cards serve as punchlines rather than mere tools for winning.
