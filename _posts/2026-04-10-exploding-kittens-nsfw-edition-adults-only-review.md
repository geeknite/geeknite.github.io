## How to play in 10 seconds or less

Take turns drawing cards from the deck, trying to avoid the exploding kitten. If you draw it, a misstep occurs and you re-draw from the deck after a penalty is paid. Some cards allow you to skip your turn, pass the danger, or sabotage others. The game ends when all but one kitten-killer is left standing — or rather, when the deck has exhausted itself and someone wins by surviving the longest, or by crafting a moment in which the room erupts with hilarity and the kind of chaotic energy that makes you question your life choices.

The NSFW flavor adds a few new cards that truly push boundaries. These can be excellent icebreakers or terrible ideas depending on who is at the table.
