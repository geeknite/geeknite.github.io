## What is Marvel's Controller BAF?

Marvel's Controller BAF is part of the Marvel Legends Series, where multiple figures come with pieces to build a larger figure. In this case, the larger figure is the Controller, a supervillain from the Marvel universe known for his mind-controlling abilities and creepy fashion sense. So, basically, he's your next cosplay inspiration.

### The Basics

In the Marvel Legends Series 2022, you get to collect individual characters, each with a piece of the Controller. Once you've collected them all, you can assemble the Controller and unleash his mind-controlling powers on your other action figures (or just stare at him in awe).

### Components

The collection includes:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed action figures of your favorite Marvel characters, like Iron Man, Thor, and Black Widow.
- **Controller Pieces**: Each figure comes with a piece of the Controller, from his head to his legs. Yes, even villains need legs.
- **Accessories**: Weapons, shields, and other cool stuff to make your action figures feel like they're ready for a Marvel movie.

**Pro Tip**: Don't lose the Controller's head. Trust me, a headless Controller is not as intimidating.

Assembling the Controller is a blend of strategy, patience, and a bit of luck.
