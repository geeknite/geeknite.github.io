## Practical Tips for Getting the Most Out of the Set

- Start with a simple scenario: place the Special Guest in a spotlight location and let the team coordinate a rescue to reach them. This gives players room to practice positioning and timing without turning the session into a straight up sprint.
- Make painting a bonding ritual: use the miniatures as an excuse to gather around with brushes and swap tips. If motivation wanes, the guest looks require a little extra treatment to shine on camera, which can be the perfect push to finish a coat.
- Build character moments: let the guests have distinct personalities that influence your session. Small banter exchanges around their quirks help create a running gag about the dramatic flair of the haunt and the eternal fashion sense of the undead.
- Use tokens for micro objectives: trigger guest abilities on specific actions to structure round based mini objectives. This adds a layer of player driven drama without overshadowing core mechanics.
