## Rug Pad and Footing: Security and Longevity
Let’s talk about the underrated hero of any rug setup: the rug pad. A quality pad helps with several tasks:
- Prevents slipping and shifting, which is especially important in high-traffic rooms or households with curious pets.
- Adds extra cushioning, improving comfort underfoot and reducing noise from footfalls.
- Improves wear patterns by distributing weight more evenly across the rug.

If you’re pairing the Paragon Seafoam Wool Area Rug with a hardwood or tile floor, a medium-density felt or rubber rug pad is a wise choice. For carpeted rooms, a thinner pad reduces the risk of the rug “bopping” when you move from one side to the other during those late-night carpet dances.

## Maintenance: How to Keep Seafoam Beautiful Without Turning It Into a Science Experiment
Care for wool: keep calm and vacuum regularly. Here are practical steps:
- Vacuum weekly with a brush attachment, working in the direction of the pile to avoid fuzzing the surface.
- Rotate every few months toeven out wear and prevent high-traffic paths from creating visible impressions.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth. For tougher stains, use a wool-safe cleaner (test in a hidden corner first) and avoid harsh solvents that can damage the wool fibers.
- Sun exposure can fade color over time. If your rug sits in direct sun, consider window treatments or periodic rotation to limit sun bleaching.
