## Care, upkeep, and tips to extend the life of your beige beauty
Beige is forgiving but not invincible. Here are practical tips to keep NAT620B-6SQ looking presentable for as long as your budget allows:

- Vacuum regularly: A weekly pass can keep dust and cat hair from building up into “character lines” that resemble abstract art. If you have pets, invest in a vacuum with good suction and a pet hair attachment. The goal is a clean base that doesn’t reveal your family’s snack history to every visitor.
- Immediate spill protocol: Blot, don’t scrub. Use a clean cloth to absorb the liquid and avoid pressing it deeper into the fibers. For stubborn stains, test a mild solution in an inconspicuous corner first. Beige loves a quiet life, not a dramatic dye drama.
- Rotating the rug: Every few months, rotate 90 degrees to distribute wear evenly. It’s a tiny rotation with big impact: it prevents visible wear patterns in high-traffic zones.
- Protective measures: Consider a rug pad to cut down on slippage and extend wear. A good pad improves comfort and reduces the likelihood of edges curling, which is a small but real buzzkill in a cozy setup.
