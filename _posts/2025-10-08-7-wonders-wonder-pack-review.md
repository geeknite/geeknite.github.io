## What is 7 Wonders Wonder Pack?

### Overview

The **7 Wonders Wonder Pack** is an expansion to the popular *7 Wonders* board game. It introduces new wonder boards, each with unique abilities and strategies to explore. This expansion is perfect for those looking to add variety and new challenges to their 7 Wonders gameplay.

### Components

The Wonder Pack includes:
- **Four new wonder boards**: The Great Wall of China, Stonehenge, Abu Simbel, and a Manneken Pis board (yes, you read that right).
- **New cards**: Additional cards that integrate seamlessly with the base game and other expansions.
- **Detailed artwork**: Each wonder board is beautifully illustrated, bringing even more visual appeal to your game nights.

## How to Play with the Wonder Pack

### Setup

Setting up the Wonder Pack is straightforward. Simply shuffle the new wonder boards and cards into your existing *7 Wonders* components. Players can then choose or randomly draw their wonder boards as usual.

### New Wonders

Each wonder board in the Wonder Pack comes with unique abilities that can significantly alter your strategy:

- **The Great Wall of China**: This wonder requires strategic placement of its stages and offers defensive bonuses.
- **Stonehenge**: Focuses on resource management and can provide extra resources each turn.
- **Abu Simbel**: Allows players to entomb leaders for victory points.
- **Manneken Pis**: A whimsical wonder that adds a humorous twist to the game with its unique abilities.

## Gameplay Experience

### Strategy and Tactics

The new wonders in the Wonder Pack offer fresh strategic options and challenges.
