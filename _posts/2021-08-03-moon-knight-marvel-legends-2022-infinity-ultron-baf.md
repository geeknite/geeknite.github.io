## The Basics

### What is a Build-A-Figure (BAF)?

Before we get into the nitty-gritty, let's talk about what a BAF is. A Build-A-Figure is a series of collectible action figures that come with parts to assemble a larger figure when you collect them all. In this case, we're talking about the Infinity Ultron BAF, which is epic enough to make any Marvel fan drool.

## Moon Knight: The Details

### Sculpt and Design

The Moon Knight figure is a masterpiece of design. The sculpt is detailed, capturing the essence of the character with precision. From the crescent moon on his chest to the intricate details on his cape, this figure is a visual treat. The paint job is immaculate, and the articulation allows for dynamic poses. Want Moon Knight to look brooding on a rooftop? Done. Want him to strike an action pose mid-battle? Easy.

### Accessories

Moon Knight comes with an array of accessories, including his signature crescent darts and staff. These accessories are not just throw-ins; they are detailed and add to the versatility of the figure.
