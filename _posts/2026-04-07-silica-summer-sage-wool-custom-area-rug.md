Durability, Care, and Maintenance
Maintenance is not a ritual reserved for monks; it is a practical routine that suits busy households. Vacuum regularly with a low-suction setting to avoid pulling fibers. For spills, blot gently with a clean cloth, then let air dry. Avoid aggressive rubbing or scrubbing that can drive stains deeper. For tougher stains, professional cleaning is recommended to protect color and fiber integrity. Wool cleans well when treated with mild detergents designed for delicate fabrics. Under a rug pad, the backing lasts longer and the surface remains more resilient to wheel chairs and desk chairs in a home office.

Sustainability and Ethical Considerations
Wool is a renewable fiber that can be sourced in ways that support sustainable farming and mindful manufacturing. The Silica Summer Sage Wool Rug is produced with attention to responsible sourcing and artisan craft where possible. While a rug can be a luxury, the choice of wool offers the potential for longer wear life, lower replacement frequency, and a smaller environmental footprint than some synthetics when cared for properly. If ecological impact matters in your decor decisions, you can request information on the supplier's sourcing and milling practices during the ordering stage.

Quick Start Care Routine
