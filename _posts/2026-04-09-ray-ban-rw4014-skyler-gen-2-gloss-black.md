The Skyler Gen 2 performs admirably in terms of distortion control. The frame curvature keeps the lenses aligned with your eyes across most of the field of view. ### Handling and Vision in Low Light
As with most polarized sunglasses, you’ll want to remove them if you step into a dimly lit space or a cafe with mood lighting. Polarized sunglasses tend to darken the ambient light, which is great for daytime glare but not ideal for indoor browsing antics. The Skyler Gen 2 remains practical for outdoor use and won’t force you to remove sunglasses the moment you walk into the shade, but don’t expect the same performance inside a dim restaurant or cinema.
