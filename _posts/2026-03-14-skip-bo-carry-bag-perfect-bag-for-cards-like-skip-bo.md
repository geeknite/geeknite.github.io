## Cleaning and maintenance

The bag is pretty forgiving when it comes to accidental spills and the occasional coffee splash. A light wipe down with a damp cloth usually suffices, and a quick air dry keeps humidity at bay. If the bag takes on a deeper stain, a gentle soap and rinse in lukewarm water followed by drying in a well-ventilated area should do the trick. The fabric choice matters here: if you treat it well enough to avoid excessive abrasion and scrapes, you can expect the bag to maintain its shape and color for a long time. If you are in a real monster mode cleaning spree, you can even run a mild machine wash on a delicate cycle, provided you remove all decks first. Your mileage may vary, and we recommend checking the care instructions from the manufacturer for any model specific guidance.
