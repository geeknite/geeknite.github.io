### Care and maintenance tips

- Vacuum regularly with a standard vacuum to keep the pile looking fresh. For high-pile portions, use a nozzle without a rotating brush to avoid fiber damage.
- For spills, blot, don\'t rub. Use a mild detergent solution only on stains you can identify. Test on a hidden corner to verify colorfastness first.
- Address edge fraying early. A small fray can be trimmed or tucked under a sofa leg, depending on the look you want. If the edge becomes too unruly, consider a simple rug binding solution from your local craft store.
- Rotate the rug every few months. The color pattern will age gracefully with usage, and you will avoid the dreaded high-traffic zone fade.
