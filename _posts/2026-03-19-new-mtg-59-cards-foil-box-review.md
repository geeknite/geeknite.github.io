## The Box vs The Booster Experience: A Quick Comparison
### Box Experience: Calm and Curious
- Pros: Reduced risk of accidental overprinting; a snapshot of MTG at a moment in time; potential for a nice foil moment.

- Cons: Potentially more expensive per card; pressure to pull the one card you actually want in the packs; higher probability of duplicates for casual collectors.
