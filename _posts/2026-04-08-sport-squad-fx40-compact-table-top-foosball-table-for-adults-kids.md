## Maintenance Tips and Care

- Keep the playing surface clean with a microfiber cloth after each session. Crumbs and dust can impair ball movement over time.
- Lightly oil the rods every few weeks if you play heavily; this keeps the action smooth and reduces noise.
- Check the leg or stand attachments if you move the FX40 around frequently. A loose bolt here and there is normal, just tighten them before you host a tournament.
- Use a soft brush to clean the area around the scoring rails to prevent debris from affecting accuracy.
