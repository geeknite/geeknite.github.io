## Cleaning and maintenance tips: keep it looking sharp without turning life into a chore
- Regular vacuuming: A quick weekly pass removes loose dirt and keeps the pile looking tidy.
- Spot cleaning: For spills, blot and use a mild dish soap solution with a soft brush. Rinse and air dry.
- Deep cleaning: Every now and then, a gentle wash with mild detergent and a hose rinse followed by air drying helps rejuvenate the color and texture.
- Stains: Be proactive with stains. If you have pets, a quick wipe-down after a spill prevents odors from becoming a weekend legend.
- Preventive care: Use outdoor rug pads or non-slip backing (the Midnight Blue rug generally comes with something in that vein) to minimize shifting and wear at the edges.
