### Why PET?

PET stands for polyethylene terephthalate, a material with a long track record in consumer packaging. It’s typically BPA-free, relatively easy to clean, and holds up well to daily office life. In a desk divider, PET panels often feel less intimidating than glass and less brittle than hard acrylic while still offering a crisp, clean visual look that fits modern office aesthetics. If you crave a more eco-friendly vibe, PET is a decent compromise between durability and recyclability, depending on your local recycling rules.
