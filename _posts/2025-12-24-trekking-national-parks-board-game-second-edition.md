## What is Trekking The National Parks?

Trekking The National Parks is a family-friendly board game where players face off to visit the most national parks, collect stones, and earn points. With stunning artwork, cool park cards, and a plethora of stones to gather, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep you coming back for more.

### The Basics

In Trekking The National Parks, players race to visit different national parks by moving their pawns across a beautifully illustrated map of the USA. Each player starts with a hand of trek cards and collects stones as they travel. The goal? To earn the most points by visiting parks, collecting stones, and achieving bonus objectives.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Map Board**: A lovely map of the USA featuring all the national parks.
- **Park Cards**: Cards representing different national parks, each with unique artwork and trivia.
- **Stones**: Collectible stones that add to your score.
- **Trek Cards**: Used for moving across the map.
- **Player Pawns**: Cute little pawns that represent you on your journey.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your dog near the stones. Those paws are the real stone thieves.

## How to Play

### Setup

Set up the game board, shuffle and deal trek cards, and place stones on the map. Choose a starting park, and you're ready to begin your adventure.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw trek cards, move their pawns, and visit parks. The synergy between the cards and the map is where Trekking The National Parks shines.

### Visiting Parks

Visiting parks is straightforward but strategic. Players must plan their moves and manage their cards to visit as many parks as possible and collect stones. Remember, strategy and a bit of luck will take you far!
