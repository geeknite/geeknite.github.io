## Maintenance and care: keeping the glow alive
Maintenance is less a labor of love and more a ritual of prevention. Here are practical moves:
- Clean surfaces with a mild soap solution and rinse with fresh water. Avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade wicker fibers or wood finishes.
- Wipe up spills promptly to prevent staining and moisture retention in cushions (if included).
- Periodically inspect the joints for loosening screws and tighten as needed.
- If you live in a climate with heavy winter weather, consider covering the set or moving it to a sheltered spot to preserve the finish.

A well-kept Lilith set will retain its color and shape longer if you treat it like you would a beloved gaming chair: give it a bit of occasional maintenance love, and it will continue to serve you for seasons rather than weeks.
