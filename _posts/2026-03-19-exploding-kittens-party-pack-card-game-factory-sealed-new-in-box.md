## Tips and strategies for the table
Strategy in Exploding Kittens Party Pack is less about perfect math and more about social engineering and timing. Here are some practical tips that work well in most playgroups:
- When in doubt, spread your defuse options early but conserve one or two for late game. The endgame can be brutal and you want a safety net.
- Card counting matters more than you think. Keeping track of how many defuse cards have shuffled back into the deck helps you gauge risk in late round reveals.
- Use the chaos cards to break up alliances that feel too comfortable. A well-placed card can turn friendly rivals into bitter enemies who suddenly remember a forgotten beef from last week.
- When you have a strong hand, consider aggressive plays that push damage onto others rather than yourself. The party energy appreciates bold moves that create memorable moments, even if you lose the hand.
- Don’t be afraid to explain your play with theatrical flair. The social moment matters as much as the mechanics, and a well-timed joke can shift the room’s energy more than a perfect hand combo.
