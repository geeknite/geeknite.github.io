### Maintenance and longevity tips
Store the decks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. A small card clip or a protective case can help prevent warping if you travel with them. Wipe away fingerprints with a soft microfiber cloth if needed, and avoid over-oiling your fingertips because a little moisture can help with grip, but too much can smear the back design.

### Handling in different environments
If you play in humid climates, your cards may feel slightly stickier at first. After a few shuffles, the stock should loosen a bit, returning to that familiar Bicycle feel. In dry air, you may find the edges snap off a little more crisply, which some players prefer for the dramatic opening display. Either way, these two decks are built for practical use rather than fragile decoration.
