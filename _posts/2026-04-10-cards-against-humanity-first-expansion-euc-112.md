## Setup, rules, and accessibility

### Quick setup
Setting up the CAH expansion is fast. The cards are clearly labeled with color-coded backs (depending on edition; ensure you know your deck). Shuffle the new white cards into your existing white-card pool; shuffle the black cards into the black-card pool. Deal and begin the rounds. The simplicity is one of the reasons CAH remains popular: you don\'t need a manual longer than a grocery list to start playing.

### Rules nuance
The rules for CAH remain intentionally simple: players choose cards, a judge picks, the winner keeps a card.
