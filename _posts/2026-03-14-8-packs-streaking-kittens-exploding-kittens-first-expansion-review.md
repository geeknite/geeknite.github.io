## Gameplay experience with the expansion
Let us talk about hands, tempo, and the kind of hours you should expect after you crack open eight packs.

### How to integrate with the base game
If you are new to Exploding Kittens, start with the base game to learn the core flow. Once you are comfortable, slide in the Streaking Kittens packs. Do not do this mid game if you want to avoid tears. The recommended approach is to rotate packs into the game, so you can gradually assess how each new card type shifts the dynamic. In practice, eight packs can be used to craft a bespoke deck tailored to your group size and play style. If you want maximum chaos, shuffle all new cards with the base deck and see what happens. If you want to keep things manageable, add a subset of new cards first and expand as your group becomes more confident with the added complexity.

### Interaction with core mechanics
The core rules of Exploding Kittens revolve around avoiding exploding cards by playing Defuse style cards and using secondary effects to complicate the draw order. Streaking Kittens introduces new ways to alter draw order, steal from another, or force a swap that can completely rewrite a round. Expect moments of pure improvised theater as you try to read the room and guess which player is most likely to bluff not only their hand but their level of enthusiasm for chaos. The hallmark of this expansion is that it rewards creative misdirection and play that leverages the psychological edge of the table.

### Practical play tips
- Watch for the hidden power of kitten cards; some trigger on your own turn, others on someone else’s. Use this to force someone else into a difficult choice.
- Manage your defuse resources carefully; eight packs increase the number of near misses you will experience per round. You want to survive long enough to deploy a perfect kitten combo at the moment of maximum chaos.
- Save wildcards for late rounds where a single card swing can decide the entire table’s mood and the group’s snack choice for the next break.
- If you enjoy memes, lean into the humor of the kitten captions. It softens the blow of a brutal misplay and keeps the night light and fun.

### Role of table dynamics
This expansion rewards players who read the room and adapt on the fly. If your group tends to tilt easily, you may want to dial back the number of wildcards in the deck to keep things from spiraling into melodrama. Conversely, if your crew thrives on chaos and banter, eight packs give you enough material to fuel at least three unforgettable moments per game.
