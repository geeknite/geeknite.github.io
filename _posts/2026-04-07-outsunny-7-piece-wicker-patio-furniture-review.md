The upside of assembly is the satisfaction of saying, with a modicum of pride, that you now own a genuine backyard fortress. ## Weather resistance and maintenance: will it survive the great outdoors?

Outdoor furniture is a commitment to weather, not a one-off purchase. The Outsunny wicker is designed to handle sun, humidity, and a quick afternoon rain. The cushions’ covers are typically removable and machine washable, which is a blessing in disguise when your kids decide the cushions are their canvas for juice art. Quick tip: when you know a storm is coming, bring the cushions inside or use furniture covers. It will extend the life of the cushions and prevent that rush of regret when you discover mildew forming on the underside after a particularly damp week.

The wicker, made to resist UV deterioration, will show some signs of aging after several seasons, mainly fading and a slight patina that says you actually used the set instead of just letting it sit there looking pretty. If you want to maximize longevity, consider storing cushions in a dry space during the winter and giving the frame a light rinse once in a while to remove dust and pollen. A gentle soap solution and a soft brush will do wonders, followed by air drying. Do not use harsh chemicals that could degrade the wicker or the cushion fabric, because this isn’t a chemistry set; it’s a set of comfortable seating for your backyard empire.
