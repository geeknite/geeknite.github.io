## Durability and Care
Durability-wise, the case is built to travel. The materials are resilient enough to survive a few table drops and accidental thrown into a bag under a bus scenario. The magnet latch remains reliable after repeated use; it takes a little effort to open when you’re balancing a hot beverage and two kids' homework in the same moment, but that’s a feature not a bug. Important care tip: store the card piles in their assigned slots to prevent movement when the case is jostled during transport. The mod deck’s action cards are more delicate than the basic stock cards; return them to their slots after each session to reduce the risk of bending or creasing.
