## Longevity, storage, and care
Deluxe minis reward long-term care. A light wash to remove any flash marks, a proper priming layer, thin layers of paint, and a sealing varnish will go a long way toward preserving Parrillo’s clean lines and Guiton’s luminous color. UV exposure is your enemy here; store the set away from direct sunlight when not in use to prevent color shift. If you are planning to reuse the sculpts for conversions or to repurpose them for another game system, careful storage and labeling will help you keep track of the more fragile bits and the character accents that define each model’s personality.
