## Overview

The **Brio Classic Figure 8 Set** is a timeless wooden train set that comes with enough pieces to create a charming figure-8 track. It includes a wooden engine, two wagons, a couple of trees, and a station platform. This set is perfect for kids aged 2 and up, and let's be honest—it's pretty fun for adults too.

### What's in the Box?

- 1 Wooden Engine
- 2 Wagons
- 14 Track Pieces
- 2 Trees
- 1 Station Platform

### Endless Creativity

The figure-8 track layout is just a starting point. The pieces are compatible with other Brio sets, allowing for endless creativity and expansions. Your kid’s imagination is the only limit!
