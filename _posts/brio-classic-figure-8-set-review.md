## What's in the Box?

When you open the box, you'll find:

- 14 pieces of wooden track

- Train station
- A bridge

The simplicity of the Brio Classic Figure 8 Set is one of its greatest strengths. It's easy to assemble, and kids can quickly start playing without needing a degree in engineering.

## Quality and Durability

Brio has been a trusted name in wooden toys for decades, and the Classic Figure 8 Set lives up to the brand's reputation. The wooden tracks are smooth and fit together effortlessly, ensuring that the trains glide along without a hitch. The paint is non-toxic and durable, meaning it can withstand the rough and tumble of playtime.

### Pros

- **High-quality wood**: Solid and durable, perfect for energetic kids.
- **Easy assembly**: Even a monkey could do it, but don't let monkeys near your toys.
- **Expandable**: Compatible with other Brio sets, so your train empire can grow!

### Cons

The Brio Classic Figure 8 Set offers endless fun and creativity. Kids can create different layouts and imagine various scenarios, from simple train rides to daring bridge crossings. It's also a great way for kids to develop fine motor skills and spatial awareness.
