## Maintenance and Longevity
Caring for your chips and case is easier than maintaining a fantasy football lineup. A simple routine keeps them looking fresh:

- Wipe chips with a dry microfiber cloth after use. If you’re worried about oils and residues, a damp cloth (water only) works wonders—just don’t soak the foam inserts.
- Avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the chip inlays or the acrylic case’s seals. If you need deeper cleaning, remove the foam and wash the case separately with mild soap and water, then dry thoroughly before reassembly.
- Keep the set out of direct sunlight for extended periods. UV exposure can dull color over time, particularly on the white and yellowish hues that creep into chip palettes from age and sunlight.
- When transporting, reuse the foam inserts to prevent chips from rattling during transit. A little control goes a long way toward preventing chips from ending up as a replacement coffee stirrer in someone’s cup.
