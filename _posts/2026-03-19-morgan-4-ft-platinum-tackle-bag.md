### Maintenance and Care Tips
To keep the Morgan 4 Ft Platinum looking and performing at its best, here are some practical care tips:
- Rinse after saltwater exposure. Salt is sneaky; a quick rinse helps prevent corrosion on the metal parts.
- Dry thoroughly before storage. For best results, open the bag and let it air-dry fully to prevent mildew inside.
- Check zippers and clips periodically. A little lubrication on zippers keeps them smooth, and securing clips prevents accidental openings during transit.
- Keep it out of extreme heat for long periods. Excessive sun and heat can degrade fabrics and impact the waterproofing layers over time.
- Organize with the internal trays. The more you arrange your gear, the longer the bag lasts under normal use. A tidy bag laughs at the chaos of a lurching boat.
