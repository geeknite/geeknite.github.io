## Practical care tips: how to protect and present this card
- Display case: Use a high-quality, UV-stabilized display case. The flame holo can be particularly reactive to direct sunlight, which can wash out the foil sheen over long periods.
- Sleeves and mats: Place the card in a rigid sleeve with a protective matte sleeve to reduce edge wear. A soft, non-abrasive sleeve helps maintain the card’s pristine the texture and edges.
- Handling: Always handle Gloom with clean hands or wear cotton gloves if you’re a serious curator who reorganizes their collection weekly. The artwork deserves delicate care—your investment in display quality will pay off in the long run with fewer micro-scratches and less surface scuff.
- Lighting: Use angled LED lighting that emphasizes holo patterns but avoids overheating. The subtle glow should feel cinematic, not scorching.
