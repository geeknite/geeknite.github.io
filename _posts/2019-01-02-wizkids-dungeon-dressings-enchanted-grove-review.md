## The Basics

### What is Enchanted Grove?

WizKids Dungeon Dressings: Enchanted Grove is a set of beautifully crafted miniatures and terrain pieces designed to bring your tabletop adventures to life. From ancient trees to mystical flora, this set provides everything you need to create an immersive forest environment for your RPG sessions.

### Components

The set includes an array of detailed miniatures, including:
- **Ancient Trees**: Towering trees with intricate bark textures and lush foliage.
- **Mystical Flora**: Various plants and fungi with a magical touch.
- **Forest Floor Pieces**: Terrain pieces for creating paths, clearings, and underbrush.
- **Special Features**: Enchanted stones, fairy rings, and other magical elements.
