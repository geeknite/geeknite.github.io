## Setup for the ultimate party night
### Space, timing, and flow
The Mega Pack shines when you have a room full of people with different playing speeds. The general guidance is to prep a central table with enough space for a couple of card stacks, plus space for drinks and snacks that do not threaten the cardboard ecosystem. The rounds move fast, which is essential for a party vibe. A good rule of thumb is to allow 20 to 25 minutes for a full session with 6 to 10 players. If your group tends to strategize the same way as a group chat, you might want to set a timer to keep everyone honest and to avoid a single player monopolizing the big decisions.

### House rules and gentle chaos
One of the best aspects of the Mega Pack is how adaptable it is to house rules without losing its core rhythm.
