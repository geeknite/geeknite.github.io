## What is Secrets of the City?

In City of Mist, players are Icons—mythic beings masquerading as everyday folks in a modern city. The Secrets of the City PDF promises to expand that sandbox by adding new investigations, new icons, and a pile of GM-ready tools to weave intrigue without turning your table into a paperwork nightmare. In plain terms: this is not a reprint with glitter; it’s a toolbox stuffed with secrets you can actually use during a three-session arc or a one-shot where nobody remembers which coat they wore last game.

## Content Deep Dive

### What’s New in the PDF
Secrets of the City adds several layers to your existing City of Mist games:
- New Investigations: Each investigation thread is designed to feel cinematic while remaining manageable in a standard 90 to 180-minute play window. These aren’t your typical “find the clue” hunts; they’re designed to escalate with the city’s secrets and the Icons’ dual identities.
- Icon Expansions: Expect a handful of new Icons, plus expanded moves and play prompts for existing Icons. The focus is on balancing power with vulnerability, a classic City of Mist trade-off that keeps sessions tense and emotionally charged.
- Locations and Factions: A handful of intricate locations are not just a backdrop but a character in themselves. Each location comes with a micro-manifesto—a little narrative engine you can slide into any scene to provoke drama.
- Secrets and Foci: The PDF leans into the theme of secrets within the city itself. Think foci and motivations tailored to secretive factions and shadowed identities, which lets you tailor sessions to your players’ deepest secrets and the city’s darkest corners.
- GM Tools: Quick encounter prompts, handouts, and a set of “scene seeds” designed to spark inspiration when you’re staring at a blank map and a coffee cup that’s starting to resemble a mini sculpture of disappointment.

If you were hoping for like-for-like reprints of existing material, you’ll be pleasantly surprised by the fresh angles. The designers lean into moral ambiguity, letting players decide how much truth they’re willing to reveal and what sort of masks they’re ready to inhabit when the city demands it.

### The Mechanics Corner: How It Plays
City of Mist is known for its blend of modern noir with mythic epicness. Secrets of the City respects that balance and adds a few tasteful tweaks rather than a heavy-handed overhaul. You’ll notice:
- The Icon System Stays Agile: New prompts for creating an Icon or tweaking an existing one ensure that you can adapt to different storytelling rhythms without breaking the internal logic of the mythic urban fabric.
- Investigation Overlay: The added investigations come with an optional overlay that keeps the pace brisk. It’s not a forced “roll a dozen dice” mechanic; it’s more like a well-timed montage where you know what beat comes next.
- Heroic versus Human Tension: The new content nudges players toward choosing what they want to protect, rather than simply collecting clues. This makes scenes feel personal and memorable, which is quintessential for City of Mist fans.

## Play Style and Session Flow

### A Night that Feels Cinematic
One of the strongest appeals of City of Mist is its cinematic tempo. Secrets of the City leans into that with structured scene progressions that mimic a TV episode arc. You’ll get:
- A gentle ramp-up in tension that crescendos into a satisfying reveal.
- Quick scene hooks that reduce prep time and boost improvised storytelling.
- Thematic threads that encourage players to reveal identity without breaking immersion.

If you run long campaigns, there’s enough material to seed multiple arcs without rewriting your entire city map. If you prefer short, punchy sessions, you can cherry-pick the best investigations and stitch them into a single evening of neon-noir mayhem.

### How to Run It: Quick Start Plan
1) Pick an Icon pair for each player and spend five minutes on a quick identity sketch. 2) Choose one investigation that aligns with your group’s vibe. 3) Use the scene seeds to outline three beats: setup, escalation, and reveal. 4) Sprinkle Location cards around the table and let the city’s secrets push players toward a personal revelation. 5) Conclude with a moral choice that branches into consequences you can track across sessions.
