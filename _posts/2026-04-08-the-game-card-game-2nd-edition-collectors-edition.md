## Strategies and tips for new players

- Focus on early tempo: the first few turns are usually about setting up your engine. Don’t overcommit to a big combo if you can’t defend it in the next round. The game punishes you for overextension with clever card interactions that punish hubris.
- Deny your opponents access to key cards: a simple but effective tactic is to anticipate which cards your opponents will want and decide whether to scoop them up or block them. If you’re playing with experienced players, this is where your table talk becomes your superpower.
- Use the environment to your advantage: some event cards alter the board state in ways that open up new synergy windows. Be mindful of timing; you’ll usually want to trigger those events when you have several adjacent effects ready to chain.
