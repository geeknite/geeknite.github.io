## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity

Because these are standard Bicycle stock, a little care goes a long way. Keep them in a dry environment; avoid excessive humidity, heat, and direct sun. If you’re a heavy practitioner, consider a small box or a card case for storage to minimize edge wear. Clean hands are important; oils from your skin can accumulate on the card faces and reduce grip over time, so quick hand-wash before practice sessions can help preserve them.

Cleaning: If you ever need to refresh the card faces, a gentle wipe with a clean microfiber cloth can help. Never use solvents on the card faces, and avoid submerging the cards in water; the air-cushion finish doesn’t like baths.

Storage: A card box or a small display case is ideal. Keep the two decks in their own sleeves or keep the color-coded approach intact by storing in the same order you’d use on stage or on a desk.
