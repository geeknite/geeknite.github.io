## Cleaning and maintenance

### Routine care
Vacuum or sweep regularly. The flat weave doesn’t trap everything as aggressively as a shag, which means fewer high-maintenance cleaning sessions and more time for nerdy hobbies.

### Stains and spills
Blot spills promptly. For stubborn stains, use a mild detergent and a soft brush. Rinse with water and air dry. If you’re unlucky enough to spill red wine, remember that patience plus a gentle touch beats panic and a scrub brush every time.

### Drying and storage
If you’re putting the rug away seasonally, roll it rather than folding to prevent creases. Store in a dry place to avoid mildew. When you bring it back out, give it a few hours under indirect sun to recover its shape before you re-lay it.

### Eco-friendly care options
For the environmentally conscious nerds among us, consider using minimal water plus a mild, eco-friendly cleaner. Drying outdoors is ideal if you have a sunny day; if not, use a well-ventilated indoor space with a fan to speed up air circulation.
