Maintenance and Cleaning
------------------------
Like any playground equipment, the trampoline needs occasional care. The mat is relatively easy to wipe down with a damp cloth. If you have a spill, a quick wipe and a gentle air-dry keep it in good shape. Check the joints and frame for signs of looseness after heavy use; tighten any screws as needed, and keep an eye on whether the legs show signs of bending under repeated jumps. The folding mechanism should be inspected periodically to prevent any pinch points.

Storage tips:
- Fold and store in a dry area to avoid rust on the frame.
- If you have pets (or mini-spirits of chaos), lock away toys around the trampoline to prevent them from turning the surface into a curious obstacle course.
- Use a dedicated cover to protect the mat from dust and sunlight exposure that can cause color fading over time.
