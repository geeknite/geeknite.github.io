### Everyday Wear and Tear

### Cleaning Tips and Care Routine
Care is key to extending any rug’s life. For spills, act fast. Blot with a clean cloth or paper towel, avoiding vigorous rubbing that can embed stains deeper into the fibers. For larger messes, check the manufacturer guidelines, but most plush rugs like this respond well to a gentle soap-and-water approach or a machine-wash-friendly option if the backing and fibers are rated for it. Vacuum regularly using a low to medium suction setting to prevent the pile from getting crushed. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners unless the stain is something truly dramatic and prize-worthy.
