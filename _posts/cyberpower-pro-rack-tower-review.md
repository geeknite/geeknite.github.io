### Line Interactive Topology: Smooth Operator

This UPS uses line-interactive topology, which means it can handle minor power fluctuations without breaking a sweat. It steps in to regulate voltage without having to switch to battery power, ensuring a smooth and continuous power supply to your precious electronics.
