The Formula 1 Core Edition Starter Pack adds a lightweight layer of “elite” flair to that classic rhythm. The exact rules can vary by edition, but the intent is to speed up the pace and inject some race-day energy into the table. Expect cards that might trigger a “race pause” or a “turbo boost” moment, which can shake up the usual savor of counting and color-matching.

Key aspects you’ll want to know:
- There are standard color/number matches, just like classic UNO.
- The elite cards introduce temporary effects that ripple through the turn order or the available moves.
- You’ll still need to declare UNO when you’re down to one card, with the added theater of a track-side celebration in card art.
