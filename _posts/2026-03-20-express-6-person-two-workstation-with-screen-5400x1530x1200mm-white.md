## What is the Express 6 Person Two-Workstation?

## Assembly Experience: How Easy Is It to Build a Team-Size Desk Setup?
Assembling a 5.4-meter long workstation is a test of patience and trust in your DIY skills or your office’s procurement team. The good news: the package includes labeled panels, screws, and a set of instructions that doesn’t require a doctoral degree in architecture to understand. The not-so-good news: there are a lot of pieces and a lot of screws. Expect a few hours of assembly if you have a small team and a couple of Allen wrenches with varying degrees of magnetic strength. We did the assembly in a healthy, non-competitive spirit, which involved a playlist, some snacks, and the occasional “maybe skip the last two screws because we might be prematurely installing the neighbors’ desk instead of ours” moment.

### Step-by-Step Pro Tips
- Create a clean floor plan first: lay out panels to ensure fit without forcing corners to contort to space.
- Sort hardware by size and type: you will thank yourself during mid-assembly stress-testing when you don’t have to rummage through a bag for the correct screw diameters.
- Use a buddy system: two people working in tandem reduced the build time from a potential half-day to a light afternoon project.
- Don’t over-tighten: you want rigidity, not cracked laminates. A gentle, consistent tightening pattern beats gorilla grip every time.

## Ergonomics and Comfort: Why Your Back Will Thank You (Eventually)
We all know ergonomics aren’t a one-magic-pill fix. It’s a suite of adjustments, habits, and sometimes, a better chair. The Express workstation provides generous legroom, and the 1200 mm height usually yields comfortable elbow alignment for most users when paired with an adjustable chair. However, it’s important to note that the desk itself is static in height. If your team comprises people of varying heights, you’ll want to offer ergonomic chairs with proper seat depth adjustability and a monitor arm setup that can adapt to different postures.

### Work Surface and Keyboard Access
A 5400 mm length offers plenty of room for dual-monitor setups, notebooks, and a growing collection of sticky notes that somehow survive the cleaning crew. The depth around 1530 mm is generous enough to place keyboards, mice, sketchpads, and cups without feeling like you’re playing a space-age game of Tetris. People who enjoy standing desks might be disappointed that the base is fixed height. However, the surface remains spacious enough for sit-stand trays or secondary adjustable platforms if you want to create an ergonomic hybrid zone.

## Cable Management and Cleanliness: The Untold Hero of Office Chic
