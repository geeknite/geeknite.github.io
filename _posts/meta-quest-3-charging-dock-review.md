Performance-wise, the Meta Quest 3 Charging Dock does not disappoint. It charges your headset and controllers quickly and efficiently. In our tests, the headset reached full charge in under 2 hours, and the controllers took about an hour. That’s plenty fast for those marathon VR sessions.

The dock is designed specifically for the Meta Quest 3, ensuring perfect compatibility. It comes with a USB-C cable, so all you need to do is plug it into a power source, and you’re good to go. No need for additional adapters or cables. It’s a true plug-and-play solution.

While the dock is optimized for the Meta Quest 3, it also supports charging other devices with a USB-C port. So, if you have other gadgets lying around, you can give them a quick charge using the dock. It’s versatile and convenient.
