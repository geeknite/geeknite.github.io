## The humor mechanism explained

Cards Against Humanity thrives on the tension between the blank space and the embedded meaning of the prompt. The edition's commentary prompts take on the role of a quirky professor guiding you through the mental machinery behind the humor. You will learn to appreciate timing, surprise, and the way cultural references interact with personal experience to ignite the laugh response.

### The meta-joke layer
This is not about the jokes on the cards; it is about the jokes about jokes.
