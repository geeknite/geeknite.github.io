## Safety and care

With great size comes the necessity of careful handling. Oversized cards can slip on slick floors, and their bulk may cause minor injuries if someone flings a card with too much gusto. A gentle approach with young players helps keep the energy at a safe level. Cleaning is straightforward: use a soft cloth to wipe away fingerprints and spills.
