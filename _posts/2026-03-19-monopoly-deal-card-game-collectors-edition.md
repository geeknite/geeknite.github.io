## How Monopoly Deal works: a quick refresher

If you are new to the game, here is the core flow in plain Geeknite terms: you are trying to collect three complete property sets. Your deck is a mix of property cards, rent cards, and action cards that let you steal, swap, or block. The goal is to assemble property sets by playing cards from your hand to your property collection while using action cards to hinder others and to defend your own plans. The Collector's Edition does not alter the core mechanics. It improves the experience through better materials and presentation, which can hasten setup time and reduce wear during intense play.

Turn structure is simple: draw, play up to three cards, and resolve actions that advance your property building or hinder your opponents. The tension comes from balancing aggression with defense, deciding whether to chase a risky steal or to shore up a nearly complete set. The pace can swing fast if players start chaining rental income and property takeovers, so the mood can grow from amicable to fierce in the blink of an eye.
