## Practical Tips for Your First 5 Plays

- Start with a pre-set mission deck to reduce setup time and avoid analysis paralysis.
- Use a timer for each round (60–90 seconds) during the early games to keep energy high and prevent late-night fatigue.
- Encourage everyone to narrate actions with silly voices. It helps with engagement and doubles as character development for players who feel left out.
- Rotate the role cards so everyone gets a turn at steering the ship and prevents the same two players from hogging all the excitement.
- Don’t stress about perfection. The joy of family games often comes from imperfect decisions that lead to hilariously memorable stories. If someone makes a questionable call that pays off, celebrate the chaos and pretend you planned it that way all along.
