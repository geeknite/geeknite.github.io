## Practical Tips for Your First 5 Plays

- Start with a pre-set mission deck to reduce setup time and avoid analysis paralysis.
- Use a timer for each round (60–90 seconds) during the early games to keep the energy high and avoid late-night fatigue.
- Encourage everyone to narrate their actions with silly voices. It helps with engagement and doubles as character development for players who feel left out.
- Rotate the role cards. This ensures everyone gets a chance to steer the ship and prevents the same two players from hogging all the excitement.
- Don’t worry about perfection. The joy of family games often comes from imperfect decisions that lead to hilariously memorable stories. If someone makes a questionable call that somehow pays off, celebrate the chaos and pretend you planned it that way all along.
