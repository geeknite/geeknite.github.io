### Care and Maintenance

Cleaning a rug is not the glamorous part of homeowner life, but it is the part that keeps you from invoking a vacuum-fight with a Roomba. For a rug marketed as back-stain resistant, the best practice is still to address spills—blot, don’t rub, and use a mild detergent if needed. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners that can degrade the fibers or steal the rug’s color. A gentle vacuum with a rotating brush helps keep the pile tidy without flattening the area that deserves attention—the design accents.
