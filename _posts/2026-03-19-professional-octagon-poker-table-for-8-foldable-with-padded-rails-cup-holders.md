After the final hand, wipe the table with a soft, damp cloth. Don’t saturate the surface—excess moisture can cause staining or warping over time. If you notice any stubborn marks, a small amount of mild soap on the cloth will do the trick. Dry immediately with a clean towel to prevent moisture from soaking into seams. The padded rails can be cleaned with a microfibre cloth; if you want to wipe down the padding’s edges, use a gentle cleaner that won’t degrade the synthetic covering. When you’re done, fold the legs and tuck the table away in its storage area.
