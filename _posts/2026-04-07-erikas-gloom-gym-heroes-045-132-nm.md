### Care, storage, and preservation tips
Preserving vintage cards like Erika's Gloom requires a light touch and some basic habits. Start with a high-quality card sleeve that fits the neutrally sized standard cards from that era. Consider investing in a binder with acid-free pages to avoid chemical residues or discoloration creeping into the edges. Humidity is the silent enemy; you want a controlled environment with an ideal humidity around 40-50 percent. For display, consider a vertical stand with a matte display frame so glare does not wash out the fine details of that vintage art.
