## Care and Longevity Tips
To get the most out of your 64 sleeves and their glorious Dragon Ball art, a few best practices will keep them looking fresh:

- Store sleeves away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading or gloss cracking over months of display. A dedicated play area with good ambient lighting is ideal.
- Keep sleeves clean by brushing off dust and lint with a soft cloth. Don’t rub aggressively on the artwork; you’re protecting tiny guardians, not erasing their lore.
- Avoid bending sleeves during transport; a sleeve tin or deck box helps maintain shape and prevents creases along the seams.
- If you notice edge wear on a few sleeves, rotate them out of circulation ahead of a tournament block to prevent a cascade of damaged cards.
