## How the Wild Foil Flux actually plays
If you are familiar with UNO you know the core rules. Add the core concept of Flux and Wild Foil and you get a new hybrid experience. The flux element could be a randomly triggered effect that changes the rules for a given turn or a small flip in the deck that introduces a new draw format. For example you might trigger a flux moment that makes all players draw two cards next turn or flip the color of play for one round. The Wild Foil aspect affects certain face cards or special action cards, giving them a shimmering finish and sometimes added in game effects like drawing extra cards or forcing a color change. The combination of these features means the flow of the game can shift quickly from a predictable routine into a chaotic almost party game like tempo. If you dislike chaotic games you may want to tailor the rules to keep flux to a minimum but this is the heart of the product a chaotic delight that keeps players engaged.

## Collectors guide and care tips
To keep your SP Chicago Bears Swift card and the rest of the deck in pristine condition you want to store the set in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight.
