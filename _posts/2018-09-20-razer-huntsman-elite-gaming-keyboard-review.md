## Switches: Clicky Heaven

The star of the show here is Razer's opto-mechanical switches. These bad boys offer an actuation point of just 1.5mm and require only 45g of force. The result? A typing and gaming experience that's both responsive and clicky. If you love the sound of keyboard clicks (and if your neighbors don't mind), the Huntsman Elite will be music to your ears.

### Pros and Cons of Opto-Mechanical Switches

#### Pros:

- **Speed**: Faster actuation than traditional mechanical switches.
- **Durability**: Rated for up to 100 million keystrokes.
- **Sound**: Satisfyingly clicky.

#### Cons:

- **Sound**: Can be too loud for shared spaces.
- **Feel**: Not everyone loves the clicky experience.

## Performance

### Gaming

In gaming scenarios, the Razer Huntsman Elite excels. The responsive switches provide an edge in fast-paced games, ensuring that your actions are registered instantly. The keyboard also includes dedicated media controls and a multi-functional digital dial, adding extra convenience for in-game adjustments.

### Typing

For typing, the Huntsman Elite is a dream. The keys are well-spaced, and the clicky feedback makes typing a joy. It's like having a mini celebration with every keystroke. However, the loud clicks might not be ideal for late-night typing sprees in a shared household.

## Software and Customization

Razer Synapse software allows for extensive customization of the Huntsman Elite. You can reprogram keys, create macros, and customize the RGB lighting to fit your gaming aesthetic. The onboard memory can store up to five profiles, so you can take your settings with you wherever you go.

## Durability and Build

The aluminum top plate ensures that the keyboard can withstand the rigors of intense gaming sessions. The keycaps are made of high-quality plastic that resists wear and tear, and the braided cable adds an extra layer of durability.

## Final Thoughts

The Razer Huntsman Elite is a top-tier gaming keyboard that offers excellent performance, build quality, and customization options. It's perfect for gamers who crave speed and responsiveness, as well as those who love the sound of clicky switches. However, if you prefer a quieter typing experience, you might want to look elsewhere.
