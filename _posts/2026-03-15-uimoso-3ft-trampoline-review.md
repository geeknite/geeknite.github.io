## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity
Like any good gadget, a little maintenance goes a long way. Here are our practical maintenance tips:
- Regular inspection: Check for loose bolts, torn padding, and zipper integrity. A quick once-over every couple of weeks can prevent minor issues from becoming major ones.
- Cleaning: Wipe down the mat with a mild soap solution and a damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the fabric or padding. Let it dry completely before storage or reassembly.
- Storage: If you’re storing the unit, fold it according to the manufacturer’s instructions and keep it in a dry, cool place. Moisture can encourage rust on metal joints and mold on padding, so you want to stay vigilant here.
- Weight management: Adhere to the weight limits and recommended use. Pushing past limits might save a few minutes of fun but could shorten the device’s lifespan—and that’s a heartbreaker for everyone involved.

With light maintenance, the Uimoso 3FT Trampoline should serve you well for many seasons of indoor giggles and chore-avoidance gambits.
