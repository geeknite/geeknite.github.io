## Care tips for long-term happiness with your rug
- Vacuum regularly to keep dirt from grinding the fibers into a dull narrative. A quick weekly run does wonders.
- Treat spills as soon as possible. Blot, don’t rub, to avoid pushing stains deeper into the weave.
- For stubborn stains, a mild detergent mixed with water works well. Test a small, inconspicuous corner first to confirm colorfastness.
- If used outdoors, bring the rug inside during extreme weather to extend its life. A simple rotation schedule can prevent wear patterns from forming in one spot.
- Consider a rug pad if placing on hard floors to enhance stability and reduce sliding.
