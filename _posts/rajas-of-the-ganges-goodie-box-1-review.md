The additional province and river tiles introduce more strategic options and replayability. Whether you're expanding your territory or navigating the Ganges, these tiles keep the game fresh and engaging.

### Elephant Tokens

Who doesn't love elephants? These tokens can be used to gain extra actions or resources, adding a layer of strategy. Just remember, with great elephants comes great responsibility.

### Karma Cards

Karma cards offer a new way to influence the game, providing opportunities to gain bonuses or hinder your opponents. They're a fun twist that adds unpredictability and excitement.

### Spice Exchange

The Spice Exchange allows players to trade spices for victory points or resources. It's a simple yet effective mechanic that adds depth to the game's economy.

### Extra Dice

More dice mean more fun, right? The extra dice included in the Goodie-Box 1 give players additional options and flexibility during their turns. Just be careful not to roll a 1, or you'll be stuck with bad karma!
