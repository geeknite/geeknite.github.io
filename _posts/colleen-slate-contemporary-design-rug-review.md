## Maintenance

Maintaining the Colleen Slate rug is relatively easy, thanks to its polypropylene construction. Regular vacuuming and occasional spot cleaning will keep it looking fresh and clean. However, be cautious with spills and stains, as the lighter areas can show dirt more easily.

### Cleaning Tips

1. **Vacuum Regularly:** Keep your rug looking pristine by vacuuming it at least once a week. This will prevent dirt and debris from settling into the fibers.
2. **Spot Cleaning:** For any spills or stains, use a mild detergent and warm water to gently clean the affected area. Avoid harsh chemicals that might damage the fibers.
3. **Professional Cleaning:** Consider professional cleaning once a year to maintain the rug's appearance and longevity.

**Pros:** 
- Easy to clean 
- Low maintenance

**Cons:** 
- Light colors show dirt more easily 
- Requires regular upkeep
