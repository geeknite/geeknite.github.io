## How this expansion changes the core game

If you already own the base game, this expansion is an easy add on that increases variety, not complexity. The core mechanic remains unchanged: a dealer reveals a black card and players submit white cards that they think best fit, with the dealer choosing a winner. The 112 new cards expand the creative space in a meaningful way. Here are the top effects you can expect from integrating this expansion into your regular CAH rotation: 

- More prompts means more potential scenarios you never thought you would face during a casual game night. The new black cards can push the conversation into surprising directions and encourage players to improvise quickly. 
- A refreshed white card pool gives you newer punchlines and setups that learners of humor can discover while still letting you recycle classic lines from your existing deck. The expansion is not about replacing older content but about enriching the mix. 
- Increased replayability. If you have played with only the core set for a while, adding new content extends the lifetime of your CAH nights by giving you fresh combinations every round. 
- Positive synergy with other expansions. CAH thrives on mixing cards from different sets. The second expansion is designed to play nicely with both the core set and the first expansion, enabling hybrid round formats that feel new and exciting. 

With all that said, there are a couple of caveats to keep in mind. More cards can extend the length of rounds if your group enjoys long, involved setups and elaborate punchlines. If you are prone to analysis paralysis or like to discuss the ethics of the jokes before playing them, you might want to preselect a handful of cards to avoid getting stuck in an overly theoretical loop. For most groups though, the expansion adds enough energy to keep rounds brisk and entertaining.

## Setup and playing with the expansion

Setting up this expansion is straightforward for anyone familiar with the core game.
