## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by M3 Chip

![M3 Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/macbook-air-m3/a/images/overview/m3__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}m3-chip)

The heart of the MacBook Air M3 is its groundbreaking M3 chip. With a 10-core CPU, 12-core GPU, and 18-core Neural Engine, this powerhouse can handle everything from video editing to playing the latest games without breaking a sweat.

### Battery Life

Apple claims up to 20 hours of battery life on a single charge, and in our tests, it lived up to the promise. Whether you're coding, designing, or binge-watching your favorite shows, the MacBook Air M3 will keep you going all day and night without needing to recharge.

## Software and Features

### macOS Monterey

The MacBook Air M3 comes with macOS Monterey, packed with new features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and redesigned Safari. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the MacBook Air stand out from the competition.

### FaceTime and Webcam

The 1080p FaceTime HD camera ensures you look your best on video calls. Coupled with the studio-quality three-mic array, it's perfect for remote meetings or catching up with friends and family.
