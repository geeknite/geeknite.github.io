## Care, maintenance, and future-proofing
If you plan to keep this disc around for a while, a little care goes a long way. Here are practical tips that will help extend the life of your used PS3 disc:

- Clean before play: use a microfiber cloth and a gentle circular motion from the center outwards. Avoid wiping toward the inner hub in a way that could scratch the data layer.
- Gentle handling: keep the disc away from heat and direct sunlight. The case may be physically sturdy, but the disc is still vulnerable to warping if left in a warm car or a sun-bleached shelf.
- Storage: store discs upright in their cases. If you’re assembling a collection, keep a soft divider to avoid case-to-disc contact while jostling in a backpack.
- Digital backups: if you own a legitimate digital copy or if you can legally create a backup, you’ll reduce wear on the physical disc and preserve your physical asset’s value.
