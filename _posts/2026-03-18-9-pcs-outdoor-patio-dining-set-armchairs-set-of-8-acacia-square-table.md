## Weather Resistance and Maintenance: How Long Will It Last You?
Outdoor furniture is a craft of patience and stubborn optimism. The acacia wood is generally good with periodic oiling and protection from prolonged wetness. The metal frame, if properly coated, handles rain without turning your patio into a metallic sculpture of rust.

Maintenance tips:
- Clean spills quickly to prevent staining.
- Use a mild soap solution for cleaning; avoid strong solvents.
- Apply a protective sealant or oil to the wood every season or two, depending on climate.
- Store cushions indoors during heavy rain or snow to prolong fabric life.

If you’re in a region with heavy humidity or salty air, you’ll want to pay extra attention to the finish. The wood can develop micro-scratches that add character but could also invite weathering if neglected. A simple routine of cleaning, drying, and oiling keeps the story on your side rather than against you.
