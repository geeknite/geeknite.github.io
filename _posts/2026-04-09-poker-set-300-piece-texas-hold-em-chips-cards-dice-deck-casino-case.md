- Each player receives two hole cards, and then five community cards appear in stages: flop, turn, and river.
- Use any combination of five cards to form the best hand. The usual hand rankings apply: from high card to royal flush.
- Betting rounds occur before the flop, after the flop, after the turn, and after the river.
