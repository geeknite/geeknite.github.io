### Quick Setup Tips
- Unpack on a clean surface to avoid losing small parts in the carpet abyss.
- Use a level to ensure the table sits evenly; a slight tilt can ruin an entire match’s credibility.
- Attach the rods with care. Overtightening is a danger; you want smooth turns, not a squeaky, squealing chorus when you spin the players.
- Install the end pieces and ensure the ball return area isn’t blocked by any packaging scraps or your own big ego.
