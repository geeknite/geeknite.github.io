### Maintenance and care

Card preservation matters more in vintage sets than in modern reprints. Here are a few practical tips:

- Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight to prevent color fading and cardboard warping.
- If you see edge wear, consider a light card-saver to slow further damage during play.
- Keep the box contents together with a simple rubber band or a small divider to simulate that original box organization.
- Avoid excessive moisture, which can warp the stock piles and cause sticking during shuffles.

If you re serious about display value, consider placing the box in a display case with a UV-filtered light to minimize color drift and keep the 80s vibe intact for years to come.
