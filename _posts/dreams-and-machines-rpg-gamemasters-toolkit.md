## What is the Dreams and Machines RPG?

Dreams and Machines is a role-playing game where players navigate a world that blends the surreal and the technological. Imagine a place where your wildest dreams can come to life but are constantly under threat from rogue machines and mysterious entities. It's like Inception meets Blade Runner, but with a lot more dice rolling.

### Components

The Gamemaster’s Toolkit comes with:
- **Core Rulebook**: The foundation of your campaigns, filled with lore, mechanics, and more plot twists than a soap opera.
- **Maps and Grid Sheets**: Beautifully designed maps to help visualize the dreamscape and mechanized regions.
- **Character Sheets**: Track your players’ progress, health, and inevitable descent into madness.

## The Gameplay

Setting up a campaign with the Gamemaster’s Toolkit is like preparing for a theatrical production. You need to lay out the maps, distribute character sheets, and familiarize yourself with the core rulebook. It sounds daunting, but once you’re in the flow, it becomes second nature.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn in Dreams and Machines is packed with possibilities. Players can explore dreamlands, battle mechanical creatures, and interact with NPCs. The toolkit provides guidelines and tips for managing these actions seamlessly.

### Combat

Combat in Dreams and Machines is both strategic and unpredictable. With the help of the Toolkit, you can create encounters that challenge your players while keeping them on their toes. The NPC and Monster Cards are particularly handy for adding variety to your battles.
