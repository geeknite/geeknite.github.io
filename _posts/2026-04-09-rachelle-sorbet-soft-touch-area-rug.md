## Keeping It Clean and Fresh

### Cleaning Schedule and Tips

A simple weekly vacuum with a sifted beater bar is enough for day to day. For spills, blot gently and treat stains early. A mild detergent solution on a soft cloth tends to lift most common household mishaps without fading the color. If you have pets, you may want to schedule a periodic deeper clean to reset fibers and remove any odors that can creep in during long habitually cozy evenings. Always check rug care labels if you plan a steam clean, and test on a small patch first to ensure color stability.

### What to Avoid

Avoid harsh chemicals, bleach, and rough scrubbing that can damage fibers and dull color. A scrub brush with stiff bristles is not your friend here. The goal is to lift stains while preserving the gentle texture that makes the Sorbet rug feel luxurious underfoot. You will also want to avoid exposing the rug to direct sun for extended periods, as long exposure can lead to color fading over months of sunlit living.
