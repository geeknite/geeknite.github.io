## Practicality and Lifestyle Fit
### Indoor vs Outdoor use
The Garden Constellation rug claims to be suitable for both indoor and covered outdoor spaces. If you are using it outside in direct sun for extended periods, expect some mild fading over many months; this is not unique to this rug, but to any outdoor friendly textile. For interior use, it works as a dramatic anchor for a seating area, a reading corner, or a stage for a cat to pretend to be a small royal.

### Cleaning and maintenance
For day to day cleaning, a simple vacuum on a gentle setting and spot cleaning with mild detergent works best. Treat spills as soon as possible, because waiting turns a quick wipe into a mini science experiment. For stubborn stains, a damp cloth with a touch of dish soap should remove most marks. If you have pets, you might see occasional shedding on the rug fibers; this is normal and easily managed with a lint roller or a quick vacuum.

### Wearing and weather
The rug is designed to handle a range of temperatures and humidity levels; however, if you plan to use it outdoors in winter, make sure your patio is not in a zone of persistent puddling. A rug pad under it can help prolong its life and keep the edges flat, which is important if your fire pit friends decide to host a drum circle close to the edge of the rug.
