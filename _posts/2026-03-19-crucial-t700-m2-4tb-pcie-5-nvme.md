## Performance tuning and practical notes
One of the joys of Gen5 storage is the potential to tune performance with a few well-chosen settings rather than deploying a lab-grade storage array. In practice, you can optimize your experience by enabling aggressive caching in your OS, ensuring TRIM is active, and making sure you have enough spare blocks so that the drive doesn t thrash during heavy use. If you are into gaming, make a habit of installing your most frequently played titles to the T700 to benefit from the best possible streaming and texture loading. If you do 4K video editing or large-scale color grading, the higher sustained throughput will be noticeable when working with local caches and project libraries.

During our testing, thermals remained reasonable with typical chassis cooling. The drive did not exhibit persistent thermal throttling in our normal test environment, though it did warm up when subjected to extreme sustained workloads in a closed case without airflow. This is nothing to panic about, but it is a reminder that airflow and a reasonable heat sink matter when you push data to the limit. If your case is a toaster with fans, you might want to reposition the drive or add a passive heatsink to help it stay in the sweet spot longer. The bottom line: keep the drive comfortable and it will keep you productive.
