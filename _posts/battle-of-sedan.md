## The Basics

### What is Battle of Sedan?

Battle of Sedan is a strategic board game that transports players to the Franco-Prussian War of 1870. You take on the role of military commanders, directing armies, planning maneuvers, and engaging in intense battles. Designed by renowned game designer Gustav von Boardgamehausen, this game captures the essence of historical warfare while providing a fun and engaging gameplay experience.

### Components

The game comes with a beautifully designed board representing the battlefield of Sedan, complete with detailed terrain features. The components include miniatures of soldiers, artillery, and cavalry units, as well as various tokens, cards, and a comprehensive rulebook. The high-quality artwork and sturdy pieces ensure a visually stunning and immersive experience.

**Fun Fact**: The game includes a special "Napoleon III" miniature that will make you shout "Vive l'Empereur!" every time you see it.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Battle of Sedan is like preparing for a grand military campaign. Lay out the board, position your troops, and get ready to strategize. The setup is straightforward, but you'll need to plan your initial moves carefully to gain the upper hand.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players perform actions such as moving troops, attacking enemy units, and using special abilities. The game uses a combination of cards and dice to determine the outcomes of battles, adding an element of luck to your strategic decisions.

### Strategy and Tactics

The game shines in its strategic depth. As a commander, you must carefully plan your maneuvers, anticipate your opponent's moves, and adapt to changing battlefield conditions. The terrain features play a crucial role in your tactics, providing opportunities for ambushes and defensive positions.
