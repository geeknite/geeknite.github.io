### Care and maintenance tips
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent warping and color bleed.
- Keep them in their tuck boxes when not in use to minimize wear and tear.
- Wipe the faces gently with a soft microfiber cloth to remove natural oils; avoid harsh cleaners.
- If the finish starts to feel a touch tacky after heavy use, a light, careful dusting with a card-specific cloth can help restore glide without changing the feel.
- Avoid exposing the decks to extreme temperature swings which can lead to warping or edge cracking over time.
