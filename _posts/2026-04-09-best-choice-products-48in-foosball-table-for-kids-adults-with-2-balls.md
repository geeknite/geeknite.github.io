## Durability and maintenance: Keeping the magic alive

Like any piece of durable entertainment hardware, this table benefits from a little maintenance. Here are some practical tips to keep the foosball table aging gracefully:

- Keep it clean. Wipe down the surface after games to prevent sticky residues that accumulate from snack breaks. A damp microfiber cloth works wonders.
- Check bolts and screws. A loose bolt can turn a thrilling tiebreaker into a wobbly disaster. A quick once-over after a few sessions helps avoid long-term wobble.
- Protect the playing surface. Avoid placing heavy objects on top or near the table that could cause dents or warping. It’s not a piano—treat it like a cherished gaming console.
- Store the balls safely. If you have kids who treat the word “ball” as a trigger for squealing, consider a small storage solution so the balls don’t vanish into the sofa cushions every other game.

Maintenance may feel like a chore, but it’s the price you pay for consistent slam-dunk-winning rememberable moments when guests show up and demand a rematch.
