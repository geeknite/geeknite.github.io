### Cleaning and care rituals
Test colorfastness by dampening a white cloth and blotting a corner. If the cloth picks up dye, you are in for a learning experience about color transfer during spills. For routine maintenance, a light vacuum with the beater off will keep the pile looking fresh. For spills, blot, blot, blot with a mild detergent solution. Avoid harsh cleaners that could disrupt the dye balance. Pet owners may want to define a designated spill-occur area at the rug edge to confine chaos and keep your sofa unscathed.

Rotation every few months helps reduce wear patterns and keeps the edge flaw from becoming a daily focal point. Synthetic fibers respond well to regular care, and with a reasonable upkeep plan, you can expect several years of service from a budget-friendly rug that wears like a badge of honor rather than a badge of shame.
