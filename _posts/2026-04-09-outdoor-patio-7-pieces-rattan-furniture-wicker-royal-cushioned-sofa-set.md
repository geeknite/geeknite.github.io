## Materials, durability, and weather resistance
Rattan and wicker are not just fashion statements; they are the armor and spine of the set. Resin wicker offers a natural look with resin-like durability. The UV inhibitors help prevent color fade when the sun decides to stage a long, dramatic performance in your yard. The frame’s metal is treated to be corrosion resistant, which matters if you live by the coast or somewhere that enjoys dramatic weather swings.

Weather resistance is a big deal for outdoor furniture. The cushions, while cozy, are not meant to stay permanently out in a downpour. Most users store the cushions in a dry place or use protective covers during heavy rain. In a pinch, you can flip the cushions to the dry side if the rain comes unexpectedly while you are hosting a backyard bash. For long-term exposure, consider applying a breathable fabric protector to the cushion covers and periodically checking for wear on the wicker weaves. The goal is to keep the set looking fresh and feeling sturdy regardless of the season.

Maintenance is not glamorous, but it is essential. A quick wipe-down after a spill or a light brushing to remove leaves and dirt is enough for most days. For more stubborn grime, a mild soap solution and a soft brush will do. Avoid abrasive cleaners that can dull the finish. If you have a particularly sunny balcony, you might want to rotate the chairs occasionally to even out any subtle sun fading. In other words, give your set some love, and it will give you many seasons of comfortable lounging and good vibes.
