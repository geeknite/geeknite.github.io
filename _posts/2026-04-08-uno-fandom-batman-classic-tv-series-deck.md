- Thematic flavor is woven into action icons and card flavor text. The deck uses stylized hero-vs-villain cues that give players quick visual reads on penalties, power plays, and strategic opportunities.

### Optional tweaks for a smoother night
If you’re hosting a game night with mixed familiarity or a larger crew, you can adopt light house rules to balance pace and interaction:
- Bat-Signal visibility: When the Bat-Signal card is played, everyone reveals one card from their hand. The player with the most Batman-related cards (as judged by the table) can choose a minor penalty—like extra draws or a skipped turn—for someone else. It’s playful, not punitive.
