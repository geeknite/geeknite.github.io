## How to Attach and Use (Practical Steps)

1) Adjust the shoulder strap to your torso length so the scabbard sits comfortably at your preferred height. 
2) Feed the blade into the mouth and secure with the retention strap. Make sure the blade is fully seated and the mouth is closed snugly. 
3) Test the draw. Do it slowly at first to ensure smooth fusion between blade and keeper. 
4) Move around to test the weight distribution. If you notice significant tugging or neck strain, tweak strap tension and maybe the scabbard position. 
5) For longer storage or travel, ensure that the blade is fully retracted and the mouth strap remains fastened to prevent accidental drawing.
