## Care and Maintenance

### Everyday Care

- Vacuum regularly to remove surface dirt and keep fibers looking crisp.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper into the pile.
- Use a mild detergent solution for spot cleaning if needed, and test on a small inconspicuous area first to ensure colorfastness.
- Rotate periodically to maintain even wear and prevent color migration from sunlight or indoor lighting.

### Stain Handling

For common culprits like coffee, juice, or pet accidents, a quick-blot approach combined with a gentle cleaning solution tends to work well. Do not soak the rug, as excess moisture can affect the backing and lead to warping or mold risk. If a stain proves stubborn, consider consulting a professional cleaner who specializes in area rugs with synthetic blends. Your wallet will thank you for not treating the rug like a sponge for every culinary misadventure.

### Protection Measures

- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and add a layer of cushion underfoot. A pad also helps with longevity by reducing friction against hard floors.
- Keep direct sunlight exposure in check to minimize color fading over time. If your space is sun-drenched, consider rotating the rug or using window treatments to balance the color integrity.
