### Power and Safety: What If the Power Goes on Strike?
Surge protection is more important than you might think. A reliable desk with a built-in power strip can save you from data loss due to power spikes and can simplify the “grounds and grounding” drama that often accompanies PC builds. Look for:

- A surge rating of at least 900–1200 joules for basic setups; higher if you’re running high-end workstations or a NAS.
- Adequate space for a power brick or two without crowding other outlets.
- A secure mount for the strip so it doesn’t wobble loose when you slide in a chair or adjust LED brightness.

Additionally, check for an accessory like USB charging ports or a USB-C PD port to power your phone or a microSD card reader. It’s a small convenience that reduces desk clutter and adds a modern touch to your workflow.
