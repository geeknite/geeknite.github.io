## Care and Maintenance
### Everyday Cleaning
Vacuum regularly, with a beater bar setting kept on low to avoid pulling fibers. A quick pass across the surface once or twice a week should keep it looking sharp. For spills, blot immediately with a clean cloth, starting from the outside and moving toward the center to avoid ring stains. A mild detergent solution is generally sufficient; avoid harsh chemicals that can dull the pattern or degrade fibers over time.

### Stain Removal and Precautions
Treat common spills (coffee, juice, wine) promptly. Blot, don’t rub, to prevent color transfer or fuzzing of the pile. For stubborn stains, use a little white vinegar solution and blot again, then rinse with water and blot dry. If you’re dealing with pet messes, a quick enzyme cleaner is a reliable choice. Always test in an inconspicuous corner first to ensure color stability.

### Rotation and Longevity
Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear and sun exposure. This is especially important in rooms with direct sunlight, as UV rays can fade certain colortones over time. The geometric pattern helps camouflage minor wear; in practice, the rug will still look intentionally styled even as it shows a few miles of wear around the edges.
