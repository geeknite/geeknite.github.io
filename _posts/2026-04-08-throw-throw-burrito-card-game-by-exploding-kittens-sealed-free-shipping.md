## How to play: a quick walkthrough

The game unfolds in rapid rounds where each player tries to collect artifact sets while navigating the danger of burrito maneuvers. Here is a compact walkthrough to give you a sense of the rhythm:

- Players take turns drawing and playing cards from their hands. Cards include artifact actions, defensive moves, and burrito challenges.
- When an artifact set is completed, the player places the set face up in front of them. The goal is to collect a certain number of artifacts before everyone else.
- Burrito cards flip the moment someone triggers them. These can create a mini duel where players fling the plush burritos toward each other, hoping to miss themselves and tag a rival. The result is a pause followed by a surge of laughter and a few scattered burrito plushies wandering across the table.
- If you miss repeatedly, you might find yourself the target of a particularly dramatic burrito barrage, which adds an extra layer of adrenaline to an already chaotic game state.

The pacing is the biggest strength here. You want to keep things moving, you want to keep everyone engaged, and you want to avoid the pitfall of one player dominating the table for too long.
