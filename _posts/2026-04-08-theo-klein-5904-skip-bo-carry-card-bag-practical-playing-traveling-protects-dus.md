There’s a small extra pocket suitable for dry erase markers or a mini rulebook. It isn’t exactly a micro-gaming station, but it’s perfectly adequate for keeping essentials at hand during a layover or a quiet time in a family car ride.

For tidy stacks, the bag shines when used with a couple of small organizers or a separate pouch for coins and score sheets.
