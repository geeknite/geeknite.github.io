## Rules and Quick Start Guide

Starting with a sealed pack means you should first shuffle it into the base deck. Then distribute your normal starting hand as if nothing changed, because you did not break the flow of the game. The expansion adds new cards into the mix, but the setup stays approachable. Here is a quick start guide to get you up to speed without leafing through a long rulebook:

1) Shuffle Barking Kittens with Exploding Kittens base deck and any other expansions you are using.
2) Deal a hand of cards to each player as you would in a normal session.
3) Place the Exploding Kitten deck in the center, with any normal defuse mechanics in place.
4) On your turn, you may draw or use any number of action cards from your hand based on the new interactions included in Barking Kittens.
5) If you draw an Exploding Kitten and have a defuse card, you can use it and continue; if you cannot defuse, you are out.
6) The game continues until only one player remains. The chaos is real, the dogs are barking, and the cats are judging your moves with a passive aggressive meow.
