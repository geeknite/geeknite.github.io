## Strategy Tips: Getting the Most Out of Streaking Kittens
If you’re aiming to extract maximum value from this expansion, here are some practical tips that work across groups while still keeping the humor in play:

- Read the room before chasing a streak. Momentum can be your friend, but chasing a streak into a trap is how you end up with a dramatic, meme-worthy loss. If you sense that the table is on edge, switch to a safer play until you have a clearer window.
- Use Mischief Cards to disrupt the leader, but mind your own position. Sometimes the best move is to keep others guessing rather than swinging for the fences. Remember that the laughing audience at your table is also a player in the game.
- Team play, sort of. In a casual group, you may find yourself coordinating a sequence of turns with a friendly rival to swing a round. It’s not official team play, but you can enjoy that positive peer pressure that makes everyone want to perform better – or at least more dramatically.
- Reserve your Defuses. Yes, you want to avoid the exploding cat, but Defuses are also a limited resource that can anchor late-game survivability. Don’t burn them too early; the last thing you want is to have your hand flooded with luck and no way to cash in on it when the clock is ticking.
- Embrace the chaos. If you’re going to lose, lose with style and a joke that becomes a caption for the next group photo. Streaking Kittens rewards players who maintain a good sense of humor about the chaos they’re unleashing.
