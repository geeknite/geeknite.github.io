## Design and Build Quality

The iPhone 16 Pro boasts a sleek and futuristic design that makes you want to show it off to strangers. The glass and aluminum body feel premium, and it's so thin it could slice bread (please don't try this). Available in Galactic Grey, Aurora Blue, and Classic Black, this phone is a fashion statement.

### Ports and Connectivity

Apple has decided to keep the beloved Lightning port, giving all your old cables another lease on life. The iPhone 16 Pro is equipped with 5G connectivity, Wi-Fi 7, and Bluetooth 5.3, ensuring you're always connected, even if your social skills aren't.

## Display and Camera

![iPhone 16 Pro Display](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/display__e7k5zj7n0heq_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}iphone-16-pro-display)

The iPhone 16 Pro features a 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display. The colors are so vibrant you'll think your eyeballs are on steroids. The 120Hz ProMotion technology ensures buttery smooth scrolling and gaming.

### Camera: Say Cheese!

The camera setup is where the iPhone 16 Pro truly shines. With a 108MP main sensor, 50MP ultra-wide, and 20MP telephoto lens, your selfies will look better than real life (sorry, reality). Night mode, portrait mode, and AI-driven enhancements make every shot Instagram-worthy.

## Performance and Battery Life

### Powered by A16 Bionic Chip

![A16 Bionic Chip](https://www.apple.com/v/iphone-16-pro/a/images/overview/chip__e4e6lfmu0sey_large.jpg){: style="float: right;"}({{ site.constants.wsib }}a16-chip)

The heart of the iPhone 16 Pro is the A16 Bionic chip. With a 12-core CPU, 18-core GPU, and a Neural Engine that could probably pass the Turing test, this phone is a performance powerhouse. Whether you're editing 4K videos, playing the latest games, or opening apps faster than you can say "Apple," the iPhone 16 Pro handles it all with ease.

### Battery Life: All-Day Champion

Apple claims up to 24 hours of battery life on a single charge, and in our tests, it didn't disappoint. No more lugging around chargers or desperately searching for power outlets in coffee shops. The iPhone 16 Pro will keep you going from dawn till dusk.

### iOS 20

The iPhone 16 Pro comes with iOS 20, packed with new features like Universal Control, Focus mode, and redesigned Safari. The seamless integration between hardware and software is what makes the iPhone experience unparalleled.

### FaceTime and Face ID

The 1080p FaceTime HD camera ensures you look your best on video calls. Coupled with enhanced Face ID technology, unlocking your phone is faster and more secure than ever before. Just don't try it with your pet's face.
