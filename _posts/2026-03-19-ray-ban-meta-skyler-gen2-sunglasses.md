## Care, maintenance, and longevity
### Cleaning and storage
Use the included microfiber cloth to wipe the lenses clean. Dirt and dust are the enemies of optical clarity, and you don’t want to scratch those Green-G15 surfaces with the rough sleeve of a hoodie. Avoid abrasive cleaners; a light wipe with a lens-safe cleaner is enough. When not in use, store them in the padded sleeve to protect the delicate hinges and to remind yourself that yes, you own something nice that deserves respect. If you’re traveling, keep the box handy to avoid misplacing them in the bottom of a backpack full of snacks and old receipts.

### Care tips for AR features
If you’re enabling AR overlays, keep the firmware updated. AR features rely on software; older firmware can drain battery life, produce glitchy overlays, or simply be slower than your grandmother’s dial-up connection. It’s not as dramatic as it sounds, but if you want a seamless experience during a hike or coffee run, make sure your device and connected apps are all up to date. Our recommendation is to keep the AR toggles lean unless you specifically want to run live mapping or a heads-up display of your notifications. In practice, keep it simple: use AR when you need it, switch it off when you don’t, and enjoy the old-school sunglasses when you want a distraction-free moment.
