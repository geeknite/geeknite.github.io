## What is City of Mist: The Complete Collection?
City of Mist started life as a tabletop roleplaying game that imagines mythic beings waking up in the modern city. Players become Heroes who have a mask identity that hides a powerful mythic persona. The core mechanic rewards storytelling and creative use of environment over mechanical optimization. The complete collection consolidates all essential books and supplements into one glossy boxed edition. It is the kind of product that begs to be left on a coffee table, not tucked away in a closet between a stack of rulebooks no one will read.

## System and mechanics
City of Mist uses a distinctive tag-based dice system. During tasks, you describe your character by naming tags that apply to the situation. If your tag helps, you get more dice to roll. A success depends on getting a certain number of sixes on a pool of six-sided dice. The system favors story-driven solutions over min-max optimization, rewarding creative use of environment, relationships, and the mythic masks your character bears. The Complete Collection maintains this design ethos by including a broad range of tags that reflect the citys many archetypes: detectives, shamans, scientists, gods, and tricksters.

### The feel of play and the city as co-author
The tag system is not just a gimmick; it is a tool for collaborative storytelling. When players name a tag like urban legend or street-level magic, the narrative springs to life around that idea. The dungeon of rules becomes a stage on which characters unveil their masks and reveal their true natures. You will find moments where a single tag can rewrite a scene mid-act, and the table shifts into a chorus of accomplice voices who want to see the myth unfold. If you enjoy games where narration matters as much as dice results, City of Mist delivers in spades.

## Playability and accessibility
One of the strong suits of this collection is accessibility. The core rules are written with a focus on player imagination rather than heavy tables and charts; you can get a game going with minimal preparation if you have a few ready-made city locations and a cast of intriguing masks. If you are new to the system, you will spend some time learning the vocabulary—tags such as grit, charm, or mythic might can be a lot to absorb at first—but the book offers a friendly glossary, example scenes, and a few quick-start adventures to ease you in. For players who enjoy world-building, City of Mist collecting has plenty of material to spark ideas: the city is a character in its own right, full of factions, hidden histories, and urban legends.

### Masks and character creation
Character creation in City of Mist is a two-stage spectacle. Stage one is the creation of a mask, the heroic persona that gives your character a mythic hook. Stage two is the awakening of the legend that lies beneath the mask. This approach can feel like a literary exercise at first, but it quickly becomes a powerful wheel for collaboration. Players brainstorm a sentence or two that captures the essence of their mask, then the GM threads in a few hints about the myth that powers the hero. The Complete Collection provides a robust set of prompts that can speed up this process while still leaving enough room for improvisation. Expect to spend a session or two letting your masks breathe before you start chasing an overarching story.

## A practical look at the mechanics in action
In practice, the system rewards a well-timed reveal, a clever use of a tag, or a dramatic beat. A character who has the perfect tag for a situation unlocks additional dice and, with a little luck, a spectacular moment of triumph. The complete collection offers a handful of encounter seeds that demonstrate how a scene can pivot on a single line of dialogue or a sudden twist in the citys weather pattern. There are moments when you realize that the game is less about rolling dice and more about putting a microphone in front of a hero and letting them perform. This is a game that wants you to improvise, and the collection gives you enough prompts to keep improvisation fresh, even after a dozen sessions.

## GMing tips and player guidance
For the game master, the complete collection provides a toolbox: ready-made prompts, location cards, and a GM section with advice on running investigations and managing plot twists.
