## Assembly, Maintenance, and Longevity

No product review would be complete without a word on assembly. The GOF 1P is designed for home installation and moderate DIY capability. Expect a moderate to heavy box count, typical hardware bags, and a straightforward instruction sheet with diagrams. The assembly should take a few hours for first-timers, perhaps longer if you insist on following every step to the letter and minor details such as cable routing are perfected before you even start. Pro tip: organize hardware and tighten gradually in a diagonal pattern rather than tightening each bolt to full torque in one go. It saves you the frustration of misalignment and reduces the risk of stripping a cam lock.

Maintenance is simple: wipe with a damp cloth, avoid direct sunlight on the espresso finish, and check screws periodically. If you have kids or pets that enjoy chewing on desk edges, consider corner guards or protective inserts. The build is sturdy enough to survive the usual daily abuse of a busy home office and, with care, should last years without major issues.
