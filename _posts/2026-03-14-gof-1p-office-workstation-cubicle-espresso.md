## Assembly, Maintenance, and Longevity

No product review would be complete without a word on assembly. The GOF 1P is designed for home installation and moderate DIY capability. Expect a moderate to heavy box count, typical hardware bags, and a straightforward instruction sheet with diagrams. The assembly should take a few hours for first-timers, perhaps longer if you insist on following every step to the letter and treat cable routing as a sacred rite. It saves you the frustration of misalignment and reduces the risk of stripping a cam lock.

Maintenance is straightforward: wipe with a damp cloth, avoid direct sunlight on the espresso finish, and check screws periodically. If you have kids or pets who like to chew on desk edges, consider corner guards or protective inserts. The build is sturdy enough to survive the usual daily abuse of a busy home office and should last many years with sensible care.
