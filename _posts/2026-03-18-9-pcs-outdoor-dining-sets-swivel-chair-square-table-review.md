## Maintenance Tricks and Pro Tips
- Remove cushions during heavy rain and store indoors to prevent mold, especially in humid climates.
- Use a soft brush to remove dust from fabric before washing to extend cushion life.
- Wipe the table top with a mild soap solution and dry to avoid water spots that can accumulate over time.
- If you notice chair swivels becoming stiff, a small amount of light lubricant on the pivot points can restore smoothness. wipe away excess to keep your outdoor space looking pristine.
- Use a simple outdoor furniture cover during off-season months to protect from snow, ice, and the occasional wayward leaf that dares to think it can claim your dining area.
