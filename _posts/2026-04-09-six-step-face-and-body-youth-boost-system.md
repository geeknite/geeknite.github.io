### Why cleansing matters in a geeky universe
Think about your skin as a small operating system. If you load it with junk—sunscreen, dust, last night’s pizza grease—it gets cranky. - A clean canvas that actually feels different: less gritty, more like satin, but without the robotics-level gloss.
