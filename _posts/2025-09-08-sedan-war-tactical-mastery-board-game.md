### What is Sedan War: Tactical Mastery?

Sedan War: Tactical Mastery is a strategic board game that simulates historical battles with an emphasis on tactical decisions and battlefield maneuvers. Designed for 2-4 players, it offers a rich blend of planning, strategy, and historical warfare, making every game a unique experience.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Sedan War is like prepping for a historical reenactment. Arrange the game board, distribute units and strategy cards, and decide on your battlefield objectives. It’s a bit like choreographing a dance, but with more cannons.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn consists of several phases:
1. **Planning Phase**: Players secretly plan their unit movements and strategies.
2. **Action Phase**: Reveal and execute your plans, moving units, and engaging in combat.
3. **Resolution Phase**: Resolve battles and adjust the battlefield according to the outcomes.

The objective is to outmaneuver your opponents, capture key positions, and fulfill your mission objectives. Remember, in war, as in board games, planning is everything.

### Strategy and Tactics

The game shines in its strategic depth. Players must balance offensive actions with defensive strategies, manage limited resources, and adapt their tactics based on the evolving battlefield. Cooperation, deception, and tactical brilliance are key to achieving victory.
