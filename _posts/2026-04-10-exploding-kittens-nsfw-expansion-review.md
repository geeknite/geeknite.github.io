## What is the Exploding Kittens NSFW Expansion?

The NSFW Expansion is a bonus pack designed to be played with the base Exploding Kittens game. It adds a new layer of cheeky humor, adult innuendos, and a slightly darker sense of humor to your already chaotic cat-versus-bomb world. Think of it as the after-hours version of a kids’ card game: if the base game is a playful kitty parade, the NSFW expansion is a late-night improv show performed by red-eyed cats who just discovered sass.

This expansion doesn’t reinvent Exploding Kittens; it amplifies the core mechanics while adding a batch of new card types. There are cards that twist the timing and target of explosions, cards that force players to perform ridiculous dares, and a few crisp one-liners that feel like they were written by a stand-up routine delivered in a carnival game booth. The “bonus” nature of the expansion means you can mix and match with the base game and other expansions if you’re into a long, drawn-out evening of questionable decision-making.

For anyone who hasn’t played Exploding Kittens before, here’s the quick gist: players take turns drawing cards from a deck. Some cards let you skip turns, peek at future cards, or steal cards from other players, while the exploding kitten card is a ticking time bomb that eliminates you unless you have a defuse card. The NSFW expansion keeps all of that intact but spices up the flavor and, in some cases, the consequences. It’s not a children’s party game; it’s the late-night party game that pretends to be polite until the flop card shows up with a cartoonishly lewd wink.

If you’re in a mood to compare, the NSFW expansion sits somewhere between a goofy stand-up routine and a chaotic improv game. It’s better with a crowd that enjoys adult humor and doesn’t mind a little ribbing about tastefully ridiculous topics. If your gaming circle includes younger players or more conservative friends, you’ll want to keep this expansion in the closet (not literally—please don’t store cardboard in a closet with toxic fumes or hungry cats).

## How to Play with the NSFW Expansion

Setting up is easy if you’ve already memorized the base game flow. Shuffle the NSFW deck into the base deck if you’re running a longer session, or keep the two decks separate and swap between them every few rounds for variety. The exact house rule is yours to define, but a few quick guidelines help:

- Establish boundaries before you start. A few players might be uncomfortable with certain topics, so a quick thumbs-up on what’s allowed keeps energy high without turning the night into a therapy session.
- Decide on a removal policy for topic-sensitive cards. If a card prompts a dare or confession, ensure it’s something that can be declined with a polite alternative. This keeps the mood inclusive and still hilariously chaotic.
- The base rules still apply: draw, play, explode, and avoid the kitten bomb. The NSFW cards modify probabilities, not the fundamental objective.

For players who want the fastest route to hilarity, try a format where you rotate who sits in “hot seat” every few rounds. The person in hot seat has to perform the card’s dare or a spinoff version you agree on. You’ll get more stories out of that chair than your diary ever produced in a year.
