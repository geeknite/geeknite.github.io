## Practical care: keeping beige beautiful without overthinking it
Care is the friend of beige in the long run. Here are practical tips that help you extend the life and look of your NAT620B-6SQ without turning rug care into a full time hobby:
- Vacuum regularly with a soft brush attachment to prevent fiber wear and to keep dust from settling into the fibers.
- Use a damp cloth for minor spills, and avoid rubbing the stain into the fibers; blotting is your best friend.
- For tougher stains, use a mild detergent diluted with water and test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure color stability.
- Rotate the rug occasionally to promote even wear and to prevent obvious pattern fading from sun exposure.
- If you plan to place it in direct sunlight, consider a UV protective window treatment or move to a spot with filtered light to preserve color consistency over time.
- Do not use harsh cleaners or bleach; beige is forgiving but not invincible, so treat it with gentle care.
