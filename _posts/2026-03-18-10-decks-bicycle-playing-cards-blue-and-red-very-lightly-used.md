## The Practicalities: Care, Longevity, and Value for Money
### Care and Longevity: Making Your Decks Last
Even though these are “very lightly used,” there are some basic best practices to maximize their lifespan:
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading of the colors.
- Keep tuck boxes flat to avoid bending at the corners; shelf height matters when you’re an overachieving cardist.
- When cleaning the surface, avoid harsh chemicals; a dry microfiber cloth works just fine for adorable micro-dust, and your grandma’s advice about “not touching the magic” holds true here.
- In the event of a slight ripple, let the deck rest for a few hours in a cool place; you’ll be surprised how quickly they flatten back out when they’re not being roughly handled.
