## Maintenance and Care Revisited
We want to remind you again that longevity comes from simple care. Wipe surfaces after a session, store chips away from strong sunlight to prevent discoloration, and keep the foam inserts clean to avoid transfer of dyes onto chips or cards. If the foam starts to show wear, replace the foam or consider upgrading to a higher grade storage solution. This is not just about aesthetics; it helps preserve the tactile feel of the chips and cards for future gatherings.

If you play in humid environments, watch for cards sticking together. A simple air-dry or a shuffle with a light dusting of cornstarch can solve minor sticking issues. For the chips, avoid immersing them in water; stones and chips do not mix well with water, and you do not want to warp the foam or discolor the chips. Prevention is easier than correction here: store in a dry place, keep away from direct sunlight, and use the case’s latch to keep the lid closed when transporting.
