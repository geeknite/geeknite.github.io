## Installation and Setup

The 4PC Clamp-on Desktop Privacy Panel is designed with the idea that you should spend less time setting up and more time pretending you’re in a quiet library with excellent Wi-Fi. Here’s a practical, no-drama guide to getting your privacy fortress assembled in record time.

1) Prep your desk: Clear a small space on the edge where you want the clamp to sit. If you’re a maximalist, you can either clamp on a single panel pair at a time or do the full four-panel configuration in one go. Either way, you’ll want the surface clean and dry to maximize the grip.
2) Attach the clamps: Place the rubber jaws around the desk edge and tighten the clamp screws by hand until snug. You should feel a little resistance—this means the clamp isn’t going to wiggle loose during a particularly intense software update.

4) Fine-tune height and angle: Adjust the panel height to your preferred eye level during video calls and tilt the screens toward or away from your monitor to find that sweet spot where nobody can read the horror that is your Excel sheet but you can still see the chat messages.
5) Optional accessories: If you want a bit more privacy, you can deploy a second layer of frosted film on the inner faces or add small magnets for an extra-chic, industrial vibe.
