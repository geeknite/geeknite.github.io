## How to play the Game Night Bundle (the long version)

Exploding Kittens is a game of push-your-luck with a social layer of misdirection. The core loop remains simple: players draw cards until someone hits an Exploding Kitten, at which point they must play a Defuse card to survive. If you lack a Defuse, you are out — but the excitement does not end there. The Game Night Bundle adds more moving parts: new Card types, possibly larger groups, and optional rules that skew more toward planned mayhem than pure random fortune.

Key card families you’ll encounter:
- Exploding Kitten cards: the fastest path to elimination unless you have a Defuse
- Defuse cards: your lifelines that let you dodge a blast and keep the round rolling
- See the Future: peek at upcoming draws to inform your next move
- Attack: push the burden to the next player, stacking turns like a chaotic domino line
- Skip: skip your turn to dodge danger, or to set up a sneaky play
- Shuffle: when fate looks dicey, scramble the order and reintroduce uncertainty
- Cat cards: sets that unlock playful bonuses or trigger goofy hijinks during play

The rhythm is part game, part performance. You watch the table’s energy rise and fall with each shuffle, each See the Future peek, and each time someone yells Defuse as if they had just found the one-in-a-million lottery ticket.

## Strategy and house rules: getting the most out of the bundle

Luck is real, but there is a surprising amount of strategy for those who want to lean into it without ruining the vibe.

- Defuse management: Defuses are precious. Distribute them so you aren’t left out in the cold if a cluster of Exploding Kitten draws looms. Don’t hoard in a way that invites calls of greed from the jury of the table.
- See the Future timing: The information from peek-ahead draws can inform tempo. If you spot a likely kitten coming, saving a Defuse for that moment becomes a reasonable risk management tactic.
- Attack and Skip usage: Attacks can push pressure onto the player directly ahead of you or punish someone who has burned through Defuses. Skips reset tense moments and give you control when the table is on the edge.
- Cat cards: A few sets unlock bonuses and extra effects. The more you collect, the more you influence the tempo. It moves it from pure luck toward a touch of strategy.
