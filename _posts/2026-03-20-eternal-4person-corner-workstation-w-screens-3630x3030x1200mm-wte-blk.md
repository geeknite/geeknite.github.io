## Ergonomics and comfort: sitting well, thinking well
Ergonomics matters when you’ve got four bodies in close quarters. A desk that’s too high or too low can ruin the vibe faster than a coffee that’s gone cold. The Eternal’s height and surface depth are tuned for standard office chairs, with enough space to adjust your chairs and position monitors without elbows clinking the edge of the desk.
