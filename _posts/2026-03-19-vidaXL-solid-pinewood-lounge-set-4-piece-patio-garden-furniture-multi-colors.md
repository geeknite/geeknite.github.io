## Maintenance and Longevity

### Cleaning and Care Tips

- Regularly wipe the wood with a dry cloth to remove dust and debris; avoid harsh chemical cleaners that can strip the protective finish.
- Apply a suitable outdoor wood sealer or oil at least once per season to maintain the wood’s natural color and protect against moisture.
- Store cushions indoors or under a breathable cover during heavy rain or winter seasons to minimize mildew risk.
- Check screws and fittings periodically; overtightening can crack wood, while loose fittings can cause rattling and wobble.

### Weather-Related Considerations

In non-tropical climates or seasons with heavy rainfall, you’ll want to cover the furniture when not in use. Pinewood is relatively forgiving, but prolonged exposure to moisture can lead to warping or splitting if the wood hasn’t been properly treated. A quality outdoor cover is your best insurance policy, and a quick mid-season maintenance session (oil, sealer, check joints) can prevent minor issues from turning into more expensive repairs.
