## Care, Storage, and Maintenance: Keeping Your Decks Ready for Action
### Storage Tips: Humidity, Heat, and Handling
Store your two decks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Heat and humidity can warp stock and fade colors over time. A simple card box or a small shelf near your gaming setup is all you need. If you re chasing longevity, consider keeping a spare in a sealed bag to protect from moisture and dust. Card fans and shuffles can create a lot of friction, but they also produce tiny amounts of sweat and oil from your hands; airing the decks out every now and then can help keep the finish from gumming up.

### Cleaning and Quick Maintenance
Gently wipe the surface with a dry microfiber cloth if you notice fingerprints or residue. Avoid harsh chemicals or water; moisture is the enemy of printed finishes and can cause the color to run or the cards to stick. If you notice a stubborn edge snagging during a flourish, a quick re-fan to reset the stock usually does the trick. For magicians who rely on long-run routines, rotating decks every few weeks is a good practice to maintain consistent feel and appearance.
