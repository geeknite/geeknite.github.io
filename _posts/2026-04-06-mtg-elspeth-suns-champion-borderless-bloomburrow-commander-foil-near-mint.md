Foil edges can be susceptible to micro-creases if mishandled, so a steady hand and a careful storage strategy pays off in the long term margins of your collection.

### Foil Quality and Handling Tips
Foils are a two-edged sword: they catch the light and elevate the vibe, but they demand care. Here is a quick playbook for your borderless Elspeth foil near mint:
- Use high-quality sleeves with a clear window to keep the foil gleam visible while protecting it from fingerprints. 
- If you are playing the card in a deck, consider a soft guard like a top load or a deck box with padding to prevent bending. 
- Avoid bending the card too aggressively when shuffling, especially if your deck has multiple borderless foils. The borderless frame can be susceptible to edge nicks if the card is forced to take too many clunking shuffles.
