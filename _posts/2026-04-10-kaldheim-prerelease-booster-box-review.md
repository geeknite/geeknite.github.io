For the Kalheim box, the limited environment is particularly flavorful because the set thrives on dynamic, often aggressive interactions between colors and a few big plays that can swing a game in a single turn.

The Prerelease Boosters in a box are designed to give you a taste of the set, not a full power curve. The joy comes from two things: first, the social experience of drafting with seven other players, and second, the moment you realize your pool has a surprisingly coherent synergy despite looking like a chaotic jumble on day one.
