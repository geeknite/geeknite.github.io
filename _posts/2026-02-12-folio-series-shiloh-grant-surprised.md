The Battle of Shiloh took place from April 6-7, 1862, in southwestern Tennessee. It was one of the early engagements of the Civil War and marked a significant turning point. The Union forces, led by General Grant, were camped near Pittsburg Landing when they were unexpectedly attacked by Confederate forces under General Albert Sidney Johnston.
