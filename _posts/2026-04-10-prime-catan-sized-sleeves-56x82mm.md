## Maintenance: care for your sleeves and they’ll take care of you
Caring for sleeves is simpler than wrestling a game-twin puzzle. A few simple steps keep them in good shape:
- Keep sleeves away from sharp edges and heat sources; extreme heat can warp thickness and clarity.
- Store sleeved decks upright in a cool, dry place; avoid damp basements where mold fantasies thrive.
- When not in use, avoid bending sleeves into awkward angles; flat storage is your friend.
- Clean sleeves occasionally with a dry microfiber cloth to remove dust. If you’ve got sticky fingers (we’ve all been there), use minimal moisture on the cloth to avoid soaking the cardboard in damp glory.

These basic steps will extend the life of your Prime sleeves and keep your table looking tidy during epic trading turns and the occasional victory dance.
