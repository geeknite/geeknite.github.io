## The Composers Behind the Magic

### Maestro Maestros

The soundtrack for 'Utopia: Une vision d'un monde meilleur' is composed by the dynamic duo, **Jean-Luc Fantastic** and **Sophie Harmonie**. Their unique blend of classical and contemporary styles brings a fresh and exciting dimension to the film’s music. Their collaboration is like peanut butter and jelly – a match made in auditory heaven.

### Behind the Scenes

Jean-Luc and Sophie spent months painstakingly crafting each piece, often drawing inspiration from the film's stunning visuals and rich narrative. Fun fact: Jean-Luc claimed that he composed the entire Chase Sequence while actually running away from a swarm of bees. Talk about dedication!
