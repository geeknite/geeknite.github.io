Custom sizing considerations
- If you have a bespoke space a narrow hallway an irregular seating area or a room with a fireplace that interrupts flow a custom size can maximize the visual impact of the motif and keep traffic patterns clean.

- If you are renting or maintaining flexibility the 5x7 is portable and easier to replace if you move to a new space or want to change the vibe with a different rug.

- Edge shaping sometimes you will see rounded corners or irregular edges in custom rugs. If your furniture layout includes angled walls or unusual alcoves this can be a nice way to maintain the cat eye narrative without fighting the geometry.

Care and maintenance
- Materials wool is a natural fiber known for durability and resilience. It can handle foot traffic and still bounce back into shape which means the cat eyes wont look like a sunken defeated emoji after a few months of daily use.

- Pile height a moderate pile height roughly 0.4–0.6 inches provides that plush feel without turning your room into a storage solution for dust bunnies.

- Cleaning tips vacuum on a low setting avoiding aggressive beater bars that can pull the fibers. For spills blot dont rub and treat promptly with a mild detergent and water solution. If you are dealing with stubborn stains professional cleaning is recommended to preserve color and texture.

- Color retention wool dyes can fade with direct sun exposure. If your rug sits in a sunny spot consider rotating it every so often or introducing window treatments to mitigate UV impact.

Comparing sizes in your space
- 5x7 anchors a small-to-medium living room or large bedroom great if you want a big visual piece without overwhelming the space.
- 6x9 or 8x10 opens up a room and creates a larger stage for the motif especially in open-plan areas.

Notes on durability and usage
- For households with pets wool s natural resilience helps resist wear from claws however it is smart to keep claws trimmed and use furniture pads under legs to minimize stress points on edges.

- For homes with kids the soft texture makes playtime gentle on little feet and the motif remains legible after routine play sessions.

Buying tips for decision makers
- Measure carefully measure length and width account for door clearance and rug placement under furniture. A well measured rug looks like it was meant to be there.
- Decide on orientation do you want the cat eye motif to run parallel to the longest wall or do you want it to appear as if the eyes are gazing toward a specific focal point
- Consider traffic in heavily used rooms a slightly lower pile option tends to wear better if you have a lighter use space but want maximal softness a mid pile is a nice compromise
- Plan for sun exposure if your space gets a lot of sunlight you might choose the custom option to ensure consistent dye absorption and minimize color shift

Shipping and returns
- Most sellers offer standard shipping with tracking and a reasonable return window For custom sizes confirm lead times and any return restrictions
- If you are ordering as a gift ask for a color swatch or fiber sample to confirm color matching with your space
