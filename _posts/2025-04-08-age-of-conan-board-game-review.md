## The Basics

### What is Age of Conan?

**Age of Conan: The Strategy Board Game** is a game where players assume the role of a leader in the Hyborian Age. Your goal? Conquer territories, complete quests, and ultimately prove that you are the most powerful force in the land. Designed for 2-4 players, this game is a mix of tactical combat, resource management, and a bit of diplomacy (because even barbarians have to talk sometimes).

### Components

The game includes a detailed game board depicting the Hyborian world, beautifully crafted miniatures, various tokens, dice, and decks of cards. The artwork is visually stunning, capturing the essence of Robert E. Howard's legendary world.

**Fun Fact**: The game features a miniature of Conan himself, ready to bring chaos and muscle-bound justice wherever he's placed.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up **Age of Conan** is like preparing for war. You'll need to lay out the board, distribute miniatures, shuffle decks, and ensure everyone understands the rules. It's quite an involved process, so make sure you have plenty of snacks and maybe a bard to sing songs of your future victories.

### Turn Phases

Each turn is divided into several phases:

1. **Strategy Phase**: Players plan their actions, including building armies, moving troops, and engaging in diplomacy (or trickery).
2. **Adventure Phase**: Draw adventure cards that represent various quests and challenges.
3. **Conan Phase**: Conan moves across the board, often causing havoc and disrupting best-laid plans.
4. **Action Phase**: Players execute their planned actions, including combat.

### Combat

Combat in **Age of Conan** is a mix of dice rolling and strategic positioning. Each player rolls combat dice, and the results determine the outcome of battles. Special abilities and cards can influence the results, adding layers of strategy.

### Winning and Losing

The game ends when a player completes their objectives or when a set number of turns have passed. The player with the most points, earned through conquering territories, completing quests, and achieving other goals, is declared the winner. If you lose, well, you can always say you fought bravely... or not.

**Pro Tip**: Always keep an eye on Conan's position. His unpredictability can either be your doom or your salvation.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: The combination of strategy, combat, and questing keeps players engaged.
- **High-Quality Components**: The artwork and miniatures are beautifully crafted and durable.
- **Replayability**: With various quests and strategies, no two games are ever the same.
- **Humorous Moments**: The unpredictable nature of the game provides countless laugh-out-loud moments.
- **Strategic Depth**: Balancing combat, resource management, and diplomacy offers a rich gaming experience.

### Cons

- **Complex Setup**: The initial setup can be time-consuming and requires patience.
- **Punishing Difficulty**: The game's difficulty can be brutal, making some sessions feel overwhelming.
- **Long Playtime**: Be prepared for a long game session, as **Age of Conan** can take several hours to complete.
