## Setting Up and Playing

### Setup

Setting up the game is like preparing for an epic quest. Lay out the game board, distribute character cards, and assemble your miniatures. Make sure to review your character's abilities and plot your strategy. Now you're ready to venture into the Forgotten Realms!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players use their character cards to perform actions. This could range from slashing enemies with Drizzt’s swords to casting spells with Catti-brie. Cooperation and strategy are key, so plan your moves wisely.

### Combat

Combat is straightforward but strategic. Characters have unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle. Did Bruenor just headbutt a goblin into submission? Classic. Remember, teamwork is essential to surviving the quests and defeating the monstrous foes.

## The Adventure

### Scenarios

The Companions of the Hall Starter introduces scenarios inspired by D&D lore. From battling orcs in the Icewind Dale to delving into the depths of the Underdark, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different tactics.

### Characters

Play as beloved characters such as Drizzt, Bruenor, Wulfgar, and Regis. Each character has distinct abilities and combos that add depth to the game. Ever wondered what it’s like to witness Drizzt’s panther Guenhwyvar in action? Now’s your chance!
