## H2: Care, Storage, and Longevity: Keeping No. 524 Pristine

Collectors who treat cardboard like an endangered species deserve praise. To ensure your Trunks No. 524 ages like a fine wine instead of a sunburned banana, here are a few practical care tips:

- Handling: Wash your hands; oils on skin can degrade the card’s surface over the long term. Avoid rubbing or bending the card aggressively.
- Sleeves: Use 2x2 or clear standard-size sleeves designed for Carddas cards; ensure the sleeve offers protection without adding excessive bulk inside your binder.
- Binders: Keep your pages away from direct sunlight; UV light can fade artwork and foil finishes. A cool, dry place protects both art and color balance.
- Storage: If you own a foil variant, consider a rigid card holder for display or a dedicated top-loader with a teeth-free edge protector to minimize edge wear.
- Cleaning: If dust accumulates, a soft brush is enough. Avoid chemical cleaners.

A little care goes a long way. Each preserved edge and pristine surface is a trophy for your future self who will thank you at a con when you show off a near-mint No. 524 and hear the audience sigh with collective respect.
