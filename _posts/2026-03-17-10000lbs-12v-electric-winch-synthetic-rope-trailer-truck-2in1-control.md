### Safety and Setup

Safety is the number one factor in any winching operation. Always wear gloves. Always inspect the rope for signs of wear. The synthetic rope is less prone to certain hazards than steel cable, but it still needs to be inspected. A properly installed winch in a sturdy mounting is essential. ### Rope Care and Maintenance

Synthetic rope requires different care than steel. It doesn't rust, but it does twist and shed fibers. We recommend inspecting for kern separation and used rope for wear. When not in use, keep rope out of direct sun, avoid UV exposure for extended periods, and store in a dry place. Clean with warm water and mild soap if need, avoid solvents, and roll the rope on a clean drum with gentle tension. Always avoid kinks and snags.
