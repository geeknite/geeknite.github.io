## Display and storage tips
A sound storing system makes a visible difference in preserving value and looking cool on your shelf:
- Use acid-free sleeves and a 9-pocket binder for a clean, organized look.
- Keep away from sunlight and humidity. UV light can haze artwork and bleach ink; humidity can cause warping or edge wear.
- Consider top-loaders or card frames for special copies if you want them to shine under room lighting.
- Regularly rotate or reorganize your binder to keep the collection fresh and accessible for trade conversations.

If you want to keep No. 135 pristine and still show it off, consider a display approach that combines protection with visibility. A glass-front shelf or a well-lit display cabinet can help the card look as iconic as its card-dass era peers.
