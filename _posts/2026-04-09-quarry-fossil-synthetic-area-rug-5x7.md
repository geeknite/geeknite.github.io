### Maintenance Tips and Care

- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and debris. Use a vacuum with a gentle setting to avoid tuft pull.
- For spills, blot—blot, don’t rub—then clean with a mild detergent and warm water. Avoid harsh chemicals.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear. It doesn’t wear the same way as a superhero, but it helps.
- If you have pets, trim nails and keep fur from tangling into the fibers.
