## Washability and Maintenance
### Washing Instructions
Here’s the good news: the cover is machine-washable. Remove the cover, toss it in the machine on a gentle cycle with cold water, and you’re back in business. If your pet has a love affair with tuna-flavored treats, or you’ve got a coffee accident that somehow found its way into fur-town, you can wash the cover without fear of losing the nap’s personality. The foam insert isn’t designed to be submerged, so it’s best to spot-clean around the edges if necessary.

### Drying Times
Air-drying is ideal to preserve loft, but if you’re in a rush for another nap, tumble dry on low with a few clean towels to cushion the nap’s delicate feelings. It dries faster than you’d expect, which is important because you never know when you’ll need to transform the bed into a “nap-on-the-go” situation in the living room, bedroom, or home office.

### Stain Resistance
Let’s be real: stains happen. The outer cover does a commendable job of hiding minor spills and pet drool, and the inner foam recovers its shape after a quick fluffing. For stubborn marks, a gentle pre-treat on a small hidden area with a mild detergent works wonders. If the fur ever starts looking more gray than fluffy, you probably have a hairball situation on your hands and not a stain—and at that point, it might be time to schedule a grooming session, or to pretend you’re the one who’s having a spa day.
