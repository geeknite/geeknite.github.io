## What is Last Night on Earth: Zombies with Grave Weapons?

### The Basics

*Last Night on Earth: Zombies with Grave Weapons* is an expansion for the popular zombie survival board game, *Last Night on Earth*. This set introduces new zombies equipped with grave weapons, adding more chaos and hilarity to your survival attempts. So, whether you're a seasoned zombie hunter or a newbie trying not to get your brains eaten, this expansion promises to spice up your game nights.

### Components

The miniature set includes:

- **Grave Weapons Zombies**: Uniquely designed zombie miniatures wielding weapons that were definitely not meant for gardening.
- **New Cards**: A deck of cards introducing new events, items, and abilities that will make you laugh and cry (mostly laugh).
- **Game Board**: Additional tiles to expand your playing area and create new scenarios.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track damage, weapons, and other essential parts of your zombie-slaying experience.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up *Last Night on Earth: Zombies with Grave Weapons* is like preparing for a zombie apocalypse — it takes time, patience, and maybe a few extra snacks. Lay out the game board, distribute miniatures and cards, and choose your heroes and zombies. Now you're ready to face the undead head-on.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their character's abilities. You can move, attack, search for items, and perform special actions. The zombies, on the other hand, are not limited by such trivial things as strategy — they move and attack anything that breathes, moans, or shouts, "Look out!"

### Combat

Combat in *Last Night on Earth* is a mix of strategy and luck. When grave weapons enter the fray, expect the unexpected. Did you plan an epic defense just to have a zombie attack you with a shovel? Welcome to the graveyard, buddy, where plans often go six feet under.
