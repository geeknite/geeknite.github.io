## Gameplay Experience: Flow, Rules, and Social Nuance
### Quick Start and House Rules
CAH s rhythm is familiar Card Czar reads the black prompt players lay down their best white cards and the best pair wins the round. The Blue Box doesn t reinvent this it polishes the process. Setup remains a brisk five minutes or less and then you re in a loop of banter and bold choices. The rules sheet exists mostly as a memory aid most groups rely on a handful of house rules to keep the night lively. If your party includes first timers these rules help avoid early awkwardness and give everyone a sense of how far to push the envelope.

### Hosting Tips and Social Norms
CAH is famous for testing boundaries and the Blue Box amplifies that test.
