## Care, Maintenance and Where to Buy (Pre-owned Tips)

When you handle a pre-owned copy, you inherit a little history. Here are a few practical tips that can help you maintain the edition in good condition:

- Inspect the board for warping or moisture marks. A flat board is easier to play on, even when you’re fighting for Marvin Gardens.
- Check the card decks for edge wear. A few creases are normal; avoid cards with major tears or missing corners.
- Ensure the money stacks are intact. If a lot of denominations are loose, you may want to re-stabilize with a small binder clip or a baggie that keeps the piles separate.
- Confirm that all tokens and components are present. A standalone token can feel lonely on the board, so if you’re missing something, check seller notes or the manufacturer’s support for replacements.
- Cleaning tips: a soft microfiber cloth with a gentle wipe is enough for most surfaces. Avoid soaking the board or money in water; you want to preserve the print and the color integrity for years of future game nights.

As for where to buy, pre-owned copies show up on marketplace platforms, thrift stores, and local game shops. If you’re shopping online, read the seller’s notes carefully, request clear photos, and ask about any missing components. In many cases, you’ll find a well-loved copy that includes a fair portion of the charm, with a price that makes your university-era gaming nights proud.
