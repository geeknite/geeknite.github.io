## Mechanics and Replay Value
Cards Against Humanity is a simple format players take turns being the Card Czar and the rest submit responses. The Nerd Bundle adds a flavor layer that encourages pairing of packs for themed nights or encourages players to mix wildcards from one pack with prompts from another. The replay value hinges on three factors: the size of your social circle the willingness of players to improvise and the ease with which you can cycle through a large pool of prompts to avoid stale jokes. The six packs expand the deck size and increase the probability of unique combos. However the fundamental mechanical loop remains the same you draw a handful of white cards and players craft the funniest or most savage response to the black prompt card.

A few tips to maximize laughs during sessions:

- Establish a few house rules to avoid dead air during lulls (time limits for responses helps a lot)

For players who love to tinker with rules this bundle lends itself to adjacency variations such as adding a quick round of rapid fire prompts or letting players trade among themselves to avoid cliques forming around certain jokes.
