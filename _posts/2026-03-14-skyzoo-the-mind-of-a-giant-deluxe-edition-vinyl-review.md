## Care, Display, and Longevity
Vinyl is a tactile artifact. To keep your Mind of a Giant Deluxe Edition sounding as crisp as possible, follow a few simple practices:

- Keep the records away from direct sunlight and heat. The deluxe edition’s color and glossy jacket like to glow when exposed to bright light, which is nice until you realize your corners have become a tiny, sun-scorched corner of the universe.
- Use a quality turntable mat and a well-balanced tonearm. The precision of this pressing deserves careful tracking; misalignment can introduce pops and asterisks where there should be breath in Skyzoo’s lines.
- Clean regularly. A microfiber cloth and a proper record cleaning solution can extend the life of your grooves, ensuring that instrumentals stay as soulful as the first spin.
- Store vertically in a cool place. It’s tempting to stack, but individual sleeves and jackets will thank you if they stand upright, like little vinyl soldiers in a shelf parade.

If you’re serious about packaging and preservation, consider a protective outer sleeve and a proper inner sleeve for extra longevity. The deluxe edition’s artwork deserves to be protected, both for its aesthetic glory and for that tactile feel when you gently slide the gatefold open on a Sunday afternoon.
