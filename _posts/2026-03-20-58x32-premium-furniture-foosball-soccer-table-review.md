## Accessories, Setup Tips, and Enhancements
### Included accessories
Most premium foosball tables include a set of standard steel rods, a handful of balls, score markers, and a couple of spare parts. If your kit misses essential pieces, contact the retailer; you shouldn’t be left to improvise with mismatched hardware. The included accessories are sufficient for regular play, while serious players can stock up with extra balls and replacement handles as needed.

### Setup optimization tips
- Level the table using the adjustable feet to minimize wobble.
- Lubricate rods with a light silicone spray if you notice stiffness.
- Check the ball return channel for obstructions after the first dozen games.
- Ensure the playing surface is clean and free of dust for consistent ball behavior.
- Place the table in a room with good lighting but not direct glare to maximize readability of the scoreboard.

### Potential upgrades
If you want to push the experience further, consider a high-quality coin mechanism if your space allows, or add protective felt pads to prevent scuffs when the table is moved for cleaning or rearranging the room. Some players also enjoy upgrading to larger, heavier balls for enhanced grip and a different feel—though your cat will likely react with confusion and a new level of cat-opts for mischief.

## Maintenance and Care Guide
- Wipe down after heavy use to reduce sweat-related residue.
- Inspect ball bearings and replace if you notice unusual resistance.
- Tighten screws periodically to maintain rigidity against enthusiastic play.
- Use a dedicated area rug or mat to reduce floor resonance when delivering powerful shots.
- Avoid direct exposure to sunlight for long periods to preserve the finish.
