### Weather resistance and maintenance
Outdoor furniture takes a beating from sun, rain, wind, and your neighbor’s questionable DIY lawn ornaments. The resin wicker is UV stabilized, which helps prevent fading and cracking, while the aluminum or steel frame resists rust. The acacia wood tabletop is durable but benefits from routine care. A quick wipe-down after rain to remove moisture, occasional application of appropriate wood sealant, and avoiding direct heat sources will keep the table looking good for years. Cushions should be stored or covered during heavy rain or winter months to prevent mildew and color fading. Removable cushion covers allow for easy washing, and air drying keeps things fresh rather than musty.

If you spot a loose screw or slight wobble, a quick tighten can fix most issues. If you notice exposed wood ends or warping after an unusually wet season, you might be dealing with something a touch beyond casual maintenance. In those cases, a light sanding and re-oiling can restore the surface and extend the wood life without sacrificing aesthetics.
