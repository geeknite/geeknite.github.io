## The Basics

### What is Zombicide: Prison Outbreak?

*CMON Zombicide: Prison Outbreak* is a cooperative board game where players take on the roles of survivors trying to escape from a zombie-infested prison. Each player controls a unique character with special abilities and weapons. The goal is to complete objectives, fend off hordes of zombies, and avoid getting bitten (unless you’re into that sort of thing).

### Components

The game comes with beautifully designed components, including:

- **Game Tiles**: Representing different sections of the prison, from cell blocks to the cafeteria. Yes, you can fight zombies over a tray of mystery meat.
- **Miniatures**: Detailed figures of survivors and zombies. You’ll love the variety, from the standard walker to the terrifying abomination.
- **Cards**: Equipment, zombie spawning, and event cards that add depth and chaos to the game.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Fun Fact**: The abomination miniature is so detailed it looks like it’s ready to audition for a zombie movie. Hollywood, take notes.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Zombicide: Prison Outbreak is like preparing for a zombie-themed escape room. Lay out the game tiles, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your characters. The setup can be a bit time-consuming, so maybe grab a snack while you do it. Brain-shaped gummies, anyone?

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their character’s abilities. You can:

- **Move**: Navigate through the prison to complete objectives.
- **Attack**: Use weapons to take down zombies. Aim for the head!
- **Search**: Find equipment and weapons in various rooms.
- **Interact**: Complete objectives and help fellow survivors.

### Zombie Phases

After players take their actions, zombies get their turn. They move, attack, and generally make things difficult. The zombie spawning cards add randomness and suspense to the game. Did you just draw another abomination card? Good luck, my friend.

## The Prison Outbreak Experience

### Strategy and Chaos

One of the best aspects of Zombicide: Prison Outbreak is the balance between strategy and chaos. You’ll need to plan your moves carefully, cooperate with your team, and adapt to the ever-changing situation. But don’t forget to laugh at the absurdity of fighting zombies in a prison cafeteria.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our team was barricaded in the laundry room, only to realize the zombies were piling up outside. It was like a scene from a sitcom – “The Laundry Room Massacre.”

### Replayability

With multiple scenarios, characters, and unpredictable zombie spawns, no two games are ever the same. Whether you’re trying to escape through the ventilation system or battling zombies in the shower, Zombicide: Prison Outbreak offers endless replayability.

**Expansion Alert**: Keep an eye out for expansions that introduce new tiles, characters, and scenarios. Your prison is about to get even more chaotic.
