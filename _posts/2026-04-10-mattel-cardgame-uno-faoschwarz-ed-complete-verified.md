### Rules and Gameplay: How the FAO Schwarz Edition Differs

#### UNO rules refresher
For those living under a rock or in a house where UNO never makes it to the table the base rules are simple you match color or number with the pile card to get rid of your hand. Special cards Skip Reverse Draw Two Wild Wild Draw Four add flavor strategy and occasional shouting matches. The game ends when someone has one card left shouting UNO to avoid a penalty. The FAO Schwarz Edition does not reinvent UNO instead it polishes the edges with a few tasteful additions. The core loop remains play a compatible card draw if you cannot keep the chaos in motion and prepare to defend your last card with a flourish.
