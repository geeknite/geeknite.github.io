## Gameplay

### Turn Phases

Each turn in **Deal with the Devil** consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions such as gathering resources, constructing buildings, and negotiating (or tricking) their opponents.

3. **Demonic Phase**: The devil may appear to offer deals that are too good to be true. Spoiler: They usually are.
