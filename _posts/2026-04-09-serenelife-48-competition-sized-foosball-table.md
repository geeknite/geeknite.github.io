## Maintenance, Longevity, and Care
No piece of hardware in a home should require a PhD in maintenance, and the Serenelife Foosball Table keeps things approachable. Here are a few practical tips to keep it in fighting shape:

- Regularly wipe down the rods with a damp microfiber cloth to remove sweat, oil, and the occasional snack crumb. A quick wipe between rounds keeps the handles from becoming a sticky burrito of grogginess.
- Lubricate the rods occasionally with a light silicone spray. Do not use heavy lubricants that attract dust, as we are not rebuilding a vintage engine here.
- Check the leg levelers every so often. Floors have a talent for going uneven over time, and a wobbly table is a recipe for missed shots and bruised egos.
- Clean the ball return area to prevent jam-ups from stray coins or foreign objects. Yes, it happens more often than you’d expect at a game night with a lot of snacks and a little alcohol.

In terms of durability, the build quality is consistent with other budget-friendly foosball tables. It’s not engineered to withstand a stadium-grade shooting match, but you don’t need it to. For a household product, it holds up well to the occasional roughhousing of a lively game night. If you are a heavy-handed player or plan to marathon through weekend tournaments, you may want to consider a table with more reinforcement. For the rest of us, this Serenelife is a reliable, friendly option that will likely survive a few years of casual play and a fair number of family bragging rights.
