Overview and core design pillars

The Red Wedding 2e Chapter Pack is designed to slot into existing House decks while offering new angles on control, tempo, and power denial. If your current deck focuses on strong late-game inflection points, you will appreciate the pack’s late-game options. If you enjoy early aggression and tempo plays, you will discover some spicy tools that can tilt the opening phase in your favor without collapsing your late-stage plan.

The pack leans into three core pillars: narrative tension, political timing, and card economy. Narrative tension is achieved by cards that reward or punish delayed reveals, misdirection, and the misreading of opponent intent. Political timing is captured in cards that reward when you pass or when you gain initiative at key moments. Card economy is addressed by a handful of cheaper options that enable you to play more frames of influence and more of the strategic cards you actually want in your hand rather than discarding to draw a new one. The result is a pack that plays with a slightly higher variance floor and a moderate variance ceiling—a sweet spot for players who enjoy both controlled drama and occasional spicy plays.
