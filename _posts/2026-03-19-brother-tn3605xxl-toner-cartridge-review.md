## Installation & Setup Tips
### A quick setup guide
1. Power down the printer and open the front cover.
2. Remove the spent cartridge (if any) and inspect the interior for stray pieces of plastic tape you might have left behind—these sneaky tapes love to cause ghosted prints.
3. Unpack the TN3605XXL, shake it lightly, and remove the protective seals.
4. Align the cartridge with the guide rails and press firmly until you hear a click.
5. Close the cover and run a calibration page. If your printer prompts you, follow the on-screen instructions.

### Common pitfalls to avoid
- Do not touch the toner surface. Oils from your skin can affect print quality.
- Do not leave the cartridge exposed to direct sunlight or heat for extended periods. Toner prefers the calm, not the zen garden sunburn.
- If you see streaks or ghosting, run a cleaning cycle and print a few test pages to re-establish the alignment.

## Maintenance, Longevity & Care
### Keeping it clean
Toner dust can accumulate; a little maintenance goes a long way. Wipe the interior with a dry microfiber cloth during routine printer maintenance to keep things clean. A dusty chamber can cause uneven deposition over time, which is exactly the situation you want to avoid on those 100-page monthly reports.

### Handling rules
Always handle the cartridge with care. Avoid touching the toner surface, and ensure you seal it back up correctly if you need to move it between rooms. If you must transport it across town, keep it upright to avoid toner spillage.

### Replacement cycle & life expectancy
A well-managed lifecycle for the TN3605XXL can span months of steady operation depending on your volume. If you notice a drop in print quality or more frequent refresh cycles, it may be time to inspect for misalignment, dust, or a need for a new cartridge. In most cases, a simple reseat and calibration is enough to restore performance.
