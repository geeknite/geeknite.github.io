## Specifications

Let's kick things off with the specs:

- **Architecture**: Haswell
- **Cores/Threads**: 2/4
- **Base Clock**: 3.5 GHz
- **TDP**: 54W
- **L3 Cache**: 3MB
- **Memory Support**: DDR3-1600
- **PCIe Version**: 3.0

Looking at these specs, you might think, "What is this, a potato?" But let's dig deeper.

## Performance

### Gaming Performance

Ah, gaming on the i3-4150. This CPU can handle older titles like *CS:GO* and *League of Legends* with decent frame rates. But if you try running *Cyberpunk 2077* or *Call of Duty: Warzone*, you'll be greeted with a slideshow that would make PowerPoint jealous. For modern gaming, you'd be better off using this CPU as a paperweight.

### Productivity Performance

For basic productivity tasks like web browsing, video streaming, and office applications, the i3-4150 can still hold its own. It's not going to set any speed records, but it'll get the job done. Video editing and rendering? Forget about it. The i3-4150 will be huffing and puffing like a marathon runner in a pie-eating contest.
