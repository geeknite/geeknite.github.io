## Care, maintenance, and long-term considerations

Caring for a rug like this is straightforward but not magical. Here are best-practice guidelines to keep it looking sharp:

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle suction setting. Avoid beater bars that can catch fibers and exacerbate any edge concerns.
- Clean spills promptly by blotting; avoid rubbing to minimize color migration.
- Use rug pads to reduce movement and to prolong the life of the fibers.
- Rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear, especially if your room has heavy foot traffic on one side.
- Address sun exposure: prolonged exposure to direct sunlight can cause fading in red tones, so rotate or use window treatments to preserve color integrity.

If you keep these steps in mind, the rug should remain a lively floor feature for years to come, with the added benefit of sparking a few design conversations along the way.
