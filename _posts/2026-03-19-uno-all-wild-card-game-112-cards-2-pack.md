## Troubleshooting and common issues
### My deck is too wild to handle
If you and your friends find the extra wilds overwhelming, dial them back. Use only standard wild cards or limit the number of wilds allowed in each round. The All Wild Card edition is designed for flexibility, and you can tailor the rules to your group without losing the core UNO flavor.

### Cards are sticking together
A quick fix is to shuffle with both decks in a pile, and then deal with smaller fan-outs to avoid edge clumping. If sticky fingers persist, consider a quick wipe with a dry microfiber cloth before and after rounds. It’s not glamorous, but it makes play smoother.
