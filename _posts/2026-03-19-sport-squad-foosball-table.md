## Maintenance and care: keeping it glorious

A little maintenance goes a long way. Here are practical tips to keep your Sport Squad singing for years:

- Wipe the surface after use to remove grease and dust. A damp microfiber cloth is enough for most spills.
- Check screws and fittings every few months. Tighten any loose hardware to maintain stability and reduce wobble.
- Lubricate rod bearings sparingly if you notice sticking. Too much lube can attract dust and create a slick, unpleasant playing surface.
- Inspect the goal areas for wear and replace any damaged pieces promptly to prevent balls from getting stuck or bouncing oddly.
- Store balls in a dedicated container to reduce misplacement and keep the table neat.

### Safety notes
Foosball is a safe activity when supervised and played with common-sense precautions. Encourage players to avoid slamming the rods, keep fingers away from the moving joints, and use safe play zones to minimize furniture dings and accidental trips. As with any interactive game, children should be supervised during enthusiastic matches to prevent roughhousing that could lead to bumps and bruises.
