Maintenance Tips and Troubleshooting

To maximize the lifespan and maintain the visual appeal, consider the following tips:

- Cover the set when not in use for extended periods or during winter. A simple breathable cover prevents dust buildup and UV damage.
- Rotate cushions seasonally if you have multiple seating options. This helps prevent uneven wear and keeps colors from settling in one place.
- Clean cushions with a mild detergent and water, then air dry completely before reassembling. Avoid machine washing unless the manufacturer explicitly instructs you to do so.
- Protect the table surface with a heat-insulating pad when placing hot dishes. This prevents heat damage and keeps your dinner from becoming a science experiment in a pit of burnt edges.
- Check screws and joints periodically and tighten as needed. Loose fittings can be the culprit behind squeaks or minor wobble during casual conversations or dramatic toast moments.
