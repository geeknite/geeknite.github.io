## Maintenance and care
Keep your bag in good shape by wiping down the exterior after use. For stubborn stains, a mild soap and water solution works well, followed by air drying. The interior stays clean with regular brushing to remove stray sleeves that migrate into corners. Avoid harsh cleaners that can degrade the fabric.
If you want to maximize the bag life, make a habit of inspecting the zippers and stitching every few weeks, especially if you carry it in rain or snow. Small signs of wear can often be addressed quickly before they become larger problems.
