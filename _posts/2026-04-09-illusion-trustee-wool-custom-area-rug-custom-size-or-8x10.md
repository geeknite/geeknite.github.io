## Custom Size Vs 8x10
- **Custom Size:** This option allows you to specify width and length within a broad range. The process typically involves measuring space, confirming square footage, and ensuring that edge-to-edge transitions align with your furniture.
- **8x10 Standard:** This common size works well in medium living rooms or home theaters. It balances presence with not overpowering a space. It provides enough surface for seating clusters yet maintains a comfortable center for your coffee table or gaming console.
- **Which to choose?** If your room has unique dimensions, or if you want a specific color to align with your gadget rigs or book shelves, go Custom. If your space is typical for a 2-to-4-seater sofa setup, 8x10 is a proven safe bet.

## Maintenance and Cleaning
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting; avoid high suction that could pull fibers.
- Blot spills quickly; wool will resist some staining but not magic. For stubborn stains, a wool-specific cleaner or a carpet care kit recommended by the maker will do the trick.
- Rotate every so often to avoid wear patterns—this is especially important in high-traffic zones.
- Professional cleaning: Some folks opt for a professional wool rug cleaning every 12-24 months depending on use. The Illusion Trustee’s wool composition should handle it well if maintained.

## Durability and Comfort
- **Comfort:** Wool piles deliver a comfortable underfoot feel. The density keeps the rug from feeling flat, and the plushness provides a nice cushion for toes.
- **Durability:** Wool fibers stand up to foot traffic better than many synthetic alternatives. The construction matters: a well-tibered tuft, proper backing, and good dye will ensure years of use.

## Price Range and Value

## Practical Buying Guide: What to Ask Before You Buy
- Is your space truly irregular? If yes, Custom Size is your friend. Confirm the max width and length, and ask about pattern alignment at edges.
- How will lighting impact the color? Natural light versus warm indoor lighting can shift hue perception. Request swatches if possible.
- Do you plan to redecorate soon? A neutral colorway might give you more flexibility later if you decide to switch furniture or paint walls.
- What about the padding? A good rug pad reduces slipping and increases longevity; it’s worth asking about padding schemes and compatibility with your flooring.
