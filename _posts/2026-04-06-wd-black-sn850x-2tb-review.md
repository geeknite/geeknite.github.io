The SN850X uses improved thermal throttling logic and a copper heat spreader in many configurations. If you’re building a compact SFF rig or a laptop, you’ll want to ensure some airflow around the drive or opt for a heatsink variant if you’re purchasing a new build where the drive’s thermals could be a bottleneck.
