## Variants, House Rules, and Longevity

Like any good party game, the Wizard Deluxe Edition invites custom rules. Here are a few ideas that can extend the life of your box beyond the standard playthrough:

- The Dramatic Re-Read: After each round, the winner must deliver a one-minute dramatic reading of the losing card, complete with exaggerated voice and hand gestures. This is not optional unless your group is allergic to theater.
- The If-Only Rule: Introduce a card that lets players replace a line with a single word chosen by the table. This tends to unleash chaotic interpretations and even more ridiculous outcomes.
- The Charity Round: A lighthearted variant where the winner of each round donates imaginary points to a chosen charity in the game world. It’s surprisingly wholesome for a game that sometimes veers into political theater.
