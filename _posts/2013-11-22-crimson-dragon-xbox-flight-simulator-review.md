## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: Engaging flight mechanics and strategic combat.
- **Stunning Visuals**: Beautifully rendered landscapes and detailed dragons.
- **Epic Storyline**: A plot that keeps you hooked from start to finish.
