## Maintenance, Cleaning, and the Practical Side of Sorbet
### Everyday Cleaning Routines
The rug is designed to be forgiving. A weekly vacuum (with a gentle setting) keeps dust and crumbs at bay. For spills, act fast: blot, don’t rub, and treat with a mild cleaner if needed. Red wine is a legendary villain; the key is to act quickly and not to panic. A quick-drying wipe-down after a spill helps prevent stains from setting in. The goal is to preserve that fresh, airy look while maintaining the rug’s plush feel.

### Stain Management and Longevity
For stubborn stains, you may need to use a gentle stain remover, but always test a small inconspicuous area first to avoid color bleeding. The Sorbet’s color-lifting properties are not magical; they’re just good dye chemistry and fiber stability that help you get back to a clean canvas without having to replace the rug every year.
