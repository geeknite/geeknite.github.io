## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity

Caring for this rug is straightforward, which is exactly what busy people want when they’re dealing with life’s other 57 tasks. Regular vacuuming with a low-suction setting helps retain pile height and prevents the fibers from getting overwhelmed by daily dust. For spills, act quickly and blot rather than rub, which helps minimize runny stains that behave like stubborn plot twists. Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear across the surface.
