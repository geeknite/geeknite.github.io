## Collectibility, market, and care
### Grading and value considerations
When evaluating a Mutants Unite sealed set, consider these factors:
- Seal integrity: look for tears, bubbling, or any signs that the plastic wrap has been compromised.
- Card stock and print quality: minor color shifts are expected given the era, but heavy misprints or edge wear on sealed cards are red flags only if they indicate tampering.
- Humidity and storage: exposure to humidity can degrade the paper and ink over time; store sealed sets in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
- Completeness: ensure you have all 55 cards and that they are organized in the correct order. While order does not affect gameplay, it helps in evaluating value and completeness for collectors.
