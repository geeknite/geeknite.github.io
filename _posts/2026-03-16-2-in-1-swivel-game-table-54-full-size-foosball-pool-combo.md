### Assembly timeline and tips

We timed the build and learned several invaluable lessons. Third, prepare a maintenance kit: a small oil bottle for the swivel, some edge protectors to avoid scuffing, and a marker to label the different modes so you don’t turn a pool table into foosball chaos in the middle of a tournament.
