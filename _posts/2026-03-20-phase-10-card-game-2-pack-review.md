What is Phase 10, again? In the simplest terms, it's a card game about completing ten prescribed phases, one after the other. Each phase calls for a different pattern of cards—some sets, some runs, and at least one phase that might feel like you're performing a highly technical card trick. Players draw and discard, trying to assemble the exact combination required for the current phase before finishing that phase and moving on to the next. The wild cards, the skip cards, the sets, the runs—all of it comes together to create a rhythm that can be as hypnotic as it is chaotic.
