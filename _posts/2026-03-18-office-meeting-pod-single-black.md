Like any fine gadget, the pod benefits from a little love. The fabric weave is designed to resist stains, but we recommend treating it like your favorite hoodie: gentle care, not a full sun-drenched laundry marathon.

Durability-wise, the pod components are modular. If a panel scuffs or a hinge squeaks after a few years, you can swap it without replacing the entire unit. Warranty details vary by region, but typical coverage will handle manufacturing defects and frame integrity for a reasonable period. Always verify the exact terms with your vendor before pulling the trigger on the purchase.

### Cleaning and Maintenance Tips
- Wipe down surfaces weekly with a soft microfiber cloth; avoid harsh solvents that may degrade the finish.
- Vacuum the interior gently to remove dust from foam layers; use a brush attachment to avoid fiber pull.
- Check door seals and hinges every few months and lubricate with a light silicone spray if you hear squeaks.
- Keep cables tidy to avoid wear; use cable sleeves or clips to reduce strain.

### Warranty and Support
Most vendors offer a multi-year warranty covering structural components and non-wearing parts. Look for support channels that include email, chat, and local service partners.
