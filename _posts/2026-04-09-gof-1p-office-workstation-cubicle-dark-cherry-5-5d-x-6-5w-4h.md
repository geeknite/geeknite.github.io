## Ergonomics and Comfort

### Desk height, knee space, and chair synergy
With a height range that approaches your typical grown-up ergonomic needs, the GOF 1P pairs well with an adjustable chair. Aim for a seat height that gives you 90-degree angles at the elbows and comfortable knee clearance; you don’t want to wedge your legs into a posture that makes your thighs feel like you are piloting a rocket. The desk depth provides enough knee room for a compact chair and perhaps a footrest if you’re chasing that extra inch of legroom during long coding sessions or spreadsheet sprints.

### Monitor placement and viewing angles
A generous width means you can house a dual-monitor setup with a center gap for a document camera or a small whiteboard. Use a monitor arm that clamps to the desk edge so you can tuck cables back into the channels. The goal is to have the top of your monitors at or slightly below eye level, reducing neck strain and the “look up to win the data war” fatigue after hours of screenshot hunting. If you’re into a 3-monitor setup, ensure you measure the arm reach and the safe clearance behind the desk to avoid bashing the cubicle wall when you adjust height.

### Ergonomic tips for long hours
- Keep the keyboard and mouse within an elbow’s reach to minimize shoulder strain.
- Use a wrist rest to maintain a neutral wrist angle and avoid the dreaded typing-on-air vibe.
- Periodically stand up and stretch; the GOF 1P’s height flexibility means you can alternate between seating and posture Shifts without needing a coffee break to recalibrate your spine.

The dark cherry veneer responds well to standard cleaning routines. Avoid abrasive cleaners and excessive moisture to prevent any veneer wear. Regular dusting keeps the finish looking sharp and helps maintain that “new desk” vibe for longer than you’d expect.

### Durability under everyday use
Given the structure and the materials involved, GOF 1P handles the daily commuter test: laptops, docking stations, and the occasional office plant stepping up as a weight for a test crunch.
