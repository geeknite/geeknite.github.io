Maintenance Tips and Best Practices
- Regularly wipe down the card slots to prevent buildup from oils and sugar residue. The sweeter your snacks, the more you’ll want to clean.
- Use fresh batteries or rechargeables for optimal performance; avoid mixing old and new batteries to prevent a dramatic performance drop mid-game.
- Store the unit in a cool, dry place to prevent condensation or fog on the plastic surfaces. Humidity is not your friend in a Shuffler’s life.
- If you’re using two decks, keep track of warping and bending from heavy use; heavy bending can cause alignment issues in rare cases. Treat your cards with respect; they’ve got a lot of drama left in them.
- If your unit begins to feel warm after prolonged use, give it a short break; electronics appreciate rest too. That momentary cool-down is basically spa time for the internal gears.
