## How to Integrate in Your Office: Practical Guidelines

### Placement Strategy
Place pods in zones that minimize disruption: near quieter corners, away from coffee machines and printers, but within a reasonable stroll from the teams that will use them most. The goal is to reduce travel friction for quick private chats while preserving visibility into the office’s overall flow.

### Scheduling and Etiquette
Establish cockpit etiquette: book ahead when you can, keep sessions within the intended time window, and avoid turning pods into impromptu client spaces for longer than necessary. The better your booking culture, the more value you’ll extract from each pod.

### Maintenance Rituals
Schedule quick daily checks, a weekly wipe-down, and a monthly inspection of the door seal and panels. This is not glamorous work, but it pays off in a quieter, more professional environment for everyone who uses the space.
