## Safety: Because Your Brain is Important

Safety is the number one priority when it comes to helmets, and the Casco Bobike Exclusive doesn't disappoint. It meets all the necessary safety standards, including CE EN 1078 certification. This means it can absorb impacts and protect your head from injuries, whether you're in a minor tumble or a major crash.

The helmet also features **reflective elements** that increase your visibility in low-light conditions. So, if you're cycling at night or in foggy weather, you'll be seen by motorists, fellow cyclists, and maybe even that one squirrel that's always trying to cross the road.
