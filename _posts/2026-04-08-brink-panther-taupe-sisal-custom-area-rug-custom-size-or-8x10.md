### Care and Maintenance
Natural fiber rugs require sensible maintenance, but they reward careful care. Here’s a practical cleaning routine:

- Vacuum on a low or upholstery setting. Not too aggressive; you don’t want to turn the surface into a rough field.
- Wipe up spills quickly with a dry cloth. Blot rather than rub to avoid spreading.
- For stains, test on a hidden area first. Use mild cleaners sparingly; natural fibers can react unpredictably to certain solutions.
- Avoid soaking; if you must clean deeply, consider professional cleaning to maintain color and fiber integrity.
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and reduce movement that could cause wear or edge curling.
- Rotate periodically to ensure even wear, just like you would with a favorite hoodie.
