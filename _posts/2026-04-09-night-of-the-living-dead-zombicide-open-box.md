## Thematic Flavor and Game Mechanics: How Night of the Living Dead Changes Zombicide

Night of the Living Dead is a branch of the Zombicide family that attempts to merge Romero-esque horror with the modular, cooperative action of Zombicide. The edition in Open Box form leans into a few thematic cues:
