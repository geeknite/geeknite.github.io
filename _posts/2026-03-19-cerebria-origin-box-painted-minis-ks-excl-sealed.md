## Assembly, painting strategy, and hobbyist tips
### Assembly reality check
Yes, these are big minis and yes, you will solder some extra time into their bones. Expect to devote painting sessions to pinning, priming, and painting—but the payoff is a dramatic, display-ready set that rewards patience. If you’re a streamer, a slow-and-steady approach makes for satisfying content; if you’re a gamer who wants to play soon, you’ll want a plan that chunks the work into weekend sessions or crowd-sourced painting sprints with friends.

### Painting workflow suggestions
- Prime in a well-ventilated space, then base coat with a mid-tone to preserve detail visibility.
- Use a contrast glaze to bring out eyes and runes without turning the figure into a disco ball.
- Limit color palettes by faction to keep models cohesive on the table.
- Finish with a matte seal to protect delicate lines during table chatter.
