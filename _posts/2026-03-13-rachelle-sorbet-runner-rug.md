## Cleaning and Maintenance
### Everyday care
Maintenance is where most people decide if a rug is truly practical. The Sorbet Runner is designed to be low to mid maintenance. Regular vacuuming on a low setting will keep the fiber lift intact and help remove dust and pet dander that accumulate in hallway environments. For most spills, blotting with a clean cloth and mild detergent is recommended. The goal is to absorb the liquid rather than rub it into the fibers, which can cause staining and fiber distortion over time. For set-in stains or more stubborn marks, a gentle spot treatment following the manufacturer guidance is advised. If you want to dive deeper into stain management, a broader cleaning guide linked earlier covers common household scenarios with practical step by step tips.
### Maintenance schedule and long term care
A suggested maintenance cadence includes vacuuming twice a week for high traffic spaces and rotating the rug every few months to ensure even wear. If your hallway takes on heavy foot traffic during mornings or evenings when you tend to juggle a coffee cup and a tote bag, consider a quick rotation to distribute wear more evenly. This practice also helps preserve color uniformity and avoids a single high-wear zone showing noticeably faster color fade or pattern distortion.
