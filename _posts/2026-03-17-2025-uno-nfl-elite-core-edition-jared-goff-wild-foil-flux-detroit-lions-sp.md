### Displayability and Preservation
Foil cards are more delicate than their non-foil counterparts. They scratch more easily if you aren’t careful with sleeves and binders. A soft sleeve plus a top-load isn’t excessive for a single SP, but if you’re going to spin a display case, consider UV-protective glass and a micro-climate that keeps humidity to a minimum. Nothing kills foil like humidity, which is ironically the opponent you’ll face during summer card-barn escapades. A proper display case with a microfiber cloth for quick gloss maintenance is your friend, especially if you intend to show this beauty off to other collectors on game night.
