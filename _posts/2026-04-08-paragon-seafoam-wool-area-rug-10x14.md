## Care and Maintenance
Caring for a premium wool rug is not a part-time job; it’s a lifestyle choice. Here are practical tips to keep the Paragon Seafoam looking fresh for years:

- Vacuum regularly with a suction-only setting. Avoid beater bars that can pull fibers.
- Rotate the rug every 6–12 months to ensure even wear and sun exposure.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; never rub, as this can embed stains deeper.
- For stubborn stains, use a wool-safe cleaner following the product’s guidelines, and always test on a hidden area first.
- Have a professional cleaning scheduled every 1–2 years if traffic is high or if you’re in a climate with fluctuating humidity.

If you’re dealing with odors, consider a mild baking soda treatment: sprinkle lightly, let sit for a few hours, then vacuum. Always ensure the rug is completely dry before placing it back on the floor to prevent mold growth. For seasonal maintenance, consider a light sun-dry in a clean, shaded area to refresh the fibers without fading the dye. Pro tip: rotate not just for wear but to even out UV exposure across the surface.
