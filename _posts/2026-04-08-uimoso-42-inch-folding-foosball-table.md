### Assembly notes and tips
- Lay out all parts before you start to avoid the classic “where did this extra screw come from?” moment.
- Use a level to ensure the playing surface is perfectly horizontal. A skewed surface ruins ball physics and your ego.
- Check the folding latches twice; a loose latch is the only thing more terrifying than a bad user review from a mid-game wobble.
- When aligning rods, ensure smooth movement without grinding noises. If you hear squeaks, gently re-lube with a light machine oil or silicone-based spray. Do not overdo it; you don’t want the players to slide around like ice skaters.

## Maintenance and care tips
A folding table is only as good as its upkeep. Here are practical steps to ensure your Uimoso 42-inch stays in top shape:
- Wipe down after use to minimize sweat and grime accumulating on the handles and playing surface.
- Inspect the rods periodically for any looseness. Tighten screws if you notice any wobble or play.
- Lubricate moving parts sparingly. A light spray of silicone-based lubricant on the joints can keep things smooth, but don’t go overboard.
- Protect the playing surface from extreme temperatures and direct sunlight, which can cause warping or fading over time.

If you adopt a routine of quick maintenance after each few sessions, you’ll find your folding foosball table remains an enjoyable centerpiece rather than a stubborn, rust-prone ornament.
