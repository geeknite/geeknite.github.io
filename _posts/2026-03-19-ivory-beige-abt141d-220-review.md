## Maintenance and Cleaning
### Routine care
Regular vacuuming is your best friend here. A routine pass every couple of days keeps dust from embedding in the fibers. For spills, address them quickly with a clean cloth or paper towel to blot rather than rub. A gentle spot cleaner suitable for wool or synthetic fibers can handle most common messes; always test in a small inconspicuous area first to avoid color changes.

### Long-term care tips
Rotate the rug occasionally to even out wear, especially in high-traffic zones. Use furniture coasters or protectors to minimize indentations from heavy items. If you have a pet with dramatic culinary experiments, a quick cleanup will be your hero. If you see pilling, a light fabric shaver can help restore the surface without removing too much fiber.
