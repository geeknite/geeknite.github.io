## Durability and Maintenance
### Weather Resistance
The combination of PE wicker and a powder-coated frame makes this set relatively resilient to the elements. It won’t turn to stone in a drizzle, and it won’t melt in a heatwave, though prolonged exposure to harsh conditions can still degrade the wicker and fade the color. That’s not a product flaw; that’s the cost of living in climate reality. If you want to maximize longevity, use furniture covers during extreme weather and bring cushions indoors when storms roll in. The set is built for durability, but it isn’t a teleportation device for time itself—so don’t leave it out there to become part of a future yard sculpture.

### Cleaning Essentials
The cleaning routine is simple: brush away loose debris, wipe with a mild soap solution, rinse with water, and air dry. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could wear down the wicker weave or the fabric. For stubborn stains on cushions, spot-clean with a fabric-safe cleaner and allow to dry completely before use. Regular maintenance is your friend here; neglect invites the drama of mold and mildew, which are basically tiny green goblins impersonating a furniture problem.

### Warranty and Reliability
Most sets in this category come with a limited warranty on frame and a separate warranty on fabric. The specifics vary by seller, but you typically get a year or two on structural integrity and some coverage on fabric discoloration or breakdown. Read the warranty details carefully, because who doesn’t love reading the fine print while sipping a beverage that might be a tad too ambitious for a Monday?
