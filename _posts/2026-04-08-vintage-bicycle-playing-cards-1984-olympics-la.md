## Care, Storage, and Longevity
Show a little patience for vintage stock. The key to keeping these decks alive is proper storage:

If you are a gamer who likes to keep decks pristine, consider a soft sleeve or a vinyl protective case to extend their life while you work through your next long marathon of tabletop gaming.
