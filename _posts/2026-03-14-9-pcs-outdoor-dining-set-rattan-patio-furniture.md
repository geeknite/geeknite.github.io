## Maintenance, Longevity, and Care

The dream of low-maintenance outdoor furniture is a reachable dream, not a myth propagated by cloud servers and glossy brochures. To maximize longevity:

- Regularly clean with a mild soap solution and rinse with water. Dry thoroughly to prevent moisture buildup in the weave.
- Use cushions with removable covers that are machine-washable when possible.
- Use a stabilizing cover during heavy rain or winter months to protect from moisture and pests.
- Check the frame occasionally for loose fittings and tighten as needed. Don’t overtighten screws; you’ll strip the threads and invite a squeaky chair orchestra.

Storage strategies can extend the life of your set. If you have a porch or covered patio, use a breathable furniture cover to prevent mold growth while still allowing air circulation. If you’re in a climate with heavy snowfall, consider storing cushions indoors while using a weatherproof frame outside to maintain its shape and keep the cushions from freezing to the frame in a Batman-style cold embrace.
