The Black WTE Screen is a badge you wear with pride. The gamma curve is well behaved, and the panel is capable of a measured brightness that makes late night coding readable without forcing your eyes into a kaleidoscope. Anti glare performance is decent and does a good job of keeping reflections in check in a bright room. For color graders and video editors, the Black WTE Screen provides a trustworthy canvas for critical grading tasks without turning your life into a constant calibration marathon.

In terms of color accuracy, the display ships with a factory calibration that is good enough for most production pipelines. If you are a color perfectionist, there is a built in 3D LUT workflow that lets you tune to your exact reference target. The bottom line: the Black WTE Screen is a serious panel with a personality that encourages you to stay in the creative flow rather than fighting with gooey icons and washed out color.
