## Display, Storage, and Day-to-Day Care

Aesthetics matter, but presentation matters just as much. The 38-card foil collection deserves a display plan that keeps the shine intact without turning your desk into a mirror maze. Here are some practical tips:

- Display options: Acrylic risers or small glass-tinished shelves let you show off the entire set with minimal glare reduction. If you’re short on space, a shallow binder with padded pages can showcase the foil art while keeping the edges protected.
- Lighting: LED lighting with a soft white or daylight hue enhances foil reflections without washing out the details. A small desk lamp with a dimmer helps you control the “Shine Factor” during game nights.
- Handling: Always handle foils with clean hands and, ideally, a textured playmat to prevent sweat and oils from reducing the foil’s gleam over time.
- Travel: If you’re taking the entire lot to a tournament, consider a travel binder with side-loading pages. The goal is to keep the foils upright and visible so you can quickly reference them during a match and still feel like you’re in a tournament montage.
