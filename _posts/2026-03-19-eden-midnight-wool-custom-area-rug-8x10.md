## Durability and Care: How to keep Midnight looking midnight
Wool is famous for durability, but it isn’t magic. Here’s how to keep your Eden Midnight rug looking fresh through the inevitable spillages, traffic, and that time you accidentally rolled an arcade cart into the room:

- Cleaning and spills: The first rule is quick action. Blot spills with a clean, dry cloth to prevent staining. Avoid rubbing, which can push liquid deeper into the fibers. For stubborn spots, use a mild wool-safe cleaner or follow the product care instructions provided by the retailer. Test any cleaner on a small, inconspicuous area first to avoid color bleed.
- Vacuuming: Use a vacuum with adjustable height and a gentle suction setting. Regular vacuuming keeps dirt from embedding into the wool fibers. Avoid beater bars on plush settings for long periods, as they can loosen fibers over time.
- Rotation: Periodically rotate the rug to even out wear. This helps address high-traffic patterns and prevents a single path from becoming the “main event.”
- Sun exposure: Wool can fade with prolonged sun exposure. If your rug is in a sunlit room, consider rotating every few months or using light curtains to balance the color over time.
- Professional care: For truly stubborn stains or a full refresh, professional wool rug cleaning is worth it. Wool fibers respond well to professional care and can extend the life of your rug beyond typical consumer cleaning cycles.

Preventive care matters as much as reactive care. If you’re a meticulous home chef or a spill-prone gamer, the Eden Midnight is capable of handling the chaos—just be prepared to wipe, blot, and occasionally call in a professional when your “snack-to-footprint ratio” reaches heroic proportions.
