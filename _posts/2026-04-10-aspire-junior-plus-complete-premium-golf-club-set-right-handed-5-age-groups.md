### Cleaning and storage
Keep the clubs clean after practice to maintain grip life and finish. Wipe the grips with a damp cloth and dry them thoroughly. Store the bag in a cool, dry place to prevent warping and fading. If you live in a damp climate, consider a small dehumidifier in the closet where you store the clubs and the snacks.

### Travel tips
When traveling with kids gear, use the headcovers to reduce nicks and dings.
