## Cleaning, maintenance, and everyday care
Synthetic rugs win on maintenance. A practical care checklist:
- Regular vacuuming with low to medium suction; go in multiple directions.
- Blot spills, don’t rub; start from the outside and work inward.
- Treat stains promptly with mild soap and water or a synthetic-safe cleaner. Test in a hidden area first.
- Quick-dry after heavy moisture to prevent mold. A fan helps.
- A non-slip rug pad not only prevents slipping but can extend life by reducing wear.

For deeper cleaning, follow manufacturer care labels. Sometimes a gentle hand-clean or machine wash is possible if the label permits.
