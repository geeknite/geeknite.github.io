## Plot Overview

Imagine this: it's April 6, 1862. Ulysses S. Grant is chilling like a villain near Pittsburg Landing, thinking he's got this whole war thing under control. Little does he know, Confederate forces under General Albert Sidney Johnston are sneaking up on him, ready to give him a wake-up call he won't forget.

The Battle of Shiloh, also known as the Battle of Pittsburg Landing, was one of the major early engagements of the American Civil War. It was a brutal slugfest that caught everyone off guard (except maybe the Confederates), and it taught both sides a thing or two about the harsh realities of war.
