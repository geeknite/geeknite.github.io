## Performance and Maintenance
### Cleaning and Care
Life happens: coffee spills, tea splashes, the occasional cat‑on‑the‑prowl moment. The Quarry Oyster area rug responds well to quick responses. Blot spills with a clean cloth or paper towel—avoid rubbing, which can push the liquid deeper into the fibers. For bigger stains, a mild detergent mixed with water works well. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first to confirm colorfastness. Vacuum regularly using a setting appropriate for low‑to‑medium pile. If you’ve got a high‑traffic zone, consider vacuuming in multiple directions to lift fibers rather than tug them into a one‑direction crush.

### Stain Resistance and Pet Friendliness
The material handles common household messes, and the color helps hide minor stains. If you have pets, your best defense is quick action and a regular cleaning schedule. We observed that light pet hairs and dust are easy to lift with a standard vacuum head. For more stubborn fur, a lint roller or an attachment designed for pet hair can help. Nothing fancy here, just practical maintenance that doesn’t require a carpet cleaning degree.

### Durability Over Time
Rugs in this category typically endure several years of real-world use if cared for properly. The Quarry Oyster’s density and fiber choice support a reasonable life span for a busy family or a long‑term gaming den. It’s not a premium‑grade rug meant for heirloom status, but it’s a reliable workhorse that ages gracefully and doesn’t demand the attention of a full weekly maintenance ritual.
