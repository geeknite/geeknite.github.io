If you’re a pet owner, you’ll care about how the rug handles fur and claws. The plush pile doesn’t trap hair like a magnet, but it does invite a friendly tumble of fluff every few days. ## Maintenance Philosophy: How to Keep Your Rug Fresh
No product is a magical totem that survives forever without care, and this rug is no exception. The maintenance philosophy for the Nike Heart Valentines Rug is simple: clean as needed, protect as possible, and enjoy as much as the joy can stand. Here are the practical steps:

- Vacuum regularly to remove dust, pet hair, and the occasional snack crumb that became a bigger plot point than in your favorite sci-fi show.
- Blot stains immediately. Don’t rub; you don’t want to rub the plot away, and you don’t want to rub those delicate fibers loose. A light blot with a clean cloth should pull most stains out before they become an epic saga.
- For tougher stains, use a mild detergent diluted in water. Test a small inconspicuous area first to ensure colorfastness. Your rug deserves to keep its blush without turning into a fossilized colorway.
- Avoid high heat and harsh chemicals. The goal is to keep the color intact and the fibers resilient, not to create a trendy fossil of the past.
- Rotate occasionally. If you tend to favor one seating area more than another, rotating the rug can keep wear even and honest, which is the kind of ethos we support.

All of this is to say: care for it like your favorite gaming controller. It’s not a sacred idol, but treat it well and you’ll get a longer, more comfortable run out of it before you have to admit you’ve worn out another segment of your living room’s story.
