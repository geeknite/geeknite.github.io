### Cleaning and Maintenance

Cleaning is the mundane hero in this story. Spills happen, and when they do, the best move is to blot up the liquid immediately with a clean cloth. For minor stains, most users report that a gentle spot-clean with a mild detergent and water does the trick. Avoid harsh solvents that might degrade the fiber blend or affect the grey's depth. Regular vacuuming is recommended to keep the pile looking sharp and to prevent dust from turning into a feature in the rug's narrative. If you have pets, consider a lint roller for quick fur pickup in between vacuum sessions, because nothing breaks immersion in your sci-fi streaming marathon like a fur tumbleweed rolling across the screen.
