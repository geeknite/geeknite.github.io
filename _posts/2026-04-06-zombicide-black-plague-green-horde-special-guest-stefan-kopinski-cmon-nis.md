## Strategy tips and early-game recommendations
Here are a few practical tips I picked up during multiple Green Horde sessions. Consider them quick-start ideas to help your squad survive longer and have more laughs while you do it:

- Pace your terrain control. Use doorways and choke-points to funnel Orcs into predictable lanes. The Green Horde creatures love to swarm through gaps, so aim to cut off lines of advance early.
- Prioritize target acquisition. Identify which Orc leaders or archers pose the most immediate threat and allocate your best damage dealers to neutralize them first. A single well-placed hit can dramatically reduce the pressure on your team.
- Balance offense and defense. Don’t overcommit to flashy takedowns; sometimes standing your ground and taking a safe hit is worth more than sprinting for a kill that obscures your next move.
- Manage your loot economy. The new loot tokens can swing late-game results. Don’t hoard everything; think about which items unlock the biggest safety margins for your final push.
- Communicate with timing. The Orc deck’s tempo can surprise you. Callouts like “save the heal for the next round” or “move now to block that corridor” can save the team from crunch moments.

If you’re new to Zombicide, don’t panic. The Green Horde is designed to be friendly to newcomers but deep enough for campaign junkies to sink some serious hours into. It rewards learning by doing, and the Orcs’ mischief ensures you’ll have stories to tell long after the last minion is minced.
