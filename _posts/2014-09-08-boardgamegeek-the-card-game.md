## Introduction

Hello, fellow board game geeks! Are you ready to dive into the meta world of *BoardGameGeek: The Card Game*? Buckle up, because this review is going to be a hilarious rollercoaster of gaming goodness. If you love board games and a good laugh, you're in the right place.

## The Basics

### What is BoardGameGeek: The Card Game?

*BoardGameGeek: The Card Game* is a card game about, well, board games! It's like Inception for board game enthusiasts. Players take on the roles of board game reviewers, trying to earn the most GeekGold by reviewing games, attending conventions, and dealing with the occasional internet troll.

### Components

The game comes with a deck of cards featuring various board games, conventions, and events. The artwork is quirky and fun, capturing the essence of the board gaming community. You'll also get a handful of tokens representing GeekGold and a rulebook that's as thick as a Eurogame's manual.

[![Components](https://i.imgur.com/6s4IJiPm.png){: style='float: right;'}]({{ site.constants.wsib }}boardgamegeek)

## The Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up *BoardGameGeek: The Card Game* is a breeze. Just shuffle the cards, deal out starting hands, and you're ready to go. It's as simple as explaining Terraforming Mars to a newbie (okay, maybe simpler).

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions such as reviewing games, attending conventions, or dealing with internet trolls. The goal is to earn the most GeekGold by the end of the game. Actions are determined by the cards in your hand, and there's a delightful mix of strategy and luck.

### Combat... Wait, There's No Combat

This isn't Gloomhaven, folks. The closest thing to combat is fending off trolls with witty comments. It's all about outsmarting your opponents and collecting those precious GeekGold tokens.

## The Humor Factor

One of the best aspects of *BoardGameGeek: The Card Game* is its humor. The game pokes fun at the quirks of the board gaming community, from the obsession with Kickstarter exclusives to the never-ending debate over sleeves. You'll find yourself laughing out loud as you play, especially when you draw the "Lose a Turn Due to Analysis Paralysis" card.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, a player drew the "Attend Essen Spiel" card right after drawing "Lose a Turn Due to Analysis Paralysis." The irony was not lost on anyone, and we laughed until we cried.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Humorous and Relatable**: Perfect for board game enthusiasts who enjoy a good laugh.
- **Easy Setup and Play**: Great for game nights when you want something light and fun.
- **Strategic Depth**: Despite its humor, there are real strategic decisions to be made.
- **Beautiful Artwork**: The quirky and fun illustrations add to the game's charm.

### Cons

- **Niche Appeal**: May not be as enjoyable for those who aren't deeply into board games.
- **Luck Factor**: The randomness of card draws can sometimes feel frustrating.
- **Troll Cards**: Dealing with internet trolls can be a bit too real for some players.

## Final Thoughts

*BoardGameGeek: The Card Game* is a delightful addition to any board game collection. It's humorous, easy to play, and offers enough strategic depth to keep things interesting. Whether you're a hardcore board gamer or just looking for a fun way to spend an evening with friends, this game is sure to deliver.
