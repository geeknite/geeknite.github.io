## Care and preservation tips

- Handling: wash your hands before handling the cards to prevent oils from degrading the finish. A microfiber cloth can keep faces free of fingerprints.
- Storage: keep the box in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. UV light can fade the colors of the cover art and effect the card stocks’ longevity.
- Humidity: ideal humidity is around 40-60%. Too humid and corners may warp; too dry and the box may crack.
- Cleaning: dust cards gently with a soft brush. Avoid water-based cleaners that could affect inks; a dry approach preserves the original finish.
- Display: if you’re displaying the set, consider a glass-front case with acid-free backing to minimize environmental contact while still sharing your treasure with guests.

These steps will help your Whitman Animal Rummy 2998 look as fresh as the day it hopped off the shelf, even years later. And if you’re ever tempted to upgrade to another Whitman title, you’ll know how to tell a keeper from a mass-produced, shelf-warming stand-in.
