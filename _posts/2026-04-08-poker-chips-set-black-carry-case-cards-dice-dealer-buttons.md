## Maintenance and Care: Keeping It Shiny

Like any good investment for your hobby, this set benefits from a little TLC. Here are practical care tips:

- Clean chips with a soft cloth and avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the color or edge. A gentle wipe-down after a few games can prevent oils and residue from building up.
- Store the set in a cool, dry place. Heat and humidity can affect the finish and the case’s fit over time.
- Inspect the deck periodically. If you notice a few bent cards, replace them rather than forcing the deck. A well-kept deck improves shuffles and reduces stray misreads during tense moments.
- Check the case zipper and foam fit. If the foam gets compressed, you can rearrange the interior to maximize the fit and protect the chips from shifting during transport.

Maintenance might not be the most thrilling part of your gaming, but it pays off in the long run. A well-kept set can outlive several room-mate seasons and still be ready for the next big game night.
