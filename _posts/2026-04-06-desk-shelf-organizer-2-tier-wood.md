## Maintenance: It’s Wood, Not a Chocolate Bar

Wood is forgiving but not immortal. To keep your organizer looking sharp:
- Dust regularly with a microfiber cloth. Don’t use harsh chemical sprays that can degrade the finish over time.
- Wipe spills immediately to prevent staining. Coffee threats are real in the wild world of office life.
- Avoid placing extremely heavy items on the top shelf for prolonged periods. If you need to store a heavier device, shift it to the bottom tier to keep the structure balanced.
- Periodically check screws or fasteners if your unit uses them. A loose connection is the difference between “shipshape” and “shipwreck.”
