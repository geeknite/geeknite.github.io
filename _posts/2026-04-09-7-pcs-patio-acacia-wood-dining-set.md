### Weather resilience and maintenance

Acacia wood holds up well in sunlight and rain, especially when treated with a protective coat. Periodic maintenance helps maintain color and structural integrity.

Care calendar:

- Every 3-6 months: clean with mild soap and water; lightly sand rough spots and reapply outdoor wood oil
- Spring and fall: check for loose screws and joints; tighten as needed
- After rain: wipe down with a soft cloth to remove moisture residue
- Winter: store cushions indoors if possible; cut down on moisture exposure with covers when not in use

If you see pale or dark patches after a season, that’s normal wood aging. A light sanding followed by oiling can even out the color and bring back your set’s warm glow.
