## Setup Guide: Quick Assembly Summary
- Inventory check: Confirm all panels, screws, bolts, and rubber feet.
- Frame assembly: Start with the base frame and attach upright supports. Tighten all fasteners in a cross pattern to ensure even stress distribution.
- Desk surface: Place the desk surface atop the frame and secure it with screws. Double-check alignment before tightening fully.
- Partition installation: Attach the cubicle panels, making sure your walls are flush and not skewed. Remember: symmetry is satisfying and helps you sleep at night.
- Cable management: Route cords through the troughs and grommets. Label critical cables for future maintenance.
- Final check: Sit, adjust chair height, test monitor distance, and revel in your new personal command center.

Estimated time: A realistic 2–4 hours, depending on your level of IKEA patience and whether you’re assembling alone or with a friend who offers unsolicited tips.
