Deck-Building Ideas: How to Make the Most of Her

Idea 1: Soldier Swarm with Lifegain Backbone

Idea 3: Clean Anti-Combo and Utility Model
- Core plan: Build a resource-friendly deck that uses Elspeth as a reliable generater of board presence while offering flexibility against combo strategies. The borderless foil art is a nice flourish, but the real value is keeping your deck tight, efficient, and capable of handling a variety of strategies with minimal fuss.
- Key considerations: Include flexible removal, resilient win conditions, and a few surprise interactions that keep opponents guessing. The opposite of a glass cannon—your deck should be sturdy, even when your commander is not at the helm.
