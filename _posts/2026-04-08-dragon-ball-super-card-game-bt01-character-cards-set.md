## Storage, care, and preservation

Care is a small but important part of the collecting game. For BT01 and similar sets, use standard card storage practices:

- Use a solid binder with soft protectors to maintain card integrity without scratching the surface.
- Keep away from direct sunlight to avoid fading of the art and gloss that these cards carry.
- Consider a desiccant pack in your storage box to manage humidity, especially in warm climates.
- If you want to protect art prints, do not subject the cards to rough handling or excessive flexing. After all, the story lives in the ink, not in the creases.
