## Core mechanics
The heart of the game is hand management, tempo, and careful planning. Each turn you will draw cards to replenish your hand, then you will play cards to achieve objectives, and finally you will discard some cards to maintain a manageable hand size. The clever trick is that many cards can be played in different ways depending on timing and the state of the board. The order you choose to resolve effects matters, and misordering can explode in your face in the most entertaining ways.

The classic edition uses a simple action economy with a handful of actions per turn. The exact number might vary depending on the variant you are playing, but the feeling remains the same: you must juggle multi step goals, anticipate opponents moves, and keep an eye on the clock.
