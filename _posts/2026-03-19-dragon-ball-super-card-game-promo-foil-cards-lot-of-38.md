## Sorting, storage, and tactical display tips
If you own 38 foil promos, you are allowed to feel a little like a curator. Here are some practical tips to get the most out of your shiny stash:
- Use uniform sleeves: invest in 9-12 common sizes to ensure all foils sit evenly in your binder. A consistent sleeve experience makes flipping through your collection a joy rather than a chore.
- Binder mapping: sort by character line, rarity, or art style. A well-planned binder makes it easier to locate the exact foil you want for trades or quick deck references.
- Protective outer covers: for display, consider protective sheets and a display binder that can handle frequent flipping. This helps preserve the gloss and avoid edge wear from friction.
- Humidity and temperature: foils can be sensitive to extreme conditions. Keep your binder in a climate-controlled space to avoid warping or fogging that sometimes affects glare.
- Cataloging: keep a small inventory list of which cards are in the lot, along with condition notes. It makes future trades simpler and helps you defend your valuation when someone questions your pricing.
