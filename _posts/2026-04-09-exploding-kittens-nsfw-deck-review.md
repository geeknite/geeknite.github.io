## How to play a quick game (fast-start guide)

For players who want to jump in without squinting at the rulebook, here’s a quick-start approach that keeps things short and spicy:

1) Shuffle the deck thoroughly and deal 7 cards to each player (or follow the version printed in your rulebook).
2) Place the Exploding Kitten deck in the center and keep the remaining deck face down.
3) On your turn, you can either draw a card or play a card from your hand. Playing cards alters the deck or impacts other players, so plan your moves carefully.
4) If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you must play a Defuse card if you have one. If you can’t Defuse, you’re out of the game. The Exploding Kitten is then reshuffled into the deck in a way that keeps everyone guessing where it’s coming from next.
5) The See the Future card lets you peek ahead at the top cards, giving you a strategic edge for the next round. Use this to plan whether to push your luck or bail out before you’re kissed by fate.
6) The game ends when only one player remains standing, preferably with a smile or a grimace depending on how many Defuses you burned through.

That’s the skeleton. The NSFW deck adds short, sharp lines and some new card effects that nudge the dynamic in interesting ways. You’ll find that a good round can swing on a single misread or a perfectly timed See the Future reveal.
