## How to Play: A Quick Overview
This edition keeps the core engine intact: players draw cards, aim to avoid getting the Exploding Kitten, and try to manipulate draws through a set of special actions. The Defuse cards let you survive a catastrophic draw, while other cards let you weaponize the deck, skip turns, or alter the order of draws. If you’ve played the standard Exploding Kittens, you’ll notice the NSFW edition adds punchy dialogue, more aggressive visuals, and a few new action cards that tilt the table dynamics toward aggressive play or bold diplomacy—depending on how you read the room.

Key points to remember:

- Objective: avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten without a Defuse card or another mitigating effect.
- Turn flow: draw a card, or play an action card that alters the draw, skip your turn, or sabotage your opponents.
- Defuse: a precious resource that saves you from blowing up; it’s the lifeline you clutch when chaos erupts.
- End of round: when a kitten detonates, the round ends and points or elimination is tallied based on surviving players.

The NSFW edition doesn’t reinvent the wheel so much as intensify it. The rules are accessible enough for a first-timer, yet the more seasoned party-game folks will enjoy the added zingers and the risk-reward calculus of pushing your luck with a saucy twist.
