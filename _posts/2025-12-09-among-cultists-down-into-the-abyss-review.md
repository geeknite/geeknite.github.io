## The Basics

### What is Among Cultists: Down Into The Abyss?

Among Cultists: Down Into The Abyss is a hidden role game with a Lovecraftian twist. Imagine if your favorite social deduction game had a love child with an H.P. Lovecraft novel, and you get the idea. Players take on the roles of investigators or cultists, each with their own secret objectives, all while trying to complete tasks and avoid the lurking horrors of the abyss.

### Components

The game comes with incredibly detailed miniatures, beautiful artwork, and a board that feels like it was ripped straight from the pages of a dark fantasy novel. The components are top-notch and add to the immersive experience.

**Pro Tip**: Don't let your pet near the miniatures. You don’t want Fluffy summoning any eldritch horrors by accident.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Among Cultists is like preparing for a ritual. Lay out the board, distribute roles, and prepare for a night of deceit and dark magic. It’s essential to make sure everyone understands their role before diving into the abyss.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players move around the board, complete tasks, and use their special abilities. However, beware! Cultists are lurking among you, ready to sabotage your efforts and summon unspeakable horrors. The tension between cooperation and suspicion is what makes this game shine.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our group spent half the night accusing the dog of being a cultist. Turns out, it was the quietest player all along. Never trust the silent type!

### Objectives

The investigators must complete their tasks and unmask the cultists, while the cultists aim to thwart the investigators' efforts and summon the ancient evil. The game ends when either all tasks are completed, the investigators are outnumbered, or the ancient evil is summoned.

With multiple roles, objectives, and scenarios, no two games of Among Cultists are ever the same. Each playthrough offers new twists, strategies, and hilarious moments, ensuring you'll keep coming back for more.

### Humor and Hilarity

The game's dark theme is balanced by its ability to create laugh-out-loud moments. Whether it's a player’s ridiculous bluff or a cultist’s botched sabotage, there’s no shortage of humor.
