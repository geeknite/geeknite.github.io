When you do this, the extra bodies become a force multiplier rather than a distraction that causes chaos therapy sessions.

### Mission Design and Variability
The new missions are crafted to push your group into new strategic neighborhoods. The variety helps prevent fatigue and keeps the table engaged, which is vital because nothing kills a game night faster than repeating the same exact sequence for three hours while you wish for a pizza to arrive early.

### Combat Tactics and Weapon Diversity
The expansion introduces new weapons and tool kits that synergize with existing gear.
