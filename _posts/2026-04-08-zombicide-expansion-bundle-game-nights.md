New missions push your group into strategic neighborhoods you didn’t even know existed. ## Practical Tips for Getting the Most Out of the Bundle
- Start with a quick intro mission to get everyone acclimated to the new mechanics before diving into longer campaigns. A gentle warm-up will save you from a dramatic table flip later.
- Use a simple action log so you can track who did what in a multi-survivor turn, reducing chaos and raising the satisfaction bar when the round ends.
- Pre-organize the new terrain tiles into labeled bags and dedicate a small area of your table to the expanded map sections. You’ll thank future you for this on night four of a rowdy campaign.
- Rotate new survivors into the group gradually to avoid overwhelming new players with too many options at once. You want enthusiasm, not a panic attack in the middle of setup.
- Make a small cheat sheet for weapons and abilities so you can reference them quickly during play rather than flipping through the rulebook every other turn.
