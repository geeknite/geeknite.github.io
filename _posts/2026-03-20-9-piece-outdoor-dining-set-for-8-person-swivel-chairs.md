Weather resistance and maintenance tips

Outdoor furniture is a commitment to sunshine, rain, and the occasional hailstorm of lawn debris. The right set should resist moisture and UV damage, while cushions should resist mold and mildew. Here are practical maintenance tips:

- Regularly wipe down the table and chairs with a mild soap solution and a soft cloth.
- When not in heavy use, store cushions in a dry place or use storage bags to prolong life.
- Use UV-protective sprays on wicker surfaces to slow down fading.
- Check screws and bolts periodically for tightness, especially after a season change when tiny critters pretend to be master builders.
- Consider a furniture cover during extreme winter weather to extend the life of your set without needing a cry for help in the form of a new set next spring.
