## Painting Tips for Kickstarter Exclusive Promo Survivors
If you are into painting, promo survivors are a dream. The extra free space on the miniature gives you more room to practice. Here are a few quick tips to maximize the look of your promo crew without turning the process into a six-week project:
- Prime with a neutral gray or brown base; it helps to pick out the armor and cloth details more clearly.
- Use a wet palette and a fine brush for armor highlights. A subtle edge highlight on the helmet rims can make the miniature pop under standard gaming lighting.
- Work on contrast first. Get the base skin tones established, then push the armor and cloth with a couple of complementary washes.
- Seal with a matte varnish to preserve the details. A satin finish can also give the mini a more “professional tabletop” vibe.
- Consider basing with a thematic texture—dark soil, molten lava effects, or cobblestones—so the character feels integrated into the Black Plague world rather than standing apart from the other minis.
