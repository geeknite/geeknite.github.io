## Introduction

Welcome, fellow detectives and mythic dreamers! Today, we're diving into the enigmatic world of **City of Mist: Fortune Row**. If you love noir settings mixed with the supernatural, this RPG expansion will tickle your fancy, and probably steal your wallet in the process.

Let's don our trench coats, grab a magnifying glass, and embark on a thrilling review that's packed with mystery, laughs, and maybe a few plot twists.

## What is City of Mist: Fortune Row?

*City of Mist: Fortune Row* is an expansion for the popular noir RPG *City of Mist*. This setting introduces new characters, locations, and scenarios that will have you solving mysteries and uncovering secrets like Sherlock Holmes on a caffeine high.

### Components

The expansion includes:
- **Character Cards**: New mythic characters to add to your roster. Ever wanted to be a detective with the powers of an ancient god? Now you can!
- **Location Maps**: Detailed maps of Fortune Row, the city's most mysterious and dangerous borough.
- **Scenario Booklets**: Engaging scenarios that will challenge your deductive skills and keep you on the edge of your seat.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Fortune Row is like preparing for a night out in the most dangerous part of town. Lay out your location maps, distribute character cards, and brace yourself for an adventure filled with twists and turns.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players can perform actions like investigating clues, confronting suspects, and using their mythic abilities to solve the case. The synergy between characters and their unique powers is where City of Mist shines.

### Combat

Combat in Fortune Row is a blend of strategy and improvisation. Players must coordinate their attacks and use their mythic abilities to defeat enemies and overcome obstacles. Remember, teamwork and quick thinking are key to surviving the mean streets of Fortune Row.

## The Story

### Scenarios

The scenarios in Fortune Row are inspired by classic noir tales, with a supernatural twist. From hunting down a rogue deity to uncovering a secret society, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different strategies.

### Characters

Fortune Row introduces a host of new characters to the mix. Play as the enigmatic Detective Hades or the cunning Femme Fatale Lilith. Each character has distinct abilities and combos that keep the game fresh and exciting.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Immersive Gameplay**: Feel like a true detective as you unravel mysteries and confront supernatural foes.
- **High-Quality Components**: The artwork and maps are top-notch, bringing the noir setting to life.
- **Replayability**: Multiple scenarios and characters ensure endless fun.
- **Strategic Depth**: Using mythic abilities and detective skills adds a layer of strategy that keeps players engaged.

### Cons

- **Complex Setup**: The initial setup can be time-consuming.
- **Luck Factor**: Some elements of the game rely heavily on luck.
- **Expansions Needed**: To keep the game fresh, expansions are almost a must.

## Final Thoughts

City of Mist: Fortune Row is a fantastic addition to any RPG collection, especially for fans of noir and the supernatural. Its immersive gameplay, high-quality components, and endless replayability make it a must-have. Just remember, teamwork and strategy are key to solving the mysteries of Fortune Row.

**Criticism Reminder**: While the game is incredibly fun, it’s not without its flaws. The setup can be time-consuming, and some elements rely heavily on luck. But for those willing to embrace the chaos, Fortune Row is an absolute blast.
