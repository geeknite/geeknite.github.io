## Maintenance and Cleaning: Keeping It Clean Without Burning Time
Synthetic fibers are generally forgiving when it comes to stains and cleaning. The Satori Royal rug is designed to resist common spills and allow quick cleaning with minimal scrubbing, which is exactly what you want after the coffee incident that somehow ended up in the theater room and on your favorite laptop (we’ve all been there).

Cleaning steps tend to be straightforward:

- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth to prevent staining.
- Use mild detergent and warm water for spot cleaning; avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the fibers or alter color.
- Vacuum regularly using a standard setting (avoid high-power beater bars that might loosen the pile).
- For more stubborn stains, consider a gentle carpet cleaner that’s recommended for synthetic fibers. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first.

Maintenance myths debunked: the rug won’t shed like a woolly sweater, and it won’t turn your living room into a “fake fur catastrophe” if you treat it with a little respect. The goal is to keep the rug looking fresh, not pristine museum-grade—unless your living space doubles as a gallery, in which case, carry on.
