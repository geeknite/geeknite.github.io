The build quality leans into the “solid-but-not-landing-gear” school of design. ## Assembly and Installation: It’s Mostly Flat-Pack Magic
### The Quick Assembly Narrative
If you’ve assembled a handful of prefab desks before, the Eternal falls into the same family: there will be a few long bolts, a surprising number of Allen keys, and a moment where you question your life choices. The manufacturer ships it in modular components designed for a two-person installation window. Expect a weekend project if you’re meticulous, and a Sunday afternoon if you’re a little too confident about your “I can assemble anything” vibe.

Steps typically look like:
1) Lay out components and verify all parts against the included parts list.
2) Attach legs to the central beam(s) using the provided hardware.
3) Align surface panels and secure to the frame with durable fasteners.
4) Route power modules and cable backbone, then mount screen arms and monitor mounts as needed.
5) Tighten, test, and sit down to the first epic meeting of the Eternal club.
