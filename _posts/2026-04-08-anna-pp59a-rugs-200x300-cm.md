## Care guide and cleaning tips

Caring for the Anna PP59A is simple and practical:

- Vacuum regularly with a low-power setting and a brushless head to prevent fiber pull.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can spread the stain and rough up the fibers.
- For stains, use a mild, pH-neutral detergent with water. Test on a small area first to ensure color safety.
- Deep cleaning is rarely necessary, but if needed, professional cleaning is recommended about every couple of years depending on usage. Do not machine wash unless the manufacturer explicitly states it’s safe for your rug model.
- Heat is your enemy. Avoid direct heat while cleaning or drying; air-dry to maintain fiber integrity and color.
