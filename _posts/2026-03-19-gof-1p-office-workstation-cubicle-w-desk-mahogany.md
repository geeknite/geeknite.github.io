### Assembly and Setup

Setting up the GOF 1P is a multi-step journey, but not an odyssey. Expect a few hours of unboxing, sorting, and the occasional curse word when a bolt seems to vanish into the abyss. The package includes clear labeling, which is a miracle in itself in the world of flat-pack furniture. The instructions are straightforward, not a cryptic map to the buried treasure of a missing screw.

- Tools you’ll probably need: a standard screwdriver, an Allen key (the IKEA-internal-emergency-weapon), a measuring tape to ensure your cubicle is centered in the room, and coffee to power through the final tightening ritual.
- A tip: assemble on a soft surface to prevent scratches on your brand-new mahogany veneer. It’s not the end of the world if you slip a screw once, but you won’t forget it either.
