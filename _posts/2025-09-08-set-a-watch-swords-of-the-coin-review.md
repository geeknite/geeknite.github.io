First things first, let's talk about the goodies inside the box. **Set a Watch: Swords of the Coin** comes with a plethora of components that will make any board game enthusiast drool like a hungry dragon.

- **Player Boards**: Beautifully designed with spaces for your heroes and their abilities. It's like having your own personal scroll of awesomeness.

- **Coins**: Yes, actual coins! Use these shiny treasures to hire mercenaries, upgrade your gear, or bribe your friends to stop hogging the healing potions.

## Gameplay

### Setup

Setting up **Set a Watch: Swords of the Coin** is a breeze. Lay out the player boards, distribute the coins, shuffle the cards, and roll the dice. Voila, you're ready to battle the forces of darkness!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players must decide who will venture out to fight monsters and who will stay back to guard the camp. It's a tough choice, like deciding between pizza and tacos.

### Combat

Combat is a blend of strategy and luck. Use your heroes' abilities, roll the dice, and play cards to defeat monsters. The challenge is real, and the stakes are high. Just don't blame the dice for your poor rolls.

## Components Quality

The quality of the components in **Set a Watch: Swords of the Coin** is top-notch. The coins feel weighty and satisfying, the dice are beautifully crafted, and the cards are durable enough to withstand countless battles. The artwork is stunning, immersing you in the game's magical world.
