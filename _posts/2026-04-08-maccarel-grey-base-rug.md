## Cleaning and Maintenance Guidelines
Even the most stylish rug needs a practical care routine. Here is a straightforward approach to keeping the Maccarel Grey Base Rug looking its best without requiring a laboratory manual

### Daily and weekly care
- Vacuum once or twice a week depending on foot traffic. Use a vacuum with adjustable height so you do not scoop up the pile inadvertently
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth, working from the outside toward the center to prevent spreading
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and prevent coloring drift from direct sunlight
- Use a rug pad if your floor is slick. It prevents shifting and adds a touch of cushion for those who take the express route across the room on a lazy Sunday

### Deep cleaning and stain treatment
- For tougher stains, apply a mild detergent solution to a clean cloth and dab gently. Avoid scrubbing as this can drive the stain deeper or distort the pile
- If a deeper clean is needed, consider a professional rug cleaning service that handles synthetic blends. They will likely use a low moisture method that protects fibers while reviving colors
- Odor removal can be aided by baking soda; sprinkle lightly, let sit, then vacuum. This helps with light odors without affecting the color or texture

### When to consider replacement
Rugs are not forever, even the durable ones. Signs that it might be time for a swap include fraying edges that cannot be repaired, persistent permanent staining that changes color beyond a simple refresh, or noticeable flattening that cannot be reversed with a professional cleaning. If you notice a significant difference in feel underfoot after multiple cleanings or if the rug no longer coordinates with your current decor, it might be time to consider a new base rug that keeps pace with your evolving style
