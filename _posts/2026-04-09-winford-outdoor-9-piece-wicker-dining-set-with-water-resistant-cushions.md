## Weather Resistance and Maintenance
Outdoor furniture faces sun, rain, wind, and the occasional pet experiment. The Winford set is designed with that in mind. The wicker’s synthetic resin includes UV inhibitors that help prevent color fade after months of exposure. The aluminum frame remains rust-free, keeping structural integrity intact even if a storm sweeps through. Cushions are breathable and quick-drying, which helps minimize mildew risk after a rain. Still, you’ll want to place cushions in a dry spot during prolonged wet weather or store them in a breathable bag when not in use for an extended period.

Maintenance tips:
- Wipe the frame with a damp cloth; avoid abrasive cleaners that might dull the finish.
- Remove cushion covers and wash according to the care label; air-dry thoroughly before reassembly.
- Use a mild soap solution for stubborn spots on wicker, and rinse clean.
- If you have a sheltered patio, you can keep the cushions outside longer, but you’ll still want to rotate and fluff them occasionally to prevent permanent flattening.
