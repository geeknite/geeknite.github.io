## Performance: Cleaning and Maintenance
Cleaning is part of the adventure. Vacuum regularly to remove dust and dinosaur bones—err, I mean dust and crumbs. For spills, blot with a clean cloth; do not rub, because rubbing is for the gym, not for fossil rugs. For deeper cleans, you can spot clean with mild detergent and water. If you want to go full Alan-Turing-who-tests-the-rug-cleaning-lab, you can consider a gentle machine wash if the manufacturer allows, but always check care labels first.

### Rug care tips
- Vacuum at least once a week.
- Rotate every month to ensure even wear.
- Use a gentle detergent if you spot-clean.
- Avoid heat exposure to preserve colors.
