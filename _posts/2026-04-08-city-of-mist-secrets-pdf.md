## How to incorporate The Secrets of the City into your table
If you’re running a City of Mist campaign, this PDF can slot into an ongoing story or anchor a new arc. Here are practical steps to maximize its value:

- Start with a single mystery: pick a new secret and weave it into a local district’s identity. Let the players choose who within the city holds the key to unlocking it. The process should feel like a collaborative heist with more talking than fighting—until it isn’t.
- Introduce a new mask early: give a player a reason to experiment with a different mask’s lens. The moment a character starts seeing through a new mask’s perspective, you’ll hear the table lean in with curiosity and a few lines of dramatic indirection.
- Use modular encounters: use the micro-scenarios as side quests to punctuate longer investigations. You’ll create natural pacing without forcing a rigid structure upon your narrative.
- Embrace the city’s rumor economy: the Secrets of the City emphasizes rumors as a resource. Treat rumors as a tangible thing—something the players can trade, challenge, or chase—rather than a loose phenomenon.
- Plan for consequences: the city’s secrets should ripple outward. Prepare a few escalation points that connect the PCs’ actions to the district’s fate. The payoff should feel earned, not handed down from a GM fiat.
