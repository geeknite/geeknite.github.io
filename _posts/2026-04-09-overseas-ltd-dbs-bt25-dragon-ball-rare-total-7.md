## Practical Buying Tips: how to chase Total 7 without losing your mind
- Set a realistic budget: Decide in advance how much you are willing to invest and treat it like a hobby tax rather than an impulse fund. If your budget is a fantasy novel, you’re likely to end up with a bookshelf full of regrets and a binder full of dreams.
- Verify authenticity: Check foil consistency, print text alignment, and the card back. Overseas prints can look pristine but still hide telltale signs of misprints or reprints. Do your due diligence; a little extra effort saves you a lot of heartache.
- Watch sale cycles: Some sellers flood the market after a big tournament, while others hold back for a premium. Timing matters as much as pricing. If you can, blend patience with occasional bold moves.
- Leverage trade networks: Build relationships with trusted traders. It’s less about haggling and more about building a small community that can vouch for cards and reduce the risk of scams.
- Understand shipping realities: Overseas shipments can involve longer wait times, customs checks, and occasionally creative packaging. Be prepared for delays and keep your sense of humor intact.
