## What is Fortune Row?
In City of Mist, Fortune Row is the mood board for your hero's life—an alleyway of myths where legends shop for presence, drama, and perhaps a consent form from destiny. The Fortune Row dice set takes that vibe and packs it into a color-coded polyhedral constellation designed to map the row’s emotional spectrum: hope, fear, swagger, and sarcasm. The kit typically includes a selections of polyhedral dice (d6, d8, d10, and d12 or a d20 variant) alongside tokens or a tray to keep the mood in check. The idea is simple: every roll is not just a number, but a story beat that nudges the scene in evocative directions.

The aesthetic is unapologetically noir with neon accents. The dice look like they crawled out of a back-alley tarot deck and got a corporate makeover. The color palette leans into smoky purple, electric teal, and brass-gold that shouts, I may be dice, but I also know budgeting. The color sets are not just pretty; they’re practical. Each hue correlates with a narrative function in Fortune Row play: green for luck and ally checks, red for danger and conflict, blue for knowledge and investigation, and gold for social influence and power. If you’re into metaphors, think of these dice as tiny fortune cookies that came with a dress code and higher expectations.

The kit often ships with a small tray or rolling mat that says premium gamer while remaining perfectly usable on a cramped apartment table. The dice themselves are resin or acrylic, with a smooth feel and crisp numerals that stay legible under dim lighting. Edges are rounded enough to avoid accidental paper-cut vibes but not so rounded that you forget what number you rolled. The finish is a respectful satin that catches the glow without blinding you when you lean in for a closer inspection of a misprinted numeral.
