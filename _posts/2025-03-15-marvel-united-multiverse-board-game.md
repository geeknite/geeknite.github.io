## What is Marvel Dice Throne?

Marvel Dice Throne is a competitive dice game where players take on the roles of iconic Marvel superheroes, each with unique abilities and decks of dice. The goal? To defeat your opponent using strategic dice rolls, special abilities, and a healthy dose of luck. With stunning artwork and cool components, this game offers an immersive experience that will keep Marvel fans and board game enthusiasts alike engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Marvel Dice Throne, players choose their superhero and roll dice to activate abilities, attack opponents, and defend themselves. Each hero has a character board, a deck of cards, and custom dice. The game is designed for 2-6 players, making it perfect for one-on-one duels or chaotic group battles.

### Components

The Marvel Dice Throne: 2-Hero Box 2 includes:
- **Character Boards**: Beautifully illustrated boards for Black Panther and Captain Marvel, detailing their abilities and stats.
- **Custom Dice**: Each hero has their own set of unique dice, adorned with symbols representing their powers.
- **Cards**: Decks of cards for each hero, used to enhance abilities, hinder opponents, or unleash devastating attacks.
- **Tokens**: Various tokens to track health, status effects, and other game elements.

**Pro Tip**: Keep an eye on Black Panther's vibranium reserves. They're more valuable than your grandma's secret cookie recipe.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Marvel Dice Throne is quick and easy. Lay out the character boards, shuffle the decks, and distribute the dice. Decide who will play as Black Panther and who will take on the role of Captain Marvel. Each player starts with a set amount of health, and the battle begins!

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players roll their dice and choose how to use them. You can attack, defend, or activate special abilities. Cooperation isn't necessary here—it's all about outsmarting and overpowering your opponent.

### Combat

Combat in Marvel Dice Throne is a blend of strategy and luck. Roll the dice, match symbols, and unleash your hero's powers. Will Black Panther's stealthy strikes outdo Captain Marvel's cosmic blasts? Only the dice know.
