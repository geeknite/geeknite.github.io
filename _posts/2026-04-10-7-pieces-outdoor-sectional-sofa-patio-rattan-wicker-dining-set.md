## Weather Resistance and Maintenance

The real test of any outdoor set is climate resilience. The rattan weave, when combined with the aluminum frame, typically handles sun, humidity, and light rain quite well. Here are practical tips we found helpful:

- Use furniture covers during prolonged rain or intense storms. It extends the life of the cushions and helps preserve the color.
- Bring cushions indoors at night or when temperatures drop significantly. Freezer-breeze cushions are not ideal for comfort or longevity.
- Clean the weave with a soft brush and a mild soapy solution. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could dull the finish.
- Check cushions for zipper wear and fabric pilling after several months of use. Replacement covers are usually available through the retailer or the brand’s official portal.

Weather resistance also means you should consider UV exposure. Sunlight can fade fabrics; a shaded patio or an offset umbrella can keep your set looking fresher, longer. If you have a fence line that blocks late-day sun, your cushions will thank you for a longer life courtesy of reduced UV bombardment.

## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

- Periodic inspection of the frame for any loosening of bolts. A quick tighten every season helps prevent creakiness and misalignment.
- Remove cushions during heavy wind events. The wind can rearrange your furniture into a companion statue for the neighbor’s garden gnomes.
- Use a gentle fabric cleaner for cushions and consider a protective spray for the fabric to maintain water resistance over time.
- Store cushions properly during winter or long periods of non-use. If space is tight, stack cushions in a dry closet or garage, keeping them away from moisture and extreme temperatures.

A practical note: if you live in an area with heavy pollen, consider a light protective mist on the weave before pollen season to minimize surface grime. It is not a maintenance miracle, but it helps reduce cleaning time and keeps your lounging vibe clean and stylish.
