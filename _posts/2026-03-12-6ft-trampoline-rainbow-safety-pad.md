## Age range safety guidelines and best practices

The manufacturer s recommended age range is typically kids aged 3 and up with weight limits that vary by model for a 6 ft trampoline the typical weight limit hovers around 200-250 lbs for casual bouncing if you are using the trampoline for multiple children or a family bounce keep a rule in place to avoid overcrowding and ensure that no single jump area is overloaded this helps protect the springs and reduces the chance of collisions

Important safety steps include
- Always place the trampoline on a flat level surface
- Use the included safety pad to cover springs and frame edges
- Supervise young children no flips or stunts unless you re trained and wearing proper mats
