## Maintenance tips for carbon steel blades
Carbon steel blades require routine care. Wipe down after handling to remove fingerprints and moisture. A light coat of oil on the blade helps prevent rust and improves edge retention. If you notice any rust spots, address them quickly with a light rust remover and reapply a fresh coat of oil. Store the dagger in a dry environment and consider occasional polishing to preserve the dark patina on the blade that gives it that dramatic stage presence.
