From a durability standpoint, the mat handles light spills with minimal staining; the microfiber top repels most immediate liquids, though you should still avoid leaving coffee stains to set—this is not a superhero cape; it’s a mat. The surface is not as plush as some premium mats, but it strikes a balance between flexibility, portability, and longevity. ## Care, Storage, and Longevity

To keep your Glimmer Bairn looking as vibrant as possible, store the mat in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. The printed surface benefits from a little UVA protection, but prolonged exposure can cause colors to fade or shift slightly. When rolling the mat for transport, do it gently with the art on the outside to minimize creases. A proper tube or rigid container helps preserve the imprint of Hamm’s linework across years of gaming sessions. If you spill something on the mat, wipe it with a damp cloth and let it air-dry; avoid harsh cleaners that might strip color or damage the print. And yes, it’s worth having a microfiber cloth in your setup to clean dust from the surface before a critical stream shot.
