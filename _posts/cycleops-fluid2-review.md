## What is the CycleOps Fluid2?

The CycleOps Fluid2 is a bike trainer that uses fluid resistance technology to provide a smooth and quiet ride. Unlike magnetic trainers, the Fluid2 simulates real-road conditions, making your indoor training session feel more like a genuine outdoor ride. It's this realistic experience that has won the hearts of many cyclists. Whether you're gearing up for a race or simply trying to stay in shape, the Fluid2 is designed to meet your needs.

## Key Features

### Fluid Resistance Technology

At the heart of the CycleOps Fluid2 is its fluid resistance system. This technology uses a silicon-based liquid to create resistance, which increases as you pedal faster.
