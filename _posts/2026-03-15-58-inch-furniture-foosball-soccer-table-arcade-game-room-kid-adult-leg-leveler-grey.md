## Maintenance and Longevity

### Cleaning and care
Foosball tables are not high maintenance, but they do appreciate a little TLC. Wipe the play surface with a damp cloth after a gaming session to remove fingerprints and snack residue. The rods can accumulate dust and grease; a light wipe with a mild cleaner is enough to keep them moving smoothly. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the surface finish or the rod coatings. Regular maintenance pays off in frictionless turns and longer life for your players.

### Durability and daily wear
The table is built to endure the rough play of enthusiastic adults and the occasional boisterous child. If you are worried about scuffs on the cabinet, a simple cabinet polish can restore the shine and keep the grey finish looking modern. The political question of whether to place a mat or rug under the table to dampen noise is optional; it will affect sound more than torque, but it can help with floor vibrations during those dramatic page turn moments in your game night.

### Warnings and safety notes
The one caveat we will state clearly is that a 58 inch foosball table is heavy. It will require more than one person to move safely. Do not attempt to lift it solo or pretend you are in a heist movie. Always use proper lifting techniques and wheels if your setup includes a wheeled base. If you plan to mount it against a wall or into a dedicated alcove, ensure that you have a plan for cable management and accessibility. A good rule is to measure your space twice and order once; given the size, your future self will thank you.
