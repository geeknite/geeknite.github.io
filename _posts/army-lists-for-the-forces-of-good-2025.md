## The Basics of Army Lists

### What Are Army Lists?

Army lists are essentially the blueprints for your forces in a strategy game. They detail the units, characters, and equipment you’ll be bringing to the battlefield. Think of it as your strategic grocery list but with more dragons and fewer vegetables.

### Components

Each army list typically includes:
- **Units**: The bread and butter of your army, ranging from basic infantry to elite warriors.
- **Characters**: Heroes and leaders who bring special abilities and buffs to your troops.
- **Equipment**: Weapons, armor, and magical items that can turn the tide of battle.
- **Special Rules**: Unique traits and abilities that give your army character and flavor.

**Pro Tip**: Don’t forget to double-check your point totals. There’s nothing more embarrassing than fielding an illegal army list and being called out by Steve, the rules lawyer.

## Top Army Lists for the Forces of Good

### The Stalwart Defenders: Humans

Humans are the jack-of-all-trades in the forces of good. They have balanced stats, versatile units, and a variety of heroes to choose from. Here’s a sample list to get you started:

#### Units
- **Spearmen**: The backbone of any human army. Cheap, reliable, and great for holding the line.
- **Archers**: Provide ranged support and pick off enemy units from a distance.
- **Knights**: Heavy cavalry that can smash through enemy lines with their devastating charge.

#### Characters
- **General**: Your army’s leader, providing leadership and buffs to nearby units.
- **Wizard**: Adds magical firepower and utility to your army.

#### Equipment
- **Sword of Justice**: A magical weapon that gives your general a combat edge.
- **Shield of Valor**: Increases the durability of your front-line units.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, my knights charged into a unit of goblins, only to roll a series of ones. It was less a glorious charge and more a comical pile-up. The goblins are probably still laughing.

### The Noble Elves: Masters of Magic and Archery

Elves are known for their superior agility, magic, and ranged capabilities. They’re perfect for players who enjoy a more finesse-based approach to warfare.

#### Units
- **Swordmasters**: Elite infantry with high attack and defense.
- **Archers**: Even better than human archers, with longer range and higher accuracy.
- **Tree Kin**: Durable units that can hold their own in melee combat.

#### Characters
- **High Elf Mage**: Provides powerful spells that can decimate enemy units or buff your own.
- **Elven Prince**: A versatile leader who excels in both combat and command.

#### Equipment
- **Bow of the Elven Prince**: An enchanted bow that never misses its mark.
- **Ring of Protection**: Grants a magical shield to your most valuable units.

**Criticism Alert**: While elves are powerful, they can be a bit pricey. Fielding an all-elite army might leave you outnumbered on the battlefield. Choose your units wisely and remember, quality over quantity.

### The Brave Dwarves: Tough and Tenacious

Dwarves are the tanks of the forces of good. They have high defense, strong melee capabilities, and some of the best artillery in the game.

#### Units
- **Dwarf Warriors**: Tough infantry that can hold the line against almost anything.
- **Ironbreakers**: Elite units with incredible durability and high damage output.
- **Cannons**: Long-range artillery that can blow enemy units to smithereens.

#### Characters
- **Dwarf King**: A powerful leader who boosts the morale and defense of nearby units.
- **Runesmith**: Adds magical runes to your army, providing buffs and protections.

#### Equipment
- **Axe of the Ancients**: A weapon that can cleave through the toughest armor.
- **Shield of the Dwarf Lord**: Provides additional defense to your frontline warriors.

**Funny Moment**: During one match, my Dwarf King challenged an enemy dragon to single combat. The dragon rolled a natural 1 and tripped over its own tail. The Dwarf King then casually walked up and bonked it on the head. Victory through comedy!

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- **Strategic Depth**: Army lists add a layer of strategy and planning that keeps the game engaging.
- **Customization**: You can tailor your army to fit your playstyle and the specific challenges of each battle.
- **Immersive Experience**: Building and commanding your own army list makes you feel like a true general.

### Cons
- **Complexity**: Creating and managing an army list can be overwhelming for newcomers.
- **Time-Consuming**: Building the perfect list and planning your strategy can take a lot of time.
- **Balance Issues**: Some army lists may be more powerful than others, leading to potential imbalance in gameplay.
