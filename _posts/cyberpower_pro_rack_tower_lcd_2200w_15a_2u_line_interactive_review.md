## Features and Performance

### Line-Interactive Topology

The Cyberpower Pro Rack Tower utilizes line-interactive topology, which means it can automatically regulate voltage without switching to battery power. This is a lifesaver for protecting your equipment from voltage fluctuations and brownouts.

### LCD Display

The front LCD panel is a nerd's dream come true. It provides real-time information on battery status, load capacity, and even estimated runtime. Plus, it just looks cool.

### Automatic Voltage Regulation (AVR)

AVR is a game-changer. It corrects minor power fluctuations without switching to battery, extending the lifespan of your UPS and ensuring that your devices get consistent power.

### Ports and Connectivity

This unit is equipped with **8 outlets** (4 battery + surge, 4 surge only), **1 serial port**, and **1 USB port**. It also comes with **PowerPanel Business Edition** software for easy management and monitoring.

**Pros:**
- Line-interactive topology
- Informative LCD display
- Automatic Voltage Regulation (AVR)
