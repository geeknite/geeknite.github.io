## Care and life-cycle thoughts

### Cleaning schedule and routine

Vacuum weekly or as needed depending on foot traffic. Use a vacuum with adjustable height to avoid snagging the braid. For spills, treat with a gentle stain remover or a mild detergent solution; always follow with a clean water rinse and air dry. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners, which can degrade fibers and degrade color. If you’re dealing with heavy foot traffic in muddy climates, schedule periodic deep cleans to maintain color integrity and the carpet’s structural integrity.

### Stain removal and color-fastness

Colorfastness is a tricky thing; even two-tone textiles can show color transfer if you’re aggressive with cleaners. Always test on a hidden corner first. If color transfer occurs, you may want to call in a pro or invest in a color-safe cleaner approved for the rug’s fiber content. In practice, this rug’s colorway is forgiving of everyday mishaps; however, if you spill red wine or mustard on it, you’ll want to address it promptly to prevent staining from setting in.
