What I loved most is the camera versatility and the battery life that lets you actually forget to charge for a day and still wake up with juice. The display is so bright you could use it as a flashlight in a blackout, and the performance is so smooth it feels like the phone is predicting your next tap before you even think it.

What could be improved is the price, which remains the most stubborn dial on the ship.
