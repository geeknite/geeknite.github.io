## Care and maintenance: keeping your deck fresh
- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Sunlight is the enemy of cardboard romance.
- After use, let the cards air-dry if they’ve become a little damp from popcorn butter or sweaty hands. Do not iron them; yes, we’ve seen that in a forum once. The results were aesthetically confusing and functionally catastrophic.
- If you notice the stock becoming tacky, consider a light fan-out and let them rest; the air-cushion finish often recovers with a little time and air flow.
- Clean hands, clean table, clean game plan. It’s not rocket science, but it helps when your accuracy relies on the cards staying in line.
