## Maintenance and care

### Cleaning and storage
Keep chips away from moisture to prevent warping or color fading. A quick wipe with a dry microfiber cloth after sessions will extend color life. For aluminum cases, a light polish can bring back some shine, but avoid harsh chemicals that may remove the anodized layers. When not in use, store in a dry closet to avoid rust on the hardware.

### Case care
The aluminum case is robust but not indestructible. When traveling, consider using a padded bag or strap to reduce impact on corners. If the case has foam, ensure foam remains intact; compressed foam may lead to chips shifting around during transport.

### Chip care
Chips are resilient but not invincible. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight or heat that can cause color fading or material fatigue. If you notice chips rubbing against one another due to compression inside the case, re-pocket them in the foam layers to maintain their shape.
