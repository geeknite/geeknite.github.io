## What is Lankhmar?

Lankhmar is a city full of intrigue, peril, and enough shady characters to make your local dive bar look like a picnic spot. This RPG setting, inspired by Fritz Leiber's classic sword and sorcery tales, immerses players in a world where every alley holds danger and every decision could be your last. The **Companion Guide** provides a treasure trove of lore, character options, and adventures to enhance your Lankhmar experience.

## The Basics

### Setting

The city of Lankhmar is a sprawling metropolis teeming with life and danger. From the high towers of the nobility to the slums of the poor, every corner of this city is crafted with meticulous detail. The Companion Guide offers extensive background on the city's districts, key locations, and notable NPCs, ensuring your adventures are rich and immersive.

### Character Options

The Companion Guide expands on the base game by introducing new character classes, backgrounds, and abilities. Whether you fancy yourself a cunning thief, a mysterious sorcerer, or a wandering swordsman, there's something here for every type of player. The guide includes detailed descriptions and stats for each option, allowing players to craft unique and memorable characters.

### Adventures

No RPG is complete without thrilling adventures, and Lankhmar delivers. The Companion Guide offers a selection of pre-made scenarios, complete with maps, NPCs, and plot hooks. From solving a murder mystery to thwarting a nefarious cult, these adventures are sure to keep players on their toes.

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Rules

The Companion Guide builds on the core rules of the **Lankhmar RPG**, adding new mechanics and refining existing ones. Combat is fast-paced and brutal, magic is unpredictable, and social interactions can be as dangerous as any physical confrontation.

### Tips for GMs

Game Masters will find a wealth of advice and tools in the Companion Guide. From tips on building tension and atmosphere to suggestions for handling unruly players (looking at you, Greg), this guide is an invaluable resource for running a smooth and engaging game.
