A note on installation and care

To maximize lifespan, consider placing the rug on a level, dry surface. If you have uneven decking, use a rug pad or small plinths at the corners to prevent edges from curling and to maintain a flat surface lover’s paradise. For maintenance, a routine brush-off and an occasional wash keeps the surface looking fresh. If you’re dealing with heavy staining, treat the spots early—don’t let a spill lurk for months in the fibers, as some stains can set in and require more aggressive cleaning.

Post-purchase care tips

- Dry thoroughly before furniture goes back on top to prevent moisture trapping.
- Store indoors during heavy winter to extend life, especially in regions with harsh weather.
- Keep the rug away from direct heat sources when drying to avoid warping.
- Rotate or invert periodically to ensure even wear across the rug’s surface.
