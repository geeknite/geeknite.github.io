## Storage, maintenance and care tips

A good compact set is not just about the moment you lift the lid. It’s about how you treat it when you park it in a bag or slide it into a travel case. Here are a few practical tips to extend the life of your set:

- Keep chips clean and dry. Wipe them with a dry cloth if they pick up a bit of dust or pocket lint. Avoid soaking or leaving them in damp environments, which can degrade the color and finish over time.
- Use the included case as designed. Don’t cram extra items that could nudge the chips or cards into corners where they don’t belong. A little organization goes a long way toward a smooth game night.
- Rotate card decks. If you are using two decks, rotate them regularly and shuffle both to keep wear even. Plastic-coated cards age gracefully, but even they appreciate a little variety.
- Inspect the edges. Chips with rough or uneven edges can cause miscounts and awkward bounces. If you notice a batch that feels off, consider rotating them out and replacing those few with a fresh batch from the same lot.
