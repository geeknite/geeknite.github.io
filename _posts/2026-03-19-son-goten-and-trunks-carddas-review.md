### Storage and display tips
- Use soft sleeves and avoid direct sunlight to preserve the colors and gloss.
- Consider a top-loader or semi-rigid display card for long-term display.
- Store away from humidity; Carddass cards are not immune to moisture-induced warping, especially when the foil is involved.
- For display: a small acrylic stand or a shallow display shelf with UV-filtering glass helps you enjoy the art without sacrificing longevity.
