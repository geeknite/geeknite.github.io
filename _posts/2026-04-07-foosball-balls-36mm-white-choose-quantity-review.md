## Compatibility and Pocket Reality Checks
### Table standard compatibility
Most home and arcade foosball tables use standard ball sizes, and the 36 mm diameter is a safe bet for compatibility. If your table uses a tighter tolerance or a slightly different diameter, you might see occasional misreads on kickoffs or passes. A quick test: place a ball on the table and see how it rolls on a straight line; if it wobbles or veers unpredictably, you may be dealing with an outlier ball or a table bug, not a personal flaw.

## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity (Deep Dive)
