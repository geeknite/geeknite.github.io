## Care and Maintenance: Keeping the Beast in Check

Maintenance is the boring yet noble hero in any rug story. Here’s the practical cheat sheet for the Martha rug:
- Vacuum regularly with a non-beater bar attachment. The goal is to lift dirt out of the pile without beating the fibers into submission.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper into the fibers.
- For common household spills (coffee, juice, tea), use a mild cleaning solution or a manufacturer-recommended rug cleaner and test on a small area first. Always follow the product instructions and perform a spot test.
- Rotate every 3–6 months to ensure even wear, especially in high-traffic zones or under furniture that creates focal pressure points.
- For stubborn stains, consider professional cleaning every 12–24 months, depending on usage. Oversized rugs like the Martha are worth the investment if they remain vibrant over time.
- Keep away from direct heat sources and avoid prolonged sun exposure to preserve color integrity.

If you’ve ever spilled something catastrophic on a rug and watched it turn into a memory, you’ll appreciate a rug that’s designed to survive the occasional culinary disaster. The synthetic blend helps with stain resistance, but even the best fibers require a little tender loving care. Think of it as a digital detox for your living room: a little routine maintenance goes a long way toward keeping the space feeling fresh and chic.
