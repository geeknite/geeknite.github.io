## Care and Maintenance: Keeping It Fresh
Caring for an oversized rug is a routine not a ritual. Here is a refined playbook that keeps your Martha rug crisp and welcoming:
- Vacuum regularly with a non beating bar attachment to lift dirt without grinding the pile into oblivion.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber fray or color transfer.
- For common spills such as coffee, juice, or tea, use a mild rug cleaner and test in a small inconspicuous area first.
- Rotate the rug every 3–6 months to promote even wear, especially if heavy furniture sits on one side year round.
- Consider professional cleaning every 12–24 months for the larger issues or stubborn stains.
- Keep away from direct heat sources and long sun exposure to guard against color fade.

A note on maintenance and on the idea that a rug is a living part of your home. The synthetic blend helps with stain resistance, but even the best fibers benefit from a light regimen. Think of it as routine maintenance for a tiny home planet. The more you care, the more it glows.
