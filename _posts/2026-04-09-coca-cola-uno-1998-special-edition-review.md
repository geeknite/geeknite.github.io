## Storage, care, and display tips
If you want this deck to remain a showroom piece for years to come, here are practical tips to ensure it keeps its luster:

- Keep away from light: Prolonged exposure to direct sunlight can cause colors on the box art to fade and card stock to become brittle.
- Regulate humidity: High humidity can warp packaging and cause cardboard to sag. A cool, dry environment is ideal.
- Use a display case: A small acrylic display case allows you to show off the Coca-Cola styling while protecting the box from dust.
- Consider a storage approach for open decks: If you plan to actually play with the cards, consider a deck separator and an organizer to reduce wear on the iconic backs.

Display considerations aside, the most important factor is to enjoy the item and preserve your memories that go beyond the face value of the cards.
