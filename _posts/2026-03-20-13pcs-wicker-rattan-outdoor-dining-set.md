## Care tips to keep the vibe fresh
- Regularly wipe down wicker with a damp cloth to remove dust and pollen.
- Use a mild detergent solution for tougher grime, then rinse and air dry away from direct sun to prevent color fade.
- Remove cushion covers when applicable and machine wash on gentle cycles. Air dry completely before reassembling.
- When heavy rain or snow is forecast, cover the furniture or store cushions indoors to prolong life.
- Inspect the weave periodically; tighten any loose connectors and replace worn cushions as needed to maintain comfort and structure.
