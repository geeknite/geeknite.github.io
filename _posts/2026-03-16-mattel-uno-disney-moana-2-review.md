## Rules and Special Cards

UNO’s magic has always lived in its wild cards and momentary chaos. ### Special cards explained

- Maui’s Trick Card: Swap cards with another player or force a quick swap-down. This one can swing momentum in dramatic fashion, allowing you to pinch a strategic swap or rescue a teammate from a looming doom of color mismatch.
- Ocean’s Whisper: A skip-and-draw hybrid that acts like a pause button for the table. It’s perfect for blocking a hot streak or letting someone who just drew seven cards catch their breath before the next round skitters into motion.
- Moana’s Rally: All players must play a card of a specific color, creating a mini color-matching test that’s surprisingly satisfying as players sprint to meet the demand.
- Kona Wind: A wild card with a beachy flourish that reshuffles the top few cards from the discard pile back into the draw stack. This one can trigger cascading plays or, more often, a glorious pile of chaos that somehow ends with a dramatic last-second draw of fate.

These cards aren’t game-breaking, but they’re designed to keep tempo lively and give you quick, unforgettable moments to share. They’re straightforward enough for younger players to grasp with a little explanation and a lot of enthusiasm.
