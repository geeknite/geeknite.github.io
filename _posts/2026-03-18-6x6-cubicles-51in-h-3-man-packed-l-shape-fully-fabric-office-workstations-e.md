If you have kids, pets, or a proclivity for art interventions in the form of crayon doodles, you can remove most marks with a gentle detergent solution and a soft sponge. The desk surfaces themselves respond well to routine cleaning; keep your keyboard and monitor area clear of clutter to maintain optimal airflow and heat dissipation.
### Durability over time
The steel frame is built for long-term service; expect the occasional scuff and a few micro-scratches that add character rather than damage. Panels can show wear over months if heavily used or misaligned during moves, but generally they hold up well. Budget constraints may push you toward simpler, less robust setups; with proper care, this system should outlast several office revamps without turning into a squeaky, tired-looking furniture sculpture.
