## What is Art Deco?

Before we dissect this masterpiece, let's talk about Art Deco. Originating in the 1920s and 1930s, Art Deco is a design style characterized by bold geometric patterns, rich colors, and lavish ornamentation. Think The Great Gatsby, but with more triangles and less existential angst.

"Utopia: Vision d'un besseren Welt" was created by the renowned artist Pierre LeGrand, known for his masterful use of color and form. LeGrand drew inspiration from the burgeoning metropolis of New York City, blending elements of futurism and classicism to create a truly unique piece.
