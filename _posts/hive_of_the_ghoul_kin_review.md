Hive Of The Ghoul-Kin is an Epic Encounters game that plunges players into a ghoul-infested dungeon. With stunningly designed miniatures and gripping scenarios, players must navigate through the labyrinth, battling ghouls and uncovering secrets.

The game comes with:

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Hive Of The Ghoul-Kin is like preparing for a haunted house party. Lay out the dungeon tiles, distribute cards and miniatures, and mentally prepare for the chaos to come. Make sure your snacks are ghoul-themed for extra immersion.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on the objectives. You might move, attack, or use special abilities. Cooperation is key, so strategize with your teammates to outwit the ghoul-kin.

### Combat

Combat in Hive Of The Ghoul-Kin is a blend of strategy and sheer luck. Each ghoul has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic attack only to roll a critical failure? Welcome to the dungeon, where plans often go hilariously awry.
