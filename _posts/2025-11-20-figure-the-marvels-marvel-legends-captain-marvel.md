## The Basics of Marvel Legends Captain Marvel

### What is a BAF Figure?

For those new to the world of action figures, a BAF or Build-A-Figure means you have to buy multiple figures to assemble one giant figure. Think of it like collecting Infinity Stones, but with less universal destruction and more display shelf satisfaction.

### Components

The Marvel Legends Captain Marvel BAF includes several interchangeable pieces and accessories:

- **Interchangeable Hands**: Because superheroes need wardrobe options too.
- **Photon Blasts**: For those epic battle poses.
- **Detailed Sculpt and Paint**: The level of detail will make you feel like you've plucked Carol Danvers right out of the comic pages.

## The Design and Sculpt

### Attention to Detail

The sculpting on this figure is nothing short of marvelous. From the intricate details on her suit to the expressive facial features, it's clear that the designers poured a lot of love into creating this figure. The paint job is equally impressive, with vibrant colors that pop and subtle shading that adds depth and realism.

### Articulation

Articulation is a huge factor for collectors, and this figure does not disappoint. With over 30 points of articulation, you can pose Captain Marvel in just about any action stance you can think of. Whether she's ready to punch a Skrull or strike a heroic pose, you'll have plenty of options.

## Accessories and Extras

### Photon Blasts and Hands

The interchangeable photon blasts are a standout feature, allowing you to recreate Captain Marvel's iconic energy attacks. The extra hands provide additional versatility, letting you switch between fists and open hands depending on your preferred pose.

### Display Stand

This figure also comes with a display stand, which is a nice touch for those who want to show off their figure in dynamic flying poses. It's sturdy and adjustable, giving you plenty of options for display.

## The Build-A-Figure Component

### Who's the BAF?

The BAF for this series is the Totally Awesome Hulk, also known as Amadeus Cho. Collecting all the figures in this wave will allow you to assemble this larger-than-life character, adding even more value to your collection.

### Collecting the Set

While it can be a bit of an investment to buy all the figures needed to complete the BAF, the end result is worth it. Each figure in the series is well-made and adds something unique to your collection. Plus, the satisfaction of finally completing the Totally Awesome Hulk is unmatched.
