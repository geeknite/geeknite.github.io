## House rules, variations, and expansion potential

A good party game invites a few house rules to fit your group’s humor and tolerance for chaos. Here are a few ideas that work well with Exploding Minions Special Edition:

- Short rounds with a fixed timer: If you’re in a hurry, set a timer for 15 minutes and see who survives the chaos. The Minion powers become even more strategic when you know the clock is ticking.
- The banana bonus: Award a silly, player-chosen penalty for the last person who successfully defuses a tricky situation. It’s silly, non-harmful, and gives everyone a moment to savor the moment.
- Quickfire mode: Limit certain minion actions to one-use-per-game to prevent any single player from monopolizing the table. This keeps the game flowing without turning into a meltdown of “I’m unstoppable.”
