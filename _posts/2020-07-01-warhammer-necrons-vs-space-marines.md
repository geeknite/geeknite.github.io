## What's in the Box?

### Miniatures: The Stars of the Show

The Necrons vs. Space Marines Battle Set comes with a glorious assortment of miniatures. On one side, we have the ancient and enigmatic Necrons, with their sleek designs and eerie green glow. On the other, the valiant and heavily armed Space Marines, ready to defend the Imperium from any threat. Here's a breakdown of the miniatures included:

- **Necrons:** 10 Necron Warriors, 3 Canoptek Scarabs, 1 Overlord
- **Space Marines:** 5 Assault Intercessors, 1 Primaris Lieutenant
  
**Pro Tip:** Try not to glue your fingers together when assembling these miniatures. It's surprisingly easy to accidentally create a new form of Necron-Space Marine hybrid.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up: The Battlefield Awaits

Setting up a game with the Necrons vs. Space Marines Battle Set is like preparing for an epic showdown. Lay out the terrain, assemble your miniatures, and get ready to roll some dice. It's time to see who will emerge victorious in this clash of titans.

### Turns and Actions: Strategy and Mayhem

Warhammer 40,000 is all about strategic planning and tactical maneuvers. Each turn, players move their miniatures, engage in combat, and attempt to complete objectives. Here's a quick rundown of the key phases:

1. **Movement:** Maneuver your units across the battlefield, positioning them for optimal advantage.
2. **Shooting:** Unleash a hail of gunfire upon your foes. Watch out for friendly fire — Space Marines aren't immune to bad aim.
3. **Assault:** Charge into close combat for brutal melee engagements. Necron claws vs. Space Marine power fists — who will come out on top?
4. **Turn End:** Regroup and prepare for the next round. Maybe take a snack break. Gaming is hungry work!

### Combat: Dice-Rolling Madness

Combat in Warhammer 40,000 involves a lot of dice rolling. Attack rolls, saving throws, damage calculations — it's like a math exam, but way more fun. The randomness of dice rolls adds an element of unpredictability, ensuring that no two battles are ever the same.
