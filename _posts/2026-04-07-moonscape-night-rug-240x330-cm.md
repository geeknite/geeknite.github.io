## Care and Maintenance
### Cleaning science in practical terms
The Moonscape Night Rug cleans like a dream in the sense that you can vacuum it and call it a day. A standard vacuum with a beater bar set to a gentle height is recommended to avoid pulling fibers or abrading the lunar texture. Spills should be blotted quickly with a clean cloth; do not rub, lest you smear the cosmos into a blotch of meteor-impact proportions. For stubborn stains, use a mild detergent diluted in water and test on a small inconspicuous corner first. If you own a steam cleaner, a light pass is possible but avoid deep steaming that might distort the pile and flatten the crater textures you paid so much attention to.

### Durability and edge care
Edges hold up well on this model, with a reinforced binding that resists fraying under normal use. A small caveat: if you have pets with long nails or kids who love dragging heavy furniture, you might see occasional edge fuzzing after long-term use. That is a common reality with textured moody moondust designs and not a fatal flaw. Regular rotation and occasional flipping (if your rug is reversible or if the backing allows) can help distribute wear evenly over the years.
