## Cleaning and Maintenance
### Everyday Care
Vacuum regularly with a low-suction setting. Avoid aggressive beater bars that can fray fibers over time. For small spill events, blot gently with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which pushes stains deeper into the fibers. If a stain lingers, treat with a mild detergent solution or a carpet-safe cleaning product, following the manufacturer’s instructions.

### Spills, Pets, and Real Life
Pets are part of the family, and accidents happen. The dual-face design can hide slight discolorations better than a single-tone rug, but you’ll still want to address spills quickly. The reversible surface is a bonus here; if one side shows more wear or staining than you’re comfortable with, flip it to the other face and call it a day. For stubborn spots, test any cleaner in an inconspicuous spot first to avoid color bleed.

### Longevity Tips
- Rotate the rug every few months to equalize wear.
- Use furniture coasters to prevent indentation marks under chairs and tables.
- Keep out of direct sun to reduce color fading; if placement requires sun exposure, consider rotating the rug to preserve both faces.
