## Maintenance, care, and storage of the Masters Edition tin

To preserve both the cards and their shiny new tin, consider the following upkeep tips:

- Store the tin in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight. Prolonged exposure can cause the tin paint to fade slightly and may affect the gloss print on the cards.
- Keep the cards in a protective sleeve if you plan to heavily reuse the deck. Sleeves add a tiny layer of protection during shuffles and can keep the edges crisp for longer.
- If you plan to display the tin, consider a light dusting every now and then. A soft microfiber cloth does the job and avoids scratching the surface.
- For collectors, a loose grip on the number of identical Masters Edition tins available can preserve resale value. If you decide to store multiple units, separate them in small containers to prevent misplacing any small inserts, promo cards, or the instruction booklet.
