## Assembly Tips and Common Issues
A few practical tips to ensure a smooth experience:
- Lay out all parts before you begin; it helps you understand how the pieces interlock and reduces the chance of assembling something backwards.
- Keep track of small screws by sorting them into labeled containers. It saves you from that moment when you realize you have three extra screws and no idea where they came from.
- Use a level during assembly to ensure the desk surface sits flat; an off-kilter desk is a feature only in bad sci-fi scripts.
- If wiring gets tangled, pause and re-route through the cable channels rather than trying to force everything through a single hole. You will thank yourself later for preventing a cable avalanche.

Common issues tend to involve alignment of panels or minor squeaks as materials settle. These are usually solvable by re-tightening screws and adjusting panel alignment. If you encounter persistent creaks, you may want to check the hardware fittings and ensure the desk is not on an uneven surface.
