To keep this set looking and playing like new, consider a simple maintenance routine:
- Wipe down metal surfaces with a microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints and dust. Avoid harsh chemicals that could affect the anodized finish.
- Inspect the hinges and latches periodically. A quick spray of silicone-based lubricant is usually enough to keep things smooth, but do not overdo it; you do not want sliding components to slip into the realm of slippery comedy during a crucial hand.

- Keep the case closed when not in use to protect the interior from dust and the occasional curious pet who thinks chips are edible.
