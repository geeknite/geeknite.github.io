## Maintenance and Care: Keeping the Cosmos Clean
Microsuede is generally forgiving, but it isn’t a self-cleaning nebula. Spill management is a must. The recommended approach: blot spills immediately with a clean cloth, avoid scrubbing, and use a mild fabric cleaner if needed. Quick checks and periodic vacuuming (with a brush attachment) will prevent dust and pet hair from dramatic accumulation. If you’re dealing with stubborn stains, consult the cleaner’s guidelines first; you don’t want to accidentally erase the micro‑fibers’ texture and end up with a rough feel where smoothness once existed.

Rotate cushions if your modular pieces come with interchangeable cushions; this helps them wear evenly and extends the life of the seat comfort. If you own pets, consider a fabric protector spray designed for microsuede to help resist staining. Always follow the manufacturer’s care instructions for the specific microsuede variant you’ve chosen, because not all microsuede fabrics are created equal—some may have slightly different nap textures or cleaning recommendations.
