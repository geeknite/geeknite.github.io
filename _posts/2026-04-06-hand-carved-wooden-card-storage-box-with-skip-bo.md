## Maintenance of the craft and extending its life

To keep your carved box looking sharp, commit to a gentle maintenance routine. Wipe with a dry or slightly damp microfiber cloth to remove dust. If the finish shows signs of dryness, apply a small amount of a furniture-safe oil or wax and rub with the grain. Let it sit for a few minutes, then buff with a clean cloth. This simple care will preserve luster and prevent cracking in the wood that could reveal a less-than-glamorous interior. Store in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading, and avoid extreme humidity to minimize warp risk.
