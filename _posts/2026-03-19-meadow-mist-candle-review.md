### How long it lasts and how to maximize it
An 8-ounce Meadow Mist candle should provide roughly 40 hours of burn time if used optimally. For best results, you should:
- Trim the wick to about 1/4 inch before lighting
- Burn 2-3 hours per session to avoid tunneling
- Keep away from drafts
- Let the melt pool reach the edges to maximize surface area
- Store in a cool, dry place to preserve fragrance

### Safety and care
Safe burning practices are essential. Always place the candle on a stable, heat-resistant surface. Keep it away from pets and curious children. Do not burn for more than 4 hours at a time. Keep the wick trimmed. If you notice something off, extinguish and inspect for wick trimming or residue; you may need to repot the candle.
