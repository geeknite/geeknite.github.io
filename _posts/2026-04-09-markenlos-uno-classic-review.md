### Maintenance and care tips
- Storage is important: keep the deck away from humidity and direct sunlight; this preserves the color integrity and prevents the cards from warping.
- Sleeves are optional but helpful: card sleeves can extend the deck’s life if you expect heavy use by kids or rowdy adults.
- Box care: consider a simple cardboard or plastic insert to keep the cards from sliding around when you move the box. A tidy box makes setup quicker and reduces the chances of misplaced cards before a big reveal.
- Quick-dial chaos management: if the chaos becomes overwhelming, pause and count to ten before the next draw. It sounds silly, but it helps preserve friendships and makes for better stories later.
