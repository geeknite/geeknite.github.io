## Maintenance and Longevity

A desk that accommodates ten people will naturally see a lot of use. The Eternal design anticipates this, with materials chosen for longevity and a finish that can weather daily life in a busy office. Routine maintenance is straightforward:

- Wipe the surface with a microfiber cloth to remove dust and fingerprint smudges. For stubborn spots, a mild cleaner on a cloth works wonders; avoid harsh solvents that could degrade the finish.
- Inspect the frame screws and joints every few months. A quick tighten keeps everything secure and reduces wobble that can develop after a long sprint or a team-building exercise that somehow ends with a chair under a heavyweight monitor.
- Check the cable channels for debris. A blocked path can cause cords to kink and wear faster. A monthly pass with a vacuum or a soft brush is all you need to keep things smooth.

Durability-wise, this desk is built to last through multiple teams and a handful of upgrade cycles. It is not a disposable commodity; it is a platform that grows with your office, much like a good friendship that endures quarterly budget meetings and the occasional mandatory seminar.
