## Visual and care guidance
### How to display Gloom AR 109 108
Always admire this card under indirect light to preserve its glare-free surface. The key is to let the card breathe in its own spotlight while you resist the urge to treat it like a trophy in a victory speech.

### Preservation and storage tips
- Use individual toploaders for each card; avoid communal storage where a single abrasive sleeve can abrade a dozen cards.
- Keep away from direct sunlight; UV light can degrade gloss and color fidelity.
- Maintain a cool, dry environment to minimize warping and whitening on the card edges.
- Rotate display attention so no single card compounds all the wear from attention and handling. Variety is the spice of a healthy collection.
