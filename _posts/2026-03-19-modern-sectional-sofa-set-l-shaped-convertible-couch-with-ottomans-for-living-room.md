## Buying Guide: What to Look For

When you shop for a modern sectional like this, you should consider a few practical points:

- Space and scalability: Measure your room before pulling the trigger. The L-shape can dominate a room if placed awkwardly. Map out different configurations to see what fits best.
- Material choices: If you have pets or kids, opt for stain-resistant fabric or durable faux leather. If you prefer a premium feel and don’t mind a bit more maintenance, go for a high-end fabric option.
- Comfort and support: Test the seating depth and back support. If you’re tall, ensure there’s enough depth to support your legs without you feeling like you’re perched on the edge of a cliff.
- Cushions vs. structure: Removable cushions are convenient but can sag over time. Look for cushions with high-density foam cores and quality seam work.
- Price and warranty: Higher price often translates to better construction and better warranty coverage. Check warranty terms for frame, springs, and upholstery.
