## What’s Inside the Folio Pack
### Inside the Box: What You Get
The folio pack is curated to feel like a sampler of the City of Mist vibe rather than a standard old-school character sheet. Expect the following core components:
- A set of character folios, each with a distinct noir-menace design, ready to record backstory, power scales, and relationships.
- A relationship map page that helps players track ties among crew, citizens, suspects, and mythic catalysts.
- Quick reference sheets for Mythos and Logos, plus a worldbuilding glossary you can reference during sessions without needing a palm pilot equivalent.
- Asset and condition cards so your players can show off relationships, items, and ongoing conditions without taping a sticky note to the map.
- Intentions and drive trackers designed to align with how City of Mist handles motive and agency.

### How Many Folios and Extras
In most bundles you’ll find 6 to 8 character folios, plus a handful of GM and reference cards. The exact count varies by edition, but the design intent remains the same: provide a tactile, elegant way to hold a character’s arc together while the world swirls around them. The cards are color coded and clearly labeled, which is a godsend when the table gets loud and everyone is leaning in to hear the latest plot twist.
