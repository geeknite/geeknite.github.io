## Care and Maintenance

Metal dice aren’t maintenance nightmares, but they do require a little extra love. Here are some practical tips to preserve that gleam:
- Dry environment: Keep them dry. Moisture is the enemy of metal finishes when exposed for long periods, especially if there are any unsealed engravings.
- Cleaning: Wipe with a soft cloth after play sessions. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could scratch the brushed finish.
- Storage: Use a padded dice tray or a soft pouch to avoid bangs against other metal or hard surfaces. A proper pouch prevents the inevitable “wait, where did I put my dice just now?” moment during a crucial turn.
- Handling: If you play with sweaty hands, consider a quick wipe between rounds. The oils in skin can accumulate and affect the surface over time, though it won’t instantly ruin the dice.

In short, treat them with the respect you’d give to any premium metal collectible, and they’ll reward you with years of gleam and drama.
