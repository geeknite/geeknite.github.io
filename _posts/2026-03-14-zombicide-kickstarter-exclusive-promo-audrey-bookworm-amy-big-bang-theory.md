### Gameplay Mechanic and Strategy
Audrey’s signature mechanic centers on card-draw manipulation and a bonus when you perform successful searches. In practice, Audrey shines in scenarios that reward exploration and information gathering. Her ability might look like “Knowledge is Power” on the card, which lets you draw an extra search card after a successful search, or grants a one-time reroll when you use a key item to unlock a door. The exact text is less important than the vibe: she makes your team feel clever, like you’re outsmarting the undead rather than simply out-muscling them.

From a strategic standpoint, Audrey encourages you to split your party in the early turns, sending a couple of survivors to gather resources while Audrey acts as the nerve center, coordinating which doors to pry open and which alleys to clean up first. If you’ve ever fretted about a board becoming a speed-run sprint, Audrey invites you to slow down, read the map, and enjoy the little dramatic beats of “we don’t know what’s around this corner.” That’s the flavor: a scholar in a shambling world, fighting with facts, not just fists.

## Practical Play Tips: Getting the Most from Audrey and Amy
### Quick Start Rules Patch for Your Table
- Audrey: After you successfully perform a search and reveal a new item card, you may draw one extra item card. If you reveal a key item, you can immediately use one of your actions to trigger a free barricade setup. This encourages a slower, tactically minded opening phase where you create mobility options and safe zones without furious early combat.
- Amy: Once per round, when you roll to attack or defend, you may reroll one die. If you spend a reroll on a critical action (e.g., a mission-critical objective or defending a teammate in a corner), you may perform a second reroll, but you must declare it before the result is shown. The logic here is to reward players who are good at risk assessment rather than those who rely solely on luck.

These rules capture the spirit of the promos: Audrey rewards careful exploration and planning; Amy rewards smart risk management and timing. Together, they create a balanced dynamic that can be surprising and deeply satisfying on longer campaigns.
