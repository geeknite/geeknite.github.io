## Painting, Display, and Hobby Potential
This is where the joy often blossoms. Three sealed guest-artist survivors mean three brand-new canvases waiting for base coats, washes, dry brushing, and the occasional basing experiment. Here are a few practical tips:
- Start with a neutral base color on skin and clothing to keep skin tones from looking unnatural when you add highlight colors later.
- Use a wet palette to keep colors smooth and to mix slightly different tones for weathering and shading.
- For bases, consider varied urban textures (broken concrete, alley bricks, slick oil slicks) to create a micro-scene that tells a story about each survivor’s arc.
- Seal with a matte or satin sealer to protect your work from tabletop wear and inadvertent coffee spills during your next game night.
