## Care and Maintenance Tips

To keep your deck in good shape, practice a few sensible habits:
- Dry hands, dry table, dry cards. If the surface is slick with moisture, consider a microfiber cloth wipe, followed by a thorough air-dry.
- Keep away from sunlight. Prolonged exposure can yellow the stock, fade the back design, and reduce the crispness of the pips.
- Store in a cool, dry place. A drawer with a gentle hum of air is ideal; a breakdown in humidity can lead to warping.
- Use a standard card case for long-term storage. A protective sleeve or dedicated deck box prevents nicks and edge wear.
