If you’re into cardistry, the two-deck configuration is a boon because you can create multi-deck flourishes by combining red and blue into a single color-splashed cascade. ### Durability and maintenance tips
Storage matters. Keep these decks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading or stickiness. If you have a habit of dropping a deck into a bag of snacks or your desk drawer of mystery no-nappers, it’s worth keeping a tiny silica packet nearby to manage humidity. If a card edge starts showing signs of wear, consider a light clean with a dry microfiber cloth—that’s all you need for a quick refresh. The Rider Back 807s aren’t fragile, but they’re not invincible either; treat them like a video game cartridge: they’ll behave better if you preserve them properly.
