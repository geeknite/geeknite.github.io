If you’re a completionist, you probably care about the packaging, the card stock, and the edge wear. The Gift Bag NM is a typical promo card: not a full expansion, but a neat collectible that’s worth keeping in good shape. Storage tips worth your attention:

- Sleeves matter: use standard Dixit sleeves, or even a slightly larger tarot-size sleeve if you’re a paranoid protector. The Gift Bag deserves to stay NM for as long as possible; there’s nothing sadder than a promo card with a bent corner and a story to tell about that corner turning to dust.
- Backing and binder: consider a binder with a page insert for Dixit promos. It’s not just about looking cool; it’s about preserving your investment when a friend asks to borrow your featured card to narrate their own dream about a suitcase of confetti.
