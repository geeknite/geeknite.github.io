## Setup and First Round: Getting Started Fast
1. Clear a space big enough for the entire table and a comfortable number of snacks that won’t melt when rage-reshuffles occur.
2. Unseal the box (if you’re lucky, it’ll slink open with a groan and a sigh from the cardboard gods).
3. Shuffle the deck well to distribute the extra cards evenly. In larger groups, you’ll want a couple of players to help with shuffling—trust us, chaos loves organization.
4. Deal a starting hand to each player. The exact hand size varies with edition, but the gist is that you should have enough cards to dodge early explosions while stealing strategic Defuses from the other players.
5. Decide on a few house rules if your group likes to customize. The Party Pack is forgiving in spirit, but a shared understanding prevents the kind of dramatic miscommunications that fuel the best party stories.

Unboxing and setup are part of the social ritual. The moment you crack open that shrink wrap, you and your crew are signing up for a night of unpredictable moves, comedic mishaps, and a few “this is fine” moments as a rival pulls off a defiance-worthy play that makes the table erupt in laughter.
