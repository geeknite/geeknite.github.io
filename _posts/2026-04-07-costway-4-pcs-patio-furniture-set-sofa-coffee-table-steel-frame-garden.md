## Assembly Experience and Tips

Assembling outdoor furniture is seldom an Olympic event, but it is not always a walk in the park either. The Costway four piece set typically requires basic hand tools and a degree of patience. On average, you should expect 30 to 60 minutes of assembly per piece if you are methodical and careful. A few practical tips to help you avoid the frustration of missing screws or upside down panels:

- Lay out all parts and check against the instruction packet before you begin. A quick inventory saves you from the post assembly drama of discovering you lost a washer in the cushions.
- Keep the workspace tidy. A cluttered patio table becomes a hazard zone for small hardware and a constant hazard for your feet.
- Start with the frame, then attach the seating components, and finally secure the cushions. This sequence helps to ensure proper alignment and reduces wobble.
- If you have a bit more experience, a low torque setting on a drill can speed things up without compromising the threads. If you are new to DIY, work slowly and enjoy the process as a quirky ritual rather than a race against time.

## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

Caring for outdoor furniture is a small ritual that pays off in time. Here are practical care tips to extend the life of your Costway set:

- Clean the frame with a mild soap solution and rinse with water. Avoid harsh cleaners that could degrade the powder coating.
- Inspect the frame periodically for rust spots and address them early with rust treatment and touch up paint if needed.
- Remove cushion covers and launder as needed. Air dry completely before reassembling to prevent mildew.
- Store cushions indoors during prolonged wet seasons or use breathable storage bags to reduce moisture buildup.
- Consider a lightweight furniture cover to shield from heavy sun exposure during peak seasons, which can help maintain the color and finish of the weave.
