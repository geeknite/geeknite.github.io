H3: Core rules that remain intact
- The deck uses color-monotone cards with numbers ranging from 0 to 9 per color, plus action cards (Skip, Reverse, Draw Two) and Wilds.
- On your turn, you play a card that matches either the color or the number (or use a Wild/Wild Draw Four when necessary).
- If you can’t play, you draw a card and pass turn.
- When you’re down to one card, you must shout UNO. If another player catches you forget to shout UNO, you may be forced to draw a penalty card until it becomes the season finale of your life—or at least until someone wins.
