Performance and Practicality

## Cleaning and Maintenance

Maintenance is where many rugs turn from hero to zero, but Kendall Oatmeal is designed for practical care. Regular vacuuming with a low suction setting helps keep surface debris at bay without pulling fibers. For spills, the rule of thumb is blot, not rub, and then treat with a mild detergent diluted in water. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade fiber integrity or alter color tones. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to prevent any one patch from showing how much you binge watch in that spot. Quick spot-cleaning is your friend, and it won t turn your living room into a lab of stains that require a chemist s intervention. Routine care is the secret sauce to keeping Kendall Oatmeal looking new longer. If you have persistent stains, consider a mild cleaning solution recommended for synthetic fibers, but avoid soaking the backing.
