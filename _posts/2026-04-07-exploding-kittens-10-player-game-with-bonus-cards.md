## House Rules and Variants
### tweak for smooth play
with ten players, you may want to consider a few house rules to prevent stalemates:
- cap the number of bonus cards in play at any given moment to avoid overloading the table with effects.
- implement a soft timer for turns to keep the pace brisk, say 30 seconds per player, with a nudge from the moderator if the group is too engrossed in banter.
- allow a single extra defuse per player at certain thresholds, but only if the group agrees on the penalty for overuse.

### optional variants
- team mode: split into two teams; teammates can discuss defuses but still cannot reveal hands. this can be hilarious in a horror movie sense when one teammate tries to bluff the other into disaster.
- draft mode: players draft a subset of bonus cards at the start, adding a strategic layer where you decide which powers to bring into the middle.
- time-bomb mode: once a bomb card is drawn, a short countdown begins and the table must decide who blinks first; this version is less about math and more about theater.
