In practice, each round presents a prompt and every player writes an answer on a slip of paper. Then, all answers are revealed and the group must decide which answer the majority will pick. If you guessed the most popular answer, you win that round’s points; if you didn’t, you watch the herd walk by without you in the center of the spotlight. The Family Edition refines the prompts to be more kid-friendly and social, so parents, teens, and younger siblings can participate without emergency therapy after each round.\n\nThis edition emphasizes inclusivity, empathy, and mild embarrassment in a way that still feels competitive.
