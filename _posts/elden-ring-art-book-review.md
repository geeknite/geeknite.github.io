### Environments: A World Like No Other

The environments in Elden Ring are nothing short of breathtaking, and the art book does them justice. With detailed illustrations of the game's various regions, you'll get a sense of the vast and varied world that awaits you. ### Weapons and Armor: Gear Up!

No FromSoftware game would be complete without an array of epic weapons and armor, and the art book showcases them in all their glory. It's a great way to appreciate the artistry that goes into creating the tools of destruction you'll wield in the game.
