## Care tips to maximize life and look
- Clean cushions regularly with a mild soap solution and a soft brush to prevent dirt buildup. Rinse thoroughly and allow to air dry completely before re-covering to avoid moisture sealing.
- Protect from direct, harsh sun for extended periods; consider sun shades or covers during peak summer months to preserve color and fabric integrity.
- Storage and rotation: rotate cushions seasonally to ensure even wear. Store covers in a dry place if you anticipate months of non-use, to avoid mildew and musty odors.
- Inspect monthly: check for loose screws or wobbly joints and address promptly to prevent more major repairs later on.
- Use protective pucks or felt pads under furniture to prevent floor scuffs when moving modules around on hard surfaces.
