## Brewing Guide
Here is the Geeknite-approved starting point for Meadow Green Tea:

- Water temperature: 75-85 C (167-185 F)
- Leaf quantity: 2-3 grams per 8 oz cup
- Steep time: 2-3 minutes in first infusion
- Second and third infusions: 1-2 minutes, adjusting as needed

Two infusions recommended for full flavor. Third infusion reveals softer sweetness that hints at honey and orchard fruit. If you oversteep, you start tasting artichoke and old gym socks; not the vibe we are after.
