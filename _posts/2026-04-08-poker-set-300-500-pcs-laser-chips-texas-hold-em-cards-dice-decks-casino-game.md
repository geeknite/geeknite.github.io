## Maintenance and Care: Keeping It Sharp

Chips and cards are built to last, but they benefit from a little care. Here are a few practical tips to keep your kit looking and performing like new:

- Keep a dedicated chip tray to prevent chips from rolling off the table when someone forgets to push their stack back in. A little organization goes a long way.
- Wipe chips occasionally with a dry microfiber. Excess moisture can dull the laser finish and leave fingerprints that won’t come off easily.
- Store decks in protective sleeves or a card box to reduce edge wear and corner curls. Plastic-coated cards tend to stand up to abuse better than plain paper variants, especially in humid environments.
- If you have a table that shifts under heavy traffic, consider a non-slip mat to prevent chips from sliding around during a tense moment in the hand.
