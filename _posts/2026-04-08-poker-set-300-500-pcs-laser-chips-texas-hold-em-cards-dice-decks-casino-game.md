## Maintenance and Care: Keeping It Sharp

Chips and cards are built to last, but they benefit from a little care. Here are practical tips to keep your kit in peak condition:

- Use a dedicated chip tray to prevent rolls and spills during tense moments.
- Wipe chips with a dry microfiber to preserve the laser finish and minimize fingerprint smudges.
- Store decks in protective sleeves or a card box to reduce edge wear and corner curls. Plastic-coated cards are generally more durable in humid environments.
- If your table shifts under heavy traffic, a non-slip mat can keep chips from sliding during dramatic hands.
