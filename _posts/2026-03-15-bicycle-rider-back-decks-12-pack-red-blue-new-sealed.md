## Maintenance and care tips
- Store decks in a cool, dry place; humidity can cause warping over long periods.
- Keep away from sunlight to preserve the color integrity of the tuck boxes.
- If you notice any edge wear, rotate your usage among red and blue decks to distribute wear evenly.
- Clean your hands before handling to prevent oils from transferring to the card faces.
