We know you expect the montage of Allen keys and Allen wrenches, and yes, there will be some of that. The assembly process for the VEVOR 55-Inch is straightforward but not trivial. It is not Ikea-level glass front dismantle-and-reassemble; you will need to invest an hour or two, depending on your knack for aligning parts and the mysterious extra screws that always appear in the wrong bag.

Here are the essential steps to a smooth assembly:
1. Unpack everything, make sure you have all the parts, and lay them out like a tiny factory line.
2. Attach the legs with the crossbar for a stable base.
3. Mount the end panels and ensure the cabinet is square; this is where a square is your friend.
4. Install the rods and players; ensure the players are oriented correctly (face the right direction for left and right players).
5. Install the playing surface and secure with screws.
6. Attach the ball return and handles; align the rods with the wheel.
7. Do a test run; adjust leg levelers as needed.

Pro-tip: keep a small bowl of spare screws on hand; you might need them when you realize you dropped one into a toe-a.

## Maintenance and Care
Your foosball table is a small ecosystem; the more you treat it well, the longer it lasts. A few simple steps can keep the table playing well for years:
- Wipe the playing surface after use to remove fingerprints and oils.
- Lubricate the rods occasionally with a light silicone-based lubricant (avoid heavy oils that attract dust).
- Check for loose screws and tighten as needed.
- Keep the table away from direct sunlight to avoid warping and color fading.
- Use a protective cover when not in use to prevent dust buildup.

If you plan to move the table, consider disassembly or at least getting help; these tables are heavy, and a solo move can lead to minor injuries or a scratched finish.
