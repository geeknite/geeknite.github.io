## Strategy Tips and Tips for Beginners
If you are new to Tripoley or this Deluxe Mat Version, here are a few rules of thumb that can help you navigate the night without turning into a human chaos algorithm:

- Focus on Michigan Rummy early to establish a reliable base. A strong meld can give you wind in your sails even if other phases go sideways.
- Watch the pot size. The pot often dictates how aggressively players bid during Poker. A bigger pot invites more drama and more opportunities to win big or to lose gracefully.
- Don’t chase a single hand. Tripoley is a marathon, not a sprint. The mat’s per-phase structure rewards flexible thinking and willingness to adapt as the night evolves.
- Practice polite bluffing. Bluff with a smile or a sly nod; the table will read you better than your actual tells, which are often nonexistent in a home environment.
- Chip management matters. The chips are the lifeblood of the night. Don’t throw them away in a moment of bravado; conserve your resources for the crucial Poker moment when the cheering crowd wants a show.
