## The Basics of THE MIND

### Components

THE MIND comes with a deck of cards numbered 1 to 100, a few life cards, and throwing stars. That's it. Simple, right? But don’t let its minimalist design fool you. These cards will challenge your mind and your friendships!

### Objective

The objective of the game is to play cards in ascending order without speaking. Yes, you read that correctly. You and your teammates must rely on intuition and non-verbal cues to play the cards in the correct sequence. Sounds easy? Think again!

## Gameplay

### Setup

To set up THE MIND, shuffle the deck and deal cards to each player. The number of cards dealt corresponds to the level you're on. In level one, each player receives one card. In level two, each player gets two cards, and so on.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Silence Phase**: Players must not speak or communicate in any way. Stare deeply into each other's eyes, and let the mind games begin!
2. **Play Phase**: Players play cards in ascending order by placing them face-up on the table. If someone plays a card out of order, you lose a life. If you successfully play all cards in sequence, you proceed to the next level.
3. **Throwing Stars**: If the team finds themselves in a sticky situation, they can use a throwing star. This allows each player to discard their lowest card, providing a strategic reset.

### Winning and Losing

To win, players must complete all levels without losing all their lives. If you lose all your lives, it's game over. Time for a rematch!

**Pro Tip**: Always keep a spare life card handy. It might not save you from your friend's terrible card intuition, but it will give you a second chance.
