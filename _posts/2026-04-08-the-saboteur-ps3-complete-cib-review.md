### How to Verify Mint Condition

Verification hinges on three things: the box edges, the manual, and the disc surface. Check the corners for crushing, the spine for readability, and the front cover art for any scuffs or fading. The manual should lie flat, with all pages intact and no standout creases. The disc should spin freely, with no deep scratches that would raise your blood pressure when testing load times. If inserts are present, ensure they are unmarred and free from discoloration, which can indicate moisture exposure or storage overzeal.
