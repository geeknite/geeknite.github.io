### The Science (Or The Not-So-Magic)
The science behind joint-support supplements is idiosyncratic. Glucosamine and chondroitin have some evidence supporting joint health in dogs, but results can be inconsistent. In the realm of large dogs, those with obesity, or dogs with advanced osteoarthritis, results are often modest. Multicare aligns with typical veterinary guidance: it’s a supplement to support joint function, not a cure-all.
