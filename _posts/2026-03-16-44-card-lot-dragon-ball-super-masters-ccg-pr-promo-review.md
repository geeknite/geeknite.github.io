## Care, Storage, and Display

Promos deserve a home that respects their special status. Here are some best practices for preserving 44-card lots and similar treasure troves:

- Sleeves: Use acid-free sleeves to reduce edge wear. If you expect frequent handling, consider semi-rigid card holders for priority display pieces.
- Binders: For display and organization, a high-quality binder with labeled dividers makes a tremendous difference. Decide on a labeling system early: by set, by foil type, or by artist if you’re that level of dedicated.
- Environment: Keep away from direct sunlight, humidity, and extreme heat. Foil shines reflect light; you don’t want to bake your cards into a matte version of themselves.
- Handling: Clean hands or gloves help. If you’re the type who touches cards like you’re about to sign them, maybe invest in a soft cloth or a microfiber glove for the dramatic reveal moments.
