### How to Care for This Card
If you plan to flip through Carddass memory lanes with this piece, here are a few practical tips:
- Sleeves matter. Use soft sleeves to protect the foil surface from abrasion. Consider a two-piece approach: inner sleeves with the card snug and outer sleeves for display. This helps prevent chipping on the corners and keeps the holo shine pristine.
- Storage solution. A premium binder with acid-free pages is ideal. Ziploc-type storage is okay for travel, but ensure the card stays flat and away from humidity.
- Handling etiquette. Oils from hands are the enemy. Handle the card with clean hands or with gloves if you plan to photograph or film a showcase.
- Display considerations. If you want to put it on a wall, consider a glass-front display case. It protects from dust and accidental touches while letting your inner geek scream with joy every time you pass by.
