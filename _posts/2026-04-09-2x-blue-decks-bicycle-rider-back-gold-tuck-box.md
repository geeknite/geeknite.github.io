## Durability and Maintenance
Durability is not a drum solo; it’s a steady rhythm you’ll hear after many practice sessions. Care tips:
- Keep decks away from moisture; humidity can affect the finish over time.
- Avoid slamming decks in drawers; a soft place to rest is a good idea.
- If you notice minor warping after heavy use, flatten gently under a book or a light, even-pressure method. Don’t try to force them straight—let gravity do most of the work.
