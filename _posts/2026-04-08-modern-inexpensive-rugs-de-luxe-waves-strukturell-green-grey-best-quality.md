### Cleaning and maintenance
Care is refreshingly practical:
- Vacuum regularly with a beater bar on a low setting, or use a suction-only attachment to reduce fiber fraying.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push liquid deeper into the pile.
- For stains, use a mild detergent solution or a rug-specific cleaner. Always test a patch in a hidden corner first.
- If you have pets, consider a pet-specific odor neutralizer and a quick weekly vacuum to prevent grime buildup.

Avoid harsh chemical cleaners and chlorine-based solutions, which can dull the pattern or subtly alter the color. The goal is stain deterrence, not a chemical arms race on your floor.
