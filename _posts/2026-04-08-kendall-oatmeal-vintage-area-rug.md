## Cleaning and maintenance: keep it fresh

routine care keeps your rug looking new longer. the Kendall Oatmeal is forgiving enough to forgive the occasional spill while requiring a reasonable care routine to avoid a dusty or matted look. vacuum regularly and address stains promptly with a gentle cleaner. avoid harsh chemicals that can affect color and fabric integrity, and test any cleaner on a hidden corner first to avoid color surprises that would make you cry at the design altar.

### Cleaning do and dont

- vacuum weekly to maintain bounce and prevent deep pile compression
- blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing which spreads stains
- use a mild, pH balanced cleaner if needed; test on a small area first
- rotate the rug every few months to promote even wear and color consistency
- use a rug pad to reduce slippage and prolong life
- avoid direct sun for long periods to minimize color fading

### Deep cleaning options

for stubborn stains or seasonal refreshes, consider professional cleaning every 12 to 18 months depending on traffic. a professional can handle delicate fibers and restore the rug without risking damage from DIY overzealous cleansing. if you decide to go DIY, stick to mild methods and avoid steam cleaning that can affect the backing or warp the fibers.
