### Sealing and authenticity concerns

With any Bandai Fest exclusive, authenticity checks are prudent. The sealed status helps, but you still want to inspect the edge sealing, the foil stamps, and the tiny differences in serial numbers if you’re lucky enough to see them. If you’re buying sealed from a retailer, you want to ensure the outer shrink wrap is intact, the insert is present, and the inner sleeve is not obviously resealed. For the truly concerned collector, there are community threads and posting guides that discuss how to spot fakes—though the best approach is to purchase from reputable sources and confirm the post-purchase return policy if your enthusiasm outruns your budget.
