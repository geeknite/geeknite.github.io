## The Basics of Asgard

Asgard is a strategic board game designed for 2-4 players. In this game, players take on the roles of gods and heroes from Norse mythology, competing to control the realms and gather followers. It's like Valhalla, but without the need to die in battle first.

### Components

The game comes with beautifully designed board elements, including the realms of Midgard, Jotunheim, and Asgard itself. The components include miniatures of gods and heroes, a deck of cards featuring various powers and items, and a plethora of tokens to track your progress and influence. The artwork is stunning, and you'll find yourself admiring the details of each piece.

**Fun Fact**: The game includes a special "Bifrost" miniature that makes you feel like you're walking on the rainbow bridge. Just try not to slip.

## Gameplay

Asgard combines elements of strategy, resource management, and tactical combat. Players must gather followers, build temples, and engage in epic battles to achieve their goals.

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Action Phase**: Players perform actions like recruiting followers, building temples, and using special abilities.
2. **Event Phase**: Draw event cards that introduce new challenges or opportunities. (Loki loves to mess things up, as per usual.)
3. **Battle Phase**: Engage in battles to control realms and gain influence. Prepare for epic showdowns!

### Winning and Losing
