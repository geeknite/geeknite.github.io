## Maintenance and Durability

Long-term satisfaction with foosball tables depends on minimal maintenance. The basics:

- Wipe the surface after use to remove fingerprints and snack residue.
- Check levelers every few weeks; uneven surfaces can ruin ball bounce.
- Lubricate the rods with manufacturer-recommended lubricant every few months.
- Inspect bearings and end caps for wear; replace if there is visible wobble.

The cabinet materials should withstand everyday use, and good-quality hardware should not loosen dramatically after a few intense matches. If you own a dog or a curious cat, a simple cover can keep the fur away from moving parts and your sanity intact.
