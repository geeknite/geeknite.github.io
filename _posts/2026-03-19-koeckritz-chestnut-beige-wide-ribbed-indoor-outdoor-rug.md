## Care and Maintenance Playbook

### Routine Cleaning

- Vacuum regularly to remove dirt and grit that could abrade the fibers over time.
- Shake or beat the rug as needed to dislodge loose debris, especially in windy climates.
- For light spills, blot with a clean cloth and rinse with mild soap and water if necessary.

### Deep Cleaning and Stain Management

- For tougher stains, mix a mild dish soap solution with water and gently scrub with a soft-bristle brush.
- Rinse and allow to air-dry completely before placing back on heavy traffic areas.
- Avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that could degrade polypropylene fibers or affect color uniformity.

### Storage and Seasonal Care

If you live in a region with cold winters or heavy seasonal rainfall, you can roll up the rug for storage to reduce exposure to weather. When you roll it, store it in a dry area to prevent any moisture buildup, which can cause mildew in rare circumstances. The rug is not a mystical artifact that withstands all climate ills, but proper care will extend its life and maintain its color and structure.
