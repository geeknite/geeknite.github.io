## Care and Maintenance
Caring for the RI Sushi Play Set is easier than assembling a craving for dragon-fruit smoothies. Here are a few practical tips:
- Wipe after play with a damp cloth and mild soap if needed. Avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the plastic over time.
- Let pieces air-dry to prevent moisture entrapment that could lead to foggy finishes or stickiness.
- Check for wear and tear after intense sessions. If any pieces begin to crack or break, set them aside to prevent small parts from becoming hazards for younger siblings.
- Store in a dry place to maintain color vibrancy and prevent warping.
