## Storage, Expansion, and Practicality
2 TB of claimed storage is generous for a digital edition, but the practical reality is more nuanced. The 2TB label sounds impressive, but you should be mindful of how much of that space will be consumed by system software, digital licenses, and game updates. For many players, the reality is that you will find yourself juggling installed games: keep your favorites and archive the rest on an external drive or re-download as needed.

External storage options remain a hot topic. You can add external USB drives for PS4 games and some PS5 titles, but many games will require the internal NVMe or official expansion for best performance. If you are planning to keep a large library of modern PS5 titles, you may want to factor in a recommendation for a fast NVMe expansion or a large external SSD to complement the 2TB internal space.
