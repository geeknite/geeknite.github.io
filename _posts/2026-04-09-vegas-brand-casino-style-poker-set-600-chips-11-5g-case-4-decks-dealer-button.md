## Care and maintenance

Like any gaming gear, longevity hinges on care. Here are some practical tips to extend the life of your set:

- Keep chips away from moisture. If a spill happens, dry them promptly to prevent warping or color bleed. A quick wipe with a soft cloth is usually enough after a game night.
- Store cards and case in a clean, dry place. The foam is forgiving, but you want to avoid long-term exposure to humidity or heat, which can affect the cards and the interior lining.
- Clean the case exterior with a mild microfiber cloth. Don’t use harsh chemicals that could degrade the faux leather or the exterior finish.
- Inspect the case latches and wheels (if present) periodically. A snug latch is nothing fancy until you’ve had a chip pile avalanche during a live stream, at which point you will appreciate the gentle but sturdy hold.
