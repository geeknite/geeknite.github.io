## Tips and Hacks for Living with Mineral 222
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and sunlight exposure.
- Use a rug pad to improve underfoot comfort and to reduce movement. It also helps with noise dampening in apartments.
- For spills, tackle them quickly but calmly. A quick blot with a dry towel followed by a light cleaning solution usually does the trick.
- Pair with a couple of throw pillows and a plant or two to create a cozy zone that invites relaxation rather than a battle against the floor.
