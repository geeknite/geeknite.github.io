## Care and Maintenance
Care is where many rugs either shine or fade into “well, that’s a nice carpet.” Wool is relatively forgiving, but proper maintenance matters. Here’s a practical care plan for the Cambridge Myrtis:

- Vacuum regularly with a low-suction setting or use a rug attachment. Avoid aggressive suction that can pull fibers and hamper the pile’s loft.
- Rotate every 3-6 months to promote even wear and to prevent sun-bleed from one side showing up as a faded stripe.
- Spot clean promptly using a mild detergent diluted in water. Blot rather than rub to reduce fiber damage. Test any cleaning solution on an inconspicuous area first.
- For deeper cleaning, consider a professional rug cleaning service that specializes in wool. Hot water extraction can be used carefully, but excessive moisture can lead to wool swelling and slow-drying times.
- Protect from direct sunlight to minimize color fading. If your setup is near a window, consider UV-blocking curtains or window films.

If you’re a neat freak, you’ll appreciate the rug’s ability to hold its shape after a good vacuum and the way the moroccan lattice remains legible after a year of living with you in your daily sci-fi marathons.

## Care Tips for Tech-Focused Spaces
Since Geeknite loves the intersection of tech and comfort, here are a few extra tips tailored to spaces with gadgets and cables:
- Cable management near the rug: Use cable covers or channels to keep cords under control. You don’t want a power cable snag to become a design feature of the rug’s pattern.
- Chair wheels care: If you have a rolling chair, consider a high-traction mat under the chair to reduce friction on the wool fibers. It helps preserve the pile while your inner racetrack designer tests out new chair maneuvers.
- Humidity considerations: Wool can handle regular home humidity, but extreme dryness or dampness can affect the fibers. Use a humidifier in dry climates and ensure the rug isn’t tucked into a humid basement corner.
- Stain protocol: For electronics lovers who snack while gaming, keep a ready stain removal kit with mild detergent. The sooner you blot a spill, the better the outcome.
