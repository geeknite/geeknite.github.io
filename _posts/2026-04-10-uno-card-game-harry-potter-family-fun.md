## H2: House Rules and Variations to Try
If you want to spice things up beyond the printed rules, here are a few house rules that preserve the fun while introducing new tactical elements:

- The Sorting Rule: At the start of the game, players draw a card to determine their “house listing” for the night. You must play according to your assigned house color for the duration of a round. Switch houses after every four rounds to mix affinities.
- Duo Duel: In 2-player games, allow a “Duel” mode where both players draw a card from two decks (UNO and HP) and the highest card wins the round. The loser draws two extra cards as a penalty, deemed as a “time to chill with a Sorting Hat.”
- Wand Wave: A single time per game, a player can declare a “Wand Wave” (a shout-out to a HP spell). This card forces all other players to discard down to a maximum of seven cards whenever they play a spell card.

These variants keep things fresh without derailing the lighthearted vibe. They’re especially good when you’ve played the base edition a dozen times and want a tiny twist without buying a brand-new game.
