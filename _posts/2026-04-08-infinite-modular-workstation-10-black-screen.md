## Practical Tips for Maximizing Your Infinite Modular Experience

- Start with a minimal viable configuration: one compute module, one storage module, and a couple of I/O bays. See how the space feels, then expand.
- Map your modules to a workflow: designate bays for specific tasks (render farm, dev lab, media archive) to keep cognitive load manageable.
- Invest in good cable sleeves and labeling. The beauty of modular systems is spoiled if the cable jungle defeats the entire purpose.
- Use the Black Screen as a status beacon rather than a constant dashboard. The goal is to keep a calm desk ambience while still having quick insight into critical system state.
- Plan your upgrade path. If you know you’ll want more compute power, choose modules with standard interfaces and upgradeable backplanes when possible.
