## Maintenance: Keeping the Set Looking Sharp

Maintenance is the quiet hero of any outdoor furniture plan. Wicker is forgiving when you know how to treat it. Here are a few maintenance notes that actually help:

- Clean with mild soap and water; avoid harsh solvents that can degrade resin.
- Dry thoroughly to prevent mildew; quick-dry foam cushions help, but you still want to air them out after rain.
- Protect from extreme heat when not in use, especially if you’re in a climate with blistering sun. A shaded area or a cover will do wonders for color retention.
- Inspect the frame for loose screws a few times in the first month; a little preventive tightening keeps the whole thing singing for seasons.

Think of maintenance as a mini-quest: each small care step is XP toward longer-lasting peace, a more beautiful deck, and fewer commitments to buying replacement pieces next year.
