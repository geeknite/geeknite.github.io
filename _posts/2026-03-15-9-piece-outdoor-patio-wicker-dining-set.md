## Maintenance: Keeping the Set Looking Sharp

Maintenance is the quiet hero of any outdoor furniture plan. Wicker forgives when you know how to treat it. Some practical tips:

- Clean with mild soap and water; avoid harsh solvents that can degrade resin.
- Dry thoroughly to prevent mildew; quick-dry foam cushions help, but air them out after rain.
- Protect from extreme heat when not in use; a shaded area or a cover helps color retention and bite-sized sunburns.
- Inspect the frame for loose screws a few times in the first month; a little preventive tightening keeps the whole ensemble singing for seasons.

Think of maintenance as a mini-quest: each small care step is XP toward longer-lasting peace, a more beautiful deck, and fewer emergency purchases next summer.
