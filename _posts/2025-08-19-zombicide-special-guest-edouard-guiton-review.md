## What is Zombicide Special Guest: Edouard Guiton?

Zombicide Special Guest: Edouard Guiton is an expansion for the popular cooperative board game Zombicide. This expansion introduces new characters designed by the talented artist Edouard Guiton, bringing his unique style and flair to the zombie apocalypse. With stunning artwork, cool miniatures, and exciting new abilities, this expansion offers an immersive experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

### The Basics

In Zombicide, players work together to survive waves of zombies while completing objectives. Each player controls a survivor with unique abilities and equipment, making teamwork essential for victory. The Edouard Guiton expansion adds new survivors and abominations, each with distinct mechanics that add variety and challenge to the game.

### Components

The expansion includes:
- **Miniatures**: Beautifully crafted miniatures of the new survivors and abominations designed by Edouard Guiton. Seriously, they're so detailed you might want to display them proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: New equipment, skills, and zombie spawn cards that add depth and excitement to the gameplay.
- **Game Board**: Additional tiles and scenarios that expand the Zombicide universe.
