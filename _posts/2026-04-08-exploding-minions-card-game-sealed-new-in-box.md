## How to play: a crash course
In short, Exploding Minions is a fast-paced card game built around bluffing, quick thinking, and occasional mild chaos-induced mortification when your carefully planned turn gets hijacked by a banana-obsessed rabbit of a card. Here’s the skeleton:
- Objective: Avoid getting blown up by one of the exploding cards while trying to force your opponents into a trap where they must play their own explosion card to keep the game going.
- Setup: Shuffle the deck. Each player draws a hand. The box is sealed like a dragon guarding a breakfast buffet; you take a moment to acknowledge that you’re about to become the breakfast buffet.
- Turn flow: On your turn you can “play” a minion card to enact its effect, or hold for a clever bluff. There are cards that force draws, skip turns, and more devious zucchinis of strategy that only make sense after you’ve said the phrase banana-lord 17 times.
- Endgame: The remaining players snap into a desperate countdown as the deck pressure builds toward a final draw, and someone inevitably yells “UNO!” even though that word never appears in the card text.

The core mechanic involves playing cards to either protect yourself or sabotage others, with a few sacrificial lambs in the form of minions who are more likely to explode than to save a corner grocery trip. Bluffing is essential here: you can pretend you’re low on cards, bluff a big move, and watch your friends’ faces transform into a kaleidoscope of fear and giggles.

The physical quality is a mixed bag that lands somewhere between “premium feel” and “we printed these on a kitchen table at midnight.” The cards are sturdy enough to survive the occasional rinse in a smoothie blender (metaphorically, we hope), and the print quality is good enough that you won’t confuse a Minion’s grin for a banana peel. Let’s be honest: this is not a strategy-heavy game. The game shines as a party staple; it’s not meant to be a long, labyrinthine experience but rather a quick, sharable experience that ends with you plotting your revenge for the next round while everyone sips their drinks with alarming sincerity.

## Durability, storage, and maintenance tips
As with any sealed-in-box product, the first thing you should do is to verify that the seal is intact and that nothing rips during the unboxing ritual.
