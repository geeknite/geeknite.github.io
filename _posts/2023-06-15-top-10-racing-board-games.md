## 2. [Flamme Rouge]({{ site.constants.wsib }}flamme-rouge)

### Overview

Flamme Rouge simulates a bicycle race, with players managing their energy levels and drafting behind other racers to gain an advantage. It's one of the standout racing games. Just don't forget to hydrate - even cardboard cyclists need water breaks! This game is my second favorite due to its unique mechanics and thrilling gameplay.

### Gameplay

Each player controls a team of two cyclists, a sprinter and a rouleur, who must work together to navigate the racecourse. The game uses a card-driven mechanic where players select cards to determine their cyclists' movement. The key to victory is managing your energy cards wisely and taking advantage of slipstreams.

### Pros

- **Strategic depth**: The card management and drafting mechanics require thoughtful planning.
- **Immersive theme**: The game captures the essence of bicycle racing beautifully.
- **Replayability**: Different track layouts and strategic decisions keep the game engaging.

### Cons
