## What is Herd Mentality?

Herd Mentality is a party game designed for 4-20 players (yes, you read that right) where the goal is to think like everyone else. It's a game of matching answers and avoiding the dreaded Pink Cow. The more you think like the herd, the more points you'll score. But if your answer is too unique, you'll be stuck with the Pink Cow, and trust me, you don't want that.

### Components

The game includes:
- **Answer Sheets**: Where players write down their answers.
- **Cow Tokens**: Used to keep score.
- **Pink Cow**: The ultimate party foul.
- **Question Cards**: The heart of the game, featuring hilarious and thought-provoking prompts.

**Pro Tip**: Keep the Pink Cow away from your snacks. It has a knack for attracting crumbs.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Herd Mentality is as simple as milking a cow (no, seriously). Just lay out the answer sheets, distribute the cow tokens, and shuffle the question cards. Now you're ready to start thinking like a herd.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, a player draws a question card and reads it aloud. Everyone writes down their answers, aiming to match as many other players as possible. The matching answers score points, while the player with the odd answer gets the Pink Cow.

### Winning and Losing

The game continues until someone reaches eight points. The player with the most points at the end wins, while the player with the Pink Cow loses. It's simple, fun, and surprisingly competitive.
