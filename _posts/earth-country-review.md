### Spain: Land of Siestas and Fiestas

Spain, where every day feels like a celebration, and naps are a national pastime. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the sunny beaches of Barcelona, Spain is a country that knows how to live life to the fullest.

#### Highlights

- **Food**: Tapas, paella, churros - your taste buds will thank you.
- **Culture**: Flamenco, bullfighting, and a history that dates back to the Romans.
- **Weather**: Sunny 365 days a year. (Okay, not really, but close enough!)
