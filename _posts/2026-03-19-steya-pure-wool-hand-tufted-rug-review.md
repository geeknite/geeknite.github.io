## Durability and Maintenance
Durability is where wool shines. Wool fibers resist wear and tear, bounce back from flattening, and handle foot traffic with grace. The tufting density also influences durability; the higher the density, the longer the rug will look sharp and resist visible wear in high-traffic zones. The Steya rug is designed to withstand daily life, including spillages that might occur when someone tries their hand at fancy coffee art. Quick clean up is recommended; blot the spill with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing, and for stubborn stains consider a gentle wool-safe cleaner following the manufacturers care instructions.

### Cleaning and Care Tips
- Vacuum with a brush attachment on low suction to avoid fuzzing the pile. - For small spills, blot immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing as it can push stain deeper. - Use a mild wool-safe cleaner if needed, test on a hidden area first. - Consider professional cleaning every few years if foot traffic is heavy; wool fibers respond well to gentle professional care. - Rotate the rug every 6 to 12 months to promote even wear.
