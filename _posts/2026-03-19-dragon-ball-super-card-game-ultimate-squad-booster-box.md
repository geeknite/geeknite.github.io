## Builder’s Guide: Deck Construction, Pacing, and Power Curve

Putting together a squad-based deck around the Ultimate Squad box requires a little planning. The pacing is the real trick: you want to assemble your squad early, push a couple of key plays by mid-game, and then close strong as your big finishers come online.

A practical approach:
- Start with a 50-card deck recommended by your local scene or your preferred online guide. Keep in mind the energy generation curve: you don’t want to stall with a hand full of high-cost units on turns when you can’t pay the energy costs.
- Reserve around 8-12 cards that you can draw into to maintain momentum. These should be flexible cards that work in multiple situations rather than a single-purpose artifact.
- Practice a couple of variations: a more aggressive squad that tries to advance quickly and a midrange approach that stabilizes, then accelerates into a late-game finisher. Seeing both play out will give you a better sense of what the box actually enables in practice.
