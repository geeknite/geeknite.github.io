## Practicality: cleaning, care, and living with a newbie rug
### Routine care
- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and hair. Use a gentle setting to avoid pulling fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Don’t rub; you’ll push stains deeper and create tiny art sculptures of coffee rings. Blot and let air-dry.
- For more stubborn spots, use a mild detergent solution or follow retailer guidelines. Always test in a small, inconspicuous area first.

### Pet-friendly considerations
If you’ve got a furry co-pilot, the ivory base might attract pet hair a touch more visibly; the blue accents can help camouflage minor accidents in high-traffic zones. A quick vacuum and spot-clean routine will keep things soda-sparkly and presentable. The key is quick action and using a stain-resistant approach offered by the rug’s material family.

### Longevity expectations
Brand-new rugs from reputable lines like QUA11 usually offer solid colorfastness and reasonable fade resistance in typical lighting. Real-world fading depends on sun exposure, room orientation, and how many times your sunbeam paints the floor with a natural glow every afternoon. If you love a dramatic sunset color shift, you may want another rug. If you want reliable color retention with daily wear, Quarry Ivory Blue stands up well.
