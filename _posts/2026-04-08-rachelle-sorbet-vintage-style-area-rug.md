## Maintenance and longevity: keeping Sorbet looking fresh
Maintenance is where this rug earns style points without asking you to become a full-time cleaner. Regular vacuuming to remove surface dust is your baseline. When it comes to stains, act quickly: blot—don’t rub—with a clean cloth to lift the spill, then use a mild stain remover recommended for synthetic blends. Avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that could dull the color depth or compromise the fibers. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color exposure. If you’re into sustainability, consider a rug pad underneath to reduce wear, extend the rug’s life, and reduce noise under foot traffic.
