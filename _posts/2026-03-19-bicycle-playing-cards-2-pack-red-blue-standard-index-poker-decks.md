## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity
Even the best decks can run into trouble if misused. Here are practical tips to extend the life of your Bicycle 2-Pack decks.

- Keep them dry: humidity and liquid spills are your cards enemy. If you spill something, wipe gently and air dry; don t put them in a heat source. 
- Store properly: use a cool, dry place and avoid heavy pressure on the decks. Plastic cases or dedicated card boxes can help. 
- Handle with care: using dry hands and avoiding harsh bends will help preserve the stock and finish. 
- Keep away from rough surfaces: rough tables and clumsy table runners can cause edge wear faster than you think. 

If you re serious about card longevity, rotate through decks to reduce stress on a single pair. The Bicycle 2-Pack isn t fragile, but it isn t a toy either. Treat it with respect, and it will serve you well for many game nights.
