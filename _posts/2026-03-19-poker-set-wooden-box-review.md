## Care Tips and Setup: Keeping the Magic Fresh
- Keep the set in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. The wood hates sunburn and the chips hate being warped by heat jugar. 
- Wipe the cards with a dry, clean cloth if they collect fingerprints or dust; avoid water or cleaning solvents which can wear card finishes. 
- When transporting, use the box’s internal compartments to prevent chips from rolling into the sofa or under the couch where they will multiply in your absence. 
- For the dice, give them a quick roll on a hard surface before the first game to ensure they aren’t misbehaving acoustically.
