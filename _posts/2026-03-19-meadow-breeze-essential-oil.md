## Safety, ethics, and best practices

Safety first, always. Even with a seemingly gentle blend like Meadow Breeze, there are a few best practices to remember:
- Do not apply undiluted essential oils to skin. Always dilute before topical use.
- Do a patch test first to check for irritation or an allergic reaction.
- Keep out of reach of children and pets. Essential oils can be concentrated and not ideal for curious noses and paws.
- If you are pregnant, nursing, or have underlying health conditions, consult with a healthcare professional before using essential oils regularly.
- Use in well ventilated spaces. Essential oils do not replace fresh air and should be used as a fragrance companion, not as the single air source.

If you want to learn more about safety guidelines, you can read a general guide on essential oils safety at https://www.verywellhealth.com/essential-oils-safety-4156959 and for a more clinical angle see https://nccih.nih.gov/health/essential-oils
