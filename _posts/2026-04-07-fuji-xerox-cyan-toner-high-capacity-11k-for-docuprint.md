## Installation Tips and Tricks: Getting the Most from Your 11K Cyan
- Pre-check: Make sure your printer is powered off during cartridge removal and replacement. This is to avoid any gossip between the printer’s sensors and your nervous energy.
- Print a calibration page: After installation, run a calibration to align color channels. If you skip this, you might end up with a blue halo around graphs that looks suspiciously like a sci-fi author’s handwriting.
- Clean the corona wire: A quick wipe can prevent specks or streaks. Don’t overdo it—don’t soak the cartridge with disinfectant, either; we’re not trying to create a chemical romance between toner and paper.
- Paper choice matters: Use good-quality coated or high-brightness paper for the best color fidelity. Thicker stock will benefit from the toner’s high-density coverage.
- Recycle properly: If your toner is from a program that supports recycling, use it. It’s good planet stuff, and your conscience will thank you in the next budget cycle.
