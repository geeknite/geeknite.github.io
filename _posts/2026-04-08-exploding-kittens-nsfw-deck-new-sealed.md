## How to play in short (for busy geeks)
Exploding Kittens NSFW is still a game about risk, bluffing, and table talk more than deep strategy. Here’s the essence in a few compact bullets:
- Setup: Remove the Exploding Kitten cards and Defuse cards as required by the number of players, shuffle, deal a starting hand, and place the remaining deck face-down as the draw pile.
- Turn order: On your turn, you draw one card, then you may play cards from your hand to alter the flow of the round. Your goal is to avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten unless you have a Defuse to survive.
- Exploding Kittens: If you draw an Exploding Kitten and have no Defuse, you’re out of the round. This triggers a cascade of reactions: mockery, desperate scrambles for Defuse cards, and maybe a dramatic reading of the feline fate card.
- Defuse cards: Your safety valves. Use them to avert doom, and you typically place the Exploding Kitten back into the draw pile in a way that keeps the table sweating a little longer.
