### Practical Care and Maintenance

To keep the deck in pristine shape, store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. The seals do a great job preserving the cards, but nothing lasts forever; even sealed items can suffer from long-term exposure to humidity or heat. If you accidently leave the deck near a window or a radiator, you might notice slight warping or color shift on the card fronts. A simple crisper drawer environment or a dedicated game storage box is enough to maintain the deck for years. If you ever decide to take the cards out for a big family night, handle them with care, shuffle gently, and remind yourself that the goal is fun, not accidental card destruction.
