### Chip composition and feel
Clay chips are the old guard, with a touch of modern polymer to improve durability. Clay without polymer tends to crack if mishandled, and polymer-only chips feel more like plastic poker chips you’d use at a kids’ arcade. Claytec tries to deliver the best of both worlds: a heft you can feel with a satisfying snap when you drop them and enough resilience to bounce back from a rough roll across the felt.

The 14-gram weight is on the heavier side for home use, but not chain-saw heavy. It’s closer to casino-grade weight, which means real players can detect subtle differences in the stack’s wobbles and flicks. If you like to perform “the stack” trick as though you’re a high-roller in a noir film, the extra heft helps your confidence. If you’re more of a chill home gamer who is worried about sending the chips skittering off the table during a shove, fear not—the weight also gives stability to your stack.
