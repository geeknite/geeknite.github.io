## Durability and Cleaning

Durability is a big deal in a high-traffic home. The Sunset Stripes Rug shows resistance to everyday wear. The stripes still read sharp after months of use, and the weave keeps its integrity even under heavy vacuum passes. Spills clean up relatively easily with a quick blot and a mild cleaner, though you should always test a small area first to avoid any color shifting. A note for the spill crowd: coffee, red wine, and soy sauce all share the same fate if treated promptly. Avoid harsh chemicals that could dull the vibrancy of the color story, and stick to mild detergent options with a gentle cloth or sponge.

Maintenance is straightforward. Regular vacuuming with a low-suction setting prevents the accumulation of dust without tugging at the fibers. Rotate the rug occasionally to ensure even wear, especially if you have sunlight streaming in from one side of the room. UV exposure can fade colors over time, so if you notice a patch looking a touch lighter after months of sun, try repositioning the rug or shuffling furniture blocks to redistribute the light exposure.
