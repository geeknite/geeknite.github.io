## Assembly Tips and Setup Tricks
As promised, here are some actionable steps to get the most out of the Express 6-Person unit without becoming a human wrench comedy show.

1) Keep the workspace clear for the first 48 hours after assembly. You will move items repeatedly as you align the frame to the floor.
2) Use the cable trays early. It is easier to route as you go than to redo after you place the desks in their final positions.
3) Confirm screen mount compatibility before choosing monitors. The two workstation rails may have different hole patterns; if you have a mix of mounting systems, plan ahead.
4) Test the tolerance of the power distribution. The last thing you want is a power strip that struggles to keep up with six users all running a screen and a coffee grinder simultaneously.
5) Label everything. A small label on each cable and a quick diagram on the desk surface will save you from a future scavenger hunt under the workstation.
6) Practice good cable etiquette with the team. This is not a solo project; coordinate with colleagues to avoid a cluster of cables that look like a slithering creature from a bad sci-fi film.
