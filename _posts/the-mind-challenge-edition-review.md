## The Basics

### What is The Mind?

For the uninitiated, **The Mind** is a cooperative card game where players work together to play cards in ascending order without any communication. Sounds simple, right? Hah! Just wait until your brain melts trying to telepathically sync with your friends.

### Components

The Challenge Edition comes with the same sleek cards and minimalistic design as the original, but with some added twists. You get new cards that introduce additional challenges, like playing cards in reverse order or matching specific colors. It's like The Mind, but on steroids.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up The Mind: Challenge Edition is as easy as pie. Each player gets a hand of cards, and the goal is to play them in the correct order without speaking. The Challenge Edition adds new types of challenges that spice things up.

### The Challenges

The new challenges include:

- **Reverse Order:** Play cards in descending order.
- **Color Match:** Play cards that match specific colors.
- **Double Trouble:** Two cards must be played at once.

Each challenge adds a unique twist to the game, making it even more brain-bending.
