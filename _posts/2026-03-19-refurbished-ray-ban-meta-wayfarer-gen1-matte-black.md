## Refurbished value and practical considerations

Refurbished gear isn’t a gamble when you’re buying from reputable sellers with a clear grading system. ## How to care for your refurbished Gen 1 sunglasses
- Clean the lenses gently with microfiber cloths and a proper lens cleaner. Resist the urge to scrub with jeans or your sleeve—this is a fashion accessory; we expect more from it than rough handling.
- Keep the hinges and temple sensors clean and free from dust. A tiny wipe-down can prevent micro-scratches that might creep into your media capture.
- Store in a protective case when not in use. The best-case scenario is that your sunglasses return to their home in the case with minimal signs of life outside of occasional smug looks in the mirror.
- Avoid extreme temperatures. Electronics don’t like being boiled in a hot car or frozen in a winter trunk; you’re basically cooking a smartphone in a space helmet and hoping for the best. Gentle treatment keeps the battery healthier longer and reduces the chance of micro-faults.
