Care and Maintenance: A Quick Field Guide

For daily care, regular vacuuming keeps dust and fur at bay. If you have a busy household, you may want to vacuum once or twice a week and spot clean as needed. For spills, blot quickly with a clean cloth rather than rubbing, which can push the liquid deeper into the fibers. If you have a stain that refuses to surrender, a mild cleaning solution applied sparingly can help. The key is to act fast and avoid strong chemicals that could degrade the fibers or colors. A quick cleaning routine can extend the rug’s life and keep its surface looking fresh and inviting for longer periods. Remember to test any cleaning product on a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure color integrity and avoid surprises that may turn the rug into a modern abstract art piece you did not sign up for.
