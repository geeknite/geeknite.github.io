## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity: A Practical Playbook
- Vacuum weekly with a standard rug attachment to maintain fiber integrity and prevent matting.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth; avoid rubbing, which can embed the stain deeper.
- For tougher spots, test a gentle cleaner on an inconspicuous patch first, then proceed with a manufacturer-approved cleaner.
- Rotate the rug every few months to encourage even wear and avoid developing a path that looks suspiciously like a game track.
- Treat the rug as a living part of your decor; the moss aesthetic works best when the surrounding furniture and textiles echo its tone rather than fight against it.
