The blade is advertised as carbon steel and sized at 18 5/8 inches. ### Sheath
The sheath is part of the package and not a throwaway add-on. A good sheath makes your dagger safer and easier to carry. With a blade this long, balance is the unsung hero. The Crusader seems to be designed to strike a balance between display and utility. - Store in a dry, well-ventilated area; avoid humidity spikes that invite rust growth.
