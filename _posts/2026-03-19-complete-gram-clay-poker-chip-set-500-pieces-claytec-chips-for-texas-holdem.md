## Care and maintenance

A quick post-game ritual matters as much as a post-game recap. Clay-based chips are durable, but they aren’t indestructible. Here are a few tips to keep your 500-piece kit looking and feeling great:

- Wipe chips with a soft, dry microfiber cloth after use to remove fingerprints and dust.
- Avoid harsh solvents or abrasive cleaners; you’re not cleaning a car engine, you’re cleaning chips.
- Store the chips in their case, ideally in a cool and dry environment. Moisture can cause the edge spots to wear slightly faster.
- If you notice chips feeling sticky after a sweaty game, a light wipe with a damp cloth, followed by drying, is usually enough.

A recommended routine is to rotate the chips if you’re playing at a shared table. That ensures the color distribution remains fair and reduces the chance of an accidental all-in due to misreadability.
