### Care Tips: Keeping It Fresh Over Time

- Wipe down the rattan with a damp cloth to remove dust and pollen. For stubborn grime, a mild soap solution works well.
- Remove cushion covers if possible and launder according to label directions. If you can t wash them, spot clean and let them dry completely before reassembling.
- When not in use, keep cushions in a dry, shaded place or under a breathable cover to avoid mildew.
- Inspect the frame annually to tighten any loose hardware; a little maintenance goes a long way toward preventing wobble issues.
- For stubborn sun bleaching, consider using UV protectant spray on the rattan; test on a small area first to ensure it doesn t alter color unexpectedly.
