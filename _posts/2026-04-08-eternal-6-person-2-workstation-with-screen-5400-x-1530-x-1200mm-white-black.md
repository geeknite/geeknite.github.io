## Maintenance, durability, and life cycle
### Cleaning and upkeep
White surfaces show dirt more readily, but the finish here is forgiving enough to withstand the weekly wipe-down without becoming a maintenance nightmare. A microfiber cloth and a mild detergent should keep the panels looking fresh. For the screen and electronic components, avoid aggressive cleaning agents and rely on a soft cloth to prevent scratches. Quick tip: designate a cleaning schedule so the entire bench doesn’t transform into a mystery upgrade night where you’re trying to identify what belongs to whom.

### Durability and wear over time
The solid frame can handle day-to-day wear, but the real test comes from long-term usage. Jostling chairs, moving monitor arms, and the occasional last-minute rearrangement can put stress on joints. The Eternal bench is designed with modularity in mind, so if a component wears or a panel detaches, you can usually swap parts rather than replace the entire setup. It’s not a miracle cure for all wear, but it’s better than buying a new configuration every two years.

### Maintenance tips
- Periodically recheck all fasteners and monitor arm clamps.
- Use cable management accessories to reduce strain on ports.
- Schedule a quarterly check-in with IT to ensure firmware and software integrations stay current.
- If you plan to relocate or reconfigure, document each step so future you won’t need to call a moving company at 3 am.
