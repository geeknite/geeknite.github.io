## The Basics

### What is Warhammer Quest: Cursed City - Terrain Pack?

Warhammer Quest: Cursed City - Terrain Pack is an expansion to the popular cooperative board game Warhammer Quest: Cursed City. This pack includes various terrain pieces that add depth and realism to your game, making the cursed city come alive (well, as alive as a city plagued by undead can be).

### Components

The terrain pack comes with beautifully crafted pieces, including buildings, barricades, and various spooky elements that will make you feel like you've stepped into a gothic horror story. The level of detail is impressive, and you'll find yourself admiring the craftsmanship as much as the gameplay.

**Pro Tip**: Keep your miniatures away from your pets. They might mistake them for chew toys!

## Gameplay Enhancements

### Setting Up

Setting up the terrain pack can feel like preparing for an epic battle. Lay out the pieces carefully, and avoid stepping on any tiny barricades—they're not just painful but also essential for your strategy.

### Immersion Factor

The terrain pack adds a whole new level of immersion to the game. From crumbling walls to eerie statues, each piece contributes to the gothic atmosphere. You'll find yourself getting lost in the cursed city, even if it's just on your dining table.

### Strategic Depth

The addition of terrain pieces introduces new strategic elements. Use buildings for cover, barricades to block enemy paths, and statues as spooky landmarks. Your tactical decisions now have a tangible impact on the gameplay.

**Funny Moment**: During one game, our group used a barricade as a makeshift dance floor for a victory celebration. Let's just say it didn't end well for our miniatures.
