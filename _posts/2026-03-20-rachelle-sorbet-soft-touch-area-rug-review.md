Spills happen—the wonderful, dramatic, life-goes-on kind of spills. For a sorbet-toned rug, you’ll want to wipe up promptly to avoid staining. The care guidance from manufacturers typically suggests blotting with a clean cloth, avoiding harsh chemical cleaners, and performing a gentle vacuuming routine that doesn’t suck the life out of the fibers. ## Care Instructions Recap and Quick Tips

- Vacuum regularly with a floor setting; avoid high suction that could pull fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper.
- Use a mild detergent for stains if necessary; test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Avoid direct contact with harsh chemical cleaners; they can break down fibers and color.
- Consider a rug pad for additional cushion and to reduce shifting.
