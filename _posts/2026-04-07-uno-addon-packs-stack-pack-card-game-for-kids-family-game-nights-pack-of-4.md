- Each pack comes with its own unique twist. Some could alter the way Draw Cards work, some could modify the Wild Cards, and others might add goofy “opportunity” cards that let players do silly tasks mid-round.

## Tips and Tricks
- Start with a single pack rule and build up: It’s tempting to unleash all four twists at once, but beginners will appreciate a slower ramp. Add a new twist every few rounds to let players acclimate.
