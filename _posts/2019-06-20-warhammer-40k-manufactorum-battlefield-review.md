## What is Warhammer 40K Battlezone: Manufactorum Battlefield?

Warhammer 40K Battlezone: Manufactorum Battlefield is a terrain set designed to bring the industrial horrors of the 41st Millennium to your tabletop battles. This set features various pieces of terrain, including ruined buildings, storage tanks, and pipelines, all meticulously crafted to immerse players in the dystopian world of Warhammer 40K. Whether you're staging a skirmish between Space Marines and Orks or a full-scale invasion of Tyranids, the Manufactorum Battlefield adds depth and realism to your games.

### Components

The set includes:

- **Ruined Buildings**: Perfect for providing cover and creating strategic choke points.
- **Storage Tanks**: Great for adding height and complexity to your battlefield.
- **Pipelines**: These connect various terrain pieces and create opportunities for ambushes and sneak attacks.
- **Scatter Terrain**: Smaller pieces like barrels, crates, and barricades add detail and flavor to your setup.
