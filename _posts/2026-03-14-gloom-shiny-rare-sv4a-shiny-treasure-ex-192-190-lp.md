### Caring for Your Shine: Practical Tips

- Use high-quality sleeves and top-loaders to preserve surface integrity. Nano-scratches are the enemy of holo foils.
- Consider a binder with a dark, non-reflective background to minimize glare in your photos.
- Keep a consistent climate and avoid humid environments that can cause warping or edge curl over time.
- For UV safety, use a display cabinet with controlled lighting rather than direct sunlight.
- When photographing, experiment with light angles to capture the sparkle without washing out the art.
