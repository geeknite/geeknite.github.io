Care and Maintenance
--------------------
Like any good gadget, this rug benefits from a handshake with basic care. Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid snagging those multi-pattern fibers. If you spill something dramatic like a magically summoned beverage, address it quickly with blotting, then clean with a mild detergent diluted in water. Avoid harsh solvents; this is not a lab experiment and your rug does not appreciate being dissolved into a new color. For stubborn stains, a specialized carpet cleaner is your best friend, preferably one that’s designed for mixed fiber blends. Remember: the goal is to keep the rug looking vibrant, not to audition for a space-age carpet-cleaning commercial.
