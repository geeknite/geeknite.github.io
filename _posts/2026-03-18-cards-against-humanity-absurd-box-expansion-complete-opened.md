## How to play

The basic idea remains the same players pair white cards with black prompts to land the funniest most outrageous answer. However Absurd Box adds new formats team prompts and a broader comedic orbit. The prompts may demand collaboration improvisation or a level of cringe that only a dedicated group can sustain for long enough to finish a round.

### Round structure

- Draw a black card the prompt arrives
- Each player selects a white card the response is brewed
- The czar or judge picks the winner
- The winner keeps the black card and optionally shouts a victory phrase to celebrate

### Why it shines in a group

Because it thrives on shared context memes in jokes and the weird traditions your friend circle has developed after years of gaming. The Absurd Box excels when you have players who are comfortable with a bit of cringe and a lot of creative thinking. If your group is strict about political correctness you will likely have a very quiet game night.
