## Rules and Gameplay: What Changes, What Stays
### Setup and Turn Order
The base game loop remains intact: draw, play, and dodge a devastating kitten explosion. The Streaking Kittens expansion adds new flavor without rewriting the entire playbook. Setup takes a hair longer than vanilla, but not so long that you need a legal pad and a stopwatch. The new cards pair well with existing ones, creating moments of tension that feel earned rather than random.

### Core Mechanics Revisited
The expansion introduces several categories of cards that interplay with the core mechanics in interesting ways:
- Information and misdirection cards that let you peek or view opponents hands, enabling smarter bait-and-switch plays
- Turn-stealing and draw-modification cards that force opponents to recalibrate their risk calculations
- Quick-explosion or defuse modifiers that raise the stakes in a single clever move
These interactions create a dynamic ebb and flow where the table shifts from calm to chaotic in a heartbeat. If you enjoy the puzzle-like feel of planning a couple of moves ahead while the cat barricades the door with a laser pointer, you will savor these additions.

### New Card Interactions
Archetypes you will encounter include:
- Hand-snoopers that reveal cards before you make a move
- Card stealers that pull a random card from a rival s hand and replace it with theirs
- Defensive toggles that alter the number of stern warnings and defuses you hold
- Quick swing cards that can reset the tempo and push a near-loss into a surprising win
These interactions reward players who stay flexible and keep a few contingency plans in their back pocket. The pace of play can swing wildly, which is exactly what makes this expansion feel like a cinematic montage of near-misses and triumphs.

## Strategy and Tactics: Reading the Room and the Cat
The expansion s strength lies in its ability to introduce deception without requiring players to memorize a new language of rules. Here are practical tips to maximize fun and minimize frustration:
- Learn the new cards by name and effect. You will thank yourself when you can spot a potential combo before the round begins
- Use bluffing strategically. The text on the new cards supports bluffing rather than punishing it, encouraging social play and misdirection
- Balance risk and reward. Don t overcommit to big plays; some of the best moments come from mid-range plays that force an opponent into a slippery choice
- Track defuses. Defuses remain the currency of safety, and you can often win by building a near-impossible-to-explode profile if you conserve them carefully
A common pitfall is chasing flashier plays at the expense of solid fundamentals. The Streaking Kittens deck rewards patient planning and short-range aggression in measured doses. If your group enjoys the thrill of a risky move that pays off, you will be pleased with the cadence this expansion brings.
