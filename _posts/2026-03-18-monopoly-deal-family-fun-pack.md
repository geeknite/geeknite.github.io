## Mechanics and quick-start refresher
Monopoly Deal is a fast, high-velocity card game about property collection and rent charges. The core mechanics are simple: draw five cards (or as many as you have less one at the start), then play up to three cards per turn, which can include rent collection, card actions, or property placements. The aim is to be the first to complete three full property sets of different colors (or as defined by the house rules you’re playing with).

The Family Fun Pack injects a few twists, but the skeleton remains intact:
- Action cards let you charge rent, steal cards from opponents, or force discards.
- Money cards function as currency for rent payments.
- Property cards are grouped into color sets. Some cards may grant wild-text properties or allow you to bend the rules around color sets.
- Occasionally, special tokens and event cards alter rent values or grant temporary protections.

The play rhythm is you draw, you play, you pass turn, and you repeat. The heart of Monopoly Deal is the negotiation—how you bargain with friends and family to build your own dream property empire while nudging your enemies toward bankruptcy. The Family Fun Pack channels that social energy through thematic complications that feel like a carnival ride rather than a courtroom, which is exactly the vibe we want for family nights.
