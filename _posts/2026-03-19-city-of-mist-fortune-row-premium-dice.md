## Maintenance tips for longevity

Like any good collectible, Fortune Row benefits from a little care. Here are practical tips:

- Wipe with a microfiber cloth after each session to remove fingerprints and oils. This preserves the finish and ensures that the colors stay vibrant over time.
- Use a padded tray or soft case for transport to avoid nicks and scratches during travel. A simple foam insert can keep the dice from colliding in a backpack or luggage.
- Keep the dice away from extreme heat and direct sunlight to prevent color fading or warping in resin variants. A cool, dry place is ideal.
- If you notice minor surface smudges, a gentle wipe with water and a soft cloth is usually sufficient. Avoid harsh cleaners that can dull the finish.
