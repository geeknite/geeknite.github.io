## Strategy and tips for players
If you want to maximize your fun rather than minimize your sanity, here are some practical tips to make the most of the 20 Card Expansion Pack:

- Read the room before you act. The best players use the new cards to nudge the table toward a favorable outcome while avoiding giving away their own position.
- Save one or two defensive cards for late game drama. That way you can steal a win from the jaws of defeat with a perfectly timed shield or counter move.
- Develop a bluff persona that is consistent yet surprising. Authenticity is your secret weapon; inconsistent tells are your liability.
- Keep a mental map of who has already used defensive plays. This helps you gauge risk and decide when to push for a finish or to stall for another turn.
- Practice with friends who appreciate chaos. The expansion shines when your group leans into the banter and the playful deception that this game invites.
