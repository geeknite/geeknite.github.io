## Maintenance and care tips
To keep the Aspire J-Lite set in good condition, follow a few simple routines:

- Wipe clubs after use to remove dirt and sweat. A damp cloth is usually enough, followed by a dry towel to prevent moisture buildup on the grip.
- Inspect grips periodically for wear. If the grip begins to wear unevenly or feels slick, consider replacing the grip portion or the entire club to avoid slippage during swinging.
- Clean the bag and keep it dry when stored. Moisture can lead to mold or mildew if the bag is left in a damp corner.
- Store away from direct sunlight for long periods to preserve color, paint, and grip material.

Maintenance is not glamorous, but it saves you from the headache of a prematurely damaged set that might trigger the infamous first-swing meltdown at the range. A little care goes a long way when you’re trying to cultivate a lifelong love of sport rather than an expensive hobby that occupies the living room.
