## Caring for the pack: storage, humidity, and display

Humidity is the arch-nemesis of vintage cards. The moment moisture hits the cardboard, you risk warping and edge wear that can hamper shuffle quality. To keep your Whitman deck in primo shape, store it in a cool, dry location. If you decide to display the deck, a shallow shadow box with a glass front keeps the card skins from curling while letting guests admire the tiny art. For those who plan to play, a protective sleeve or a simple card clip can help preserve the edges during rounds with animated small hands.
