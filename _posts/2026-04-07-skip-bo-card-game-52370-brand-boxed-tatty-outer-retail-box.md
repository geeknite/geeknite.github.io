## How to care for Skip-Bo cards and keep them playable longer

Here are a few practical tips to extend the life of your deck, especially when the outer box is tatty and the inner contents are all that stand between you and a good time:

- Store in a cool, dry place: humidity and heat can warp cardboard and cause card warping. A dedicated game cabinet or a sturdy shoebox with dividers can do wonders.
- Use card sleeves if you want extra protection: great for preserving the face cards and avoiding edge wear during intense games. It’s optional but can be a lifesaver if you intend to use this set regularly.
- Shuffle with care: a respectable shuffle can minimize edge wear. For older decks, you may want to avoid aggressive riffle shuffles and lean on overhand or gentle square shuffles to extend life.
- Clean hands before play: oily fingertips can degrade card surfaces faster. A quick wipe or a glass of water on a napkin can help you stay in the game longer without the cards paying the price.
- Label and separate: if the box insert is missing or damaged, consider using containers or rubber bands to keep the deck organized. There is nothing more frustrating than hunting for a missing wild card in the middle of a heated round.
