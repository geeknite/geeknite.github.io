## What is the Starfinder: Galaxy Exploration Manual?

### Overview

The *Starfinder: Galaxy Exploration Manual* is an essential resource for players and game masters alike in the Starfinder universe. It offers comprehensive tools for creating planets, civilizations, and adventures. With this manual, you can forge new worlds, populate them with intriguing species, and set the stage for epic space opera campaigns.

### Contents

The manual is packed with:

- **World-Building Tools**: Guidelines for creating diverse planets and environments.
- **Species and Cultures**: Information on various alien species and their customs.
- **Adventure Hooks**: Ideas to inspire countless space-faring scenarios.
- **Rules and Mechanics**: Detailed explanations of the game mechanics specific to exploration and planetary interaction.
