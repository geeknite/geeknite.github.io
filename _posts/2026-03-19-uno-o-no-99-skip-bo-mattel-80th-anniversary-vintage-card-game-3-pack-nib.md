### Skip-Bo: The King of Sequencing

Skip-Bo, if you’ve never played it, is the practical calculus of card games: sort, draw, and drop cards in ascending order to empty your stock piles first. It’s approachable, deceptively cerebral, and a reliable way to remind your siblings that you learned math by stacking numbers in a line while pretending you were building a tiny tower of corporate dominance in your kitchen table. In this 3-pack, Skip-Bo anchors the set with its familiar approach to piles and discard games, giving players a satisfying sense of control even as the deck conspires to bend the space-time continuum whenever the draw pile runs low.
