Practicality and Maintenance

For tougher stains, a gentle detergent solution and a soft brush can do wonders. Spots are usually manageable with a little patience and a lot of coffee, because you know, caffeine fuels the cleanup.

Rug Pad
Pair the Kendall with a non-slip rug pad to prevent creeping. On wood floors, this helps with safety and with keeping the rug in place if your living room occasionally morphs into a mini obstacle course.

Durability is the name of the game for budget-friendly tufted rugs, and Kendall&apos;s build typically stands up to daily traffic in a normal household. Warranty varies by retailer, but most come with at least a one-year coverage for manufacturing defects, and longer for the truly lucky. If you have pets, the cut pile can show claw marks; a quick grooming with a carpet rake or a gentle brush can help restore its look, if not its heroic personality.

Care and Cleaning
- Vacuum regularly with a standard wand vacuum on a low to medium suction setting to avoid pulling fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fuzzing.
- For deeper cleaning, use a mild detergent solution or carpet cleaner designed for synthetic fibers.
- Allow to dry completely to prevent mildew; if you have seasonally humid air, you’ll want to air it out or use a dehumidifier for a day.
