Dragon Ball Super Card Game cards typically measure around 63x88 mm, with sleeves commonly sized at 66x91 mm. ## Care and maintenance tips
- Store sleeves away from direct sunlight and heat to prevent warping or discoloration.
- Keep them in a deck box or binder to minimize edge wear and to protect the artwork from scuffs.
- Clean the outer surface with a soft microfiber cloth if you notice fingerprints or smudges; avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the coating.
- Rotate decks to distribute wear evenly, especially if you’re testing multiple builds for a tournament.
