## Storage, Care, and Long-Term Value

Aesthetics and value are enhanced when you treat your cards properly. Here are practical tips for preserving BT01 rares:

- Use quality sleeves with a protective backer and a sturdy binder that supports acid-free sheets. This prevents edge wear and keeps foil cards from dulling due to mishandling.
- Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent color fade on foil artwork. Light exposure is a silent enemy of the vibrancy you paid for.
- Store in a cool, dry environment. Humidity can cause warping and binder issues over the long term.
- Consider a basic card hard case for your most valuable pulls, especially if you plan to trade or display.

If you’re thinking about investment potential, remember that the DBSCG market can swing depending on tournaments, regional popularity, and new set announcements. Rare cards from BT01 could appreciate or depreciate based on how the meta evolves and how the community values particular card effects. The prudent collector keeps a diversified portfolio of pulls—some for the shelf, some for the team, and some for the swapmeet corner of your next local event.
