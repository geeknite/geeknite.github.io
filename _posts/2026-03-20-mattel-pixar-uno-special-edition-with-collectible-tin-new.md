## Care, Maintenance, and Storage Tips
- Keep the tin in a cool, dry place. Direct sunlight can fade the art over time, and nobody wants a sun-bleached Buzz Lightyear.

- If you’ve got kids who treat UNO like a strategic battleground, you may want to remind them to hand back the cards neatly. A quick five-second ritual before packing up saves the long-term viability of the deck.
