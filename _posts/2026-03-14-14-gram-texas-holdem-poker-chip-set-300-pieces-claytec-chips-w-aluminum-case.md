## Maintenance and longevity

Maintenance is not a glamorous topic, but it matters when you want your chips to last. Claytec chips are designed to resist typical wear and tear from home usage, yet like any gaming implement, they benefit from a few routine care steps:

- Keep chips dry and away from moisture. Moisture can affect color integrity and edge care over long periods. A dry, cool room is ideal for storage.
- Wipe with a soft cloth if you notice fingerprints or smudges. Avoid chemical cleaners that could degrade the finish.
- Avoid exposure to direct sunlight for long durations since color fading can occur over months to years of use.
- Rotate stacks during long sessions to prevent edge wear in heavily used color denominations.
