A light brushing can also keep the weave looking crisp. Regular maintenance sessions help extend the life of the weave and keep it looking sharp.

## Weather Resistance and Durability
### Material and Coating Reality
Wicker sets use a resin weave that mimics natural wicker while offering resistance to moisture and sun. The frame under the weave is typically metal—aluminum or steel—coated to resist corrosion. Expect weather exposure to cause some fading over time, which is normal. The goal is to minimize rust and keep the structure solid enough to handle year after year of seasonal gatherings.

### Wind, Rain, and Seasonal Care
Patio furniture lives an outdoors life and must endure day-night cycles and occasional gusts. Secure the set if your area experiences strong winds. If you cover it during winter or heavy rain months, you will extend its life span. Allow cushions to dry fully before storage to prevent mold and mildew. A simple storage plan can be the difference between a set that feels new forever and a set that ages like a well-loved paperback.

## Maintenance and Care
### Quick-Tip Regimen
- Regularly wipe away dust and debris to keep the weave looking sharp. 
- Cover cushions when not in use to preserve color and shape. 
- Rotate cushions periodically to promote even wear. 
- Inspect the frame for loose bolts and tighten as needed. 

### Deep-Care Recommendations
A soft brush or cloth with mild soap and water does wonders. Avoid strong solvents that can degrade the finish. If you live in an area with high humidity or heavy rainfall, consider storing cushions in a dry space during extreme weather and using breathable covers to curb moisture buildup. Regular maintenance will keep the set looking fresh and ready for a marathon hosting session.
