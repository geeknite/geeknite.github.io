## Weather Resistance and Durability
### Material and Coating Reality
Wicker sets use a resin weave that mimics natural wicker while offering moisture and sun resistance. The frame beneath is typically aluminum or steel, coated to resist corrosion. Expect some fading over time, which is normal and part of the “distressed chic patio look.” The goal is to minimize rust and maintain structural integrity through year after year of seasonal entertaining.

### Wind, Rain, and Seasonal Care
Outdoor life means you’ll face wind gusts and sudden showers. Secure the set if your area experiences strong winds, and consider covers or storage during off-seasons to extend the life. Ensure cushions dry fully before storage to prevent mold and mildew. A simple storage plan can be the difference between a set that ages like a fine wine and one that ages like a well-loved paperback with a dog-eared spine.

## Maintenance and Care
### Quick-Tip Regimen
- Regularly wipe away dust and debris to keep the weave looking sharp.
- Cover cushions when not in use to preserve color and shape.
- Rotate cushions periodically to promote even wear.
- Inspect the frame for loose bolts and tighten as needed.

### Deep-Care Recommendations
A soft brush or cloth with mild soap and water works wonders. Avoid solvents that can degrade the finish. If you live in a high-humidity area or endure heavy rainfall, consider storing cushions in a dry space during extreme weather and using breathable covers to curb moisture buildup. Regular maintenance will keep the set looking fresh and ready for a marathon hosting session.
