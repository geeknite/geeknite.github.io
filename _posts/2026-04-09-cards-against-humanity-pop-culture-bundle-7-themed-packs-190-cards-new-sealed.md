## How to play with the bundle

If you already own traditional CAH, integrating Bundle 7 is straightforward: shuffle the seven new packs into your existing white/black deck or run them as a standalone mini-game for a theme night. The prompts are designed to be compatible with standard CAH rules: white cards (the answers) and black cards (the questions). A key strategy with bundle packs is to group players who love pop culture versus players who hate the current pop culture cycle. You’ll get some delightful friction as players pull cards about clout-chasing on social media or the visceral nostalgia of a 90s hero that never aged well in CGI terms.

To maximize laughs, consider these practical tips:

- Pre-pick a theme to kick off a round: There is real joy in a quick, on-the-fly setup where you announce that tonight’s theme is “movies with more explosions than sense.” The game becomes less about the cards’ raw humor and more about the players’ improv skills.
- Set a time limit for rounds: CAH can drift into indecisive territory. A crisp 60- to 90-second limit per round keeps the energy up and reduces the chance that someone will stall the game just to savor a single card’s innuendo.
- Consider a “seating ritual”: assign seats around the table to encourage bragging rights for top players who pull the best combos. It’s a small social nudge that can add a layer of friendly competition to a casual party.
