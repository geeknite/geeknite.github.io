## Gameplay and Rule Variants

Standard UNO logic is still the backbone here: match color or number to discard cards, draw from the pile if you cannot, and play special cards to disrupt opponents. The Mini Party edition adds a layer of party-oriented twists designed to quicken the pace and coax laughs from your crew.

The core gameplay remains straightforward. You play a card that matches either color or number, you say a single word to go with your action, and you hope to be the one to have the last card in hand.
