### Paint Tips and Display Ideas
- Start with a primed white base to maximize the preapplied colors that show through under different light sources.
- Use a thin glaze to re-emphasize the shadows in the face. Do not flood the miniature; it will look muddy and you will regret every paint choice that day.
- For the cloth, use a cool gray base and a slightly lighter shade for highlights to maintain readability of the design under table lighting.
- If you want a stand out display model, consider a scenic base that matches your table theme, like a ruined alley or a mausoleum floor with subtle moss.
- Seal with a matte or satin finish depending on your preference for table presence. A satin finish can evoke cinematic lighting without being overly glossy.
