## Installation Tips and Compatibility

- Form factor is M.2 2280; ensure your motherboard has space for the extra height from the heatsink. Some compact builds or laptops may not be able to accommodate it.
- PCIe 4.0 x4 interface means you unlock full speed if your motherboard supports Gen4. If you are on PCIe 3.0, you will still benefit from the drive but not the top tier speeds.
- Update firmware early and often for performance improvements and fixes.
- Keep airflow in mind. The heatsink helps, but you still want a sensible airflow plan to maximize sustained performance.
