## Care, Storage, and Display Tips

If you plan to keep this deck for years, you’ll want to treat it as a delicate artifact rather than an everyday disposable item. Here are a few practical tips:

- Storage: Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fade and stock warping. A simple plastic sleeve or a small display box with a velvet lining can keep it pristine between sessions.
- Handling: When shuffling, use a deck that’s comfortable for you. If the stock feels a bit stiff at first, give it a few sessions; like a fine wine, a well-aged deck loosens up with warm hands and seasoned practice.
- Cleaning: A light dusting with a soft brush is sufficient. Avoid using water or cleaners; you don’t want to turn your deck into a soggy Victorian pamphlet.
- Display: For collectors, a glass-topped display box or a dedicated shelf can showcase the deck without subjecting it to the daily wear-and-tear of a gaming table. The 1885 theme is as much an art piece as a functional deck, so treat it like a prized painting with cards.
