### Tips and Tricks for Getting the Most Out of It
- Start with a quick calibration to ensure the rods move smoothly and that the ball returns align properly. A little tweaking at this stage saves headaches later.

- Keep a small maintenance kit nearby: cloth, a bottle of rod oil, and a tiny hex wrench for quick adjustments. 
- Introduce a rotation system for matches to ensure everyone gets equal play time. This is not only fair but helps preserve friendships that are often tested during late night foosball marathons.
