## Display, care, and long-term keeping
Foil cards require a touch more tender loving care than standard cards. Here are a few practical tips:
- Use sleeves designed for larger card sets to reduce handling wear.
- Store upright in a case or binder with acid-free pages to preserve both color and foil integrity.
- Keep away from direct sunlight and high humidity to prevent foil desaturation and edge curling.
- If you photograph, use diffused light to minimize harsh reflections; you’ll get more even results that highlight the yellow foil without overwhelming the image.

Display ideas: a dedicated shelf with small light sources can accent the foil without turning your space into a carnival of glare. A simple acrylic display stand paired with a white backdrop often yields the cleanest photos, allowing the Krys Barnes card to do most of the talking.
