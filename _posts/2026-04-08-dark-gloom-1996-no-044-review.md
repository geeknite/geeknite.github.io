## Care, Preservation, and Display Best Practices
A card like Dark Gloom deserves a little ritual of care. Here are practical steps to ensure future generations can admire the same elegance you do today:

- Handling: Always wash hands before touching cards and avoid eating when you handle them. A greasy thumbprint is the enemy of a pristine vintage print. If you must snack, use a clean white cloth to wipe off any crumbs away from the card area.
- Sleeves and protection: Use sleeves designed for vintage cards with a cornerstone of stiffness. A solid sleeve keeps the art crisp and reduces the odds of edge wear. A rigid top loader can be a great next step for long-term display.
- Humidity and temperature: The 1990s era prints are not fans of high humidity or extreme heat. Store cards in a cool, dry place and avoid direct sunlight that could fade the foil and colors. A simple, well-lit cabinet away from windows is often enough to keep the vibe intact.
- Handling display: If you want to show off your Dark Gloom, do so in a controlled environment. A binder with clear sleeves or a small display frame can let you enjoy the card without subjecting it to every random coffee spill or pet hair catastrophe that might occur in your living room.
