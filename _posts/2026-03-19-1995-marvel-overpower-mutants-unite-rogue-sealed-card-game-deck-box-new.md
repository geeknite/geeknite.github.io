## Care, Storage, and Display Tips

### How to Store a Sealed 1995 Box in 2026

- Keep the box in a controlled environment: avoid direct sunlight, extreme heat, humidity, and the occasional family member who says, “This is just cardboard; we can store it under the bed.”
