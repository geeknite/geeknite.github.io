## The Basics

### What is Fortified Manor of The Empire?

Fortified Manor of The Empire is an expansion for the Warhammer series that introduces new structures, units, and scenarios. It enhances the base game by adding an extra layer of strategy and depth, perfect for players who love intricate tactical gameplay. The expansion comes with beautifully crafted miniatures, detailed terrain pieces, and a plethora of new rules to keep you on your toes.

### Components

The expansion includes:
- **Miniatures**: Exquisitely detailed new units that will make your battlefield look epic.
- **Terrain Pieces**: Fortified walls, towers, and buildings that add a new dimension to your battles.
- **Scenario Cards**: New missions and objectives to challenge your strategic skills.
- **Rulebook**: Comprehensive and easy to follow, even when you're knee-deep in enemy territory.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Fortified Manor of The Empire is like preparing for a grand siege. Lay out the terrain, position your units, and get ready for an epic showdown. The setup process is straightforward but detailed, ensuring that every battle feels unique and immersive.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw scenario cards and take actions based on their units' abilities. You can move, attack, defend, and use special abilities to gain the upper hand. The synergy between different units and the new fortified structures adds a layer of strategy that will keep you engaged for hours.

### Combat

Combat in Fortified Manor of The Empire is a blend of tactical planning and luck. Each unit has unique stats and abilities, making every decision crucial. Will you breach the walls with a frontal assault or sneak in through an unguarded gate? The choice is yours, but remember, the dice gods can be fickle.
