## What Causes Vertigo?

Vertigo can be triggered by various factors including inner ear issues, migraines, or even a bad case of the spins after a night out. For travelers, it's often the result of sudden changes in elevation, motion sickness, or just the overwhelming beauty of a new place.

> **Travel Tip:** Always carry a bit of ginger candy or anti-nausea medication if you're prone to vertigo.

## Vertigo Survival Guide

Traveling with vertigo requires a bit of preparation. Here are some tips:

- **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water. Dehydration can worsen vertigo.
- **Rest:** Ensure you get enough sleep. Fatigue is a vertigo's best friend.
- **Medication:** Carry prescribed vertigo medication and know its side effects.
- **Movement:** Avoid sudden movements and take it slow.
