Maintenance and Cleaning
The reality of owning a rug this size is that maintenance becomes a small but disciplined lifestyle. Vacuuming is recommended weekly to manage dust and pet hair, but you’ll want to avoid heavy agitation that can cause pilling in some polypropylene blends. If a spill occurs, the recommended technique is to blot gently with a clean cloth or paper towel—do not rub, or you risk pushing the stain deeper into the fibers. For tougher stains, a mild detergent diluted with water can be used, and you should always test the cleaning solution on an inconspicuous corner first to avoid color bleed. For pet owners, it is wise to implement a weekly rotation or a slight repositioning to prevent wear patterns from forming in highly trafficked areas. A good tip is to place a durable, flat-bottomed rug pad underneath to extend life and reduce slipping on wooden floors.
