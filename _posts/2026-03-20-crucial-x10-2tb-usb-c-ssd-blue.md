### Performance essentials
Speed matters when you’re copying large files, editing 4K video, or trying to outrun a cloud-sync nightmare. The Crucial X10 claims impressive performance for a portable SSD, so we put it through a couple of real-world tests rather than relying on synthetic numbers alone. Across these tests, the X10 delivered consistent results that feel above average for a portable SSD in this price tier. ## Quick formatting tips for your own setup
If you plan to copy this drive’s content into a fresh OS install or a clean project folder, here’s a short checklist to keep your process smooth:
1) Back up any existing data on your target drive before formatting.
2) Use a compatible file system that matches your workflow; for cross-OS use, ExFAT is often a good middle ground.
3) For Windows, enable fast startup only after the initial test boots have completed; you might want to disable it for disk health.
4) Periodically run a quick TRIM or optimization when the drive is not in heavy use.
5) Use the included utility or your preferred software for firmware updates and health monitoring if available.
