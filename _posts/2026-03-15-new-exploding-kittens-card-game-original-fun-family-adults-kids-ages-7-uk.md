## What is Exploding Kittens?
 
Exploding Kittens is a fast-paced, family-friendly card game created by Elan Lee, The Oatmeal’s Matthew Inman, and Shane Small. The core premise is simple, and that is a dangerous thing for planning humans: draw cards from a deck while avoiding the dreaded Exploding Kitten. Defuse cards can neutralize the danger; other action cards shake up the table. In other words, Russian roulette with kittens, but the kittens are surprisingly friendly about it.

## How to set up and play in a typical UK living room

1. Unbox the deck and shuffle all the cards. You will find Exploding Kitten cards, Defuse cards, and various action cards.
2. Each player draws seven cards. Yes, seven. We Brits know the value of a strong opening hand, even in chaotic games.
3. Decide who goes first. Hint: the fastest tea-maker at the table takes the first turn.
4. On your turn, draw a card. Then you may play one or more action cards and pass the turn. The goal is to avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten unless you can Defuse it.
5. If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you must Defuse it with a Defuse card. Defuse cards are limited, so you will need clever play and your enemies’ mistakes to survive.
6. When a Defuse is used, the Exploding Kitten is shuffled back into the draw pile, increasing tension as the deck grows.
7. The last player without an Exploding Kitten wins.

The rules are short and forgiving, which makes the game approachable for younger players but a playground for chaotic adult play. The real magic lies in the timing of play cards and the social pressure of calling a Nope. The Nope card, which can stop any action, is the tiny star that keeps the table gasping and laughing at the same time.

Here is a quick UK-specific tip: when you see a See the Future card, peek at the top three cards and map out a safe plan. It is not cheating, it is strategy. The See the Future card gives you glimpses of what could be coming, letting you survive long enough to savor the smug look on your friend’s face when they misplay a card and ruin themselves.
