## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

Wool is inherently resilient but not invincible. The care routine for a rug like this balances routine maintenance with occasional deep-clean rituals. Treat the Cat Eyes Blue Wool Rug with a blend of regular vacuuming, preventive care, and light spot handling when necessary. Here are practical tips you can actually use.

### Regular Cleaning Routine

- Vacuum at least once a week using a gentle setting to avoid catching the wool fibers on high-suction power. Turn the rug over occasionally to vacuum the underside; a balanced approach prevents uneven wear.
- Use a vacuum with a brush attachment that is not overly aggressive, to protect the pile from flattening in high-traffic zones.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even foot traffic and sun exposure across all areas.

### Spot Treatment and Stain Management

- For minor spills, blot gently with a clean cloth rather than rubbing, which can push the stain deeper and encourage piling.
- Use a mild wool-safe cleaner if necessary. Always test in a small, inconspicuous area before applying to a larger patch.
- For stubborn stains, consider professional cleaning rather than DIY interventions that risk color bleed or fiber damage.

### Airing and De-Pilling

- Wool rugs can benefit from occasional airouts to reduce trapped odors. If you’ve got cats who have opinions on the scent of their territory, an airy room occasionally can help.
- De-pilling is normal with wool. A gentle fabric shaver or de-pilling comb can revive the surface, helping to maintain a plush look without compromising the integrity of the fibers.

### Care for Pets

Pets are part of the rug’s life story. The Cat Eyes Blue Wool Rug is designed to withstand regular use, but you should still monitor for snagging or claw-induced wear. A protective underlay can further protect the rug from nails and keep shedding at bay on hardwood floors. If your cat loves to knead, the plushness of wool will invite it, which is part of the rug’s charm and reasons you bought it in the first place.
