## What is the Red All-Out Dice Set?
### The concept behind All-Out dice
Zombicide leans hard into cinematic action. The All-Out Dice Set captures that moment when the survivors go full tilt, charging through a horde with dramatic flair. The red dice are designed to emphasize success outcomes during aggressive, multi-action turns. In practice, these dice tilt the odds toward high-impact results — but there’s a price: higher risks or the possibility of misfires if you chase too many fireworks at once.

If you like a little crunch to your probability, the All-Out dice give you plenty to discuss as you plan your next push. The set invites two kinds of table talk: “Did I roll that or did the dice owe me money?” and “If I chain this, can I survive the next zombie wave without losing a limb or my sense of humor?” It’s a design choice that rewards bold play while reminding you that risk is a feature, not a bug.

The CMON kit markets this as a compact, color-coded upgrade. In the standard presentation, you get a stack of red dice with bold white icons ready to strike fear into the hearts of cardboard zombies. The contents include:

The red dice come with a modified face distribution designed to push players toward decisive moments. The trade-off is that misfires or critical failures loom larger when you bank too much on a single roll.
