## Cleaning and Maintenance

Maintenance is where a rug really earns its keep in the busy household. For the Satori Canvas, the goal is simple: remove dirt, treat stains promptly, and avoid soaking the rug for extended periods. Here are practical steps that align with typical consumer routines:

- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt buildup. Use a vacuum without a beater bar setting that’s too aggressive to avoid fuzzing the fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push the stain deeper into the pile.
- For most stains, a mild detergent mixed with water works well. Gently dab the area, then blot with a dry cloth to lift moisture.
- For deeper cleanings, consider professional carpet cleaning services that are compatible with synthetic rugs, or use a gentle cycle on a machine-washable rug if your model supports it. If you plan to machine wash, verify the rug’s care label and only use cold water and mild detergent.

A well-maintained rug can look fresh for years. For the ghoulie of the household, a clean rug reduces allergen buildup and makes your living space look more put-together—like you pressed the entire room into a neat schedule rather than letting it drift like a plot line in a soap opera.
