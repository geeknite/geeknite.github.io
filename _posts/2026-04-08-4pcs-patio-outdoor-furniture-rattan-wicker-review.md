## Design and build quality
### Materials
Most 4PCS wicker sets use a powder‑coated steel frame paired with synthetic PE rattan wicker. The idea is to deliver the warm, natural look of rattan without the maintenance headaches that real wicker demands (which are many and glorious, in a tragic soap opera way). The frame should feel sturdy and solid, capable of supporting adults and the occasional oversized beach tote without creaking like an old radiator.

Cushions are typically wrapped in Olefin fabric—chosen for colorfastness, UV resistance, and stain resilience. The inner foam is designed to be quick‑dry and resilient, so you aren’t left with soggy cushions after an unexpected rainstorm or a long afternoon of beach games and dramatic lounging.

The wicker weave tends to be a blend of classic curves and modern angles. If you’re a color‑theory nerd, you’ll notice how these tones play nicely with outdoor rugs, potted plants, and the glow of sunset without clashing with your neighbor’s neon flamingo statue.

### Comfort and ergonomics
Cushion thickness around 3 inches for the seats and supportive back cushions tend to strike a balance between plushness and resilience. The seating height is generally friendly for a wide range of body types, making it easy to snuggle into the corner of the sofa or stretch out an ankle on the coffee table’s edge. The wicker arms are typically smooth and rounded rather than angular, which helps when you lean in for a group chat or a spontaneous board game session.

### Weather resistance and care
Outdoor furniture should be prepared for a parade of weather: sun, rain, wind, and the occasional gust of destiny. The PE wicker is designed to resist fading from UV exposure and to hold up against humidity. Cushions with removable covers can be washed and dried, which is a nicer experience than rolling a soggy cushion back into a fixed cover after a storm.

Caretaking tips: wipe the frame with a soft cloth and mild soapy water; rinse; air dry. If your climate brings heavy rain or snowfall, consider storing cushions indoors or using breathable covers to extend life. A quick annual check of screws and connectors is sensible, especially if you have enthusiastic kids or pets who treat your patio like a playground.

### Tips for a smooth build
- Clear a generous workspace so you’re not juggling pieces on a wobbly deck.
