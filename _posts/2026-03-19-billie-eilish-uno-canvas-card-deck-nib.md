#### Authenticity and care tips

- Check the edition number and any seals inside the packaging to confirm it is indeed a limited edition unit. 
- If the deck feels too light or boxes show obvious signs of tampering, approach with caution. Counterfeit collectibles do exist, and the risk is higher when a product carries pop culture cachet. 
- Store the box in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Canvas style surfaces can fade with prolonged sun exposure, and you will want to preserve that visual impact for as long as possible. 
- Keep the cards clean and avoid harsh solvents when cleaning. A dry microfiber cloth can take care of dust while the textured surface will resist fingerprints better than gloss finishes.
