## Maintenance and Care

Like any fixed blade that isn’t stainless, the Honsoku Crusader Quillon benefits from a basic maintenance routine:

1. Wipe the blade after handling to remove oils and fingerprints.
2. Lightly oil the blade to prevent moisture-related corrosion.
3. Inspect the edge for any nicks or chips after cosplay sessions or photoshoots and seek professional sharpening if needed.
4. Store in a dry location; avoid damp basements and humid closets.
5. Keep the sheath clean and free of moisture so it protects the blade and retains its finish around the edge steel.

If you treat the blade like a co‑star in a fantasy epic, it will treat you as a responsible prop owner who ensures it stays dramatic and functional for years to come.
