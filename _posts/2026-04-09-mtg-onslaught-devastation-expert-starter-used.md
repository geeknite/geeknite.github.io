## Condition notes

When evaluating a used package, the main task is to assess the state of the most valuable cards. Are there major creases? Are the cards shuffle-friendly and not split at the seams? Is the deck still in a playable order? For collectors, the aesthetic matters: can you lift the box and claim that it looks like a relic from a more chaotic era, even if the gameplay is a tad rough around the edges?

### How to evaluate a used deck

- Check the decklist for misprints or mis-sorted cards
- Inspect the edges for whitening and nicks
- Try to shuffle to test card integrity
- Look for signs of water damage around the corners
