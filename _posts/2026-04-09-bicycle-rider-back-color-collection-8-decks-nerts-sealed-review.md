## Care, Handling, and Maintenance
To keep your eight-deck collection in prime condition, consider a few best practices. First, keep them away from moisture; humidity can affect stock and finish. Store them in a cool place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fade on the outer sleeves. When you’re not using them, re-seal the deck if possible or keep the whole set in the original packaging to preserve the seal’s integrity. During gameplay, avoid bending the edges excessively and try to prevent water spills—yes, even gamer water bottles can be dangerous to a well-curated deck. Regular light shuffles will also help the stock break in without introducing unnecessary stress that might cause warping. If you have children or enthusiastic pets around, a dedicated storage box and a practical “no touching” policy will be your best friends in preserving the set’s visual appeal.
