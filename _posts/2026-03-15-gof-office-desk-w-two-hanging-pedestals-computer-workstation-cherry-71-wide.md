If you love the zen of assembling furniture, GOF doesn’t disappoint. The instruction manual is clear enough to follow, but there are always a few moments where you’ll wonder if the cam locks are actually designed for a different product entirely and you’re just optimistic. Here’s a rough outline of the process:

1) Unpack with careful attention; don’t drop the desktop on your foot; you’ll survive, but your pride might not.
2) Attach the two hanging pedestals to the underside with the supplied screws. They align with the pre-drilled holes, which is a nice change from furniture that expects you to invent your own hardware store strategy.
3) Mount the desk surface onto the frame. If you have a friend, now is a good time to recruit a helper to avoid bending your back like a pretzel.

The assembly time will vary depending on your dexterity and whether you’ve chosen to drink coffee or tea while you work. There are a few minor alignment quirks in some builds—nothing that a gentle nudge with a rubber mallet can’t fix, but something you’ll want to check before you tighten everything down. The overall process, once you get into the rhythm, is a satisfying puzzle with a crisp, predictable end state: a desk that looks like it belongs in a showroom and performs like a workhorse.

### Cable Management: A Tidy Oasis (Mostly)

Cable management is where many desks fail spectacularly. Not GOF. The underside channels and the included clips provide a framework for the spaghetti of power bricks and HDMI cables that typically lives on your desk like a tiny, rebellious creature. We recommend planning your cable routes before you mount the desktop to minimize the shuffling that comes with “adjustments.” A little cable sauce—zip ties or twist ties—will help you create a clean silhouette rather than a chaotic sculpture. In practice, you’ll still see a couple of cords peeking out, which is fine—real world desks are never perfectly tidy; that would be boring. The trick is to keep the visible cables in check while the rest of the system hums along in quiet, efficient fashion.
