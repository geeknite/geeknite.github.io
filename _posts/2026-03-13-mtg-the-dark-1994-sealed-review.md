## Authenticity and Packaging: How to Check a Real 1994 The Dark Booster
With vintage boosters, you want to avoid replicas and mis-packaged fakes. Here are some practical checks:

- Look for the old style wrapper, typically a simple plastic wrap with a paper insert. If the packaging looks suspiciously modern or the printing looks off, approach with caution.
- Examine the card stock and gloss of the cards inside. Real The Dark cards from 1994 have a weight and finish that feels different from modern reprints; if you can compare with an authenticated card, you will get a sense for the difference.
- Check the card backs and border color. The Dark features a distinctive dark border that matches other early sets; if you find a pack that looks too perfect, you might be staring at a shiny but non vintage spoof.
