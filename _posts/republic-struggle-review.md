## What is Republic Struggle?

Republic Struggle is a strategy board game where players take on the roles of leaders of different republics. The game incorporates diplomacy, warfare, and resource management, allowing players to experience the complexities of historical conflicts. Will your republic rise to glory or crumble under the weight of political intrigue? The choice is yours.

### The Basics

Each player controls a republic with unique abilities and strengths. The game involves managing resources, negotiating with other players, and engaging in tactical warfare. The goal is to achieve supremacy through a combination of strategic planning and cunning diplomacy.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed miniatures representing the leaders and armies of different republics.
- **Cards**: A deck of cards for each republic, showcasing their abilities, actions, and special powers.
- **Game Board**: A beautifully illustrated board depicting the map of the republics and their territories.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track resources, influence, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Hide your snacks well; the political intrigue may lead to some snack-nabbing among players.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Republic Struggle is like preparing for a grand historical reenactment. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your republic. Now you're ready to embark on your strategic quest.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their republic's abilities. You can gather resources, negotiate alliances, launch attacks, and more. Cooperation is key, but beware—political backstabbing is a common occurrence.

### Combat

Combat in Republic Struggle is a blend of strategy and luck. Each republic has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic invasion just to have your ally betray you? Welcome to the chaotic world of republic politics.
