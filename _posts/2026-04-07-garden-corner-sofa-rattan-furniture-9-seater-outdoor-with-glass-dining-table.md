### Maintenance that won’t ruin your weekend
Cushions: removable covers should be machine-washable. If you’re like me and you forget to close the zipper before the washing machine, consider a spare set. Cushions drying indoors while the rain passes is a solid strategy to keep them fresh. Rattan care is simple: a gentle wipe with a damp cloth every so often, and a light oiling or UV protectant if you live where the sun behaves like a celebrity spending the summer hiatus in your sun-kissed yard. The glass top wants a microfiber cloth and some glass cleaner weekly, especially during pollen season, or after you decide to host a brunch where the orange juice somehow ends up everywhere.

### Safety and warranties
Most sets of this scale offer a warranty on the frame and a separate warranty for fabric. Read the fine print for cushion covers and zipper wear. The product’s safety hinges on stable assembly and ensuring that the glass is securely placed. If you have small kids or pets, consider corner guards for sharp edges and a plan to cover the glass during heavy rain to prevent debris from turning your glass table into a modern art sculpture.
