### Color, Texture, and Light Play

The colorway leans toward charcoal and ash with flecks that catch the light differently as the sun arcs across your floor. ## Maintenance and Longevity

Maintenance is pleasantly straightforward. Regular vacuuming on low suction keeps dirt at bay. A weekly sweep is enough for most living spaces; if your home doubles as an art studio for you and your kids, you might want to do a more frequent pass around high-traffic zones. Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to keep the color from graying due to sun exposure in a single hot spot.

Stains? Treat them promptly with a mild detergent and a damp cloth. Avoid bleach-based cleaners, which will not suddenly transform the rug into the fabric of myth; they will simply dull the color and leave you with foul memories of ruined afternoon plans. For deep cleaning, consider professional cleaning every 12–18 months depending on traffic. It is not a couture gown; it is a rug, but it benefits from occasional pampering.

Edge integrity remains solid with regular use. We did not observe fraying or curling at the edges in our tests, which means you will not be chasing loose threads with a needle while sipping coffee and pretending to be a responsible adult.
