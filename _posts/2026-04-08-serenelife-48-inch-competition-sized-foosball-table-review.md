## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity: Keeping the Table in Peak Form
Like any good gadget, this foosball table rewards a little upkeep. The maintenance is straightforward and won’t demand you jump through hoops or spend your weekend performing ritual maintenance ceremonies.

### Cleaning and Care
- Wipe the playing surface with a soft microfiber cloth after heavy use to remove fingerprints and smudges. Use a mild cleaner if needed, but steer clear of harsh chemicals that could degrade the surface.
- Lubricate the rods periodically with a light silicone-based lubricant. Do not overdo it; a little goes a long way. Excess oil can attract dust and make the playing surface sticky, which is not ideal for the ball’s acceleration or your sneakers.
- Check the leg braces occasionally for looseness. Tighten as needed to preserve stability. A wobbly table is a bad vibe, and vibe matters in the foosball universe.
- Inspect the cup holders to ensure they remain firmly attached. A loose cup holder is the kind of problem that creates chaos during a dramatic last-second goal.

### Longevity Expectations
