## What is 7TV - Heroes 2?

7TV - Heroes 2 is a skirmish miniatures game that lets players recreate scenes from their favorite action TV shows. Imagine directing your own episodes where heroes clash with villains, epic battles unfold, and everything goes hilariously wrong. The game combines strategy, nostalgia, and a dash of humor to create an experience that will keep you and your friends entertained for hours.

### The Basics

In 7TV - Heroes 2, players form teams of heroes and villains, each with unique abilities and stats. The goal is to complete objectives while sabotaging your opponents with clever strategies and unexpected plot twists. The game is designed to mimic the feel of a TV show, complete with episodes, cliffhangers, and dramatic moments.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Quirky and detailed miniatures representing your favorite TV characters. You'll want to display these proudly on your shelf.
- **Cards**: Each character and action has corresponding cards that add flavor and strategy to the game.
- **Game Board**: Various locations where your episodes take place. From secret lairs to bustling city streets, the settings are as dynamic as your imagination.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track progress, health, and various effects during the game.

**Pro Tip**: Keep the miniatures away from your pet. Those claws are the real villains.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up 7TV - Heroes 2 is like preparing for a TV show marathon. Lay out the game board, distribute cards and miniatures, and choose your heroes and villains. Grab some snacks and you're ready for action.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and take actions based on their characters' abilities. You can move, attack, rescue civilians, and more. Cooperation and strategizing with your teammates are key to executing epic scenes and surviving the chaos.

### Combat

Combat in 7TV - Heroes 2 is a blend of strategy and luck. Each character has unique mechanics that add challenges and twists to the game. Did you plan an epic battle just to have your hero trip over their own feet? Welcome to the world of TV show drama.
