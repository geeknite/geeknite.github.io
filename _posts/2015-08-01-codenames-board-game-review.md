## The Basics

### What is Codenames?

### Components

The game includes a deck of word cards, a series of key cards that show the layout of the words, and some innocent bystander cards (because every secret mission needs some collateral damage, right?). The artwork is simple yet effective, and the high-quality components ensure the game will survive many a heated game night.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setup is straightforward and quick. Place the word cards on the table in a 5x5 grid, select a key card, and give each team their innocent bystander cards. The spymasters sit on one side of the table and their teammates on the other. Now, you're ready to dive into the world of secret codes and hidden meanings.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, the spymaster gives a one-word clue and a number indicating how many words on the board are related to that clue. The teammates then discuss and guess the words they think are correct. If they guess a word correctly, they cover it with their team's card. If they guess incorrectly, they cover it with an innocent bystander card (or worse, the assassin card).

### Strategy and Wordplay

The game shines in its strategic depth and clever wordplay. As the spymaster, you must carefully choose your clues to connect multiple words while avoiding the assassin. For the teammates, communication and deduction are key. You'll need to think creatively and anticipate your spymaster's thought process to succeed.

### Replayability
