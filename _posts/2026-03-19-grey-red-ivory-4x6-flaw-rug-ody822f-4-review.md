## Care, maintenance, and practical life hacks
Caring for a rug is not glamorous, but it is essential if you want to preserve the color balance and weave integrity over time.

### Cleaning basics
Regular vacuuming with a gentle setting is advisable to prevent fiber crush. For spills, quick blotting with a soft cloth is a no brainer. Avoid harsh chemicals and bleach; check stain removal guidelines for the fiber blend if you go fancy with clean up. If you fear lingering odors, consider a light application of a gentle carpet cleaner, testing on a small inconspicuous area first to ensure no color bleed occurs.

### Rug pads and floor protection
A quality rug pad helps prevent slippage, provides a buffer against chair legs, and can extend the rug life by reducing wear on the fibers. In homes with active pets and children, an anti slip pad plus regular rotation of the rug can help distribute wear evenly and maintain the overall look for longer.

### Long term durability tips
Rotate the rug every few months to ensure color fading and wear patterns distribute evenly. Avoid prolonged direct sunlight that can affect color depth. Periodic professional cleaning every couple of years can refresh the pile and extend the rug's life beyond basic household care. A good approach is to treat the rug as a dynamic element of your space, not as a static artifact waiting to be polished once in a blue moon.
