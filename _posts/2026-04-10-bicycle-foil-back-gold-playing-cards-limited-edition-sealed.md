## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity: Keeping the Gold Shiny
A foil deck demands a little extra respect. Here are practical tips to keep the gold looking legendary for as long as possible:
- Keep it dry. Water and foil finishes do not mix well with the long-term romance of humidity and fog machines.
- Avoid oils. Finger oils will dull the shine and can create a fingerprint gallery that your audience will notice during reigns of awe.
- Use a proper case. A protective sleeve or a display box can prevent scratching when the deck is not in use.
- Don’t over-handles. The more you grip, the more you risk scuffing the foil. Practice those fans with a light touch.

If you are a meticulous collector, you might also time a gentle application of a card-safe dusting cloth before storage. It’s not glamorous, but it helps in the long run. For those who love to experiment, try to keep one deck sealed and one deck in rotation to separate display value from playability.
