## Practical considerations: care, maintenance, and life after checkout
### Cleaning and maintenance
Rugs with bold color work best with simple maintenance routines. A quick vacuum on low height and a periodic rotation to even out wear can keep the colors vibrant. If spills happen, act fast: blot, don’t rub, and consider a stain repellent spray designed for synthetic fibers. The goal is to maintain the “new rug” vibe for as long as possible before the novelty wears off and you start stepping on it with oven mitts just to pretend you’re a museum curator examining a modern tapestry.

### Durability and living-with-flaws
A flaw that is cosmetic often integrates seamlessly with daily life, becoming part of the rug’s personality. If the flaw is at the edge or a corner, it might not be a problem in an open room where the edge is less visible.
