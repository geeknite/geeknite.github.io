## Custom size versus 8x10 options

This is where the Moonstone Mink decision tree gets interesting. You can opt for a custom size or the standard 8x10. Each path has its own advantages, and the choice largely depends on room geometry, budget, and how much drama you want to create with floor space.

### Custom size

Custom sizing is the big flex move. If your room has unusual dimensions — a long narrow hallway, a sunken living area, or a corner full of quirky furniture — a custom size can maximize coverage and reduce awkward leftover floor space. The upside is precise fit, better anchoring of furniture, and the ability to protect high traffic zones without compromising on style. The downsides include a longer lead time, potential for higher cost, and the need to measure like a surgical planner to avoid ordering something that feels off once it arrives.

To order a custom size, you typically start with a room measurement pass. A good approach is to map the rug via three anchors: under the main seating group, under the coffee table footprint, and a comfort margin around the edges so the rug feels intentional rather than tacked on. The process often involves confirming width, length, and edge finish preferences, as well as the backing option. ### 8x10 as a trusty standard

The 8x10 is the workhorse of area rugs, a size that commonly fits most living rooms and medium sized bedrooms with a confident presence. It provides ample surface for furniture grouping without swallowing the entire floor. If you are chasing a balanced look where the rug sits under the front legs of a sofa or under a coffee table cluster, 8x10 is often a reliable choice. In many homes, it becomes the anchor point for decor stories — a place where you set down a lamp, a plant, and perhaps a stack of design magazines that you will pretend you will read someday.

If your space is smaller, you might look at 5x8 or 6x9 variants, which can be equally appealing but may require tighter furniture placement to avoid looking floaty. Moonstone Mink works well in this size too, provided you adjust the surrounding furniture to maintain proportion. ## Durability and maintenance

Synthetic rugs have their own flavor of durability compared to natural fiber options. Moonstone Mink is typically designed to resist staining and to recover its shape after you move a sofa or dance into the room with an impressive stride. The key maintenance moves are simple:

- Vacuum regularly on a low suction setting to lift fibers and prevent matting.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber damage.
- For stubborn stains, a mild solution of water and a tiny amount of gentle detergent can help. Always test on a small inconspicuous area first.
- Avoid high heat or aggressive chemical cleaners that can degrade synthetic fibers over time.

A note on longevity: you are buying a rug that is designed to feel premium while staying wallet friendly. With proper care, it should remain comfortable and visually appealing for several years. If you want to extend life further, place a quality rug pad underneath to reduce wear and keep the rug flat on glossy floor finishes.
