### Care, Storage, and Display Tips for Your Gold-Laden Loot
- Store upright and away from direct sunlight to minimize foil glare and potential warping.
- Use a dedicated box or insert to prevent heavy cards from pressing into the foil surfaces.
- Wipe gently with a dry microfiber cloth if fingerprints accumulate; avoid liquids on foil surfaces.
- If you plan to display, a low-traffic shelf with indirect lighting can showcase the foil without triggering glare fatigue for players.
- For those who want to keep pages of foil pristine, consider a clear card sleeve made for standard UNO cards and then place the whole deck in a rigid storage box. This might not be stock-standard, but it’s a practical approach for collectors.
