### Care, Quick Tips, and Nerd‑Approved Practices
- Vacuum regularly; avoid harsh suction on the edges. A gentle pass keeps the fibers happy without loosening the weave.
- Treat stains promptly with a mild detergent solution. Blot, don’t rub; we’re not erasing the past, we’re cleaning the future.
- Use a rug pad for stability and longevity. It reduces movement and extends the rug’s life under chairs and gaming wheels.
- Rotate every few months to distribute wear evenly; this helps prevent a sunburn line or foot traffic pattern from forming in your favorite gaming zone.
- If you have pets, keep nail trims in check to minimize snags. Your rug will thank you in the long run.
