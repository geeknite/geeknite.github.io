## Maintenance, Cleaning, and Longevity

### Routine Care
- Vacuum high-traffic areas at least twice a week.
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear patterns.
- Use a rug pad to prevent slipping and extend life.

### Stain Management
For common spills (coffee, wine, mysterious sauces), blot gently with a clean cloth and mild soap solution. Avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper and damage the pile. If a stain persists, test a small inconspicuous area first and consider a dedicated upholstery cleaner suitable for synthetic blends.

### Longevity and Replacement Cycles
A well-maintained rug in this category can last several years, especially with careful care. The bold pattern helps disguise minor wear as it develops, which is a nice feature for high-traffic living areas. If you expect to redecorate every 2-3 years, this rug still makes sense because its color and pattern age gracefully and remain stylish longer than you might fear.
