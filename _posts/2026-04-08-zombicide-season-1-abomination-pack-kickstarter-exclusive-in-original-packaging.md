## Display, storage, and care tips

If you plan to display the Abomination Pack, consider a few practical approaches:
- Use acrylic display cubes for each figure to protect from dust while allowing intense lighting to highlight the sculpt accents
- Mount the larger minions on a shallow display base to keep them level and well-proportioned on shelves
- Consider a climate-controlled display environment; resin and certain plastics can react poorly to humidity and temperature swings over long periods
- Keep the original packaging in a protective sleeve or outer box for archival purposes if you want to preserve the box illustration and insert artwork

For game-night storage, a dedicated tub with labeled compartments works wonders. Use padding to prevent movement during transport and add a small, soft foam liner to cushion the big figures. If you’re transporting multiple big items, you could even create a modular foam tray insert that stacks neatly in your carry case. The goal is to avoid scuffs, bent limbs, and the dreaded tray migration during transit—the horror of watching an abomination shift mid-car ride is truly an experience you don’t want to relive.
