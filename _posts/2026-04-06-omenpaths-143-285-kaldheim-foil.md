## Pricing, storage, and care
### How to store your foil gem
Moisture control matters, even for MTG cards. For foil cards, humidity and temperature swings can impact the surface gloss and edging over time. A good binder with separate sleeves and a climate-controlled room will extend the life of the foil sheen and keep the corners sharp. Consider using top-loading sleeves for extra protection if you own more than a couple of these beauties.

### Handling tips for long-term radiance
Handle the foil with clean hands, avoid slack in the sleeve, and keep it away from abrasive surfaces that might scratch the surface or dull the shimmer. If you plan to run it in a deck, make sure you sleeve it with care, as repeated slides in and out of binders can cause micro-scratches that detract from the foil’s intended magic. A small micro-ritual of gentle handling can preserve the shine for years of display and occasional play.
