## How the game is played: quick refresher

### The basics
Family Feud-style games revolve around survey questions and top-answer lists. Two teams face off, trying to guess the most popular responses to prompts like “Name something you do before going to bed” or “Something you can’t spell without a few vowels.” Players buzz in when they think they know an answer. Points are awarded based on the popularity of the answer, with the top answers carrying the most weight. The Platinum Signature Game mix keeps the classic feel but pumps up the production value with a durable board, crisp buzzers, and a few modern conveniences.

### The Brown Edition rules twist
The brown edition doesn’t ditch the core rules; it enhances them with a few quality-of-life touches:
