## Practical tips for game night

To maximize the fun, consider these steps:

- Decide on a rotation order or designate a reader so that the same players do not dominate every round.
- Set a few ground rules about content before you begin and remind players who may be sensitive about certain topics.
- Have a backup activity ready for when the laughter becomes too intense or when the table needs a quick rest.
- Use a timer to maintain pace. A round that lasts too long can kill momentum; a quick round keeps energy high.
- Keep drinks and snacks handy but do not place the snacks on the game area; the last thing you want is a spilled drink on a stack of cards.
- If you are playing with a group that includes non-English speakers or players who struggle with certain references, provide a short glossary or indicate that some prompts may require time to process.
