## What is Codex Card Time Strategy?
Codex Card Time Strategy CCTS is a card-drafting, time-manipulation euro-style game where players attempt to control outcomes by playing time as a resource. The core idea: players accumulate time shards and spend them to rewind or accelerate turns, enabling you to chain combos that look like chaotic origami but function with the grace of a well-timed web-slinging punchline. The Deluxe Set expands the base game with premium components, extra scenarios, and a more complex time-tuning toolkit. The vibe is pulp sci-fi meets steampunk, with a dash of mystical codex lore that makes the flavor text feel like a secret handshake.

## Core mechanics

- Time as resource: Each turn you allocate time shards to actions; certain cards let you rewind to earlier states, others accelerate to future states, it's like a very polite time heist.

- Card drafting: At the start of each round, players choose cards from a display, building a fragile engine of synergy. The card pool evolves as time shards shift the order of play, making planning feel existential.

- Victory conditions: Points are earned by completing temporal objectives, achieving chain combos, and meeting scenario-specific secrets.

- Table presence: The Deluxe Set brings more tokens, a larger insert tray, and thick cardstock that refuses to warp under mild humidity. The cards have linen finish that feels like a whispered secret.

## Deluxe Set components

- Cards: Premium poker-sized cards with foil accents in some variants, a color palette that looks like someone spilled a rainbow into a time machine.

- Time tokens and trackers: The deluxe set includes crystal-like tokens for time shards and an elaborate track to monitor the flow of time across rounds.

## Gameplay deep dive

### Time manipulation mechanics

- Rewind and Fast-Forward: Players can rewind to a previous state, or fast-forward to the next phase, but with cost. This creates a very dynamic turn structure where you think ahead, but sometimes you realize your plan is a fragile house of cards.

- Temporal Chains: The game rewards players for chaining time actions in a sequence. The synergy between card effects can produce a glorious cascade of outcomes; if you pull it off, you feel like you just solved a puzzle while juggling fire.

### Card drafting and time synergy

- Drafting phase: The display offers a set of cards with varied time costs. You weigh short-term gains against long-term setup.

- Time synergy: Some cards interact with time tokens to create combos that feel wonderfully elegant and sometimes hilariously chaotic.

- Theme clarity: The deluxe components help convey the idea that you are manipulating time, even if the underlying math is a little abstract.

- Example: A typical turn might involve drafting a time-shift card, paying with shards, and then triggering a sequence of events that unlocks future scoring opportunities.

- The risk: If you overspend on time, you might be locked out of critical actions later. The tension is real, and the moment you step into the final phase is when you realize you built your own paradox.

### Victory and scoring

- Objectives: Each scenario provides a unique set of objectives; some revolve around aligning time streams, others require collecting specific codex entries.

- Score tracks: The deluxe set's scoring track is robust; you may need to watch the track like a clockmaker watches a precision instrument.

## Sleeper tips and strategies

- Start with a flexible engine: Build a core engine that can adapt to tempo shifts; you will regret a hyper-specific plan when the time tokens do a twist.

- Manage your time shards: Don t hoard too much; you need to spend to unlock actions but you don t want to burn all time early.

- Keep an eye on the scenario objective: It s easy to chase a flashy combo and miss the victory condition.

- Use rewind strategically: Rewinding a wrong turn can undo a bad mistake; use it to maximize the efficiency of your chain.

- Think in sequences: The best turns come from chaining multiple actions in a planned sequence that uses time to create a feedback loop of scoring.
