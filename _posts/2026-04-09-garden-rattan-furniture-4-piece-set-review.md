## Assembly and maintenance: how hard is it, really?

Let’s be honest: any furniture that ships in a box demands at least a few screws, an Allen wrench, and a moment of existential crisis about whether you’ll ever finish. The Garden Rattan 4 Piece Set is a fairly standard assembly job that you can confidently complete in about 60–90 minutes if you’re methodical and not distracted by a streaming service or the scent of freshly baked cookies. The instruction manual is concise, with labeled parts and a clear torque guidance, which helps prevent overtightening that could crack the synthetic wicker. Pro tip: lay out bolts in the order you’ll use them and pre-thread the smaller screws into the provided washers to avoid gnashing your teeth in frustration.

Maintenance-wise, the set is a breeze. Wipe down the wicker with a damp cloth occasionally to remove dust and pollen. If you spill something, soap and water do the trick; avoid harsh chemicals that could degrade the wicker fibers. Cushions are removable and machine-washable for quick cleanup. For heavy stains or mildew after a long winter, a gentle fabric-safe cleaner does wonders. If you’re worried about colorfastness, consider applying a light fabric protector on the cushion covers before the season kicks off.

A few practical tips to keep things feeling fresh over the years:
- Use cushions with removable covers and store them when not in use for extended periods.
- Consider using protective covers during storms or high-wind periods.
- Rotate cushions to prevent uneven wear and keep colors balanced.
- Check the legs occasionally for wobble and tighten if necessary.
