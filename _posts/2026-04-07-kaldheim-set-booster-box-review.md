## How to Approach Sealed Play with Kaldheim Set Boosters
Sealed play is a social sport with rules and rituals. Here’s a quick, practical guide for maximizing your experience without losing your mind:

### Sorting Strategy
- Separate the rares and mythics first, then the foils, then the rest. It’s like doing laundry: you want the valuable pieces to emerge clean and ready to sleeve.
- Build around the best synergy your pulls offer. Kaldheim’s Norse vibes reward cohesive theme-building, but you’ll often end up with a mix of colors. The fun lies in discovering a workable structure that doesn’t scream “we forgot to run a mana base.”

### Draft vs Set Booster Mindset
If you’re playing sealed with a group, Set Booster packs offer a more curated experience than pure draft boosters. You may have a stronger chance of pulling a lead card for a deck, but you’ll also encounter the occasional “this is not synergy” moment that will inspire you to either improvise or retreat to your notes app for a strategic pivot.

### Deck and Play Testing
Even if your goal is purely display, setting up a casual two-player match is a good idea. Play some games, log your results, and identify which cards were unexpectedly useful and which were outclassed by the snow-covered flavor. You might discover a card you’d previously overlooked becomes the backbone of a quirky deck with a fun gimmick that makes future games feel like an in-joke between you and the game itself.
