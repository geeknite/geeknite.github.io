### The Foil Quality: Do the Shrink-Wraps Jingle in the Night?
Borderless foils can be a little more brittle or more robust depending on the factory and the year. In NM (near mint) examples, you shouldn’t expect edge dents or heavy scuff marks. The surface should reflect light with a clean, rainbow-like sheen rather than a dull mirror finish. If you’re comparing two NM borderless foils, you’ll notice subtle differences in how the light interacts with the artwork—the card looks expensive in the way a high-end action figure looks expensive: more designed, less printed, more “I could stare at this for hours and pretend I’m in a sunlit temple.”

## The Aesthetic Utility: Storage, Display, and Handling
If you’re the sort of collector who enjoys displaying cards as part of your hobby, borderless foils are the apiary of attention: people will hover, lean in, and say, wow, that reflection looks like a tiny sun in a bottle. For practical handling:

- Sleeving: Use a high-clarity inner sleeve and a top-loading outer sleeve for display or safe transport. Foils shine brighter behind semi-opaque sleeves because you can watch the light do its dance without micro-scratches stealing the spotlight.
- Storage: Keep them in a temperature-stable environment; foils can become warped if exposed to heat or humidity over long periods. A climate-controlled binder is your friend.
- Handling: Hold by the edges, avoid touching the surface, and don’t forget to thank the card for existing in your life. It’s polite to appreciate the sun’s generosity, even if the sun is a fictional card game symbol.

## Care and Handling: Practical Guide for Your Elspeth Gem
- Protection first: A borderless foil deserves the best. Use a sturdy binder, slip the card into a protective sleeve, and consider a top-loader if you’re transporting it to a game night.
- Environment: Keep away from direct sunlight for extended periods. Foil colors are bright when you want them, but prolonged sun exposure can fade the glow and leave you with a wistful memory in the corners of the art.
- Cleaning: If fingerprints appear, a clean, dry microfiber cloth is your friend. Don’t scrub aggressively; the surface is delicate, and you don’t want to accidentally turn a price sticker into a permanent etching.
- Grading: If you’re thinking of getting this reviewed by a grader, provide clear pictures in good lighting; borderless foils are judged similarly to other foils, but any edge warranty may be stricter due to borderless corners.
