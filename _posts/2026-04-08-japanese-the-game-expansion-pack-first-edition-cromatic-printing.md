Mechanics and how the expansion changes the game
-------------------------------------------------
The core thrill of The Game is linear card management under strict constraints and minimal information sharing. The expansion pack introduces three modular rule tweaks that you can mix and match, like spice varieties for a dish you’re not sure you actually want to eat but are determined to enjoy because your friends are watching you pretend to be an adult. Here’s a quick look at the three modules you’re likely to encounter in this Cromatic edition:

### Module A: Color Cascade
This module adds a color-driven cascade effect where certain color discard sequences trigger cascading penalties if you misorder. It forces players to coordinate time windows—the moment to discard a particular color becomes as critical as choosing a window seat on a long, loud bus ride. The color cues are bright enough to be deciphered at a glance but subtle enough that you aren’t just reading a map of every potential disaster.

### Module B: Hidden Hands
A clever twist that introduces hidden hand mechanics. You’ll clue players in on how many cards you hold of a given color or range, but not which exact numbers. This module invites the group to deduce collective memory and read subtle tells. It feels like a party game and a strategy game had a polite, slightly grumpy baby.

### Module C: Chromatic Endgame
The expansion’s grand finale module shifts the pacing, forcing a blurted sprint toward a risky finish. It’s the color-version of a fire drill with glitter. Endgame pacing becomes more volatile, and the satisfaction of pulling off a well-timed discard becomes deliciously intoxicating.

Module highlights and tactical tips

- In Color Cascade, timing is king. If you’re late to the cascade, you risk more penalties. Communicate non-verbally with your table by pacing your discards to create a rhythm. The payoff of pulling off a cascade cleanly feels earned and deliciously merciful to your tired brain.
- In Hidden Hands, you’ll want to pay attention to others’ discards as you glean what remains in the deck. It’s a puzzle about perception and trust. You’ll want to minimize reckless plays, but you’ll also want to seize opportunities when patterns align.
- Chromatic Endgame rewards bold, well-coordinated risk-taking. Don’t fear the last few cards; instead, trust the chain you’ve built with your teammates and go for the finish when the timing is right. This is where a little bravado, properly timed, can become a heroic moment that earns you a toast and a high-five.
