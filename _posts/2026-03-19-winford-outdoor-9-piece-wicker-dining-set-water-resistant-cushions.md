## Practicality: Assembly, Cleaning, and Everyday Life
### Cleaning and Care
- Wicker: Dust with a soft brush or microfiber. Wipe with a damp cloth as needed.
- Cushions: If removable, machine-wash the covers according to the label. If not, spot-clean with a mild detergent and water. Let air dry completely before reattaching or using.
- Table: Wipe with a mild cleaner; avoid abrasive products that can dull the finish.
- Weather: Use a furniture cover during extreme weather; store cushions indoors during heavy rain or snow if possible.
