## Care and longevity: how to keep this floor model in top form

- Clean after use: wipe the blade and handle to remove moisture and residue.
- Dry and oil: a light oil coating prevents rust on carbon steel.
- Store properly: keep it in a dry environment; don’t leave it in a damp corner of your garage.
- Sharpen as needed: carbon steel holds an edge well, but you’ll want to touch it up with a sharpening stone or a guided system after heavy use.
- Age gracefully: if you decide to display it, consider a dedicated display case with controlled humidity to preserve that showroom charm.
