## What is Exploding Kittens Family Edition

The Family Edition is designed for families who want a fast paced chaotic vibe without mature content. It uses the same deck mechanic of drawing cards until an exploding kitten appears, unless a defuse card removes the risk. The main differences lie in text, art style, and a rules sheet written for clarity and speed. The result is a game that teaches the concept quickly, plays in about 15 to 25 minutes, and leaves players eager for a rematch.

### How to set up and play in a few minutes
Setup takes only a few minutes and you can be playing before the next delivery person arrives with snacks. The turn structure is simple: on your turn you may play an action card or draw a card. The goal remains to avoid drawing the exploding kitten, unless you can deploy a defuse at the last second. Defuse cards are the ultimate safety net but you may not always have them in hand, which start the real table talk.

### Card types and how they feel in practice
- Exploding Kitten cards present the hazard that drives the tension of the game.
- Defuse cards let you defuse an exploding kitten and offer a chance to put the kitten back into the deck in a safe way.
- Action cards such as See the Future, Attack, Shuffle, and Skip manipulate the draw order and the flow of the round.
- Cat Cards add flavor and sometimes grant extra moves or clever play options.
- Counter or Nope style cards allow you to push back on the effects of other players, creating dramatic moments at the table.

### See the Future and simple strategy
See the Future cards let you peek at the top portion of the draw pile. This helps you plan risk versus reward, but do not forget the social aspect. The Family Edition rewards clever play that keeps the table engaged and avoids locking anyone out with a big power play.
