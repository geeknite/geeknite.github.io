### Cleaning and care routines

Maintenance is straightforward: vacuum regularly to remove dust and crumbs, with a quick spot clean for spills. The woven construction helps with resistance to wear, and the stripes hide minor discolorations that might emerge over time. For spills, blot rather than rub to prevent fiber distortion, then use a mild cleaning solution suitable for synthetic and natural blends. If you prefer a deeper refresh, rotating the rug periodically can help it wear evenly and avoid traffic pattern wearing in the same spots. A rug pad is recommended for improved grip and longevity, especially on slick floors; it also reduces slippage under chairs, preventing scuffs on the floor beneath.
