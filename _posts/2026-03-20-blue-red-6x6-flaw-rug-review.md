- Vacuum regularly with a gentle approach to avoid pulling on the flaw’s delicate sense of drama.

- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to avoid the color block forming a “center-of-the-room spotlight.”
- Use a rug pad to reduce curling and add extra cushioning underfoot, because your feet deserve a spa day too.
