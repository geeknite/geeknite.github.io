## How It Plays: A Vintage Rummy Experience
Rummy is a classic game of set-building and memory. Animal Rummy 4493 adds a thematic twist that makes it approachable to families and appealing to older players who love a good retro vibe.

### Setup and Flow
To begin, deal a number of cards to each player (the exact number depends on how you decide to set the game). The rest of the deck becomes a draw pile, with the top card placed face up to start the discard pile. Players take turns drawing from the stock or discard pile, then discarding unwanted cards to approach the goal: form valid melds that include runs and sets, while minimizing your deadwood. The first player to empty their hand wins or set a target score if you are playing a longer session.

### Core Rules with a Thematic Twist
The Animal Rummy twist lies in how you interpret suits and values. The animal icons help players remember the relative strength of cards, and you may decide to grant special bonuses to certain animal runs or to use the Tiger as a wild card with limited usage. The rules are simple enough for kids yet flexible enough to incorporate more complex strategies for adults who want a more challenging game. It is the kind of design that rewards practice and pattern recognition rather than brute force.

### Game Variants
If you want to spice things up, you can add variants such as:
- Wild Animal: The Tiger acts as a wild card that can substitute for any card in a meld, with some limitations
- Bonus Runs: Certain sequences of cards yield extra points or allow special actions
- Memory Challenge: After each round, players recall the cards drawn or discarded to score bonus points

Combine these variants with the original rules and the nostalgia skyrockets.
