### Assembly notes
- Two people recommended for steady alignment of rods
- Levelers on the legs help with uneven floors
- A small amount of silicone lubricant on the rod bearings can improve glide but avoid overdoing it
- Keep spare screws and washers in a dedicated container to avoid the classic missing piece scenario
