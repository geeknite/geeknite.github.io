### Maintenance and care during peak use
Foosball is surprisingly fussy about cleanliness. The balls pick up dust and lint, the rods collect fingerprints, and the playfield benefits from a quick wipe-down after rough games. A soft microfiber cloth with a mild cleaner does wonders. Avoid aggressive chemical cleaners that could weaken the surface texture over time. Lubricate the rods lightly if they feel sticky after intense sessions; do not overdo it, or you’ll turn your play into a skating rink for the ball. Regular inspection of leg levelers keeps the table stable and stops those “we’re playing in a mountain range” vibes.
