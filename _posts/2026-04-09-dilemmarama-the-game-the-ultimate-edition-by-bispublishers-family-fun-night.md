Gameplay Loop: How the Dilemmas Run
-----------------------------------
At its core, Dilemmarama is a negotiation and storytelling game. A dilemma card presents a scenario, sometimes with two dilemmas that feel equally tempting (or equally terrible). Each player pitches a solution or a stance, backed by a short rationale. Then players vote on whose reasoning is the most convincing or who best embodies the character or role being played. That’s the engine: persuasion, empathy, and a dash of theatricality.

Role cards add a little spice. A role might grant you a bonus for a particular kind of argument or restrict you to a specific ethical framework, nudging you toward certain outcomes. That’s where the game shines: the roles encourage you to see your friends and family in different lights, which can be absolutely hilarious or mildly terrifying depending on your family dynamics.
