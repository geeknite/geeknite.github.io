Tips to Maximize Battery Lifespan
---------------------------------
- Calibrate after installation: A quick full discharge and recharge cycle helps the Quest Pro learn the new battery’s capacity.
- Avoid deep discharges: Do not regularly drop to zero before recharging; top up when practical.
- Use a quality charger: A USB-C PD charger around 30W or higher is ideal for fast, safe charging.
- Store smartly: If you’re storing the headset for extended periods, keep the battery around 40–60% charge and in a cool, dry place.
- Airflow matters: Keep the headset in a well-ventilated area during charging to prevent heat buildup that can degrade longevity.
