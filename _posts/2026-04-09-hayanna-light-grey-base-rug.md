Durability and Cleaning
Let’s be honest: life happens. Spills are not if, but when. Hayanna is designed with stain resistance in mind, which means you can spill red wine, curry, or a suspicious sauce and still pretend you didn’t see it until the next morning. Quick dab with a clean cloth and a gentle detergent is usually enough; for tougher stains, blotting and a mild cleaning solution will do the trick without dissolving your ambitions.

Maintenance tips:
- Blot, don’t rub. Rubbing is a one-way ticket to a frayed edge and a dramatic sigh.
- Use a rug pad to prevent curling and to add a little extra cushion underfoot.
- Vacuum regularly, but if you have a rollerThatBetterNotBeYourNeighbor vacuum, use it on low power to avoid fiber meltdown.
