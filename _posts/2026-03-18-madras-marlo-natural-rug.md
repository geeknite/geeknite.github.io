## Care & Maintenance
Natural fiber rugs require routine care. Vacuum regularly using a gentle setting. Avoid heavy suction that can pull fibers. For stains, blot with a clean cloth and treat with a mild cleaning solution approved for natural fibers. Do not rub vigorously. If the rug gets smudged from a curious toddler or a clumsy adult, you can spot clean and air dry. Note that natural fiber rugs may shed a little at first; this is normal and typically subsides within a few weeks with regular care. For odor control, light maintenance like baking soda can help, but avoid overuse as it can cause a white residue.

### Weekly Care Routine
- Vacuum once a week with a low-suction setting
- Rotate the rug every few months to even out wear
- Spot clean spills immediately and blot not rub
- Use a rug pad to reduce friction and extend life
