Storage matters. Blue Bicycle decks, especially with such rich blue hues, can pick up moisture and warp if stored poorly. The recommended approach is to keep them in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. The key is to handle them with dry hands and to avoid sweaty grips mid-performance; nothing ruins a dramatic reveal faster than a damp deck sliding out of your fingers like a slippery eel.
