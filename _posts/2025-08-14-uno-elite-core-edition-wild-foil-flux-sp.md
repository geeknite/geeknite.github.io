## How to play with Flux SP at your table
If you want to implement Flux SP in a smooth, non chaotic way, here are a few house rules that can help:

- Start with vanilla UNO for the first round, then introduce Flux SP cards gradually to avoid your table becoming a war zone.
- Use a 2 minute rule to explain Flux SP effects to new players. The goal is to be inclusive, not to trigger a mini protest in the kitchen.
- If you want a high chaos session, allow Flux SP cards to be played at any time during a turn, but impose a reward for creative use; maybe the player who successfully triggers a Flux SP combination gets an extra turn.

When in doubt, lean into the fun. UNO by nature is a social game. The Elite Core Edition invites more social negotiation and a few more opportunities for dramatic preening as you claim how you saved the table with a perfectly timed Flux SP move.
