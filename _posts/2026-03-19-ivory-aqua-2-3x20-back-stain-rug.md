## Care and Maintenance Recommendations
- Vacuum regularly to prevent dirt buildup and preserve color. 
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing to prevent fiber fraying. 
- For liquid spills, use a light detergent solution and test in an inconspicuous area first. 
- Consider a rug pad for added cushion and underfoot stability, especially in high traffic zones. 
- Schedule professional cleaning when needed, particularly for stubborn stains that refuse to yield to DIY methods.
