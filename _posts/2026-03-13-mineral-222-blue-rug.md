## Cleaning and Care: A Practical Guide

- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to maintain pile height and avoid flattening the surface.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth. Avoid aggressive rubbing which can push stains deeper into the fibers.
- For spot cleaning, use a mild detergent diluted in water; test a small area first to ensure no color bleed.
- If you need deep cleaning, consult a professional rug cleaner who understands synthetic fibers and mineral-inspired dyes. Do not throw the rug into a hot dryer; air-drying is preferable.
- Protect the rug from direct sunlight to minimize color fading over time, especially if your space is sun-drenched for extended periods.
