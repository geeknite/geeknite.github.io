## Introduction
City of Mist: Urban Legends - Sourcebook lands at the intersection of neon-soaked noir and myths that haunt the city’s shadows. It’s the kind of book that makes you want to doodle your own legend on a coffee-stained napkin and pretend you’re a GM with a better tie than your players have excuses. In Geeknite’s house style, we approach this with equal parts admiration and playful skepticism, because a good supplement should spark ideas without making you memorize a second constitution.

### What this book is trying to be
Urban Legends is a GM-focused expansion that grants you more legends, more masks, more locale prompts, and more ways to weave myth into daily life. The tone remains true to City of Mist: it’s not about godlike fighters; it’s about ordinary people who become extraordinary through mythic connections. The Sourcebook doesn’t wipe the slate clean; it gives you stamps, stickers, and guidance to re-theme your campaign toward urban mythic storytelling.

### What it isn’t
It isn’t a campaign in a box. It isn’t a new edition. It isn’t a skip-to-the-boss-battle manual. It’s a toolbox for the GM who loves exploring the city’s mythic layers and who wants a few narrative levers to pull during a game night.

## The meat: what’s inside

### New legends and Mythos options
The core of the book sits on a shelf of new legends: characters who are shaped by the city and whose powers often reflect the stake they hold in a particular block or district. Each Legend includes: origin sketch, ties to neighborhoods, a set of unique abilities, suggested antagonists, and a handful of scene prompts. The writing is strong; the ideas are punchy. Expect a subway ghost who negotiates transit debts with rumor merchants, a newspaper clerk who can separate truth from rumor by listening to the city’s heartbeat, and a street musician who channels weather into melodies that reveal hidden entrances.

### More masks for investigations
Masks are the lenses through which players interpret the world. Urban Legends adds several new masks that fit the game’s tone while giving players new ways to express their identities and to trade in mythic capital. The masks aren’t just flavour; they influence how scenes unfold. Examples include The Mirror and The Circuit, both of which unlock narrative traps and counterplay options that players can leverage for dramatic reveals and clever solves.

### Tools for the GM: seeds, cues, and micro-arcs
Here the book truly shines. You’ll find modular arc seeds that you can deploy in a single session or stitch into longer campaigns. The seeds come with: recommended antagonists, conflict arcs, and payoff notes. There are locale cards describing districts with keyed rumors, danger nodes, and local myths. Encounter prompts, ready-to-use for impromptu sessions, help you escalate tension without re-planning. The GM’s toolbox includes micro-arcs that you can weave across a city-wide narrative, plus a few “twist cards” to pivot when your plan runs out of steam.

### The art of city-building: layout and visuals
The page layout is clean and navigable. There’s a readability boost to the book’s structure that makes it easy to scan for the right prompts in a crunch moment. The colors reflect the neon glow that defines the game’s mood, and the imagery supports the tone without overshadowing the text. The art direction feels purposeful, with a clear intent to evoke mood while giving space for GM and player imagination.

### Playstyle notes: balancing density with pacing
Urban Legends is dense, but not in a way that shuts you down. The best approach is to cherry-pick what your table can handle and then build from there. The book provides guidance for pacing, so you can escalate a session in measured steps rather than dropping everything at once. If your group loves sprawling arcs, you’ll enjoy the breadth; if you’re more tuned to intimate moments, you can still harvest quiet, character-driven scenes within its content.

### Sample encounters and narrative hooks
To give you a sense of how this plays in practice, Urban Legends includes a handful of ready-to-run moments designed to be dropped into existing arcs. Picture a rooftop chase where a mythic entity negotiates a deadline with a journalist who can see living cross-stitches in fabric. Or a late-night hospital corridor where a legend’s power leaks through the ward’s architecture, forcing investigators to choose between saving a patient and closing a gate to a different dimension. Each hook comes with escalation steps, so you’ll know what to push next session.

### Tone, safety, and safety notes
The book does not shy away from mature themes, but it treats them with care. There are content notes and optional safety guidelines to help you handle sensitive topics responsibly at the table. It’s a modern packet for the era of safety tools and consent in role-playing games, which is an essential feature for a game that thrives on personal stakes and intimate character moments.

### System mechanics and balance in practice
Urban Legends preserves City of Mist’s core resolution mechanic: a die pool that rewards narrative logic and mythic flair over raw damage output. The new content introduces additional layers that can complement or overwhelm if used greedily. The recommendation is to introduce a couple of legends per session and monitor the table’s energy. If you’re a GM who enjoys weighty, myth-informed decisions, you’ll find plenty of interesting options without feeling overwhelmed.

### How this compares with the core book
If you loved the base game’s vibe, Urban Legends amplifies it with more texture and a richer set of prompts to drive sessions. It’s less about system novelty and more about narrative scaffolding. For players, the new masks and legends provide fresh avenues for character arcs; for GMs, the seeds and locale cards offer a faster path to an engaging city night rather than hours of pre-game planning.

### Thematic reflections: myth in the mundane
What Urban Legends captures better than most expansions is the sense that myths are not monuments but living, breathing things that respond to the city’s pulse. The legends are not distant gods; they’re figures who might be seen in a coffee shop or heard in a subway car. The Sourcebook treats the modern city as a living myth machine that’s always on the verge of waking up at the wrong moment.

### Practical advice for new and returning players
If you’re new to City of Mist, approach Urban Legends as a cooperative writing prompt. Don’t try to memorize every legend or every rule. Instead, pick two or three threads that feel alive and build from those. If you’re returning after playing the core book, you’ll appreciate the density of ideas but still have enough structure to keep sessions flowing.

### Final notes: value, accessibility, and recommendation
There’s a lot to like here. Urban Legends offers a robust toolkit, a clean layout, and a myth-forged urban canvas that you can paint with the night’s glow. It’s not a replacement for your core game; it’s a supplement that expands your storytelling options and gives you a wider palette for mood and tension.
