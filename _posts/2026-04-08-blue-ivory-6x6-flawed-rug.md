### Spills and Stains
- Immediate action: Blot spills with a clean cloth. Do not rub, because rubbing is basically the rug telling you, \"I am not your enemy, I am your responsibility.\" If you spill something like coffee or juice, dab gently and work outward from the center. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners; a mild detergent solution is typically sufficient.
- Tough stains: For more stubborn stains, test a small inconspicuous area first. If the stain is persistent, you may consider a professional cleaning service or a spot-cleaning approach recommended by the retailer. ### Maintenance Tips
