## Quick Start Guide
If you’re a time-crunched host who wants to start a game without a formal training session, here’s a brisk, no-fluff quick-start:

1) Shuffle the black and white decks. 2) Decide who will be Card Czar for the first round. 3) The Card Czar draws a black card and reads it aloud. 4) Each player chooses one white card in secret and hands it to the Card Czar. 5) The Card Czar reads all candidate responses and selects a winner. 6) Repeat with the next player as Card Czar until you hit your target score.

Pro tip: Use the quick-start guide in the box to minimize downtime. You’ll get through rounds faster and avoid those awkward silences that sometimes occur when someone tries to explain the scoring in the middle of a party.
