## Maintenance Mindset: Cleaning, Care, and Long-Term Charm

Aesthetics aside, a rug’s life hinges on maintenance. Here are practical routines for the Serenade Shilo Silver Rug:

- Vacuum weekly with a standard setting to prevent particle buildup that dulls the reflective fibers.
- Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper into the pile.
- For more stubborn stains, a mild detergent solution and a test patch in an inconspicuous corner will tell you if color bleed or texture change is likely.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and to avoid “sunny spots” where daylight can fade the fibers slightly.

For the eco-conscious among us, consider rotating your rug to preserve its color balance across seasons and light exposure. It’s a small ritual that pays off in long-term visual consistency.
