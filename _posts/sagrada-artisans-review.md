## What is Sagrada: Artisans?

### Game Components

First things first, let's talk about the components. The game comes with a stunning board, colorful dice, player pieces shaped like stained glass tools, and a deck of cards representing various artistic elements. The artwork is breathtaking, capturing the essence of stained glass artistry.

### Gameplay

Sagrada: Artisans is a dice-drafting and window-crafting game where players take on the role of master artisans competing to create the most beautiful stained glass window. Each turn involves drafting dice and placing them on your player board to meet specific pattern requirements and score points.

**Pro Tip:** Think twice before placing that purple die. The wrong move could shatter your hopes of creating a masterpiece.

### Strategic Depth

Unlike its predecessor, Artisans introduces more intricate patterns and objectives that require careful planning and foresight. The game encourages players to think several steps ahead, balancing immediate needs with long-term goals.

**Criticism Alert:** The increased complexity might be daunting for newcomers. However, if you're up for the challenge, the rewards are well worth the effort.

Whether you prefer solo gaming or competing with friends, Sagrada: Artisans caters to all preferences. The solo mode offers a satisfying challenge, while the multiplayer mode provides endless fun and strategic battles.
