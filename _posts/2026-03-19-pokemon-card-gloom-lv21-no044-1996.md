## Authenticity and Preservation: Care Tips for the 1996 Era
- Handling: Use soft sleeves and a gentle touch. Avoid finger oils or wet fingers; your 1996 gem deserves a spa day, not a smudge party.
- Storage: Maintain a cool, dry shelf. UV exposure is the enemy of any true vintage; keep your binder under glass or in a climate-controlled cabinet if you can swing it.
- Grading: If you plan to send LV.21 No. 044 to a grading service, document its condition carefully, photograph it against a neutral background, and keep your original packaging (if present) intact. The more you treat it like a time capsule, the more you’ll enjoy the process of preserving a tiny piece of 1996 for future generations who will stand in awe of your binder sleeves.
