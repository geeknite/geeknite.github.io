## Comfort, Maintenance, and Everyday Use
### Comfort underfoot and real-world testing
Foot comfort isn’t just luxury; it’s a design decision with practical consequences. The Carvan rug offers a pleasant texture for daily life, with enough cushion to feel indulgent during couch-to-floor transitions but not so much that you need a forklift to move around. It’s ideal for family life, gaming marathons, and casual work-from-home stints where you want a soft perch for a laptop and a spot to rest your feet during a long Zoom call.

### Cleaning and care basics
Life happens: spills, crumbs, pet hair, and the occasional coffee mishap. The Carvan rug responds well to routine care. Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting and rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and consistent color exposure. For spills, blot gently with a clean cloth; avoid rubbing, which can push stains deeper into the pile. Use a mild stain remover if necessary, following the manufacturer’s instructions. Do not saturate the fibers—excess moisture can cause shrinkage or color bleeding. For persistent stains, consult a professional rug cleaner who specializes in synthetic or blended fibers. Proactive care typically keeps the rug looking fresh for years rather than months.
