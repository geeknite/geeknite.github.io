## Comfort, Maintenance, and Everyday Use
### Comfort underfoot and foot traffic reality check

### Cleaning and care basics
Life happens. Spills, crumbs, and the occasional pet mishap will occur. The good news is that the Carvan rug responds well to routine care. Regular vacuuming with a low suction setting helps remove dust and hair, while rotating the rug every few months helps ensure even wear and color exposure. For spills, blot gently with a clean cloth, avoid rubbing, and use a mild stain remover if necessary, following the manufacturer instructions. Do not over-wet the fibers, as excess moisture can cause shrinkage or color bleeding. For stubborn stains, consult a professional rug cleaner who specializes in synthetic or mixed fiber rugs. A little proactive maintenance goes a long way toward keeping the rug looking fresh for years rather than months.

### Real world usage scenarios

## Maintenance and Longevity
### Routine care and long term expectations
A rug is a long-term commitment, especially a 240x320 piece that becomes part of the daily rhythm. Expect a simple cleaning ritual: vacuum weekly, rotate quarterly, and address stains promptly. If you have a pet that tends to choose certain spots as their favorite lapping zone, consider a protective pad underneath or a strategic furniture layout to minimize wear. Lightly brush or shake out the rug if you notice a drift of fibers in one direction; this helps restore the pile to its original loft without chemical intervention. Over time, color and texture subtly adjust to your living pattern. This is not a showroom fixture; it is a living, helpful part of your home that will learn your routine and reward you with consistent performance.
