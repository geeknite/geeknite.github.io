## Customization and Sizing
- Sizes available typically include common dimensions (e.g., 4x6 ft, 5x8 ft, 6x9 ft, 8x10 ft) with the possibility of custom scaling.
- You can specify edge finishes and choose between a fringed edge, a clean bound edge, or a braided border.

## Maintenance and Longevity
- Wool rugs are known for resilience and natural stain resistance. For wool, immediate blotting of spills is essential, followed by a gentle vacuum on a low setting. Avoid high-suction machines that pull the fibers excessively; a hand-held or low-power vacuum works well.
- For deeper cleaning, professional rug cleaning is recommended every couple of years, depending on traffic. A careful approach helps preserve the texture and ensures longevity.
- Rotation can help wear evenly—the same way you rotate game strategy in a tabletop RPG campaign.
