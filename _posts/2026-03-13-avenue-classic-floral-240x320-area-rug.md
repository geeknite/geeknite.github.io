## Cleaning, Maintenance, and Longevity Tips
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling up fibers.
- Wipe up spills promptly to minimize staining and avoid set-in marks.
- Use rug pads to improve stability and prevent slipping on hard floors.
- Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear and color preservation.
- For stubborn stains, test a small, inconspicuous area first and consider professional cleaning for delicate finishes.

## Maintenance Philosophy: Keeping It Fresh Over Time
- Inspect the rug after heavy use for any loose strands or frayed edges; address promptly to prevent unraveling.
- Use a rug pad to reduce movement, which helps prevent curling edges that can become tripping hazards.

- Protect from direct sun exposure for extended periods to prevent color fading; rotate the rug if possible to even out exposure.
