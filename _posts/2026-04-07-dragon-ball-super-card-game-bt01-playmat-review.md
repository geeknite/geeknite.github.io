The BT01 Playmat's material choices are designed with longevity in mind. The microfibers resist pilling and keep their smooth surface after multiple sessions. The laminate layer on the top helps guard against minor spills, grease, and the inevitable finger oils that come with clutch plays. ### Care and Longevity Tips
- Do not machine wash or soak the mat; this can loosen stitching and degrade the print.
- Wipe with a soft, damp cloth to remove fingerprints or minor spills; air dry fully before rolling.
- For long-term storage, roll the mat rather than folding it to minimize crease lines that could become permanent.
- Keep away from sharp objects and heat sources that could warp the rubber base or stretch the fabric.

### Common Mistakes and How to Avoid Them
- Storing the mat in damp or hot conditions can warp the edges and degrade the surface across months of storage.
- Dropping heavy items on the mat during setup can cause indentations; use a soft table cover or a protective mat underneath when transporting.
- Using rough or abrasive cleaners can damage the surface; stick to mild soap and water or a dedicated microfiber cleaner.
