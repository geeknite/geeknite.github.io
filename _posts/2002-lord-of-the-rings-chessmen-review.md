## What's in the Box?

The 2002 Lord of the Rings Chessmen set includes 32 intricately designed chess pieces inspired by characters from J.R.R. Tolkien's legendary saga. The pieces are made from high-quality materials and feature exquisite details that capture the essence of each character. From Aragorn to Frodo, every piece is a miniature work of art.

### The Good Guys

On one side, we have the forces of good, led by Aragorn as the King, Gandalf as the Queen (well, there's a twist for you), and Frodo as the Bishop. The pawns are represented by the brave soldiers of Gondor. Each piece is meticulously crafted to reflect the noble spirit of Middle-earth's heroes.

### The Bad Guys

Facing them are the forces of evil, with the Dark Lord Sauron as the King, the Witch-king of Angmar as the Queen (gender roles are a bit flexible in Middle-earth), and Saruman as the Bishop. The pawns are the fearsome Orcs. These pieces are equally impressive, embodying the malevolent power of their dark counterparts.
