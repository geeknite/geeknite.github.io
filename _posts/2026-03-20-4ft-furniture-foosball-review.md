## Maintenance, Durability, and Ongoing Care

Like any furniture piece that doubles as a game station, the 4 ft foosball table benefits from occasional maintenance. Here are the practical tips we’ve gathered from our sessions:

- Wipe down after use: a quick microfiber wipe to remove fingerprints and coffee rings keeps the surface looking fresh. Do not use harsh cleaners; the surface finish is friendly, not fearless.
- Check leg levelness: if you notice the table wobbling during a crucial shot, adjust the feet to restore a level field. Stability matters more than you think when you’re in mid-trennel of a heated match.
- Inspect rods and bearings: look for slight looseness or creaks; a small drop of silicone spray on the joints (applied carefully) can dramatically improve smoothness.
- Cup holders: empty and clean them occasionally to prevent sticky residue from building up. If you have kids, you know the ritual: one cup, two cup, three cup, all clean.

Durability-wise, the table holds up under normal family use and occasional game-night mayhem. It isn’t a heavy-duty industrial unit, but it makes reasonable compromises between affordability and longevity. If you crave a table with professional-grade heft, you might want to step up to a larger model—but you will pay a premium and acquire a larger footprint.
