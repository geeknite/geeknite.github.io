The print remained crisp, though the edges sometimes curled a bit when the blanket was left in a heap on the chair, giving me the vibe of a dragon in suspended animation. It’s not drama queen-level drama; it’s a nap-friendly shape-shifter.

### Washing, Drying, and Storage Notes

If you’re the organizational type, you’ll want to fold it neatly; if you’re me, you’ll toss it into the laundry pile and hope for the best—spoiler: hope wins, but only this round. Avoid high heat to preserve colors, and if you’re planning to rehome it into a closet for the summer, a light folding on a breathable hanger works fine.
