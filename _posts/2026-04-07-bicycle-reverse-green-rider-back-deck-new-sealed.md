## Maintenance, Care, and Longevity

Caring for playing cards isn’t glamorous, but it pays off in the long run. Here are a few tips to keep your Bicycle Reverse Green deck performing at its best:

- Keep them dry. Humidity and moisture can cause warping and edge chatter. A simple dry environment for storage goes a long way.
- Avoid extreme heat. Heat can affect the finish and make the cards more prone to sticking when you riffle.
- Clean hands. Oils and fingerprints can accumulate, reducing a deck’s glide. A quick wipe with a clean microfiber cloth when needed is sufficient.
- Use a case. A small card case or a padded container protects the deck during travel and storage. If you’re a traveler, you’ll appreciate the extra protection a case provides after a long session.

If you’re serious about card projection or live performance, consider using a dusting spray or light corner guard for longer sessions. These are optional accessories that some performers use to extend the life of their stock, especially when they perform in environments with dust or loose fibers in the air.
