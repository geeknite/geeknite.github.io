## Maintenance, care, and lifespan of your white dice
Fortune favors the careful player, and a little upkeep goes a long way toward keeping your dice readable and presentable. Here are a few practical tips to maximize lifespan and readability:

- Cleaning: wipe down with a soft, damp cloth after long sessions. If you’re a neat freak, a quick wipe with a mild soap solution is perfectly fine; ensure no moisture sits on the board after drying.
- Storage: keep them in a dedicated tray or bag to avoid chipping and to prevent them from rolling into couch cushions or the mysterious under-shelf abyss.
- Handling: avoid dropping dice on metal or rocky surfaces where they could take a microscopic dent. On a plastic or wooden table, they should survive most practical gaming marathons without incident.
- Reading: if you notice the pips becoming faint after heavy use, consider a light re-inking with a permanent marker designed for dice, but proceed with care and test on a spare die first. This is a last-resort move, not a recommended routine.
