## Care and maintenance: keeping the gear sharp
Maintenance is the dull but essential part of owning any gaming kit. Here are practical tips to keep your set looking and performing well for as long as you want to keep showing off at the table:

- Wipe the case with a soft microfiber cloth to remove fingerprints and dust. Aluminum surfaces look great when polished, and they stay that way with gentle care rather than harsh solvents that could dull the finish.
- Avoid exposing the chips to direct sunlight for extended periods. Prolonged sun exposure can cause color fading or warping on some chip materials. If you have a sunlit gaming nook, consider a quick towel over the surface or a stylish blinds setup that keeps your chips vibrant.
- Store the deck and chips securely in their trays when not in use. A little organization goes a long way in preventing misplacement and ensures you do not spend ten minutes searching for the red chip on a busy night.
- Inspect the case latches periodically. If they start to feel loose or wobbly, a light tightening can prevent the lid from popping open mid play. Do not overtighten, or you may strip the threads and create a drama you do not want in your gaming life.
