### Setup tips for optimal results
- Ensure the guides are snug to the width of the paper. Loose guides cause skew and misfeeds.
- Do not overload beyond the stated capacity; it invites jams that will test your philosophy of patience.
- Run a small calibration batch after installation to verify alignment with your scanner or printer.
- If you are using coated or glossy stock, test a few sheets first as these can reflect drop-in alignment and cause feed discrepancies.
- Clean the entry rollers occasionally; a light wipe with a dry lint-free cloth maintains crisp contact with the stock.

Under normal usage, the EL300952 shows good longevity.
