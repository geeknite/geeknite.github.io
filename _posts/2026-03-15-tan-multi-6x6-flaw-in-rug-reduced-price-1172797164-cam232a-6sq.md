### Cleaning and Maintenance
Maintenance is where budgets and sanity align in rug land. The general rule for polypropylene is regular vacuuming and quick response to stains. The TAN MULTI CAM232A-6SQ should respond well to standard rug shampoos if you want to refresh the fibers every so often.
