## Maintenance and Care
Outdoor furniture is a love-hate relationship with the weather. You love that it brings people together; you hate that rain can ruin the finish and sap the color over time. Here are practical tips to keep this set looking fresh for multiple summers:

- Clean regularly with a mild soap solution and soft brush to remove dirt and pollen. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the finish.
- Inspect joints and hardware annually; tighten anything that feels loose and replace rust-prone fasteners with stainless steel equivalents.
- Protect cushions with removable covers, and store cushions indoors during heavy rain or winter months to extend cushion life.
- Consider applying a protective sealant or outdoor wood oil designed for acacia to maintain moisture resistance and color. Reapply as recommended by the product instructions.

With regular care, your acacia set will continue to age like a fine wine rather than a wilted salad leaf.
