## Maintenance and longevity tips
- Keep the decks away from moisture; the linen finish can snag if the cards get damp. A quick wipe with a dry cloth can help maintain surface friction after a long gaming session.
- Store the decks in a cool, dry place. Heat can cause slight warping over time, especially if you carry them on trips in the summer sun.
- Avoid washing the cards with water or cleaners; the ink is not designed to tolerate moisture. If you must clean, use a mild, dry microfiber cloth and air dry.
- If the corners start to fray after heavy use, consider a light corner rub with a soft eraser to reset the edges; cosmetic only, not structural.
