## What is Empire of Man Battalion: The Old World?

Empire of Man Battalion: The Old World is a strategy board game set in a richly detailed and immersive world. Players take on the roles of commanders leading their battalions to victory through cunning tactics, diplomacy, and occasionally, sheer dumb luck. The game combines elements of traditional wargaming with fantastical lore, creating an experience that's both challenging and wildly entertaining.

### The Basics

In Empire of Man Battalion: The Old World, players must manage resources, deploy units, and engage in epic battles. The goal is to dominate the map, crush your enemies, and maybe even make a few friends along the way (before inevitably betraying them for strategic advantage).

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Miniatures**: Detailed figures representing different units and heroes. Some are so intricate you'll consider taking up painting miniatures as a new hobby.
- **Cards**: Each deck contains spells, tactics, and events that can turn the tide of battle.
- **Game Board**: A beautifully illustrated map of the Old World, featuring mountains, forests, and strategically important locations.
- **Tokens and Markers**: Used to track resources, unit statuses, and more. Just try not to lose any pieces to the carpet monster.

**Tip**: Keep snacks away from the table. No one wants greasy fingerprints on their meticulously painted miniatures.

## How to Play

### Setup

Setting up Empire of Man Battalion is akin to preparing for a medieval feast. It requires careful placement of units, distribution of cards, and perhaps a quick refresher on the rulebook (or two). Once everything is in place, it's time to plan your first move.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players can move their units, engage in combat, cast spells, and perform various actions based on their cards. The game requires strategic planning and adaptability, as your opponents will constantly try to outmaneuver you.

### Combat

Combat in Empire of Man Battalion is a blend of strategy and chance. Dice rolls determine the outcomes of battles, but clever tactics and the right combination of units can significantly tip the odds in your favor.
