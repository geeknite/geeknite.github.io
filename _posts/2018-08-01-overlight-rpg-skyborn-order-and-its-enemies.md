## The World of Overlight

### What is Overlight RPG?

Overlight RPG is a role-playing game set in a world beautifully constructed with layers of colors and dimensions, known as the Seven Realms. Players take on the roles of Skyborn, beings who harness the power of the light to navigate these realms, solve mysteries, and battle foes.

### Components

The game comes with stunning artwork, character sheets, dice, and a comprehensive rulebook. And let’s not forget the Game Master Screen, which is both functional and a piece of art.

## The Skyborn Order

### Becoming a Skyborn

Players can choose to become members of the Skyborn Order, harnessing different powers based on the realm they hail from. Each realm offers unique abilities, advantages, and a splash of color that complements your character’s personality.

### Skills and Abilities

From the fiery strength of Pyre to the gentle healing of Veil, each realm’s abilities are well-balanced and offer diverse gameplay experiences. Being a Skyborn means you can tailor your character’s skills to fit your preferred playstyle.

## Enemies and Challenges

### Facing Foes

The Overlight RPG isn’t just about pretty colors and peaceful exploration. You’ll face formidable enemies that challenge your combat skills and strategic thinking. Whether it’s battling the sinister forces of Umbra or negotiating with the tricky inhabitants of Zenith, there’s never a dull moment.

### Combat Mechanics

Combat in Overlight is dynamic and relies heavily on teamwork. The dice mechanics are straightforward, yet offer depth through various modifiers and abilities. Expect thrilling battles that require cooperation and cunning.

## The Game Master Screen

### The Ultimate GM Tool

The Game Master Screen is designed to aid the GM in managing the game efficiently. It includes quick reference tables, crucial game mechanics, and beautiful artwork that’ll make you want to hang it on your wall.

### Enhancing Gameplay

Using the GM Screen enhances the overall gameplay by providing the GM with easy access to important information. This allows for smoother game flow and more immersive storytelling.
