- Sort chips by denomination before game night to speed up stacking and betting. A small table rack is a nice touch but not strictly necessary.

- Establish betting structures early. If you’re playing a friendly home game, you can start with a modest buy-in and a simple structure (e.g., 25/50 with a 5 ante) to keep things moving.

- Consider color-blind accessibility: ensure that color choices have sufficient contrast, or add denomination stickers to the chips for quick reference.
