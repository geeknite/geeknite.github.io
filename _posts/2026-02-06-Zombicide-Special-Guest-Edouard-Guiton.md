### Setting Up

Setting up Zombicide: Special Guest - Edouard Guiton involves laying out the modular tiles to create your game board, distributing character cards, and preparing the zombie spawn deck. Make sure to have some snacks ready—this is going to be an intense session.

### Turns and Actions
