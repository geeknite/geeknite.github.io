## Maintenance: Cleaning, Storage, and Care Tips

### Cleaning and Handling Tips

If you are handling a sealed deck, you can minimize exposure to environmental elements by storing it in a protective box. For a vintage deck, the goal is to minimize environmental stress. Keep the deck away from humidity swings and heat sources.
