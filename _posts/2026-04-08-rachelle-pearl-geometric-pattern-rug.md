### Cleaning and maintenance tips: practical, not preachy

- Vacuum regularly with a low suction setting to avoid pulling fibers
- Blot spills immediately with paper towels; avoid rubbing to prevent fuzzing
- For stubborn spots, use a mild detergent and cool water, then air dry
- Avoid direct sun for long periods to minimize fading
- Rotate periodically to ensure even wear across high-traffic areas
