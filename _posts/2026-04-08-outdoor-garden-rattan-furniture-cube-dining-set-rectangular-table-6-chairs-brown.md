## Care, Maintenance, and Longevity Tips

- Regular cleaning: wipe down every week with a mild soap solution and a soft cloth. Avoid abrasive sponges that could dull the weave.
- Cushion care: if cushions are included, use removable covers and wash according to label directions. If not, consider fabric covers in seasonal colors for added protection.
- Weather plans: in extreme weather, store cushions indoors and cover the set with a breathable outdoor cover to extend life.
- Storing cushions: when not in use for extended periods, store cushions in a dry, ventilated space to prevent mildew.
- UV protection: while resin weaves are UV resistant, you can extend the life of the finish by rotating cushions and using protective covers during peak sun hours.
