## How to play in five minutes or less
### Setup
Players draft stock piles and build piles, then draw from the central pile. The goal is to empty your stock pile by playing cards in ascending order on the building piles. If you draw a wild skip card you can substitute any number. The first player to deplete their stock pile wins the round.

### The core rules in plain language
- Move cards from your hand to the open build piles if they fit the next number.
- Use wild Skip-Bo cards to fill gaps or extend sequences. They are teleports for your strategy, and sometimes the table's collective hope.
- Draw up to your hand limit when your turn comes around, then decide whether to press your luck with a risky play or play it safe.

### Strategy quick-start tips
Think ahead by two turns, not two games. Watch your opponents for patterns and try to break their plans with a well-timed Skip-Bo play. Remember, the length of your game night is a marathon, not a sprint; pacing is everything.
