## The Basics

### What is Conflict of Heroes: Storms of Steel?

Conflict of Heroes: Storms of Steel! 3rd Edition is a tactical board game that simulates the battles on the Eastern Front during World War II. This iteration promises enhanced mechanics, improved components, and enough historical detail to make a history professor shed a tear of joy.

### Components

The game comes with:
- **Maps**: Large, double-sided maps depicting various terrains and battlefields.
- **Counters**: Hundreds of counters representing infantry, vehicles, and terrain features.
- **Cards**: Action cards that drive the gameplay and add strategic depth.
- **Dice**: Because what's a war game without a bit of randomness?

## Gameplay

### Setup

Setting up Conflict of Heroes: Storms of Steel is like preparing for a major offensive. You've got maps to lay out, counters to organize, and rulebooks to consult. It's a ritual that might take longer than some actual battles.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players alternate activating units to move, attack, or perform special actions. The action cards add a layer of strategy, as you must decide the best moment to use them to gain an advantage.

### Combat

Combat is resolved by rolling dice and consulting tables. It's a blend of strategy, luck, and occasionally shouting, "Why does my tank always miss?!"

## The Story

### Scenarios

Conflict of Heroes: Storms of Steel comes with a variety of scenarios that recreate historical battles. From the initial German blitzkrieg to the Soviet counteroffensive, each scenario offers unique challenges and requires different strategies.

### Historical Accuracy

One of the game's strengths is its attention to historical detail. The scenarios are meticulously researched, and the game mechanics strive to represent real-world tactics and conditions.
