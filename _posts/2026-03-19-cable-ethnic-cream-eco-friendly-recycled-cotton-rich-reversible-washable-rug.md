## Care and Maintenance Without Losing Your Mind
Care is where the grown-ups and the geeks unite. A rational maintenance routine pays dividends over time:

- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting to avoid pulling threads. Think of it as a firmware update for your living space.
- Blot spills promptly; rubbing often makes things worse by pushing the stain deeper and fuzzing the fibers.
- For stains, a mild detergent solution works well. Always test on a hidden corner first—privacy for your rug is a thing.
- Wash on a gentle cycle with cold water. Hot water can shrink fibers and trigger an emotional crisis in the cable-knit texture.
- Air dry flat to maintain shape and prevent warping. The aim is crisp ridges, not soggy noodles of despair.

The result is a rug that rewards a simple care routine with maximum everyday comfort. It’s not a maintenance diva; it’s a pragmatic, friendly addition that fits busy lives, apartment living, and late-night debugging escapades.
