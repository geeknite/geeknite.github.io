### Care and Maintenance Without Losing Your Mind
Care is where the geeks unite with the grown-ups. A rational approach to maintenance pays off in a big way over time:

- Vacuum weekly with a low-suction setting to avoid pulling threads. Think of it as a firmware update for your living space.
- Blot spills promptly; avoid rubbing, which only rubs the problem in and can cause fuzzing. Rubbing is for your ego, not for rug resilience.
- For stains, a mild detergent solution works well. Always test on a hidden corner first—your recycled cotton deserves a privacy screen too.
- When washing, use a gentle cycle with cold water. Hot water can shrink fibers and cause a dramatic “unexpected shrinkage” scene.
- Air dry flat to maintain shape and prevent warping. The goal is to keep those cable-knit ridges crisp rather than crushed into a soggy noodle of despair.

The result is a rug that rewards minimal ritual care with maximum everyday comfort. It’s not a maintenance-heavy diva; it’s a pragmatic, friendly addition that fits busy lives, apartment living, and late-night debugging sessions with a cup of coffee that never quite lands where it should.
