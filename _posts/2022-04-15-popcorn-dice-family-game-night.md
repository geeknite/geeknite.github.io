## The Basics of Popcorn Dice

Popcorn Dice is a fast-paced, luck-based game designed for 2-6 players. The game revolves around rolling dice to make popcorn (surprise, surprise!) while avoiding burnt kernels. Think of it as cooking popcorn without the risk of setting off the smoke alarm.

### Components

The game includes:

- **Dice**: Six custom dice featuring popcorn, kernels, and burnt popcorn images.
- **Popcorn Bucket**: A cute little bucket to hold your dice (adorable and functional!).
- **Scorecards**: Sheets to track your delicious progress.
- **Rulebook**: Clear instructions with illustrations to get you popping in no time.

**Fun Fact**: The dice are designed to look like actual popcorn, making you crave a snack during gameplay. Just don't eat them!

## Gameplay

Popcorn Dice is all about rolling dice, making popcorn, and avoiding the dreaded burnt kernels. Players take turns rolling the dice and collecting popcorn. The more popcorn you collect, the higher your score. But beware of those burnt kernels—they'll spoil your batch and your score!

### Turn Phases

Each turn consists of several phases:

1. **Roll Phase**: Players roll all six dice.
2. **Popcorn Phase**: Separate the popcorn from the burnt kernels. You can choose to roll again or stop and score.
3. **Score Phase**: Record your score based on the number of popcorn dice rolled.

### Winning and Losing

To win, players must have the highest score after all rounds are complete. If you end up with more burnt kernels than popcorn, it's game over for your batch.

**Pro Tip**: Roll wisely and know when to stop. The more you roll, the higher the risk of burning your batch!
