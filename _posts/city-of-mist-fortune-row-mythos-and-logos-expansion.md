## What is City of Mist: Fortune Row?

City of Mist: Fortune Row is an expansion to the popular role-playing game *City of Mist*. This expansion introduces new Mythos and Logos cards, characters, and scenarios that plunge players deeper into the enigmatic streets of Fortune Row. Prepare for twists, turns, and plot twists that would make Sherlock Holmes scratch his head.

### Components

This expansion includes:
- **Mythos Cards**: Unleash your character's supernatural abilities with new Mythos cards. Ever wanted to wield the power of ancient gods? Now's your chance.
- **Logos Cards**: Balance your mystical abilities with real-world skills. Your detective prowess will be put to the test.
- **Character Sheets**: New characters with unique backstories and abilities. Will you be the next legendary investigator?
- **Scenario Book**: Dive into new mysteries with detailed scenarios that challenge your deductive skills.

**Pro Tip**: Keep your Mythos and Logos balanced. Too much of one, and you'll either find yourself lost in mystical madness or stuck with mundane problems.

## Gameplay

### Setting Up

Setting up Fortune Row feels like preparing for a noir film. Lay out the cards, distribute character sheets, and choose your scenario. The mood is set; now it's time to unravel the mystery.

### Turns and Actions

Each turn, players draw cards and use them to perform actions, solve clues, and confront adversaries. The synergy between your Mythos and Logos abilities is where Fortune Row shines. Plan your moves carefully; every decision could lead to a breakthrough... or a dead end.

### Combat and Investigation

Combat in Fortune Row is strategic and intense. Use your Mythos powers to gain the upper hand, while your Logos skills help you navigate the gritty streets. Remember, teamwork and strategy are your best allies in this misty world.

## The Story

### Scenarios

Fortune Row introduces new scenarios inspired by classic noir and mythological themes. From solving ancient curses to confronting shadowy figures, each scenario offers unique challenges that require both brains and brawn.

### Characters

This expansion brings a host of new characters to the mix. Play as the enigmatic detective, the mythic hero, or even the cursed scholar. Each character has distinct abilities and backstories that add depth to the game.
