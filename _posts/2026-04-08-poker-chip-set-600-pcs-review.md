## Durability and maintenance tips
- Keep chips away from liquids. A spill on a 600-piece set is a dramatic tragedy with a fog of soda and a chorus of “oh no, the blue one is ruined.” If you spill, dry immediately and rotate the deck to minimize staining on the cards.
- Clean the felt occasionally. A quick vacuum or brush keeps the surface free of dust and crumbs, which is essential for maintaining a professional look during long sessions.
- Inspect the case latches every few rounds. A loose latch can turn your traveling set into a luggage horror show. If you notice looseness, tighten or replace; you want the case to stay closed, not become a melodramatic prop.
- Replace decks as needed. If your group plays weekly for a year, you’ll likely want to swap decks a handful of times. Worn cards can cause unpredictable shuffles and misreads, which no one wants when the stakes are a takeout pizza and a soda fountain.
