If you’ve played the base Exploding Kittens, the Party Pack will feel familiar but turbocharged: faster rounds, more hands waiting for a turn, and the ever-looming threat of an exploding kitty that never gets old unless you’re the person who has to draw it. The core objective remains delightfully simple: avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten, use Defuse cards to survive, and leverage chaos cards to push misfortune toward someone else with a wink. The Party Pack leans into social play, so expect more talking, more bluffing, and more table-side one-liners that cut through dull strategy with a punchline.

- Encourage players to lean into humor rather than friction. This is a party game that thrives on energy; a tense mood collapses the moment the first pun lands.
