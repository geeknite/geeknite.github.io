## In the wild: real world usage and tips

### A week with the dashboards
I ran a campaign night with four heroes and the dashboards in attack mode. The first thing I noticed was the speed at which we could check a hero’s status between combat rounds. ### Common gotchas and how to avoid them

- Use a microfiber cloth to clean the dry erase surface between sessions. Dry erase markers can leave ghosting if you push too hard, which is not ideal when you want a fresh slate for the next round.
- If your group plays with expanded zombie variants, you may want to adapt the dashboard labels to suit the extra data points. The beauty of dry erase is you can rewrite sections to suit your play style.
