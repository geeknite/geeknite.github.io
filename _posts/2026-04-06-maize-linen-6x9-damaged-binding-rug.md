## Care, Maintenance, and the Reality of Spills

No rug review would be complete without a frank chat about upkeep. The linen-like surface is fairly durable for daily wear, but there are caveats:
- Vacuum regularly with a gentle setting to avoid pulling on the binding’s threads. A beater brush is not your friend here.
- For spills, blot—not rub. The last thing you want is a coffee ring with a story arc of its own.
- If stains stubbornly appear, treat them with a mild detergent solution and test in a discreet corner first. Avoid harsh chemicals; this is home, not a chemical reactor.
- Temperature and humidity swings affect materials differently. If you live in a place with dramatic seasonal shifts, consider rotating the rug slightly to minimize wear at the binding edges.
