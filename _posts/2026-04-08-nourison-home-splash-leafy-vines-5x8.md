## Cleaning and Maintenance
Outdoor rugs can become magnets for dirt and pollen. This rug responds well to a straightforward cleaning routine: a quick shake to remove loose debris, a rinse with a gentle garden hose, and a light scrub with a mild detergent if there are spots or kitchen splashes. Do not use harsh solvents; stick to mild soap and water. When it dries, fluff by running a gloved hand over the surface or laying it flat in a sunny spot. For persistent stains, consider using a soft-bristled brush. Always ensure the rug is fully dry before placing it back on the deck to prevent mold.
