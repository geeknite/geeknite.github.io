## Display, Storage, and Presentation Tips

A card like this deserves display gear that respects its value and its shine. Here are practical tips that will help you keep that Flux glow intact while letting the image speak for itself:
- Sleeves first, then top loaders — always. A soft sleeve reduces friction and the possibility of micro-scratches that ruin the surface sheen.
- Use a rigid top loader with a semi-rigid outer case if you plan to transport it to show events. The goal is to minimize flex and protect the foil surface from contact with abrasive surfaces.
- Consider a display binder with individual page sleeves designed for premium cards. It keeps the card protected, organized, and easy to admire without removing it from its micro-habitat.
- Lighting matters. A balanced desk lamp with adjustable brightness will reveal the Flux pattern in all its glory without washing out the image. If you can swing it, a gentle, cool light enhances the holographic aura without creating a blob of glare on the surface.
- Storage environment matters. Keep the card in a climate-controlled area away from direct sunlight. UV exposure can dull foil finishes over time, which is the collector equivalent of letting your plant photosynthesize in a sunlit window while neglecting it for weeks.
