## In the Box: Unboxing and Assembly Experience

### Assembly Timeline and Tips
- Set up a clean workspace: the floor is not a good place for tiny screws to audition for a role in your life.
- Sort parts into labeled piles: this is not a time for heroic improvisation. Small parts tend to disappear into the abyss of desk drawers, never to be seen again except in your nightmares.
- Tighten progressively: start with initial torque on cross braces, then snug up panels in a clockwise pilgrimage across the frame.
- Check alignment: if your panels look crooked, don’t force them. Loosen a bit, realign, then tighten again. The curse of misalignment is real and its name is wobble.

If you are the type who loves the ritual, you can pretend you are assembling a tiny migratory fortress rather than a workstation. It is a little meditative, a little ritualistic, and surprisingly satisfying when the last screw slides in with a satisfying click. In our test, the process took about an afternoon and a playlist that ranged from synthwave to lo-fi study vibes. Not bad for a piece of furniture that might one day host your dream startup or a very serious Zoom call where your coworker wears sunglasses indoors.
