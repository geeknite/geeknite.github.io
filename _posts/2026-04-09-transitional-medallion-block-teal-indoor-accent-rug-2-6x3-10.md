## Maintenance and Cleaning

Polypropylene rugs are relatively easy to manage. Routine vacuuming with a standard vacuum will take care of dust and crumbs. For spills, blotting (not rubbing) is key; most stains respond well to a mild soap solution. If you’re dealing with heavy traffic or persistent stains, you can perform spot-cleaning tests in an inconspicuous area before going full-on rescue mission.

### Quick care checklist

- Vacuum weekly to prevent grit buildup.
- Attend spills promptly with blotting, not scrubbing.
- Use a gentle cleaning solution—no harsh chemicals unless you’re playing a chemistry-themed scavenger hunt.
- Rotate the rug every few months to avoid uneven wear from sun exposure or the jealous glare of your cat.
