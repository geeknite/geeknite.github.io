## Authenticity, Storage, and Care Tips for Sealed Items
If you want to keep your sealed copy pristine and maximize long-term value, here are some practical tips:
- Store in a climate-controlled environment with stable humidity and temperature. Card degradation is sneaky; a few degrees and a few percent humidity swing can affect card stock over years.
- Use acid-free sleeves and proper shelving. A dark, cool shelf reduces UV exposure and helps prevent yellowing of inner materials.
- Avoid stacking heavy items on top of sealed cards. The integrity of the seal can be compromised by weight and pressure, especially if the outer box is already a little worn.
- Regularly inspect the packaging for any creeping moisture or seal loosening. If you detect a change, address it early to prevent long-term issues.
- Consider a proper grading service if you intend to flip the copy or add it to a formal collection. A professional evaluation can help with resale value and provenance.
