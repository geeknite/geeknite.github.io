## Gameplay ideas for a dynamic family night

Hold’em is ideally suited for family fun because you can scale difficulty and stakes up or down in a friendly, low-pressure environment. Here are a few ideas to keep your family night lively without spiraling into an all-night tournament:

- Family-friendly limbo poker: set a soft ceiling on bets and allow kids to go all-in for fun with their lowest-denomination chips. This creates a sense of excitement without financial pressure. It’s amazing how a tiny chip can feel like a mountain when a kid shouts all-in and their face reveals a winning grin.
