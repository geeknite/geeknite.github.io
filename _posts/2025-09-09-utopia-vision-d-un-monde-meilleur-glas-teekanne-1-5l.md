## Performance

### Brewing Experience

Brewing tea in the **Utopia Glas Teekanne** is like watching a beautiful ballet. The glass body lets you see your tea leaves dance and swirl, releasing their flavors slowly. It's mesmerizing and oddly therapeutic. Plus, the stainless steel infuser ensures that none of those leaves make their way into your cup to ruin your zen moment.

### Heat Retention

One of the standout features of this teapot is its excellent heat retention. The borosilicate glass keeps your tea warm for a surprisingly long time. Say goodbye to those lukewarm tea horror stories!

### Ease of Cleaning

Cleaning the **Utopia Glas Teekanne** is a breeze. The smooth glass surface and removable infuser mean you don't have to wrestle with tea stains. Just a quick rinse and it's ready for your next tea adventure.
