### How the new kittens change turn flow
The Streaking Kitten card can be added to your standard deck to introduce a conditional trigger. For example, drawing a Streaking Kitten might let you steal a card from someone else’s hand, or force a player to draw extra cards as a penalty for misdeed. The exact text varies depending on your edition, but the core concept remains the same: a kitten card prompts a chain of micro-choices that escalate the tension without derailing the primary meltdown you signed up for.

In practice, you’ll notice the pace of the game shifts.
