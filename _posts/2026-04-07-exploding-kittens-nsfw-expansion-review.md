## How the NSFW Expansion Changes Gameplay

The core engine of Exploding Kittens remains unchanged: you draw a card each turn, you try to avoid drawing an exploding kitten, and you use kitten-powered strategies to avoid disasters while triggering your opponents to face-card misfortune. The NSFW expansion slides into this with a few notable shifts:

- New insult and taunt cards that force players to perform or react. Some cards lean into pop culture humor, others lean into gut-level jokes. The result is a round where players not only calculate cards but also read each other’s comfort zones.
- Additional variants that encourage or require players to describe a scenario before playing a card. This can be hilariously awkward in the best way.
- A couple of riskier effects that can dramatically swing the endgame. These are designed for high-risk, high-reward moments and should be used with consent from the table.

If you prefer a more structured approach, you can implement the NSFW pack in stages rather than flipping it all at once. Start by introducing a small subset of cards and gradually fold more in as players get acquainted with the tone.
