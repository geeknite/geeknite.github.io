## The Draft Pack Experience

A highlight of this edition is the Draft Pack, a concept that nudges UNO toward the “draftable” side of card games without leaving its radical simplicity behind. How does it work in practice? You open the Draft Pack during your turn window, which becomes the arena for a mini-draft session. Each player selects a few cards that offer temporary specials or strategic twists for the main game. It’s a light-touch “let’s tinker” approach that keeps the core UNO vibes intact while injecting a touch of variety. If you’re tired of the same “play a card, draw a card, hope you don’t get stuck” loop, the Draft Pack is a friendly injection of chaos that rather excels at generating bizarre, memorable moments.
